Radiana petiolata

Radiana petiolata, commonly known as the slender leaf radish, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often cultivated for its edible leaves and roots, which have a mild, peppery flavor.

Radiana petiolata is relatively easy to grow and can be a valuable addition to home gardens.
