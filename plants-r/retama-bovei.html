Retama bovei

Retama bovei, commonly known as the Bove's retama, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, appearing in clusters.

Retama bovei is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
