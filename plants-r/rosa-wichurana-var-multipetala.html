Rosa wichurana var. multipetala

Rosa wichurana var. multipetala, commonly known as the Chinese pink or the pink rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is known for its abundant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features glossy, dark green foliage and thornless stems, making it an attractive ornamental plant. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
