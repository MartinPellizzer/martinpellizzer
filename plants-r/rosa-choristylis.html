Rosa choristylis

Rosa choristylis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in regions of South Africa, Namibia, and Botswana. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Rosa choristylis typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shrubland or woodland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
