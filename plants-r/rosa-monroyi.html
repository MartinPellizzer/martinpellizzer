Rosa monroyi

Rosa monroyi, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy white flowers that open at night, emitting a strong fragrance. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa monroyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its nocturnal blooms.
