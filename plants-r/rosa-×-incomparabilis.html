Rosa × incomparabilis

Rosa × incomparabilis, commonly known as the "Crimson Glory," is a hybrid rose species prized for its vibrant, deep red flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces large, fragrant blooms that are often double or semi-double in form, with a showy appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and valuable for its horticultural diversity.
