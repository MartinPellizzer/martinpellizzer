Rubus schlickumii

Rubus schlickumii, commonly known as Schlick's blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Rubus.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. This shrub typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has woody stems covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and appear in clusters, while its fruit is a dark purple or black berry.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
