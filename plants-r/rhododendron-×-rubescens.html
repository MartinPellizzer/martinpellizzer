Rhododendron × rubescens

Rhododendron × rubescens, commonly known as the rosebay rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink to reddish in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron × rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
