Rosa meldensis

Rosa meldensis, commonly known as the Meldens rose, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and compound leaves. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a fragrant scent, and they bloom in late summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
