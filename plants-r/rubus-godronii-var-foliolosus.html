Rubus godronii var. foliolosus

Rubus godronii var. foliolosus, commonly known as the Godron's raspberry.

This variant is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, and the fruit is a small, aggregate berry.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
