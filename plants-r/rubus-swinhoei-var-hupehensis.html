Rubus swinhoei var. hupehensis

Rubus swinhoei var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Hupeh raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
