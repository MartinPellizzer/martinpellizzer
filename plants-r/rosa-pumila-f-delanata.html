Rosa pumila f. delanata

Rosa pumila f. delanata, commonly known as the Delanata shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a deep red center. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and its stems are densely covered in short, stiff bristles. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in colder climates.
