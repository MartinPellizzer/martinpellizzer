Rosa miyoshii

Rosa miyoshii, commonly known as the Miyoshi rose, is a species native to Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are pink to red in color, often with a white base. Rosa miyoshii is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its beauty.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.
