Rosa × waitziana var. macrantha

Rosa × waitziana var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered Rosa × waitziana, is a hybrid rose species originating from China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 5 cm in diameter, typically in shades of pink or red. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period and fragrance.
