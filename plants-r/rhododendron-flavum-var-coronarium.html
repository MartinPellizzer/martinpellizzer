Rhododendron flavum var. coronarium

Rhododendron flavum var. coronarium, commonly known as the golden flame rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its role in native ecosystems.
