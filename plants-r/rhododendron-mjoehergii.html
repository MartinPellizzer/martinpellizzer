Rhododendron mjoehergii

Rhododendron mjoehergii, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

Rhododendron mjoehergii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
