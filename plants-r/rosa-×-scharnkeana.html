Rosa × scharnkeana

Rosa × scharnkeana, commonly known as the Scharnke's rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double to full blooms in shades of pink and red. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
