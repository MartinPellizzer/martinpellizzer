Ruellia putumayensis

Ruellia putumayensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian ruellia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Ecuador.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Ruellia putumayensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
