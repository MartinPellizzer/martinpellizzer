Rytigynia viridissima

Rytigynia viridissima, commonly known as greenleaf rytigynia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rytigyniaceae.

Rytigynia viridissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
