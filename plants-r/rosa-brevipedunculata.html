Rosa brevipedunculata

Rosa brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color and bloom in early summer.

Rosa brevipedunculata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
