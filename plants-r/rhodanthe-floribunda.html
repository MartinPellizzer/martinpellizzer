Rhodanthe floribunda

Rhodanthe floribunda, commonly known as the showy rhodanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with red or orange centers, which appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Rhodanthe floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
