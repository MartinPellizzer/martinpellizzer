Rubus caesius subsp. subsericeus

Rubus caesius subsp.

subsericeus, commonly known as the red raspberry. This subspecies is a perennial shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and open areas.
