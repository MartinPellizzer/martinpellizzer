Rabdosia bulleyana

Rabdosia bulleyana, commonly known as the Chinese blueweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, clustered in whorls along the stem, and bloom during the summer months.

Rabdosia bulleyana is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and aromatic properties.
