Rotala myriophylloides

Rotala myriophylloides, commonly known as the flame violet or water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright red or purple, giving it its striking appearance. The plant has a dense root system that helps stabilize water surfaces and improve water quality. It thrives in shallow, still waters with moderate to high light exposure.

Rotala myriophylloides is popular in aquascaping for its ornamental value and ease of care.
