Rosa × colpogena subsp. glabricolligata

Rosa × colpogena subsp.

glabricolligata, commonly known as the glabricolligata rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae. It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to parts of Central Asia.

The plant typically features thorned stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a fragrant aroma, and bloom in early summer.

It is cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
