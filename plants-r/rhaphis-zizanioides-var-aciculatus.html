Rhaphis zizanioides var. aciculatus

Rhaphis zizanioides var. aciculatus, commonly known as the slender reed grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or pale brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important plant for ecological studies and habitat conservation.
