Rosa afzeliana f. robusta

Rosa afzeliana f. robusta, commonly known as the robust rose, is a horticultural form of the species Rosa afzeliana.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and larger, more robust flowers compared to the typical form. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
