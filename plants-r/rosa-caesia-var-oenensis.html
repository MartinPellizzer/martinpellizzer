Rosa caesia var. oenensis

Rosa caesia var. oenensis, commonly known as the Canary Island rose, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with serrated edges and thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a rare species in botanical collections.
