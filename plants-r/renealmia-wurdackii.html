Renealmia wurdackii

Renealmia wurdackii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Renealmia wurdackii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
