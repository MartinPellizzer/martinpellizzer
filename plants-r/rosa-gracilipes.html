Rosa gracilipes

Rosa gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, arching stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in pink or white hues. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, while its fruits are aggregate fruits known as rose hips.

Rosa gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
