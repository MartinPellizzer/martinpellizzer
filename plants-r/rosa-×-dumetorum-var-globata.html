Rosa × dumetorum var. globata

Rosa × dumetorum var. globata, commonly known as the globular rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dense, rounded clusters of flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
