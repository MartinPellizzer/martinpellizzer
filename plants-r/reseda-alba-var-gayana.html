Reseda alba var. gayana

Reseda alba var. gayana, commonly known as white reseda, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or disturbed areas.

Reseda alba var. gayana is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
