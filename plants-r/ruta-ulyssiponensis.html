Ruta ulyssiponensis

Ruta ulyssiponensis, commonly known as Ulysses' rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnate leaves and small yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.

Its fragrance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
