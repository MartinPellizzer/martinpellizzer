Ruschia lutea

Ruschia lutea, commonly known as the yellow Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically green and covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Ruschia lutea is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
