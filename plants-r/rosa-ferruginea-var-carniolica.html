Rosa ferruginea var. carniolica

Rosa ferruginea var. carniolica, commonly known as the Carpathian rose, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its foliage turns a striking orange-red in the autumn, adding to its ornamental value.

It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
