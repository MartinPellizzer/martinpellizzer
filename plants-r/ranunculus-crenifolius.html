Ranunculus crenifolius

Ranunculus crenifolius, commonly known as the serratedleaf buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a basal rosette of leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a wavy or serrated edge. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals, typically appearing in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
