Rubus × megathamnus

Rubus × megathamnus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It is native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
