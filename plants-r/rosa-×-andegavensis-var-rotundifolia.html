Rosa × andegavensis var. rotundifolia

Rosa × andegavensis var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose with rounded leaves, is a hybrid rose species originating from the Andes region.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded foliage and fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of 1 to 2 meters, and is well-suited to temperate climates. It has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for garden borders or container planting.

Its botanical features include serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species.
