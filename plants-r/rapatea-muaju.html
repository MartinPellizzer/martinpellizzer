Rapatea muaju

Rapatea muaju, commonly known as the Muaju palm, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a large, fibrous drupe that contains a single seed.

Rapatea muaju is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
