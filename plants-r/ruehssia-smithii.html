Ruehssia smithii

Ruehssia smithii, commonly known as the Smith's ruehssia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along its stems. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters during the spring. Ruehssia smithii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
