Ranunculus repens var. linearilobus

Ranunculus repens var. linearilobus, commonly known as the linear-lobed buttercup, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils along stream banks and wet meadows. The plant features slender stems with trifoliate leaves that have elongated, linear lobes. Its flowers are yellow with numerous petals and are produced in early spring.

It is often found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
