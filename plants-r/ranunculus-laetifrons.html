Ranunculus laetifrons

Ranunculus laetifrons, commonly known as the cheerful face or the yellow buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow petals and a central cluster of stamens, which give it a distinctive, almost cheerful appearance. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant has palmately divided leaves and produces small, showy flowers that bloom in early summer.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is noted for its delicate, ephemeral beauty.
