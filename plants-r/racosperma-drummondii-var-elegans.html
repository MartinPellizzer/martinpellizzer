Racosperma drummondii var. elegans

Racosperma drummondii var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant drummond's acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions. Its small, pod-like fruits contain seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its survival in challenging habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
