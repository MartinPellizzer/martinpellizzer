Rosa communis var. tenuissima

Rosa communis var. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender rose, is a compact shrub that produces small, delicate flowers.

It is characterized by its narrow, arching stems and fragrant, pale pink to white blooms. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with finely toothed margins, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and subtle floral display.
