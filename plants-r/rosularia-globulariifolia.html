Rosularia globulariifolia

Rosularia globulariifolia, commonly known as the globular leaf rose.

This succulent plant is native to the arid regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.
