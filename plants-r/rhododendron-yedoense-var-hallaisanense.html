Rhododendron yedoense var. hallaisanense

Rhododendron yedoense var. hallaisanense, commonly known as the Hallaisan azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
