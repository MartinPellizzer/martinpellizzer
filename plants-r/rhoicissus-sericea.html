Rhoicissus sericea

Rhoicissus sericea, commonly known as silver leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by heart-shaped, yellowish fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Rhoicissus sericea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding plants.
