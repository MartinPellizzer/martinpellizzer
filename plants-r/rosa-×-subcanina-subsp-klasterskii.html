Rosa × subcanina subsp. klasterskii

Rosa × subcanina subsp.

klasterskii, commonly known as the Klasterskii rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers.

The plant typically has thorny stems and heart-shaped, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in horticultural breeding.
