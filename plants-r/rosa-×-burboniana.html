Rosa × burboniana

Rosa × burboniana, commonly known as the Bourbon rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink to red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa × burboniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
