Ranunculus mediogracilis

Ranunculus mediogracilis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers with five to seven petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
