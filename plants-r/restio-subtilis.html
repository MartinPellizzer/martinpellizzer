Restio subtilis

Restio subtilis, commonly known as the slender restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Restio subtilis is adapted to dry conditions and plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils.
