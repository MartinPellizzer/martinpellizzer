Ratibida columnifera var. appendiculata

Ratibida columnifera var. appendiculata, commonly known as the narrowleaf prickly goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy yellow blooms. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, and thrives in moist, sunny conditions. It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its native environment.
