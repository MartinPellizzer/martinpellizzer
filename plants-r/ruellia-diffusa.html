Ruellia diffusa

Ruellia diffusa, commonly known as spreading ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and come in shades of blue, purple, or white.

Ruellia diffusa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
