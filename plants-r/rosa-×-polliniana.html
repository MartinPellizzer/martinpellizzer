Rosa × polliniana

Rosa × polliniana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid shrub that originates from China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces rose-like hips that are edible. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.

Rosa × polliniana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
