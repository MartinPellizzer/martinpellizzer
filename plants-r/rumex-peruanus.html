Rumex peruanus

Rumex peruanus, commonly known as Peruvian sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible leaves. The plant thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
