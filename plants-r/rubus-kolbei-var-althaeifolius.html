Rubus kolbei var. althaeifolius

Rubus kolbei var. althaeifolius, commonly known as the althaeifolius raspberry, is a variant of the Kolbe raspberry native to parts of Central Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its unique foliage and fruiting habits, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
