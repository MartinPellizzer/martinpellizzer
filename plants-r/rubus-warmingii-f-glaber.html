Rubus warmingii f. glaber

Rubus warmingii f. glaber, commonly known as the glabrous raspberry.

It is a variant of the raspberry species characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves. This plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of Asia. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its edible berries and ornamental value.
