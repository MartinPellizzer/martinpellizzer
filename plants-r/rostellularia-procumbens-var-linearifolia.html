Rostellularia procumbens var. linearifolia

Rostellularia procumbens var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rostellularia, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its prostrate growth habit and linear, lance-shaped leaves.

It typically features small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in terminal inflorescences. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually arranged in a basal rosette, and the stems are hairy and flexible.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
