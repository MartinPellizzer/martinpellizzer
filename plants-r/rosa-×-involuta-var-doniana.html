Rosa × involuta var. doniana

Rosa × involuta var. doniana, commonly known as the Doniana rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
