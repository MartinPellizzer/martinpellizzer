Rosa × nitidula

Rosa × nitidula, commonly known as the white evergreen rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and evergreen foliage that remains lush throughout the year. The plant produces small, white flowers with a subtle fragrance, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it suitable for garden borders and container planting.

Rosa × nitidula is valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
