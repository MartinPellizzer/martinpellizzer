Ruta pilosa

Ruta pilosa, commonly known as hairy rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its aromatic, branched stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The leaves are typically pinnately divided and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
