Rosa malyi var. atrichopoda

Rosa malyi var. atrichopoda, commonly known as the velvet rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its densely hairy stems and leaves, which give it a velvety appearance. The flowers are typically pink to reddish in color and bloom in late summer. It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
