Ruschia dekenahii

Ruschia dekenahii, commonly known as the Deken's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

Ruschia dekenahii thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
