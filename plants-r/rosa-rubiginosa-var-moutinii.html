Rosa rubiginosa var. moutinii

Rosa rubiginosa var. moutinii, commonly known as the Moutinii rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to reddish-pink flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped rose hips that are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
