Rhynchosia reptans

Rhynchosia reptans, commonly known as the creeping cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive yellow flower that blooms in clusters. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various growing conditions.
