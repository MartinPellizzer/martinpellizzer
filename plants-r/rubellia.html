Rubellia

Rubellia, commonly known as the red or pink-flowered shrub, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Rubiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, often clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The leaves are typically opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Rubellia species are often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.

These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
