Rungia laeta

Rungia laeta, commonly known as the white-flowered piper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or vine, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Rungia laeta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
