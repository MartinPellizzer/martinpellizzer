Rhaphithamnus venustus

Rhaphithamnus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful thornbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.

Rhaphithamnus venustus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for local wildlife.
