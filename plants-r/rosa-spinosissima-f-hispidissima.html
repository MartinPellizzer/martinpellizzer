Rosa spinosissima f. hispidissima

Rosa spinosissima f. hispidissima, commonly known as the hispidissima rose, is a botanical variant of the spiny rose.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and hairy foliage, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature, making it suitable for garden cultivation.
