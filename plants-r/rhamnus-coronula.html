Rhamnus coronula

Rhamnus coronula, commonly known as the crown thorn or yellow buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in spring. Its fruit is a small, dark blue berry that contains seeds surrounded by a fleshy, sticky coating.

Rhamnus coronula is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
