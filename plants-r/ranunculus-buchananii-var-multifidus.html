Ranunculus buchananii var. multifidus

Ranunculus buchananii var. multifidus, commonly known as the multifidus buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, bright yellow flowers with deeply divided petals that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are palmately divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its charming, early spring blooms.
