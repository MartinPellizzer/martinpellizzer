Rosa Rhaetica Var. Rupifraga

Rosa rhaetica var. rupifraga is a rare and hardy rose species native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is known for its rugged growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, rocky environments. This variety features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. Its unique characteristics make it a sought-after plant among horticultural enthusiasts. Despite its resilience, it remains a delicate and endangered species in its natural habitat.
