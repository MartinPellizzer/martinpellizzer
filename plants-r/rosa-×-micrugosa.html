Rosa × micrugosa

Rosa × micrugosa, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically produces multiple stems with narrow, glossy leaves. It is well-suited for container gardening and garden borders due to its ornamental value.

Rosa × micrugosa thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
