Rosa corallina

Rosa corallina, commonly known as coral rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that produces showy, pink to red flowers with a coral-like appearance. The plant has woody stems and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Rosa corallina is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
