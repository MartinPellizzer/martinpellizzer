Rosa blanda

Rosa blanda, commonly known as the meadow rose, is a low-growing shrub native to North America.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa blanda thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
