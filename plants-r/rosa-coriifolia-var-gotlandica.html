Rosa coriifolia var. gotlandica

Rosa coriifolia var. gotlandica, commonly known as Gotland rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Baltic region.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches with small, elliptical leaves that are hairy beneath. Its flowers are typically borne singly or in pairs along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
