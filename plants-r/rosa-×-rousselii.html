Rosa × rousselii

Rosa × rousselii, commonly known as the Rousselii rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its large, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × rousselii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
