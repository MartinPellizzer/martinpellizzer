Rhaphithamnus spinosus f. albiflorus

Rhaphithamnus spinosus f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered spiny shrub, is a rare variant of the native Australian species Rhaphithamnus spinosus.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny stems and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
