Rapistrum rugosum var. venosum

Rapistrum rugosum var. venosum, commonly known as the veiny dock, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lobed leaves that often have prominent veins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is typically found in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks.

The species is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
