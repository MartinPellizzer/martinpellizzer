Rosa sphaeroidea f. eriostyla

Rosa sphaeroidea f. eriostyla, commonly known as the eriostyle rose, is a botanical variant of the spherical rose.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded form and compact growth habit. The plant features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the stems are covered in thorns.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings for its unique appearance.
