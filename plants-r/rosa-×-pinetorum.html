Rosa × pinetorum

Rosa × pinetorum, commonly known as the Pineapple Rose, is a hybrid rose species that combines traits from both wild and cultivated roses.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pineapple-like fragrance and its dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, often with a deep, rich coloration. It is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Rosa × pinetorum is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions, though it prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
