Rhodoptera roseus

Rhodoptera roseus, commonly known as the pink-winged rose.

This plant is a perennial shrub characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly serrated, with a soft, velvety texture. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

It is also known for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
