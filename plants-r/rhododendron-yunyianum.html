Rhododendron yunyianum

Rhododendron yunyianum, commonly known as the Yunyian rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows at high elevations, where it thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to white, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron yunyianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
