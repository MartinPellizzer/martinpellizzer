Rosa fedoseevii

Rosa fedoseevii, commonly known as the Fedoseev's rose, is a species native to the Caucasus region.

It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa fedoseevii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
