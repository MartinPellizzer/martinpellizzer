Rosa caesia var. adenophora

Rosa caesia var. adenophora, commonly known as the腺花蔷薇, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is known for its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
