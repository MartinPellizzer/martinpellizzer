Ruta boissieriana

Ruta boissieriana, commonly known as the Mexican rue, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like citrus. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its aromatic properties are due to the presence of volatile oils in its foliage and flowers.

Ruta boissieriana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic effects.
