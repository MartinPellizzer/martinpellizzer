Ruschia octojugis

Ruschia octojugis, commonly known as the eight-lobed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often decorative foliage. The plant features compact, rounded growth with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purple tinge. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental element.

Ruschia octojugis is well-suited for rock gardens and arid landscapes due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
