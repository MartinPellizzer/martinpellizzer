Rhododendron stewartianum var. aiolosalpinx

Rhododendron stewartianum var. aiolosalpinx, commonly known as the Greek mountain rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

This variety is also noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
