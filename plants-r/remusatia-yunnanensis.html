Remusatia yunnanensis

Remusatia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshes and ponds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and wetland restoration projects.
