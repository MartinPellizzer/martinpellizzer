Rosa rubella var. mediterranea

Rosa rubella var. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean pink rose, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticulture.
