Rosa heckeliana

Rosa heckeliana, commonly known as the Heckel rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with thorny stems typical of many rose species.

Rosa heckeliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
