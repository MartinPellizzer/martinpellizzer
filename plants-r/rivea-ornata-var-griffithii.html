Rivea ornata var. griffithii

Rivea ornata var. griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's rivea, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its flowers emit a mild fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
