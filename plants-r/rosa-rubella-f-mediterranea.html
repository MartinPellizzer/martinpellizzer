Rosa rubella f. mediterranea

Rosa rubella f. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean pink rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa rubella.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which are typically smaller than those of the typical form. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. Its foliage is dark green and aromatic, adding to its ornamental value.

This cultivar is valued for its compact growth habit and suitability for rock gardens and borders.
