Rebutia einsteinii var. rubroviridis

Rebutia einsteinii var. rubroviridis, commonly known as the Red-veined Rebutia, is a succulent belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to South America, specifically Argentina, and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its flowers, which can be yellow, orange, or red, bloom in early summer and are often tubular in shape.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners alike.
