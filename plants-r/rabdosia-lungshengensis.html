Rabdosia lungshengensis

Rabdosia lungshengensis, commonly known as the Guangxi Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Guangxi region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and often display a range of colors from white to purple.

Rabdosia lungshengensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
