Rosa × dumetorum var. deseglisei

Rosa × dumetorum var. deseglisei, commonly known as the Deseglise rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features make it a hardy species adapted to cold climates and well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
