Rhynchanthera simplicicaulis

Rhynchanthera simplicicaulis, commonly known as the simple-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems that bear small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

Rhynchanthera simplicicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
