Rottboellia rottboellioides

Rottboellia rottboellioides, commonly known as the slender rottbolliagrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and agricultural areas. It has a fibrous root system and is drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many parts of the world.
