Rourea ovalis

Rourea ovalis, commonly known as the oval leaf rourea, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters, followed by fleshy, spiny fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental leaves, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
