Rosa russelliana

Rosa russelliana, commonly known as the Russell rose, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa russelliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
