Ruellia dura

Ruellia dura, commonly known as hardy ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early fall. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Ruellia dura is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
