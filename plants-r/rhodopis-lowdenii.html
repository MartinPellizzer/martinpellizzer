Rhodopis lowdenii

Rhodopis lowdenii, commonly known as the lowdenii rhododendron, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate cooler climates.

Rhodopis lowdenii is also noted for its ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
