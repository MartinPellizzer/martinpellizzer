Ruschia drepanophylla

Ruschia drepanophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Ruschia drepanophylla is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
