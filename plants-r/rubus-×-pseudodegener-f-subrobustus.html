Rubus × pseudodegener f. subrobustus

Rubus × pseudodegener f. subrobustus, commonly known as the robust blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its vigorous growth and thorny stems. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, dark red to black drupelets. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges and a rough texture.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its flavorful berries.
