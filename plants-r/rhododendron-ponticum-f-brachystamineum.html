Rhododendron ponticum f. brachystamineum

Rhododendron ponticum f. brachystamineum, commonly known as the dwarf Pontic rhododendron, is a compact shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy clusters of pinkish-white flowers in late spring. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

However, it can be invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
