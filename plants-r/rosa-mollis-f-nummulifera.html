Rosa mollis f. nummulifera

Rosa mollis f. nummulifera, commonly known as the Nummulite Rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, which is native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and historical use in perfumery.
