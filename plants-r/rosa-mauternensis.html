Rosa mauternensis

Rosa mauternensis, commonly known as the Mautern rose, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Rosa mauternensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
