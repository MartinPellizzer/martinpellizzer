Rhododendron × planetum

Rhododendron × planetum, commonly known as the hybrid rhododendron, is a cross between Rhododendron catawbiense and Rhododendron maximum.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is native to the eastern United States and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscape design in temperate climates.
