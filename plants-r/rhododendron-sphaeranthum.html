Rhododendron sphaeranthum

Rhododendron sphaeranthum, commonly known as the spherical rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically thrives in cool, moist environments with acidic soil, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Rhododendron sphaeranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
