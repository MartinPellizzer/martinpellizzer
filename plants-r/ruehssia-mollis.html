Ruehssia mollis

Ruehssia mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Ruehssia mollis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along stream banks.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
