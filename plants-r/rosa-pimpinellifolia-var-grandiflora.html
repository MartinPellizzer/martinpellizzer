Rosa pimpinellifolia var. grandiflora

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
