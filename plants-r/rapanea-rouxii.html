Rapanea rouxii

Rapanea rouxii, commonly known as the Malagasy boxwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
