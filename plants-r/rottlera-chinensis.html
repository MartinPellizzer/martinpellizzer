Rottlera chinensis

Rottlera chinensis, commonly known as Chinese rottlera, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small white or yellowish flowers. The plant has elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its fruits are small, round, and turn from green to yellow when mature.

Rottlera chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
