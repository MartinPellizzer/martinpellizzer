Ribes giraldii

Ribes giraldii, commonly known as the Sierra grape.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States, particularly in California and Nevada. The plant features small, simple leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring. Its fruit is a small, edible berry that ripens to a red color in late summer.

Ribes giraldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
