Rixea azurea

Rixea azurea, commonly known as the blue rixea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in racemes, and are typically white or pale blue in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
