Renggeria

Renggeria, commonly known as the "glass plant," is a unique member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds. Renggeria is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
