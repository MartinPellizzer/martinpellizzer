Rosa afzeliana f. erubescens

Rosa afzeliana f. erubescens, commonly known as the crimson-flowered rose, is a botanical cultivar characterized by its vibrant red blooms.

It is a hybrid rose species originating from the genus Rosa, known for its ornamental value in gardens. The plant typically features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and have a strong, pleasant fragrance.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of climates.
