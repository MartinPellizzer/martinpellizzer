Rhododendron shaoakouense

Rhododendron shaoakouense, commonly known as the Shaoakou rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Zhejiang province.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic, well-drained soils. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
