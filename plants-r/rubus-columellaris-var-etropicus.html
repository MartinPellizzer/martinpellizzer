Rubus columellaris var. etropicus

Rubus columellaris var. etropicus, commonly known as the tropical raspberry, is a variant of the bramble genus Rubus.

It is characterized by its erect or semi-erect growth habit and produces small, edible berries. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated margins, and its flowers are pink to white in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of flavorful fruit.
