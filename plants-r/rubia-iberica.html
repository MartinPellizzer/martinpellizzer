Rubia iberica

Rubia iberica, commonly known as Spanish bugleweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant has slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom from spring to early summer.

Rubia iberica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
