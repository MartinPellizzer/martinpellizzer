Racosperma meiospermum

Racosperma meiospermum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds.

It is adapted to dry environments and plays a role in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.
