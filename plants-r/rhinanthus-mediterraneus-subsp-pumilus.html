Rhinanthus mediterraneus subsp. pumilus

pumilus, commonly known as the dwarf yellow-rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family. It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on its host plants for water and nutrients.
