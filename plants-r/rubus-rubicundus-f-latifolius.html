Rubus rubicundus f. latifolius

Rubus rubicundus f. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf raspberry, is a botanical variant of the common raspberry.

It is characterized by its broader, more robust leaves compared to the typical species. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red and edible.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild flavor.
