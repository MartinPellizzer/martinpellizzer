Rosa marietani

Rosa marietani, commonly known as the Marietani rose, is a rare and ancient species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its deep red petals and fragrant aroma, which are highly prized in traditional perfumery and horticulture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring, and its stems are covered in thorny projections. Rosa marietani is known for its resilience to harsh climates, thriving in rocky soils with minimal water.

Due to its rarity and historical significance, it is considered a valuable specimen in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
