Rapanea reflexiflora

Rapanea reflexiflora, commonly known as the reflexed-flowered evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Rapanea reflexiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
