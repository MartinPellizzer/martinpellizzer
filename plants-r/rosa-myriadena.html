Rosa myriadena

Rosa myriadena, commonly known as the many-seed rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its fruit is a type of capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Rosa myriadena is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
