Rosa brevistyla var. systyla

Rosa brevistyla var. systyla, commonly known as the short-styled rose, is a variant of the Rosa brevistyla species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant produces both short-styled and long-styled flowers, a feature that is significant in its pollination biology. It is native to the western United States, particularly California, and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
