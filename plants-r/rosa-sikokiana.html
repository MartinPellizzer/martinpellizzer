Rosa sikokiana

Rosa sikokiana, commonly known as the Sikkim rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers and its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.

Rosa sikokiana is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
