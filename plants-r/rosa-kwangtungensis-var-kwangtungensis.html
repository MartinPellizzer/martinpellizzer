Rosa kwangtungensis var. kwangtungensis

Rosa kwangtungensis var. kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Kwangtung rose, is a species native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of pinnate leaves with serrated edges, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
