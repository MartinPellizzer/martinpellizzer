Ranunculus repens var. lucidus

Ranunculus repens var. lucidus, commonly known as the glossy water buttercup, is a variant of the creeping buttercup.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its bright yellow flowers and glossy, waxy leaves. The plant typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows, riverbanks, and marshy areas. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette and have a distinctive lobed shape.

The flowers are short-stemmed and bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to its habitat.
