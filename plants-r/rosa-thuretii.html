Rosa thuretii

Rosa thuretii, commonly known as the Thuret's rose, is a shrub native to the Pyrenees region of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Rosa thuretii is typically found in rocky or wooded areas, thriving in well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
