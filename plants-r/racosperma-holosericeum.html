Racosperma holosericeum

Racosperma holosericeum, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or pale green. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The silky acacia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand arid conditions.
