Rosa communis var. sphaeroidea

Rosa communis var. sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globe rose, is a cultivar characterized by its rounded, full blooms.

It is a hybrid of Rosa communis, which is native to China, and is prized for its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves, typical of many rose species. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a double petal structure that adds to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.
