Rubus schleicheri var. mitis

Rubus schleicheri var. mitis, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a variant of the blackberry species native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and thorny stems. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it produces small, dark purple berries.

The botanical features of this variant make it distinct from other Rubus species, often found in shaded, moist environments.
