Rhododendron xenium

Rhododendron xenium, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron xenium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
