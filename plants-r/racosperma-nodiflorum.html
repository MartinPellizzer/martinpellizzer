Racosperma nodiflorum

Racosperma nodiflorum, commonly known as the clustered racemosa, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, often thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Racosperma nodiflorum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native range.
