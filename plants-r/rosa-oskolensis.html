Rosa oskolensis

Rosa oskolensis, commonly known as the Siberian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the cold climates of Siberia and northern Russia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has thorny stems and serrated, dark green leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh winter conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with cold-hardy climates.

Rosa oskolensis is also noted for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its resilience to frost.
