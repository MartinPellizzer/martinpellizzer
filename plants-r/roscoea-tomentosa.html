Roscoea tomentosa

Roscoea tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly roscoea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, deep red to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and lance-shaped, hairy leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Roscoea tomentosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and unique foliage.
