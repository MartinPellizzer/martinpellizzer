Reseda virescens

Reseda virescens, commonly known as green reseda, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological importance.

Reseda virescens is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
