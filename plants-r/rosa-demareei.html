Rosa demareei

Rosa demareei, commonly known as the Demaree rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Rosa demareei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
