Relbunium hirsutum

Relbunium hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy relbunium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Relbunium hirsutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
