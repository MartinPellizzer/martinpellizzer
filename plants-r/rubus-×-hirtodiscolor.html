Rubus × hirtodiscolor

Rubus × hirtodiscolor, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits called drupes, which are typically red when ripe. The plant is native to North America and is widely cultivated for its flavorful berries.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for home gardens and commercial agriculture.
