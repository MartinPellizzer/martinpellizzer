Rhamnus standleyana

Rhamnus standleyana, commonly known as the Standley's buckthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 5 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a distinctive waxy coating.

The small yellowish-green flowers are followed by small, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain seeds.
