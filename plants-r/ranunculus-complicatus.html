Ranunculus complicatus

Ranunculus complicatus, commonly known as the twisted buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers with a distinctive, deeply lobed pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are compound, with a complex arrangement of serrated leaflets.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
