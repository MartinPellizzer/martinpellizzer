Rosa Glauca Subvar. Pilosula

Rosa glauca subvar. pilosula is a unique and lesser-known variant of the rose family known for its striking appearance and hardy nature. This subvariety is characterized by its pale blue flowers and dense, fuzzy foliage that gives it a distinctive look. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soils. Its resilience makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance yet visually appealing plants. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more common rose varieties.
