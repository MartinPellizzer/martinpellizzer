Rhododendron sinofalconeri

Rhododendron sinofalconeri, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is often found in cloud forests and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
