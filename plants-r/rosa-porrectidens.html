Rosa porrectidens

Rosa porrectidens, commonly known as the straight-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces oval, green leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are often clustered in small groups at the ends of the stems. Rosa porrectidens is native to parts of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
