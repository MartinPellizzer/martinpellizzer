Rubus × vesticaulis

Rubus × vesticaulis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

This plant is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (common blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry). It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and the fruit is a small, red or orange berry that is tart and edible.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in wetlands and forested areas.
