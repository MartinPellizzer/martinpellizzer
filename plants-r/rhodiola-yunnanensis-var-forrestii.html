Rhodiola yunnanensis var. forrestii

Rhodiola yunnanensis var. forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan rhodiola, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is valued for its adaptogenic properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
