Rosa × conica

Rosa × conica, commonly known as the conical rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, conical-shaped blooms that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces fragrant flowers with multiple layers of petals, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.

Rosa × conica is known for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
