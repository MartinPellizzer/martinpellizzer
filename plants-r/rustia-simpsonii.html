Rustia simpsonii

Rustia simpsonii, commonly known as Simpson's rustia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Rustia simpsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
