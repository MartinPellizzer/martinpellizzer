Rosa afzeliana var. stebleri

Rosa afzeliana var. stebleri, commonly known as the Stebleri rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

It is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
