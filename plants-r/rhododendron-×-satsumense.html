Rhododendron × satsumense

Rhododendron × satsumense, commonly known as the Satsuma azalea, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to temperate climates.

Rhododendron × satsumense is often used in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant spring blooms and compact growth habit.
