Rubus scibilis

Rubus scibilis, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of numerous drupes, which are typically bright red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

The berries are not only edible but also have historical uses in traditional medicine.
