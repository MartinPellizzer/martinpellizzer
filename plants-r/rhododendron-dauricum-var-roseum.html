Rhododendron dauricum var. roseum

Rhododendron dauricum var. roseum, commonly known as the Daurian rose rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to rose-red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
