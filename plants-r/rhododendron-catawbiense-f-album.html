Rhododendron catawbiense f. album

Rhododendron catawbiense f. album, commonly known as the Catawba White Rhododendron, is a cultivar of the Catawba rhododendron.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is evergreen, with leathery, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its white blooms. Native to the Appalachian Mountains, it thrives in acidic, well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in horticulture for its visual impact in gardens and landscapes.
