Renanthera bilinguis

Renanthera bilinguis, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pinkish-purple flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often with a sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
