Rosa marcyana f. mariascheinensis

Rosa marcyana f. mariascheinensis, commonly known as the Mariaschein rose, is a unique hybrid rose with distinctive botanical features.

It is a compact, shrub-like plant with dense, thornless stems and dark green foliage. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a layered, full bloom that adds ornamental value. It is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
