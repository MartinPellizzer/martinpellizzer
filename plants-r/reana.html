Reana

Reana, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous root system. Reana thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
