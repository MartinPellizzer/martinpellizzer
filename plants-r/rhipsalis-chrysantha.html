Rhipsalis chrysantha

Rhipsalis chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow mistletoe cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a climbing cactus with long, slender stems that can reach up to 10 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to small, hair-like structures, and it is typically found growing on trees in humid environments.

Rhipsalis chrysantha is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
