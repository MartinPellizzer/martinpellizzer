Rosa bridgesii

Rosa bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridgess rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky environments. The plant features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it produces edible rose hips in the fall.

Rosa bridgesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
