Rosalesia glandulosa

Rosalesia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular rose.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is native to South America and thrives in moist, temperate climates. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a distinctive glandular texture on the underside.

Rosalesia glandulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
