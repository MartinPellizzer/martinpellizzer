Rhododendron mengtszense

Rhododendron mengtszense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high elevations, often in areas with cool and moist climates. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in its native habitat, supporting local biodiversity.
