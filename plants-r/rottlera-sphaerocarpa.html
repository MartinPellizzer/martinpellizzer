Rottlera sphaerocarpa

Rottlera sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe kola, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its rounded, spherical fruits that resemble small globes. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves. Its fruits are typically harvested when they turn a deep red color, indicating maturity.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
