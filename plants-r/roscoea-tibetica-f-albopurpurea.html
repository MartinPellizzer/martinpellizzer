Roscoea tibetica f. albopurpurea

Roscoea tibetica f. albopurpurea, commonly known as the Tibetan roscoea with purple-pink flowers, is a striking hybrid variety of the Roscoea genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that display a mix of white and purplish-pink hues, making it a popular choice among horticulturists. The plant features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens or as a container plant.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
