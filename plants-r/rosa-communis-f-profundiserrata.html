Rosa communis f. profundiserrata

Rosa communis f. profundiserrata, commonly known as the deep-toothed rose, is a botanical variant of the common rose.

It is characterized by its deeply serrated leaves and vibrant, fragrant flowers. The plant is native to temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. Its foliage is often a deep green, and the flowers can range in color from pink to white.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
