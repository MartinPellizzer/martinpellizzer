Rudbeckia laciniata

Rudbeckia laciniata, commonly known as the cutleaf coneflower, is a tall, herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features large, showy yellow flowers with dark central cones, which bloom from late summer into early fall. The plant has large, deeply lobed, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Rudbeckia laciniata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
