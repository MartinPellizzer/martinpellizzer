Rosa mollis f. atona

Rosa mollis f. atona, commonly known as the soft rose or atona rose, is a hybrid form of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with multiple stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.

This variety is prized for its delicate appearance and subtle fragrance, which adds charm to both garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
