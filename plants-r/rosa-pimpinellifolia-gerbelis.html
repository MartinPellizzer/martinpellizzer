Rosa pimpinellifolia gerbelis

Rosa pimpinellifolia gerbelis, commonly known as the Gerbelis rose, is a species of shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy species.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.
