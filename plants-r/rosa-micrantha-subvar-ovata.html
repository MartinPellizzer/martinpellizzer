Rosa micrantha subvar. ovata

Rosa micrantha subvar. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright stems with thorns and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white. The leaves are ovate, with a serrated margin and a slightly hairy surface. This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to temperate climates.
