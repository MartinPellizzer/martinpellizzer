Rosa maluqueri

Rosa maluqueri, commonly known as the Maluqueri rose, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of 5 to 7 oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa maluqueri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
