Richterago caulescens

Richterago caulescens, commonly known as the slender-stemmed bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
