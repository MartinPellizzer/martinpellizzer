Rosa sepium agrestis

Rosa sepium agrestis, commonly known as the field rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Rosa sepium agrestis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems.
