Rosa placidula

Rosa placidula, commonly known as the calm rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Korea, where it grows in woodland and mountainous regions. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rosa placidula is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
