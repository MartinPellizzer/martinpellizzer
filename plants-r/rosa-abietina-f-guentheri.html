Rosa abietina f. guentheri

Rosa abietina f. guentheri, commonly known as the Alpine rose or mountain rose, is a frost-resistant shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for high-altitude environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, ornamental plants.
