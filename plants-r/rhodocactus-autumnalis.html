Rhodocactus autumnalis

Rhodocactus autumnalis, commonly known as the autumnal red cactus, is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that can grow up to two meters in height. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the autumn season, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in its harsh environment.

Rhodocactus autumnalis is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in desert ecosystems.
