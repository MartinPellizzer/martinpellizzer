Ricinocarpus nigritianus

Ricinocarpus nigritianus, commonly known as the African blackwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Bombacaceae and is characterized by its dark, heavy wood and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous flat, winged seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
