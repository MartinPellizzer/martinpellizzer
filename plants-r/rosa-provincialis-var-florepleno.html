Rosa provincialis var. florepleno

Rosa provincialis var. florepleno, commonly known as the double-flowered provincial rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and its ability to produce abundant, double blooms throughout the growing season. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a rich, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. This variety is well-suited for garden borders or containers due to its manageable size and ornamental value.

It requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
