Rockhausenia graminifolia, commonly known as the grassy rockhausenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in a composite head.
