Rosa × sauzeana

Rosa × sauzeana, commonly known as the Sauzeana rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The Rosa × sauzeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
