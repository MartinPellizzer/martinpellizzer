Rubus questieri microgene patulistamineus

Rubus questieri microgene patulistamineus, commonly known as the Patulite raspberry.

This plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color, containing multiple drupelets. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have serrated margins, typical of many Rubus species.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or hedgerow environments.
