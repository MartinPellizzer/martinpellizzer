Rosa lanceolata var. heterotricha

Rosa lanceolata var. heterotricha, commonly known as the slender rose, is a variant of the lanceleaf rose.

It is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Himalayas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, giving it its scientific name. Its flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a fragrant aroma.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
