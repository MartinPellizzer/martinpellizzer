Rosa stylosa var. fastigiata

Rosa stylosa var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright or columnar rose, is a compact, bushy shrub that exhibits a unique vertical growth habit.

It features narrow, upright stems covered in small, thornless leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces due to its compact size.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
