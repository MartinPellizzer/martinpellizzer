Racosperma baileyanum

Racosperma baileyanum, commonly known as the Baileyanum acacia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a woody stem and small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
