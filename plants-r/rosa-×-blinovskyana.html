Rosa × blinovskyana

Rosa × blinovskyana, commonly known as the Blinovsky hybrid rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Rosa × blinovskyana is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
