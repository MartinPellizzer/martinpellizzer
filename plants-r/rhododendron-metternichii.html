Rhododendron metternichii

Rhododendron metternichii, commonly known as the Metternich rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in Nepal and India.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences, and the leaves are leathery with a waxy coating.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
