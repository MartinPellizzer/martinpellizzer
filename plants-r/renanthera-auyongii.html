Renanthera auyongii

Renanthera auyongii, commonly known as the Auyong's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or red coloration with contrasting yellow and white markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for epiphytic growth, often attaching to trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Renanthera auyongii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
