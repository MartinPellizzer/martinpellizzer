Rosa sempervirens var. micrantha

Rosa sempervirens var. micrantha, commonly known as the shrub rose or evergreen rose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains lush throughout the year, making it suitable for year-round ornamental use. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It is well-suited for hedges, borders, or container gardening due to its hardy nature and adaptability.

Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem structure and a tendency to form a broad, rounded shape.
