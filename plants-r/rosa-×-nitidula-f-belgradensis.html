Rosa × nitidula f. belgradensis

Rosa × nitidula f. belgradensis, commonly known as the Belgrade rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that remains attractive throughout the growing season. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant in its native range.
