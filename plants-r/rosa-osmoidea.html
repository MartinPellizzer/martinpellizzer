Rosa osmoidea

Rosa osmoidea, commonly known as the fragrant rose or osmifera rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Tien Shan and Pamir mountains. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are dark green and slightly hairy, and the stems are covered in thorns.

Rosa osmoidea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated for its aromatic blooms and hardy nature.
