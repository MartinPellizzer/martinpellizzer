Rosa mollis f. parvella

Rosa mollis f. parvella, commonly known as the dwarf evergreen rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the western United States.

It features small, glossy evergreen leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with a delicate, fragrant scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its slow growth rate and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
