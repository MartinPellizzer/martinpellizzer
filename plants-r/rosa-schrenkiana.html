Rosa schrenkiana

Rosa schrenkiana, commonly known as the Central Asian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruits are red, hairy achenes that are dispersed by wind.

Rosa schrenkiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its historical and medicinal uses.
