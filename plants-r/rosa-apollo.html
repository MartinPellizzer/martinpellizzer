Rosa apollo

Rosa apollo, commonly known as the Apollo rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces fragrant blooms throughout the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
