Rosa skalnjkiana

Rosa skalnjkiana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Kashmir and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, Rosa skalnjkiana is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical gardens.
