Rhaphiolepis Deflexa

Rhaphiolepis deflexa is a unique evergreen shrub known for its vibrant foliage and ornamental appeal. Native to parts of East Asia, it thrives in humid climates and well-drained soils. This plant features glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall, adding seasonal interest to gardens. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small spaces and container gardening. With its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, Rhaphiolepis deflexa is a valuable addition to modern landscaping.
