Rhododendron zheguense

Rhododendron zheguense, commonly known as the Zheguan rhododendron, is a species native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and acidic soil. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron zheguense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical gardens.
