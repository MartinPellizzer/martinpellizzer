Rotang equestris

Rotang equestris, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible fruits that contain a sticky, aromatic pulp. Its latex, known as shea butter, is widely used in cosmetics and skincare products.

The plant also plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
