Restrepia angustilabia

Restrepia angustilabia, commonly known as the narrow-lipped restrepia, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Restrepia angustilabia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
