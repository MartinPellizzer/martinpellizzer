Racosperma mitodes

Racosperma mitodes, commonly known as the yellow wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces yellow flowers in spring. Its seed pods are slender and contain several seeds.

Racosperma mitodes is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought conditions.
