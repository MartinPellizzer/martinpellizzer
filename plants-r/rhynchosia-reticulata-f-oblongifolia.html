Rhynchosia reticulata f. oblongifolia

Rhynchosia reticulata f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaved snout bean, is a variant of the species Rhynchosia reticulata, which belongs to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, oblong leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, typical of many leguminous species. Its seed pods are usually elongated and contain several small, round seeds.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
