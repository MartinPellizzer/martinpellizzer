Rhynchosia lukafuensis

Rhynchosia lukafuensis, commonly known as the Lukafu legume, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

Its seeds are encased in pods and can be used for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
