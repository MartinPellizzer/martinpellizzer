Rosa × gravetii var. purpurascens

Rosa × gravetii var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It features dark green, slightly hairy leaves that turn a striking reddish-purple in the fall. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage color and showy blooms.
