Ronnbergia fraseri

Ronnbergia fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence that consists of numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Ronnbergia fraseri is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens for its unique floral display.
