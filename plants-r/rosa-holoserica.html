Rosa holoserica

Rosa holoserica, commonly known as the Chinese moonflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, single blooms that open at night. The plant has pinnate leaves with 5 to 9 leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silvery hairs. It produces red, hairy fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Rosa holoserica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique flowering habit.
