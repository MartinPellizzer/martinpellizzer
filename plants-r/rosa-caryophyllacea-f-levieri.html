Rosa caryophyllacea f. levieri

Rosa caryophyllacea f. levieri, commonly known as the Levieri's rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers with pink to red petals and a distinctive, slightly hairy appearance. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with a leathery texture and a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This unique form of Rosa caryophyllacea is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
