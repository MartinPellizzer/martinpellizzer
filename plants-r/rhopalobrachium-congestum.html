Rhopalobrachium congestum

Rhopalobrachium congestum, commonly known as the clustered bracted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it is often found in coastal regions.

The orchid's unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
