Rhynchosia speciosa

Rhynchosia speciosa, commonly known as the showy cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple base. The plant produces leguminous pods that contain edible seeds, which are used as a food source in some cultures. Rhynchosia speciosa is a climbing vine with trifoliate leaves and is often found in disturbed habitats.
