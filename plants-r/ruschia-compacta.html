Ruschia compacta

Ruschia compacta, commonly known as the compact Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color, arranged in a dense, compact rosette. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used as a ground cover or in container gardening.
