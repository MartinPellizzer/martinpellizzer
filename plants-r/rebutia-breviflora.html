Rebutia breviflora

Rebutia breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Rebutia breviflora is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
