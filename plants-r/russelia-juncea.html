Russelia juncea

Russelia juncea, commonly known as the velvet flower or velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Russelia juncea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
