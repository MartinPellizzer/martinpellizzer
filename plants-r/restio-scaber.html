Restio scaber

Restio scaber, commonly known as the rough restio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family, which is characteristic of the fynbos biome. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with narrow, linear leaves that are rough to the touch. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Restio scaber is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
