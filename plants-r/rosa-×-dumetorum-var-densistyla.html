Rosa × dumetorum var. densistyla

Rosa × dumetorum var. densistyla, commonly known as the dense-styled rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardy nature and attractive floral display.
