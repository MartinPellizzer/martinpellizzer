Robinia caragana var. altagana

Robinia caragana var. altagana, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves that are pinnately arranged, with each leaflet being oval and slightly serrated. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in racemes during the spring, followed by flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
