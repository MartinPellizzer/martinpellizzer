Rubus shihae

Rubus shihae, commonly known as the Shiha's blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
