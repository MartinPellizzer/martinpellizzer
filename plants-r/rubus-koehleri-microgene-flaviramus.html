Rubus koehleri microgene flaviramus

Rubus koehleri microgene flaviramus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered raspberry.

This plant is a species of bramble native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trailing growth habit. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its hairy stems and compound leaves. The fruit is typically a small, aggregate berry, similar to other Rubus species.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
