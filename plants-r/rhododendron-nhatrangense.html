Rhododendron nhatrangense

Rhododendron nhatrangense, commonly known as Nhatrang rhododendron, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with moist, acidic soils. The plant features large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive white or pale pink throat. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, providing a dense canopy that supports various wildlife.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
