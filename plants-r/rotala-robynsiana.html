Rotala robynsiana

Rotala robynsiana, commonly known as Robyn's waterwheel, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features whorled leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to water gardens. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Rotala robynsiana is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help purify water in pond settings.
