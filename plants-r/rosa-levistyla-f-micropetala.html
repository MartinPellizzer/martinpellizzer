Rosa levistyla f. micropetala

Rosa levistyla f. micropetala, commonly known as the miniature evergreen rose, is a compact shrub with dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant flowers.

It is a naturally occurring form of Rosa levistyla, characterized by its reduced petal size and more delicate appearance compared to the species. The plant is native to the western United States and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and small spaces.
