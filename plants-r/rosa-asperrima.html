Rosa asperrima

Rosa asperrima, commonly known as the rough rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. Rosa asperrima is typically found in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
