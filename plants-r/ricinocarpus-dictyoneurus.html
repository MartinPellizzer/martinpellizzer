Ricinocarpus dictyoneurus

Ricinocarpus dictyoneurus, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a large, tropical palm species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, orange-red fruits. Its trunk is typically unbranched and covered with a fibrous, spongy layer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
