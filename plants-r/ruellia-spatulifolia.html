Ruellia spatulifolia

Ruellia spatulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Ruellia spatulifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
