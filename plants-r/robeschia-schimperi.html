Robeschia schimperi

Robeschia schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in high-altitude regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
