Rosa × centifolia f. andrewsii

Rosa × centifolia f. andrewsii, commonly known as the French centifolia rose, is a fragrant shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its numerous, small, pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements. It is valued for its strong perfume and is often used in perfumery and culinary applications.

This cultivar is a hybrid between Rosa centifolia and Rosa damascena, exhibiting traits of both parent species.
