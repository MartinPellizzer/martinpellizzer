Ruschia densiflora

Ruschia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
