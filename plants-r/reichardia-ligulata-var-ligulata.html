Reichardia ligulata var. ligulata

Reichardia ligulata var. ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dandelion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a flower head composed of small yellow flowers, which later develop into fluffy white seeds. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and other damp environments.

Reichardia ligulata var. ligulata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
