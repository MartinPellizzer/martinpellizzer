Rosa × oetea

Rosa × oetea, commonly known as the 'Crimson Glory' rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double petals, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a versatile and visually striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
