Rosa mollis f. nacophora

Rosa mollis f. nacophora, commonly known as the soft rose or woolly rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa mollis species.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is native to regions in South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
