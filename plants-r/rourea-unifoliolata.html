Rourea unifoliolata

Rourea unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf rourea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are typically clustered at the ends of its branches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
