Rhopalopilia poggei var. bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's rhopalopilia, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spurred flowers that display a combination of purple and white hues.
