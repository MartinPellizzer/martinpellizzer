Ribes mediatum

Ribes mediatum, commonly known as the flatstem currant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, often reddish stems and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Ribes mediatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
