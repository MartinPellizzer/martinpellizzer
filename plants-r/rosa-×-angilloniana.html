Rosa × angilloniana

Rosa × angilloniana, commonly known as the Angilloniana rose, is a hybrid shrub native to France.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant blooms that typically appear in late spring to early summer. The plant produces large, semi-double to fully double pink flowers with a strong, sweet scent. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × angilloniana prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth and flowering.
