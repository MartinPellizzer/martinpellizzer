Rosa monticola alpestris

Rosa monticola alpestris, commonly known as the alpine rock rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly the Alps and Carpathians. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically small, oval, and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the harsh alpine environment.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats at high altitudes.
