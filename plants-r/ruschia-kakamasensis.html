Ruschia kakamasensis

Ruschia kakamasensis, commonly known as the Kakamas Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, star-shaped leaves. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
