Rosa malyi f. diplotricha

Rosa malyi f. diplotricha, commonly known as the double-flowered Rosa Malyi, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa malyi species.

It is characterized by its double blooms, which consist of multiple layers of petals, giving it a more elaborate appearance compared to single-flowered varieties. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates. It is valued for its ornamental qualities, particularly in gardens and floral arrangements.

The plant typically produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
