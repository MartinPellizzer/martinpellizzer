Rubus vestii var. incertus

Rubus vestii var. incertus, commonly known as the uncertain bramble, is a variant of the bramble genus Rubus.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, and the fruits are small, aggregate berries. This plant is native to parts of East Asia, including China and Japan.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in taxonomic studies due to its uncertain classification.
