Rosa granata

Rosa granata, commonly known as the granate rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces bright red, orange, or yellow berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. Rosa granata is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
