Ruellia occidentalis var. lundelliorum

Ruellia occidentalis var. lundelliorum, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
