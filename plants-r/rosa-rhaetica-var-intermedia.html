Rosa rhaetica var. intermedia

Rosa rhaetica var. intermedia, commonly known as the Alpine rose or Rhaetian rose, is a flowering shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Rosa rhaetica var. intermedia is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
