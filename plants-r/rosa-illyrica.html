Rosa illyrica

Rosa illyrica, commonly known as the Illyrian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include simple, pinnate leaves and hip fruits that contain several seeds.

Rosa illyrica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
