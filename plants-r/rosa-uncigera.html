Rosa uncigera

Rosa uncigera, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in countries like Argentina, Bolivia, and Chile. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with slender, pointed leaflets.

Rosa uncigera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
