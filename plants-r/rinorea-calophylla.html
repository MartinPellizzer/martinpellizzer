Rinorea calophylla

Rinorea calophylla, commonly known as the glossy leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Rinorea calophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
