Rhaphiolepis elliptica

Rhaphiolepis elliptica, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red berries that persist through winter, adding visual interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental berries and hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping and garden design.
