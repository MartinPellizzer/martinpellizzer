Rhododendron metternichii f. albiflorum

Rhododendron metternichii f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered metternich rhododendron, is a unique hybrid with striking white blooms.

It is a subshrub with dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. Native to the mountainous regions of China, it thrives in cool, humid climates with acidic soil. The flowers are funnel-shaped and bloom in early spring, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
