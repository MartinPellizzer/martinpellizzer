Rosa cinnamomea f. serrata

Rosa cinnamomea f. serrata, commonly known as the serrated cinnamon rose, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of central China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer, often with a distinct cinnamon scent. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often sought after by horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.
