Rhynchosia nummularia

Rhynchosia nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with slender stems and small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Rhynchosia nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
