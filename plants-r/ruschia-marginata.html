Ruschia marginata

Ruschia marginata, commonly known as the margined ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinct white or yellow margins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Ruschia marginata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.
