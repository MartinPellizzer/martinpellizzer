Rapuntium dentatum

Rapuntium dentatum, commonly known as the toothed cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, alternate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Rapuntium dentatum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along roadsides and in fields.
