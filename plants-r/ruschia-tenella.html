Ruschia tenella

Ruschia tenella, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking silver-blue foliage with a fuzzy texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
