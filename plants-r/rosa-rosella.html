Rosa rosella

Rosa rosella, commonly known as the scarlet rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa rosella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
