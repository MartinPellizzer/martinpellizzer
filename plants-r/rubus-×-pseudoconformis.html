Rubus × pseudoconformis

Rubus × pseudoconformis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
