Ranunculus × arendsii

Ranunculus × arendsii, commonly known as the Arendsii buttercup, is a hybrid species within the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, double flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough texture and serrated edges.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various garden conditions.
