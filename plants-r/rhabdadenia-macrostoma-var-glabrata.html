Rhabdadenia macrostoma var. glabrata

Rhabdadenia macrostoma var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed rhabdadenia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are smooth and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
