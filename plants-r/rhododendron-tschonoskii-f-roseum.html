Rhododendron tschonoskii f. roseum

Rhododendron tschonoskii f. roseum, commonly known as the Roseum Rhododendron, is a cultivar of the Tschonoskii rhododendron.

It is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is a compact shrub with dense, leathery foliage that provides an evergreen structure. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
