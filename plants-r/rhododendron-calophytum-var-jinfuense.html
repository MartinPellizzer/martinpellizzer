Rhododendron calophytum var. jinfuense

Rhododendron calophytum var. jinfuense, commonly known as the Jinfu rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and prefers cool, humid climates. Its botanical features include compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
