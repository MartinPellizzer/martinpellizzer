Ruellia gigantea

Ruellia gigantea, commonly known as the giant ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 feet. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Ruellia gigantea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
