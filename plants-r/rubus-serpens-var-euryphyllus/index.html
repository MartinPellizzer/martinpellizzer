Rubus Serpens Var. Euryphyllus

Rubus serpens var. euryphyllus is a unique variant of the evergreen shrub known for its distinctive leaf shape and adaptability to various environments. This plant is native to regions with mild climates and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its broad, leathery leaves and thornless stems make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. While it shares traits with its parent species, it exhibits notable variations in growth habits and foliage. Due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal, it has gained attention among botanists and horticulturists alike.
