Rhipsalis funalis

Rhipsalis funalis, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a climbing cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its soft, green, branched stems that often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
