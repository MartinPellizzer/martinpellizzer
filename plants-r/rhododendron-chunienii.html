Rhododendron chunienii

Rhododendron chunienii, commonly known as the Hainan rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the island of Hainan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. The plant features large, leathery leaves that are dark green and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from pink to red, blooming in spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
