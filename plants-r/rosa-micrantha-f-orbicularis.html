Rosa micrantha f. orbicularis

Rosa micrantha f. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color. The plant has rounded, dark green leaves and produces rose hips that are orange-red when mature. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its compact size and hardiness.

This variety is well-suited for container growing and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
