Rubus tiliifrons

Rubus tiliifrons, commonly known as the tiliifrons raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Vietnam, and Thailand. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit, a small, red, edible berry, is similar to other raspberries but has a distinct flavor.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
