Rosa rubiginosa vaillantina

Rosa rubiginosa vaillantina, commonly known as the red-stemmed rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Rosa rubiginosa vaillantina is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
