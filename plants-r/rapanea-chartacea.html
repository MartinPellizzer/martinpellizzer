Rapanea chartacea

Rapanea chartacea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters, and it produces dark blue or black berries.

Rapanea chartacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
