Rottboellia compressa subvar. gracilis

Rottboellia compressa subvar. gracilis, commonly known as slender flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and disturbed soils. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
