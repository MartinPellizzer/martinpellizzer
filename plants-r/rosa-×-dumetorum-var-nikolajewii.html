Rosa × dumetorum var. nikolajewii

Rosa × dumetorum var. nikolajewii, commonly known as the Nikolajewii rose, is a compact shrub with dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cooler climates. The plant produces pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics include a trailing growth habit and a high degree of cold tolerance.
