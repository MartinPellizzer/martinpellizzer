Ruellia indigotica

Ruellia indigotica, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

Ruellia indigotica is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
