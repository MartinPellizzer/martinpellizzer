Rosa lucida florepleno

Rosa lucida florepleno, commonly known as the full-flowered rose, is a species of shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, depending on the variety. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa lucida florepleno is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
