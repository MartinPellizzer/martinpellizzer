Restio Calcicola

Restio calcicola is a rare and delicate plant species native to the arid regions of South Africa. It thrives in limestone-rich soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. This grass-like plant is part of the Restiaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its study provides valuable insights into plant adaptations in extreme climates.
