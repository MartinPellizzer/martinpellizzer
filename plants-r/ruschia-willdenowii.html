Ruschia willdenowii

Ruschia willdenowii, commonly known as the Cape plume, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of delicate, feather-like flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps reduce water loss.

Ruschia willdenowii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
