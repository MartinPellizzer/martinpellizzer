Rosa iljinii

Rosa iljinii, commonly known as the Iljin rose, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to the Far East of Russia.

It is characterized by its clustered, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
