Rosa caesia subsp. eucaesia

Rosa caesia subsp.

eucaesia, commonly known as the European shrub rose, is a species native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its stems are armed with thorns, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
