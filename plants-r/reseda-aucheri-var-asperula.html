Reseda aucheri var. asperula

Reseda aucheri var. asperula, commonly known as the woolly reseda, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean and coastal climates.
