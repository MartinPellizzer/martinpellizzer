Rosettea sizenandoi var. sizenandoi

Rosettea sizenandoi var. sizenandoi, commonly known as the Sizenando's rosette, is a unique member of the Bromeliaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that form a central cup, which collects water. The leaves are typically a vibrant green, often with a waxy texture and a subtle sheen. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well adapted to epiphytic growth on trees.

Its botanical features make it a striking and relatively rare specimen in the bromeliad family.
