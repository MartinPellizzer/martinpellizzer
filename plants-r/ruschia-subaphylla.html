Ruschia subaphylla

Ruschia subaphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental appeal makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
