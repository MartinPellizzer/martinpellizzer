Rosa × cottetii

Rosa × cottetii, commonly known as the Cottetii rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively disease-resistant.

Rosa × cottetii thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
