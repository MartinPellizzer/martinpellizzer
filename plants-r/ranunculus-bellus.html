Ranunculus bellus

Ranunculus bellus, commonly known as the beautiful buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves and is often found in moist, temperate habitats. Ranunculus bellus is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

However, it is considered invasive in some regions due to its competitive nature.
