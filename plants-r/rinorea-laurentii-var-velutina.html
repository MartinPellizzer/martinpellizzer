Rinorea laurentii var. velutina

Rinorea laurentii var. velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf rinorea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
