Rosa communis subvar. fallens

Rosa communis subvar. fallens, commonly known as the autumn rose, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its stems are typically covered in thorns. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

The fallens subvariety is noted for its vibrant fall coloration, with leaves turning shades of red and orange before winter.
