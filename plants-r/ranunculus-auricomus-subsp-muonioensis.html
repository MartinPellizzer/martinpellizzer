Ranunculus auricomus subsp. muonioensis

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

muonioensis, commonly known as the Muonio buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions. Its leaves are palmately divided with a hairy surface, and it has a short, stout rhizome.

This subspecies is relatively rare and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
