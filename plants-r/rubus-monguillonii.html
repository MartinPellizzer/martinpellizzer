Rubus monguillonii

Rubus monguillonii, commonly known as Monguillon's raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern China. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and it has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats within forested areas.
