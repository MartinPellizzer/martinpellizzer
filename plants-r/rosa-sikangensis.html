Rosa sikangensis

Rosa sikangensis, commonly known as the Sichuan rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in rocky or forested habitats. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in thorns.

Rosa sikangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
