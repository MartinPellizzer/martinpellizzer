Rosa afzeliana var. brizzii

Rosa afzeliana var. brizzii, commonly known as the Brizzii rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from China.

It is characterized by its semi-evergreen to evergreen foliage, which retains its color throughout the year. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often appreciated by horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
