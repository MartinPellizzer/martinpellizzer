Rosa alpina var. multiplex

Rosa alpina var. multiplex, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is adapted to cold, mountainous environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is a hardy species that thrives in full sun.

Rosa alpina var. multiplex is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh climates.
