Restrepia dusenii

Restrepia dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's restrepia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates and is adapted to cool, humid environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, providing structural support for its aerial roots.

Restrepia dusenii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
