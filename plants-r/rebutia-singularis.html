Rebutia singularis

Rebutia singularis, commonly known as the singular rebutia, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
