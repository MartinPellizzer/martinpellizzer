Rosa × barthae

Rosa × barthae, commonly known as the Bartha rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa centifolia.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and abundant, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of pink and white. The plant is notable for its hardy nature, making it suitable for a variety of climates and garden conditions. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its showy flowers.

Rosa × barthae is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its beauty and resilience.
