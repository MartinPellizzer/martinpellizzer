Rossittia

Rossittia, commonly known as the roseapple tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant white flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to apples but have a sweet, aromatic flavor. Rossittia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental and fruit-bearing plant in tropical regions.
