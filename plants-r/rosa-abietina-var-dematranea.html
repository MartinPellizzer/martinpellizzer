Rosa abietina var. dematranea

Rosa abietina var. dematranea, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, slightly hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its roots are adapted to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils at high altitudes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand cold climates.
