Rosa × collina f. incerta

Rosa × collina f. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain hill rose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its variability in flower color and form, which contributes to its classification as a form.
