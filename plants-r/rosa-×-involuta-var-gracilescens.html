Rosa × involuta var. gracilescens

Rosa × involuta var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender curling rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces narrow, arching stems with small, glossy leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

This variety is relatively hardy and adaptable, making it suitable for a variety of garden conditions.
