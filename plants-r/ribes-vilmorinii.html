Ribes vilmorinii

Ribes vilmorinii, commonly known as the Vilmorin currant, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Ribes.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with alternate, simple, and ovate leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, which are edible and have a tart flavor.

Ribes vilmorinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of berries in horticultural settings.
