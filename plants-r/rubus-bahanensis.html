Rubus bahanensis

Rubus bahanensis, commonly known as the Bahan raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along forest edges.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and lesser-known member of the Rubus genus.
