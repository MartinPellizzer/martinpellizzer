Rubus × cavaticaulis

Rubus × cavaticaulis, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, edible berries that are a mix of blackberry and raspberry flavors.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential in horticulture.
