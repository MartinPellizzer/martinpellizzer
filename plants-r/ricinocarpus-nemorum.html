Ricinocarpus nemorum

It is a climbing shrub that can grow up to 20 meters in height, characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and distinctive, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain toxic seeds, which are a significant concern for livestock and humans.
