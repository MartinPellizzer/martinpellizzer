Racosperma spirorbe subsp. solandri

Racosperma spirorbe subsp.

solandri, commonly known as the Solander's racosperma, is a shrub native to eastern Australia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits.
