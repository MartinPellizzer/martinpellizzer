Rosa indica rubra

Rosa indica rubra, commonly known as the Crimson Indian Rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Rosa indica rubra is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in traditional medicine.
