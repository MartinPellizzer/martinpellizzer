Reseda aucheri subsp. rechingeri

Reseda aucheri subsp.

rechingeri, commonly known as the Alpine Reseda, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers.
