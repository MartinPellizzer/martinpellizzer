Roemeria hybrida var. simplex

Roemeria hybrida var. simplex, commonly known as the simple roemeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
