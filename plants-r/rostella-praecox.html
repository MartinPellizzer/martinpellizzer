Rostella praecox

Rostella praecox, commonly known as the early rostellia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Rostella praecox is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
