Rosa afzeliana f. binnensis

Rosa afzeliana f. binnensis, commonly known as the Binn's rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa afzeliana.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and adaptability to various soil types.
