Rhynchospora fujiiana f. retrososcabra

Rhynchospora fujiiana f. retrososcabra, commonly known as the retro-serrated sedge, is a botanical variant of the species Rhynchospora fujiiana.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
