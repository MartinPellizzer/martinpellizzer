Rapuntium alatum

Rapuntium alatum, commonly known as the winged cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, rocky habitats. Its seeds are small and contain essential oils, which contribute to its aromatic properties.

Rapuntium alatum is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
