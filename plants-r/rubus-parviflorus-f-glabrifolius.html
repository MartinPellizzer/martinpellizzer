Rubus parviflorus f. glabrifolius

Rubus parviflorus f. glabrifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leaved raspberry.

This plant is a variant of the common raspberry, characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves. It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, typically found in temperate regions. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges.

It produces small, aggregate fruits known as raspberries, which are edible and often used in culinary applications.
