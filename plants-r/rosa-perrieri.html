Rosa perrieri

Rosa perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri rose, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a serrated edge.

Rosa perrieri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
