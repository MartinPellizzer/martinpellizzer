Rosa disparilis

Rosa disparilis, commonly known as the variable rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea, and is characterized by its diverse range of flower colors and petal shapes. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rosa disparilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
