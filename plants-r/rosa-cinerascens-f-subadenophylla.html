Rosa cinerascens f. subadenophylla

Rosa cinerascens f. subadenophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a horticultural form of the species Rosa cinerascens.

It is characterized by its pale silver-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color, adding visual appeal to gardens. It is a compact shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

This cultivar is valued for its unique foliage and is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic qualities.
