Rosa × fontqueriana

Rosa × fontqueriana, commonly known as the French rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa × fontqueriana is often used in gardens for its beauty and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
