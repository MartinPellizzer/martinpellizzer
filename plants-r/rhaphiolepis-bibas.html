Rhaphiolepis bibas

Rhaphiolepis bibas, commonly known as the Japanese rosebay, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that provide a dense canopy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Rhaphiolepis bibas is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardiness.
