Rosa litophylla

Rosa litophylla, commonly known as the rock rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the rocky habitats of southern Europe and western Asia. This plant is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in well-drained, calcareous soils. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa litophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging environments.
