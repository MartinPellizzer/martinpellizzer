Rhynea

Rhynea, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has palm-like leaves that are compound and arranged in a fan shape. Rhynea is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and unique foliage.

It thrives in full sun and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
