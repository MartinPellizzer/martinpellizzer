Rosa × sagotii

Rosa × sagotii, commonly known as the Sagot rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It is native to the Philippines and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

Rosa × sagotii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
