Rosa mollis f. grossella

Rosa mollis f. grossella, commonly known as the grossella rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a slight blush of red. The plant has small, glossy, dark green leaves that are densely packed along its stems. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
