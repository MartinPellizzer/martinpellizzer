Rosa pendulina var. globosa

Rosa pendulina var. globosa, commonly known as the globe rose, is a cultivar of the rose family characterized by its unique, rounded, globe-like form.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is a compact shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
