Rosa annesiensis

Rosa annesiensis, commonly known as the Annesi rose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the island of Sardinia, where it grows in rocky and coastal environments. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and its thorns are slender and curved.

Rosa annesiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
