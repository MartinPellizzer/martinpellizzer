Roldana hartwegii var. subcymosa

Roldana hartwegii var. subcymosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved roldana, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a bluish-green hue and a hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.
