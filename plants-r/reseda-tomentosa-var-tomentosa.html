Reseda tomentosa var. tomentosa

Reseda tomentosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as wooly reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Reseda tomentosa var. tomentosa is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
