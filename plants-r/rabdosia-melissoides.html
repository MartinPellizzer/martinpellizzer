Rabdosia melissoides

Rabdosia melissoides, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both garden cultivation and ornamental use.

Rabdosia melissoides is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
