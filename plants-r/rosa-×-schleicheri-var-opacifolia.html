Rosa × schleicheri var. opacifolia

Rosa × schleicheri var. opacifolia, commonly known as the Opacifolia Rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features upright growth with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are glossy and slightly waxy. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

The varietal name "opacifolia" refers to its thick, opaque foliage, which distinguishes it from other rose varieties.
