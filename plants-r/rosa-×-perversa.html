Rosa × perversa

Rosa × perversa, commonly known as the "villainous rose," is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its deep red, semi-double to fully double flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its striking appearance and vigorous growth, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
