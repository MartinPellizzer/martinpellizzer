Regalia dura

Regalia dura, commonly known as the hard crown plant, is a unique species native to arid regions.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater.

Regalia dura plays an important role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
