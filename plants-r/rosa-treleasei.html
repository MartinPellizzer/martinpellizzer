Rosa treleasei

Rosa treleasei, commonly known as the Trelease rose, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub-like plant with woody stems and compound leaves that are typically trifoliate. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Rosa treleasei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
