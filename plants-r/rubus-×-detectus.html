Rubus × detectus

Rubus × detectus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry.

This plant is a cross between different species of the Rubus genus, resulting in a unique combination of traits. It typically produces dark purple to black berries that are prized for their flavor and nutritional value. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its thorny stems and dense foliage make it a hardy, resilient shrub.
