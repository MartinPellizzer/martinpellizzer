Rebutia knizei

Rebutia knizei, commonly known as the Knizei's moon cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant features a short, thick stem with areoles that bear spines and clusters of woolly hairs. Its flowers, which can be yellow, orange, or red, bloom in late spring and early summer.

Rebutia knizei is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun to thrive.
