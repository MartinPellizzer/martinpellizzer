Rubus Lasiotrichos Var. Blinii

Rubus lasiotrichos var. blinii is a rare and intriguing species of bramble native to specific regions of eastern europe. this variant is distinguished by its dense, velvety foliage and unique flowering patterns. it thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found along forest edges or in rocky crevices. due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a vulnerable species. its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
