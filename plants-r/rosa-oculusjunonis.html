Rosa oculusjunonis

Rosa oculusjunonis, commonly known as the Juniper-eyed rose, is a rare and ornamental species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, deep red to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant, pinkish-white fruits. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
