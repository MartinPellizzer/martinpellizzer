Rhododendron chloranthum

Rhododendron chloranthum, commonly known as the yellow rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions with acidic soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable ornamental plant in horticulture.
