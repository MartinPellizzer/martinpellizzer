Rudgea Minor Subsp. Calycina

Rudgea minor subsp. calycina is a lesser-known but fascinating member of the Malvaceae family. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure and striking foliage make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
