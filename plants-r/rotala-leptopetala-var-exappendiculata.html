Rotala leptopetala var. exappendiculata

Rotala leptopetala var. exappendiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rotala, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often floating on the water surface. It thrives in shallow, still waters with moderate sunlight and is often used in pond landscaping.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for water gardeners seeking to control algae growth.
