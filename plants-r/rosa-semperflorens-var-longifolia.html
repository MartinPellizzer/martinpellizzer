Rosa semperflorens var. longifolia

Rosa semperflorens var. longifolia, commonly known as the everblooming rose or the miniature rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly serrated edge. This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ease of maintenance.
