Rosa × discreta

Rosa × discreta, commonly known as the Discreta rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × discreta is relatively disease-resistant and suitable for garden borders or as a shrub in temperate climates.
