Rumex acetosa var. fontanopaludosus

Rumex acetosa var. fontanopaludosus, commonly known as swamp sorrel, is a variant of the common sorrel plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the veins. It produces small, greenish flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

This variety is often found in wetlands and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
