Rhododendron hancockii var. longisepalum

Rhododendron hancockii var. longisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal rhododendron, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant features dense, dark green foliage and is typically found in high-altitude, humid environments.
