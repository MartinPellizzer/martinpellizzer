Rulingia hermaniifolia

Rulingia hermaniifolia, commonly known as the Hairy Rulingia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
