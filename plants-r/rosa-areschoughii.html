Rosa areschoughii

Rosa areschoughii, commonly known as the Areschoug rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. This shrub typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The leaves are dark green, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are thorny.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
