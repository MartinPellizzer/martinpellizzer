Reseda fruticulosa var. attenuata

Reseda fruticulosa var. attenuata, commonly known as the slender mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, pale yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the cooler months.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
