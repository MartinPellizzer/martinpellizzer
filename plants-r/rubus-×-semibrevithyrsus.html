Rubus × semibrevithyrsus

Rubus × semibrevithyrsus, commonly known as the dwarf blackberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, dark red to black berries. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves with serrated edges.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
