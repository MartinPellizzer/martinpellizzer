Ribes sanguineum var. splendens

Ribes sanguineum var. splendens, commonly known as the California flame currant, is a deciduous shrub native to western North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red or orange in color. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn yellow in the fall.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
