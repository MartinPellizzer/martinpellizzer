Ricinocarpos marginatus

Ricinocarpos marginatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, green, ovate leaves with a narrow margin and its yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. It is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate saline conditions.

Its seeds are small and contain compounds that may have medicinal or toxic properties.
