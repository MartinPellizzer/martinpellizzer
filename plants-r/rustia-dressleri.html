Rustia dressleri

Rustia dressleri, commonly known as the Desert Beauty, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, storing water in its thick stems and leaves. It blooms during the rainy season, typically in winter, adding a striking visual contrast to the desert landscape.

Rustia dressleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
