Randia oxygyna

Randia oxygyna, commonly known as the white randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Randia oxygyna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
