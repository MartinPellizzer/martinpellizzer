Rosa communis var. oblonga

Rosa communis var. oblonga, commonly known as the oval rose, is a variant of the common rose characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped blooms.

This plant typically features pink to red flowers with a subtle fragrance, and its leaves are serrated and medium green. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates, requiring well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.

The oval rose is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and is relatively low maintenance once established.
