Rosa roffavieri

Rosa roffavieri, commonly known as the Roffavieri rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color. Rosa roffavieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.
