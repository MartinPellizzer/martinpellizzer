Rotala Mexicana Subsp. Pusilla

Rotala mexicana subsp. pusilla is a small, aquatic plant native to the southeastern united states. it thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is known for its delicate, floating leaves. this plant is often used in freshwater aquariums due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal. it produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a touch of beauty to its environment. despite its small size, it plays an important role in maintaining water quality and supporting aquatic life.
