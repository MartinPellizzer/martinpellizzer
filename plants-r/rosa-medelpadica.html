Rosa medelpadica

Rosa medelpadica, commonly known as the Medelpad rose, is a species native to southern Scandinavia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red, with five to seven petals. It produces hips, which are red, fleshy fruits containing seeds.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens in temperate climates.
