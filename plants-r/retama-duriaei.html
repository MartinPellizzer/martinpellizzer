Retama duriaei

Retama duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei whitethorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone areas.

Retama duriaei is adapted to dry climates and is considered a hardy, drought-tolerant species.
