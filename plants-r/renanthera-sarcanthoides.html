Renanthera sarcanthoides

Renanthera sarcanthoides, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Renanthera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces aerial roots, which are typical of many orchid species.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
