Rhapis laosensis

Rhapis laosensis, commonly known as the Laos bamboo palm, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a compact, clump-forming palm with slender, arching leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant features a short, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, brownish layer. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Rhapis laosensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering requirements.
