Rosa × iserana

Rosa × iserana, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a hybrid species native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and alpine landscapes.
