Ruschia rigidicaulis

Ruschia rigidicaulis, commonly known as the rigid stem Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
