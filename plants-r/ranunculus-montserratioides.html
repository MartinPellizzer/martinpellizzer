Ranunculus montserratioides

Ranunculus montserratioides, commonly known as the Montserratioides buttercup, is a species of flowering plant in the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula, particularly found in Spain and Portugal. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. It produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, which bloom in early spring.

The leaves are palmately divided, with a distinctive appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.
