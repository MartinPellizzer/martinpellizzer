Rotala leptopetala f. abbreviata

Rotala leptopetala f. abbreviata, commonly known as the compact rotala, is a compact aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in diameter, giving it a dense, bushy appearance. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquascaping for its ornamental value. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Its short stature makes it ideal for foreground planting in planted tanks.
