Ranunculus auricomus subsp. modestifrons

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

modestifrons, commonly known as the modest-fronded buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It features delicate, yellow flowers with five broadly ovate petals, typically arranged in a single bloom atop slender stems.

The leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. This subspecies is characterized by its smaller, more compact flowers compared to the typical *R. auricomus*.

It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks.
