Rhododendron × stanwellianum

Rhododendron × stanwellianum, commonly known as the Stanwell rhododendron, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rhododendron catawbiense and Rhododendron maximum.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is evergreen, with leathery leaves that provide year-round structure. It typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
