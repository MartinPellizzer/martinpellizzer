Rosa × fertilis

Rosa × fertilis, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a popular ornamental plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Rosa damascena and Rosa gallica, resulting in a plant with showy, fragrant blooms. The flowers are typically single or semi-double, with vibrant colors ranging from white to deep red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.

This cultivar is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is a staple in gardens and floral arrangements.
