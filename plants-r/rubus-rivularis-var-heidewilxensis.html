Rubus rivularis var. heidewilxensis

Rubus rivularis var. heidewilxensis, commonly known as the Heidewilx raspberry, is a shrub native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and local biodiversity.
