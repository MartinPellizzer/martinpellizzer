Rubus sprengelii var. nemocharis

Rubus sprengelii var. nemocharis, commonly known as the Nemocharis raspberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It produces small, edible berries that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces white or pink flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cool, temperate climate.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
