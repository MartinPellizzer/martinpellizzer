Rhododendron sutchuenense

Rhododendron sutchuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in cool, humid climates at high altitudes. The plant features dense, leathery leaves and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
