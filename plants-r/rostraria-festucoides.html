Rostraria festucoides

Rostraria festucoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf rostraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, commonly referred to as grasses.

It is a perennial species characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green flowers. Rostraria festucoides is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
