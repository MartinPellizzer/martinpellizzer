Ruta thesioides

Ruta thesioides, commonly known as mountain rue, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, berry-like fruits. Ruta thesioides thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
