Rhagodia prostrata

Rhagodia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate rhagodia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its sprawling habit and small, greenish-white flowers. Rhagodia prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
