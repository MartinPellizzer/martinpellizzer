Rhododendron tuba

Rhododendron tuba, commonly known as the tubular rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that range in color from deep pink to violet. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rhododendron tuba thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.
