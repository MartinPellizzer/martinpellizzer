Rosa × majorugosa

Rosa × majorugosa, commonly known as the Majorugosa rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering, typically producing large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage that provides a dense canopy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders and hedging.

Rosa × majorugosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
