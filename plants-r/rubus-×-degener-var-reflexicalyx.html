Rubus × degener var. reflexicalyx

Rubus × degener var. reflexicalyx, commonly known as the reflexed blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the flowers are usually white or pale pink.

The berries are dark purple to black when ripe, and they are often used in culinary applications.
