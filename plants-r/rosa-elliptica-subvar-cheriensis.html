Rosa elliptica subvar. cheriensis

Rosa elliptica subvar. cheriensis, commonly known as the Cherries Rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size.

Its ornamental value comes from its fragrant blooms and attractive foliage.
