Reissantia indica var. astericantha

Reissantia indica var. astericantha, commonly known as the starflower or astericantha, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
