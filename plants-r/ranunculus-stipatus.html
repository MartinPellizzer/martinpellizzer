Ranunculus stipatus

Ranunculus stipatus, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, bright yellow flowers with 5 to 15 petals, typically appearing in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are covered in a soft, woolly coating. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Ranunculus stipatus is often found in moist soils and is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.
