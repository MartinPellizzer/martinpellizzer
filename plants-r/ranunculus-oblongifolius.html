Ranunculus oblongifolius

Ranunculus oblongifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically borne on slender stems. The plant has heart-shaped or broadly ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Ranunculaceae family.
