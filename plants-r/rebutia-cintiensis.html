Rebutia cintiensis

Rebutia cintiensis, commonly known as the Cintiensis Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. Rebutia cintiensis is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
