Romulea albomarginata

Romulea albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin gladiolus, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

Romulea albomarginata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
