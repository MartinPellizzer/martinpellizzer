Roella dregeana var. nitida

Roella dregeana var. nitida, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers in spring, followed by edible, fleshy fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
