Rosa bohemica var. annae

Rosa bohemica var. annae, commonly known as Bohemian rose anna, is a hybrid rose species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and suitable for hedge or shrub borders.
