Rosa × belnensis

Rosa × belnensis, commonly known as the German tea rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is relatively disease-resistant and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

Rosa × belnensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
