Rubroshorea macrophylla

Rubroshorea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf redshorea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often reddish in color when young. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
