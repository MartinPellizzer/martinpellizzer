Rhododendron augustinei f. rubrum

Rhododendron augustinei f. rubrum, commonly known as the Red-flowered Augustine Rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy flowers. The plant produces large, showy blooms in shades of red and pink, typically appearing in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes where its vibrant flowers can be appreciated.
