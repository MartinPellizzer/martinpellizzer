Rhododendron caucasicum var. stramineum

Rhododendron caucasicum var. stramineum, commonly known as the Caucasian yellow rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its leathery, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique coloration and compact growth habit.
