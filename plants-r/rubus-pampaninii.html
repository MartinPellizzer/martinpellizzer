Rubus pampaninii

Rubus pampaninii, commonly known as the Pampas raspberry, is a species of bramble native to South America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of native flora.
