Rubus × burbensis

Rubus × burbensis, commonly known as the Burbans rose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many rose species. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits, known as raspberries, are aggregate fruits formed from multiple small drupes.

Rubus × burbensis is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its edible fruits.
