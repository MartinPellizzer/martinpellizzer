Rhododendron × gandavense f. plenum

Rhododendron × gandavense f. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered rhododendron, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to produce large, showy flowers. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant floral displays.
