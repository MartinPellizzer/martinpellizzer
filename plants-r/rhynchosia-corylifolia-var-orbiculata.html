Rhynchosia corylifolia var. orbiculata

Rhynchosia corylifolia var. orbiculata, commonly known as the broad-leaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its palmately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing edible seeds. It is often cultivated for its nutritional value and as a forage crop.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.
