Reseda muricata var. muricata

Reseda muricata var. muricata, commonly known as the rough Reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, stiff hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
