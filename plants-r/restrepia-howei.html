Restrepia howei

Restrepia howei, commonly known as the Howei's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the dry season. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Restrepia howei is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
