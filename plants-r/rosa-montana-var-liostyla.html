Rosa montana var. liostyla

Rosa montana var. liostyla, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double blooms in late spring to early summer. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

The species is also known for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions.
