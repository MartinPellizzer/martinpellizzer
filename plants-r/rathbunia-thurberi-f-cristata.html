Rathbunia thurberi f. cristata

Rathbunia thurberi f. cristata, commonly known as the cristate thurberia, is a unique cultivar of the thurberia plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flattened, and often rugose growth habit, which gives it a striking appearance. This plant is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is typically found in wetland areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

The f. cristata form is particularly noted for its unusual, crested growth pattern that sets it apart from the standard variety.
