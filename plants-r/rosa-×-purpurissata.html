Rosa × purpurissata

Rosa × purpurissata, commonly known as the deep purple rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, deep purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub, typically reaching heights of 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

The flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
