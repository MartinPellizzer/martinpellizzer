Rosa micrantha var. eriostyla

Rosa micrantha var. eriostyla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color, blooming in spring. The plant has slender, thornless stems and narrow, elliptical leaves with a soft, hairy texture. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental flowers and adaptability to cooler climates.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
