Russelia ternifolia

Russelia ternifolia, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with slender, hairy stems and opposite leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators.

Russelia ternifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
