Rungia eberhardtii

Rungia eberhardtii, commonly known as the dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Rungia eberhardtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
