Rosa communis var. minor

Rosa communis var. minor, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its compact size makes it ideal for borders, rock gardens, and as a decorative element in landscaping.
