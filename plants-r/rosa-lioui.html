Rosa lioui

Rosa lioui, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in clusters. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

Rosa lioui is also noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand colder climates.
