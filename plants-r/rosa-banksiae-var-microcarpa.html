Rosa banksiae var. microcarpa

Rosa banksiae var. microcarpa, commonly known as the miniature bank's rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape and covered in fine hairs. It is characterized by its dwarf growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and resilience in various growing conditions.
