Rumex scutatus var. hastifolius

Rumex scutatus var. hastifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape, with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides or in wet meadows.

Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional cuisine and herbal remedies.
