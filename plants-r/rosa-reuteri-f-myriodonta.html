Rosa reuteri f. myriodonta

Rosa reuteri f. myriodonta, commonly known as the myriodonta rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, thornless stems and small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silvery-white down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
