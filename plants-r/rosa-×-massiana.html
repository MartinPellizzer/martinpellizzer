Rosa × massiana

Rosa × massiana, commonly known as the Alba Plena rose, is a hybrid rose species that combines characteristics of both *Rosa alba* and *Rosa × damascena*.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with five to seven leaflets. The plant produces large, double flowers with white petals and a subtle fragrance, blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Rosa × massiana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
