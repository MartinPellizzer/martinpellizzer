Reseda aucheri subsp. transitoria

Reseda aucheri subsp.

transitoria, commonly known as the Transitory Reseda, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It features slender, upright stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
