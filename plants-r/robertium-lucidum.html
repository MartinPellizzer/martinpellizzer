Robertium lucidum

Robertium lucidum, commonly known as the glossy rosette plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy, glossy surface. The plant forms a compact rosette structure, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
