Rabdosia namikawana

Rabdosia namikawana, commonly known as the Namikawa Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually purple or blue in color.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
