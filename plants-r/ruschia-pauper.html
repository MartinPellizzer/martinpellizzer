Ruschia pauper

Ruschia pauper, commonly known as the poor man's ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and low maintenance make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking easy-to-care-for succulents.
