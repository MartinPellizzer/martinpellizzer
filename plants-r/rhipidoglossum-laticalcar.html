Rhipidoglossum laticalcar

Rhipidoglossum laticalcar, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South Africa, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

Rhipidoglossum laticalcar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
