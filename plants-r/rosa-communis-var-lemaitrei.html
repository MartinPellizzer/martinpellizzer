Rosa communis var. lemaitrei

Rosa communis var. lemaitrei, commonly known as the Lemaitre rose, is a rare and ornamental variety of the rose plant.

It is characterized by its deep pink, semi-double flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
