Roldana Kerberi Var. Manantlanensis

Roldana kerberi var. manantlanensis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. It is known for its unique, velvety leaves that display a range of colors from deep green to purple. This variant of Roldana kerberi is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts due to its distinctive appearance and slow growth rate. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires careful attention to light and soil conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of horticulture.
