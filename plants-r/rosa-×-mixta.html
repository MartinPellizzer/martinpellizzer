Rosa × mixta

Rosa × mixta, commonly known as the hybrid rose, is a species resulting from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa chinensis.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a layered petal structure. Its thorny stems and dense foliage make it a hardy ornamental shrub suitable for hedges or garden borders.

Rosa × mixta is also valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to different climates.
