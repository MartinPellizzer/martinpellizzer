Ruschia hexamera var. longipetala

Ruschia hexamera var. longipetala, commonly known as the Long-petioled Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. The leaves are typically thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

The plant is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
