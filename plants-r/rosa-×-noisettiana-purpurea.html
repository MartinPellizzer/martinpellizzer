Rosa × noisettiana purpurea

Rosa × noisettiana purpurea, commonly known as the deep pink musk rose, is a hybrid rose species known for its rich, deep pink flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and valuable in horticultural practices.
