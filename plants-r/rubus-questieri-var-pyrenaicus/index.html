Rubus Questieri Var. Pyrenaicus

Rubus questieri var. pyrenaicus is a rare and fascinating plant species native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula. This variant of the common bramble is known for its delicate appearance and unique ecological adaptations. It thrives in rocky and shaded environments, showcasing a resilient nature despite its fragile look. The plant's flowers are small but striking, often displaying shades of pink and white. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
