Ranunculus telonensis

Ranunculus telonensis, commonly known as the Persian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a hairy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Ranunculus telonensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
