Rosa micrantha f. varians

Rosa micrantha f. varians, commonly known as the variable shrub rose, is a botanical variant of the shrub rose species Rosa micrantha.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and compact form, making it suitable for hedging or ornamental planting. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
