Rosa globularis

Rosa globularis, commonly known as the globe rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit. The plant produces showy, double flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged in an alternate pattern.

Rosa globularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms.
