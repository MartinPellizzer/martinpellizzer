Ruschia integra

Ruschia integra, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a striking visual appeal. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months, adding color to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a potted plant.
