Rosa × anemonoides

Rosa × anemonoides, commonly known as the Anemonoides rose, is a hybrid shrub that combines traits of roses and anemones.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red, during the spring and early summer. Its foliage consists of finely cut, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blossoms.

Rosa × anemonoides thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
