Rabdosia pharica

Rabdosia pharica, commonly known as the fragrant leaved rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emit a strong, spicy scent when crushed.

Rabdosia pharica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
