Rosa micrantha f. vallesiaca

Rosa micrantha f. vallesiaca, commonly known as the Vallesina rose, is a compact shrub native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is well adapted to high-altitude environments and requires well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its delicate beauty.
