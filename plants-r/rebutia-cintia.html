Rebutia cintia

Rebutia cintia, commonly known as the Orange Ghost Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the dry regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces vibrant orange to yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Rebutia cintia is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
