Rosa heckeliana var. szyszylowiczii

Rosa heckeliana var. szyszylowiczii, commonly known as the Szyszylowicz's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features upright, thornless stems with dark green, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
