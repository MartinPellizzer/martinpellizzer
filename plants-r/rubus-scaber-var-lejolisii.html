Rubus scaber var. lejolisii

Rubus scaber var. lejolisii, commonly known as the roughberry or lejolis raspberry, is a variant of the brambles in the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its prickly stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or orange when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
