Ramonda × vandedemii

Ramonda × vandedemii, commonly known as the hybrid alpine bellflower, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Its foliage consists of broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Ramonda × vandedemii thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forested slopes.
