Rubia tenuifolia var. stenophylla

Rubia tenuifolia var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leafed woodruff, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
