Rumex pulcher subsp. pulcher

Rumex pulcher subsp.

pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive reddish or purple underside.

The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
