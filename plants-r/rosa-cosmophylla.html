Rosa cosmophylla

Rosa cosmophylla, commonly known as the cosmopolitan rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to various regions across the globe, which contributes to its common name. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and serrated, with a glossy green upper surface.

Rosa cosmophylla is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to different climates.
