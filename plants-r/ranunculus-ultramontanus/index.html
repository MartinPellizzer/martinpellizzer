Ranunculus Ultramontanus

Ranunculus ultramontanus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. This species is known for its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is now considered endangered in many parts of its range. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant.
