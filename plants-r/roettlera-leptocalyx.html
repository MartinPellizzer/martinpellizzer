Roettlera leptocalyx

Roettlera leptocalyx, commonly known as the slender-leaved roettlera, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Roettlera leptocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
