Rotala taiwaniana

Rotala taiwaniana, commonly known as Taiwan waterwheel, is a species of aquatic plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which are typically bright green and slightly pointed. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and marshes. Its roots help stabilize sediment, making it beneficial for aquatic ecosystems.

Rotala taiwaniana is also popular in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
