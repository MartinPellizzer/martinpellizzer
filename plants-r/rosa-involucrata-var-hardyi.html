Rosa involucrata var. hardyi

Rosa involucrata var. hardyi, commonly known as the Hardy rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa involucrata species, which is native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is known for its hardiness, capable of surviving in colder climates compared to other rose varieties. Its foliage is dark green and dense, providing an attractive backdrop to its blooms.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience.
