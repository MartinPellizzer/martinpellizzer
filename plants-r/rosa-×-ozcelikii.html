Rosa × ozcelikii

Rosa × ozcelikii, commonly known as the Turkish rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and the production of large, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × ozcelikii is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
