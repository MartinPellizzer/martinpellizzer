Ranunculus huetii var. glabrescens

Ranunculus huetii var. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous huetii buttercup, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, usually bright-colored flowers. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a smooth, glabrous surface, which distinguishes it from other varieties of Ranunculus. It produces small, yellowish flowers with numerous stamens, typical of the genus.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
