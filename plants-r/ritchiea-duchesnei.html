Ritchiea duchesnei

Ritchiea duchesnei, commonly known as the Dusky Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish flowers, which are often adorned with intricate white or green markings. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, tall flower spike that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Ritchiea duchesnei is considered a conservation concern.
