Rytilix granularis

Rytilix granularis, commonly known as the granular leaf beetle, is a species of leaf beetle belonging to the family Chrysomelidae.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped body and a distinctive granular texture on its elytra. This plant-feeding beetle is often found on various species of plants, particularly those in the genus Eucalyptus. Its coloration typically ranges from reddish-brown to dark brown, with intricate patterns that provide camouflage in its natural habitat.

Due to its specific host preferences, it plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native environment.
