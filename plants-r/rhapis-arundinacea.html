Rhapis arundinacea

Rhapis arundinacea, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender, arching leaves that give it a graceful, palm-like appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time. Its foliage is dark green and often has a slightly glossy texture.

Rhapis arundinacea is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering requirements.
