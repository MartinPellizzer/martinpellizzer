Reynoutria scandens var. cristata

Reynoutria scandens var. cristata, commonly known as the curly burr bamboo, is a variant of the giant bamboo species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curling leaves that form a unique, ornamental appearance. This plant is a clump-forming perennial, often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it requires careful management in natural environments.
