Rhododendron vestitum

Rhododendron vestitum, commonly known as the woolly rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its flowers are usually pink to red, appearing in clusters during the spring.

Rhododendron vestitum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in mountainous regions.
