Rosa rubelliflora

Rosa rubelliflora, commonly known as the red-flowered rose, is a species of shrub native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pinkish-red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa rubelliflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
