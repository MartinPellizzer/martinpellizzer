Ranunculus elbrusensis var. aucheri

Ranunculus elbrusensis var. aucheri, commonly known as the Elbrus buttercup, is a rare and striking alpine plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in rocky or alpine meadows.
