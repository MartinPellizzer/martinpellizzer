Rotala heteropetala var. engleri

Rotala heteropetala var. engleri, commonly known as Engler's heteropetala rotala, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features bright green, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or pink, especially when exposed to strong light. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the water surface. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value.

Its adaptability to various water conditions makes it a popular choice for both natural and artificial aquatic habitats.
