Rolandra fruticosa

Rolandra fruticosa, commonly known as the rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

Rolandra fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
