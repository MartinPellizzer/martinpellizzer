Rubus vestitus var. concolor

Rubus vestitus var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth blackberry.

It is a variant of the blackberry plant characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically tart and used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruits.
