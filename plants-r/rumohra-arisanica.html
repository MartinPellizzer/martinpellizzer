Rumohra arisanica

Rumohra arisanica, commonly known as the Japanese silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white plumes that appear in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Rumohra arisanica is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
