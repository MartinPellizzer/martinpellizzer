Rhododendron kiyosumense subsp. kiyosumense

Rhododendron kiyosumense subsp.

kiyosumense, commonly known as the Kiyosumi rhododendron, is a species native to Japan. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
