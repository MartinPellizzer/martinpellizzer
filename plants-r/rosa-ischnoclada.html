Rosa ischnoclada

Rosa ischnoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with five petals and a strong fragrance.

Rosa ischnoclada is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
