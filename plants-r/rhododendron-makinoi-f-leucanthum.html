Rhododendron makinoi f. leucanthum

Rhododendron makinoi f. leucanthum, commonly known as the white-flowered form of Makino's rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, leathery foliage that provides a lush green backdrop for its showy white blooms. The flowers are typically tubular with a white corolla, often adorned with a faint pink or red hue at the base. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add a delicate, elegant touch to garden landscapes.
