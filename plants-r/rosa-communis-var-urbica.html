Rosa communis var. urbica

Rosa communis var. urbica, commonly known as the urban rose, is a cultivar of the common rose that thrives in urban environments.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This variety is well-suited for small spaces and can tolerate pollution and compacted soils, making it ideal for city gardens. Its leaves are dark green and serrated, while its stems are covered in thorns.

The plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to urban conditions.
