Rosa × impariserrata

Rosa × impariserrata, commonly known as the double shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and is native to China. The plant typically produces fragrant, double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. Its botanical features include thorny stems, compound leaves with serrated edges, and a robust root system.

Rosa × impariserrata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
