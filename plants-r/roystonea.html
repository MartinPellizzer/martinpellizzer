Roystonea

Roystonea, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fruit, which contains a single seed. Roystonea species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are used in landscaping.

They thrive in warm, humid climates and require well-drained soil for optimal growth.
