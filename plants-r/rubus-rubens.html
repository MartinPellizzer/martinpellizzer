Rubus rubens

Rubus rubens, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a species of shrub in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces bright red, aggregate fruits that are edible and widely cultivated for their flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Rubus rubens is also known for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local wildlife.
