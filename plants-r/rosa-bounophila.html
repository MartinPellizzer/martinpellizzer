Rosa bounophila

Rosa bounophila, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five to seven leaflets and thorny stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Rosa bounophila is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
