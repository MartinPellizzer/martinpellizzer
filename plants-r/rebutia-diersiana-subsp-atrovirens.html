Rebutia diersiana subsp. atrovirens

Rebutia diersiana subsp.

atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America, specifically Argentina. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit.

The plant features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, spiny areoles, and it produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative cactus in rock gardens and greenhouses.
