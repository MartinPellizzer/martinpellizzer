Rosa rubiginosa var. jenensis

Rosa rubiginosa var. jenensis, commonly known as the Jenens rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features thorny stems and is characterized by its dark red to purplish stems, which are often covered in a waxy bloom. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to pale red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, while the plant is generally hardy and drought-tolerant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and resilience.
