Ritchiea glossopetala

Ritchiea glossopetala, commonly known as the broad-leafed cycad, is a unique species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces cones, with the female cones being particularly large and ornate. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered an important species for conservation efforts.
