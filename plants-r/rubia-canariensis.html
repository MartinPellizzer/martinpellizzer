Rubia canariensis

Rubia canariensis, commonly known as Canary rue, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically oval and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rubia canariensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
