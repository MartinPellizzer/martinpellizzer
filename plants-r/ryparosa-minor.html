Ryparosa minor

Ryparosa minor, commonly known as the lesser rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has woody stems and small, leathery leaves that help it conserve water in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant for rock gardens.

Ryparosa minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
