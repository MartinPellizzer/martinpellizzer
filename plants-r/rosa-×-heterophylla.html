Rosa × heterophylla

Rosa × heterophylla, commonly known as the everglast rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a hardy ornamental plant. The plant typically produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with thorny stems that provide structural support.

Rosa × heterophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
