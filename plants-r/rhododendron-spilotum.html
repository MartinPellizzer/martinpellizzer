Rhododendron spilotum

Rhododendron spilotum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in acidic soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. The plant features dense clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and often have a waxy coating.

Rhododendron spilotum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
