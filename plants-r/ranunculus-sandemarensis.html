Ranunculus sandemarensis

Ranunculus sandemarensis, commonly known as the Sandemarensis buttercup, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high mountain regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features finely divided, feather-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
