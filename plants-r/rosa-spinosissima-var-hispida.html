Rosa spinosissima var. hispida

Rosa spinosissima var. hispida, commonly known as the hispid rose, is a thorny shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, upright growth with deep green, ovate leaves covered in fine, stiff hairs. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its stems are armed with sharp spines, and its roots are fibrous and drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in gardens.
