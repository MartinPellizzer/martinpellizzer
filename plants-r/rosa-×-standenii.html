Rosa × standenii

Rosa × standenii, commonly known as the Standen rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range from pink to deep red in color. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of blooms that are highly valued in perfumery and horticulture.

Rosa × standenii is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
