Rubus ribis-folius

Rubus ribis-folius, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe, containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
