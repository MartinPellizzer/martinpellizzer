Rosa omeiensis

Rosa omeiensis, commonly known as the Mei River rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its botanical features include compound leaves and an upright growth habit.

Rosa omeiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
