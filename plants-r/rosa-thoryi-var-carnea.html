Rosa thoryi var. carnea

Rosa thoryi var. carnea, commonly known as the pink thoryi rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features glossy, dark green foliage and produces showy, semi-double flowers that range from pale pink to deep rose in color. The plant is known for its strong, pleasant fragrance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is a valued addition to both landscape design and floral arrangements.
