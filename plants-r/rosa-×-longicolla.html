Rosa × longicolla

Rosa × longicolla, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has thorny stems. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is known for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.

Rosa × longicolla is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
