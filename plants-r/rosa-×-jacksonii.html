Rosa × jacksonii

Rosa × jacksonii, commonly known as the Jackson's rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and is typically deciduous. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × jacksonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
