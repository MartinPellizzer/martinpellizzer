Rabdosia kurzii

Rabdosia kurzii, commonly known as the narrowleaf rabdosia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters.

Rabdosia kurzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
