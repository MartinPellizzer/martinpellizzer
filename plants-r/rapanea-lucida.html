Rapanea lucida

Rapanea lucida, commonly known as the shining evergreen or glossy leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, the Philippines, and Malaysia. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries.

Rapanea lucida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
