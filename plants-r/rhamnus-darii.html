Rhamnus darii

Rhamnus darii, commonly known as the Darii buckthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is native to parts of Central Asia, including regions of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a small, smooth, black drupe that ripens in the fall.

Rhamnus darii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
