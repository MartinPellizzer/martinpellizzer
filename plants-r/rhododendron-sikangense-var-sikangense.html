Rhododendron sikangense var. sikangense

Rhododendron sikangense var. sikangense, commonly known as the Sichuan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high elevations, often in humid, cool climates. The plant features dense clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
