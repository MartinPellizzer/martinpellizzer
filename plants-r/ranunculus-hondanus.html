Ranunculus hondanus

Ranunculus hondanus, commonly known as the Cape buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has hairy stems and leaves. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers bloom in spring.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
