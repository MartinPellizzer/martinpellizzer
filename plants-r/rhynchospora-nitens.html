Rhynchospora nitens

Rhynchospora nitens, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered spikelets. Native to wetland habitats, it thrives in moist, acidic soils, often found in bogs and marshes. The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers, which are typically green or reddish in color.

Rhynchospora nitens plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
