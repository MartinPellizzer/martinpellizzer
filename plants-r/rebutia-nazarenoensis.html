Rebutia nazarenoensis

Rebutia nazarenoensis, commonly known as the Nazareno's Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and ribbed structure. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, with spines that provide protection and reduce water loss.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
