Rhodophiala ananuca

Rhodophiala ananuca, commonly known as the golden bell orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South America.

Rhodophiala ananuca is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
