Rosa × centifolia var. muscosa

Rosa × centifolia var. muscosa, commonly known as the mossy garden rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in perfumery due to their strong, sweet scent.

This variety is relatively hardy and adaptable to various soil types, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
