Rondeletia odorata subsp. grandifolia

Rondeletia odorata subsp.

grandifolia, commonly known as the sweet shower or sweet shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color.

The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
