Rubus × flavinanus

Rubus × flavinanus, commonly known as the yellow raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange fruits. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many Rubus species. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces aggregate fruits composed of small drupes.

This plant is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
