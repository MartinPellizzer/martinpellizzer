Radermachera elmeri var. fragrans

Radermachera elmeri var. fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant radermachera, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter beneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by elliptical, reddish-brown fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and pleasant scent.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
