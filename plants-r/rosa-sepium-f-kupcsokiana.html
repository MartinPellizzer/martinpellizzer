Rosa sepium f. kupcsokiana

Rosa sepium f. kupcsokiana, commonly known as the Kupcsokiana rose, is a unique hybrid within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the blossoms. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
