Rosa carolina var. florepleno

Rosa carolina var. florepleno, commonly known as the double-flowered Carolina rose, is a cultivar of the native Rosa carolina species.

It is characterized by its abundant, double blooms that are typically pink to red in color, offering a striking visual display. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to full double flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves.

This variety is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in partial to full sun with well-drained soil.
