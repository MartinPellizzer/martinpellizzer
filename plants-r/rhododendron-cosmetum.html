Rhododendron cosmetum

Rhododendron cosmetum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and prefers cool, moist climates. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in early spring.

Rhododendron cosmetum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
