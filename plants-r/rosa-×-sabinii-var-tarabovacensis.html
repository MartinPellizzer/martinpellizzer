Rosa × sabinii var. tarabovacensis

Rosa × sabinii var. tarabovacensis, commonly known as the Tarabovac rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its foliage is dark green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
