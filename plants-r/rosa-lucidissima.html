Rosa lucidissima

Rosa lucidissima, commonly known as the brilliant rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its stems are covered in thorns, typical of many rose species.

Rosa lucidissima is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
