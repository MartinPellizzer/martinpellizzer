Rosa onoei

Rosa onoei, commonly known as the Japanese rose or Onoe rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa onoei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
