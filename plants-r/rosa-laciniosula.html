Rosa laciniosula

Rosa laciniosula, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with thin, elongated leaflets that give it a delicate appearance.

Rosa laciniosula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sunny environments.
