Restrepia aristulifera

Restrepia aristulifera, commonly known as the aristulifera orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with distinctive red or purple markings.

Restrepia aristulifera thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
