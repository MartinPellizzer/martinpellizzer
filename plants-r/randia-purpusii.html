Randia purpusii

Randia purpusii, commonly known as the purple randia, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a reddish hue. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Randia purpusii is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
