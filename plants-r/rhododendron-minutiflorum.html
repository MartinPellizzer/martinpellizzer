Rhododendron minutiflorum

Rhododendron minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf flowered rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its blossoms, which appear in late spring, are usually pink to purple in color and are borne in racemes.

Rhododendron minutiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
