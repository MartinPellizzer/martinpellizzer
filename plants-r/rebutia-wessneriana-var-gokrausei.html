Rebutia wessneriana var. gokrausei

Rebutia wessneriana var. gokrausei, commonly known as the Gokrausei Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to southern Africa.

It features dense, woolly spines and rounded, ribbed stems that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
