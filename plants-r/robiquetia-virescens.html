Robiquetia virescens

Robiquetia virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered bellflower, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, with a bell-shaped corolla. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Robiquetia virescens is valued for its unique coloration and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
