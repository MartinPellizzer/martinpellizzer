Rosa globulifera

Rosa globulifera, commonly known as the globular rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has fragrant blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa globulifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
