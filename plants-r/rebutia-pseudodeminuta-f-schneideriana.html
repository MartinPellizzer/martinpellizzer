Rebutia pseudodeminuta f. schneideriana

Rebutia pseudodeminuta f. schneideriana, commonly known as the Schneider's Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a spherical or cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, which are a characteristic feature of many cacti.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its compact size and ornamental flowers.
