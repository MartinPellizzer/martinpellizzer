Rosa coriifolia var. incana

Rosa coriifolia var. incana, commonly known as the hairy rose or Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, arching branches and small, white to pale pink flowers that appear in early spring. The leaves are typically oval with a slightly hairy surface, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
