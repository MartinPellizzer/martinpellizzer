Rustia haitiensis

Rustia haitiensis, commonly known as Haitian rustia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, round fruits.
