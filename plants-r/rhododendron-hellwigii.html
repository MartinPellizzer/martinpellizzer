Rhododendron hellwigii

Rhododendron hellwigii, commonly known as the Hellwig's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in high-altitude forests and rocky slopes. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the spring.

Rhododendron hellwigii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
