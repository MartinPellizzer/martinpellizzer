Russelia floribunda

Russelia floribunda, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces showy inflorescences that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Russelia floribunda is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
