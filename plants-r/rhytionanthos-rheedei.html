Rhytionanthos rheedei

Rhytionanthos rheedei, commonly known as the Red-hot Poker, is a striking succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky flower spikes that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, giving it its common name. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a visually appealing addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
