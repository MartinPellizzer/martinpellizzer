Rhamnus alaternus var. picenensis

Rhamnus alaternus var. picenensis, commonly known as the Picene buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern Europe, particularly Italy.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible but typically not consumed by humans.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
