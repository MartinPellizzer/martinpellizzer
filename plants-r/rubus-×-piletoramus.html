Rubus × piletoramus

Rubus × piletoramus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
