Rosa communis var. haberiana

Rosa communis var. haberiana, commonly known as the Haberian rose, is a rare and ornamental variety of the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
