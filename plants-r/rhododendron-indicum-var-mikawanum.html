Rhododendron indicum var. mikawanum

Rhododendron indicum var. mikawanum, commonly known as the Mikawanum azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
