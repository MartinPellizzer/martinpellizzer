Racosperma tropicum

Racosperma tropicum, commonly known as the tropical racosperma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Australia and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow or white pea-like flowers. Its pods contain small, round seeds that are often dispersed by wind.

Racosperma tropicum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
