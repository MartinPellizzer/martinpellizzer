Rosa × gracilescens

Rosa × gracilescens, commonly known as the everglast rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is typically a shrub with multiple stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, often with a high degree of variability in color. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × gracilescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
