Rubus × pseudocymigerus

Rubus × pseudocymigerus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, dark berries that are often used in culinary applications. The plant is native to regions of eastern Asia, including China and Japan.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for both its ornamental and edible qualities.
