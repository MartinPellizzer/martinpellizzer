Rosa × firmicaulis

Rosa × firmicaulis, commonly known as the everblooming shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species known for its continuous flowering throughout the growing season.

It is characterized by its dense, thorned stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers. The plant is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for borders, containers, and small garden spaces.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its showy blooms.
