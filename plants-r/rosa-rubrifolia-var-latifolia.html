Rosa rubrifolia var. latifolia

Rosa rubrifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf redleaf rose, is a variant of the redleaf rose species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically reddish in color, especially when young. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and floral display.
