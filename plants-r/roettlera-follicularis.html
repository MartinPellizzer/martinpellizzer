Roettlera follicularis

Roettlera follicularis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in humid, well-drained conditions. Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
