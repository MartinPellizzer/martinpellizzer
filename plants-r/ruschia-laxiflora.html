Ruschia laxiflora

Ruschia laxiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
