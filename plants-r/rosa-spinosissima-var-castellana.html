Rosa spinosissima var. castellana

Rosa spinosissima var. castellana, commonly known as the Castellana rose, is a compact shrub with dense, thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves.

It produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in garden borders or as a hedge.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.
