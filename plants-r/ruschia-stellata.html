Ruschia stellata

Ruschia stellata, commonly known as the star Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, which bloom in spring. The plant has fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Ruschia stellata is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
