Rabdosia stenophylla

Rabdosia stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas.

Rabdosia stenophylla is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
