Rubus × acicularis

Rubus × acicularis, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are oval in shape. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a dark purple to black berry, typically harvested in late summer.
