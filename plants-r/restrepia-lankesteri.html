Restrepia lankesteri

Restrepia lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankester's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high Andes of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with yellowish markings. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its alpine habitat.

Restrepia lankesteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
