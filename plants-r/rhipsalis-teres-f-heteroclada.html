Rhipsalis teres f. heteroclada

Rhipsalis teres f. heteroclada, commonly known as the silver mist cactus, is a unique hybrid form of the Rhipsalis genus.

It is a climbing or trailing cactus native to Brazil, characterized by its soft, pale green stems that often have a silvery or white waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in winter, adding a delicate ornamental touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact growth and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
