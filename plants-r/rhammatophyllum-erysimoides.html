Rhammatophyllum erysimoides

Rhammatophyllum erysimoides, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of tiny, succulent-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its adaptations include reduced leaf size and thickened stems, which help conserve water in dry environments.

Rhammatophyllum erysimoides is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in harsh, sun-exposed conditions.
