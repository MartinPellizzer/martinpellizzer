Roylea

Roylea, commonly known as the star anise plant, is a member of the Schisandraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Roylea species typically grow as shrubs or small trees, with compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers.

The fruit contains aromatic compounds that contribute to its use in both culinary and medicinal applications.
