Roodia braunsii

Roodia braunsii, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. Roodia braunsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
