Rhynchospora oligantha var. breviseta

Rhynchospora oligantha var. breviseta, commonly known as the short-hair sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

Rhynchospora oligantha var. breviseta plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
