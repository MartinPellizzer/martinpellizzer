Rosa suionum

Rosa suionum, commonly known as the Japanese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their beauty and scent.

Rosa suionum is also valued for its historical use in traditional Japanese culture and horticulture.
