Rubus × pseudobloxamii

Rubus × pseudobloxamii, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color, depending on the variety. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible berries.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological and genetic traits from its parent species.
