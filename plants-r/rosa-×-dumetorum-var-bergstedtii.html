Rosa × dumetorum var. bergstedtii

Rosa × dumetorum var. bergstedtii, commonly known as Bergstedt's shrub rose, is a hybrid species originating from Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and is typically found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, silvery-white hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
