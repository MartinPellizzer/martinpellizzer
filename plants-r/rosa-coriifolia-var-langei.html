Rosa coriifolia var. langei

Rosa coriifolia var. langei, commonly known as the Lange's shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thornless stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers in early spring, followed by bright red hips in autumn. Its leaves are compound, with ovate to elliptical leaflets that are glossy and dark green.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
