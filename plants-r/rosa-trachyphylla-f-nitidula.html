Rosa trachyphylla f. nitidula

Rosa trachyphylla f. nitidula, commonly known as the glossy rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, glossy leaves that are slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and borders due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
