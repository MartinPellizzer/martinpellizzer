Rabdosia rubescens var. lushiensis

Rabdosia rubescens var. lushiensis, commonly known as the Lushan Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

This variety is often found in rocky or forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
