Rosa × setonensis

Rosa × setonensis, commonly known as the Seton's rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering, typically producing pink to red blooms. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape, with a slightly serrated margin. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × setonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
