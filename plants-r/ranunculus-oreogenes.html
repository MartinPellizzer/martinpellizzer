Ranunculus oreogenes

Ranunculus oreogenes, commonly known as the Sierra larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, blue to purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Ranunculus oreogenes is an herbaceous perennial that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
