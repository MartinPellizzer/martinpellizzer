Rosa pimpinellifolia var. reversa

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. reversa, commonly known as the reverse white shrub rose, is a unique variant of the shrub rose species.

It is characterized by its white flowers that contrast with its dark green foliage, creating a striking visual effect. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, thornless stems and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small gardens or containers.

Its botanical features include simple, pinnate leaves and a distinctive reverse coloration compared to the typical pink or red flowers of its parent species.
