Rosa kulikalanica

Rosa kulikalanica, commonly known as the Caucasian rose, is a species native to the Caucasus region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or white, with five to seven petals. Rosa kulikalanica is known for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
