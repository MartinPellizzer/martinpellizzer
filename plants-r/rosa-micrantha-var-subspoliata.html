Rosa micrantha var. subspoliata

Rosa micrantha var. subspoliata, commonly known as the slender shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, wiry stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

This variety is less commonly cultivated than other roses but is appreciated for its delicate appearance and resilience.
