Rhipsalis puniceodiscus

Rhipsalis puniceodiscus, commonly known as the red disc cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are often followed by red, spherical fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
