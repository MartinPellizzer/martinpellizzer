Rubus roseolus

Rubus roseolus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into the familiar red, hairy berries. These berries are rich in antioxidants and are commonly used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Rubus roseolus thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.
