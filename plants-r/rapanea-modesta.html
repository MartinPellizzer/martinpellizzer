Rapanea modesta

Rapanea modesta, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, Japan, and Korea, and is often found in forested areas. The plant is evergreen, with dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
