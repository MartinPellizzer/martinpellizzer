Rosa graveolens f. billetii

Rosa graveolens f. billetii, commonly known as the Billet's rose, is a fragrant, climbing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dark green, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with a strong, sweet scent. The plant is noted for its vigorous growth and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both floral arrangements and hedging.
