Rosa micrantha var. avelloi

Rosa micrantha var. avelloi, commonly known as the Avello's rock rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces oval, leathery leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hair. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
