Rebutia chrysacantha

Rebutia chrysacantha, commonly known as the golden rebutia, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are fleshy and green, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
