Reynoutria compacta

Reynoutria compacta, commonly known as the dwarf giant bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact growth habit and tall, slender culms. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
