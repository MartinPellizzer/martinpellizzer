Rosa × palezieuxii

Rosa × palezieuxii, commonly known as the palest rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its pale pink to almost white flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub, typically growing to a height of 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its stems are thorny.

Rosa × palezieuxii is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
