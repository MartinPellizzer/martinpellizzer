Ruta chalepensis var. angustifolia

Ruta chalepensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf rock rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and hardy.

Its aromatic foliage contains compounds that have been used in traditional herbal medicine.
