Rhamnus staddo var. deflersii

Rhamnus staddo var. deflersii, commonly known as the dwarf yellow buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish-green leaves and red or orange berries. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it forms part of the local vegetation.

The species is noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh climatic conditions.
