Rhododendron saluenense var. saluenense

Rhododendron saluenense var. saluenense, commonly known as the Saluenese rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates.

Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range from pink to red, depending on the specific cultivar. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
