Rosa obtusifolia f. vallesiaca

Rosa obtusifolia f. vallesiaca, commonly known as the Vallesian rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub-like perennial with woody stems and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rounded or obtuse shape, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.
