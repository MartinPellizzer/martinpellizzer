Ribesiodes clusiifolium

Ribesiodes clusiifolium, commonly known as the glossy leaf bignay, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
