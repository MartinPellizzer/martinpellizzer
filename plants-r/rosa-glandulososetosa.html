Rosa glandulososetosa

Rosa glandulososetosa, commonly known as the glandular-hairy rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its glandular hairs on the stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers, typically in clusters, during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges and a hairy texture.

Rosa glandulososetosa is native to regions in Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrance.
