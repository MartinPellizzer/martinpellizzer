Rytidosperma richardsonii

Rytidosperma richardsonii, commonly known as the rigid grass or sand grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Rytidosperma richardsonii is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
