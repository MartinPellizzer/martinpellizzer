Rosa tomentosa f. micans

Rosa tomentosa f. micans, commonly known as the fuzzy rose or silky rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa tomentosa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and pale pink to white flowers. The plant is native to regions of Asia, particularly China, and thrives in temperate climates. It is often grown for its ornamental value due to its unique texture and soft floral display.

The cultivar is relatively low maintenance and suitable for gardens and landscapes.
