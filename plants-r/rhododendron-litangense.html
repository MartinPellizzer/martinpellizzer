Rhododendron litangense

Rhododendron litangense, commonly known as the Litang rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Sichuan province.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions at elevations between 2,500 and 4,000 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range in color from pink to white.

Rhododendron litangense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in high-altitude forests and alpine meadows.
