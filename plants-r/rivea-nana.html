Rivea nana

Rivea nana, commonly known as the dwarf rivea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Rivea nana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
