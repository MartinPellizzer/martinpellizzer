Rhaphithamnus rotundifolius

Rhaphithamnus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leafed rhaphithamnus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and resilient plant in its native habitat.
