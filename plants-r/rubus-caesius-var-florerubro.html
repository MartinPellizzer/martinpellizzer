Rubus caesius var. florerubro

Rubus caesius var. florerubro, commonly known as the red-flowered blackberry.

This variant is characterized by its dark purple to black berries that ripen in late summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus. Its flowers are usually pink to red, adding ornamental value to gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.
