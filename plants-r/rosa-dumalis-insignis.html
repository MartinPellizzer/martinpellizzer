Rosa dumalis insignis

Rosa dumalis insignis, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, serrated leaves that are adapted to cold and arid conditions. It is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to alpine environments.

Rosa dumalis insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
