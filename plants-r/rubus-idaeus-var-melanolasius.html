Rubus idaeus var. melanolasius

Rubus idaeus var. melanolasius, commonly known as blackberry.

This variant is characterized by its dark, almost black fruit that ripens in late summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, white flowers in the spring, which develop into the edible berries.

The fruit is rich in antioxidants and has a sweet-tart flavor profile.
