Rubus ribisoideus

Rubus ribisoideus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as raspberries, which are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Rubus ribisoideus is valued for its edible fruits and historical use in traditional medicine.
