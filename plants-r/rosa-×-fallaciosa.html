Rosa × fallaciosa

Rosa × fallaciosa, commonly known as the Pink Stripe.

It is a hybrid rose species resulting from the cross between Rosa bracteata and Rosa rugosa. This plant is characterized by its vibrant pink flowers with a distinctive white stripe running down the center of each petal. The shrub typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features thorny stems and dark green foliage.

It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
