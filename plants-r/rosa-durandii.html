Rosa durandii

Rosa durandii, commonly known as the Durand rose, is a species native to the Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and thorny stems typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable botanical species.
