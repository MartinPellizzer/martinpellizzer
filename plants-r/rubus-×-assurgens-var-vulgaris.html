Rubus × assurgens var. vulgaris

Rubus × assurgens var. vulgaris, commonly known as blackberry bramble, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits that develop from multiple ovaria. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are rich in antioxidants and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its growth habit makes it both a valuable food source and a challenging weed in agricultural settings.
