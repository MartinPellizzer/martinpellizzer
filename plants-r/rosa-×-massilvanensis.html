Rosa × massilvanensis

Rosa × massilvanensis, commonly known as the Massilvanensis rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly serrated edge.

Rosa × massilvanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
