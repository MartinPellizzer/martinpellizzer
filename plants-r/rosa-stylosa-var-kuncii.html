Rosa stylosa var. kuncii

Rosa stylosa var. kuncii, commonly known as the Kuncii climbing rose, is a variant of the stylosa rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and long, flexible canes that make it ideal for trellises or arbors. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with a delicate, almost translucent appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and encourage flowering.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to different garden settings.
