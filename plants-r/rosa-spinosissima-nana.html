Rosa spinosissima nana

Rosa spinosissima nana, commonly known as the dwarf spiny rose, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring. The plant is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in moderate climates.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate blooms and unique, compact growth habit.
