Radiola linoides

Radiola linoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved radiola, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
