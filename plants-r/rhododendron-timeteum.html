Rhododendron timeteum

Rhododendron timeteum, commonly known as the Timeteum rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, preferring acidic, well-drained soils. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Rhododendron timeteum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and botanical collections.
