Rottboellia tranchellii

Rottboellia tranchellii, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, slender leaves with a reddish tint. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed sites.

Rottboellia tranchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
