Rourea parallela

Rourea parallela, commonly known as the silky tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive silken appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
