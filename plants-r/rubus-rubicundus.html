Rubus rubicundus

Rubus rubicundus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits known as raspberries, which are typically bright red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodlands and disturbed areas.

Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
