Rhynchelythrum nigricans

Rhynchelythrum nigricans, commonly known as black-stemmed thistle, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dark, woody stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It is typically found in dry, sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Rhynchelythrum nigricans is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
