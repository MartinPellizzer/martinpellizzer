Rosa polyantha var. hiburiensis

Rosa polyantha var. hiburiensis, commonly known as the Hiburiensis shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a bushy growth habit, making it suitable for hedges or small garden borders. It is relatively low maintenance and resistant to common rose diseases.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its repeat blooming and adaptability to various soil types.
