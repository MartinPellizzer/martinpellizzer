Rosa jundzilliana f. schubertii

Rosa jundzilliana f. schubertii, commonly known as Schubert's double-flowered rose, is a compact shrub with a spreading growth habit.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces large, double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is notable for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape.

This hybrid rose is prized for its showy appearance and hardy nature.
