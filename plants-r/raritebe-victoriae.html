Raritebe victoriae

Raritebe victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria's Rarity, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue petals and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically from late autumn to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
