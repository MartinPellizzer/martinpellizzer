Racosperma Sclerophyllum Var. Pilosum

Racosperma sclerophyllum var. pilosum is a unique variant of the genus Racosperma, known for its distinctive hairy foliage and hardy nature. Native to the arid regions of Australia, this plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are thick and leathery, helping to conserve water in low-rainfall areas. The plant's ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils makes it a valuable study in ecological resilience. Understanding its biology offers insights into plant adaptation strategies in extreme climates.
