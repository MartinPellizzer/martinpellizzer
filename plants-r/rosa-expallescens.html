Rosa expallescens

Rosa expallescens, commonly known as the white rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and have a subtle, sweet fragrance.

Rosa expallescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
