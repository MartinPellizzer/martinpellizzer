Rhododendron laojunense

Rhododendron laojunense, commonly known as the Laojun rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features dense clusters of bell-shaped flowers, which are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron laojunense is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
