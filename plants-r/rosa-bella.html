Rosa bella

Rosa bella, commonly known as the Beauty Rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically reaches a height of 1 to 2 feet and has dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Rosa bella is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
