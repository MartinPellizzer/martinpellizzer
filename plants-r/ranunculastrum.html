Ranunculastrum

Ranunculastrum, commonly known as the false water dropwort, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

These plants are typically herbaceous and can be found in moist, temperate regions. They are characterized by their simple, alternate leaves and showy, usually white or yellow flowers. Ranunculastrum species often grow in wetlands or along streams, thriving in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments.

The genus includes several species, some of which are used in horticulture for their ornamental value.
