Raphiolepis

Raphiolepis, commonly known as firethorn, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Primulaceae.

It consists of several species, most notably Raphiolepis indica, which is native to China and widely cultivated in gardens. Its foliage is typically glossy and evergreen, providing year-round interest.

Raphiolepis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in hedges and as a decorative shrub.
