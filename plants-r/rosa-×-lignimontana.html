Rosa × lignimontana

Rosa × lignimontana, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × lignimontana is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
