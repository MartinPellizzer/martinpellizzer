Rhododendron hunnewellianum subsp. hunnewellianum

Rhododendron hunnewellianum subsp.

hunnewellianum, commonly known as the Hunnewell rhododendron, is a shrub native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
