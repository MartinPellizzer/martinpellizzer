Rubus × falsiacuticuspis

Rubus × falsiacuticuspis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its dark red to black berries. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its flavorful fruits and ornamental value.
