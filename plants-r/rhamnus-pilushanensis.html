Rhamnus pilushanensis

Rhamnus pilushanensis, commonly known as the Pilushan buckthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, simple leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
