Rosa × bonhominis

Rosa × bonhominis, commonly known as the Bonhomme rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the summer season. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × bonhominis is also valued for its disease resistance and hardy nature, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
