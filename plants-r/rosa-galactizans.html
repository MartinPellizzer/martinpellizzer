Rosa galactizans

Rosa galactizans, commonly known as the Milky Rose, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which are often described as having a milky or opalescent appearance. The plant produces fragrant blooms that typically appear in the summer months. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa galactizans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique aesthetic.
