Rosa × conspicua

Rosa × conspicua, commonly known as the double-flowered rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically have multiple layers of petals, giving them a full, rounded appearance. The plant is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, producing fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white.

Rosa × conspicua is a popular choice for floral arrangements and as a decorative plant in landscaping.
