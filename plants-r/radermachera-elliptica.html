Radermachera elliptica

Radermachera elliptica, commonly known as the glossy sander, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that features dark green, elliptical leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
