Rhytionanthos nodosum

Rhytionanthos nodosum, commonly known as the knotty flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant splash of color to its surroundings. The plant has a woody stem with distinctive knotted nodes, which give it its common name. Its leaves are typically alternate and palmately lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rhytionanthos nodosum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
