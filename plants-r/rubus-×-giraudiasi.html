Rubus × giraudiasi

Rubus × giraudiasi, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, dark red to black berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.
