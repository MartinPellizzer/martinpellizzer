Rosa communis var. laciniata

Rosa communis var. laciniata, commonly known as the cutleaf rose, is a cultivar characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are deeply lobed and serrated, giving the plant a unique, ornamental appearance. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a delicate fragrance. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for both its aesthetic appeal and its suitability for garden cultivation.
