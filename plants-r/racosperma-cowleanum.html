Racosperma cowleanum

Racosperma cowleanum, commonly known as the Cowleanum acacia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it plays a role in soil stabilization due to its deep root system.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
