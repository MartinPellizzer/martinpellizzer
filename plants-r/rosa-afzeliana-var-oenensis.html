Rosa afzeliana var. oenensis

Rosa afzeliana var. oenensis, commonly known as the Oenensis rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its humid, tropical habitat.
