Rosa × scotinophylla

Rosa × scotinophylla, commonly known as the Sydney Hardy Rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant typically produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season.

Rosa × scotinophylla is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
