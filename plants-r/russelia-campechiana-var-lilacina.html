Russelia campechiana var. lilacina

Russelia campechiana var. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-flowered russelia, is a vigorous, herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features striking, tubular, lilac-pink flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
