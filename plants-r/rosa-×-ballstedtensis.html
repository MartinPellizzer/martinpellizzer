Rosa × ballstedtensis

Rosa × ballstedtensis, commonly known as the Ballstedt rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorned stems and glossy, dark green foliage. It is native to Germany and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Rosa × ballstedtensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
