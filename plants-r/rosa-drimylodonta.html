Rosa drimylodonta

Rosa drimylodonta, commonly known as the Drimylodonta rose, is a rare and ancient species of flowering plant.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central America and is known for its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.
