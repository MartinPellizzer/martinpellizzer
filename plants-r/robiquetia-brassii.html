Robiquetia brassii

Robiquetia brassii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Robiquetia brassii is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
