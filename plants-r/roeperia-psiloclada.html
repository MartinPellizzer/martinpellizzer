Roeperia psiloclada

Roeperia psiloclada, commonly known as the golden bell orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South America.

This herbaceous plant features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically golden yellow with a contrasting purple or brown lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Roeperia psiloclada is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
