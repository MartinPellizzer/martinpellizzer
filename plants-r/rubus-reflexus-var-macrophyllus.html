Rubus reflexus var. macrophyllus

Rubus reflexus var. macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant characterized by its notably larger leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to regions of North America. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black when mature. Its leaves are usually serrated and have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its edible berries and ornamental value.
