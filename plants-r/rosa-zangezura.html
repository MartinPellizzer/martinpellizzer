Rosa zangezura

Rosa zangezura, commonly known as the Afghan rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in the summer months. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and perfumery due to their aromatic properties.

Rosa zangezura is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
