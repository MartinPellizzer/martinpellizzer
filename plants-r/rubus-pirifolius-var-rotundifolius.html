Rubus pirifolius var. rotundifolius

Rubus pirifolius var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the Japanese raspberry, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with rounded lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
