Reseda stellata

Reseda stellata, commonly known as starry reseda, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and has thin, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Reseda stellata is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
