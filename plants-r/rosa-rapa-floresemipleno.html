Rosa rapa floresemipleno

Rosa rapa floresemipleno, commonly known as the double-flowered Chinese rose, is a hybrid species originating from China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically double or semi-double, with numerous petals arranged in layers. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds.

Rosa rapa floresemipleno is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
