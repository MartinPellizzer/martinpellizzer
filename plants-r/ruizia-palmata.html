Ruizia palmata

Ruizia palmata, commonly known as the red-barked palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-bark and tall, slender trunk. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are pinnate, with long, narrow leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance.

Ruizia palmata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
