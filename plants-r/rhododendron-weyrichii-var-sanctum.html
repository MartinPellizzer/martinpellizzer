Rhododendron weyrichii var. sanctum

Rhododendron weyrichii var. sanctum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and mountainous terrain, thriving in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
