Rhododendron hunnewellianum

Rhododendron hunnewellianum, commonly known as the Hunnewell rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partially shaded to sunny conditions. Its botanical features include broad, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining soil stability in its native habitat.
