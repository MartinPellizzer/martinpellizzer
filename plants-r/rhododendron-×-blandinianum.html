Rhododendron × blandinianum

Rhododendron × blandinianum, commonly known as the Blandin hybrid, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
