Rubroshorea scaberrima

Rubroshorea scaberrima, commonly known as the rough-leaved redshorea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, scaly bark and dark, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
