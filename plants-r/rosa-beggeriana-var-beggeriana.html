Rosa beggeriana var. beggeriana

Rosa beggeriana var. beggeriana, commonly known as the Himalayan rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, which are typical features of many rose species. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
