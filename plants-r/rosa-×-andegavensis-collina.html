Rosa × andegavensis collina

Rosa × andegavensis collina, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and profuse blooms of semi-double, pink flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and serrated, dark green leaves. It is native to the northeastern United States and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Rosa × andegavensis collina is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in temperate climates.
