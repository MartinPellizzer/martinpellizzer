Rhipsalis gonocarpa

Rhipsalis gonocarpa, commonly known as the red fruit cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the Cactaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its slender, branching stems that are often covered in small, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
