Rosa × maassii

Rosa × maassii, commonly known as the Dutch yellow rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the Netherlands.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically single or semi-double in form. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

This rose is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
