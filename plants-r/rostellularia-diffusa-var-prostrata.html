Rostellularia diffusa var. prostrata

Rostellularia diffusa var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate rostellularia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous species with creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
