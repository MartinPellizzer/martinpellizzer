Ranunculus reichenbachii

Ranunculus reichenbachii, commonly known as the Sierra Lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with five to seven petals. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
