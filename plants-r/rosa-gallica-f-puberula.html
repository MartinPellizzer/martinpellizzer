Rosa gallica f. puberula

Rosa gallica f. puberula, commonly known as the French garden rose, is a cultivar of the Gallica rose species.

It is characterized by its dark red to purple flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is also valued for its historical significance in horticulture and its use in perfumery.
