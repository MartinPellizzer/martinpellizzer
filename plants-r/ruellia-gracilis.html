Ruellia gracilis

Ruellia gracilis, commonly known as slender ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Ruellia gracilis is often found in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially sunny environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
