Rhododendron torquescens

Rhododendron torquescens, commonly known as the twisted rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron torquescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
