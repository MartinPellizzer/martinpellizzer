Rousseauxia dionychoides

Rousseauxia dionychoides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
