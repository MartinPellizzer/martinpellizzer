Ribes × recens

Ribes × recens, commonly known as the hybrid gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub that results from the crossbreeding of *Ribes nigrum* (black currant) and *Ribes rubrum* (red currant).

It typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters and features upright, thorny stems with alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, edible berries that range in color from red to black, depending on the variety. These berries are rich in vitamin C and are often used in jams, juices, and desserts.

Ribes × recens is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses, making it a popular choice in gardens and agricultural settings.
