Ranunculus taisanensis

Ranunculus taisanensis, commonly known as the Taisan buttercup, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy, often colorful flowers. The plant typically features delicate, palmately divided leaves and vibrant yellow or white petals. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
