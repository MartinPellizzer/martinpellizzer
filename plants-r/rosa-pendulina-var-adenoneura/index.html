Rosa Pendulina Var. Adenoneura

Rosa pendulina var. adenoneura is a unique and delicate species of climbing rose known for its elegant, pendulous flowers. This variety is prized for its soft pink blooms that cascade gracefully from its stems. Native to the mountainous regions of China, it thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soil. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of refined beauty. Despite its charm, it requires careful attention to ensure its health and longevity.
