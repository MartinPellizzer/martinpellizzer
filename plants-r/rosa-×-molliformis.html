Rosa × molliformis

Rosa × molliformis, commonly known as the everblooming shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season. The plant typically features deep green, leathery foliage and clusters of fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink or white flowers. It is well-suited for hedging and ornamental landscaping due to its compact form and continuous blooming.

Rosa × molliformis thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
