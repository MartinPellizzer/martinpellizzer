Rhododendron protistum var. protistum

Rhododendron protistum var. protistum, commonly known as the Japanese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
