Rabdosia tenuifolia

Rabdosia tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or stream sides.

Rabdosia tenuifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
