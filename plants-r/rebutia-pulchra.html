Rebutia pulchra

Rebutia pulchra, commonly known as the rainbow cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant features small, spiny stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers, which come in shades of pink, red, and yellow, bloom during the warmer months.

Rebutia pulchra is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
