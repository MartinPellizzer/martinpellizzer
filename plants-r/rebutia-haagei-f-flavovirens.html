Rebutia haagei f. flavovirens

Rebutia haagei f. flavovirens, commonly known as the Golden Green Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a spherical or elongated stem with a greenish-yellow hue, adorned with small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Rebutia haagei f. flavovirens requires well-draining soil, bright sunlight, and minimal water to thrive.
