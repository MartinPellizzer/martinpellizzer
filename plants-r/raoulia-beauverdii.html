Raoulia beauverdii

Raoulia beauverdii, commonly known as the Solomon Islands hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the South Pacific.

Raoulia beauverdii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.
