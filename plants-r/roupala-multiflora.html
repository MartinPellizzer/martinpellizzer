Roupala multiflora

Roupala multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered roupala, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. The plant has a rounded crown and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
