Rohdea chinensis var. tsinlingensis

Rohdea chinensis var. tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling lily, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It blooms with small, white or pale pink flowers that appear in spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shady, moist garden environments.
