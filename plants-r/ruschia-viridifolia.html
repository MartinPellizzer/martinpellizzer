Ruschia viridifolia

Ruschia viridifolia, commonly known as the greenleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its compact size and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
