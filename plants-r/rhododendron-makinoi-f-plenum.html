Rhododendron makinoi f. plenum

Rhododendron makinoi f. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered rhododendron, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with a rounded, bushy form. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and evergreen foliage, which provide year-round visual interest.
