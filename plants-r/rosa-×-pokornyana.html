Rosa × pokornyana

Rosa × pokornyana, commonly known as the Pokorny rose, is a hybrid rose species that combines traits from various Rosa species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces fragrant, double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. It is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely used in gardens and landscaping.
