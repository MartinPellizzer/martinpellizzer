Raveniopsis linearis

Raveniopsis linearis, commonly known as the slender raveniopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Raveniopsis linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
