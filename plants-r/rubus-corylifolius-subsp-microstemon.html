Rubus corylifolius subsp. microstemon

Rubus corylifolius subsp.

microstemon, commonly known as the small-stamen raspberry, is a shrub native to eastern Asia. It features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant produces red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Rubus genus.
