Rhodax rossmaessleri

Rhodax rossmaessleri, commonly known as the Rossmaessler's rhodax, is a rare and striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky, sunny habitats and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical collections and conservation efforts.
