Rhododendron catawbiense f. compactum

Rhododendron catawbiense f. compactum, commonly known as the Catawba rosebay rhododendron, is a compact form of the parent species.

It features dense, upright growth with broad, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers in the spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
