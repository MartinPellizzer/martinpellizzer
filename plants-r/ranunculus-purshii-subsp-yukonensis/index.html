Ranunculus Purshii Subsp. Yukonensis

Ranunculus purshii subsp. yukonensis is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the Yukon region of Canada. It is known for its delicate, cup-shaped blooms that range in color from white to pale yellow. This subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments such as meadows and forest floors. Its presence is an indicator of healthy, undisturbed ecosystems. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
