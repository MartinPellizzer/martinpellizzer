Renealmia thyrsoidea subsp. thyrsoidea

Renealmia thyrsoidea subsp.

thyrsoidea, commonly known as the red-flowered umbrella grass, is a member of the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and distinctive inflorescence that resembles an umbrella.

The plant produces bright red bracts that surround the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in wetland habitats and is often found in marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting aquatic and wetland ecosystems.
