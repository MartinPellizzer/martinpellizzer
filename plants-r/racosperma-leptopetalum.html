Racosperma leptopetalum

Racosperma leptopetalum, commonly known as the slender-petaled acacia, is a small shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
