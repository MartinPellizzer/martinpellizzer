Rosa afzeliana f. horrida

Rosa afzeliana f. horrida, commonly known as the rough rose, is a horticultural form of the species Rosa afzeliana.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and foliage, which give it a rugged appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China and Vietnam.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in gardens.
