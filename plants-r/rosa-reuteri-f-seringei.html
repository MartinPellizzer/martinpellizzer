Rosa reuteri f. seringei

Rosa reuteri f. seringei, commonly known as the Serine rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is a hybrid form of Rosa reuteri, characterized by its unique floral structure and ornamental value.

This plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
