Rhinopetalum stenantherum

Rhinopetalum stenantherum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowering period occurs during the late spring to early summer months.

Rhinopetalum stenantherum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
