Rebutia wessneriana var. beryllioides

Rebutia wessneriana var. beryllioides, commonly known as the Berylline Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green with a bluish tinge, and it produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ease of care.

It requires bright sunlight, well-draining soil, and minimal water to thrive.
