Rubus × semitereticaulis

Rubus × semitereticaulis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of tiny drupelets, which are typically dark red to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible berries.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
