Rosa gallica f. assimilis

Rosa gallica f. assimilis, commonly known as the French pink or the old garden rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa gallica.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in clusters and have a delicate, almost translucent appearance. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is valued for its historical significance and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
