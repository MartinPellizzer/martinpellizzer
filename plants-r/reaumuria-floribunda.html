Reaumuria floribunda

Reaumuria floribunda, commonly known as the red sand-river, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and North America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has adapted to harsh environments with its succulent leaves and deep root system. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in desert or steppe habitats.

Reaumuria floribunda plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
