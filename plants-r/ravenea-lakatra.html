Ravenea lakatra

Ravenea lakatra, commonly known as the Lakatra Ravenea, is a striking member of the Arecaceae family native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves that give it a tropical appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in branched inflorescences, followed by bright red or orange berries. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Ravenea lakatra thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
