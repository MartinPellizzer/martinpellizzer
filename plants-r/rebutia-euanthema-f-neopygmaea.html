Rebutia euanthema f. neopygmaea

Rebutia euanthema f. neopygmaea, commonly known as the miniature rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features small, rounded stems that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Its foliage is often covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.
