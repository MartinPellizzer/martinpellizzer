Racosperma venulosum

Racosperma venulosum, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrublands and woodlands. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

Racosperma venulosum is valued for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation and its use in traditional medicine.
