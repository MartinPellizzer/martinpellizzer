Rosa sjunikii

Rosa sjunikii, commonly known as the Armenian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and an upright growth habit.

Rosa sjunikii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
