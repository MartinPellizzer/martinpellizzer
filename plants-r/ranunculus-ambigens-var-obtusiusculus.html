Ranunculus ambigens var. obtusiusculus

Ranunculus ambigens var. obtusiusculus, commonly known as the blunt-leaf buttercup, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, usually yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided and have a distinctive, rounded shape.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in regions with cool, temperate climates.
