Reseda phyteuma subsp. diffusa

Reseda phyteuma subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as the hairy reseda, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, clustered flowers and linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in early summer.

The subspecies diffusa is distinguished by its hairy stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance compared to other reseda species.
