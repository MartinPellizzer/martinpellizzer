Rosa moyesii

Rosa moyesii, commonly known as the Moseley rose, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces rose hips that are often used in traditional medicine. Rosa moyesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for its hardiness and adaptability.

It is also noted for its historical significance in botanical research.
