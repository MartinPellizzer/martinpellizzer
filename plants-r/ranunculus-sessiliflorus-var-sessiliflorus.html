Ranunculus sessiliflorus var. sessiliflorus

Ranunculus sessiliflorus var. sessiliflorus, commonly known as the sessile-flowered buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, palmate leaves and showy, yellow flowers with five to seven petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a short, thick rhizome that helps it survive in its native habitat.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a bright touch of yellow to the landscape.
