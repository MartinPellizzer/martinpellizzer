Rosa acmenophylla

Rosa acmenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are typically narrow and serrated, with a slightly hairy surface.

Rosa acmenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
