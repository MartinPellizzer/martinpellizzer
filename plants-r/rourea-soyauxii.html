Rourea soyauxii

Rourea soyauxii, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in subtropical gardens.
