Rhaphiolepis Petiolata

Rhaphiolepis petiolata is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern China and Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters of pink or red throughout the spring and summer. The plant features glossy dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its colorful blossoms. It is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its adaptability and beauty make it a popular choice for both landscape design and container gardening.
