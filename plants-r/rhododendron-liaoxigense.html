Rhododendron liaoxigense

Rhododendron liaoxigense, commonly known as the Liao Xi Rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of northern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron liaoxigense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
