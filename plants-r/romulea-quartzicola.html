Romulea quartzicola

Romulea quartzicola, commonly known as the quartz rock orchid, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
