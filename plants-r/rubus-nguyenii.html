Rubus nguyenii

Rubus nguyenii, commonly known as Nguyen's blackberry, is a species of bramble native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes various shrubs with thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, dark berries that are typically consumed raw or used in culinary applications. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
