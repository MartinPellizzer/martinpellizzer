Rosa wahlenbergii

Rosa wahlenbergii, commonly known as the Swedish rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to northern Europe, particularly Sweden and parts of Norway. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Rosa wahlenbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
