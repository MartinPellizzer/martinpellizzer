Romulea linaresii

Romulea linaresii, commonly known as the Linares' romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that bloom in late summer. Romulea linaresii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
