Rhododendron nitens

Rhododendron nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that often has a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
