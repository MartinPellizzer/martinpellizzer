Rubroshorea smithiana

Rubroshorea smithiana, commonly known as the Smith's redshorea, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red bark and glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming during the wet season. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires careful protection.
