Ribes binominatum

Ribes binominatum, commonly known as the redcurrant, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, red, edible berries. The flowers are typically yellow and appear before the leaves in early spring.
