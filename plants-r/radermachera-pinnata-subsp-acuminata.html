Radermachera pinnata subsp. acuminata

Radermachera pinnata subsp.

acuminata, commonly known as the glossy-leafed radermachera, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves with a pointed tip, which give it its subspecific name "acuminata.".

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by reddish-brown berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
