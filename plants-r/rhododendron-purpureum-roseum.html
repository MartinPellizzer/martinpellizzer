Rhododendron purpureum roseum

Rhododendron purpureum roseum, commonly known as the Roseum Rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features dense, broad leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
