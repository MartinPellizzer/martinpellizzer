Rosa × eliasii

Rosa × eliasii, commonly known as the Eliasii rose, is a hybrid shrub rose originating from the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × eliasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
