Rhynchosia pentheri var. pentheri

Rhynchosia pentheri var. pentheri, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, three-lobed pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
