Rhododendron lagopus var. tsurugisanense

Rhododendron lagopus var. tsurugisanense, commonly known as the Tsurugisanense Rhododendron, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its compact size and striking floral display in horticultural settings.
