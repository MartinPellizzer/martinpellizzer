Rhaphidophora kanehirae

Rhaphidophora kanehirae, commonly known as the Kanehirae arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
