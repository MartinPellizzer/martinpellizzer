Ruellia rubiginosa

Ruellia rubiginosa, commonly known as the redstem ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white. Ruellia rubiginosa is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habits.
