Rhipsalis Puniceodiscus Var. Chrysocarpa

Rhipsalis puniceodiscus var. chrysocarpa is a captivating cactus species native to Brazil. Known for its vibrant red stems and delicate yellow flowers, it stands out among its relatives. This variety thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. With proper care, it can flourish and add a touch of exotic beauty to any space.
