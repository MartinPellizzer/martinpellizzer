Rosa communis var. augustodinensis

Rosa communis var. augustodinensis, commonly known as the Augustodinensis rose, is a cultivar prized for its rich, deep red blooms and fragrant scent.

It is a hybrid tea rose that exhibits a compact growth habit with sturdy stems and dark green foliage. The flowers are typically large, with multiple layers of petals that open fully to reveal a vibrant color. This variety is well-suited for garden borders, containers, or as a cut flower due to its long-lasting blooms.

It requires regular pruning and well-drained soil to thrive and maintain its ornamental qualities.
