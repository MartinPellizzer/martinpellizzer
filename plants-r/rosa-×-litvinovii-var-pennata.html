Rosa × litvinovii var. pennata

Rosa × litvinovii var. pennata, commonly known as the Pennate Hybrid Rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
