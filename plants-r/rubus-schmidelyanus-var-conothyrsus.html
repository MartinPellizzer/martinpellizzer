Rubus schmidelyanus var. conothyrsus

Rubus schmidelyanus var. conothyrsus, commonly known as the Schmidel's raspberry.

It is a shrub native to the Andes, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in cooler, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
