Rosa × lucida

Rosa × lucida, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa chinensis.

It is characterized by its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardens. The plant typically features glossy, dark green foliage and fragrant, semi-double blooms in shades of pink and white. It is a hardy shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Rosa × lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
