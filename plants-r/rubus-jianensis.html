Rubus jianensis

Rubus jianensis, commonly known as the Jianshan blackberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of flowering plants in the Rosaceae family. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems, trifoliate leaves, and aggregate fruits composed of small drupes. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
