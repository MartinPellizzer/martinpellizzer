Roupala brasiliensis var. velutina

Roupala brasiliensis var. velutina, commonly known as velvet roupala, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its smooth, dark bark and dense, glossy foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical, with a velvety texture that gives the plant its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing edible seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
