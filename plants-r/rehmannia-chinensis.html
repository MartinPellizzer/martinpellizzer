Rehmannia chinensis

Rehmannia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese foxglove, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, tubular flowers. Rehmannia chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese medicine.
