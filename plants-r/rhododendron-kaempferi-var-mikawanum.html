Rhododendron kaempferi var. mikawanum

Rhododendron kaempferi var. mikawanum, commonly known as the Mikawa azalea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers in late spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
