Rebutia robustispina

Rebutia robustispina, commonly known as the robust spiny euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick, cylindrical stem that is often covered in spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. Its leaves are typically modified into spines, reducing water loss and adapting it to arid environments.

Rebutia robustispina is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
