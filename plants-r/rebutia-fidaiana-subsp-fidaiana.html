Rebutia fidaiana subsp. fidaiana

Rebutia fidaiana subsp.

fidaiana, commonly known as the Fidaiana Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Brazil. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
