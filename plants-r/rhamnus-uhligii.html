Rhamnus uhligii

Rhamnus uhligii, commonly known as the Uhlig's buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible for some bird species.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.
