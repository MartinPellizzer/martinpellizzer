Rosa montana var. montemartinii

Rosa montana var. montemartinii, commonly known as the Montemartinii rose, is a compact shrub with dense, thornless growth.

It features deep green, glossy foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its abundant blooms. The flowers are typically pink to red, with a semi-double to fully double form, and they appear in clusters throughout the growing season. This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of conditions, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrance and long-lasting bloom period.
