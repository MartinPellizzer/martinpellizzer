Ruttya ovata

Ruttya ovata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and a sympodial growth habit.

Ruttya ovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among gardeners.
