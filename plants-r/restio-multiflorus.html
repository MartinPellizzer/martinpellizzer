Restio multiflorus

Restio multiflorus, commonly known as the many-flowered restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Restio multiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
