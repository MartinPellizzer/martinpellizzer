Rosa coriifolia var. winslowii

Rosa coriifolia var. winslowii, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has thorny stems and elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal display of flowers and foliage.
