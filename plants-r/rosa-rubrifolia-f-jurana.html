Rosa rubrifolia f. jurana

Rosa rubrifolia f. jurana, commonly known as the Jurana rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa rubrifolia species, characterized by its distinctive red foliage and showy pink to white flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The leaves are particularly notable for their vibrant red coloration, which becomes more pronounced in the autumn. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and botanical collections.
