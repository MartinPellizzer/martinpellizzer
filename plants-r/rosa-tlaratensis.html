Rosa tlaratensis

Rosa tlaratensis, commonly known as the Tlaratensis rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, round fruits known as hips.

Rosa tlaratensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
