Rubia nitida

Rubia nitida, commonly known as slender woodruff, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia nitida is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
