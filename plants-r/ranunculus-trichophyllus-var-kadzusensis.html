Ranunculus trichophyllus var. kadzusensis

Ranunculus trichophyllus var. kadzusensis, commonly known as the Kadzusen ranunculus, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with prominent black veins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
