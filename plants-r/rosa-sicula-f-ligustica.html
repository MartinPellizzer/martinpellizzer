Rosa sicula f. ligustica

Rosa sicula f. ligustica, commonly known as the Ligustic rose, is a compact shrub native to southern Italy.

It features dense, thornless stems that bear fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in Mediterranean horticulture.
