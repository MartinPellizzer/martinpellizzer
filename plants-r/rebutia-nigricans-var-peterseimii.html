Rebutia nigricans var. peterseimii

Rebutia nigricans var. peterseimii, commonly known as the Peterseim's globose cactus, is a compact, spherical cactus native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
