Ruellia extensa

Ruellia extensa, commonly known as the broadleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. Ruellia extensa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.
