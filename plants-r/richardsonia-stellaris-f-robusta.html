Richardsonia stellaris f. robusta

Richardsonia stellaris f. robusta, commonly known as the robust starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
