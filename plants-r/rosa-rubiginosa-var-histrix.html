Rosa rubiginosa var. histrix

Rosa rubiginosa var. histrix, commonly known as the rough rose or hispid rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep pink. Its fruits are reddish berries that contain numerous seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to dry, rocky soils.
