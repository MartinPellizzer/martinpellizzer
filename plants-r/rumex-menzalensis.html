Rumex menzalensis

Rumex menzalensis, commonly known as the Menzalens dock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib. Rumex menzalensis is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
