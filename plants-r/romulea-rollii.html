Romulea rollii

Romulea rollii, commonly known as the "dwarf crown orchid," is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer, often in clusters atop slender stems. Its unique morphology and striking appearance make it a popular choice among succulent and bulb enthusiasts.
