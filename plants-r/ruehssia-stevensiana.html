Ruehssia stevensiana

Ruehssia stevensiana, commonly known as the Mexican rue, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ruehssia stevensiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
