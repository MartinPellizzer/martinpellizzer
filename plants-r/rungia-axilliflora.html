Rungia axilliflora

Rungia axilliflora, commonly known as the wax plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems and leaf axils. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Rungia axilliflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
