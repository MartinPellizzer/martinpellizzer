Rigiolepis uroglossa var. uroglossa

Rigiolepis uroglossa var. uroglossa, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is the bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer.

The species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
