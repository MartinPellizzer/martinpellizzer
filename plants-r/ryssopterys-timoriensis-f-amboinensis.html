Ryssopterys timoriensis f. amboinensis

Ryssopterys timoriensis f. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina tree fern, is a distinctive member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. Native to the islands of Timor and nearby regions, this plant thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in tropical gardens and as a houseplant in cooler climates.
