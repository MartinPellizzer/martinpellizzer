Rosa × nyaradyana

Rosa × nyaradyana, commonly known as the Nyaradyana rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between different Rosa species.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season. The plant typically features fragrant, semi-double to full flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and leathery, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blossoms.

Rosa × nyaradyana is well-suited for garden cultivation and is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
