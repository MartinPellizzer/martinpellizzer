Rotala andamanensis

Rotala andamanensis, commonly known as the Andaman waterwheel, is a semi-aquatic plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It features whorled leaves that are typically bright green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often floating on the water's surface. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Rotala andamanensis is often cultivated in aquariums and ponds for its ornamental value and ability to oxygenate water.
