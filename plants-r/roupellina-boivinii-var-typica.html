Roupellina boivinii var. typica

Roupellina boivinii var. typica, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.
