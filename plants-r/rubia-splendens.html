Rubia splendens

Rubia splendens, commonly known as the splendid borage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically growing in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are hairy and often have a mild, aromatic scent.

Rubia splendens is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
