Rorippa Microtites

Rorippa microtites is a small aquatic plant commonly found in freshwater environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many familiar garden plants. This species is known for its delicate, floating leaves and tiny white flowers that bloom in the summer. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms. Its presence is often an indicator of clean, well-oxygenated water.
