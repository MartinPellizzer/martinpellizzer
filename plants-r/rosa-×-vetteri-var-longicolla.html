Rosa × vetteri var. longicolla

Rosa × vetteri var. longicolla, commonly known as the long-stalked hybrid rose, is a vigorous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its long, slender stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various garden conditions.
