Ricinocarpus anisodontus

Ricinocarpus anisodontus, commonly known as the white sand cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant has a tall, unbranched stem that can grow up to several meters in height, with a thick, fibrous trunk. Its leaves are large, pinnately compound, and are arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female plants.
