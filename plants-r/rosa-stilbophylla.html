Rosa stilbophylla

Rosa stilbophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. It produces fragrant pink to white flowers in the summer months, and its stems are covered in fine, velvety hairs.

Rosa stilbophylla is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
