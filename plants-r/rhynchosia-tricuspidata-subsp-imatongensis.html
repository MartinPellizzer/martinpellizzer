Rhynchosia tricuspidata subsp. imatongensis

Rhynchosia tricuspidata subsp.

imatongensis, commonly known as the Imatong climbing bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in racemes.

The plant produces elongated pods containing several seeds, which are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in savanna and forest edge habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and nutritional benefits, it is sometimes cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.
