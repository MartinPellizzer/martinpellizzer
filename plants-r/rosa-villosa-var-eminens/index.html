Rosa Villosa Var. Eminens

Climbing rosa, scientifically known as Rosa villosa var. eminens, is a vigorous vine native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. This species is prized for its lush, fragrant blooms and its ability to climb trellises or fences with ease. Its hairy stems and dark green leaves make it a distinctive addition to gardens and landscapes. The flowers, which range from white to pink, add beauty and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
