Rosa rubiginosa subvar. rotundifolia

Rosa rubiginosa subvar. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf shrub rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, round-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant pink or red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, hairy rose hip that contains seeds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
