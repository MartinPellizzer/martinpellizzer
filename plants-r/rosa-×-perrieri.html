Rosa × perrieri

Rosa × perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant, deep pink flowers with a strong, spicy scent. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and is known for its vigorous growth habit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The Perrieri rose is also valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
