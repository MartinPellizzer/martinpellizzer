Rosa spinetorum

Rosa spinetorum, commonly known as the spiny rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Rosa spinetorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
