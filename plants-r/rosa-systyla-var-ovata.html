Rosa systyla var. ovata

Rosa systyla var. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf rock rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne on short pedicels. The leaves are oval in shape, hence the variety name "ovata," and are usually covered in a fine layer of soft hairs. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
