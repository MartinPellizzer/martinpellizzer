Rosa polyanthema

Rosa polyanthema, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and profuse clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, producing pink to white flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa polyanthema is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
