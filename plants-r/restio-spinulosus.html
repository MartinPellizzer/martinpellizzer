Restio spinulosus

Restio spinulosus, commonly known as the spinulose restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine, bristly hairs.
