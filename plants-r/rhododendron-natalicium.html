Rhododendron natalicium

Rhododendron natalicium, commonly known as the Natal flame or Natal rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in humid, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or orange center. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Rhododendron natalicium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
