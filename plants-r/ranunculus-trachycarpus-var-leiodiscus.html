Ranunculus trachycarpus var. leiodiscus

Ranunculus trachycarpus var. leiodiscus, commonly known as the smooth-seeded buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features a basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves that are deeply lobed. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, often surrounded by numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, less hairy seeds compared to other variants of the species.
