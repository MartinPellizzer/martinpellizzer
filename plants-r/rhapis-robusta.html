Rhapis robusta

Rhapis robusta, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful, palm-like appearance. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding a lush aesthetic to indoor and outdoor spaces.

Rhapis robusta is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and landscaping purposes.
