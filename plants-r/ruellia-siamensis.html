Ruellia siamensis

Ruellia siamensis, commonly known as the Siam ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Ruellia siamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
