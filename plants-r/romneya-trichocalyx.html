Romneya trichocalyx

Romneya trichocalyx, commonly known as the California poppy.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to California, characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers and feathery foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
