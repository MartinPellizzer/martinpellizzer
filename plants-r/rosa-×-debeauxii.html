Rosa × debeauxii

Rosa × debeauxii, commonly known as the DeBeauvoir rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of pink to red. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × debeauxii prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
