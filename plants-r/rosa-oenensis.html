Rosa oenensis

Rosa oenensis, commonly known as the Spanish rose or the wine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in perfumery and traditional medicine due to their aromatic properties.

Rosa oenensis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
