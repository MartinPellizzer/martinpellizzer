Rivea sorsogonensis

Rivea sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Bignonia, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with orange veins. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces long, slender fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
