Rhododendron dachengense

Rhododendron dachengense, commonly known as the Dacheng rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in spring. Its leaves are typically leathery and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Rhododendron dachengense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
