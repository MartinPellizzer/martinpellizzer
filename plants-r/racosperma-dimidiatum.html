Racosperma dimidiatum

Racosperma dimidiatum, commonly known as the yellow wattle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has feathery, compound leaves and is part of the legume family, Fabaceae. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Racosperma dimidiatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
