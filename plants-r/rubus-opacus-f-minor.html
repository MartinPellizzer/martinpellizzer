Rubus opacus f. minor

Rubus opacus f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf blackberry.

This is a subspecies of the blackberry plant, characterized by its smaller size and compact growth habit. It features dark green, trifoliate leaves and produces small, dark purple berries. The plant is typically found in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and thickets.

It has a shallow root system and is relatively hardy, making it suitable for cultivation in cooler climates.
