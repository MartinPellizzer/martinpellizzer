Rubia yunnanensis

Rubia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan madder, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots contain a red dye used historically in traditional dyeing practices.

Rubia yunnanensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
