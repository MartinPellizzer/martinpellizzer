Rhamnus cardiosperma

Rhamnus cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed buckthorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange to red berries that contain a single, heart-shaped seed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique fruit structure.
