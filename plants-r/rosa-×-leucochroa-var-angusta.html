Rosa × leucochroa var. angusta

Rosa × leucochroa var. angusta, commonly known as the narrowleaf everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, narrow foliage. The plant produces abundant clusters of pale pink to white flowers throughout the growing season. It is a vigorous grower that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental rose.
