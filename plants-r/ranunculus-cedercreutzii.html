Ranunculus cedercreutzii

Ranunculus cedercreutzii, commonly known as the Cedercreutz buttercup, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and showy, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
