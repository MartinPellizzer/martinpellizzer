Ribes glabrifolium

Ribes glabrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Ribes glabrifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for wildlife and supporting local biodiversity.
