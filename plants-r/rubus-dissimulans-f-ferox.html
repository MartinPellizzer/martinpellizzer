Rubus dissimulans f. ferox

Rubus dissimulans f. ferox, commonly known as the ferocious blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant native to certain regions of North America.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and large, dark purple to black berries. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its thorny stems and dense foliage make it a resilient and sometimes invasive species.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is closely related to other Rubus species.
