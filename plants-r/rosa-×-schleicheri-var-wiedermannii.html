Rosa × schleicheri var. wiedermannii

Rosa × schleicheri var. wiedermannii, commonly known as the Wiedermann's hybrid rose, is a cultivar prized for its vibrant, semi-double to fully double flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant blooms in shades of pink to red, often with a high degree of color variation. It is typically cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

This variety is well-suited for growing in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
