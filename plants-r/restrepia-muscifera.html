Restrepia muscifera

Restrepia muscifera, commonly known as the musk orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Restrepia muscifera is valued for its unique fragrance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
