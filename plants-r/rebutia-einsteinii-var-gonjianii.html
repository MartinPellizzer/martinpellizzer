Rebutia einsteinii var. gonjianii

Rebutia einsteinii var. gonjianii, commonly known as the Einstein's Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color, with spines that are often reddish or brown. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick stems.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements in cultivation.
