Rhododendron rugosum var. kinabaluense

Rhododendron rugosum var. kinabaluense, commonly known as the Kinabalu rhododendron, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in montane forests. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems and a compact form.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
