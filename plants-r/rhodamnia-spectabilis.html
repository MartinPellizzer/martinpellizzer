Rhodamnia spectabilis

Rhodamnia spectabilis, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. The plant is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters in height, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Rhodamnia spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
