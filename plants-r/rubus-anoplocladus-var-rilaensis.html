Rubus anoplocladus var. rilaensis

Rubus anoplocladus var. rilaensis, commonly known as the Rila raspberry, is a shrub native to the Rila Mountains in Bulgaria.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
