Rosa obtusifolia var. obtusifolioides

Rosa obtusifolia var. obtusifolioides, commonly known as the blunt-leaved rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a rounded base and a slightly hairy texture. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
