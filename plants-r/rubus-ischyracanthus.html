Rubus ischyracanthus

Rubus ischyracanthus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the eastern United States, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is closely related to other raspberry and blackberry species.
