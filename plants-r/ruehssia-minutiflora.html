Ruehssia minutiflora

Ruehssia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf flaxflower, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Ruehssia minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
