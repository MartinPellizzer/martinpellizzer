Rosa krockeri

Rosa krockeri, commonly known as the Krocker rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Chile, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rosa krockeri is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience in well-drained soil.
