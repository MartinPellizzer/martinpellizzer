Rebutia vizcarrae

Rebutia vizcarrae, commonly known as the Vizcarras Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
