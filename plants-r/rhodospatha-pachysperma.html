Rhodospatha pachysperma

Rhodospatha pachysperma, commonly known as the thick-seeded orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its robust, erect growth habit. The plant produces large, showy flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of yellow and orange. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, adapting it to its often-dry habitat.

Rhodospatha pachysperma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
