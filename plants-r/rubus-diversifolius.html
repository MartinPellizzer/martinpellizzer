Rubus diversifolius

Rubus diversifolius, commonly known as the variable-leaf bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern North America and is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes and sizes. The plant produces clusters of white flowers that give way to red, edible berries. Its thorny stems and sprawling growth habit make it a common weed in disturbed areas.

The botanical features of Rubus diversifolius reflect its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
