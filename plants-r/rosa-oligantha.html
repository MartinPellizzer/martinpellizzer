Rosa oligantha

Rosa oligantha, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. This shrub typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender stems and small, fragrant flowers. The flowers are usually pink or white, and they bloom in spring.

Rosa oligantha is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
