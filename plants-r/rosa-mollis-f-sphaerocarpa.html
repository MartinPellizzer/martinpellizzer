Rosa mollis f. sphaerocarpa

Rosa mollis f. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the spherical-fruit Rosa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, thornless stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its most distinctive feature is the spherical, rounded fruit, which sets it apart from other Rosa species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
