Ranunculus auricomus subsp. pergracilis

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

pergracilis, commonly known as the slenderleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It features delicate, palmately lobed leaves that are typically narrow and finely divided.

The flowers are bright yellow with 5-8 sepals that resemble petals, and they bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
