Rosa oreogeton

Rosa oreogeton, commonly known as the desert rose or oreogeton rose, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Rosa oreogeton is adapted to survive in harsh environments with low rainfall and high temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of desert flora.
