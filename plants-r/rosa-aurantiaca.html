Rosa aurantiaca

Rosa aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the eastern Mediterranean region and is characterized by its vibrant orange-colored blooms. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its flowers are usually single or semi-double, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa aurantiaca is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
