Ranunculus convexiusculus

Ranunculus convexiusculus, commonly known as the convex-leaved buttercup, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow petals and heart-shaped leaves, which are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Native to regions of Europe and western Asia, this plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. It blooms from late spring to early summer, producing clusters of small, showy flowers.

The plant is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
