Rubus schleicheri var. erectiflorens

Rubus schleicheri var. erectiflorens, commonly known as the erect-flowered blackberry.

It is a shrub native to China, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. Its flowers are erect and white, often appearing in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
