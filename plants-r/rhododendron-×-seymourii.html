Rhododendron × seymourii

Rhododendron × seymourii, commonly known as the Seymour rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and thrives in acidic, well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to various climatic conditions.
