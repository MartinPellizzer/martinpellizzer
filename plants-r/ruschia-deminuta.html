Ruschia deminuta

Ruschia deminuta, commonly known as the dwarf ruschia, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a slight powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements, it is well-suited for xeriscaping and arid environments.
