Ruschia pungens

Ruschia pungens, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, spiky flower spikes that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
