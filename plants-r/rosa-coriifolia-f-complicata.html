Rosa coriifolia f. complicata

Rosa coriifolia f. complicata, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the fragrant rose, is a unique cultivar of the rose species Rosa coriifolia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, with a strong, sweet fragrance. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
