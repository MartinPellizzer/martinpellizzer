Rotala fontinalis

Rotala fontinalis, commonly known as water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, waxy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are usually light green to bluish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or white, emerging from the water surface. Rotala fontinalis thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It is often used in aquascaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
