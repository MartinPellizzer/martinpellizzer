Rosa atra

Rosa atra, commonly known as the dark rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its deep purple to black petals, which contrast with its rich green foliage. Native to regions of Europe and Asia, this plant typically grows in woodland areas and along stream banks. The flowers are fragrant and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rosa atra is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in gardens and landscapes.
