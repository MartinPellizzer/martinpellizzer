Rosa × centifolia muscosacristata

Rosa × centifolia muscosacristata, commonly known as the Muscosacristata rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in gardens and perfumery due to its strong floral scent.
