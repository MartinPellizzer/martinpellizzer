Rubus venustus

Rubus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful raspberry, is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
