Rhododendron argyrophyllum var. omeiense

Rhododendron argyrophyllum var. omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its silver-white to gray-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist climate.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and horticultural collections.
