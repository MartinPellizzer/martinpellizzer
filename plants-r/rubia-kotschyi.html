Rubia kotschyi

Rubia kotschyi, commonly known as Kotschyi's Rubia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Southwest Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their ornamental and medicinal value. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia kotschyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
