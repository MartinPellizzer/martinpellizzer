Rosa × dumetorum var. valdefoliosa

Rosa × dumetorum var. valdefoliosa, commonly known as the Valdefoliosa shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dark green, trifoliate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often used in hedging or as a decorative shrub. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate water and pruning for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent winter buds and attractive foliage.
