Raoulia cinerea

Raoulia cinerea, commonly known as the silver raoulia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate beauty to its habitat. Raoulia cinerea has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to the harsh maritime climate of its native region.
