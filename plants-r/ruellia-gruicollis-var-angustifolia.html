Ruellia gruicollis var. angustifolia

Ruellia gruicollis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
