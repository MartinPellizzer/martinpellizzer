Rhysotoechia florulenta

Rhysotoechia florulenta, commonly known as the starlight orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant has a complex root system that allows it to thrive in nutrient-poor soil, and its leaves are broad and waxy to reduce water loss.
