Ruta bungei

Ruta bungei, commonly known as Chinese rue, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

Ruta bungei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
