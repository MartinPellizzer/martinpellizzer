Restio pulcher

Restio pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Restio pulcher is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
