Rupicapnos numidica subsp. delicatula

Rupicapnos numidica subsp.

delicatula, commonly known as the delicate rock rose, is a shrub native to North Africa and the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are small, leathery, and evergreen, providing year-round interest.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
