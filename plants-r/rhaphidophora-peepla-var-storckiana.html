Rhaphidophora peepla var. storckiana

Rhaphidophora peepla var. storckiana, commonly known as the Storck's prayer plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy, glossy surface. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage typically exhibits a variegated coloration, featuring shades of green, silver, and pink.

This ornamental plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
