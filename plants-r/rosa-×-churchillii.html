Rosa × churchillii

Rosa × churchillii, commonly known as the Churchill rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa rugosa and Rosa bracteata.

It is characterized by its hardy nature, making it suitable for coastal and harsh environments. The plant typically features dense, thorny stems and produces fragrant, deep pink to red flowers. Its foliage is dark green and evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

Rosa × churchillii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
