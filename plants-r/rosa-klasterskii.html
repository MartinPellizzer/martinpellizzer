Rosa klasterskii

Rosa klasterskii, commonly known as the Klasterskii rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat. Rosa klasterskii is adapted to harsh climates, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species, suitable for cultivation in arid or semi-arid environments.
