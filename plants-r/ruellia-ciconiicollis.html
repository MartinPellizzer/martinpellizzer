Ruellia ciconiicollis

Ruellia ciconiicollis, commonly known as the scarlet roselight, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and branching.

Ruellia ciconiicollis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or disturbed areas.
