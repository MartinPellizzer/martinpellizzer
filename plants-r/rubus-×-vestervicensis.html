Rubus × vestervicensis

Rubus × vestervicensis, commonly known as the Vesterbrogård raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its flavorful berries. It typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves and a shrub-like growth habit.
