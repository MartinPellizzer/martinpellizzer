Rosa afzeliana var. holubyana

Rosa afzeliana var. holubyana, commonly known as the Holubyan rose, is a variant of the Afghan rose, native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to reddish-pink flowers. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
