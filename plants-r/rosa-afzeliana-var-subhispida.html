Rosa afzeliana var. subhispida

Rosa afzeliana var. subhispida, commonly known as the hairy rose or African rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. It produces oval, red-orange hips that contain seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
