Rhus ernestii

Rhus ernestii, commonly known as the Ernest's sumac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like poison ivy and mango. Rhus ernestii is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
