Ruellia Salviifolius

Ruellia salviifolius is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant blue or purple flowers. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is native to regions in South America. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
