Rosa dumalis f. sphaeroidea

Rosa dumalis f. sphaeroidea, commonly known as the spherical rose, is a unique cultivar characterized by its distinctive rounded, globe-like blooms.

This form of the Rosa dumalis species features tightly packed, semi-double flowers that create a compact, spherical shape. The plant typically has upright, thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It is a hardy shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its unusual flower form and vibrant, often pink or red hues.
