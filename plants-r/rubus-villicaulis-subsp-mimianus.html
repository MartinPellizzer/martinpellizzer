Rubus villicaulis subsp. mimianus

Rubus villicaulis subsp.

mimianus, commonly known as the Mimianus blackberry, is a shrub native to China. It is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
