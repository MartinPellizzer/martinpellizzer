Racosperma leptophlebum

Racosperma leptophlebum, commonly known as the slender-leaved acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale green, arranged in racemes, and it produces flat, legume-like pods.
