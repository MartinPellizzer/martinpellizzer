Ryania chocoensis

Ryania chocoensis, commonly known as the yellow ryania, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Ryania chocoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both garden cultivation and ecological restoration projects.
