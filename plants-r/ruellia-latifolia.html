Ruellia latifolia

Ruellia latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and has clusters of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Ruellia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
