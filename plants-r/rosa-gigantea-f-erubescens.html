Rosa gigantea f. erubescens

Rosa gigantea f. erubescens, commonly known as the Crimson Giant Rose, is a striking cultivar of the Rosa gigantea species, characterized by its large, showy flowers.

The plant features deep red petals that transition to a lighter hue at the edges, creating a visually appealing contrast. It is a vigorous grower, producing long canes that can reach significant heights, making it suitable for trellises or as a focal point in gardens. The foliage is dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental value throughout the growing season.

This variety is prized for its dramatic blooms and is often cultivated in warmer climates where it can thrive year-round.
