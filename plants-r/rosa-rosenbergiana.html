Rosa rosenbergiana

Rosa rosenbergiana, commonly known as the Persian rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the high altitudes of Central Asia, particularly in regions like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its large, deep pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a shrub-like growth habit.

Rosa rosenbergiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
