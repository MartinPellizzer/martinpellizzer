Rhododendron pittosporifolium

Rhododendron pittosporifolium, commonly known as the Pittosporum-leaved rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and lavender, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a velvety texture. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
