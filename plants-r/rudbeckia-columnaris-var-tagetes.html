Rudbeckia columnaris var. tagetes

Rudbeckia columnaris var. tagetes, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features vibrant orange to red flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has upright, branching stems that are covered in rough, hairy texture. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a serrated edge and a grayish-green color.

Rudbeckia columnaris var. tagetes thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
