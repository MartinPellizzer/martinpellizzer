Ravenea amara

Ravenea amara, commonly known as the red-rimmed Ravenea, is a striking ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are often adorned with red or purple margins, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ravenea amara is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soil and partial shade.
