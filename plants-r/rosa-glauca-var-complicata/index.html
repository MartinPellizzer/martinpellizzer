Rosa Glauca Var. Complicata

Bluebush rose, scientifically known as Rosa glauca var. complicata, is a captivating shrub native to the arid regions of western North America. This resilient plant thrives in harsh environments, showcasing its ability to adapt to extreme conditions. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blue-gray foliage make it a striking addition to any landscape. The rose produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer, adding a touch of elegance to its rugged appearance. Due to its unique characteristics, it is both a valuable ornamental plant and an important part of its native ecosystem.
