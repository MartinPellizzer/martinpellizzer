Reynoutria forbesii

Reynoutria forbesii, commonly known as the purple stem arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often arrowhead-shaped. The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Reynoutria forbesii is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
