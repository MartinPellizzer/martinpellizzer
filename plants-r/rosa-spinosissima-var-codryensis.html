Rosa spinosissima var. codryensis

Rosa spinosissima var. codryensis, commonly known as the Codry's spiny rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
