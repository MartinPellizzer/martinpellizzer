Rosa blinii

Rosa blinii, commonly known as the Blinii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Europe and parts of western Siberia, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are compound, typically consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa blinii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
