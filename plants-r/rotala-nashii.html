Rotala nashii

Rotala nashii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water plantain, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.

Rotala nashii is often used in aquascaping due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
