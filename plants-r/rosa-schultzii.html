Rosa schultzii

Rosa schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Rosa schultzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively low maintenance once established.
