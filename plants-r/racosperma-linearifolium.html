Racosperma linearifolium

Racosperma linearifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody structure with a branching stem and is often found in dry, sandy soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
