Rubus × semitimendus

Rubus × semitimendus, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and the fruit is a aggregate of small drupes, often dark red to black in color.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential for fruit production in suitable climates.
