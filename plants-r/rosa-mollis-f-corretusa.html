Rosa mollis f. corretusa

Rosa mollis f. corretusa, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, which is native to the Mediterranean region.

This plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a soft, fuzzy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is a compact shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking to add visual interest to their landscapes.
