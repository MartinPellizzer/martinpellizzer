Rosa coriifolia var. friesii

Rosa coriifolia var. friesii, commonly known as the Friesii shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
