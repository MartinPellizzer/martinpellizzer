Rumex kunthii

Rumex kunthii, commonly known as the prairie sorrel, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. It has a short stem with a basal rosette of leaves, and its roots are fibrous.

Rumex kunthii is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
