Rosa alpina var. sublaevis

Rosa alpina var. sublaevis, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellowish hue at the base. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that are densely covered with fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in garden settings.
