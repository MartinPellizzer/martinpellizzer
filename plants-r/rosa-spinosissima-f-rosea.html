Rosa spinosissima f. rosea

Rosa spinosissima f. rosea, commonly known as the pink spiny rose, is a cultivated form of the spiny rose, characterized by its soft pink flowers and thorny stems.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with dense, dark green foliage. The flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is valued for its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant blooms.
