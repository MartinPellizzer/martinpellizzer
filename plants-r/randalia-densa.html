Randalia densa

Randalia densa, commonly known as the dense randalia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. Randalia densa thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
