Racosperma heterophyllum

Racosperma heterophyllum, commonly known as the variable acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody structure with a spreading habit, often found in dry, sandy soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
