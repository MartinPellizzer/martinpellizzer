Rosa unterkrauteri

Rosa unterkrauteri, commonly known as the Alpine rock rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, particularly the Alps. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are small, dark green, and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand harsh alpine conditions.

Rosa unterkrauteri is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
