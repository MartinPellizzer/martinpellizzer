Ranunculus peduncularis var. peduncularis

Ranunculus peduncularis var. peduncularis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, upright stem. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and can be found in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are palmately divided with serrated edges, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its bright, cheerful flowers.
