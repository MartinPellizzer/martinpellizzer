Rosa foecundissima

Rosa foecundissima, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant has compound leaves with five to seven leaflets and is characterized by its thorny stems. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and thrives in full sun.

Rosa foecundissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
