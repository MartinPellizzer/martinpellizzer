Rhynchanthera adenophora

Rhynchanthera adenophora, commonly known as the hairy root orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Australia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with good drainage. Rhynchanthera adenophora is a relatively rare and specialized species, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
