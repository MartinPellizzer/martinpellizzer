Rupicapnos africana var. battandieri

Rupicapnos africana var. battandieri, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of bright red berries that persist through winter. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking berries and architectural form.
