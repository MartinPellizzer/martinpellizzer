Ruyschia spixiana

Ruyschia spixiana, commonly known as the Spix's ruyschia, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces vibrant yellow spathes with red berries, which are a key feature in its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Ruyschia spixiana is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
