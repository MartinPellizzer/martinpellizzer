Rosa Arvensis Proles Repens

Rosa arvensis proles repens is a common weed found in disturbed soils across temperate regions. It is known for its sprawling growth habit and ability to spread rapidly through runners. The plant produces small pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are trifoliate and have a serrated edge. Despite its invasive tendencies, it has been used in traditional medicine for its mild astringent properties.
