Rubus ranftii

Rubus ranftii, commonly known as the Ranft's blackberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of shrubs commonly referred to as blackberries or raspberries. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically dark red to black in color. It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

The botanical features of Rubus ranftii make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
