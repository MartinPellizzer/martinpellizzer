Rhaphidophora engleri

Rhaphidophora engleri, commonly known as the Engler's arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Rhaphidophora engleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and ease of care.
