Rubia walteri

Rubia walteri, commonly known as Walter's Rubia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow, with two lips, and it produces small, woody fruits.

Rubia walteri is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
