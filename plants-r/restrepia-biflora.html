Restrepia biflora

Restrepia biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered restrepia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a few fleshy, green leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and often have a faintly colored lip.

Restrepia biflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
