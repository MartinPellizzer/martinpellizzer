Rhamnus jujuba

Rhamnus jujuba, commonly known as the Chinese date or jujube, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into edible, reddish-brown fruits. The fruit has a sweet, nutty flavor and is rich in vitamins and minerals.

It is widely cultivated for its fruit and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
