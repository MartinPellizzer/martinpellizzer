Rabdosia excisa

Rabdosia excisa, commonly known as the silver leaf rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Rabdosia excisa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
