Rosa mollis var. doerfleri

Rosa mollis var. doerfleri, commonly known as the Doerfler's evergreen rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains evergreen throughout the year, providing year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. It has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
