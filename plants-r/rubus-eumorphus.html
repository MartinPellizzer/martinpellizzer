Rubus eumorphus

Rubus eumorphus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are clusters of small, red, edible drupes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and thickets.

Rubus eumorphus is valued for its culinary uses and plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
