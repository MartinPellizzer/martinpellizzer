Rotang spectabilis

Rotang spectabilis, commonly known as the magnificent ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, often with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-white in color, and the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.
