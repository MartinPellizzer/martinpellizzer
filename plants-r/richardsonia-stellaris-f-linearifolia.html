Richardsonia stellaris f. linearifolia

Richardsonia stellaris f. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a perennial herb with a low-growing habit, featuring basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in early spring. Its foliage is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves, which give it its specific epithet "linearifolia.".

This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and streamside habitats.
