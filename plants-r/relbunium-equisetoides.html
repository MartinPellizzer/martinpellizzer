Relbunium equisetoides

Relbunium equisetoides, commonly known as the slender clubmoss, is a small evergreen shrub native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and small, scale-like leaves. Due to its slow growth and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
