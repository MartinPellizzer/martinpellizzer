Rupicapnos numidica subsp. sarcocapnoides

Rupicapnos numidica subsp.

sarcocapnoides, commonly known as the Numidian rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months.

The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
