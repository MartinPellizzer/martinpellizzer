Rosa fastuosa

Rosa fastuosa, commonly known as the showy rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in coastal areas with well-drained soil. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a deep red center. Its leaves are serrated and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rosa fastuosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
