Rosa × miljackae

Rosa × miljackae, commonly known as the Serbian rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Balkans.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and historical significance, it is often appreciated in horticultural collections.
