Rhodiola himalensis subsp. bouvieri

Rhodiola himalensis subsp.

bouvieri, commonly known as the Himalayan rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.

Due to its resilience and unique adaptations, it is of interest in both botanical research and traditional medicine.
