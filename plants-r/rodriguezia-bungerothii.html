Rodriguezia bungerothii

Rodriguezia bungerothii, commonly known as the Bungert's rodriguezia, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter, making it a dramatic addition to any landscape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in tropical regions.
