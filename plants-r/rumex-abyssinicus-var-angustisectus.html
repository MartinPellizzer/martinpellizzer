Rumex abyssinicus var. angustisectus

Rumex abyssinicus var. angustisectus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dock, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It features basal leaves that are elongated and narrow, with a smooth or slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters atop slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a leafy green vegetable.
