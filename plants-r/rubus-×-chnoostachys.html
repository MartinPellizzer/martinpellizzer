Rubus × chnoostachys

Rubus × chnoostachys, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that range in color from red to black.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to coastal environments.
