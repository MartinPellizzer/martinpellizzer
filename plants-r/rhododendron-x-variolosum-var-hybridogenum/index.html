Rhododendron × Variolosum Var. Hybridogenum

Rhododendron × variolosum var. hybridogenum is a unique hybrid plant known for its striking floral displays. It combines the characteristics of its parent species, resulting in vibrant flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions. It thrives in acidic soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its beauty and resilience make it a standout in the world of flowering shrubs.
