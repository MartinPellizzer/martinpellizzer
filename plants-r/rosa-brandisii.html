Rosa brandisii

Rosa brandisii, commonly known as the Brandis rose, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces oval, yellowish fruits that contain numerous seeds. Rosa brandisii thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in botanical studies.
