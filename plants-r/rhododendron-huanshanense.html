Rhododendron huanshanense

Rhododendron huanshanense, commonly known as the Huanshan rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Huanshan Mountain region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in cool, moist environments at high altitudes. The plant produces striking clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and red during the spring. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a dense, compact arrangement.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
