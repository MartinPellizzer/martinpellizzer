Rosa cinnamomea f. foecundissima

Rosa cinnamomea f. foecundissima, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a form of Rosa cinnamomea, a species native to China, and is known for its continuous blooming throughout the growing season. The plant features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a cinnamon-like scent. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, glossy foliage.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
