Rinorea rudolphiana

Rinorea rudolphiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Rinorea rudolphiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
