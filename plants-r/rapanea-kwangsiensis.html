Rapanea kwangsiensis

Rapanea kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi boxwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China, particularly the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe, containing a single seed.
