Rhus polyneura var. hylophila

Rhus polyneura var. hylophila, commonly known as the silky maple or silky ash, is a variant of the silky ash tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its smooth, pale bark and palmately lobed leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The leaves turn bright yellow in autumn, adding ornamental value to the landscape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in spring, which are followed by spherical, spiny fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
