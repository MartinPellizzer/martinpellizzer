Rollandia calycina var. kaalae

Rollandia calycina var. kaalae, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring large, showy flowers with five petals. The leaves are typically green, heart-shaped, and have a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
