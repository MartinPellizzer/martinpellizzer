Rhamnobrina heterophylla

Rhamnobrina heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf rhamnobra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant typically grows in shrubland habitats, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are small and clustered, with both male and female flowers occurring on the same inflorescence.

The species is notable for its ecological adaptability and is found in parts of southern Africa.
