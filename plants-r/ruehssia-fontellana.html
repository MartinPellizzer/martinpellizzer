Ruehssia fontellana

Ruehssia fontellana, commonly known as the dwarf flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening or as a decorative hedge. Its leaves are glossy and leathery, while the flowers emit a subtle, spicy fragrance.

Ruehssia fontellana is relatively low maintenance, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
