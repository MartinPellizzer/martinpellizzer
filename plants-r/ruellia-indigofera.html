Ruellia indigofera

Ruellia indigofera, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Ruellia indigofera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
