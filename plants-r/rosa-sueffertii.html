Rosa sueffertii

Rosa sueffertii, commonly known as the Sueffert's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Mongolia, and Siberia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa sueffertii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
