Raputia praetermissa

Raputia praetermissa, commonly known as the forgotten raputia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with a single, broad, arrow-shaped leaf. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a green spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
