Rutidea lasiosiphon

Rutidea lasiosiphon, commonly known as the wooly siphon, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
