Ranunculus aquatilis var. drouetii

Ranunculus aquatilis var. drouetii, commonly known as the water buttercup variant Drouetii, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in shallow water bodies such as ponds and streams, where it thrives in moist, muddy substrates. The plant features delicate, floating leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are bright yellow with a distinctive ring of sepals, often appearing in clusters.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting aquatic ecosystems.
