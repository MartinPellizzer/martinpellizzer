Rhinanthus pallidus

Rhinanthus pallidus, commonly known as the pale rhinoceros flower, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers.

Rhinanthus pallidus is often found in areas with sandy or loamy soils and is considered a rare and declining species in parts of its range.
