Rhododendron × cartonianum

Rhododendron × cartonianum, commonly known as the Cartman rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a wide variety of forms and colors, making it a popular choice among horticulturists.
