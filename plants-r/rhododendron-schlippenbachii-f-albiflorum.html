Rhododendron schlippenbachii f. albiflorum

Rhododendron schlippenbachii f. albiflorum, commonly known as the Whiteflower Schlippenbach rhododendron, is a unique hybrid plant characterized by its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage that provides a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
