Ruschia limbata

Ruschia limbata, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a silvery or grayish coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
