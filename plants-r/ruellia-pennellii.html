Ruellia pennellii

Ruellia pennellii, commonly known as the purple ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of trumpet-shaped, violet-blue flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Ruellia pennellii is also known for its adaptability and ease of care in garden settings.
