Rosa × koehneana

Rosa × koehneana, commonly known as the Koehne rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its foliage is dark green with a slightly serrated edge. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × koehneana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
