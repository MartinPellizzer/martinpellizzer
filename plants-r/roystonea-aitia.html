Roystonea aitia

Roystonea aitia, commonly known as the African date palm, is a tall, slender palm native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a tall, unbranched trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. The plant produces large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in branched inflorescences, and it produces edible, sweet fruits resembling dates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
