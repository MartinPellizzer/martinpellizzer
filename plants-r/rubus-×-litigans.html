Rubus × litigans

Rubus × litigans, commonly known as the blackberry-loganberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus loganobaccus (loganberry), resulting in a plant with distinctive botanical features. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces aggregate fruits that range from dark purple to black in color.

This hybrid is valued for its robust growth and flavorful berries, making it a popular choice for cultivation.
