Rhinanthus × kunzii

Rhinanthus × kunzii, commonly known as the hybrid common toadflax, is a cross between Rhinanthus minor and Rhinanthus glabellus.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with two lips, characteristic of the Orobanchaceae family. Its roots can form symbiotic relationships with nitrogen-fixing bacteria, enhancing soil fertility.

This species is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, contributing to ecological diversity in these environments.
