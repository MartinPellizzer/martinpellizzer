Rosa pseudindica

Rosa pseudindica, commonly known as the Indian rose or the pink rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in traditional medicine and perfumery.

Rosa pseudindica is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
