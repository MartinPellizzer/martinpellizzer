Rhynchospora chinensis subsp. chinensis

Rhynchospora chinensis subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as Chinese spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia. It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

The plant features slender, grass-like stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers arranged in a cylindrical shape.

The species is characterized by its narrow leaves and is often found in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
