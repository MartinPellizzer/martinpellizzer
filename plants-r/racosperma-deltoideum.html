Racosperma deltoideum

Racosperma deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid senna, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in dry, sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Racosperma deltoideum is often used in traditional medicine and for its ornamental value.
