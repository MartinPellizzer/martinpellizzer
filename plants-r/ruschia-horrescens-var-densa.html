Ruschia horrescens var. densa

Ruschia horrescens var. densa, commonly known as the dense Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
