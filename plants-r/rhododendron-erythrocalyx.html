Rhododendron erythrocalyx

Rhododendron erythrocalyx, commonly known as the red calyx rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers are usually pink to red in color.

Rhododendron erythrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
