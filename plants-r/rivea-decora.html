Rivea decora

Rivea decora, commonly known as the golden vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and a rapid growth habit, making it suitable for trellises or as a hanging plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Rivea decora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
