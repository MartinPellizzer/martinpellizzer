Rosa excurrens

Rosa excurrens, commonly known as the evergreen rose, is a species native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Rosa excurrens is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
