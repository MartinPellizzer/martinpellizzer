Rutidea tenuicaulis

Rutidea tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed legume, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, adapting to poor soil conditions. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually pink or purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
