Rosa spinosissima var. iranica

Rosa spinosissima var. iranica, commonly known as the Iranian spiny rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
