Ribes laxiflorum var. inermis

Ribes laxiflorum var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth-flowered gooseberry, is a shrub native to the western United States.

The variety inermis is distinguished by the absence of thorns on its stems, making it less prickly than other Ribes species.
