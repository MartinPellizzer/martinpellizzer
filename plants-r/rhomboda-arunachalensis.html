Rhomboda arunachalensis

Rhomboda arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly in Arunachal Pradesh.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Rhomboda arunachalensis is a relatively rare species, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
