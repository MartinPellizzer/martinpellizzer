Rhinorchis longipedicellata

Rhinorchis longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive hooded lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
