Ruta fumariifolia

Ruta fumariifolia, commonly known as the smoky rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that turns brown as it matures.

Ruta fumariifolia is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
