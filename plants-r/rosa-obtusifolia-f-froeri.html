Rosa obtusifolia f. froeri

Rosa obtusifolia f. froeri, commonly known as the Froer's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in thorny, reddish-brown bark. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
