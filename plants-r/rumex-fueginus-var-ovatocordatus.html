Rumex fueginus var. ovatocordatus

Rumex fueginus var. ovatocordatus, commonly known as the ovate cordate sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streambanks. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple underside. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, producing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is characterized by its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
