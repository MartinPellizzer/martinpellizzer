Rhododendron dilatatum var. kiyosumense

Rhododendron dilatatum var. kiyosumense, commonly known as the Kiyosumi rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
