Ranunculus obliquifolius

Ranunculus obliquifolius, commonly known as the zigzag buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five to eight petals and numerous stamens, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often arranged in a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Ranunculus obliquifolius is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
