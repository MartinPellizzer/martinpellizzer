Rhamnus hainanensis

Rhamnus hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan buckthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Hainan Island, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species.

The plant has a woody stem and is often found in forested areas, contributing to the local biodiversity.
