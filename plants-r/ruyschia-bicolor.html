Ruyschia bicolor

Ruyschia bicolor, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are dark green on top and silvery white underneath. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath a spathe.

Ruyschia bicolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
