Rhamnus utilis var. szechuanensis

Rhamnus utilis var. szechuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan buckthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible but have a laxative effect. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
