Rosa × litvinovii var. slobodianii

Rosa × litvinovii var. slobodianii, commonly known as the Slobodianii rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant is native to the Caucasus region and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to adapt to a range of environmental conditions.
