Rosa provincialis blanda

Rosa provincialis blanda, commonly known as the soft provincial rose, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate pink flowers and thornless stems, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is valued for its fragrance and is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic appeal.
