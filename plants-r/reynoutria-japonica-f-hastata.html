Reynoutria japonica f. hastata

Reynoutria japonica f. hastata, commonly known as Giant Reed, is a reed-like plant native to East Asia.

It is a vigorous perennial herb that grows in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist environments. The plant features tall, hollow stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a bluish-green hue.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in dense clusters, typically in late summer.
