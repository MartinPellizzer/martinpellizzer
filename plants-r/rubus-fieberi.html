Rubus fieberi

Rubus fieberi, commonly known as the Fieberi raspberry, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
