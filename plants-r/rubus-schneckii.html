Rubus schneckii

Rubus schneckii, commonly known as the Schneck's blackberry, is a species of shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested in the fall. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

Rubus schneckii is often found in moist woodlands and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
