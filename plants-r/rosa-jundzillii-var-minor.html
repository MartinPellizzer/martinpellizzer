Rosa jundzillii var. minor

Rosa jundzillii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
