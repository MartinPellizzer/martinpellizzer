Rhaptostylum ovatum

Rhaptostylum ovatum, commonly known as the slender leaved orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Rhaptostylum ovatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
