Rosa fragilis

Rosa fragilis, commonly known as the delicate rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, with five petals and numerous stamens. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa fragilis is characterized by its weak stem and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
