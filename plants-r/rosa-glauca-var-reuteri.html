Rosa glauca var. reuteri

Rosa glauca var. reuteri, commonly known as the Reuter's blue rose, is a variant of the blue rose, characterized by its striking deep blue petals.

This plant is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
