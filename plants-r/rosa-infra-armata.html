Rosa infra-armata

Rosa infra-armata, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or white.

Rosa infra-armata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
