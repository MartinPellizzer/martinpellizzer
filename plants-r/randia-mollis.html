Randia mollis

Randia mollis, commonly known as the small randia or velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in branched inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Randia mollis is often found in disturbed areas and can be invasive in certain regions.
