Rubus villiramus

Rubus villiramus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the mountainous regions of South America. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are typically harvested in the fall. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the stems are covered in fine, prickly hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
