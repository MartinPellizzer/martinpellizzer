Rohdea henryi

Rohdea henryi, commonly known as Henry's rohdea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for shaded environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Rohdea henryi is also valued for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soil conditions.
