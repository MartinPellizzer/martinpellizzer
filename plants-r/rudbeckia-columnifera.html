Rudbeckia columnifera

Rudbeckia columnifera, commonly known as the tall coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive black center. The plant typically grows between 2 to 5 feet in height and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often covered in fine, silky hairs.

Rudbeckia columnifera is a perennial that is well-suited for gardens and naturalized areas due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
