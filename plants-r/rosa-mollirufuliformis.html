Rosa mollirufuliformis

Rosa mollirufuliformis, commonly known as the soft-hued rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil with partial sunlight. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a soft, velvety texture.

Rosa mollirufuliformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
