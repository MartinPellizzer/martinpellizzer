Rosa provincialis hybrida

Rosa provincialis hybrida, commonly known as the Provincial Hybrid Rose, is a hybrid species originating from Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant typically features fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

Rosa provincialis hybrida is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both garden beds and container planting.
