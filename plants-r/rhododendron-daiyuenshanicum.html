Rhododendron daiyuenshanicum

Rhododendron daiyuenshanicum, commonly known as the Daiyuenshan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
