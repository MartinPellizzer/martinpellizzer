Rosa polyantha var. erubescens

Rosa polyantha var. erubescens, commonly known as the everblooming shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compact, thornless stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is renowned for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for the blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
