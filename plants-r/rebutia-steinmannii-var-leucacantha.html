Rebutia steinmannii var. leucacantha

Rebutia steinmannii var. leucacantha, commonly known as the White-veined Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem with a ribbed structure, typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are often adorned with white veins. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid conditions.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for desert landscaping.
