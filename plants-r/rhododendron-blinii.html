Rhododendron blinii

Rhododendron blinii, commonly known as the Blinii rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partially shaded to sunny conditions. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron blinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden environments.
