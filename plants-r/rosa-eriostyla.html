Rosa eriostyla

Rosa eriostyla, commonly known as the silky rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically pinnately compound, with fine, silky hairs that give the plant its common name. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
