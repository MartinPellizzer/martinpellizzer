Riedlea Peruviana

Riedlea peruviana is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Peru. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. This species belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
