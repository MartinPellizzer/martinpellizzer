Rebutia xanthocarpa var. salmonea

Rebutia xanthocarpa var. salmonea, commonly known as the salmon-colored rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features compact, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with a pale pink or salmon hue, especially under direct sunlight. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
