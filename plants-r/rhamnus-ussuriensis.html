Rhamnus ussuriensis

Rhamnus ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, oval-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, reddish berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their astringent properties.
