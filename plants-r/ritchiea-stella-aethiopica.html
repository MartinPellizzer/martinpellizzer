Ritchiea stella-aethiopica

Ritchiea stella-aethiopica, commonly known as the star-like Ritchiea, is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the humid forests of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and a cluster of long, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange, adding a vibrant splash of color to its natural habitat. It thrives in high light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
