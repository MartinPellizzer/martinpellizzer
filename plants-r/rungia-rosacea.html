Rungia rosacea

Rungia rosacea, commonly known as the red-flowered shrub, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
