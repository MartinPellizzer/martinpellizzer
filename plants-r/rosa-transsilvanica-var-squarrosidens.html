Rosa transsilvanica var. squarrosidens

Rosa transsilvanica var. squarrosidens, commonly known as the Transylvanian squarrose rose, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to Romania.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with serrated leaflets and a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
