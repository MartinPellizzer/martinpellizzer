Rubus rivularis var. elegans

Rubus rivularis var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant blackberry.

This variant is a shrub native to temperate regions, characterized by its trailing growth habit and thorny stems. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges and a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are usually dark purple to black when ripe.

It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in woodland edges and stream banks.
