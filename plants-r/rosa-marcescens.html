Rosa marcescens

Rosa marcescens, commonly known as the autumn rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant produces woody, thorny stems and has compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom, which gives them a distinctive appearance.

Rosa marcescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal beauty.
