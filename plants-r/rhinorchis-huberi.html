Rhinorchis huberi

Rhinorchis huberi, commonly known as the Hubei orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive pinkish-purple color and a fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Rhinorchis huberi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
