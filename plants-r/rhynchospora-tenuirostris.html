Rhynchospora tenuirostris

Rhynchospora tenuirostris, commonly known as slender-beaked sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its triangular, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale to brownish flowers, typically blooming in late summer. It is native to wetlands and marshy areas in the eastern United States.
