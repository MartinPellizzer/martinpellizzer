Rhamnus shortii

Rhamnus shortii, commonly known as Short's buckthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. This shrub typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, dark berries.

The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, alternate leaves.
