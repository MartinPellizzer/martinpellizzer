Rubus argenteus var. roseiflorus

Rubus argenteus var. roseiflorus, commonly known as the silverleaf raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America. The plant features silvery-white undersides on its leaves, giving it its common name. Its flowers are pink to rose-colored, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a small, edible berry with a tart flavor, often used in jams and culinary applications.
