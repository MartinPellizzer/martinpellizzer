Ruagea floribunda

Ruagea floribunda, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Ruagea floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide shade in warm climates.
