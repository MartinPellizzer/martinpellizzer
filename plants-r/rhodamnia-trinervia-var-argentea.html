Rhodamnia trinervia var. argentea

Rhodamnia trinervia var. argentea, commonly known as the silver-leafed rhodamnia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rainforests and moist woodlands, thriving in shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in suitable climates.
