Rugelia

Rugelia, commonly known as the "Chinese lantern" plant, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its flowers are small and clustered, often white or yellow, and are surrounded by the striking red or orange bracts.

Rugelia thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
