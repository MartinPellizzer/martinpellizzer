Remirea wightiana

Remirea wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Sri Lanka. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and pendant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
