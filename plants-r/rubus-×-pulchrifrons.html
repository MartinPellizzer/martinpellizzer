Rubus × pulchrifrons

Rubus × pulchrifrons, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically small and tart. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
