Rosa micrantha f. histrix

Rosa micrantha f. histrix, commonly known as the hispid rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in summer. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
