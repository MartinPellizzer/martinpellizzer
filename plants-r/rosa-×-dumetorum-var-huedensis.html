Rosa × dumetorum var. huedensis

Rosa × dumetorum var. huedensis, commonly known as the Hueden rose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically has dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
