Rubus weihei

Rubus weihei, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
