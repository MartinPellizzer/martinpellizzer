Rosa × improvisa

Rosa × improvisa, commonly known as the "Imperial Rose," is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is native to the United States and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Rosa × improvisa prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a showy, hardy plant.
