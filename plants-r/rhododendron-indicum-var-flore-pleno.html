Rhododendron indicum var. flore-pleno

Rhododendron indicum var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the full-flowered Indian rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, clustered flowers.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to the Himalayas, where it thrives in cool, moist, acidic soils. The plant produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It requires partial shade and well-drained soil to flourish.
