Ruyschiana palmata

Ruyschiana palmata, commonly known as the red-flowered rock cactus, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, columnar stem and vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a thick, waxy cuticle that helps it retain moisture in its dry environment. Its leaves are small and spiny, providing additional protection from herbivores.

Ruyschiana palmata is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure, making it a resilient and visually appealing succulent.
