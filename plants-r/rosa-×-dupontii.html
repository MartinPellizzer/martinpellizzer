Rosa × dupontii

Rosa × dupontii, commonly known as the Dupont rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering, producing large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink and white. The plant has an upright habit with thorned stems and dark green, leathery foliage. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Rosa × dupontii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden conditions.
