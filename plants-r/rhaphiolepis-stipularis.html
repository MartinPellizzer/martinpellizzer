Rhaphiolepis stipularis

Rhaphiolepis stipularis, commonly known as the Japanese rose or evergreen rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It features small, fragrant pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms make it suitable for garden borders and container planting.
