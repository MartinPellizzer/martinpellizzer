Rosa sepium var. lemanii

Rosa sepium var. lemanii, commonly known as the Leman's rose, is a hybrid rose species with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its semi-double to fully double flowers, which are typically a deep pink to crimson color. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively hardy nature.
