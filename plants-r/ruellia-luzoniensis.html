Ruellia luzoniensis

Ruellia luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

Ruellia luzoniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
