Ranunculus dregei

Ranunculus dregei, commonly known as the Cape buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of palmate leaves with a hairy texture.

Ranunculus dregei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
