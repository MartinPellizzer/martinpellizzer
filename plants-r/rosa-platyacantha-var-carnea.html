Rosa platyacantha var. carnea

Rosa platyacantha var. carnea, commonly known as the pink shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thorned stems and small, oval, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem structure and a preference for well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
