Rottlera zollingeri

Rottlera zollingeri, commonly known as the Zollinger's rottlera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rottleriaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
