Rourea mimosoides

Rourea mimosoides, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in coastal and saline environments. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.
