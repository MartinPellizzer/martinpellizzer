Rhodophiala araucana

Rhodophiala araucana, commonly known as the Araucaria or the Andean pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Rhodophiala araucana is adapted to cold, mountainous climates and can thrive in well-drained soils.
