Rubus informifolius

Rubus informifolius, commonly known as the soft-leaved blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern Asia, characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but often tart. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
