Rumfordia revealii

Rumfordia revealii, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and parts of Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a distinctive yellowish-green color.

Rumfordia revealii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along riverbanks.
