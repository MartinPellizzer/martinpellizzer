Rosa mixta

Rosa mixta, commonly known as the hybrid rose, is a species that results from the crossbreeding of different Rosa species.

It is characterized by its diverse range of flower colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, which makes it highly popular in horticulture. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Rosa mixta thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its flowers are often fragrant and can bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
