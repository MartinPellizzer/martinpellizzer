Rapuntium obovatum

Rapuntium obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed, obovate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces rounded, hairy seed pods that contain numerous small, round seeds. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, calcareous soils.

Rapuntium obovatum is a perennial herb that is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves and seeds.
