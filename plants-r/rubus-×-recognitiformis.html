Rubus × recognitiformis

Rubus × recognitiformis, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces bright red, edible berries that are popular for culinary use. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features include compound leaves with serrated edges and a dense root system that aids in nutrient absorption.
