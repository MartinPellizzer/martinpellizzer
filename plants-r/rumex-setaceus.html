Rumex setaceus

Rumex setaceus, commonly known as the slender dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. Its small, greenish flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom in the spring.

Rumex setaceus is characterized by its strong, slightly sour odor and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
