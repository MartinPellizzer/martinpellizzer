Rebutia aureiflora var. blossfeldii

Rebutia aureiflora var. blossfeldii, commonly known as the Blossfeld's golden cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to South America.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has a dense covering of fine, white to pale green hairs, which help protect it from intense sunlight. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant flowers and unique, compact growth form.
