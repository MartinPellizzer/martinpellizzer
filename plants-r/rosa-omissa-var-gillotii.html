Rosa omissa var. gillotii

Rosa omissa var. gillotii, commonly known as the Gillot's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has thornless stems and glossy, dark green foliage that provides attractive winter interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and seasonal floral display.
