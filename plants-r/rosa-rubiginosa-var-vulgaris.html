Rosa rubiginosa var. vulgaris

Rosa rubiginosa var. vulgaris, commonly known as the rough rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruits are red, hairy berries that contain seeds.

Rosa rubiginosa var. vulgaris is often used in hedges and for its ornamental value.
