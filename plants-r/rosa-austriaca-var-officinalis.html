Rosa austriaca var. officinalis

Rosa austriaca var. officinalis, commonly known as the Austrian officinal rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to central and eastern Europe, particularly thriving in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers and thorny stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and traditional pharmacology.
