Rochefortia stellata var. cristalensis

Rochefortia stellata var. cristalensis, commonly known as the Crystalensis Rochefortia, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy, star-shaped flowers. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are often a vibrant green, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its blooms, which can be white or pale yellow, appear in late spring to early summer.

This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its adaptability to a range of light conditions.
