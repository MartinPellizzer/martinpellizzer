Rhododendron retusum var. epilosum

Rhododendron retusum var. epilosum, commonly known as the Japanese azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, upright growth with clusters of showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a smooth texture, often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This variety is valued for its compact size and vibrant floral display.
