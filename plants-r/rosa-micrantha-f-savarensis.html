Rosa micrantha f. savarensis

Rosa micrantha f. savarensis, commonly known as the Savarensis rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa micrantha.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in southern France and Italy. This plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
