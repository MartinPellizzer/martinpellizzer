Rosa mesto

Rosa mesto, commonly known as the sad rose, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to almost black flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ornamental plant.
