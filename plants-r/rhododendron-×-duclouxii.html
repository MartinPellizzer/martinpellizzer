Rhododendron × duclouxii

Rhododendron × duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
