Ricinus communis f. picturatus

Ricinus communis f. picturatus, commonly known as the painted castor, is a striking variant of the castor oil plant.

It is characterized by its colorful, variegated leaves that display shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
