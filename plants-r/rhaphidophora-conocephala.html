Rhaphidophora conocephala

Rhaphidophora conocephala, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant features a short, thick rhizome and produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage is typically a vibrant green with a glossy texture, making it visually appealing.
