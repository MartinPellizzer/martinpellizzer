Ranunculus baurii

Ranunculus baurii, commonly known as the Baur's buttercup, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features showy, yellow flowers with 5 to 12 petals that bloom in spring. The plant has a hairy stem and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Ranunculus baurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms.
