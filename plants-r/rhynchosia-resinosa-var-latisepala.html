Rhynchosia resinosa var. latisepala

Rhynchosia resinosa var. latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled cowpea, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive floral structure.

It features showy, yellow flowers with broad, spreading sepals that give it its varietal name. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types.

Botanically, it is notable for its compound leaves and its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
