Rosa × schottiana var. dollineriana

Rosa × schottiana var. dollineriana, commonly known as the Dollinerian rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated edges, and the stems are armed with thorns.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
