Rubus kinashii f. macrophylla

Rubus kinashii f. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf raspberry, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thick, hairy stems and broad, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
