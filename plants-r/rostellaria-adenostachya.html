Rostellaria adenostachya

Rostellaria adenostachya, commonly known as the slender rostellaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth, where it thrives in humid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, and the flowers are small, with intricate petal structures typical of orchids.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
