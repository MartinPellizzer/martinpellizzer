Ruellia Macrantha

Ruellia macrantha is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, low-maintenance plants.
