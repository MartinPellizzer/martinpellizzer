Rosa provincialis multiplex

Rosa provincialis multiplex, commonly known as the Provincial Rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
