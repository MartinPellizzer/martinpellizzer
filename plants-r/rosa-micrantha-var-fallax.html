Rosa micrantha var. fallax

Rosa micrantha var. fallax, commonly known as the fallax rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves with finely serrated edges, and its stems are covered in short, stiff hairs. It is a deciduous species, shedding its leaves in the autumn, and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
