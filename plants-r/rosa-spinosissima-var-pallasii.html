Rosa spinosissima var. pallasii

Rosa spinosissima var. pallasii, commonly known as the Pallas's prickly rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
