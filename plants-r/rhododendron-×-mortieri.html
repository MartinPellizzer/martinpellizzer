Rhododendron × mortieri

Rhododendron × mortieri, commonly known as the Japanese rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, evergreen foliage that is dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Rhododendron × mortieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
