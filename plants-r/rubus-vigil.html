Rubus vigil

Rubus vigil, commonly known as the watchful bramble, is a shrub native to temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, usually white or pink flowers. The plant produces edible berries that ripen in the late summer, attracting various wildlife species. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Rubus vigil is known for its hardy nature and ability to adapt to a variety of environmental conditions.
