Rubus foliosus f. procerobothrys

Rubus foliosus f. procerobothrys, commonly known as the tall-leafed raspberry.

This botanical variant is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces red, hairy berries that are typically edible, though they may have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
