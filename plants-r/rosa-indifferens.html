Rosa indifferens

Rosa indifferens, commonly known as the indifferent rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub-like plant with small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to warm, sunny climates.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable botanical specimen.
