Rebutia diersiana var. minor

Rebutia diersiana var. minor, commonly known as the miniature rebutia, is a compact cactus species native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in rock gardens and containers.
