Rosa abietina f. inermis

Rosa abietina f. inermis, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a botanical variety characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season.

Rosa abietina f. inermis is valued in horticulture for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.
