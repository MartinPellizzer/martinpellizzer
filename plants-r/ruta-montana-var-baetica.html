Ruta montana var. baetica

Ruta montana var. baetica, commonly known as Spanish rue, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to Mediterranean gardens.

Its historical use in medicine and as a culinary herb highlights its significant botanical and cultural value.
