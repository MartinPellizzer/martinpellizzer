Rosa × stephanocarpa

Rosa × stephanocarpa, commonly known as the everblooming rosa.

It is a hybrid rose species that exhibits a unique ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It is valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to various soil types.

This rose is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental beauty and long-lasting bloom.
