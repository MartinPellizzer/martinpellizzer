Rhododendron epipastum

Rhododendron epipastum, commonly known as the alpine rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Rhododendron epipastum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable climatic conditions.
