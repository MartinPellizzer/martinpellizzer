Rosa × dumetorum var. schavschetica

Rosa × dumetorum var. schavschetica, commonly known as the Schavschetica rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
