Rhynchosia sigmodes

Rhynchosia sigmodes, commonly known as the broadleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers. Its seeds are often used as a protein-rich food source in various cuisines.

The plant's botanical structure supports its role in both ecological and agricultural systems.
