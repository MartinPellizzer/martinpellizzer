Relbunium catarinense

Relbunium catarinense, commonly known as the Santa Catarina dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and produces seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Relbunium catarinense is adapted to thrive in temperate climates with well-drained soil.
