Rosa rosomena

Rosa rosomena, commonly known as the Rosomena rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and a rich, sweet scent.

Rosa rosomena is valued for ornamental purposes and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
