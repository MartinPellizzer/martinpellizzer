Rabdosia menthoides

Rabdosia menthoides, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Rabdosia menthoides is valued for its medicinal properties, including its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
