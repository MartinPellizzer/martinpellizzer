Rosularia kokanica

Rosularia kokanica, commonly known as the Kokanica rosebush, is a perennial plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically oval and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Rosularia kokanica is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a resilient plant in its natural habitat.
