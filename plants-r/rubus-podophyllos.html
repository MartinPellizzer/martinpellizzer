Rubus podophyllos

Rubus podophyllos, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright red, aggregate fruits that are highly valued for their flavor and culinary uses. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Rubus podophyllos is also known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens.
