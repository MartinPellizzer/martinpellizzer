Rhipsalis cassythopsis

Rhipsalis cassythopsis, commonly known as the silver mist cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is a climbing cactus with long, thin, branched stems that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
