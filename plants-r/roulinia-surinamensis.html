Roulinia surinamensis

Roulinia surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible starch.
