Rosa × steinbachensis

Rosa × steinbachensis, commonly known as the Steinbach rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with thorny stems typical of many rose species.

Rosa × steinbachensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively disease-resistant, making it a popular choice for gardens.
