Ribes × koehneanum

Ribes × koehneanum, commonly known as the hybrid currant, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae.

It is a cross between Ribes sanguineum (bloodberry) and Ribes nigrum (black currant), resulting in a plant that exhibits traits of both parents. The hybrid is characterized by its attractive, glossy red berries that are typically edible and used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile ornamental and fruit-bearing plant.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is widely cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
