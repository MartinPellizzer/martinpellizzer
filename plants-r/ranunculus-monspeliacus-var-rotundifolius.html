Ranunculus monspeliacus var. rotundifolius

Ranunculus monspeliacus var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers with five to eight petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are round or heart-shaped, with a hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and it is often found in calcareous soils.
