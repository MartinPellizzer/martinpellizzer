Rosa hybrida var. spectabilis

Rosa hybrida var. spectabilis, commonly known as the Knock Out rose, is a popular hybrid rose celebrated for its vigorous growth and disease resistance.

It produces abundant, semi-double to fully double flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a favored choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its long blooming season and low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
