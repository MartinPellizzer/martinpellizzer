Rosa × rehderi

Rosa × rehderi, commonly known as the Rehder's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. The plant is native to China and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. It typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, serrated leaves.

Rosa × rehderi is valued in horticulture for its fragrance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
