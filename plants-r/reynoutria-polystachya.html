Reynoutria polystachya

Reynoutria polystachya, commonly known as giant waterweed, is a perennial aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its long, flexible stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often floating on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically found in the water or on the soil surface. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, with a smooth surface and a slight waxy coating.

Reynoutria polystachya is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of aquatic environments.
