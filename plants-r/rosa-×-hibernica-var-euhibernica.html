Rosa × hibernica var. euhibernica

Rosa × hibernica var. euhibernica, commonly known as the European hybrid rose, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a strong fragrance. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide dense foliage. It is well adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly used in gardens and landscaping.
