Rhododendron wuense

Rhododendron wuense, commonly known as the Wu rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic, well-drained soils. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and bloom in late spring.

Rhododendron wuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
