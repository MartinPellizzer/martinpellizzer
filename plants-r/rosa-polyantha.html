Rosa polyantha

Rosa polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese pink or polyantha rose, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of small, double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant produces fragrant, five-petaled blooms that appear in early summer. Its leaves are dark green, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Rosa polyantha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
