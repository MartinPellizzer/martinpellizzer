Rhaphidospora glabra var. pubescens

Rhaphidospora glabra var. pubescens, commonly known as the smooth-spored grass.

It is a grass species characterized by its glabrous (smooth) surface and pubescent (hairy) variant, which distinguishes it from other members of the genus. The plant typically features slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Botanically, it is notable for its unique spore morphology and ecological adaptability in various habitats.
