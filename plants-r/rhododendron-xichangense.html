Rhododendron xichangense

Rhododendron xichangense, commonly known as the Xichang rhododendron, is a species native to China, particularly in the Sichuan province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and cool temperatures. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring season. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
