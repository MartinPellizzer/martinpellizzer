Randia dilleniacea

Randia dilleniacea, commonly known as the Dilleniaceae or the Dilleniaceae family plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers that are clustered in umbels. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, with simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.

Randia dilleniacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
