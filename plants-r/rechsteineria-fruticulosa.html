Rechsteineria fruticulosa

Rechsteineria fruticulosa, commonly known as the desert broom, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and small, narrow leaves.
