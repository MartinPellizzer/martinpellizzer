Rudgea catharinae

Rudgea catharinae, commonly known as the Catrina's rudgea, is a species of flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. Rudgea catharinae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
