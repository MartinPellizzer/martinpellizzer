Renarda

Renarda, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Renarda is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
