Rosa orbicans

Rosa orbicans, commonly known as the orbiculate rose, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive orb-shaped flowers, which are typically pink or white in color. The plant produces fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa orbicans is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
