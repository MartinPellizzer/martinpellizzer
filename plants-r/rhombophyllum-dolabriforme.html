Rhombophyllum dolabriforme

Rhombophyllum dolabriforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rhombic-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
