Rosa communis var. grenieriana

Rosa communis var. grenieriana, commonly known as the Grenieriana rose, is a cultivar prized for its ornamental value.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rosa genus. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is often used in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
