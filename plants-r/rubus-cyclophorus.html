Rubus cyclophorus

Rubus cyclophorus, commonly known as the cyclophorus raspberry, is a species of shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the understory of forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and local biodiversity.
