Rosa mollis resinosa

Rosa mollis resinosa, commonly known as the resinous moss rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It produces a sticky resin that helps protect it from herbivores and environmental stressors.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique botanical features.
