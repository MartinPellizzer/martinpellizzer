Rosa montana f. longepedunculata

Rosa montana f. longepedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate mountain rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa montana species, known for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from deep pink to crimson, often with a bicolor pattern. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorned stems, typical of many rose species. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

This cultivar is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
