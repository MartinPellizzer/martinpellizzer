Rosa gallica var. livescens

Rosa gallica var. livescens, commonly known as the French pink, is a compact, thorny shrub native to Europe.

It produces showy, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery.
