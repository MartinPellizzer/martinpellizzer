Ranunculus ficaria f. luxurians

Ranunculus ficaria f. luxurians, commonly known as the velvetleaf buttercup, is a variant of the European buttercup characterized by its lush, dense foliage.

It is a herbaceous perennial with heart-shaped leaves that are typically deeply lobed and covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, often appearing in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens with the right conditions.
