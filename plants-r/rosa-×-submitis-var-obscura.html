Rosa × submitis var. obscura

Rosa × submitis var. obscura, commonly known as the dark rose or obscura rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its deep, dark pink to almost black flowers.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves, typical of many rose species. The flowers are usually borne singly or in small clusters, and they emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking color and resilience.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
