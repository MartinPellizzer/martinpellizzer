Ranunculus kvistii

Ranunculus kvistii, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often covered in fine hairs.

Ranunculus kvistii is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season, flowering early in the spring.
