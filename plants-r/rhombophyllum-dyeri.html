Rhombophyllum dyeri

Rhombophyllum dyeri, commonly known as the silver leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem covered in pale, almost silver-colored tubercles. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
