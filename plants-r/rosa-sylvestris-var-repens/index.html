Rosa Sylvestris Var. Repens

Rosa sylvestris var. repens is a low-growing, sprawling rose known for its delicate pink flowers and vigorous growth habit. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, this variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its creeping stems allow it to form dense mats, making it an excellent ground cover in gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, it holds historical and ornamental value among horticultural enthusiasts. This plant's resilience and beauty make it a unique addition to both wild and cultivated environments.
