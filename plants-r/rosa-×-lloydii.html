Rosa × lloydii

Rosa × lloydii, commonly known as the Lloyd’s rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × lloydii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
