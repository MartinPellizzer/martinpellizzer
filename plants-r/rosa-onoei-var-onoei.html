Rosa onoei var. onoei

Rosa onoei var. onoei, commonly known as the Japanese rose or Onoe rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a dense, ornamental foliage. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its floral display.
