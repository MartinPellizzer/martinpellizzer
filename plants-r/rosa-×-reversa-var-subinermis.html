Rosa × reversa var. subinermis

Rosa × reversa var. subinermis, commonly known as the pink musk rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its unique floral structure and fragrance.

It features double blooms with a high number of petals, often displaying a soft pink to white coloration. The plant is known for its strong, musky scent, which is more pronounced than that of many other rose varieties. It typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and aroma.
