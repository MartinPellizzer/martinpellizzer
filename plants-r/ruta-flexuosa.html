Ruta flexuosa

Ruta flexuosa, commonly known as flexible rue, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features aromatic, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive, slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its medicinal properties have been historically valued for their antispasmodic and analgesic effects.

Ruta flexuosa thrives in well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded environments.
