Ruellia Scarlatina

Ruellia scarlatina is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico. Known for its vibrant red blooms and lush green foliage, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden or landscape. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its bold appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both visual impact and hardiness. With proper care, Ruellia scarlatina can thrive in a variety of conditions, making it a versatile addition to both wild and cultivated environments.
