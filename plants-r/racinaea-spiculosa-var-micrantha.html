Racinaea spiculosa var. micrantha

Racinaea spiculosa var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and a striking inflorescence that consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant is native to the wetlands and marshy areas of Central and South America. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquatic and bog gardens.
