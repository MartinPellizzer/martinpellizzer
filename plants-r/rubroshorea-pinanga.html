Rubroshorea pinanga

Rubroshorea pinanga, commonly known as the redshore flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in tropical regions.
