Rosa wichurana f. rosiflora

Rosa wichurana f. rosiflora, commonly known as the Rosy Floribunda rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering.

It is native to South America, specifically Argentina, and is known for its vibrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that range in color from soft pinks to deep reds. The plant typically produces clusters of flowers, often with a sweet, spicy fragrance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
