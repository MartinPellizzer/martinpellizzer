Rubus × pseudorosellus

Rubus × pseudorosellus, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rubus ursinus (the raspberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (the cloudberry). This plant is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in wet, acidic soils in forested areas.
