Rebutia glomeriseta

Rebutia glomeriseta, commonly known as the clustered rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem covered in dense clusters of small, tubercles, which give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in the spring. Its succulent leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Rebutia glomeriseta is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
