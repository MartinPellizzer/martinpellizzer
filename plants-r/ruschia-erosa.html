Ruschia erosa

Ruschia erosa, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
