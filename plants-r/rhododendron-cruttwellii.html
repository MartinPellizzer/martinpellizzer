Rhododendron cruttwellii

Rhododendron cruttwellii, commonly known as the Cruttwell's rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high elevations, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves and produces dense clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers in spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with acidic soil and partial shade.

Rhododendron cruttwellii is also notable for its role in the local ecosystems of its native habitat.
