Rhynchosia clivorum var. caudata

Rhynchosia clivorum var. caudata, commonly known as the long-tailed cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with elongated leaflets and produces striking violet to purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and distinctive, elongated pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used as a forage crop or ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for ecological restoration and soil enrichment.
