Rosularia × tadzhikistana

Rosularia × tadzhikistana, commonly known as the Tadjikistan rosebay, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and produces showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its leaves are usually covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This species is native to Central Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
