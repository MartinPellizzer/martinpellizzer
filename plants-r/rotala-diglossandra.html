Rotala diglossandra

Rotala diglossandra, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a semi-aquatic species native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has slender, branching stems that often trail or float on the water's surface. Its leaves are oval-shaped and slightly pointed, with a smooth texture.

Rotala diglossandra is popular in aquariums and water gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist environments.
