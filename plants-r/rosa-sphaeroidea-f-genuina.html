Rosa sphaeroidea f. genuina

Rosa sphaeroidea f. genuina, commonly known as the globeflower rose, is a hybrid form of the globeflower (Rosa sphaeroidea).

It is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shaded areas.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
