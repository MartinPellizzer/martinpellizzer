Randia celastroidea

Randia celastroidea, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Randia celastroidea is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
