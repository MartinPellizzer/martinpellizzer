Ruschia fenestrata

Ruschia fenestrata, commonly known as the window leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy leaves with distinctive window-like holes, which give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
