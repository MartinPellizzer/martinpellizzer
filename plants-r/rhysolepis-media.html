Rhysolepis media

Rhysolepis media, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, compact growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant in coastal and arid environments.

Rhysolepis media is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
