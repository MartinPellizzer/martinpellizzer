Rhododendron guizhouense

Rhododendron guizhouense, commonly known as the Guizhou rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Guizhou Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are usually dark green and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from pink to red, and they bloom in early spring.

The plant is well adapted to the cool, humid climate of its native habitat and is often found in forested areas.
