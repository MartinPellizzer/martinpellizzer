Ramium elongatum

Ramium elongatum, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Ramium elongatum is often found in moist, grassy areas and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
