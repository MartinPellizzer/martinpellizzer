Rohdea annulata

Rohdea annulata, commonly known as the golden bamboo or Japanese bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green, giving it a tropical appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
