Rosa × andegavensis var. squarrosidens

Rosa × andegavensis var. squarrosidens, commonly known as the Andegavensis squarrosidens rose, is a hybrid species native to the United States, particularly in the southeastern region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
