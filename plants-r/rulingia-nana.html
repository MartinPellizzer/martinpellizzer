Rulingia nana

Rulingia nana, commonly known as the dwarf rulingia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its compact size makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Rulingia nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded to partially sunny conditions.
