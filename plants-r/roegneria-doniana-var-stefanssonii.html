Roegneria doniana var. stefanssonii

Roegneria doniana var. stefanssonii, commonly known as the Stefansson's timothy grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing, tufted habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in cold, nutrient-poor environments, often found in tundra and alpine habitats.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, making it an important component of polar ecosystems.
