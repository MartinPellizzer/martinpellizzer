Rehmannia rupestris

Rehmannia rupestris, commonly known as the rock rehmannia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, preferring well-drained environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, pale yellow, and clustered in racemes, blooming in late summer.

Rehmannia rupestris is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
