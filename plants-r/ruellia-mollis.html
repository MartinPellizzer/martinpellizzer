Ruellia mollis

Ruellia mollis, commonly known as soft ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are softly hairy and have a velvety texture. Its flowers are trumpet-shaped, with five petals, and are usually blue or purple in color.

Ruellia mollis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
