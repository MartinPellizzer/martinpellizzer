Rubus scaber f. ellipticus

Rubus scaber f. ellipticus, commonly known as the rough raspberry.

It is a variant of the common raspberry, characterized by its elliptical leaves and slightly prickly stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along the edges of gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
