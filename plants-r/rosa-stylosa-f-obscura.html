Rosa stylosa f. obscura

Rosa stylosa f. obscura, commonly known as the dark-stemmed shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white. The plant is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in partial to full sun. Its dark stems and foliage give it a distinctive appearance, making it a unique addition to gardens.

It is relatively low maintenance and suitable for USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8.
