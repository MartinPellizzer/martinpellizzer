Rebutia dasyphrissa

Rebutia dasyphrissa, commonly known as the Woolly Rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It features compact, rounded stems that are covered in dense, woolly white or grayish hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Rebutia dasyphrissa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
