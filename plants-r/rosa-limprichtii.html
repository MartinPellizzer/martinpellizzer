Rosa limprichtii

Rosa limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants.

Rosa limprichtii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
