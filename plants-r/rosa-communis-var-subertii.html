Rosa communis var. subertii

Rosa communis var. subertii, commonly known as the Subertii rose, is a rare and ornamental variant of the common rose.

It is characterized by its deep pink to crimson flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, aromatic foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens with adequate space.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a cultivar of interest in botanical collections.
