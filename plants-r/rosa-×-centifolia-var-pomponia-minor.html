Rosa × centifolia var. pomponia-minor

Rosa × centifolia var. pomponia-minor, commonly known as the miniature pink hybrid tea rose, is a compact variety of the Centifolia rose, known for its small, double blooms.

It features a bushy growth habit with dark green, glossy foliage and produces fragrant, pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms. It is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

The plant is often used in landscape design for its compact size and continuous flowering.
