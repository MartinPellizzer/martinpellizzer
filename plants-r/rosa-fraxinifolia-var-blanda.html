Rosa fraxinifolia var. blanda

Rosa fraxinifolia var. blanda, commonly known as the softleaf shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features pale yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance, blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are finely divided, giving the plant a delicate, fern-like appearance. It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
