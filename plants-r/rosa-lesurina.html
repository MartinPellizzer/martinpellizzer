Rosa lesurina

Rosa lesurina, commonly known as the Lesurina rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Rosa lesurina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
