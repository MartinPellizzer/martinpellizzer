Rosa reuteri var. coriifolia

Rosa reuteri var. coriifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salt spray and well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
