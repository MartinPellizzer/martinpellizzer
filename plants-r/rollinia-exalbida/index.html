Rollinia Exalbida

Rollinia exalbida is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is known for its large, waxy leaves that shimmer with a unique iridescent sheen. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. Its fruit, a large, spiky capsule, contains edible seeds that are prized in local cuisines. Despite its beauty, Rollinia exalbida remains relatively unknown to the wider botanical community.
