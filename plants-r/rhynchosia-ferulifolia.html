Rhynchosia ferulifolia

Rhynchosia ferulifolia, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while the stems are slender and branched.
