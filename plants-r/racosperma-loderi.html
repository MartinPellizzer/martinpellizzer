Racosperma loderi

Racosperma loderi, commonly known as the Loder's acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters, which are typical of many acacia species.

Racosperma loderi is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
