Rupicapnos africana var. colorata

Rupicapnos africana var. colorata, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
