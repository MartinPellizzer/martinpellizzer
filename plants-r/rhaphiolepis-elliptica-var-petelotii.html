Rhaphiolepis elliptica var. petelotii

Rhaphiolepis elliptica var. petelotii, commonly known as the pink-flowered boxwood, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy texture and produces clusters of small, pink flowers in the spring. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it suitable for topiary and formal garden designs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring minimal maintenance once established.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
