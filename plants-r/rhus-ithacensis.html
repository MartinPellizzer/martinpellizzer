Rhus ithacensis

Rhus ithacensis, commonly known as the Ithaca buckthorn, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other plants like poison ivy and mango. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture.
