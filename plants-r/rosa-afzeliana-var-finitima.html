Rosa afzeliana var. finitima

Rosa afzeliana var. finitima, commonly known as the African rose or the Cape rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping and horticultural use.
