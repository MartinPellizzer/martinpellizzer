Ranunculus prolixidens

Ranunculus prolixidens, commonly known as the long-stemmed buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers with numerous petals arranged in a cup-like shape. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and thrives in partial to full sun. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often have a hairy texture.

Ranunculus prolixidens is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
