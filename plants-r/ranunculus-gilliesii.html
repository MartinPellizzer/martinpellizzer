Ranunculus gilliesii

Ranunculus gilliesii, commonly known as the Stewart's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers bloom from late winter to early spring.

Ranunculus gilliesii is often found in the South Island of New Zealand and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
