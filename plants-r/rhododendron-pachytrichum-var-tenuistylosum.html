Rhododendron pachytrichum var. tenuistylosum

Rhododendron pachytrichum var. tenuistylosum, commonly known as the narrow-styled rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
