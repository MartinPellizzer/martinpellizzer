Rosa micrantha var. gremlii

Rosa micrantha var. gremlii, commonly known as the Gremli's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range from white to light pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves that provide dense foliage. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient choice for gardens in similar conditions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
