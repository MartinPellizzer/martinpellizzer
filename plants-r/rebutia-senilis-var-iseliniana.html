Rebutia senilis var. iseliniana

Rebutia senilis var. iseliniana, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a low-growing, spineless cactus native to South America.

It features a compact, rounded shape with a smooth, pale green to bluish-green stem. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
