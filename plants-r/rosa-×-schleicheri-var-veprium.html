Rosa × schleicheri var. veprium

Rosa × schleicheri var. veprium, commonly known as the Veprium rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
