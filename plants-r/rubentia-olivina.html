Rubentia olivina

Rubentia olivina, commonly known as the olive-backed sunbird.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly in regions with tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Rubentia olivina is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
