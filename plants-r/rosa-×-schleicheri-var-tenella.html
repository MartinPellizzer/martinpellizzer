Rosa × schleicheri var. tenella

Rosa × schleicheri var. tenella, commonly known as the miniature everblooming rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with dense, thornless growth.

It produces abundant clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white throughout the growing season. The plant is notable for its ability to bloom continuously from late spring to early autumn. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for the blooms.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces due to its compact size and ornamental value.
