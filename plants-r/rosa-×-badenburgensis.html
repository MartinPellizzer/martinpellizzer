Rosa × badenburgensis

Rosa × badenburgensis, commonly known as the Badenburg rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is a deciduous shrub, known for its robust growth and thorny stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features include compound leaves with serrated edges and showy blooms that attract pollinators.
