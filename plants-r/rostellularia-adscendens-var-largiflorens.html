Rostellularia adscendens var. largiflorens

Rostellularia adscendens var. largiflorens, commonly known as the large-flowered rostellularia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, tubular flowers. The flowers are typically purple or violet in color and are arranged in dense, terminal clusters. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
