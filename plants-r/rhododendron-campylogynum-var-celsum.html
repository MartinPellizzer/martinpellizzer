Rhododendron campylogynum var. celsum

Rhododendron campylogynum var. celsum, commonly known as the Celestial Rhododendron, is a unique variant of the campylogynum species.

It is characterized by its striking, deep purple to almost black flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of southern China and is adapted to cool, humid climates. Its foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens and botanical collections.
