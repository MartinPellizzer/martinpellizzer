Rhynchosia caribaea var. picta

Rhynchosia caribaea var. picta, commonly known as the painted snout bean, is a tropical legume native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with contrasting patterns. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible seeds.
