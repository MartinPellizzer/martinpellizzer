Rubus amplithyrsus

Rubus amplithyrsus, commonly known as the broad-leaved blackberry.

It is a species of shrub belonging to the Rubus genus in the Rosaceae family. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and thorny stems, typical of many brambles. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color.

This plant is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, wooded areas.
