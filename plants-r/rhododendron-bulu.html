Rhododendron bulu

Rhododendron bulu, commonly known as the Bornean rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and thrives in acidic soils. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from pink to red.

Rhododendron bulu is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
