Ranunculus chinghoensis

Ranunculus chinghoensis, commonly known as the Chingho buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, typically yellow or white petals. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in spring. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
