Rubus menglaensis

Rubus menglaensis, commonly known as the Mengla blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, dark purple to black berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The berries are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese cuisine and medicine.
