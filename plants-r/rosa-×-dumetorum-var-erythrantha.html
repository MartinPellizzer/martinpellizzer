Rosa × dumetorum var. erythrantha

Rosa × dumetorum var. erythrantha, commonly known as the red-stemmed dog rose, is a hybrid shrub native to western Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are pinnately compound, with oval to lance-shaped leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs. This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hedging and ornamental gardening.
