Rhodiola namlingensis

Rhodiola namlingensis, commonly known as the Namling rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Tibet.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. This plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to harsh alpine conditions. It is often found growing at elevations above 3,500 meters.
