Rumex × attenuatus

Rumex × attenuatus, commonly known as the broad-leaved dock, is a hybrid plant in the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, and it is typically found in moist, disturbed soils. Rumex × attenuatus is a perennial species that can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
