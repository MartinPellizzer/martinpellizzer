Rebutia inflexiseta

Rebutia inflexiseta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved burro's tail, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rounded stems covered in dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
