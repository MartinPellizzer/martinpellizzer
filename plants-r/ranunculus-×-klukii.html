Ranunculus × klukii

Ranunculus × klukii, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its showy, double-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, featuring finely divided, fern-like leaves that are alternate along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Ranunculus × klukii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
