Rubus gongshanensis var. gongshanensis

Rubus gongshanensis var. gongshanensis, commonly known as Gongshan blackberry, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested when fully ripe. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in local agriculture.
