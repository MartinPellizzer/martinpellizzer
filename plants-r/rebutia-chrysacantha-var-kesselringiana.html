Rebutia chrysacantha var. kesselringiana

Rebutia chrysacantha var. kesselringiana, commonly known as the Kesselringiana Rebutia, is a succulent native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant features short, cylindrical stems covered in small, spiny tubercles. Its flowers are typically borne in the axils of the stems and open in a cup-like shape.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in well-draining soil and full sun.
