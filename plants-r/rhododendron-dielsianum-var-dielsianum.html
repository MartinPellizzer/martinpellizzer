Rhododendron dielsianum var. dielsianum

Rhododendron dielsianum var. dielsianum, commonly known as the Diels' rhododendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this species thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and cool, moist climates. The plant is often found in forest understories and rocky habitats at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture, though it requires specific care to flourish.
