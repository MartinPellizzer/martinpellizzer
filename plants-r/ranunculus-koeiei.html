Ranunculus koeiei

Ranunculus koeiei, commonly known as the Japanese buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers with 5 to 15 petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a short, stout stem with trifoliate leaves that are deeply lobed. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ranunculus koeiei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
