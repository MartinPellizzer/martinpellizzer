Restrepia reichenbachiana

Restrepia reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's restia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and it has linear, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Restrepia reichenbachiana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
