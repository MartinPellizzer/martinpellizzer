Ruta corsica

Ruta corsica, commonly known as Corsican Rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, lobed leaves that are aromatic and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into narrow, winged fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
