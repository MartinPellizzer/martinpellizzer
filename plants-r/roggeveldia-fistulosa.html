Roggeveldia fistulosa

Roggeveldia fistulosa, commonly known as the Roggeveld lily, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped, bright green leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
