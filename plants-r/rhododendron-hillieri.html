Rhododendron hillieri

Rhododendron hillieri, commonly known as the hillieri rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cool, humid climates.
