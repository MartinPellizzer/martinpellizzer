Rosa × axmannii

Rosa × axmannii, commonly known as the Axmann's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its semi-evergreen to deciduous shrub form, with thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

Rosa × axmannii is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to a variety of soil conditions.
