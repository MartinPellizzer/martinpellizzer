Rhacoma managuatillo

Rhacoma managuatillo, commonly known as the Managua fig.

It is a tropical plant native to Central America, particularly found in the humid lowland areas of Nicaragua. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are often consumed fresh or used in local cuisine. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture and a mild, aromatic scent.

Rhacoma managuatillo thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, sunny climate to flourish.
