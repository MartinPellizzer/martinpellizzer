Rubus divexiramus var. carneus

Rubus divexiramus var. carneus, commonly known as the red-flowered raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its distinctive red flowers and thick, fleshy fruits. It is a shrub native to specific regions in Asia, typically found in forested areas. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces a strong, aromatic scent.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species in horticulture.
