Rosa hirtifolia var. gracilenta

Rosa hirtifolia var. gracilenta, commonly known as the slender hairy rose, is a shrub-like species native to parts of Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a slight blush. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming.
