Ruschia geminiflora

Ruschia geminiflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It blooms during the summer months and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
