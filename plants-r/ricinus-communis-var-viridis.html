Ricinus communis var. viridis

Ricinus communis var. viridis, commonly known as the green castor oil plant, is a variant of the castor oil plant characterized by its predominantly green foliage.

It is a flowering plant in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae, and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The leaves are large, palmately lobed, and often have a velvety texture. It produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences.
