Ruellia tubiflora

Ruellia tubiflora, commonly known as the redstem ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of tubular, blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ruellia tubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
