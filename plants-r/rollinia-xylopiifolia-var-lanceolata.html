Rollinia xylopiifolia var. lanceolata

Rollinia xylopiifolia var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate rollinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, sweet-tart fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit-bearing potential, it is cultivated in subtropical and tropical regions worldwide.
