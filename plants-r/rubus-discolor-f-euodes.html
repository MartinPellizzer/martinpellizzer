Rubus discolor f. euodes

Rubus discolor f. euodes, commonly known as the fragrant raspberry.

This plant is a variant of the common raspberry, characterized by its distinct fragrance and slightly different growth habits. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with a serrated edge, and the flowers are pinkish-white in color.

The fruit, while edible, is generally smaller and less tart compared to the standard raspberry.
