Rotala anamika

Rotala anamika, commonly known as the dwarf rotala, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically bright green, often with a slight red or purple hue on the underside. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is well-suited for aquariums due to its compact growth habit. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Rotala anamika is often used in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
