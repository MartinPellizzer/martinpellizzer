Rumohra berteroana

Rumohra berteroana, commonly known as the grass tree or *Punga*, is a distinctive plant native to the Pacific Islands, including Hawaii, Tahiti, and other parts of Oceania.

The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts.
