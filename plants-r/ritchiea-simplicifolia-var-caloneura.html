Ritchiea simplicifolia var. caloneura

Ritchiea simplicifolia var. caloneura, commonly known as the slender-leafed cycad, is a unique variant of the cycad species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, fan-like pattern. The plant produces large, cone-like structures, with the male and female cones developing on separate plants. Its slow growth rate and preference for well-drained, acidic soils make it a challenging but rewarding specimen for cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
