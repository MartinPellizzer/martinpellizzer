Rubus retrospiciens

Rubus retrospiciens, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as berries, which are typically red when mature. These berries are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in culinary applications.

Rubus retrospiciens thrives in temperate climates and is native to parts of North America.
