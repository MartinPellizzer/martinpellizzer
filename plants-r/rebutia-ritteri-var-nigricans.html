Rebutia ritteri var. nigricans

Rebutia ritteri var. nigricans, commonly known as the Black Night Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green with dark purple or black markings, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique coloration and compact size, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
