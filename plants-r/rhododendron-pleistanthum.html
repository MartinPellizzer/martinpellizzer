Rhododendron pleistanthum

Rhododendron pleistanthum, commonly known as the broadleaf rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features dense, dark green leaves that are broad and ovate, often with a waxy surface. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
