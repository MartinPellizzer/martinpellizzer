Rubus × floricomus

Rubus × floricomus, commonly known as the hybrid raspberry or ornamental raspberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus idaeus (red raspberry) and Rubus strigosus (blackberry), resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that range in color from red to black, depending on the parent species. Its foliage is typically compound, with serrated leaves that provide a textured appearance.

Rubus × floricomus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits, thriving in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
