Rubus schlechtendahlii

Rubus schlechtendahlii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal blackberry, is a species of bramble native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are typically harvested when fully ripe. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rubus genus, known for its ornamental and culinary potential.
