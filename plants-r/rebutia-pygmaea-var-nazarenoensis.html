Rebutia pygmaea var. nazarenoensis

Rebutia pygmaea var. nazarenoensis, commonly known as the Nazarenoensis Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in short, cylindrical spines and small, rounded areoles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
