Rosa Lutetiana Var. Dumalis

Rosa lutetiana var. dumalis is a rare and intriguing rose species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This variant is known for its unique floral characteristics and hardy nature, making it a subject of interest among botanists and horticulturists. Its flowers display a striking blend of colors, often featuring a deep red hue with soft pink accents. The plant thrives in cooler climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited distribution, conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival in the wild.
