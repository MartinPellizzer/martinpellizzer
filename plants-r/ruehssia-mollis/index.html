Ruehssia Mollis

Ruehssia mollis is a unique plant known for its striking appearance and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. This evergreen shrub features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that add a modern touch to any landscape. Its clusters of small, fragrant white flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators and adding visual interest. Despite its beauty, Ruehssia mollis is relatively low maintenance, making it an excellent option for gardeners of all skill levels.
