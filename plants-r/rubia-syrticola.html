Rubia syrticola

Rubia syrticola, commonly known as the Syrticola borage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a splash of color to its habitat. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to the arid environments it inhabits.

Rubia syrticola is often found in rocky soils and open areas, where it thrives in full sun.
