Rubus validus

Rubus validus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a perennial shrub native to North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as berries, which are typically bright red when mature. These berries are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in jams and teas.

Rubus validus thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in woodland edges and disturbed areas.
