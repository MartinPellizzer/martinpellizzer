Ruschia elevata

Ruschia elevata, commonly known as the mountain ruschia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its succulent characteristics. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring.
