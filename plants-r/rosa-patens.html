Rosa patens

Rosa patens, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its continuous blooming throughout the growing season. The plant produces fragrant, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

Rosa patens is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
