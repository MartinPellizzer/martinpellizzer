Rhodophiala soratensis

Rhodophiala soratensis, commonly known as the Sorato lily, is a member of the Alstroemeriaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and a flowering stem that bears a single, large, bell-shaped bloom. Its botanical characteristics make it a sought-after species for botanical gardens and horticultural collections.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.
