Rubus phyllostachys var. productus

Rubus phyllostachys var. productus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, red berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has serrated, alternate leaves. Its botanical features include a woody structure with dense foliage and a distinctive flowering pattern.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruits, though caution is advised due to its thorny nature.
