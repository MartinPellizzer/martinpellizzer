Ruschia floribunda

Ruschia floribunda, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It blooms in spring and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Ruschia floribunda thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
