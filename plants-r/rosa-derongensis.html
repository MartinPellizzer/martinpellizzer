Rosa derongensis

Rosa derongensis, commonly known as the Dungan rose, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals.

It produces aggregate fruits called rosehips, which are rich in vitamin C. Rosa derongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for both horticultural and medicinal purposes.
