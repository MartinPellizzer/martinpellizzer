Rosa lanceolata

Rosa lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of eastern and southern Africa, where it thrives in woodland and shrubland habitats. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with five petals and numerous stamens, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rosa lanceolata is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand various environmental conditions.
