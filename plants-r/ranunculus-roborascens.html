Ranunculus roborascens

Ranunculus roborascens, commonly known as the tall buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to Europe and western Asia, often found in moist meadows and along stream banks. The plant features upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, supporting large, showy yellow flowers with 5-10 sepals. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
