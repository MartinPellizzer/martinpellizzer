Rubia olivieri

Rubia olivieri, commonly known as the olive ropa or olive balm, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.

Rubia olivieri thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
