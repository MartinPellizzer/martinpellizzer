Rhynchospora japonica var. curvoaristata

Rhynchospora japonica var. curvoaristata, commonly known as the curved-awned spikegrass, is a perennial herb native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a cylindrical spike that bears small, three-lobed flowers.

This variety is often found in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils.
