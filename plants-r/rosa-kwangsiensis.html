Rosa kwangsiensis

Rosa kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces rose hips that are orange-red in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa kwangsiensis is also valued for its historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
