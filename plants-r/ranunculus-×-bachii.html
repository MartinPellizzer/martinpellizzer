Ranunculus × bachii

Ranunculus × bachii, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers in a range of colors including yellow, orange, and red, often with a dark central spot. The plant has palmately divided leaves that are typically deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens.
