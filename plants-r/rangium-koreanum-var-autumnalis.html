Rangium koreanum var. autumnalis

Rangium koreanum var. autumnalis, commonly known as the Korean autumn bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in the autumn season. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its seasonal bloom and compact growth habit.
