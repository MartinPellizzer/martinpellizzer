Rhamnus heldreichii var. ancyrensis

Rhamnus heldreichii var. ancyrensis, commonly known as the Ancyrense Privet, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
