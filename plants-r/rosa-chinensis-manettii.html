Rosa chinensis manettii

Rosa chinensis manettii, commonly known as the Manettii rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, single or double flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white, during the spring and summer months. Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural cultivation.

The flowers are composed of multiple layers of petals and are often used in floral arrangements and perfumery.
