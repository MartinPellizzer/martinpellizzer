Roettlera zeylanica

Roettlera zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka and parts of southern India.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble the common star anise. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a strong, spicy aroma.

The fruit, which is harvested when ripe, is used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties.
