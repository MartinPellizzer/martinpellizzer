Rinorea gracilipes

Rinorea gracilipes, commonly known as the slender rinorea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a distinctive violet or purple hue.

Rinorea gracilipes is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
