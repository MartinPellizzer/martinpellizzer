Restio junceus

Restio junceus, commonly known as slender restio, is a grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in open vegetation types such as fynbos and renosterveld.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
