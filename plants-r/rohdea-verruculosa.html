Rohdea verruculosa

Rohdea verruculosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rosette of broad, leathery, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
