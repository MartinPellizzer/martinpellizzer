Racosperma crombiei

Racosperma crombiei, commonly known as the Crombie's acacia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a woody structure and adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
