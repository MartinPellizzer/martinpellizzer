Rottlera laccifera

Rottlera laccifera, commonly known as the Indian spurge or red spurge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its smooth, pale green stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. Rottlera laccifera is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
