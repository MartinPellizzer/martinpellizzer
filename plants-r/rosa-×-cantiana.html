Rosa × cantiana

Rosa × cantiana, commonly known as the Canada rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

Rosa × cantiana is also known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
