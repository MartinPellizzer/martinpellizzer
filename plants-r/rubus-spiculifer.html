Rubus spiculifer

Rubus spiculifer, commonly known as the spiny bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits, known as raspberries, are aggregate fruits formed from multiple drupes.

Rubus spiculifer is typically found in temperate regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
