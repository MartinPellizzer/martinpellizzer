Rebutia einsteinii f. steineckei

Rebutia einsteinii f. steineckei, commonly known as the Einstein's Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
