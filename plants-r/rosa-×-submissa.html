Rosa × submissa

Rosa × submissa, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, often with a high degree of variability in color. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, providing a lush backdrop to its blooms.

Rosa × submissa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
