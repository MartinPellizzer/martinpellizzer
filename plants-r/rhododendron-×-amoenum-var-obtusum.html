Rhododendron × amoenum var. obtusum

Rhododendron × amoenum var. obtusum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, often blooming in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal interest throughout the year.
