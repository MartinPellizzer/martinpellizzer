Rosa hybrida f. assurgens

Rosa hybrida f. assurgens, commonly known as the climbing rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its vigorous climbing habit.

It features large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has thorned stems and is often used in garden design for its ornamental value. Its botanical name reflects its hybrid origin and its tendency to ascend, making it suitable for trellises and arbors.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and flowering.
