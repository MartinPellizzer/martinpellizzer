Ruellia rotundifolia

Ruellia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streambanks. Ruellia rotundifolia is a perennial herb that can grow up to 1 meter in height.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in warm climates.
