Russelia hintonii

Russelia hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's russelia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms during the warmer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Russelia hintonii thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
