Rosa sphaeroidea var. gregaria

Rosa sphaeroidea var. gregaria, commonly known as the globular rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, rounded growth with small, glossy leaves and produces fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant is characterized by its unique, spherical shape, which is formed by the close clustering of its stems and foliage. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

The variety is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
