Rosa soulieana var. soulieana

Rosa soulieana var. soulieana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
