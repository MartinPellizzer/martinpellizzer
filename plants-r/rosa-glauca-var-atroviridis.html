Rosa glauca var. atroviridis

Rosa glauca var. atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green shrub rose, is a variant of the glaucous rose that exhibits distinct botanical features.

It is characterized by its dark green foliage, which contrasts with its pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with thorny stems and dense, aromatic leaves. Its flowers are usually small and borne in clusters, adding to its ornamental value.

This variety is native to specific regions in Europe and is valued for its hardiness and unique coloration.
