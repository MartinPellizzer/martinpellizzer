Rosa pimpinellifolia var. intermedia

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental addition to gardens in temperate climates.
