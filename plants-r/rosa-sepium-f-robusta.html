Rosa sepium f. robusta

Rosa sepium f. robusta, commonly known as the robust rose, is a horticultural form of the shrub rose.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is native to China and has been cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Rosa sepium f. robusta is well-suited for hedging and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
