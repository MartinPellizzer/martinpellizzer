Rosa trachyphylla var. jundzilliana

Rosa trachyphylla var. jundzilliana, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
