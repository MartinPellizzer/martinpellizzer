Rosa gentilis var. levipes

Rosa gentilis var. levipes, commonly known as the slender shrub rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces rose hips, which are the fruit containing the seeds. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is well-suited to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
