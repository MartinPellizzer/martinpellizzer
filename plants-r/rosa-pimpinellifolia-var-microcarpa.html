Rosa pimpinellifolia var. microcarpa

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. microcarpa, commonly known as the miniature rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand dry conditions. It is often found growing in rocky soils and sunny exposures.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
