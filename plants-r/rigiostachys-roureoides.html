Rigiostachys roureoides

Rigiostachys roureoides, commonly known as the Andean orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, thriving in cool, temperate climates. Its flowers are often brightly colored, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
