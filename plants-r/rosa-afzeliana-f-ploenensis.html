Rosa afzeliana f. ploenensis

Rosa afzeliana f. ploenensis, commonly known as the Ploenensis rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
