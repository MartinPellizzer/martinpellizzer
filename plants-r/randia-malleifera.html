Randia malleifera

Randia malleifera, commonly known as the Florida starfruit or mallow starfruit, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with hairy stems and palmate leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that give way to orange, star-shaped fruits.
