Rosa mollis f. delicatula

Rosa mollis f. delicatula, commonly known as the soft rose or delicate rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, which is native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thornless stems, making it suitable for ornamental gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, and it is relatively low maintenance.

This cultivar is valued for its aesthetic appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
