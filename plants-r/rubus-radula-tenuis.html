Rubus radula tenuis

Rubus radula tenuis, commonly known as the slender blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its trailing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It is native to regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile.

Due to its delicate growth habit, it is often found in moist, shaded environments.
