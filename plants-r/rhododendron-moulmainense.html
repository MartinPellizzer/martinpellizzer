Rhododendron moulmainense

Rhododendron moulmainense, commonly known as the Moulmain rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron moulmainense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
