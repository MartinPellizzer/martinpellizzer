Rebutia haagei var. elegantula

Rebutia haagei var. elegantula, commonly known as the Elegant Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem with a smooth, pale green surface covered in small, white to light yellow tubercles. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Rebutia haagei var. elegantula requires well-draining soil, bright sunlight, and minimal water to thrive.
