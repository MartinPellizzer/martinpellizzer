Ruta linearis

Ruta linearis, commonly known as linear ruta or narrowleaf rue, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and small, aromatic, gray-green leaves with a distinctive linear shape. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Ruta linearis is valued for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal in gardens.
