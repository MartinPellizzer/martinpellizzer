Rosa nitens var. subfirmula

Rosa nitens var. subfirmula, commonly known as the soft-stemmed evergreen rose, is a shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
