Ruschia festiva

Ruschia festiva, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adorned with white or purple markings. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
