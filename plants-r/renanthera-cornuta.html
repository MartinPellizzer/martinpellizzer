Renanthera cornuta

Renanthera cornuta, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated floral spikes that bear showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces vibrant, often pink or red blooms with a unique, horn-like structure at the base of the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Renanthera cornuta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
