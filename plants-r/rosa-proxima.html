Rosa proxima

Rosa proxima, commonly known as the near rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. Rosa proxima is native to regions of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrance.
