Rosa kerneri var. zoisaeana

Rosa kerneri var. zoisaeana, commonly known as the Zoisaeana rose, is a compact, bushy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant is well adapted to cool, dry climates and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both alpine and traditional rose gardens.
