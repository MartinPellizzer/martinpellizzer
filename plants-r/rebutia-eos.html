Rebutia eos

Rebutia eos, commonly known as the "Eos Cactus" or "Morning Glory Cactus," is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow to orange flowers that open in the morning, giving it its common name. Rebutia eos is a low-maintenance cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
