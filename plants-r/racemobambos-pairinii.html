Racemobambos pairinii

Racemobambos pairinii, commonly known as the Philippine bamboo orchid, is a unique plant that combines features of both bamboo and orchids.

The plant has a bamboo-like stem but produces delicate, orchid-like flowers in clusters. Its growth habit involves forming clumps, with long, hollow stems that can reach several meters in height.

The botanical interest of Racemobambos pairinii lies in its hybrid characteristics and its role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
