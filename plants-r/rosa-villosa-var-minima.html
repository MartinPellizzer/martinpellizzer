Rosa villosa var. minima

Rosa villosa var. minima, commonly known as the miniature evergreen rose, is a compact shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains evergreen throughout the year. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, decorative shrub.
