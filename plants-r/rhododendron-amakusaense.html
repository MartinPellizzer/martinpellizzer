Rhododendron amakusaense

Rhododendron amakusaense, commonly known as the Amakusa rhododendron, is a species native to the Amakusa Islands in Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color on the underside. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are typically pink to reddish in color, blooming in late spring.

The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
