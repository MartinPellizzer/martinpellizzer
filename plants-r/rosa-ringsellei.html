Rosa ringsellei

Rosa ringsellei, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada rose, is a species native to the western United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky soils at high elevations. The plant features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in thorny prickles.

Rosa ringsellei is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
