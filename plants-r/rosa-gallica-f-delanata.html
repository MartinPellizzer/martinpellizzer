Rosa gallica f. delanata

Rosa gallica f. delanata, commonly known as the French pink or Delanata rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa gallica.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This rose is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery and gardening.
