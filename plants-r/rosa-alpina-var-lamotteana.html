Rosa alpina var. lamotteana

Rosa alpina var. lamotteana, commonly known as the Alpine rose or Lamotte's rose, is a variant of the alpine rose that thrives in high-altitude environments.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves adapted to cold climates. It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and prefers well-drained, rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in alpine gardens.
