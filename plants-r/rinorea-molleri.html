Rinorea molleri

Rinorea molleri, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Rinorea molleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
