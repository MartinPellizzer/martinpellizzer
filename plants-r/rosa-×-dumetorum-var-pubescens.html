Rosa × dumetorum var. pubescens

Rosa × dumetorum var. pubescens, commonly known as the downy rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its pubescent (hairy) stems and foliage, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is native to Europe and is often found in woodland edges and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
