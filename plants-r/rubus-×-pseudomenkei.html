Rubus × pseudomenkei

Rubus × pseudomenkei, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus lacrima-floris (currant). The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces aggregate fruits that range from dark purple to black in color.

This species is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
