Rhynchodia slootenii

Rhynchodia slootenii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Rhynchodia slootenii is adapted to arid environments, featuring succulent leaves that store water.
