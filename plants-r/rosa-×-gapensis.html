Rosa × gapensis

Rosa × gapensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has woody stems with thorny branches and dark green, serrated leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × gapensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
