Rosa uncinella var. heterotricha

Rosa uncinella var. heterotricha, commonly known as the hairy rose, is a variant of the prickly rose native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and winter-hardiness.
