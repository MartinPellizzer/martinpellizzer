Russelia glandulifera

Russelia glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves, which are covered in glandular structures. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Russelia glandulifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its showy blooms and ease of care.
