Rosa helleri

Rosa helleri, commonly known as the Heller's rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features arching stems with dark green, serrated leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Rosa helleri is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
