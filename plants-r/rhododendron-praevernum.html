Rhododendron praevernum

Rhododendron praevernum, commonly known as the purple-flowered rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, often with a glossy appearance.

Rhododendron praevernum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
