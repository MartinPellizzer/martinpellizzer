Ruellia chartacea

Ruellia chartacea, commonly known as the slender ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tubular and come in shades of blue, purple, or white, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Ruellia chartacea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
