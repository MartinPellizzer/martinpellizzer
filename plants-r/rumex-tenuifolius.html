Rumex tenuifolius

Rumex tenuifolius, commonly known as narrowleaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, slender stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist soils and is often found along stream banks and in wet meadows.

Narrowleaf dock is known for its medicinal uses and is also considered a weed in agricultural settings.
