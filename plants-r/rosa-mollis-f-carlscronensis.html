Rosa mollis f. carlscronensis

Rosa mollis f. carlscronensis, commonly known as the Carlscronensis rose, is a compact, floriferous shrub native to Sweden.

It features dark green, glossy foliage and produces abundant clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has a low-growing habit with thorny stems and is well-suited for borders or containers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
