Rauvolfia Major

Rauvolfia major is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its calming effects. Its alkaloid content, including reserpine, has made it significant in modern pharmacology.
