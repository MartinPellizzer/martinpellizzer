Rosa grenieri

Rosa grenieri, commonly known as the Grenada rose, is a species of shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a woody stem structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in tropical and subtropical climates.

Rosa grenieri is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery.
