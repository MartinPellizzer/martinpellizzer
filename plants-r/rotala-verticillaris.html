Rotala verticillaris

Rotala verticillaris, commonly known as the spiked waterwheel, is a versatile aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features whorled leaves that are typically bright green and can be submerged or floating, depending on the water conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged, followed by distinctive, spiky fruits. It is widely used in pond and aquarium settings for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Rotala verticillaris thrives in warm, nutrient-rich waters and is relatively easy to maintain in aquatic environments.
