Rubus × ciuriensis

Rubus × ciuriensis, commonly known as the blackberry-loganberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus loganobaccus (loganberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are pink to white, and it produces edible berries that are larger and sweeter than its parent species.

This hybrid is valued for its fruit quality and is often cultivated for commercial and culinary purposes.
