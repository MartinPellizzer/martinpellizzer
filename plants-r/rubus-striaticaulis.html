Rubus striaticaulis

Rubus striaticaulis, commonly known as the striate bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits called drupes, which are typically red or black in color. It is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in forested or shaded areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
