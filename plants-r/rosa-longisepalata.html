Rosa longisepalata

Rosa longisepalata, commonly known as the long-sepal rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant features fragrant, pink to red petals and numerous stamens, giving it a lush, decorative appearance. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa longisepalata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
