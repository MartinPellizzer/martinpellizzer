Rosa rufula

Rosa rufula, commonly known as the rough rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in woodland and scrub habitats. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Rosa rufula produces hips, which are the fruit of the plant and contain seeds encased in a fuzzy, red outer layer.
