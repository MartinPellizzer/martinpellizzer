Rubus × iasiensis

Rubus × iasiensis, commonly known as the Iasi raspberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many Rubus species. The plant produces aggregate fruits, commonly referred to as raspberries, which are typically red but can also be yellow or orange in color. It is native to eastern Europe and is often found in woodland areas and along forest edges.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and for commercial purposes.
