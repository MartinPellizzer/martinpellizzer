Rosa × dumetorum subvar. brachtii

Rosa × dumetorum subvar. brachtii, commonly known as the Bracht's shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

This subvariety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
