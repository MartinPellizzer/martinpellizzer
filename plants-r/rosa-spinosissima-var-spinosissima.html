Rosa spinosissima var. spinosissima

Rosa spinosissima var. spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny rose, is a shrub-like species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
