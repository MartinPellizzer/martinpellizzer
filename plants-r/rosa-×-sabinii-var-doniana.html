Rosa × sabinii var. doniana

Rosa × sabinii var. doniana, commonly known as the Doniana rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and vigorous flowering.

It produces semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The variety is prized for its ornamental value and historical significance in rose breeding programs.
