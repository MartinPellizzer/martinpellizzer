Rhynchospora macrostachya var. patula

Rhynchospora macrostachya var. patula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved beakspur, is a flowering plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically brown or reddish in color.
