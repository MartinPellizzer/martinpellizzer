Rosa × spinulifolia var. grandifolia

Rosa × spinulifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered bristly rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its robust growth and showy, fragrant flowers.

It typically features deep red to pink blooms that are large and often double, with a velvety texture. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that are covered in a fine layer of down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
