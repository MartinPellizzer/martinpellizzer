Rosa × brueggeri

Rosa × brueggeri, commonly known as the Brueggeri rose, is a hybrid shrub rose originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of pink to red. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × brueggeri thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.
