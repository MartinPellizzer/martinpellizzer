Rubroshorea pubistyla

Rubroshorea pubistyla, commonly known as the redshore flamebush, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
