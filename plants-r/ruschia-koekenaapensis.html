Ruschia koekenaapensis

Ruschia koekenaapensis, commonly known as the koekenaap or "cookie goat," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
