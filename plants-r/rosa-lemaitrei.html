Rosa lemaitrei

Rosa lemaitrei, commonly known as the Madagascar pink rose, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

The flowers bloom in spring and are known for their delicate, velvety texture.
