Rebutia senilis subsp. chrysacantha

Rebutia senilis subsp.

chrysacantha, commonly known as the golden rebutia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features compact, rounded stems that are typically green with a slight bluish tint.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months, which are highly attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are fleshy and cylindrical, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
