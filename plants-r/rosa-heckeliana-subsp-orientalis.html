Rosa heckeliana subsp. orientalis

Rosa heckeliana subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Hecker's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family. It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan.

This subspecies is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
