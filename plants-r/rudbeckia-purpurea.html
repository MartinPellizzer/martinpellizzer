Rudbeckia purpurea

Rudbeckia purpurea, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and purple to red petals. The plant has lance-shaped, green leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Rudbeckia purpurea is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
