Radermachera glandulosa

Radermachera glandulosa, commonly known as the glossy shield fern, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
