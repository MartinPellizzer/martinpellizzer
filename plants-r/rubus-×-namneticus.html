Rubus × namneticus

Rubus × namneticus, commonly known as the blackberry-bush hybrid, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, and it produces berries that range from red to black in color.

This hybrid is often found in moist, acidic soils in temperate regions.
