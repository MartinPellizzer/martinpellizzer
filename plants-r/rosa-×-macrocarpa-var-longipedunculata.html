Rosa × macrocarpa var. longipedunculata

Rosa × macrocarpa var. longipedunculata, commonly known as the large-flowered shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose varieties. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
