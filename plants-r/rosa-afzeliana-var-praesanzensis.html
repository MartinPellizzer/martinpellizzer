Rosa afzeliana var. praesanzensis

Rosa afzeliana var. praesanzensis, commonly known as the Prasanzens rose, is a shrub-like plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
