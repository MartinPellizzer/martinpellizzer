Rebutia gonjianii

Rebutia gonjianii, commonly known as the Gonji's moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is a low-growing, compact cactus with a short, thick stem and a dense covering of spines. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring or early summer. Its small size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Rebutia gonjianii is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate sunlight.
