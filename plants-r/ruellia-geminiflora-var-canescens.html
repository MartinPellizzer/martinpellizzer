Ruellia geminiflora var. canescens

Ruellia geminiflora var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery-white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom from spring to summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
