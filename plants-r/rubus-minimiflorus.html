Rubus minimiflorus

Rubus minimiflorus, commonly known as the dwarf flowered blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of western North America. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit, a small, tart berry, is typically consumed by wildlife rather than humans.

The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus.
