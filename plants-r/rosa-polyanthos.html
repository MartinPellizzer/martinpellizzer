Rosa polyanthos

Rosa polyanthos, commonly known as the polyantha rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and abundant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and is well-suited for hedging or ornamental gardens. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and alternate, while its flowers come in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white.

Rosa polyanthos is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
