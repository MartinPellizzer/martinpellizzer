Renanthera annamensis

Renanthera annamensis, commonly known as the Annam orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant red or orange petals. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor, and it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

The Annam orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses.
