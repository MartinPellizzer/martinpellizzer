Rapanea runssorica

Rapanea runssorica, commonly known as the red-leafed evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in color during the dry season. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in tropical regions.
