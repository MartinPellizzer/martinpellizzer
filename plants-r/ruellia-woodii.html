Ruellia woodii

Ruellia woodii, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue to violet flowers in the spring. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Ruellia woodii thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
