Rosa × Cottetii Var. Aberrans

Rosa × cottetii var. aberrans is a rare and intriguing hybrid rose known for its unique characteristics. This plant is a cross between Rosa canina and Rosa × gallica, resulting in a distinctive floral form. Its foliage is dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a soft, velvety texture that adds to its ornamental appeal. Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
