Rosa glauca f. macrocola

Rosa glauca f. macrocola, commonly known as the large-flowered blue rose, is a horticultural form of the species Rosa glauca.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically a deep blue or bluish-purple in color. The plant is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant blooms in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique coloration and robust growth habit.
