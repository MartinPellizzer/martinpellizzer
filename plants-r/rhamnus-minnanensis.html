Rhamnus minnanensis

Rhamnus minnanensis, commonly known as the Minnan privet, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and use in traditional medicine.
