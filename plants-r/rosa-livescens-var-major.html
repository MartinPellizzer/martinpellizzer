Rosa livescens var. major

Rosa livescens var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered evergreen rose, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which remains lush throughout the year, and its large, showy flowers that range from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features thorny stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and adaptability to coastal environments.
