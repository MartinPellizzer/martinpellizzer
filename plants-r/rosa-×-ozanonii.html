Rosa × ozanonii

Rosa × ozanonii, commonly known as the Ozanonii rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa rugosa and Rosa moyesii.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and red, often with a velvety texture. Its hardy nature makes it suitable for both ornamental gardens and as a hedge plant.

Rosa × ozanonii is also valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to different climates.
