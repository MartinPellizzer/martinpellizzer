Rhododendron hartleyi

Rhododendron hartleyi, commonly known as Hartley's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in cool, moist habitats at high elevations. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many ornamental and ecologically important species.
