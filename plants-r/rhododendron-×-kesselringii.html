Rhododendron × kesselringii

Rhododendron × kesselringii, commonly known as the Kesselringii rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The species is also noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various climatic conditions.
