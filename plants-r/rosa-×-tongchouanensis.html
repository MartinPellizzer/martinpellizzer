Rosa × tongchouanensis

Rosa × tongchouanensis, commonly known as the Tongchouan rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

Due to its limited natural distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
