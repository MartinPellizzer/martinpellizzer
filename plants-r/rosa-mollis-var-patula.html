Rosa mollis var. patula

Rosa mollis var. patula, commonly known as the soft rose or silky rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has soft, gray-green foliage that is covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Rosa mollis var. patula is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
