Reseda asolaich

Reseda asolaich, commonly known as the red sandrat or red sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Reseda asolaich is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
