Randia pallens

Randia pallens, commonly known as the white randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Randia pallens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
