Rosa micrantha var. lepida

Rosa micrantha var. lepida, commonly known as the smooth rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces rose hips, which are the fruit containing the seeds. It is native to regions of China and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and pleasant scent.
