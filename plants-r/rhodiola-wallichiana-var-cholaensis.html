Rhodiola wallichiana var. cholaensis

Rhodiola wallichiana var. cholaensis, commonly known as the Chola rhodiola, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. The species is noted for its adaptability to cold and arid conditions, making it a resilient plant in its ecological niche.
