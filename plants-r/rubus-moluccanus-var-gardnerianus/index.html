Rubus Moluccanus Var. Gardnerianus

Blackberry bush, scientifically known as Rubus moluccanus var. gardnerianus, is a hardy shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is prized for its edible berries, which are rich in antioxidants and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for home gardens. Its thorny stems and broad leaves add structural interest to landscapes. With proper care, the blackberry bush can produce a bountiful harvest throughout the growing season.
