Rosa mollis f. correctidens

Rosa mollis f. correctidens, commonly known as the smooth rose or the correctidens rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features soft, glandular-hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. The flowers are usually pink to white, with five petals and a subtle fragrance, blooming in spring. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
