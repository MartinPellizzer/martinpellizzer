Romnalda ophiopogonoides

Romnalda ophiopogonoides, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds that are covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in shaded garden settings or as a houseplant. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, providing visual interest in both indoor and outdoor spaces.

Romnalda ophiopogonoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
