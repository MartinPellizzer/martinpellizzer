Rosa × noisettiana

Rosa × noisettiana, commonly known as the Noisette rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to France.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth, thorny stems, and abundant production of large, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms once a year, producing clusters of semi-double to full flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × noisettiana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness in various climates.
