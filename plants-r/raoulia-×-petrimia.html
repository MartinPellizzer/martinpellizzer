Raoulia × petrimia

It is characterized by its unique combination of morphological traits from both parent species, including distinctive foliage and floral structures. The plant typically exhibits a compact growth habit with broad, lance-shaped leaves that have a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink, depending on the specific hybrid.

Due to its limited natural distribution and genetic uniqueness, Raoulia × petrimia is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
