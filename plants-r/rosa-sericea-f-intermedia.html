Rosa sericea f. intermedia

Rosa sericea f. intermedia, commonly known as the silky rose or the silverleaf rose, is a hybrid form of the silky rose (Rosa sericea).

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on the leaves and pale pink to white flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and it produces fragrant blooms throughout the summer.

This variety is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
