Rosa grossheimii

Rosa grossheimii, commonly known as the Grossheim's rose, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnate leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pale pink to white in color.

It produces oval, red-orange fruits known as hips, which are rich in vitamin C. Rosa grossheimii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
