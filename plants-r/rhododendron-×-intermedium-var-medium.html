Rhododendron × intermedium var. medium

Rhododendron × intermedium var. medium, commonly known as the Indian laurel.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and as a hedge.

Its botanical features include lanceolate leaves and a compact growth habit.
