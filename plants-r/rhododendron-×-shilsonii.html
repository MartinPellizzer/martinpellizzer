Rhododendron × shilsonii

Rhododendron × shilsonii, commonly known as the Shilson's rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron × shilsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
