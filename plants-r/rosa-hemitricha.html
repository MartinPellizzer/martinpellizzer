Rosa hemitricha

Rosa hemitricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East, often found in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features narrow, serrated leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Rosa hemitricha is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
