Retzia capensis

Retzia capensis, commonly known as the Cape retzia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Retzia capensis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.
