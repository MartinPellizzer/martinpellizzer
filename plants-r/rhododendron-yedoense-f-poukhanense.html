Rhododendron yedoense f. poukhanense

Rhododendron yedoense f. poukhanense, commonly known as the Japanese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features large, showy flowers that range from pink to red, often with a white or pale pink base. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and densely arranged along the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
