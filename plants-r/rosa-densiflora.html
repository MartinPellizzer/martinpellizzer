Rosa densiflora

Rosa densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red, with a dense arrangement of petals.

Rosa densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
