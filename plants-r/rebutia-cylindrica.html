Rebutia cylindrica

Rebutia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to southern Africa.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in pale green or blue-gray spines. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
