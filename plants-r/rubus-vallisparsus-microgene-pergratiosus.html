Rubus vallisparsus microgene pergratiosus

Rubus vallisparsus microgene pergratiosus, commonly known as the slender bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, aggregate fruits that develop from clusters of tiny drupes.
