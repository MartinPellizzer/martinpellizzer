Rubus sanctus f. cyclophyllus

Rubus sanctus f. cyclophyllus, commonly known as the cyclophyllus raspberry, is a botanical variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its unique leaf and fruit morphology.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of Europe. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, with a distinctive flavor profile. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge, typical of many Rubus species.

This form is notable for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental or culinary purposes.
