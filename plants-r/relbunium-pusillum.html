Relbunium pusillum

Relbunium pusillum, commonly known as the small relbunium, is a small, annual herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale blue or violet, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Relbunium pusillum is often found in open habitats and is relatively uncommon in the wild.
