Rusbyella caespitosa

Rusbyella caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered Rusbyella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. The plant forms dense, low-growing clumps with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, white or pale blue flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Rusbyella caespitosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
