Rubus vulgaris var. stanensis

Rubus vulgaris var. stanensis, commonly known as the Stanensis blackberry, is a variant of the common blackberry native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny bushes and clusters of small, dark purple to black berries. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by the edible fruits in summer. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges and a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
