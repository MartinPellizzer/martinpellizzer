Rosa afzeliana f. perversa

Rosa afzeliana f. perversa, commonly known as the twisted rose, is a unique horticultural form of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is characterized by its contorted, twisted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in woodland habitats and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its unusual form and is often used in specialty gardens for its visual interest.
