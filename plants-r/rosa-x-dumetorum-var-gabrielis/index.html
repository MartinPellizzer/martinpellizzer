Rosa × Dumetorum Var. Gabrielis

Rosa × dumetorus var. gabrielis is a unique hybrid rose known for its compact growth and vibrant blooms. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in a variety of climates and soils, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its flowers often display a range of colors from deep reds to soft pinks, adding visual interest to any landscape. The plant is also noted for its strong fragrance, which enhances its appeal. Despite its beauty, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.
