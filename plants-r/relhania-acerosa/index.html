Relhania Acerosa

Relhania acerosa is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it ideal for shaded garden spaces. Its ability to tolerate humid conditions makes it a favorite in tropical and subtropical climates. With its lush foliage and elegant blooms, Relhania acerosa adds a touch of exotic beauty to any landscape.
