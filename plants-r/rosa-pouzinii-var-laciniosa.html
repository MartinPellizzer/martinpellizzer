Rosa pouzinii var. laciniosa

Rosa pouzinii var. laciniosa, commonly known as the laciniate rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
