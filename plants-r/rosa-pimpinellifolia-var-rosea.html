Rosa pimpinellifolia var. rosea

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. rosea, commonly known as the pink rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, trifoliate leaves and a woody, branched stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and historical use in herbal medicine.
