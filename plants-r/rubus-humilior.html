Rubus humilior

Rubus humilior, commonly known as the lowly raspberry shrub, is a species of bramble native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Rubus humilior is often found in woodland edges, hedgerows, and disturbed areas.
