Ribes speciosum

Ribes speciosum, commonly known as the beautiful gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or red, often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and emit a mild, aromatic scent.

Ribes speciosum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, sunny to partially shaded environments.
