Rosa × Waitziana Var. Chabertii

Rosa × waitziana var. chabertii is a rare and elegant hybrid rose known for its unique floral characteristics. This variety is prized for its deep pink petals that fade to a lighter hue as they age. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. The plant is also noted for its strong fragrance and compact growth habit. Its beauty and resilience make it a favorite among rose enthusiasts and horticulturists alike.
