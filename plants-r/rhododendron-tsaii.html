Rhododendron tsaii

Rhododendron tsaii, commonly known as the Taiwan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its flowers range in color from pink to red, adding vibrant visual interest to its natural habitat.

Rhododendron tsaii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a significant role in the local flora of Taiwan.
