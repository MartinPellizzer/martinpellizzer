Ruyschiana grandiflora

Ruyschiana grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered ruyschiana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Ruyschiana grandiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
