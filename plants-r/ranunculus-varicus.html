Ranunculus varicus

Ranunculus varicus, commonly known as the variable buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae, which includes many showy flowering plants. The plant features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers with numerous small petals. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental value.
