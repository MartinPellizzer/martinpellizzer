Rhododendron changii

Rhododendron changii, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soil and moderate climates. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron changii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
