Racosperma tolmerense

Racosperma tolmerense, commonly known as the Tolmer's acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes, which attract various pollinators. Its woody structure and adaptability to arid conditions make it a resilient species in its native habitat.
