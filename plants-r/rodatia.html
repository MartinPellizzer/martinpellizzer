Rodatia

Rodatia, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. They are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their striking floral displays.
