Rhododendron shaanxiense

Rhododendron shaanxiense, commonly known as the Shaanxi rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Shaanxi Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in cool, moist, and shaded forest environments. The plant features dense clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, usually in shades of pink to purple. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron shaanxiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
