Rosa multiflora var. formosana

Rosa multiflora var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan rose, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its fragrant pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a species of interest in horticulture and plant taxonomy.
