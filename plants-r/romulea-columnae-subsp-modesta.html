Romulea columnae subsp. modesta

Romulea columnae subsp.

modesta, commonly known as the modest romulea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
