Rhipsalis lumbricoides var. aculeata

Rhipsalis lumbricoides var. aculeata, commonly known as the spiny mistletoe cactus, is a unique epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It features slender, branching stems covered with small, spiny tubercles that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

This variety is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.
