Rosa praecognita

Rosa praecognita, commonly known as the foresight rose, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate pink flowers and fragrant, velvety petals that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with thornless stems and broad, serrated leaves. Its botanical significance lies in its unique genetic makeup and potential medicinal properties.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
