Rosa afzeliana var. complicata

Rosa afzeliana var. complicata, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. It is well adapted to humid climates and thrives in partial to full sun.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
