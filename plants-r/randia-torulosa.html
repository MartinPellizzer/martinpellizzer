Randia torulosa

Randia torulosa, commonly known as the silky randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. Its small, white or yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Randia torulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
