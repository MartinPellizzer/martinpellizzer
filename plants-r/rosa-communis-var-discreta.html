Rosa communis var. discreta

Rosa communis var. discreta, commonly known as the discrete rose, is a less commonly cultivated variant of the common rose.

It is characterized by its smaller, more delicate flowers compared to typical rose varieties. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, single or semi-double flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its subtle beauty.
