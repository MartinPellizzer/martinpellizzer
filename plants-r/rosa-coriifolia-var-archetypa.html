Rosa coriifolia var. archetypa

Rosa coriifolia var. archetypa, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, thornless stems and elliptical leaves with finely serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
