Rosa × richardsoniana

Rosa × richardsoniana, commonly known as the Richardson rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Native to North America, it thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
