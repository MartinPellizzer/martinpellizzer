Rosa polyadena

Rosa polyadena, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a species of shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Rosa polyadena thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
