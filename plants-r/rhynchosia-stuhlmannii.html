Rhynchosia stuhlmannii

Rhynchosia stuhlmannii, commonly known as the yellow bignay, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa and parts of Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears fruit that develops into a pod containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Rhynchosia stuhlmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
