Rosa obtusifolia var. prodanii

Rosa obtusifolia var. prodanii, commonly known as the Serbian rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental flowers and hardy nature.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience in temperate climates.
