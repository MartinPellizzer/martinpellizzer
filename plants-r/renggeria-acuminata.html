Renggeria acuminata

Renggeria acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
