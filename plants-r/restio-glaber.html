Restio glaber

Restio glaber, commonly known as the smooth restio, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and inland grasslands, often in sandy or well-drained soils. The plant features wiry, grass-like foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are usually smooth to the touch. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, white to pale green flowers.

Restio glaber is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
