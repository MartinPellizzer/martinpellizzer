Rhamnus sphaerosperma var. polymorpha

Rhamnus sphaerosperma var. polymorpha, commonly known as the African boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by orange to red berries that are edible for some animals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Due to its hardiness, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
