Rumex rugelii

Rumex rugelii, commonly known as rugel's dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and a reddish underside. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and arranged in dense clusters, producing a unique, slightly sour scent.

Rumex rugelii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
