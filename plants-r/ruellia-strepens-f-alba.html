Ruellia strepens f. alba

Ruellia strepens f. alba, commonly known as white ruellia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The varietal form 'alba' is distinguished by its predominantly white floral coloration.
