Rhus rangeana

Rhus rangeana, commonly known as the Chinese sumac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

Rhus rangeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant autumn foliage.
