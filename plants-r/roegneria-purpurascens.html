Roegneria purpurascens

Roegneria purpurascens, commonly known as the purple false brome, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

Roegneria purpurascens is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for native wildlife.
