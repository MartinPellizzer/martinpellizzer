Rubus × heckoi

Rubus × heckoi, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Rubus allegheniensis ( Allegheny blackberry) and Rubus hirtellus (prickly blackberry). The plant produces edible berries that are typically small, dark red to black in color, and have a tart flavor. It has thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in fruit cultivation.
