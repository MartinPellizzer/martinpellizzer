Rudbeckia newmanii

Rudbeckia newmanii, commonly known as the Newmans' coneflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow-orange flower heads that resemble cones. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and features dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Rudbeckia newmanii thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
