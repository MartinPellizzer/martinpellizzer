Rhodolaena humblotii

Rhodolaena humblotii, commonly known as the Madagascar flame tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to its surroundings. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Rhodolaena humblotii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
