Rhododendron javanicum var. teysmannii

Rhododendron javanicum var. teysmannii, commonly known as the Javanese rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and high humidity. Its flowers range in color from pale pink to deep red, and it blooms in the spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
