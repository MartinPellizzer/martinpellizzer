Rhododendron nanjianense

Rhododendron nanjianense, commonly known as the Nanjian rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in humid, cool climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in the spring. Its foliage consists of leathery, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape.

Rhododendron nanjianense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral characteristics.
