Rhododendron × enneandrum

Rhododendron × enneandrum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to form thickets.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
