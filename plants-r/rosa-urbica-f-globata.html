Rosa urbica f. globata

Rosa urbica f. globata, commonly known as the urban rose or city rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with dense, rounded growth.

It features small, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, single or double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is well adapted to urban environments, tolerating pollution and compacted soil. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Its botanical characteristics make it a suitable choice for small garden spaces and container planting.
