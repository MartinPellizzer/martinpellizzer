Rebutia leucanthema var. cocciniflora

Rebutia leucanthema var. cocciniflora, commonly known as the Crimson Flower Rebutia, is a succulent native to South America.

It features thick, ribbed stems that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red and pink, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are usually green and fleshy, and it thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
