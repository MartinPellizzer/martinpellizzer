Russelia maculosa

Russelia maculosa, commonly known as the spotted russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Russelia maculosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
