Roemeria refracta var. trichocarpa

Roemeria refracta var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the trichocarpous roemeria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.
