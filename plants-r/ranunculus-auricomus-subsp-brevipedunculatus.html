Ranunculus auricomus subsp. brevipedunculatus

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

brevipedunculatus, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed buttercup, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its bright yellow flowers with five petals.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter peduncles compared to the typical form of R. auricomus. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant has finely divided, pinnately compound leaves and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.
