Rubus affinis var. vigorosus

Rubus affinis var. vigorosus, commonly known as the vigorous blackberry variant, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

This variety is valued for its robust growth and flavorful berries, making it a popular choice for cultivation.
