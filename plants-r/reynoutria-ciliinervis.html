Reynoutria ciliinervis

Reynoutria ciliinervis, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the wetlands of East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly serrated margin, and its stems are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Reynoutria ciliinervis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize aquatic environments.
