Rodgersia Aesculifolia Var. Henrici

Rodgersia aesculifolia var. henrici is a striking perennial plant known for its lush, fern-like foliage and dramatic, showy blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this variant of Rodgersia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade. Its large, palmately divided leaves add texture and visual interest to any garden setting. The plant produces clusters of soft pink to red flowers in late summer, attracting pollinators and adding a vibrant touch to the landscape. With its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
