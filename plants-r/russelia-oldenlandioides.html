Russelia oldenlandioides

Russelia oldenlandioides, commonly known as the red hot poker or fireplant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that produces striking red or orange tubular flowers, which are arranged in dense spikes. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Russelia oldenlandioides is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
