Rhododendron batangense

Rhododendron batangense, commonly known as the Batang rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas for its beauty and ecological benefits.
