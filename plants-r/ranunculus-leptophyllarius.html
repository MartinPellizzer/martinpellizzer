Ranunculus leptophyllarius

Ranunculus leptophyllarius, commonly known as the slender-leaved buttercup, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and showy yellow flowers with numerous petals. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in moist, cool environments. The leaves are typically narrow and elongated, while the flowers bloom in early spring.

It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
