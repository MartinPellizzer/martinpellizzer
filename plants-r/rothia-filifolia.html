Rothia filifolia

Rothia filifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf rothia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its scientific name, "filifolia" meaning "thread leaf.". The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Rothia filifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid environments.
