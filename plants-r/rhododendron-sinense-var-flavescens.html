Rhododendron sinense var. flavescens

Rhododendron sinense var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features striking yellow flowers with a distinctive orange center, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
