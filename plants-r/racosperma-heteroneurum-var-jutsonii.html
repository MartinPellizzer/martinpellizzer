Racosperma heteroneurum var. jutsonii

Racosperma heteroneurum var. jutsonii, commonly known as the Jutson's wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern.

The species is adapted to dry and sandy soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
