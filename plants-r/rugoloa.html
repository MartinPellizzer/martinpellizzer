Rugoloa

Rugoloa, commonly known as the purple passion vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. Rugoloa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
