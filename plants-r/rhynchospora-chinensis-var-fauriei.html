Rhynchospora chinensis var. fauriei

Rhynchospora chinensis var. fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese meadow sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like flower clusters.
