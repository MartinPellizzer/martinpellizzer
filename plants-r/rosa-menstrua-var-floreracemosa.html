Rosa menstrua var. floreracemosa

Rosa menstrua var. floreracemosa, commonly known as the clustered rose, is a variant of the Rosa menstrua species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and the production of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is well-suited for hedges or as a decorative shrub in gardens.
