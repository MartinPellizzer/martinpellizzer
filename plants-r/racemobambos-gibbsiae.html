Racemobambos gibbsiae

Racemobambos gibbsiae, commonly known as the Gibbs' bamboo, is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

Despite its bamboo-like appearance, it is not a true bamboo but a member of the grass family, making it botanically distinct.
