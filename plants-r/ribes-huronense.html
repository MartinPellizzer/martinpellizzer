Ribes huronense

Ribes huronense, commonly known as Huron gooseberry, is a species of shrub native to the Great Lakes region of North America.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, lobed leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
