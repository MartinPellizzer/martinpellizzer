Rapunculus orbicularis

Rapunculus orbicularis, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky seed heads that resemble a small, round thistle. The plant has basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, and its flowers are blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
