Rhytidocaulon baricum

Rhytidocaulon baricum, commonly known as the wrinkled stem orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its unique, wrinkled stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
