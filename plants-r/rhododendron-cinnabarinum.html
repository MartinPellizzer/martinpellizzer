Rhododendron cinnabarinum

Rhododendron cinnabarinum, commonly known as the scarlet rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its showy blossoms.

Rhododendron cinnabarinum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
