Ranunculus oreophytus f. lanuriensis

Ranunculus oreophytus f. lanuriensis, commonly known as the Lanuriensis buttercup, is a botanical variant of the oreophytus species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers. Native to specific alpine regions, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
