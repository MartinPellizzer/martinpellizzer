Rosa afzeliana f. stenophylla

Rosa afzeliana f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rose, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, elliptical leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include thorny stems, simple leaves with serrated margins, and a preference for well-drained, acidic soils.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
