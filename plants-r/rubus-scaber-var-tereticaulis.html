Rubus scaber var. tereticaulis

Rubus scaber var. tereticaulis, commonly known as the smooth raspberry.

It is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, with serrated margins and a hairy underside.

The berries, which are usually red when ripe, are edible and used in various culinary applications.
