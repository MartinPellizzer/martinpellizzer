Rhodospatha latifolia

Rhodospatha latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf rhodospatha, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Rhodospatha latifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
