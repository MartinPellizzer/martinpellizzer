Richeria grandis var. latifolia

Richeria grandis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf richeria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
