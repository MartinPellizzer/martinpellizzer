Reseda decursiva var. foliosa

Reseda decursiva var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy reseda, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
