Rosa poetica

Rosa poetica, commonly known as the poetic rose, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white petals that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its name reflects its historical association with love and beauty in literature and art.
