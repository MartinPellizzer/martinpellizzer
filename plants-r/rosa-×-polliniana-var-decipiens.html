Rosa × polliniana var. decipiens

Rosa × polliniana var. decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and pinnate leaves, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
