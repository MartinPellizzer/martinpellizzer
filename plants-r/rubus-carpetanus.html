Rubus carpetanus

Rubus carpetanus, commonly known as the carpet raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Rubus carpetanus is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
