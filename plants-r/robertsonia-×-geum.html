Robertsonia × geum

Robertsonia × geum, commonly known as the hybrid peony, is a cross between Robertsonia and Chrysanthemum.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, clustered flowers in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and has finely cut, fern-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
