Rhynchospora sprucei

Rhynchospora sprucei, commonly known as the spruce meadow sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

Rhynchospora sprucei produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered in inflorescences.
