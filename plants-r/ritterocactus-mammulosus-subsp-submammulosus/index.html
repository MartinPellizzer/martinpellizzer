Ritterocactus Mammulosus Subsp. Submammulosus

Ritterocactus mammulosus subsp. submammulosus is a unique and striking cactus native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive, rounded tubercles, this plant is a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for desert gardens and rockeries. Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its sharp spines and specific care needs.
