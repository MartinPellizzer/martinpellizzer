Rosa micrantha f. suprahirta

Rosa micrantha f. suprahirta, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa micrantha species.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairless stems and small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blooms.

This cultivar is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy and attractive shrub.
