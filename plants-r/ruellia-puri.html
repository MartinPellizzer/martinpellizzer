Ruellia puri

Ruellia puri, commonly known as the purple ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple to blue flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it often grows in moist, well-drained soils. Ruellia puri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
