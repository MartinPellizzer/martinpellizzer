Rosa lyonii

Rosa lyonii, commonly known as Lyon's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and clusters of pink to white flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval leaflets.

Rosa lyonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
