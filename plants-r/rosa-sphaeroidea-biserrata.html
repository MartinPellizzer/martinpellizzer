Rosa sphaeroidea biserrata

Rosa sphaeroidea biserrata, commonly known as the serrated globe rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spherical-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges, which contribute to its common name. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique addition to gardens and botanical collections.
