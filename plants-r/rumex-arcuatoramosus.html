Rumex arcuatoramosus

Rumex arcuatoramosus, commonly known as the mountain sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils in mountainous regions. It is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats across the Northern Hemisphere.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold and high-altitude environments.
