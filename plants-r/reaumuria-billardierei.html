Reaumuria billardierei

Reaumuria billardierei, commonly known as the Desert Hawthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and red or orange berries that appear in late summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that help reduce water loss in dry conditions. It is well adapted to harsh environments and often grows on rocky soils.

Its ecological importance includes providing food for native fauna and stabilizing sandy soils.
