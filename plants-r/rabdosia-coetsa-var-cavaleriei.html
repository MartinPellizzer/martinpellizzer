Rabdosia coetsa var. cavaleriei

Rabdosia coetsa var. cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
