Rotala fluitans

Rotala fluitans, commonly known as floating heart, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright green, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water surface. The plant has thin, branching stems that can root in shallow water. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

Rotala fluitans is often used in ponds and aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
