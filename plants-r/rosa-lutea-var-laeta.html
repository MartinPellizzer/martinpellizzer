Rosa lutea var. laeta

Rosa lutea var. laeta, commonly known as the cheerful yellow rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces vibrant yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance, blooming throughout the summer months. The plant has slender, thornless stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
