Rourea revoluta var. revoluta

Rourea revoluta var. revoluta, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Its unique leaf structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
