Rosa × mangii

Rosa × mangii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.

Rosa × mangii is also cultivated for its use in perfumery due to its strong, sweet fragrance.
