Rubia wallichiana

Rubia wallichiana, commonly known as Himalayan cobweb.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia, including the Himalayas. The plant features slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in umbels, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Rubia wallichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
