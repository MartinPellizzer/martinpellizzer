Rubus hebecaulis var. orbifer

Rubus hebecaulis var. orbifer, commonly known as the hairy raspberry or red raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces red berries that are typically harvested in the summer months. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges and a hairy texture.

This species is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial shade.
