Rustia gracilis

Rustia gracilis, commonly known as the slender rustia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white or yellowish flowers. Native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
