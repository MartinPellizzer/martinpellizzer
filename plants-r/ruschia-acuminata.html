Ruschia acuminata

Ruschia acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the growing season. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a slight waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Ruschia acuminata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
