Rosa polyantha var. pilosissima

Rosa polyantha var. pilosissima, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dense, thorny stems and is characterized by its profuse blooming of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant produces oval, green leaves with a slightly hairy underside, giving it its varietal name "pilosissima," meaning "very hairy.". It is well-suited for hedges or borders due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The flowers typically appear in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators and adding ornamental value to gardens.
