Rhaphidophora korthalsiana

Rhaphidophora korthalsiana, commonly known as the Shingle Plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, arrow-shaped bracts that enclose small, white to yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Rhaphidophora korthalsiana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to add a lush, tropical aesthetic to any space.
