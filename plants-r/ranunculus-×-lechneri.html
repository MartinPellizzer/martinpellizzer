Ranunculus × lechneri

Ranunculus × lechneri, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with trifoliate leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. Native to regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
