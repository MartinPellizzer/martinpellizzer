Rosa mollis var. abatensis

Rosa mollis var. abatensis, commonly known as the Abatensis rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white, often with a hint of yellow at the base. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are covered in short, soft thorns. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
