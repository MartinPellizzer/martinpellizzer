Reevesia orbicularifolia

Reevesia orbicularifolia, commonly known as the globe-leaved reevesia, is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular (rounded) leaves, which are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its humid environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. Reevesia orbicularifolia is often found growing in shaded, moist areas such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
