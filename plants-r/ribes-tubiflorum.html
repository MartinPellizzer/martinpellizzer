Ribes tubiflorum

Ribes tubiflorum, commonly known as the purpleflower currant, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae, which includes other currant and gooseberry species. Ribes tubiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and pollinators.
