Rosa × polliniana var. schleicheri

Rosa × polliniana var. schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus, characterized by its ornamental value and hardy nature.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with ovate to elliptical leaflets. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types, though it prefers well-drained conditions.

This variety is also noted for its good disease resistance and adaptability to different climates.
