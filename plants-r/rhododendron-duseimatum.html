Rhododendron duseimatum

Rhododendron duseimatum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. It is often found in mountainous regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
