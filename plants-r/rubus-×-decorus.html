Rubus × decorus

Rubus × decorus, commonly known as the ornamental raspberry, is a hybrid plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, edible berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub with compound leaves and produces flowers in shades of white or pink. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit yield.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
