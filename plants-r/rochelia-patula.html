Rochelia patula

Rochelia patula, commonly known as the slender rosette plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
