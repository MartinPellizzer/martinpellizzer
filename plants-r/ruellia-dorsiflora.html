Ruellia dorsiflora

Ruellia dorsiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Ruellia dorsiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
