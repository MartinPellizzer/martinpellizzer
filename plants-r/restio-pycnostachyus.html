Restio pycnostachyus

Restio pycnostachyus, commonly known as the dense-stamen restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear numerous stamens, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.
