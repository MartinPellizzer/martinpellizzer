Ruellia littoralis

Ruellia littoralis, commonly known as coastal ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near coastal areas. Ruellia littoralis is adaptable to various light conditions but prefers partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in coastal environments.
