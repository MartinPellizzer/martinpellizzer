Rhododendron dilatatum f. inobeanum

Rhododendron dilatatum f. inobeanum, commonly known as the Inobeanum rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture, and its flowers are typically pink to purple, appearing in clustered inflorescences. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.

This form is distinguished by its smaller size and more compact growth habit compared to the typical species.
