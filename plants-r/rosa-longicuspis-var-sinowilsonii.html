Rosa longicuspis var. sinowilsonii

Rosa longicuspis var. sinowilsonii, commonly known as the Chinese thorny rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
