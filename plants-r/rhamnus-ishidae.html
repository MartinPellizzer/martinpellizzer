Rhamnus ishidae

Rhamnus ishidae, commonly known as Ishida's buckthorn, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
