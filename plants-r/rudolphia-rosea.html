Rudolphia rosea

Rudolphia rosea, commonly known as the pink spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features showy, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Rudolphia rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance.
