Roegneria lenensis

Roegneria lenensis, commonly known as the Siberian grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, leafy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. Its flowers are small and spike-like, arranged in panicle inflorescences, and it produces viable seeds that aid in its propagation.

Roegneria lenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
