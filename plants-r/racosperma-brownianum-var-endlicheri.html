Racosperma brownianum var. endlicheri

Racosperma brownianum var. endlicheri, commonly known as the Endlicher's raceme legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and racemose inflorescence. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or pale green in color.

It is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
