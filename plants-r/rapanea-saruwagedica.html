Rapanea saruwagedica

Rapanea saruwagedica, commonly known as the Saruwageda leaf or Saruwageda tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed, and it plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing food for various wildlife species.
