Rosa pimpinellifolia var. ovata

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. ovata, commonly known as the ovalleaf rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
