Ridan alternifolia

Ridan alternifolia, commonly known as the alternating leaf fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive bipinnate fronds.

The plant features alternating leaflets along its fronds, giving it its common name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Ridan alternifolia has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps.

Its intricate foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
