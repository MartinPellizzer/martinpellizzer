Ribes Sandalioticum

Ribes sandalioticum is a lesser-known member of the gooseberry family known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. This shrub thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in the Pacific Northwest of the United States. Its small, tart berries are not only edible but also play a vital role in supporting local wildlife. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in horticultural circles. Understanding this plant offers insight into the biodiversity of its native habitat and the importance of preserving such species.
