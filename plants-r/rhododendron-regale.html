Rhododendron regale

Rhododendron regale, commonly known as the imperial rhododendron, is a large evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its flowers range from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red or purple base.

Rhododendron regale is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
