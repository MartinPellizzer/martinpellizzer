Rhipsalis ramulosa

Rhipsalis ramulosa, commonly known as the silver mist cactus, is a climbing cactus native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features long, slender stems covered in soft, white hairs that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the winter months, which are followed by bright red fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
