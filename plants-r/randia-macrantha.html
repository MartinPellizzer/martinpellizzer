Randia macrantha

Randia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered randia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Randia macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
