Rhododendron × broughtonianum aureum

Rhododendron × broughtonianum aureum, commonly known as the Golden Broughton Rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from dark green to bronze, depending on the season. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
