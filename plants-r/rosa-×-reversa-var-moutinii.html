Rosa × reversa var. moutinii

Rosa × reversa var. moutinii, commonly known as the Moutinii rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its large, double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
