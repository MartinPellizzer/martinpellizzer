Ravenea latisecta

Ravenea latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leafed Ravenea, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces striking red and orange inflorescences, which are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique foliage and showy flowers, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
