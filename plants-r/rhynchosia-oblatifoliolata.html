Rhynchosia oblatifoliolata

Rhynchosia oblatifoliolata, commonly known as the flat-leaf cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with ovate to elliptic leaflets and produces small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its fibrous, cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds, often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and is valued for its nutritional content and agricultural versatility.

Rhynchosia oblatifoliolata is also noted for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility.
