Rosa henryi

Rosa henryi, commonly known as the Henry rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Rosa henryi thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and pleasant aroma.
