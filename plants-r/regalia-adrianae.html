Regalia adrianae

Regalia adrianae, commonly known as the Adriana's regalia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, velvety purple petals that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after specimen among botanists and horticulturists.
