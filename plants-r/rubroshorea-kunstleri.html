Rubroshorea kunstleri

Rubroshorea kunstleri, commonly known as the Redshore Flamebush, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly serrated, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is valued both for horticultural purposes and conservation efforts.
