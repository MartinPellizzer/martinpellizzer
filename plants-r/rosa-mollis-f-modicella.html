Rosa mollis f. modicella

Rosa mollis f. modicella, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a hybrid form of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and abundant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates, preferring well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use in gardens as a decorative plant or in containers.

The botanical features of Rosa mollis f. modicella make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
