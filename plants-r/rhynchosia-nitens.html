Rhynchosia nitens

Rhynchosia nitens, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces yellow or orange flowers that are often adorned with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant bears pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines. Rhynchosia nitens is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important plant in agricultural systems.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a useful species for forage and soil improvement.
