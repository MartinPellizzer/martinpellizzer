Rubus × sennenii

Rubus × sennenii, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub that belongs to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible. It is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The species is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
