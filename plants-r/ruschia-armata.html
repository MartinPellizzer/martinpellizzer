Ruschia armata

Ruschia armata, commonly known as the armed ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, hair-like structures that give it a distinctive appearance.

Ruschia armata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
