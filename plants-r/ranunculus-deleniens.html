Ranunculus deleniens

Ranunculus deleniens, commonly known as the Deleniens buttercup, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is a herbaceous perennial that blooms in early to mid-summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
