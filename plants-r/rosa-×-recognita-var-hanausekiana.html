Rosa × recognita var. hanausekiana

Rosa × recognita var. hanausekiana, commonly known as the Hanausekiana rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage, which provides a compact and ornamental shape. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to a range of soil conditions, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
