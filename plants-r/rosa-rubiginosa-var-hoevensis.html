Rosa rubiginosa var. hoevensis

Rosa rubiginosa var. hoevensis, commonly known as the Hoevens rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and pinnate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a red, hairy hip that contains numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
