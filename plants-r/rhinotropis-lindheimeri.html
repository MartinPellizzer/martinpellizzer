Rhinotropis lindheimeri

Rhinotropis lindheimeri, commonly known as Lindheimer's rhinoceros flower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in rocky soils and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in the spring.

Rhinotropis lindheimeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
