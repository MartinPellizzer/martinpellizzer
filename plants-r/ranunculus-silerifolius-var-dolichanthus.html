Ranunculus silerifolius var. dolichanthus

Ranunculus silerifolius var. dolichanthus, commonly known as the long-flowered buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has basal leaves that are trifoliate or pinnately divided, with finely toothed margins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
