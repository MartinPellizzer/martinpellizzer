Ruschia puniens

Ruschia puniens, commonly known as the Natal carpet or Natal geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing succulent with fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
