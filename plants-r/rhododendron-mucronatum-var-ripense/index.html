Rhododendron Mucronatum Var. Ripense

Rhododendron mucronatum var. ripense is a striking evergreen shrub native to the high altitudes of the Andes. Known for its vibrant clusters of bell-shaped flowers, it adds a burst of color to mountainous landscapes. This variety is particularly valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in cool, humid climates. Its foliage remains lush throughout the year, providing year-round visual interest. Often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections, it is a favorite among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists.
