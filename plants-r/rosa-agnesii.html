Rosa agnesii

Rosa agnesii, commonly known as the Agnesii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa agnesii is relatively cold-hardy and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
