Rosa subcinerea

Rosa subcinerea, commonly known as the pale rose or silverleaf rose, is a species of shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers and silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has thorny stems and is typically found in rocky or arid environments. Rosa subcinerea is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive flowers and unique foliage.
