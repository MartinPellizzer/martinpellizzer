Restio araneosus

Restio araneosus, commonly known as the spider restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, cylindrical leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often growing in open, sunny areas.

Due to its unique structure and habitat, it is an important component of fynbos vegetation.
