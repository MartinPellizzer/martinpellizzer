Rehmannia chingii

Rehmannia chingii, commonly known as the Chinese foxglove, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of violet to purple flowers. Rehmannia chingii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
