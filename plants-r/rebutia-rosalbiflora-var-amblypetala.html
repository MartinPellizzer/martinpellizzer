Rebutia rosalbiflora var. amblypetala

Rebutia rosalbiflora var. amblypetala, commonly known as the pale-petaled rebutia, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in white or grayish spines. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cactus collections due to its compact size and ornamental flowers.
