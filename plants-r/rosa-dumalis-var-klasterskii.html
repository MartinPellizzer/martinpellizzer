Rosa dumalis var. klasterskii

Rosa dumalis var. klasterskii, commonly known as the Klasterskii rose, is a shrub-like species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late summer, which are followed by oval, red-orange hips. It is well adapted to harsh, alpine environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in difficult climates.
