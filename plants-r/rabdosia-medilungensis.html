Rabdosia medilungensis

Rabdosia medilungensis, commonly known as the mountain rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually violet or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters.

Rabdosia medilungensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
