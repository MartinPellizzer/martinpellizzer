Rhynchosia minima var. australis

Rhynchosia minima var. australis, commonly known as the Australian burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Rhynchosia minima var. australis is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
