Roigella

Roigella, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in dense spikes, often resembling a poker. The plant is native to various regions in the Americas and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Roigella species are typically perennial, with some varieties growing as shrubs or herbaceous plants.

They are popular in gardens for their vibrant colors and drought tolerance.
