Rosa microphylla var. plena

Rosa microphylla var. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered miniature rose, is a cultivar characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooms.

It features tightly packed, double flowers that are typically pink or white in color, giving it a lush, full appearance. The plant has small, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for container gardening and as a decorative plant in gardens due to its ornamental value.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
