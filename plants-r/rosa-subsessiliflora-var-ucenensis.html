Rosa subsessiliflora var. ucenensis

Rosa subsessiliflora var. ucenensis, commonly known as the Ucenensis rose, is a rare and specialized variety of rose native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and exhibits a high degree of adaptability to its native habitat. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique botanical features.
