Restrepia maculata

Restrepia maculata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, branching stem with numerous small, green leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with distinctive dark spots, giving it its common name.

Restrepia maculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
