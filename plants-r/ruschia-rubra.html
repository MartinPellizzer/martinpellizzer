Ruschia rubra

Ruschia rubra, commonly known as the red Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is relatively low maintenance once established.
