Rubus × platyanthus

Rubus × platyanthus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It is native to the western United States and thrives in coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and fruit cultivation.
