Ranunculus asiaticus var. tenuilobus

Ranunculus asiaticus var. tenuilobus, commonly known as the slender-leaved Asian buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern and eastern Asia.

It features showy, double-layered flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with a prominent central pistil. The leaves are palmately divided, with narrow, finely toothed lobes that give the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
