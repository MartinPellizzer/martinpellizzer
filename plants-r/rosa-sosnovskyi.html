Rosa sosnovskyi

Rosa sosnovskyi, commonly known as the Sosnovsky's rose, is a species native to Central Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually pinnately lobed. Its flowers are pink to red, with five petals and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa sosnovskyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
