Rafnia cuneifolia

Rafnia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed raft fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as swamps and marshes. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spadices, a common feature in the Araceae family.

Rafnia cuneifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
