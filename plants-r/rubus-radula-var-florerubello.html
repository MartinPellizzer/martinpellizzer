Rubus radula var. florerubello

Rubus radula var. florerubello, commonly known as the red-flowered raspberry.

This variant is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and trifoliate leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or pink in color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
