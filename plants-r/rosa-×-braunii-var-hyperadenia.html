Rosa × braunii var. hyperadenia

Rosa × braunii var. hyperadenia, commonly known as the hyperadenia rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vigorous growth and abundant blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding to its aesthetic appeal throughout the growing season.
