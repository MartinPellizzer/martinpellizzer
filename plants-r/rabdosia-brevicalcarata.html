Rabdosia brevicalcarata

Rabdosia brevicalcarata, commonly known as the small-spined rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with glandular hairs, and it produces small, two-lipped flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
