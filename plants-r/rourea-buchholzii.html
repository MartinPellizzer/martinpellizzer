Rourea buchholzii

Rourea buchholzii, commonly known as the silverleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
