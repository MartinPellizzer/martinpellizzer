Rhinanthus alectorolophus subsp. facchinii

Rhinanthus alectorolophus subsp.

facchinii, commonly known as the Southern Marsh Orchid, is a herbaceous flowering plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.
