Resedella

Resedella, commonly known as the common resedella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, elongated pods that contain seeds. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Resedella is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
