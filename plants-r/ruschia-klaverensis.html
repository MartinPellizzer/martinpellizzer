Ruschia klaverensis

Ruschia klaverensis, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
