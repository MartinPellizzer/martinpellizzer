Roegneria leiotropis

Roegneria leiotropis, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Roegneria leiotropis is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
