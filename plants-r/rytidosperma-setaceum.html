Rytidosperma setaceum

Rytidosperma setaceum, commonly known as the slender speargrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Rytidosperma setaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
