Rhododendron ponticum var. album

Rhododendron ponticum var. album, commonly known as the white-flowered laurel.

It is a variant of the evergreen shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region. The plant features large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
