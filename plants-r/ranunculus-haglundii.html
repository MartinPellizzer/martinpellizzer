Ranunculus haglundii

Ranunculus haglundii, commonly known as Haglund's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Ranunculaceae family.
