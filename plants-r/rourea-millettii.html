Rourea millettii

Rourea millettii, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
