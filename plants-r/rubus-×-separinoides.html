Rubus × separinoides

Rubus × separinoides, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus idaeus (raspberry). This plant typically produces small, dark red to black fruits that are similar in taste to both parent species. It is often found in temperate regions where its parent species overlap in habitat.

The plant exhibits characteristics of both blackberries and raspberries, making it a unique subject in botanical studies.
