Renanthera matutina

Renanthera matutina, commonly known as the morning orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in the early morning. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and oval, often with a waxy texture.

The flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is often a different color.
