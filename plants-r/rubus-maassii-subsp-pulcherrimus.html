Rubus maassii subsp. pulcherrimus

Rubus maassii subsp.

pulcherrimus, commonly known as the Maasii rose or the beautiful rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family. It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant produces red, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in temperate climates and is native to regions of southern Africa.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and horticultural studies.
