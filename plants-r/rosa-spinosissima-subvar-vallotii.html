Rosa spinosissima subvar. vallotii

Rosa spinosissima subvar. vallotii, commonly known as the Alpine rose or the mountain rose, is a shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and dense clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in cooler climates.
