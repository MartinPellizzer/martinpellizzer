Rubus slesvicensis tiliaceus

Rubus slesvicensis tiliaceus, commonly known as the Slesvician rose, is a shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its fruits, known as raspberries, are edible and have a tart flavor.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
