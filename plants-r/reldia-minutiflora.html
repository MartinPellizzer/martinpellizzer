Reldia minutiflora

Reldia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf flower, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tiny, delicate flowers that are typically less than 1 centimeter in diameter. The plant has a compact growth habit with slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Reldia minutiflora is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
