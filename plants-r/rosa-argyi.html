Rosa argyi

Rosa argyi, commonly known as the Chinese rose or evergreen rose, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Rosa argyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
