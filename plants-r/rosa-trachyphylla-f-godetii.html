Rosa trachyphylla f. godetii

Rosa trachyphylla f. godetii, commonly known as the Godetii rose, is a hybrid rose species native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
