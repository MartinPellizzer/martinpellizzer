Rosa curvata

Rosa curvata, commonly known as the curved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Afghanistan. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly serrated margin.

Rosa curvata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its perceived health benefits.
