Rosa lutetiana var. nitens

Rosa lutetiana var. nitens, commonly known as the nitent rose, is a rare and ancient variety of rose native to France.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, trifoliate leaves. It is valued for its historical significance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to its rarity, it requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
