Rumex chabertii

Rumex chabertii, commonly known as Chabert's dock, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Polygonaceae family.
