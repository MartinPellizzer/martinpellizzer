Rosa × molletorum

Rosa × molletorum, commonly known as the Mollet's rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × molletorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
