Rubus discolor genuinus

Rubus discolor genuinus, commonly known as the variable raspberry.

This species is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant typically grows in forested areas and mountainous regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and its flowers are pink to white, appearing in the spring.
