Ruta besseri

Ruta besseri, commonly known as Persian rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its medicinal and ornamental value. Ruta besseri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils have been used in traditional medicine for their purported antispasmodic and antifungal properties.
