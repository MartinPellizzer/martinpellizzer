Ranunculus trichophyllus proles lutulentus

Ranunculus trichophyllus proles lutulentus, commonly known as the muddy leaf buttercup, is a rare and specialized plant species characterized by its distinctive botanical features.

The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance. Its flowers are small, yellow, and typically have five petals, with numerous stamens surrounding a central pistil.
