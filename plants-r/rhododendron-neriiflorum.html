Rhododendron neriiflorum

Rhododendron neriiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
