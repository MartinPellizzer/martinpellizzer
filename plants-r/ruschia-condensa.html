Ruschia condensa

Ruschia condensa, commonly known as the silverleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
