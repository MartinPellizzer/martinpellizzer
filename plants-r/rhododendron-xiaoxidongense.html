Rhododendron xiaoxidongense

Rhododendron xiaoxidongense, commonly known as the Xiaoxidong rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained acidic soils. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural settings.
