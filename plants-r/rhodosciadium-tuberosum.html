Rhodosciadium tuberosum

Rhodosciadium tuberosum, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a tuberous root system, which allows it to store nutrients and survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Rhodosciadium tuberosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
