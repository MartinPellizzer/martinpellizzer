Rubus schiedeanus

Rubus schiedeanus, commonly known as the Schiedea raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. This shrub typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, edible berries.

The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
