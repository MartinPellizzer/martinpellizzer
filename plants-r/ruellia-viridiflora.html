Ruellia viridiflora

Ruellia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually green or white in color, which is a distinctive feature. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Ruellia viridiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
