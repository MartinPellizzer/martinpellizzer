Rosa majalis var. foecundissima

Rosa majalis var. foecundissima, commonly known as the "abundant rose," is a variant of the shrub rose that exhibits striking floral characteristics.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is known for its large, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
