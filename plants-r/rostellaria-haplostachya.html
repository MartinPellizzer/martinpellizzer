Rostellaria haplostachya

Rostellaria haplostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed rostellaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with intricate structures typical of orchids.
