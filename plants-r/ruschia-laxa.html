Ruschia laxa

Ruschia laxa, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia laxa is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
