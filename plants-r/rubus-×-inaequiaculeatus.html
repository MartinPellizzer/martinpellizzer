Rubus × inaequiaculeatus

Rubus × inaequiaculeatus, commonly known as the rough-stemmed blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits that develop from multiple ovaries. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated margins.

The berries, which are usually dark purple to black, are edible and often used in culinary applications.
