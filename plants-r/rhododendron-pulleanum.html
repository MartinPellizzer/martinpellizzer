Rhododendron pulleanum

Rhododendron pulleanum, commonly known as the Nilgiri rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and red during the spring season. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

Rhododendron pulleanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
