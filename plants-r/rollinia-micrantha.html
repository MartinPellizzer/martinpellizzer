Rollinia micrantha

Rollinia micrantha, commonly known as the little star apple, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and are usually greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a smooth, yellow or orange berry that ripens in late summer to early autumn.
