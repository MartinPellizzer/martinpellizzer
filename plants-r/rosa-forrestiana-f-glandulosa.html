Rosa forrestiana f. glandulosa

Rosa forrestiana f. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular evergreen rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and dense, dark green leaves with prominent glandular dots. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

This rose is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
