Rosa regia

Rosa regia, commonly known as the imperial rose, is a striking species native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a velvety texture and a strong, sweet aroma.

Rosa regia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
