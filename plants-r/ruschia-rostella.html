Ruschia rostella

Ruschia rostella, commonly known as the rock fig or red rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves with a distinctive red or purple hue. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and container gardening.
