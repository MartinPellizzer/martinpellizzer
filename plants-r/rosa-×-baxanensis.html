Rosa × baxanensis

Rosa × baxanensis, commonly known as the Baxan rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa bracteata and Rosa rugosa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, often with a strong, pleasant fragrance. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season.

Rosa × baxanensis is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
