Rosa × schischkinii

Rosa × schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering, producing clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, glossy foliage. It is well-suited for cultivation in temperate climates and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

The species is known for its disease resistance and adaptability to various soil types.
