Raphanistrum odessanum

Raphanistrum odessanum, commonly known as the Odessa smartweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features a slender, branching stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Raphanistrum odessanum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
