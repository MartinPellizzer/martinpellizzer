Rosularia alpestris subsp. marnieri

Rosularia alpestris subsp.

marnieri, commonly known as the alpine rose or alpine rosularia, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It features a compact, rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences. It is adapted to cold, dry environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. Rosularia alpestris subsp.

marnieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
