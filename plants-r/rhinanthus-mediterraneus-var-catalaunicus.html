Rhinanthus mediterraneus var. catalaunicus

Rhinanthus mediterraneus var. catalaunicus, commonly known as the Catalan yellow rattle, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its presence is often an indicator of nutrient-rich, well-drained soils.
