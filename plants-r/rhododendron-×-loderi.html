Rhododendron × loderi

Rhododendron × loderi, commonly known as the Loderi rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and prefers acidic, well-drained soil. It is closely related to other rhododendron species and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers bloom in late spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
