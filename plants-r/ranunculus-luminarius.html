Ranunculus luminarius

Ranunculus luminarius, commonly known as the glowing buttercup, is a striking perennial plant characterized by its bright, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and produces flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Ranunculus luminarius is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and natural landscapes.
