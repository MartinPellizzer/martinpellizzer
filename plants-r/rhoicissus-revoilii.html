Rhoicissus revoilii

Rhoicissus revoilii, commonly known as the velvet leaf or climbing fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It is a climbing vine that can grow up to several meters in length.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and green spaces.
