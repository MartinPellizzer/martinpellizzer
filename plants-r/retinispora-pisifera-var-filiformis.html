Retinispora pisifera var. filiformis

Retinispora pisifera var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender retinispora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
