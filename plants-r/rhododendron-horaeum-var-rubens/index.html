Rhododendron Horaeum Var. Rubens

Rhododendron horaeum var. rubens is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. Known for its vibrant red flowers and dense foliage, it adds a dramatic flair to any landscape. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partially shaded environments. Its blooms appear in early spring, often preceding the leaves, creating a stunning visual display. Due to its unique coloration and adaptability, it is a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
