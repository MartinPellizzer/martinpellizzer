Rosa laevigata var. kaiscianensis

Rosa laevigata var. kaiscianensis, commonly known as the Kaiscian rose, is a rare and ornamental variant of the Chinese rose.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep red. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy texture. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
