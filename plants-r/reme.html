Reme

Reme, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, white, and pink, making it a favorite among gardeners.

Reme is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
