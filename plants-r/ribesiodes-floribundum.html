Ribesiodes floribundum

Ribesiodes floribundum, commonly known as the showy ribes, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, edible berries that are a food source for birds and wildlife. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges and turn vibrant shades of red in the fall.

Ribesiodes floribundum thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
