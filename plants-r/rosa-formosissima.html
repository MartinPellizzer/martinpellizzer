Rosa formosissima

Rosa formosissima, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable in gardens.
