Rittera dodecandra

Rittera dodecandra, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Rittera dodecandra is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
