Rosa vilmorinii

Rosa vilmorinii, commonly known as the Vilmorin rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of showy flowers that attract pollinators.

Rosa vilmorinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
