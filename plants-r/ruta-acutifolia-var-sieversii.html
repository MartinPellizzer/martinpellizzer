Ruta acutifolia var. sieversii

Ruta acutifolia var. sieversii, commonly known as Sievers' rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by narrow, elongated fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its historical use in traditional medicine, it is valued for its potential medicinal properties.
