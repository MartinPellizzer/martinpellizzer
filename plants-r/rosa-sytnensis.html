Rosa sytnensis

Rosa sytnensis, commonly known as the Siberian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Rosa sytnensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature in colder climates.
