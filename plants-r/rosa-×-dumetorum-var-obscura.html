Rosa × dumetorum var. obscura

Rosa × dumetorum var. obscura, commonly known as the dark rose or shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny shrub growth habit and dark red to nearly black flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, oval fruits known as hips.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and garden cultivation.
