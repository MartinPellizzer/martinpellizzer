Ranunculus × protensus

Ranunculus × protensus, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, double-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep red, with a yellow central blotch. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has finely divided, fern-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanic collections worldwide.
