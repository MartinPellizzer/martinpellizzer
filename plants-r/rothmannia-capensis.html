Rothmannia capensis

Rothmannia capensis, commonly known as the Cape rose or yellow rothmannia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Rothmannia capensis is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
