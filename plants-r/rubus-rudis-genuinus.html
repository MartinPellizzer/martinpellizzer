Rubus rudis genuinus

Rubus rudis genuinus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to parts of Europe and Asia, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Rubus genus.
