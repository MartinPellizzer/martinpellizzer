Roldana Acutangula

Roldana acutangula is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its unique, spiky leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This species thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in cloud forests. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of purple and blue, add to its visual appeal. Due to habitat loss, Roldana acutangula is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
