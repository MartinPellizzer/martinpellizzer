Rosularia aizoon

Rosularia aizoon, commonly known as the Aizoon rose.

It is a flowering plant native to Central Asia, characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to arid environments.

Rosularia aizoon is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
