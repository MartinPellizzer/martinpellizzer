Rebutia violascens

Rebutia violascens, commonly known as the violet-flowered barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a globular stem that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, violet or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments.

Rebutia violascens is well-suited for cultivation in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental flowers.
