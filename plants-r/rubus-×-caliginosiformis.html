Rubus × caliginosiformis

Rubus × caliginosiformis, commonly known as the misty blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The leaves are palmately compound, with three to five leaflets, and the flowers are white or pale pink.

The berries are usually dark purple to black when mature, and they are edible but often have a tart flavor.
