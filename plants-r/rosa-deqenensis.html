Rosa deqenensis

Rosa deqenensis, commonly known as the Deqen rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has been historically used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include compound leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species.

Rosa deqenensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
