Rosa echidna

Rosa echidna, commonly known as the echidna rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Rosa echidna is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
