Retinispora obtusa var. aurea

Retinispora obtusa var. aurea, commonly known as the golden retinispora, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that exhibits a vibrant golden-yellow hue, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use as a hedge or ornamental plant in gardens.

Retinispora obtusa var. aurea is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
