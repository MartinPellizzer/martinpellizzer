Rhododendron kiangsiense

Rhododendron kiangsiense, commonly known as the Kiangsi rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Jiangxi Province.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in suitable regions.

Rhododendron kiangsiense is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
