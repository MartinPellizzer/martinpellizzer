Rosa afzeliana f. myriodonta

Rosa afzeliana f. myriodonta, commonly known as the Myriodonta rose, is a horticultural form of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate drainage.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to different climates.
