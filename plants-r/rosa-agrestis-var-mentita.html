Rosa agrestis var. mentita

Rosa agrestis var. mentita, commonly known as the Mentita rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are well adapted to dry conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and low-maintenance plant suitable for various climates.
