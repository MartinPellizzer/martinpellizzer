Rebutia rutiliflora

Rebutia rutiliflora, commonly known as the golden-flowered rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Rebutia rutiliflora is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and ornamental value.
