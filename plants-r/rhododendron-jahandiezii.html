Rhododendron jahandiezii

Rhododendron jahandiezii, commonly known as the Persian rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude forests and shrublands, preferring cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its flowers range in color from pink to red, adding vibrant visual interest to its natural habitat.

Rhododendron jahandiezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
