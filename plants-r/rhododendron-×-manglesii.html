Rhododendron × manglesii

Rhododendron × manglesii, commonly known as the Mangles' rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its botanical features include thick, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a landscape plant.
