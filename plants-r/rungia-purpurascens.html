Rungia purpurascens

Rungia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple passionvine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, featuring heart-shaped leaves and showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal. Rungia purpurascens thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable environments.
