Rosa × scotophylla

Rosa × scotophylla, commonly known as the moonflower rose, is a hybrid species of rose that combines traits from various Rosa species.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night, making it a unique addition to gardens. The plant typically has thorned stems and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its nocturnal blooming habit, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
