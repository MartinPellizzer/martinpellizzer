Rosa biserrata f. oenensis

Rosa biserrata f. oenensis, commonly known as the Oenensis rose, is a botanical hybrid native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with serrated edges, typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
