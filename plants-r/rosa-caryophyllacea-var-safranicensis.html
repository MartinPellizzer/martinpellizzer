Rosa caryophyllacea var. safranicensis

Rosa caryophyllacea var. safranicensis, commonly known as the Safranien rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, thorny stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal climates.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and unique floral characteristics.
