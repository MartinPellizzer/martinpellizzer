Rhynchospora guaramacalensis

Rhynchospora guaramacalensis, commonly known as the Guaramacal sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in Venezuela and parts of Colombia. This perennial herb typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and along riverbanks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and birds.
