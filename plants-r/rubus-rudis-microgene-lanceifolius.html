Rubus rudis microgene lanceifolius

Rubus rudis microgene lanceifolius, commonly known as the slender raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thin, lance-shaped leaves and small, red berries that ripen in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowers are white with five petals and are arranged in clusters.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
