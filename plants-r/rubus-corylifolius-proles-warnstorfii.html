Rubus corylifolius proles warnstorfii

Rubus corylifolius proles warnstorfii, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry or hairy raspberry, is a species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature. It is native to East Asia and thrives in temperate climates.

Botanically, it is notable for its hairy foliage and its role in traditional Chinese medicine.
