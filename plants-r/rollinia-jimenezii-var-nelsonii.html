Rollinia jimenezii var. nelsonii

Rollinia jimenezii var. nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's rollinia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, broad-leafed structure. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a strong fragrance. Its fruit is a fleshy, spiny berry that contains multiple seeds.
