Rubus × hemichlorostachys

Rubus × hemichlorostachys, commonly known as the red-berried blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The berries are typically dark red to black in color and are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its edible fruits.
