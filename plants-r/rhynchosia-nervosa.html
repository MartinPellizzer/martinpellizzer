Rhynchosia nervosa

Rhynchosia nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined snout bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features slender, twining stems and pinnately compound leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated pods containing several elliptical seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands.

Rhynchosia nervosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
