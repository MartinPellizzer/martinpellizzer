Ranunculus auricomus subsp. supramedialis

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

supramedialis, commonly known as the Alpine buttercup, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family. It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly the Alps.

This subspecies is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers and trifoliate leaves. It thrives in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high altitudes.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season.
