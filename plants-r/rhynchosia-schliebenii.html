Rhynchosia schliebenii

Rhynchosia schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant bears cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands.

Rhynchosia schliebenii is valued for its nutritional seeds and is used in traditional agriculture and cuisine.
