Rosa biondii

Rosa biondii, commonly known as the blond rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers and thorny stems. Rosa biondii typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental species within the rose genus.
