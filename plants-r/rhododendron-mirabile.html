Rhododendron mirabile

Rhododendron mirabile, commonly known as the magnificent rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive yellow or red throat. The plant produces leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments with partial shade.

Rhododendron mirabile is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
