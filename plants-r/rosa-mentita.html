Rosa mentita

Rosa mentita, commonly known as the Mentha rose, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Mentha species.

It is characterized by its aromatic foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Rosa mentita is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of climates.
