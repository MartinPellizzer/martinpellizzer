Rosa communis var. senticosoides

Rosa communis var. senticosoides, commonly known as the prickly rose, is a perennial shrub native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant produces rose hips, which are the fruit containing the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often found in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal uses.
