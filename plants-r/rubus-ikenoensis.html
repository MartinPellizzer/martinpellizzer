Rubus ikenoensis

Rubus ikenoensis, commonly known as the Ikeno bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Japan, specifically found in the Kii Peninsula region. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruits, which are edible, develop into red, hairy berries.

Rubus ikenoensis is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream sides.
