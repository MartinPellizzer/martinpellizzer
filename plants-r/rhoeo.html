Rhoeo

Rhoeo, commonly known as the "everlasting" plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are often variegated with shades of white, pink, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist soil.

Rhoeo is known for its ability to retain its vibrant colors for extended periods, hence its common name.
