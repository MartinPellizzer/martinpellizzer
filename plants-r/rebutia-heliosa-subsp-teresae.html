Rebutia heliosa subsp. teresae

Rebutia heliosa subsp.

teresae, commonly known as the Sunburst Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It features a short, rounded stem with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months.

The plant has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the soil surface. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
