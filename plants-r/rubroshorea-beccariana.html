Rubroshorea beccariana

Rubroshorea beccariana, commonly known as the redshorea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its reddish bark and elongated leaves. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and is known for its hard, durable wood. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by woody fruits containing seeds.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
