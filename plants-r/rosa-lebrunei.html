Rosa lebrunei

Rosa lebrunei, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces red, hairy fruits called hips. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Rosa lebrunei is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
