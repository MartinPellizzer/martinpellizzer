Rosa cariotii

Rosa cariotii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals. The plant produces hips, which are the fruit containing numerous seeds. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and blooms.

Rosa cariotii is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
