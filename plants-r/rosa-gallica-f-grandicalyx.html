Rosa gallica f. grandicalyx

Rosa gallica f. grandicalyx, commonly known as the French rose or the old garden rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa gallica species.

It is characterized by its deep red, semi-double flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is native to Europe and has been cultivated for centuries for its ornamental value.

This rose is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
