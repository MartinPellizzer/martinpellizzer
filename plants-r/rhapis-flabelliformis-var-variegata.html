Rhapis flabelliformis var. variegata

Rhapis flabelliformis var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated lady palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features gracefully arching, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, creating a visually appealing foliage. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

This ornamental plant is popular in indoor gardening for its aesthetic value and low maintenance requirements.
