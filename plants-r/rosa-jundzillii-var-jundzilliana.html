Rosa jundzillii var. jundzilliana

Rosa jundzillii var. jundzilliana, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its botanical features include simple, ovate leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
