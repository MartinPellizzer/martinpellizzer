Rosa kamtchatica var. nitens

Rosa kamtchatica var. nitens, commonly known as the Kamchatka rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Russian Far East.

It features upright, thorny stems and produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is well adapted to cold climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer.
