Rhynchospora splendens f. laetevirens

Rhynchospora splendens f. laetevirens, commonly known as the Splendid False Spike-rush, is a horticultural form of the species Rhynchospora splendens.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
