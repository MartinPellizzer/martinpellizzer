Ranunculus oreophytus var. gunae

Ranunculus oreophytus var. gunae, commonly known as the Sierra larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, typically blue or purple flowers. The plant has palmately divided leaves and is often found in moist, rocky habitats. It is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
