Rosa acicularis subsp. acicularis

Rosa acicularis subsp.

acicularis, commonly known as the needleleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family. It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is a shrub that can grow up to 1 meter in height, with thorny stems and dense foliage.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
