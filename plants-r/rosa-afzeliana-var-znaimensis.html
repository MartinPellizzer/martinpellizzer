Rosa afzeliana var. znaimensis

Rosa afzeliana var. znaimensis, commonly known as the Znaim rose, is a rare and endemic species native to the Carpathian Mountains in Romania.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces showy, semi-double pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets with a slightly serrated margin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance and ecological significance.
