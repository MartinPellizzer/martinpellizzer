Rosa ciesielskii var. curvata

Rosa ciesielskii var. curvata, commonly known as the Curved Rosa, is a rare and unique variant of the Rosa ciesielskii species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curved petals that give it an unusual and ornamental appearance. This plant is native to specific regions in Central Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its botanical features include a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
