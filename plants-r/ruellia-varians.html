Ruellia varians

Ruellia varians, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Ruellia varians thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
