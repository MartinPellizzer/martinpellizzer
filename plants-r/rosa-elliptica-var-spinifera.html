Rosa elliptica var. spinifera

Rosa elliptica var. spinifera, commonly known as the spiny elliptic rose, is a shrub-like species native to parts of Asia.

It features thorny stems and elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental appeal and hardiness.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
