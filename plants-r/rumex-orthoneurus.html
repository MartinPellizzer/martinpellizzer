Rumex orthoneurus

Rumex orthoneurus, commonly known as the narrowleaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features upright, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, greenish-white inflorescences, and it produces triangular, two-lobed fruits.

Rumex orthoneurus is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in moist meadows.
