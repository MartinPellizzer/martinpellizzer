Rochea odoratissima

Rochea odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented rochea, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, fragrant white to pale pink flowers. The plant is known for its strong, sweet aroma, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Rochea odoratissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance in gardens and greenhouses.
