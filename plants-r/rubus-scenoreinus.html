Rubus scenoreinus

Rubus scenoreinus, commonly known as the alpine raspberry.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in China and the Himalayas. The plant produces small, red berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

Rubus scenoreinus is well adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
