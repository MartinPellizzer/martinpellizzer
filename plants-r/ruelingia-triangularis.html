Ruelingia triangularis

Ruelingia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular euphorbia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its triangular-shaped stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
