Rubia peregrina var. dalmatica

Rubia peregrina var. dalmatica, commonly known as Dalmatian bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers bloom in early summer.

Dalmatian bugloss is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
