Rovaeanthus

Rovaeanthus, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Rovaeanthus thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its roots are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
