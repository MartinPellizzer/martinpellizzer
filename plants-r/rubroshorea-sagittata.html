Rubroshorea sagittata

Rubroshorea sagittata, commonly known as the red-veined arrowleaf, is a striking plant characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves with prominent red veins.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of yellow or orange.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, Rubroshorea sagittata is a sought-after species in horticulture and herbal practices.
