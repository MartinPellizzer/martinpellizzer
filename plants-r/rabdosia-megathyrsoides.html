Rabdosia megathyrsoides

Rabdosia megathyrsoides, commonly known as the blue-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its most distinctive feature is its clusters of sky-blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
