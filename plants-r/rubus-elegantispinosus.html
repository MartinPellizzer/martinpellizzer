Rubus elegantispinosus

Rubus elegantispinosus, commonly known as the elegant thorny raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its slender, spiny stems and clusters of white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
