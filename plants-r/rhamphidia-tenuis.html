Rhamphidia tenuis

Rhamphidia tenuis, commonly known as the slender tailed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.
