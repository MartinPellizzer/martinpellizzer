Rubus obscurus microgene delicaticaulis

Rubus obscurus microgene delicaticaulis, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

This plant is a species of bramble characterized by its small, dark berries and thorny stems. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and thickets. The leaves are elliptical with a delicate, finely toothed margin.

Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it produces small, edible fruits that are less common than those of other Rubus species.
