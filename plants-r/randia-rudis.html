Randia rudis

Randia rudis, commonly known as the slender randia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 13,000 species of flowering plants. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in cymes, and it produces small, round berries that are edible.
