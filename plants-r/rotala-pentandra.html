Rotala pentandra

Rotala pentandra, commonly known as the spiked waterwheel plant, is a floating aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features whorled leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy surface. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are often borne in clusters. Its root system helps stabilize the substrate in freshwater environments.

Rotala pentandra is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
