Rhododendron carneum

Rhododendron carneum, commonly known as the pink rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron carneum prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.
