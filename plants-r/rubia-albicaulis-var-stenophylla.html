Rubia albicaulis var. stenophylla

Rubia albicaulis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved madder, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species known for their medicinal and dye-producing properties. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, typically appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

The roots of this plant contain alkaloids and have historically been used in traditional medicine and natural dye production.
