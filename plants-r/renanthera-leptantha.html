Renanthera leptantha

Renanthera leptantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. Its leaves are typically elongated and grass-like, while the flowers display a range of colors, often with a distinctive yellow lip.

Renanthera leptantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
