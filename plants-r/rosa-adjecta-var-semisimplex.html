Rosa adjecta var. semisimplex

Rosa adjecta var. semisimplex, commonly known as the semi-simplex rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with a rounded growth habit.

It features glossy, dark green foliage and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its simple, thornless stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

This variety is prized for its manageable size and prolonged blooming period.
