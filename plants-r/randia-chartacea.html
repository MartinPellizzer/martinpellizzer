Randia chartacea

Randia chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf randia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are thick and papery in texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. Randia chartacea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
