Rosa mutans

Rosa mutans, commonly known as the variable rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Afghanistan. The plant is characterized by its variable flower color, which can range from white to pink and even red, depending on environmental conditions. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a serrated margin, and its stems are thorny.

Rosa mutans is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
