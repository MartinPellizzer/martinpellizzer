Roegneria thoroldiana var. laxiuscula

Roegneria thoroldiana var. laxiuscula, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a cool-season grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fine, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear fluffy, pale green to golden florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
