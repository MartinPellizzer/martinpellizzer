Rhododendron lanatum var. luciferum

Rhododendron lanatum var. luciferum, commonly known as the Lucifer Rhododendron, is a unique variant of the lanatum species.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. Native to specific regions in Asia, this plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and partial shade. Its botanical features make it a striking addition to gardens and natural landscapes.

The variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to certain horticultural conditions.
