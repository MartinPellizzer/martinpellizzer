Ruellia nummularia

Ruellia nummularia, commonly known as silverleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped, silver-velvet leaves that give it its common name. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple and bloom in the summer months.

Ruellia nummularia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
