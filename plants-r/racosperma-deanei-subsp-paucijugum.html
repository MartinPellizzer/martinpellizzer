Racosperma deanei subsp. paucijugum

Racosperma deanei subsp.

paucijugum, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a shrub native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that appear in clustered spikes.

The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
