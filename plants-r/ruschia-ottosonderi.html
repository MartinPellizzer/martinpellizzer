Ruschia ottosonderi

Ruschia ottosonderi, commonly known as the flame flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
