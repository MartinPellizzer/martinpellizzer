Radinocion flexuosum

Radinocion flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible radinocion, is a unique plant species characterized by its serpentine growth pattern.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features slender, curling stems that can twist and turn, giving it a distinctive, almost whimsical appearance. Its leaves are small, lance-shaped, and arranged spirally along the stem.

Radinocion flexuosum is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils, making it a resilient and adaptable species.
