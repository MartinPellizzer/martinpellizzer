Rivea Corymbosa Var. Mollissima

Rivea corymbosa var. mollissima is a striking climbing plant known for its velvety foliage and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to tropical regions of South America, this variety thrives in warm, humid environments with plenty of sunlight. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and hanging baskets. The plant's soft, hairy leaves give it a distinctive texture that sets it apart from other members of the Rivea genus. Cultivated for both its beauty and its ability to add a lush, exotic feel to any space, it remains a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
