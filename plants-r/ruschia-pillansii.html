Ruschia pillansii

Ruschia pillansii, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clusters of bright orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually blue-green in color and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Ruschia pillansii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
