Rebutia fischeriana

Rebutia fischeriana, commonly known as the Fischer's moon cactus, is a low-growing, compact cactus native to South America.

It features a swollen, spherical stem that is often brightly colored, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its compact size and vibrant appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
