Rosa mollis f. areschougii

Rosa mollis f. areschougii, commonly known as the Areschoug's soft rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its semi-climbing growth habit and produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant features soft, fuzzy foliage that gives it its common name, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various climatic conditions.
