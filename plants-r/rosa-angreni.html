Rosa angreni

Rosa angreni, commonly known as the Armenian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for centuries.

Rosa angreni is also known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh climates.
