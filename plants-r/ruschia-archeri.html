Ruschia archeri

Ruschia archeri, commonly known as the red-leaf ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or purple in color, which help it store water and protect against intense sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
