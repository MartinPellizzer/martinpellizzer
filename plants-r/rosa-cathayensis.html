Rosa cathayensis

Rosa cathayensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its stems are often armed with thorns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Rosa cathayensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
