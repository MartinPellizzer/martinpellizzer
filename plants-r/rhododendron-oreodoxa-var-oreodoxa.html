Rhododendron oreodoxa var. oreodoxa

Rhododendron oreodoxa var. oreodoxa, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows at high elevations, often in moist, acidic soils. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
