Ruschia odontocalyx

Ruschia odontocalyx, commonly known as the toothed-ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a slightly wavy edge that gives them a distinctive appearance.

Ruschia odontocalyx is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
