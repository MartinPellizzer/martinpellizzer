Rosa murithii

Rosa murithii, commonly known as the Himalayan rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found at high altitudes.

Rosa murithii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
