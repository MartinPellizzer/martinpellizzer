Rubus hainanensis

Rubus hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Rubus.

It is native to the tropical regions of southern China, particularly Hainan Island. This shrub typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and the fruit is a red, hairy berry that develops from the flower.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
