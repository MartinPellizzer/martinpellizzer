Robiquetia lyonii

Robiquetia lyonii, commonly known as Lyon's rock cress, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are basal, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Robiquetia lyonii thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
