Russelia teres

Russelia teres, commonly known as the velvetleaf russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Russelia teres is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
