Rosa × martinii

Rosa × martinii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is native to China and is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. Its foliage consists of pinnate leaves with serrated edges, and it has thorny stems typical of many rose species.

Rosa × martinii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
