Rosa communis var. brizoniana

Rosa communis var. brizoniana, commonly known as the Brizonian rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is well adapted to harsh climates, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its hardy nature and long blooming period.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
