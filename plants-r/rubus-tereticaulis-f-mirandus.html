Rubus tereticaulis f. mirandus

Rubus tereticaulis f. mirandus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

This plant is a flowering shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shady areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in native landscaping.
