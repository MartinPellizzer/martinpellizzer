Rosmarinus eriocalyx var. pallescens

Rosmarinus eriocalyx var. pallescens, commonly known as silver rosemary, is a variety of the rosemary plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow and aromatic, similar to other rosemary species, but with a more pronounced silver hue. It produces small, pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
