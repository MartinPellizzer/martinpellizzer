Rosa viscaria var. ovata

Rosa viscaria var. ovata, commonly known as the oval evergreen rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and densely arranged along its stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in spring. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a decorative hedge due to its dense growth habit.

Rosa viscaria var. ovata is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
