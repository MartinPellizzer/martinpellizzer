Rabdosia lophanthoides var. brachyantha

Rabdosia lophanthoides var. brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage and floral display.
