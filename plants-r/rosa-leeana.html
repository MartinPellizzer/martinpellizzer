Rosa leeana

Rosa leeana, commonly known as the Leeana rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Rosa leeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
