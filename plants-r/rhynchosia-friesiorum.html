Rhynchosia friesiorum

Rhynchosia friesiorum, commonly known as the African blue bean, is a tropical legume native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are often clustered in racemes. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in traditional agriculture.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and useful in sustainable farming practices.
