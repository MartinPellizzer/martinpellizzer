Rhododendron hanceanum

Rhododendron hanceanum, commonly known as the Hance rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, pink to lavender flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

However, it can be invasive in certain areas if not managed properly.
