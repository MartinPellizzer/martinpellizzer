Rhododendron amoenum

Rhododendron amoenum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has dark green, leathery leaves. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

The flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to the landscape.
