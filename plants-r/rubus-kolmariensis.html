Rubus kolmariensis

Rubus kolmariensis, commonly known as the Kolmari raspberry, is a species of bramble native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and rocky areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
