Rubus × fulgentissimus

Rubus × fulgentissimus, commonly known as the golden raspberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-red fruits, which are typically smaller than those of common raspberries. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges and thrives in temperate climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible berries.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the plant is known for its vigorous growth habit.
