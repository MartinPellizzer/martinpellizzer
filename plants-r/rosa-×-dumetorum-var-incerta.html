Rosa × dumetorum var. incerta

Rosa × dumetorum var. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, often pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a low shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, and it produces oval, red-brown fruits called hips.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
