Rosa × dumetorum var. bicolor

Rosa × dumetorum var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, making it suitable for hedging or ornamental use. The plant produces fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinct bicolor pattern on the petals. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
