Restrepia chameleon

Restrepia chameleon, commonly known as the chameleon orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, often colorful flower that can change hues depending on environmental conditions, giving it its name. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces small, intricate flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Restrepia chameleon is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
