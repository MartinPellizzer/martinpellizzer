Rosa × segretii

Rosa × segretii, commonly known as the Segretii rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa × centifolia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is typically shrub-like, with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Rosa × segretii is often appreciated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
