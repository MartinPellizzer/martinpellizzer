Restio stereocaulis

Restio stereocaulis, commonly known as the tufted restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Restio stereocaulis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
