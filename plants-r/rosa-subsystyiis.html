Rosa subsystyiis

Rosa subsystyiis, commonly known as the Armenian rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its thorny stems and compact form make it a hardy shrub suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Rosa subsystyiis is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil conditions.
