Rosa moutinii

Rosa moutinii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Rosa moutinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens in temperate climates.
