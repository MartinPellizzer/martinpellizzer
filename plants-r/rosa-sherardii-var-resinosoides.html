Rosa sherardii var. resinosoides

Rosa sherardii var. resinosoides, commonly known as the resinous shrub rose, is a variant of the shrub rose native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and its ability to thrive in harsh, rocky environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its resilience in challenging climates.
