Rhynchospora subulirostris

Rhynchospora subulirostris, commonly known as the slender-beak sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in the southeastern United States.

Rhynchospora subulirostris plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
