Rosa gallicorepens

Rosa gallicorepens, commonly known as the French climbing rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, pink to white flowers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Rosa gallicorepens prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
