Rhodanthe gossypina

Rhodanthe gossypina, commonly known as the silky centipede plant, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

Rhodanthe gossypina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
