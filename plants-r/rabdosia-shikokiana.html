Rabdosia shikokiana

Rabdosia shikokiana, commonly known as Shikoku Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia, particularly found in Japan and China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with square stems and opposite leaves. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or blue, arranged in whorled clusters.

Rabdosia shikokiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
