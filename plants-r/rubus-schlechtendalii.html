Rubus schlechtendalii

Rubus schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal's raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to China, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
