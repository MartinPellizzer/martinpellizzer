Rollandia lanceolata var. viridiflora

Rollandia lanceolata var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered lanceleaf rollandia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green, with a prominent midrib. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique coloration and foliage.
