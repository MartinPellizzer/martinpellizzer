Rosa jackii var. pilosa

Rosa jackii var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

Rosa jackii var. pilosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
