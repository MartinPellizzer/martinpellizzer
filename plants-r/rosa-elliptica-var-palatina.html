Rosa elliptica var. palatina

Rosa elliptica var. palatina, commonly known as the Palatinate rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to Europe.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in clusters, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and historical landscapes.
