Rhaphidophora korthalsii

Rhaphidophora korthalsii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features distinctive, striped leaves with a dark green background and lighter green to white veins, creating a zebra-like pattern. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has a compact, bushy form. Its leaves are glossy and can reach up to 30 centimeters in length, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Rhaphidophora korthalsii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
