Rhabdadenia lucida

Rhabdadenia lucida, commonly known as the shining rhabdadenia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
