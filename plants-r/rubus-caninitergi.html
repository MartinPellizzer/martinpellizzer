Rubus caninitergi

Rubus caninitergi, commonly known as the sharp-lobed blackberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically small and highly aromatic. Its leaves are palmately lobed with serrated edges, and the stems are covered in prickly thorns.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in shrublands and forest edges.
