Romanschulzia guerrerensis

Romanschulzia guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrero orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests at high elevations. Its botanical structure includes slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
