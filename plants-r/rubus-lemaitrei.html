Rubus lemaitrei

Rubus lemaitrei, commonly known as the Lemaitre raspberry, is a species of bramble native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for use in horticulture, it has attracted interest among plant enthusiasts.
