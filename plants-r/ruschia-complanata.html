Ruschia complanata

Ruschia complanata, commonly known as the flatleaf ruschia, is a perennial succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, flat, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for arid climates.
