Rhodamnia fordii

Rhodamnia fordii, commonly known as the Chinese rose or Ford's rhododendron, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, pink to red flowers. Rhodamnia fordii prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.
