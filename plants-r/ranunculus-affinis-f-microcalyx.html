Ranunculus affinis f. microcalyx

Ranunculus affinis f. microcalyx, commonly known as the miniature buttercup, is a botanical variant of the common buttercup.

It is characterized by its smaller, more compact growth habit compared to the typical Ranunculus affinis. The plant features delicate, bright yellow flowers with deeply divided petals, often adorned with a central greenish-black spot. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
