Rhododendron carringtoniae

Rhododendron carringtoniae, commonly known as the Carrington's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in high-altitude forests and rocky slopes. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink to purple flowers in the spring.
