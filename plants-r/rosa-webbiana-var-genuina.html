Rosa webbiana var. genuina

Rosa webbiana var. genuina, commonly known as the Webb's rose.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of northern Africa and the Middle East. The plant features thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are compound, with serrated leaflets, and it produces oval, red-hued hips in autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
