Renanthera breviflora

Renanthera breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Renanthera breviflora is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
