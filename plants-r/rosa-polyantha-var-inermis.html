Rosa polyantha var. inermis

Rosa polyantha var. inermis, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season. The plant features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often appearing in clusters. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the flowers.

It is well-suited for gardens and containers due to its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements.
