Rubus × pseudochingii

Rubus × pseudochingii, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color, similar to blackberries. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

This hybrid species exhibits traits from both blackberries and raspberries, making it unique in morphology and flavor.
