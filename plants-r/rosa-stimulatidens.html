Rosa stimulatidens

Rosa stimulatidens, commonly known as the stimulant rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are known for their strong, aromatic scent and are often used in perfumery.

Rosa stimulatidens is valued not only for its ornamental qualities but also for its historical use in traditional medicine.
