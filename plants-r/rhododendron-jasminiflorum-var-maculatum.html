Rhododendron jasminiflorum var. maculatum

Rhododendron jasminiflorum var. maculatum, commonly known as the spotted rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, large, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
