Rhynchosia Galactioides

Rhynchosia galactioides is a unique legume native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is known for its striking yellow flowers that resemble small stars. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. This plant is both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitats.
