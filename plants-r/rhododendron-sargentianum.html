Rhododendron sargentianum

Rhododendron sargentianum, commonly known as the Sargent rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in cool, moist climates and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

Rhododendron sargentianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
