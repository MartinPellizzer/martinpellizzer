Rytidosperma tenuius

Rytidosperma tenuius, commonly known as the slender wild oat, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Rytidosperma tenuius is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
