Rosa foliosa

Rosa foliosa, commonly known as the leafy rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves that are often divided into numerous leaflets. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Rosa foliosa is native to regions of Asia, particularly China, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
