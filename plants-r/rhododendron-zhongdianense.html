Rhododendron zhongdianense

Rhododendron zhongdianense, commonly known as the Zhongdian rhododendron, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in mountainous, acidic soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. It is often found in forested areas at elevations ranging from 2,500 to 4,000 meters.

Due to its unique habitat and ornamental value, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
