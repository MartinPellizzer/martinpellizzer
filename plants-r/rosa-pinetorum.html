Rosa pinetorum

Rosa pinetorum, commonly known as the pineapple rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen foliage and its distinctive, pineapple-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Rosa pinetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal climates.
