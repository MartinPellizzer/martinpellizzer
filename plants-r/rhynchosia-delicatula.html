Rhynchosia delicatula

Rhynchosia delicatula, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. Its seeds are often used in traditional cuisines and have nutritional value.

Rhynchosia delicatula is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in agroforestry systems.
