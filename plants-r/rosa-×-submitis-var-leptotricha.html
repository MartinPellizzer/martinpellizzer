Rosa × submitis var. leptotricha

Rosa × submitis var. leptotricha, commonly known as the slender-stemmed rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often arching stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are pinnately compound, with finely serrated leaflets, and the plant produces rose hips that are often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
