Rhinanthus deminutus

Rhinanthus deminutus, commonly known as the dwarf yellow-rattle, is a small, annual plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom from June to August. Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and declining species in many parts of its range.
