Ribes nigrum f. chlorocarpum

Ribes nigrum f. chlorocarpum, commonly known as the blackcurrant with green berries, is a botanical variant of the blackcurrant plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive green berries that remain unripe and do not turn the typical black color of the standard blackcurrant. The plant belongs to the family Grossulariaceae and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

This form is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique fruit coloration.
