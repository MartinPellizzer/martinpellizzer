Rosa afzeliana var. pseudolagenaria

Rosa afzeliana var. pseudolagenaria, commonly known as the African rose or the Cape rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. Its botanical characteristics include a woody structure and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
