Romulea komsbergensis

Romulea komsbergensis, commonly known as the Komsberg sand orchid, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, grass-like leaves and delicate, bell-shaped flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
