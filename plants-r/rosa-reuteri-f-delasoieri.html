Rosa reuteri f. delasoieri

Rosa reuteri f. delasoieri, commonly known as the Delasoi rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle blush. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces rose hips that are ornamental and edible. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its hardy nature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
