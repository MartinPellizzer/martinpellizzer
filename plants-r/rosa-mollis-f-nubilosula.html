Rosa mollis f. nubilosula

Rosa mollis f. nubilosula, commonly known as the clouded rose, is a hybrid form of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers that often appear in clusters. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, where it thrives in cooler, moist environments. Its foliage is covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
