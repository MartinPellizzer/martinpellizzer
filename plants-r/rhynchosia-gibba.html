Rhynchosia gibba

Rhynchosia gibba, commonly known as the spurred cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals, which give it its common name, and its trifoliate leaves. Rhynchosia gibba produces small, yellow or orange flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.
