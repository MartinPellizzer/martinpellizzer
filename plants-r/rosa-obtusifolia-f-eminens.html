Rosa obtusifolia f. eminens

Rosa obtusifolia f. eminens, commonly known as the elevated shrub rose, is a compact, shrub-like form of the species Rosa obtusifolia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features thorned stems and dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

This cultivar is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
