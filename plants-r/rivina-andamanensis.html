Rivina andamanensis

Rivina andamanensis, commonly known as the Andaman Rivina, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
