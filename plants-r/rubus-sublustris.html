Rubus sublustris

Rubus sublustris, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a species of bramble native to North America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright red berries that are typically harvested in the summer months. These berries are rich in antioxidants and are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Its botanical features make it an important part of the ecosystem in its native habitat.
