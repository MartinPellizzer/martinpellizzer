Rosa macrophylla var. hookeriana

Rosa macrophylla var. hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's rose, is a hybrid rose species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can be pink, red, or white, depending on the cultivar. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round interest in gardens.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.
