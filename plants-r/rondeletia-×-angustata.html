Rondeletia × angustata

Rondeletia × angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf rondeletia, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
