Rosa pissardii

Rosa pissardii, commonly known as the Pissard's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are often used in perfumery due to their strong, sweet scent.

Rosa pissardii is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its aromatic properties.
