Rhagodia deltophylla

Rhagodia deltophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed burr medic, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive burr-like seed pods. It is well adapted to drought conditions and has a deep root system for water retention.

Rhagodia deltophylla is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
