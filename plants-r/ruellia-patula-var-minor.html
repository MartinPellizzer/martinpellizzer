Ruellia patula var. minor

Ruellia patula var. minor, commonly known as small-leaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 12 to 18 inches. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a dark green color. Its flowers are tubular and come in shades of blue or purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
