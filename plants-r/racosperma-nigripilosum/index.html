Racosperma Nigripilosum

Racosperma nigripilosum is a unique flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive appearance. The plant features dark purple stems and hairy foliage, giving it a rugged look. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid regions. Despite its tough exterior, it plays an important role in its ecosystem.
