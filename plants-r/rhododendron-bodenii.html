Rhododendron bodenii

Rhododendron bodenii, commonly known as the Boden's rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of Nepal and India.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features large, showy flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron bodenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
