Rebutia pseudodeminuta f. albiseta

Rebutia pseudodeminuta f. albiseta, commonly known as the White-leafed Rebutia, is a rare and striking hybrid within the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and small, rounded stems that are often covered in a dense layer of white or pale green hairs. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the spring and early summer. Its unique foliage and floral display make it a prized addition to specialized cactus collections.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
