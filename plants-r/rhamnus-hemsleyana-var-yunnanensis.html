Rhamnus hemsleyana var. yunnanensis

Rhamnus hemsleyana var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan buckthorn, is a shrub native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by orange to reddish berries in the fall. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and seasonal fruit display.
