Rabdosia dakglayensis

Rabdosia dakglayensis, commonly known as the Yunnan blue sage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and spikes of blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
