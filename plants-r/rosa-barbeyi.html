Rosa barbeyi

Rosa barbeyi, commonly known as the Barbey rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Mexico. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne on long, slender stems. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often found in shaded, moist habitats.

Rosa barbeyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
