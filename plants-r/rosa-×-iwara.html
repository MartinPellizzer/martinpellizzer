Rosa × iwara

Rosa × iwara, commonly known as the Iwara rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Due to its beauty and fragrance, Rosa × iwara is often cultivated for both ornamental and aromatic purposes.
