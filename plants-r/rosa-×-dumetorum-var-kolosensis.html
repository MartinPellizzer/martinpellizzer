Rosa × dumetorum var. kolosensis

Rosa × dumetorum var. kolosensis, commonly known as the Kolosinskaya rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often grown as a decorative plant in temperate climates.
