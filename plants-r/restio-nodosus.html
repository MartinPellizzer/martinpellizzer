Restio nodosus

Restio nodosus, commonly known as the knob restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often tinged with red or purple. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinctive knobby base, giving it its common name.
