Ruscus × murtae

Ruscus × murtae, commonly known as evergreen euonym.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits.

Ruscus × murtae is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
