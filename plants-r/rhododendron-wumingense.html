Rhododendron wumingense

Rhododendron wumingense, commonly known as the Wuming rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Guangxi and Guangdong provinces.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for horticultural and ecological studies.
