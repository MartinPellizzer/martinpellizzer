Rosa mollis f. vingensis

Rosa mollis f. vingensis, commonly known as the Vingensis rose, is a unique hybrid cultivar within the rose family.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in a range of pastel shades. This plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of approximately 1 to 2 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and subtle fragrance, making it a prized addition to botanical gardens and private collections.
