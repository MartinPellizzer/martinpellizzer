Rosa watsonii

Rosa watsonii, commonly known as Watson's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained, loamy soils.

Rosa watsonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
