Rosa hartmanii

Rosa hartmanii, commonly known as Hartman's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it produces red, hairy fruits known as hips.

Rosa hartmanii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
