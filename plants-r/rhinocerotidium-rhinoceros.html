Rhinocerotidium rhinoceros

Rhinocerotidium rhinoceros, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy leaves and striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white.
