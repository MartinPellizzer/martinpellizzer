Rubia Peregrina Var. Splendens

Rubia peregrina var. splendens is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant red flowers and unique appearance. Native to specific regions in the Mediterranean, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This variant of Rubia peregrina stands out due to its more intense coloration compared to its common relatives. Its beauty has made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its allure, it remains elusive and hard to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
