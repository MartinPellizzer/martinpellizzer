Retama webbii

Retama webbii, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in arid and rocky soils. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Retama webbii is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
