Rosa sphaerica

Rosa sphaerica, commonly known as the globe rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its spherical, tightly packed flower heads that resemble small globes, hence its common name.

Its flowers are typically pink or white, and it produces fragrant, edible hips that are rich in vitamin C. Rosa sphaerica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for both garden use and traditional medicinal purposes.
