Ribes tubulosum

Ribes tubulosum, commonly known as the slender gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its tubular flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, yellow or orange berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Ribes tubulosum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woods or rocky slopes.
