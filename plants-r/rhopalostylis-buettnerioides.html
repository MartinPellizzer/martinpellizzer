Rhopalostylis buettnerioides

Rhopalostylis buettnerioides, commonly known as the Buettners' palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Pacific islands, particularly found in French Polynesia. The plant is characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. It grows in coastal and lowland areas, often in sandy soils.

This palm is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
