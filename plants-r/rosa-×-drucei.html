Rosa × drucei

Rosa × drucei, commonly known as the Druce rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its semi-climbing growth habit and produces fragrant, single or double flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces hip fruits that are typically red or orange. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × drucei is native to Europe and is well adapted to temperate climates.
