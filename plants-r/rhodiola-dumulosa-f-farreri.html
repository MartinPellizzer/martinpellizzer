Rhodiola dumulosa f. farreri

Rhodiola dumulosa f. farreri, commonly known as the Fareri's rhodiola, is a succulent plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rosette-forming leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid environments. Due to its unique adaptations, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
