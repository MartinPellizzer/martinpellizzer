Rosa andrzeiowskii

Rosa andrzeiowskii, commonly known as the Andrzeiowski rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a fragrant aroma. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Rosa andrzeiowskii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
