Rosa mareyana

Rosa mareyana, commonly known as the Mareyana rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with oval-shaped leaflets that have a slightly serrated edge.

Rosa mareyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
