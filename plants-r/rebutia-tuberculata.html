Rebutia tuberculata

Rebutia tuberculata, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in spiny, fleshy tubercles. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Rebutia tuberculata is often grown as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
