Rosa Hybrida F. Fimbriata

Rosa hybrida f. fimbriata is a unique cultivar of the common rose known for its distinctive fringed petals. This ornamental plant is prized for its intricate, lace-like appearance that adds visual interest to gardens. The fringing along the edges of its blooms creates a soft, flowing effect that sets it apart from other rose varieties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its beauty and adaptability have made it a favorite in both landscape design and floral arrangements.
