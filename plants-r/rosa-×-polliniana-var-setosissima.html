Rosa × polliniana var. setosissima

Rosa × polliniana var. setosissima, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and continuous flowering make it a valuable addition to gardens in temperate climates.
