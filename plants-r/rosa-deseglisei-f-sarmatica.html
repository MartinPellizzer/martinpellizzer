Rosa deseglisei f. sarmatica

Rosa deseglisei f. sarmatica, commonly known as the Sarmatian Rosa, is a botanical variant of the Rosa deseglisei species.

It is native to the Caucasus region and is characterized by its robust growth and dense foliage. The plant typically features thorny stems and produces showy, pink to red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in temperate climates.
