Rhynchospora discolor

Rhynchospora discolor, commonly known as the pale spike-rush, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and produces small, pale green to reddish flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. Rhynchospora discolor plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and birds.
