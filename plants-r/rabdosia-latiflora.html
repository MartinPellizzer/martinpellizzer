Rabdosia latiflora

Rabdosia latiflora, commonly known as broadleaf rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or blue, arranged in dense clusters.

Rabdosia latiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
