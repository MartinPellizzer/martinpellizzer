Rupicapnos numidica subsp. numidica

Rupicapnos numidica subsp.

numidica, commonly known as the Numidian rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, evergreen leaves that are leathery and densely packed.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping.
