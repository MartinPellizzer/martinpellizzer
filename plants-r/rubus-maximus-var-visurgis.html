Rubus maximus var. visurgis

Rubus maximus var. visurgis, commonly known as the Visurgis blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black berries that are typically larger than those of other Rubus species. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges and has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

The fruit is valued for its intense flavor and is used in both culinary and commercial applications.
