Rosa multiflora var. taoyuanensis

Rosa multiflora var. taoyuanensis, commonly known as the Taoyuan rose, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable in native habitats.
