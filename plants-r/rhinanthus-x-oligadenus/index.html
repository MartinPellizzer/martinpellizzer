Rhinanthus × Oligadenus

Rhinanthus × oligadenus is a hybrid plant that combines traits from two parent species, Rhinanthus minor and Rhinanthus pseudofloridus. It is commonly found in grasslands and open habitats across Europe. This plant is known for its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its hybrid nature makes it unique in the genus Rhinanthus. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in ecological studies due to its rarity and limited distribution.
