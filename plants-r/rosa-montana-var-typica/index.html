Rosa Montana Var. Typica

Rosa montana var. typica is a hardy shrub known for its vibrant pink and white blooms. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. This variety is prized for its fragrance and ornamental value in gardens. It is also resistant to common rose diseases, making it a reliable choice for gardeners. Its adaptability and beauty make it a popular selection for both landscaping and cut flower arrangements.
