Rubus moluccanus var. swinhoei

Rubus moluccanus var. swinhoei, commonly known as the Moluccan raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
