Restrepia driessenii

Restrepia driessenii, commonly known as the Driessen's restrepia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white to pale yellow blooms that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Restrepia driessenii is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
