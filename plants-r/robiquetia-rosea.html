Robiquetia rosea

Robiquetia rosea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Robiquetia rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
