Rubus discolor f. polyanthemus

Rubus discolor f. polyanthemus, commonly known as the showy blackberry.

This variant is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its larger, more showy flowers compared to the typical blackberry. It features trifoliate leaves and produces edible berries that are similar in appearance to common blackberries. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in both beauty and edible crops.
