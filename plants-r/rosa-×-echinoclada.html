Rosa × echinoclada

Rosa × echinoclada, commonly known as the prickly rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
