Riedelia fulgens

Riedelia fulgens, commonly known as the fiery riedelia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Riedelia fulgens is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
