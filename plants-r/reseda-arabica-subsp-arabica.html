Reseda arabica subsp. arabica

Reseda arabica subsp.

arabica, commonly known as yellowrocket, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden environments.

Its rapid growth and ability to reseed itself make it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
