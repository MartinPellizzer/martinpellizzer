Ravenala

Ravenala, commonly known as the "Peaceful Forest" or "Bishop's Plume," is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that radiate outward in a crown-like formation. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is known for its ornamental value. Ravenala produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters near the base of the plant.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
