Rosa cinnamomea var. gemella

Rosa cinnamomea var. gemella, commonly known as the cinnamon rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive cinnamon-scented foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The leaves are ovate with a smooth texture and a subtle, aromatic scent. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its unique fragrance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscape design.
