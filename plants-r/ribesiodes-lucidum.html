Ribesiodes lucidum

Ribesiodes lucidum, commonly known as the shining ribes, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant is characterized by its smooth, shiny bark and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.

The fruit, which is edible, is typically a small, red or black berry that ripens in late summer.
