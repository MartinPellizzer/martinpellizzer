Raphia gilletii

Raphia gilletii, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a large tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its massive, fan-shaped leaves that can reach lengths of up to 6 meters. The plant produces large, yellowish-orange inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers. Its trunk is typically smooth and fibrous, with a height that can exceed 15 meters.

Raphia gilletii is well adapted to wet, humid environments and plays an important role in its native ecosystem.
