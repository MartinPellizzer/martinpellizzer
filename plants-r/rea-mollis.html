Rea mollis

Rea mollis, commonly known as the silky oaks, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often silvery-white in color and covered with a fine, silky hair. The plant produces small, pea-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. Rea mollis is native to Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
