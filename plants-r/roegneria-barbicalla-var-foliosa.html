Roegneria barbicalla var. foliosa

Roegneria barbicalla var. foliosa, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.
