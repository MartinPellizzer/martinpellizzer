Rosa communis var. boulluana

Rosa communis var. boulluana, commonly known as the Boullueana rose, is a cultivar prized for its distinctive floral characteristics.

It features large, semi-double blooms with a deep pink hue that gradually fades to a softer tone at the edges. The plant is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blossoms.

This variety is well-suited for garden borders and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.
