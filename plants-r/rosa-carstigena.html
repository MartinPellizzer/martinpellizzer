Rosa carstigena

Rosa carstigena, commonly known as the Karoo rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has needle-like leaves that are adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment. Rosa carstigena is often found growing in rocky soils and along riverbanks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its challenging growing requirements.
