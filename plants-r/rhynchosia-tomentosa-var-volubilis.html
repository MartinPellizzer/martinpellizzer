Rhynchosia tomentosa var. volubilis

Rhynchosia tomentosa var. volubilis, commonly known as the hairy sicklepod, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems that enable it to climb and spread along vegetation. The plant produces small, yellow,蝶形花 (butterfly-shaped) flowers, typical of the Fabaceae family. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets covered in a dense, woolly pubescence.

The fruit is a slender, curved pod that splits open to release its seeds.
