Rosa × marshallii

Rosa × marshallii, commonly known as the Marshall rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant has a bushy habit with thorned stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × marshallii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
