Rosa chinensis f. mutabilis

Rosa chinensis f. mutabilis, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that can change color from white to pink to red as they bloom. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features dark green, glossy leaves. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its continuous blooming habit and adaptability to various climates.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and floral arrangements.
