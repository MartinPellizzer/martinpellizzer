Rosa × recognita var. pilipes

Rosa × recognita var. pilipes, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed Rosa × recognita, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its hairy stems and foliage.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, often with a layered appearance. It is native to China and is cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
