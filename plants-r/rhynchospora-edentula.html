Rhynchospora edentula

Rhynchospora edentula, commonly known as the toothless spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, preferring moist to saturated soils. Rhynchospora edentula plays a role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
