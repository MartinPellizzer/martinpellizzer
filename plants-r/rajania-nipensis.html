Rajania nipensis

Rajania nipensis, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on tree trunks or branches in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its humid environment.

Rajania nipensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in Filipino horticulture.
