Ranunculus montellii

Ranunculus montellii, commonly known as the Sierra larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, blue to purple flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and meadows. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the stems are often hairy.

Ranunculus montellii is an herbaceous perennial that blooms in late spring to early summer.
