Reichardia arabica

Reichardia arabica, commonly known as the Arabian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Reichardia arabica is often found in rocky crevices and along coastal dunes, showcasing its resilience to extreme climatic conditions.
