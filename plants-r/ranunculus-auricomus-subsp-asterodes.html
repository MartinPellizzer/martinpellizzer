Ranunculus auricomus subsp. asterodes

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

asterodes, commonly known as the starry buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. It features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers with prominent, star-shaped petals.

The subspecies is distinguished by its larger, more showy flowers compared to the typical R. auricomus. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
