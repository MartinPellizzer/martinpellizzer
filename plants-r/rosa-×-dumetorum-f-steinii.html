Rosa × dumetorum f. steinii

Rosa × dumetorum f. steinii, commonly known as the Stein's shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, making it suitable for hedging or ornamental use. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, often with a subtle scent. It is native to regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its hardy nature, it is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
