Rosa tomentella var. carionii

Rosa tomentella var. carionii, commonly known as the Carpathian rose, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in native plant gardens.
