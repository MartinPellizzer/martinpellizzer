Rosa × dumetorum var. fastigiata

Rosa × dumetorum var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright shrub rose, is a compact, bushy plant with a unique upright growth habit.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, rounded shape. The flowers are usually pale pink to white, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa multiflora and Rosa rugosa.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in various garden settings.
