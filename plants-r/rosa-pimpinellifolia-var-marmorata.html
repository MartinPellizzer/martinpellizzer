Rosa pimpinellifolia var. marmorata

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. marmorata, commonly known as the marble pink rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a marbled pattern on the petals. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal bloom.
