Rubus sikkimensis var. sikkimensis

Rubus sikkimensis var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim raspberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
