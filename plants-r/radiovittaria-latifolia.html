Radiovittaria latifolia

Radiovittaria latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Radiovittaria latifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
