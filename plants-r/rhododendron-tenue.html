Rhododendron tenue

Rhododendron tenue, commonly known as the slender rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are typically clustered at the ends of its branches. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a sought-after species in horticulture and conservation efforts.
