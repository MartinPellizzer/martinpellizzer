Rhododendron × beimaense

Rhododendron × beimaense, commonly known as the Beima rhododendron, is a hybrid plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments at high altitudes. Its botanical features include elongated, leathery leaves and a compact, shrub-like growth habit.

Rhododendron × beimaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
