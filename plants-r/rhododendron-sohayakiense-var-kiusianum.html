Rhododendron sohayakiense var. kiusianum

Rhododendron sohayakiense var. kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its showy, bell-shaped flowers. The blooms, which range in color from pink to red, are typically borne in clusters and appear in early spring. This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to woodland gardens.
