Rhamnus laoshanensis

Rhamnus laoshanensis, commonly known as the Laoshan buckthorn, is a shrub native to China, specifically found in the Laoshan Mountains.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by small, fleshy berries that are typically reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
