Ranunculus auricomus subsp. stenocolpus

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

stenocolpus, commonly known as the narrowleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it is often found in calcareous soils.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Ranunculus auricomus.
