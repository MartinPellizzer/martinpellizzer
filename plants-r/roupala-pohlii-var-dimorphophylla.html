Roupala pohlii var. dimorphophylla

Roupala pohlii var. dimorphophylla, commonly known as the silver fern, is a unique plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored leaves that display a mix of silver and green hues. The plant's foliage consists of two distinct leaf types, known as dimorphophylla, which are arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
