Ranunculus pulchellus var. yinshanensis

Ranunculus pulchellus var. yinshanensis, commonly known as the Yinshan buttercup, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
