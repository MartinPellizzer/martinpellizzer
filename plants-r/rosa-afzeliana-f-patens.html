Rosa afzeliana f. patens

Rosa afzeliana f. patens, commonly known as the spreading Rosa afzeliana, is a shrub-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces fragrant, single or double flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to spread via runners, making it suitable for ornamental and hedging purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
