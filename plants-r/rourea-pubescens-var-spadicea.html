Rourea pubescens var. spadicea

Rourea pubescens var. spadicea, commonly known as the Spadicea rourea, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, hairy foliage, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
