Rubus lowii var. panalabanensis

Rubus lowii var. panalabanensis, commonly known as the Panalaban raspberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
