Randia aculeata

Randia aculeata, commonly known as the spiny randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and small, clustered yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

Randia aculeata is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
