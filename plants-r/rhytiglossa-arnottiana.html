Rhytiglossa arnottiana

Rhytiglossa arnottiana, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in rainforests or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a yellow or green throat, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Rhytiglossa arnottiana is a perennial herb that plays a role in its native ecosystem by providing nectar for various pollinators.
