Rosa doluchanovii

Rosa doluchanovii, commonly known as the Doluchanov rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa doluchanovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
