Rosa schulzei

Rosa schulzei, commonly known as the Schulze's rose, is a species of shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Rosa schulzei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

It is also valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
