Rubus ocyriacus

Rubus ocyriacus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a shrub native to eastern North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that turn bright red when mature. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Rubus ocyriacus is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.
