Rosa spinosissima var. oligotricha

Rosa spinosissima var. oligotricha, commonly known as the thorny rose or the spiny Rosa, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets with a slightly serrated edge. This variety is often found in rocky or dry habitats, where it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient plant, suitable for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
