Rosa lucida var. alba

Rosa lucida var. alba, commonly known as the white form of the lucid rose, is a cultivar characterized by its pale pink to white flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of Asia. The plant typically features slender, thornless stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch to gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aesthetic beauty.
