Rosa × duffortii f. subarvensis

Rosa × duffortii f. subarvensis, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and is well adapted to alpine environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

The subspecies subarvensis is distinguished by its slightly smaller flowers and more compact form compared to other varieties of Rosa × duffortii.
