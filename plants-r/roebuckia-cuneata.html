Roebuckia cuneata

Roebuckia cuneata, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with red bracts, which are often mistaken for petals. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

The botanical features of Roebuckia cuneata make it an attractive ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
