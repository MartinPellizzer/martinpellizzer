Rhaganus lucidus

Rhaganus lucidus, commonly known as the shimmering ragan, is a rare flowering plant native to the temperate forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that reflect light, giving it a luminous appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
