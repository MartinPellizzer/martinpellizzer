Rosa atrovirens

Rosa atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
