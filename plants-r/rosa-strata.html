Rosa strata

Rosa strata, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a species native to the Himalayas and parts of China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to produce flowers continuously throughout the growing season. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, long-blooming shrub.

Rosa strata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
