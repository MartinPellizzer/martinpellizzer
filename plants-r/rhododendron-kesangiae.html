Rhododendron kesangiae

Rhododendron kesangiae, commonly known as the Kesangia rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayan region of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron kesangiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its mountainous habitat.
