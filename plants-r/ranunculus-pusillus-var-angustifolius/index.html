Ranunculus Pusillus Var. Angustifolius

Ranunculus pusillus var. angustifolius is a small, delicate flowering plant known for its strikingly beautiful blooms. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This variant of the lesser buttercup is distinguished by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike.
