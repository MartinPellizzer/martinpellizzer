Rubus jelskii

Rubus jelskii, commonly known as the Jelskii raspberry, is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant produces small, edible berries that are similar in appearance to other raspberry species. Its leaves are typically compound, with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are pink or white.

Rubus jelskii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit.
