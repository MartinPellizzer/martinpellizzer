Rhynchosia galactioides var. parvifolia

Rhynchosia galactioides var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered cowpea, is a herbaceous legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with stipules, and its small, yellow flowers are typically clustered in racemes. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to warm climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a forage crop.
