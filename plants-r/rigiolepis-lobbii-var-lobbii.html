Rigiolepis lobbii var. lobbii

Rigiolepis lobbii var. lobbii, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, yellow, berry-like fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange and are borne in clusters.
