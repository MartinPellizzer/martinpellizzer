Rhododendron horlickianum

Rhododendron horlickianum, commonly known as the Horlick's rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. Its flowers are showy, with pink to purple petals that bloom in late spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
