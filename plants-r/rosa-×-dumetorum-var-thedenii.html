Rosa × dumetorum var. thedenii

Rosa × dumetorum var. thedenii, commonly known as the Denison rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink to red flowers. The leaves are compound, typically consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets. This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and attractive appearance.
