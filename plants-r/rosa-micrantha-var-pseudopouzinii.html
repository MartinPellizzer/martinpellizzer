Rosa micrantha var. pseudopouzinii

Rosa micrantha var. pseudopouzinii, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and pinnate leaves with ovate leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact size and attractive blooms make it suitable for borders or as a specimen plant.
