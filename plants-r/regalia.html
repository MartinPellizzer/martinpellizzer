Regalia

Regalia, commonly known as *Clerodendrum trichotomum*, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with a distinctive yellow or white center. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are typically orange or red when mature. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Regalia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
