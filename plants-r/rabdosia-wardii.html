Rabdosia wardii

Rabdosia wardii, commonly known as the mountain rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
