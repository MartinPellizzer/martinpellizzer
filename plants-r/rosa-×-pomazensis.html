Rosa × pomazensis

Rosa × pomazensis, commonly known as the Polish rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pink to red flowers with a strong, sweet scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × pomazensis is valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
