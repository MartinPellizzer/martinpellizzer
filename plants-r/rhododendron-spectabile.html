Rhododendron spectabile

Rhododendron spectabile, commonly known as the "Bridal Wreath" rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces large, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its blooming period adds vibrant color to landscapes, particularly in regions with cool climates.
