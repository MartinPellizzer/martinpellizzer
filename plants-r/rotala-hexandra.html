Rotala hexandra

Rotala hexandra, commonly known as the water hyssop, is a aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features whorled leaves that are typically bright green and often have a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in shallow water and is often used in pond landscaping for its ornamental value.

Rotala hexandra is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
