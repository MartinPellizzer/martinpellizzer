Reichantha reichenbachiana

Reichantha reichenbachiana, commonly known as the giant reichena, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.
