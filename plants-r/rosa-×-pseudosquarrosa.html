Rosa × pseudosquarrosa

Rosa × pseudosquarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and its ability to produce multiple layers of petals. The plant typically features large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and the stems are covered in fine, soft hairs.
