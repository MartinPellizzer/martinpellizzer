Rhynchosia harmsiana var. burchellii

Rhynchosia harmsiana var. burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with erect, branching stems and small, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, followed by distinctive burr-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
