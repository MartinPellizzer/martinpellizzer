Rubus belonostachys

Rubus belonostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed blackberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the mountainous regions of western North America. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are white or pale pink, and it produces small, dark purple berries.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
