Rosa xanthinoides

Rosa xanthinoides, commonly known as the yellow sand rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are typically simple and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rosa xanthinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
