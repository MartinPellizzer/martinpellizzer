Rubus × nobilis

Rubus × nobilis, commonly known as the blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces edible berries that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features include palmately compound leaves and flowers with five pink to white petals.
