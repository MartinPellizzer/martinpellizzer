Rhoeo Spathacea F. Variegata

Variegated zebra plant, scientifically known as Rhoeo spathacea f. variegata, is a strikingly ornamental foliage plant prized for its unique leaf patterns. Its leaves are adorned with bold white and green stripes that resemble the markings of a zebra, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Easy to care for, it requires minimal watering and regular pruning to maintain its vibrant appearance. With its eye-catching design and low maintenance needs, the Variegated zebra plant is a favorite among plant enthusiasts and designers alike.
