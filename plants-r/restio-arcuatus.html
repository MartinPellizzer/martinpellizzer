Restio arcuatus

Restio arcuatus, commonly known as the zigzag restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate, spike-like inflorescences. Restio arcuatus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
