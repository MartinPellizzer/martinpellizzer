Rheum hotaoense

Rheum hotaoense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhubarb, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers in late spring. Its roots are valued in traditional Chinese medicine for their medicinal properties.

Rheum hotaoense thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
