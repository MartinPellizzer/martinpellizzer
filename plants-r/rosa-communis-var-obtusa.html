Rosa communis var. obtusa

Rosa communis var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leafed rose, is a cultivar characterized by its robust growth and abundant flowering.

It features large, velvety petals that are typically a deep pink to red hue, with a slightly fragrant aroma. The plant has broad, serrated leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is also noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand moderate cold temperatures.
