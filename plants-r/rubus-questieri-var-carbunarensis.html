Rubus questieri var. carbunarensis

Rubus questieri var. carbunarensis, commonly known as the Carpathian blackberry, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has prickly stems and trifoliate leaves, characteristic of the Rubus genus. It thrives in forested areas with well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its edible fruits and is sometimes cultivated for local use.
