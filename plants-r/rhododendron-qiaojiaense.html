Rhododendron qiaojiaense

Rhododendron qiaojiaense, commonly known as the Qiaojia rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province.

It is a shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high altitude and cool climates. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or pale pink throat. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing a dense canopy that supports local biodiversity.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
