Rubus ulmifolius proles prasinifolius

Rubus ulmifolius proles prasinifolius, commonly known as the green-leaved blackberry.

This plant is a variant of the common blackberry, characterized by its distinctively dark green foliage. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with a serrated edge, and the plant produces small, dark purple berries.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.
