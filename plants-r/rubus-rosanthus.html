Rubus rosanthus

Rubus rosanthus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces edible berries that are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.

Rubus rosanthus typically grows in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
