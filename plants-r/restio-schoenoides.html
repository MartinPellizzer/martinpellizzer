Restio schoenoides

Restio schoenoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restio, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, often forming dense, low-growing clumps. It is adapted to withstand coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
