Rosa hybrida genuina

Rosa hybrida genuina, commonly known as the English rose, is a species of shrub in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in a wide range of colors, including pink, red, white, and yellow. The plant is native to the United Kingdom and is known for its strong fragrance and vigorous growth. It typically has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, which are typical features of many rose species.

Rosa hybrida genuina is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for floral arrangements.
