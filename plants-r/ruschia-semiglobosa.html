Ruschia semiglobosa

Ruschia semiglobosa, commonly known as the globe ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rounded clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that give it a globular appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia semiglobosa is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative succulent due to its compact form and attractive foliage.
