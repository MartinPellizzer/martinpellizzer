Rosa jundzillii var. robusta

Rosa jundzillii var. robusta, commonly known as the robust Rosa, is a vigorous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to harsh climates and can thrive in poor soils, making it a resilient species.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects.
