Rosa × salaevensis var. lerchii

Rosa × salaevensis var. lerchii, commonly known as the Lerchii rose, is a hybrid species originating from Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in the fall, and is well-suited to temperate climates. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its showy blooms.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature.
