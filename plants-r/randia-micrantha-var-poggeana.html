Randia micrantha var. poggeana

Randia micrantha var. poggeana, commonly known as the Pogge's randia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
