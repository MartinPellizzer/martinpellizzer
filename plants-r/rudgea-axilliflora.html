Rudgea axilliflora

Rudgea axilliflora, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid vine, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a strong fragrance. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its woody stems that can grow quite long. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
