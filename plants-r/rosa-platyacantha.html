Rosa platyacantha

Rosa platyacantha, commonly known as the flat-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa platyacantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
