Rupicapnos delicatula

Rupicapnos delicatula, commonly known as the delicate rock cress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants known for their cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
