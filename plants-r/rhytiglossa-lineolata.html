Rhytiglossa lineolata

Rhytiglossa lineolata, commonly known as the lineated orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender, often branched stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive yellow or greenish lip.

Rhytiglossa lineolata is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
