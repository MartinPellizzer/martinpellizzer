Ruschia muiriana

Ruschia muiriana, commonly known as the Muir's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
