Ranunculus petroczenkoi

Ranunculus petroczenkoi, commonly known as the Karst ranunculus, is a rare flowering plant native to the karst landscapes of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. It is often found in rocky crevices and stream banks, where it forms dense clusters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
