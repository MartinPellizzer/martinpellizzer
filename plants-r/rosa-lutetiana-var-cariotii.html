Rosa lutetiana var. cariotii

Rosa lutetiana var. cariotii, commonly known as the Cariotii rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa lutetiana species, which is native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and fragrance.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
