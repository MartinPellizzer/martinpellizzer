Rosa afzeliana var. semipilosa

Rosa afzeliana var. semipilosa, commonly known as the silky rose, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental foliage and showy blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
