Rossioglossum krameri

Rossioglossum krameri, commonly known as the Krameri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant purple or violet hue. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rossioglossum krameri thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
