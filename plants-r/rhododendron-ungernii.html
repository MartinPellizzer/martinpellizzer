Rhododendron ungernii

Rhododendron ungernii, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to China and is part of the Ericaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are usually dark green and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals and a range of colors from pink to red, blooming in late spring.

Rhododendron ungernii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
