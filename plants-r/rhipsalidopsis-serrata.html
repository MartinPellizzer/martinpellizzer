Rhipsalidopsis serrata

Rhipsalidopsis serrata, commonly known as the zigzag cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its branching stems that are often covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
