Rubia chinensis var. pedicellata

Rubia chinensis var. pedicellata, commonly known as Chinese balm, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow or greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
