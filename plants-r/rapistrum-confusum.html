Rapistrum confusum

Rapistrum confusum, commonly known as the confused dock, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found along roadsides and in fields. It has alternate, lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters.

The seeds are enclosed in a capsule and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to spread easily.
