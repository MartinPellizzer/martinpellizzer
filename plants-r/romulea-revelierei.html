Romulea revelierei

Romulea revelierei, commonly known as the Orange Star Flower, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, orange-yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, rounded bulbs that store nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is also valued in horticultural collections.
