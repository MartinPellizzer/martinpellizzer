Rosa × polliniana var. stylaris

Rosa × polliniana var. stylaris, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its semi-evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardiness and continuous blooming make it a valued addition to gardens in temperate climates.
