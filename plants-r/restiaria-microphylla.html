Restiaria microphylla

Restiaria microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved restiaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restiariaceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Restiaria microphylla is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
