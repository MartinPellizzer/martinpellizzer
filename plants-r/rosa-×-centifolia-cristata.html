Rosa × centifolia cristata

Rosa × centifolia cristata, commonly known as the crinkled cabbage rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its unique, ruffled foliage and fragrant, pink blooms.

It is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring multiple stems with thornless or slightly thorned branches. The leaves are lobed and have a distinctive crinkled or curled appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. Its flowers are borne in clusters and emit a sweet, old-fashioned perfume, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth and blooming.
