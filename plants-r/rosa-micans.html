Rosa micans

Rosa micans, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. It produces fragrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rosa micans is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blossoms.
