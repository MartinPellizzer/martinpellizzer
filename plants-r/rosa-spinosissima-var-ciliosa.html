Rosa spinosissima var. ciliosa

Rosa spinosissima var. ciliosa, commonly known as the silky bristled rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely hairy stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. Its foliage is dark green and densely arranged, providing a lush backdrop for its blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
