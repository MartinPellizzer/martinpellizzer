Rhynchospora corniculata var. macrostachya

Rhynchospora corniculata var. macrostachya, commonly known as the broad-stemmed beak-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically reddish in color. It thrives in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks.

The varietal name *macrostachya* refers to its relatively large, robust flowering spikes.
