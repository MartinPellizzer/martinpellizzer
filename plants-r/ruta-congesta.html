Ruta congesta

Ruta congesta, commonly known as the clustered rue, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in mild climates.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
