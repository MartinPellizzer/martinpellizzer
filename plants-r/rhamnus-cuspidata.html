Rhamnus cuspidata

Rhamnus cuspidata, commonly known as the Japanese buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by dark purple berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Rhamnus cuspidata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract wildlife.
