Rubia mollugo

Rubia mollugo, commonly known as lesser bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant features slender stems that often lie prostrate on the ground, covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.
