Rosa × boursoultii

Rosa × boursoultii, commonly known as the Boursoultii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a dense, thorny structure. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
