Rosa × andegavensis f. karlovicensis

Rosa × andegavensis f. karlovicensis, commonly known as the Karlovicensis form of the Andegavensis hybrid, is a botanical variant of the rose species.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong fragrance. It is native to Central Europe and thrives in temperate climates.

This form is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and hardiness.
