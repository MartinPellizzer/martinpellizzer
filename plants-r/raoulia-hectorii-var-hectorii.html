Raoulia hectorii var. hectorii

Raoulia hectorii var. hectorii, commonly known as Hector's raoulia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its woody stems are often covered in a rough, bark-like layer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
