Rhododendron medoense var. medoense

Rhododendron medoense var. medoense, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The variety medoense is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and unique floral characteristics.
