Rungia bisaccata

Rungia bisaccata, commonly known as the two-bag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 12,000 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a two-valved capsule, giving it the common name "two-bag plant.".
