Rosa introspectans

Rosa introspectans, commonly known as the introspective rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets.

Rosa introspectans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
