Ruellia cusia

Ruellia cusia, commonly known as the velvetleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Ruellia cusia is a perennial shrub that thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
