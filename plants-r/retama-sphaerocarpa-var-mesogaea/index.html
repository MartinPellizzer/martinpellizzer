Retama Sphaerocarpa Var. Mesogaea

Retama sphaerocarpa var. mesogaea is a rare and hardy shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its compact growth and distinctive spherical fruits that turn red in autumn. This plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to harsh climates. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its resilience, it remains a lesser-known species among gardeners and botanists alike.
