Raphanus Sylvestris Subsp. Maritimus

Raphanus sylvestris subsp. maritimus is a hardy wild plant commonly found along coastal regions. It thrives in salty, sandy soils and is often seen growing near the shoreline. This subspecies is known for its robust root system and ability to withstand harsh maritime conditions. Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines. The plant plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local ecosystems.
