Rosa × leibicensis

Rosa × leibicensis, commonly known as the Leibicenses rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This rose is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
