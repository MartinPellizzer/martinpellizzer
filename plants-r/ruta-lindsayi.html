Ruta lindsayi

Ruta lindsayi, commonly known as Lindsay's rue, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like citrus. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Ruta lindsayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
