Rosa × collina f. oberbergensis

Rosa × collina f. oberbergensis, commonly known as the Oberbergensis shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is native to Central Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
