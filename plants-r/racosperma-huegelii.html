Racosperma huegelii

Racosperma huegelii, commonly known as the yellow wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Racosperma huegelii plays an important role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.
