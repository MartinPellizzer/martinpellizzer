Rosa × bengyana

Rosa × bengyana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China and widely cultivated for its ornamental value.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often used in hedges or as a decorative plant in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × bengyana is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine.
