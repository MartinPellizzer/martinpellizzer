Ruschia microphylla

Ruschia microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often used in rock gardens and as a container plant.
