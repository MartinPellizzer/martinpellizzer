Rhododendron sajanense

Rhododendron sajanense, commonly known as the Sajanese rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Russia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable climatic conditions.
