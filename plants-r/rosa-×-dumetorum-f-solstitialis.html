Rosa × dumetorum f. solstitialis

Rosa × dumetorum f. solstitialis, commonly known as the "Solstitial rose," is a horticultural form of a hybrid rose species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, depending on the specific cultivar. It is typically found in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

This rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its visual and aromatic qualities.
