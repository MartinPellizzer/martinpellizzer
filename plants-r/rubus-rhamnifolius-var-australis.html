Rubus rhamnifolius var. australis

Rubus rhamnifolius var. australis, commonly known as the Australian blackberry, is a species native to Australia.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This variety is notable for its hardiness and adaptability to different climates.
