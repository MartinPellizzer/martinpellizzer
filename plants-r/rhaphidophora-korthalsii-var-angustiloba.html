Rhaphidophora korthalsii var. angustiloba

Rhaphidophora korthalsii var. angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rhaphidophora, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and a deep green color, often with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by spiny, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is appreciated for its compact growth and ornamental foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
