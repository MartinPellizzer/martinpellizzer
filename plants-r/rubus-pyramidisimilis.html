Rubus pyramidisimilis

Rubus pyramidisimilis, commonly known as the Pyramidal Raspberry, is a species of brambles belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems, which are typical of many Rubus species. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature, similar to other raspberries. Its leaves are usually serrated and have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.
