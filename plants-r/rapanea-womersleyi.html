Rapanea womersleyi

Rapanea womersleyi, commonly known as the Womersley's holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is a small evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and glossy, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The small white flowers are followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species.
