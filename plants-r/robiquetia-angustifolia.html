Robiquetia angustifolia

Robiquetia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Robiquetia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
