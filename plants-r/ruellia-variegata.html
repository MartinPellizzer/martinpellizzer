Ruellia variegata

Ruellia variegata, commonly known as the variegated ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Ruellia variegata thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
