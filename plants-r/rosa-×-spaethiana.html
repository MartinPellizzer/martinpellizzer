Rosa × spaethiana

Rosa × spaethiana, commonly known as the Spaethiana rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that typically range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is a deciduous shrub, known for its upright growth habit and thorny stems. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with elliptical leaflets that are serrated along the edges.

Rosa × spaethiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
