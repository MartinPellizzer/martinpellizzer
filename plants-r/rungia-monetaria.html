Rungia monetaria

Rungia monetaria, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found growing along stream banks or in forest undergrowth.

Rungia monetaria is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional uses in herbal medicine.
