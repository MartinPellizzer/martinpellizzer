Ruyschia longistylis

Ruyschia longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled ruyschia, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often dark green with prominent veins. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female parts, a common trait in the Araceae family.

Ruyschia longistylis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
