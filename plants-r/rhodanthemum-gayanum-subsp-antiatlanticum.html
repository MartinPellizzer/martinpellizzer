Rhodanthemum gayanum subsp. antiatlanticum

Rhodanthemum gayanum subsp.

antiatlanticum, commonly known as the Atlantic rhodanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Atlantic coast. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens. Its foliage is typically evergreen, with narrow, leathery leaves that help it withstand salty air and strong winds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging maritime environments.
