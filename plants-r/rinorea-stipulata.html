Rinorea stipulata

Rinorea stipulata, commonly known as the velvet leaf or red velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red or purple foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.

Rinorea stipulata is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
