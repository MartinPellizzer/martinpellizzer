Rosa platyacantha var. densiflora

Rosa platyacantha var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. Its dense flowering habit gives it its common name, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
