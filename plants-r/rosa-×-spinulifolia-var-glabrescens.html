Rosa × spinulifolia var. glabrescens

Rosa × spinulifolia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its thornless stems and compact growth habit.

It features fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly hairy underside, and its roots are fibrous and well-developed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and container planting.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
