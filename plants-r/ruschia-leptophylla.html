Ruschia leptophylla

Ruschia leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, shrub-like growth with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
