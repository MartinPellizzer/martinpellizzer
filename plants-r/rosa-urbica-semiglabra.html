Rosa urbica semiglabra

Rosa urbica semiglabra, commonly known as the urban rose, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. The plant produces fragrant pink or white flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly rough texture.

Rosa urbica semiglabra is often found in urban environments, such as along sidewalks and in disturbed soils, making it an adaptable and resilient plant.
