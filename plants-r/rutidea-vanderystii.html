Rutidea vanderystii

Rutidea vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, a common reproductive strategy in the legume family. Native to South America, it thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
