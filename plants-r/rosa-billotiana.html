Rosa billotiana

Rosa billotiana, commonly known as the Chilean rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is often found in coastal regions. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa billotiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
