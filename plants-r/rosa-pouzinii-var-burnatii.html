Rosa pouzinii var. burnatii

Rosa pouzinii var. burnatii, commonly known as the Burnatii rose, is a compact shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces hips that are ornamental and edible. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.

The varietal form burnatii is distinguished by its specific flower color and growth characteristics.
