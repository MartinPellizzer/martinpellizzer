Rosa × aveyronensis

Rosa × aveyronensis, commonly known as the Aveyron rose, is a hybrid shrub native to France.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × aveyronensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
