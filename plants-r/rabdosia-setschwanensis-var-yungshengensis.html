Rabdosia setschwanensis var. yungshengensis

Rabdosia setschwanensis var. yungshengensis, commonly known as Yungsheng rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pale purple to pink, clustered in whorls along the stems.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
