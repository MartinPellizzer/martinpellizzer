Ruellia erecta var. elongata

Ruellia erecta var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated ruellia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ruellia erecta var. elongata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
