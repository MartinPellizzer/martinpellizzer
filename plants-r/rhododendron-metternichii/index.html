Rhododendron Metternichii

Rhododendron metternichii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the Himalayas. It is known for its vibrant, showy blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments with acidic soil. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
