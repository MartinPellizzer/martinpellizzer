Ruta taurica

Ruta taurica, commonly known as Taurian rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately divided leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves and flowers contain volatile oils that contribute to its distinctive scent and medicinal properties.

Ruta taurica is used in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and antispasmodic benefits.
