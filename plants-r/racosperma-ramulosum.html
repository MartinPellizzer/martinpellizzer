Racosperma ramulosum

Racosperma ramulosum, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid and semi-arid regions.

Racosperma ramulosum is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
