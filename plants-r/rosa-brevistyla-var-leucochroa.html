Rosa brevistyla var. leucochroa

Rosa brevistyla var. leucochroa, commonly known as the white-stemmed shrub rose, is a variety of the shrub rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
