Ritterocactus langsdorfii

Ritterocactus langsdorfii, commonly known as the Langsdorf's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, spiny plant that typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters. The plant features a ribbed stem covered in white to yellowish spines. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the tips of the stems.

Ritterocactus langsdorfii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
