Ribes dubium

Ribes dubium, commonly known as the yellow currant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae, which also includes other currants and gooseberries. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Ribes dubium produces small, edible berries that ripen to a reddish-purple color in late summer.
