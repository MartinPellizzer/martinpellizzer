Rosa catalaunica

Rosa catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalonian rose, is a species native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color. Rosa catalaunica produces fragrant, single or semi-double blooms in the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in traditional medicine.
