Rosa × schleicheri var. kalksburgensis

Rosa × schleicheri var. kalksburgensis, commonly known as the Kalksburg rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
