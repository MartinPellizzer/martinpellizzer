Rosa dumalis f. livida

Rosa dumalis f. livida, commonly known as the alpine pink rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dark green, velvety leaves and produces striking pink to pale pink flowers with a slightly fragrant aroma. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. It is a cold-hardy species, capable of surviving in harsh, high-altitude conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
