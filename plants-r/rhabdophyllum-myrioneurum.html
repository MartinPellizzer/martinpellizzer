Rhabdophyllum myrioneurum

Rhabdophyllum myrioneurum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Rhabdophyllum myrioneurum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
