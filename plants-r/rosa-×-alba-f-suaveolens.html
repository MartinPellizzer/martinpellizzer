Rosa × alba f. suaveolens

Rosa × alba f. suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented white rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its fragrant, white flowers.

It is a cultivar of Rosa alba, known for its delicate, semi-double blooms that emit a sweet, heady aroma. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and pleasant fragrance.
