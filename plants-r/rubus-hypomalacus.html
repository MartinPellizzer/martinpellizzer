Rubus hypomalacus

Rubus hypomalacus, commonly known as the red raspberries.

It is a species of brambles native to the eastern United States, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is closely related to other raspberry and blackberry species.

Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
