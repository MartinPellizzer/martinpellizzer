Rosa heckeliana var. sicula

Rosa heckeliana var. sicula, commonly known as the Sicilian rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
