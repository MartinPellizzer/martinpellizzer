Racosperma hyaloneurum

Racosperma hyaloneurum, commonly known as the white-veined acacia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions. Its seeds are encased in pods and are adapted for dispersal by animals or wind.
