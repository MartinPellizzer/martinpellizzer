Ranunculus auricomus subsp. leptomeris

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

leptomeris, commonly known as the slenderleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive yellow center.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are usually alternate along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural gardens.
