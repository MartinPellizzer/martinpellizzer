Rhagadiolus stellatus

Rhagadiolus stellatus, commonly known as the star-like orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture.

Rhagadiolus stellatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
