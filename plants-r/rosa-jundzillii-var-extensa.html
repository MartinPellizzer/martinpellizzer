Rosa jundzillii var. extensa

Rosa jundzillii var. extensa, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, thorny stems and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in moisture retention in its arid environment. It is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

The variety extensa is distinguished by its broader foliage compared to other varieties of Rosa jundzillii.
