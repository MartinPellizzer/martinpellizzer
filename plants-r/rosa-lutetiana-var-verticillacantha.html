Rosa lutetiana var. verticillacantha

Rosa lutetiana var. verticillacantha, commonly known as the upright thorned rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems, which give it a distinct appearance. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blossoms.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
