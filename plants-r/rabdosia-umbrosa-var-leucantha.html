Rabdosia umbrosa var. leucantha

Rabdosia umbrosa var. leucantha, commonly known as white-flowered rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
