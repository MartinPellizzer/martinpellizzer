Ruellia rusbyi

Ruellia rusbyi, commonly known as the Florida ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as hammocks and wetlands. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in summer.

Ruellia rusbyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially sunny conditions.
