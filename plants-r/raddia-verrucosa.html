Raddia verrucosa

Raddia verrucosa, commonly known as the rough raddia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

Raddia verrucosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
