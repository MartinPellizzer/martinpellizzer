Rubus vestitus var. subcylindricus

Rubus vestitus var. subcylindricus, commonly known as the hairy raspberry.

It is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it distinct from other Rubus species, particularly in its foliage and fruit structure.
