Randia binata

Randia binata, commonly known as the white randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its leaves are opposite, simple, and often elliptical in shape. Randia binata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a range of tropical environments.
