Rosa cottetii

Rosa cottetii, commonly known as the Cottet rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Corsica, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are serrated and have a slightly hairy texture, typical of many rose species.

Rosa cottetii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
