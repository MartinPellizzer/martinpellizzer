Rosa desvauxii

Rosa desvauxii, commonly known as the Desvaux's rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub-like plant with slender, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing pink to white petals that are often tinged with red. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it prefers well-drained soil in sunny locations.

Rosa desvauxii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
