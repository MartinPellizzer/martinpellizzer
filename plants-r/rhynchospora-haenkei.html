Rhynchospora haenkei

Rhynchospora haenkei, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, inconspicuous flowers. Rhynchospora haenkei plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms.
