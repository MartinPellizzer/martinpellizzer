Rebutia pygmaea f. atrovirens

Rebutia pygmaea f. atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to southern Africa.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically a deep green color, often with a slightly woolly texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its unique coloration and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
