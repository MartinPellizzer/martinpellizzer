Rosa × nitidula var. typica

Rosa × nitidula var. typica, commonly known as the smooth rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its smooth, thornless stems and glossy, dark green foliage. The flowers are typically pink to red, with a sweet fragrance, and bloom in late spring to early summer. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile addition to many landscapes.
