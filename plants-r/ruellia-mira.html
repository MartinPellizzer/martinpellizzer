Ruellia mira

Ruellia mira, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Ruellia mira thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and blooms.
