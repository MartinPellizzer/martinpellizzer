Racosperma helmsianum

Racosperma helmsianum, commonly known as the Blue Ash or Helms' Acacia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, fluffy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, thread-like leaflets.
