Rhamnus oleoides var. aegaea

Rhamnus oleoides var. aegaea, commonly known as the Aegean buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by small, oval berries that are typically dark blue when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
