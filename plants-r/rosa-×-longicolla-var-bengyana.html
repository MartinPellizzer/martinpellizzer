Rosa × longicolla var. bengyana

Rosa × longicolla var. bengyana, commonly known as the Bengyana rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep red. The plant produces thorny stems and has compound leaves with serrated edges. It is native to regions in Asia, particularly China, and thrives in temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
