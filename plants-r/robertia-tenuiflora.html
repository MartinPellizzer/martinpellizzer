Robertia tenuiflora

Robertia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered Robertia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely known.
