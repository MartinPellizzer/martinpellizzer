Rosa guentheri

Rosa guentheri, commonly known as the Guenther rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa guentheri is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and is valued for its hardiness in cooler climates.
