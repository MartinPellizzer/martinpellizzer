Rorippa morisonii

Rorippa morisonii, commonly known as the Morrison's watercress, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating, with small white flowers that bloom in late summer.
