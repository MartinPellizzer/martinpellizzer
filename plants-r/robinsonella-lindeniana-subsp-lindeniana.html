Robinsonella lindeniana subsp. lindeniana

Robinsonella lindeniana subsp.

lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenhof lichen, is a foliose lichen belonging to the family Pilocarpaceae. It typically grows on bark and wood in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions.

The thallus is leafy, with a greenish to yellowish color and a distinct, wrinkled appearance. It has a slow growth rate and is often used in ecological studies to monitor air quality.

This lichen is relatively rare and is of interest to mycologists and conservationists.
