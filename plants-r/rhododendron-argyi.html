Rhododendron argyi

Rhododendron argyi, commonly known as the Chinese laurel, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron argyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
