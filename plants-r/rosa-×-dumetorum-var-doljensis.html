Rosa × dumetorum var. doljensis

Rosa × dumetorum var. doljensis, commonly known as the Doljensk rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its hardiness and ability to withstand colder climates.
