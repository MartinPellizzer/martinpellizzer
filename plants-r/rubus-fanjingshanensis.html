Rubus fanjingshanensis

Rubus fanjingshanensis, commonly known as the Fanjingshan raspberry, is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
