Rosa indica salicifolia

Rosa indica salicifolia, commonly known as the salicifolia rose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant features serrated, alternate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Rosa indica salicifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
