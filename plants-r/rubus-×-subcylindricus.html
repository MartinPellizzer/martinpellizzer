Rubus × subcylindricus

Rubus × subcylindricus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as raspberries, which are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is widely cultivated for its edible fruits.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both commercial agriculture and home gardening.
