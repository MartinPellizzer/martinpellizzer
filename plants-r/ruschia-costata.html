Ruschia costata

Ruschia costata, commonly known as the serrated Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are often red or purple in color, with a distinctive serrated edge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia costata is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
