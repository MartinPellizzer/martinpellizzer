Reaumuria kaschgarica var. nanschanica

Reaumuria kaschgarica var. nanschanica, commonly known as the Nanschan reaumuria, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, forming dense clumps that help prevent soil erosion.

This variety is of interest to botanists and ecologists due to its resilience in harsh environments.
