Rosa rubiginosa f. atroviridis

Rosa rubiginosa f. atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green rose, is a hybrid form of the shrub rose.

It is characterized by its dark green foliage and fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of down.
