Rourea glabra var. floribunda

Rourea glabra var. floribunda, commonly known as the smooth rourea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, often with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its stems are woody and can be branched, contributing to its structural form.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, well-drained conditions.
