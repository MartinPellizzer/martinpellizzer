Ranunculus Bongardii Var. Earlei

Bongard's buttercup, scientifically known as Ranunculus bongardii var. earlei, is a rare and striking member of the buttercup family. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its bright yellow flowers, often with a distinctive black center, make it a standout in any botanical collection. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and unique species.
