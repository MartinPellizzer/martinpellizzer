Rosa gallica f. micropetala

Rosa gallica f. micropetala, commonly known as the miniature gallica rose, is a compact shrub native to Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in garden settings for its delicate appearance.

This variety is a subspecies of Rosa gallica, distinguished by its smaller petals and more compact growth habit.
