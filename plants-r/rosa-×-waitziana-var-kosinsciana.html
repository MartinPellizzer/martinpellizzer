Rosa × waitziana var. kosinsciana

Rosa × waitziana var. kosinsciana, commonly known as the Kosinski rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant produces semi-double to fully double blooms, often with a strong, sweet scent. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.

The leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, adding to its visual appeal.
