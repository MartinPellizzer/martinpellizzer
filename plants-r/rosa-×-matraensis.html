Rosa × matraensis

Rosa × matraensis, commonly known as the Matra rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Matra Mountains in Hungary. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems, compound leaves, and fragrant, semi-double pink to red flowers. It typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Rosa × matraensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
