Rubia chitralensis

Rubia chitralensis, commonly known as Chitral rue, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale yellow, with five petals.

Rubia chitralensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
