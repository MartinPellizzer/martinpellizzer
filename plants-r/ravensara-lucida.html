Ravensara lucida

Ravensara lucida, commonly known as the silver-leafed raven's arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, silvery-white pubescence. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and greenhouse enthusiasts.
