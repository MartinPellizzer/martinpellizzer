Rhododendron javanicum var. gymnocarpum

Rhododendron javanicum var. gymnocarpum, commonly known as the Java rhododendron, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, providing a striking backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
