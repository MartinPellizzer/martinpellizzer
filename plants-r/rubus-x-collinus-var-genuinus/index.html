Rubus × Collinus Var. Genuinus

Rubus × collinus var. genuinus is a fascinating hybrid plant belonging to the rose family. It is known for its unique characteristics that set it apart from its parent species. This variant often exhibits a mix of traits from both parent plants, making it a subject of interest for botanists. Its appearance can vary widely, depending on environmental conditions and genetic factors. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively understudied in comparison to other Rubus species.
