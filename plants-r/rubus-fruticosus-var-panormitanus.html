Rubus fruticosus var. panormitanus

Rubus fruticosus var. panormitanus, commonly known as the Mediterranean blackberry, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
