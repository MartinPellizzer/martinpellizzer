Rosa stenocarpa

Rosa stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Afghanistan, Iran, and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated edges, and the fruit is a woody, ellipsoid capsule.

Rosa stenocarpa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
