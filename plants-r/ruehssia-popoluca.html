Ruehssia popoluca

Ruehssia popoluca, commonly known as the Mexican rue, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
