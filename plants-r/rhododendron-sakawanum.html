Rhododendron sakawanum

Rhododendron sakawanum, commonly known as the Sakawan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms.

Rhododendron sakawanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
