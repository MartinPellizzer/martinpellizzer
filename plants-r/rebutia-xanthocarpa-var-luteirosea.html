Rebutia xanthocarpa var. luteirosea

Rebutia xanthocarpa var. luteirosea, commonly known as the yellow fruit rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season, followed by orange to red fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is popular among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
