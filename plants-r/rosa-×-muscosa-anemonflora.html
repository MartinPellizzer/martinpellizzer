Rosa × muscosa anemonflora

Rosa × muscosa anemonflora, commonly known as the Anemoniflora rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, anemone-like flowers that bloom in a range of soft pink to white hues. The plant typically grows as a shrub with arching stems and thorny branches, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and fragrance.
