Rhodax rotundifolius

Rhodax rotundifolius, commonly known as the rounded-leaf rhodax, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
