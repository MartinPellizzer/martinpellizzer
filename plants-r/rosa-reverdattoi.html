Rosa reverdattoi

Rosa reverdattoi, commonly known as the Sicilian rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa reverdattoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for both its beauty and its use in perfumery.
