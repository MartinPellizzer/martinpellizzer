Rubus macrostachys microgene pruinifer

Rubus macrostachys microgene pruinifer, commonly known as the silverleaf blackberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its densely hairy, silver-gray leaves and thorny stems. The plant produces small, dark purple fruits that are typically edible. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
