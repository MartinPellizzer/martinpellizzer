Rubus revealii

Rubus revealii, commonly known as the Reveal's bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three leaflets that are serrated along the edges.

The fruit, a type of aggregate berry, is edible and often used in local cuisines.
