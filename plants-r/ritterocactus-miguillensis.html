Ritterocactus miguillensis

Ritterocactus miguillensis, commonly known as the Miguillen cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, spiny cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features a cylindrical stem covered in short, sharp spines and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring. It has a shallow root system adapted to survive in dry, rocky soils.

Its botanical structure makes it well-suited for xerophytic environments, showcasing adaptations to extreme desert conditions.
