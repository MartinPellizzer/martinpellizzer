Rubus leucophaeus f. subcaesius

Rubus leucophaeus f. subcaesius, commonly known as the purple-leafed raspberry.

This botanical variant is characterized by its distinctive purplish foliage, which sets it apart from the typical green leaves of the species. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to specific regions in Europe. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique leaf coloration.
