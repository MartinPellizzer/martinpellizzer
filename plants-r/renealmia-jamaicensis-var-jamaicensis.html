Renealmia jamaicensis var. jamaicensis

Renealmia jamaicensis var. jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican renealmia, is a perennial herb native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive, grass-like appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
