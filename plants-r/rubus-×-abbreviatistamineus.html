Rubus × abbreviatistamineus

Rubus × abbreviatistamineus, commonly known as the dwarf blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small stature and thorny stems. It produces small, dark berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. The plant is native to parts of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Its botanical features include compound leaves and a complex flowering structure typical of the Rubus genus.
