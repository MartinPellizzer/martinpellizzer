Rhodamnia globosa

Rhodamnia globosa, commonly known as the globular honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its spherical, woody fruit that resembles a small globe, which is a distinctive botanical feature. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal regions.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
