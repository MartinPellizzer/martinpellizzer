Rosa obtusifolia var. bidentata

Rosa obtusifolia var. bidentata, commonly known as the Bidentate shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features upright, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun conditions.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
