Rosa brachystema

Rosa brachystema, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, small pink to white flowers, and narrow, serrated leaves. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, dry conditions.

Rosa brachystema is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
