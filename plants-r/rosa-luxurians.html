Rosa luxurians

Rosa luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant rose, is a species native to the temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit, producing long, arching canes that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are typically pink to deep red, with a strong, sweet fragrance. Rosa luxurians is well-suited for hedging or as a decorative plant in gardens due to its dense growth and ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal blooming.
