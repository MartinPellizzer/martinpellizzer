Reseda ochradeni

Reseda ochradeni, commonly known as the red sandalwood or ochradeni reseda, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs.
