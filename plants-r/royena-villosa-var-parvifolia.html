Royena villosa var. parvifolia

Royena villosa var. parvifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf rock rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
