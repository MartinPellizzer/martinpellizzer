Rosa × dumetorum subvar. gracilenta

Rosa × dumetorum subvar. gracilenta, commonly known as the slender shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically borne singly or in small clusters. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
