Rhododendron vesiculiferum

Rhododendron vesiculiferum, commonly known as the balloon flower, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often adorned with a distinctive waxy bloom. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron vesiculiferum is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
