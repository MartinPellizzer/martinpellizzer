Roupala montana var. heterophylla

Roupala montana var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable mountain roupala, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves ranging from ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, white flowers appear in spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits that are attractive to birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to different garden conditions.
