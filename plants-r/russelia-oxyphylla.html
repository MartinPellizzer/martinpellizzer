Russelia oxyphylla

Russelia oxyphylla, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. Russelia oxyphylla thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract hummingbirds.
