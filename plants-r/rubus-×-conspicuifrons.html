Rubus × conspicuifrons

Rubus × conspicuifrons, commonly known as the showy blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its large, dark purple to black berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves, often found in temperate regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruit is prized for its flavor and nutritional value.

This species is valued in both horticulture and wild plant ecosystems.
