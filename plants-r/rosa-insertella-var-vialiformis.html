Rosa insertella var. vialiformis

Rosa insertella var. vialiformis, commonly known as the Vialiform Rosa, is a rare and unique variant of the Rosa insertella species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that resemble the shape of a vial, hence its common name. This plant is native to specific regions in Europe and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is of interest to plant enthusiasts and researchers alike.
