Rosa caryophyllacea f. scepusiensis

Rosa caryophyllacea f. scepusiensis, commonly known as the Scepusian rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of oval, dark green leaves with serrated edges.

This variety is native to specific regions in Europe and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
