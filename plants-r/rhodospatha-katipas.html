Rhodospatha katipas

Rhodospatha katipas, commonly known as the Katipan orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture to retain moisture.

Rhodospatha katipas is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
