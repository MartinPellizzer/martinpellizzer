Rosa scandens var. ovata

Rosa scandens var. ovata, commonly known as the climbing rose or evergreen rose, is a variety of the dog rose.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces fragrant, pink flowers. The plant has glossy, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden trellises or as a ground cover.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
