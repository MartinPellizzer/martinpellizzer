Russelia steyermarkii

Russelia steyermarkii, commonly known as the cloud plant or everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or well-drained soils. Its foliage is evergreen, with leathery leaves that provide a lush, green backdrop to its showy blooms.

Russelia steyermarkii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
