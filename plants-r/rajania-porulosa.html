Rajania porulosa

Rajania porulosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and the presence of tendrils for climbing.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
