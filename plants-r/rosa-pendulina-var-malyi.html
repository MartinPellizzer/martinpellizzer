Rosa pendulina var. malyi

Rosa pendulina var. malyi, commonly known as the Malyi weeping rose, is a unique cultivar characterized by its cascading growth habit and delicate pink to white flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features thornless stems that hang gracefully, making it ideal for vertical gardens or trellises. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its fragrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
