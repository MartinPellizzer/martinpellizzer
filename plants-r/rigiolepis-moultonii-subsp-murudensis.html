Rigiolepis moultonii subsp. murudensis

Rigiolepis moultonii subsp.

murudensis, commonly known as the Murudensis sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its desert habitat. It grows low to the ground, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion.

This subspecies is valued for its resilience and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
