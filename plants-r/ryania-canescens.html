Ryania canescens

Ryania canescens, commonly known as the red cedar or ryania, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rubiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and dark green, leathery leaves. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Ryania canescens is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
