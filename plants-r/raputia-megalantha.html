Raputia megalantha

Raputia megalantha, commonly known as the giant raputia, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and collections of tropical plants.
