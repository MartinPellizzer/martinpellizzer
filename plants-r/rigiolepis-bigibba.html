Rigiolepis bigibba

Rigiolepis bigibba, commonly known as the double-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, double-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a fragrant aroma. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Rigiolepis bigibba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
