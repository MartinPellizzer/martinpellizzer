Rollinia sphaerantha

Rollinia sphaerantha, commonly known as the globe starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Caricaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spherical fruit that resembles a small star. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, while the flowers are small and clustered in panicles.
