Rosa × caviniacensis

Rosa × caviniacensis, commonly known as the Caviniacensis rose, is a hybrid rose species that exhibits a range of botanical characteristics.

It typically features large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, with a semi-double to full bloom structure. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval, serrated leaflets, and it produces thorny stems typical of many rose species. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates, requiring well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
