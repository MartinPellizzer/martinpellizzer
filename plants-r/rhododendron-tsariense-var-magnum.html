Rhododendron tsariense var. magnum

Rhododendron tsariense var. magnum, commonly known as the Tsari Magnolia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows in high-altitude forests and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with acidic soil and partial shade.
