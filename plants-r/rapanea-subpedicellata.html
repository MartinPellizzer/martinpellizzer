Rapanea subpedicellata

Rapanea subpedicellata, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth margin and a glossy surface.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
