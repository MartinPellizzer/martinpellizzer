Restio eriophorus

Restio eriophorus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in coastal and mountainous regions.

Restio eriophorus is often found in fynbos vegetation and is adapted to the region's Mediterranean climate.
