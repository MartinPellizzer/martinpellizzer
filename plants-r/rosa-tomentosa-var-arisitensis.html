Rosa tomentosa var. arisitensis

Rosa tomentosa var. arisitensis, commonly known as the Arisitani rose, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, thorny stems covered in a woolly, grayish-white pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and unique appearance.
