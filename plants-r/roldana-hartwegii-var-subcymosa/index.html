Roldana Hartwegii Var. Subcymosa

Roldana hartwegii var. subcymosa is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It is known for its unique, clustered growth pattern and vibrant, often blue or purple flowers. This variant of Roldana is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance. Despite its beauty, it remains understudied and is threatened by habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve this rare botanical gem for future generations.
