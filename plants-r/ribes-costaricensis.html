Ribes costaricensis

Ribes costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican gooseberry, is a shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various species of flowering plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks and in forest understories. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it produces small, aggregate fruits that are often red or yellow in color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
