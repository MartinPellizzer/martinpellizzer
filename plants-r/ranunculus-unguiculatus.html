Ranunculus unguiculatus

Ranunculus unguiculatus, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and showy yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, known for its showy, often brightly colored flowers.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
