Reaumuria Hypericoides Var. Cistoides

Reaumuria hypericoides var. cistoides is a unique plant species native to arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia. It is known for its low-growing, shrub-like form and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions makes it an important component of its ecosystem. This variety is often mistaken for other members of the Reaumuria genus due to its similar appearance.
