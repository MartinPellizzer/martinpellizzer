Rosa alpina var. vulgaris-variegata

Rosa alpina var. vulgaris-variegata, commonly known as the alpine rose or variegated alpine rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand cold temperatures, making it a popular choice for gardeners in cooler climates.
