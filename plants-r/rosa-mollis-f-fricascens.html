Rosa mollis f. fricascens

Rosa mollis f. fricascens, commonly known as the soft rose or fricascens rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and delicate pink to white flowers. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in temperate climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrance.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
