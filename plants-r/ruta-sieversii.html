Ruta sieversii

Ruta sieversii, commonly known as Sievers' rue, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
