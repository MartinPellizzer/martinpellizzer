Ronnbergia aciculosa

Ronnbergia aciculosa, commonly known as the needle-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that resemble needles, which help it conserve water in its arid environment. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
