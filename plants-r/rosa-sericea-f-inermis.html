Rosa sericea f. inermis

Rosa sericea f. inermis, commonly known as the silky rose or silver rose, is a horticultural form of the species Rosa sericea.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-white undersides on the leaves and pale pink to white flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of western North America, particularly in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
