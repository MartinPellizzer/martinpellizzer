Rhinanthus angustifolius subsp. apterus

Rhinanthus angustifolius subsp.

apterus, commonly known as the slender yellow-rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family. It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on host plants for water and nutrients while still performing photosynthesis.

The subspecies apterus is distinguished by its slender leaves and reduced calyx compared to other subspecies of Rhinanthus.
