Roupala martii var. pinnata

Roupala martii var. pinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved roupala, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnate leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed drupe that contains a single seed.

Roupala martii var. pinnata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
