Rosa × lereschei

Rosa × lereschei, commonly known as the Leresche rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to the United States, particularly in the southeastern region. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blossoms.

Rosa × lereschei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in moderate climates.
