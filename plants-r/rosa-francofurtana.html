Rosa francofurtana

Rosa francofurtana, commonly known as the Frankfurt rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to Central Europe and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, glossy leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy, semi-double flowers.

Rosa francofurtana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
