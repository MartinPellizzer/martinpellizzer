Rosularia semiensis

Rosularia semiensis, commonly known as the semi-enthusiast rose.

This plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. The flowers are small, pinkish-white, and typically appear in clusters during the summer months.

Rosularia semiensis is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant for cultivation in dry climates.
