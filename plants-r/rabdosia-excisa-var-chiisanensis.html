Rabdosia excisa var. chiisanensis

Rabdosia excisa var. chiisanensis, commonly known as the Japanese blue violet, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
