Reicheocactus pseudoreicheanus

Reicheocactus pseudoreicheanus, commonly known as the false reicheocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in small, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
