Rhododendron arboreum var. album-speciosum

Rhododendron arboreum var. album-speciosum, commonly known as the White Himalayan Rhododendron, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are leathery and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in cool, moist climates with partial shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and alpine landscapes.
