Rosa chloribeggeriana

Rosa chloribeggeriana, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a bluish-green hue, and its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in late summer. Rosa chloribeggeriana is adapted to cold climates and has a compact growth habit.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in challenging environments.
