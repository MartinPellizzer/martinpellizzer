Rhododendron oreodoxa var. adenostylosum

Rhododendron oreodoxa var. adenostylosum, commonly known as the Adenostylosum rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
