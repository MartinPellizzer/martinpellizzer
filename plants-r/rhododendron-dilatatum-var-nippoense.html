Rhododendron dilatatum var. nippoense

Rhododendron dilatatum var. nippoense, commonly known as the Japanese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to reddish-purple flowers in spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and natural landscapes.
