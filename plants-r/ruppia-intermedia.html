Ruppia intermedia

Ruppia intermedia, commonly known as sea rocket, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family RuppIaceae and is often found in coastal marshes and brackish waters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It plays a role in stabilizing shorelines and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
