Rosa × stylosoformis var. pseudodumetorum

Rosa × stylosoformis var. pseudodumetorum, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and compact form, making it suitable for hedges or ornamental use. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
