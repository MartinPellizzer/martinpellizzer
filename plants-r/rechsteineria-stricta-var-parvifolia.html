Rechsteineria stricta var. parvifolia

Rechsteineria stricta var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rechsteineria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a creeping rhizome and produces small, clustered flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically with a hairy surface. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
