Rubus hebecaulis microgene curtipetiolulatus

Rubus hebecaulis microgene curtipetiolulatus, commonly known as the slender-stemmed raspberry.

This plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thin, wiry stems and small, trifoliate leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. The species is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
