Reseda gracilis

Reseda gracilis, commonly known as slender reseda, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, elongated leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to regions of Europe and Asia and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Reseda gracilis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
