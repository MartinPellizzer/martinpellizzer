Rosa × kalksburgensis

Rosa × kalksburgensis, commonly known as the Kalksburg rose, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits a range of botanical characteristics.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring arching, thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, depending on the cultivar. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is well-suited to temperate climates.

Rosa × kalksburgensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
