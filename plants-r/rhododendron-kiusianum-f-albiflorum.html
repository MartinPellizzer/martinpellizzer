Rhododendron kiusianum f. albiflorum

Rhododendron kiusianum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the Whiteflower Kiusian Rhododendron, is a botanical variant characterized by its pale or white flowers.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
