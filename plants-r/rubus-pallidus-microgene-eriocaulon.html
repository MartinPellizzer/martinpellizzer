Rubus pallidus microgene eriocaulon

Rubus pallidus microgene eriocaulon, commonly known as the pale raspberry.

This plant is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, thornless stems. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters. The fruit is a red, hairy berry that ripens in late summer.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.
