Rosa × odorata f. ochroleuca

Rosa × odorata f. ochroleuca, commonly known as the white floribunda rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers.

It is a compact, shrub-like plant with multiple stems and is known for its profuse blooming habit. The leaves are typically dark green and slightly serrated, while the stems are often thornless. This variety is well-suited for garden borders and containers due to its hardy nature and adaptability.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, producing fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.
