Rosa × wheldonii

Rosa × wheldonii, commonly known as the Wheldon's rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically has glossy, dark green foliage and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
