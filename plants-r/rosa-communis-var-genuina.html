Rosa communis var. genuina

Rosa communis var. genuina, commonly known as the genuine common rose, is a variety of the widespread Rosa communis species.

It is characterized by its simple, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features thorny stems and serrated, dark green leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in horticulture.
