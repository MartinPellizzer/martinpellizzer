Retama raetam var. duriaei

Retama raetam var. duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei Retama, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it resilient in harsh environments. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access water from lower soil layers.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.
