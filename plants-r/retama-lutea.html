Retama lutea

Retama lutea, commonly known as yellow retama, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.

Retama lutea is also valued for its role in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
