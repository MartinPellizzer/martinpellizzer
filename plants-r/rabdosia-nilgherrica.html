Rabdosia nilgherrica

Rabdosia nilgherrica, commonly known as the Nilgiri rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within forests. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pale blue or white, and they bloom during the monsoon season.

Rabdosia nilgherrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
