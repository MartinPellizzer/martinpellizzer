Rhodiola feiyongii

Rhodiola feiyongii, commonly known as the Yunnan rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and fleshy, often blue-green leaves. It is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
