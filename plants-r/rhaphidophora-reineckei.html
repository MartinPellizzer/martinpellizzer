Rhaphidophora reineckei

Rhaphidophora reineckei, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep, rich coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Rhaphidophora reineckei is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
