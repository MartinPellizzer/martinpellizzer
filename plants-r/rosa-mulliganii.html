Rosa mulliganii

Rosa mulliganii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and is valued for its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Rosa mulliganii is also known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand mild frosts.
