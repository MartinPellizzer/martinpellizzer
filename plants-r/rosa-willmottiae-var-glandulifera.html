Rosa willmottiae var. glandulifera

Rosa willmottiae var. glandulifera, commonly known as the Willmott's rose with glandular leaves, is a hybrid rose species native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its dark green, glandular-leafed foliage and fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, though it can tolerate partial shade.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
