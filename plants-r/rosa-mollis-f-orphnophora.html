Rosa mollis f. orphnophora

Rosa mollis f. orphnophora, commonly known as the soft rose or the silky rose, is a botanical variation of the Rosa mollis species.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
