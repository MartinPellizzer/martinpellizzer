Ranunculus chinensis

Ranunculus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers with bright yellow petals and a hairy center, typically blooming in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves that are palmately lobed, and it prefers moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Ranunculus chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for centuries in gardens.
