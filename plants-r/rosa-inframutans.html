Rosa inframutans

Rosa inframutans, commonly known as the pink bankstone rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding ornamental value throughout the growing season.

Rosa inframutans is prized for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
