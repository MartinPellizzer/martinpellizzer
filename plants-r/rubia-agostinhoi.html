Rubia agostinhoi

Rubia agostinhoi, commonly known as the Agostinho's rue, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in Africa, particularly in countries like Angola and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are typically simple and opposite, with a slightly serrated margin.

Rubia agostinhoi is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.
