Rhus media

Rhus media, commonly known as smooth sumac, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and features compound leaves with 15 to 31 pairs of leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles, which are followed by red, fuzzy fruits that attract birds and other wildlife. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Rhus media is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
