Rotala juniperina

Rotala juniperina, commonly known as the Juniper-like Waterwheel, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features whorled leaves that are often dark green with a reddish or purple hue, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in the summer months. Its roots form a dense mat, which helps stabilize sediment in water bodies.

Rotala juniperina is often used in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
