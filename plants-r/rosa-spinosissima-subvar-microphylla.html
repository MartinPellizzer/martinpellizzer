Rosa spinosissima subvar. microphylla

Rosa spinosissima subvar. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf spiny rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves with finely serrated edges and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
