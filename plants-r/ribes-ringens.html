Ribes ringens

Ribes ringens, commonly known as twinflower, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in late summer.

Ribes ringens is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
