Rosa reuteri var. genuina

Rosa reuteri var. genuina, commonly known as the dwarf yellow rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a slight pinkish tinge. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a dense, bushy growth habit. It is well-suited for container gardening and rock gardens due to its size and ornamental value.

Rosa reuteri var. genuina prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
