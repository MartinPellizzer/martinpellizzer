Rebutia nogalesensis

Rebutia nogalesensis, commonly known as the Nogales giant cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments with minimal rainfall.

Rebutia nogalesensis is often cultivated for its striking appearance and resilience in harsh conditions.
