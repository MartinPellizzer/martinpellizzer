Rubus × matouschekii

Rubus × matouschekii, commonly known as the Matouschek raspberries, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It is often cultivated for its edible berries, which have a tart flavor.

This species is valued in horticulture for its adaptability and disease resistance.
