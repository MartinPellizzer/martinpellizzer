Rumex xanthorhizus

Rumex xanthorhizus, commonly known as yellow-root sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features a basal rosette of smooth, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces upright flowering stems topped with clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
