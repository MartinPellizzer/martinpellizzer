Rosa × gracilenta

Rosa × gracilenta, commonly known as the slender garden rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and semi-double to fully double flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically produces fragrant blooms in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with serrated leaf margins.

Rosa × gracilenta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively disease-resistant compared to other rose varieties.
