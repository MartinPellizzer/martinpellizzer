Ranunculus × abbatis-vendelinii

Ranunculus × abbatis-vendelinii, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and delicate appearance.
