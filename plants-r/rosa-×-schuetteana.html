Rosa × schuetteana

Rosa × schuetteana, commonly known as the Schuette rose, is a hybrid tea rose prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green foliage. The plant produces large, semi-double blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.

Rosa × schuetteana is also known for its disease resistance and adaptability to various climates.
