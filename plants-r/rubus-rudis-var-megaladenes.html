Rubus rudis var. megaladenes

Rubus rudis var. megaladenes, commonly known as the giant raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its large, robust thorns and sprawling growth habit. It produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The fruits are notably larger than those of typical raspberries, with a tart flavor.

It is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils across temperate regions.
