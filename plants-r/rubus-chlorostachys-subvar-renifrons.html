Rubus chlorostachys subvar. renifrons

Rubus chlorostachys subvar. renifrons, commonly known as the silverleaf blackberry, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries and silvery-white undersides on its leaves. The plant has alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with a distinctive silver-green coloration on the underside. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring.

The subvariety renifrons is distinguished by its broadly rounded leaf base and its unique leaf venation pattern.
