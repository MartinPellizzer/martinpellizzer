Reseda odorata var. neilgherrensis

Reseda odorata var. neilgherrensis, commonly known as the Himalayan reseda, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat succulent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
