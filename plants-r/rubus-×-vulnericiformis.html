Rubus × vulnericiformis

Rubus × vulnericiformis, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus idaeus (raspberry), resulting in a plant that exhibits characteristics of both parent species. The plant produces aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color, with a firm, tart flavor. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
