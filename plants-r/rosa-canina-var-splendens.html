Rosa canina var. splendens

Rosa canina var. splendens, commonly known as the splendid dog rose, is a cultivar of the dog rose species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and hedgerows.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery and herbal medicine.
