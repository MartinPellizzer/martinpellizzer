Rosa micrantha var. trichostyla

Rosa micrantha var. trichostyla, commonly known as the hairy-styled rose, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white to pink. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its fruit is a red, hairy follicle that matures in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
