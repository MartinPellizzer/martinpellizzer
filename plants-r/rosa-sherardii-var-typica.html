Rosa sherardii var. typica

Rosa sherardii var. typica, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
