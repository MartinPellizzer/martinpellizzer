Rapanea belepensis

Rapanea belepensis, commonly known as the Bornean rapanea, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
