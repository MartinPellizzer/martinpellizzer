Richea milliganii

Richea milliganii, commonly known as the Milligan's richea, is a unique plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often in areas with high altitudes and cool climates. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
