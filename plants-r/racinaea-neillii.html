Racinaea neillii

Racinaea neillii, commonly known as the Neill's racinaea, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts.
