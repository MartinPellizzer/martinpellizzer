Rhamphidia rubens

Rhamphidia rubens, commonly known as the red orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking red and yellow flowers, which are often fragrant. Rhamphidia rubens typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.
