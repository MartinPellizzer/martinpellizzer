Rubus × pseudohorridicaulis

Rubus × pseudohorridicaulis, commonly known as the hybrid raspberry, is a perennial shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a variety of morphological traits from its parent species.
