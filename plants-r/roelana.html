Roelana

Roelana, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Roelana, is a rare and exotic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that can range from white to deep red, with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring humid and shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Botanically, Roelana is noted for its complex floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
