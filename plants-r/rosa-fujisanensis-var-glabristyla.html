Rosa fujisanensis var. glabristyla

Rosa fujisanensis var. glabristyla, commonly known as the Japanese mountain rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the high altitudes of Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a deep green color. This variety is well adapted to cool, moist environments and thrives in partial to full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to flourish in rocky, well-drained soils.
