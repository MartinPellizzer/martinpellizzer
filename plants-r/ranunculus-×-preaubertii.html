Ranunculus × preaubertii

Ranunculus × preaubertii, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting central blotch. The plant has palmate, deeply lobed leaves that are typically hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ranunculus × preaubertii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
