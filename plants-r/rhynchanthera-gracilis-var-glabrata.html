Rhynchanthera gracilis var. glabrata

Rhynchanthera gracilis var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth slender orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive spurred shape typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
