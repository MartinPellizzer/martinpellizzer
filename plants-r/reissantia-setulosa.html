Reissantia setulosa

Reissantia setulosa, commonly known as the hairy reissantia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
