Rhododendron kiusianum

Rhododendron kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusi rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron kiusianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
