Rolofa

Rolofa, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, silvery-gray foliage that provides a unique visual appeal. The plant typically has small, glossy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Rolofa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic value and air-purifying properties.
