Robiquetia mooreana

Robiquetia mooreana, commonly known as the Moore's rock fig, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its woody, shrub-like structure. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of the Moraceae family. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Robiquetia mooreana is adapted to arid environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops.
