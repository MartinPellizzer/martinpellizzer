Rosenbergia trianae

Rosenbergia trianae, commonly known as the Triana's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Rosenbergia trianae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
