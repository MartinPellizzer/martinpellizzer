Rubus Moluccanus Var. Fordii

Rubus moluccanus var. fordii is a fascinating species of bramble native to eastern Asia. It is known for its distinctive appearance and unique growth habits. This plant thrives in a variety of environments, including forests and shrublands. Its berries are often used in traditional medicine and local cuisines. Due to its ornamental value, it has also become popular among gardeners and horticulturists.
