Ruschia graminea

Ruschia graminea, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
