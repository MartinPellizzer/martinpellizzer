Rosa × spinulifolia vestita

Rosa × spinulifolia vestita, commonly known as the velvet rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant is native to the eastern United States and thrives in partial shade to full sun. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
