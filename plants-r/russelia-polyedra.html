Russelia polyedra

Russelia polyedra, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Russelia polyedra is often cultivated for its showy blooms and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
