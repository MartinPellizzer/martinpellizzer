Ruellia nudiflora var. grandiflora

Ruellia nudiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Ruellia nudiflora var. grandiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
