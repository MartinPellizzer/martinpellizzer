Rosa Trachyphylla F. Kuhbergensis

Rosa trachyphylla f. kuhbergensis is a rare and unique cultivar of the rose family known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. This plant is a form of the prickly rose, which is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It features dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves that thrive in cooler climates. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, adding a delicate touch to its rugged exterior. Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
