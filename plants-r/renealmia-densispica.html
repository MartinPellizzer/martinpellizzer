Renealmia densispica

Renealmia densispica, commonly known as the dense-spined renealmia, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with prominent, dense spines along the margins. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
