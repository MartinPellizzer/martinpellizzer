Rorippa Sarmentosa

Rorippa sarmentosa is a small, herbaceous plant commonly found in moist environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, known for its distinctive flower structures. This species is often seen growing along stream banks and in wet meadows. Its creeping growth habit allows it to spread easily across disturbed soils. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
