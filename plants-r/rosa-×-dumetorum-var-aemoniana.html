Rosa × dumetorum var. aemoniana

Rosa × dumetorum var. aemoniana, commonly known as the Aemonian shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and ovate, serrated leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem structure, simple leaves with a hairy underside, and a preference for well-drained, fertile soils.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
