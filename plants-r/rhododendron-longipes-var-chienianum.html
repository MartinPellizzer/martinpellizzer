Rhododendron longipes var. chienianum

Rhododendron longipes var. chienianum, commonly known as the Chien's evergreen rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and prefers partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in spring and attract pollinators.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
