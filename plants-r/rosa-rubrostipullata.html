Rosa rubrostipullata

Rosa rubrostipullata, commonly known as the red-stippled rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a distinctive red or reddish-brown stippling on the petals.

Rosa rubrostipullata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
