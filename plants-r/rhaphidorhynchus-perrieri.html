Rhaphidorhynchus perrieri

Rhaphidorhynchus perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's rhynchus, is a unique plant species belonging to the family Rhynchophyta.

The plant exhibits a limited distribution, primarily found in specific tropical regions with high humidity.
