Rosa × Complicata F. Genuina

Genuine rosa, scientifically known as Rosa × complicata f. genuina, is a rare and captivating hybrid rose prized for its unique floral characteristics. This cultivar is distinguished by its compact growth habit and vibrant, velvety blooms that come in a range of colors from deep red to soft pink. Native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States, it thrives in cool, moist climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its delicate fragrance and ornamental value make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant, often overlooked in favor of more common rose varieties.
