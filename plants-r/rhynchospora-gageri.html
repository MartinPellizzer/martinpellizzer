Rhynchospora gageri

Rhynchospora gageri, commonly known as Gager's spikegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color, often arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Rhynchospora gageri thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.
