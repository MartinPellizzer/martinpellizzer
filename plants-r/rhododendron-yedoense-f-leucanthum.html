Rhododendron yedoense f. leucanthum

Rhododendron yedoense f. leucanthum, commonly known as the Ueno azalea, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a compact shrub with dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The blooms are typically pale pink to white, with a distinctive, almost porcelain-like appearance. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in horticultural displays.
