Rhaphiolepis jiulongjiangensis

Rhaphiolepis jiulongjiangensis, commonly known as the Jiulongjiang rose.

It is a shrub native to China, characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy pink or red flowers. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of fragrant flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
