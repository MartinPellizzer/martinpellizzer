Ruschia Umbellata

Ruschia umbellata is a striking succulent native to southern Africa known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. This plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its clusters of showy blooms appear in late spring, adding a dramatic touch to any garden. Easy to care for, it requires minimal water and full sun to flourish. A popular choice for rock gardens and containers, it's a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
