Ritterocactus horstii

Ritterocactus horstii, commonly known as the Horstii cactus, is a species native to the deserts of northern Mexico.

It is a small, columnar cactus with a distinctive rib structure and a gray-green to blueish coloration. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are often followed by red or orange fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments, with spines that provide shade and reduce water loss.

Ritterocactus horstii is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
