Rosa mollis f. lygaea

Rosa mollis f. lygaea, commonly known as the Lygaea rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a slight blush of red. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a favored plant among gardeners interested in cultivating native or adapted flora.
