Rubus micans var. purpureiflorus

Rubus micans var. purpureiflorus, commonly known as the purpleflower bramble, is a variant of the bramble genus Rubus.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a red, edible berry similar to raspberries.

This species is typically found in moist, shaded environments in temperate regions.
