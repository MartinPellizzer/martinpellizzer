Rhinotropis lindheimeri var. eucosma

Rhinotropis lindheimeri var. eucosma, commonly known as Lindheimer's rhinocerosweed, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
