Rosa wichurana f. ohwii

Rosa wichurana f. ohwii, commonly known as the Ohwi's rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering, with flowers that range from white to deep pink. The plant typically produces semi-double to fully double blooms, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various garden conditions.
