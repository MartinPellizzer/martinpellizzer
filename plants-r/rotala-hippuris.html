Rotala hippuris

Rotala hippuris, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern on the water's surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and marshes.

Rotala hippuris is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in water gardens or as a decorative plant in aquariums.
