Reynosia latifolia

Reynosia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf reynosia, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by spherical, orange-red fruits. Reynosia latifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
