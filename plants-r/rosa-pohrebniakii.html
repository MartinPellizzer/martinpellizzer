Rosa pohrebniakii

Rosa pohrebniakii, commonly known as the Ukrainian funeral rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Ukraine.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with serrated edges, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry environments.

Due to its cultural significance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
