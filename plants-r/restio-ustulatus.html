Restio ustulatus

Restio ustulatus, commonly known as the scorched restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Restio ustulatus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
