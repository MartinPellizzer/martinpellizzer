Rosa lindoeensis

Rosa lindoeensis, commonly known as the Lindoe rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered with a fine layer of white hairs.

Rosa lindoeensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
