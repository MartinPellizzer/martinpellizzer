Rubus virilis

Rubus virilis, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a perennial shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of numerous drupelets, which are typically bright red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Rubus virilis is valued for its edible berries and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
