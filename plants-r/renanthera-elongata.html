Renanthera elongata

Renanthera elongata, commonly known as the elongated orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces aerial roots that help it climb and attach to trees or other structures. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Renanthera elongata is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
