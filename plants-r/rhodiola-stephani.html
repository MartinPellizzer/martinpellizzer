Rhodiola stephani

Rhodiola stephani, commonly known as the Armenian rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia.

It is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
