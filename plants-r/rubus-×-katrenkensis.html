Rubus × katrenkensis

Rubus × katrenkensis, commonly known as the Katrenkens raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is known for its edible, red berries. It has been cultivated for its flavor and ornamental value.

Its botanical features include compound leaves and a shrub-like growth habit.
