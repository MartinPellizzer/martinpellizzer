Rosa karelinii

Rosa karelinii, commonly known as the Siberian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems with thorny branches and pinnately compound leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. Rosa karelinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in cold climates.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
