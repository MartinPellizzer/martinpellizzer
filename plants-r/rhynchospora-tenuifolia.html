Rhynchospora tenuifolia

Rhynchospora tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf beakspur, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. This plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, wetlands, and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.
