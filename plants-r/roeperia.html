Roeperia

Roeperia, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

These plants are typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. They have a compact growth habit with hairy leaves and are often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Due to their ornamental value and ecological significance, they are of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
