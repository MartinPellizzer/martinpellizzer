Rhododendron bergii

Rhododendron bergii, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in Nepal, Bhutan, and northern India.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in cool, moist environments. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and densely arranged along its stems. Its flowers are showy, ranging from pink to deep red, and bloom in spring.

Rhododendron bergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
