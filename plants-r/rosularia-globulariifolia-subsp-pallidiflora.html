Rosularia globulariifolia subsp. pallidiflora

Rosularia globulariifolia subsp.

pallidiflora, commonly known as the paleflower globular rose. It is a low-growing, succulent shrub native to arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant features dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical stems that form a rosette structure. Its flowers are small, pale yellow, and bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and arid landscapes.
