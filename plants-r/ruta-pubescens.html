Ruta pubescens

Ruta pubescens, commonly known as common rue, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features aromatic, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive bitter scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into narrow, elongated fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry environments.

Ruta pubescens is valued in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in gardens.
