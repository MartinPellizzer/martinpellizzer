Rosa coziae

Rosa coziae, commonly known as the Cozia rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Romania, where it thrives in rocky and forested habitats. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are covered in thorny projections.

Rosa coziae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
