Rhododendron Tsangpoense Var. Pruniflorum

Rhododendron tsangpoense var. pruniflorum is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of eastern Tibet. This variant of the rhododendron is known for its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to its mountainous habitat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in dense forests and alpine meadows. Due to its limited range and fragile ecosystem, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from climate change and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure for future generations.
