Reseda aucheri var. rechingeri

Reseda aucheri var. rechingeri, commonly known as the Alpine reseda, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Reseda aucheri var. rechingeri is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
