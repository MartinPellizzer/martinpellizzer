Rosa mollis var. nana

Rosa mollis var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf shrub rose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has dense, dark green foliage that provides a lush appearance throughout the growing season. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

It is generally hardy and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
