Rubus milliformis f. latifolius

Rubus milliformis f. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf raspberry.

This variant is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its broader leaves compared to the typical form. It produces aggregate fruits composed of small, drupe-like berries. The plant is typically found in moist, shady habitats such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species within its native range.
