Rhododendron jiulongshanense

Rhododendron jiulongshanense, commonly known as the Jiulongshan rhododendron, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and high humidity. Its floral display consists of pink to red blooms, which appear in spring.
