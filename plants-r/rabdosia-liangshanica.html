Rabdosia liangshanica

Rabdosia liangshanica, commonly known as the Liangshan rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Liangshan region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
