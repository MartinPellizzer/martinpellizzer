Rhynchosia fleckii

Rhynchosia fleckii, commonly known as the Fleck's cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

Rhynchosia fleckii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
