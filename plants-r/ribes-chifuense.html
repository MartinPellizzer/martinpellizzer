Ribes chifuense

Ribes chifuense, commonly known as the Chifu gooseberry, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Ribes.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green berries that are typically harvested in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges, while its flowers are usually pink or white.

Ribes chifuense is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential culinary uses.
