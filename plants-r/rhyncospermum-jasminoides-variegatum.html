Rhyncospermum jasminoides variegatum

Rhyncospermum jasminoides variegatum, commonly known as the variegated jasmine vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its attractive, variegated foliage with white and green patterns, and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers.
