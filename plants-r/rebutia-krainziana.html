Rebutia krainziana

Rebutia krainziana, commonly known as the Krainz's Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, ribbed stems. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in the spring. Rebutia krainziana is well adapted to arid environments, with a shallow root system and a tendency to form clumps over time.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
