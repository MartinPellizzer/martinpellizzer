Rosa amansii

Rosa amansii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, red berries that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Rosa amansii thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
