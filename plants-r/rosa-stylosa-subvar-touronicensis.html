Rosa stylosa subvar. touronicensis

Rosa stylosa subvar. touronicensis, commonly known as the Touronicensis rose, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
