Rosenia nestleroides

Rosenia nestleroides, commonly known as the Nestler's rose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family, which includes roses and other shrubs. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. It typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and prefers cool, temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
