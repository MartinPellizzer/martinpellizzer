Rebis ebracteolata

Rebis ebracteolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rebis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Rebis ebracteolata is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing a robust root system for water retention.
