Rosa vassilczenkoi

Rosa vassilczenkoi, commonly known as the Altai rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its botanical features include hirsute stems and ovate leaves with serrated edges.

Rosa vassilczenkoi is adapted to harsh alpine environments, showcasing resilience to cold and arid conditions.
