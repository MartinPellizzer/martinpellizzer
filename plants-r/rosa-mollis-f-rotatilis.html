Rosa mollis f. rotatilis

Rosa mollis f. rotatilis, commonly known as the curled rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa mollis species.

It is characterized by its tightly curled, spiral-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a compact shrub, typically growing to a height of about 1 to 2 feet.

This variety is well-suited for small garden spaces and is often used as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage.
