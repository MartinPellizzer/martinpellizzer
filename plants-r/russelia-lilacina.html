Russelia lilacina

Russelia lilacina, commonly known as the purple glorybower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from lavender to deep purple. The plant produces showy clusters of blooms that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Russelia lilacina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.
