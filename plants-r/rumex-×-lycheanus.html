Rumex × lycheanus

Rumex × lycheanus, commonly known as the Chinese sorrel, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in hue. It is native to East Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and edible leaves, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional cuisine.
