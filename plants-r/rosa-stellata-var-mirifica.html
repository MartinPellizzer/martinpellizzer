Rosa stellata var. mirifica

Rosa stellata var. mirifica, commonly known as the starry rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa stellata species, which is native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate water and pruning for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers.
