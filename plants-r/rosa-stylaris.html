Rosa stylaris

Rosa stylaris, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and the Korean Peninsula. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with finely serrated leaflets.

Rosa stylaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
