Rabdosia polystachys var. phyllodioides

Rabdosia polystachys var. phyllodioides, commonly known as the silverleaf rabdosia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
