Rubus tiponensis

Rubus tiponensis, commonly known as the Tipon raspberry, is a species of bramble native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of shrubs commonly referred to as blackberries or raspberries. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas of forests.

Its botanical features include thorny stems, trifoliate leaves, and a complex floral structure typical of the Rubus genus.
