Ribes sylvestre var. smidtianum

Ribes sylvestre var. smidtianum, commonly known as Smidt's gooseberry, is a variant of the European gooseberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant features small, green, edible berries that turn red when mature. Its leaves are broadly ovate with serrated edges and a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
