Rattraya ramosa

Rattraya ramosa, commonly known as the hairy rat-tail plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive hairy stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Rattraya ramosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
