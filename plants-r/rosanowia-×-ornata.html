Rosanowia × ornata

Rosanowia × ornata, commonly known as the hybrid rose.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of variability in flower color and form.
