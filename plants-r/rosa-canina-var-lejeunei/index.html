Rosa Canina Var. Lejeunei

Rosa canina var. lejeunei is a rare and lesser-known subspecies of the common dog rose. It is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and parts of southern Europe. This variety is distinguished by its smaller, more compact growth and unique floral characteristics. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
