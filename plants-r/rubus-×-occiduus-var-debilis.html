Rubus × occiduus var. debilis

Rubus × occiduus var. debilis, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

This variant is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its delicate and often sprawling growth habit. It produces small, dark berries that are typically less robust than those of its parent species. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves and thorny stems, typical of many Rubus species.
