Rosa × salaevensis f. perrieri

Rosa × salaevensis f. perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri rose, is a hybrid rose species with distinctive botanical features.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, typically pink to red, with a prominent central bloom. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
