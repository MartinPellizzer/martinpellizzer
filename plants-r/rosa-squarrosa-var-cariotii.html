Rosa squarrosa var. cariotii

Rosa squarrosa var. cariotii, commonly known as the Cariotii rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorned stems and heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in a woolly layer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
