Rhapis subtilis

Rhapis subtilis, commonly known as the slender reed palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a compact, upright palm with narrow, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by black, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
