Roldana Anisophylla

Roldana anisophylla is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its strikingly different shaped leaves, which are narrow on one side and broad on the other. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its delicate flowers, which bloom in shades of white and pink, add to its ornamental appeal. Due to habitat loss, Roldana anisophylla is now considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
