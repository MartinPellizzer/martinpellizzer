Rubus clethrocharis

Rubus clethrocharis, commonly known as the narrowleaf blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. The plant produces small, dark berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive narrow shape.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
