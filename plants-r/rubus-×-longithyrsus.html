Rubus × longithyrsus

Rubus × longithyrsus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
