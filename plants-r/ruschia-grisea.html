Ruschia grisea

Ruschia grisea, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
