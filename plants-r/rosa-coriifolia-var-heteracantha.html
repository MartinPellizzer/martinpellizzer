Rosa coriifolia var. heteracantha

Rosa coriifolia var. heteracantha, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
