Rosa rubiginosa var. timbalii

Rosa rubiginosa var. timbalii, commonly known as the Timbalai rose, is a shrub-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features thorny stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and is often found in coastal and wetland areas. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.

The species is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
