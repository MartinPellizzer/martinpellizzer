Rosa ludingensis

Rosa ludingensis, commonly known as the Luding rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with thorny stems and pinnate leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rosa ludingensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
