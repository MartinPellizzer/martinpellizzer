Ruellia lechleri

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon rainforest. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. Its sap is highly viscous and has been historically used for its medicinal properties.
