Rhaptopetalum soyauxii

Rhaptopetalum soyauxii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, erect stem that bears a single, showy flower. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces a distinctive, fragrant flower with a yellow lip and violet petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
