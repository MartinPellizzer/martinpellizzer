Rubus caflischii proles thelybatos

Rubus caflischii proles thelybatos, commonly known as the Caflisch's raspberry.

This plant is a species of bramble characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to specific regions in South America. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the flowers are white with five petals.

The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor.
