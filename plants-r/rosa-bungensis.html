Rosa bungensis

Rosa bungensis, commonly known as the Bungensis rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Rosa bungensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
