Rosa × cimelium

Rosa × cimelium, commonly known as the Cimelium rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and the production of large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has a bushy habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Rosa × cimelium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
