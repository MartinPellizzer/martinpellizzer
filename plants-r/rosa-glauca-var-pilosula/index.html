Rosa Glauca Var. Pilosula

Rosa glauca var. pilosula is a rare and striking plant known for its unique appearance and hardy nature. This variant of the blue rose is characterized by its fuzzy, silvery foliage and pale pink blooms that stand out in any garden. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its limited availability and specific growing requirements. Cultivating this plant can be a rewarding challenge for experienced gardeners seeking something truly distinct.
