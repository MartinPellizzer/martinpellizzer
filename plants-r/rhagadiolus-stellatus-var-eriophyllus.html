Rhagadiolus stellatus var. eriophyllus

Rhagadiolus stellatus var. eriophyllus, commonly known as the star-shaped daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence with white ray florets surrounding a central yellow disk. The plant typically grows in well-drained sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
