Reseda suffruticosa subsp. barrelieri

Reseda suffruticosa subsp.

barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's Reseda, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in dense clusters.

The plant has woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for dry, sunny environments.
