Rhododendron esetulosum

Rhododendron esetulosum, commonly known as the Hainan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters and blooming in the spring.

Rhododendron esetulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
