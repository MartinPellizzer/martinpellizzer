Rubus schleicheri var. radbae

Rubus schleicheri var. radbae, commonly known as the Radbae raspberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Vietnam.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe, similar to other raspberries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and for local use.
