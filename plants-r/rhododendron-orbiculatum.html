Rhododendron orbiculatum

Rhododendron orbiculatum, commonly known as the Japanese azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It features dense, compact growth with showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant floral display. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
