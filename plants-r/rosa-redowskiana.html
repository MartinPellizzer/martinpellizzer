Rosa redowskiana

Rosa redowskiana, commonly known as the Redowskiana rose, is a species native to eastern Europe and western Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color, appearing in late spring to early summer. Rosa redowskiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

It requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
