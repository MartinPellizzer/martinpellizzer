Ruschia erecta

Ruschia erecta, commonly known as the upright ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to arid landscapes.
