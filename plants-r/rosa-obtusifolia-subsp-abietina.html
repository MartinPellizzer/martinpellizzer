Rosa obtusifolia subsp. abietina

Rosa obtusifolia subsp.

abietina, commonly known as the Alpine rose or mountain rose, is a shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, pink to white flowers.

The plant produces rose hips that are typically red or orange, containing the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh climatic conditions.
