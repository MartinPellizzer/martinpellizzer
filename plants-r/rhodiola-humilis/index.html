Rhodiola Humilis

Rhodiola humilis is a hardy perennial plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and other mountainous regions. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is known for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and low nutrient soils. This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported adaptogenic properties. Its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers make it a unique and resilient member of the Crassulaceae family. Despite its modest appearance, Rhodiola humilis holds significant ecological and medicinal value.
