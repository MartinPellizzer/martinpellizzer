Rosa kalmiussica var. kelleriana

Rosa kalmiussica var. kelleriana, commonly known as the Keller's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dense, thornless stems that form a rounded shape, making it an attractive ornamental plant. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing year-round visual interest.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for garden borders or as a hedge.
