Regelia inops

Regelia inops, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or among leaf litter. The plant has long, slender stems and produces small, pale green or white flowers that are unique in appearance.
