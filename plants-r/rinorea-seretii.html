Rinorea seretii

Rinorea seretii, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season.

Rinorea seretii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and riverine areas.
