Rosa laurentiae

Rosa laurentiae, commonly known as the Laurentian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a velvety texture. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa laurentiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
