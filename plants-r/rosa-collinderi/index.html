Rosa Collinderi

Rosa collinderi is a rare and delicate species of wild rose native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. This plant is known for its striking pink flowers and fragrant scent, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky outcrops and forested areas. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Rosa collinderi is now classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical treasure.
