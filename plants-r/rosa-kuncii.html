Rosa kuncii

Rosa kuncii, commonly known as the Kuncii rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a serrated margin. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with five petals and a prominent central pistil.

Rosa kuncii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
