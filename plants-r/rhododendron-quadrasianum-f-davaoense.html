Rhododendron quadrasianum f. davaoense

Rhododendron quadrasianum f. davaoense, commonly known as the Davao rhododendron, is a unique hybrid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained acidic soil. Its foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

This rare species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
