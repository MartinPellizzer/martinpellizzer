Rosa subcinnamomea

Rosa subcinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to crimson flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Rosa subcinnamomea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine.
