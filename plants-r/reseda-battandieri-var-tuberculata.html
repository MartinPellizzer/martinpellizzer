Reseda battandieri var. tuberculata

Reseda battandieri var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate reseda, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
