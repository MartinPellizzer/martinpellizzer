Rosa fargesii

Rosa fargesii, commonly known as the Farges rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to reddish-pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins and a dense covering of fine hairs. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Rosa fargesii is also valued for its role in traditional Chinese medicine.
