Radiusia sericea

Radiusia sericea, commonly known as silverwood tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, silver-gray bark and long, slender leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery hair. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
