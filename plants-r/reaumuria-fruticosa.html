Reaumuria fruticosa

Reaumuria fruticosa, commonly known as the sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to arid and coastal regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is characterized by its yellow or orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces flowers that are typically yellow or green. It thrives in sandy or saline soils and is often found in coastal dunes or dry habitats.

Reaumuria fruticosa plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing food for wildlife.
