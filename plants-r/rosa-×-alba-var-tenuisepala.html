Rosa × alba var. tenuisepala

Rosa × alba var. tenuisepala, commonly known as the slender-sepal white rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which often have a delicate, almost translucent appearance. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its sepals are notably narrow and elongated, giving it the specific epithet *tenuisepala*.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
