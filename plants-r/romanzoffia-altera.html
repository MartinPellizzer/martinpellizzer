Romanzoffia altera

Romanzoffia altera, commonly known as the slender waterwheel plant, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of narrow, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Romanzoffia altera is often used in aquascaping due to its attractive appearance and ability to oxygenate water.
