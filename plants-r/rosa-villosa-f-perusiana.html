Rosa villosa f. perusiana

Rosa villosa f. perusiana, commonly known as the Perusian shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
