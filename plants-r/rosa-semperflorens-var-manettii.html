Rosa semperflorens var. manettii

Rosa semperflorens var. manettii, commonly known as the Manettii shrub rose, is a compact, everblooming shrub rose that produces abundant clusters of small, fragrant flowers.

It is native to the southern United States and thrives in full sun to partial shade, requiring well-drained soil for optimal growth. The plant is known for its hardiness and disease resistance, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warmer climates. Its foliage is dark green and dense, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

This variety is often used in borders, containers, and as a hedge due to its versatility and ornamental value.
