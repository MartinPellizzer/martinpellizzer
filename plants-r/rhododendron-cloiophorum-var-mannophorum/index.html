Rhododendron Cloiophorum Var. Mannophorum

Rhododendron cloiophorum var. mannophorum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It is known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variant of the rhododendron is particularly prized for its unique coloration and compact growth habit. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered in its natural range. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful and delicate species.
