Rosa reuteri f. sandbergeri

Rosa reuteri f. sandbergeri, commonly known as the Sandberger's rose, is a compact shrub with dense, upright growth habit.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the blooms. It is native to the western United States and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
