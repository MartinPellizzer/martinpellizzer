Rhododendron qinghaiense

Rhododendron qinghaiense, commonly known as the Qinghai rhododendron, is a species native to the Qinghai Province in China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, thriving in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its botanical features include broad, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well adapted to its alpine habitat.

Rhododendron qinghaiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in mountainous ecosystems.
