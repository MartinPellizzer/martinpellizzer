Rosa edita

Rosa edita, commonly known as the European rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in woodlands and gardens.

Rosa edita is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery and medicine.
