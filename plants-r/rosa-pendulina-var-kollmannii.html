Rosa pendulina var. kollmannii

Rosa pendulina var. kollmannii, commonly known as the Kollmann's weeping rose, is a cultivar of the shrub rose that features long, drooping stems and abundant, fragrant flowers.

It is characterized by its soft pink to white blooms that appear in early summer. The plant has narrow, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its cascading form.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its graceful appearance.
