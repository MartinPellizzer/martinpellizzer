Ruellia lactea

Ruellia lactea, commonly known as the milkweed ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Ruellia lactea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
