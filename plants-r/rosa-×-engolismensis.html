Rosa × engolismensis

Rosa × engolismensis, commonly known as the Engolismen rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The Engolismen rose is valued for its hardiness and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season.
