Richetia subcylindrica

Richetia subcylindrica, commonly known as the slender rock cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers in early spring. It is characterized by its subcylindrical stem, which is often covered in fine hairs.
