Rosa mollis f. fericula

Rosa mollis f. fericula, commonly known as the soft rose or silky rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a slight blush of red. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Rosa mollis f. fericula is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
