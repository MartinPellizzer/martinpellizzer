Rosa skofitzii

Rosa skofitzii, commonly known as the Skofitzii rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets arranged alternately along the stem.

Rosa skofitzii is well adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
