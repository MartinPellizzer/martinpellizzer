Rupicapnos rifana

Rupicapnos rifana, commonly known as the red-flowered rock rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage, with dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Rupicapnos rifana is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
