Ranunculus stellaris var. supernova

Ranunculus stellaris var. supernova, commonly known as the starry buttercup, is a striking member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that typically display a mix of yellow and white petals. The plant features a basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to bloom in early spring.
