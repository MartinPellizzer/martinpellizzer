Ricinocarpus welwitschianus

Ricinocarpus welwitschianus, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds.
