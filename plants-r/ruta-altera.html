Ruta altera

Ruta altera, commonly known as lesser rock-rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Ruta altera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
