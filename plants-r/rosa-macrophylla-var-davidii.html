Rosa macrophylla var. davidii

Rosa macrophylla var. davidii, commonly known as the David's shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features broad, glossy leaves and has a vigorous growth habit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is well-suited to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
