Rubus phoenicolasius f. albiflorus

Rubus phoenicolasius f. albiflorus, commonly known as the whiteflower Chinese raspberry, is a variant of the Chinese raspberry species.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit, similar to raspberries, is edible and has a tart flavor.

This cultivar is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
