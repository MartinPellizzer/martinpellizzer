Rosa zhongdianensis

Rosa zhongdianensis, commonly known as the Zhongdian rose, is a species native to the Yunnan province in China.

It is a shrub that typically grows in alpine meadows at high elevations. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant pink to red flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and has a relatively short growing season.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
