Rosa wichuraiana var. taisensis

Rosa wichuraiana var. taisensis, commonly known as the Taisan rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is typically shrub-like in growth habit. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
