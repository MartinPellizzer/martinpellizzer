Ranunculus oligodon

Ranunculus oligodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features delicate, palmately divided leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it produces small, hairy fruits that contain several seeds.

Ranunculus oligodon is often found in calcareous soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
