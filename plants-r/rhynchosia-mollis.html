Rhynchosia mollis

Rhynchosia mollis, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.
