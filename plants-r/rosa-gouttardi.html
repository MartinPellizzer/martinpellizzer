Rosa gouttardi

Rosa gouttardi, commonly known as the Gouttard rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Its stems are covered in thorns, and it is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Rosa gouttardi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
