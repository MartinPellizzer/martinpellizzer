Rhapis puhuongensis

Rhapis puhuongensis, commonly known as the Puhuong bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its slender, clustered stems and gracefully arching leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by edible, round fruits.
