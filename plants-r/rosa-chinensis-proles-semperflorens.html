Rosa chinensis proles semperflorens

Rosa chinensis proles semperflorens, commonly known as the everblooming China rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, double flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant is a shrub with dark green, glossy leaves and thorned stems, typical of many rose species. It is highly valued for its continuous blooming throughout the growing season, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese culture for their aesthetic and symbolic significance.
