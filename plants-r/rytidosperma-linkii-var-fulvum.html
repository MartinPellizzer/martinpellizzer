Rytidosperma linkii var. fulvum

Rytidosperma linkii var. fulvum, commonly known as the golden speargrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often tinged with a golden hue, particularly at the base. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are adorned with pale yellow to golden flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
