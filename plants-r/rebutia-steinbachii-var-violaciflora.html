Rebutia steinbachii var. violaciflora

Rebutia steinbachii var. violaciflora, commonly known as the violet-flowered Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a dense cluster of cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, violet-pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its succulent nature allows it to store water in its stems, making it drought-tolerant.

It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental flowers.
