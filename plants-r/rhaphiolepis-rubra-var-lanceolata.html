Rhaphiolepis rubra var. lanceolata

Rhaphiolepis rubra var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, orange-red flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant seasonal color make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
