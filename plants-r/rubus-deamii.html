Rubus deamii

Rubus deamii, commonly known as Deam's blackberry, is a species of shrub in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
