Rubus insericatus var. callistemon

Rubus insericatus var. callistemon, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as drupes, which are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Asia.
