Rhododendron temenium var. temenium

Rhododendron temenium var. temenium, commonly known as the Temenian rhododendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and high humidity. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers can range in color from pink to red.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
