Ranunculus calvitorus

Ranunculus calvitorus, commonly known as the smooth meadow buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with five to eight petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are usually smooth and rounded, with a hairy underside. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Ranunculus calvitorus is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.
