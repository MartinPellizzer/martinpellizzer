Rosa × speciosa

Rosa × speciosa, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant produces deep pink to red petals with a subtle, sweet fragrance. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
