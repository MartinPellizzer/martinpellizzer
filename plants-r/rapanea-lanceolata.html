Rapanea lanceolata

Rapanea lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf evergreen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dense, evergreen foliage.
