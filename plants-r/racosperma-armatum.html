Racosperma armatum

Racosperma armatum, commonly known as the silky wattle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features feathery, bipinnate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, followed by elongated seed pods.

Racosperma armatum is adapted to various soil types and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.
