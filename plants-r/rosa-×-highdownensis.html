Rosa × highdownensis

Rosa × highdownensis, commonly known as the Highdown rose, is a hybrid shrub that originates from England.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant is typically found in coastal areas and is well adapted to salty air and sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Rosa × highdownensis is a member of the Rosaceae family and is closely related to other rose species.
