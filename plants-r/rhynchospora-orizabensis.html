Rhynchospora orizabensis

Rhynchospora orizabensis, commonly known as the Orizaba sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
