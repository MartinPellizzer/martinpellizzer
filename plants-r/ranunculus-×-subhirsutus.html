Ranunculus × subhirsutus

Ranunculus × subhirsutus, commonly known as the hairy buttercup, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in moist, grassy areas.

Ranunculus × subhirsutus is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and landscapes.
