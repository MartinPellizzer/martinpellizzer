Rubus rivularis var. flexisetus

Rubus rivularis var. flexisetus, commonly known as the flexible blackberry.

This variant is a shrub native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It features trailing or prostrate stems with dense, thorny growth. The leaves are trifoliate, with serrated margins and a dark green upper surface.

Its flowers are white or pale pink, followed by edible berries that are smaller and more acidic than typical blackberries.
