Ranunculus sceleratiformis

Ranunculus sceleratiformis, commonly known as the death's-head hawkmoth plant, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features striking yellow flowers with a distinctive black spot at the base, resembling a skull, hence its common name. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
