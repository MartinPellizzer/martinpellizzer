Rosa × waitziana var. chabertii

Rosa × waitziana var. chabertii, commonly known as the Chabert's hybrid rose, is a shrub-like plant native to China.

It features dense, thorny stems and is characterized by its compact growth habit. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
