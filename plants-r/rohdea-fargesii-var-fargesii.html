Rohdea fargesii var. fargesii

Rohdea fargesii var. fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese basket fern, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its rosette of stiff, lance-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, often with a slight bluish tint. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring, followed by red-brown berries.

This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
