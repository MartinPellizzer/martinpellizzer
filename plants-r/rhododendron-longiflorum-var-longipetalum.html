Rhododendron longiflorum var. longipetalum

Rhododendron longiflorum var. longipetalum, commonly known as the longipetalum rhododendron, is a variant of the longiflorum species native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, with long, narrow petals. The plant is an evergreen shrub that thrives in cool, moist climates with acidic soil. Its botanical features include dense foliage and a compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.
