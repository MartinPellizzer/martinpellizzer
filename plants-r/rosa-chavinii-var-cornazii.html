Rosa chavinii var. cornazii

Rosa chavinii var. cornazii, commonly known as the Cornazii rose, is a hybrid rose species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are covered in a fine layer of down. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
