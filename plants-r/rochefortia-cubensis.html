Rochefortia cubensis

Rochefortia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
