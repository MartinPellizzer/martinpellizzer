Rosa × reversa alleizettei

Rosa × reversa alleizettei, commonly known as the Allemann's rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It features dense, thornless stems and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces fragrant, single or double blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
