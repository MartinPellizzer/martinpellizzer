Rosa davurica f. marrettii

Rosa davurica f. marrettii, commonly known as the Manchurian rose, is a cold-hardy shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features thorned stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in temperate climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners in cooler regions.
