Rhynchodia rhynchosperma

Rhynchodia rhynchosperma, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Rhynchodia rhynchosperma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
