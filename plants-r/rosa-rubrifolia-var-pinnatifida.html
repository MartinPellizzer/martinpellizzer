Rosa rubrifolia var. pinnatifida

Rosa rubrifolia var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the red-leafed rose with pinnate foliage, is a variant of the Rosa rubrifolia species.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or reddish-purple leaves, which contrast with its typical pink to white flowers. The plant is a deciduous shrub, native to certain regions of Asia, particularly China. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and blooming habit make it a visually appealing addition to gardens and landscapes.
