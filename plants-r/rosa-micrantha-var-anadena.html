Rosa micrantha var. anadena

Rosa micrantha var. anadena, commonly known as the Anadenan rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including China, Vietnam, and Myanmar. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, and its shrub-like growth habit. It produces rose hips that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
