Rosa × ficticia

Rosa × ficticia, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a cultivated variety resulting from the crossbreeding of different Rosa species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × ficticia is widely appreciated in gardens for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements.
