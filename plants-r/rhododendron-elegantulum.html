Rhododendron elegantulum

Rhododendron elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a slight pubescence on the underside. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its aesthetic appeal.
