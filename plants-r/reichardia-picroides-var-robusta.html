Reichardia picroides var. robusta

Reichardia picroides var. robusta, commonly known as the broadleaf buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a wavy margin and a hairy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically arranged in a cluster at the top of the stems. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, meadows, and along stream banks.

Reichardia picroides var. robusta is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
