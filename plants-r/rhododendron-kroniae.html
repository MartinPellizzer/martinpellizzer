Rhododendron kroniae

Rhododendron kroniae, commonly known as the Kronsfeld rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. It is often found in forested areas at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
