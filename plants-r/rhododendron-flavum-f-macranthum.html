Rhododendron flavum f. macranthum

Rhododendron flavum f. macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered yellow rhododendron, is a hybrid form of the yellow rhododendron.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is evergreen, with dense, leathery leaves that provide year-round foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental planting in temperate climates.
