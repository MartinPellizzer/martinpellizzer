Rosa poterium

Rosa poterium, commonly known as the Poterium rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable.
