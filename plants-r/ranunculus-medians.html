Ranunculus medians

Ranunculus medians, commonly known as the Persian buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, with a distinctive central pistil. The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are often hairy and covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens with their delicate, buttercup-like appearance.
