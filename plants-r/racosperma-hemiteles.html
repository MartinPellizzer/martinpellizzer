Racosperma hemiteles

Racosperma hemiteles, commonly known as the yellow wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Racosperma hemiteles is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.
