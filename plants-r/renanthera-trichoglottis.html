Renanthera trichoglottis

Renanthera trichoglottis, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the warmer months.

Renanthera trichoglottis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
