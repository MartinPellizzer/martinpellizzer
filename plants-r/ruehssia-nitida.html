Ruehssia nitida

Ruehssia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf ruehssia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

Ruehssia nitida is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
