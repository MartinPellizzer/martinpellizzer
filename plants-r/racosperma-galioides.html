Racosperma galioides

Racosperma galioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as a shrub with slender, wiry stems and small, bipinnate leaves. Its yellow flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom during the autumn season.
