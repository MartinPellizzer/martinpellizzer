Rosa aldersonii

Rosa aldersonii, commonly known as the Alderson's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. This shrub typically grows in rocky or arid environments, exhibiting a compact form with thorny stems. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with five petals, and bloom in the spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh conditions.
