Rosa × francofurtensis

Rosa × francofurtensis, commonly known as the Frankfurt rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding visual appeal throughout the growing season.

Rosa × francofurtensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively disease-resistant, making it a popular choice in gardens.
