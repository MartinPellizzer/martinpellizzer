Rosa mollis f. boeoschista

Rosa mollis f. boeoschista, commonly known as the Boeoschista rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is a compact shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate appearance.
