Rubus granulatus var. krasanii

Rubus granulatus var. krasanii, commonly known as the Krasanii raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves that are typically trifoliate. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits composed of tiny drupes, which are usually red when ripe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
