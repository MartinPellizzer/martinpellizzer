Ruellia nudiflora var. insularis

Ruellia nudiflora var. insularis, commonly known as the island ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
