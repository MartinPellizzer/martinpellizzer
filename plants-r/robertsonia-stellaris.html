Robertsonia stellaris

Robertsonia stellaris, commonly known as the star-flowered Robertsonia, is a unique flowering plant native to the wetlands of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features a short, sturdy stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with a central yellowish area that resembles a star.

Robertsonia stellaris is well adapted to moist environments and is often found growing in swampy or seasonally flooded areas.
