Ribesiodes sessiliflorum

Ribesiodes sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessile-flower ribes, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features small, clustered white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Ribesiodes sessiliflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing food for pollinators and wildlife.
