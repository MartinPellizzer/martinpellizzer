Rosa nuttalliana

Rosa nuttalliana, commonly known as the Nuttall's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnate, with 5 to 9 leaflets, and its stems are often covered in thorns.

Rosa nuttalliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
