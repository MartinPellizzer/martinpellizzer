Rosa borderi

Rosa borderi, commonly known as the Border rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the borders of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with thorny stems and fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets, and it produces hips that are typically red or orange in color.

Rosa borderi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
