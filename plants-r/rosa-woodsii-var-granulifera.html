Rosa woodsii var. granulifera

Rosa woodsii var. granulifera, commonly known as the granular woods rose, is a variant of the woods rose native to the western United States.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The flowers are pink to red, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a hairy rose hip that contains several seeds.

This plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
