Racosperma ligustrinum

Racosperma ligustrinum, commonly known as the yellow wattle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry woodlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
