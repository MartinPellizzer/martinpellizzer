Radermachera Xylocarpa

Radermachera xylocarpa is a unique and lesser-known plant species that belongs to the legume family. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. This plant is known for its striking foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions. Its name comes from the Latin words for "wood" and "carp," reflecting its woody structure and fruit characteristics. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the world of horticulture.
