Rubus obscurus subsp. oegocladus

Rubus obscurus subsp.

oegocladus, commonly known as the dark bramble or blackberry bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family. It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

This subspecies is notable for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
