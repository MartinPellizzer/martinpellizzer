Rhinopetalum Gibbosum

Gibbous rhinopetalum, scientifically known as Rhinopetalum gibbosum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique plant is characterized by its fleshy, bulb-like pseudobulbs and its dramatic, upward-facing flowers that resemble a crown. Its name derives from the rounded, convex shape of its rhinopetalum, which gives it a distinctive and almost mythical appearance. Despite its beauty, Gibbous rhinopetalum is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a challenging species to cultivate. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare orchid in its natural habitat.
