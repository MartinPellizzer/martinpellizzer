Rhododendron ripense

Rhododendron ripense, commonly known as the alpine rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It typically grows in high-altitude habitats, often in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron ripense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
