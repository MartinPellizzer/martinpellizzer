Rudgea pungens

Rudgea pungens, commonly known as the spiny rudgea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Rudgea pungens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
