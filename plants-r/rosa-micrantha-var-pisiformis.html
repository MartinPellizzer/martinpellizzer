Rosa micrantha var. pisiformis

Rosa micrantha var. pisiformis, commonly known as the flat-stemmed rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its slender, often arching stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets that are oval and serrated. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
