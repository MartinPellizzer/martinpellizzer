Ruschia fourcadei

Ruschia fourcadei, commonly known as the Fourcade's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in small, spiky leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and colorful blooms make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
