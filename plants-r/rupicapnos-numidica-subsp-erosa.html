Rupicapnos numidica subsp. erosa

Rupicapnos numidica subsp.

erosa, commonly known as the Algerian rock rose, is a shrub native to North Africa. It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a slight red tinge.

The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
