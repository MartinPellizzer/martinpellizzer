Rubus ravi

Rubus ravi, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a perennial shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its biennial growth habit, with flowering occurring on the previous year's growth. The plant produces clusters of red, hairy fruits that are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. Its leaves are alternate, palmately lobed, and covered in fine, stiff hairs.

Rubus ravi thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
