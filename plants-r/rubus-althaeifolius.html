Rubus althaeifolius

Rubus althaeifolius, commonly known as the althaeifolius blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or hedgerow environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and its edible berries.
