Rhynchosia diversifolia var. prostrata

Rhynchosia diversifolia var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate sicklepod, is a low-growing annual legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, prostrate stems that spread along the ground, often rooting at the nodes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by slender, spiral-shaped pods containing several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
