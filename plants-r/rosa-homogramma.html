Rosa homogramma

Rosa homogramma, commonly known as the Hawaiian rose, is a species native to the island of Hawaii.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa homogramma is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of Hawaii and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
