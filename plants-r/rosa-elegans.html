Rosa elegans

Rosa elegans, commonly known as the elegant rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with five to seven elliptical leaflets.

Rosa elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
