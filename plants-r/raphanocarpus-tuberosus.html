Raphanocarpus tuberosus

Raphanocarpus tuberosus, commonly known as the tuberous raphanocarpus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and features small, white or yellowish flowers. Its tuberous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its delicate foliage and flowering structure.
