Rosa dinotoides

Rosa dinotoides, commonly known as the Dinotoides rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink or white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Rosa dinotoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
