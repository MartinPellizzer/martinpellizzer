Rossolis

Rossolis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, colorful flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for indoor gardening and container planting.
