Rosa antitraaenii

Rosa antitraaenii, commonly known as the Antitraaenii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in parts of Brazil and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are typically pinnately compound, with serrated edges, and the stems are covered in soft, fuzzy hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
