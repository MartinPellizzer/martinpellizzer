Rosa reuteri f. subbiserrata

Rosa reuteri f. subbiserrata, commonly known as the serrated rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. The leaves are dark green and slightly serrated, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
