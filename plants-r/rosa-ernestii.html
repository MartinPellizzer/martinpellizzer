Rosa ernestii

Rosa ernestii, commonly known as the Ernest rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe. This shrub typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a subtle fragrance, and bloom in late summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
