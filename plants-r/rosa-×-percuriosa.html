Rosa × percuriosa

Rosa × percuriosa, commonly known as the Chinese pink rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Rosa × percuriosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.
