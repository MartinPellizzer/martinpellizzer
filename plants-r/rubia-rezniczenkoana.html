Rubia rezniczenkoana

Rubia rezniczenkoana, commonly known as the Rezniczenko's madder, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Central Asia and is characterized by its slender stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
