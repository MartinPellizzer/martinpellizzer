Rhamnus Nipponica

Rhamnus nipponica is a deciduous shrub native to Japan known for its ornamental value and adaptability. It features small, glossy leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in autumn. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers in spring, followed by small, tart berries that attract wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its resilience and aesthetic appeal make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
