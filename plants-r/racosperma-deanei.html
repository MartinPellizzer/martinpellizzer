Racosperma deanei

Racosperma deanei, commonly known as the silky wattle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils. It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and can fix nitrogen in the soil.

Racosperma deanei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and restoration projects.
