Rapuntium minutum

Rapuntium minutum, commonly known as the small cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in wetlands. It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Rapuntium minutum is an annual plant that blooms from early spring to early summer.
