Rabdosia Nervosa

Rabdosia nervosa is a flowering plant native to southern Asia, known for its attractive blue to purple flowers and aromatic foliage. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, commonly referred to as the mint family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent. Rabdosia nervosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure. Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
