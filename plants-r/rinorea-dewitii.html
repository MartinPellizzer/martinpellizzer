Rinorea dewitii

Rinorea dewitii, commonly known as the dwarf silverleaf.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters.

Rinorea dewitii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
