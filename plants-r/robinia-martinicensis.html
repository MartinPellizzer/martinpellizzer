Robinia martinicensis

Robinia martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique robinia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, with a broad, spreading crown. The leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming in the spring.

The tree produces legume pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
