Rhexia delicatula

Rhexia delicatula, commonly known as the pinkroot, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features small, showy pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a soft, velvety texture.
