Rosa spaldingii var. parkeri

Rosa spaldingii var. parkeri, commonly known as the Spalding's rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptic leaflets, often with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
