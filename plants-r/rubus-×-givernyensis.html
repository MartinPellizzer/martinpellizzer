Rubus × givernyensis

Rubus × givernyensis, commonly known as the Giverny raspberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible berries.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in different populations.
