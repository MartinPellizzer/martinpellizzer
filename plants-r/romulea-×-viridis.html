Romulea × viridis

Romulea × viridis, commonly known as the green romulea, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, grass-like foliage and vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a splash of color to gardens.

Romulea × viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
