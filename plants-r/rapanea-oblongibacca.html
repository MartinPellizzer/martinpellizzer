Rapanea oblongibacca

Rapanea oblongibacca, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and ornamental purposes.
