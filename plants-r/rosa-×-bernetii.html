Rosa × bernetii

Rosa × bernetii, commonly known as the Bernetii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces red hips that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Rosa × bernetii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
