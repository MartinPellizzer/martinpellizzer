Rungia guineensis

Rungia guineensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically dark green and velvety in texture.

Rungia guineensis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
