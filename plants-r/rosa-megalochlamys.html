Rosa megalochlamys

Rosa megalochlamys, commonly known as the giant rose or large-flowered rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa megalochlamys is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
