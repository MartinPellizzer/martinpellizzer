Rhododendron sugaui

Rhododendron sugaui, commonly known as the Sugaui rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the understory of temperate forests. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinct serrated edge, while its flowers are highly fragrant.

Rhododendron sugaui is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
