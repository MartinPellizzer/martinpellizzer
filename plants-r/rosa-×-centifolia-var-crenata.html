Rosa × centifolia var. crenata

Rosa × centifolia var. crenata, commonly known as the crenated cabbage rose, is a hybrid rose species prized for its ornamental value.

It is characterized by its deep pink to crimson flowers, which are often double or semi-double in form. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is also valued for its fragrance and is often used in perfumery and floral arrangements.
