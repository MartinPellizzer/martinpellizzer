Rosa kwangtungensis f. roseoliflora

Rosa kwangtungensis f. roseoliflora, commonly known as the roseolate Kwangtung rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, semi-double pink to red flowers. The foliage is typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant produces small, red hips in the autumn.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual beauty.
