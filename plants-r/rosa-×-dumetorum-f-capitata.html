Rosa × dumetorum f. capitata

Rosa × dumetorum f. capitata, commonly known as the alpine rose or shrub rose, is a compact, hardy plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa, characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces pink to red flowers in early summer, which are borne on short pedicels. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, and it is well adapted to cold climates.

Rosa × dumetorum f. capitata is often used in rock gardens and alpine settings due to its ornamental value and resilience.
