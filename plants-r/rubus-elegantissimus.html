Rubus elegantissimus

Rubus elegantissimus, commonly known as the elegant raspberry, is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, particularly China and Korea. The plant is characterized by its arching canes and clusters of small, red, edible fruits that ripen in late summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a serrated margin and a slightly hairy texture.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental and small-scale cultivation.
