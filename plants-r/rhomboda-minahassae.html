Rhomboda minahassae

Rhomboda minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa rhombod.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and vibrant green color. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in terminal inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow.

Rhomboda minahassae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
