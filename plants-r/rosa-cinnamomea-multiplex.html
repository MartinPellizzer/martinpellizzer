Rosa cinnamomea multiplex

Rosa cinnamomea multiplex, commonly known as the cinnamon rose, is a hybrid rose species that exhibits a variety of botanical characteristics.

It is known for its fragrant, deep pink to crimson flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Rosa cinnamomea multiplex is valued for its hardiness and adaptability, making it a popular choice among rose enthusiasts.
