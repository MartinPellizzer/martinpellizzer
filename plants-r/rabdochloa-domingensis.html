Rabdochloa domingensis

Rabdochloa domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican grass, is a herbaceous plant native to the Caribbean region, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native habitat.
