Rosa × reversa

Rosa × reversa, commonly known as the double-flowered pink tea rose, is a hybrid rose species known for its lush, velvety petals.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and high ornamental value. The plant typically produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink and white, often with a layered, full appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Rosa × reversa is also valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to various climate conditions.
