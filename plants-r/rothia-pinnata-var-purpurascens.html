Rothia pinnata var. purpurascens

Rothia pinnata var. purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-leafed Rothia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which becomes more intense in cooler temperatures. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and relatively low maintenance requirements.
