Rosa × dumetorum pilosa

Rosa × dumetorum pilosa, commonly known as the prickly rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its adaptability to harsh environments.

The leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.
