Rhododendron xinganense

Rhododendron xinganense, commonly known as Xingan rhododendron, is a species native to China, particularly found in the Xingan Mountains.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous, acidic soils with high humidity. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the local ecosystem by supporting various pollinators.
