Rosa lereschii

Rosa lereschii, commonly known as the Leresch rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a fragrant aroma.

Rosa lereschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
