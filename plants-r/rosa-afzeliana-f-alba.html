Rosa afzeliana f. alba

Rosa afzeliana f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the Afzelius rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa afzeliana.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with the typical pink or red hues of the parent species. The plant is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly in countries like South Africa and Namibia. It typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves.

Its botanical features make it a valuable subject for study in horticulture and plant taxonomy.
