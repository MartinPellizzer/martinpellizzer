Ruschia stylosa

Ruschia stylosa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine white powder. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink or red, typically in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
