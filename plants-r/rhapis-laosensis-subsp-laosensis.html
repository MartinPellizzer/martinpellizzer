Rhapis laosensis subsp. laosensis

Rhapis laosensis subsp.

laosensis, commonly known as the Laotian bamboo palm, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Laos and Vietnam. It is a compact, clumping palm with slender, dark green leaves that are fan-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by dark blue-black berries. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for container cultivation in cooler climates.
