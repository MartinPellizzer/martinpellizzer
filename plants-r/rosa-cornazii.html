Rosa cornazii

Rosa cornazii, commonly known as the Cornazii rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces red hips in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Rosa cornazii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
