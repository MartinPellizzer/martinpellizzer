Rendlia obtusifolia

Rendlia obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaf rendlia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a rounded base. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
