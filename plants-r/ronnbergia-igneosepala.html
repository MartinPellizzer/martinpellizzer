Ronnbergia igneosepala

Ronnbergia igneosepala, commonly known as the flame-veined orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red sepals and petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small but highly ornamental, often displaying a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues.

Ronnbergia igneosepala thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
