Rhus khasiana

Rhus khasiana, commonly known as Khasi sumac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like the cashew and poison ivy. Rhus khasiana is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental features and traditional uses.
