Rhamnella vitiensis

Rhamnella vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Vanuatu.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Rhamnella vitiensis plays a role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various insect species.
