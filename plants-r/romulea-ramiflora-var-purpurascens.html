Romulea ramiflora var. purpurascens

Romulea ramiflora var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features grass-like leaves and produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant is characterized by its striking floral display, which typically appears in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
