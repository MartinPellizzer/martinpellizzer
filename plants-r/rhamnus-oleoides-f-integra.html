Rhamnus oleoides f. integra

Rhamnus oleoides f. integra, commonly known as the smooth-fruited buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue-black berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
