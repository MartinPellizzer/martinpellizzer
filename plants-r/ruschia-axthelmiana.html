Ruschia axthelmiana

Ruschia axthelmiana, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
