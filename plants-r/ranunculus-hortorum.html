Ranunculus hortorum

Ranunculus hortorum, commonly known as the garden buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with a dark center. The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are typically hairy and serrated. It thrives in cool, moist conditions and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and gardens.

Ranunculus hortorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in horticulture.
