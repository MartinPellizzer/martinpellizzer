Raphanus niger var. rotundus

Raphanus niger var. rotundus, commonly known as the round black radish, is a cultivated variety of the radish plant.

It is characterized by its round, dark purple to black skin and a firm, crisp flesh with a mild, slightly spicy flavor. This variety is often grown for its ornamental value as well as its edible roots. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

It is a cool-season crop, often harvested in the early part of the growing season.
