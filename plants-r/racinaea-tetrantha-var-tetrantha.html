Racinaea tetrantha var. tetrantha

Racinaea tetrantha var. tetrantha, commonly known as the four-branch racinaea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence known as a spadix, enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are pollinated by insects.
