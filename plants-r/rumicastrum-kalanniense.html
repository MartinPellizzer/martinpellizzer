Rumicastrum kalanniense

Rumicastrum kalanniense, commonly known as the Kalanni shrub, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and clustered flowers. The plant exhibits succulent leaves that help it retain water in its harsh environment. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
