Rehmannia solanifolia

Rehmannia solanifolia, commonly known as the purple-rooted nightshade, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems, lance-shaped leaves, and small, violet flowers. Its roots are the primary part used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
