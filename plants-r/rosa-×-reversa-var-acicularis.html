Rosa × reversa var. acicularis

Rosa × reversa var. acicularis, commonly known as the slender pink climbing rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and delicate, fragrant flowers.

It produces semi-double to double petals that range in color from pale pink to deep red, depending on the cultivar. The plant features thorny stems and glossy, dark green foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to cover trellises or fences.
