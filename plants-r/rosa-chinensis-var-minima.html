Rosa chinensis var. minima

Rosa chinensis var. minima, commonly known as the miniature pink China rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has simple, trifoliate leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double blooms throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in partial to full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
