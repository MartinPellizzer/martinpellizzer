Rhamnus oleoides subsp. oleoides

Rhamnus oleoides subsp.

oleoides, commonly known as the smooth buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments.

The plant features small, oval leaves that are glossy green on top and lighter in color underneath. Its flowers are small and yellowish, appearing in clusters, followed by dark blue-black berries that are edible for some bird species.

The bark is smooth and grayish, becoming fissured with age, and the plant is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions.
