Rhynchosia memnonia var. discolor

Rhynchosia memnonia var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated flame bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a legume pod containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
