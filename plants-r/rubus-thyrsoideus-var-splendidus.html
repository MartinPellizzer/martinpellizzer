Rubus thyrsoideus var. splendidus

Rubus thyrsoideus var. splendidus, commonly known as the showy blackberry, is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant bears aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is native to parts of North America.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
