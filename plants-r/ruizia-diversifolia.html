Ruizia diversifolia

Ruizia diversifolia, commonly known as the African flame or flame tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple oval-shaped leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. Ruizia diversifolia thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and floral displays.
