Rosa × suberecta

Rosa × suberecta, commonly known as the upright rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Rosa × suberecta thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
