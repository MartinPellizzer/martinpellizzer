Riedelia graminea var. diversifolia

Riedelia graminea var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated riedelia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
