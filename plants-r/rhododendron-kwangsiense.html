Rhododendron kwangsiense

Rhododendron kwangsiense, commonly known as the Guangxi rhododendron, is a species native to southern China, particularly in the Guangxi region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous areas with acidic soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron kwangsiense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
