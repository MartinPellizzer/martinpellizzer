Ranunculus glabritorus

Ranunculus glabritorus, commonly known as the California buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and foliage.

Ranunculus glabritorus is often found in California and parts of Oregon, where it thrives in cool, temperate climates.
