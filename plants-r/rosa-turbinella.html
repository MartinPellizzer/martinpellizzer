Rosa turbinella

Rosa turbinella, commonly known as the turbinella rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.

Rosa turbinella is also valued for its historical use in perfumery and traditional medicine.
