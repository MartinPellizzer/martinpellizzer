Rhododendron oligocarpum

Rhododendron oligocarpum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are pink to red, often appearing in clusters, and bloom in spring.

Rhododendron oligocarpum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
