Rosa spinosissima var. liostyla

Rosa spinosissima var. liostyla, commonly known as the Chinese prickly rose, is a variant of the Rosa spinosissima species native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms.
