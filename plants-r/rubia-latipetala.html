Rubia latipetala

Rubia latipetala, commonly known as broad-petaled gentian, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia latipetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
