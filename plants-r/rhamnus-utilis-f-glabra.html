Rhamnus utilis f. glabra

Rhamnus utilis f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-fruited buckthorn, is a shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, oval berries that are typically dark purple when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or hedgerows.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
