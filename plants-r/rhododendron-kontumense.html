Rhododendron kontumense

Rhododendron kontumense, commonly known as the Kontum rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at elevations ranging from 1,000 to 2,500 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron kontumense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
