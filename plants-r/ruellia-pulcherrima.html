Ruellia pulcherrima

Ruellia pulcherrima, commonly known as the red-flowered ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ruellia pulcherrima is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
