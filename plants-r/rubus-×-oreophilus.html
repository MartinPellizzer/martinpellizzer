Rubus × oreophilus

Rubus × oreophilus, commonly known as the alpine raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces aggregate fruits, typically red or yellow, which are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
