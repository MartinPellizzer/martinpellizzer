Rosa columnifera

Rosa columnifera, commonly known as the upright rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa columnifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in cooler climates.
