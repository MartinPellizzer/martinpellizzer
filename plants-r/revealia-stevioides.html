Revealia stevioides

Revealia stevioides, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Revealia stevioides is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
