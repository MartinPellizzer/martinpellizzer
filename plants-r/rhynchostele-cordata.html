Rhynchostele cordata

Rhynchostele cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Rhynchostele cordata is valued for its starchy roots, which are used in traditional cuisine and medicine.
