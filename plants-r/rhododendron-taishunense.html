Rhododendron taishunense

Rhododendron taishunense, commonly known as the Taishun rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Fujian province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and are borne in clusters.

Rhododendron taishunense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
