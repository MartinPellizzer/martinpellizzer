Rubimons paniculatus

Rubimons paniculatus, commonly known as the red panic grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense clusters of small, reddish-brown florets. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant color and feathery appearance, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Rubimons paniculatus is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
