Rotang

Rotang, commonly known as *Daemonorops irrasus*, is a type of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark, fibrous fruits. Its fibrous material is highly valued for its strength and durability.

Rotang is often cultivated for its utility in traditional crafts and construction.
