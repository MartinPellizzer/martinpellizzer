Ritterocactus mueller-melchersii

Ritterocactus mueller-melchersii, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
