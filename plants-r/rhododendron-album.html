Rhododendron album

Rhododendron album, commonly known as the white rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas and parts of southern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is evergreen, with broad, leathery leaves that provide dense foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist climates.

Rhododendron album is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and woodland settings.
