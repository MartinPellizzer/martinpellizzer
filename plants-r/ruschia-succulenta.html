Ruschia succulenta

Ruschia succulenta, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright red, torch-like flower spikes that bloom in summer. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
