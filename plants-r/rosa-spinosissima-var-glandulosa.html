Rosa spinosissima var. glandulosa

Rosa spinosissima var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular spinous rose, is a variant of the spinous rose species.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and glandular structures on its leaves and stems, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
