Rubus macrophyllus subsp. piletostachys

Rubus macrophyllus subsp.

piletostachys, commonly known as the large-leaf blackberry, is a shrub native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and thorny stems, typical of the Rubus genus.

The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, dark red to black drupes. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
