Rhynchodia capusii

Rhynchodia capusii, commonly known as the Cape rose or African rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rhynchodia capusii is known for its adaptability to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
