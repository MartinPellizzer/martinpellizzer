Rhododendron × pulchrum

Rhododendron × pulchrum, commonly known as the hybrid rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often appearing in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron × pulchrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
