Richardia stellaris

Richardia stellaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Richardia stellaris is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
