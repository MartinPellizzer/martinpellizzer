Racosperma pentadenium

Racosperma pentadenium, commonly known as the broad-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically broad and arranged in pairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Racosperma pentadenium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping in regions with suitable climates.
