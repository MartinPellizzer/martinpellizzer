Ranunculus × felixii

Ranunculus × felixii, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, usually double-layered flowers that come in a range of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with palmately divided leaves and a short, upright stem. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
