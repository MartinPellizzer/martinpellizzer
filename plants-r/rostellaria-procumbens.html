Rostellaria procumbens

Rostellaria procumbens, commonly known as the creeping rostellaria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features slender, prostrate stems with opposite, lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in inflorescences, often with a faint fragrance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
