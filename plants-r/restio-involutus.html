Restio involutus

Restio involutus, commonly known as the spiral restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, spirally twisted leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.
