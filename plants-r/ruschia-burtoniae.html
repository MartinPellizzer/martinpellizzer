Ruschia burtoniae

Ruschia burtoniae, commonly known as the Burton's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental appeal and hardiness, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
