Rosa × inelegans

Rosa × inelegans, commonly known as the 'Belle de Jour' rose, is a hybrid shrub rose characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant, fragrant blooms.

It produces semi-double to full flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a delicate, perfumed scent. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy flowers. It is typically grown in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively disease-resistant.

Rosa × inelegans thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both landscape and container gardening.
