Rosa Canina Var. Leucantha

Rosa canina var. leucantha is a rare and elegant variant of the dog rose known for its pale pink to white blooms. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and open fields. Its delicate appearance contrasts with the more common red and yellow varieties of Rosa canina. The flowers emit a subtle fragrance and are popular in gardens for their beauty and historical significance. This unique cultivar is cherished by botanists and gardeners alike for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
