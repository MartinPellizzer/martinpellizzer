Racosperma demissum

Racosperma demissum, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in dense clusters.

Racosperma demissum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
