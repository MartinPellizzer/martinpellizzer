Rhododendron ziyuanense

Rhododendron ziyuanense, commonly known as the Ziyuan rhododendron, is a species native to China, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
