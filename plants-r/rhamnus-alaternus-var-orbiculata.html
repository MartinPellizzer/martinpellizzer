Rhamnus alaternus var. orbiculata

Rhamnus alaternus var. orbiculata, commonly known as the rounded buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible but often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in herbal remedies.
