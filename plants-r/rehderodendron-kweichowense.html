Rehderodendron kweichowense

Rehderodendron kweichowense, commonly known as the Kweichow rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and cool temperatures. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
