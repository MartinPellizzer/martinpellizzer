Ranunculus longicaulis var. pseudohirculus

Ranunculus longicaulis var. pseudohirculus, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright stems that are usually hairy, with trifoliate leaves that have serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow with numerous petals and a central cluster of stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
