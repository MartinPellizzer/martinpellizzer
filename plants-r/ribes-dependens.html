Ribes dependens

Ribes dependens, commonly known as the trailing gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae, which includes other species of gooseberries and currants. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, with slender stems that often root at the nodes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and have serrated margins.
