Rosa arvensis occurrens

Rosa arvensis occurrens, commonly known as the field rose, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are pink to white, often appearing in clusters, and are followed by red-orange hips.

Rosa arvensis occurrens is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
