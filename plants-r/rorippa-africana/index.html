Rorippa Africana

Rorippa africana is a small aquatic plant native to sub-Saharan Africa. It thrives in shallow waters and wetlands, often found along the edges of ponds and streams. This species is known for its delicate, floating leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its ability to adapt to various water conditions makes it a resilient member of the Brassicaceae family. Despite its modest appearance, Rorippa africana plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems.
