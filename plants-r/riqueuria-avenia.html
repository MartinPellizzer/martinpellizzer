Riqueuria avenia

Riqueuria avenia, commonly known as the silverleaf riqueuria, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, with a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

Riqueuria avenia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
