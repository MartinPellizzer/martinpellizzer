Rhododendron javanicum subsp. schadenbergii

Rhododendron javanicum subsp.

schadenbergii, commonly known as the Javanese rhododendron, is a species native to Indonesia. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and well-drained acidic soils. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
