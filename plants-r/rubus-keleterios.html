Rubus keleterios

Rubus keleterios, commonly known as the silverleaf raspberry, is a species of brambles belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, silver-tomentose leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color, similar to other raspberries. It is native to high-altitude regions of South America and thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
