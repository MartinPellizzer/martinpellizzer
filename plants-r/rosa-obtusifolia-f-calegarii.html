Rosa obtusifolia f. calegarii

Rosa obtusifolia f. calegarii, commonly known as the Calegarii rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in late spring. The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This form is a natural variant of Rosa obtusifolia, known for its hardiness and adaptability to coastal conditions.
