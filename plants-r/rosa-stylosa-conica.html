Rosa stylosa conica

Rosa stylosa conica, commonly known as the conical-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Nepal. The plant is characterized by its upright, woody stems that often form a conical shape, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five to seven petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa stylosa conica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
