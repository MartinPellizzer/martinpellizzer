Rebutia ritteri

Rebutia ritteri, commonly known as the Ritter's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Rebutia ritteri is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in dry climates.
