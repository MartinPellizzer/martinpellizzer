Rosa macrophylla var. crasseaculeata

Rosa macrophylla var. crasseaculeata, commonly known as the Big Leaf Hybrid Rose, is a cultivar prized for its large, fragrant blooms and vigorous growth.

It is a hybrid of Rosa macrophylla and Rosa rugosa, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and broad, leathery leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its flowers range in color from white to deep pink, often with a strong, sweet fragrance.
