Rosa × wietziana

Rosa × wietziana, commonly known as the Austrian rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically produces thorned stems and has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. It is native to Central Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Rosa × wietziana prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for shrub borders and hedging.
