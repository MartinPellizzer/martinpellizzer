Rotala tenella f. fluviatilis

Rotala tenella f. fluviatilis, commonly known as the water rotala, is a compact aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, bright green leaves that are typically oval or rounded in shape. The plant produces tiny white flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in shallow water and is often used in aquariums and ponds for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an effective plant for oxygenating water and providing habitat for aquatic life.
