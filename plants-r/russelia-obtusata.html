Russelia obtusata

Russelia obtusata, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Russelia obtusata is also known for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
