Relbunium hirtum f. floribundum

Relbunium hirtum f. floribundum, commonly known as the hairy relbunium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to specific regions in Europe and is often found in grassy habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species within its native range.
