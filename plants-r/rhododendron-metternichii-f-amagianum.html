Rhododendron metternichii f. amagianum

Rhododendron metternichii f. amagianum, commonly known as the Amagian rhododendron, is a rare and striking plant native to the island of Amagami in Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions with acidic soil. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This unique cultivar is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance.
