Rinzia medifila

Rinzia medifila, commonly known as the slender rinzia, is a small flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas. It is often found in regions with a temperate to subtropical climate.

Rinzia medifila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
