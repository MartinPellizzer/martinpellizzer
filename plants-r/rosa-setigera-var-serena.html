Rosa setigera var. serena

Rosa setigera var. serena, commonly known as the serena climbing rose, is a variant of the willowleaf rose.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, willow-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping for its graceful appearance.
