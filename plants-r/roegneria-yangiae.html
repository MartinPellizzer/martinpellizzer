Roegneria yangiae

Roegneria yangiae, commonly known as the Yang's timothygrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and open woodlands.

Roegneria yangiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
