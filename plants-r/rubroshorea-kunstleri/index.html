Rubroshorea Kunstleri

Rubroshorea kunstleri is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant red foliage and unique growth patterns. Native to a limited region in Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high moisture levels. Its distinctive appearance has made it a popular subject among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, the species faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are ongoing to ensure its survival for future generations.
