Rosa cosruleifolia

Rosa cosruleifolia, commonly known as the Cosruleifolia rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges, typical of many rose species. It is native to certain regions of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in the wild.
