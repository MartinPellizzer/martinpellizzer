Rumex bonariensis

Rumex bonariensis, commonly known as the Brazilian dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has broad, serrated leaves that are typically green on top and pale underneath. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences, producing yellowish-green blooms.

Rumex bonariensis is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
