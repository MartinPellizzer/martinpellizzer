Rutidea olenotricha

Rutidea olenotricha, commonly known as the woolly legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its root system has nitrogen-fixing capabilities, making it valuable for soil enrichment.

Rutidea olenotricha is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
