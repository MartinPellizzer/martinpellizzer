Rhaphidophora talamauana

Rhaphidophora talamauana, commonly known as the black sword plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark, glossy, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
