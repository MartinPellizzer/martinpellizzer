Rhipsalis alternata

Rhipsalis alternata, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its thin, branching stems that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the winter months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its leaves are reduced to small, spiny structures, typical of many cactus species.

Rhipsalis alternata is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
