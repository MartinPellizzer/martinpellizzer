Rosa nitidula

Rosa nitidula, commonly known as the smooth rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, thornless stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, pinnate, and covered with fine, silky hairs.

Rosa nitidula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in horticulture for its attractive blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
