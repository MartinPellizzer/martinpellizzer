Ranunculus × buchlyallii

Ranunculus × buchlyallii, commonly known as the Buchlyallii buttercup, is a hybrid species within the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a serrated margin, and its stems are usually hairy. Native to the western United States, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its natural preference for wild environments.
