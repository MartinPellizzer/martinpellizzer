Rhaponticoides iconiensis

Rhaponticoides iconiensis, commonly known as the Iconien mountain gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the eastern Himalayas.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its showy, blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.
