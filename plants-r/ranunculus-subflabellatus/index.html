Ranunculus Subflabellatus

Ranunculus subflabellatus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its striking, bright yellow petals that resemble a miniature sun. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Due to its limited habitat and susceptibility to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and unique plant.
