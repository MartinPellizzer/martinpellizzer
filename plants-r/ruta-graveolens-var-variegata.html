Ruta graveolens var. variegata

Ruta graveolens var. variegata, commonly known as variegated rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation, which adds ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by distinctive, winged fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its strong scent and toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
