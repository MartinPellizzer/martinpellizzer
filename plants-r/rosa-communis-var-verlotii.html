Rosa communis var. verlotii

Rosa communis var. verlotii, commonly known as the Verlotii rose, is a variety of the common rose that is prized for its ornamental value.

It features large, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color, with a velvety texture. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
