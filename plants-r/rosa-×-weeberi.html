Rosa × weeberi

Rosa × weeberi, commonly known as the Weeberi rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa rugosa and Rosa chinensis.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is known for its hardy nature, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates, making it suitable for both garden and landscape use. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × weeberi is often used in hedging and as a decorative plant due to its durability and aesthetic appeal.
