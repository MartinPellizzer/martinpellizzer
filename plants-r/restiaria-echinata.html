Restiaria echinata

Restiaria echinata, commonly known as the spiny restiaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered with small, sharp spines. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Restiaria echinata is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
