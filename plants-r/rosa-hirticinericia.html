Rosa hirticinericia

Rosa hirticinericia, commonly known as the woolly-bristly rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Rosa hirticinericia is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
