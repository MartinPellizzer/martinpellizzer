Rubus rotundior

Rubus rotundior, commonly known as the round-leaved blackberry, is a species of shrub in the蔷薇科 (Rosaceae) family.

It is native to parts of Europe and western Asia, and it is characterized by its compound leaves and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils.

Rubus rotundior is often found in woodland edges and open habitats, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
