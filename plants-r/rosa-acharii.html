Rosa acharii

Rosa acharii, commonly known as the red sandalwood rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa acharii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
