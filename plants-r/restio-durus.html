Restio durus

Restio durus, commonly known as the hard restio, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to southern Africa.

It is a grass-like plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and tall, cylindrical inflorescences. Restio durus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
