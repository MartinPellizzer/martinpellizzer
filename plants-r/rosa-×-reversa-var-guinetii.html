Rosa × reversa var. guinetii

Rosa × reversa var. guinetii, commonly known as the Guinet's double-flowered rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its large, fully double blooms.

It features a vigorous growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, lobed foliage. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a strong, sweet fragrance. It is well-suited for garden cultivation and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

This variety requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
