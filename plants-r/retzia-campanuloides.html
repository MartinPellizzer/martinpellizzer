Retzia campanuloides

Retzia campanuloides, commonly known as the bell-shaped retzia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many bell-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Retzia campanuloides typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
