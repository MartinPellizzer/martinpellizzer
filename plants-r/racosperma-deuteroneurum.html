Racosperma deuteroneurum

Racosperma deuteroneurum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, linear leaflets, and it produces long, cylindrical pods that contain seeds.

It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
