Rosa alpina var. plena

Rosa alpina var. plena, commonly known as the Alpine double rose, is a hybrid variety of the alpine rose.

It is characterized by its dense, double blooms that are typically pink or white in color. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in cool, high-altitude environments. It has fragrant flowers and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
