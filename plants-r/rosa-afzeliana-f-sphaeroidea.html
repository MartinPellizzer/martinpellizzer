Rosa afzeliana f. sphaeroidea

Rosa afzeliana f. sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globe rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to China.

It features dense, rounded growth with small, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its rounded form, which gives it its common name, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
