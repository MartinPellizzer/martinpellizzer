Rosa × momiyamae

Rosa × momiyamae, commonly known as the Momiyama rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin.

Rosa × momiyamae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively disease-resistant, making it a popular choice for gardens.
