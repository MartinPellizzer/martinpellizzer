Rhytionanthos cornutum

Rhytionanthos cornutum, commonly known as the horned flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant features a thick, rugged trunk with a distinctive flaky bark, and its leaves are pinnately arranged with long, narrow leaflets. Rhytionanthos cornutum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
