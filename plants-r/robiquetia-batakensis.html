Robiquetia batakensis

Robiquetia batakensis, commonly known as the Batak star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Robiquetia batakensis is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
