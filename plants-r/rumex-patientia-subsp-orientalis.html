Rumex patientia subsp. orientalis

Rumex patientia subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as wet meadows and riverbanks.

The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and prominent veins. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal spikes, producing a modest amount of seeds.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
