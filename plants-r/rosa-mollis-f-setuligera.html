Rosa mollis f. setuligera

Rosa mollis f. setuligera, commonly known as the soft rose or woolly rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in woodland and rocky habitats. It is a shrub that can grow up to 1-2 meters in height, with thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
