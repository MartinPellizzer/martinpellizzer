Rhaphidophora celebica

Rhaphidophora celebica, commonly known as the Celebes arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its starchy rhizomes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse settings.
