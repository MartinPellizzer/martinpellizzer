Rhaphiolepis indica var. liukiuensis

Rhaphiolepis indica var. liukiuensis, commonly known as the Liukiu rosebay, is a deciduous shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy and visually appealing plant.
