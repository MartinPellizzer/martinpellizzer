Riedelia graminea var. elata

Riedelia graminea var. elata, commonly known as the tall riedelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
