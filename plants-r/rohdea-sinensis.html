Rohdea sinensis

Rohdea sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a striking ornamental plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or purple tint along the veins. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both gardeners and interior decorators.
