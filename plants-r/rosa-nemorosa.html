Rosa nemorosa

Rosa nemorosa, commonly known as the wood rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to Europe and western Asia and typically grows in woodland areas. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its stems are thorny, and it has dark green, serrated leaves.

Rosa nemorosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
