Rostellularia Glandulosa

Glandular rostellularia, scientifically known as Rostellularia glandulosa, is a rare and intriguing species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This unique plant is characterized by its striking, glandular structures that cover its leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. Despite its rarity, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting local biodiversity. Its ornamental value has also made it a subject of interest among botanists and horticulturists. However, due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant conservation challenges.
