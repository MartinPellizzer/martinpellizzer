Roldana sinuata

Roldana sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom in clusters.

Roldana sinuata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
