Romulea macowanii var. alticola

Romulea macowanii var. alticola, commonly known as the highland romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of delicate, yellowish-white flowers.
