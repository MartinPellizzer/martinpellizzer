Ricinocarpodendron borneense

Ricinocarpodendron borneense, commonly known as the Bornean ricin tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its fruit is a capsule containing toxic seeds, which contain the potent toxin ricin.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often avoided in horticultural cultivation.
