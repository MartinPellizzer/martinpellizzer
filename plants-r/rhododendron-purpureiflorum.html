Rhododendron purpureiflorum

Rhododendron purpureiflorum, commonly known as the purple-flowered rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, including regions in India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and elliptical in shape. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually purple or pink, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron purpureiflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
