Rosa obscura

Rosa obscura, commonly known as the obscure rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its small, pale pink to white flowers and thorny stems. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa obscura is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
