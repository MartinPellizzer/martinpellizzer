Rabdosia umbrosa

Rabdosia umbrosa, commonly known as darkleaf rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are dark green and often have a velvety texture, while its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple.

Rabdosia umbrosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
