Rhododendron × gandavense var. plenum

Rhododendron × gandavense var. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered gandavense rhododendron, is a hybrid plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage is typically dark green and remains attractive throughout the growing season.
