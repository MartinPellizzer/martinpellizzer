Rosa pernetiana

Rosa pernetiana, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in cold, high-altitude environments. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged in an alternate pattern along the stems.

Rosa pernetiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
