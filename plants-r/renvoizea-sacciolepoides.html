Renvoizea sacciolepoides

Renvoizea sacciolepoides, commonly known as the giant sacciolepoid, is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
