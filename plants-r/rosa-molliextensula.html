Rosa molliextensula

Rosa molliextensula, commonly known as the silky rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. Native to southern China, this plant thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

The plant produces oval, red-orange fruits known as hips, which are rich in vitamin C. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
