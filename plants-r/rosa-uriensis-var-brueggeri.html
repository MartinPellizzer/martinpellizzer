Rosa uriensis var. brueggeri

Rosa uriensis var. brueggeri, commonly known as the Brueggeri roses, is a cultivar of the shrub rose species Rosa uriensis.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of semi-double to double pink flowers. The plant typically produces fragrant, pale pink petals that are often adorned with a slight white edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This variety is also valued for its disease resistance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
