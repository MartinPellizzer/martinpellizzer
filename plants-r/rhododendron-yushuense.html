Rhododendron yushuense

Rhododendron yushuense, commonly known as the Yushu rhododendron, is a species native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its botanical features include leathery leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well adapted to its alpine habitat.

This species is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance.
