Rungia Clauda

Rungia clauda is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 13,000 species of flowering plants. This plant is known for its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Rungia clauda is a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
