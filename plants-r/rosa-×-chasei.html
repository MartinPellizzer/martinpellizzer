Rosa × chasei

Rosa × chasei, commonly known as the Chase rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is a deciduous shrub, often featuring thorny stems and compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Rosa × chasei is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various climates.
