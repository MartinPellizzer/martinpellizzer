Ranunculus flammula var. filiformis

Ranunculus flammula var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender yellow buttercup, is a low-growing herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, leafy stem that often lies close to the ground. The plant's leaves are deeply divided into narrow, thread-like segments, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
