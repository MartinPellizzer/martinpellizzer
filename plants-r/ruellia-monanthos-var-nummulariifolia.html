Ruellia monanthos var. nummulariifolia

Ruellia monanthos var. nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
