Rinorea liberica

Rinorea liberica, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in humid, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Rinorea liberica prefers indirect sunlight and is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper conditions.
