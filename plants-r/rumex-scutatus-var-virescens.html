Rumex scutatus var. virescens

Rumex scutatus var. virescens, commonly known as the green-leaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to rhomboid in shape, with a distinctive wavy margin and a dark green, often glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, greenish to reddish flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found along stream banks and in wet meadows.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
