Rosa imperialis

Rosa imperialis, commonly known as the imperial rose, is a rare and ornamental species native to the high altitudes of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Rosa imperialis is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
