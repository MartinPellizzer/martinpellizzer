Reinera baypinensis

Reinera baypinensis, commonly known as the Baypinensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces a tall, branched inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
