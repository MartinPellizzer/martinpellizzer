Racosperma nematophyllum

Racosperma nematophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wattle, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters, which are typical of the genus Racosperma.
