Rotheca Myricoides Var. Discolor

Rotheca myricoides var. discolor is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. Native to specific regions of the southeastern United States, it thrives in moist, acidic soils and partial shade. This variety is particularly noted for its ability to change color throughout the seasons, creating a dynamic visual display. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among gardeners and landscapers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to maintain its health and vibrant appearance.
