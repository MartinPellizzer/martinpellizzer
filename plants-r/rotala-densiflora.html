Rotala densiflora

Rotala densiflora, commonly known as dense-flowered water hyssop, is a semi-aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features compact, bushy growth with small, dark green leaves that are often submerged or floating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, making it a popular choice for water gardens and pond landscapes.

Rotala densiflora is known for its rapid growth and ability to provide dense coverage in aquatic environments.
