Roegneria laxinodis

Roegneria laxinodis, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear spike-like inflorescences with small, two-lipped flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Roegneria laxinodis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
