Rubus villicauliformis

Rubus villicauliformis, commonly known as the silky raspberry.

It is a species of flowering plant in the bramble family, Rubiaceae, characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
