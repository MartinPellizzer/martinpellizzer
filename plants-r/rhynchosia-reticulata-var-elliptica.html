Rhynchosia reticulata var. elliptica

Rhynchosia reticulata var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features palmately compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive red or purple calyx. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
