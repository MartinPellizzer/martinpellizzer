Rinorea macrantha

Rinorea macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered rinorea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, smooth seeds.

Rinorea macrantha is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
