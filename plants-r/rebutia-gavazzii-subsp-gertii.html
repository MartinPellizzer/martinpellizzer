Rebutia gavazzii subsp. gertii

Rebutia gavazzii subsp.

gertii, commonly known as the Gertii Rebutia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features compact, rounded stems that are typically green with a subtle bluish tinge.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
