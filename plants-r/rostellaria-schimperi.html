Rostellaria schimperi

Rostellaria schimperi, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive, showy inflorescence that features brightly colored, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture efficiently.

Rostellaria schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
