Rhaphiolepis glabrescens

Rhaphiolepis glabrescens, commonly known as the glossy abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has smooth, glossy green leaves that turn red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
