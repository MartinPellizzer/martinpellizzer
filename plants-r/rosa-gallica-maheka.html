Rosa gallica maheka

Rosa gallica maheka, commonly known as the Maheka rose, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant produces rose hips, which are the fruit containing the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
