Restrepia vittata var. biflora

Restrepia vittata var. biflora, commonly known as the biflora orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its habitat.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and resilience in cultivation.
