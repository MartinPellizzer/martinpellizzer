Rhodiola Bhutanica

Bhutan rose, scientifically known as Rhodiola bhutanica, is a rare and unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of Bhutan. This hardy shrub thrives in cold climates and is often found growing in rocky and arid terrains. It is valued not only for its medicinal properties but also for its striking floral display. The plant has gained attention for its potential health benefits and is increasingly sought after by herbalists and researchers. Its resilience and beauty make it a symbol of the rugged natural landscapes of Bhutan.
