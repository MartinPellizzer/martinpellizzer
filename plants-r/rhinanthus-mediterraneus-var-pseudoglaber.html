Rhinanthus mediterraneus var. pseudoglaber

Rhinanthus mediterraneus var. pseudoglaber, commonly known as the Mediterranean yellow-rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on its host plants for water and nutrients while still performing photosynthesis. It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is of interest in ecological studies due to its parasitic nature and habitat specificity.
