Rosa × dumetorum f. urbica

Rosa × dumetorum f. urbica, commonly known as the urban rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thornless stems, making it well-suited for urban environments. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a strong, sweet scent. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, with a serrated margin, and it thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to city conditions.
