Rhodanthemum gayanum

Rhodanthemum gayanum, commonly known as the Sri Lankan rhodanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to red, with yellow centers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Rhodanthemum gayanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
