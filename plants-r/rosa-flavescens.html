Rosa flavescens

Rosa flavescens, commonly known as the yellow rose, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Rosa flavescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
