Rodriguezia decora

Rodriguezia decora, commonly known as the pink hibiscus, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and a woody stem, making it a distinctive addition to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Rodriguezia decora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
