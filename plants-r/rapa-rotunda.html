Rapa rotunda

Rapa rotunda, commonly known as the round cabbage, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower. The plant features rounded, fleshy leaves that form a rosette at the base, with small white or yellow flowers emerging from the center. Its leaves are edible and have a mild, slightly sweet flavor.

Rapa rotunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its culinary uses.
