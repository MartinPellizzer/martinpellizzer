Robiquetia minimiflora

Robiquetia minimiflora, commonly known as the dwarf-flowered robiquetia, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features delicate, hairy stems and produces tiny, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters, making it a compact and ornamental species. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Robiquetia minimiflora thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.
