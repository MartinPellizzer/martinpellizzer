Ricotia candiriana

Ricotia candiriana, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, showy flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The seeds are enclosed in a fibrous, hairy capsule that splits open when mature to release the seeds.
