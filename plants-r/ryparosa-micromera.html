Ryparosa micromera

Ryparosa micromera, commonly known as the small-fruited rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense cover that helps prevent soil erosion.

Ryparosa micromera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
