Rosa stocksii

Rosa stocksii, commonly known as the Stocks rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the eastern United States, particularly found in moist woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Rosa stocksii has slender, arching stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
