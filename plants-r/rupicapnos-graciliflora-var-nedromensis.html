Rupicapnos graciliflora var. nedromensis

Rupicapnos graciliflora var. nedromensis, commonly known as the Nedrom's slender-flowered rockrose, is a rare and striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its clusters of small, tubular, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features leathery, elliptical leaves with a rough texture, adapted to withstand harsh, rocky environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by botanists and gardeners interested in rare flora.
