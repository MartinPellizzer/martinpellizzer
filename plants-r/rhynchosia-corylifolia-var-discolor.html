Rhynchosia corylifolia var. discolor

Rhynchosia corylifolia var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated pods containing flat, oval seeds.
