Rosa grupnensis

Rosa grupnensis, commonly known as the Groupner's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers during the summer months. Rosa grupnensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and relatively rare species within the rose genus.
