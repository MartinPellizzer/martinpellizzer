Ruschia longipes

Ruschia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several feet in length, often adorned with small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. Its foliage is typically blue-green, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Ruschia longipes is prized in horticulture for its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
