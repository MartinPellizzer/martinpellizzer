Ranunculus hornemannii

Ranunculus hornemannii, commonly known as the Hornemann's buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, palmate leaves and showy, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in spring, and it is often found in rocky or clay soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
