Rhynchospora panicifolia

Rhynchospora panicifolia, commonly known as the panicled flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to wetland habitats in the southeastern United States, it thrives in moist, sandy soils. It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
