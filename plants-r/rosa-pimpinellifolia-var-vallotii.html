Rosa pimpinellifolia var. vallotii

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. vallotii, commonly known as the Alpine rose or mountain rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are densely packed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
