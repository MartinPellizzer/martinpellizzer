Ruschia laxipetala

Ruschia laxipetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, low-growing stems and linear, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the tips. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its adaptability and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
