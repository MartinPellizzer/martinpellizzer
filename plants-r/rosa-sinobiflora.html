Rosa sinobiflora

Rosa sinobiflora, commonly known as the Chinese twin-flower rose, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters of two. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, semi-double flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
