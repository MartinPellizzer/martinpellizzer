Rosa sepium var. ambigua

Rosa sepium var. ambigua, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The variety ambigua is distinguished by its slightly different flower structure and growth habits compared to the typical Rosa sepium.
