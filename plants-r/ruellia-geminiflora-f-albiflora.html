Ruellia geminiflora f. albiflora

Ruellia geminiflora f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a bluish-green hue.
