Rosa floribunda minor

Rosa floribunda minor, commonly known as the miniature floribunda rose, is a compact, flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, thornless stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Rosa floribunda minor is often used in garden borders and as a decorative plant due to its persistent flowering and compact growth habit.
