Rosa setosa

Rosa setosa, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features thorny stems and simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with five to seven petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa setosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also valued in traditional medicine.
