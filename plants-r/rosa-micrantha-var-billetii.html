Rosa micrantha var. billetii

Rosa micrantha var. billetii, commonly known as the Billet's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and thorny stems. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and is known for its ornamental value. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color.

It is a deciduous shrub that blooms in the spring and is valued for both its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
