Rabdosia stenodonta

Rabdosia stenodonta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Rabdosia stenodonta is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
