Rubus × perfoliosus

Rubus × perfoliosus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and northern Baja California. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves with a distinctive lobed shape. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring.

The plant produces edible berries that are similar in taste to blackberries but have a more tart and aromatic profile.
