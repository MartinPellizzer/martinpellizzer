Rosa traaenii

Rosa traaenii, commonly known as the Taaen's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with upright stems and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are typically pinnately divided, and the flowers are usually pink or red in color.

Rosa traaenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
