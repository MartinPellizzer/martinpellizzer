Rhodiola tibetica

Rhodiola tibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is well adapted to cold, arid environments and can thrive at elevations above 3,000 meters. It is often found growing in rocky soils and alpine meadows.
