Rosa moschata florepleno

Rosa moschata florepleno, commonly known as the musk rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has arching, thorned stems and dark green, lobed leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. Its flowers are often used in perfumery due to their rich, musky scent.

Rosa moschata florepleno is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
