Rothia tenuifolia

Rothia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf rothia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a fibrous root system.

Rothia tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
