Rhododendron scabrum f. linearisepalum

Rhododendron scabrum f. linearisepalum, commonly known as the linear-sepal rhododendron, is a botanical variant characterized by its elongated, narrow sepals.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to regions of Asia, particularly China. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves that are often leathery in texture. Its flowers are typically small, clustered, and display a range of colors from pink to red, depending on environmental conditions.

This form is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
