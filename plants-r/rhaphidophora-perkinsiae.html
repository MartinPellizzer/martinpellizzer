Rhaphidophora perkinsiae

Rhaphidophora perkinsiae, commonly known as the Perkins' arrowroot.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy, dark green, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers followed by edible, starchy tubers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Rhaphidophora perkinsiae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
