Rotala decumbens

Rotala decumbens, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are typically light green to reddish in color, often with a white or pale underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow, which develop into small, round fruits. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and lakes.

Rotala decumbens is popular in water gardens for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
