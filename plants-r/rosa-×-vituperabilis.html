Rosa × vituperabilis

Rosa × vituperabilis, commonly known as the "Crimson Glory," is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa rugosa and Rosa multiflora.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth, thorny stems, and large, showy blooms that range from deep red to orange-red in color. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates, making it a popular choice for landscaping. Its foliage is dark green and dense, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers.

Rosa × vituperabilis is also valued for its strong fragrance and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season.
