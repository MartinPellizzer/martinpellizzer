Ribes menziesii var. leptosmum

Ribes menziesii var. leptosmum, commonly known as the slender gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to western North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories and stream banks. The plant features small, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in early spring. Its fruit is a small, tart berry that ripens in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
