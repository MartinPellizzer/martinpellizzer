Ranunculus polyanthemos subsp. polyanthemos

Ranunculus polyanthemos subsp.

polyanthemos, commonly known as the showy buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States. It features delicate, bright yellow flowers with 5 to 15 petals, often arranged in a star-like pattern.

The plant has trifoliate leaves with deeply lobed divisions, and its stems are hairy and upright. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
