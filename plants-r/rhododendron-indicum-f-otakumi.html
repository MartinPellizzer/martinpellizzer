Rhododendron indicum f. otakumi

Rhododendron indicum f. otakumi, commonly known as the Otakumi rhododendron, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and slightly elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
