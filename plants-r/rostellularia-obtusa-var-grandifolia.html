Rostellularia obtusa var. grandifolia

Rostellularia obtusa var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered rostellularia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive rostellum, a fleshy appendage at the base of each petal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
