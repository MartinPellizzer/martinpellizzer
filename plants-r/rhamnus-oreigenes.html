Rhamnus oreigenes

Rhamnus oreigenes, commonly known as the Ethiopian buckthorn, is a shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid and semi-arid climates of its native habitat.

Due to its ecological significance and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species.
