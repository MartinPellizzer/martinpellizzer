Russelia pennelliana

Russelia pennelliana, commonly known as the red-flowered russelia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of tubular, red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Russelia pennelliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
