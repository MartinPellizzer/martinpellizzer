Rhododendron eudoxum var. eudoxum

Rhododendron eudoxum var. eudoxum, commonly known as the Eudoxus rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range in color from white to pink.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
