Rosa × damascena aurora

Rosa × damascena aurora, commonly known as the Aurora rose, is a hybrid rose species prized for its ornamental value.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features thorny stems with dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The botanical characteristics of Rosa × damascena aurora make it a popular choice for both landscape design and cut flower arrangements.
