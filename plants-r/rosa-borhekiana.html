Rosa borhekiana

Rosa borhekiana, commonly known as the Borhek's rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features simple, pinnately compound leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red. Rosa borhekiana is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
