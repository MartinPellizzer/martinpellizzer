Racosperma wanyu

Racosperma wanyu, commonly known as the Wanyu acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of northern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to drought conditions, with deep roots that allow it to survive in nutrient-poor soils.

It plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
