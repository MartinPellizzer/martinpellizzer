Rhododendron × sikkimense

Rhododendron × sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim rhododendron, is a hybrid species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
