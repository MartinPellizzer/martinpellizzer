Rosa mollis f. latiaculeata

Rosa mollis f. latiaculeata, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, often with a subtle scent. It is well-suited for container gardening and as a decorative shrub in gardens.

This cultivar thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
