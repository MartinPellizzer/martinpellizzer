Rosa attenuipes

Rosa attenuipes, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its slender, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine, silvery hairs.

Rosa attenuipes is typically found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
