Rhynchosia ambacensis subsp. chellensis

Rhynchosia ambacensis subsp.

chellensis, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa. It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant produces elliptical, flat pods containing several seeds. It is adapted to grow in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
