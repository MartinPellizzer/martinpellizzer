Rhynchanthera matthaei

Rhynchanthera matthaei, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rhynchanthera matthaei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
