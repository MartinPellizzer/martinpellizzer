Rhododendron nudipes var. kirishimense

Rhododendron nudipes var. kirishimense, commonly known as the Kirishima rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring acidic, well-drained soils. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.
