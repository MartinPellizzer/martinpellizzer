Rosa nanothamnus

Rosa nanothamnus, commonly known as the dwarf thorned rose, is a compact shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, thorny stems and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate blooms and manageable size.
