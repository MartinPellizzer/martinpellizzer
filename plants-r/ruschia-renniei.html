Ruschia renniei

Ruschia renniei, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
