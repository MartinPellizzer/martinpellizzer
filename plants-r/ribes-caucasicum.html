Ribes caucasicum

Ribes caucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the genus Ribes within the family Grossulariaceae. The plant produces small, yellow or orange berries that are edible and often used in jams and preserves. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

Ribes caucasicum is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
