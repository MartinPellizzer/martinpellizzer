Rotang niger

Rotang niger, commonly known as African shea tree, is a tropical tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is valued for its hard, durable wood. The tree can grow up to 25 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a rough, fissured bark. Its leaves are alternate, compound, and often used in traditional medicine.

The fruit produces a yellowish pulp that is edible and has a mild, nutty flavor.
