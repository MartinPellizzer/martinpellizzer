Rhynchanthera maximowiczii

Rhynchanthera maximowiczii, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant features a robust rhizome and produces numerous pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
