Rosa × areitiana

Rosa × areitiana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant is native to China and has been cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges.

Rosa × areitiana is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various climates.
