Rubus subrudis

Rubus subrudis, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble within the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its flowers are white or pale pink, and it is valued for both its ornamental and edible qualities.
