Rosa planispina

Rosa planispina, commonly known as the flat-stemmed rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its simple, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in pink or white. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically solitary and appear at the tips of the stems.

Rosa planispina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
