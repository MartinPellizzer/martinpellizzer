Rosa cinnamomea var. plenissima

Rosa cinnamomea var. plenissima, commonly known as the Chinese rose or cinnamon rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa cinnamomea species.

It is characterized by its abundant, double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It produces fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to early autumn.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
