Rosa sicula var. maroccana

Rosa sicula var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it highly drought-tolerant. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in challenging climates.
