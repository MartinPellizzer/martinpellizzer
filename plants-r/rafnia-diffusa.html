Rafnia diffusa

Rafnia diffusa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its roots are deep and extensive.

Rafnia diffusa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks for its vibrant floral display.
