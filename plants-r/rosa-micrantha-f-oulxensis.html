Rosa micrantha f. oulxensis

Rosa micrantha f. oulxensis, commonly known as the Oulxensis rose, is a botanical variant of the shrub rose species Rosa micrantha.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins. Native to specific regions in China, it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
