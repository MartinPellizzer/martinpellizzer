Rhipsalis boliviana

Rhipsalis boliviana, commonly known as the silver mist cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems that are often covered in a silvery, powdery coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the winter months, adding a delicate beauty to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
