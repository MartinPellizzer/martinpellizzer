Ruschia stricta var. turgida

Ruschia stricta var. turgida, commonly known as the Fat-tailed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
