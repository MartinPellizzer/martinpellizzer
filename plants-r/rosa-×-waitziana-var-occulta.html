Rosa × waitziana var. occulta

Rosa × waitziana var. occulta, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and is often used as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rosa × waitziana var. occulta is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun.
