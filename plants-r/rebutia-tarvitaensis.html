Rebutia tarvitaensis

Rebutia tarvitaensis, commonly known as the Tarvitaensis Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America, specifically Peru.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
