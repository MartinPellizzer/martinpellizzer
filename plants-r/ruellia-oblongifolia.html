Ruellia oblongifolia

Ruellia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ruellia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped to ovate leaves that are typically smooth and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple, which bloom from summer to fall. Ruellia oblongifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
