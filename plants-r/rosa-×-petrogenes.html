Rosa × petrogenes

Rosa × petrogenes, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers.

It is a hybrid between Rosa damascena and Rosa gallica, resulting in a variety that exhibits a wide range of colors and flower forms. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are usually single or double blooms, with a strong perfume, making it a favorite in gardens and floral arrangements.

Rosa × petrogenes requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive, and it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and historical significance in horticulture.
