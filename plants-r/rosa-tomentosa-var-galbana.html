Rosa tomentosa var. galbana

Rosa tomentosa var. galbana, commonly known as the woolly rose or the galbana rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy grayish-white hairs covering its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its foliage is typically dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in various climates.
