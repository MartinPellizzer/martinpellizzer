Rubus × hayekii

Rubus × hayekii, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rubus ursinus (slippery elm) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry). This plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in temperate regions. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in jams and desserts.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and its potential in food and pharmaceutical applications.
