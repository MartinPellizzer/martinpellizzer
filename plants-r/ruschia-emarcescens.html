Ruschia emarcescens

Ruschia emarcescens, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
