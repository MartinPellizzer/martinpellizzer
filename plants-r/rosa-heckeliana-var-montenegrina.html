Rosa heckeliana var. montenegrina

Rosa heckeliana var. montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegro rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Balkan region.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature in temperate climates.
