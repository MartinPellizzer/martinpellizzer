Ruta chalepensis

Ruta chalepensis, commonly known as pale bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, featuring blue or purple flowers and fuzzy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant has a strong, aromatic scent and is often found in rocky or dry soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional remedies.

Ruta chalepensis is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments, making it a resilient species.
