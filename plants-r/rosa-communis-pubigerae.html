Rosa communis pubigerae

Rosa communis pubigerae, commonly known as the soft-hairy rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
