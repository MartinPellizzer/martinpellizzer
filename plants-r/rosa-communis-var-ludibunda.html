Rosa communis var. ludibunda

Rosa communis var. ludibunda, commonly known as the laughing rose, is a cultivar prized for its vibrant, colorful blooms.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, double flowers that range from pink to red. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture further enhance its appeal.
