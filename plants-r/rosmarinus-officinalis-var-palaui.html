Rosmarinus officinalis var. palaui

Rosmarinus officinalis var. palaui, commonly known as Palaui rosemary, is a variety of the rosemary plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that are rich in essential oils, particularly camphor and pinene. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with gray-green foliage and small blue or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

This variety is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its strong fragrance and therapeutic properties.
