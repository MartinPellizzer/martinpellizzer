Rabdosia shikokiana var. intermedia

Rabdosia shikokiana var. intermedia, commonly known as the Shikoku intermediate rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in whorls.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
