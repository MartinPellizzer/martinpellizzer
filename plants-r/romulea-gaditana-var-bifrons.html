Romulea gaditana var. bifrons

Romulea gaditana var. bifrons, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula glorybower, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
