Ruellia splendidula

Ruellia splendidula, commonly known as the magnificent ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of blue, violet, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia splendidula thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
