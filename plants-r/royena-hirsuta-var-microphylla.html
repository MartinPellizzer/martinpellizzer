Royena hirsuta var. microphylla

Royena hirsuta var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved hirsute roya, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a desirable ornamental plant.
