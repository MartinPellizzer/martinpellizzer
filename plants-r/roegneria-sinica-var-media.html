Roegneria sinica var. media

Roegneria sinica var. media, commonly known as the Chinese grass or the mountain grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, spike-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

Roegneria sinica var. media is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fine texture and visual interest.
