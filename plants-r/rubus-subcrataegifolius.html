Rubus subcrataegifolius

Rubus subcrataegifolius, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in woodland areas and along streambanks, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

Its flowers are white or pale pink, and it is often found in association with other brambles in the Rubus genus.
