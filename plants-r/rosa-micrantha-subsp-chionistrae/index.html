Rosa Micrantha Subsp. Chionistrae

Rosa micrantha subsp. chionistrae is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It is known for its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
