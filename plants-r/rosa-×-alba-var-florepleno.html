Rosa × alba var. florepleno

Rosa × alba var. florepleno, commonly known as the double white rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, full blooms that are typically white with a slight pinkish tinge. The plant produces large, rounded leaves with serrated edges and a fragrant, sweet scent. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is known for its abundant flowering and hardy nature, making it a popular choice for both landscape and cut flower use.
