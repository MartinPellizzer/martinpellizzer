Rosa tsinlingensis

Rosa tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rosa tsinlingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
