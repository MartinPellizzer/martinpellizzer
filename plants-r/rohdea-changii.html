Rohdea changii

Rohdea changii, commonly known as the Chinese lily or zigzag plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features distinctive, sword-shaped leaves with a zigzag pattern along the midrib, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Rohdea changii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its unique foliage.
