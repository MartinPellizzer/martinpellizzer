Rapuntium puberulum var. glabellum

Rapuntium puberulum var. glabellum, commonly known as the smooth rapunculus, is a variant of the rapunculus species characterized by its smooth, glabrous surface.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many flowering plants with small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically features a short, stout stem with alternate, simple leaves that are often lobed or toothed. Its flowers are usually bright yellow, and it produces small, round fruits known as silique.
