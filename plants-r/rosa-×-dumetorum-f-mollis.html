Rosa × dumetorum f. mollis

Rosa × dumetorum f. mollis, commonly known as the soft-hairy rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and hairy, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in damp, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
