Radiogrammitis multifolia

Radiogrammitis multifolia, commonly known as the feather moss, is a small, evergreen moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and tiny, needle-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant forms dense, carpet-like mats on forest floors, often found in temperate and boreal regions. Radiogrammitis multifolia plays an important role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its ecosystem.

Its simple yet elegant structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in controlled environments.
