Rhynchosia reticulata var. kuntzei

Rhynchosia reticulata var. kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's spiderflower, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its striking, spider-like flowers are typically bright yellow or orange. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
