Rosa × andegavensis

Rosa × andegavensis, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It is native to the northeastern United States and is well adapted to temperate climates.

Rosa × andegavensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
