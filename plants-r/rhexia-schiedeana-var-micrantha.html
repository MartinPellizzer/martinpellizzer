Rhexia schiedeana var. micrantha

Rhexia schiedeana var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered rhexia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils in woodland or swampy areas. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
