Rosa canina var. elliptica

Rosa canina var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic dog rose, is a variant of the common dog rose.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and fragrant pink flowers. The plant produces red, velvety hips that are rich in vitamin C. It is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
