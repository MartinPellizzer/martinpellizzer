Racosperma curvatum

Racosperma curvatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with slender stems and small, elongated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
