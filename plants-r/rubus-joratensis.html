Rubus joratensis

Rubus joratensis, commonly known as the Jorat raspberry, is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of Switzerland.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of shrubs known for their edible fruits. The plant produces small, red, aggregate fruits that are similar in appearance to other raspberries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
