Rodriguezia planifolia

Rodriguezia planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaf rodriguezia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features broad, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, moist soils.

Rodriguezia planifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded to partially sunny conditions.
