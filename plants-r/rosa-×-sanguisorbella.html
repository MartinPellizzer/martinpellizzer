Rosa × sanguisorbella

Rosa × sanguisorbella, commonly known as the hybrid rugosa rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its rugged, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for both garden and natural settings.
