Roegneria multiculmis

Roegneria multiculmis, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, awned spikelets. Roegneria multiculmis is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control in suitable climates.
