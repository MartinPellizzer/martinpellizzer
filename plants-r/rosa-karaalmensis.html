Rosa karaalmensis

Rosa karaalmensis, commonly known as the Karoo rose, is a species of shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has woody stems and produces rose-like blooms that appear in late summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The leaves are simple and alternate, often covered in fine hairs.
