Roystonea stellata

Roystonea stellata, commonly known as the star palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, spiny fruits. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize the soil in its natural habitat.

Roystonea stellata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant foliage and adaptability to various climates.
