Rosa jundzillii var. godetii

Rosa jundzillii var. godetii, commonly known as the Godetii rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its showy blooms and compact growth habit.
