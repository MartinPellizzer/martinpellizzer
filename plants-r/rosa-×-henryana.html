Rosa × henryana

Rosa × henryana, commonly known as the Henry rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and the production of large, showy flowers that can be pink, red, or white in color. The plant typically features thorny stems and pinnate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Rosa × henryana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
