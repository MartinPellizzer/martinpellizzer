Rosa rubiginosa var. hirta

Rosa rubiginosa var. hirta, commonly known as the woolly rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its stems are typically covered in a thick layer of fine, fuzzy hairs.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
