Rabdosia racemosa

Rabdosia racemosa, commonly known as Chinese violet or blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Rabdosia racemosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally used in traditional medicine.
