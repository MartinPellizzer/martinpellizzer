Rottboellia protensa

Rottboellia protensa, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and culms that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish-brown in hue. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib.

Rottboellia protensa is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
