Rosa × centifolia belgica

Rosa × centifolia belgica, commonly known as the Belgian centifolia rose, is a hybrid species of rose originating from Belgium.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is well-suited for cultivation in temperate climates and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and essential oil production.
