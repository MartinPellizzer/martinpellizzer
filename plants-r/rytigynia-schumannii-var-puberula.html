Rytigynia schumannii var. puberula

Rytigynia schumannii var. puberula, commonly known as the roughleaf rytigynia, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its rough, pubescent leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often borne in the axils of the leaves. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

This plant is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
