Rogiera latifolia

Rogiera latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved rogeria, is a member of the Rosaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
