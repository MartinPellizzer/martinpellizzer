Rhaphidophora banosensis

Rhaphidophora banosensis, commonly known as the Bano's arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

Rhaphidophora banosensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
