Rosa cardinalis

Rosa cardinalis, commonly known as the cardinal rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of deep red, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are dark green with a slightly hairy texture. Rosa cardinalis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
