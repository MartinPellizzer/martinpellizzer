Restiaria gaudichaudii

Restiaria gaudichaudii, commonly known as the New Caledonian restiaria, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Restiariaceae and is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments within the tropical rainforests of its native habitat.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
