Rourea intermedia

Rourea intermedia, commonly known as the silverleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped, silvery-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Rourea intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
