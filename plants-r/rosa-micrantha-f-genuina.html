Rosa micrantha f. genuina

Rosa micrantha f. genuina, commonly known as the dwarf garden rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its botanical characteristics include thorny stems and a generally bushy growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
