Rosa abietina subvar. chenevardii

Rosa abietina subvar. chenevardii, commonly known as the Alpine rose or mountain rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, single or double pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This subvariety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
