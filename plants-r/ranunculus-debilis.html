Ranunculus debilis

Ranunculus debilis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or white, with five to eleven petals. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Ranunculus debilis is noted for its short stature and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
