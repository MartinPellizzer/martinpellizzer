Rosa mollis f. notoensis

Rosa mollis f. notoensis, commonly known as the Notoense rose, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its soft, hairy stems and small, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant produces pink to white blooms in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticulture.
