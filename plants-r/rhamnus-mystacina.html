Rhamnus mystacina

Rhamnus mystacina, commonly known as the Mediterranean buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its fruits are small, dark berries that mature in late summer.

Rhamnus mystacina is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
