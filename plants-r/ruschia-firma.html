Ruschia firma

Ruschia firma, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
