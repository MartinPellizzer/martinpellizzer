Rhododendron hypopitys

Rhododendron hypopitys, commonly known as the alpine rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in the Himalayas and eastern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bell-shaped, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil and partial to full shade. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Rhododendron hypopitys is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations.
