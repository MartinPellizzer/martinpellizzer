Rhododendron maxiongense

Rhododendron maxiongense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and acidic soils. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

Rhododendron maxiongense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
