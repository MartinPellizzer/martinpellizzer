Rosa communis var. andegavensis

Rosa communis var. andegavensis, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose, is a variety of the common rose with distinct botanical characteristics.

It features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in perfumery and traditional medicine due to their aromatic properties.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
