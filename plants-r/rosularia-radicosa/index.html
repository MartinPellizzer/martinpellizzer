Rosularia Radicosa

Rosularia radicosa is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It is known for its compact, rosette-like growth pattern and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering, which can lead to root rot.
