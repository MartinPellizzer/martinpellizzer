Rosa decora var. kuhbergensis

Rosa decora var. kuhbergensis, commonly known as the Kuhberg rose, is a compact, bushy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for alpine gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
