Rubus foliosus microgene flexuosus

Rubus foliosus microgene flexuosus, commonly known as the flexible leaf raspberry, is a shrub native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and small, aggregate fruits that ripen to a deep red color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas. Its flowers are white with five petals and are followed by the characteristic raspberry-like fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
