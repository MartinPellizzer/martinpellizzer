Roegneria sajanensis

Roegneria sajanensis, commonly known as the Sajan grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tien Shan and Pamir Mountains. This perennial grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Roegneria sajanensis is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
