Rosa webbiana var. latifolia

Rosa webbiana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf rock rose, is a species of shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and is often found in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and drought tolerance.
