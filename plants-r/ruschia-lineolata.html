Ruschia lineolata

Ruschia lineolata, commonly known as the striped Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
