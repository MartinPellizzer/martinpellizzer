Rosa × venustula

Rosa × venustula, commonly known as the 'Alba Plena' rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its double, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and a woody stem structure typical of rose species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × venustula prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
