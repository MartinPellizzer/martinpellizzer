Rubus waisbeckeri

Rubus waisbeckeri, commonly known as the Waisbecker's raspberry, is a species of bramble native to South America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
