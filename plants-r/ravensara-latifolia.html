Ravensara latifolia

Ravensara latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf raven's arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a distinctive, waxy texture. The plant produces striking red spathes that enclose its yellowish spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Ravensara latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
