Rhododendron campanulatum var. aeruginosum

Rhododendron campanulatum var. aeruginosum, commonly known as the Azure Bell Rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a contrasting white throat. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
