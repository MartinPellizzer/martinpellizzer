Rosa spinosissima var. microcarpa

Rosa spinosissima var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf spinous rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense growth habit and seasonal blooms.
