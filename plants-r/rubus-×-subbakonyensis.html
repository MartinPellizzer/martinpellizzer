Rubus × subbakonyensis

Rubus × subbakonyensis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry) and Rubus × fruticosus (bramble). The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color, with a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
