Rhoeo spathacea

Rhoeo spathacea, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and green. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhoeo spathacea is often used in landscaping for its vibrant foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
