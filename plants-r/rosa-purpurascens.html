Rosa purpurascens

Rosa purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf rose, is a species native to the eastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features deep green foliage that turns a striking reddish-purple in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its flowers are usually pink to red, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rosa purpurascens is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
