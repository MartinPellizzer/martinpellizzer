Roegneria angustiglumis

Roegneria angustiglumis, commonly known as slenderawn fescue, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, spike-like inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has fibrous roots and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Roegneria angustiglumis is often found in association with other grasses and forbs in prairie and wetland ecosystems.
