Ruschia dyeri

Ruschia dyeri, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
