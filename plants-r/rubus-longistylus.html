Rubus longistylus

Rubus longistylus, commonly known as the long-styled raspberry.

It is a species of brambles belonging to the Rubus genus in the family Rosaceae. This plant is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits, which are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five to seven petals and prominent stamens.
