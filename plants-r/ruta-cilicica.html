Ruta cilicica

Ruta cilicica, commonly known as common rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant contains volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, pungent scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or dry environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it both a useful herb and a decorative plant in gardens.
