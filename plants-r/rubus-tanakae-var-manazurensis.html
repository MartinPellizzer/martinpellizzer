Rubus tanakae var. manazurensis

Rubus tanakae var. manazurensis, commonly known as the Manazuren raspberry, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
