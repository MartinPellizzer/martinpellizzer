Rhodospatha neillii

Rhodospatha neillii, commonly known as the Neill's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant pink or red hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rhodospatha neillii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
