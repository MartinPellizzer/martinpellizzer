Rabdosia chionantha

Rabdosia chionantha, commonly known as the silver-haired rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. It produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the spring.

Rabdosia chionantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
