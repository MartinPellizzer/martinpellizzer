Rhodostemonodaphne cyclops

Rhodostemonodaphne cyclops, commonly known as the cyclops banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive aerial roots that form a dense network around its trunk, creating a unique appearance. The plant has large, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
