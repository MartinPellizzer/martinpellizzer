Rosa × borbonica

Rosa × borbonica, commonly known as the Bourbon rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the island of Réunion.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep red. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Rosa × borbonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
