Rottboellia diversiflora

Rottboellia diversiflora, commonly known as the African grass or broadleaf grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features erect, slender stems and produces dense clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is known for its rapid growth and is often used in erosion control due to its extensive root system.

Rottboellia diversiflora is also cultivated as an ornamental plant in some regions.
