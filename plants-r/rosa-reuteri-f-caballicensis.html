Rosa reuteri f. caballicensis

Rosa reuteri f. caballicensis, commonly known as the Caballicensis rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces hips that are typically red or orange in color. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in mild climates.
