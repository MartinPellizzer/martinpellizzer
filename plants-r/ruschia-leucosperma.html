Ruschia leucosperma

Ruschia leucosperma, commonly known as the white-pearled Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
