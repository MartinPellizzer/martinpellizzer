Rosa × yensis

Rosa × yensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa × yensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
