Riencourtia tenuifolia var. intermedia

Riencourtia tenuifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the slender leafed riencourtia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
