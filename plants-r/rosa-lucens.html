Rosa lucens

Rosa lucens, commonly known as the luminous rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its fragrant, pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, serrated leaves. Its stems are covered in soft, silvery hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance.

Rosa lucens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
