Ruellia ansericollis

Ruellia ansericollis, commonly known as the silver-leaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves and clusters of blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Ruellia ansericollis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
