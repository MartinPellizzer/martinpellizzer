Rosa × polliniana var. dura

Rosa × polliniana var. dura, commonly known as the hardy shrub rose, is a hybrid species that exhibits robust growth and cold tolerance.

It is characterized by its dense, thorned stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink to red flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy blooms that attract pollinators. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant flowers.

This variety is well-suited for garden borders and hedges due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
