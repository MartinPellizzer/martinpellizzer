Rulingia madagascariensis var. andringitrensis

Rulingia madagascariensis var. andringitrensis, commonly known as the Andringitra rulingia, is a unique plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its rough, fissured bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the montane rainforests of the Andringitra region.
