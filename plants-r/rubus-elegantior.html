Rubus elegantior

Rubus elegantior, commonly known as the elegant raspberry, is a species of bramble native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, red, edible fruits. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The berries are used in culinary applications and are also valued for their ornamental appeal.
