Rubus bonatii

Rubus bonatii, commonly known as the Bonati raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for cultivation, it is occasionally grown in gardens.
