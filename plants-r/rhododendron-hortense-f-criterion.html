Rhododendron hortense f. criterion

Rhododendron hortense f. criterion, commonly known as the Criterion rhododendron, is a hybrid plant prized for its ornamental value.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red, often with a distinctive white throat. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ability to add vibrant color to gardens in cooler climates.
