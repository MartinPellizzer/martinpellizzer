Richtersveldia columnaris

Richtersveldia columnaris, commonly known as the columnar rock rose, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that resemble columns, giving it its common name. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the top of the stems.

Richtersveldia columnaris is a striking example of xerophytic adaptation in the succulent flora of the region.
