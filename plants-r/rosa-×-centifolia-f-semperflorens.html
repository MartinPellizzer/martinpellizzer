Rosa × centifolia f. semperflorens

Rosa × centifolia f. semperflorens, commonly known as the perpetual bloom cabbage rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season.

It is a fragrant plant with semi-double to full blooms that range in color from white to deep pink, depending on the cultivar. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to bloom continuously, enhancing gardens with its persistent floral display.
