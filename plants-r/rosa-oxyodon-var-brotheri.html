Rosa oxyodon var. brotheri

Rosa oxyodon var. brotheri, commonly known as the Brother's rose, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Rosa oxyodon species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thornless stems, which make it suitable for small garden spaces. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop for the blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
