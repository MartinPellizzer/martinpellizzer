Rhinorchis jaguariahyvae

Rhinorchis jaguariahyvae, commonly known as the jaguariahyva orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees in the understory of rainforests. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
