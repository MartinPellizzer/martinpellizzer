Rosa sassnowskyana

Rosa sassnowskyana, commonly known as the Siberian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the cold climates of Siberia and the Russian Far East.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is well adapted to harsh environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.

Rosa sassnowskyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in cold climates.
