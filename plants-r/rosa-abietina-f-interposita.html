Rosa abietina f. interposita

Rosa abietina f. interposita, commonly known as the Alpine rose or the mountain rose, is a botanical hybrid that exhibits unique characteristics.

It is a compact shrub with dense, dark green foliage and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant is native to the alpine regions of Europe and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in challenging garden conditions.
