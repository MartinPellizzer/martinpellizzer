Rhododendron × rosifaciens

Rhododendron × rosifaciens, commonly known as the rose-faced rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The species is also noted for its ability to withstand colder temperatures compared to other rhododendron hybrids.
