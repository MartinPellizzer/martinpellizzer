Ragatelus

Ragatelus, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
