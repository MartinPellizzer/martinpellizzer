Rapanea samoensis

Rapanea samoensis, commonly known as the Samoan holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the islands of Samoa and nearby parts of the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and oval in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green.
