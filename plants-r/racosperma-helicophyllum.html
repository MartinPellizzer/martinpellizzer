Racosperma helicophyllum

Racosperma helicophyllum, commonly known as the spiral-leaved acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the legume family. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Racosperma helicophyllum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in dry climates.
