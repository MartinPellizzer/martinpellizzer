Ruschia concava

Ruschia concava, commonly known as the concave ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive concave shape. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Ruschia concava is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
