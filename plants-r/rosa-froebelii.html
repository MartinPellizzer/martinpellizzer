Rosa froebelii

Rosa froebelii, commonly known as the Froebel's rose, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces hips that are red when mature. It is a deciduous plant, shedding its leaves in the fall.

Rosa froebelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance in suitable climates.
