Rosa × moorei

Rosa × moorei, commonly known as the Moore's rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa × moorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
