Rustia secundiflora

Rustia secundiflora, commonly known as the slender rustia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Rustia secundiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
