Rudgea medians

Rudgea medians, commonly known as the Brazilian candlewood, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

Rudgea medians is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its dense foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
