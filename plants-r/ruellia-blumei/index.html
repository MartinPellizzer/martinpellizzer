Ruellia Blumei

Ruellia blumei is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy texture. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish.
