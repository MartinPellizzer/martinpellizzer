Rosa communis var. firmula

Rosa communis var. firmula, commonly known as the firm-flowered rose, is a cultivar prized for its robust growth and showy blooms.

It is characterized by its compact, upright shrub form with dark green, glossy leaves. The flowers are typically large, fragrant, and come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. This variety is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its disease resistance and adaptability to various soil types.

It requires full sun and regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote continuous flowering.
