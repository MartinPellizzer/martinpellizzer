Rinorea pubescens

Rinorea pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and covered in fine pubescence.

Rinorea pubescens is often used in traditional medicine and can also be grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
