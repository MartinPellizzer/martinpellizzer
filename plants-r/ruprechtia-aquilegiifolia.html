Ruprechtia aquilegiifolia

Ruprechtia aquilegiifolia, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white or yellow throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
