Roscyna americana

Roscyna americana, commonly known as the American roscyna, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Roscyna americana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
