Rosa cuneata

Rosa cuneata, commonly known as the Chinese rose or evergreen rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China and Korea.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Rosa cuneata is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which remains on the plant throughout the year.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
