Racosperma convallium

Racosperma convallium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the genus Racosperma.

The species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to drought conditions.
