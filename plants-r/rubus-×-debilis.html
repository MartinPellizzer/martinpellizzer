Rubus × debilis

Rubus × debilis, commonly known as the weak blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, edible berries. The plant typically produces dark purple to black fruits that are often used in jams and desserts. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a variety of morphological traits that can vary between individual plants.
