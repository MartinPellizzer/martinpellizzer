Rhaphidophora minor

Rhaphidophora minor, commonly known as the small arrowroot.

It is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive midrib. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by triangular, edible tubers.

Rhaphidophora minor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
