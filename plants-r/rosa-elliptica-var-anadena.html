Rosa elliptica var. anadena

Rosa elliptica var. anadena, commonly known as the Anadenan rose, is a compact shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly serrated. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable choice for both landscape design and conservation efforts.
