Reseda alba var. leucantha

Reseda alba var. leucantha, commonly known as white reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate and enduring blooms.
