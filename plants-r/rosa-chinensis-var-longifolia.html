Rosa chinensis var. longifolia

Rosa chinensis var. longifolia, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its clustered, fragrant flowers that typically bloom throughout the year. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and thorned stems, typical of many rose species. It is a shrub that can grow up to several feet tall, making it suitable for both ornamental and landscape use.

Its adaptability to various climates has made it a popular choice in horticulture worldwide.
