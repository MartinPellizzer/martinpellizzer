Rosa × pimpinellifolioalpina

Rosa × pimpinellifolioalpina, commonly known as the alpine evergreen rose, is a hybrid species that combines traits from various Rosa species.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year, giving it an evergreen appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens with suitable climatic conditions.
