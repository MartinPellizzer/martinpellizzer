Rhododendron vanvuurenii

Rhododendron vanvuurenii, commonly known as the Van Vuuren rhododendron, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers well-drained, acidic soils. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 3 meters, with multiple stems and a compact growth habit.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
