Raphanocarpus stefaninii var. membranosa

Raphanocarpus stefaninii var. membranosa, commonly known as the membranous raphanocarpus, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a hairy surface. This variety is native to southern China and is typically found in moist, shaded environments.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, elongated pods containing seeds.
