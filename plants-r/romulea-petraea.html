Romulea petraea

Romulea petraea, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are grass-like and often form a rosette at the base.

Romulea petraea is adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
