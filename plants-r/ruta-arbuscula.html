Ruta arbuscula

Ruta arbuscula, commonly known as the common rue, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features aromatic, pinnately compound leaves with a strong, peppery scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruits are small, dry capsules containing several seeds.

Ruta arbuscula is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, and it has been used historically for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
