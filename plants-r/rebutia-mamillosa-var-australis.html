Rebutia mamillosa var. australis

Rebutia mamillosa var. australis, commonly known as the Australian rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded stems that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and containers.
