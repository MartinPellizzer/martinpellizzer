Rubia aitchisonii

Rubia aitchisonii, commonly known as Aitchison's rue.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan and India. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to reddish-brown. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia aitchisonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
