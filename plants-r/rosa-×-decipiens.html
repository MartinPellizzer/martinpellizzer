Rosa × decipiens

Rosa × decipiens, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa chinensis.

It is characterized by its ability to produce flowers continuously throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant typically features robust, thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa × decipiens thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, exhibiting good resistance to diseases and pests.
