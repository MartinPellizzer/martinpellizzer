Ricinocarpus Brevipes

Banyan tree, scientifically known as Ricinocarpus brevipes, is a remarkable species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This tree is renowned for its unique appearance, characterized by its sprawling aerial roots that form a dense network of support. It can grow to impressive heights, often reaching over 30 meters, making it a dominant feature in its ecosystem. The banyan tree plays a vital role in providing habitat and shelter for various wildlife species. Its cultural and ecological significance has made it a symbol of resilience and longevity in many communities.
