Rhododendron hortense f. noordtianum

Rhododendron hortense f. noordtianum, commonly known as the Noordtianum rhododendron, is a hybrid form of the common rhododendron.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant is native to the Netherlands and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for horticultural collections and landscape design.
