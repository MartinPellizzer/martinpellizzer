Renealmia costaricensis

Renealmia costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican renealmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
