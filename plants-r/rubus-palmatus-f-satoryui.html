Rubus palmatus f. satoryui

Rubus palmatus f. satoryui, commonly known as the Satoryu raspberry, is a unique form of the common raspberry.

It is characterized by its hairy, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically reddish in color when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or shaded areas.

Due to its distinctive morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
