Rosa canina f. amoena

Rosa canina f. amoena, commonly known as the Amoena dog rose, is a cultivated form of the common dog rose.

It is characterized by its larger, more showy flowers compared to the typical Rosa canina. The plant produces pink to red flowers with a fragrant scent, making it popular in gardens. It has thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves, typical of the Rosa genus.

This variety is often used for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
