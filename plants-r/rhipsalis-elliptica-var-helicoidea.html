Rhipsalis elliptica var. helicoidea

Rhipsalis elliptica var. helicoidea, commonly known as the spiral cactus or helicoide cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraling growth pattern, with stems that twist around each other in a helical formation. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
