Rebutia nigricans subsp. carmeniana

Rebutia nigricans subsp.

carmeniana, commonly known as Carmen's dark rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America. It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem with a dark green to bluish-gray coloration.

The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
