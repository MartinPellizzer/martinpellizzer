Rubus × semiadornatiformis

Rubus × semiadornatiformis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

This plant is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rubus ursinus (black raspberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry). It typically produces small, tart berries with a unique combination of flavors. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, acidic soils.

Its botanical features include prickly stems and trifoliate leaves, making it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
