Rhaphidophora nigrescens

Rhaphidophora nigrescens, commonly known as the black nightshade fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that have a velvety texture and a distinctive blackish hue when mature. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
