Rosa davidii var. persetosa

Rosa davidii var. persetosa, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with oval-shaped leaflets and serrated edges.

Rosa davidii var. persetosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
