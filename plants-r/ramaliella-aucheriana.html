Ramaliella aucheriana

Ramaliella aucheriana, commonly known as the glassy moss or glassy fern, is a unique and visually striking plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it a glass-like appearance, which is a result of its high water content and specialized cell structures. Its rhizomes are slender and often form dense mats, contributing to its ability to colonize various substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
