Rondeletia hirta var. aitonii

Rondeletia hirta var. aitonii, commonly known as the velvetleaf rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming throughout the year. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
