Rhododendron eclecteum

Rhododendron eclecteum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a slight pubescence. Its flowers are usually pink to red, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron eclecteum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
