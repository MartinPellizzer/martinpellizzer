Rosa labrosula

Rosa labrosula, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the pink rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and is often found in forested areas and mountainous regions. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are thorny.

Rosa labrosula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
