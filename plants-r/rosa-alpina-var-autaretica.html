Rosa alpina var. autaretica

Rosa alpina var. autaretica, commonly known as the Alpine rose or Alpenrosen, is a variant of the alpine rose native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorned stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and serrated, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Rosa alpina var. autaretica is adapted to harsh alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
