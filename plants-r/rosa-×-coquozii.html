Rosa × coquozii

Rosa × coquozii, commonly known as the Coquozii rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse flowering, typically producing large, showy blooms in shades of pink to red. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it thrives in a variety of soil types and climates.
