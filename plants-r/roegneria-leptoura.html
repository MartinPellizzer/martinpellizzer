Roegneria leptoura

Roegneria leptoura, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Roegneria leptoura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
