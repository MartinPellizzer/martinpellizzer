Rytigynia rubiginosa subsp. cymigera

Rytigynia rubiginosa subsp.

cymigera, commonly known as the red-stemmed rytigynia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil. It belongs to the family Rytigyniaceae and is characterized by its reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in cymes, giving it its subspecies name. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
