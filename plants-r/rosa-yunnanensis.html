Rosa yunnanensis

Rosa yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have serrated edges.

Rosa yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
