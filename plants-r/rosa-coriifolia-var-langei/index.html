Rosa Coriifolia Var. Langei

Rosa coriifolia var. langei is a unique and rare variant of the rose known for its striking, almost ethereal appearance. This plant is native to specific regions in China and is prized for its delicate, lace-like leaves and fragrant blossoms. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and private collections. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public, highlighting the need for greater awareness and protection.
