Rosa platyacantha var. kokanica

Rosa platyacantha var. kokanica, commonly known as the Kokanica rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features dense, thornless stems with dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both ornamental gardening and hedging.

The variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
