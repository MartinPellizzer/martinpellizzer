Robbia martii

Robbia martii, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered together, and its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture. Robbia martii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
