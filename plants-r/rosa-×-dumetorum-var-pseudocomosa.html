Rosa × dumetorum var. pseudocomosa

Rosa × dumetorum var. pseudocomosa, commonly known as the alpine rose or mountain rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink, often with a deep center. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in garden settings.
