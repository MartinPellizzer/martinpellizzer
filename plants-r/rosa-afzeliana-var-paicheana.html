Rosa afzeliana var. paicheana

Rosa afzeliana var. paicheana, commonly known as the Paichean rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
