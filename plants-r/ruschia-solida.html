Ruschia solida

Ruschia solida, commonly known as the hardy ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia solida is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
