Roemeria hybrida var. tenuifolia

Roemeria hybrida var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved roemeria, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its delicate, finely divided leaves and umbellate inflorescences.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is found in regions with temperate climates. The plant features a tall, slender stem that supports clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its foliage is notable for its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded garden environments.
