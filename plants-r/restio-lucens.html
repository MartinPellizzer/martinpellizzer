Restio lucens

Restio lucens, commonly known as the shining restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Restio lucens typically grows in wet, coastal areas and is often found in sandy or clay soils.

It plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and provides habitat for various small organisms.
