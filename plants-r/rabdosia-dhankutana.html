Rabdosia dhankutana

Rabdosia dhankutana, commonly known as Dhankuta Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of Nepal.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its small, tubular flowers are usually violet or blue, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Rabdosia dhankutana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
