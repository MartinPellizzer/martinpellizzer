Rostellularia chiengmaiensis

Rostellularia chiengmaiensis, commonly known as the Chiengmai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Thailand.

Rostellularia chiengmaiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
