Rollinia rigidiflora

Rollinia rigidiflora, commonly known as the rigid-flowered rollinia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family, which includes a variety of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a spherical, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or orange as it matures.

Rollinia rigidiflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
