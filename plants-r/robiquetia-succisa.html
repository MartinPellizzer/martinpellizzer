Robiquetia succisa

Robiquetia succisa, commonly known as the rock rose.

It is a succulent plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange blooms during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its adaptability to drought conditions makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
