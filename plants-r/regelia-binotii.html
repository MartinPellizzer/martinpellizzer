Regelia binotii

Regelia binotii, commonly known as the silver leaf regelia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that exhibits a unique bicolor pattern of silver and green. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and summer months. Regelia binotii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its distinctive foliage and long-lasting blooms, which add visual interest to gardens throughout the growing season.
