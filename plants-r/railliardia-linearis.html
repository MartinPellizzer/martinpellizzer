Railliardia linearis

Railliardia linearis, commonly known as the slender rain lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features strap-like, linear leaves that are typically green and smooth. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Railliardia linearis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and ornamental addition to tropical gardens.
