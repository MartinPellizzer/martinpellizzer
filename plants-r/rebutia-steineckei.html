Rebutia steineckei

Rebutia steineckei, commonly known as the "Snowball Cactus," is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are typically blue-gray in color, with a distinctive rounded shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and early summer. Its foliage is succulent, allowing it to store water efficiently in arid environments.

Rebutia steineckei is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
