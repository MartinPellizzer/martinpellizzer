Rosa × naumannii

Rosa × naumannii, commonly known as the Naumann's rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa × naumannii is also valued for its use in perfumery due to the aromatic compounds present in its blooms.
