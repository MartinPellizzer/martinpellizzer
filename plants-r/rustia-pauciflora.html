Rustia pauciflora

Rustia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered rustia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its small, clustered flowers are often pink or red, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
