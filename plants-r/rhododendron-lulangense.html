Rhododendron lulangense

Rhododendron lulangense, commonly known as the Lulang rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its botanical features include showy inflorescences and a robust root system adapted to rocky and forested habitats.

Rhododendron lulangense is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native environment.
