Rhododendron kaliense

Rhododendron kaliense, commonly known as the Kaliense rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms.

Rhododendron kaliense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
