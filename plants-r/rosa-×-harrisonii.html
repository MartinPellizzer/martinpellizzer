Rosa × harrisonii

Rosa × harrisonii, commonly known as the Harrison's rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and a shrub-like growth habit. The plant typically features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × harrisonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
