Raphia monbuttorum var. mortehanii

Raphia monbuttorum var. mortehanii, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a large, ornamental palm native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach lengths of up to 6 meters. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil to grow optimally. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.

The species is also valued for its resilience and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
