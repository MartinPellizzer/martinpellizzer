Rhynchosia corylifolia f. glabrior

Rhynchosia corylifolia f. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leafed cowpea, is a variant of the cowpea species characterized by its reduced pubescence.

It is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features compound leaves with palmately arranged leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are edible and rich in protein.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential agricultural use in sustainable farming systems.
