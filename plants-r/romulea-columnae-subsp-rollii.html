Romulea columnae subsp. rollii

Romulea columnae subsp.

rollii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in exotic and rare plants.
