Rinorea angustifolia subsp. natalensis

Rinorea angustifolia subsp.

natalensis, commonly known as Natal slenderleaf, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and valuable species in its native habitat.
