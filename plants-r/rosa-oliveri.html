Rosa oliveri

Rosa oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's rose, is a shrub-like species native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, thorny stems and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically borne singly or in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Rosa oliveri is well adapted to the arid and rocky environments of its native habitat.
