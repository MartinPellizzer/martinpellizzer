Rosa maeotica

Rosa maeotica, commonly known as the Crimean rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Black Sea region.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in the summer months. Rosa maeotica is adapted to sandy soils and coastal environments, making it resilient to salt spray.

It plays an important role in the local flora and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
