Rosa elliptica var. billetii

Rosa elliptica var. billetii, commonly known as the Billet's elliptical rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden environments.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of climates.
