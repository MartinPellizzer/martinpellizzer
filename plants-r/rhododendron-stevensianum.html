Rhododendron stevensianum

Rhododendron stevensianum, commonly known as the Scottish heather, is a flowering plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blossoms.

Rhododendron stevensianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting local wildlife.
