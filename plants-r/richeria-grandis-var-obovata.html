Richeria grandis var. obovata

Richeria grandis var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed richeria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, obovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
