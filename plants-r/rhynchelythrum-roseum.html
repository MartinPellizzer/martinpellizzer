Rhynchelythrum roseum

Rhynchelythrum roseum, commonly known as the roseum bluishgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green leaves and small, pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and delicate floral display.

Rhynchelythrum roseum is relatively low-maintenance and drought-tolerant, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
