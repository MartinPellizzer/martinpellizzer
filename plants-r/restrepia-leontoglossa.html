Restrepia leontoglossa

Restrepia leontoglossa, commonly known as the leopard orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with flowers that resemble leopards' spots, hence its common name. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Restrepia leontoglossa is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
