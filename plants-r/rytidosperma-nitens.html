Rytidosperma nitens

Rytidosperma nitens, commonly known as the slender spinifex, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its fine, wiry foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often forming dense tussocks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue due to a dense covering of hairs.

Rytidosperma nitens is well adapted to drought conditions and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
