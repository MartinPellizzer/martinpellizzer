Rhynchospora nervosa var. subfiliformis

Rhynchospora nervosa var. subfiliformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, typical of the sedge family.
