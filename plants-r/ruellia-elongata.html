Ruellia elongata

Ruellia elongata, commonly known as the slender ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. Ruellia elongata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
