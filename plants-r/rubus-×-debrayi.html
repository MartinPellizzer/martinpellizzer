Rubus × debrayi

Rubus × debrayi, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry, is a hybrid plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
