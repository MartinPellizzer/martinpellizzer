Rubus sprengeliiflorus

Rubus sprengeliiflorus, commonly known as the Sprengel's rose.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features prickly stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It produces red or pink berries that are edible but often tart in flavor.

Native to certain regions of South America, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance.
