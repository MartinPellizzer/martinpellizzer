Rebutia steinmannii var. melanocentra

Rebutia steinmannii var. melanocentra, commonly known as the Black-centred Rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It features compact, rounded stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive dark center, which gives it its common name. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
