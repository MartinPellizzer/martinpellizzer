Rapanea villicaulis

Rapanea villicaulis, commonly known as the silky-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with soft, silky hairs on the leaf surfaces. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Rapanea villicaulis is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
