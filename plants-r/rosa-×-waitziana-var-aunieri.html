Rosa × waitziana var. aunieri

Rosa × waitziana var. aunieri, commonly known as the Aunier's rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
