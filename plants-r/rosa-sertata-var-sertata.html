Rosa sertata var. sertata

Rosa sertata var. sertata, commonly known as the compact rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thornless stems that form a rounded, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its compact size and continuous flowering.
