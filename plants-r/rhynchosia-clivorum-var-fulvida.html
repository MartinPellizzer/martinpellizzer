Rhynchosia clivorum var. fulvida

Rhynchosia clivorum var. fulvida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered climbing bean, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves with rounded lobes. The flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive red or orange throat, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant produces legume pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.
