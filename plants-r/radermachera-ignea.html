Radermachera ignea

Radermachera ignea, commonly known as the flame maple, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, maple-like leaves that are usually a vibrant red or orange, especially when young. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are followed by round, red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Radermachera ignea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
