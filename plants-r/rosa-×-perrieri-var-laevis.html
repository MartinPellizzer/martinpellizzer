Rosa × perrieri var. laevis

Rosa × perrieri var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth evergreen rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is a cold-hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and evergreen nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
