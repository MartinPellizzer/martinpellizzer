Rosa communis var. martinii

Rosa communis var. martinii, commonly known as the Martinii rose, is a cultivar of the common rose.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
