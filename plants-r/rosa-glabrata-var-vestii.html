Rosa glabrata var. vestii

Rosa glabrata var. vestii, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that are highly ornamental and attract pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Rosa glabrata var. vestii is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
