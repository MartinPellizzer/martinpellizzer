Rhamnus theezans

Rhamnus theezans, commonly known as the Ethiopian buckthorn, is a shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by black, fleshy berries that are rich in nutrients. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Rhamnus theezans is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or semi-arid regions.
