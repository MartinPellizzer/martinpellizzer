Rosa afzeliana var. kmetiana

Rosa afzeliana var. kmetiana, commonly known as the Kmetian rose, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It features fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It is often found in woodland and scrub habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for horticultural and ecological studies.
