Rosa diracantha

Rosa diracantha, commonly known as the African rose or desert rose, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It features thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink or red. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rosa diracantha is well adapted to dry climates and can thrive with minimal water.
