Rosa jenensis

Rosa jenensis, commonly known as the Jenish rose, is a species of shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges and is typically found in rocky or arid environments. Rosa jenensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.

It is also noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
