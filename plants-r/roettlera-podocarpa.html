Roettlera podocarpa

Roettlera podocarpa, commonly known as the tropical tree or the African olive, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, with a dense, rounded crown. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or greenish-white. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Roettlera podocarpa is native to tropical Africa and is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.
