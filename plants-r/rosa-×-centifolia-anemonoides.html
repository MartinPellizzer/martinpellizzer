Rosa × centifolia anemonoides

Rosa × centifolia anemonoides, commonly known as the cabbage rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, rounded form.

It features large, fragrant flowers with multiple layers of petals that resemble cabbage leaves, hence its common name. The plant produces abundant blooms in shades of pink, white, and red, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. It is typically grown as a shrub or in containers, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
