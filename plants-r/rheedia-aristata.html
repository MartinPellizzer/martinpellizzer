Rheedia aristata

Rheedia aristata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Central and South America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy, floating on the water's surface. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. Rheedia aristata thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often used in ornamental ponds.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an effective choice for water gardeners seeking to control algae growth.
