Ruehssia guaranitica

Ruehssia guaranitica, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
