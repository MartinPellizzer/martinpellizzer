Rohmooa

Rohmooa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. Rohmooa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
