Rudolfiella aurantiaca

Rudolfiella aurantiaca, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a striking species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical rainforest environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often with a waxy texture, and it prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Rudolfiella aurantiaca is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
