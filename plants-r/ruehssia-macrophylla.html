Ruehssia macrophylla

Ruehssia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant ruehssia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in gardens.
