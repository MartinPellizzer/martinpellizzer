Rochetia choensis

Rochetia choensis, commonly known as the Chinese rochetia, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Rochetia choensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
