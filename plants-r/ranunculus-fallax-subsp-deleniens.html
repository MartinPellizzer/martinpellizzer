Ranunculus fallax subsp. deleniens

Ranunculus fallax subsp.

deleniens, commonly known as the Deleniens buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with numerous stamens.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
