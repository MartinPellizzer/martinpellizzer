Ranunculus cryptanthus

Ranunculus cryptanthus, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and sometimes pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a hairy texture that helps protect it from harsh weather conditions.

Ranunculus cryptanthus is a herbaceous perennial that blooms in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
