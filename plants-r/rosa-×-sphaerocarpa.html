Rosa × sphaerocarpa

Rosa × sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and is native to China. The plant produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and covered with fine hairs.

Rosa × sphaerocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a decorative plant.
