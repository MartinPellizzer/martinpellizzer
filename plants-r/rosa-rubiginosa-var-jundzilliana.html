Rosa rubiginosa var. jundzilliana

Rosa rubiginosa var. jundzilliana, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a shrub-like species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in challenging growing conditions.
