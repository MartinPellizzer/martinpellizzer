Rosa communis var. stenacantha

Rosa communis var. stenacantha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rose, is a botanical variant of the common rose.

It is characterized by its narrow, serrated leaves and fragrant, pink to white flowers. This plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.

The species is also valued for its historical use in perfumery and herbal medicine.
