Rubus × stenoacanthoides

Rubus × stenoacanthoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, with a hairy underside.

It is native to regions of eastern Asia and is often cultivated for its edible berries and ornamental value.
