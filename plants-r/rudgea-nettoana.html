Rudgea nettoana

Rudgea nettoana, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive purple or brown lip.
