Rosa desiderata

Rosa desiderata, commonly known as the "desired rose," is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rosa desiderata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
