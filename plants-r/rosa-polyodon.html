Rosa polyodon

Rosa polyodon, commonly known as the Japanese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its upright growth habit and fragrant pink flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often armed with thorns. Rosa polyodon thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

It is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its historical use in traditional medicine.
