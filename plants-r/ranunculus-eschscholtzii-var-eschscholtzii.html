Ranunculus eschscholtzii var. eschscholtzii

Ranunculus eschscholtzii var. eschscholtzii, commonly known as the Eschscholtz's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its delicate, herbaceous appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
