Rosa omeiensis var. polyphylla

Rosa omeiensis var. polyphylla, commonly known as the multifoliate rose, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features compound leaves with numerous leaflets, giving it a lush, bushy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and unique botanical features, it is highly valued in horticultural collections.
