Rubus × geraniifolius

Rubus × geraniifolius, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces bright red, juicy berries that are typically harvested in the summer months. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a serrated edge, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value in gardens.
