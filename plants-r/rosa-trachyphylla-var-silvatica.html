Rosa trachyphylla var. silvatica

Rosa trachyphylla var. silvatica, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features narrow, elliptic leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
