Rhynchosia pseudoteramnoides

Rhynchosia pseudoteramnoides, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
