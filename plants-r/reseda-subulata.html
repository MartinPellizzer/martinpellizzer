Reseda subulata

Reseda subulata, commonly known as the needle-leaved reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
