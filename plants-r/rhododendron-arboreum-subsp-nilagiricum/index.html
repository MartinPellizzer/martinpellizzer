Rhododendron Arboreum Subsp. Nilagiricum

Rhododendron arboreum subsp. nilagiricum is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Western Ghats in India. It is known for its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter and early spring. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in dense forests and rocky slopes. Its beauty makes it a prized specimen among horticulturists and botanists. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and climate change.
