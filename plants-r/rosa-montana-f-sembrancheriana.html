Rosa montana f. sembrancheriana

Rosa montana f. sembrancheriana, commonly known as the Sembrancheriana rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are finely toothed and provide a lush backdrop to its blooms. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
