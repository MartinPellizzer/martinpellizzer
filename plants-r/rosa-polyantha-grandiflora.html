Rosa polyantha grandiflora

Rosa polyantha grandiflora, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy blossoms. It is native to China and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Rosa polyantha grandiflora is well adapted to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.
