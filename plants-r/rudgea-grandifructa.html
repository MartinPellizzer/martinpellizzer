Rudgea grandifructa

Rudgea grandifructa, commonly known as the giant fruit tree, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family native to the rainforests of South America.

Its leaves are typically broad and opposite, with a smooth texture and a strong, aromatic scent.

Rudgea grandifructa thrives in humid, shaded environments and plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
