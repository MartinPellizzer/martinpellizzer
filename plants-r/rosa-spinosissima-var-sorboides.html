Rosa spinosissima var. sorboides

Rosa spinosissima var. sorboides, commonly known as the Sorboides rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative hedge.
