Rosa charbonneaui

Rosa charbonneaui, commonly known as the charbonneau rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a silvery underside, which helps reflect sunlight in hot climates.

Rosa charbonneaui is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to challenging growing conditions.
