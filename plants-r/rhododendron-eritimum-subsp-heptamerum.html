Rhododendron eritimum subsp. heptamerum

Rhododendron eritimum subsp.

heptamerum, commonly known as the Eritrean rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Eritrea. It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and mountainous regions, thriving in cool, humid climates.

The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
