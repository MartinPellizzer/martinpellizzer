Renealmia bracteosa

Renealmia bracteosa, commonly known as the pink dwarf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. Renealmia bracteosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems supporting various wildlife species.
