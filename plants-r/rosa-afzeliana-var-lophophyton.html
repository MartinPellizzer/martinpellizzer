Rosa afzeliana var. lophophyton

Rosa afzeliana var. lophophyton, commonly known as the Lophophyton rose, is a shrub-like species native to southern Africa.

It features dense, thorny stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has serrated, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

The flowers bloom in spring and are often followed by small, red-orange fruits.
