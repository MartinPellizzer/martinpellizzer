Rosa calva var. formosana

Rosa calva var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Taiwan.

It features slender, thornless stems and lanceolate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include simple, alternate leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
