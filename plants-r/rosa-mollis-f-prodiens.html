Rosa mollis f. prodiens

Rosa mollis f. prodiens, commonly known as the prodiens form of the soft rose, is a botanical variant characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and delicate pink flowers.

It is a flowering shrub native to parts of Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges, typical of the Rosa genus. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is often cultivated for both its aesthetic appeal and horticultural interest.
