Rhaponticum satzyperovii

Rhaponticum satzyperovii, commonly known as the Siberian cow-wheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

Rhaponticum satzyperovii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
