Rosa schweinfurthii

Rosa schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in spring. Its botanical characteristics include simple, alternate leaves and an upright growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
