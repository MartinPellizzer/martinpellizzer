Reseda alba var. minor

Reseda alba var. minor, commonly known as white eryngo, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Its foliage is aromatic and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
