Rosa spinosissima var. medelingensis

Rosa spinosissima var. medelingensis, commonly known as the Medelingensis rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa spinosissima species, which is native to Europe.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a compact shrub that is well-suited for garden borders or as a decorative hedge.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in temperate climates.
