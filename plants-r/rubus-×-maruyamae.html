Rubus × maruyamae

Rubus × maruyamae, commonly known as the Maruyama raspberry, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It is native to Japan and is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible berries.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in its botanical features.
