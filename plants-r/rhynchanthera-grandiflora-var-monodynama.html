Rhynchanthera grandiflora var. monodynama

Rhynchanthera grandiflora var. monodynama, commonly known as the large-flowered rhynchanthera, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically a mix of white and pink hues. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
