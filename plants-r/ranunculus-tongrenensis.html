Ranunculus tongrenensis

Ranunculus tongrenensis, commonly known as the Tongren buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and showy, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
