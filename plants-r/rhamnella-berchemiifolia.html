Rhamnella berchemiifolia

Rhamnella berchemiifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rhamnella, is a species of flowering plant in the Rhamnaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its small, opposite leaves that are often narrow and have a leathery texture. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Rhamnella berchemiifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
