Rodriguezia leochilina

Rodriguezia leochilina, commonly known as the pink-flowered Rodriguezia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has palm-like leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and ecological value, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
