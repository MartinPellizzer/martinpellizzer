Raoulia grandiflora

Raoulia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered raoulia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, with vibrant colors ranging from yellow to orange. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Raoulia grandiflora is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in local ecosystems.
