Rosa multiflora var. mokanensis

Rosa multiflora var. mokanensis, commonly known as the Mokanensis rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The varietal form is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and moderate climate conditions.
