Rhynchosia phaseoloides

Rhynchosia phaseoloides, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a tropical annual legume native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain small, round seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
