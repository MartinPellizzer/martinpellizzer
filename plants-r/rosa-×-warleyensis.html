Rosa × warleyensis

Rosa × warleyensis, commonly known as the Warley rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorned stems. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa × warleyensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
