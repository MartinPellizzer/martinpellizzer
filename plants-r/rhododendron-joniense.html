Rhododendron joniense

Rhododendron joniense, commonly known as the Joni rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, specifically found in regions of Nepal and India.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at elevations between 2,000 and 3,500 meters. The plant features large, showy flowers that are usually pink to purple in color, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blossoms.

Rhododendron joniense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the local ecosystems of its native habitat.
