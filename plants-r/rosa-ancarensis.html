Rosa ancarensis

Rosa ancarensis, commonly known as the AnCarensis rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. It is adapted to the volcanic soils and mild climate of its native habitat.

Rosa ancarensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
