Rosa moschata var. nastarana

Rosa moschata var. nastarana, commonly known as the Nastarana rose, is a cultivar characterized by its unique floral structure and fragrance.

It features double blooms with a high number of petals, often displaying a soft pink to white coloration. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is well-suited for container gardening. It produces hips that are smaller and less showy compared to other Rosa moschata varieties.

This cultivar thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.
