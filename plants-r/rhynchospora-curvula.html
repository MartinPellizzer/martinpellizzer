Rhynchospora curvula

Rhynchospora curvula, commonly known as the hooked swamp sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves.
