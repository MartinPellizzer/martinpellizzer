Ribesiodes concinnum

Ribesiodes concinnum, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late spring. Its berries are red and edible, though they are often consumed by birds.

The shrub thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and thickets.
