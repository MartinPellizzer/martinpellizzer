Rosa aunisiensis

Rosa aunisiensis, commonly known as the Aunisiensis rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tien Shan and Pamir mountain ranges. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with dense, thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa aunisiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
