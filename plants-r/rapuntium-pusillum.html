Rapuntium pusillum

Rapuntium pusillum, commonly known as the small rapunculus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, arranged in racemes. It produces small, round, green pods that contain several seeds.

Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.
