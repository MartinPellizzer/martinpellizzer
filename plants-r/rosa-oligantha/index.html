Rosa Oligantha

Rosa oligantha is a rare and delicate species of wild rose native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is known for its small, pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical gem from further decline.
