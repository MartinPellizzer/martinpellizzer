Ranunculus chaffanjonii

Ranunculus chaffanjonii, commonly known as the Chaffanjon buttercup, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this species thrives in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and a short, hairy stem.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
