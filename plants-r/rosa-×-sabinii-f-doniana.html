Rosa × sabinii f. doniana

Rosa × sabinii f. doniana, commonly known as the Doniana rose, is a floriferous hybrid rose characterized by its abundant, fragrant blooms.

It is a compact shrub with dark green, glossy foliage and thornless stems, making it suitable for garden borders or containers. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a layered, full bloom that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. This variety is prized for its disease resistance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ease of maintenance.
