Ranunculus auricomus subsp. obliquifolius

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

obliquifolius, commonly known as the zigzag buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe. It features finely divided, serrated leaves that give it a distinctive, lace-like appearance.

The plant produces showy yellow flowers with prominent, petal-like sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants with delicate foliage.
