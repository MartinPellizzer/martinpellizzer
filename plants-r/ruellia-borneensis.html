Ruellia borneensis

Ruellia borneensis, commonly known as the Borneo ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect or semi-woody growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Ruellia borneensis is often found in moist, shaded environments like forest understories and along riverbanks.
