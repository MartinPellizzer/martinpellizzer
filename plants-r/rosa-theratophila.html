Rosa theratophila

Rosa theratophila, commonly known as the silky rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Rosa theratophila is native to parts of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
