Rubus rubellus var. plantaurellensis

Rubus rubellus var. plantaurellensis, commonly known as the Pyrenean raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its reddish stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and localized species within its native habitat.
