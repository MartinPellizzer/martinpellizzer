Rugendasia

Rugendasia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Rugendasia is native to regions in Africa, South America, and parts of Asia.

It is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.
