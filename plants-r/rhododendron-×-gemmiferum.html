Rhododendron × gemmiferum

Rhododendron × gemmiferum, commonly known as the gemmiferum rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, evergreen foliage and produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a parent in many rhododendron hybrids.
