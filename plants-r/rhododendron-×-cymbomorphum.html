Rhododendron × cymbomorphum

Rhododendron × cymbomorphum, commonly known as the purpleleaf rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive purple or bronze underside. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes across temperate regions.
