Rhodothyrsus macrophyllus

Rhodothyrsus macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf rhodothyrsus, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in warm climates for its attractive foliage and floral display.
