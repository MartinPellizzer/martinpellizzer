Racosperma distans

Racosperma distans, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring deep root systems and small, waxy leaves to reduce water loss. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.

Racosperma distans is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
