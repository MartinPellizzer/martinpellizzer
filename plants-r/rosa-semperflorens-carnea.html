Rosa semperflorens carnea

Rosa semperflorens carnea, commonly known as the everblooming pink rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, often with a white center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Rosa semperflorens carnea is valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom continuously under proper care.
