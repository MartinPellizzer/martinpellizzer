Rauhia decora

Rauhia decora, commonly known as the ornamental ginger plant, is a tropical perennial that features striking, variegated foliage.

Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Rauhia decora is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces due to its aesthetic appeal.
