Rubus centiformis var. sublagerbergii

Rubus centiformis var. sublagerbergii, commonly known as the sublagerbergii raspberry, is a variant of the centiformis raspberry species.

It is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in the Andean regions of Peru and Bolivia. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits composed of multiple drupes, which are typically red when ripe. It has compound leaves with serrated margins and thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in local horticulture.
