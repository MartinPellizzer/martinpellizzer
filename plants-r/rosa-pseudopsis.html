Rosa pseudopsis

Rosa pseudopsis, commonly known as the Japanese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, thornless stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Rosa pseudopsis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
