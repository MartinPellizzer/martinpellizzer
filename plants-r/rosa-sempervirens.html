Rosa sempervirens

Rosa sempervirens, commonly known as evergreen roses, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant produces fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa sempervirens is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
