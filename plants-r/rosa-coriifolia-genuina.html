Rosa coriifolia genuina

Rosa coriifolia genuina, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and a woody stem covered in thorns. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable in native habitats.
