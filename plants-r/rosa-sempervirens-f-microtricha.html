Rosa sempervirens f. microtricha

Rosa sempervirens f. microtricha, commonly known as the miniature evergreen rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with dense, dark green foliage that remains evergreen throughout the year.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit and is well-suited for container gardening or as a decorative hedge. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, slightly serrated edge.

This cultivar is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
