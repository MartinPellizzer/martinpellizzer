Rubia trachyantha

Rubia trachyantha, commonly known as the yellow woodrush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Rubia trachyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
