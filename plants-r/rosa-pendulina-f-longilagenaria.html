Rosa pendulina f. longilagenaria

Rosa pendulina f. longilagenaria, commonly known as the long-stemmed hanging rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa pendulina species.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems that drape gracefully, making it ideal for hanging baskets or trellises. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blossoms.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance, thriving in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.
