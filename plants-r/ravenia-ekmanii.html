Ravenia ekmanii

Ravenia ekmanii, commonly known as the Natal ravenia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
