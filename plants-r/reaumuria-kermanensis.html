Reaumuria kermanensis

Reaumuria kermanensis, commonly known as Kerman's reaumuria, is a low-growing shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Ephedraceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and small, scale-like leaves. Reaumuria kermanensis plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
