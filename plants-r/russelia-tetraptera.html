Russelia tetraptera

Russelia tetraptera, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Russelia tetraptera is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
