Rhododendron degronianum var. yakushimanum

Rhododendron degronianum var. yakushimanum, commonly known as the Yakushimanum Rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its showy, bell-shaped flowers. The blooms, which range from pink to white, are typically borne in clusters and are highly fragrant. This variety thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in cooler, moist climates.
