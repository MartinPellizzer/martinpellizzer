Rhododendron dabanshanense

Rhododendron dabanshanense, commonly known as the Dabanshan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
