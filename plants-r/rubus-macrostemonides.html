Rubus macrostemonides

Rubus macrostemonides, commonly known as the large-stamen raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to parts of East Asia, particularly China. The plant is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces small, edible berries.

It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
