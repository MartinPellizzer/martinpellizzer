Rosa systyla var. oblonga

Rosa systyla var. oblonga, commonly known as the oblonga rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features upright, thorny stems and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It is relatively drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
