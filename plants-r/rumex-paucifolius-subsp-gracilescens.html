Rumex paucifolius subsp. gracilescens

Rumex paucifolius subsp.

gracilescens, commonly known as slender dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows, grasslands, and along streambanks.

The plant has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, with a reddish or purple stem. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense, erect spikes, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This species is often found in open, sunny habitats and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with proper drainage.
