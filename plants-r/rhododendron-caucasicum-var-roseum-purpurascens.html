Rhododendron caucasicum var. roseum-purpurascens

Rhododendron caucasicum var. roseum-purpurascens, commonly known as the Caucasian rose-purple rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple. The plant typically grows in mountainous areas with acidic, well-drained soils. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forested regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
