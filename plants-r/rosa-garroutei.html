Rosa garroutei

Rosa garroutei, commonly known as the French rose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Rosa garroutei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sunlight to thrive.
