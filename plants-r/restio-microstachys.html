Restio microstachys

Restio microstachys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restio, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in coastal and sandy habitats, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
