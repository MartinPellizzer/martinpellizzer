Robinsonia berteroi

Robinsonia berteroi, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a distinctive yellowish-green hue. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in clusters during the spring.

The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
