Rhododendron × azaleoides odoratum

Rhododendron × azaleoides odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant azaleoides, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant, sweet fragrance, which is particularly noticeable in the spring.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
