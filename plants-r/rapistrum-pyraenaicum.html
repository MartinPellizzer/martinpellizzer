Rapistrum pyraenaicum

Rapistrum pyraenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean rapeseed, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and distinctive, elongated seed pods. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cooler climates.
