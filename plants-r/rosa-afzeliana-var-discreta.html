Rosa afzeliana var. discreta

Rosa afzeliana var. discreta, commonly known as the discrete rose, is a lesser-known variant of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Laos. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and it produces rose hips that are typically red or orange in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
