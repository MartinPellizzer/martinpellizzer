Rubus arduennensis var. erosulus

Rubus arduennensis var. erosulus, commonly known as the Arduennes bramble, is a shrub native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
