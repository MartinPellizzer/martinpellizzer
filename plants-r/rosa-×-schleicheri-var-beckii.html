Rosa × schleicheri var. beckii

Rosa × schleicheri var. beckii, commonly known as the Beck's shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is typically hardy in USDA zones 5 through 8. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The variety is noted for its disease resistance and adaptability to various soil types.
