Rosa × nicholsonii

Rosa × nicholsonii, commonly known as the Nicholson rose, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits a range of botanical characteristics.

It typically features dense, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often in clusters. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Rosaceae family.

Rosa × nicholsonii is well adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil.
