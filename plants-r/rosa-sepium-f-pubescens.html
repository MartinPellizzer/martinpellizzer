Rosa sepium f. pubescens

Rosa sepium f. pubescens, commonly known as the fuzzy rose, is a hybrid cultivar within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its dense, pubescent (hairy) foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, often with a deep center. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
