Rosa mollis subf. impallens

Rosa mollis subf.

impallens, commonly known as the soft rose or silky rose, is a sub species of the Rosa mollis species. It is characterized by its soft, silken foliage and delicate pink to white flowers.

The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
