Rhinanthus glacialis subsp. subalpinus

Rhinanthus glacialis subsp.

subalpinus, commonly known as the subalpine yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is a member of the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.
