Rosa suffulta var. relicta

Rosa suffulta var. relicta, commonly known as the relic rose, is a rare and ancient variety of the rose family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its limited distribution and historical significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
