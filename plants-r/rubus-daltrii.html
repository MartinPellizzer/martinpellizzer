Rubus daltrii

Rubus daltrii, commonly known as the Daltrii blackberry, is a species of brambles native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or scrubland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
