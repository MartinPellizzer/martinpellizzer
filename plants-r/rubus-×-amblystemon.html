Rubus × amblystemon

Rubus × amblystemon, commonly known as the blackberry bramble, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, which are typical of the Rubus genus. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as drupes, that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
