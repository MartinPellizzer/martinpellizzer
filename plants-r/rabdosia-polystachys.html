Rabdosia polystachys

Rabdosia polystachys, commonly known as the hairy leaved rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Rabdosia polystachys is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
