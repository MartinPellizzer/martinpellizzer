Rebutia tunariensis

Rebutia tunariensis, commonly known as the Tunariensis Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America, specifically Bolivia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and short, cylindrical stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Rebutia tunariensis is well adapted to arid environments, with spines that provide protection and reduce water loss.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
