Remijia roraimae var. auyantepuiensis

Remijia roraimae var. auyantepuiensis, commonly known as the Roraima orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the tepuis of the Guiana Shield.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as yellow and red. The plant thrives in the unique microclimates of these isolated tabletop mountains, where high humidity and nutrient-poor soils create a specialized habitat. Its growth is closely tied to the specific ecological conditions of the region, making it highly sensitive to environmental changes.

Botanists study this species to better understand the adaptations of plants in extreme and isolated ecosystems.
