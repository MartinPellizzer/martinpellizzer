Rosa torssandri

Rosa torssandri, commonly known as the Torssandri rose, is a shrub-like plant native to South Africa.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, which are typical features of many rose species. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

The Torssandri rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
