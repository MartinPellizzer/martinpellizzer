Rosa × Ravellae

Rosa × ravellae is a unique hybrid rose known for its striking floral display and hardy nature. This plant is a cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa bracteata, resulting in a plant that combines the best traits of both parents. It thrives in coastal environments and is highly resistant to salt spray and harsh weather conditions. The deep pink to red flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes. Due to its resilience and beauty, Rosa × ravellae has become a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and low maintenance.
