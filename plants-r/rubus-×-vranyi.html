Rubus × vranyi

Rubus × vranyi, commonly known as the Vrany blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is native to Central Europe and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the flowers are usually white or pale pink.

The berries are dark purple to black when mature and are valued for their flavor and culinary use.
