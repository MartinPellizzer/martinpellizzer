Rhaphiolepis glabrescens var. victoriensis

Rhaphiolepis glabrescens var. victoriensis, commonly known as the Victoria firethorn, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, orange-red flowers in late spring. The plant is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
