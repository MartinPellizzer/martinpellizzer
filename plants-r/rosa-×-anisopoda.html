Rosa × anisopoda

Rosa × anisopoda, commonly known as the everblooming shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and is native to the eastern United States. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × anisopoda is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
