Rubus × praelongus

Rubus × praelongus, commonly known as the long-stemmed blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in temperate climates. Its berries are typically dark purple to black, with a tart flavor.

It is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
