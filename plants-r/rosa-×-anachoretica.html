Rosa × anachoretica

Rosa × anachoretica, commonly known as the Holy Thorn, is a hybrid rose species originating from the cross between Rosa canina and Rosa gallica.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is typically shrub-like, with dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit. It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in some regions.
