Rubus × lengyelii

Rubus × lengyelii, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry-loganberry.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus loganobaccus (loganberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The shrub typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are small and white, and it produces dark purple to black berries that are larger than typical blackberries.

This hybrid is valued for its fruit quality and is often cultivated for commercial use.
