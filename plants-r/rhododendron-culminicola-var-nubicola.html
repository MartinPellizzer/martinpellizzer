Rhododendron culminicola var. nubicola

Rhododendron culminicola var. nubicola, commonly known as the alpine rhododendron, is a rare and specialized plant adapted to high-altitude environments.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in the Himalayas and adjacent areas. This variant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which helps it withstand harsh climatic conditions. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
