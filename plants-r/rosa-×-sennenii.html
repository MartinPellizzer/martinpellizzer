Rosa × sennenii

Rosa × sennenii, commonly known as the Japanese rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves.

Rosa × sennenii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscape design.
