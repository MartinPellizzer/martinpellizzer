Ranunculus schoddei

Ranunculus schoddei, commonly known as the Schodde's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tasmania.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, typically yellow or white petals. The plant produces a single, large, cup-shaped flower with numerous stamens and a central pistil. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Ranunculus schoddei is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
