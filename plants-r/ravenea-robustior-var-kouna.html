Ravenea robustior var. kouna

Ravenea robustior var. kouna, commonly known as the Kouna Ravenea, is a striking cycad-like plant native to Madagascar.

It features a stout, unbranched trunk that can grow up to several meters tall, with a fibrous, spongy bark. Its foliage consists of large, pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically pollinated by insects.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
