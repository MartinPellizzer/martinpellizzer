Rotala densiflora f. abietina

Rotala densiflora f. abietina, commonly known as the dense-flowered water lettuce, is a variant of the water lettuce species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like growth pattern and its ability to thrive in aquatic environments. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that float on the water surface, often with a bluish or silver sheen. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne on short stalks above the water.

This cultivar is popular in aquariums and water gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
