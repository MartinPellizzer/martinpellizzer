Rhododendron zhangjiajieense

Rhododendron zhangjiajieense, commonly known as the Zhangjiajie rhododendron, is a rare and unique species native to the Zhangjiajie National Forest Park in China.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to the mountainous landscape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, thriving in the cool, humid climate of the region. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its showy blossoms.

This species is of significant botanical interest due to its limited distribution and ornamental value.
