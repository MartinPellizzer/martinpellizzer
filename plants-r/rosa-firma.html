Rosa firma

Rosa firma, commonly known as the firm rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy, single or double petals. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges.

Rosa firma is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
