Rosa × gravereauxiana

Rosa × gravereauxiana, commonly known as the Gravereauxiana rose, is a hybrid shrub rose originating from France.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features dark green, glossy foliage and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × gravereauxiana is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
