Rhynchosia sennaarensis var. macrocalyx

Rhynchosia sennaarensis var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered senna, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers with prominent orange markings. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Fabaceae family.
