Rubus × albinitens

Rubus × albinitens, commonly known as the alpine raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and cool climates.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
