Rhamnus communis var. argenteovariegata

Rhamnus communis var. argenteovariegata, commonly known as the silver-variegated buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves with striking white and green variegation, which enhance its ornamental value. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but often used as ornamental fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for hedging and ornamental planting.
