Rabdosia pantadenia

Rabdosia pantadenia, commonly known as the golden rabbitbrush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive yellowish-green color. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink to purple in color.
