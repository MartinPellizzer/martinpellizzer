Rosa mediata

Rosa mediata, commonly known as the double white rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

Rosa mediata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
