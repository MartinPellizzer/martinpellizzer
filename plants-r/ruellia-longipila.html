Ruellia longipila

Ruellia longipila, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue to violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Ruellia longipila is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
