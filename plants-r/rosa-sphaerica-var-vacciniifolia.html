Rosa sphaerica var. vacciniifolia

Rosa sphaerica var. vacciniifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf rose, is a unique variant of the rose species characterized by its striking foliage.

This plant features dense, rounded growth habits with leaves that turn a vibrant red during the autumn season. Its flowers are typically small and pink, blooming in clusters along the stems. The plant is native to certain regions of China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
