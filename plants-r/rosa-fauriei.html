Rosa fauriei

Rosa fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese tea rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Rosa fauriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
