Rosa Spinosissima Var. Tuschetica

Rosa spinosissima var. tuschetica is a rare and striking variant of the rose known for its unique appearance and historical significance. This plant is characterized by its deep red petals and dense, thorny stems that give it a rugged, wild look. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in harsh, arid environments. Its name, meaning "very spiny," reflects its distinctive physical traits and defensive adaptations. Despite its challenging growing conditions, this rose is highly valued for its beauty and resilience.
