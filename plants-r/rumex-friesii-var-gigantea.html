Rumex friesii var. gigantea

Rumex friesii var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically dark green with a wavy margin. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it identifiable by its distinctive foliage and reproductive structures.
