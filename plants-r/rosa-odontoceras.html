Rosa odontoceras

Rosa odontoceras, commonly known as the toothed rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated edges that give it its common name.

The fruit is a red, hairy rose hip that contains the seeds.
