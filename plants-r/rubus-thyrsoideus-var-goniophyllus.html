Rubus thyrsoideus var. goniophyllus

Rubus thyrsoideus var. goniophyllus, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a perennial shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as raspberries, which are typically red when mature. These fruits are borne on curved pedicels and are rich in vitamins and antioxidants.

It thrives in temperate climates and is widely cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
