Rosa cinerascens var. lasioclada

Rosa cinerascens var. lasioclada, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a variant of the cinerascens rose species.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on the leaves, which give it its common name. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is known for its ornamental value. It produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive fuzzy texture.
