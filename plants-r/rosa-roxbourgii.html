Rosa roxbourgii

Rosa roxbourgii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rosa roxbourgii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
