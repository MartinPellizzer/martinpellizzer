Rhododendron lukiangense subsp. admirabile

Rhododendron lukiangense subsp.

admirabile, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southwest China. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red.

The plant is an evergreen shrub with leathery, elliptical leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
