Rosa sphaerica var. bautschensis

Rosa sphaerica var. bautschensis, commonly known as the Bautsch's globe rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its round, dense growth habit and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its unique spherical shape and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
