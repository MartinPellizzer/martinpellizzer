Rosa arvensis var. laevipes

Rosa arvensis var. laevipes, commonly known as the smooth field rose, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and simple, usually pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and have smooth margins, which distinguish it from other rose varieties. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along riverbanks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
