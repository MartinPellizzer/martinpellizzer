Restiaria rotundifolia

Restiaria rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed restiaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of round, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is considered a valuable species for ecological studies and conservation efforts.
