Rosa diennea

Rosa diennea, commonly known as the Dienne's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves and is typically found in rocky or alpine environments. Rosa diennea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

It is also notable for its adaptability to harsh climates and well-drained soils.
