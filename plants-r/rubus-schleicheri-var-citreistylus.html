Rubus schleicheri var. citreistylus

Rubus schleicheri var. citreistylus, commonly known as the yellow-styled blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry genus characterized by its distinctive yellow styles in the flower.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Vietnam and Indonesia. The plant is a shrub with prickly stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruit is a dark purple to black aggregate berry.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral features.
