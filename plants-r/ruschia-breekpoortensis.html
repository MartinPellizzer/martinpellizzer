Ruschia breekpoortensis

Ruschia breekpoortensis, commonly known as the Breekpoort Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The leaves are typically fleshy and cylindrical, often with a bluish or purplish hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as a drought-tolerant ornamental.
