Renealmia platycolea

Renealmia platycolea, commonly known as the flat-stemmed renealmia, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.
