Rumex × gemlikensis

Rumex × gemlikensis, commonly known as the Gemlik dock, is a hybrid species within the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a distinctive yellowish or reddish bloom. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is native to regions in Turkey and is often found in moist, calcareous soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
