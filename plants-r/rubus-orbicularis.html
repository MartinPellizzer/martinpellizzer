Rubus orbicularis

Rubus orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaved blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular (rounded) leaves and produces small, dark berries. The plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia and thrives in temperate climates. It is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with thorny stems and dense foliage.

The berries are typically harvested in late summer and are used in culinary applications.
