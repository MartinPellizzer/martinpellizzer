Rhododendron argyrophyllum subsp. omeiense

Rhododendron argyrophyllum subsp.

omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China. It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance in its natural habitat.

The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in early spring.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural settings.
