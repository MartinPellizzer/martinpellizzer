Rosa micrantha f. macracantha

Rosa micrantha f. macracantha, commonly known as the large-flowered shrub rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, thornless stems and produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. Its foliage consists of dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
