Rubus eous

Rubus eous, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits called drupes, which are typically bright red when mature. These fruits are rich in antioxidants and are widely used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Rubus eous thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.
