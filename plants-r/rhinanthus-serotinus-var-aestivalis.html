Rhinanthus serotinus var. aestivalis

Rhinanthus serotinus var. aestivalis, commonly known as the summer yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on other plants for water and nutrients through its root system. Its ecological importance lies in its role as a nectar source for pollinators like bees and hoverflies.
