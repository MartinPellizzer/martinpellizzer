Rosa Yunnanensis

Rosa yunnanensis is a rare and beautiful species of rose native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan province in China. Known for its striking pink to red flowers and fragrant scent, this plant is highly valued in both horticulture and traditional medicine. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found growing in forested areas at high altitudes. The plant's unique appearance and medicinal properties have made it a subject of interest for botanists and herbalists alike. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Rosa yunnanensis is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
