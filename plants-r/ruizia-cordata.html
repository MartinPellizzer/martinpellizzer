Ruizia cordata

Ruizia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf ruizia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Ruizia cordata is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
