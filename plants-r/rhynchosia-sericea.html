Rhynchosia sericea

Rhynchosia sericea, commonly known as silky cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and delicate, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. Rhynchosia sericea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
