Rosa × margittaiana var. revirescens

Rosa × margittaiana var. revirescens, commonly known as the Reviving Margitta rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse blooming habit, producing large, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
