Rubus ellipticifrons

Rubus ellipticifrons, commonly known as the ellipticifrons raspberry.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. Its leaves are elliptical in shape and have a serrated margin.

It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in cloud forests.
