Richterago

Richterago, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with the flowers appearing in late summer to early autumn.
