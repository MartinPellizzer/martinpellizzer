Ranunculus × segretii

Ranunculus × segretii, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive central tuft of stamens. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with simple, alternate, and usually hairy leaves. Its flowers bloom in early summer and are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens.

The species is part of the Ranunculaceae family, known for its diverse and often striking floral displays.
