Rhododendron herzogii var. occidentale

Rhododendron herzogii var. occidentale, commonly known as the western Himalayan rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of western Nepal and northern India.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
