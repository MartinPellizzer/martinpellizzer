Rhododendron × standishii

Rhododendron × standishii, commonly known as the Standish rhododendron, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rhododendron catawbiense and Rhododendron maximum.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
