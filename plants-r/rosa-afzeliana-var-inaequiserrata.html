Rosa afzeliana var. inaequiserrata

Rosa afzeliana var. inaequiserrata, commonly known as the serrated rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins, which are more pronounced on the upper side, giving it its specific epithet. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Rosa genus.
