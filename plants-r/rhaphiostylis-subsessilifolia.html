Rhaphiostylis subsessilifolia

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, sword-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.
