Rosa mollis f. acleistodonta

Rosa mollis f. acleistodonta, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. It produces fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
