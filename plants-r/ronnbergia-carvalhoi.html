Ronnbergia carvalhoi

Ronnbergia carvalhoi, commonly known as the Carvalho's spider orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, thin leaves and delicate, white flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is typically found in shaded, humid environments. Its growth habit is terrestrial, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
