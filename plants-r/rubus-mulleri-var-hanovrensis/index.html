Rubus Mulleri Var. Hanovrensis

Rubus mulleri var. hanovrensis is a lesser-known species of raspberry native to eastern asia. it belongs to the bramble family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, edible berries. this variety is often confused with other rubus species due to its similar appearance. despite its modest size, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat. further study is needed to fully understand its role in local ecosystems.
