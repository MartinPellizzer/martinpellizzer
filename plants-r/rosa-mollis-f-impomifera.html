Rosa mollis f. impomifera

Rosa mollis f. impomifera, commonly known as the soft rose or silky rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, which is native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with multiple stems and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

This cultivar is valued for its delicate appearance and is often used in floral arrangements and as a decorative plant.
