Ruschia maxwellii

Ruschia maxwellii, commonly known as Maxwell's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
