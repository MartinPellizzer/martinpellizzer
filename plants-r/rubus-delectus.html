Rubus delectus

Rubus delectus, commonly known as the delicious raspberry, is a species of bramble within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of tiny, bright red drupes that are highly aromatic and flavorful. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its edible berries and ornamental value in gardens.
