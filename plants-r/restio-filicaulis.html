Restio filicaulis

Restio filicaulis, commonly known as the fern-like restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that resemble fern fronds, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters.

Restio filicaulis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
