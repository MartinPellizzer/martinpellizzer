Railliardia × thyrsiflora

Railliardia × thyrsiflora, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Railliardia and Thyrsiflora.

It is native to regions in South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Railliardia × thyrsiflora requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
