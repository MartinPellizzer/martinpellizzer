Rubus hebecaulis

Rubus hebecaulis, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of South America. The plant features arching stems with thorns and compound leaves that are typically trifoliate. Its flowers are small and white, often appearing in clusters, and it produces small, edible berries that are similar to blackberries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
