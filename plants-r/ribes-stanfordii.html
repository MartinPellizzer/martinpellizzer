Ribes stanfordii

Ribes stanfordii, commonly known as Stanford gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various species of flowering plants and berries. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges, while its flowers are usually pink or white.

The fruit, a small, yellowish berry, is edible and often used in jams and preserves.
