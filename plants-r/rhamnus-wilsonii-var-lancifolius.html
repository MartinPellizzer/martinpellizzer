Rhamnus wilsonii var. lancifolius

Rhamnus wilsonii var. lancifolius, commonly known as Wilson's buckthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its simple, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by elliptical, dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
