Rhododendron × norbitonense var. broughtonianum

Rhododendron × norbitonense var. broughtonianum, commonly known as the Broughton's rhododendron, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to violet. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partially shaded to sunny conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Rhododendron genus.
