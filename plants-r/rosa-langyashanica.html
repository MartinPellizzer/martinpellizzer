Rosa langyashanica

Rosa langyashanica, commonly known as the Langya Mountain rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are simple and serrated, with a glossy green surface.

Rosa langyashanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
