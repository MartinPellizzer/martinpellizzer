Rosa villosa f. obtusata

Rosa villosa f. obtusata, commonly known as the fuzzy rose or smoothleaf rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa villosa species.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a delicate, fragrant aroma. This variety is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of climates.
