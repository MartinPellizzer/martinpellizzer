Rosa × harmsiana

Rosa × harmsiana, commonly known as the Harms rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is native to South Africa and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

Rosa × harmsiana prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscape design.
