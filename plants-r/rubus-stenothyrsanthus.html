Rubus stenothyrsanthus

Rubus stenothyrsanthus, commonly known as the narrow-thorned raspberry.

It is a species of bramble within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Its botanical features make it an important subject in studies of plant morphology and ecology.
