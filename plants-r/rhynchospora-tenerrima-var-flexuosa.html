Rhynchospora tenerrima var. flexuosa

Rhynchospora tenerrima var. flexuosa, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy habitats in North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically occur in dense clusters. The plant thrives in saturated soils and is often found in bogs, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often curved or flexuous, giving it its varietal name *flexuosa*.

Rhynchospora tenerrima var. flexuosa plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
