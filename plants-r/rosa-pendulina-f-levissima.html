Rosa pendulina f. levissima

Rosa pendulina f. levissima, commonly known as the hanging rose or the weeping rose, is a unique cultivar characterized by its cascading, drooping branches.

This plant is a flowering shrub that belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with a delicate appearance that adds charm to gardens. The foliage is dark green and provides a striking contrast to the blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
