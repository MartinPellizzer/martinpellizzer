Rourea zenkeri

Rourea zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's rourea, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Rourea zenkeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
