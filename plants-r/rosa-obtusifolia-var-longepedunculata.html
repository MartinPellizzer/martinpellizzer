Rosa obtusifolia var. longepedunculata

Rosa obtusifolia var. longepedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncled shrub rose, is a variant of the shrub rose species native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
