Rhododendron heliolepis var. oporinum

Rhododendron heliolepis var. oporinum, commonly known as the Oporinum rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist climates and well-drained acidic soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable regions.
