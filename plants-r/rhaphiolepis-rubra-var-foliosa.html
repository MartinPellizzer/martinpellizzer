Rhaphiolepis rubra var. foliosa

Rhaphiolepis rubra var. foliosa, commonly known as the glossy abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that turns reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it suitable for hedges or containers.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
