Reissantia Setulosa

Coral tree, scientifically known as Reissantia setulosa, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa. It is celebrated for its vibrant coral-pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The tree's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces around the world. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive. Its ornamental value is matched only by its adaptability to various climates.
