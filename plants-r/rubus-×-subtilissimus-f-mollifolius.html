Rubus × subtilissimus f. mollifolius

Rubus × subtilissimus f. mollifolius, commonly known as the soft-leaved blackberry.

This variant is a hybrid within the Rubus genus, characterized by its finely textured, velvety leaves. It produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
