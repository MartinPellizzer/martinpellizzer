Rosa × dumetorum var. schultesii

Rosa × dumetorum var. schultesii, commonly known as the Schultes' shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a compact, thorny shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive floral display.
