Rebutia einsteinii f. schmiedcheniana

Rebutia einsteinii f. schmiedcheniana, commonly known as the Schmiedcheniana Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a dense rosette of short, cylindrical stems that are typically greenish-gray with a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, erect pedicels. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

The varietal form f. schmiedcheniana is distinguished by its unique coloration and growth habit compared to the typical form of Rebutia einsteinii.
