Rhododendron metternichii var. pentamerum

Rhododendron metternichii var. pentamerum, commonly known as the Pentamerous Rhododendron, is a unique variant of the species native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which provides a lush green backdrop for its striking floral display. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with five petals, which is a distinguishing feature of the 'pentamerous' variety. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
