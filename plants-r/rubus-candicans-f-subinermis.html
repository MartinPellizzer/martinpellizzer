Rubus candicans f. subinermis

Rubus candicans f. subinermis, commonly known as the silky raspberry.

This variant of the raspberry plant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive, almost velvety appearance. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe, though they may vary in color depending on the cultivar. The plant is typically found in moist, shady environments, such as woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a unique and attractive species within the Rubus genus.
