Ruschia tetrasepala

Ruschia tetrasepala, commonly known as the four-lobed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
