Rhamnus globosa var. meyeri

Rhamnus globosa var. meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's globe thorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, thorn-covered stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, reddish berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

The species is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
