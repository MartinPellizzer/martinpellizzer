Rhodophiala montana

Rhodophiala montana, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Rhodophiala montana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
