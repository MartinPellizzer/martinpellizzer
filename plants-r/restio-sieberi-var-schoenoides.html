Restio sieberi var. schoenoides

Restio sieberi var. schoenoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in coastal or inland areas.
