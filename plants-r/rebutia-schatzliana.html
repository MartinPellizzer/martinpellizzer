Rebutia schatzliana

Rebutia schatzliana, commonly known as the Schatzliana Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered in a layer of white, waxy bloom. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stem. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
