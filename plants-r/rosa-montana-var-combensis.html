Rosa montana var. combensis

Rosa montana var. combensis, commonly known as the Combensian rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and abundant, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically produces semi-double to full blooms with a pleasant, sweet scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
