Rotanga

Rotanga, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and are often used as a decorative element in homes and gardens. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Rotanga is also known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.
