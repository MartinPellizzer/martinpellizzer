Rosa lactescens

Rosa lactescens, commonly known as the milk rose, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub-like plant with woody stems and evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a milky sap that gives it its common name. Rosa lactescens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
