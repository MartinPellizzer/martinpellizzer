Rhododendron schadenbergii

Rhododendron schadenbergii, commonly known as the Schadenberg rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, specifically in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are usually pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron schadenbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
