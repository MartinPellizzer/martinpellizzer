Rapanea nitida

Rapanea nitida, commonly known as the slender leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Rapanea nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
