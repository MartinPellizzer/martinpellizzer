Ranunculus laetus var. leipoensis

Ranunculus laetus var. leipoensis, commonly known as the Leipoenese buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features showy, yellow flowers with five to eight petals, typically arranged in a cup-like formation. The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are alternate along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
