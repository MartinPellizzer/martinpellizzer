Rhynchosia lewisii

Rhynchosia lewisii, commonly known as Lewis's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil through its root nodules. It produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rhynchosia lewisii is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
