Rothia lanata

Rothia lanata, commonly known as the woolly rothia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rothia lanata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and visual appeal.
