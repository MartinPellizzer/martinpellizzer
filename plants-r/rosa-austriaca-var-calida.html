Rosa austriaca var. calida

Rosa austriaca var. calida, commonly known as the Austrian rose variety Calida, is a compact shrub that produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and thrives in cooler, temperate climates. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a strong, upright growth habit. It is relatively disease-resistant and suitable for small gardens or container planting.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
