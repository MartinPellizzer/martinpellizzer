Rubus entomodontos

Rubus entomodontos, commonly known as the hairy raspberry.

It is a species of bramble in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, red berries that are typically edible, though they may have a tart flavor. Its leaves are palmately compound, with serrated edges and a hairy texture.

Rubus entomodontos is native to temperate regions of the northern hemisphere and thrives in woodland and disturbed soils.
