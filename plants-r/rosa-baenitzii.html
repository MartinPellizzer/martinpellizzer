Rosa baenitzii

Rosa baenitzii, commonly known as the Baenitz rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Andes Mountains in South America, specifically in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems, fragrant pink to white flowers, and pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Rosa baenitzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
