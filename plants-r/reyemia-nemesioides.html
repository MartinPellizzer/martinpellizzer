Reyemia nemesioides

Reyemia nemesioides, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often with a greenish or yellowish hue.

Reyemia nemesioides is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
