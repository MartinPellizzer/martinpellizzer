Rhynchosia luschnathiana

Rhynchosia luschnathiana, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to South America.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets and produces vibrant orange to red flowers. The plant bears edible pods that contain kidney-shaped beans, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Rhynchosia luschnathiana is valued both for its ornamental appeal and agricultural significance.
