Rosa urbica var. trichoneura

Rosa urbica var. trichoneura, commonly known as the urban rose or the trichoneura rose, is a variant of the common rose native to Europe.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. The flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often found in urban environments, hence its common name.
