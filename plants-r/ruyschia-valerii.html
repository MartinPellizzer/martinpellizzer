Ruyschia valerii

Ruyschia valerii, commonly known as the silver sword or silver grass, is a striking evergreen plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of stiff, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, Ruyschia valerii is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
