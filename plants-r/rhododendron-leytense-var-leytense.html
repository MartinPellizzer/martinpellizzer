Rhododendron leytense var. leytense

Rhododendron leytense var. leytense, commonly known as the Leyte rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and acidic soil conditions. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
