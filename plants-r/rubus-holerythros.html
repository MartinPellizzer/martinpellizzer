Rubus holerythros

Rubus holerythros, commonly known as the scarlet raspberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the eastern United States, characterized by its red-orange fruits that are both ornamental and edible. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges and has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is often found in woodland edges and moist thickets, contributing to local biodiversity.
