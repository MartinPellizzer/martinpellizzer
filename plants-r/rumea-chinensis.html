Rumea chinensis

Rumea chinensis, commonly known as Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are succulent, fleshy, and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Rumea chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
