Rumex × khorasanicus

Rumex × khorasanicus, commonly known as the Khorasan dock, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It is native to Central Asia and is often found in moist, disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
