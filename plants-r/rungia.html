Rungia

Rungia, commonly known as the "siam weed," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Rubiaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. They typically grow as shrubs or small trees, often with smooth, glossy leaves. Rungia species are known for their clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The fruits are usually small, fleshy berries that contain several seeds.
