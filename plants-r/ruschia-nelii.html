Ruschia nelii

Ruschia nelii, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as part of a succulent collection due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
