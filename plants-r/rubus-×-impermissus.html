Rubus × impermissus

Rubus × impermissus, commonly known as the Himalayan blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Himalayas, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces large, dark purple to black berries that are highly valued for their flavor and nutritional content. It is a vigorous shrub with thorny stems and trifoliate leaves, often growing in dense thickets.

Its botanical characteristics make it both an important wild plant and a cultivated species in various regions.
