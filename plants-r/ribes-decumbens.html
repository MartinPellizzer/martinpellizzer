Ribes decumbens

Ribes decumbens, commonly known as creeping gooseberry, is a low-growing shrub native to North America.

It features prostrate stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its berries are typically red or orange, containing numerous seeds.

Ribes decumbens is often found in moist, open habitats such as streambanks and woodlands.
