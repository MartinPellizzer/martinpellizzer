Rytigynia Rubiginosa Subsp. Cymigera

Rytigynia rubiginosa subsp. cymigera is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Rytigyniaceae family. Native to the rainforests of northern South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its striking red and orange flowers make it a visually appealing addition to botanical gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and rarely cultivated outside its natural habitat. Understanding this species can enhance our appreciation for the diversity of plant life in the tropics.
