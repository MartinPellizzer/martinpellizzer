Ruellia achimeniflorum

Ruellia achimeniflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Ruellia achimeniflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
