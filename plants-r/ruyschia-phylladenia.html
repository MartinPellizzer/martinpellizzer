Ruyschia phylladenia

Ruyschia phylladenia, commonly known as the velvetleaf ruyschia, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Ruyschia phylladenia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
