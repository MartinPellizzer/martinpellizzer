Radermachera pinnata var. glabra

Radermachera pinnata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leafed radermachera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
