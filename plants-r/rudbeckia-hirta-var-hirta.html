Rudbeckia hirta var. hirta

Rudbeckia hirta var. hirta, commonly known as the yellow coneflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in coarse, hairy texture. The plant produces showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central brown disk, which is typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are alternate, rough to the touch, and often have serrated edges.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
