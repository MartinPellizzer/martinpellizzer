Richardia rosea

Richardia rosea, commonly known as the roseleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Richardia rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
