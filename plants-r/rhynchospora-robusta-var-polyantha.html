Rhynchospora robusta var. polyantha

Rhynchospora robusta var. polyantha, commonly known as the showy spikegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

The leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a waxy coating, which helps the plant retain moisture in its moist environment.
