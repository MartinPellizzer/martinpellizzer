Romulea pylia

Romulea pylia, commonly known as the spring bell, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically blooms in spring and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. Romulea pylia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and compact growth habit.
