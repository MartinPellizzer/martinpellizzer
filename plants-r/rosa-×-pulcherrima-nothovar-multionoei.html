Rosa × pulcherrima nothovar. multionoei

Rosa × pulcherrima nothovar. multionoei, commonly known as the multionoei rose, is a hybrid cultivar prized for its vibrant, showy blooms.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping and as a cut flower due to its ornamental value and long-lasting blooms.
