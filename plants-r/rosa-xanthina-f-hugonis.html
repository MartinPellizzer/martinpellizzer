Rosa xanthina f. hugonis

Rosa xanthina f. hugonis, commonly known as the Hugoni rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to China.

It features dense, thornless stems and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged in odd-pinnate patterns, adding to its ornamental value. This variety is prized for its disease resistance and adaptability to various soil types.

It is often used in landscaping for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
