Rabdosia fangii

Rabdosia fangii, commonly known as Fang's Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
