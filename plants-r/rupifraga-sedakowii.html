Rupifraga sedakowii

Rupifraga sedakowii, commonly known as the Kyzylkum sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are succulent and adapted to store water, which helps it survive in dry conditions.
