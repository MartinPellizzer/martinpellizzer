Rosa × schleicheri var. typica

Rosa × schleicheri var. typica, commonly known as the Schleicher's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically produces thorny stems and has dark green, serrated leaves. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa × schleicheri var. typica prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
