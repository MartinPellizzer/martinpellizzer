Ruizia triflora

Ruizia triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered ruizia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. Ruizia triflora thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden spaces.

Its unique foliage and elegant flowering make it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
