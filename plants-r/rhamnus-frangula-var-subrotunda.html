Rhamnus frangula var. subrotunda

Rhamnus frangula var. subrotunda, commonly known as the smooth-fruited buckthorn, is a variant of the European buckthorn.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are typically smooth on both sides. Its small, black berries are produced in clusters and are an important food source for birds.

The bark is smooth and dark reddish-brown, often peeling in thin strips.
