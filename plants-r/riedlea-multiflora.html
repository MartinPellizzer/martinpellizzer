Riedlea multiflora

Riedlea multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered riedlea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Riedlea multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
