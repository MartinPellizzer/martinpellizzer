Rosa serafinii f. veridica

Rosa serafinii f. veridica, commonly known as the Serafini rose, is a rare and ornamental plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It is native to specific regions in Asia and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
