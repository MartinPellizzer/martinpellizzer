Rosa × mucatscheviensis

Rosa × mucatscheviensis, commonly known as the Caucasian rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dark green, serrated leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters and has a dense, bushy habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its beauty and scent.
