Rourea martiana

Rourea martiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and usually have five petals.

Rourea martiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
