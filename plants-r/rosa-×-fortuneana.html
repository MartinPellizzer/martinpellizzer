Rosa × fortuneana

Rosa × fortuneana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from China.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink or white flowers. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval-shaped with serrated edges.

Rosa × fortuneana is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types and climates.
