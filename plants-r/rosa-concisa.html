Rosa concisa

Rosa concisa, commonly known as the compact rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub-like plant with small, tightly clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh, arid conditions. Rosa concisa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth habit.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rocky or dry soil environments.
