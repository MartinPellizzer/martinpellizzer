Romulea gigantea

Romulea gigantea, commonly known as the giant romulea, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, showy inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces clusters of vivid orange or yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in exotic flora.
