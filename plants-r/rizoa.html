Rizoa

Rizoa, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Rizoa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
