Rapanea laetevirens

Rapanea laetevirens, commonly known as the bright green holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that remain vibrant throughout the year. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries.
