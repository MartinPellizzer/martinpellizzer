Rubus × robustissimus

Rubus × robustissimus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It is native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States and thrives in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
