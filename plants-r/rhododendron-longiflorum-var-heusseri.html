Rhododendron longiflorum var. heusseri

Rhododendron longiflorum var. heusseri, commonly known as the Heusser's rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, leathery foliage with dark green, elliptical leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
