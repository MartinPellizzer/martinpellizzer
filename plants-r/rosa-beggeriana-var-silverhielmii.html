Rosa beggeriana var. silverhielmii

Rosa beggeriana var. silverhielmii, commonly known as the silver-leaved rock rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance and helps it reflect sunlight in its harsh alpine environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners interested in cultivating plants from arid and mountainous regions.
