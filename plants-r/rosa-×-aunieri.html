Rosa × aunieri

Rosa × aunieri, commonly known as the Aunieri rose, is a hybrid shrub rose originating from crosses between Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep crimson. The plant typically produces semi-double to fully double petals, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and encourage repeat blooming.

Rosa × aunieri is also valued for its historical use in perfumery and its ability to attract pollinators.
