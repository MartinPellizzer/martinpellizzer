Rapanea matensis

Rapanea matensis, commonly known as the Malagasy laurustin, is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark blue or black berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
