Rabdosia flexicaulis

Rabdosia flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or blue. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties.

Rabdosia flexicaulis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
