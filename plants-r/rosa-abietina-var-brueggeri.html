Rosa abietina var. brueggeri

Rosa abietina var. brueggeri, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and alpine settings.
