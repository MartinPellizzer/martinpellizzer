Rosa minutifolia f. albiflora

Rosa minutifolia f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered miniature rose, is a compact, flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are predominantly white, with some cultivars displaying pale pink or cream hues. The plant has slender, thornless stems and small, dark green leaves with finely serrated edges. It blooms repeatedly throughout the growing season, producing clusters of delicate blooms that attract pollinators.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens, containers, or as a border plant due to its ornamental value and compact size.
