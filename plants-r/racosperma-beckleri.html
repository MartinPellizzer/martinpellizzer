Racosperma beckleri

Racosperma beckleri, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the acacia genus.
