Rhododendron shanii

Rhododendron shanii, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in cool and moist environments. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. Rhododendron shanii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
