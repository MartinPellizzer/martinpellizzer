Rosa arguteserrata

Rosa arguteserrata, commonly known as the serrated rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of China and is characterized by its distinctive serrated leaf margins and fragrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and produces rose hips that are used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental gardening and ecological studies.
