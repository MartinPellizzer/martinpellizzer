Ruellia macrophylla var. lutea

Ruellia macrophylla var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow-flowered ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
