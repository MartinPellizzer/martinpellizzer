Ranunculus lenormandii proles lutarius

Ranunculus lenormandii proles lutarius, commonly known as the Lutarian buttercup, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow to orange flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.
