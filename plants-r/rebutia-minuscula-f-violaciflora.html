Rebutia minuscula f. violaciflora

Rebutia minuscula f. violaciflora, commonly known as the violet-flowered Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features small, rounded stems that are typically green with a slightly woolly texture. The plant produces clusters of delicate, violet-pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is often covered in a fine layer of white to pale blue hairs, giving it a soft appearance.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a well-draining soil environment.
