Rinorea javanica

Rinorea javanica, commonly known as the Java rinorea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are small, white or pale blue, and are arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Rinorea javanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
