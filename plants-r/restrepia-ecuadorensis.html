Restrepia ecuadorensis

Restrepia ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian restrepia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the dry season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
