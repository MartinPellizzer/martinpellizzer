Rubus komoriensis

Rubus komoriensis, commonly known as the Komor raspberry, is a species of bramble native to the Japanese islands of Okinawa and the Ryukyu Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe, similar to other raspberry species. Its flowers are white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Rubus genus.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
