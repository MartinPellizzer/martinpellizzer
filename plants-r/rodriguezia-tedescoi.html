Rodriguezia tedescoi

Rodriguezia tedescoi, commonly known as the Tedesco's rodriguezia, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter, giving it a dramatic and ornamental appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in warm, moist environments with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its unique foliage and floral display, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
