Rubus × insignior

Rubus × insignior, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus idaeus (raspberry), resulting in a plant with unique genetic traits. The plant produces aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color, with a tart and aromatic flavor. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This hybrid is valued for its culinary uses and ornamental value in gardens.
