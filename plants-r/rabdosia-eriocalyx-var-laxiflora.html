Rabdosia eriocalyx var. laxiflora

Rabdosia eriocalyx var. laxiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
