Roettlera racemosa

Roettlera racemosa, commonly known as the slender raceme.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Roettlera racemosa is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
