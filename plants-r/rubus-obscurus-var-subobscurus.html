Rubus obscurus var. subobscurus

Rubus obscurus var. subobscurus, commonly known as the dark bramble or subdark bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit, a red, raspberry-like berry, is edible but often has a tart flavor.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.
