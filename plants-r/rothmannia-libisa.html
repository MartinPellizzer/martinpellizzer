Rothmannia libisa

Rothmannia libisa, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The flowers, which bloom in summer, are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive red or purple lip.
