Robiquetia ramosii

Robiquetia ramosii, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
