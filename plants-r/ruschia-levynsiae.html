Ruschia levynsiae

Ruschia levynsiae, commonly known as the Levyns' Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
