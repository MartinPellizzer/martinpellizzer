Reinwardtiodendron celebicum

Reinwardtiodendron celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes tallowtree, is a flowering plant native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and its role in the local ecosystems of its native habitat.
