Rubus alterniflorus var. gymnostachys

Rubus alterniflorus var. gymnostachys, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems, typical of the Rubus genus. It produces bright red berries that are often used in culinary applications. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its leaves are alternate, with serrated edges, and it blooms with white to pale pink flowers in the spring.
