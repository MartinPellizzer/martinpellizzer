Rubus schleicheri var. ovatipetalus

Rubus schleicheri var. ovatipetalus, commonly known as the broad-petaled bramble, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with broad, ovate petals that distinguish this variety from others in the species. It produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
