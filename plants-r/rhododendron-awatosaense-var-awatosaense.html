Rhododendron awatosaense var. awatosaense

Rhododendron awatosaense var. awatosaense, commonly known as the Awatosa endemic rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
