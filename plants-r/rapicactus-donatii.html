Rapicactus donatii

Rapicactus donatii, commonly known as the Donatii's cactus, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a compact, globular cactus with a thick, ribbed stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features prominent areoles from which spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
