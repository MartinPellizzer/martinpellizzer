Rousseauxia Aurata

Rousseauxia aurata is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant golden blooms. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike. The plant's name honors the French botanist Jean-Baptiste Rousseaux, who first documented it. Despite its beauty, Rousseauxia aurata remains elusive and is rarely found in cultivation.
