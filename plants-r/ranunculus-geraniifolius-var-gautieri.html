Ranunculus geraniifolius var. gautieri

Ranunculus geraniifolius var. gautieri, commonly known as the Gautier's buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five to seven petals, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has trifoliate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
