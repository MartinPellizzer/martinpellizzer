Rosa sepium var. stipularis

Rosa sepium var. stipularis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species originating from China.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves with prominent stipules that are often mistaken for leaves. It is well-suited for cultivation in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Rosa sepium var. stipularis is also notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its resistance to common rose diseases.
