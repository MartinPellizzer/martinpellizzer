Rhododendron forrestii var. tumescens

Rhododendron forrestii var. tumescens, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high elevations. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
