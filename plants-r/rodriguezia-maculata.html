Rodriguezia maculata

Rodriguezia maculata, commonly known as the spotted rodriguezia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with distinctive red or purple spots. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
