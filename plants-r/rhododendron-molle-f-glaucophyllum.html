Rhododendron molle f. glaucophyllum

Rhododendron molle f. glaucophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf azalea, is a flowering plant characterized by its distinctive glaucous foliage.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in a waxy, bluish-white coating, giving it a unique appearance.
