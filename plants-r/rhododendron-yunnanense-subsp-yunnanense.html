Rhododendron yunnanense subsp. yunnanense

Rhododendron yunnanense subsp.

yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink.

The plant typically grows in cool, humid climates at elevations between 2,000 and 3,500 meters. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the forest understory.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
