Rhododendron lateritium

Rhododendron lateritium, commonly known as the alpine rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in China and the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron lateritium is adapted to cold climates and requires well-drained soil to thrive.
