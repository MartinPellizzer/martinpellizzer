Rubus radula f. robustior

Rubus radula f. robustior, commonly known as the robust raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its larger, more robust fruit.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of multiple drupelets, which are typically red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for cultivation in temperate climates.

Its hardy nature and flavorful berries make it valuable for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
