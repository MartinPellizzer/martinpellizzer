Ropalon

Ropalon, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Ropalon is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.
