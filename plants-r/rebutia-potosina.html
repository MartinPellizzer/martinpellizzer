Rebutia potosina

Rebutia potosina, commonly known as the Potosi moon cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its flowers, which can be yellow, orange, or red, bloom in the spring and are often striking in appearance.

Rebutia potosina is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
