Ruschia peersii

Ruschia peersii, commonly known as the silver leaf ruischia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, dense rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that help it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
