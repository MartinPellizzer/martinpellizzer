Ranunculus rutenbergii

Ranunculus rutenbergii, commonly known as the Rutenberg buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features palmately divided leaves and produces small, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
