Rhodamnia siamensis

Rhodamnia siamensis, commonly known as the Siam rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers.
