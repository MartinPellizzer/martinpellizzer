Rosa tomentosa f. sandomiriensis

Rosa tomentosa f. sandomiriensis, commonly known as the Sandomiriensis rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa tomentosa species.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly gray foliage and fragrant, pink to reddish-pink flowers. The plant is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
