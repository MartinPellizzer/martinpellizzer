Rhaphidophora brevispathacea

Rhaphidophora brevispathacea, commonly known as the short-veined arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves with short, parallel veins. Rhaphidophora brevispathacea is also valued for its starchy roots, which are used in traditional cooking in some regions.
