Rumex helolapathum

Rumex helolapathum, commonly known as the narrowleaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes, stream banks, and floodplains. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib and a waxy coating. Its flowers are small, greenish, and arranged in clusters, and it produces oval, dark brown seeds.

Rumex helolapathum is often found in disturbed soils and can tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
