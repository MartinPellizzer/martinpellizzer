Rosa sepium var. intermedia

Rosa sepium var. intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin, and its stems are often thorny. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The variety intermedia is known for its compact growth habit and adaptability to various climates.
