Ruellia radicans

Ruellia radicans, commonly known as spreading ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a trailing or climbing herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ruellia radicans is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it both a valuable garden plant and a potential weed.
