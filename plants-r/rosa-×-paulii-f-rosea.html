Rosa × paulii f. rosea

Rosa × paulii f. rosea, commonly known as the pink hybrid rose, is a cultivar that exhibits a range of botanical features.

It is a flowering shrub characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant blooming habit. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, often with a layered appearance. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × paulii f. rosea is well-suited for gardens and landscapes, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
