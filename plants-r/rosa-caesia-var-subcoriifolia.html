Rosa caesia var. subcoriifolia

Rosa caesia var. subcoriifolia, commonly known as the silky rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a silky, felt-like texture on both sides, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
