Rhaphis elongata

Rhaphis elongata, commonly known as the silver sword plant, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, known for its striking, sword-shaped leaves.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Rhaphis elongata is relatively low maintenance and can thrive in a variety of indoor environments with proper care.
