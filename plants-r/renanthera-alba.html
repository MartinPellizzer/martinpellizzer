Renanthera alba

Renanthera alba, commonly known as the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Renanthera alba is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
