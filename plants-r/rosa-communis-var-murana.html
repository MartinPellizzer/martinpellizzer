Rosa communis var. murana

Rosa communis var. murana, commonly known as the Murana rose, is a cultivar prized for its compact growth habit and abundant flowering.

It features semi-double to full blooms with a rich pink hue, often displaying a gradient from deep red at the base to lighter pink at the edges. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet, making it suitable for borders, containers, or as a shrub. Its foliage is dark green and aromatic, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
