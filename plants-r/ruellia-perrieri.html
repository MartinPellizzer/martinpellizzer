Ruellia perrieri

Ruellia perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the Caribbean.

Ruellia perrieri thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, preferring warm, humid climates.
