Retama monosperma var. webbii

Retama monosperma var. webbii, commonly known as the Webb's retama, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves with pinnate arrangement. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain a single seed, which is a defining feature of the species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The botanical features of this plant make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
