Rosa × giennensis

Rosa × giennensis, commonly known as the Giennensis rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant is native to southern Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features include thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves.
