Rosa cinerascens var. intricata

Rosa cinerascens var. intricata, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features finely cut, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and xeriscaping.
