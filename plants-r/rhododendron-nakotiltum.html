Rhododendron nakotiltum

Rhododendron nakotiltum, commonly known as the Nakotiltum rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayan region of Nepal.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in mountainous areas with high altitude and cool, moist climates. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the local flora of its native habitat.
