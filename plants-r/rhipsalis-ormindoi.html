Rhipsalis ormindoi

Rhipsalis ormindoi, commonly known as the Brazilian mistletoe cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a climbing cactus with slender, flexible stems that can trail or climb along supports. Rhipsalis ormindoi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
