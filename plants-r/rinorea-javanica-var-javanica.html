Rinorea javanica var. javanica

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal racemes. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
