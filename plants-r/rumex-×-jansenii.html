Rumex × jansenii

Rumex × jansenii, commonly known as the hybrid sorrel, is a perennial plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rumex acetosa (common sorrel) and Rumex acetobipenis (false sorrel). The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a reddish coloration on the veins, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
