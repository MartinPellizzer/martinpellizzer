Rosa heimerlii

Rosa heimerlii, commonly known as the Heimerli rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit.

Rosa heimerlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
