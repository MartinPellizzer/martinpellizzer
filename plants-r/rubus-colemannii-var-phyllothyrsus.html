Rubus colemannii var. phyllothyrsus

Rubus colemannii var. phyllothyrsus, commonly known as the Evergreen Blackberry, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of California.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and evergreen foliage, which remains green throughout the year. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are typically harvested in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a distinctive silvery underside.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal climates.
