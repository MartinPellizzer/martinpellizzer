Rubroshorea Flaviflora

Rubroshorea flaviflora is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique red-brown bark, it stands out among its botanical relatives. This species is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists due to its ornamental value and ecological significance. Unfortunately, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
