Rubus × duffourii

Rubus × duffourii, commonly known as the Himalayan blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
