Rhynchosia discolor var. lanceolata

Rhynchosia discolor var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces distinctive yellow or orange flowers followed by burr-like seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
