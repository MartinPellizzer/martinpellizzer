Rubus carpinifolius var. lentiginosus

Rubus carpinifolius var. lentiginosus, commonly known as the blackberry or raspberry variant, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that ripen to a dark purple or black color. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variant is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
