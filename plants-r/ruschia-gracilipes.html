Ruschia gracilipes

Ruschia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, often during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
