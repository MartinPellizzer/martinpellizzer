Rosa euratiana

Rosa euratiana, commonly known as the Eurasian rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. Its flowers bloom in the spring and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Rosa euratiana is also valued for its historical use in perfumery and traditional medicine.
