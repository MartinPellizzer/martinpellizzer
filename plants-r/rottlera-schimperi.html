Rottlera schimperi

Rottlera schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian star anise, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

Rottlera schimperi is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
