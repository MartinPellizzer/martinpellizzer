Rosa micrantha f. heteracantha

Rosa micrantha f. heteracantha, commonly known as the heteracantha rose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with thorny stems and alternate, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink, which bloom in the spring. Its botanical characteristics include simple, ovate leaves and hips that are usually red or orange when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
