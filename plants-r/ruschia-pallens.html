Ruschia pallens

Ruschia pallens, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery white coating, which gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
