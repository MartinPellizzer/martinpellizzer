Rosa mollissima f. geisingensis

Rosa mollissima f. geisingensis, commonly known as the Geisingen rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa mollissima species.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-hairy foliage and small, pale pink to white flowers. This form is native to Central Europe and is often found in woodland and shaded areas. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively low maintenance.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
