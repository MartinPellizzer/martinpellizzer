Rubus × utschii

Rubus × utschii, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically a mix of red and black in color, similar to its parent species. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
