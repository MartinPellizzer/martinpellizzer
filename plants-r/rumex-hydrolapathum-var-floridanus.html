Rumex hydrolapathum var. floridanus

Rumex hydrolapathum var. floridanus, commonly known as Florida sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants characterized by their distinctive leaf shapes and often reddish stems. Its botanical structure supports its role in wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and birds.
