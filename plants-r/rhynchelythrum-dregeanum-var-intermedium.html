Rhynchelythrum dregeanum var. intermedium

Rhynchelythrum dregeanum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate rhynchelythrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks. The plant features slender, erect stems with alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and are typically pink or purple in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
