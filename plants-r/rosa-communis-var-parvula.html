Rosa communis var. parvula

Rosa communis var. parvula, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a compact cultivar of the rose family.

It features small, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has a bushy growth habit with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces due to its compact size.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
