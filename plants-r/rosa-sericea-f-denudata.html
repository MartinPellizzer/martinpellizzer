Rosa sericea f. denudata

Rosa sericea f. denudata, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa sericea species.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on the leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to parts of Asia, particularly China, and thrives in temperate climates. It produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens with well-drained soil.
