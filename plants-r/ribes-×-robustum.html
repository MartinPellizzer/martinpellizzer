Ribes × robustum

Ribes × robustum, commonly known as the hybrid gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Grossulariaceae family.

It is a cross between Ribes sanguineum (red gooseberry) and Ribes rubrum (white gooseberry), resulting in a plant with distinctive ornamental features. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with serrated edges, and the plant produces clusters of small, edible berries that range in color from red to yellow. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and support local wildlife.
