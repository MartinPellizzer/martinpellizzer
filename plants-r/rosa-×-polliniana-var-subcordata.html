Rosa × polliniana var. subcordata

Rosa × polliniana var. subcordata, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant flowering, with blooms that typically appear throughout the growing season. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
