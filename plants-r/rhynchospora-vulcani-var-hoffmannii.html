Rhynchospora vulcani var. hoffmannii

Rhynchospora vulcani var. hoffmannii, commonly known as Hoffmann's swamp arrowgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it produces small, three-lobed flowers.

This variant is notable for its limited distribution and is often found in specific regions with particular hydrological conditions.
