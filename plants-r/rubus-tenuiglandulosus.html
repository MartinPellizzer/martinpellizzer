Rubus tenuiglandulosus

Rubus tenuiglandulosus, commonly known as the slender glandular raspberry, is a species of bramble native to North America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
