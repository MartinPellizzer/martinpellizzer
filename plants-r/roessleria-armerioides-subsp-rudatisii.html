Roessleria armerioides subsp. rudatisii

Roessleria armerioides subsp.

rudatisii, commonly known as the Redstem false nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky soils.

The plant features slender, reddish stems and small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spikelike inflorescences and are usually greenish-white in color.

It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
