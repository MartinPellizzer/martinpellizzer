Ranunculus × aurigeranus

Ranunculus × aurigeranus, commonly known as the golden shield, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive black center. The plant features trifoliate leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
