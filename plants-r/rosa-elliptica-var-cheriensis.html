Rosa elliptica var. cheriensis

Rosa elliptica var. cheriensis, commonly known as the Cherries Rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, thorny stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers in early summer. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for the blooms. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its visual appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
