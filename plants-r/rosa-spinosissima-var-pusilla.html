Rosa spinosissima var. pusilla

Rosa spinosissima var. pusilla, commonly known as the miniature spiny rose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for small garden spaces.
