Ruellia luzona

Ruellia luzona, commonly known as Luzon bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
