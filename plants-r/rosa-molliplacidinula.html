Rosa molliplacidinula

Rosa molliplacidinula, commonly known as the soft-leaf rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to light pink. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
