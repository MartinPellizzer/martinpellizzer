Ruehssia Engleriana

Ruehssia engleriana is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the tropical regions of South America. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in dense rainforests where it clings to tree trunks and branches. This plant is known for its striking, lance-shaped leaves and delicate, star-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its elusive habitat and limited distribution. Its conservation status is of great concern, as deforestation and habitat loss threaten its survival.
