Rosa forrestiana

Rosa forrestiana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Rosa forrestiana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens around the world.
