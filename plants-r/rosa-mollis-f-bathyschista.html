Rosa mollis f. bathyschista

Rosa mollis f. bathyschista, commonly known as the deep-rooted rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, velvety foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America and is adapted to moist, shaded environments.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in woodland gardens.
