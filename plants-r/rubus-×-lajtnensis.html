Rubus × lajtnensis

Rubus × lajtnensis, commonly known as the Lajtnen's blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, known for its distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many Rubus species. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color, and are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
