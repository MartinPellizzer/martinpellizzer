Ranunculus rotundatissimus

Ranunculus rotundatissimus, commonly known as the roundleaf buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rounded, palmate leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many other buttercup species.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
