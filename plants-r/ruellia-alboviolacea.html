Ruellia alboviolacea

Ruellia alboviolacea, commonly known as white viola ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale violet flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
