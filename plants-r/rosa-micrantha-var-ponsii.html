Rosa micrantha var. ponsii

Rosa micrantha var. ponsii, commonly known as the Ponsii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically produces small, oval leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
