Rapa Napus

Rapa napus is a hardy plant known for its resilience in harsh environments. It thrives in coastal areas and can withstand strong winds and salty air. This species is often used in landscaping due to its ability to prevent soil erosion. Its foliage is typically silver or gray, giving it a distinctive appearance. Rapa napus plays an important role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and supporting local wildlife.
