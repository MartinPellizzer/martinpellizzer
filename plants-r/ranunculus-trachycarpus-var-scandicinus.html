Ranunculus trachycarpus var. scandicinus

Ranunculus trachycarpus var. scandicinus, commonly known as the scandinavian buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe.

It features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and showy yellow flowers with prominent black centers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. It is often found in meadows, stream banks, and wetlands, contributing to the biodiversity of these habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a native, hardy plant.
