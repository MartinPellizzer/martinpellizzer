Rosa arcana

Rosa arcana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Rosa arcana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both landscape design and horticultural study.
