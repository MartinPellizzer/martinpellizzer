Rosselia

Rosselia, commonly known as the orchid.

It is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae, native to various regions in South America. Rosselia species are typically small to medium-sized orchids with intricate, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors. They are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants without being parasitic, and they thrive in humid, tropical environments.

These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated by enthusiasts for their unique and delicate blooms.
