Rosa × reversa var. ponsi

Rosa × reversa var. ponsi, commonly known as the Ponsi rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering.

It features large, fragrant flowers with a distinctive double bloom, often in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems typical of many rose varieties. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its adaptability to various soil types and climate conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in both ornamental and landscape settings.
