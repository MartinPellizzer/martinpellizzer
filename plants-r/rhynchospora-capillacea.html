Rhynchospora capillacea

Rhynchospora capillacea, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color, arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Rhynchospora capillacea thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in bogs, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.
