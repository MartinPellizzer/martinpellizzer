Rosa micro-onoei

Rosa micro-onoei, commonly known as the Japanese rose or miniature rose, is a species native to Japan.

It is a compact shrub with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
