Rawsonia transjubensis

Rawsonia transjubensis, commonly known as the Transkei rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, particularly found in the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
