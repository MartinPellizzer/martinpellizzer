Rosa singoensis

Rosa singoensis, commonly known as the Singo rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. Rosa singoensis typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
