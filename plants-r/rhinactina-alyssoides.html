Rhinactina alyssoides

Rhinactina alyssoides, commonly known as the silverleaf alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers and silver-gray foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Rhinactina alyssoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
