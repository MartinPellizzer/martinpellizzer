Rotheca nepalense

Rotheca nepalense, commonly known as Himalayan silver bush, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the family Labiatae, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in dense clusters.
