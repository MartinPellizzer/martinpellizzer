Rubus × semiripartii

Rubus × semiripartii, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus fruticosus (bramble), resulting in a plant with distinctive botanical features. The plant typically has thorny stems and trifoliate leaves, with dark green upper surfaces and pale undersides. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruit is a large, juicy, dark red to black berry.

This hybrid is valued for its edible fruit and is commonly cultivated for both commercial and ornamental purposes.
