Rosa sempervirens subvar. acutifolia

Rosa sempervirens subvar. acutifolia, commonly known as the evergreen rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that remain on the plant year-round. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. It is evergreen, making it a valuable addition to gardens in mild climates.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it suitable for hedges or container gardening.
