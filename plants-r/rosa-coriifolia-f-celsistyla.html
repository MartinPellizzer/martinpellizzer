Rosa coriifolia f. celsistyla

Rosa coriifolia f. celsistyla, commonly known as the silky rose or Chinese pink, is a botanical variant of the rose species Rosa coriifolia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fuzzy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features thornless stems and ovate, serrated leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Native to China, it thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a striking yet low-maintenance plant.
