Rosa Graveolens F. Lugdunensis

Rosa graveolens f. lugdunensis is a rare and fragrant cultivar of the rose known for its intense perfume and delicate pink blooms. This variety is prized by horticulturists and gardeners for its unique scent and ornamental value. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and partial sunlight. The plant is often used in perfumery and as a symbol of love and devotion in floral traditions. Its historical significance adds to its allure, making it a sought-after specimen in botanical collections.
