Rosa foliolosa var. alba

Rosa foliolosa var. alba, commonly known as the white leaf rose, is a variant of the foliolosa rose species.

It is characterized by its distinctive white to pale pink flowers and its unique leaf structure, which features deeply lobed foliage. This plant is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Bolivia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and delicate floral display.
