Rubus hunanensis

Rubus hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan bramble, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
