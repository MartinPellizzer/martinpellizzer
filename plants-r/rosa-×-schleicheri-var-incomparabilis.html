Rosa × schleicheri var. incomparabilis

Rosa × schleicheri var. incomparabilis, commonly known as the Schleicher's rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its large, double flowers that range in color from deep red to pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
