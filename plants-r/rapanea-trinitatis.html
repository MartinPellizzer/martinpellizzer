Rapanea trinitatis

Rapanea trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidadian evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the island of Trinidad. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
