Rhinanthus rumelicus

Rhinanthus rumelicus, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially relies on other plants for nutrients through its root system. Its ecological role is significant in promoting biodiversity by reducing the dominance of dominant grass species.
