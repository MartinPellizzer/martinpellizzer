Rosa pendulina f. malyi

Rosa pendulina f. malyi, commonly known as the Malyi weeping rose, is a unique cultivar of the rose species Rosa pendulina.

It is characterized by its pendulous, arching stems that produce clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant is native to China and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is particularly noted for its delicate foliage and graceful, flowing appearance.
