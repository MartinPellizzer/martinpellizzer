Rosa coriifolia var. anceps

Rosa coriifolia var. anceps, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, thornless stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its delicate beauty.
