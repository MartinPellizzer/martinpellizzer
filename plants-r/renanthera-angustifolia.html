Renanthera angustifolia

Renanthera angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Renanthera angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
