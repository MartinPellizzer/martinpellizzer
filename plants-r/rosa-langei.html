Rosa langei

Rosa langei, commonly known as the Lange's rose, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa langei is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
