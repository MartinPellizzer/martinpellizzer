Rebutia calliantha f. hyalacantha

Rebutia calliantha f. hyalacantha, commonly known as the Hyalacantha Rebutia, is a unique succulent belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and smooth, pale green to bluish-gray stems. The plant features short, spiny stems and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. It is native to South America, specifically Brazil, and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
