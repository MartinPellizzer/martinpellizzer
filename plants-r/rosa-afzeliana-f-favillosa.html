Rosa afzeliana f. favillosa

Rosa afzeliana f. favillosa, commonly known as the favillosa rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa afzeliana species, native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thornless stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
