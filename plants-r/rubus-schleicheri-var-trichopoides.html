Rubus schleicheri var. trichopoides

Rubus schleicheri var. trichopoides, commonly known as the hairy bramble, is a variant of the bramble genus Rubus native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
