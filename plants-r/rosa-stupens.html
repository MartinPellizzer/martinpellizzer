Rosa stupens

Rosa stupens, commonly known as the stunning rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. Rosa stupens typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters.

Its foliage is aromatic and its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their beauty and fragrance.
