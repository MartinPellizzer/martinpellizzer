Rubus humistratus

Rubus humistratus, commonly known as the lowland raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically pink or white, and it produces small, edible berries.

Rubus humistratus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
