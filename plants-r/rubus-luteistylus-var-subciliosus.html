Rubus luteistylus var. subciliosus

Rubus luteistylus var. subciliosus, commonly known as the yellow-styled raspberry.

This variant is a shrub characterized by its hairy stems and yellowish styles on its flowers. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. The leaves are serrated and have a distinctive pubescent surface.

It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in forested areas.
