Rosa × pimpinellifoliocanina

Rosa × pimpinellifoliocanina, commonly known as the Canadian climbing rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Canada.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and has a distinctive, finely cut appearance.

Rosa × pimpinellifoliocanina is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness in cooler climates.
