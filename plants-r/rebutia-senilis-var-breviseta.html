Rebutia senilis var. breviseta

Rebutia senilis var. breviseta, commonly known as the short-stemmed old man cactus, is a compact, slow-growing species native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with a distinctive grayish-green color and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
