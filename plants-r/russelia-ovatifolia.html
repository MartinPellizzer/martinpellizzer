Russelia ovatifolia

Russelia ovatifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which has a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Russelia ovatifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
