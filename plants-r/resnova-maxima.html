Resnova maxima

Resnova maxima, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble flames, with vibrant orange and red hues. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Resnova maxima thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts.
