Ruschia schneideriana

Ruschia schneideriana, commonly known as the Schneider's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in dense, woolly white or silver-gray hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a slightly crinkled texture.

Ruschia schneideriana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
