Restiaria macrantha

Restiaria macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered restiaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restiaceae family and is characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance with long, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
