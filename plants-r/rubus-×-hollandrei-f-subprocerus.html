Rubus × hollandrei f. subprocerus

Rubus × hollandrei f. subprocerus, commonly known as the Dutch blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and local wildlife habitats.
