Rosa Afzeliana Subsp. Coriifolia

Coral rose, scientifically known as Rosa afzeliana subsp. coriifolia, is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to the mountainous regions of China. It is prized for its vibrant coral-pink flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and thorny stems that give it a rugged yet elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and hardiness. With its unique color and resilience, coral rose is a standout addition to any landscape.
