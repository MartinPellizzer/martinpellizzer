Ruschia karrachabensis

Ruschia karrachabensis, commonly known as the Karrachabens Mountain Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
