Rosa smoljanensis

Rosa smoljanensis, commonly known as the Smoljanovskaya rose, is a rare and ancient species native to the mountainous regions of the former Yugoslavia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is often found in rocky or forested habitats. Rosa smoljanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in traditional horticulture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
