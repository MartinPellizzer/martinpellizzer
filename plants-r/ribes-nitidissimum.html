Ribes nitidissimum

Ribes nitidissimum, commonly known as the glossy gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly China and Nepal.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are followed by edible, bright red berries in late summer. These berries are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary applications.

Ribes nitidissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and pollinators.
