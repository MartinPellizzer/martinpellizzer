Rubus Radula Var. Genevieri

Rubus radula var. genevieri is a rare and intriguing species of bramble found primarily in the mountainous regions of southern France. This variant of the common blackberry plant exhibits unique characteristics that set it apart from its relatives. Its small, dark berries are highly sought after by foragers and local communities. Despite its limited distribution, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat. Due to habitat loss and climate change, this plant is now considered endangered and requires urgent conservation efforts.
