Rosa afzeliana var. bovonnazensis

Rosa afzeliana var. bovonnazensis, commonly known as the Bovonnaz rose, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Cameroon.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Its botanical features include simple, serrated leaf margins and an upright growth habit.

This variety is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
