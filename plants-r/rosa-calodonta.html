Rosa calodonta

Rosa calodonta, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. Rosa calodonta is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
