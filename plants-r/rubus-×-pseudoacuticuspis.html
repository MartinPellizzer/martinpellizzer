Rubus × pseudoacuticuspis

Rubus × pseudoacuticuspis, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits that develop from multiple ovaria. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are alternate, palmately lobed, and have a serrated margin.

The fruit, which is a berry, is usually dark purple to black when ripe and is often used in culinary applications.
