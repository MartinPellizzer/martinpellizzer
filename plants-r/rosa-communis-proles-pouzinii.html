Rosa communis proles pouzinii

Rosa communis proles pouzinii, commonly known as the Pouzinii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
