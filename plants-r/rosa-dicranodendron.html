Rosa dicranodendron

Rosa dicranodendron, commonly known as the woolly rose, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in a fine, fuzzy layer.

Rosa dicranodendron is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
