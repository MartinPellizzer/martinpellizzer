Rhodanthe chlorocephala subsp. splendida

Rhodanthe chlorocephala subsp.

splendida, commonly known as the Splendour everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and is often found in coastal and sandy soils.

The plant is part of the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is known for its showy, long-lasting blooms. It typically grows in open habitats and is adapted to dry, well-drained conditions.
