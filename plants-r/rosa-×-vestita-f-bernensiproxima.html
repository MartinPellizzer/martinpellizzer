Rosa × vestita f. bernensiproxima

Rosa × vestita f. bernensiproxima, commonly known as the Bernensiproxima rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant flowering.

It originates from crosses between Rosa × vestita and Rosa × berensii, resulting in a plant with robust stems and dense foliage. The flowers are typically large, fragrant, and come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. This variety is prized for its disease resistance and adaptability to various garden conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
