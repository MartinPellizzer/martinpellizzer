Rosa vilmorina

Rosa vilmorina, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist climates. The plant produces showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and covered in a fine layer of downy hair.

Rosa vilmorina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
