Rubus phyllostachys f. angustifolius

Rubus phyllostachys f. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its slender, elongated leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. Its botanical features include thorny stems and a woody structure, typical of many Rubus species.

It is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value in gardens.
