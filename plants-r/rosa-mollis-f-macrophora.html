Rosa mollis f. macrophora

Rosa mollis f. macrophora, commonly known as the large-flowered Rosa mollis, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, which is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 5 cm in diameter, typically in shades of pink to red. The foliage is dense and glossy, with dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
