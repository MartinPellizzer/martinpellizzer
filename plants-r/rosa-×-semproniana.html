Rosa × semproniana

Rosa × semproniana, commonly known as the Semproniana rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to produce abundant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a layered, full bloom that attracts pollinators.

Rosa × semproniana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
