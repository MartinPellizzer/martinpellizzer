Ruellia perrieri var. triangularis

Ruellia perrieri var. triangularis, commonly known as the triangular ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its triangular-shaped leaves, which give it its specific name. The plant produces clusters of blue or purple flowers, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
