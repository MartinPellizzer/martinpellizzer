Rhododendron × kuratanum

Rhododendron × kuratanum, commonly known as the Kuratana rhododendron, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rhododendron kuratanskyi and Rhododendron agastum.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool, moist, and acidic soils. Its botanical features include thick, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

It is often found in the Himalayas and other high-altitude areas of Asia.
