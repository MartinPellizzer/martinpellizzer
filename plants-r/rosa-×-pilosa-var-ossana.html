Rosa × pilosa var. ossana

Rosa × pilosa var. ossana, commonly known as the Ossana rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa pilosa and other species.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink, often with a fragrant aroma. Its foliage is dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
