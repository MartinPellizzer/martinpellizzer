Rangaeris trilobata

Rangaeris trilobata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
