Ribes liouanum

Ribes liouanum, commonly known as Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are similar in appearance to those of the common gooseberry. These berries are rich in vitamin C and have been historically used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental and culinary value.
