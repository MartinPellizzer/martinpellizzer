Remijia riveroana

Remijia riveroana, commonly known as the Caribbean skyflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet petals and is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with small, lance-shaped leaves. It is adapted to salt spray and can thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Remijia riveroana is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
