Rubus tanakae var. subinermis

Rubus tanakae var. subinermis, commonly known as the Japanese bramble or subinermis raspberry, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Botanically, it is characterized by its hairy stems and small, white flowers that bloom in early summer.
