Rubus schleicheri var. flavipes

Rubus schleicheri var. flavipes, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed blackberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered with fine pubescence.

The flowers are white or pale pink, and the fruit is a dark purple to black berry with a sweet-tart flavor.
