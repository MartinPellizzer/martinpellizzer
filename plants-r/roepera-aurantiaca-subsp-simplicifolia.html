Roepera aurantiaca subsp. simplicifolia

Roepera aurantiaca subsp.

simplicifolia, commonly known as the orange-flowered roepera, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its bright orange flowers and simple, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, and the species is known for its vibrant coloration. Roepera aurantiaca subsp.

simplicifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
