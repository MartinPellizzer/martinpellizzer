Rebutia brunescens

Rebutia brunescens, commonly known as the dark green euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick, cylindrical stem covered in dense, spiny bracts. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically modified into spines, reducing water loss in arid environments.

Rebutia brunescens is well adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal watering to thrive.
