Rubus palmatus f. ribisifolius

Rubus palmatus f. ribisifolius, commonly known as the red-leaf raspberry, is a variant of the common raspberry plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive red foliage, which sets it apart from the typical green leaves of the species. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
