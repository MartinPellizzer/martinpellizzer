Reichenbachia paraguayensis

Reichenbachia paraguayensis, commonly known as the Paraguay arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green.

Reichenbachia paraguayensis is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
