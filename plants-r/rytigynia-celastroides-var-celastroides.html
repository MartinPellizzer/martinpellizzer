Rytigynia celastroides var. celastroides

Rytigynia celastroides var. celastroides, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits that resemble star-shaped berries. The plant has simple, opposite leaves with a glossy surface and emits a faint, aromatic scent. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruit.
