Rumex litoralis

Rumex litoralis, commonly known as sea sorrel, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to maritime environments.

Rumex litoralis is often found growing in tidal zones and along rocky shores.
