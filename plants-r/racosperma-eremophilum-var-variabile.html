Racosperma eremophilum var. variabile

Racosperma eremophilum var. variabile, commonly known as the variable spinifex, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry foliage and small, pale green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is highly drought-tolerant.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various native wildlife species.
