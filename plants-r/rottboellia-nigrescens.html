Rottboellia nigrescens

Rottboellia nigrescens, commonly known as blackgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive potential in some areas.

Botanically, it is valued for its hardiness and adaptability in different ecosystems.
