Rhamnus lycium

Rhamnus lycium, commonly known as Chinese buckthorn, is a shrub native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and reddish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and ornamental value.

Rhamnus lycium is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
