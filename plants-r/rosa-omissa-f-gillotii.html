Rosa omissa f. gillotii

Rosa omissa f. gillotii, commonly known as the Gillotii rose, is a rare and cultivar form of the Rosa omissa species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Due to its rarity, it is often sought after by horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.
