Rhyssocarpus pubescens

Rhyssocarpus pubescens, commonly known as the hairy root orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
