Rhinorchis

Rhinorchis, commonly known as the orchid mantis, is a unique plant that combines the features of an orchid and a mantis.

It is a type of orchid that has evolved to resemble a praying mantis, a trait that helps it blend into its environment. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and green, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of insect wings.

Rhinorchis is primarily grown as a decorative plant due to its unusual appearance and rarity.
