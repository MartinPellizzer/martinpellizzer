Rosa micrantha var. danubialis

Rosa micrantha var. danubialis, commonly known as the Danube rose, is a shrub native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and fragrant blooms.
