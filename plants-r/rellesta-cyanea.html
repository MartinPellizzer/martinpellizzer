Rellesta cyanea

Rellesta cyanea, commonly known as the blue bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Rellesta cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with high altitude conditions.
