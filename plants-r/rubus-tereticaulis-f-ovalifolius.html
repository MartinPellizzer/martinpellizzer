Rubus tereticaulis f. ovalifolius

Rubus tereticaulis f. ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaf raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its distinctive oval-shaped leaves.

This plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to regions of North America. It produces small, red berries that are typically consumed fresh or used in culinary applications. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
