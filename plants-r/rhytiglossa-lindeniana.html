Rhytiglossa lindeniana

Rhytiglossa lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns.

Rhytiglossa lindeniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
