Rotala minuta

Rotala minuta, commonly known as the dwarf rotala, is a small aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It has delicate, waxy leaves that are typically bright green and often have a slight red or pink hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Rotala minuta is popular in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
