Ronnbergia deleonii

Ronnbergia deleonii, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that feature a bright red lip and a white or pale pink dorsal sepal. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Ronnbergia deleonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
