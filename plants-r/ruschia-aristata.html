Ruschia aristata

Ruschia aristata, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for container gardening.
