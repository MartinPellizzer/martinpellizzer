Rosa lutea f. bessei

Rosa lutea f. bessei, commonly known as the yellow rose of Besse, is a cultivar of the species Rosa lutea, characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers.

It is a compact, bushy shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are borne singly or in clusters and have a strong, sweet fragrance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
