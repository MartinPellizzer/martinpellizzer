Rebutia kariusiana

Rebutia kariusiana, commonly known as the Kariusian rebutia, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
