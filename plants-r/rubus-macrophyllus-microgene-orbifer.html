Rubus macrophyllus microgene orbifer

Rubus macrophyllus microgene orbifer, commonly known as the giant raspberry.

This plant is a shrub characterized by its large, broad leaves and thorny stems. It produces aggregate fruits composed of multiple drupes, which are typically red when ripe. The species is native to eastern Asia and thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Rosaceae family.
