Rhodiola sangpo-tibetana

Rhodiola sangpo-tibetana, commonly known as the Tibetan rose.

It is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, particularly in Tibet and Nepal. The plant features fleshy, reddish roots and has small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. It is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Rhodiola sangpo-tibetana is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine.
