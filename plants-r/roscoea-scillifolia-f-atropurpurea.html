Roscoea scillifolia f. atropurpurea

Roscoea scillifolia f. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered roscoea, is a striking rhizomatous herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It features vibrant, showy inflorescences with pale yellow to orange-red flowers surrounded by broad, purple bracts. The foliage consists of large, elliptical leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is prized for its unique and colorful display.

This cultivar is a popular choice among gardeners seeking to add a tropical touch to woodland gardens.
