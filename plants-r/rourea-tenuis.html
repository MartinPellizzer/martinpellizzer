Rourea tenuis

Rourea tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually narrow and elliptical in shape. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

Rourea tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
