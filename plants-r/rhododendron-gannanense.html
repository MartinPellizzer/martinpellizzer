Rhododendron gannanense

Rhododendron gannanense, commonly known as the Gannan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant pink or red flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
