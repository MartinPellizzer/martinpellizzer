Rhodomyrtus verecunda

Rhodomyrtus verecunda, commonly known as the broadleaf rhodomyrtus, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, fragrant pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Rhodomyrtus verecunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Indigenous practices.
