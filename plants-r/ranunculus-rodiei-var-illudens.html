Ranunculus rodiei var. illudens

Ranunculus rodiei var. illudens, commonly known as the golden shield or yellow shield, is a striking member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish center. The plant features palmate leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in cool climates.
