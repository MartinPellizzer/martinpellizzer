Rhododendron glischrum var. adenosum

Rhododendron glischrum var. adenosum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from pink to crimson, often with a distinctive white or pale throat. The plant typically grows in high-altitude forests and alpine meadows, thriving in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitats.
