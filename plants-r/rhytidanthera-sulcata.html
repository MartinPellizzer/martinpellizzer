Rhytidanthera sulcata

Rhytidanthera sulcata, commonly known as the sulcate sandstone lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water.
