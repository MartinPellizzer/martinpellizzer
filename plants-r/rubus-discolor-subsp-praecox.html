Rubus discolor subsp. praecox

Rubus discolor subsp.

praecox, commonly known as the early red raspberry, is a shrub native to eastern North America. It is characterized by its biennial growth habit, with flowering occurring on the previous year's growth.

The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and open thickets.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
