Rosa coriifolia subvar. lucida

Rosa coriifolia subvar. lucida, commonly known as the shining Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It produces red, hairy fruits that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in partial shade to full sun.
