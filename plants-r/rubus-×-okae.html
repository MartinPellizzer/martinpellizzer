Rubus × okae

Rubus × okae, commonly known as the Japanese blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its dark purple to black fruits. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that are popular in culinary applications.

The botanical features of Rubus × okae make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
