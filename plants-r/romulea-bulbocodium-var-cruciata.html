Romulea bulbocodium var. cruciata

Romulea bulbocodium var. cruciata, commonly known as the starry romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a purple tinge. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its delicate appearance and unique floral structure.
