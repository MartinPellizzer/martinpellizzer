Rubus schizostylus

Rubus schizostylus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically consumed by wildlife. It has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
