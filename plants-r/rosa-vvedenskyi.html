Rosa vvedenskyi

Rosa vvedenskyi, commonly known as the Altai rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are covered in thorny projections.

Rosa vvedenskyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also studied for its potential in horticultural and ecological applications.
