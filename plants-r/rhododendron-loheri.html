Rhododendron loheri

Rhododendron loheri, commonly known as the Loher's rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, cool environments. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are usually dark green and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from pink to red, blooming in early spring.

Rhododendron loheri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
