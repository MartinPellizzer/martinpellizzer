Ravenia rosea

Ravenia rosea, commonly known as the rose apple tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its ornamental flowers, which are typically pink or white and have a fragrant scent. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to apples but have a tangy, sweet flavor. Ravenia rosea thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.

Its leaves are glossy and dark green, contributing to its visual appeal.
