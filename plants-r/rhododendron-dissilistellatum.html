Rhododendron dissilistellatum

Rhododendron dissilistellatum, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy, and it produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white.
