Rosa × verticillacantha

Rosa × verticillacantha, commonly known as the upright climbing rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × verticillacantha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
