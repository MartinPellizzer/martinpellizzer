Rosa × housei

Rosa × housei, commonly known as the Japanese rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It features fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
