Rosa driouxii

Rosa driouxii, commonly known as the Drioux rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and China. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a striking contrast to the blooms.

Rosa driouxii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
