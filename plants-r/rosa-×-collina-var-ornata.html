Rosa × collina var. ornata

Rosa × collina var. ornata, commonly known as the ornate hill rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and production of fragrant, semi-double to double pink or white flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It is well-suited for garden cultivation, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
