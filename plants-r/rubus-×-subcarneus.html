Rubus × subcarneus

Rubus × subcarneus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces edible berries that are typically red when mature, though some varieties may have yellow or orange fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its fruit and ornamental value.

Rubus × subcarneus is known for its vigorous growth and ability to spread through runners.
