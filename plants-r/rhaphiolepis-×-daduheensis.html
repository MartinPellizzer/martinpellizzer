Rhaphiolepis × daduheensis

Rhaphiolepis × daduheensis, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features small, bright orange to red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant white flowers in the spring. It is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
