Ruschia namusmontana

Ruschia namusmontana, commonly known as the Namibian Ruschia, is a flowering plant native to the Namib Desert.

It is a low-growing succulent with fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
