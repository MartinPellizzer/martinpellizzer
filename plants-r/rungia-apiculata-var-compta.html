Rungia apiculata var. compta

Rungia apiculata var. compta, commonly known as the small-leaved rungia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
