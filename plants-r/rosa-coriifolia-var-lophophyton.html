Rosa coriifolia var. lophophyton

Rosa coriifolia var. lophophyton, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers in spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice in gardens and for floral arrangements.
