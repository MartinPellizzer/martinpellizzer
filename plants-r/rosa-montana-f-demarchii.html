Rosa montana f. demarchii

Rosa montana f. demarchii, commonly known as the Demarchi's mountain rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, thornless stems and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, providing an attractive foliage contrast. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and hardy nature.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of growing conditions.
