Rebutia wessneriana subsp. beryllioides

Rebutia wessneriana subsp.

beryllioides, commonly known as the Berylline Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America. It features a spherical or cylindrical stem with a smooth, pale green to bluish-gray surface.

The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its small size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
