Ranunculus × prietoi

Ranunculus × prietoi, commonly known as the hybrid buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, double-layered flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it produces a cluster of small, yellow stamens in the center of the flower.

Ranunculus × prietoi is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
