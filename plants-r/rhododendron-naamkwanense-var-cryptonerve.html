Rhododendron naamkwanense var. cryptonerve

Rhododendron naamkwanense var. cryptonerve, commonly known as the Namkwan cryptonerve rhododendron, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats, often in forest understories where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive venation pattern, which is a key identifying feature.
