Rosa × andegavensis f. macranthoides

Rosa × andegavensis f. macranthoides, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red in color. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
