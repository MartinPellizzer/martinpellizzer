Rabdosia bulleyana var. foliosa

Rabdosia bulleyana var. foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, often hairy leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical variety. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in dense spike-like clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
