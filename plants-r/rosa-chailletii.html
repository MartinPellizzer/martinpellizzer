Rosa chailletii

Rosa chailletii, commonly known as the Chaillet rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tian Shan and Pamir Mountains. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves, and it is known for its hardiness in cold climates.

Rosa chailletii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in horticulture.
