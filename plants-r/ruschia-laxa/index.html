Ruschia Laxa

Ruschia laxa is a strikingly beautiful succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its unique appearance and easy care make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. With its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms, Ruschia laxa is both ornamental and hardy.
