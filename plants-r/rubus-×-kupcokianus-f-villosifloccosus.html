Rubus × kupcokianus f. villosifloccosus

Rubus × kupcokianus f. villosifloccosus, commonly known as the woolly floccose blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with a hairy texture. The flowers are small and typically white or pink, with a woolly appearance due to the dense pubescence. The fruit is aAggregate berry, usually dark purple to black when mature.

This plant is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
