Rubia tenuis

Rubia tenuis, commonly known as slender woodruff, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside.
