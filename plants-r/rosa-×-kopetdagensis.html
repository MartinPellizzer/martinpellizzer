Rosa × kopetdagensis

Rosa × kopetdagensis, commonly known as the Kopetdag rose, is a hybrid species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Rosa × kopetdagensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
