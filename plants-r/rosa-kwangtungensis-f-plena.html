Rosa kwangtungensis f. plena

Rosa kwangtungensis f. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered Kwangtung rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa kwangtungensis.

It is characterized by its abundant, double blooms that consist of numerous petals, giving it a full, velvety appearance. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This rose is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
