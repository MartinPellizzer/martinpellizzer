Rhododendron imperator

Rhododendron imperator, commonly known as the Emperor rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive spurred shape.

Rhododendron imperator thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
