Rubus latifrons var. aciculatus

Rubus latifrons var. aciculatus, commonly known as the slender blackberry, is a variant of the broad-fruited blackberry species.

It is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces small, dark berries that are edible.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit.
