Ribes menziesii var. amarum

Ribes menziesii var. amarum, commonly known as bittercurrant, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The species is valued for its ecological role in supporting wildlife and its ornamental potential in gardens.
