Rosa juzepczukiana

Rosa juzepczukiana, commonly known as the Juzepczuk's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly Kazakhstan and Russia. This shrub typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
