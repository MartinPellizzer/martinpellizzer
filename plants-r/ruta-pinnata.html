Ruta pinnata

Ruta pinnata, commonly known as the creeping rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, green, trifoliate leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Ruta pinnata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
