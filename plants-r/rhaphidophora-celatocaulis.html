Rhaphidophora celatocaulis

Rhaphidophora celatocaulis, commonly known as the hidden-stem arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a short, underground stem.
