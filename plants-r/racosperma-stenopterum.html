Racosperma stenopterum

Racosperma stenopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in revegetation projects.

Its seeds are edible and have been utilized by Indigenous Australians for food and medicinal purposes.
