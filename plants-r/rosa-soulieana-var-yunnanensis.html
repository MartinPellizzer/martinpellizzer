Rosa soulieana var. yunnanensis

Rosa soulieana var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various climates.
