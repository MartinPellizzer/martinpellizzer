Ribes rubrum var. glabellum

Ribes rubrum var. glabellum, commonly known as the smooth red currant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or pink in color, which ripen in late summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is notable for its smooth, hairless stems and its ornamental value in gardens.
