Ritterocactus langsdorfii subsp. multiceps

Ritterocactus langsdorfii subsp.

multiceps, commonly known as the multiceps ritterocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
