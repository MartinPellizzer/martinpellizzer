Rubus cyclophyllus f. subumbrosus

Rubus cyclophyllus f. subumbrosus, commonly known as the shaded raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of certain ecosystems.
