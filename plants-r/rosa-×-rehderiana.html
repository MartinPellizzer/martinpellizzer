Rosa × rehderiana

Rosa × rehderiana, commonly known as the Rehder's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to China. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × rehderiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
