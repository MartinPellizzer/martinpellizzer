Rosa livescens var. genuina

Rosa livescens var. genuina, commonly known as the Alpine rose or the alpine pink, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is well adapted to cold climates and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
