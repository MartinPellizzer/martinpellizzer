Rhynchosia alluaudii

Rhynchosia alluaudii, commonly known as the African flame bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It produces striking red or orange flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Rhynchosia alluaudii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
