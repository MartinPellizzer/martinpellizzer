Rosa afzeliana var. pulchella

Rosa afzeliana var. pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful rose or the African rose, is a species of shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces thorny stems and has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
