Rosa × turbinata f. gorenkensis

Rosa × turbinata f. gorenkensis, commonly known as the Gorenkenska rose, is a hybrid species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse blooming, producing large, semi-double to double pink flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrance and ability to attract pollinators.
