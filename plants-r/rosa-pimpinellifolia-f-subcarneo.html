Rosa pimpinellifolia f. subcarneo

Rosa pimpinellifolia f. subcarneo, commonly known as the pale-flowered rock rose, is a cultivar of the shrub rose species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is dark green and leathery, providing an attractive evergreen structure.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal and arid environments.
