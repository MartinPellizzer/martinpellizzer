Rosa afzeliana var. pubescens

Rosa afzeliana var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy rose or Chinese rose, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for gardens and landscapes.
