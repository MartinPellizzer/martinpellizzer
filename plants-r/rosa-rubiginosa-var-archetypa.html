Rosa rubiginosa var. archetypa

Rosa rubiginosa var. archetypa, commonly known as the rough rose or shrub rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with serrated leaflets and a distinctive rough texture.

Rosa rubiginosa var. archetypa is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and visual appeal.
