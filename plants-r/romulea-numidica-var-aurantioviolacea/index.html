Romulea Numidica Var. Aurantioviolacea

Orange violet, scientifically known as Romulea numidica var. aurantioviolacea, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its vibrant orange-pink flowers that bloom in spring, creating a vivid contrast against its deep green foliage. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in changing environmental conditions.
