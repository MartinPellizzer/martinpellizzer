Ruttya bernieri

Ruttya bernieri, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually small, leathery, and arranged in a compact manner.

Ruttya bernieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
