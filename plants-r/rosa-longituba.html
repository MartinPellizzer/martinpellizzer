Rosa longituba

Rosa longituba, commonly known as the long-stemmed rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant produces elliptical leaves with serrated edges and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. Rosa longituba is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both horticultural collections and ecological studies.
