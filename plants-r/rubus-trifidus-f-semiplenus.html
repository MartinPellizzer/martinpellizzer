Rubus trifidus f. semiplenus

Rubus trifidus f. semiplenus, commonly known as the semi-plenum blackberry, is a variant of the trifid blackberry.

It is a perennial shrub native to the temperate regions of the Americas. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with three leaflets. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces small, dark purple berries.

This form is notable for its compact growth and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
