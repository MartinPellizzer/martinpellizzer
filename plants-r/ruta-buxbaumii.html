Ruta buxbaumii

Ruta buxbaumii, commonly known as the Mexican rue, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and aromatic, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round, orange fruits. Ruta buxbaumii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.

Its adaptability to dry climates makes it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
