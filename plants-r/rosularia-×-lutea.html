Rosularia × lutea

Rosularia × lutea, commonly known as the yellow rose of Sharon, is a hybrid shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dense, upright growth with leathery, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Rosularia × lutea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
