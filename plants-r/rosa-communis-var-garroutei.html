Rosa communis var. garroutei

Rosa communis var. garroutei, commonly known as the Garroutei rose, is a cultivar renowned for its large, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white.

It is a deciduous shrub native to China, characterized by its thorny stems and dark green foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with a compact and bushy habit. Its flowers are borne on long pedicels and are often used in ornamental gardening for their beauty and scent.

This variety is well-suited to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
