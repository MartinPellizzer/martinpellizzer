Rosa hieronymii

Rosa hieronymii, commonly known as the Hieronymus rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico and Guatemala. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color, with five to seven petals. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Rosa hieronymii is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
