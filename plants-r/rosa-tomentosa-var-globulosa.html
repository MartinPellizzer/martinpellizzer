Rosa tomentosa var. globulosa

Rosa tomentosa var. globulosa, commonly known as the woolly rose or globe rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, fuzzy gray-white foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often in clusters. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Rosa tomentosa var. globulosa is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
