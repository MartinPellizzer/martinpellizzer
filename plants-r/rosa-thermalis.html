Rosa thermalis

Rosa thermalis, commonly known as the thermal rose, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions with geothermal activity.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and fragrant scent, which are typical of many rose species. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa thermalis is often found near hot springs and volcanic areas, where its unique habitat contributes to its distinctive botanical features.
