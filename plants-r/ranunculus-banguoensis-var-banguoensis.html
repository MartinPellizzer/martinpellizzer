Ranunculus banguoensis var. banguoensis

Ranunculus banguoensis var. banguoensis, commonly known as the Banguo Ranunculus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features delicate, palmately divided leaves and produces small, clustered flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
