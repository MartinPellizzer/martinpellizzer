Rhynchosia elegans var. elegans

Rhynchosia elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.
