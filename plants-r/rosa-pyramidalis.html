Rosa pyramidalis

Rosa pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramid rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are red, hairy berries that mature in autumn.

Rosa pyramidalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
