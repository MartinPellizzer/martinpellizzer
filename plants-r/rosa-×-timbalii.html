Rosa × timbalii

Rosa × timbalii, commonly known as the Timbalai rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to the island of Java in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep red. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is known for its vigorous growth habit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The Timbalai rose is also valued for its use in perfumery due to the strong, sweet scent of its blooms.
