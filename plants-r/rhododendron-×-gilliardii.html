Rhododendron × gilliardii

Rhododendron × gilliardii, commonly known as the Gilliard's rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
