Rubus odoratus f. bifarius

Rubus odoratus f. bifarius, commonly known as the fragrant raspberry.

This plant is a flowering shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. The plant is native to parts of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and hedgerows.
