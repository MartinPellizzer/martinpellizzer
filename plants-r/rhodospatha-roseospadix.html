Rhodospatha roseospadix

Rhodospatha roseospadix, commonly known as the red spadix orchid, is a striking tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, red spadix surrounded by a showy, white to pink bract, creating an eye-catching floral display. Native to the humid rainforests of Central and South America, this plant thrives in warm, moist environments with high humidity. Its unique inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, greenish flowers enclosed within the spadix.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering conditions.
