Rebutia heliosa var. cajasensis

Rebutia heliosa var. cajasensis, commonly known as the Cajas Sun Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to the Andean region of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with dense, pale green to blue-gray spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are often tubular in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
