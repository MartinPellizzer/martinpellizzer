Rutidea mollis

Rutidea mollis, commonly known as the silky rutia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Rutidea mollis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appeal make it a desirable plant for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
