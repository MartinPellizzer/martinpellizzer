Rourea neglecta var. neglecta

Rourea neglecta var. neglecta, commonly known as the Neglected Rourea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for cultivation in warm, humid climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique fruit structure.
