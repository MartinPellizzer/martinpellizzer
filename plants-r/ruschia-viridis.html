Ruschia viridis

Ruschia viridis, commonly known as the green Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and vibrant foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
