Rubus × hemistenopetalus

Rubus × hemistenopetalus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces small, aggregate fruits with a distinctive red or black coloration. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its flavorful berries.

The hybrid nature of this plant results from crosses between Rubus species, contributing to its unique botanical traits.
