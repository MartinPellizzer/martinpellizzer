Rosa afzeliana var. vera

Rosa afzeliana var. vera, commonly known as the Persian rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Rosa afzeliana var. vera is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
