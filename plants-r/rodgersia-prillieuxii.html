Rodgersia prillieuxii

Rodgersia prillieuxii, commonly known as the silver lace Rodgersia, is a perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, palmately divided, silver-green leaves that provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces showy, pale pink to lavender flowers in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for adding texture and color to shaded landscapes.
