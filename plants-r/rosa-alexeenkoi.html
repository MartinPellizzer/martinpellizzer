Rosa alexeenkoi

Rosa alexeenkoi, commonly known as the Siberian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is typically found in rocky or well-drained soils. It is a cold-hardy species, capable of surviving in harsh climates with low temperatures.

Rosa alexeenkoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
