Rhododendron wiltonii

Rhododendron wiltonii, commonly known as the Wilton's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its ornamental value is highly appreciated in horticulture due to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron wiltonii is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitat.
