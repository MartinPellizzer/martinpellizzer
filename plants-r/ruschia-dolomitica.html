Ruschia dolomitica

Ruschia dolomitica, commonly known as the Dolomitica Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
