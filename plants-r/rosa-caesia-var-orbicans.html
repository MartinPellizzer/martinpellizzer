Rosa caesia var. orbicans

Rosa caesia var. orbicans, commonly known as the orbiculate rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa caesia species, which is native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, rounded flower form, which sets it apart from other rose varieties. The plant typically features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
