Rubus aequiserrulatus

Rubus aequiserrulatus, commonly known as the serrated blackberry.

It is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces edible berries that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along forest edges.

Due to its ornamental and edible qualities, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
