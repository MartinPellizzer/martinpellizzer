Roella prostrata

Roella prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate roella, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms a dense mat of foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Roella prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth habit.
