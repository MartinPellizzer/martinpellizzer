Rottboellia arabica

Rottboellia arabica, commonly known as African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a reddish hue. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Rottboellia arabica is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for landscaping purposes.
