Reicheocactus

Reicheocactus, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and a dense covering of spines, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
