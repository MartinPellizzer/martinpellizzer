Rosa × insignis

Rosa × insignis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, while its stems are thorny.

Rosa × insignis is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
