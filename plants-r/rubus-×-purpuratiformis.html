Rubus × purpuratiformis

Rubus × purpuratiformis, commonly known as the purple-leafed raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive purple foliage and red berries. It is native to regions of East Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are often used in culinary applications.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant leaf color and attractive floral display.
