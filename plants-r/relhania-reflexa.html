Relhania reflexa

Relhania reflexa, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of white, spherical flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a leathery texture with a bluish-green color.

Relhania reflexa thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
