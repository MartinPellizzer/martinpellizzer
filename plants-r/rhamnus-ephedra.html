Rhamnus ephedra

Rhamnus ephedra, commonly known as the Chinese buckthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, reddish berries that are a food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping and erosion control in dry environments.
