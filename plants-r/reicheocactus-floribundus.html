Reicheocactus floribundus

Reicheocactus floribundus, commonly known as the Showy Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne in clusters at the stem tips. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
