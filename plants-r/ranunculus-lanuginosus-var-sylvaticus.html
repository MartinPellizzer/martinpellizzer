Ranunculus lanuginosus var. sylvaticus

Ranunculus lanuginosus var. sylvaticus, commonly known as the silky buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, palmate leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a silky texture. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five to seven petals, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
