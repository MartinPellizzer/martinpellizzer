Rosa xanthina f. normalis

Rosa xanthina f. normalis, commonly known as the yellow rose of China, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically produces fragrant flowers with a strong, sweet scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and a woody stem structure typical of many rose species.
