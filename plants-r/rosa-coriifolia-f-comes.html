Rosa coriifolia f. comes

Rosa coriifolia f. comes, commonly known as the pink sand rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges, and its blooms typically appear in early spring. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The variety 'comes' is distinguished by its pale pink to white flower color, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
