Ravenea beentjei

Ravenea beentjei, commonly known as the Beentje's Ravenea, is a striking member of the Arecaceae family, native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant produces striking, feather-like inflorescences with bright yellow flowers, which are followed by orange-red fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
