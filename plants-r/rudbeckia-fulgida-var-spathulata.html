Rudbeckia fulgida var. spathulata

Rudbeckia fulgida var. spathulata, commonly known as the showy rudbeckia, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange disk flowers surrounded by broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden landscapes due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
