Rosa viscaria var. mentita

Rosa viscaria var. mentita, commonly known as the Mentita rose, is a compact, bushy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dark green, glossy foliage and produces small, fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, flowering shrub.
