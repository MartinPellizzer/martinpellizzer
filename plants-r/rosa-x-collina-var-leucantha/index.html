Rosa × Collina Var. Leucantha

Rosa × collina var. leucantha is a rare and delicate hybrid rose known for its soft white blooms and compact growth habit. Native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe, this variety thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soils. Its flowers, often described as ethereal and fragrant, make it a prized addition to gardens and floral arrangements. Despite its beauty, it is relatively uncommon in cultivation due to its specific growing requirements. This plant serves as a testament to the diversity and elegance found within the rose family.
