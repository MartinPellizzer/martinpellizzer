Rhododendron lindaueanum var. lindaueanum

Rhododendron lindaueanum var. lindaueanum, commonly known as the Lindaue's rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers are usually pink to red in color, adding vibrant visual interest to the landscape.
