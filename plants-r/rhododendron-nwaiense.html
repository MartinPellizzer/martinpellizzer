Rhododendron nwaiense

Rhododendron nwaiense, commonly known as the Nwai rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of northern Thailand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from pink to white.

Rhododendron nwaiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
