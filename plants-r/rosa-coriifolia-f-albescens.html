Rosa coriifolia f. albescens

Rosa coriifolia f. albescens, commonly known as the white form of the Chinese rose, is a compact shrub characterized by its pale pink to white flowers.

It features simple, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, fragrant aroma. The plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun. Its flowers bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and compact growth habit.
