Ravenia linearis

Ravenia linearis, commonly known as the slender ravenia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom in the spring. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it often has a spreading growth habit.

Ravenia linearis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
