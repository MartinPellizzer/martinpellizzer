Rhipsalis pulchra

Rhipsalis pulchra, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its branching, leafless stems that are covered in a dense, silvery-white layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions make it a popular houseplant.

Rhipsalis pulchra is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and well-draining soil.
