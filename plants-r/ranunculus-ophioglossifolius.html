Ranunculus ophioglossifolius

Ranunculus ophioglossifolius, commonly known as the goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and bright yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
