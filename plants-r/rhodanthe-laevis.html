Rhodanthe laevis

Rhodanthe laevis, commonly known as the smooth rhodanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, oblong seeds that are dispersed by wind.
