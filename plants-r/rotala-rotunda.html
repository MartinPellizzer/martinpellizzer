Rotala rotunda

Rotala rotunda, commonly known as the roundleaf waterwheel, is a compact aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, round leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around its stems. The plant produces tiny white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in shallow freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums and ponds.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate a range of water conditions make it a popular choice for aquatic gardening.
