Rosa caryophyllacea f. firmicaulis

Rosa caryophyllacea f. firmicaulis, commonly known as the firm-stemmed rock rose, is a flowering plant characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, upright stems.

It features small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white, often in clusters along the stems. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It is native to rocky or arid environments and is well adapted to dry conditions.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in gardens with well-draining soil.
