Rosa provisa f. pomiformis

Rosa provisa f. pomiformis, commonly known as the globe rose, is a compact, shrub-like rose with a unique rounded form.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a slight blush of red. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit and is well-suited for container gardening or as a decorative shrub. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens.
