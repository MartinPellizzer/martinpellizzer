Rourea macrocalyx

Rourea macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and thick, leathery leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It is often found in humid, lowland areas with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
