Rhynchosia lineata

Rhynchosia lineata, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a tropical legume native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. Rhynchosia lineata is valued for its nutritional content and is often used in traditional agriculture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adaptable to various soil types.
