Rubus × nessensifolius

Rubus × nessensifolius, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that belongs to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color, depending on the variety. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a mix of traits from both blackberries and raspberries.
