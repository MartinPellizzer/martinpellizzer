Rosa × guinetii

Rosa × guinetii, commonly known as the Guinet rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × guinetii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
