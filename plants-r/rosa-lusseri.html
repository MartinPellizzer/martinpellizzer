Rosa lusseri

Rosa lusseri, commonly known as the Lusseri rose, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
