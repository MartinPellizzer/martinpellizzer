Rottlera japonica

Rottlera japonica, commonly known as Japanese rottlera, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.

Rottlera japonica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
