Rhododendron sikangense

Rhododendron sikangense, commonly known as the Sichuan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool and moist environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron sikangense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
