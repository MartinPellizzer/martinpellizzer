Rosa hollandrei

Rosa hollandrei, commonly known as the Dutch rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Netherlands and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and produces fragrant blooms in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa hollandrei is well-suited for garden cultivation and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
