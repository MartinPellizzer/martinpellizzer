Rorippa rusticana

Rorippa rusticana, commonly known as the watercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist or aquatic environments. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by water.

Rorippa rusticana is valued for its peppery flavor and is often used in salads and as a garnish.
