Ribes altissimum

Ribes altissimum, commonly known as the Sitka gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have serrated edges.

Ribes altissimum is adapted to moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
