Rosa stiptophylla

Rosa stiptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to regions of China and the Himalayas, it thrives in cool, temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural studies.
