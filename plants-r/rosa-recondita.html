Rosa recondita

Rosa recondita, commonly known as the hidden rose, is a rare and delicate species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems, dark green foliage, and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are often short-lived, opening only briefly before closing, which adds to its mystique.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Rosa recondita is considered a rare and endangered species.
