Rhynchosia reticulata f. balansae

Rhynchosia reticulata f. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae passionflower, is a rare and ornamental climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and ability to fix nitrogen. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and it produces small, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds.
