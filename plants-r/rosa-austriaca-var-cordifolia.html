Rosa austriaca var. cordifolia

Rosa austriaca var. cordifolia, commonly known as the Austrian rose with heart-shaped leaves, is a cultivar of the Rosa austriaca species.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped foliage, which gives it its botanical name. The plant produces fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
