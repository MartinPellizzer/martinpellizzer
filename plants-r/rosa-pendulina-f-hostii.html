Rosa pendulina f. hostii

Rosa pendulina f. hostii, commonly known as the Hostii form of the weeping rose, is a unique cultivar characterized by its cascading, fragrant blooms.

It features long, drooping stems that produce clusters of semi-double pink flowers, which are particularly showy in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and floral display.

This cultivar is valued for its elegant appearance and is often used in landscaping to add a touch of romance and beauty.
