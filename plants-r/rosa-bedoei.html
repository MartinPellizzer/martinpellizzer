Rosa bedoei

Rosa bedoei, commonly known as the Namib Desert rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia and Angola.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to extreme drought conditions. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its harsh environment. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

Rosa bedoei is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its resilience in challenging climates.
