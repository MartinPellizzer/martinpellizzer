Rabdosia macrocalyx

Rabdosia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered Rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to tropical Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, pale purple to blue flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
