Ranunculus reflexus

Ranunculus reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has palmate leaves with deeply lobed edges, and its stems are usually hairy. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Ranunculus reflexus is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, known for its showy blooms and often toxic sap.
