Rosa taronensis

Rosa taronensis, commonly known as the Taronian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Taronian region of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.

Rosa taronensis is also noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in temperate climates.
