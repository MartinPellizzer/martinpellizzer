Rhynchanthera villosissima

Rhynchanthera villosissima, commonly known as the hairy rhynchanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and private collections.
