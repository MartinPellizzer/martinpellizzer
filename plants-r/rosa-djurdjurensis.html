Rosa djurdjurensis

Rosa djurdjurensis, commonly known as the Swedish rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Scandinavia and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in perfumery due to their strong, sweet scent.

Rosa djurdjurensis is valued for both its ornamental beauty and historical significance in botanical studies.
