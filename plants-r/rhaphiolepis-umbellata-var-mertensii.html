Rhaphiolepis umbellata var. mertensii

Rhaphiolepis umbellata var. mertensii, commonly known as the Mertens' rosebay rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It prefers well-drained, acidic soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
