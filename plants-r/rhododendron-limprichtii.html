Rhododendron limprichtii

Rhododendron limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude forests and alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist climates. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with broad, leathery leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are showy, with petals that range from pink to deep red, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron limprichtii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its striking appearance.
