Rubus scaber var. oligotrichus

Rubus scaber var. oligotrichus, commonly known as the rough-bracted raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as drupes, which are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Rubus genus.
