Rabdosia brachythyrsa

Rabdosia brachythyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pale blue to violet flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Rabdosia brachythyrsa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
