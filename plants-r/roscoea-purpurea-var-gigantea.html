Roscoea purpurea var. gigantea

Roscoea purpurea var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant purple roscoea, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, violet-pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Due to its size and vibrant blooms, it is a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded borders.
