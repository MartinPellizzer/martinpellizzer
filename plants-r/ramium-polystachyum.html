Ramium polystachyum

Ramium polystachyum, commonly known as the silverleaf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and rushes. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in spikelets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in humid climates.
