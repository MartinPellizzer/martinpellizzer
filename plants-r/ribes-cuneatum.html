Ribes cuneatum

Ribes cuneatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf currant, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves. Ribes cuneatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
