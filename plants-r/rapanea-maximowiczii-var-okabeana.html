Rapanea maximowiczii var. okabeana

Rapanea maximowiczii var. okabeana, commonly known as Okabe's boxwood, is a variant of the evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves.
