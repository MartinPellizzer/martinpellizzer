Rubus sikkimensis var. canescens

Rubus sikkimensis var. canescens, commonly known as the Sikkim raspberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
