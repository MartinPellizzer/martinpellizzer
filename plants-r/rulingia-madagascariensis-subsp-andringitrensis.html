Rulingia madagascariensis subsp. andringitrensis

Rulingia madagascariensis subsp.

andringitrensis, commonly known as the Andringitra rulingia, is a rare endemic plant found exclusively in the eastern rainforests of Madagascar. It is a woody shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces fleshy, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.
