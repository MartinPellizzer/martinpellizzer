Rhododendron latoucheae var. ionanthum

Rhododendron latoucheae var. ionanthum, commonly known as the Ionanthum rhododendron, is a rare and striking plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep pink to crimson, often with a white throat. The plant typically grows in high-altitude forests and alpine meadows, preferring cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique beauty.
