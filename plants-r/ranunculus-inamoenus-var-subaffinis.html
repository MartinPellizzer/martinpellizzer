Ranunculus inamoenus var. subaffinis

Ranunculus inamoenus var. subaffinis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
