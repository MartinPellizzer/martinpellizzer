Ruschia horrescens

Ruschia horrescens, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spiky flower spikes that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
