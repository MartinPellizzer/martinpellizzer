Rhododendron ririei

Rhododendron ririei, commonly known as the Ririe rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or forested areas. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and elliptical in shape, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron ririei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
