Rondeletia laniflora

Rondeletia laniflora, commonly known as the woolly rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Rondeletia laniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and vibrant blooms.
