Rulingia althaeifolia

Rulingia althaeifolia, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
