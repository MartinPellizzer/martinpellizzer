Rolfea Elata

Rolfea elata is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. Its unique appearance, featuring vibrant blue and purple flowers, makes it a standout among its botanical relatives. This species thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in cloud forests and mountainous terrain. Due to its limited habitat and delicate nature, Rolfea elata is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into adaptation and ecological balance in extreme climates.
