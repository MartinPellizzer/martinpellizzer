Robiquetia woodfordii

Robiquetia woodfordii, commonly known as the Woodford's lily, is a striking bulbous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, often fragrant scent. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and succulent, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, making it a valuable addition to rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
