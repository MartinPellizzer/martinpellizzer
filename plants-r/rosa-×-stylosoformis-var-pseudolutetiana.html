Rosa × stylosoformis var. pseudolutetiana

Rosa × stylosoformis var. pseudolutetiana, commonly known as the French pink rose, is a hybrid shrub native to France.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage and is known for its compact growth habit. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
