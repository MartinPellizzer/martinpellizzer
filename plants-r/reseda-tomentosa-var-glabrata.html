Reseda tomentosa var. glabrata

Reseda tomentosa var. glabrata, commonly known as silky reseda, is a herbaceous plant native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It features hairy, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence, giving it a soft, silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its stems are erect and somewhat woody at the base, with a tendency to branch profusely.

Reseda tomentosa var. glabrata is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
