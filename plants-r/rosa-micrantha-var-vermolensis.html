Rosa micrantha var. vermolensis

Rosa micrantha var. vermolensis, commonly known as the Vermolens rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin, and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in humid, shaded environments. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.

This variety is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
