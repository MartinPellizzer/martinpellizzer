Racosperma merinthophorum

Racosperma merinthophorum, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets, and it produces yellow flowers in spikes. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
