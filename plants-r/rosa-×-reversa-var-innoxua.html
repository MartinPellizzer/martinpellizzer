Rosa × reversa var. innoxua

Rosa × reversa var. innoxua, commonly known as the Alba Plena rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its double, white petals.

It is a cultivar of the Rosa × reversa, which is a cross between Rosa gallica and Rosa damascena. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and aromatic leaves. It produces fragrant flowers that bloom in spring and early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
