Russelia colombiana

Russelia colombiana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers, which are highly attractive to hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil to flourish.

Russelia colombiana is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms and ability to add a tropical flair to landscapes.
