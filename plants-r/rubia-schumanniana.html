Rubia schumanniana

Rubia schumanniana, commonly known as Schumacher's rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rubia schumanniana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
