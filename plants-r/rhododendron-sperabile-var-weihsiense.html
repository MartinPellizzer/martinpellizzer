Rhododendron sperabile var. weihsiense

Rhododendron sperabile var. weihsiense, commonly known as the Weihsiense Rhododendron, is a variant of the broadleaf rhododendron native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture, typical of many rhododendron species. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
