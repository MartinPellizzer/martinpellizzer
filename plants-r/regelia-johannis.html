Regelia johannis

Regelia johannis, commonly known as the royal regelia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its ability to produce large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are dark green and often have a slight pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Regelia johannis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
