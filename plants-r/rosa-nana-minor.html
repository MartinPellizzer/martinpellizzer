Rosa nana minor

Rosa nana minor, commonly known as the dwarf garden rose, is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet.

It features small, fragrant flowers in a range of colors including pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are covered in a fine layer of downy hair. It is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Rosa nana minor is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
