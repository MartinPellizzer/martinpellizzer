Renanthera caloptera

Renanthera caloptera, commonly known as the orchid with the pink and white flowers, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy blooms that often feature a mix of pink, white, and green hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.
