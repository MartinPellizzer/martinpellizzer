Ruschia pauciflora

Ruschia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its vibrant and often showy flowers. The plant features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often blooming in spring.

Ruschia pauciflora is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
