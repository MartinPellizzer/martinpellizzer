Rubus thyrsoideus f. rhamnifolius

Rubus thyrsoideus f. rhamnifolius, commonly known as the red raspberry leaf form, is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, which are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are usually red when ripe, similar to other raspberry species. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its edible berries.
