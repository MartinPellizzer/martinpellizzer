Rosa alpina var. turbinata

Rosa alpina var. turbinata, commonly known as the Alpine rose or alpine rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, single or semi-double pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically dark green, glossy, and arranged in an alternate pattern along the stems.

Rosa alpina var. turbinata thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous habitats.
