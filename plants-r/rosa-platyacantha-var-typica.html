Rosa platyacantha var. typica

Rosa platyacantha var. typica, commonly known as the flat-stemmed rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its hips, or fruits, are red and ornamental, often used in dried flower arrangements.

It is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
