Rubus macrostachys microgene clethrocharis

Rubus macrostachys microgene clethrocharis, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, juicy drupelets. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it is valued for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
