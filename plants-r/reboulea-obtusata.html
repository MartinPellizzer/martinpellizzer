Reboulea obtusata

Reboulea obtusata, commonly known as the broad-leaved senna, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Reboulea obtusata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
