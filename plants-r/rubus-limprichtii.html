Rubus limprichtii

Rubus limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's blackberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes various types of raspberries and blackberries. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces small, dark berries that are edible.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and less commonly cultivated species.
