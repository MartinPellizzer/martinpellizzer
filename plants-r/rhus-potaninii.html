Rhus potaninii

Rhus potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese sumac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other plants like poison ivy and cashews. Rhus potaninii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
