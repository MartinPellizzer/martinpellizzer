Romulea columnae var. occidentalis

Romulea columnae var. occidentalis, commonly known as the western columnar romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, grass-like foliage and clusters of vivid orange or yellow flowers. The plant produces small, rounded bulbs that store nutrients, allowing it to survive in its arid environment. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
