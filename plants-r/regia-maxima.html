Regia maxima

Regia maxima, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to certain regions of South America. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and can grow up to several feet in height. Its flowers are typically vibrant in color, ranging from white to deep purple, and bloom during the wet season.

Regia maxima is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
