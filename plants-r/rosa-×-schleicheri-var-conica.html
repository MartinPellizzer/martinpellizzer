Rosa × schleicheri var. conica

Rosa × schleicheri var. conica, commonly known as the conical Rosa, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, rounded form.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a velvety texture. The plant is native to Central Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun exposure. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its compact size and continuous blooming.

The variety is particularly noted for its resistance to common rose diseases, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
