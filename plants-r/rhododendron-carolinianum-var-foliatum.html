Rhododendron carolinianum var. foliatum

Rhododendron carolinianum var. foliatum, commonly known as the Carolina rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to reddish-purple flowers in the spring. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
