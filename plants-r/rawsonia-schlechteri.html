Rawsonia schlechteri

Rawsonia schlechteri, commonly known as the red-stemmed pincushion.

It is a South African shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and vibrant red stems. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
