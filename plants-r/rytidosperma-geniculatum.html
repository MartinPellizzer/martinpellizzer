Rytidosperma geniculatum

Rytidosperma geniculatum, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag or sinuous growth pattern, which gives it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Rytidosperma geniculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
