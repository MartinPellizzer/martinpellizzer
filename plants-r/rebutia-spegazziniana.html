Rebutia spegazziniana

Rebutia spegazziniana, commonly known as the Spegazzini's rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and short, cylindrical stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Rebutia spegazziniana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
