Rudgea macrophylla

Rudgea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved rudgea, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Rubiaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Rudgea macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral displays.
