Rosa spinosissima f. pyriformis

Rosa spinosissima f. pyriformis, commonly known as the pear-shaped Rosa spinosissima, is a horticultural form of the prickly rose.

It is characterized by its unique, rounded, pear-shaped blooms that are typically pink to red in color. The plant produces thorny stems and has small, serrated leaves that are typically dark green. It is a compact shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental flowers and hardy nature, making it suitable for garden use in temperate climates.
