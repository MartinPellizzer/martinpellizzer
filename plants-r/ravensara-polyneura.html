Ravensara polyneura

Ravensara polyneura, commonly known as the Tasmanian nutmeg, is a small evergreen tree native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces distinctive, three-lobed fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional Aboriginal medicine. Ravensara polyneura thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cool temperate rainforests.

Its unique scent and medicinal properties make it a valuable species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
