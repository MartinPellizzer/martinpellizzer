Rosa × dumetorum var. epilosa

Rosa × dumetorum var. epilosa, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense foliage and small, often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in alpine gardens.
