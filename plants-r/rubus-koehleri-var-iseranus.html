Rubus koehleri var. iseranus

Rubus koehleri var. iseranus, commonly known as the Iseran raspberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
