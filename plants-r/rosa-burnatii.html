Rosa burnatii

Rosa burnatii, commonly known as the Burnatii rose, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant produces hips, which are ornamental and contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Rosa burnatii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
