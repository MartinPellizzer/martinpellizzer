Rubus albisequens

Rubus albisequens, commonly known as the white-seeded raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of South America. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
