Rosa mollis f. pyrifera

Rosa mollis f. pyrifera, commonly known as the soft rose or the fuzzy rose, is a hybrid form of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy texture on the stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance in suitable growing conditions.
