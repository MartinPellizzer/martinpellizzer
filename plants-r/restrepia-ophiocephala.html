Restrepia ophiocephala

Restrepia ophiocephala, commonly known as the Ophiocephala orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in harsh environments. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a subtle fragrance.

Restrepia ophiocephala is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
