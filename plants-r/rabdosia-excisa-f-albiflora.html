Rabdosia excisa f. albiflora

Rabdosia excisa f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, clustered in whorls along the stems.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
