Rhododendron indicum var. japonicum

Rhododendron indicum var. japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and landscapes.
