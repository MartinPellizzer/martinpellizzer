Rabdosia javanica

Rabdosia javanica, commonly known as Java Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It often grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Rabdosia javanica is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
