Robiquetia micramphora

Robiquetia micramphora, commonly known as the small-leafed sand mat, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
