Rhynchostylis praemorsa

Rhynchostylis praemorsa, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or near streams. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its striking blooms.

Rhynchostylis praemorsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
