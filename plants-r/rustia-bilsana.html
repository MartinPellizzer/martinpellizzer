Rustia bilsana

Rustia bilsana, commonly known as the Bilsana rustia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Rustia bilsana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
