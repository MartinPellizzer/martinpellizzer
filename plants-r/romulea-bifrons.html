Romulea bifrons

Romulea bifrons, commonly known as the spring bell or yellow bell, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

Romulea bifrons is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
