Russelia standleyi

Russelia standleyi, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Russelia standleyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
