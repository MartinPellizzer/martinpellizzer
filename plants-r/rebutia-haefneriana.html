Rebutia haefneriana

Rebutia haefneriana, commonly known as the Crimson Moon cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and red during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
