Reaumuria przewalskii

Reaumuria przewalskii, commonly known as the Przewalski's saxaul, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is well adapted to harsh environments, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and is often found in desert ecosystems.

Its resilience makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
