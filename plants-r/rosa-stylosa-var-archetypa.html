Rosa stylosa var. archetypa

Rosa stylosa var. archetypa, commonly known as the archetypal pink bush rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of semi-double, pink flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticultural breeding.
