Rosa godetii

Rosa godetii, commonly known as the Godetii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains in South America. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa godetii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
