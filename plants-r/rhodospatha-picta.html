Rhodospatha picta

Rhodospatha picta, commonly known as the painted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often display vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features long, slender leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Rhodospatha picta is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
