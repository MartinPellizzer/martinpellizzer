Rosa micrantha f. ovata

Rosa micrantha f. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are densely arranged along its stems. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
