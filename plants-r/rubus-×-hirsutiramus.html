Rubus × hirsutiramus

Rubus × hirsutiramus, commonly known as the hairy blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically sweet and juicy. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
