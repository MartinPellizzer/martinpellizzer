Rosa communis var. firma

Rosa communis var. firma, commonly known as the firm rose, is a cultivar of the common rose, characterized by its robust growth and abundant blooming.

It features deep pink to red flowers with a strong fragrance, typically appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has a bushy habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types, provided it receives adequate sunlight and drainage.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
