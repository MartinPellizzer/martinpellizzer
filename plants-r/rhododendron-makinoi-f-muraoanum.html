Rhododendron makinoi f. muraoanum

Rhododendron makinoi f. muraoanum, commonly known as the Murao's rhododendron, is a unique hybrid variety native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which provide a lush, evergreen appearance throughout the year. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in late spring, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
