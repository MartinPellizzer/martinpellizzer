Rosa rubiginosa f. intermedia

Rosa rubiginosa f. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Blush Rose, is a hybrid form of the Rosa rubiginosa species.

It is characterized by its semi-double to double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticulture.
