Rubus × albelliformis

Rubus × albelliformis, commonly known as the alpine raspberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits, which are typically red or yellow when mature. The plant is native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and is adapted to cold, mountainous environments. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
