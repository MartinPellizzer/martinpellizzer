Ravenala agatheae

Ravenala agatheae, commonly known as the African paradise tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its palm-like appearance, with large, fan-shaped leaves that radiate from a central stem. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for landscaping and botanical gardens.
