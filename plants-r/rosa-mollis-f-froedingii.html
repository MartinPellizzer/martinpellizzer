Rosa mollis f. froedingii

Rosa mollis f. froedingii, commonly known as the Froedings' soft rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white, often with a hint of red at the base. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its delicate blooms. It is typically grown as a garden ornamental due to its ornate appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

The variety is noted for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
