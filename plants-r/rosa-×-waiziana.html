Rosa × waiziana

Rosa × waiziana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is native to China and is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering. The plant typically produces fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Rosa × waiziana is also valued for its use in perfumery due to the aromatic compounds in its flowers.
