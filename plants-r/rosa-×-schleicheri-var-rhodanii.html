Rosa × schleicheri var. rhodanii

Rosa × schleicheri var. rhodanii, commonly known as the Rhodanis rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from deep pink to red. The plant features thorny stems and serrated, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
