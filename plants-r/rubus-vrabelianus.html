Rubus vrabelianus

Rubus vrabelianus, commonly known as the Vrabel's blackberry, is a species of bramble native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically small and have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodlands and shrublands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
