Rosa syringorosea

Rosa syringorosea, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces red or orange hips, which are the fruit of the plant. Rosa syringorosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
