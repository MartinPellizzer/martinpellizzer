Rubus rosifolius f. coronarius

Rubus rosifolius f. coronarius, commonly known as the silverleaf raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its distinctive silvery-white undersides on its leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to regions of eastern Asia, including China and Japan. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. Its foliage is densely covered with fine, white hairs, giving it a unique appearance.

This form is prized for both its ornamental value and its potential in horticultural cultivation.
