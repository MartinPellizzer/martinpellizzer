Rosa communis proles ravaudii

Rosa communis proles ravaudii, commonly known as the Ravaudii rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the mountainous regions of France.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, deep pink flowers. The plant has narrow, glossy leaves and is typically resistant to common rose diseases. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent foliage and seasonal blooming pattern.
