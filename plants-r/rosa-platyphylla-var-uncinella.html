Rosa platyphylla var. uncinella

Rosa platyphylla var. uncinella, commonly known as the hooked leaf rose, is a shrub-like plant native to southern China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The leaves are distinctive for their broad, flat shape and hooked tips, which give the variety its name. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This plant is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to grow in a variety of soil conditions.
