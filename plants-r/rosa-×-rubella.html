Rosa × rubella

Rosa × rubella, commonly known as the deep red rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, deep red petals that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It produces fragrant flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening.

Rosa × rubella requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, hardy plants.
