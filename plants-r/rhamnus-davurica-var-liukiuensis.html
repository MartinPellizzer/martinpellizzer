Rhamnus davurica var. liukiuensis

Rhamnus davurica var. liukiuensis, commonly known as the Liukiu buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It typically grows in coastal and mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface and serrated edges. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, appearing in clusters, followed by small, yellowish fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
