Rochelia retorta

Rochelia retorta, commonly known as the twisted rochelia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Rochelia retorta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
