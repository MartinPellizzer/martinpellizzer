Rhododendron Stenophyllum Subsp. Angustifolium

Rhododendron stenophyllum subsp. angustifolium is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It is known for its vivid pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and dense growth habit make it a unique addition to botanical collections. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts.
