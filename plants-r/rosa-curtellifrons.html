Rosa curtellifrons

Rosa curtellifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are narrow and serrated, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for specialized gardens and botanical collections.
