Rosa micrantha f. cuneifoliolata

Rosa micrantha f. cuneifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed miniature rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are characterized by their narrow, lance-shaped leaflets, giving the plant its specific epithet. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.

This cultivar is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and delicate beauty.
