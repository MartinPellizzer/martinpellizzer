Rubus mucronifer microgene latiorifolius

Rubus mucronifer microgene latiorifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf raspberry, is a shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
