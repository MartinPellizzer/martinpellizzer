Rutidea smithii subsp. smithii

Rutidea smithii subsp.

smithii, commonly known as the Smith's rutidea, is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae. It is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, typically found in arid and semi-arid environments.
