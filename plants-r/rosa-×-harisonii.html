Rosa × harisonii

Rosa × harisonii, commonly known as the Harison's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × harisonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
