Rubus odoratus f. heteradenius

Rubus odoratus f. heteradenius, commonly known as the fragrant raspberry.

This botanical variant is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its distinctive, aromatic berries. It features thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus. The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals.

The fruit is edible, though it is often smaller and less sweet compared to other raspberry varieties.
