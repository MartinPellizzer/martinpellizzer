Rosa × tongtchouanensis

Rosa × tongtchouanensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosa.

It is native to China and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, while its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants.

Rosa × tongtchouanensis is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions.
