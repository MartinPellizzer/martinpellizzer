Rosa micrantha f. keratiophora

Rosa micrantha f. keratiophora, commonly known as the Keratiophora rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, thornless stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pale pink to white blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of serrated, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the flowers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
