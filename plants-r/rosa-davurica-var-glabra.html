Rosa davurica var. glabra

Rosa davurica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It is well adapted to temperate climates and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
