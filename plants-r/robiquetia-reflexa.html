Robiquetia reflexa

Robiquetia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Robiquetia reflexa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
