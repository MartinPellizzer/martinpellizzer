Rosa afzeliana var. paupercula

Rosa afzeliana var. paupercula, commonly known as the slender rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces rose hips that are typically red or orange, containing the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
