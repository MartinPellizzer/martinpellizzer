Rosa moschata var. yunnanensis

Rosa moschata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan musk rose, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
