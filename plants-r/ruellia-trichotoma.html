Ruellia trichotoma

Ruellia trichotoma, commonly known as the hairy ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are tubular and usually come in shades of blue or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Ruellia trichotoma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
