Rapistrum linnaeanum

Rapistrum linnaeanum, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It is often found in maritime environments, where it tolerates salt spray and strong winds.

The species is also valued for its edible leaves and seeds, which have been used in traditional cuisines.
