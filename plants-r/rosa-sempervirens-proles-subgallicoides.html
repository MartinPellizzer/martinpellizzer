Rosa sempervirens proles subgallicoides

Rosa sempervirens proles subgallicoides, commonly known as the French rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which remains lush throughout the year. The plant produces fragrant, pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its stems are covered in thorns, and it has a compact growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
