Rhododendron ledifolium var. noordtianum

Rhododendron ledifolium var. noordtianum, commonly known as the Noordt's leathery rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, humid climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
