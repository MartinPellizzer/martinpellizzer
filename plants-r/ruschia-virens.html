Ruschia virens

Ruschia virens, commonly known as the greenleaf ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia virens is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
