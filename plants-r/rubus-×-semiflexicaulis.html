Rubus × semiflexicaulis

Rubus × semiflexicaulis, commonly known as the trailing blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rubus genus, which includes various brambles and blackberries. It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to spread along the ground through stolons. The plant produces small, aggregate berries that are typically dark purple to black in color.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
