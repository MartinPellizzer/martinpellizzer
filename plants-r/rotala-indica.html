Rotala indica

Rotala indica, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Rotala indica thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in ponds and slow-moving streams.

It is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
