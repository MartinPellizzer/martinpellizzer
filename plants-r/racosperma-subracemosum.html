Racosperma subracemosum

Racosperma subracemosum, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in racemes, typical of the genus Racosperma.

It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient component of native vegetation.
