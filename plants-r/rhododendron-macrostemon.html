Rhododendron macrostemon

Rhododendron macrostemon, commonly known as the large-flowered rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive purple or red throat. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron macrostemon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
