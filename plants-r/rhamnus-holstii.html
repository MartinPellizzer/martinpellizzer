Rhamnus holstii

Rhamnus holstii, commonly known as the Holst's buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by black, egg-shaped berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Rhamnus holstii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
