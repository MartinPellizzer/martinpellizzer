Rosa spinosissima f. javalambrensis

Rosa spinosissima f. javalambrensis, commonly known as the Javalambrense rose, is a unique horticultural form of the spiny rose.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces or container cultivation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering during dry periods.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and historical significance in botanical gardens.
