Rhizanthella

Rhizanthella, commonly known as the underground orchid, is a unique plant species native to Australia.

The plant relies on a symbiotic relationship with specific fungi to obtain nutrients, which is essential for its survival. Rhizanthella produces flowers that are typically pale yellow or white, and they bloom only once every few years.
