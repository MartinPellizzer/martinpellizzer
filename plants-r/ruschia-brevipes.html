Ruschia brevipes

Ruschia brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and drought tolerance, it is well-suited for coastal and arid environments.
