Ruizia fragrans

Ruizia fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant ruizia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ruizia fragrans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
