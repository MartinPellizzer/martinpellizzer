Rosa agrestis var. deserta

Rosa agrestis var. deserta, commonly known as the desert rose, is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features woody stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in dry environments. It produces edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Rosa agrestis var. deserta is well adapted to harsh climates and requires minimal water to survive.
