Rosa sepium var. discosa

Rosa sepium var. discosa, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dark red, almost black flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.

The species is also noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
