Ranunculus palmgrenii

Ranunculus palmgrenii, commonly known as the palmgren's buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often colorful flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves with lobes that resemble a hand, and its flowers are usually bright yellow or white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
