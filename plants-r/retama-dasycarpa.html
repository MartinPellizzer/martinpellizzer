Retama dasycarpa

Retama dasycarpa, commonly known as the hairy retama, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and woody stems covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

Retama dasycarpa is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
