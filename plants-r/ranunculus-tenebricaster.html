Ranunculus tenebricaster

Ranunculus tenebricaster, commonly known as the shadowy buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, glandular stem. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Its leaves are palmately divided and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Ranunculus tenebricaster is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
