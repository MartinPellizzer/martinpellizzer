Rhamnus yoshinoi var. velvetina

Rhamnus yoshinoi var. velvetina, commonly known as the velvetleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that has a soft, velvety texture. The leaves are typically elliptical with a pointed tip and are arranged alternately along the stems. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by small, dark blue berries.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
