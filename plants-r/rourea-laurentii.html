Rourea laurentii

Rourea laurentii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of white, woolly flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture with a slightly serrated margin.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
