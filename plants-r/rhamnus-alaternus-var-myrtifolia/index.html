Rhamnus Alaternus Var. Myrtifolia

Rhamnus alaternus var. myrtifolia is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species within the Rhamnaceae family. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and rocky habitats. Its leaves are elliptical with a leathery texture, and it produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring. The plant is often mistaken for other species due to its similar appearance. Despite its modest size, it holds ecological and ornamental value in certain regions.
