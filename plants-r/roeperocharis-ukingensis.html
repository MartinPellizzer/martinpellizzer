Roeperocharis ukingensis

Roeperocharis ukingensis, commonly known as the Ukinge's orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the montane forests of eastern Africa.

The plant has a short rhizome and produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to its shady, humid habitat.
