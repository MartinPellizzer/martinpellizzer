Rosa pendulina subvar. nuriae

Rosa pendulina subvar. nuriae, commonly known as the Nura's weeping rose, is a cultivar characterized by its cascading, pinkish-white flowers.

It is a hybrid of Rosa pendulina, known for its delicate, drooping blooms that create a soft, ornamental display. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

This variety is prized for its graceful appearance and is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic appeal.
