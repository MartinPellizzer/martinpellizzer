Rosa favratii

Rosa favratii, commonly known as the favrati rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring. Its botanical characteristics include hairy stems and thorny branches, typical of many rose species.

Rosa favratii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
