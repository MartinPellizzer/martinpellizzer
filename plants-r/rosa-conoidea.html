Rosa conoidea

Rosa conoidea, commonly known as the conical rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive conical shape, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five to seven petals and a prominent central pistil.

Rosa conoidea produces fragrant blooms in late spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
