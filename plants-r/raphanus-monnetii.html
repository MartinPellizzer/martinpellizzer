Raphanus monnetii

Raphanus monnetii, commonly known as the narrowleaf radish, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy as well. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in racemes, typical of the family.

The plant produces round, purple-tinged pods that contain several small, round seeds.
