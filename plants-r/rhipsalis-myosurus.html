Rhipsalis myosurus

Rhipsalis myosurus, commonly known as the silver mist cactus, is a unique epiphytic cactus native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairless stems that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the rainy season, typically in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a houseplant rather than being grown outdoors.
