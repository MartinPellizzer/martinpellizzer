Rosa graveolens subsp. jordanii

Rosa graveolens subsp.

jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family. It is native to the arid regions of the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It has woody stems and small, pinnate leaves.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
