Rhynchosia lablab

Rhynchosia lablab, commonly known as cowpea or lablab bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein. Rhynchosia lablab is often cultivated for both its seeds and its forage value.

It thrives in warm climates and is adaptable to various soil types.
