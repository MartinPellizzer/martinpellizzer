Rhododendron × vanhoeffenii

Rhododendron × vanhoeffenii, commonly known as the Van Hoeffen rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its hardiness and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
