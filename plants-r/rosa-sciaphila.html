Rosa sciaphila

Rosa sciaphila, commonly known as the shade rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Rosa sciaphila is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
