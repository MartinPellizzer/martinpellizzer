Rosa bonnieri

Rosa bonnieri, commonly known as the Bonnier rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its botanical features include showy blooms that appear in late spring to early summer.

Rosa bonnieri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
