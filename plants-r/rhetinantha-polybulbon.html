Rhetinantha polybulbon

Rhetinantha polybulbon, commonly known as the clustered bristle orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, hairy, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in specialty gardens.
