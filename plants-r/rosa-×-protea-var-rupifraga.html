Rosa × protea var. rupifraga

Rosa × protea var. rupifraga, commonly known as the rugose rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its rugged, thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
