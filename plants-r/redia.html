Redia

Redia, commonly known as the redia plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its striking red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and dark green color.

Redia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
