Rosa roopiae var. compressa

Rosa roopiae var. compressa, commonly known as the compressed rose, is a unique variant of the Rosa roopiae species.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, tightly packed flowers. The plant typically features dark green, glossy foliage and produces fragrant, pinkish-white blooms. It is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
