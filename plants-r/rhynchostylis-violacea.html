Rhynchostylis violacea

Rhynchostylis violacea, commonly known as the violet-stem orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to violet-colored stems and leaves, which are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing fragrant, violet to purple flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Rhynchostylis violacea is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
