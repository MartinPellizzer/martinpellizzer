Randia laurifolia

Randia laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is dark green and glossy, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Randia laurifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
