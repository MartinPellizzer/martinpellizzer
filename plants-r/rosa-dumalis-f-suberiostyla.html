Rosa dumalis f. suberiostyla

Rosa dumalis f. suberiostyla, commonly known as the alpine rose or mountain rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa dumalis.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cool, moist, well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to alpine environments.
