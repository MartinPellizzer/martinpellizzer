Ruta officinalis

Ruta officinalis, commonly known as common rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant has aromatic, dark green leaves with a distinctive, somewhat bitter scent. Its small yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Ruta officinalis is often used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated as an ornamental plant.
