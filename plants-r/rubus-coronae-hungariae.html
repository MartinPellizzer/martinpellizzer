Rubus coronae-hungariae

Rubus coronae-hungariae, commonly known as the Hungarian crown raspberry, is a shrub native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in woodland areas and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticulture.
