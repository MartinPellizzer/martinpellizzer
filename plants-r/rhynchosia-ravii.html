Rhynchosia ravii

Rhynchosia ravii, commonly known as the African bean or yellow cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit, with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several edible seeds. Rhynchosia ravii is valued for its nutritional content, as the seeds are rich in protein and can be cooked like other legumes.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
