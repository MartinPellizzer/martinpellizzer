Ribes dioicum

Ribes dioicum, commonly known as redcurrant shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, red, edible berries. The plant has alternate, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and streambanks.

Ribes dioicum is also known for its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.
