Remusatia ornata

Remusatia ornata, commonly known as the ornate arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns, giving it its ornamental name. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize large areas efficiently.

Remusatia ornata is valued in water gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
