Rosa stylosa rosella

Rosa stylosa rosella, commonly known as the pink-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often pinkish stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces showy, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa stylosa rosella thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and open woodlands.
