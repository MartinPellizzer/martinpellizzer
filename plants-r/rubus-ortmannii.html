Rubus ortmannii

Rubus ortmannii, commonly known as the Ortmann's blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it flowers with white or pale pink blossoms.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
