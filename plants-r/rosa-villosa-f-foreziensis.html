Rosa villosa f. foreziensis

Rosa villosa f. foreziensis, commonly known as the Foreziensis rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in the spring. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in cooler, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in cultivation.
