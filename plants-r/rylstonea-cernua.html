Rylstonea cernua

Rylstonea cernua, commonly known as the drooping rylstonea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stem.

Rylstonea cernua is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to cascade elegantly from containers or hanging baskets.
