Rosa cavaleriei

Rosa cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species native to China and belongs to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.

The species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
