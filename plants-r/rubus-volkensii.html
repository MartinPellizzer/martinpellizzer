Rubus volkensii

Rubus volkensii, commonly known as the Volkens' raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is a shrub that produces small, red, edible fruits similar to raspberries. Its leaves are typically compound, with three to five leaflets, and it has thorny stems.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
