Rhododendron sinense var. flammeum

Rhododendron sinense var. flammeum, commonly known as the Flame Rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic, well-drained soil. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
