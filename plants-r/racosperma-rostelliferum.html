Racosperma rostelliferum

Racosperma rostelliferum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Its seeds are often used in reseeding programs to restore degraded lands.
