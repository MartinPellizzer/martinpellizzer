Rapanea lechleri

Rapanea lechleri, commonly known as the Amazonian rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is valued for its latex, which is used to produce natural rubber. The plant has large, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody fruits containing several seeds.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat and is also used in traditional medicine.
