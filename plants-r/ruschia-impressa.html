Ruschia impressa

Ruschia impressa, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing form with fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or pale veins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
