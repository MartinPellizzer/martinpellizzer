Rubus zixishanensis

Rubus zixishanensis, commonly known as the Zixishan blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
