Ruellia nitens

Ruellia nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Ruellia nitens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.

It is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
