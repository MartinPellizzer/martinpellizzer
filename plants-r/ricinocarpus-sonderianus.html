Ricinocarpus sonderianus

Ricinocarpus sonderianus, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange to red fruits that are edible when ripe. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant fruit color.
