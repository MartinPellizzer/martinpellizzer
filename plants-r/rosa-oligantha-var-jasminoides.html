Rosa oligantha var. jasminoides

Rosa oligantha var. jasminoides, commonly known as the jasmine rose, is a fragrant shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that emit a strong, sweet scent reminiscent of jasmine. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic qualities.
