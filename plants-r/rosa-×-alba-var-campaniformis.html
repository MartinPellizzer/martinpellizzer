Rosa × alba var. campaniformis

Rosa × alba var. campaniformis, commonly known as the campanula rose, is a hybrid rose species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is notable for its large, showy flowers that resemble bells, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a fragrant scent.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
