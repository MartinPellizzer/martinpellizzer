Rohdea siamensis

Rohdea siamensis, commonly known as the Siam lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces elegant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, often resembling lilies in appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural collections.
