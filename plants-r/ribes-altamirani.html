Ribes altamirani

Ribes altamirani, commonly known as the Altamirano gooseberry, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various shrubs and vines commonly found in temperate regions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges, while its flowers are small and clustered.

The berries, which are edible, are usually yellow or orange and contain a high concentration of antioxidants.
