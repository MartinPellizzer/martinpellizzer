Rebutia deminuta f. pseudominuscula

Rebutia deminuta f. pseudominuscula, commonly known as the miniature Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive greenish-gray color and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
