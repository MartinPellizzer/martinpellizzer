Rhaphiolepis merguiensis

Rhaphiolepis merguiensis, commonly known as the Chinese holly or flame tree, is a flowering shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it suitable for both landscape and container gardening.
