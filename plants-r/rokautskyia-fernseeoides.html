Rokautskyia fernseeoides

It features a compact, creeping rhizome with small, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a characteristic feature of ferns. It thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments, often growing in rocky or forested areas.
