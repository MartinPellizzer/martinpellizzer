Rubus chlorophyllus

Rubus chlorophyllus, commonly known as the greenleaf raspberry.

It is a species of bramble belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its dark green leaves and small, edible berries. The plant is native to certain regions of Asia and is known for its vigorous growth habit. Its leaves are typically serrated and have a dense arrangement, contributing to its dense canopy.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the plant produces fruit in the summer months.
