Rosa rouletii

Rosa rouletii, commonly known as the Chinese moonflower, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late summer to early autumn. Rosa rouletii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both horticultural collections and ecological landscapes.
