Rosa × margittaiana

Rosa × margittaiana, commonly known as the Margitta rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to Europe. The plant typically features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa × margittaiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
