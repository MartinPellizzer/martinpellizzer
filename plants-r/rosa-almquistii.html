Rosa almquistii

Rosa almquistii, commonly known as the Almquist rose, is a rare and endemic species native to the Sierra Nevada mountains of California.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets.

Rosa almquistii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
