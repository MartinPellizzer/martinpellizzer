Radermachera elmeri

Radermachera elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's satinleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Radermachera elmeri is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
