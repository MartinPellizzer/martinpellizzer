Rosa × burgundica minor

Rosa × burgundica minor, commonly known as the miniature Burgundy rose, is a compact hybrid rose that exhibits a variety of flower colors, including red, pink, and white.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant blooms and dense, thorny stems, typical of many rose species. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth. Its compact size makes it ideal for containers, garden borders, or as a specimen plant.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and suitability for both novice and experienced gardeners.
