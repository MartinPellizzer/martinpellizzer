Rosa prostacta

Rosa prostacta, commonly known as the prostrate rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of only a few inches, making it well-suited for ground cover. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rosa prostacta is native to regions of Europe and Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
