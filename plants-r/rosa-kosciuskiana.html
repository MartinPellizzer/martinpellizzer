Rosa kosciuskiana

Rosa kosciuskiana, commonly known as the Kosciuszko rose, is a species native to Australia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

The Rosa kosciuskiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
