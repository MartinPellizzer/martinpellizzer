Reseda lutea var. ramosissima

Reseda lutea var. ramosissima, commonly known as yellowrocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Reseda lutea var. ramosissima is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
