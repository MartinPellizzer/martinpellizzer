Ranunculus foliosus

Ranunculus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy buttercup, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are deeply lobed and its showy, yellow or white flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a short stem with alternate leaves and produces small, aggregate fruits.

Ranunculus foliosus is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
