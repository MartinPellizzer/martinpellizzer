Racosperma heteroneurum var. prolixum

Racosperma heteroneurum var. prolixum, commonly known as the long-leaf racosperma, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
