Reinhardtia gracilis var. rostrata

Reinhardtia gracilis var. rostrata, commonly known as the slender fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often near streams or in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
