Ranunculus lucescens

Ranunculus lucescens, commonly known as the luminous buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers with bright yellow petals and a central cluster of stamens. The plant typically grows in rocky or meadow habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss.

The species is noted for its early spring blooming habit, adding vibrant color to mountainous landscapes.
