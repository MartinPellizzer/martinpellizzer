Rosa mollis f. profusidens

Rosa mollis f. profusidens, commonly known as the profusely flowering Rosa mollis, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features dense, thornless stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces abundant clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to light pink. These blooms appear in spring and summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is well-suited for garden borders or containers due to its compact growth habit and ornamental value.
