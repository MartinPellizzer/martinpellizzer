Russelia leptopoda

Russelia leptopoda, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. Russelia leptopoda thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative trailing plant.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
