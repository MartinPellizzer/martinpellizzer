Rhododendron niko-montanum f. plenum

Rhododendron niko-montanum f. plenum, commonly known as the Full-leaf Rhododendron, is a hybrid plant characterized by its dense, leathery foliage.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. The plant typically features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in late spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a container plant.

The f. plenum form is distinguished by its fully developed leaves, which are broader and more densely packed than in the species form.
