Rosa subatrichostylis

Rosa subatrichostylis, commonly known as the slender-styled rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it grows in rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets, and it produces small, red or yellow fruits known as hips.

Rosa subatrichostylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
