Rosa reuteri f. haberiana

Rosa reuteri f. haberiana, commonly known as the Haberiana rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
