Ravensara perrieri

Ravensara perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's ravensara, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. Ravensara perrieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.

It thrives in humid, shady conditions and requires well-drained, acidic soil.
