Rollandia lanceolata var. glaberrima

Rollandia lanceolata var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smoothleaf rollandia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a slight fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
