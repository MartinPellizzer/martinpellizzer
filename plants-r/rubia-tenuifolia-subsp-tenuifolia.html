Rubia tenuifolia subsp. tenuifolia

Rubia tenuifolia subsp.

tenuifolia, commonly known as slender woodruff, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender, creeping stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, pale blue or violet flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

The leaves and flowers are used in traditional herbal medicine and are also valued for their aromatic properties.
