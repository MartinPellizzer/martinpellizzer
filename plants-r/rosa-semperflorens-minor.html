Rosa semperflorens minor

Rosa semperflorens minor, commonly known as the everblooming miniature rose, is a compact shrub native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it ideal for containers or hanging baskets. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its shape.

Rosa semperflorens minor is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant colors and low maintenance requirements.
