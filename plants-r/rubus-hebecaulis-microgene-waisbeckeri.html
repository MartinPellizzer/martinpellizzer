Rubus hebecaulis microgene waisbeckeri

Rubus hebecaulis microgene waisbeckeri, commonly known as the small-stemmed blackberry shrub, is a species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, thornless stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically small and firm. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and partial sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in local food systems.
