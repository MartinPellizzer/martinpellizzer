Rosa × salaevensis var. lereschei

Rosa × salaevensis var. lereschei, commonly known as the Leresche rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces semi-double to fully double petals in shades of pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.

This variety is also valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to various climatic conditions.
