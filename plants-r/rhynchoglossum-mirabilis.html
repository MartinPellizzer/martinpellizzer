Rhynchoglossum mirabilis

Rhynchoglossum mirabilis, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Rhynchoglossum mirabilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
