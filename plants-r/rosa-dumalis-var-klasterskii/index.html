Rosa Dumalis Var. Klasterskii

Rosa dumalis var. klasterskii is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This variant of the Rosa dumalis species is known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. It thrives in harsh environments, showcasing its adaptability to extreme climates. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in shades of pink and white. Due to its limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
