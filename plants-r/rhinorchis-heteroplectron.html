Rhinorchis heteroplectron

Rhinorchis heteroplectron, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rhinorchis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Rhinorchis heteroplectron is found in parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.
