Rumex nipponicus

Rumex nipponicus, commonly known as Japanese dock, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin. Its flowers are small and greenish, arranged in dense clusters atop tall, leafless stalks.

Rumex nipponicus is known for its ability to thrive in wet soils and is often used in traditional medicine.
