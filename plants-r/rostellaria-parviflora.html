Rostellaria parviflora

Rostellaria parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered rostellaria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a short, narrow spur. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its compact appearance.

Rostellaria parviflora is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia.
