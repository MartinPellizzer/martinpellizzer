Rosa euonymifolia

Rosa euonymifolia, commonly known as the European shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, arching stems with thorns and pinnately compound leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are red, berry-like structures that persist into autumn, adding ornamental value.

Rosa euonymifolia is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
