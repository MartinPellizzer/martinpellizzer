Rebutia aureiflora f. rubriflora

Rebutia aureiflora f. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered form of the golden-flowered rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features dense, cylindrical stems that are typically green or blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are predominantly red or pink, contrasting sharply with its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
