Rosa × alpestris

Rosa × alpestris, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant typically produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine habitats.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, Rosa × alpestris is a popular choice among gardeners in colder climates.
