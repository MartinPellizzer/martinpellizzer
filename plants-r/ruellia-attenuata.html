Ruellia attenuata

Ruellia attenuata, commonly known as the slender ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet, featuring lance-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Ruellia attenuata thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
