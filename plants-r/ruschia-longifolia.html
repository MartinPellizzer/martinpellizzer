Ruschia longifolia

Ruschia longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often blue-green in color, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
