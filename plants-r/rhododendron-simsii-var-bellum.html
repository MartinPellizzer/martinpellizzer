Rhododendron simsii var. bellum

Rhododendron simsii var. bellum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, and thrives in acidic, well-drained soil. It prefers partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
