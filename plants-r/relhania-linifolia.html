Relhania linifolia

Relhania linifolia, commonly known as the silky-leaved relhania, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Relhania linifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in native plant gardens.
