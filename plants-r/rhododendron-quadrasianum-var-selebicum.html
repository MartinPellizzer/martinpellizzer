Rhododendron quadrasianum var. selebicum

Rhododendron quadrasianum var. selebicum, commonly known as the Celebes rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers during the wet season. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
