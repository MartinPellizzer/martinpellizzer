Rubus × pseudostereacanthoides

Rubus × pseudostereacanthoides, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that ripen to a deep red or black color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in its botanical features.
