Renealmia oligotricha

Renealmia oligotricha, commonly known as the small-leaved marsh grass, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves with a fine, hairy texture on the underside. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats.

Renealmia oligotricha plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
