Rubus koehleri var. reflexicalyx

Rubus koehleri var. reflexicalyx, commonly known as the reflexed leaf raspberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. The leaves are distinctive, with a reflexed (curled backward) margin, giving it its specific epithet. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
