Rosa micrantha var. serrata

Rosa micrantha var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in the spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in woodland and streamside habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
