Ribes sanguineum f. splendens

Ribes sanguineum f. splendens, commonly known as the brilliant redcurrant, is a hybrid form of the redcurrant plant.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and fruit, which are both visually appealing and edible.
