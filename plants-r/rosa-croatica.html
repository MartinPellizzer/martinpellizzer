Rosa croatica

Rosa croatica, commonly known as the Croatian rose, is a species native to the Balkan region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets.

Rosa croatica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
