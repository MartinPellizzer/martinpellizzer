Randia glomerata

Randia glomerata, commonly known as clustered randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a slightly hairy texture.

Randia glomerata is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and along riverbanks.
