Rosa lutetiana var. dumalis

Rosa lutetiana var. dumalis, commonly known as the French rose or the white garden rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa lutetiana species.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and fragrance.

This variety is well-suited for shrub borders and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
