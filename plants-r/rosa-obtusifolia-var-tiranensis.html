Rosa obtusifolia var. tiranensis

Rosa obtusifolia var. tiranensis, commonly known as the Tiranese shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody structure and the ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
