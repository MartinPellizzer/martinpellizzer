Racosperma catenulatum

Racosperma catenulatum, commonly known as the catenary acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.

Its seeds are often used in traditional practices by Indigenous communities.
