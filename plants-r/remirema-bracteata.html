Remirema bracteata

Remirema bracteata, commonly known as the golden bract fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically golden-yellow in color. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are composed of small, lance-shaped pinnae that give it a distinctive appearance.

Remirema bracteata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and air-purifying qualities.
