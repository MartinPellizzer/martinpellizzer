Rosa mollis subf. lentula

Rosa mollis subf.

lentula, commonly known as the soft rose or the slender rose, is a sub-species of the rose genus Rosa. It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white.

The plant has slender, arching stems and produces small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
