Rosa rubiginosa var. gremlii

Rosa rubiginosa var. gremlii, commonly known as the Gremlii rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fuzzy rose hip that contains seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
