Rhynchosia schoelleri

Rhynchosia schoelleri, commonly known as the yellow orchid bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive orchid-like appearance, which contribute to its ornamental value. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is characterized by its slender, twining stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Rhynchosia schoelleri is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and can also be used in traditional herbal medicine.
