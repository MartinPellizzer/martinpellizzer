Rosenbergia macrostoma

Rosenbergia macrostoma, commonly known as the giant swamp tarplant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in wet, marshy environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive bilaterally symmetrical shape.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing food and shelter for various insects and small animals.
