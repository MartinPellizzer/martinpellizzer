Rosa coriifolia var. neglecta

Rosa coriifolia var. neglecta, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.

The varietal form 'neglecta' is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
