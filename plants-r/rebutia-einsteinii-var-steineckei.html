Rebutia einsteinii var. steineckei

Rebutia einsteinii var. steineckei, commonly known as the Einstein's Rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features compact, rounded stems that often branch out into a low, bushy form. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for rock gardens or succulent collections.
