Rubus salteri calvatus

Rubus salteri calvatus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that are often used in culinary applications.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
