Roystonea dunlapiana

Roystonea dunlapiana, commonly known as the Dunlap's royal palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a regal appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits, which are edible and contain seeds. Roystonea dunlapiana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant and resilient nature.
