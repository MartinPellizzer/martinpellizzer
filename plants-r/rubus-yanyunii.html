Rubus yanyunii

Rubus yanyunii, commonly known as Yanyun blackberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of flowering plants commonly referred to as blackberries or raspberries. The plant produces small, dark-colored berries that are typically harvested when fully ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with moist, well-drained soil.

The botanical features of Rubus yanyunii include its thorny stems, trifoliate leaves, and aggregate fruits composed of tiny drupelets.
