Rubus × mollidens

Rubus × mollidens, commonly known as the Everbearing Raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. This plant is native to the eastern United States and thrives in temperate climates. It produces fruit throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for commercial cultivation.

The botanical features include trifoliate leaves and pink to white flowers that bloom in the spring.
