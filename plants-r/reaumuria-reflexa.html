Reaumuria reflexa

Reaumuria reflexa, commonly known as the sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Eurasia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It is well adapted to saline and sandy soils, making it a hardy species in harsh environments.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing dunes and providing food for wildlife.
