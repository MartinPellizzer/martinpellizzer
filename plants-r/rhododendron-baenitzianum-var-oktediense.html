Rhododendron baenitzianum var. oktediense

Rhododendron baenitzianum var. oktediense, commonly known as the Oktediense Rhododendron, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. It is valued for its compact growth habit and ability to add vibrant color to shaded garden spaces.

Due to its limited natural range and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
