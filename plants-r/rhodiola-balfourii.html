Rhodiola balfourii

Rhodiola balfourii, commonly known as the Tibetan rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia, including the Tibetan Plateau.

Its resilience and unique botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological and pharmaceutical research.
