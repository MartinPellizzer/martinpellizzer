Rosa aciphylla var. gracilescens

Rosa aciphylla var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender-stemmed rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often sparsely branched stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
