Rosa × sabinii var. coronata

Rosa × sabinii var. coronata, commonly known as the Queen of the Night rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that bloom at night, a unique trait among roses. The plant produces deep pink to almost black petals, which are often adorned with a white or pale pink edge. It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
