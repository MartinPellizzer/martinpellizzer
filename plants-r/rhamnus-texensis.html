Rhamnus texensis

Rhamnus texensis, commonly known as Texas buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the southern United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in regions such as Texas and New Mexico. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. Its flowers are clustered in small racemes and are typically greenish-white in color.

The fruit is a small, smooth, black berry that matures in the fall.
