Rubus holosericeus

Rubus holosericeus, commonly known as the silky raspberry, is a species of bramble native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, red berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its flowers are white with five petals and are followed by the characteristic raspberry-like fruits.
