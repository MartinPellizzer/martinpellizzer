Ruellia palmeri

Ruellia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia palmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
