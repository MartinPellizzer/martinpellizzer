Ramosmania

Ramosmania, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a type of grass-like plant that grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features a rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that help reduce water loss through evaporation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale blue.

Ramosmania is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
