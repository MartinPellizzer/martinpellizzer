Rosa afzeliana var. pastoralis

Rosa afzeliana var. pastoralis, commonly known as the Pastoral Rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in summer. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to harsh climatic conditions.
