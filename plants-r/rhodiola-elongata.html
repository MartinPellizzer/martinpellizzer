Rhodiola elongata

Rhodiola elongata, commonly known as the longstem rhodiola, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. Rhodiola elongata is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic uses.
