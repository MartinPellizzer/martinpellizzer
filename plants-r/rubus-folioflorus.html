Rubus folioflorus

Rubus folioflorus, commonly known as the leaf-flowered raspberry.

It is a species of bramble in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its trailing growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, similar to other raspberries. Its leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.
