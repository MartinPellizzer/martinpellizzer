Rooksbya euphorbioides var. olfersii

Rooksbya euphorbioides var. olfersii, commonly known as the Olfers' rooksbya, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick, branching stem covered in dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its compact growth and ornamental appeal.
