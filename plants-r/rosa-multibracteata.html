Rosa multibracteata

Rosa multibracteata, commonly known as the Chinese rose or evergreen rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its stems are often covered in thorns, and the leaves are compound, consisting of multiple oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa multibracteata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
