Ruschia varians

Ruschia varians, commonly known as the variable Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
