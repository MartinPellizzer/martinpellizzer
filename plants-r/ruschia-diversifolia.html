Ruschia diversifolia

Ruschia diversifolia, commonly known as the variable Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive white or pale pink margin. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, or yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and unique appearance make it a versatile addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
