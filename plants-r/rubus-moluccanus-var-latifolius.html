Rubus moluccanus var. latifolius

Rubus moluccanus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf raspberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of small, red or yellow fruits. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
