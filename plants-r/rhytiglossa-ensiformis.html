Rhytiglossa ensiformis

Rhytiglossa ensiformis, commonly known as the sword leaf orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, violet-colored flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
