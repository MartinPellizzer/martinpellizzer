Rhaphiolepis indica var. angustifolia

Rhaphiolepis indica var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf Chinese rose, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has glossy, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in pairs along the stems. It is evergreen in warmer climates and can be grown in USDA hardiness zones 7 through 9.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
