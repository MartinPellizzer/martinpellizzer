Rosa lucieae var. onoei

Rosa lucieae var. onoei, commonly known as the Onoe rose, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms. It is well-suited for container gardening and small garden spaces due to its compact growth habit.

Rosa lucieae var. onoei is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
