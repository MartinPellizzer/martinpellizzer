Rubus runssorensis var. kiwuensis

Rubus runssorensis var. kiwuensis, commonly known as the Kiwuensis raspberry, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and the fruits are edible, though often tart.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential horticultural value.
