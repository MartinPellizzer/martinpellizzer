Rapistrella × ramosissima

Rapistrella × ramosissima, commonly known as the hybrid firebush, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between *Rhaponticum × ramosissimum* and *Rhaponticum × ramosissimum*, resulting in a plant with distinctive orange-red bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows in wetlands and along waterways, thriving in moist, sandy soils. It produces small yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, seed-filled capsules.

Its ornamental value is due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
