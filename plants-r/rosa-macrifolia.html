Rosa macrifolia

Rosa macrifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 10 cm in length. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Rosa macrifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
