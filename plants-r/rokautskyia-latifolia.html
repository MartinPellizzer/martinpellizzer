Rokautskyia latifolia

Rokautskyia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved rokautsky, is a rare and unique species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by distinctive, elongated seed pods. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires a cool, temperate climate.
