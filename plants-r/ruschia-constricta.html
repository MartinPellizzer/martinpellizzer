Ruschia constricta

Ruschia constricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a compact rosette. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Ruschia constricta is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
