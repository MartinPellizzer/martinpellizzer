Rubus corylifolius var. ferox

Rubus corylifolius var. ferox, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red berries that are edible but have a tart, acidic flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
