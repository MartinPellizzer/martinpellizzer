Rosa pubens

Rosa pubens, commonly known as the pubescent rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy or pubescent stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, often found in woodlands and meadows.

Rosa pubens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
