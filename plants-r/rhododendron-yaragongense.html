Rhododendron yaragongense

Rhododendron yaragongense, commonly known as the Yaragong rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Rhododendron yaragongense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the local biodiversity of its habitat.
