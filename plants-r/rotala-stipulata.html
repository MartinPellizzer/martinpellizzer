Rotala stipulata

Rotala stipulata, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It has dark green, waxy leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a white underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and lakes.

Rotala stipulata is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
