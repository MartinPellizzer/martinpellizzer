Restrepiella lenkenhoffii

Restrepiella lenkenhoffii, commonly known as the Lenkenhoff's restrepiella, is a rare and elusive orchid species native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms in late summer, with flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange-red. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
