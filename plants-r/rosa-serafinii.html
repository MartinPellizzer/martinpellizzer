Rosa serafinii

Rosa serafinii, commonly known as the Serafini rose, is a rare and delicate species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has slender, arching stems and narrow, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
