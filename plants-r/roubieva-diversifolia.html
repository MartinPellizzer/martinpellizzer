Roubieva diversifolia

Roubieva diversifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and long blooming period.

Roubieva diversifolia is often cultivated as a houseplant because of its adaptability to indoor conditions.
