Rosa spinosissima var. vallotii

Rosa spinosissima var. vallotii, commonly known as the Vallot's thorned rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features densely branched stems covered in sharp, hooked prickles, which provide natural protection. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval-shaped, with serrated edges.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
