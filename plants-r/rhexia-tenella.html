Rhexia tenella

Rhexia tenella, commonly known as pinkroot, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, acidic soils in woodland areas. The plant features showy pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
