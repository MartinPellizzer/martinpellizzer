Rosa arvatica var. mentita

Rosa arvatica var. mentita, commonly known as the Mentita rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
