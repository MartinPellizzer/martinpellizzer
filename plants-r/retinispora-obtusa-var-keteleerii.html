Retinispora obtusa var. keteleerii

Retinispora obtusa var. keteleerii, commonly known as the Keteleerii retinas, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous tiny seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
