Rosa × penzanceana

Rosa × penzanceana, commonly known as the Penzance rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from England.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is typically cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a hint of red. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season.

Rosa × penzanceana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
