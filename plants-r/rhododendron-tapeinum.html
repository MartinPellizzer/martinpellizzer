Rhododendron tapeinum

Rhododendron tapeinum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are often dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters and blooming in early spring.

Rhododendron tapeinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
