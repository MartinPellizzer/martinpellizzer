Ruschia meyerae

Ruschia meyerae, commonly known as the Meyer's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, vibrant orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
