Rosa macrostylis

Rosa macrostylis, commonly known as the large-styled rose, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with showy, elongated stamens. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of 5–7 oval leaflets.

Rosa macrostylis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
