Rapuntium nicotianifolium

Rapuntium nicotianifolium, commonly known as the Nicotiana-like rapunzel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often found in gardens and wild areas where it can spread through its rhizomatous root system.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
