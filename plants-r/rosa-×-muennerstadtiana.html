Rosa × muennerstadtiana

Rosa × muennerstadtiana, commonly known as the Muennerstadt rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its semi-climbing or shrub-like growth habit and is native to Central Europe. The plant produces fragrant, double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, and it prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

Rosa × muennerstadtiana is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and relatively disease-resistant nature.
