Rhynchosia edensis

Rhynchosia edensis, commonly known as the African snout bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. Rhynchosia edensis is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
