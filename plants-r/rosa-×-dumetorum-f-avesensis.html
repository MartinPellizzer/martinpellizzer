Rosa × dumetorum f. avesensis

Rosa × dumetorum f. avesensis, commonly known as the Avesensis rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is particularly valued for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various garden conditions.
