Rosularia alpestris var. alpestris

Rosularia alpestris var. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine rose.

It is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features a compact rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. Its flowers are typically pink or white, blooming in early summer.

It is well adapted to cold climates and is often found growing in rocky or gravelly soils.
