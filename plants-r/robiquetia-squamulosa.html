Robiquetia squamulosa

Robiquetia squamulosa, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
