Ruellia togoensis

Ruellia togoensis, commonly known as Togo ruellia, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Ruellia togoensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
