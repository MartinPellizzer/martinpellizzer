Reichertia linarifolia

Reichertia linarifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved reichertia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Reichertia linarifolia is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
