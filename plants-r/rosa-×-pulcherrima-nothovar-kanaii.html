Rosa × pulcherrima nothovar. kanaii

Rosa × pulcherrima nothovar. kanaii, commonly known as the Kanaii rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Hawaii.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The Kanaii rose is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
