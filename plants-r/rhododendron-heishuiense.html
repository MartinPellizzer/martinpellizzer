Rhododendron heishuiense

Rhododendron heishuiense, commonly known as the Heishui rhododendron, is a species native to China, particularly found in the Sichuan province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and cool climates. The plant features large, leathery leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. Its flowers are showy, with pink to red petals that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Rhododendron heishuiense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
