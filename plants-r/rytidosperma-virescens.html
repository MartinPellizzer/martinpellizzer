Rytidosperma virescens

Rytidosperma virescens, commonly known as the green speargrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Rytidosperma virescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
