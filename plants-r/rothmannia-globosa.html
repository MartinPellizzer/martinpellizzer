Rothmannia globosa

Rothmannia globosa, commonly known as the globular rothmannia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Rothmannia globosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique form and delicate floral display.
