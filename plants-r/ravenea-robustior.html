Ravenea robustior

Ravenea robustior, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a striking evergreen plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, orange-red fruits. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.

Ravenea robustior is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in partial shade with well-draining soil.
