Roettlera kompsobaea

Roettlera kompsobaea, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a star-shaped appearance. The plant typically grows in shady, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
