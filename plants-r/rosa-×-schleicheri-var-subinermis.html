Rosa × schleicheri var. subinermis

Rosa × schleicheri var. subinermis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed Rosa.

It is a hybrid rose species characterized by its slender, often thornless stems and compact growth habit. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with serrated leaflets that provide a lush appearance.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
