Rosa × reversa var. mediterranea

Rosa × reversa var. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically in spring. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.
