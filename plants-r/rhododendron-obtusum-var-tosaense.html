Rhododendron obtusum var. tosaense

Rhododendron obtusum var. tosaense, commonly known as the Tosa rhododendron, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial shade. It is a deciduous shrub that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
