Rubus costaricanus var. discolor

Rubus costaricanus var. discolor, commonly known as the Costa Rican raspberry.

This plant is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. The leaves are usually alternate and have a distinctive, serrated margin.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to Central America.
