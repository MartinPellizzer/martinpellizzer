Rehderodendron gongshanense

Rehderodendron gongshanense, commonly known as Gongshan rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and high humidity. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapting to the cool, moist climate of its native habitat.
