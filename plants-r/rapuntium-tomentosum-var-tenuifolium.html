Rapuntium tomentosum var. tenuifolium

Rapuntium tomentosum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of gray-green, fuzzy leaves that are narrow and linear in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are surrounded by spiny bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and resilient member of the Asteraceae family.
