Rumex songaricus

Rumex songaricus, commonly known as the Songarica sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf shapes and flowering structures. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a reddish tint and serrated edges. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.
