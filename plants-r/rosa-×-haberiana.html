Rosa × haberiana

Rosa × haberiana, commonly known as the Haber's rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Caucasus and parts of Turkey. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy shrub suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
