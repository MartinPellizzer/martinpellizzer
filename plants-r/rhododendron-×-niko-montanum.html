Rhododendron × niko-montanum

Rhododendron × niko-montanum, commonly known as the Niko-Montanum Rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
