Rosa lyonensis

Rosa lyonensis, commonly known as the Lyon rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically pinnately compound, with serrated edges.

Rosa lyonensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
