Richardella speciosa

Richardella speciosa, commonly known as the showy richardella, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces vibrant yellow to orange blooms. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Richardella speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
