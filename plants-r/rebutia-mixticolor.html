Rebutia mixticolor

Rebutia mixticolor, commonly known as the variegated rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features compact, rounded stems with thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated in color, displaying shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and indoor gardeners.
