Rebutia steinmannii var. costata

Rebutia steinmannii var. costata, commonly known as the Costate Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a rounded stem with a smooth, pale green surface and distinctive, wavy, rib-like structures. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
