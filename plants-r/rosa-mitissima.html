Rosa mitissima

Rosa mitissima, commonly known as the Japanese rose or sweet william rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, including Japan, Korea, and China. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Rosa mitissima is characterized by its upright growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in perfumery.
