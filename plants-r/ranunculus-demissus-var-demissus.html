Ranunculus demissus var. demissus

Ranunculus demissus var. demissus, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five to eight sepals that resemble petals, and it has a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. It is characterized by its finely divided foliage and is often found in temperate regions.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
