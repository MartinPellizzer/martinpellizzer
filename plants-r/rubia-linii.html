Rubia linii

Rubia linii, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia linii is also known for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional remedies.
