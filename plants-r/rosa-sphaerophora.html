Rosa sphaerophora

Rosa sphaerophora, commonly known as the globe rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and small, globe-shaped flowers. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves, and it produces fragrant blooms in shades of pink and white.

Rosa sphaerophora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
