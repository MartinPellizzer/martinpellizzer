Rosa davidii var. subinermis

Rosa davidii var. subinermis, commonly known as the Chinese pink rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and winter-hardy nature.
