Roeperia ledifolia

Roeperia ledifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed roeperia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, pale blue to violet flowers bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to its foliage.

Roeperia ledifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
