Rhodothyrsus

Rhodothyrsus, commonly known as the pink torch or pink torch lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers, which resemble torches, hence its common name. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming, it is highly valued in horticulture.
