Rubus × chnoophyllus var. macrodontus

Rubus × chnoophyllus var. macrodontus, commonly known as the large-fruited blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its large, dark purple to black berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruits develop from aggregate ovaries.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
