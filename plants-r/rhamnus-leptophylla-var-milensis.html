Rhamnus leptophylla var. milensis

Rhamnus leptophylla var. milensis, commonly known as the Milen's buckthorn, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are edible for some wildlife. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

The species is of interest in ecological studies due to its role in supporting local biodiversity.
