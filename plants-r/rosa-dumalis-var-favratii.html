Rosa dumalis var. favratii

Rosa dumalis var. favratii, commonly known as the Alpine rose or rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, while its fragrance is often described as sweet and delicate.

Rosa dumalis var. favratii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
