Rotala heteropetala

Rotala heteropetala, commonly known as the variable waterwheel plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful, spiral-arranged leaves that can range from red to violet, often with a contrasting white or yellow margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, typically in shallow water or moist soil.

Rotala heteropetala is popular in aquatic gardening for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
