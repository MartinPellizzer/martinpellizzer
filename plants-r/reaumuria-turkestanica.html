Reaumuria turkestanica

Reaumuria turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water in dry environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a splash of color to its harsh habitat. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in nutrient-poor soils.

Reaumuria turkestanica plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and preventing desertification in its native range.
