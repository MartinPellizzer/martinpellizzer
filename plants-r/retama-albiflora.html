Retama albiflora

Retama albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered retama, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. Retama albiflora is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
