Rhododendron schlechteri

Rhododendron schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Rhododendron schlechteri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to a variety of soil conditions.
