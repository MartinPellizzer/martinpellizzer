Roystonea maisiana

Roystonea maisiana, commonly known as the African palm, is a tall, slender palm native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a tall, unbranched stem with a smooth, grayish-brown surface. The plant produces large, fan-shaped leaves that are typically light green and can reach several meters in diameter. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits that are yellow when ripe.

Roystonea maisiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
