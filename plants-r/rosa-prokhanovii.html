Rosa prokhanovii

Rosa prokhanovii, commonly known as the Prokhanov rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it has thorny stems typical of many rose species.

Rosa prokhanovii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
