Ruschia dilatata

Ruschia dilatata, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or Physalis peruviana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that enclose a cluster of seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and colorful fruiting structures.
