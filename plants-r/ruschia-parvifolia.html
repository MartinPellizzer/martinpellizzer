Ruschia parvifolia

Ruschia parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, bushy growth with small, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
