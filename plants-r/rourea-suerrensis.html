Rourea suerrensis

Rourea suerrensis, commonly known as the silverleaf rourea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically a silvery-green color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
