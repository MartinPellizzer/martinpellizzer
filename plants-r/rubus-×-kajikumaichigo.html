Rubus × kajikumaichigo

Rubus × kajikumaichigo, commonly known as the Kajikumaichigo Raspberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, known for its unique fruit characteristics.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces small, aggregate fruits composed of numerous drupelets. The plant is native to Japan and is prized for its sweet, aromatic berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote fruiting.

This cultivar is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
