Rubus illustris

Rubus illustris, commonly known as the Chinese bramble, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as drupes, which are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Rubus illustris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
