Rubus ecklonii

Rubus ecklonii, commonly known as the Ecklon's blackberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including rocky soils and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
