Rosa oligophylla

Rosa oligophylla, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or white and are known for their delicate appearance.

Rosa oligophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
