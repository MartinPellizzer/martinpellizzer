Rosa mollis f. patula

Rosa mollis f. patula, commonly known as the soft rose or pink rose, is a compact shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces small, red hips in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
