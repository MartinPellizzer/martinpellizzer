Rosa afzeliana var. lindii

Rosa afzeliana var. lindii, commonly known as the Lind's rose or the African rose, is a species native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its botanical characteristics include simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a woody structure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
