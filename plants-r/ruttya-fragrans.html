Ruttya fragrans

Ruttya fragrans, commonly known as the sweet-smelling tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture.

Ruttya fragrans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
