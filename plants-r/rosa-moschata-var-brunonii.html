Rosa moschata var. brunonii

Rosa moschata var. brunonii, commonly known as the Brunonii musk rose, is a rare and fragrant variant of the musk rose.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers with a strong, musky scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique fragrance.
