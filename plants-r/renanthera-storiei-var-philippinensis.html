Renanthera storiei var. philippinensis

Renanthera storiei var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple showy blooms arranged in a raceme, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native habitat.
