Rosa micrantha var. rotundata

Rosa micrantha var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaved shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to light pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate to round in shape, with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy surface. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is also noted for its resistance to common rose diseases, making it a reliable choice for gardeners.
