Ribes laurifolium var. yunnanense

Ribes laurifolium var. yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan gooseberry, is a variant of the evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit display.
