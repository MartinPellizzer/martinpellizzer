Rosa megacarpa

Rosa megacarpa, commonly known as the giant rose or large-fruited rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant produces large, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are pinnate, with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates.

Rosa megacarpa is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its historical significance in herbal remedies.
