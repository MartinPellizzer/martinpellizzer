Rhododendron pachysanthum

Rhododendron pachysanthum, commonly known as the giant rhododendron, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has leathery, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and provide dense foliage. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions with acidic soil.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
