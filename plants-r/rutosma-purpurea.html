Rutosma purpurea

Rutosma purpurea, commonly known as purple rutosma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and woody stems, which are typical of the Rutaceae family. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a slightly rough texture.

Rutosma purpurea is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and may have some traditional medicinal uses.
