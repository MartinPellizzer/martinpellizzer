Rosa montana var. ravaudii

Rosa montana var. ravaudii, commonly known as the Ravaudii rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense, thornless stems that form a rounded shape, making it suitable for rock gardens or small spaces. The plant produces showy clusters of fragrant, pink to crimson flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing year-round visual interest.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for perennial gardens.
