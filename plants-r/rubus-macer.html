Rubus macer

Rubus macer, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble within the Rosaceae family, characterized by its prickly stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens.
