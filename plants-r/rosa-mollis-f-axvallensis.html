Rosa mollis f. axvallensis

Rosa mollis f. axvallensis, commonly known as the Axvall's soft rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained, loamy soil. It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and is often found in woodland or hedgerow environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
