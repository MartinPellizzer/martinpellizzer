Rosa suffulta

Rosa suffulta, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly the Alps and Carpathians. This perennial shrub is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. Its flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa suffulta thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine climates.
