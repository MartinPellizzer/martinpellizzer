Rhynchospora umbraticola var. broadwayi

Rhynchospora umbraticola var. broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway blackroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, thriving in shaded, moist environments. This variety is distinguished by its dark-colored rhizomes and is often found in association with other wetland flora.
