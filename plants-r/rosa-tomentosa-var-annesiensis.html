Rosa tomentosa var. annesiensis

Rosa tomentosa var. annesiensis, commonly known as the Annesiensis rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, grayish-white fuzzy foliage and small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant produces oval, red-brown fruits called hips, which are edible and often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
