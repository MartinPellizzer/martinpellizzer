Rhododendron × robustissimum

Rhododendron × robustissimum, commonly known as the mountain rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist climates with acidic soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and woodland settings.
