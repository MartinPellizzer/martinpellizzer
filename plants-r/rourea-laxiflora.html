Rourea laxiflora

Rourea laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered rourea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are often borne in dense inflorescences and are visited by various pollinators.

Rourea laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its foliage and floral display.
