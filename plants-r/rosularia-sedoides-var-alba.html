Rosularia sedoides var. alba

Rosularia sedoides var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered rosularia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming stems covered in dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
