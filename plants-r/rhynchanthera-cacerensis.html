Rhynchanthera cacerensis

Rhynchanthera cacerensis, commonly known as the Caceres orchid, is a species of orchid native to specific regions in South America.

The plant typically features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and elongated leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on lateral shoots and exhibit a range of colors, including shades of pink and purple.

Rhynchanthera cacerensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
