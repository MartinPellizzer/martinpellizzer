Ruschia brevifolia

Ruschia brevifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
