Restio oligocephalus var. glaber

Restio oligocephalus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white to pale blue flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in coastal or inland areas.
