Rosa × odorata f. erubescens

Rosa × odorata f. erubescens, commonly known as the scarlet rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and dense, thorny stems. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its historical use in perfumery.
