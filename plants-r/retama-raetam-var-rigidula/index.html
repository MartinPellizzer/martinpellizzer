Retama Raetam Var. Rigidula

Retama raetam var. rigidula is a resilient shrub native to the arid regions of the Mediterranean and North Africa. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and poor soil conditions. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Despite its tough appearance, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Its unique adaptations make it an important species in ecological restoration efforts.
