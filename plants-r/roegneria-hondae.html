Roegneria hondae

Roegneria hondae, commonly known as Hondan oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Roegneria hondae is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in high-altitude environments.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
