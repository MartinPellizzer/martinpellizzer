Rosa × dumetorum var. scaphusiensis

Rosa × dumetorum var. scaphusiensis, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
