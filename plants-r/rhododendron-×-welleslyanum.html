Rhododendron × welleslyanum

Rhododendron × welleslyanum, commonly known as the Wellingtonia rhododendron, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rhododendron sinogrande and Rhododendron × superbum.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant is evergreen, with leathery, dark green leaves that provide dense foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
