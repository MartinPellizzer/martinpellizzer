Rhododendron macrosmithii

Rhododendron macrosmithii, commonly known as the Macrosmith's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant is evergreen, featuring dense, leathery leaves that provide year-round foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
