Rosa pendulina f. coccialba

Rosa pendulina f. coccialba, commonly known as the Crimson Climber rose, is a compact shrub with distinctive features.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to deep red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises. Its foliage is dark green and densely packed, providing a lush backdrop for the vibrant blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
