Rhynchosia psoraloides

Rhynchosia psoraloides, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators. Its pods contain edible seeds that are used as a food source in some cultures.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
