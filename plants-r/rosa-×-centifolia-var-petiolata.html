Rosa × centifolia var. petiolata

Rosa × centifolia var. petiolata, commonly known as the Persian rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers.

It is a compact shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy blooms that are highly valued in perfumery and horticulture. It is native to the Middle East and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and historical floral arrangements.
