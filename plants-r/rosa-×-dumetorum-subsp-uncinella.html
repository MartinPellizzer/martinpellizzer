Rosa × dumetorum subsp. uncinella

Rosa × dumetorum subsp.

uncinella, commonly known as the hooked bramble rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruits are red, hairy rosehips that contain seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
