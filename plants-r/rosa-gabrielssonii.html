Rosa gabrielssonii

Rosa gabrielssonii, commonly known as Gabrielsson's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America, specifically in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Rosa gabrielssonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
