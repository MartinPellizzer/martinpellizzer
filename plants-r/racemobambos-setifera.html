Racemobambos setifera

Racemobambos setifera, commonly known as the slender bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall bamboo-like appearance.

Racemobambos setifera is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
