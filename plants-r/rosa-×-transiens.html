Rosa × transiens

Rosa × transiens, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa chinensis.

It is characterized by its ability to produce flowers continuously throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white, and are often fragrant.

Rosa × transiens is well-suited to a range of climates and requires minimal maintenance once established.
