Roldana lineolata

Roldana lineolata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
