Rustia angustifolia

Rustia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf rustia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Rustia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
