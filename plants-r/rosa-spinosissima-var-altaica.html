Rosa spinosissima var. altaica

Rosa spinosissima var. altaica, commonly known as the Altai spiny rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping and has some traditional medicinal uses.
