Rebutia albiflora

Rebutia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Rebutia albiflora is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
