Rauvolfia densiflora

Rauvolfia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flower rauvolfia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
