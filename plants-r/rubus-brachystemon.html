Rubus brachystemon

Rubus brachystemon, commonly known as the red-berried blackberry.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae, native to eastern North America. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pink, and it produces small, red berries that are edible.

This plant thrives in woodland areas and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
