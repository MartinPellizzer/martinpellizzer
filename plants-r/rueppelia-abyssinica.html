Rueppelia abyssinica

Rueppelia abyssinica, commonly known as Ethiopian rue, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its small, white or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland areas, often in regions with a dry climate. It has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement.
