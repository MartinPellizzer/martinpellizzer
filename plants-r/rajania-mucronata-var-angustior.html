Rajania mucronata var. angustior

Rajania mucronata var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Rajania, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
