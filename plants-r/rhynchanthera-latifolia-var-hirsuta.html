Rhynchanthera latifolia var. hirsuta

Rhynchanthera latifolia var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy broadleaf rhynchanthera, is a tropical orchid native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, hairy leaves that provide a distinctive texture and appearance. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
