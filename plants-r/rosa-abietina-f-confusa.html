Rosa abietina f. confusa

Rosa abietina f. confusa, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a botanical variant of the alpine rose species.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and oval-shaped, while its flowers are pink to red in color.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cold climates.
