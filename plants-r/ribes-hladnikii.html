Ribes hladnikii

Ribes hladnikii, commonly known as the Hladnik's gooseberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, red, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
