Rhododendron sikangense var. exquisitum

Rhododendron sikangense var. exquisitum, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It features striking, large, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from deep pink to crimson, often with a distinctive white throat. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated, leathery, and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
