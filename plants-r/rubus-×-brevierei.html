Rubus × brevierei

Rubus × brevierei, commonly known as the dwarf blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its small, thorny stems and dense growth habit. The plant produces small, dark red to black berries that are typically edible, though they may be tart in flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is cultivated in gardens and used in some local cuisines.
