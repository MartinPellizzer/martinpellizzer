Rhododendron wolongense

Rhododendron wolongense, commonly known as the Wolong rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant typically grows in cool, humid climates at high altitudes, often in forested areas with acidic soil. Its botanical features include leathery leaves and a compact, shrub-like growth habit.

Rhododendron wolongense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
