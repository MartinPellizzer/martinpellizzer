Rhopalostylis baueri

Rhopalostylis baueri, commonly known as the Baueri's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
