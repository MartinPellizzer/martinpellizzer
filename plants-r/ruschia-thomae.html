Ruschia thomae

Ruschia thomae, commonly known as the Thomae's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. Due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
