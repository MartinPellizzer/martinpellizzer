Retama raetam

Retama raetam, commonly known as the white retama, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy species.

Retama raetam is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
