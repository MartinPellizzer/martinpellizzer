Rhapis excelsa

Rhapis excelsa, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.

Rhapis excelsa prefers partial shade and is relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
