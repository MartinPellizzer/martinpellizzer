Roupala media

Roupala media, commonly known as the silver birch, is a deciduous tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its smooth, white bark that peels in thin, papery layers. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in catkins during the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a serrated margin.

Roupala media is often found in forest margins and along stream banks, thriving in well-drained soils.
