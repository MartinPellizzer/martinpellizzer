Rhizirideum acutangulum

Rhizirideum acutangulum, commonly known as the sharp-angled rhizirideum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
