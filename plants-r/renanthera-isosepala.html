Renanthera isosepala

Renanthera isosepala, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and is part of the Orchidaceae family. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are spurred and have a distinctive shape, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a challenging but rewarding plant to cultivate.
