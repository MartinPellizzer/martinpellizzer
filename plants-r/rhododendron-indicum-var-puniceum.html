Rhododendron indicum var. puniceum

Rhododendron indicum var. puniceum, commonly known as the Kashmiri rose or Puniceum rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
