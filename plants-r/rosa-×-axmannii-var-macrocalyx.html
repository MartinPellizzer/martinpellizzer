Rosa × axmannii var. macrocalyx

Rosa × axmannii var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of large, fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in thorns. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature in temperate climates.
