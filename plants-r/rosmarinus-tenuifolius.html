Rosmarinus tenuifolius

Rosmarinus tenuifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved rosemary, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its essential oils.

Rosmarinus tenuifolius thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
