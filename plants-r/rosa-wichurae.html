Rosa wichurae

Rosa wichurae, commonly known as the Wichura rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Andes mountains in South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers and thorny stems. It typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates.

Rosa wichurae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
