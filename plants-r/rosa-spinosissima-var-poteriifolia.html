Rosa spinosissima var. poteriifolia

Rosa spinosissima var. poteriifolia, commonly known as the Poteriifolia rose, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and distinctive foliage.
