Rhynchosia erlangeri

Rhynchosia erlangeri, commonly known as the African butterbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that attract pollinators. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several bean-like seeds.

Rhynchosia erlangeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible seeds.
