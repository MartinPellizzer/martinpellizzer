Rosa mollirotigera

Rosa mollirotigera, commonly known as the soft-haired rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of China, Kazakhstan, and Mongolia. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it its common name.

Rosa mollirotigera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
