Rhododendron lutescens f. shimianense

Rhododendron lutescens f. shimianense, commonly known as the Shimian yellow rhododendron, is a unique variant of the species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in early spring, making it a notable ornamental plant. The plant typically grows in dense, moist forests at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool and shaded conditions. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to the vibrant yellow blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
