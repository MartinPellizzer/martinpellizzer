Rhododendron fargesii

Rhododendron fargesii, commonly known as the Sichuan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist climates. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are often adorned with a distinctive red or purple blotch.

Rhododendron fargesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded woodland environments.
