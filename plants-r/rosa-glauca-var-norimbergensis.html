Rosa glauca var. norimbergensis

Rosa glauca var. norimbergensis, commonly known as the Norimbergensis rose, is a compact shrub native to Europe.

It features pale blue to violet flowers with a distinctive powdery bloom, and its foliage is typically grayish-green. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

This variety is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of climates with proper care.
