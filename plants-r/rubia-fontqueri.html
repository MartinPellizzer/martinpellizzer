Rubia fontqueri

Rubia fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri's bedstraw, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia fontqueri is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
