Ranunculus flammula var. tenuifolius

Ranunculus flammula var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It prefers moist, shady habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
