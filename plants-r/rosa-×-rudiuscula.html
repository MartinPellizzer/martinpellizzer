Rosa × rudiuscula

Rosa × rudiuscula, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, single or double flowers in a variety of colors. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × rudiuscula is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
