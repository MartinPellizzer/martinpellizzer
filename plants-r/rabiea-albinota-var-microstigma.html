Rabiea albinota var. microstigma

Rabiea albinota var. microstigma, commonly known as the slender white bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, white, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive pale blue throat. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often covered in a fine, whitish bloom that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
