Rosa reuteri var. implexa

Rosa reuteri var. implexa, commonly known as the twisted rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a dense, thorny growth habit with dark green, leathery leaves. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its unique, twisted stems and compact form make it a distinctive addition to gardens and landscapes.
