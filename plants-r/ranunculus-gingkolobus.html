Ranunculus gingkolobus

Ranunculus gingkolobus, commonly known as the Chinese buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features showy, yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, glandular stem. The plant has palmately divided leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Ranunculaceae family.
