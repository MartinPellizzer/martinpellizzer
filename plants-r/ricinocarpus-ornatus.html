Ricinocarpus ornatus

Ricinocarpus ornatus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods containing flat, seed-like structures. It thrives in humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture making.
