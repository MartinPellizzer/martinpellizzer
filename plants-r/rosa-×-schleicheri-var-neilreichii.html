Rosa × schleicheri var. neilreichii

Rosa × schleicheri var. neilreichii, commonly known as the Neilreich's rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces semi-double to double pink flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant features glossy, dark green foliage and thorny stems typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
