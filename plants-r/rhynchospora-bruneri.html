Rhynchospora bruneri

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and spike-like inflorescences. Rhynchospora bruneri plays a vital role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
