Restio setuligerus

Restio setuligerus, commonly known as the slender restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.
