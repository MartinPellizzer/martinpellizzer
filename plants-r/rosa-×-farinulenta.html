Rosa × farinulenta

Rosa × farinulenta, commonly known as the Farinulenta rose, is a hybrid rose species known for its compact growth habit and abundant flowering.

It typically produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, depending on the cultivar. The plant has a bushy structure with thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders and container planting.

Rosa × farinulenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, suitable for temperate climates.
