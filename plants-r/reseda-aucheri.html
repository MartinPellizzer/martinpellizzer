Reseda aucheri

Reseda aucheri, commonly known as velvetleaf reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are velvety and dark green, with a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
