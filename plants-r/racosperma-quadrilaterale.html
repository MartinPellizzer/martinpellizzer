Racosperma quadrilaterale

Racosperma quadrilaterale, commonly known as the quadrilateral acacia, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its slender, often woody stems and small, clustered leaves. Racosperma quadrilaterale is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
