Rosa × reversa var. gentiliformis

Rosa × reversa var. gentiliformis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, red follicles that contain numerous seeds. It is typically grown for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in gardens.

This variety is also known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
