Rebutia pseudodeminuta

Rebutia pseudodeminuta, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact, rounded stems covered in small, spiky tubercles. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the growing season. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with bright sunlight.

Rebutia pseudodeminuta is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
