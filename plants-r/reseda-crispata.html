Reseda crispata

Reseda crispata, commonly known as slender reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Reseda crispata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
