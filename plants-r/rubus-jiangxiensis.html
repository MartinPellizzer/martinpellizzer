Rubus jiangxiensis

Rubus jiangxiensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi raspberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of shrubs known for their edible fruits. The plant typically features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruit is a aggregate of small drupes.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions of Jiangxi Province.
