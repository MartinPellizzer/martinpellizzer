Rebutia raulii

Rebutia raulii, commonly known as the Raulii Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a compact plant, typically growing to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters.

Rebutia raulii is well adapted to dry environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
