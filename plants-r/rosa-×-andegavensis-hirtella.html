Rosa × andegavensis hirtella

Rosa × andegavensis hirtella, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy coating. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
