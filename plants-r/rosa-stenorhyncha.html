Rosa stenorhyncha

Rosa stenorhyncha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, and its narrow, serrated leaves. Rosa stenorhyncha is typically shrub-like in growth habit, with thorny stems and evergreen foliage.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
