Rhodopis planisiliqua var. lowdenii

Rhodopis planisiliqua var. lowdenii, commonly known as the Lowdenii rhodopis, is a rare and unique variant of the rhodopis plant.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that exhibit a range of colors from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale pink or white, adding a subtle elegance to its appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in temperate forest regions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
