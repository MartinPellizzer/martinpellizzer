Rosa × dumetorum var. clujensis

Rosa × dumetorum var. clujensis, commonly known as the Cluj rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Carpathian region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
