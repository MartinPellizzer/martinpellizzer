Rosa hostii

Rosa hostii, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a species of shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, leathery leaves and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The flowers are typically pink to red in color and have a distinctive, almost lantern-like shape. It is a perennial plant that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

Rosa hostii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
