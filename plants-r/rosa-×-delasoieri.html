Rosa × delasoieri

Rosa × delasoieri, commonly known as the Delasoi rose, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with thorny branches and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double to full flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × delasoieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
