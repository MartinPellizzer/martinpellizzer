Reseda elata

Reseda elata, commonly known as narrowleaf reseda, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in loose spikes and bloom in the summer months.

Reseda elata is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
