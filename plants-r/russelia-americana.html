Russelia americana

Russelia americana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and South America.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers, which attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Russelia americana is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking floral display.
