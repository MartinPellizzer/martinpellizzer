Ruta oreojasme

Ruta oreojasme, commonly known as the "Mexican rue" or "Mexican rue," is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like citrus. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and pinnately compound leaves with a strong, pungent odor. It thrives in warm, dry climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Ruta oreojasme is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
