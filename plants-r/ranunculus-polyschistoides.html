Ranunculus polyschistoides

Ranunculus polyschistoides, commonly known as the rock ranunculus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is characterized by its short stature and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species.
