Roscoea capitata var. scillifolia

Roscoea capitata var. scillifolia, commonly known as the pink ginger or scillifolia roscoea, is a rhizomatous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, pale pink to lavender-colored inflorescences that resemble those of the genus *Zantedeschia*. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy and grow in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires partial shade and well-drained soil to flourish.
