Rosa oblongicalyx

Rosa oblongicalyx, commonly known as the oblongleaf rose, is a species native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring arching stems covered in thorns. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are elliptical in shape, with a slightly serrated margin, and they are arranged alternately along the stems.

The rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
