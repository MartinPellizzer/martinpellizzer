Ruellia anthracina

Ruellia anthracina, commonly known as purple rosel.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular, violet to purple flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia anthracina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
