Rumex limoniastrum

Rumex limoniastrum, commonly known as coastal sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as coastal dunes and rocky soils. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is known for its adaptability to saline and arid conditions, making it a resilient plant in coastal environments.
