Rollandia pinnatifida

Rollandia pinnatifida, commonly known as the Brazilian water lily, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in shallow water. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy surface to repel water.

Rollandia pinnatifida plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
