Rosa livescens var. aliothii

Rosa livescens var. aliothii, commonly known as the Aliothii rose, is a rare and ornamental variant of the Rosa livescens species.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. It has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are covered in thorny buds.

This variety is prized for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
