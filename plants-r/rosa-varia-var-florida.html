Rosa varia var. florida

Rosa varia var. florida, commonly known as the Florida rose, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Rosa varia var. florida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
