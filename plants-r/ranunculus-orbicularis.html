Ranunculus orbicularis

Ranunculus orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular (rounded) leaves and showy yellow flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and the plant is often found in calcareous soils.

Ranunculus orbicularis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
