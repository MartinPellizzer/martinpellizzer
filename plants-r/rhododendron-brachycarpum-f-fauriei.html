Rhododendron brachycarpum f. fauriei

Rhododendron brachycarpum f. fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's dwarf rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture, and its flowers are typically small, pink to purple, and bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. It prefers well-drained, acidic soil and partial to full shade, thriving in cool, moist environments.

Its ornamental value lies in its neat form and delicate floral display.
