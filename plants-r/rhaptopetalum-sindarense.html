Rhaptopetalum sindarense

Rhaptopetalum sindarense, commonly known as the Kashmir rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, particularly in regions of Jammu and Kashmir. The plant is characterized by its delicate pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
