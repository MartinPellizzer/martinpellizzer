Rosa inodora f. bernensis

Rosa inodora f. bernensis, commonly known as the Bernens rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its large, double flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features a vigorous growth habit with arching stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, showy flowers.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
