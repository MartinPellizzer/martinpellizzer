Rosa spinosissima var. arenivaga

Rosa spinosissima var. arenivaga, commonly known as the desert rose or sand rose, is a shrub-like plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has adapted to harsh environments with its succulent leaves and deep root system for water retention. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its resilience and ability to bloom in challenging conditions.
