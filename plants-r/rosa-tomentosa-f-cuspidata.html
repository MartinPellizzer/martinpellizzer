Rosa tomentosa f. cuspidata

Rosa tomentosa f. cuspidata, commonly known as the woolly rose or cushion rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.

It features hairy, grayish-green foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to rocky or arid soils and thrives in full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
