Rosa rousseauiorum

Rosa rousseauiorum, commonly known as the Rousseaui's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are typically compound, with serrated edges, and the stems are often covered in thorns.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
