Rhinanthus pallens

Rhinanthus pallens, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is an annual plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on other plants for water and nutrients through its root system. Rhinanthus pallens plays an important role in promoting biodiversity by stimulating the growth of grasses and other plants through its parasitic relationship.
