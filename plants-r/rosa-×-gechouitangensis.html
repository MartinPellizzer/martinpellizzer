Rosa × gechouitangensis

Rosa × gechouitangensis, commonly known as the Gechouitang rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both landscape design and cut flower arrangements.
