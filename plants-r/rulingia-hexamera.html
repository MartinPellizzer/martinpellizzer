Rulingia hexamera

Rulingia hexamera, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
