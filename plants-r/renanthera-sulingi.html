Renanthera sulingi

Renanthera sulingi, commonly known as the Suling orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of up to 12 individual blooms. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white petals with a yellow lip, and it typically grows as an epiphyte on trees in humid, shaded environments. It prefers warm, moist conditions and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often grown by orchid enthusiasts.
