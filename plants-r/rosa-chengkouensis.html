Rosa chengkouensis

Rosa chengkouensis, commonly known as the Chengkou rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Sichuan province, where it grows in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are thorny.

Rosa chengkouensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
