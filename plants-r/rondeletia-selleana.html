Rondeletia selleana

Rondeletia selleana, commonly known as the pink starflower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Rondeletia selleana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
