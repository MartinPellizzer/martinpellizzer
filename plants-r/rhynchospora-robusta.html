Rhynchospora robusta

Rhynchospora robusta, commonly known as the robust spikegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands and marshes of North America.

The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, showcasing its adaptability to aquatic and semi-aquatic environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in clusters, and it plays a role in providing habitat for various aquatic insects and birds.

Rhynchospora robusta is often found in regions with high humidity and is an indicator species for wetland health.
