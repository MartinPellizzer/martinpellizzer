Rosa kwangtungensis

Rosa kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Kwangtung rose, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals. It produces oval, red berries that contain numerous seeds.

This rose is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
