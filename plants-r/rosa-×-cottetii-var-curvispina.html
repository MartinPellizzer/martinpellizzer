Rosa × cottetii var. curvispina

Rosa × cottetii var. curvispina, commonly known as the curly-spined rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a compact shrub, often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and hedging.
