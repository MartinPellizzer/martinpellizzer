Ribes elegans

Ribes elegans, commonly known as the elegant gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various species of flowering plants and berries. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters and blooming in early spring.

The fruit is a small, yellowish berry that ripens in late summer, attracting birds and other wildlife.
