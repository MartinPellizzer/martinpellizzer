Rupicapnos africana var. dubuisii

Rupicapnos africana var. dubuisii, commonly known as the Natal fan palm, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fan-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both gardens and natural habitats.
