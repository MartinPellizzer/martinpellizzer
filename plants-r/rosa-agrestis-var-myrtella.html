Rosa agrestis var. myrtella

Rosa agrestis var. myrtella, commonly known as the Myrtle Rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its delicate blooms. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and hardy nature.

The varietal name "myrtella" refers to its resemblance to the myrtle plant in appearance and fragrance.
