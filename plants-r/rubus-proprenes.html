Rubus proprenes

Rubus proprenes, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This species is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits called drupes, which are typically red when mature. The plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its edible fruits and is commonly cultivated in gardens and commercial farms.
