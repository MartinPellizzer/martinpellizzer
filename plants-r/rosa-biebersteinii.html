Rosa biebersteinii

Rosa biebersteinii, commonly known as the Tibetan rose or Himalayan rose, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Rosa biebersteinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
