Rosa × turbinata var. orbessanea

Rosa × turbinata var. orbessanea, commonly known as the Orbessane rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
