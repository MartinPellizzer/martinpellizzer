Rapanea borneensis

Rapanea borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean rapanea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed surrounded by a fleshy, edible pulp.

This species plays an important role in the ecological balance of its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
