Rhododendron bagobonum

Rhododendron bagobonum, commonly known as the Bagobo rhododendron, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its floral display ranges from white to pink and purple, making it a striking ornamental plant.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
