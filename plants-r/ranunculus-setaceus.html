Ranunculus setaceus

Ranunculus setaceus, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bright yellow flowers with deeply notched petals. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. It has a basal rosette of leaves and produces a single flower stem that bears its showy blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
