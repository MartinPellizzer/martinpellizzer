Rubus rudis var. discerptus

Rubus rudis var. discerptus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a variant of the blackberry plant characterized by its slender, trailing growth habit. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically less showy than other blackberry varieties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves and thorny stems, typical of the Rubus genus.
