Rhododendron suoilenhense

Rhododendron suoilenhense, commonly known as the Suiyuan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of northern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron suoilenhense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
