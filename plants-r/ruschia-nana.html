Ruschia nana

Ruschia nana, commonly known as the dwarf ruschia, is a low-growing succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or blue-green in color, with a textured surface. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
