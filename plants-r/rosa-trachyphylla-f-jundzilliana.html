Rosa trachyphylla f. jundzilliana

Rosa trachyphylla f. jundzilliana, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a rough, leathery texture, and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is well-suited to coastal and rocky environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness, making it a popular choice in garden settings.

The subspecies is notable for its unique floral structure and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
