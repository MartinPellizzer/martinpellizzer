Rosa mollis subf. fricascens

Rosa mollis subf.

fricascens, commonly known as the soft rose or fricascens rose, is a subform of the species Rosa mollis. It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

The plant features fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with finely serrated margins, and the stems are covered in soft, downy hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
