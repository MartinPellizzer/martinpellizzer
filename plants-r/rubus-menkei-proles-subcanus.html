Rubus menkei proles subcanus

Rubus menkei proles subcanus, commonly known as the silver-haired raspberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.
