Ruagea ovalis

Ruagea ovalis, commonly known as the oval leaf ruagea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale yellow, while the fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule.

Ruagea ovalis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
