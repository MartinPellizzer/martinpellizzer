Rubus Cuspidiferus Var. Oegacanthus

Blackberry bush, scientifically known as Rubus cuspidiferus var. oegacanthus, is a thorny shrub that thrives in temperate climates. Its dark green leaves and showy white flowers make it a striking addition to any garden. The plant produces sweet, juicy berries that are highly prized for their flavor and nutritional value. While it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish, it is relatively hardy and adaptable. This versatile plant not only offers culinary delights but also supports local wildlife and pollinators.
