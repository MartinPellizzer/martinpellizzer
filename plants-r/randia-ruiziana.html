Randia ruiziana

Randia ruiziana, commonly known as the Brazilian star-apple or *Randia ruiziana*, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
