Rubus nubigenus subsp. ruizii

Rubus nubigenus subsp.

ruizii, commonly known as the cloud raspberry, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America. It is characterized by its small, edible berries that are typically red or yellow in color.

The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring season. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
