Racosperma websteri

Racosperma websteri, commonly known as the Western Australian acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in dry, sandy soils.

It is often used in rehabilitation projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for native wildlife.
