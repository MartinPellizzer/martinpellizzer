Rosa mollis f. macrifolia

Rosa mollis f. macrifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered soft rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a cultivar of Rosa mollis, characterized by its notably larger and more showy flowers compared to the typical species. The plant features fragrant, pink to white petals that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is soft and velvety, with a pleasant scent, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate water and regular pruning for optimal growth.
