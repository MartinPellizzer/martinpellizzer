Rosa reuteri f. doleritica

Rosa reuteri f. doleritica, commonly known as the Doleritica rose, is a compact shrub with dark green, leathery foliage that exhibits a striking silver-white underside.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pale pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the Canary Islands and is well adapted to dry, rocky environments. It has a slow growth rate and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its ornamental appeal.

Its unique foliage and subtle floral display make it a prized addition to specialized gardens.
