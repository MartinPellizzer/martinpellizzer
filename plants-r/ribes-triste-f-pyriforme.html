Ribes triste f. pyriforme

Ribes triste f. pyriforme, commonly known as the weeping currant, is a variant of the currant plant characterized by its drooping, cascading branches.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae, which includes various species of currants and gooseberries. The plant features small, edible berries that are typically red or black when mature. Its foliage is dark green and slightly glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
