Rostellaria quinqueangularis

Rostellaria quinqueangularis, commonly known as the five-angled rostellaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, five-angled floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
