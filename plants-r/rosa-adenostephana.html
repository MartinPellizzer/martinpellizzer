Rosa adenostephana

Rosa adenostephana, commonly known as the Adenostephana rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge.

Rosa adenostephana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
