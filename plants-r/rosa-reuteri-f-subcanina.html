Rosa reuteri f. subcanina

Rosa reuteri f. subcanina, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed rock rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in spring. The plant has smooth, woody stems and dark green, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plant.
