Rosa scheutzii

Rosa scheutzii, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, serrated leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for garden cultivation.

Rosa scheutzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
