Rosa tomentella var. lessiniensis

Rosa tomentella var. lessiniensis, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense, woolly foliage that provides insulation against cold temperatures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in cooler climates.
