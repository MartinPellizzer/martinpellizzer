Ruyschia lepidota

Ruyschia lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf ruyschia, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish or yellowish in color.

Ruyschia lepidota is often found in tropical and subtropical regions where the climate is humid and warm.
