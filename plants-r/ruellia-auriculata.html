Ruellia auriculata

Ruellia auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

Ruellia auriculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
