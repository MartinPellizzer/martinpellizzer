Rebutia chrysacantha var. schieliana

Rebutia chrysacantha var. schieliana, commonly known as the Schiel's golden rebutia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South America.

It features rounded, fleshy stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are small, oval, and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance and ease of care.
