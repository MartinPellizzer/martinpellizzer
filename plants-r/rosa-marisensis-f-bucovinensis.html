Rosa marisensis f. bucovinensis

Rosa marisensis f. bucovinensis, commonly known as the Bucovina sea rose, is a rare and ornamental cultivar of the Rosa marisensis species.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and glossy, dark green foliage. This plant is native to the Carpathian region and is adapted to temperate climates with well-drained soil. It typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The Bucovina sea rose is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
