Rhynchospora ruiziana var. triceps

Rhynchospora ruiziana var. triceps, commonly known as the three-ribbed spikegrass, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color, and it blooms during the warm season.
