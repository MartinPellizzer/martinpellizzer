Richardia lateralis

Richardia lateralis, commonly known as the narrowleaf Richardia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage rather than its flowers.
