Rosa boursaultiana

Rosa boursaultiana, commonly known as the Boursault rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub-like plant with upright stems and dense, thorny growth. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa boursaultiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
