Rottlera hookeriana

Rottlera hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's rottlera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rottleriaceae.

It is native to the Andes mountains of South America, specifically in regions of Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, cool climates and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in horticulture.
