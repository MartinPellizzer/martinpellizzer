Reboudia pinnata var. boissieri

Reboudia pinnata var. boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's sensitiveplant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding inward when disturbed. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique foliage and easy care make it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native flora.
