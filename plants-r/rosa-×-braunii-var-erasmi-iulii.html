Rosa × braunii var. erasmi-iulii

Rosa × braunii var. erasmi-iulii, commonly known as the Erasmi-Iulii rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its semi-climbing growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with a deep red color and a strong fragrance. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in dense, thorny prickles. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
