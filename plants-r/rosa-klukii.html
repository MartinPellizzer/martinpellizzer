Rosa klukii

Rosa klukii, commonly known as Kluk's rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pale pink to white in color. Rosa klukii produces hips, which are the fruiting bodies containing seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
