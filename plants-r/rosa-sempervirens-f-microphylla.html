Rosa sempervirens f. microphylla

Rosa sempervirens f. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature evergreen rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with dense, dark green foliage.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant is evergreen, retaining its leaves throughout the year, making it suitable for year-round landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, requiring minimal pruning.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
