Rauhia multiflora

Rauhia multiflora, commonly known as the multi-flowered rauhia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Rauhia multiflora is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
