Rabdosia anisochila

Rabdosia anisochila, commonly known as the hairy leaved rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Rabdosia anisochila is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
