Rosa × dumetorum var. aliena

Rosa × dumetorum var. aliena, commonly known as the alien shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in a variety of soil types and climates.

Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces edible hips that are rich in vitamin C. This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
