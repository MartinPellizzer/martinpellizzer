Reseda germanicopolitana var. glabra

Reseda germanicopolitana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth reseda, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. This variety is distinguished by its lack of pubescence, giving it a sleek appearance compared to other Reseda species.
