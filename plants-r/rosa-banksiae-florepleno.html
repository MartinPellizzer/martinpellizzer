Rosa banksiae florepleno

Rosa banksiae florepleno, commonly known as the Banks' rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to full blooms throughout the summer months. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Rosa banksiae florepleno is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
