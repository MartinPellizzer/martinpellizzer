Rhododendron kiyosumense

Rhododendron kiyosumense, commonly known as the Kiyosumi rhododendron, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, while the flowers range in color from pink to white.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
