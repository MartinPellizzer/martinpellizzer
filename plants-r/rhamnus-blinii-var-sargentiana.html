Rhamnus blinii var. sargentiana

Rhamnus blinii var. sargentiana, commonly known as Sargent's buckthorn, is a shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, oval leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, smooth, black drupe that ripens in the fall.

This species is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
