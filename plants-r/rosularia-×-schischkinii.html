Rosularia × schischkinii

Rosularia × schischkinii, commonly known as the hybrid rose bush, is a succulent plant characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, rounded leaves.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is a natural hybrid between Rosularia lutea and Rosularia tibetica. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a container plant.
