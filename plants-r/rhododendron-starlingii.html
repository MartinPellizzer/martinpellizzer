Rhododendron starlingii

Rhododendron starlingii, commonly known as the Starling's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are borne on short pedicels and are often accompanied by a distinctive, sweet fragrance.

Rhododendron starlingii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
