Rhododendron yakushimanum var. intermedium

Rhododendron yakushimanum var. intermedium, commonly known as the Izu rhododendron, is a shrub native to Japan.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
