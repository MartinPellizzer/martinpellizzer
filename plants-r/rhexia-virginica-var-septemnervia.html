Rhexia virginica var. septemnervia

Rhexia virginica var. septemnervia, commonly known as the seven-veined purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with seven distinct veins. Its floral structure consists of both ray and disk flowers, typical of the Asteraceae family.
