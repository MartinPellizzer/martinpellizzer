Rubus × callianthus

Rubus × callianthus, commonly known as the evergreen bramble, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, edible berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
