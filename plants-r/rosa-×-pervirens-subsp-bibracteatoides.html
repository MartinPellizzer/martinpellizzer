Rosa × pervirens subsp. bibracteatoides

Rosa × pervirens subsp.

bibracteatoides, commonly known as the Bibracteate rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus. It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage.

The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with a distinctive, rounded shape. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in garden settings.
