Rosularia globulariifolia subsp. globulariifolia

Rosularia globulariifolia subsp.

globulariifolia, commonly known as the globular rosebay, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed.

The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
