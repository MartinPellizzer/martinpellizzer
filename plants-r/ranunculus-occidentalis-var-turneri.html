Ranunculus occidentalis var. turneri

Ranunculus occidentalis var. turneri, commonly known as the Sierra larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, blue to purple petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are deeply lobed and its stems are often hairy.

It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
