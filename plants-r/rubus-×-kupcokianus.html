Rubus × kupcokianus

Rubus × kupcokianus, commonly known as the Kupcokian raspberry, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces red or yellow berries that are typically small and aggregate in clusters. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
