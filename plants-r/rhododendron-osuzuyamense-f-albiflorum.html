Rhododendron osuzuyamense f. albiflorum

Rhododendron osuzuyamense f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered form of Osuzuyamense rhododendron, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the pale blooms.
