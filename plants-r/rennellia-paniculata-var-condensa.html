Rennellia paniculata var. condensa

Rennellia paniculata var. condensa, commonly known as the Rennell Island pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the island of Rennell in the Solomon Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, feathery leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear fragrant, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found growing in sandy soils near the shoreline.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
