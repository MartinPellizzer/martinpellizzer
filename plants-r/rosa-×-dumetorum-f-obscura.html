Rosa × dumetorum f. obscura

Rosa × dumetorum f. obscura, commonly known as the dark form of the shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy foliage and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in pink or white. The plant is known for its hardy nature, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The f. obscura form is distinguished by its darker pigmentation and more compact growth compared to other forms of Rosa × dumetorum.
