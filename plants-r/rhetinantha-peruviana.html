Rhetinantha peruviana

Rhetinantha peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky soil in which it is commonly found.

Rhetinantha peruviana is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
