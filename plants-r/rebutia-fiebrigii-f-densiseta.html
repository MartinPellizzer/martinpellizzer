Rebutia fiebrigii f. densiseta

Rebutia fiebrigii f. densiseta, commonly known as the dense-hairied rebutia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem with dense, woolly white to grayish hairs covering its surface. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is fleshy and adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in arid environments.
