Ranunculus dingjiensis

Ranunculus dingjiensis, commonly known as the Dingjiensis buttercup, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves and has a short, hairy stem. It blooms in early spring, producing flowers that range from yellow to white in color.
