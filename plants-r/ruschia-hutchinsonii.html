Ruschia hutchinsonii

Ruschia hutchinsonii, commonly known as the mountain fireplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and vibrant, showy flowers. Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
