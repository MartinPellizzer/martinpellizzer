Rorippa boissieri

Rorippa boissieri, commonly known as the narrowleaf watercress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of slender, elongated leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the summer months. Rorippa boissieri is an annual or biennial herb that is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
