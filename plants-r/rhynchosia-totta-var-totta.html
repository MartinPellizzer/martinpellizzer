Rhynchosia totta var. totta

Rhynchosia totta var. totta, commonly known as the African violet or the violet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces showy, purple or violet flowers that are clustered in racemes, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
