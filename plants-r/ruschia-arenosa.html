Ruschia arenosa

Ruschia arenosa, commonly known as the sandruschia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Ruschia arenosa is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
