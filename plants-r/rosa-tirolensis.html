Rosa tirolensis

Rosa tirolensis, commonly known as the Tyrolean rose, is a species of shrub native to the Alps.

It produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

Rosa tirolensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery.
