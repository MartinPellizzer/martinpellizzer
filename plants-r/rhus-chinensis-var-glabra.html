Rhus chinensis var. glabra

Rhus chinensis var. glabra, commonly known as smooth sumac, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have an overall smooth texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in upright clusters, followed by bright red, spiny fruits that mature in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for ornamental use.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
