Reichardia tingitana var. pinnatifida

Reichardia tingitana var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the African daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, linear leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, arranged in a flat, daisy-like cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its popularity in horticulture.
