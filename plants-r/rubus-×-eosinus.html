Rubus × eosinus

Rubus × eosinus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically bright red when ripe. The plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is widely cultivated for its edible berries.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
