Rumex × schultesii

Rumex × schultesii, commonly known as the hybrid sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rumex acetosa (common sorrel) and Rumex acetobulus (broad-leaved dock). The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a reddish or purple underside, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in its foliage and growth habits.
