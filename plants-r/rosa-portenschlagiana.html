Rosa portenschlagiana

Rosa portenschlagiana, commonly known as the Portenschlag rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its large, fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy flowers that attract pollinators.

Rosa portenschlagiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature.
