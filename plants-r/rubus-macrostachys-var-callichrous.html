Rubus macrostachys var. callichrous

Rubus macrostachys var. callichrous, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its erect, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces bright red, aggregate fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The species is cultivated for its flavorful berries and is also valued in traditional medicine.
