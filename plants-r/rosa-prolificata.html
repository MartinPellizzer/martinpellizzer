Rosa prolificata

Rosa prolificata, commonly known as the prolific rose, is a species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink flowers. The plant produces numerous stems and has large, serrated leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Rosa prolificata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
