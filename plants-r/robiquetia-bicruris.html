Robiquetia bicruris

Robiquetia bicruris, commonly known as the twin-winged lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Robiquetia bicruris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
