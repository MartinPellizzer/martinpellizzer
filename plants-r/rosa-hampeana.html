Rosa hampeana

Rosa hampeana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with serrated edges and produces woody stems that can become thorny. Rosa hampeana is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
