Rosa kozlowskii

Rosa kozlowskii, commonly known as the Kozlowski rose, is a species native to the Caucasus region.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Rosa kozlowskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
