Rosa pouzinii var. pubescens

Rosa pouzinii var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent Rosa pouzinii, is a variant of the Rosa pouzinii species, which is native to southern Africa.

This plant is characterized by its hairy or pubescent stems and leaves, which provide a distinctive texture. The flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and a fragrant scent. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
