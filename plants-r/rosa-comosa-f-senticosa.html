Rosa comosa f. senticosa

Rosa comosa f. senticosa, commonly known as the fragrant rose or sweet-scented rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa comosa species.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and is often grown as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance, particularly in the evening. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic qualities in gardens and floral arrangements.
