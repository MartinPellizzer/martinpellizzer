Rupicapnos oranensis var. gracilis

Rupicapnos oranensis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender flamebrush, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, red-orange bracts that resemble flames, which are prominent during the flowering season. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for dry, coastal environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall compact and shrub-like appearance.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
