Ruschia frutescens

Ruschia frutescens, commonly known as the fireplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, bushy growth with fleshy, triangular leaves that are often red or purple in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
