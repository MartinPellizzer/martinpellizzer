Rindera Lanata F. Cyanoptera

Rindera lanata f. cyanoptera is a rare and striking plant known for its unique blue-green foliage. This cultivar is a variation of the base species Rindera lanata, which is native to certain regions of the southern United States. Its leaves are thick and waxy, giving it a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other plants in its family. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and horticulturists alike.
