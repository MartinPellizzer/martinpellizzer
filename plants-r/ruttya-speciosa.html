Ruttya speciosa

Ruttya speciosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ruttya speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
