Rosa lucidissima f. laevis

Rosa lucidissima f. laevis, commonly known as the smoothleaf garden rose, is a cultivar characterized by its smooth, hairless foliage and showy, fragrant flowers.

It typically produces semi-double to fully double blooms in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the specific variety. The plant is a deciduous shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic contribution and ability to attract pollinators.
