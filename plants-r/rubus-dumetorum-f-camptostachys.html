Rubus dumetorum f. camptostachys

Rubus dumetorum f. camptostachys, commonly known as the erect blackberry, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as drupes, which are typically dark purple to black in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have serrated margins.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated for its edible fruits.
