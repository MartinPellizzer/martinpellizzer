Ruschia stenopetala

Ruschia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petalled Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia stenopetala is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
