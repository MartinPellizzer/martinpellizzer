Rosa × bigeneris

Rosa × bigeneris, commonly known as the double-flowered shrub rose, is a hybrid species that combines traits from various rose species.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and its ability to produce abundant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features large, semi-double to fully double pink or white blooms that are highly ornamental. Rosa × bigeneris thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and hedges.

Its compact form and persistent flowering make it a popular choice for both landscape design and cut flower arrangements.
