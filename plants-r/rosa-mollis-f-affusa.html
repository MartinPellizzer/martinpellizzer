Rosa mollis f. affusa

Rosa mollis f. affusa, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable in diverse habitats.
