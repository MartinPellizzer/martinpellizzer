Rotala belgaumensis

Rotala belgaumensis, commonly known as the Belgaum water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to India.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish.

Rotala belgaumensis is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide oxygen to the water.
