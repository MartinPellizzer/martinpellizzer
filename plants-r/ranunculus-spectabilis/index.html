Ranunculus Spectabilis

Ranunculus spectabilis, commonly known as the showy buttercup, is a striking perennial plant native to the western United States. It is celebrated for its vibrant, colorful blooms that range from yellow to deep red, often with a distinctive black center. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. With its beauty and ecological value, Ranunculus spectabilis plays an important role in both ornamental horticulture and local ecosystems.
