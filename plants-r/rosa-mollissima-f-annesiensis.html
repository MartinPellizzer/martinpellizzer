Rosa mollissima f. annesiensis

Rosa mollissima f. annesiensis, commonly known as the Annenskaya rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa mollissima species.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and delicate pink to white flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticultural cultivation.
