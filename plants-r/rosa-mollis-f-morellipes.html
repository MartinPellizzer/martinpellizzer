Rosa mollis f. morellipes

Rosa mollis f. morellipes, commonly known as the soft rose or morellipes rose, is a hybrid form of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and pleasant fragrance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
