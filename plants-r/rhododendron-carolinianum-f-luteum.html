Rhododendron carolinianum f. luteum

Rhododendron carolinianum f. luteum, commonly known as the yellow flame azalea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange-red, blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its colorful display.
