Ricinocarpus villosus var. grandifolius

Ricinocarpus villosus var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved ricinocarpus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and large, ovate leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny pods containing flat, bean-like seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
