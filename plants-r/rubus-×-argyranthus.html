Rubus × argyranthus

Rubus × argyranthus, commonly known as the silverleaf blackberry, is a hybrid plant within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are typically edible. It is native to the western United States and thrives in temperate climates.

Botanically, it is a shrub with thorny stems and a complex genetic background due to its hybrid nature.
