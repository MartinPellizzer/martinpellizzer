Rubus hirsutus f. politulus

Rubus hirsutus f. politulus, commonly known as the smooth raspberry.

It is a variant of the common raspberry, characterized by its smoother, less hairy stems and leaves compared to the typical form. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in horticulture and plant taxonomy.
