Rosa coriifolia var. virescens

Rosa coriifolia var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, thornless stems with a distinctive, branched growth habit. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth, waxy surface that turns a vibrant green in summer. Its flowers are pale pink to white, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
