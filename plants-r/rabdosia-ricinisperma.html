Rabdosia ricinisperma

Rabdosia ricinisperma, commonly known as the velvetleaf rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
