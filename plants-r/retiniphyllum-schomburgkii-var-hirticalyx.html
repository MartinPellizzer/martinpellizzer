Retiniphyllum schomburgkii var. hirticalyx

Retiniphyllum schomburgkii var. hirticalyx, commonly known as the hirticalyx retiniphyllum, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and horticultural displays.
