Rhynchostylis rieferi

Rhynchostylis rieferi, commonly known as the Riefer's spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and a raceme of showy, yellow-orange flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil.

Rhynchostylis rieferi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
