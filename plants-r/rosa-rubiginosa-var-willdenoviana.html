Rosa rubiginosa var. willdenoviana

Rosa rubiginosa var. willdenoviana, commonly known as the willdenovian rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink to reddish flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are orange-red hips that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by birds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature in coastal and dry environments.
