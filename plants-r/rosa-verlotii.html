Rosa verlotii

Rosa verlotii, commonly known as the Verlot's rose, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a prominent central pistil. It produces oval, red fruits called hips, which are edible and often used in traditional medicine.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
