Rosa rubiginosa var. fallax

Rosa rubiginosa var. fallax, commonly known as the autumn rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark red to almost black flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking flower color and hardy nature.
