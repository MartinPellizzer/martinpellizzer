Rubia reiseri

Rubia reiseri, commonly known as Reiser's woodruff, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, trifoliate leaves, and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Rubia reiseri is considered a rare and endangered species.
