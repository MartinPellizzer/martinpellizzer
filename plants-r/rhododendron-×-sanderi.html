Rhododendron × sanderi

Rhododendron × sanderi, commonly known as the Sander's rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in temperate regions.
