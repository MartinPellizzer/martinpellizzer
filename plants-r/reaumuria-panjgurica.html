Reaumuria panjgurica

Reaumuria panjgurica, commonly known as the Panjgur tamarisk, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the family Tamaricaceae and is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and pale yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a hardy species in harsh environments. It plays a significant role in stabilizing desert soils and preventing erosion.

Its ecological importance is notable in the semi-arid and desert ecosystems of its native habitat.
