Ruschia watermeyeri

Ruschia watermeyeri, commonly known as the Watermeyer's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
