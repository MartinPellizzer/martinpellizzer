Rosa × noisettana

Rosa × noisettana, commonly known as the Noisette rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France in the early 19th century.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of pink to white. The plant produces thorny stems and heart-shaped leaves, typical of many rose species. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Rosa × noisettana is also valued for its role in the development of many modern rose cultivars.
