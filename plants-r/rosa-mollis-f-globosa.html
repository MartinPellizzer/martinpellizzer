Rosa mollis f. globosa

Rosa mollis f. globosa, commonly known as the globular rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged in an alternate pattern along the stems. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.

It is relatively disease-resistant and requires minimal pruning to maintain its compact form.
