Rapuntium excelsum

Rapuntium excelsum, commonly known as the giant cress, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and edible leaves, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
