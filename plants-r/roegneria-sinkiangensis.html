Roegneria sinkiangensis

Roegneria sinkiangensis, commonly known as the Xinjiang feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the arid regions of western China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.

Its ornamental value has also led to its cultivation in some regions for landscaping purposes.
