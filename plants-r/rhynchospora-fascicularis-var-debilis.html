Rhynchospora fascicularis var. debilis

Rhynchospora fascicularis var. debilis, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its inflorescence is usually a spike-like structure that emerges from the base of the plant.

This variety is distinguished by its delicate, weak stem and is often confused with other similar species in the Cyperaceae family.
