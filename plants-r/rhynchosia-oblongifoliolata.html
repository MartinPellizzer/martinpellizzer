Rhynchosia oblongifoliolata

Rhynchosia oblongifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with oblong, slightly heart-shaped leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.

Its adaptability to various soil types and climate conditions contributes to its widespread cultivation.
