Rubus multiformis nothovar. delicatior

Rubus multiformis nothovar. delicatior, commonly known as the delicate raspberry, is a hybrid plant characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, raspberry-like fruits.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated margins and produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically pink to red in color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained, fertile soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique flavor profile.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit variability in growth and fruit characteristics.
