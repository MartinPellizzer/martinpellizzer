Rhododendron borneense subsp. villosum

Rhododendron borneense subsp.

villosum, commonly known as the Bornean hairy rhododendron, is a species native to the tropical regions of Borneo. It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats at moderate elevations. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

This subspecies is valued for its horticultural appeal and ecological role in its native environment.
