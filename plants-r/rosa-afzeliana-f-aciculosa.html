Rosa afzeliana f. aciculosa

Rosa afzeliana f. aciculosa, commonly known as the needle-leaved rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This form is native to certain regions of Asia, particularly in China and Korea. The plant produces fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
