Rosa simkovicsii

Rosa simkovicsii, commonly known as the Simkovicsii rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white down. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa simkovicsii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
