Rosa × gallicoides var. pseudoscandens

Rosa × gallicoides var. pseudoscandens, commonly known as the French rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
