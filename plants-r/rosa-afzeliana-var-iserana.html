Rosa afzeliana var. iserana

Rosa afzeliana var. iserana, commonly known as the Iseran rose, is a shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorned stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous habitats. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in a dense layer of grayish hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
