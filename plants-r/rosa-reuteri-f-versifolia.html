Rosa reuteri f. versifolia

Rosa reuteri f. versifolia, commonly known as the variegated rock rose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation, which adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental foliage and modest blooms make it a unique addition to Mediterranean-style landscapes.
