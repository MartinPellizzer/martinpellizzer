Rosa bodinieri

Rosa bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces red, berry-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Rosa bodinieri is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
