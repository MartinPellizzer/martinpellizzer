Rhoicissus verdickii

Rhoicissus verdickii, commonly known as the velvet leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Rhoicissus verdickii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
