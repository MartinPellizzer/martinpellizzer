Rosa slancensis

Rosa slancensis, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and China. The plant is characterized by its slender, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, pinnate, and covered in fine hairs, while its thorns are short and curved.

Rosa slancensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
