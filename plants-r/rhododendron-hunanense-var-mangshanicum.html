Rhododendron hunanense var. mangshanicum

Rhododendron hunanense var. mangshanicum, commonly known as the Mangshan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and high humidity. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing vibrant pink to purple blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
