Racinaea spiculosa var. spiculosa

Racinaea spiculosa var. spiculosa, commonly known as the spiky racinaea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
