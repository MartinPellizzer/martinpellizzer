Rodriguezia decora var. picta

Rodriguezia decora var. picta, commonly known as the painted rodriguezia, is a striking tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant features showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is popular in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
