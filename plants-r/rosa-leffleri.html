Rosa leffleri

Rosa leffleri, commonly known as the white knockout rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has arching, thornless stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Rosa leffleri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its elegant appearance.
