Rubus schummelii f. hansenii

Rubus schummelii f. hansenii, commonly known as the Schummel's raspberry.

It is a flowering plant species belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as drupes, which are typically red or yellow when mature. It is native to South America, specifically Chile, and thrives in temperate climates.

The foliage is usually dense, with trifoliate leaves that have serrated edges.
