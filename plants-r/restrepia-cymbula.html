Restrepia cymbula

Restrepia cymbula, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often among moss or leaf litter. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Restrepia cymbula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
