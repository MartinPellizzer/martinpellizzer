Rhododendron luteum

Rhododendron luteum, commonly known as the yellow laurel, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron luteum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
