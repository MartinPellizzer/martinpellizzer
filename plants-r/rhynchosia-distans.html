Rhynchosia distans

Rhynchosia distans, commonly known as the African bean or wild cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with stipules. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Rhynchosia distans is valued for its nutritional seeds and use in traditional medicine.
