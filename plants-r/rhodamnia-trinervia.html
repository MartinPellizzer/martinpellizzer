Rhodamnia trinervia

Rhodamnia trinervia, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer, which are followed by elongated, woody pods.

Rhodamnia trinervia thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in gardens and urban landscapes.
