Rhododendron dayaoshanense

Rhododendron dayaoshanense, commonly known as Dayaoshan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments. Its floral display, which ranges from pink to red, makes it a notable ornamental species.

Rhododendron dayaoshanense is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
