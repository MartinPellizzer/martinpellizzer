Rabdosia provicarii

Rabdosia provicarii, commonly known as the purple-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of purple or pink flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rabdosia provicarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
