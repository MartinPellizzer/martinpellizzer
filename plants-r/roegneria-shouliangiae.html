Roegneria shouliangiae

Roegneria shouliangiae, commonly known as the Shouliangian wheatgrass, is a grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear florets with pale green to golden hues. It is typically found in moist, temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental and potential agricultural uses.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
