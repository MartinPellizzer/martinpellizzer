Ruellia Strepens Var. Micrantha

Ruellia strepens var. micrantha is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for gardens and containers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and moisture conditions for optimal growth.
