Rubus festivus var. eufestivus

Rubus festivus var. eufestivus, commonly known as the Festival Raspberry, is a variant of the Raspberry plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along the edges of forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
