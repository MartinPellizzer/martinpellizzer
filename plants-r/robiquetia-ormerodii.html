Robiquetia ormerodii

Robiquetia ormerodii, commonly known as the Namib Desert lily, is a unique plant adapted to extreme arid conditions.

It is native to the Namib Desert in Namibia, where it thrives in sandy soils with minimal rainfall. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the brief rainy season. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and deterring herbivores.

Robiquetia ormerodii plays a vital role in the desert ecosystem, providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to biodiversity in one of the harshest environments on Earth.
