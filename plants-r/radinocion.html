Radinocion

Radinocion, commonly known as the silverleaf spurge, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a waxy, silver-gray coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the growing season, typically in spring or early summer. Its roots are deep and extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Radinocion is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
