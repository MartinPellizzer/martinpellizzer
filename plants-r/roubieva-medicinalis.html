Roubieva medicinalis

Roubieva medicinalis, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, velvety leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant contains alkaloids and other secondary compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties. It is often found in rocky or arid soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its toxic nature, it should be handled with care.
