Rhododendron burgravianum

Rhododendron burgravianum, commonly known as the Burgravian rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant.

Rhododendron burgravianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in specific garden conditions.
