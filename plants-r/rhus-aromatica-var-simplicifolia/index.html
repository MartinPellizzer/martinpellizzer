Rhus Aromatica Var. Simplicifolia

Rhus aromatica var. simplicifolia is a hardy shrub native to the eastern united states. it is known for its fragrant leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. this plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil conditions. it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. its adaptability makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
