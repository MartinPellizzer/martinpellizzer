Rhododendron yaoshanense

Rhododendron yaoshanense, commonly known as the Yaoshan rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are leathery and oval-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Rhododendron yaoshanense is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
