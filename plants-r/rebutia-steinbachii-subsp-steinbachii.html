Rebutia steinbachii subsp. steinbachii

Rebutia steinbachii subsp.

steinbachii, commonly known as the Desert Cactus or the Rainbow Cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family. It is native to the arid regions of Central America, particularly Mexico.

This species is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
