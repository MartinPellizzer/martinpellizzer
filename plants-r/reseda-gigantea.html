Reseda gigantea

Reseda gigantea, commonly known as giant reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Reseda gigantea is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
