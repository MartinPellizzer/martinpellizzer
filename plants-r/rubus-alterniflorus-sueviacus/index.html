Rubus Alterniflorus Sueviacus

The rubus alterniflorus sueviacus is a rare and elusive shrub native to the cold regions of northern europe. it belongs to the rosaceae family and is known for its distinctive alternating floral arrangement. this plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in boreal forests and tundra-like environments. due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species. its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
