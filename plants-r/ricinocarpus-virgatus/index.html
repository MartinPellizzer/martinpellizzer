Ricinocarpus Virgatus

Ricinocarpus virgatus is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and distinctive leaf structure. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests. Its scientific name reflects its historical significance in traditional medicine. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds that require caution during handling.
