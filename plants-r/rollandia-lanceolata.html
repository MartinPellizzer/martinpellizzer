Rollandia lanceolata

Rollandia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a fleshy, tuberous root that is often used as a starchy food source. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Rollandia lanceolata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
