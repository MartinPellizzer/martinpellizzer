Ranunculus petiolaris

Ranunculus petiolaris, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has a short, upright stem with heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
