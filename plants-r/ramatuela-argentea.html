Ramatuela argentea

Ramatuela argentea, commonly known as silverleaf ramatuela, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in rocky or arid environments due to its drought-tolerant nature.

Ramatuela argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
