Ribesiodes polypodioides

Ribesiodes polypodioides, commonly known as the golden polypody, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green to golden in color. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

Ribesiodes polypodioides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in humid conditions.
