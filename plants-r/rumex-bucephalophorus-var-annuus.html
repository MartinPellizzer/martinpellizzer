Rumex bucephalophorus var. annuus

Rumex bucephalophorus var. annuus, commonly known as the annual sorrel, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short, erect stems. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

This variant is an annual species, completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
