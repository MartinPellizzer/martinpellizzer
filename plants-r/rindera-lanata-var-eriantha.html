Rindera lanata var. eriantha

Rindera lanata var. eriantha, commonly known as the woolly rindera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which provides protection against herbivores and extreme weather conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological adaptations, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
