Rosa abietina f. monnieri

Rosa abietina f. monnieri, commonly known as the Alpine rose or mountain rose, is a compact shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its short, woody stems and is well adapted to cold, rocky environments. It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Rosa abietina f. monnieri is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
