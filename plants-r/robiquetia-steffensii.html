Robiquetia steffensii

Robiquetia steffensii, commonly known as the Steffens' lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of elegant, spurred flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette that helps it conserve water.

Robiquetia steffensii is well-suited to rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
