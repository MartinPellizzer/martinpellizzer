Rosa mollis f. progestula

Rosa mollis f. progestula, commonly known as the soft rose or progestula rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white, often with a subtle scent. The plant has soft, fuzzy foliage that gives it its common name, and it produces rose hips that are typically orange-red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
