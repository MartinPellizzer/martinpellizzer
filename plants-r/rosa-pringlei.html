Rosa pringlei

Rosa pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rosa pringlei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
