Ruschia subglobosa

Ruschia subglobosa, commonly known as the globular Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of succulents. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
