Rosa terscolensis

Rosa terscolensis, commonly known as the Terscolensis rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, red berries that contain seeds.

Rosa terscolensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
