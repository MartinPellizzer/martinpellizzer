Rhododendron konori var. phaeopeplum

Rhododendron konori var. phaeopeplum, commonly known as the Konori dwarf rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, making it suitable for small gardens or rockeries. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, thriving in cool, moist climates.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
