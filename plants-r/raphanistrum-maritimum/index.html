Raphanistrum Maritimum

Raphanistrum maritimum is a hardy, salt-tolerant grass species commonly found in coastal areas. It thrives in sandy or muddy soils along shorelines and estuaries. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an important part of coastal ecosystems. Despite its resilience, it is often overlooked for its ecological significance.
