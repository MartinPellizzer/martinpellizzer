Rosa micrantha f. lusseri

Rosa micrantha f. lusseri, commonly known as the Lusseri rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals and a subtle, sweet scent. The plant has slender, thornless stems and dark green, serrated leaves that are glossy in appearance. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit.

The foliage remains evergreen in mild climates, adding ornamental value throughout the year.
