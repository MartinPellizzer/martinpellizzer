Rubus deweveri

Rubus deweveri, commonly known as Dewever's raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. This plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the flowers are white or pale pink.

It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
