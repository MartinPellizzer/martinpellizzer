Ranunculus multistaminifer

Ranunculus multistaminifer, commonly known as the many-stamen buttercup, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers with numerous stamens, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and prefers well-drained soils. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the flowers bloom in early spring.

It is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.
