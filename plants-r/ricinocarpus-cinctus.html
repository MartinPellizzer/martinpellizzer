Ricinocarpus cinctus

Ricinocarpus cinctus, commonly known as the mangrove fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family. Ricinocarpus cinctus thrives in saline and brackish water environments, making it an important component of mangrove ecosystems.
