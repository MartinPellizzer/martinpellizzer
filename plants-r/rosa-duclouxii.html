Rosa duclouxii

Rosa duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and pinnate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include simple, ovate leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Rosa duclouxii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in cooler, high-altitude environments.
