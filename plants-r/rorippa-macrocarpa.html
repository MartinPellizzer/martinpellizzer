Rorippa macrocarpa

Rorippa macrocarpa, commonly known as broadleaf watercress, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that are often floating or submerged in water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist or aquatic environments, such as ponds, streams, and wetlands.
