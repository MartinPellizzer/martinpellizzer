Rubus × pseudophyllostachys

Rubus × pseudophyllostachys, commonly known as the hybrid raspberry-loganberry.

This plant is a hybrid between Rubus idaeus (raspberry) and Rubus × loganobaccus (loganberry), resulting in a unique combination of traits. It typically produces large, dark red berries with a sweet-tart flavor. The plant has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.
