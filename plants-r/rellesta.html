Rellesta

Rellesta, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and elusive plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that resemble ghostly apparitions in the moonlight. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that emit a subtle, sweet scent, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Rellesta thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and collectors alike.
