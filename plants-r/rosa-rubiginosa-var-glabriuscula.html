Rosa rubiginosa var. glabriuscula

Rosa rubiginosa var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth bristled rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are dark green and covered with fine hairs, which become less prominent as the plant matures. This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in perfumery.
