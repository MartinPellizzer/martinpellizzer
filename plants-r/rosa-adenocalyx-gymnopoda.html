Rosa adenocalyx gymnopoda

Rosa adenocalyx gymnopoda, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed rose, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The flowers are often followed by oval, red berries that contain the seeds.
