Radermachera fenicis

Radermachera fenicis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Radermachera fenicis is also valued for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.
