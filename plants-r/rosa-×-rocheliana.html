Rosa × rocheliana

Rosa × rocheliana, commonly known as the Rocheliana rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse blooming habit, often producing fragrant, semi-double to double flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
