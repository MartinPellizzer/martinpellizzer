Reimarochloa oligostachya

Reimarochloa oligostachya, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver or blue-green. Its inflorescence consists of small, spike-like clusters of flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated.

Reimarochloa oligostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
