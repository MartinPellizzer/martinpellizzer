Rechsteineria lateritia

Rechsteineria lateritia, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features a short, fibrous root system and produces a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in a spike, with a distinctive, sweet scent.
