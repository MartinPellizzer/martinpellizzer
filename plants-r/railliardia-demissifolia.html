Railliardia demissifolia

Railliardia demissifolia, commonly known as the soft-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are soft and velvety to the touch. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in shades of white and green, and bloom during the wet season.

Railliardia demissifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
