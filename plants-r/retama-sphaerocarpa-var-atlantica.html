Retama sphaerocarpa var. atlantica

Retama sphaerocarpa var. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic retama, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula and northern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, spherical, woody fruits that are typically yellow or brown in color. The plant produces small white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are evergreen, narrow, and leathery, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
