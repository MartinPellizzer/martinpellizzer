Rhopalopilia pallens var. pallens

Rhopalopilia pallens var. pallens, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It produces small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
