Rubus tuyamae

Rubus tuyamae, commonly known as the Tuyama bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruits are berries that are typically red when mature, though they are not widely cultivated for consumption.

Rubus tuyamae is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
