Rubus × immanis

Rubus × immanis, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits known as blackberries, which are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or hedgerow environments.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
