Rubus schleicheri var. hyperanthus

Rubus schleicheri var. hyperanthus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This variant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. The plant is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some culinary uses in traditional contexts.
