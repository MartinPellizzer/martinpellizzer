Rosa cinnamomea var. majalis

Rosa cinnamomea var. majalis, commonly known as the Cinnamon Rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its stems are covered in a distinctive cinnamon-colored bark, which becomes more pronounced with age.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
