Rosa dolabrifolia

Rosa dolabrifolia, commonly known as the broad-bladed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its large, broad, ovate leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Rosa dolabrifolia is typically found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the rose genus.
