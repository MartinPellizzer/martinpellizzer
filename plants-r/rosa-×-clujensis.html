Rosa × clujensis

Rosa × clujensis, commonly known as the Cluj rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage that provides a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Rosa × clujensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
