Rosa Pimpinellifolia Subvar. Fissisepala

Rosa pimpinellifolia subvar. fissisepala is a rare and striking member of the rose family known for its unique floral characteristics. This subvariety is distinguished by its deeply lobed petals and a distinctive sepals that split apart, giving it a dramatic appearance. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, rocky environments. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after plant among horticulturists and botanists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in cultivation due to its specific growing requirements.
