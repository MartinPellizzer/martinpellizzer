Rosa × anemoniflora

Rosa × anemoniflora, commonly known as the anemoniflora rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage, which provides a lush backdrop for its showy flowers. The plant produces semi-double to double pink to white blooms that are reminiscent of anemone flowers, hence its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Rosa × anemoniflora is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
