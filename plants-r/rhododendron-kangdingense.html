Rhododendron kangdingense

Rhododendron kangdingense, commonly known as the Kangding rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments at high altitudes, often in forested areas. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the local biodiversity of its habitat.
