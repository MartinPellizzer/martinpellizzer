Rosa × gandogeriana

Rosa × gandogeriana, commonly known as the Gandoger rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to the United States.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
