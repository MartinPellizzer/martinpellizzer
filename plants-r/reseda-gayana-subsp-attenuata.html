Reseda gayana subsp. attenuata

Reseda gayana subsp.

attenuata, commonly known as the slender reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, pink or white flowers.
