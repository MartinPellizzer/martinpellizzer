Rhytionanthos elegans

Rhytionanthos elegans, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique floral display makes it a notable species in horticulture and botanical gardens.
