Restrepia australis

Restrepia australis, commonly known as the Southern Lady's Slipper, is a unique orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

Restrepia australis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
