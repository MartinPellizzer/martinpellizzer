Rosa decora

Rosa decora, commonly known as the showy pink rose, is a species native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its vibrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Rosa decora thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
