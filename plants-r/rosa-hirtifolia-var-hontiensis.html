Rosa hirtifolia var. hontiensis

Rosa hirtifolia var. hontiensis, commonly known as the Hontien rose, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features upright, sparsely branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with ovate to lanceolate leaflets that have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
