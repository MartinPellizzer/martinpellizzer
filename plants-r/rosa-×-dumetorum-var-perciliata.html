Rosa × dumetorum var. perciliata

Rosa × dumetorum var. perciliata, commonly known as the alpine rose or mountain rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated margins, and the stems are covered in dense, glandular hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
