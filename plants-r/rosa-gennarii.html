Rosa gennarii

Rosa gennarii, commonly known as the Gennari rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a woody follicle containing several seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Rosa gennarii is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
