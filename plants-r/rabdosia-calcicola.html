Rabdosia calcicola

Rabdosia calcicola, commonly known as the limestone rabdosia, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in rocky, calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

Rabdosia calcicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
