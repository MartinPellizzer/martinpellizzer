Rosa × nitidula f. blondaeana

Rosa × nitidula f. blondaeana, commonly known as the Blondaeana rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and vigorous flowering.

It produces large, fully double flowers in shades of pink to white, often with a slight blush of red. The plant is noted for its strong fragrance and is prized in gardens for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and suitable for both landscape and container gardening.
