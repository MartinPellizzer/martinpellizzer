Rabdosia umbrosa f. albiflora

Rabdosia umbrosa f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The species is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
