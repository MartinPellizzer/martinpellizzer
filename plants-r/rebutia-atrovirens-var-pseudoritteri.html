Rebutia atrovirens var. pseudoritteri

Rebutia atrovirens var. pseudoritteri, commonly known as the Ritter's Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features dark green, ribbed stems that are adorned with white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. The plant has a dense, spiny coating that helps protect it from herbivores and excessive water loss. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
