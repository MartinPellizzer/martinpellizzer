Rosa sweginzowii

Rosa sweginzowii, commonly known as the Sweginzow's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a serrated margin, and its stems are covered in soft, white hairs.

Rosa sweginzowii is typically found in rocky or arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
