Rubus amoenus var. hispanicus

Rubus amoenus var. hispanicus, commonly known as the Spanish raspberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe, resembling raspberries in appearance. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
