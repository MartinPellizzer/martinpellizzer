Rosa × chabertii

Rosa × chabertii, commonly known as the Chabert rose, is a hybrid shrub rose originating from France.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and is typically grown as a climbing or bush rose. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × chabertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its beauty and hardiness.
