Rhaphiolepis angustissima

Rhaphiolepis angustissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf rosebay, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
