Rhoicissus thunbergii

Rhoicissus thunbergii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
