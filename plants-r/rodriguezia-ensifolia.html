Rodriguezia ensifolia

Rodriguezia ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf rodriguezia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
