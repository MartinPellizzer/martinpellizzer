Ranunculus auricomus subsp. obscuroviridis

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

obscuroviridis, commonly known as the dark-green leaf buttercup, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers with prominent, showy sepals.

The subspecies is distinguished by its dark green foliage, which contrasts with the bright yellow petals. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in temperate regions with cool, humid climates.
