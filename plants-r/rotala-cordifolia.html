Rotala cordifolia

Rotala cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf rotala, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green and slightly waxy. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in shallow freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value.

Rotala cordifolia is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for aquatic life and improve water quality.
