Rosa sempervirens subsp. microphylla

Rosa sempervirens subsp.

microphylla, commonly known as the evergreen rose or microphylla rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and small, dark green, leathery leaves.

The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in spring and summer. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial shade.

Its evergreen nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in temperate climates.
