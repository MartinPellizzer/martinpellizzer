Roscoea debilis var. debilis

Roscoea debilis var. debilis, commonly known as the slender roscoea, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a short rhizome and produces striking, pale pink to lilac-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and delicate appearance, it is a sought-after plant among horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.
