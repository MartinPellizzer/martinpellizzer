Rosa × nitidula var. calcarea

Rosa × nitidula var. calcarea, commonly known as the calcarea rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically has glossy, dark green foliage that is often covered in a fine, whitish coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, though it may require protection in colder climates.
