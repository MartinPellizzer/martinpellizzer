Rhododendron eclecteum var. eclecteum

Rhododendron eclecteum var. eclecteum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range in color from white to pink and purple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable acidic soil and partial shade.
