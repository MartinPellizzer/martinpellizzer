Rosa × waitziana var. timeroyi

Rosa × waitziana var. timeroyi, commonly known as the Timeroy hybrid rose, is a botanical variety characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thornless canes.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming throughout the summer season. The plant is native to China and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding visual interest even when not in bloom.
