Rabdosia macrophylla

Rabdosia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and can grow up to 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
