Rosa elliptica f. klukii

Rosa elliptica f. klukii, commonly known as the Kluk's elliptic rose, is a unique horticultural form of the elliptic rose.

It is characterized by its elongated, elliptical-shaped flowers that are typically a deep, rich red. The plant produces fragrant blooms that are often borne singly on long, slender stems. It is a compact shrub with dark green, glossy foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
