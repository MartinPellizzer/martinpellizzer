Rhapis laosensis subsp. macrantha

Rhapis laosensis subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the Lao bamboo palm, is a clumping palm native to Southeast Asia. It features slender, arching leaves that are typically a deep green color, giving it a lush, tropical appearance.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
