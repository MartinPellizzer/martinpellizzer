Rosa oligogynia

Rosa oligogynia, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly the Alps and Carpathians. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Rosa oligogynia is well adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
