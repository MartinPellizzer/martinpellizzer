Rosa lutetiana var. glaucescens

Rosa lutetiana var. glaucescens, commonly known as the French pink or glaucous rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa lutetiana species.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive bluish-gray hue on the petals. The plant typically has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It is native to France and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

This variety thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
