Rosa × Gravetii

Garden rose, scientifically known as Rosa × gravetii, is a popular ornamental plant cherished for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature. This hybrid species has been cultivated for centuries, blending the traits of various rose species to produce a wide range of colors and forms. With its adaptability to different climates, it thrives in gardens around the world, making it a staple in horticulture. Its fragrant flowers and lush foliage add both beauty and fragrance to any landscape. Whether used in borders, containers, or as cut flowers, the garden rose remains a symbol of elegance and resilience.
