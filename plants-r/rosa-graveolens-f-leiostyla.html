Rosa graveolens f. leiostyla

Rosa graveolens f. leiostyla, commonly known as the white garden rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers.

It is a compact shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its flowers bloom in early summer and are often used in ornamental gardening.

It is valued for its delicate appearance and pleasant aroma.
