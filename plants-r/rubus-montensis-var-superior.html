Rubus montensis var. superior

Rubus montensis var. superior, commonly known as the superior raspberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, with a tart flavor. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and thorny stems, typical of the Rubus genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential use in local cuisines.
