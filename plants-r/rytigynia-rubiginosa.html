Rytigynia rubiginosa

Rytigynia rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed rytigynia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Rytigyniaceae and is characterized by its reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely known outside of botanical circles.
