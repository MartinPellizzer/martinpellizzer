Rosa hispanica var. escurialensis

Rosa hispanica var. escurialensis, commonly known as the Escurial rose, is a compact shrub native to Spain.

It features dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
