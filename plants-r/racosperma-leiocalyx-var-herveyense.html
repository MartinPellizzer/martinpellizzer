Racosperma leiocalyx var. herveyense

Racosperma leiocalyx var. herveyense, commonly known as Hervey's wattle, is a shrub native to Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is typically found in sandy soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments.

The variety is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological traits.
