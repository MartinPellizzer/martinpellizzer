Ranunculus linearis

Ranunculus linearis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions.

Ranunculus linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
