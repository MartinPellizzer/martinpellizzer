Rosa × polliniana var. umbellata

Rosa × polliniana var. umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features deep green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose varieties. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The variety is well-suited for container growing and can thrive in a range of soil types with adequate sunlight.
