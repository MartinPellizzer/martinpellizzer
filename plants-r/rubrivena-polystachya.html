Rubrivena polystachya

Rubrivena polystachya, commonly known as the red vein polystachya, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polystachyaceae.

It is characterized by its striking red veins in its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

Rubrivena polystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
