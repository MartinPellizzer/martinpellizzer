Rhamnus × intermedia subsp. istriaca

Rhamnus × intermedia subsp.

istriaca, commonly known as Istrian buckthorn, is a hybrid shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers.

The plant produces small, dark red berries that are a food source for various birds and mammals. It typically grows in woodland edges and rocky soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in regional flora.
