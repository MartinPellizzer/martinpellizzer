Rhus rigida

Rhus rigida, commonly known as the prickly ash or smooth sumac, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes other plants like poison ivy and mango. It is often found in woodlands, stream banks, and disturbed areas, and it can tolerate a range of soil conditions.
