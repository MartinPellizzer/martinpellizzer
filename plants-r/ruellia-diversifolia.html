Ruellia diversifolia

Ruellia diversifolia, commonly known as the variable ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.

Ruellia diversifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
