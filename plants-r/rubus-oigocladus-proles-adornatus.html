Rubus oigocladus proles adornatus

Rubus oigocladus proles adornatus, commonly known as the Ornate Raspberry, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves.

It produces small, aggregate fruits known as drupes, which are typically red or yellow when mature. The plant is native to temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually white or pink, appearing in clusters during the spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
