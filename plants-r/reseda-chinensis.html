Reseda chinensis

Reseda chinensis, commonly known as Chinese Reseda, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs.

Reseda chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
