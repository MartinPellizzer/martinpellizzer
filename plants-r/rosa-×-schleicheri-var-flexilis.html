Rosa × schleicheri var. flexilis

Rosa × schleicheri var. flexilis, commonly known as the flexible shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × schleicheri var. flexilis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
