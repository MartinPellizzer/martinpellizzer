Rosa mollis f. expallescens

Rosa mollis f. expallescens, commonly known as the soft rose or woolly rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa mollis species.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance.

This form is a natural mutation that showcases the genetic diversity within the Rosa genus.
