Rhododendron iyoense

Rhododendron iyoense, commonly known as the Iyo Island rhododendron, is a species native to the island of Iyo in Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from pink to red.

Rhododendron iyoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
