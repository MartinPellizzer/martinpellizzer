Rapanea Latifolia

Rapanea latifolia is a striking evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its broad, leathery leaves that provide a lush green canopy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
