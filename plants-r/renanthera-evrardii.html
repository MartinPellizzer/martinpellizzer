Renanthera evrardii

Renanthera evrardii, commonly known as the Evrard's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that serves as a landing platform for pollinators.

Renanthera evrardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or botanical gardens.
