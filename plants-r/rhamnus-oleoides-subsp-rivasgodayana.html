Rhamnus oleoides subsp. rivasgodayana

Rhamnus oleoides subsp.

rivasgodayana, commonly known as the Iberian buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically dark blue to black when mature. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean shrublands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.
