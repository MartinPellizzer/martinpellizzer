Rosa alpina var. saboiensis

Rosa alpina var. saboiensis, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand cold temperatures, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
