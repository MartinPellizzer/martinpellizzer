Rudgea ventuariana

Rudgea ventuariana, commonly known as the Brazilian peacock flower, is a striking plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

Due to habitat loss, Rudgea ventuariana is considered vulnerable and is a subject of conservation efforts in its native range.
