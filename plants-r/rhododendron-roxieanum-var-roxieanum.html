Rhododendron roxieanum var. roxieanum

Rhododendron roxieanum var. roxieanum, commonly known as the Roxieanum Rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 6 feet, making it a striking addition to woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
