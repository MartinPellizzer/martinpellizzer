Rubus × formosulus

Rubus × formosulus, commonly known as the Formosan raspberry, is a hybrid plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
