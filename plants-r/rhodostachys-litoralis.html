Rhodostachys litoralis

Rhodostachys litoralis, commonly known as the coastal rhodostachys, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is well adapted to saline and sandy environments, making it a hardy species found in maritime climates. Its root system helps stabilize dunes and prevent erosion.

Rhodostachys litoralis plays an important ecological role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
