Rosa × collina var. urbica

Rosa × collina var. urbica, commonly known as the urban roses, is a hybrid rose species that thrives in urban environments.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to withstand pollution and compacted soils. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical name reflects its origin from crosses between Rosa collina and other rose species.
