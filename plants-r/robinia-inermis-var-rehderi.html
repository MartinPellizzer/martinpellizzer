Robinia inermis var. rehderi

Robinia inermis var. rehderi, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous legume native to China.

It features compound leaves that are usually pinnately divided into numerous small, oval leaflets. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by long, slender pods containing flattened seeds. It is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing ability.

This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in urban environments.
