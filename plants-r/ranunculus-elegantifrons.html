Ranunculus elegantifrons

Ranunculus elegantifrons, commonly known as the elegant fronded buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers with bright yellow petals and a central cluster of stamens, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has delicate, trifoliate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Ranunculaceae family.
