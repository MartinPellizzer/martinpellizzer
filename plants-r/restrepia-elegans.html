Restrepia elegans

Restrepia elegans, commonly known as the elegant restrepia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle purple tinge. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Restrepia elegans is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
