Rhododendron liangshanicum

Rhododendron liangshanicum, commonly known as the Liangshan rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Liangshan region in Sichuan Province.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in mountainous areas with acidic soil and high humidity, preferring cool, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
