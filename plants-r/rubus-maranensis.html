Rubus maranensis

Rubus maranensis, commonly known as the Maraneng blackberry, is a species of bramble native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained, loamy conditions.

The botanical features of Rubus maranensis make it a notable species within the Rubus genus.
