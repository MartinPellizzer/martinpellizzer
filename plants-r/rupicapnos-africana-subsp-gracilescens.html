Rupicapnos africana subsp. gracilescens

Rupicapnos africana subsp.

gracilescens, commonly known as the slender African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and coastal plain regions.

The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
