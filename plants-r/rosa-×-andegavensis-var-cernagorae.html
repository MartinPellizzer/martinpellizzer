Rosa × andegavensis var. cernagorae

Rosa × andegavensis var. cernagorae, commonly known as the Cernagora rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, slightly hairy leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
