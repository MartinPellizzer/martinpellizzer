Rosa ramusculosa

Rosa ramusculosa, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. Rosa ramusculosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
