Rhagadiolus stellatus var. intermedius

Rhagadiolus stellatus var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate starflower, is a rare and ornamental plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is often sought after by horticulturists and botanists.
