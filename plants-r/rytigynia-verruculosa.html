Rytigynia verruculosa

Rytigynia verruculosa, commonly known as the roughleaf rytigynia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Rytigyniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
