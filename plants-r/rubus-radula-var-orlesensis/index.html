Rubus Radula Var. Orlesensis

The rubus radula var. orlesensis is a rare and unique variant of the common bramble plant. It is native to the mountainous regions of southeastern europe. This plant is known for its distinctive leaf shape and small, edible berries. it thrives in rocky and acidic soils, often found in secluded forest clearings. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
