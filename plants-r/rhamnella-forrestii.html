Rhamnella forrestii

Rhamnella forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or orange, which attract birds and other wildlife. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and floral display.

Rhamnella forrestii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for gardens and landscapes.
