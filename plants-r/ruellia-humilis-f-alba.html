Ruellia humilis f. alba

Ruellia humilis f. alba, commonly known as white ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, showy white flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, ornamental plant with a delicate floral display.
