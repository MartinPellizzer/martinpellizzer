Raputiarana

Raputiarana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dazzling display during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a favorite in both residential and public landscapes.
