Rhododendron × keditii

Rhododendron × keditii, commonly known as the Kediti rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist climates with acidic soil. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron × keditii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
