Rosa rubiginosa f. pallida

Rosa rubiginosa f. pallida, commonly known as the pale rose or pale shrub rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub-like species with thorny stems and small, pale pink to almost white flowers. The plant produces oval, orange-red fruits known as hips, which are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and persistent winter buds.
