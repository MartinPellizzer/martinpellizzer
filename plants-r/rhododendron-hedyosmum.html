Rhododendron hedyosmum

Rhododendron hedyosmum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features broad, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Rhododendron hedyosmum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
