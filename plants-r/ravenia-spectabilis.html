Ravenia spectabilis

Ravenia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy ravenia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, panicle-like inflorescences that display vibrant red or orange flowers, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Ravenia spectabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its showy floral displays.
