Rosa pimpinellifolia var. dunalis

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. dunalis, commonly known as the Dunal's rock rose, is a compact, shrubby species native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to reddish in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, often thornless stems that are well adapted to rocky and dry environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, with finely divided leaflets that give it a delicate appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
