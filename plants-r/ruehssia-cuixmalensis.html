Ruehssia cuixmalensis

Ruehssia cuixmalensis, commonly known as the Cuixmalensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its structure includes areoles, which are specialized structures that bear the spines and flowers.

Ruehssia cuixmalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
