Rhaphiolepis indica f. impressivena

Rhaphiolepis indica f. impressivena, commonly known as the impressivena rosemary.

This compact shrub features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly aromatic scent. It produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to China and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
