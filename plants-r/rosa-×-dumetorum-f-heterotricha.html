Rosa × dumetorum f. heterotricha

Rosa × dumetorum f. heterotricha, commonly known as the "hedge rose," is a hybrid species of rose native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody structure, simple or slightly lobed leaves, and an extensive root system that helps it thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
