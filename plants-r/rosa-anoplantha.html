Rosa anoplantha

Rosa anoplantha, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Rosa anoplantha is valued not only for its ornamental qualities but also for its potential use in traditional medicine.
