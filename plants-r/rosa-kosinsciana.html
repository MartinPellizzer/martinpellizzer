Rosa kosinsciana

Rosa kosinsciana, commonly known as the Kosinskian rose, is a species native to the Caucasus region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color. Rosa kosinsciana produces fragrant, single or semi-double blooms in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
