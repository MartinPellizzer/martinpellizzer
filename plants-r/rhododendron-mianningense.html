Rhododendron mianningense

Rhododendron mianningense, commonly known as the Mianning rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Sichuan province.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with acidic soil. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a significant role in the local biodiversity of its habitat.
