Rubus pseudopersicinus

Rubus pseudopersicinus, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, native to China. The plant is a shrub that produces small, dark red berries, which are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are typically compound, with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
