Rubus × insigniformis

Rubus × insigniformis, commonly known as the red-berried blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
