Rhododendron calleryi

Rhododendron calleryi, commonly known as the Chinese fringeflower, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive fringed appearance. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Rhododendron calleryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
