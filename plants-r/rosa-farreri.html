Rosa farreri

Rosa farreri, commonly known as the Farrer's rose, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets.

Rosa farreri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
