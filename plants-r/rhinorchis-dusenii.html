Rhinorchis dusenii

Rhinorchis dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a spurred lip and a compact, showy inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Rhinorchis dusenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for conservation and study.
