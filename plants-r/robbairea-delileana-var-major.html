Robbairea delileana var. major

Robbairea delileana var. major, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf burn. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to bloom throughout the year.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
