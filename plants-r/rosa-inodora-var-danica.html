Rosa inodora var. danica

Rosa inodora var. danica, commonly known as the Danish garden rose, is a compact shrub native to Europe.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a bushy growth habit, making it suitable for borders or containers. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
