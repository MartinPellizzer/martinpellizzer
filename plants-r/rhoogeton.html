Rhoogeton

Rhoogeton, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Rhoogeton is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and attractive foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
