Rosa pouzinii f. robusta

Rosa pouzinii f. robusta, commonly known as the robust form of the Pouzin's rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering.

It typically features large, fragrant pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has sturdy stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is often prized for its ornamental value and resilience in temperate climates.
