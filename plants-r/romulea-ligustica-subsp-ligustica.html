Romulea ligustica subsp. ligustica

Romulea ligustica subsp.

ligustica, commonly known as the Ligurian glory-of-the-snow, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in early spring.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and floral characteristics.
