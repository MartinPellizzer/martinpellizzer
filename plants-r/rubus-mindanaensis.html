Rubus mindanaensis

Rubus mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Mindanao. The plant is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pink, and it produces small, dark purple to black berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has potential uses in local agriculture and traditional medicine.
