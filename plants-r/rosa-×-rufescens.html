Rosa × rufescens

Rosa × rufescens, commonly known as the red rugosa rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its foliage is dark green and dense, providing good ground cover.

Rosa × rufescens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and resistance to disease.
