Rhaphiolepis prinoides

Rhaphiolepis prinoides, commonly known as the Japanese rosebay, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Rhaphiolepis prinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
