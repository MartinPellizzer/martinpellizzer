Rupicapnos oranensis

Rupicapnos oranensis, commonly known as the Oranese rockrose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of northern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure. It is often found in high-altitude habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
