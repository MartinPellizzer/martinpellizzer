Rebutia einsteinii var. conoidea

Rebutia einsteinii var. conoidea, commonly known as the Einstein's Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a rounded, segmented stem with spiny areoles, and it produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance. It requires well-draining soil and minimal water, making it an ideal choice for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
