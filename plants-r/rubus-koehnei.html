Rubus koehnei

Rubus koehnei, commonly known as the Koehne's bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits, known as raspberries, are edible and have a tart flavor.

Rubus koehnei is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for both its ornamental and culinary uses.
