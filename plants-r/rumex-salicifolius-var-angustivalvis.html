Rumex salicifolius var. angustivalvis

Rumex salicifolius var. angustivalvis, commonly known as narrow-leaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides, riverbanks, and in wet meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
