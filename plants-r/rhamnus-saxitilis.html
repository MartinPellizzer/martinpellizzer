Rhamnus saxitilis

Rhamnus saxitilis, commonly known as the saxifrage buckthorn, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white hair. Its flowers are greenish-yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, dark berries.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environments.
