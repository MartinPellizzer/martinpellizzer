Ruellia caroliniensis var. heteromorpha

Ruellia caroliniensis var. heteromorpha, commonly known as the Carolina rosemont, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland environments. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
