Ravenia shaferi var. ekmanii

Ravenia shaferi var. ekmanii, commonly known as the Ekman's ravenia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink, during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
