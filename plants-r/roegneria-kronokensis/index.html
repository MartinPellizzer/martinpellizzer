Roegneria Kronokensis

Roegneria kronokensis is a rare and intriguing grass species native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories and along stream banks. This plant is notable for its unique floral structure and its role in supporting local ecosystems. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
