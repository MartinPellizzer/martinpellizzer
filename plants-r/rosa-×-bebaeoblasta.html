Rosa × bebaeoblasta

Rosa × bebaeoblasta, commonly known as the Bebaeoblasta rose, is a hybrid rose species that combines traits from various Rosa species.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its beauty and hardiness.
