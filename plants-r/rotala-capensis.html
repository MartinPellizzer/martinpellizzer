Rotala capensis

Rotala capensis, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a tropical aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of small, bright green leaves that often have a red or purple underside. The plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

Rotala capensis is often used in water gardens and aquariums due to its ornamental value and ability to provide oxygen to the water.
