Rhododendron xishuiense

Rhododendron xishuiense, commonly known as the Xishui rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Xishui region in Hubei Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous areas with cool, moist climates. The plant features showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, often in shades of pink to red. Its leaves are elliptical and have a leathery texture, adapted to the humid, shaded environments it prefers.

Rhododendron xishuiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
