Rebutia diersiana var. atrovirens

Rebutia diersiana var. atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in small, spiny areoles, and it produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments. It requires minimal water and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
