Rubus reflexus var. hui

Rubus reflexus var. hui, commonly known as the Hui blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry species native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
