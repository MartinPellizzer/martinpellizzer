Rhus radaelijawel

Rhus radaelijawel, commonly known as the red-leafed sumac, is a deciduous shrub native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with multiple leaflets that turn vibrant shades of red in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that are attractive to birds. Rhus radaelijawel thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
