Romulea flava var. minor

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. This variant is distinguished by its smaller flowers and more compact growth compared to the typical form of Romulea flava.
