Rosa indica var. noisettiana

Rosa indica var. noisettiana, commonly known as the Noisettiana rose, is a variety of the Indian rose that is prized for its strong, sweet fragrance.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. The flowers are typically large, with five to seven petals, and come in shades of pink, red, and white. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in perfumery due to its aromatic properties.

It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.
