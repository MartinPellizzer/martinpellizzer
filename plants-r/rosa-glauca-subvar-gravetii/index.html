Rosa Glauca Subvar. Gravetii

Bluebush rose, scientifically known as Rosa glauca subvar. gravetii, is a resilient and striking shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. With its silvery-blue foliage and vibrant pink to red blooms, it stands out in harsh desert environments. This variety of rose is prized for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening. Despite its tough exterior, the Bluebush rose offers a beautiful and low-maintenance addition to any landscape.
