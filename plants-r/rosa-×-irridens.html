Rosa × irridens

Rosa × irridens, commonly known as the Persian rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant is native to the Caucasus region and has been cultivated for centuries for its ornamental value. It typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage.

Rosa × irridens prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
