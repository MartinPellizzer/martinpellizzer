Rohdea fimbriata

Rohdea fimbriata, commonly known as the fragrant lily or Chinese lily, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves with a distinctive wavy margin, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in a loose panicle. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Rohdea fimbriata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
