Rosularia marnieri

Rosularia marnieri, commonly known as the rock rose or stone rose, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit, with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Rosularia marnieri is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
