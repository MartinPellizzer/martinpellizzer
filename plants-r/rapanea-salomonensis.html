Rapanea salomonensis

Rapanea salomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands holly, is a small evergreen tree native to the South Pacific, particularly the Solomon Islands.

Rapanea salomonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
