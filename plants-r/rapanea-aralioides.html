Rapanea aralioides

Rapanea aralioides, commonly known as the African holly, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Rapanea aralioides is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
