Renanthera pulchella

Renanthera pulchella, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and grows as an epiphyte, typically on trees in humid environments. It requires bright, indirect light and high humidity to thrive.

Renanthera pulchella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
