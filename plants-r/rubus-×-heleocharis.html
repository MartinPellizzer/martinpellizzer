Rubus × heleocharis

Rubus × heleocharis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or dewberry.

It is a member of the Rubus genus, which includes various species of brambles and raspberries. This plant is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along the edges of forests.

The plant's botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
