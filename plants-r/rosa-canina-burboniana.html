Rosa canina burboniana

Rosa canina burboniana, commonly known as the Bourboniana rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in the spring. The plant's leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets. Its fruit, known as a hip, is a red, fuzzy capsule that contains several achenes.

Rosa canina burboniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery and herbal medicine.
