Rosa spinosissima mitis

Rosa spinosissima mitis, commonly known as the soft-spined rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, thornless stems and small, fragrant pink flowers. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
