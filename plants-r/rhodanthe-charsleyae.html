Rhodanthe charsleyae

Rhodanthe charsleyae, commonly known as the Yellow Rocket, is a flowering plant native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimetres. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in late summer to autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Rhodanthe charsleyae is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
