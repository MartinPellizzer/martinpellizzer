Rosa lutetiana var. sphaerica

Rosa lutetiana var. sphaerica, commonly known as the spherical shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features small, rounded, thornless stems that form a dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hair.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
