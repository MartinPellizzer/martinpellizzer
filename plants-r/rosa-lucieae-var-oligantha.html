Rosa lucieae var. oligantha

Rosa lucieae var. oligantha, commonly known as the sparse-flowered rock rose, is a rare and hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or arid environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that are densely arranged along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
