Rosa blyttii var. aasgaardstrandica

Rosa blyttii var. aasgaardstrandica, commonly known as the Aasgaardstrand rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the coastal regions of Norway.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically during late summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a dense, thorny stem structure typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
