Rassia

Rassia, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. Rassia is prized for its long blooming period and ability to produce vibrant flowers in a variety of colors.
