Rodriguezia pulchra

Rodriguezia pulchra, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking pink and red bracts that surround its small yellow flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rodriguezia pulchra is relatively low maintenance but requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish.
