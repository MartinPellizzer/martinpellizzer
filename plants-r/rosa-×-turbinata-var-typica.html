Rosa × turbinata var. typica

Rosa × turbinata var. typica, commonly known as the Turbinated Rose, is a hybrid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, turban-shaped blooms that are typically pink to red in color. The plant features thorny stems and serrated, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their ornamental value.
