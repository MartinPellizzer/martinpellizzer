Romulea keniensis

Romulea keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan glory-of-the-snow, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces tuberous roots and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Romulea keniensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant, star-shaped blooms.
