Rhodax

Rhodax, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Rhodax thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
