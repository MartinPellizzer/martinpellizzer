Rhododendron rubrobracteatum

Rhododendron rubrobracteatum, commonly known as the red bract rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in dense forests and is often found at elevations between 1,000 and 2,500 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
