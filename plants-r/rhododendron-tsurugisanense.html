Rhododendron tsurugisanense

Rhododendron tsurugisanense, commonly known as the Tsurugisanense azalea, is a species native to Japan.

It is a shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool and moist environments. The plant features dense clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron tsurugisanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
