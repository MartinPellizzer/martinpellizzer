Rafflesia sharifah-hapsahiae

Rafflesia sharifah-hapsahiae, commonly known as the Sarawak Rafflesia, is a rare and striking member of the Rafflesia genus, known for its massive, fleshy花朵 that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter.

The plant is native to the rainforests of Borneo, specifically in the state of Sarawak, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers emit a strong, unpleasant odor reminiscent of decaying flesh, which attracts flies and other insects for pollination.
