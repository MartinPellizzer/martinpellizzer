Ruschia maxima

Ruschia maxima, commonly known as the giant ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tubular leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid regions.
