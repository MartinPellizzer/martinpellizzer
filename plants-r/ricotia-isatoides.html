Ricotia isatoides

Ricotia isatoides, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and its leaves are alternate, lobed, and covered in fine hairs. Ricotia isatoides thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

It is sometimes considered a weed but has historical uses in traditional medicine.
