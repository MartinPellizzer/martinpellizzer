Rubus fraxinifolius var. yushunii

Rubus fraxinifolius var. yushunii, commonly known as the Yushunii blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, dark purple to black fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are usually thorny. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
