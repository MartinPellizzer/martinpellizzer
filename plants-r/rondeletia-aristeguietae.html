Rondeletia aristeguietae

Rondeletia aristeguietae, commonly known as the Andean rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in cloud forests or rocky areas. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, with five petals and a prominent, spurred lower petal. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
