Ranunculus yinshanensis

Ranunculus yinshanensis, commonly known as the Yinshan buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive central pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and has palmate leaves with three to five lobes. It is a herbaceous perennial that blooms in early spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
