Raoulia subsericea

Raoulia subsericea, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a yellowish-green color.

Raoulia subsericea is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in some areas.
