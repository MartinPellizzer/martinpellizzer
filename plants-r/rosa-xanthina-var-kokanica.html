Rosa xanthina var. kokanica

Rosa xanthina var. kokanica, commonly known as the Kokanica yellow rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and aroma.
