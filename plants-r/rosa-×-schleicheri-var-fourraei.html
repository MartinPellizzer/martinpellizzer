Rosa × schleicheri var. fourraei

Rosa × schleicheri var. fourraei, commonly known as the Fourrae's rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from South Africa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in garden settings.
