Reseda undata var. leucantha

Reseda undata var. leucantha, commonly known as white sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, clustered flowers and pinnately lobed leaves.
