Rubus × pseudodilatatifolius

Rubus × pseudodilatatifolius, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus × fruticosus (bramble), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant features thorny stems, alternate leaves with serrated margins, and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. Its fruits are aggregate berries, typically dark purple to black when mature, and are prized for their flavor and culinary uses.

This hybrid is known for its vigorous growth and adaptability to various soil types.
