Rosa sempervirens var. anemoniflora

Rosa sempervirens var. anemoniflora, commonly known as the Anemone-flowered Rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of pale pink to white flowers that resemble anemones. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has thorny stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive flowers and foliage, which provide year-round interest.
