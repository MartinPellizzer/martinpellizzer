Rosa × dumetorum f. globata

Rosa × dumetorum f. globata, commonly known as the globular shrub rose, is a compact, thornless variety of rose that exhibits a rounded, bushy growth habit.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate floral display to gardens. The plant is characterized by its dense foliage and short, upright stems, making it ideal for container gardening or as a border plant. It is relatively disease-resistant and requires minimal pruning, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Its unique form and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
