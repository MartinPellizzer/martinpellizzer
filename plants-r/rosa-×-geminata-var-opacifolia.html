Rosa × geminata var. opacifolia

Rosa × geminata var. opacifolia, commonly known as the opacifolia rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that is notably thick and leathery, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
