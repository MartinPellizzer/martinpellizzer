Ruellia geminiflora var. nudipes

Ruellia geminiflora var. nudipes, commonly known as the naked-stem ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems that are often leafless near the base. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
