Rotang latispinus

Rotang latispinus, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction. It also produces a valuable oil from its seeds, used in cosmetics and traditional medicine.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
