Rhododendron nantouense

Rhododendron nantouense, commonly known as the Nantou rhododendron, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is often found in mountainous regions, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
