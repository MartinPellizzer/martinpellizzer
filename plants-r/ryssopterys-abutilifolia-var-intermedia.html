Ryssopterys abutilifolia var. intermedia

Ryssopterys abutilifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate sarsaparilla, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its leaves are large and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually green or yellowish in color.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
