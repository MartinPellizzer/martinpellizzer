Rosa × manettii

Rosa × manettii, commonly known as the Manettii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Rosa × manettii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and as a hedge plant.
