Rosa borkhausenii

Rosa borkhausenii, commonly known as the Borkhausen rose, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub-like plant with thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing pink to red flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa borkhausenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
