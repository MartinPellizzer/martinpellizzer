Rubia chinensis

Rubia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese cobweb plant, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.
