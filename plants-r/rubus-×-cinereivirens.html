Rubus × cinereivirens

Rubus × cinereivirens, commonly known as the silverleaf blackberry, is a hybrid plant in the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, dark berries that are typically edible. It is native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States and thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is often appreciated for its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
