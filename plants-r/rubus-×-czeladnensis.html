Rubus × czeladnensis

Rubus × czeladnensis, commonly known as the Czeladnenskaya blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color. The plant produces small, oval leaves with serrated edges and a dense, bushy growth habit. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible berries.

The hybrid nature of this plant contributes to its adaptability and resilience in various soil conditions.
