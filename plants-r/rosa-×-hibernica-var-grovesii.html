Rosa × hibernica var. grovesii

Rosa × hibernica var. grovesii, commonly known as the Grovesii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, leathery foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.

This variety is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in partial shade to full sun.
