Restrepia brachypus

Restrepia brachypus, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Restrepia brachypus has a short rhizome and is known for its ability to thrive in cooler climates.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
