Rhodiola hideoi

Rhodiola hideoi, commonly known as the Hideo's rhodiola, is a rare and lesser-known species of the Rhodiola genus.

It is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, particularly in parts of China and Russia. This plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
