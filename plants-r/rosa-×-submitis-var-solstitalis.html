Rosa × submitis var. solstitalis

Rosa × submitis var. solstitalis, commonly known as the Solstice Rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its striking, deep red flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. The plant is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both landscape design and cut flower arrangements.
