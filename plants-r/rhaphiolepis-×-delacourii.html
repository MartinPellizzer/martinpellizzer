Rhaphiolepis × delacourii

Rhaphiolepis × delacourii, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features showy, orange-red berries that persist through winter, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is evergreen, with a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for hedges or container planting.

The berries are toxic to humans and animals, so care should be taken when handling or planting this species.
