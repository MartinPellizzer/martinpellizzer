Rosa cryptostylis

Rosa cryptostylis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in coastal and sandy environments. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.

It produces oval, red-brown fruits known as hips, which are rich in vitamin C. Rosa cryptostylis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
