Rosa alpina var. reversa

Rosa alpina var. reversa, commonly known as the alpine rose reverse form, is a rare variant of the alpine rose.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe. This plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
