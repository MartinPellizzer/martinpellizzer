Rhododendron serpyllifolium f. usukiunzen

Rhododendron serpyllifolium f. usukiunzen, commonly known as the Usuki-unzen form of serpyllifolium rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage.

It produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to tolerate partial shade.
