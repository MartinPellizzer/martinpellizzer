Ranunculus arachnoideus

Ranunculus arachnoideus, commonly known as the spider lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving them a soft appearance.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
