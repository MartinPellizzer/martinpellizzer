Rosa infraenandri

Rosa infraenandri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, serrated leaves and fragrant pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often found in woodland edges and rocky habitats.

Rosa infraenandri is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in horticulture.
