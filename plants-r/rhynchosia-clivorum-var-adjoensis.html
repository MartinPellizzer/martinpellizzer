Rhynchosia clivorum var. adjoensis

Rhynchosia clivorum var. adjoensis, commonly known as the Adjoensis climbing bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. Its climbing habit is supported by tendrils, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional agriculture.
