Rosa × assurgens

Rosa × assurgens, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa chinensis.

It is characterized by its ability to produce flowers continuously throughout the growing season, making it highly valued in horticulture. The plant typically features upright, thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage. Its flowers come in a wide range of colors, including pink, red, white, and yellow, often with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa × assurgens is well-suited for coastal areas due to its salt tolerance and resilience to harsh weather conditions.
