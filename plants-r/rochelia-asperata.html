Rochelia asperata

Rochelia asperata, commonly known as the rough rochelia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Rochelia asperata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
