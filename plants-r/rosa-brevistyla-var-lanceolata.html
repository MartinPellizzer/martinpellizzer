Rosa brevistyla var. lanceolata

Rosa brevistyla var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or as a specimen plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cultivated landscapes.
