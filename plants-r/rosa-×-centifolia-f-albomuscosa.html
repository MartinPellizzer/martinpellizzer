Rosa × centifolia f. albomuscosa

Rosa × centifolia f. albomuscosa, commonly known as the white musk rose, is a fragrant hybrid rose characterized by its white petals and strong, musky scent.

It is a cultivar of the centifolia rose, known for its numerous petals and ornamental value. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and flower production.

This variety is often cultivated for its aromatic flowers used in perfumery and floral arrangements.
