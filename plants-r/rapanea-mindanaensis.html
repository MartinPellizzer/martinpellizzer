Rapanea mindanaensis

Rapanea mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland to montane rainforests, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. Its fruits are small, woody drupes that contain a single seed, and it plays a role in the ecological diversity of its habitat.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
