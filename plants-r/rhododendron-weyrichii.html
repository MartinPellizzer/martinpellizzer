Rhododendron weyrichii

Rhododendron weyrichii, commonly known as the Weyrich rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayas, particularly found in regions of Nepal, India, and Bhutan.

It is a shrub that typically grows at high elevations, often in mountainous and alpine environments. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from pink to red, and they bloom in early spring.

Rhododendron weyrichii is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
