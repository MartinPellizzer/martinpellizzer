Rosa × harisonii var. vorbergii

Rosa × harisonii var. vorbergii, commonly known as the Vorberg rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to the southeastern United States.

It features semi-evergreen foliage that turns bronze in winter, providing year-round visual interest. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink flowers with a slight red flush, blooming in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
