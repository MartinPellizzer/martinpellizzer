Rhynchospora terminalis var. crassipes

Rhynchospora terminalis var. crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

Its morphology is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, with rhizomes that help it spread and establish in wetland ecosystems.
