Ranunculus Aequalis

Ranunculus aequalis is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its delicate petals and distinctive leaf patterns make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds that require caution when handling. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and care requirements of this unique plant.
