Rosa spinosissima f. vallotii

Rosa spinosissima f. vallotii, commonly known as the Vallot's prickly rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with five petals and a prominent central pistil. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in gardens and natural landscapes.
