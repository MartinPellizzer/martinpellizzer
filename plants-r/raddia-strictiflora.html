Raddia strictiflora

Raddia strictiflora, commonly known as the rigid-flowered raddia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, upright leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple. It blooms during the cooler months, usually from late winter to early spring.

Raddia strictiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
