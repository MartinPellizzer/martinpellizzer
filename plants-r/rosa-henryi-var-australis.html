Rosa henryi var. australis

Rosa henryi var. australis, commonly known as the Australian rose, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features dense, thorny stems and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in garden settings.
