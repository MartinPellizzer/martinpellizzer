Rubus × semibloxamii

Rubus × semibloxamii, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a vigorous shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves that are typically trifoliate. It produces dark purple to black berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. The plant is native to the coastal regions of California and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental value and its role in local ecosystems.
