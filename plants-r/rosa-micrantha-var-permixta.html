Rosa micrantha var. permixta

Rosa micrantha var. permixta, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a variety characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming.

It features semi-double to fully double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage and thorny stems typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and fragrance.
