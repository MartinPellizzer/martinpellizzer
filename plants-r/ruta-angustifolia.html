Ruta angustifolia

Ruta angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Ruta angustifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal remedies.
