Ricinodendron staudtii

Ricinodendron staudtii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to central and western Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has a tall, straight trunk with smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing flat, elliptical seeds.
