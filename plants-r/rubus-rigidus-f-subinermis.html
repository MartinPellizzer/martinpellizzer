Rubus rigidus f. subinermis

Rubus rigidus f. subinermis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed blackberry.

This botanical variant is characterized by its slightly less prickly stems compared to the typical Rubus rigidus. It produces small, dark purple to black berries that are often used in jams and desserts. The plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, with compound leaves and a dense growth habit.
