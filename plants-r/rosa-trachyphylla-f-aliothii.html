Rosa trachyphylla f. aliothii

Rosa trachyphylla f. aliothii, commonly known as the aliothii form of the rough rose, is a horticultural variant of the species Rosa trachyphylla.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a strong, pleasant fragrance. This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
