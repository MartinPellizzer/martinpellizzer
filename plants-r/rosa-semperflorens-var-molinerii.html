Rosa semperflorens var. molinerii

Rosa semperflorens var. molinerii, commonly known as the Molinerii everblooming rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. This variety is known for its exceptional cold hardiness and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The flowers are borne on long, slender stems and are often used in floral arrangements due to their delicate appearance.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
