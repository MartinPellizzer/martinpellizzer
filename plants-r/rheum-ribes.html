Rheum ribes

Rheum ribes, commonly known as white sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, woodland areas. Rheum ribes produces edible leaves that have a tangy, sour flavor, similar to rhubarb.

It is also known for its medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
