Rosa polyantha var. glabrifoliolata

Rosa polyantha var. glabrifoliolata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved polyantha rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant flowering.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming repeatedly throughout the growing season. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves with reduced leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is well-suited for garden borders and containers due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

This variety is prized for its disease resistance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
