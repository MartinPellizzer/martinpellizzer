Rhododendron pronum

Rhododendron pronum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron pronum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
