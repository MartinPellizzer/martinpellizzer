Roegneria linczevskii

Roegneria linczevskii, commonly known as the Linczevskii's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are usually green to purple in hue. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Roegneria linczevskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
