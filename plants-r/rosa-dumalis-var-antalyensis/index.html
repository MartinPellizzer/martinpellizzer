Rosa Dumalis Var. Antalyensis

Rosa dumalis var. antalyensis is a rare and fascinating species of rose native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. This variant is known for its delicate pink flowers and fragrant scent, making it a sought-after plant among horticulturists. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment to flourish. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and is often protected in conservation programs. Its unique beauty and ecological significance highlight the importance of preserving such rare botanical treasures.
