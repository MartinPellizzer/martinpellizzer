Rosa sinica var. fortuneana

Rosa sinica var. fortuneana, commonly known as the Chinese rose or Fortune's rose, is a shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and thorny stems. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

This variety is also appreciated for its adaptability to various soil types and climates.
