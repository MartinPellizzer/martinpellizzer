Russelia alternifolia

Russelia alternifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces clusters of tubular, violet to purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Russelia alternifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds.
