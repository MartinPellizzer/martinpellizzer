Ruschia griquensis

Ruschia griquensis, commonly known as the Grootfontein ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. Its small, star-shaped flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Ruschia griquensis is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.
