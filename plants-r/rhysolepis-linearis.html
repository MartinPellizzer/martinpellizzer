Rhysolepis linearis

Rhysolepis linearis, commonly known as the slender rock fern, is a species of fern native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that grow in a dense, compact form. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its fronds are typically a bright green color and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Rhysolepis linearis is a low-maintenance plant that is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
