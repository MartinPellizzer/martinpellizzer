Rosa micrantha f. diminuta

Rosa micrantha f. diminuta, commonly known as the miniature shrub rose, is a compact, thornless rose species native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a dense, bushy growth habit. It is well-suited for container gardening and small garden spaces due to its compact size.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
