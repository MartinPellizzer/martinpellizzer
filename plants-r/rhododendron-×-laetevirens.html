Rhododendron × laetevirens

Rhododendron × laetevirens, commonly known as the purple laurel.

It is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the eastern Himalayas, characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates. Its flowers range in color from pink to deep purple, blooming in early spring.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in specific garden conditions.
