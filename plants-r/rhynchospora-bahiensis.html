Rhynchospora bahiensis

Rhynchospora bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptation to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.
