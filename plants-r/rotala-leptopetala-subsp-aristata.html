Rotala leptopetala subsp. aristata

Rotala leptopetala subsp.

aristata, commonly known as the arrowhead water plant, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia. It features small, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy coating.

The plant produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate floral touch to its appearance. It thrives in shallow water and is often used in water gardens for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth makes it suitable for pond filtration and as a decorative element in aquariums.
