Ruteria tenuifolia

Ruteria tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ruteria, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are small, dry berries that contain several seeds.

Ruteria tenuifolia is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
