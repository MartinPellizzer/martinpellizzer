Rhododendron jiewhoei

Rhododendron jiewhoei, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, with a dense covering of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, appearing in clusters and blooming in early spring.

Rhododendron jiewhoei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
