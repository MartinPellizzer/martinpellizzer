Ribes peruvianum

Ribes peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian gooseberry, is a shrub native to the Andes region of South America.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae, which includes other species of gooseberries and currants. The plant produces small, yellow or orange berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.
