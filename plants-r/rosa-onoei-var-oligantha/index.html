Rosa Onoei Var. Oligantha

Rosa onoei var. oligantha is a rare and delicate species of flowering plant native to Japan. It is known for its small, clustered blooms and compact growth habit. This variety is often found in shaded, moist environments within its natural habitat. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after addition to specialized gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
