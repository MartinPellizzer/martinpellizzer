Ricotia tenera

Ricotia tenera, commonly known as the silverleaf ricotia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, giving it a silvery appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
