Raimannia mollissima

Raimannia mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist lowland areas.

Raimannia mollissima is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
