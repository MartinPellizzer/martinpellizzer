Rhaponticoides ruthenica var. binnatifida

Rhaponticoides ruthenica var. binnatifida, commonly known as the slender rhubarb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are typically reddish in color and covered with soft, hairy foliage. The leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a distinctive reddish tint, while the flowers are small and clustered in panicles. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that give way to reddish-brown capsules containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
