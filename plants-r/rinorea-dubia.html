Rinorea dubia

Rinorea dubia, commonly known as the silverleaf rinorea, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that often have a silvery or grayish underside, giving it its common name. Rinorea dubia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
