Ribes macrocalyx

Ribes macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red or orange when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
