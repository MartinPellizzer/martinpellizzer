Rinorea mezilii

Rinorea mezilii, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Rinorea mezilii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
