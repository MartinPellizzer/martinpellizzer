Rosa mollis f. argutella

Rosa mollis f. argutella, commonly known as the soft rose or silky rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a variant of Rosa mollis, characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and delicate pink flowers. This plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.

The species is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
