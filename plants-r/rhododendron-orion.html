Rhododendron orion

Rhododendron orion, commonly known as the Orion rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its flowers are usually a vibrant red or pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rhododendron orion prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
