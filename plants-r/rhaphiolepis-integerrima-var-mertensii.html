Rhaphiolepis integerrima var. mertensii

Rhaphiolepis integerrima var. mertensii, commonly known as the Mertens' rosebay rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that turn red in the autumn. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red during the spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and seasonal foliage changes.
