Rosa charmeli

Rosa charmeli, commonly known as the Turkish rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the regions of Turkey, the Balkans, and parts of the Middle East. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated edge.

Rosa charmeli is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for both its flowers and its fragrance.
