Rosa Canina F. Ambigua

Rosa canina f. ambigua is a unique variant of the dog rose known for its distinct floral characteristics. This form exhibits an unusual growth pattern that sets it apart from typical Rosa canina plants. It thrives in specific ecological conditions, making it a rare and sought-after specimen. Its historical significance in horticulture adds to its appeal among plant enthusiasts. Understanding this form offers insight into the adaptability and diversity of rose species.
