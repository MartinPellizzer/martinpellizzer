Rollinia surinamensis

Rollinia surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam starfruit, is a tropical plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Suriname and neighboring regions. The plant produces a distinctive fruit with a star-shaped appearance when mature, which is often eaten fresh or used in culinary applications. Botanically, it features simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers.

The fruit's unique morphology and flavor make it a notable species in tropical botany.
