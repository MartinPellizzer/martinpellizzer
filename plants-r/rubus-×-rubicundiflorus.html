Rubus × rubicundiflorus

Rubus × rubicundiflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
