Racosperma proximum

Racosperma proximum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seed pods are cylindrical and contain several small, hard seeds.
