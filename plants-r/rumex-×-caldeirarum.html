Rumex × caldeirarum

Rumex × caldeirarum, commonly known as the hybrid sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-veined. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and edible leaves, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
