Ruschia obtusifolia

Ruschia obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaved Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
