Rotala Indica

Rotala indica is a popular aquatic plant known for its vibrant red and green foliage. It thrives in freshwater environments and is commonly used in aquariums and ponds. This plant is easy to care for and can grow rapidly under proper conditions. Its compact size makes it ideal for foreground planting in planted tanks. Rotala indica adds both aesthetic value and ecological benefits to aquatic ecosystems.
