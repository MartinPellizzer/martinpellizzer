Rupicapnos numidica subsp. praetermissa

Rupicapnos numidica subsp.

praetermissa, commonly known as the Moroccan rock rose, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is characterized by its woody stems and small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens with well-drained soil.
