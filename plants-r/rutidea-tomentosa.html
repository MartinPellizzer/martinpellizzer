Rutidea tomentosa

Rutidea tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly legume, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three leaflets that are covered in a soft, hairy texture.

Rutidea tomentosa is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
