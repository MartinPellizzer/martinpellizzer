Rollinia glaziovii

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a fleshy, star-shaped fruit that is typically green when unripe and turns yellow or red when mature.
