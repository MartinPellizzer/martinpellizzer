Rumex × callianthemus

Rumex × callianthemus, commonly known as the hybrid sorrel, is a perennial plant that exhibits characteristics of both Rumex and Callianthemum species.

It typically features heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin, and its foliage is often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Due to its hybrid nature, it can exhibit a wide range of morphological variations.
