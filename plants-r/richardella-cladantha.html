Richardella cladantha

Richardella cladantha, commonly known as the desert star or desert star cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, globular shape. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in the summer months.

It is well adapted to drought conditions, with reduced leaves and thick, fleshy stems for water storage.
