Rosa × karakalensis

Rosa × karakalensis, commonly known as the Karakalinsky rose, is a hybrid species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its thorny stems and dense foliage make it a hardy shrub suitable for ornamental gardening.

Rosa × karakalensis is also valued for its use in perfume and culinary applications due to its aromatic properties.
