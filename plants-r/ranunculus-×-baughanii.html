Ranunculus × baughanii

Ranunculus × baughanii, commonly known as the Baughan's buttercup, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are upright and somewhat woody.

Ranunculus × baughanii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
