Ribesium rubrum

Ribesium rubrum, commonly known as the red campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Ribesium rubrum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
