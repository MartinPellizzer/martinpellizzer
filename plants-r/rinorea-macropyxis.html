Rinorea macropyxis

Rinorea macropyxis, commonly known as the giant leaf rinorea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
