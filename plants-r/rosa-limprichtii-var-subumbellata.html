Rosa limprichtii var. subumbellata

Rosa limprichtii var. subumbellata, commonly known as the subumbellate rose, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and thrives in cool, moist climates. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare species in the wild.
