Ruellia morongii

Ruellia morongii, commonly known as Morong's ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Ruellia morongii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
