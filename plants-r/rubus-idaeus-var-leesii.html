Rubus idaeus var. leesii

Rubus idaeus var. leesii, commonly known as the Leesii raspberry, is a variant of the red raspberry that exhibits distinct botanical characteristics.

It is a perennial shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, known for its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe, though some cultivars may show variations in color. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its flavorful berries and ornamental value in gardens.
