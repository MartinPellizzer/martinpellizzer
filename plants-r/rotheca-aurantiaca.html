Rotheca aurantiaca

Rotheca aurantiaca, commonly known as the goldenrope, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flower heads. Rotheca aurantiaca is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits to pollinators.
