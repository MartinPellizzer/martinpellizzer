Rosa × collina var. clusiana

Rosa × collina var. clusiana, commonly known as the Clusian rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically produces thorny stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
