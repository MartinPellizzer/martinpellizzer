Roulinia acrotricha

Roulinia acrotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flower heads. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces three-sided, cylindrical stems.
