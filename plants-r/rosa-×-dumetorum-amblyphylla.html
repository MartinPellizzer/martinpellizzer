Rosa × dumetorum amblyphylla

Rosa × dumetorum amblyphylla, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice among gardeners in cooler climates.
