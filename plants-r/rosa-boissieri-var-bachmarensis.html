Rosa boissieri var. bachmarensis

Rosa boissieri var. bachmarensis, commonly known as the Bachmaren rose, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Caucasus region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
