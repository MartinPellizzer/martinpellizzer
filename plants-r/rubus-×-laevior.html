Rubus × laevior

Rubus × laevior, commonly known as the smooth blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thornless stems and dark purple to black berries. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Its foliage is usually serrated with a medium green color, and it produces clusters of small, edible fruits.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both commercial and home gardening purposes.
