Regalia goliath

Regalia goliath, commonly known as the giant lily, is a striking plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to three feet in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine due to their purported healing properties. It thrives in warm, moist environments and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical name, *Lilium regale*, reflects its regal appearance and its status as a rare and sought-after species.
