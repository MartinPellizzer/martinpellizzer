Rapuntium debile

Rapuntium debile, commonly known as the slender watercress, is a small aquatic plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has small, arrow-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating on the water's surface. Its flowers are tiny and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Rapuntium debile is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich waters.
