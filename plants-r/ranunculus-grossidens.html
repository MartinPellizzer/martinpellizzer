Ranunculus grossidens

Ranunculus grossidens, commonly known as the large-flowered buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers with numerous petals and a central cluster of stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a hairy surface that aids in water retention.

Ranunculus grossidens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
