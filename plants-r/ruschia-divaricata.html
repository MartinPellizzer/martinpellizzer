Ruschia divaricata

Ruschia divaricata, commonly known as the variegated Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used in rock gardens or as a bedding plant.
