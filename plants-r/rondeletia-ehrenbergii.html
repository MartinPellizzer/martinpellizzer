Rondeletia ehrenbergii

Rondeletia ehrenbergii, commonly known as the red spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
