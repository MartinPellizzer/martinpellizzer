Rhododendron ornatum

Rhododendron ornatum, commonly known as the Japanese azalea, is a flowering shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.
