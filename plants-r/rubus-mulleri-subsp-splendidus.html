Rubus mulleri subsp. splendidus

Rubus mulleri subsp.

splendidus, commonly known as the splendid raspberry, is a shrub native to China. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its biennial growth habit.

The plant produces bright red, aggregate fruits that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated margins, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its medicinal properties.
