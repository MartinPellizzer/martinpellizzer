Riedelia graminea var. graminea

Riedelia graminea var. graminea, commonly known as the grassy riedelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare and requires careful cultivation.
