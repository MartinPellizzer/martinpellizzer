Robiquetia punctata

Robiquetia punctata, commonly known as the spotted bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple, with distinctive yellow spots on the petals. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Robiquetia punctata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
