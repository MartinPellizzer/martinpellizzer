Rebutia pseudominuscula

Rebutia pseudominuscula, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, globular stem that is typically green or blue-green in color, with a distinct, colorful crown that can range from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Rebutia pseudominuscula requires well-draining soil and full sun to thrive, making it a relatively easy plant to care for.
