Rubus oxyphyllus

Rubus oxyphyllus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. The plant features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are pinkish-white and give way to bright red berries that are rich in antioxidants.

The botanical characteristics of Rubus oxyphyllus make it a valuable species for both ecological and culinary purposes.
