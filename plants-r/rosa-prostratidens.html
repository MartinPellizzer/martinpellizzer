Rosa prostratidens

Rosa prostratidens, commonly known as the prostrate dog rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of less than 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with five to seven leaflets that are oval-shaped and serrated.

Rosa prostratidens is often found in rocky or coastal areas and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
