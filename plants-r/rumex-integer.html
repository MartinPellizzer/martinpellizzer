Rumex integer

Rumex integer, commonly known as broadleaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish flowers that develop into reddish-brown seeds. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

Rumex integer is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
