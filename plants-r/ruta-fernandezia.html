Ruta fernandezia

Ruta fernandezia, commonly known as the Fernandez's rue, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like citrus and rue. The plant features small, green, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or white and are clustered in umbels.

Ruta fernandezia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, aromatic scent.
