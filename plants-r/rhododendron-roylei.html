Rhododendron roylei

Rhododendron roylei, commonly known as the Kashmiri rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions at elevations between 1,500 and 3,500 meters. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant.

Rhododendron roylei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
