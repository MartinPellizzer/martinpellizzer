Rosa capnoides

Rosa capnoides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa capnoides is drought-tolerant and well-suited for xeriscaping in arid climates.
