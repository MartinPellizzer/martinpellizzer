Rosa × subcristata

Rosa × subcristata, commonly known as the Shrub Rose, is a hybrid rose species prized for its hardiness and ornamental value.

It typically grows as a compact shrub with multiple stems, featuring dense, thorny foliage. The plant produces abundant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a fragrant aroma. It is well-suited for garden borders, hedges, and containers due to its adaptability to various soil types.

Rosa × subcristata is also valued for its disease resistance and ability to thrive in full sun.
