Ruschia clavata

Ruschia clavata, commonly known as the clustered Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
