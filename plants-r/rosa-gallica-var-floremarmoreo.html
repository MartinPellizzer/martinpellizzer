Rosa gallica var. floremarmoreo

Rosa gallica var. floremarmoreo, commonly known as the Marmalade Rose, is a hybrid rose species prized for its unique floral characteristics.

It features double blooms with a distinctive marmalade-yellow color, often with a subtle pink or white overlay. The plant is native to the Caucasus region and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It has a compact growth habit and produces a strong, pleasant fragrance.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
