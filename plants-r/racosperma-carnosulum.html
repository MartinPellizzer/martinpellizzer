Racosperma carnosulum

Racosperma carnosulum, commonly known as the woolly wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Racosperma carnosulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
