Rubus exsul

Rubus exsul, commonly known as the desert raspberry, is a shrub native to arid regions of North America.

It produces small, edible berries that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it a resilient species in harsh environments.
