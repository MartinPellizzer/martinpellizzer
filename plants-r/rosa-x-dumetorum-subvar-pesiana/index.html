Rosa × Dumetorum Subvar. Pesiana

Rosa × dumetorus subvar. pesiana is a lesser-known but fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It belongs to the rose family, known for its thorny stems and fragrant blooms. This subvariety is distinguished by its compact growth and dark, almost blackish foliage. While not as widely cultivated as other roses, it holds ecological and horticultural value. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike.
