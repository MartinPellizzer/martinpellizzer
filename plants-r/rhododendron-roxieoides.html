Rhododendron roxieoides

Rhododendron roxieoides, commonly known as the Roxie rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
