Rubus subvulsus

Rubus subvulsus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of brambles belonging to the Rubus genus in the Rosaceae family. This plant is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its edible berries.
