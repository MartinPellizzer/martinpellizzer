Rourea splendida

Rourea splendida, commonly known as the splendour rourea, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
