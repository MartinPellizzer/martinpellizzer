Ruellia cordifolia

Ruellia cordifolia, commonly known as heartleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Ruellia cordifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
