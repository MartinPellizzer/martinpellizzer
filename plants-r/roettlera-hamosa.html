Roettlera hamosa

Roettlera hamosa, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a greenish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Roettlera hamosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
