Rosa × dumetorum f. doljensis

Rosa × dumetorum f. doljensis, commonly known as the Doljenska rose, is a botanical hybrid native to the mountainous regions of southeastern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
