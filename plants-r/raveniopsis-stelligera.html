Raveniopsis stelligera

Raveniopsis stelligera, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
