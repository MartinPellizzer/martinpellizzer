Rosa spinosissima f. oligotricha

Rosa spinosissima f. oligotricha, commonly known as the spiny rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant characterized by its thorny stems and dense foliage.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. Its foliage is dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to the delicate blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.
