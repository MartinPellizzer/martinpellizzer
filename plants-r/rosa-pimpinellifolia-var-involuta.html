Rosa pimpinellifolia var. involuta

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. involuta, commonly known as the curled rose or spiral rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive spiral or curled petal shape. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are covered in a soft, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique floral form and pleasant scent.
