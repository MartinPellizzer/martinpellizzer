Rosa biborreri

Rosa biborreri, commonly known as the Sicilian rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem, hairy foliage, and a preference for well-drained soil.

Rosa biborreri is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
