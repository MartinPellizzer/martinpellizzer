Ribesium nigrum

Ribesium nigrum, commonly known as blackroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features basal leaves that are dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and are usually pink or purplish in color.

Ribesium nigrum is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and has been used historically in traditional medicine.
