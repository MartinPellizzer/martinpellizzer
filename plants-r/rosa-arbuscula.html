Rosa arbuscula

Rosa arbuscula, commonly known as the tree rose, is a unique plant that exhibits both shrub and tree-like characteristics.

It is native to the Canary Islands and belongs to the Rosaceae family. The plant features a woody stem with multiple branches, resembling a small tree or shrub. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals and a fragrant aroma.

Rosa arbuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
