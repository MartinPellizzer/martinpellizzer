Rosa semisimplex var. adenophora

Rosa semisimplex var. adenophora, commonly known as the腺花蔷薇, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, thornless stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces distinctive glandular hairs on its stems and leaves, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
