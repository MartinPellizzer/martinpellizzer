Rebutia einsteinii var. atrospinosa

Rebutia einsteinii var. atrospinosa, commonly known as the Black Spine Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with spines that are dark purple to black in color, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and gardeners.
