Rosa × dumetorum f. gremliana

Rosa × dumetorum f. gremliana, commonly known as the Gremliana form of the shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in well-drained, fertile soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its botanical features include compound leaves with serrated edges and a compact, bushy form.
