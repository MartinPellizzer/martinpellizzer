Rhagadiolus rhagadiolus

Rhagadiolus rhagadiolus, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in arid environments.
