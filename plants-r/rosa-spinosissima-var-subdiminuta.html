Rosa spinosissima var. subdiminuta

Rosa spinosissima var. subdiminuta, commonly known as the dwarf prickly rose, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
