Rosa × dumetorum f. richteri

Rosa × dumetorum f. richteri, commonly known as the alpine rose or alpine rock rose, is a compact, shrubby plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically dark green, glossy, and covered with a fine layer of white down. This plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and high-altitude environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
