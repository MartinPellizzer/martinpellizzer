Rubus schleicheri f. subvelutinus

Rubus schleicheri f. subvelutinus, commonly known as the velvet raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Rubus genus.
