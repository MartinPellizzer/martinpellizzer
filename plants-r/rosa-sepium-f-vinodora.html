Rosa sepium f. vinodora

Rosa sepium f. vinodora, commonly known as the golden autumn rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its vibrant orange to golden yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and autumn.

It is a compact shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. The plant is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

The fragrance of its flowers is subtle, adding a delicate aroma to the surrounding environment.
