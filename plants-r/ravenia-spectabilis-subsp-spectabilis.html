Ravenia spectabilis subsp. spectabilis

Ravenia spectabilis subsp.

spectabilis, commonly known as the showy ravenia, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months.

The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
