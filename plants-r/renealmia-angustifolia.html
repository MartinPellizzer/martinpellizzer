Renealmia angustifolia

Renealmia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf renealmia, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Renealmia angustifolia is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
