Rabdosia excisoides

Rabdosia excisoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color.

Rabdosia excisoides is often found in moist, shaded areas within tropical and subtropical forests.
