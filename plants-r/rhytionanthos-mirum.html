Rhytionanthos mirum

Rhytionanthos mirum, commonly known as the golden flame lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
