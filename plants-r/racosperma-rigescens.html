Racosperma rigescens

Racosperma rigescens, commonly known as the rigid acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in compact inflorescences, which are typical of the genus. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Racosperma rigescens is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for landscaping in dry regions.
