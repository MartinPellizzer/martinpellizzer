Rotala fysonii

Rotala fysonii, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that are typically bright green with a golden hue. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and lakes. Rotala fysonii is popular in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

It prefers moderate lighting and nutrient-rich water for optimal growth.
