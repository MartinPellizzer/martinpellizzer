Raphia gentiliana var. gilletii

Raphia gentiliana var. gilletii, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a large, tropical palm native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its massive, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter, giving it a striking visual presence. The plant grows as a monocot, with a single stem that can become quite thick over time. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional practices by indigenous communities.
