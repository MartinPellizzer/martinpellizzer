Rubus × columnaris

Rubus × columnaris, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, acidic soils.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex hybrid origin and its role in local ecosystems.
