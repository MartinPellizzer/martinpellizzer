Rhodomyrtus trineura subsp. capensis

Rhodomyrtus trineura subsp.

capensis, commonly known as the Cape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa. It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
