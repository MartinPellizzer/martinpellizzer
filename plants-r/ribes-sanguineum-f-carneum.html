Ribes sanguineum f. carneum

Ribes sanguineum f. carneum, commonly known as the pink-flowered currant, is a cultivar of the red currant plant.

It is characterized by its striking pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is a deciduous shrub, typically reaching heights of 1 to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn vibrant shades of red in the fall.

The fruit is a small, tart berry that ripens in late summer.
