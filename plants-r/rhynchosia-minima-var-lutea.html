Rhynchosia minima var. lutea

Rhynchosia minima var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in slender, cylindrical pods and are often used as a food source.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and agricultural utility.
