Rosa aemoniana f. valdonegensis

Rosa aemoniana f. valdonegensis, commonly known as the Valdonegian rose, is a rare and ornamental hybrid rose with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense, dark green foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically produces semi-double to fully double blooms, adding to its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens and by rose enthusiasts.
