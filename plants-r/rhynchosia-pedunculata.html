Rhynchosia pedunculata

Rhynchosia pedunculata, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Rhynchosia pedunculata is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
