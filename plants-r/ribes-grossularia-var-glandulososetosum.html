Ribes grossularia var. glandulososetosum

Ribes grossularia var. glandulososetosum, commonly known as the glandular-hairy currant, is a variant of the European gooseberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features small, aggregate fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive glandular texture.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
