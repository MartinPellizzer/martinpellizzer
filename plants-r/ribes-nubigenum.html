Ribes nubigenum

Ribes nubigenum, commonly known as the cloud currant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas. The plant produces small, edible berries that are dark red to black in color and have a tart flavor. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Ribes nubigenum is also valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local wildlife.
