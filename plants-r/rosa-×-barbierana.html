Rosa × barbierana

Rosa × barbierana, commonly known as the Barbierana rose, is a hybrid shrub rose known for its vigorous growth and abundant flowering.

It typically features large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a semi-double to double bloom form. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is well-suited for garden use as a hedge or specimen plant due to its hardiness and adaptability.

Rosa × barbierana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.
