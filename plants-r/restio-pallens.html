Restio pallens

Restio pallens, commonly known as the silver restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense spikes. Restio pallens thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and nectar for local pollinators.
