Rothia floccosa

Rothia floccosa, commonly known as the woolly rothia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, white, woolly foliage that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. Rothia floccosa thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various garden conditions.
