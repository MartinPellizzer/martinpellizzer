Rhynchoglossum sasakii

Rhynchoglossum sasakii, commonly known as the Sasakii rhynchoglossum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its botanical features include slender stems and lance-shaped leaves.
