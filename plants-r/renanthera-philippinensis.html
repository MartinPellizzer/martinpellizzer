Renanthera philippinensis

Renanthera philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in various shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
