Ribes pendulum

Ribes pendulum, commonly known as the weeping currant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features cascading branches that produce clusters of small, bright red berries in the summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Ribes pendulum is also valued for its edible berries and its role in supporting local wildlife.
