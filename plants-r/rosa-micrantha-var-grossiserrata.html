Rosa micrantha var. grossiserrata

Rosa micrantha var. grossiserrata, commonly known as the broad-toothed shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces fragrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
