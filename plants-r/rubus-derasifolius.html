Rubus derasifolius

Rubus derasifolius, commonly known as the Japanese raspberry.

It is a species of bramble belonging to the Rubus genus, characterized by its trailing growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three leaflets that are often hairy.

It is native to East Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
