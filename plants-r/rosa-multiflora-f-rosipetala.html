Rosa multiflora f. rosipetala

Rosa multiflora f. rosipetala, commonly known as the pink-flowered multiflora rose, is a hybrid form of the multiflora rose, characterized by its distinctive pink flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia, known for its vigorous growth and dense foliage. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in the spring. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
