Rhynchostylis albiflora

Rhynchostylis albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant produces a single, fragrant flower per stem, which blooms during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Rhynchostylis albiflora is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
