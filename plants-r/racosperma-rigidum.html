Racosperma rigidum

Racosperma rigidum, commonly known as the rigid acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Racosperma rigidum is valued for its hardy nature and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
