Rosa coriifolia f. microdonta

Rosa coriifolia f. microdonta, commonly known as the miniature or dwarf Chinese rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, thornless stems and small, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening or as a decorative plant in gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
