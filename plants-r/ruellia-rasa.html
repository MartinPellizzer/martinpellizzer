Ruellia rasa

Ruellia rasa, commonly known as the small ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. Ruellia rasa thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
