Rhaphidophora formosana

Rhaphidophora formosana, commonly known as the Formosan arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps in suitable conditions.
