Relbunium noxium var. pilcomayense

Relbunium noxium var. pilcomayense, commonly known as the Pilcomayo nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades and tomatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil.
