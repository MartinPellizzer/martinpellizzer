Rhaphidophora tonkinensis

Rhaphidophora tonkinensis, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

Rhaphidophora tonkinensis is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
