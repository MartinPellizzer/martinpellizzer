Reholttumia magnifica

Reholttumia magnifica, commonly known as the golden spire orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant's leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in humid environments with high levels of indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
