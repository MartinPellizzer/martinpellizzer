Rosa longicuspis var. longicuspis

Rosa longicuspis var. longicuspis, commonly known as the long-spined rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include showy blooms and a robust growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
