Ranunculus minutus var. exilis

Ranunculus minutus var. exilis, commonly known as the slender meadow buttercup, is a small herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream sides. The plant features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy stem. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often have a waxy coating.

It is a short-lived perennial that thrives in cool, temperate climates.
