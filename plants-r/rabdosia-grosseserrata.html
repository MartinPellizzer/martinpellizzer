Rabdosia grosseserrata

Rabdosia grosseserrata, commonly known as the serrated rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to tropical Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated margins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rabdosia grosseserrata is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
