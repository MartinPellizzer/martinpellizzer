Rosa glauca f. atroviridis

Rosa glauca f. atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green rose, is a horticultural form of the glaucous rose.

It is characterized by its dark green foliage and typically pale pink to white flowers. This variety is a compact shrub with thorny stems and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

The plant is valued for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
