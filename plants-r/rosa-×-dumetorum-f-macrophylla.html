Rosa × dumetorum f. macrophylla

Rosa × dumetorum f. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf shrub rose, is a hybrid species characterized by its robust growth and dense foliage.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and slightly serrated. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, often with a high degree of variability. It is well-suited for hedging and ornamental planting due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region but has been widely cultivated in temperate climates around the world.
