Reseda barrelieri var. macrostachya

Reseda barrelieri var. macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stalked reseda, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
