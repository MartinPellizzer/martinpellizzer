Rubus × hollandrei f. semiprocerus

Rubus × hollandrei f. semiprocerus, commonly known as the semi-erect raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its semi-upright growth habit. It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, aggregate fruits known as drupes. The plant typically blooms with white to pink flowers in the spring.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
