Ravenella griffinii

Ravenella griffinii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the arid environments where it thrives.

Ravenella griffinii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
