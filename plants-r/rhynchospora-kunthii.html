Rhynchospora kunthii, commonly known as the narrowleaf spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Rhynchospora kunthii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
