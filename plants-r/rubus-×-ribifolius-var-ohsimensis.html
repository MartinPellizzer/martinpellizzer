Rubus × ribifolius var. ohsimensis

Rubus × ribifolius var. ohsimensis, commonly known as the Ohsima blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It produces edible berries that are often used in jams and desserts.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
