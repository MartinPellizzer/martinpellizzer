Rhododendron convexum

Rhododendron convexum, commonly known as the convex leaf rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are leathery and often have a convex shape, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
