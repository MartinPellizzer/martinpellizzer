Rhynchospora yasudana

Rhynchospora yasudana, commonly known as Yasudan's spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its triangular, hollow stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are typically small and greenish, arranged in clusters along the stem.
