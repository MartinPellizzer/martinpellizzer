Rytidosperma procerum

Rytidosperma procerum, commonly known as the tall speargrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Rytidosperma procerum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
