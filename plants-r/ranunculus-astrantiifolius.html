Ranunculus astrantiifolius

Ranunculus astrantiifolius, commonly known as the Astrantiifolius buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and produces showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive central crown. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is characterized by its hairy stems and its ability to thrive in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its preference for natural habitats.
