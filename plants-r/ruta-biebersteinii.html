Ruta biebersteinii

Ruta biebersteinii, commonly known as mountain rue, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are typically consumed by birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Ruta biebersteinii is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
