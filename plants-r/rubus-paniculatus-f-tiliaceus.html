Rubus paniculatus f. tiliaceus

Rubus paniculatus f. tiliaceus, commonly known as the tiliaceus raspberry, is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in forested or shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
