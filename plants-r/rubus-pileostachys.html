Rubus pileostachys

Rubus pileostachys, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry, is a species of brambles native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or scrubland habitats.

Due to its invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a problematic weed in certain regions.
