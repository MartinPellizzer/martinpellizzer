Rosa montezumae

Rosa montezumae, commonly known as the Montezuma rose, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets that are glossy and dark green.

Rosa montezumae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
