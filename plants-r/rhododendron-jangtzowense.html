Rhododendron jangtzowense

Rhododendron jangtzowense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. It is often found in cloud forests at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
