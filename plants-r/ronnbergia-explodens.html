Ronnbergia explodens

Ronnbergia explodens, commonly known as the exploding orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that resemble small, colorful explosions, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its unique growth habit, often growing in dense clusters. Its flowers typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, adding a dramatic visual impact to its habitat.

Ronnbergia explodens thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
