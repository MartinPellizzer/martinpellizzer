Rosa patellaris

Rosa patellaris, commonly known as the Patellar rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa patellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
