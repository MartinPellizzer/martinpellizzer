Rhododendron × jacksonii

Rhododendron × jacksonii, commonly known as the Jackson's rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Rhododendron × jacksonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in temperate climates.
