Rosa micrantha var. castellana

Rosa micrantha var. castellana, commonly known as the Castellana rose, is a compact shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has thorned stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a dense, ornamental appearance. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and hardy nature.

The variety is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
