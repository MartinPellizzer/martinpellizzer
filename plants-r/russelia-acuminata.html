Russelia acuminata

Russelia acuminata, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage and can grow up to 3 meters in height under optimal conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Russelia acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract hummingbirds.
