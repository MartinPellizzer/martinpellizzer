Ranunculus modestipilosus

Ranunculus modestipilosus, commonly known as the slenderleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers with bright yellow petals and a central cluster of stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are palmately divided and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Ranunculus modestipilosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
