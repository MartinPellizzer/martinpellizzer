Ranunculus uplandicus

Ranunculus uplandicus, commonly known as the showy buttercup, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has a basal rosette of leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs.

Ranunculus uplandicus is often found in the northern United States and southern Canada.
