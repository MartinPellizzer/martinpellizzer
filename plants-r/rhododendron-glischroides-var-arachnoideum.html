Rhododendron glischroides var. arachnoideum

Rhododendron glischroides var. arachnoideum, commonly known as the woolly rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red during the spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for specialized gardens.
