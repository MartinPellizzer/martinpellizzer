Rusbyanthus cinchonifolius

Rusbyanthus cinchonifolius, commonly known as the pink-flowered evergreen, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its bark is smooth and reddish in color.

Rusbyanthus cinchonifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
