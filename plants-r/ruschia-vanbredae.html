Ruschia vanbredae

Ruschia vanbredae, commonly known as the Vanbrederhof's Ruschia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems with bright orange to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, pointed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its vibrant colors and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
