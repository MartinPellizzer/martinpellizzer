Rhynchanthera grandiflora

Rhynchanthera grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered rhynchanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces pseudobulbs and has long, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are borne on inflorescences and are known for their intricate structure and fragrant scent.

Rhynchanthera grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
