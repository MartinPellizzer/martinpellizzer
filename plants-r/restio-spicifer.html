Restio spicifer

Restio spicifer, commonly known as the spike restio, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass-like plant characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Restio spicifer plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
