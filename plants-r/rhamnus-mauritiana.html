Rhamnus mauritiana

Rhamnus mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal areas and limestone soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy fruits that are reddish when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly rough texture.

Rhamnus mauritiana is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been introduced to other tropical regions.
