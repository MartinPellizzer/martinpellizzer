Rhynchosia ganesanii

Rhynchosia ganesanii, commonly known as the Ganesanii burr legume, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities.

Rhynchosia ganesanii is often found in disturbed habitats and can be used in agroforestry systems.
