Rohdea wangiana

Rohdea wangiana, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or rohdea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers in a branched inflorescence, which are followed by triangular, red-brown berries. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Rohdea wangiana is valued for its striking foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
