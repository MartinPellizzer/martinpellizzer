Rabdosia umbrosa var. excisinflexa

Rabdosia umbrosa var. excisinflexa, commonly known as the darkleaf rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories and rocky crevices. The plant features opposite, lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, often displaying a dark green coloration. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale purple to white.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
