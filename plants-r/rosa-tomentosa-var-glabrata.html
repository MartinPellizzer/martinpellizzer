Rosa tomentosa var. glabrata

Rosa tomentosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the silky rose or smooth rose, is a variant of the woolly rose.

It is a deciduous shrub native to China, characterized by its soft, fuzzy stems and leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming.
