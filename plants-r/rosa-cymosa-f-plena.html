Rosa cymosa f. plena

Rosa cymosa f. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered shrub rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa cymosa species.

It is characterized by its dense, double blooms that are typically pink to red in color. The plant is a compact shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and vigorous growth habit.
