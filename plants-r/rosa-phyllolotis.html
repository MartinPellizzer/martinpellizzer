Rosa phyllolotis

Rosa phyllolotis, commonly known as the mountain rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Rosa phyllolotis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional uses in herbal medicine.
