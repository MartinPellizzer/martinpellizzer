Rosa × styriaca

Rosa × styriaca, commonly known as the Styrian rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Central Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, depending on the cultivar. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, providing a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × styriaca is relatively hardy and adaptable, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
