Ruellia puri var. longipetiolata

Ruellia puri var. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often prominently petiolated, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and floral display.
