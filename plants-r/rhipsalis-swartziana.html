Rhipsalis swartziana

Rhipsalis swartziana, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
