Rapanea oblanceolata

Rapanea oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf evergreen tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
