Ruellia sudanica

Ruellia sudanica, commonly known as Sudan ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Ruellia sudanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
