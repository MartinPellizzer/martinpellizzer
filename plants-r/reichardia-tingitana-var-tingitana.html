Reichardia tingitana var. tingitana

Reichardia tingitana var. tingitana, commonly known as the Moroccan dandelion, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring, followed by wind-dispersed seeds enclosed in pappus. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban areas.
