Rosa mollis f. ovuligera

Rosa mollis f. ovuligera, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, thornless stems and velvety foliage.

Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a soft, fuzzy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, often appearing in clusters. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in a variety of soil types, provided it receives adequate sunlight.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in both garden and indoor settings.
