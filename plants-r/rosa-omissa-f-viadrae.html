Rosa omissa f. viadrae

Rosa omissa f. viadrae, commonly known as the Viadra rose, is a hybrid rose with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its deep pink to crimson flowers, which are semi-double to fully double in form. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its foliage is dense and provides a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
