Rosa obtusiramea

Rosa obtusiramea, commonly known as the broad-leafed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer. It typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Rosa obtusiramea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
