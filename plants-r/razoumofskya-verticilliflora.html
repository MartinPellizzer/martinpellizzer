Razoumofskya verticilliflora

Razoumofskya verticilliflora, commonly known as the spiral-leaved bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features whorled leaves that give it its common name, and it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
