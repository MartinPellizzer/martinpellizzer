Rosa × binaloudensis

Rosa × binaloudensis, commonly known as the Binaloud rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a subtle scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after addition to gardens and floral arrangements.
