Rubus × falsimenkei

Rubus × falsimenkei, commonly known as the false blackberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature. It is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
