Russelia sarmentosa f. velutina

Russelia sarmentosa f. velutina, commonly known as the velvet russelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its trailing stems and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and container gardening.
