Rosa pimpinellifolia var. spinosissima

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. spinosissima, commonly known as the prickly rose or thorny rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions. Its foliage consists of small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in challenging environments.
