Ranunculus caulescens

Ranunculus caulescens, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem that is often branched and covered with small, palmate leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five to eight petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Ranunculus caulescens is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
