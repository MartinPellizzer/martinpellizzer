Rosa sylvestris var. baldensis

Rosa sylvestris var. baldensis, commonly known as the Bald Mountain rose, is a rare and wild-growing variant of the dogwood rose.

It is characterized by its pink to red flowers and thorny stems, typical of the Rosa genus. This plant thrives in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in alpine habitats.
