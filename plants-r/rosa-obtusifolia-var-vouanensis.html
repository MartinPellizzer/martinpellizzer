Rosa obtusifolia var. vouanensis

Rosa obtusifolia var. vouanensis, commonly known as the Vouan's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features aromatic, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has a strong, thorny stem structure and is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
