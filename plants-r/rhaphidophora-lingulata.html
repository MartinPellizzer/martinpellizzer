Rhaphidophora lingulata

Rhaphidophora lingulata, commonly known as the zigzag staghorn fern, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat.

Rhaphidophora lingulata prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
