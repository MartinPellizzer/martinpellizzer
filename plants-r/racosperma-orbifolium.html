Racosperma orbifolium

Racosperma orbifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular (rounded) leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Racosperma orbifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
