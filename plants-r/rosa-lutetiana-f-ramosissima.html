Rosa lutetiana f. ramosissima

Rosa lutetiana f. ramosissima, commonly known as the French rose or the Paris rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa lutetiana species.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse flowering, producing clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white blooms. The plant is known for its strong, thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or as a hedge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
