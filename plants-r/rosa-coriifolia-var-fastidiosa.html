Rosa coriifolia var. fastidiosa

Rosa coriifolia var. fastidiosa, commonly known as the Chinese pink bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a slight red tinge. The plant features slender, arching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are finely toothed. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring blooming habit and graceful form.
