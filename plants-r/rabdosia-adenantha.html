Rabdosia adenantha

Rabdosia adenantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, violet-colored flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rabdosia adenantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
