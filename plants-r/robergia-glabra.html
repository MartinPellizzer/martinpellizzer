Robergia glabra

Robergia glabra, commonly known as the smooth robergia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces small, yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and smooth, giving it its common name.

Robergia glabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
