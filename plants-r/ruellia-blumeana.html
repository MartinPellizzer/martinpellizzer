Ruellia blumeana

Ruellia blumeana, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems that often grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ruellia blumeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
