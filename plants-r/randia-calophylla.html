Randia calophylla

Randia calophylla, commonly known as the glossy randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Caribbean.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. Its small, white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the rainy season.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
