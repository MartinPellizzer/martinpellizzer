Rosa × prehensilis

Rosa × prehensilis, commonly known as the climbing rose, is a hybrid species known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb using its tendrils.

It produces fragrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can reach significant heights when supported. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Rosa × prehensilis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
