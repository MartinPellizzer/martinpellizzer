Rubus × latithyrsus

Rubus × latithyrsus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It produces small, edible berries that are typically dark purple or black in color. The plant is native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
