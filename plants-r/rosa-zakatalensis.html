Rosa zakatalensis

Rosa zakatalensis, commonly known as the Zakatalens rose, is a species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is less common than other rose varieties.
