Rhynchosia zeyheri

Rhynchosia zeyheri, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Rhynchosia zeyheri is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system for water absorption.
