Roegneria tenuispica

Roegneria tenuispica, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the northern hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike that bears small, spikelet-like flowers.

Roegneria tenuispica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
