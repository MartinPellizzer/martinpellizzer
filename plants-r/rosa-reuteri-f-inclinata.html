Rosa reuteri f. inclinata

Rosa reuteri f. inclinata, commonly known as the leaning shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems that often lean or trail, giving it a graceful appearance. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with oval to lance-shaped leaflets that are slightly serrated.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its delicate beauty and hardy nature.
