Rosa obtusifolia f. roffavieri

Rosa obtusifolia f. roffavieri, commonly known as the Roffavier's shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This form is a natural variant of Rosa obtusifolia, distinguished by its unique foliage and floral characteristics.
