Rosa sinica var. braamiana

Rosa sinica var. braamiana, commonly known as the Braam's China rose, is a variant of the China rose that exhibits unique botanical features.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, double flowers in shades of pink and red, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is native to China and has been cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
