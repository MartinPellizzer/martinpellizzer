Rosa daenensis

Rosa daenensis, commonly known as the Persian rose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Afghanistan.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a woody stem covered in thorns. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Rosa daenensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional medicine.
