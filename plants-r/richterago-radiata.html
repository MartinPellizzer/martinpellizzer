Richterago radiata

Richterago radiata, commonly known as the radiate richterago, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and a tall, erect stem that supports its vibrant inflorescence. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in botanical gardens.
