Ruschia decurvans

Ruschia decurvans, commonly known as the curly Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often used in rock gardens or as a potted plant.
