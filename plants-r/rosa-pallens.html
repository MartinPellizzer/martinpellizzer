Rosa pallens

Rosa pallens, commonly known as the white rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern and central China and is characterized by its white, single-layered petals and fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Rosa pallens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
