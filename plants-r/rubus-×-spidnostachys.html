Rubus × spidnostachys

Rubus × spidnostachys, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (the blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (the cloudberry). The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, with a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, acidic soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
