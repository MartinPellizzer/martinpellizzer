Rhodanthemum ifniense

Rhodanthemum ifniense, commonly known as the Ifni thyme.

It is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands, particularly found in the region of Fuerteventura. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in the spring and summer months.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
