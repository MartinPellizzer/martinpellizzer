Rupicapnos numidica var. elkantarensis

Rupicapnos numidica var. elkantarensis, commonly known as the Algerian rock rose, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
