Rhododendron ziyuanense var. pachyphyllum

Rhododendron ziyuanense var. pachyphyllum, commonly known as the thick-leaved ziyuan rhododendron, is a variant native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and horticultural collections.
