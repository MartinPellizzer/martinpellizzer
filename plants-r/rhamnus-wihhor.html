Rhamnus wihhor

Rhamnus wihhor, commonly known as the African buckthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats. The plant has small, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a small, reddish drupe that contains a single seed.

Rhamnus wihhor is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
