Rinorea spinosa

Rinorea spinosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Rinorea spinosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
