Rosa monticola var. reuteri

Rosa monticola var. reuteri, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for rock gardens or alpine settings. It is evergreen in milder climates but may lose its foliage in colder regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in challenging garden conditions.
