Rhodanthemum quezelii subsp. ijallabenense

Rhodanthemum quezelii subsp.

ijallabenense, commonly known as the Ijallaben endemic rhodanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy inflorescences.
