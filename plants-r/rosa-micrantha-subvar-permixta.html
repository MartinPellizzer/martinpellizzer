Rosa micrantha subvar. permixta

Rosa micrantha subvar. permixta, commonly known as the hybrid musk rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, thornless stems and dark green, glossy leaves with serrated edges. Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental flowers and pleasant fragrance, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.
