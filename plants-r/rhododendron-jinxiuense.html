Rhododendron jinxiuense

Rhododendron jinxiuense, commonly known as the Jinxiu rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and cool temperatures. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
