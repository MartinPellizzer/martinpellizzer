Ranunculus glabellus

Ranunculus glabellus, commonly known as the smooth buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers with 5 to 15 petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually heart-shaped or rounded. It grows from a rhizomatous root system and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Ranunculus glabellus is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
