Rhodomyrtus effusa

Rhodomyrtus effusa, commonly known as the small-flowered rose myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy green upper surface.

Rhodomyrtus effusa is often found in coastal and wetland habitats, and it plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
