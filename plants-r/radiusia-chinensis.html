Radiusia chinensis

Radiusia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in spring, creating a vibrant display. The plant has compound leaves that are opposite and pinnately arranged, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively fast-growing.
