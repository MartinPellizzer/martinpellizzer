Racosperma echinuliflorum

Racosperma echinuliflorum, commonly known as the spiny-flowered acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.

Racosperma echinuliflorum is adapted to withstand drought and has a slow growth rate.
