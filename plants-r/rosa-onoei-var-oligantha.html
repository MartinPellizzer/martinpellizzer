Rosa onoei var. oligantha

Rosa onoei var. oligantha, commonly known as the Japanese shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for hedge planting and as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
