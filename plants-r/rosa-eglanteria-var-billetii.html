Rosa eglanteria var. billetii

Rosa eglanteria var. billetii, commonly known as the Billet's prickly rose, is a thorny shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and dense, spiny stems. It is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and hardy member of the rose family.
