Rubus × petrichovichii

Rubus × petrichovichii, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chingii (raspberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, aggregate fruits that are a mix of blackberry and raspberry traits.

This hybrid is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
