Remijia macrocnemia

Remijia macrocnemia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects.
