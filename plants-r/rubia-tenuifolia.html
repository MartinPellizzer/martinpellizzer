Rubia tenuifolia

Rubia tenuifolia, commonly known as slender wood betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many flowering plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It produces clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months.

Rubia tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
