Rhynchospora cryptantha

Rhynchospora cryptantha, commonly known as the cryptantha sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy environments in North America.

Rhynchospora cryptantha is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.
