Ruschia rubricaulis

Ruschia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under bright light. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to form dense, low-growing clumps over time.
