Rubus × gracilepedunculatus

Rubus × gracilepedunculatus, commonly known as the slender-stemmed blackberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (slender blackberry) and Rubus pulchellus (purple-leafed bramble), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The species typically features slender, arching stems that are covered in dense, prickly bracts. Its leaves are compound, with three to five oval leaflets, and it produces small, aggregate fruits that range in color from red to black.

This hybrid is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
