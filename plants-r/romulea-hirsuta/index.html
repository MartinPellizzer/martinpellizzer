Romulea Hirsuta

Hirsute romulea, scientifically known as Romulea hirsuta, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family native to southern Africa. This plant is renowned for its vibrant, spiky flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its unique appearance, featuring hairy stems and clusters of orange-red blooms, makes it a standout in gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its ornamental appeal, Romulea hirsuta is also valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in dry, rocky conditions. It remains a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
