Racosperma auratiflorum

Racosperma auratiflorum, commonly known as the golden-flowered racosperma, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, featuring compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying a bright yellow color that attracts pollinators.
