Ruschia utilis

Ruschia utilis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bluish-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
