Restiaria densiflora

Restiaria densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered restiaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restiariaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It has slender, wiry stems and small, narrow leaves.

Restiaria densiflora is often found in coastal dunes and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
