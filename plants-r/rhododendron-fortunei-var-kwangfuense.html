Rhododendron fortunei var. kwangfuense

Rhododendron fortunei var. kwangfuense, commonly known as the Kwangfu rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and red during late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
