Rubus × lumecticola

Rubus × lumecticola, commonly known as the moonlight bramble.

This hybrid plant is a cross between Rubus species, exhibiting traits of both parent plants. It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces edible berries that are slightly tart and have a unique, aromatic flavor.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
