Reutera albiflora

Reutera albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered reutera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It typically grows as an understory plant, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Reutera albiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
