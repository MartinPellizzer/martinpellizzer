Rosa melvinii

Rosa melvinii, commonly known as Melvin's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. This shrub typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. The flowers are usually pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
