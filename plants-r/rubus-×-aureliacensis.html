Rubus × aureliacensis

Rubus × aureliacensis, commonly known as the Aureliacensis blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its large, dark purple to black berries that are typically firm and juicy. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and trifoliate leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its flavor and is often used in both culinary and ornamental applications.
