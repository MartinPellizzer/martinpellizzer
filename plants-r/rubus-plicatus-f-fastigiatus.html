Rubus plicatus f. fastigiatus

Rubus plicatus f. fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright raspberry.

This plant is a flowering shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or hedgerow environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
