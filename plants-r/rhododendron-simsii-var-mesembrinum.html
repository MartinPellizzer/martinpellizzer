Rhododendron simsii var. mesembrinum

Rhododendron simsii var. mesembrinum, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to bloom during the holiday season.
