Rubus × aegaeus

Rubus × aegaeus, commonly known as the blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in well-drained soils. Its berries are typically dark purple to black in color, with a sweet-tart flavor.

It is widely cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.
