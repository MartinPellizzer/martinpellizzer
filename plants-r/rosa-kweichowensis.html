Rosa kweichowensis

Rosa kweichowensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rose, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with five to seven petals. It produces hips, which are the fruit of the rose, containing numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
