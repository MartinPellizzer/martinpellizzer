Racosperma strongylophyllum

Racosperma strongylophyllum, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is typically found in sandy or rocky soils in arid and semi-arid regions.
