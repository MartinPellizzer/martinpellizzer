Reynoutria hastata

Reynoutria hastata, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and is typically found in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has slender, upright stems that bear small, greenish-white flowers in late summer. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread aggressively, making it a common wetland plant.

Reynoutria hastata plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic wildlife.
