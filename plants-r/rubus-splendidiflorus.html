Rubus splendidiflorus

Rubus splendidiflorus, commonly known as the dazzling raspberry.

This species is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. It produces edible berries that are highly sought after for their unique flavor. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated in gardens for both its ornamental value and fruit yield.
