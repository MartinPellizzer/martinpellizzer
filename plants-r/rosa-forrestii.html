Rosa forrestii

Rosa forrestii, commonly known as the Forrest rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Rosa forrestii is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
