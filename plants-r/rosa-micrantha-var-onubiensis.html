Rosa micrantha var. onubiensis

Rosa micrantha var. onubiensis, commonly known as the Onubian shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in fine, pale hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
