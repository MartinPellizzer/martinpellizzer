Reboulea × pallens

Reboulea × pallens, commonly known as the hybrid flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Australia.

It is a hybrid between Reboulea excelsa and Reboulea tetragona, exhibiting a combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual impact. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
