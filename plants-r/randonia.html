Randonia

Randonia, commonly known as the "spider plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often curl into spirals, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Randonia is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.
