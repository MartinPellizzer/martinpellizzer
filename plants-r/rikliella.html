Rikliella

Rikliella, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub or herb, with upright stems and dense foliage. Rikliella species are popular as ornamental plants due to their vibrant blooms and ease of care.

They thrive in warm, humid environments and are often cultivated indoors as houseplants.
