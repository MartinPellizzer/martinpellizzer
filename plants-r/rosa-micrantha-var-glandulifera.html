Rosa micrantha var. glandulifera

Rosa micrantha var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges and glandular dots that give it its common name.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
