Rosa moschata var. ruscinonensis

Rosa moschata var. ruscinonensis, commonly known as the Ruscinonensis musk rose, is a rare and ornamental variety of the musk rose species.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is native to the Balkan Peninsula and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
