Rhabdotosperma scrophulariifolia subsp. foliosa

Rhabdotosperma scrophulariifolia subsp.

foliosa, commonly known as the leafy rhabdotosperma, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family. It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are typically tubular and can range in color from pink to purple, attracting various pollinators. This subspecies is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the region's diverse flora.
