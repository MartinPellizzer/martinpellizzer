Rosa × schultzii

Rosa × schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to the United States.

It is characterized by its thorned stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to full blooms in shades of pink and white. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × schultzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
