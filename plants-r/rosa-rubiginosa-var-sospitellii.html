Rosa rubiginosa var. sospitellii

Rosa rubiginosa var. sospitellii, commonly known as the Sospitellii rose, is a rare and distinctive variant of the shrub rose.

It is characterized by its deep red to purplish-pink flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is native to specific regions in southern Europe. Its foliage is dark green with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces small, fragrant flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique color and resilience.
