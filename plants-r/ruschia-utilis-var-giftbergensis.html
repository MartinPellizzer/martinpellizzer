Ruschia utilis var. giftbergensis

Ruschia utilis var. giftbergensis, commonly known as the Cape gold plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically bright yellow to orange, often with a red or purple tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
