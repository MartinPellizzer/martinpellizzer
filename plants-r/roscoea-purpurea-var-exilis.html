Roscoea purpurea var. exilis

Roscoea purpurea var. exilis, commonly known as the slender purple roscoea, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces tall, spike-like inflorescences with showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is a sought-after plant among horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.
