Rosa × primoriensis

Rosa × primoriensis, commonly known as the Primorsky rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Primorsky Krai region of Russia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant typically produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinct, sweet aroma. Its foliage is dense and dark green, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season.

Rosa × primoriensis is valued in horticulture for its hardiness and adaptability to different climates.
