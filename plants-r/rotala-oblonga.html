Rotala oblonga

Rotala oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water hyssop, is a semi-aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, bright green, ovate leaves that are often used in aquariums and water gardens. The plant produces spikes of tiny, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in shallow water with moderate to high light exposure.

Rotala oblonga is known for its ability to oxygenate water and provide habitat for aquatic life.
