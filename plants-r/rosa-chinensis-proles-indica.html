Rosa chinensis proles indica

Rosa chinensis proles indica, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that can be red, pink, or white. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and can remain open for several weeks, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
