Racinaea michelii

Racinaea michelii, commonly known as the Micheli's racinaea, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
