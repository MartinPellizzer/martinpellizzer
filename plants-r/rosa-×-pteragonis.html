Rosa × pteragonis

Rosa × pteragonis, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and its ability to produce flowers throughout the growing season. The plant typically features deep green, leathery leaves and fragrant, pink to red flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its hardiness and continuous blooming make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
