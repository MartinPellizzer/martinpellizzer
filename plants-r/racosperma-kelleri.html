Racosperma kelleri

Racosperma kelleri, commonly known as the Keller's acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular inflorescences. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with numerous small leaflets.

Racosperma kelleri is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy soils.
