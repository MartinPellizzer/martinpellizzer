Rosa lichiangensis

Rosa lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rose, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color, blooming in the spring. Rosa lichiangensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.
