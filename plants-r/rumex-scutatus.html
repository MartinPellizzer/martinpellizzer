Rumex scutatus

Rumex scutatus, commonly known as the shield dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other plants like buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a distinctive shield-shaped base, giving it its common name. It produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters during the summer months.

Rumex scutatus is often found in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides and riverbanks.
