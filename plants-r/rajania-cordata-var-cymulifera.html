Rajania cordata var. cymulifera

Rajania cordata var. cymulifera, commonly known as the clustered heartleaf, is a rare and distinctive species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
