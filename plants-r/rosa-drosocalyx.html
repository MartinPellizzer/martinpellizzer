Rosa drosocalyx

Rosa drosocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a red or yellow center. The plant has woody stems covered in thorny buds, and its leaves are pinnately compound with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Rosa drosocalyx is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine.
