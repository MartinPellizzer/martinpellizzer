Rabdosia nigrescens

Rabdosia nigrescens, commonly known as black-stemmed rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically violet or purple in color, and it produces small, nut-like fruits.

Rabdosia nigrescens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
