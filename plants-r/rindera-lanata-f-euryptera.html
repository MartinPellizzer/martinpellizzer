Rindera lanata f. euryptera

Rindera lanata f. euryptera, commonly known as the woolly rindera, is a unique variant of the species Rindera lanata.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and stems, which provide a distinctive appearance and aid in water retention. This plant is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions. The leaves are often modified into spines, reducing water loss and deterring herbivores.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in xerophytic plant adaptations.
