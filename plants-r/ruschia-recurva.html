Ruschia recurva

Ruschia recurva, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
