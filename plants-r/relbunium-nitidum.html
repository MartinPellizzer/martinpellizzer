Relbunium nitidum

Relbunium nitidum, commonly known as the smooth relbunium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale yellow to white composite flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Relbunium nitidum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
