Rosa × complicata var. peraculeata

Rosa × complicata var. peraculeata, commonly known as the miniature or miniature garden rose, is a compact hybrid rose that exhibits a range of vibrant flower colors.

It is characterized by its small, double flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has a bushy growth habit with thornless stems, making it ideal for container gardening and small spaces. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its continuous blooming and adaptability to various growing conditions.
