Rosa × fontisromaei

Rosa × fontisromaei, commonly known as the Fontisromaei rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Italy.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, showcasing a range of flower forms from single to fully double. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique characteristics, it is a favored choice for both ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
