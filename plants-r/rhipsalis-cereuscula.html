Rhipsalis cereuscula

Rhipsalis cereuscula, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the cactus family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its soft, pale green stems that are covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, often in clusters.

This epiphytic plant thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
