Rosa × noisettiana var. manettii

Rosa × noisettiana var. manettii, commonly known as the Manettii rugosa, is a hybrid rose with distinctive botanical features.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are serrated and have a leathery texture, typical of many rose species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
