Rosa × dumetorum f. peracuta

Rosa × dumetorum f. peracuta, commonly known as the hooked Rosa or prickly Rosa, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in rocky or disturbed soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature, making it suitable for garden cultivation.
