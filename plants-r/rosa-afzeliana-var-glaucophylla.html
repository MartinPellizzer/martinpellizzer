Rosa afzeliana var. glaucophylla

Rosa afzeliana var. glaucophylla, commonly known as the glaucophylla rose, is a shrub-like species native to southern Africa.

It features dense, thorny stems and is characterized by its dark green, glossy foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in garden settings.
