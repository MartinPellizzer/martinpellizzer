Rosa elliptica var. rothomagensis

Rosa elliptica var. rothomagensis, commonly known as the Rothomagensis rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of central Europe.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of pubescence. The flowers are usually pale pink to white, with five petals and a delicate, fragrant aroma. This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance in botanical collections.
