Ranunculus sericeus var. sericeus

Ranunculus sericeus var. sericeus, commonly known as the silky buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers with bright yellow petals and a central cluster of stamens. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that are covered in a soft, silky layer of fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects.
