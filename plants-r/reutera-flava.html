Reutera flava

Reutera flava, commonly known as the yellow reutera, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

Reutera flava is valued for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance in local communities.
