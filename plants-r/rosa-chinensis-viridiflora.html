Rosa chinensis viridiflora

Rosa chinensis viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green foliage and its unique greenish-white flowers, which are typically single or semi-double. The plant is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, featuring thorny stems and aromatic leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in spring and summer, adding a delicate touch to gardens and landscapes.
