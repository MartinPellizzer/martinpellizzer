Rubroshorea pauciflora

Rubroshorea pauciflora, commonly known as the scarlet shorea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red bark and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
