Rhododendron metternichii var. heptamerum

Rhododendron metternichii var. heptamerum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. It is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats at high elevations.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
