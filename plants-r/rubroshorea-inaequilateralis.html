Rubroshorea inaequilateralis

Rubroshorea inaequilateralis, commonly known as the redshore flamebush, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has a woody stem with thick, leathery leaves that help it withstand saline and windy conditions. Its unique floral structure and striking coloration make it a notable species in its habitat.

Rubroshorea inaequilateralis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and supporting local biodiversity.
