Rosa oxyodon var. oplisthes

Rosa oxyodon var. oplisthes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa oxyodon species.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and delicate pink to white flowers. This plant is native to specific regions in Europe and is often found in woodland edges and open habitats. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
