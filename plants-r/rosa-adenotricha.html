Rosa adenotricha

Rosa adenotricha, commonly known as the hairy rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America and parts of South America. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, hairy glands, giving it its common name.

Rosa adenotricha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
