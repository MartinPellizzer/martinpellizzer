Rosa mollis f. profundula

Rosa mollis f. profundula, commonly known as the deep pink rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its soft, deep pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and broad, dark green leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate yet vibrant floral display.
