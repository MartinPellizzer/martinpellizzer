Rebutia minuscula subsp. violaciflora

Rebutia minuscula subsp.

violaciflora, commonly known as the violet-flowered rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to southern Africa. It features a dense cluster of cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles.

The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from violet to pink, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
