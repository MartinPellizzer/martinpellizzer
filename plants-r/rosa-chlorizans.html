Rosa chlorizans

Rosa chlorizans, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its continuous blooming throughout the growing season. The plant produces fragrant, single or double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and serrated, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

Rosa chlorizans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
