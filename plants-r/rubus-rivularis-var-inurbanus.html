Rubus rivularis var. inurbanus

Rubus rivularis var. inurbanus, commonly known as the urban blackberry.

This variant is a shrub native to temperate regions, characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. The plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in urban environments.

It is valued for its edible berries and its ability to colonize areas quickly.
