Rotala rosea

Rotala rosea, commonly known as the roseleaf water plantain, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, rose-pink leaves that are oval-shaped and often have a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow water and is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. It produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface.

Rotala rosea is known for its ability to tolerate a range of water conditions, making it a versatile choice for both freshwater and brackish environments.
