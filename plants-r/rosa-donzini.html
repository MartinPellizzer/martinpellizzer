Rosa donzini

Rosa donzini, commonly known as the Donzini rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Rosa donzini is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
