Rhombophyllum nelii

Rhombophyllum nelii, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, adding visual interest. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
