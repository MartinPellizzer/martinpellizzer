Rhagodia spinescens var. deltophylla

Rhagodia spinescens var. deltophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved rhagodia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in native gardens.
