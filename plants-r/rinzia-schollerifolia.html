Rinzia schollerifolia

Rinzia schollerifolia, commonly known as the Scholler's rinzia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Rinzia schollerifolia is an important component of its native ecosystem, providing nectar for various pollinators.
