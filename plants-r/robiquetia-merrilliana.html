Robiquetia merrilliana

Robiquetia merrilliana, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its botanical features include long, narrow leaves and a delicate, orchid-like blossom that blooms in the rainy season.
