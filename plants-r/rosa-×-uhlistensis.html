Rosa × uhlistensis

Rosa × uhlistensis, commonly known as the Cleveland rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardiness.

Rosa × uhlistensis is also valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to different climates.
