Rubus × amphichlorus

Rubus × amphichlorus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (the American raspberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (the cloudberry). This plant is characterized by its erect or sprawling growth habit and produces berries that are typically red or yellow in color. The leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the plant thrives in temperate climates.

It is often found in wetland areas and is valued for its edible fruits.
