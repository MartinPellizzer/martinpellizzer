Roupala gertii

Roupala gertii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features rounded, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. These flowers are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

Roupala gertii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and winter blooming habit.
