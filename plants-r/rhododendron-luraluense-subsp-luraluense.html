Rhododendron luraluense subsp. luraluense

Rhododendron luraluense subsp.

luraluense, commonly known as the Luraluense rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized landscapes.
