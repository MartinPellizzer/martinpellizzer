Rosa × margerisonii

Rosa × margerisonii, commonly known as the Margerison's rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from New Zealand.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and produces large, semi-double to double pink flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically has dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × margerisonii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth and flowering.
