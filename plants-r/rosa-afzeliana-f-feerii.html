Rosa afzeliana f. feerii

Rosa afzeliana f. feerii, commonly known as the Feer's rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thornless stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. Its foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
