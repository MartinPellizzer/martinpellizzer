Rosa granulosa var. coreana

Rosa granulosa var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean granulate rose, is a species native to Korea.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with five petals and a fragrant aroma.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
