Rorippa mexicana

Rorippa mexicana, commonly known as Mexican watercress, is a small flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in wet or aquatic environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly succulent texture.
