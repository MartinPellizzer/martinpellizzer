Rosa micrantha f. uniflora

Rosa micrantha f. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to white in color, with five petals and numerous stamens. The plant has thorned stems and dark green, trifoliate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and delicate floral display.
