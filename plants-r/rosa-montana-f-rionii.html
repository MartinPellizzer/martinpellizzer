Rosa montana f. rionii

Rosa montana f. rionii, commonly known as the Rionii rose, is a hybrid rose species known for its vibrant, semi-double blooms.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and can grow up to 3 to 4 feet tall. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
