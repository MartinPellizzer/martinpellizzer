Rhododendron × carnarvonii

Rhododendron × carnarvonii, commonly known as the Carnarvon rhododendron, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
