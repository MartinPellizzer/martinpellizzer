Rosa onoei f. plena

Rosa onoei f. plena, commonly known as the Japanese hybrid tea rose, is a floriferous cultivar characterized by its abundant, full blooms.

It features large, double flowers with a rich, velvety texture and a wide range of colors, often including shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is a hybrid between Rosa onoei and Rosa damascena, resulting in a robust, thorny shrub with dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to produce optimal flower display.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
