Ruellia polysperma

Ruellia polysperma, commonly known as the common ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia polysperma is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
