Rosa afzeliana var. curiosa

Rosa afzeliana var. curiosa, commonly known as the curious rose, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and vibrant, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, and its fruit is a follicle containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
