Rosa miniata

Rosa miniata, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a compact shrub prized for its small, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to Europe and western Asia. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its blooms come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white.

Rosa miniata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
