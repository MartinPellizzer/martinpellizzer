Ranunculus rubellinodes

Ranunculus rubellinodes, commonly known as the red-stemmed buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and bright yellow flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
