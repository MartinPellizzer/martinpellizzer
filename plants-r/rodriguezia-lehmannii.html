Rodriguezia lehmannii

Rodriguezia lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's rodriguezia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
