Regalia titan

Regalia titan, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, showy flowers that can reach up to three feet in diameter. The plant's botanical name, *Vanda regalia*, reflects its regal appearance and its association with the genus Vanda. It thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, often growing as an epiphyte on trees.

Its unique structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.
