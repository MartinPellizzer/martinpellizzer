Rouxia × hordeoides

Rouxia × hordeoides, commonly known as the false barley, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
