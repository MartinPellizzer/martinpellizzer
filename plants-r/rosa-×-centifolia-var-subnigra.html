Rosa × centifolia var. subnigra

Rosa × centifolia var. subnigra, commonly known as the dark red garden rose, is a hybrid rose species prized for its rich, deep red blooms.

It is a cultivar of Rosa centifolia, which is known for its numerous petals and fragrant flowers. The variety subnigra specifically features darker, almost blackish-red hues, making it visually striking. This rose is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance in perfumery.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote flowering.
