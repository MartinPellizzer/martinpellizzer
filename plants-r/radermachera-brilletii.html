Radermachera brilletii

Radermachera brilletii, commonly known as the velvetleaf maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
