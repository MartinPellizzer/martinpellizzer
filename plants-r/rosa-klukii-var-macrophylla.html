Rosa klukii var. macrophylla

Rosa klukii var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed Klukii rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a valuable addition to gardens.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
