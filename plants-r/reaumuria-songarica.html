Reaumuria songarica

Reaumuria songarica, commonly known as the Songarica sandwort, is a low-growing shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperature fluctuations. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor firmly in loose, nutrient-poor substrates.

Reaumuria songarica plays a role in stabilizing desert ecosystems and is often found in steppe and desert scrub habitats.
