Rubus litacanthus

Rubus litacanthus, commonly known as the spiny blackberry, is a species of bramble native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are usually dark purple to black in color. Its flowers are white or pale pink, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
