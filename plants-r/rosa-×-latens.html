Rosa × latens

Rosa × latens, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub that combines traits from various rose species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the flowers.

Rosa × latens is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
