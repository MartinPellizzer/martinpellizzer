Ravenea moorei

Ravenea moorei, commonly known as the Moore's Ravenea, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, palm-like leaves that can reach up to three meters in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and are arranged in a fan shape at the top of the plant. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries.

This species is well-suited for warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to thrive.
