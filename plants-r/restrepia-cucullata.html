Restrepia cucullata

Restrepia cucullata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats at high elevations. The plant produces striking, helmet-shaped flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky substrates, while its leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Restrepia cucullata is appreciated for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
