Rubus setchuenensis var. omeiensis

Rubus setchuenensis var. omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei raspberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, red, edible fruits. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges and white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
