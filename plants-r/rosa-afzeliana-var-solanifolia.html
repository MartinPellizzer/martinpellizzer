Rosa afzeliana var. solanifolia

Rosa afzeliana var. solanifolia, commonly known as the Solanifolia rose, is a variant of the Rosa afzeliana species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to orange flowers. The leaves are typically pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
