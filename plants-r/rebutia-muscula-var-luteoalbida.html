Rebutia muscula var. luteoalbida

Rebutia muscula var. luteoalbida, commonly known as the yellow-white moon cactus, is a hybrid cactus species originating from South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded stem that often displays a striking yellow or white coloration. The plant typically produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for container gardening and rockeries.
