Restio spiculatus

Restio spiculatus, commonly known as the spike restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It produces small white or pale blue flowers, which are clustered in dense spikes.
