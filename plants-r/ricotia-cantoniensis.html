Ricotia cantoniensis

Ricotia cantoniensis, commonly known as the Chinese thornapple, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

The species is often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides, showing some level of adaptability to different environments.
