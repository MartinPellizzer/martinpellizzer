Rhynchosia rojasii

Rhynchosia rojasii, commonly known as the pink-flowered burr legume, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink or purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, and it has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil. Rhynchosia rojasii is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
