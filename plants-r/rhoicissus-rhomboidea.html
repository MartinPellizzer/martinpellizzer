Rhoicissus rhomboidea

Rhoicissus rhomboidea, commonly known as the heartleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

Rhoicissus rhomboidea is also valued for its ability to tolerate various soil types and its role in supporting local wildlife.
