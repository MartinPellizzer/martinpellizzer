Rosa × charrieri

Rosa × charrieri, commonly known as the Charrier rose, is a hybrid shrub native to France.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, serrated leaves. It is well-suited for hedging and ornamental use due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements, and the plant is relatively disease-resistant.
