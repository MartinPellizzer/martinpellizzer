Rosa friedlaenderiana

Rosa friedlaenderiana, commonly known as the Friedlander rose, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Rosa friedlaenderiana is also notable for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
