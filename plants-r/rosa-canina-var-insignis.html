Rosa canina var. insignis

Rosa canina var. insignis, commonly known as the dog rose variant insignis, is a less commonly cultivated form of the dog rose.

It is characterized by its smaller, more delicate flowers compared to the typical Rosa canina. The plant produces bright red hips that are often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
