Ruyschiana thymiflora

Ruyschiana thymiflora, commonly known as the sweet thyme plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, yellowish flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ruyschiana thymiflora is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its antiseptic and digestive benefits.
