Rupicapnos reboudiana

Rupicapnos reboudiana, commonly known as the silverleaf beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, showy purple flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by clusters of bright blue berries in the fall. The plant has aromatic, finely textured foliage that turns bronze in the winter. It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
