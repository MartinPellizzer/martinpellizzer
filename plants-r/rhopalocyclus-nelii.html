Rhopalocyclus nelii

Rhopalocyclus nelii, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in a raceme, with a distinctive coloration.
