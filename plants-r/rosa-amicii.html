Rosa amicii

Rosa amicii, commonly known as the Amici's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Rosa amicii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
