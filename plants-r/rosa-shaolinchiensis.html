Rosa shaolinchiensis

Rosa shaolinchiensis, commonly known as the Shaolin Rose, is a rare and ancient species of rose native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring. It is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in Chinese horticulture.

Due to its rarity and specific cultivation requirements, it is considered a collector's item among rose enthusiasts.
