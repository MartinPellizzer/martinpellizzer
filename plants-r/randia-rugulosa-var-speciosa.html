Randia rugulosa var. speciosa

Randia rugulosa var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable environments.
