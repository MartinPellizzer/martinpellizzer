Rumex paucifolius var. gracilescens

Rumex paucifolius var. gracilescens, commonly known as slender dock, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright, slender stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences, and it produces oval, brown seeds in a cupule.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in wetland habitats.
