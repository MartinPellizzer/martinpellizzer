Rhodothamnus camtschaticus

Rhodothamnus camtschaticus, commonly known as the Caucasian firethorn, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, bright red berries that are highly ornamental. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
