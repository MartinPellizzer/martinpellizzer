Relbunium hypocarpium var. indecorum

Relbunium hypocarpium var. indecorum, commonly known as the elegant bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The variety 'indecorum' is distinguished by its more elongated flowers and slightly different leaf morphology compared to the typical form.
