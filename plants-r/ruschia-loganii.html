Ruschia loganii

Ruschia loganii, commonly known as the Logan's ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Ruschia loganii is valued for its low maintenance and ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
