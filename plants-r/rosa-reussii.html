Rosa reussii

Rosa reussii, commonly known as the Reuss rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Caucasus and parts of Turkey. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa reussii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
