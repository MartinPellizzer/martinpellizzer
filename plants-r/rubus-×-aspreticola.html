Rubus × aspreticola

Rubus × aspreticola, commonly known as the aspreticola raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that are similar to other raspberry varieties.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological traits from its parent species.
