Rebutia xanthocarpa var. elegans

Rebutia xanthocarpa var. elegans, commonly known as the golden fruit rebutia, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features compact, globular stems covered in dense, woolly gray-green foliage. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that turns orange as it matures.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water.
