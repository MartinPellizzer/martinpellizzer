Rhododendron camtschaticum var. pumilum

Rhododendron camtschaticum var. pumilum, commonly known as the Caucasian dwarf rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
