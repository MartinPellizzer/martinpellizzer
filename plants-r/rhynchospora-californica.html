Rhynchospora californica

Rhynchospora californica, commonly known as the California sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grasses, and is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow leaves. Rhynchospora californica plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
