Roegneria latiglumis

Roegneria latiglumis, commonly known as the broad-leafed wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale to dark green florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Roegneria latiglumis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
