Rapuntium purpurascens

Rapuntium purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, cruciform flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
