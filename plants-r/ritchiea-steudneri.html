Ritchiea steudneri

Ritchiea steudneri, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cycadaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth form. The plant features large, stiff, feather-like leaves that are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. Ritchiea steudneri is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
