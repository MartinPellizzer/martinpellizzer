Rubus pronatiflorus

Rubus pronatiflorus, commonly known as the prostrate raspberry.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its trailing growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.

The botanical features of Rubus pronatiflorus make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and ecological research.
