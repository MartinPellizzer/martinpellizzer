Roegneria kamczadalorum

Roegneria kamczadalorum, commonly known as the Kamchatka wheatgrass, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the cold climates of the Russian Far East, particularly in the Kamchatka Peninsula. This plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows and tundra.

Roegneria kamczadalorum is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
