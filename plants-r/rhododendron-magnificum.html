Rhododendron magnificum

Rhododendron magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent rhododendron, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are dark green and arranged in a dense, bushy habit. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical gardens worldwide.
