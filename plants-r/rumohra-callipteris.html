Rumohra callipteris

Rumohra callipteris, commonly known as the slender wall fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread across rocky or wooded areas.

The leaves are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, elegant appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
