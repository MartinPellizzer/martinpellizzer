Rebutia violaceostaminata

Rebutia violaceostaminata, commonly known as the violet-staminated rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and pale green to blue-gray stems. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant violet flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are covered in fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Rebutia violaceostaminata is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
