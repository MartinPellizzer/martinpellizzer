Rosa × altobracensis

Rosa × altobracensis, commonly known as the Altobracensis rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, semi-double to fully double flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and broad, dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
