Rotang manan

Rotang manan, commonly known as *Pterocarpus indicus*, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, durable wood, which is highly valued for furniture and decorative purposes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by pod-like fruits containing flat, winged seeds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The tree thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.
