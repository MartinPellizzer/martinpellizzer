Rosa communis var. delphinensis

Rosa communis var. delphinensis, commonly known as the Delphinium rose, is a hybrid plant that combines characteristics of roses and delphiniums.

It typically features showy, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a bushy growth habit with lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often grown for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
