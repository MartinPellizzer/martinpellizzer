Rosa × zachariasiana

Rosa × zachariasiana, commonly known as the Zachariasiana rose, is a hybrid rose species that combines traits from various Rosa species.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse flowering, typically producing large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink and white. The plant is deciduous, shedding its foliage in autumn, and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both decorative and cut flower purposes.
