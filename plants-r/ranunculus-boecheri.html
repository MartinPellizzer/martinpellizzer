Ranunculus boecheri

Ranunculus boecheri, commonly known as the Sierra lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy flowering plants. The plant features delicate, palmately divided leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, petal-less flowers. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and conservation efforts.
