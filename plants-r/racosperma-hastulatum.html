Racosperma hastulatum

Racosperma hastulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Racosperma hastulatum is typically found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
