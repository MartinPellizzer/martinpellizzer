Ranunculus cuneiformis

Ranunculus cuneiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features distinctive yellow flowers with five broadly ovate petals, often with a darker central blotch. The plant has deeply lobed, trifoliate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Ranunculus cuneiformis is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions.
