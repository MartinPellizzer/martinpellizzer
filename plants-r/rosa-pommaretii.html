Rosa pommaretii

Rosa pommaretii, commonly known as the Pommaretti rose, is a shrub-like plant native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.

Rosa pommaretii is also valued for its historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
