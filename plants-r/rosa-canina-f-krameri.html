Rosa canina f. krameri

Rosa canina f. krameri, commonly known as the Krameri rose, is a hybrid form of the dog rose.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
