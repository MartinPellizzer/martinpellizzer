Rhynchostylis gigantea subsp. violacea

Rhynchostylis gigantea subsp.

violacea, commonly known as the violet-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking violet-colored stems.

The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive violet or purple hue, often with a contrasting white or cream center. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
