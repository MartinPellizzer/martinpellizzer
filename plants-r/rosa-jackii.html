Rosa jackii

Rosa jackii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with serrated edges and produces hip fruits that contain several seeds. Rosa jackii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
