Rosa hybrida f. brachystylis

Rosa hybrida f. brachystylis, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant blooms.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. Its flowers come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow.

Due to its size and beauty, it is often cultivated as a potted plant or in containers.
