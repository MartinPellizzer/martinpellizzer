Rosa montana f. chavinii

Rosa montana f. chavinii, commonly known as the Chavinii mountain rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It features fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
