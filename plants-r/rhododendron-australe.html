Rhododendron australe

Rhododendron australe, commonly known as the Australian rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Rhododendron australe thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
