Rhododendron fangchengense

Rhododendron fangchengense, commonly known as Fangcheng rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
