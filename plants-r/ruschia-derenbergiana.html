Ruschia derenbergiana

Ruschia derenbergiana, commonly known as the Cape groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often grown as a decorative indoor or outdoor plant. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy texture, and it is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Ruschia derenbergiana prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.
