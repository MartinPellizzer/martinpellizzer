Ruta suaveolens

Ruta suaveolens, commonly known as sweet rue, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies.
