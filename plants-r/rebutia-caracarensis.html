Rebutia caracarensis

Rebutia caracarensis, commonly known as the Caracarensis Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant typically produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it stores water in its fleshy stems, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Rebutia caracarensis is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
