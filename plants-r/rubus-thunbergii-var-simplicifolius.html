Rubus thunbergii var. simplicifolius

Rubus thunbergii var. simplicifolius, commonly known as the simple-leaved bramble, is a variant of the bramble genus Rubus.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruits are aggregate berries that develop from the flowering stems.

This species is native to East Asia and is often found in woodland edges and shrublands.
