Rosa × alpestris var. insularum

Rosa × alpestris var. insularum, commonly known as the Alpine rose or Island rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is well adapted to cold climates and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
