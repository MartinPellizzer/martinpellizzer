Rosa × dufftii

Rosa × dufftii, commonly known as the Dufftii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green foliage. Its botanical features include compound leaves with five to seven leaflets and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa × dufftii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
