Rosularia sedoides

Rosularia sedoides, commonly known as the sand rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its arid habitat. Rosularia sedoides thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
