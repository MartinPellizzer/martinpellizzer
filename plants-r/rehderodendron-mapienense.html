Rehderodendron mapienense

Rehderodendron mapienense, commonly known as the Mapien rose.

This plant is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Vietnam. It is characterized by its dense clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are simple, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a glossy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
