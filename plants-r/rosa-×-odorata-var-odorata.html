Rosa × odorata var. odorata

Rosa × odorata var. odorata, commonly known as the garden rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. Its flowers are typically borne on long pedicels and are composed of five petals and numerous stamens.

This variety is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
