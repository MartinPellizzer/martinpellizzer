Rhododendron indicum var. luteum

Rhododendron indicum var. luteum, commonly known as the yellow rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
