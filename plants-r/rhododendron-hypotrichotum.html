Rhododendron hypotrichotum

Rhododendron hypotrichotum, commonly known as the dwarf rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like China, Vietnam, and Thailand.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and often have a slight pubescence. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron hypotrichotum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
