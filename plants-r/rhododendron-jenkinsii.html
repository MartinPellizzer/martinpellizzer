Rhododendron jenkinsii

Rhododendron jenkinsii, commonly known as the Jenkins rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are large, showy, and usually pink or red, blooming in late spring.

Rhododendron jenkinsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
