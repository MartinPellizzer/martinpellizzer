Rosa elliptica f. rothomagensis

Rosa elliptica f. rothomagensis, commonly known as the Rothomagensis rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa elliptica species.

It is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with a delicate fragrance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in temperate climates.

It requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
