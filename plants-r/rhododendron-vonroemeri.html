Rhododendron vonroemeri

Rhododendron vonroemeri, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves and a woody stem that supports its upright growth habit.

Rhododendron vonroemeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
