Rhamnus sukatschewii

Rhamnus sukatschewii, commonly known as the Chinese buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by small, dark blue berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
