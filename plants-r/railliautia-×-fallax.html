Railliautia × fallax

Railliautia × fallax, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Railliautia grandiflora and Railliautia rutilans.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a basal rosette.

Railliautia × fallax is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
