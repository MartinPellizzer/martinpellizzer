Rubus macrostemon

Rubus macrostemon, commonly known as the large-fruited blackberry, is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it grows in woodland and scrub habitats. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems, trifoliate leaves, and aggregate fruits that develop from multiple ovaria. Its berries are typically dark purple to black in color and have a tart flavor.

Rubus macrostemon is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
