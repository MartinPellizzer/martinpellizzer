Rytidostylis filiformis

Rytidostylis filiformis, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in soil.

This orchid is often found in lowland and montane forests, where it thrives in humid, shaded conditions.
