Renealmia polystachia

Renealmia polystachia, commonly known as the purple panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, feathery inflorescences that produce small, purple to pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy.

Renealmia polystachia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its attractive floral display.
