Raillardella scaposa var. eisenii

Raillardella scaposa var. eisenii, commonly known as the Eisen's raillardella, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
