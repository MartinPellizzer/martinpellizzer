Richea sprengelioides

Richea sprengelioides, commonly known as the coastal richea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong winds.

Richea sprengelioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in coastal ecosystems.
