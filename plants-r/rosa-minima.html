Rosa minima

Rosa minima, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. Rosa minima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and compact size.

Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually thorny.
