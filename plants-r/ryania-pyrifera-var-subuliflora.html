Ryania pyrifera var. subuliflora

Ryania pyrifera var. subuliflora, commonly known as the needle-leaved ryania, is a tropical shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are follicles that contain several seeds, and the plant is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its unique foliage.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
