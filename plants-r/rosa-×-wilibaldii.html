Rosa × wilibaldii

Rosa × wilibaldii, commonly known as the Wilibaldii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and profuse display of semi-double to double pink flowers. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are typically thornless. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
