Rosa micrantha f. oblongicalyx

Rosa micrantha f. oblongicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with five petals, blooming in early summer. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
