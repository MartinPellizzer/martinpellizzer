Retama raetam subsp. gussonei

Retama raetam subsp.

gussonei, commonly known as the Mediterranean broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves, which are adapted to arid conditions.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. It has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in dry environments.
