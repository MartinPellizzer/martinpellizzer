Rhododendron jasminiflorum var. heusseri

Rhododendron jasminiflorum var. heusseri, commonly known as the Heusser's jasmine rhododendron, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms.

This variety is valued for its unique fragrance and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
