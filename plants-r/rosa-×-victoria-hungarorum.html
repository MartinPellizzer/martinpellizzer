Rosa × victoria-hungarorum

Rosa × victoria-hungarorum, commonly known as the Hungarian Victoria rose, is a hybrid rose known for its large, fragrant flowers.

It features deep red petals with a dark red center, creating a striking visual effect. The plant is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This rose is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
