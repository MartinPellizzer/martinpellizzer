Rhamnus crenata var. xianjuensis

Rhamnus crenata var. xianjuensis, commonly known as the Xianju boxthorn, is a shrub native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves with crenate margins and produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It is often found in rocky or forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.

The variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
