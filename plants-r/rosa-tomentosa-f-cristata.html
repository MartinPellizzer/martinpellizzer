Rosa tomentosa f. cristata

Rosa tomentosa f. cristata, commonly known as the woolly rose or cushion rose, is a unique cultivar of the species Rosa tomentosa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and soft, white to pale pink flowers. The plant features a thick, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It is often grown as a decorative plant for its ornamental value and compact form.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
