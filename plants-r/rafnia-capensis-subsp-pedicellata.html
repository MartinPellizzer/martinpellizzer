Rafnia capensis subsp. pedicellata

Rafnia capensis subsp.

pedicellata, commonly known as the Cape pedicellate starfish, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
