Ruellia alba

Ruellia alba, commonly known as white ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia alba is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often used in ornamental gardening.
