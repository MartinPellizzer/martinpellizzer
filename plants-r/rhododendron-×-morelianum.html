Rhododendron × morelianum

Rhododendron × morelianum, commonly known as the Morelian rhododendron, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between Rhododendron mucronatum and Rhododendron sinogrande.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves that provide an attractive backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
