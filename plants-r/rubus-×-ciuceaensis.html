Rubus × ciuceaensis

Rubus × ciuceaensis, commonly known as the Ciucea raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
