Rapanea schwackeana

Rapanea schwackeana, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the white blossoms. The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in garden settings.
