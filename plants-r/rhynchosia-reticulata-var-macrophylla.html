Rhynchosia reticulata var. macrophylla

Rhynchosia reticulata var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf snout bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange with a prominent red calyx. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
