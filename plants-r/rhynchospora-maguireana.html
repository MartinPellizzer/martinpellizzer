Rhynchospora maguireana

Rhynchospora maguireana, commonly known as the Maguire's spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Rhynchospora maguireana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
