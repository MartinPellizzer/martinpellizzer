Rhynchosia courtallensis

Rhynchosia courtallensis, commonly known as the pink-flowered legume, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is distinguished by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its trifoliate leaves and hairy stems are typical of the genus Rhynchosia.

It is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as forest edges and riverbanks.
