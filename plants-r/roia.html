Roia

Roia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white, and bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Roia is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in indirect light and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
