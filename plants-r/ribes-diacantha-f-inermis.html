Ribes diacantha f. inermis

Ribes diacantha f. inermis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed gooseberry, is a variant of the species Ribes diacantha.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features slender, smooth stems and produces small, yellowish-green berries. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it typically grows in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
