Rosa bovernieriana var. kerneri

Rosa bovernieriana var. kerneri, commonly known as the Kern's rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to reddish-pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
