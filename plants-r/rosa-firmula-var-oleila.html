Rosa firmula var. oleila

Rosa firmula var. oleila, commonly known as the Oleila rose, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in clusters. The plant produces fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to coastal and rocky environments, showing resistance to salt spray and drought.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.
