Rosa szaboi var. paklenicae

Rosa szaboi var. paklenicae, commonly known as the Paklenica rose, is a rare and unique variety of rose native to Croatia.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers with a faint red hue at the base, which bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered a valuable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
