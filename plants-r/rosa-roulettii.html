Rosa roulettii

Rosa roulettii, commonly known as the Rouletti rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance.

Rosa roulettii is also valued for its fragrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
