Rubus × czornebogensis

Rubus × czornebogensis, commonly known as the Czornebogen raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is cultivated for its edible berries. Its botanical features include compound leaves and a complex flowering structure.

It is often found in wild or semi-wild environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
