Ranunculus aconitifolius var. flexicaulis

Ranunculus aconitifolius var. flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible leaf buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves that are often described as resembling those of the aconite plant. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Ranunculaceae family.
