Rollinia schunkei

Rollinia schunkei, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

The flowers are small, white, and fragrant, often clustered in inflorescences at the base of the leaves.
