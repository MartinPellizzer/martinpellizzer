Ranunculus exilis

Ranunculus exilis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and showy yellow or white flowers with numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Ranunculus exilis is valued in horticulture for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
