Rosa × braunii

Rosa × braunii, commonly known as the Braun's rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces oval, green leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers often have a prominent center with a yellowish hue. Rosa × braunii is native to China and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
