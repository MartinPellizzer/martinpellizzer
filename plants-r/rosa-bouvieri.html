Rosa bouvieri

Rosa bouvieri, commonly known as the Boucher rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces red, hairy fruits known as hips. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Rosa bouvieri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
