Rebutia atrovirens

Rebutia atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and dark green, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Rebutia atrovirens is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
