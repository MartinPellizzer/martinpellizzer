Ribes vulgare

Ribes vulgare, commonly known as European gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the genus Ribes in the family Grossulariaceae. The plant produces small, yellow or green berries that are typically edible and used in culinary applications. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges.
