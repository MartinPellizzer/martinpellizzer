Rubus × bellissimus

Rubus × bellissimus, commonly known as the beautiful blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically sweet and aromatic. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along woodland edges.

Botanically, it exhibits traits of both its parental species, showcasing genetic diversity in its morphology and fruit quality.
