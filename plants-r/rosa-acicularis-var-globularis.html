Rosa acicularis var. globularis

Rosa acicularis var. globularis, commonly known as the globular rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, thornless stems and produces numerous small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a dense, rounded growth habit, making it an ornamental choice for garden settings. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are trifoliate, adding a lush appearance to the plant.

It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
