Racosperma excelsum var. angustum

Racosperma excelsum var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf waratah, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
