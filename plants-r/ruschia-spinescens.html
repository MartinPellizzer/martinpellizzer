Ruschia spinescens

Ruschia spinescens, commonly known as the spiny Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spiny leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
