Rosa × dumetorum subsp. obtusifolia

Rosa × dumetorum subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The leaves are typically gray-green with a dense covering of fine hairs, and the stems are covered in thorns. This subspecies is well adapted to cold climates and grows in rocky or alpine environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
