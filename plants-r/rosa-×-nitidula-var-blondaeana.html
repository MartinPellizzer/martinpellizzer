Rosa × nitidula var. blondaeana

Rosa × nitidula var. blondaeana, commonly known as the Blondaeana rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, single to semi-double blooms.

It typically produces pale yellow to cream-colored flowers that are often adorned with a faint blush of pink, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that remains attractive throughout the growing season. It is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its fragrance and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
