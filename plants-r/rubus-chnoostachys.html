Rubus chnoostachys

Rubus chnoostachys, commonly known as the red raspberry, is a shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits known as raspberries, which are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruits.

Rubus chnoostachys is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
