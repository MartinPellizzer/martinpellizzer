Rourea accrescens

Rourea accrescens, commonly known as the evergreen rourea, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a leathery texture with a smooth margin.

Rourea accrescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
