Ranunculus Royleanus

Ranunculus royleanus is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its delicate petals and unique leaf structure make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it is also noted for its toxic properties, containing compounds that can be harmful to humans and animals. This plant exemplifies the diversity and complexity found in the plant kingdom.
