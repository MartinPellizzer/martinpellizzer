Rohria cynaroides

Rohria cynaroides, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
