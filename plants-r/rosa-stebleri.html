Rosa stebleri

Rosa stebleri, commonly known as the Persian rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Iran, Afghanistan, and the Caucasus. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a woody stem with thorny branches.

Rosa stebleri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual appeal.
