Ranunculus orbiculatus

Ranunculus orbiculatus, commonly known as the globe ranunculus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive black center. The plant has palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ranunculus orbiculatus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early spring bloom.
