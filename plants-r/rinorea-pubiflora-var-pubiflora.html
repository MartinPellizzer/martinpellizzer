Rinorea pubiflora var. pubiflora

Rinorea pubiflora var. pubiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf rinorea, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
