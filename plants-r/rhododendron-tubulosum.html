Rhododendron tubulosum

Rhododendron tubulosum, commonly known as the tubular rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of tubular-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rhododendron tubulosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
