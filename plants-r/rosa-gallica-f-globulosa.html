Rosa gallica f. globulosa

Rosa gallica f. globulosa, commonly known as the Globe Rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with dense, rounded growth.

It features small, tightly packed flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces rose hips that are spherical and often red or orange in color. It is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and historical use in perfumery and herbal medicine.
