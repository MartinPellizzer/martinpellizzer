Racemobambos schultzei

Racemobambos schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to parts of Africa.

Racemobambos schultzei is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
