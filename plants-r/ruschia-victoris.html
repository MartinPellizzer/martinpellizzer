Ruschia victoris

Ruschia victoris, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability to arid conditions and low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among gardeners.
