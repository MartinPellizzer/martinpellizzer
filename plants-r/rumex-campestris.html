Rumex campestris

Rumex campestris, commonly known as broadleaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by triangular, reddish-brown seed capsules. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in fields.

Rumex campestris is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
