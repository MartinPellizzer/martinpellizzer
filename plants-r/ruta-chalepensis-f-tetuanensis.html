Ruta chalepensis f. tetuanensis

Ruta chalepensis f. tetuanensis, commonly known as the Tetuanena Rue, is a variant of the common rue plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant contains volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, bitter odor and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal remedies.
