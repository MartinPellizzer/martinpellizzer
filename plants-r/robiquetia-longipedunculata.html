Robiquetia longipedunculata

Robiquetia longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-peduncled orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its long, slender peduncles support clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
