Rhynchospora umemurae var. yakusimensis

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in the understory of evergreen forests. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a robust rhizome that helps it anchor in the nutrient-poor soil of its native habitat.
