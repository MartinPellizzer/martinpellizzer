Rosa extensifrons

Rosa extensifrons, commonly known as the broad-fruited rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets arranged along the stem.

The fruit, a red, fuzzy rose hip, matures in the fall and is an important food source for various wildlife species.
