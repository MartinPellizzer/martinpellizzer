Rubus iodes f. laxus

Rubus iodes f. laxus, commonly known as the loose-stemmed raspberry.

This is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. It typically grows in open woodlands and disturbed areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers in the spring, followed by edible red berries in the summer.

Its foliage is usually compound, with serrated leaflets and a distinctive, aromatic scent.
