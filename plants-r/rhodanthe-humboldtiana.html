Rhodanthe humboldtiana

Rhodanthe humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt's rhodanthe, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

Rhodanthe humboldtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
