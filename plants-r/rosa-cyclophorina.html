Rosa cyclophorina

Rosa cyclophorina, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species native to China and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit.

It features dense, thorny stems and pinnate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late spring to early summer. Rosa cyclophorina is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It is also notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
