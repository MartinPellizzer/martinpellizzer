Rosa modicella

Rosa modicella, commonly known as the Modicella rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Rosa modicella is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance in suitable climates.
