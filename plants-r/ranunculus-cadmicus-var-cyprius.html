Ranunculus cadmicus var. cyprius

Ranunculus cadmicus var. cyprius, commonly known as the Cyprus ranunculus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, palmate leaves and showy yellow flowers with prominent black markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. It is characterized by its short stature and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some areas.
