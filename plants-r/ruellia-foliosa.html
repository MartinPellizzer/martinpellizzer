Ruellia foliosa

Ruellia foliosa, commonly known as the leafy ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue, purple, or white. The plant has heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Ruellia foliosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens and landscapes.
