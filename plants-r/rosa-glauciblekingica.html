Rosa glauciblekingica

Rosa glauciblekingica, commonly known as the Glaucous Blushing Rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its pale blue to white flowers with a distinctive blushing pink hue that appears as they mature. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are often covered in thorns.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
