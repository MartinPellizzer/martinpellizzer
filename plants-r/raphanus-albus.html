Raphanus albus

Raphanus albus, commonly known as the white radish, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia and is characterized by its white, fleshy root and green, lobed leaves. Its root is often cultivated for its crisp texture and mild flavor, while the leaves are also edible.
