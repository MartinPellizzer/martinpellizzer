Rosa afzeliana var. caballicensis

Rosa afzeliana var. caballicensis, commonly known as the Caballic rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorned stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
