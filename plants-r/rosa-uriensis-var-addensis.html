Rosa uriensis var. addensis

Rosa uriensis var. addensis, commonly known as the Addiscombe rose, is a rare and distinctive cultivar of the rose family.

It is characterized by its deep pink, semi-double flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its rarity, it is often sought after by horticultural enthusiasts and conservationists.
