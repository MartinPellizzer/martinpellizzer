Rabdosia rubescens f. lushanensis

Rabdosia rubescens f. lushanensis, commonly known as the Lushan mountain rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
