Rosa micrantha f. robusta

Rosa micrantha f. robusta, commonly known as the robust rose, is a hybrid form of the shrub rose Rosa micrantha.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering, producing clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent foliage and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
