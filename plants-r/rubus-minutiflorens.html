Rubus minutiflorens

Rubus minutiflorens, commonly known as the small-flowered blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern Australia, characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, dark berries. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
