Reseda globulosa

Reseda globulosa, commonly known as globular mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, showy pink or purple flowers. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves and stems adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique globular seed pods add an ornamental feature to its overall appearance.
