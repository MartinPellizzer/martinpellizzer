Ruellia philippinensis

Ruellia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
