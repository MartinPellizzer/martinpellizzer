Relbunium laevigatum

Relbunium laevigatum, commonly known as the smoothleaf relbunium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant features slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, with small, pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring.

Relbunium laevigatum is noted for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and conservationists due to its limited distribution.
