Rosa cucumerina

Rosa cucumerina, commonly known as the cucumber rose, is a unique flowering plant characterized by its edible, cucumber-like fruits.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features delicate pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and the stems are hairy and somewhat prickly.

The fruit, which resembles a small cucumber, is typically green and can be harvested when immature for consumption.
