Renanthera flos-aeris

Renanthera flos-aeris, commonly known as the sky flower, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high altitudes of Southeast Asia.

This species is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble floating petals, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its blooming period typically occurs during the cooler months, and it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, Renanthera flos-aeris is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
