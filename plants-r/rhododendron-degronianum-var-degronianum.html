Rhododendron degronianum var. degronianum

Rhododendron degronianum var. degronianum, commonly known as the Degronean rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
