Rubus × polituliformis

Rubus × polituliformis, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically a mix of red and black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
