Rosa semisimplex

Rosa semisimplex, commonly known as the simple rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, single-layered petals and is native to parts of Asia. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and produces fragrant flowers in a variety of colors. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
