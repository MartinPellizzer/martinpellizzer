Rapistrum maritimum

Rapistrum maritimum, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its bright green, succulent leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found along shorelines and salt marshes.

Sea rocket is also known for its edible leaves and has been used in traditional cuisines.
