Rubia maillardii

Rubia maillardii, commonly known as Maillard's rubia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Rubia maillardii is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
