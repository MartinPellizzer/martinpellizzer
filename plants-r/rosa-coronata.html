Rosa coronata

Rosa coronata, commonly known as the Crowned Rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, single or double flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with ovate to lanceolate leaflets.

Rosa coronata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to dry, sunny climates.
