Rhipsalis lorentziana

Rhipsalis lorentziana, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It features long, slender stems that are often covered in a silvery, waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
