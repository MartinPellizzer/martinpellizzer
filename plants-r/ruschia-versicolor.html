Ruschia versicolor

Ruschia versicolor, commonly known as the variable Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially under stress or in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia versicolor is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
