Ruta microcarpa

Ruta microcarpa, commonly known as Mexican rue, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Ruta microcarpa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
