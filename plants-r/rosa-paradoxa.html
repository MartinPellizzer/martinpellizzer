Rosa paradoxa

Rosa paradoxa, commonly known as the paradox rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thornless stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Rosa paradoxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
