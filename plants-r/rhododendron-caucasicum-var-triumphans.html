Rhododendron caucasicum var. triumphans

Rhododendron caucasicum var. triumphans, commonly known as the Caucasian Triumphant Rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
