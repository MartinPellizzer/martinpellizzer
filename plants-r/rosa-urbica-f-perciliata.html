Rosa urbica f. perciliata

Rosa urbica f. perciliata, commonly known as the urban rose or city rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to Europe and is often found growing in urban environments, such as along streets and in gardens. Its adaptability to various soil types and tolerance to pollution make it a resilient species.

The flowers are typically used in ornamental gardening and can also be harvested for culinary purposes.
