Rhododendron delicatulum var. delicatulum

Rhododendron delicatulum var. delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a dense foliage that supports a variety of wildlife.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to add vibrant color to shaded landscapes.
