Ribes nigrum f. xanthocarpum

Ribes nigrum f. xanthocarpum, commonly known as the yellow-fruited gooseberry, is a botanical variant of the common gooseberry.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow berries, which differ from the typical red or black fruits of the species. The plant belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in well-drained soil.
