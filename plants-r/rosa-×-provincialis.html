Rosa × provincialis

Rosa × provincialis, commonly known as the Provincial rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × provincialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery and medicine.
