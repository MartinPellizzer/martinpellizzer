Ritchiea jansii

Ritchiea jansii, commonly known as the Jansii's cycad, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched stem and a crown of large, feathery leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. Ritchiea jansii is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
