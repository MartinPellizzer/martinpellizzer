Rhododendron neriiflorum subsp. agetum

Rhododendron neriiflorum subsp.

agetum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, preferring acidic soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.

Its subspecific distinction highlights its unique adaptations to its native habitat.
