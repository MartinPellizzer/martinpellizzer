Rondeletia purdiei var. glabrior

Rondeletia purdiei var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth rondeletia, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.

The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, giving the plant a refined appearance.
