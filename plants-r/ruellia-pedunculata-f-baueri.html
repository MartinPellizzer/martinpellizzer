Ruellia pedunculata f. baueri

Ruellia pedunculata f. baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of tubular, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.

Ruellia pedunculata f. baueri is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in garden beds.
