Rabdosia muliensis

Rabdosia muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
