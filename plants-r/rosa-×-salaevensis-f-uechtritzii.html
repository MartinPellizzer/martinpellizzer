Rosa × salaevensis f. uechtritzii

Rosa × salaevensis f. uechtritzii, commonly known as the Uechtritzii rose, is a hybrid rose species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. The flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five to seven petals and a prominent, fragrant scent. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
