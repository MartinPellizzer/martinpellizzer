Rhododendron nudipes f. gokanosyoense

Rhododendron nudipes f. gokanosyoense, commonly known as the Gokanosyo rhododendron, is a rare and unique variant of the rhododendron species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically features broad, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a valuable addition to shaded garden landscapes.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized nurseries.
