Rhamnus hemsleyana var. hemsleyana

Rhamnus hemsleyana var. hemsleyana, commonly known as the Hemsley's buckthorn, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
