Rhododendron ningyuenense

Rhododendron ningyuenense, commonly known as the Ningyuen rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist climates. It is often found in forest understories, where it forms part of the understory vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
