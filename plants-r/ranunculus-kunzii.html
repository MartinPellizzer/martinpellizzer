Ranunculus kunzii

Ranunculus kunzii, commonly known as the Kunzii buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features palmately divided leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be challenging to grow outside its native habitat.
