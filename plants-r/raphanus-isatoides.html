Raphanus isatoides

Raphanus isatoides, commonly known as the oriental mustard or yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines and herbal medicine.
