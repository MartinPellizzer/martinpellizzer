Rosa tomentosa f. angulosa

Rosa tomentosa f. angulosa, commonly known as the angular woolly rose, is a botanical variant of the woolly rose species.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy grayish-white pubescence covering its stems and leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in gardens.
