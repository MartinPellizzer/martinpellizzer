Rosa baitagensis

Rosa baitagensis, commonly known as the Baitagani rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its botanical features include hairy leaves and a strong, pleasant aroma, making it a desirable ornamental plant.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
