Rosa mollis f. argutiformis

Rosa mollis f. argutiformis, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and delicate pink to white flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub native to China, often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a subtle fragrance to the garden.

The foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, providing year-round visual interest.
