Rosa sericea var. polyphylla

Rosa sericea var. polyphylla, commonly known as the silky rose or the silverleaf rose, is a variant of the silky rose species native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves and clusters of fragrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost velvety appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
