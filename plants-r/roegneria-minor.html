Roegneria minor

Roegneria minor, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a cool-season grass native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, disturbed areas.

Roegneria minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
