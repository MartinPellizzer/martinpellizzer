Rosa × polliniana var. ambigua

Rosa × polliniana var. ambigua, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its semi-evergreen to evergreen foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
