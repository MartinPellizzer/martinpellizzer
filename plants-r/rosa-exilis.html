Rosa exilis

Rosa exilis, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thin, wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with five petals and a subtle fragrance.

Rosa exilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
