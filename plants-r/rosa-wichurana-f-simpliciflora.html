Rosa wichurana f. simpliciflora

Rosa wichurana f. simpliciflora, commonly known as the simple-flowered Rosa wichurana, is a botanical variant of the Rosa wichurana species.

It is characterized by its simplified floral structure, featuring fewer petals compared to other forms of the species. This cultivar typically produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white. It is a deciduous shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and fragrant blooms.
