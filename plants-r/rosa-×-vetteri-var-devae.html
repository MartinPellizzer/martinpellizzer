Rosa × vetteri var. devae

Rosa × vetteri var. devae, commonly known as the "Devon rose," is a hybrid rose species originating from the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from deep pink to crimson. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and historical botanical collections.
