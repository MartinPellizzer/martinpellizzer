Rosa × chabertii var. walteri

Rosa × chabertii var. walteri, commonly known as Walter's hybrid rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
