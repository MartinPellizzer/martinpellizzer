Rubus × perconfertus

Rubus × perconfertus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. It features dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. The plant is native to eastern Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some use in traditional herbal medicine.
