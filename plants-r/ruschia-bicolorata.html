Ruschia bicolorata

Ruschia bicolorata, commonly known as the two-colored Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a distinctive red or purple stripe along the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and rockeries.
