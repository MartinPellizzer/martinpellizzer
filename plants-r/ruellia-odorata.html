Ruellia odorata

Ruellia odorata, commonly known as sweet rosel.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Ruellia odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued for its fragrance, which contributes to its common name.
