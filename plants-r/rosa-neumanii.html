Rosa neumanii

Rosa neumanii, commonly known as the San Diego rose, is a species of flowering plant native to California.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers with five petals, which bloom in the spring. It is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in coastal climates.

Rosa neumanii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
