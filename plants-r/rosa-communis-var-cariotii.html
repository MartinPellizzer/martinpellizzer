Rosa communis var. cariotii

Rosa communis var. cariotii, commonly known as the Cariotti's rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It typically produces semi-double to fully double blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, depending on the specific cultivar. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy flowers. It is well-suited for garden borders, containers, and as a shrub due to its hardiness and adaptability.

The botanical features of Rosa communis var. cariotii make it a popular choice for both ornamental and cut flower purposes.
