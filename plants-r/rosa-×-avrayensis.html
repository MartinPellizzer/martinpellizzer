Rosa × avrayensis

Rosa × avrayensis, commonly known as the Avrayensis rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × avrayensis is relatively disease-resistant and is suitable for both garden beds and container planting.
