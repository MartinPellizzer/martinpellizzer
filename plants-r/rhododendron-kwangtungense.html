Rhododendron kwangtungense

Rhododendron kwangtungense, commonly known as the Guangdong rhododendron, is a species native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy in appearance. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, appearing in clusters during the spring.

Rhododendron kwangtungense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
