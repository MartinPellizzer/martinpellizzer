Rhododendron sayeri

Rhododendron sayeri, commonly known as the Sayer's rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high elevations. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many ornamental and evergreen plants.

Rhododendron sayeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
