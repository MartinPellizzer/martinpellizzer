Rosenbergiodendron formosum var. nitidum

Rosenbergiodendron formosum var. nitidum, commonly known as the glossy leaf cycad, is a unique cycad species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage composed of long, narrow, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, yellowish to greenish cones, which are typical of cycads. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
