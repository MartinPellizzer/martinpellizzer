Rhaphiolepis umbellata var. ovata

Rhaphiolepis umbellata var. ovata, commonly known as the ovalleaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This species is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
