Ruta holopetala

Ruta holopetala, commonly known as the Mexican rue, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring aromatic, dark green leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are dry, capsule-like structures that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Ruta holopetala is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
