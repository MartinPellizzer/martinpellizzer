Rubus crataegifolius f. itoensis

Rubus crataegifolius f. itoensis, commonly known as the Ito's bramble, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with leaflets that are elliptical and have a serrated margin. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are generally red or black in color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
