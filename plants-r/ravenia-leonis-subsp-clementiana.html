Ravenia leonis subsp. clementiana

Ravenia leonis subsp.

clementiana, commonly known as the Clementiana ravenia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy.

The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, brown seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.
