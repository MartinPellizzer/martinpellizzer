Rosa spinosissima subvar. granatensis

Rosa spinosissima subvar. granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense prickly rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include elliptical leaves with serrated edges and a woody structure that supports its rugged appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
