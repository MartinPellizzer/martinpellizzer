Rhododendron calophytum subsp. jinfuense

Rhododendron calophytum subsp.

jinfuense, commonly known as the Jinfu rhododendron, is a species native to China. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils and high humidity. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range in color from pink to red.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and horticultural collections.
