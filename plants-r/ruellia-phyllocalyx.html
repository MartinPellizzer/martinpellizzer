Ruellia phyllocalyx

Ruellia phyllocalyx, commonly known as the showy ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ruellia phyllocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
