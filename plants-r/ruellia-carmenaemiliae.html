Ruellia carmenaemiliae

Ruellia carmenaemiliae, commonly known as Carmen's ruellia, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Mexico. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to violet tubular flowers. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Ruellia carmenaemiliae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
