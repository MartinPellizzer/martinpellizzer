Ruschia serrulata

Ruschia serrulata, commonly known as the serrated ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ruschia serrulata is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
