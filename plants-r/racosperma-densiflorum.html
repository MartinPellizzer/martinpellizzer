Racosperma densiflorum

Racosperma densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower acacia, is a small shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Racosperma densiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
