Rhynchospora terminalis f. pallida

Rhynchospora terminalis f. pallida, commonly known as the pale form of the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, pale green leaves. The inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes and wetlands.

This form is distinguished by its reduced pigmentation compared to the typical variety.
