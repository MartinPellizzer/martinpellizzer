Rhododendron saruwagedicum var. alpinum

Rhododendron saruwagedicum var. alpinum, commonly known as the Alpine Rhododendron, is a compact shrub native to the high-altitude regions of Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink to reddish-purple flowers in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained, acidic soil. It is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas at elevations above 1,500 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for specialized gardens and botanical collections.
