Rapistrum clavatum

Rapistrum clavatum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other plants like mustard and cabbage.

Rapistrum clavatum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
