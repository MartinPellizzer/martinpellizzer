Ranunculus tener

Ranunculus tener, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features a basal rosette of finely divided, fern-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in its arid habitat. It blooms in early spring and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Ranunculus tener is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.
