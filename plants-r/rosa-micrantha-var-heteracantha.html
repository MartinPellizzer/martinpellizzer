Rosa micrantha var. heteracantha

Rosa micrantha var. heteracantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with elliptical to ovate leaflets that are typically narrow and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant pink to white flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its stems are armed with thorns, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
