Rosa × reversa var. sauzeana

Rosa × reversa var. sauzeana, commonly known as the Sauzean rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its double, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep red. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
