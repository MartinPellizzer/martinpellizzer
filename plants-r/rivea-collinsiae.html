Rivea collinsiae

Rivea collinsiae, commonly known as the Collins' morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the morning. The plant produces violet to blue flowers with a prominent white throat, and it blooms throughout the growing season.
