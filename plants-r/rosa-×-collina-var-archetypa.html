Rosa × collina var. archetypa

Rosa × collina var. archetypa, commonly known as the archetypal hill rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically produces thorny stems and has dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
