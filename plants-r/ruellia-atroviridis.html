Ruellia atroviridis

Ruellia atroviridis, commonly known as the black-veined ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a perennial herb with opposite, ovate leaves that have dark green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Ruellia atroviridis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
