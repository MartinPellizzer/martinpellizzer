Rosa × boulengeri

Rosa × boulengeri, commonly known as the Boulenger rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and abundant, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically produces thorny stems and has compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × boulengeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
