Rosa rubiginosa var. sphaerocarpa

Rosa rubiginosa var. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the sticky rose or the red-stemmed rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive red or orange-brown stems, which are covered in a sticky, resinous substance. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with oval-shaped leaflets that have serrated edges.

The variety sphaerocarpa is notable for its rounded, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds.
