Rosa ferruginea f. pubescens

Rosa ferruginea f. pubescens, commonly known as the rusty rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa ferruginea.

It is characterized by its hairy or pubescent stems and foliage, which give it a distinctive, textured appearance. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and unique foliage texture.
