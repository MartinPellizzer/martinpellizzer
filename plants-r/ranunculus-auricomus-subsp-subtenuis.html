Ranunculus auricomus subsp. subtenuis

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

subtenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features bright yellow flowers with five petal-like sepals that resemble miniature cups.

The leaves are typically deeply lobed and have a distinctive, delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

The plant is characterized by its finely divided foliage and is often found in temperate climates.
