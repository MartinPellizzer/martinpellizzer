Ricinodendron gracilius

Ricinodendron gracilius, commonly known as the slender ricin tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
