Rosa pimpinellifolia var. islandica

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. islandica, commonly known as the Island Strawberry Tree, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces edible fruits that resemble small strawberries, hence its common name. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the unique climate of the islands.

The leaves are typically lobed and covered in a fine, whitish pubescence.
