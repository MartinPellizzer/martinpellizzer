Rapanea rivularis

Rapanea rivularis, commonly known as the river rapanea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often found in the axils of the leaves. The fruit is a small, dark drupe that contains a single seed.
