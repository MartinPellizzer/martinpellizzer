Rhodocactus bahiensis subsp. minensis

Rhodocactus bahiensis subsp.

minensis, commonly known as the Bahia minensis cactus, is a species native to Brazil. It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant features a short, thick stem with a ribbed structure and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and cactus enthusiasts.
