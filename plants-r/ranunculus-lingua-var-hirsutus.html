Ranunculus lingua var. hirsutus

Ranunculus lingua var. hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy tongue buttercup, is a variant of the tongue buttercup species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered with fine, hairy textures. Its flowers are typically yellow with brown or black markings, and they bloom in early spring.

The hairy stems and leaves distinguish this variety from other ranunculus species.
