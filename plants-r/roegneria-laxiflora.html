Roegneria laxiflora

Roegneria laxiflora, commonly known as the slender floret grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, delicate florets that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a smooth texture, and it produces inflorescences that are often spike-like in structure.

Roegneria laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
