Rosa rubiginosa f. dolorosa

Rosa rubiginosa f. dolorosa, commonly known as the dark red rose, is a botanical variant of the shrub rose species Rosa rubiginosa.

It is characterized by its deep, almost blackish-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly velvety texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in perfumery due to the aromatic qualities of its blooms.
