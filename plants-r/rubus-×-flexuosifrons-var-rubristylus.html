Rubus × flexuosifrons var. rubristylus

Rubus × flexuosifrons var. rubristylus, commonly known as the red-styled blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are typically small and firm. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its flowers are white to pale pink, and the fruit is valued for its flavor and use in culinary applications.
