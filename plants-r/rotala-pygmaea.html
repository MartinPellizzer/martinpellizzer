Rotala pygmaea

Rotala pygmaea, commonly known as dwarf rotala, is a small aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, rounded leaves that are typically bright green, often with a slight reddish tinge under bright light. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value.

Rotala pygmaea is known for its easy care and ability to provide dense coverage in planted tanks.
