Rothmannia annae

Rothmannia annae, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a warm, humid climate. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal regions.

Rothmannia annae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens around the world.
