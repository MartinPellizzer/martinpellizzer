Ruschia intricata

Ruschia intricata, commonly known as the starburst pincushion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
