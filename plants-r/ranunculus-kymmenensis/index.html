Ranunculus Kymmenensis

Ranunculus kymmenensis is a rare aquatic plant found in the Kymmeneline Lake system. It thrives in shallow, nutrient-rich waters with soft substrates. This species is notable for its delicate yellow flowers and unique ecological adaptations. Due to habitat loss and pollution, it faces significant conservation challenges. Protecting this plant is crucial for maintaining the biodiversity of its native environment.
