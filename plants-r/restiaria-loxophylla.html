Restiaria loxophylla

Restiaria loxophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restiaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Restiaceae and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in coastal and inland regions.

Restiaria loxophylla is often found in fynbos vegetation and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat.
