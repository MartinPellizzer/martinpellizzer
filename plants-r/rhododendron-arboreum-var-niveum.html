Rhododendron arboreum var. niveum

Rhododendron arboreum var. niveum, commonly known as the Himalayan snow rhododendron, is a shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with acidic soil, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, providing a lush green backdrop to the striking blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
