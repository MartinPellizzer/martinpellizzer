Racosperma gnidium

Racosperma gnidium, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular inflorescences.

Racosperma gnidium is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
