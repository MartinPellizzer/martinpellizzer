Rosa × dumetorum var. vernalis

Rosa × dumetorum var. vernalis, commonly known as the spring shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its stems are often covered in thorns. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
