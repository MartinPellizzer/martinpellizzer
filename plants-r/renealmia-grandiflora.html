Renealmia grandiflora

Renealmia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
