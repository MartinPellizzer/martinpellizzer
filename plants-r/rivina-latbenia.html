Rivina latbenia

Rivina latbenia, commonly known as the red-leaf rivina, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
