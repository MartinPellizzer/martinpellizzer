Richardia aurata

Richardia aurata, commonly known as the golden star grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Richardia aurata is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its potential health benefits.
