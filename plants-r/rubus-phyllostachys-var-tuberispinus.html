Rubus phyllostachys var. tuberispinus

Rubus phyllostachys var. tuberispinus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, red, edible berries. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to the Rubus genus.
