Ranunculus ficaria subsp. nudicaulis

Ranunculus ficaria subsp.

nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, segmented stem.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a distinctive hairy texture.

It is characterized by its short, compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
