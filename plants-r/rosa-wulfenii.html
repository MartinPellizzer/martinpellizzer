Rosa wulfenii

Rosa wulfenii, commonly known as the Wulfen's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late summer.

Rosa wulfenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
