Rhododendron × maculosissimum

Rhododendron × maculosissimum, commonly known as the spotted rhododendron, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are elliptical with a leathery texture, and the flowers are often adorned with distinctive spots or streaks.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
