Rosa micrantha var. nemorosa

Rosa micrantha var. nemorosa, commonly known as the shrub rose or wood rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems with small, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant has small, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces hips that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in woodland environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

This variety is well-suited for naturalized landscapes and garden settings.
