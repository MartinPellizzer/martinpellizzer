Ruprechtia hamanii

Ruprechtia hamanii, commonly known as the Japanese forest bell, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue and white. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green.
