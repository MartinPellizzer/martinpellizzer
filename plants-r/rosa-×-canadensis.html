Rosa × canadensis

Rosa × canadensis, commonly known as the Canada rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to North America.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and dense, arching growth habit. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa × canadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
