Rhododendron tsoi var. tsoi

Rhododendron tsoi var. tsoi, commonly known as the Tsoi rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
