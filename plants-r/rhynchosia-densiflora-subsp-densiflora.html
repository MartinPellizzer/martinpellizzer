Rhynchosia densiflora subsp. densiflora

Rhynchosia densiflora subsp.

densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered rhynchosia, is a species of legume native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its densely clustered flowers, which are typically yellow or orange in color.

The plant has pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive floral display.
