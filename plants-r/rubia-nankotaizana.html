Rubia nankotaizana

Rubia nankotaizana, commonly known as the Nankotaizan balm, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rubia nankotaizana is often cultivated for its aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
