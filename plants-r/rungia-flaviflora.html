Rungia flaviflora

Rungia flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered rungia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rungia flaviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
