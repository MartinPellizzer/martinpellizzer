Rosa abscondita

Rosa abscondita, commonly known as the hidden rose, is a rare and elusive species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely serrated leaves and produces hips that are typically red or orange in color. Rosa abscondita is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh, alpine conditions.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
