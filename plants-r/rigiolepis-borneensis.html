Rigiolepis borneensis

Rigiolepis borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in the dense canopy. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive central spur.

Rigiolepis borneensis plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat, providing nectar for various pollinators.
