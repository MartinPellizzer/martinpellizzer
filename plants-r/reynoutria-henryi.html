Reynoutria henryi

Reynoutria henryi, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies, it can become a problematic species in certain ecosystems.
