Ranunculus menyuanensis

Ranunculus menyuanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy and often toxic plants. The species is characterized by its bright yellow petals and a hairy stem, typical of the genus Ranunculus. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
