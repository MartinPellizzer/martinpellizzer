Rosa kurganica

Rosa kurganica, commonly known as the Kurgan rose, is a species native to Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in the summer months. Its leaves are dark green, alternate, and have serrated edges.

Rosa kurganica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
