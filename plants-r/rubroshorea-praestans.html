Rubroshorea praestans

Rubroshorea praestans, commonly known as the flame shorea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense canopy of broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody, elongated fruits containing numerous seeds. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing a rugged appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in lowland rainforests.
