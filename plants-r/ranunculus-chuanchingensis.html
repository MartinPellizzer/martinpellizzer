Ranunculus chuanchingensis

Ranunculus chuanchingensis, commonly known as the Chuanching buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, usually yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are palmately divided, and it produces small, follicle-like fruits.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
