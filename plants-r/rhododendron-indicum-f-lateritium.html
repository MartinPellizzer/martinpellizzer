Rhododendron indicum f. lateritium

Rhododendron indicum f. lateritium, commonly known as the rock rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
