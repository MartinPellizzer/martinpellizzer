Rosa spinosissima f. macropetala

Rosa spinosissima f. macropetala, commonly known as the large-flowered spinous rose, is a botanical variant of the spinous rose species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, showcasing a vibrant range of colors from white to deep red. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves that are typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticultural traditions.
