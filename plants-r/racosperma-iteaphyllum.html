Racosperma iteaphyllum

Racosperma iteaphyllum, commonly known as the silky wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a delicate, silky appearance. It produces yellow flowers in winter, which are clustered in cylindrical spikes.

Racosperma iteaphyllum is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.
