Russelia sarmentosa var. polyedra

Russelia sarmentosa var. polyedra, commonly known as the velvetleaf russelia, is a climbing perennial plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its stems are flexible and can trail or climb, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained supports.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
