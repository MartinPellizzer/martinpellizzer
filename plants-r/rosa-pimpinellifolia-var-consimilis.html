Rosa pimpinellifolia var. consimilis

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. consimilis, commonly known as the similar rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and a woody stem structure typical of many rose species. It is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and arid gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive choice for ornamental gardening and ecological landscaping.
