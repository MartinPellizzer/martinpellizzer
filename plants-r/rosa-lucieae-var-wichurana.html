Rosa lucieae var. wichurana

Rosa lucieae var. wichurana, commonly known as the Wichura rose, is a hybrid rose species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically produces small, oval leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its beauty and fragrance.
