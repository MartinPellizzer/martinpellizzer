Rosa araaedena

Rosa araaedena, commonly known as the Crimson Rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated edge.

Rosa araaedena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
