Rubus martensenii

Rubus martensenii, commonly known as Martensen's raspberry, is a shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
