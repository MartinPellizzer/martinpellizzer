Rubus runssorensis

Rubus runssorensis, commonly known as the Runssore blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are rich in antioxidants and have a tart flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
