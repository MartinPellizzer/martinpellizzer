Rosa nubilascens

Rosa nubilascens, commonly known as the misty rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with ovate leaflets that have a slightly hairy texture.

Rosa nubilascens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
