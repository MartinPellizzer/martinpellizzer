Rourea Calophylla

Rourea calophylla is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the wider horticultural community.
