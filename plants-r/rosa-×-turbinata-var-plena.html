Rosa × turbinata var. plena

Rosa × turbinata var. plena, commonly known as the double turbinated rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its dense, full blooms.

It features large, rounded flowers with multiple layers of petals, giving it a lush, opulent appearance. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
