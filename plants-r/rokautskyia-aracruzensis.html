Rokautskyia aracruzensis

Rokautskyia aracruzensis, commonly known as the Aracruz orchid, is a rare and exotic orchid species native to the Atlantic rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the dense canopy of the rainforest. Its botanical features include long, slender leaves and a complex root system adapted to nutrient-poor environments.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
