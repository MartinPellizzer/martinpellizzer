Rosa laevigata f. semiplena

Rosa laevigata f. semiplena, commonly known as the Chinese semiplena rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its double flowers, which are composed of numerous petals that give it a full, fluffy appearance. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
