Rosa micrantha var. mairei

Rosa micrantha var. mairei, commonly known as the Maire rose, is a shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, hairy rosehip that matures in autumn.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
