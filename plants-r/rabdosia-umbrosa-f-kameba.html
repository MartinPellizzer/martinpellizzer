Rabdosia umbrosa f. kameba

Rabdosia umbrosa f. kameba, commonly known as the Kameba form of the dark leaved rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or violet in color.

This form is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
