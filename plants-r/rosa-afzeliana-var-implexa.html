Rosa afzeliana var. implexa

Rosa afzeliana var. implexa, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically borne in tight, rounded inflorescences. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and hardiness, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates.

The variety 'implexa' is noted for its compact growth habit and abundant flowering, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
