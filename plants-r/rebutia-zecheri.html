Rebutia zecheri

Rebutia zecheri, commonly known as the Zecheri Cactus, is a species native to South America, specifically found in parts of Argentina and Bolivia.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Rebutia zecheri is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
