Rosa × vorbergii

Rosa × vorbergii, commonly known as the Vorberg rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and North America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hedges.

Rosa × vorbergii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
