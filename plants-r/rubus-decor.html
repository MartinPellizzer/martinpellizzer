Rubus decor

Rubus decor, commonly known as the decorative raspberry.

It is a species of shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
