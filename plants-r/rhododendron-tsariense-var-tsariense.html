Rhododendron tsariense var. tsariense

Rhododendron tsariense var. tsariense, commonly known as the Tsari rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as part of native plant restoration projects.
