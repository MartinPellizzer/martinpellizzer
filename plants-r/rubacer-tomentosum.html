Rubacer tomentosum

Rubacer tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves and stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Rubacer tomentosum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
