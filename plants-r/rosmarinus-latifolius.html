Rosmarinus latifolius

Rosmarinus latifolius, commonly known as broadleaf rosemary, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, needle-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine white bloom. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for herb gardens.

Its aromatic properties make it valuable both in culinary and medicinal applications.
