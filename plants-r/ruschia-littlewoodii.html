Ruschia littlewoodii

Ruschia littlewoodii, commonly known as the Littlewood's Ruschia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
