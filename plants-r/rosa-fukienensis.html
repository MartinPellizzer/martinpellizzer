Rosa fukienensis

Rosa fukienensis, commonly known as the Fukien rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Rosa fukienensis is also valued for its historical significance in horticulture and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
