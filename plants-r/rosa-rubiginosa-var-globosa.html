Rosa rubiginosa var. globosa

Rosa rubiginosa var. globosa, commonly known as the globular rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant produces rose hips that are typically red or orange, containing the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact form and long-lasting blooms.
