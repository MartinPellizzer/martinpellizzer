Rumex schischkinii

Rumex schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like rhubarb and buckthorn. The plant features distinctive arrow-shaped leaves with a reddish tint and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
