Rubus inframollis

Rubus inframollis, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested in the fall. It has compound leaves with serrated edges and thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and local biodiversity.
