Rubus sprengelii var. sprengelii

Rubus sprengelii var. sprengelii, commonly known as the Sprengel's blackberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are typically harvested in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
