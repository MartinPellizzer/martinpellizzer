Rosa elliptica var. jordanii

Rosa elliptica var. jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's elliptic rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its stems are woody and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
