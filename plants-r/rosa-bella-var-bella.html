Rosa bella var. bella

Rosa bella var. bella, commonly known as the Bella Beauty rose, is a compact shrub that produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers.

It is a hybrid tea rose known for its vibrant color and strong fragrance, which makes it popular in gardens and floral arrangements. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage that provides a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 4 feet and is hardy in USDA zones 5 through 9.

This variety requires regular watering and well-drained soil to thrive and maintain its ornamental qualities.
