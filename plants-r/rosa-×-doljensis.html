Rosa × doljensis

Rosa × doljensis, commonly known as the Serbian rose, is a hybrid species native to the Balkans.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa × doljensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
