Rebutia senilis var. elegans

Rebutia senilis var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
