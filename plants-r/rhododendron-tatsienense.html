Rhododendron tatsienense

Rhododendron tatsienense, commonly known as the Sichuan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in cool, moist habitats at high altitudes, often in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a significant role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
