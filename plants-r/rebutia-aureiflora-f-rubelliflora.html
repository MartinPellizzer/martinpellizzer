Rebutia aureiflora f. rubelliflora

Rebutia aureiflora f. rubelliflora, commonly known as the ruby-flowered rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded stems that often bear a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers, typically in shades of red or pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is usually green with a slight bluish tint, and the plant is well adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
