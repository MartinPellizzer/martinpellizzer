Rubus ribifolius

Rubus ribifolius, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces bright red berries that are typically harvested in the summer months. These berries are rich in antioxidants and have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
