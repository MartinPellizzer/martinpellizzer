Reevesia sinica

Reevesia sinica, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Reevesia sinica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in horticultural circles for its beauty and resilience.
