Rungia latior var. hirsuta

Rungia latior var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy broadleaf rungia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
