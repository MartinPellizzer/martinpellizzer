Rosa janii

Rosa janii, commonly known as the Janii rose, is a species of shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits called rose hips.

Rosa janii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
