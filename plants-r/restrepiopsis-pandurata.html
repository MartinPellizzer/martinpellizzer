Restrepiopsis pandurata

Restrepiopsis pandurata, commonly known as the Pandurata orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences and small, intricate flowers with a distinctive coloration. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
