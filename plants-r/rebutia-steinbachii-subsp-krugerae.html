Rebutia steinbachii subsp. krugerae

Rebutia steinbachii subsp.

krugerae, commonly known as the Kruger's Rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded stems that are often ribbed and covered in a dense layer of white to pale green woolly hair.

The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
