Rumex × transbaicalicus

Rumex × transbaicalicus, commonly known as the Transbaikal sorrel, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple at the base. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and it thrives in moist, temperate climates. It is native to regions of Central Asia and is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on its parent species.
