Rhynchospora graminosa

Rhynchospora graminosa, commonly known as slender spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy environments in North America.

Rhynchospora graminosa plays an important ecological role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
