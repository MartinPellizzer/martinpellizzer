Rosa polyantha var. quelpaertensis

Rosa polyantha var. quelpaertensis, commonly known as the Quelpaert rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces oval, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is well-suited for cultivation in gardens due to its ornamental value and hardiness.

The flowers typically range in color from white to pink, adding a delicate beauty to the landscape.
