Rourea villosa

Rourea villosa, commonly known as the silky rourea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its stems are often woody and can develop a rough texture over time.

Rourea villosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
