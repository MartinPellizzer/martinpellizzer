Rosa × dumetorum var. pseudoincerta

Rosa × dumetorum var. pseudoincerta, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces rose hips that are orange-red in color. It is well adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
