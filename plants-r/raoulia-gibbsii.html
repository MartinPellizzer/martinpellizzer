Raoulia gibbsii

Raoulia gibbsii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring dense, rounded foliage. Raoulia gibbsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
