Riocreuxia bolusii

Riocreuxia bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
