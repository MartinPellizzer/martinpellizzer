Rhododendron leiboense

Rhododendron leiboense, commonly known as the Leibo rhododendron, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in shaded, cool environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron leiboense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
