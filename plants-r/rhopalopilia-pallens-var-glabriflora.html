Rhopalopilia pallens var. glabriflora

Rhopalopilia pallens var. glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered rhopalopilia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
