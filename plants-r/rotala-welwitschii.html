Rotala welwitschii

Rotala welwitschii, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a floating aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features whorled leaves that are often bright green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically submerged or floating. It is widely used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate water.

Rotala welwitschii thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving waters with moderate light exposure.
