Rosa sertorum

Rosa sertorum, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and pinnate leaves with five to seven leaflets. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Rosa sertorum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
