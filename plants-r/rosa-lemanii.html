Rosa lemanii

Rosa lemanii, commonly known as the Leman's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, particularly the Alps. This perennial shrub produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
