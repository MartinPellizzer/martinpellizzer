Ribes ruizii

Ribes ruizii, commonly known as the Andean gooseberry, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Ribes.

It is native to the Andes Mountains of South America, where it thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it has a woody stem that can grow as a shrub.

Ribes ruizii is valued not only for its edible fruits but also for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
