Rebutia schmiedcheniana

Rebutia schmiedcheniana, commonly known as the Schmiedchen's Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Bolivia.

It is a low-growing, compact plant with a short, thick stem that often branches at the base. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Rebutia schmiedcheniana is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
