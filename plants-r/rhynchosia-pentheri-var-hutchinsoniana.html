Rhynchosia pentheri var. hutchinsoniana

Rhynchosia pentheri var. hutchinsoniana, commonly known as the purple-flowered snout bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces showy, purple or violet flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant bears elongated, narrow pods that contain several seeds, and it is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open woodland and grassland habitats, where it thrives in full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
