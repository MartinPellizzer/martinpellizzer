Rosa communis var. dumetorum

Rosa communis var. dumetorum, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a compact, low-growing variety of rose that thrives in temperate climates.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which remains lush throughout the year in mild climates. It is often cultivated as a hedging plant or for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its hardy nature, it is well-suited for coastal and urban environments.
