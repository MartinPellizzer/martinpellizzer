Rosa heckeliana var. thessala

Rosa heckeliana var. thessala, commonly known as the Thessalian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It features arching, thornless stems that produce clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges, and its root system is fibrous and well-developed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
