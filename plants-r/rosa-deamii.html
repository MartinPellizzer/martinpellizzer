Rosa deamii

Rosa deamii, commonly known as the Deam's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with a distinctive yellow center, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include pinnately compound leaves and stipules, which are small leaf-like structures at the base of the leaves.

Rosa deamii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
