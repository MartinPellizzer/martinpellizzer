Rhododendron × caucasicoarboreum

Rhododendron × caucasicoarboreum, commonly known as the Caucasian shrub rhododendron, is a hybrid species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
