Ranunculus tricuspis var. variifolia

Ranunculus tricuspis var. variifolia, commonly known as the variable trifid buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, trifid (three-lobed) leaves that are often variegated, contributing to its ornamental value. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its delicate foliage and vibrant flowers, it is a valued addition to gardens and natural habitats.
