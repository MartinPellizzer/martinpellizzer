Rosa davyi

Rosa davyi, commonly known as the Chinese tea rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with thorny stems and dark green foliage. Rosa davyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial sunlight for optimal growth.
