Rosa strictidens

Rosa strictidens, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Rosa strictidens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
