Roegneria ciliaris var. japonensis

Roegneria ciliaris var. japonensis, commonly known as the Japanese forest grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It features slender, dark green leaves with fine, silky hairs along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear delicate, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense, attractive ground covers.
