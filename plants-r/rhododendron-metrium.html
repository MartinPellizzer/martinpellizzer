Rhododendron metrium

Rhododendron metrium, commonly known as the dwarf rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are dark green and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in early spring.

Rhododendron metrium thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
