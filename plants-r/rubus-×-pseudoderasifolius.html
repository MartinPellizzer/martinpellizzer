Rubus × pseudoderasifolius

Rubus × pseudoderasifolius, commonly known as the red-berried blackberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and edible berries.
