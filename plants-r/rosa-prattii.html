Rosa prattii

Rosa prattii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright stems with thorny branches and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, hairy capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Rosa prattii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
