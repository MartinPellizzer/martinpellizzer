Rubus bayeri var. rumorum

Rubus bayeri var. rumorum, commonly known as the Rumor raspberry, is a wild shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
