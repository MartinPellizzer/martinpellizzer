Ribes nigrum f. apiifolium

Ribes nigrum f. apiifolium, commonly known as the blackcurrant with parsley-like leaves, is a hybrid form of the blackcurrant plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided leaves that resemble parsley, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are similar to those of the common blackcurrant. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.

This form is valued in both horticulture and culinary applications due to its aesthetic appeal and fruit quality.
