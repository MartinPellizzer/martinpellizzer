Rosa Micrantha Var. Suprahirta

Rosa micrantha var. suprahirta is a rare and delicate species of rose native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its small, fragrant flowers and compact growth habit, this variety is highly sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. It thrives in cool, moist climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public due to its limited distribution and challenging growing conditions. This plant offers a unique opportunity to explore the diversity of the rose family in natural and cultivated settings.
