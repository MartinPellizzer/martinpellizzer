Rhinacanthus angulicaulis

Rhinacanthus angulicaulis, commonly known as the zigzag flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging branches and showy, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces woody, spiny fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking addition to tropical gardens.
