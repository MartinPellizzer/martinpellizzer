Rosa × muretii

Rosa × muretii, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its continuous flowering throughout the growing season, which sets it apart from many other rose varieties. The plant typically features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. It is a compact shrub, often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Rosa × muretii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
