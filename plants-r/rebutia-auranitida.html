Rebutia auranitida

Rebutia auranitida, commonly known as the golden rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are typically green or yellowish in color, adorned with small, spiny areoles. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Rebutia auranitida is prized in succulent collections for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
