Ruta albiflora

Ruta albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered rue, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features aromatic, pinnately compound leaves with a strong, pungent odor. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Ruta albiflora is valued for its medicinal properties and historical use in herbal remedies.
