Rosa × reversa var. longicolla

Rosa × reversa var. longicolla, commonly known as the long-stemmed hybrid rose, is a cultivar characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant flowering.

It features large, fragrant flowers with a rich, velvety texture, typically in shades of pink to red. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in thorns. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
