Rapatea sphaerocephala

Rapatea sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globular rapatea, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical rosette of dark green, leathery leaves that form a compact, almost globular shape. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with numerous white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare succulents.
