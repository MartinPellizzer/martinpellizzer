Roupala longipetiolata

Roupala longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled roupala, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The fruit is a spherical, woody capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
