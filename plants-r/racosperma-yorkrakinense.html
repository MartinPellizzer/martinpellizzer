Racosperma yorkrakinense

Racosperma yorkrakinense, commonly known as the Yorkrakin wattle, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of northern Australia, particularly in Queensland. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences, and its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas with a warm, humid climate.
