Rubus × negotiosus

Rubus × negotiosus, commonly known as the blackberry bramble, is a hybrid plant species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of many bramble species. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are clusters of small drupes that develop from the flowering stems. These fruits are usually dark purple to black in color and have a tart, sweet flavor.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along fences and hedgerows.
