Rosa × surreyana

Rosa × surreyana, commonly known as the Surrey rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its semi-climbing growth habit and is native to the United Kingdom. The plant typically produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa × surreyana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
