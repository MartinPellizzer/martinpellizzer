Rhynchosia cytisoides

Rhynchosia cytisoides, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used as a forage crop or for ornamental purposes.

Rhynchosia cytisoides is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
