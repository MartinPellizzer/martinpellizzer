Rosa jundzilliana f. typica

Rosa jundzilliana f. typica, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa jundzilliana species, characterized by its vibrant pink flowers and upright growth habit.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant, semi-double blooms in late spring to early summer. The plant features glossy, dark green foliage and thorny stems typical of many rose varieties. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

This rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
