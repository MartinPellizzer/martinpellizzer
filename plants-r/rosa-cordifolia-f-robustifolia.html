Rosa cordifolia f. robustifolia

Rosa cordifolia f. robustifolia, commonly known as the robust rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are heart-shaped, giving it the species name *cordifolia*. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with a subtle scent, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in colder climates.
