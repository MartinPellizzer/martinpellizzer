Rosa mollis f. egressidens

Rosa mollis f. egressidens, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a hybrid variety of the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thornless stems, making it suitable for ornamental gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring regular watering during dry periods.

This cultivar is valued for its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.
