Roupala skinneri

Roupala skinneri, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions or on rocky outcrops.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its winter interest.
