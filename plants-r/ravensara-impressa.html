Ravensara impressa

Ravensara impressa, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. Ravensara impressa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental value make it a popular species for horticultural use.
