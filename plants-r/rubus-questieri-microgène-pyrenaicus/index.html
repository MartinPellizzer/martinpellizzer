Rubus Questieri Microgène Pyrenaicus

The rubus questieri microgène pyrenaicus is a rare and elusive plant native to the Pyrenees region. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and is known for its delicate, pinkish flowers. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and faces threats from climate change and human activity. Botanists have studied it extensively to better understand its ecological role and conservation needs. This plant represents a unique example of biodiversity in mountainous ecosystems.
