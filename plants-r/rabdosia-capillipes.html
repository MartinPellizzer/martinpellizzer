Rabdosia capillipes

Rabdosia capillipes, commonly known as the slender-leaved rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Rabdosia capillipes is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
