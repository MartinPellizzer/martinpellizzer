Rhynchospora exaltata var. ovalis

Rhynchospora exaltata var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval spikegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in the southeastern United States.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a basal rosette structure. The plant produces upright, spike-like inflorescences bearing small, pale green to brownish flowers. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes, and it is often found in shallow water or saturated soils.

Rhynchospora exaltata var. ovalis plays a role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and birds.
