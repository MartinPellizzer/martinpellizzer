Rapanea mandonii

Rapanea mandonii, commonly known as the Madagascar laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its bark is smooth and dark in color, while its leaves are glossy and lance-shaped.

Rapanea mandonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
