Rubus × decoratus

Rubus × decoratus, commonly known as the ornamental raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, edible berries. It typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. The plant produces showy white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by red or yellow fruits in the summer.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
