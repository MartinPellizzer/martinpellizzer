Rhinorchis nilssonii

Rhinorchis nilssonii, commonly known as the Nilsson's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to greenish flowers with distinctive, elongated petals and a spurred lip. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist environment.

Rhinorchis nilssonii is a rare and delicate species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
