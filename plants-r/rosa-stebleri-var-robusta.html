Rosa stebleri var. robusta

Rosa stebleri var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta rose, is a vigorous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dark green, glossy foliage and produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in horticulture for its hardiness and resilience.

The flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.
