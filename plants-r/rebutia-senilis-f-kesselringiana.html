Rebutia senilis f. kesselringiana

Rebutia senilis f. kesselringiana, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense, woolly covering of white to pale gray hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost silvery appearance. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its fuzzy foliage. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
