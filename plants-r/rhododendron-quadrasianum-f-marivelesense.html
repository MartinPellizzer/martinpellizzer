Rhododendron quadrasianum f. marivelesense

Rhododendron quadrasianum f. marivelesense, commonly known as the Mariveles rhododendron, is a rare and unique plant species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking pink to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within montane forests. Its foliage consists of thick, leathery leaves that are dark green in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
