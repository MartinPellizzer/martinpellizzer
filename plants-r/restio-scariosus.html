Restio scariosus

Restio scariosus, commonly known as the silver restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.
