Rosa rupestris

Rosa rupestris, commonly known as the rock rose, is a species of shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows on rocky soils and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and its stems are often covered in thorns.

Rosa rupestris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and resilience.
