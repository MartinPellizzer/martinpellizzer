Ribes aureum var. villosum

Ribes aureum var. villosum, commonly known as golden currant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features attractive, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildlife habitats.
