Rosa × notabilis

Rosa × notabilis, commonly known as the Queen of the Roses, is a hybrid rose species renowned for its large, showy flowers.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, glossy foliage. The plant produces fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive double or semi-double petal structure. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

Rosa × notabilis is a popular choice in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
