Robertwightia Auriculata

Auriculate robertwightia, scientifically known as Robertwightia auriculata, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the western ghats of india. it is characterized by its unique auriculate leaves that resemble small ears, giving it its common name. this plant is highly valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens. due to its limited natural habitat and slow growth rate, it is considered a conservation concern. its delicate blooms and distinctive foliage make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
