Rosa rubiginosa var. leiostyla

Rosa rubiginosa var. leiostyla, commonly known as the red-stemmed rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to white. Its fruit is a red, hairy rose hip that contains numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
