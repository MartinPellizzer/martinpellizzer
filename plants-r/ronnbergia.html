Ronnbergia

Ronnbergia, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and are characterized by their tall, grass-like foliage and showy, spike-like inflorescences. Ronnbergia species typically produce vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom during the warmer months. They prefer well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to their ornamental value, Ronnbergia plants are often grown as houseplants or in gardens with warm climates.
