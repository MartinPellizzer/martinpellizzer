Rubus vestitus f. albiflorus

Rubus vestitus f. albiflorus, commonly known as the whiteflowered bramble, is a variant of the bramble plant characterized by its pale or white flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of Europe. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are usually smaller and more delicate compared to the typical Rubus vestitus.

This form is often appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens.
