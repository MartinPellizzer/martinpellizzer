Rorippa Kurdica

Rorippa kurdica is a rare aquatic plant native to the mountainous regions of Kurdistan. It thrives in freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. This species is known for its small, floating leaves and delicate white flowers. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Protecting Rorippa kurdica is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
