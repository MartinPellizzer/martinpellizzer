Rubus elzinus

Rubus elzinus, commonly known as the elzins raspberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of brambles and raspberries. The plant features prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces small, edible berries.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
