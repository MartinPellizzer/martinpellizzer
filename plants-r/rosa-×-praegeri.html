Rosa × praegeri

Rosa × praegeri, commonly known as the German tea rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has thorny stems with dark green, glossy foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The botanical features of Rosa × praegeri make it both ornamental and valuable for its historical use in perfumery and tea production.
