Rodriguezia teuscheri

Rodriguezia teuscheri, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and striking clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, winged fruits that disperse its seeds by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rodriguezia teuscheri is relatively low maintenance and suitable for gardens in USDA hardiness zones 9 through 11.
