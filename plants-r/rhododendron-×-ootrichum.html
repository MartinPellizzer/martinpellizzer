Rhododendron × ootrichum

Rhododendron × ootrichum, commonly known as the Ooty rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments at high altitudes, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are borne on short pedicels and are often accompanied by large, leathery bracts.

Rhododendron × ootrichum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas for its beauty and ecological importance.
