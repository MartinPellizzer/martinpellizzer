Raputia sigmatanthus

Raputia sigmatanthus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging inflorescence and vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are grass-like and form a rosette, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its arid habitat.

Raputia sigmatanthus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
