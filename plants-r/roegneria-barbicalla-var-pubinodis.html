Roegneria barbicalla var. pubinodis

Roegneria barbicalla var. pubinodis, commonly known as the silvergrass or velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, awned florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscape gardens and ecological restoration projects.
