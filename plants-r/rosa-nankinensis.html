Rosa nankinensis

Rosa nankinensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers in the spring. Its leaves are pinnate, with 5 to 9 leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, reddish hairs.

Rosa nankinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
