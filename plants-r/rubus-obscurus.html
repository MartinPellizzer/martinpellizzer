Rubus obscurus

Rubus obscurus, commonly known as the dusky bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, pale yellow flowers in the spring. Its fruits are aggregate berries that ripen to a dark red color in late summer.

Rubus obscurus is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in temperate regions of North America.
