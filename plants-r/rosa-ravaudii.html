Rosa ravaudii

Rosa ravaudii, commonly known as the Ravaudii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it has thorny stems typical of many rose species.

Rosa ravaudii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and perfume.
