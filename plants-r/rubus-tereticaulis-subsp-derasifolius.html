Rubus tereticaulis subsp. derasifolius

Rubus tereticaulis subsp.

derasifolius, commonly known as the slender raspberry, is a shrub native to parts of North America. It is characterized by its thin, thornless stems and trifoliate leaves with a distinctive, narrow leaflet shape.

The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential use in horticulture.
