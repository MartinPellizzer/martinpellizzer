Rhododendron Japonicum

Rhododendron japonicum is a striking evergreen shrub native to Japan known for its vibrant floral display. It features dense clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white during spring. This plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in many regions beyond its native habitat. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and frost damage.
