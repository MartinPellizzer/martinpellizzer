Reimaria acuta var. villosa

Reimaria acuta var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy sharp-lobed fern, is a variant of the Reimaria acuta species.

It is characterized by its finely hairy leaves, which give it a distinctive texture. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
