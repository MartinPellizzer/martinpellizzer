Reseda aucheri subsp. bracteata

Reseda aucheri subsp.

bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate eryngo, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, with flowering stems that are usually unbranched.
