Rhododendron × gandavense

Rhododendron × gandavense, commonly known as the Gandhara rhododendron, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. Native to the mountainous regions of northern India and Nepal, this species thrives in cool, humid climates with well-drained acidic soils. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems and a broad, rounded form.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
