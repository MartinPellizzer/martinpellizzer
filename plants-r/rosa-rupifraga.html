Rosa rupifraga

Rosa rupifraga, commonly known as the rock rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems. Its flowers are typically pink or red, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Rosa rupifraga is often found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
