Rhaphidophora luchunensis

Rhaphidophora luchunensis, commonly known as the Luchun arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value and edible rhizomes make it both a horticultural and agricultural interest.
