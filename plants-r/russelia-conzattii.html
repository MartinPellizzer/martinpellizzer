Russelia conzattii

Russelia conzattii, commonly known as the Copperleaf, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, copper-colored leaves that remain throughout the year, providing a unique visual appeal. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Russelia conzattii is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it a low-maintenance choice for ornamental gardens.
