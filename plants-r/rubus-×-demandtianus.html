Rubus × demandtianus

Rubus × demandtianus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (wineberry) and Rubus gebleri (blackberry), resulting in a plant with distinctive botanical features. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces edible berries that range in color from red to black.

This hybrid is valued for its fruit quality and adaptability to various growing conditions.
