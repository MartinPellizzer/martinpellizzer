Reseda fruticulosa var. gayana

Reseda fruticulosa var. gayana, commonly known as Gayana everlasting, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is well adapted to dry conditions, making it a hardy species suitable for arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The flowers are also used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and delicate appearance.
