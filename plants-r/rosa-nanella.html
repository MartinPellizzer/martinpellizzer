Rosa nanella

Rosa nanella, commonly known as the miniature rose or dwarf rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming throughout the summer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a generally bushy growth habit. It is well-suited for container gardening and rock gardens due to its size and ornamental appeal.

Rosa nanella requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
