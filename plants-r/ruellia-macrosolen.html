Ruellia macrosolen

Ruellia macrosolen, commonly known as the large-leaved ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers bloom throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ruellia macrosolen is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
