Rosa × vulcanica

Rosa × vulcanica, commonly known as the Vulcanic Rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the volcanic regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and strong, fragrant scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often used in ornamental landscaping.
