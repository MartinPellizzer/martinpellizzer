Rosa eglanteria f. obovatocuneata

Rosa eglanteria f. obovatocuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf prickly rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its distinctive, narrow, ovate to cuneate leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant typically produces thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. Its foliage is often covered in a fine layer of white down, giving it a silvery appearance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in temperate climates.
