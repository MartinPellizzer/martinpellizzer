Rosa mollis f. ericii

Rosa mollis f. ericii, commonly known as the ericaceous rose, is a cultivar of the shrub rose species Rosa mollis.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and profuse flowering, typically producing small, fragrant pink or white blooms. The plant is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salty air and well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
