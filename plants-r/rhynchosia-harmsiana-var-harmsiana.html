Rhynchosia harmsiana var. harmsiana

Rhynchosia harmsiana var. harmsiana, commonly known as the African bean or wild cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with elliptic to ovate leaflets and produces small, yellowish to orange flowers. The plant bears cylindrical, hairy pods containing several elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

This species is valued for its potential in sustainable agriculture and soil enrichment.
