Randia uncaria

Randia uncaria, commonly known as the wild coffee plant, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers followed by bright red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to regions in Central and South America.
