Rapanea orohenensis

Rapanea orohenensis, commonly known as the Madagascar laurel, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurel and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Rapanea orohenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
