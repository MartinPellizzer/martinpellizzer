Rosa mollis f. sethii

Rosa mollis f. sethii, commonly known as the Sethii rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly fuzzy texture, and its flowers are typically pale pink to white, with a delicate, almost scentless fragrance. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its compact growth habit and long blooming period. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental and hedging purposes.
