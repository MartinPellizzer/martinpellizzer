Roegneria japonensis

Roegneria japonensis, commonly known as the Japanese basket grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Roegneria japonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
