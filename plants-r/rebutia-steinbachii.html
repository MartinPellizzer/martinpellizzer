Rebutia steinbachii

Rebutia steinbachii, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has a short, thick stem covered in spines, which help to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its leaves are typically modified into spines, and it stores water in its stem for survival during dry periods.

Rebutia steinbachii is well-suited for cultivation in rock gardens or containers due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
