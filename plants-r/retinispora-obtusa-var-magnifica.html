Retinispora obtusa var. magnifica

Retinispora obtusa var. magnifica, commonly known as the magnifica retinispora, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or silver veins. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or red, which bloom throughout the warmer months. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical climates.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
