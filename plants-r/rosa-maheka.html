Rosa maheka

Rosa maheka, commonly known as the Maheka rose, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its stems are woody and covered in thorns, which are characteristic of many rose species.

Rosa maheka is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
