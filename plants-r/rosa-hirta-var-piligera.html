Rosa hirta var. piligera

Rosa hirta var. piligera, commonly known as the rough rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring. Its flowers are followed by oval, red-orange hips that persist into winter.

Rosa hirta var. piligera is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
