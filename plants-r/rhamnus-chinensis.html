Rhamnus chinensis

Rhamnus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue to black berries that are rich in anthocyanins. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky areas.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
