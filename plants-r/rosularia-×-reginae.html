Rosularia × reginae

Rosularia × reginae, commonly known as the Queen's Rosularia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit with dense clusters of small, rounded leaves. The plant produces showy, single flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is popular among rock garden enthusiasts.
