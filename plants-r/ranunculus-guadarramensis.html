Ranunculus guadarramensis

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions. It is a herbaceous perennial with a short flowering period in spring.
