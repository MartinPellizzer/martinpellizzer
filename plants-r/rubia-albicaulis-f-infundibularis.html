Rubia albicaulis f. infundibularis

Rubia albicaulis f. infundibularis, commonly known as the white-flowered bedstraw, is a variant of the bedstraw genus Rubia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which contrast with the typical red or pink hues of other Rubia species. The plant is a low-growing perennial with slender stems and small, opposite leaves. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and rare form within the Rubia genus.
