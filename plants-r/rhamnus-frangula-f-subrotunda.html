Rhamnus frangula f. subrotunda

Rhamnus frangula f. subrotunda, commonly known as the rounded-fruited buckthorn, is a variant of the European buckthorn.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded fruiting bodies that differ from the typical elongated form of the species. The plant is a deciduous shrub, typically found in moist, shaded woodlands across Europe. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, with a slightly serrated margin.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.
