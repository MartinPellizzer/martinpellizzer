Rosa venosa f. microphylla

Rosa venosa f. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature pink musk rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for container gardening or as a border plant.

It requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
