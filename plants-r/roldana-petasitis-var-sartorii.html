Roldana petasitis var. sartorii

Roldana petasitis var. sartorii, commonly known as the Sartorii's roldana, is a variant of the roldana plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and pink. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and hairy stems, which aid in its survival in humid conditions.
