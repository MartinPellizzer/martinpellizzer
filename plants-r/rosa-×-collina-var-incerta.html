Rosa × collina var. incerta

Rosa × collina var. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain hill rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland edges and rocky slopes, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in horticulture.
