Rhabdophyllum quintasii

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and minimal leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
