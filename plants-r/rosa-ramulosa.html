Rosa ramulosa

Rosa ramulosa, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or dry soils. The plant features thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets that are narrow and pointed.

Rosa ramulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
