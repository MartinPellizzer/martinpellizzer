Rosa × dumetorum var. deceali

Rosa × dumetorum var. deceali, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub with dense, thornless stems that often form a mat-like structure. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is well adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in poor soil and full sun.
