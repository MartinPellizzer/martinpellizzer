Rosa micrantha var. bolosii

Rosa micrantha var. bolosii, commonly known as the Bolosii rose, is a rare and unique variant of the Rosa micrantha species.

It is native to the coastal regions of California, where it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. This plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. Its foliage is typically dark green and densely packed, providing a lush contrast to the delicate blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
