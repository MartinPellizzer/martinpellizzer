Restrepia tolimensis

Restrepia tolimensis, commonly known as the Tolima orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean region of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous flowers in shades of white and green. Its inflorescence is often adorned with a distinctive green or white lip.

Restrepia tolimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
