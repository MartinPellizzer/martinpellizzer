Ribes nevadense var. grantii

Ribes nevadense var. grantii, commonly known as the Grant's gooseberry, is a shrub native to the western United States, particularly in Nevada and California.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-green berries that are typically edible. This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local wildlife.
