Renanthera moluccana

Renanthera moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with red or purple markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
