Rosa laxa var. laxa

Rosa laxa var. laxa, commonly known as the lax rose, is a species of shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wildlife habitats.
