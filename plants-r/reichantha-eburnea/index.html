Reichantha Eburnea

Reichantha eburnea is a rare and delicate orchid native to the high altitudes of southern China. Known for its small, pale green leaves and striking white flowers, it thrives in cool, humid environments. This plant is highly sought after by botanists and collectors due to its limited distribution and unique appearance. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and endangered species.
