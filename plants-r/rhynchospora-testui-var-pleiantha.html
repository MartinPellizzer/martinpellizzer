Rhynchospora testui var. pleiantha

Rhynchospora testui var. pleiantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes that bloom in late summer. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in moist soils.
