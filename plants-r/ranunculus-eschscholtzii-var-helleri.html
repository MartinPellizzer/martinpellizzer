Ranunculus eschscholtzii var. helleri

Ranunculus eschscholtzii var. helleri, commonly known as Heller's buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to California.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a short flowering period in spring and is characterized by its finely toothed foliage.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitat.
