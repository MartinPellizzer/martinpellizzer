Rhododendron tuhanense

Rhododendron tuhanense, commonly known as the Tuhan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range in color from pink to red.

Rhododendron tuhanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
