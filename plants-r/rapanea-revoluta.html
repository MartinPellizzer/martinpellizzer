Rapanea revoluta

Rapanea revoluta, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
