Rutidea dupuisii subsp. dupuisii

Rutidea dupuisii subsp.

dupuisii, commonly known as the Guatemalan coral bean, is a species native to Central America. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
