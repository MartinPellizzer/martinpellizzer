Rhododendron × pulchrum var. phoeniceum

Rhododendron × pulchrum var. phoeniceum, commonly known as the Flame Azalea, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with a distinctive crimson hue. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and produces dense clusters of blooms in spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaped areas.
