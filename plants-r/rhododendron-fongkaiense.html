Rhododendron fongkaiense

Rhododendron fongkaiense, commonly known as the Fongkai rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in cool, moist habitats at high elevations, often in forested areas with acidic soil. Its botanical features include leathery leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
