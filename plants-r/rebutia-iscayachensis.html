Rebutia iscayachensis

Rebutia iscayachensis, commonly known as the Iscaya Rebutia, is a flowering cactus native to the Andes Mountains of Peru.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
