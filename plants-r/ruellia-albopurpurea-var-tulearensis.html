Ruellia albopurpurea var. tulearensis

Ruellia albopurpurea var. tulearensis, commonly known as the Tulearensis Ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It features striking violet and white flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
