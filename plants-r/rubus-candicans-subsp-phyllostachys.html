Rubus candicans subsp. phyllostachys

Rubus candicans subsp.

phyllostachys, commonly known as the silverleaf raspberry, is a shrub native to parts of East Asia. It is characterized by its arching stems and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically silvery-white underneath, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional cuisines.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
