Rhododendron obscurum

Rhododendron obscurum, commonly known as the dark rhododendron, is a species native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and typically pink or reddish in hue.

Rhododendron obscurum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
