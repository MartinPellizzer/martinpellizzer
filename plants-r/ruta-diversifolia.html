Ruta diversifolia

Ruta diversifolia, commonly known as the variable rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves and small yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its historical use in herbal medicine dates back to ancient times.
