Ruschia socia

Ruschia socia, commonly known as the red-stemmed Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically red or purple in color, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange, often blooming in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a waxy surface that reduces water loss.

Ruschia socia is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
