Rytidosperma thomsonii

Rytidosperma thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Thomson's feather grass is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-maintenance settings.
