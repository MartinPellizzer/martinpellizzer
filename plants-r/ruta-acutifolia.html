Ruta acutifolia

Ruta acutifolia, commonly known as sharpleaf rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow, and often sharply pointed leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its strong scent and medicinal properties, it has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
