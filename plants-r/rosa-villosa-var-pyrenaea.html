Rosa villosa var. pyrenaea

Rosa villosa var. pyrenaea, commonly known as the Pyrenean rose, is a shrub native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It features thorny stems and is characterized by its dense, fuzzy foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
