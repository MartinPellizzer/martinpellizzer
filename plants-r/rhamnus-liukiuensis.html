Rhamnus liukiuensis

Rhamnus liukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for certain bird species. Rhamnus liukiuensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food and habitat for various wildlife.
