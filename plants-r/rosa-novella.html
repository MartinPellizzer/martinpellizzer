Rosa novella

Rosa novella, commonly known as the New Rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its flowers are often used in perfumes and as ornamental garden plants.

Rosa novella is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
