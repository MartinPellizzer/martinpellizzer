Rosa jundzillii var. decora

Rosa jundzillii var. decora, commonly known as the Decorated Jundzillii rose, is a shrub-like species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright growth with thorny stems and is characterized by its large, double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the showy blooms. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
