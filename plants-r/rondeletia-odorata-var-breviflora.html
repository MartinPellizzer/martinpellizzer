Rondeletia odorata var. breviflora

Rondeletia odorata var. breviflora, commonly known as the sweetshrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters of pink or white. The plant has a woody stem and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
