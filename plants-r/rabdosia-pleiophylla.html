Rabdosia pleiophylla

Rabdosia pleiophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Rabdosia pleiophylla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
