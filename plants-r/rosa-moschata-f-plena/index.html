Rosa Moschata F. Plena

Double musk rose, scientifically known as Rosa moschata f. plena, is a captivating hybrid of the musk rose, prized for its fragrant blooms and ornamental value. This variety is celebrated for its double-layered petals that create a lush, full appearance, enhancing its visual appeal in gardens. The plant's delicate scent, reminiscent of musk, adds a sensory dimension to its beauty, making it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial sunlight, requiring minimal care while delivering stunning floral displays. With its rich history and timeless charm, the double musk rose remains a cherished addition to both traditional and modern gardens.
