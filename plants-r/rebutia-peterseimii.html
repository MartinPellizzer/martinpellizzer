Rebutia peterseimii

Rebutia peterseimii, commonly known as the Peterseim's moon cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the deserts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green, ribbed body. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
