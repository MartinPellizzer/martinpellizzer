Rosa modesta

Rosa modesta, commonly known as the modest rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly serrated margin.

Rosa modesta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
