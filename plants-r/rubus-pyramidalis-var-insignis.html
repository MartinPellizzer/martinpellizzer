Rubus pyramidalis var. insignis

Rubus pyramidalis var. insignis, commonly known as the Pyramidal Raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
