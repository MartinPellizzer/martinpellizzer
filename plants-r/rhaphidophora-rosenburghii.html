Rhaphidophora rosenburghii

Rhaphidophora rosenburghii, commonly known as the Rosenburgh's arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Rhaphidophora rosenburghii is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
