Rhaphiolepis cavaleriei

Rhaphiolepis cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible but should be consumed in moderation. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rhaphiolepis cavaleriei is often cultivated for its vibrant winter berries and hardy nature.
