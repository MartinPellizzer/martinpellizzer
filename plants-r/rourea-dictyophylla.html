Rourea dictyophylla

Rourea dictyophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
