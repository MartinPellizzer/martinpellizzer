Rhododendron × psammogenes var. psammogenes

Rhododendron × psammogenes var. psammogenes, commonly known as the sand dune rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and arid environments. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in challenging conditions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
