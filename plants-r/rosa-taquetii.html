Rosa taquetii

Rosa taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and serrated.

Rosa taquetii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
