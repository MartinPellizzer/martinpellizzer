Raoulia albosericea

Raoulia albosericea, commonly known as the white silky raoulia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
