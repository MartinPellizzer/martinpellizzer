Rosa Xanthina Var. Ecae

Rosa xanthina var. ecae is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. Known for its striking yellow blooms and fragrant scent, this variety stands out among other roses for its unique coloration. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a conservation concern. This plant is highly valued by horticulturists and botanists alike for its beauty and ecological significance.
