Rosa × belgica blanda

Rosa × belgica blanda, commonly known as the Belgian Blush rose, is a hybrid shrub rose that exhibits a compact growth habit.

It features fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from pale pink to white, often with a subtle blush of red. The plant produces dark green, glossy foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its blooms. It is typically grown in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

This rose is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its beauty and fragrance.
