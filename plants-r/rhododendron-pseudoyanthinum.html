Rhododendron pseudoyanthinum

Rhododendron pseudoyanthinum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in the Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features dense clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Rhododendron pseudoyanthinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a significant role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
