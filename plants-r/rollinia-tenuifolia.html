Rollinia tenuifolia

Rollinia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed rollinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. Rollinia tenuifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
