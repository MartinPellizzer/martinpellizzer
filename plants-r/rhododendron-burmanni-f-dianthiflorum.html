Rhododendron burmanni f. dianthiflorum

Rhododendron burmanni f. dianthiflorum, commonly known as the Dianthiflorum rhododendron, is a unique hybrid within the Rhododendron genus.

It is characterized by its striking floral morphology, featuring showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, thriving in cool, moist environments with acidic soil. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in specialized gardens due to its specific care requirements.
