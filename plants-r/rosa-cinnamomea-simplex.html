Rosa cinnamomea simplex

Rosa cinnamomea simplex, commonly known as the cinnamon rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
