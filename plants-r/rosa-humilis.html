Rosa humilis

Rosa humilis, commonly known as the dwarf rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Rosa humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its compact size and hardy nature.
