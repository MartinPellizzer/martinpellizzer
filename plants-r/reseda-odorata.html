Reseda odorata

Reseda odorata, commonly known as sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Reseda odorata is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
