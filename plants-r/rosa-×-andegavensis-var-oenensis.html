Rosa × andegavensis var. oenensis

Rosa × andegavensis var. oenensis, commonly known as the Oenensis rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its semi-climbing growth habit and produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is native to the Iberian Peninsula. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
