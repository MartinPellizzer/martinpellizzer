Radermachia amoena

Radermachia amoena, commonly known as the red-flowered radermachia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, also known as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces vibrant red or pink flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
