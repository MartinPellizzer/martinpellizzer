Rivea glabrata

Rivea glabrata, commonly known as the velvet tangle vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rivea glabrata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
