Rubus × hololeucus

Rubus × hololeucus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and covered in fine hairs.

The fruit, which ripens in summer, is valued for its tart flavor and is widely used in culinary applications.
