Rhododendron oldhamii var. glandulosum

Rhododendron oldhamii var. glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular oldham rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic, well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a distinctive glandular texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
