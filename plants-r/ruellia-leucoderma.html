Ruellia leucoderma

Ruellia leucoderma, commonly known as whiteleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically light green to white in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet, blooming throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ruellia leucoderma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
