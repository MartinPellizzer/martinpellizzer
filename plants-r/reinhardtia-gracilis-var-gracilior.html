Reinhardtia gracilis var. gracilior

Reinhardtia gracilis var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in coastal areas or near freshwater sources. This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
