Rhynchospora hieronymi f. compacta

Rhynchospora hieronymi f. compacta, commonly known as the compact marsh sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale to brownish flowers, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, and is often found in temperate regions.
