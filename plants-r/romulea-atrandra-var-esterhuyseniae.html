Romulea atrandra var. esterhuyseniae

Romulea atrandra var. esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, rounded bulbs that help it survive in arid conditions. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage structure.
