Rapanea albiflorens

Rapanea albiflorens, commonly known as the whiteflowered rapanea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, giving it a delicate appearance.

Rapanea albiflorens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
