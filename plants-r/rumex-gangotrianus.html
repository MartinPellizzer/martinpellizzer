Rumex gangotrianus

Rumex gangotrianus, commonly known as the Gangotri dock, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of alpine flora and ecological adaptations.
