Rhoicissus microphylla

Rhoicissus microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved cat's whiskers, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. Its tendrils allow it to climb and twine around other plants or structures.

Rhoicissus microphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
