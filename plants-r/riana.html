Riana

Riana, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. Riana produces small, round berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
