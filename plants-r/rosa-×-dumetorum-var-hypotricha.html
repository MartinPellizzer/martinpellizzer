Rosa × dumetorum var. hypotricha

Rosa × dumetorum var. hypotricha, commonly known as the Alpine rose or shrub rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its foliage consists of dark green, elliptical leaves with serrated edges, and it is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand cold climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging conditions.
