Rhododendron augustinei var. rubrum

Rhododendron augustinei var. rubrum, commonly known as the Red-flowered Augustine Rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, broad leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of deep red flowers during the spring season. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
