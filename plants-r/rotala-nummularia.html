Rotala nummularia

Rotala nummularia, commonly known as the pennywort, is a small aquatic plant native to Asia.

It features bright green, round leaves that resemble coins, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shallow waters and is often used in aquariums due to its ornamental value. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer.

Rotala nummularia is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions, making it a popular choice for both freshwater and brackish environments.
