Rhododendron tatsienense var. tatsienense

Rhododendron tatsienense var. tatsienense, commonly known as the Sichuan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are often adorned with a distinctive, hairy covering.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
