Rosa multiflora var. momienensis

Rosa multiflora var. momienensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose or evergreen rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has elliptical leaves with serrated edges and produces rose hips that are rich in vitamin C. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
