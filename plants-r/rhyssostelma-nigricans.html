Rhyssostelma nigricans

Rhyssostelma nigricans, commonly known as the black-stemmed spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in xerophytic gardens.
