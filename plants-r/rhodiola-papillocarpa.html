Rhodiola papillocarpa

Rhodiola papillocarpa, commonly known as the mountain rhodiola, is a perennial plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and Central Asia.

It is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for its purported adaptogenic and antidepressant effects.

Rhodiola papillocarpa plays an important ecological role in alpine ecosystems, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various insect species.
