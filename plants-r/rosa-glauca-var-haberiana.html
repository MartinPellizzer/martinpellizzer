Rosa glauca var. haberiana

Rosa glauca var. haberiana, commonly known as the Haber's blue rose, is a rare and striking cultivar of the Rosa glauca species.

It is characterized by its pale blue to lavender-colored petals, which give it a unique and ethereal appearance. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.
