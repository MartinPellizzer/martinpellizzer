Richardia sericea

Richardia sericea, commonly known as silky richardia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
