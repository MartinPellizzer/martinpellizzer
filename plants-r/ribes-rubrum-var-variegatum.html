Ribes rubrum var. variegatum

Ribes rubrum var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated gooseberry, is a variant of the red gooseberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of green and white. Its small, yellow berries are edible and often used in jams and preserves.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
