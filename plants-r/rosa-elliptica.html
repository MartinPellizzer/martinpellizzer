Rosa elliptica

Rosa elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to regions of eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. Rosa elliptica typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
