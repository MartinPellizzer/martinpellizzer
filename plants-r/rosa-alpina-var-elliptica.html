Rosa alpina var. elliptica

Rosa alpina var. elliptica, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in alpine gardens.
