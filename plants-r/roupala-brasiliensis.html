Roupala brasiliensis

Roupala brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian roupala, is a small evergreen tree native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, gray bark. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
