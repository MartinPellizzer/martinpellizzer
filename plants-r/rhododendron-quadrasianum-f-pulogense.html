Rhododendron quadrasianum f. pulogense

Rhododendron quadrasianum f. pulogense, commonly known as the Pulongensis rhododendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The species is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a subspecies with unique floral and foliar traits.
