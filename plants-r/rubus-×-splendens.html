Rubus × splendens

Rubus × splendens, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California. This plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in late spring.

The plant produces edible berries that are small, tart, and often used in jams and desserts.
