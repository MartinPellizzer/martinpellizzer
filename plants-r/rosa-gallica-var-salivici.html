Rosa gallica var. salivici

Rosa gallica var. salivici, commonly known as the French rose, is a compact shrub native to Europe.

It produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is known for its strong, sweet scent. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance in perfumery.

This variety is relatively hardy and adaptable to a range of soil types.
