Rhodanthe Pyrethrum

Red hot poker, scientifically known as Rhodanthe pyrethrum, is a striking perennial plant native to New Zealand. Its name comes from the fiery red or orange tubular flowers that resemble poker flames, creating a dramatic visual impact in gardens. This hardy plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. With its long blooming period from late spring to early autumn, it adds vibrant color to landscapes throughout the growing season. Its resilience and showy appearance make it a favorite among gardeners seeking bold, colorful plants.
