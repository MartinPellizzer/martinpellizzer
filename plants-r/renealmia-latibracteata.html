Renealmia latibracteata

Renealmia latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract renealmia, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often concealed within leaf sheaths, a feature typical of many grasses.

Renealmia latibracteata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands.
