Rosa jebei

Rosa jebei, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets and produces fragrant, pink or white flowers. Rosa jebei is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
