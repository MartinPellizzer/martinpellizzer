Rosa jacutica

Rosa jacutica, commonly known as the Central Asian rose, is a species native to Central Asia and parts of the Middle East.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky environments. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa jacutica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its beautiful blooms and hardy nature.
