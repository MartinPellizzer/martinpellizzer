Rosa × lapicidinarum

Rosa × lapicidinarum, commonly known as the Lapicidinarum rose, is a hybrid shrub that belongs to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges and has a bushy growth habit. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This rose is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
