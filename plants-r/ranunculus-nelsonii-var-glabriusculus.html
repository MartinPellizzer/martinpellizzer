Ranunculus nelsonii var. glabriusculus

Ranunculus nelsonii var. glabriusculus, commonly known as the Nelson's buttercup, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs.
