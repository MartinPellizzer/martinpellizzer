Richardia hastata

Richardia hastata, commonly known as the slenderleaf richardia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 3 feet tall. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, slightly serrated edge.

Richardia hastata is often found in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade.
