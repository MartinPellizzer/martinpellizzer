Ruschia minutifolia

Ruschia minutifolia, commonly known as the dwarf ruschia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Ruschia minutifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant in rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.
