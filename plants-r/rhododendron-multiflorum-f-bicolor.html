Rhododendron multiflorum f. bicolor

Rhododendron multiflorum f. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor rhododendron, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, which typically feature a combination of white and pink hues. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China and Myanmar. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
