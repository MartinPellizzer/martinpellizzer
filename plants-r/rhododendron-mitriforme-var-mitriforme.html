Rhododendron mitriforme var. mitriforme

Rhododendron mitriforme var. mitriforme, commonly known as the Chinese flame azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically bright red or orange-red, often with a yellow throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
