Rorippa Teres

Rorippa teres is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant known for its delicate, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in shallow water environments and is commonly found in ponds and wetlands. This species is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible plants. Its simple structure and adaptability make it a valuable subject for ecological studies. Despite its modest appearance, Rorippa teres plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems.
