Ribes × bullatosilvestre

Ribes × bullatosilvestre, commonly known as the hybrid gooseberry.

This plant is a cross between Ribes nigrum (black currant) and Ribes rubrum (red currant), resulting in a species with unique botanical characteristics. It typically exhibits traits of both parent species, such as dark red to black berries and vigorous growth habits. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments, thriving in temperate climates.

Its berries are edible and have been used in culinary and medicinal applications.
