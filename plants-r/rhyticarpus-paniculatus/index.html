Rhyticarpus Paniculatus

Rhyticarpus paniculatus is a unique and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Australia. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring clusters of bright yellow flowers that stand out against its lush green foliage. This species belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
