Rhododendron cockburnii

Rhododendron cockburnii, commonly known as the Cockburn's rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its flowers are pinkish-white with a distinctive red throat, blooming in early spring.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
