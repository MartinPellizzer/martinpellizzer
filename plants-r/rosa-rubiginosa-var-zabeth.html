Rosa rubiginosa var. zabeth

Rosa rubiginosa var. zabeth, commonly known as the Zabeth rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a reddish-brown hip, which contains achenes surrounded by a fleshy outer layer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
