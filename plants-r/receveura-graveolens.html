Receveura graveolens

Receveura graveolens, commonly known as the stinking vining plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with a strong, unpleasant odor. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often overlooked due to its pungent scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Despite its negative fragrance, it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
