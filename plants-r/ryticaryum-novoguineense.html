Ryticaryum novoguineense

Ryticaryum novoguineense, commonly known as the New Guinea ryticaryum, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant belongs to the family Ryticaraceae and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a humid, warm climate.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural habitat.
