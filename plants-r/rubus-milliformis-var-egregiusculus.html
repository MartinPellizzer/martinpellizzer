Rubus milliformis var. egregiusculus

Rubus milliformis var. egregiusculus, commonly known as the alpine raspberry, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine regions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh mountain climates.
