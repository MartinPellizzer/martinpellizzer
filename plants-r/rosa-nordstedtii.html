Rosa nordstedtii

Rosa nordstedtii, commonly known as the Nordstedt's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa nordstedtii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
