Rosa × eliasii var. iberica

Rosa × eliasii var. iberica, commonly known as the Iberian rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems typical of the Rosa genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
