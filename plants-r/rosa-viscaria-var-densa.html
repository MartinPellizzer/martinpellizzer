Rosa viscaria var. densa

Rosa viscaria var. densa, commonly known as the dense thorned rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, slightly hairy leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in summer. The plant is characterized by its dense growth habit and thorny stems, which provide structural support. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedging plant.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand mild frosts, though it may require protection in colder climates.
