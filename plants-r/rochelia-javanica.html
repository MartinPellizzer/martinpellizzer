Rochelia javanica

Rochelia javanica, commonly known as the Javanese Rochelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rochelia javanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
