Rhododendron arboreum var. album

Rhododendron arboreum var. album, commonly known as the white rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has broad, leathery leaves and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
