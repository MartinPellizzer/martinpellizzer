Ruellia haenkeana f. purpurea

Ruellia haenkeana f. purpurea, commonly known as purple ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to violet flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ruellia haenkeana f. purpurea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
