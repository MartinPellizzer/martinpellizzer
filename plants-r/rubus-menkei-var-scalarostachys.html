Rubus menkei var. scalarostachys

Rubus menkei var. scalarostachys, commonly known as the scalycaly strawberry, is a species of shrub in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit, a small red berry, is edible and has a tart flavor, often used in local cuisines.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
