Rhaphiolepis sieboldii

Rhaphiolepis sieboldii, commonly known as the Japanese rosebay, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its fragrant blossoms.
