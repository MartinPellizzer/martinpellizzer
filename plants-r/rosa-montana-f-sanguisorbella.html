Rosa montana f. sanguisorbella

Rosa montana f. sanguisorbella, commonly known as the Crimson Glow rose, is a cultivar prized for its vibrant, deep red flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fragrant blooms. The plant features glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its showy flowers. It is typically grown as a shrub or climber and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is well-suited for garden borders, containers, or as a decorative hedge.
