Rosa Alpina Var. Monspeliaca

Rosa alpina var. monspeliaca is a rare and elegant subshrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its delicate pink flowers and compact growth habit, making it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and alpine collections. This variety thrives in cool, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky crevices at high elevations. Its historical significance in botanical studies adds to its allure among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
