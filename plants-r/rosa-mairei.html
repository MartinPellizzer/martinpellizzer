Rosa mairei

Rosa mairei, commonly known as the Maire's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the island of New Caledonia, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are alternate, pinnate, and covered in fine hairs.

Rosa mairei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
