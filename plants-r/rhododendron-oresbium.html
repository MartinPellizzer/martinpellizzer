Rhododendron oresbium

Rhododendron oresbium, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
