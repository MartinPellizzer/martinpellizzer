Rhamnus davurica

Rhamnus davurica, commonly known as the Chinese buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue, fleshy berries. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Rhamnus davurica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine.
