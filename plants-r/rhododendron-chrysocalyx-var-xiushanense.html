Rhododendron chrysocalyx var. xiushanense

Rhododendron chrysocalyx var. xiushanense, commonly known as the Xiushan rhododendron, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant floral display.
