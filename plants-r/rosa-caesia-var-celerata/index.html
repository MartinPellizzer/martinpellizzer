Rosa Caesia Var. Celerata

Rosa caesia var. celerata is a captivating variety of rose known for its delicate beauty and fragrant blooms. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, where it thrives in cooler climates. Its flowers, which range in color from pale pink to deep red, are often adorned with a subtle white or light green hue. The shrub is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes. Despite its elegance, it requires careful attention to soil and watering conditions to flourish.
