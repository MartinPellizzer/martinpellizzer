Rosa heldreichii

Rosa heldreichii, commonly known as the Hellenic rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a yellow center. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets, and are covered in fine hairs.

Rosa heldreichii is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
