Rosa camellia

Rosa camellia, commonly known as the camellia rose, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Rosa chinensis and Rosa multiflora.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that remain on the shrub throughout the year. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The camellia rose is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature, suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
