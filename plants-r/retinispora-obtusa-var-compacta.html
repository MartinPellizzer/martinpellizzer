Retinispora obtusa var. compacta

Retinispora obtusa var. compacta, commonly known as the compact retinispora, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings for its unique appearance.
