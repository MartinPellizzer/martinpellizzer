Romulea barbata

Romulea barbata, commonly known as the barbed romulea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscape designs.
