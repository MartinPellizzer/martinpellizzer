Rosa heterostyla

Rosa heterostyla, commonly known as the variable rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Tajikistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and simple, alternate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Rosa heterostyla is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
