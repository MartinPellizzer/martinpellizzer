Restio nutans

Restio nutans, commonly known as the nodding restio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family, which is characterized by its grass-like appearance and specialized adaptations to fire-prone environments. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, typically appearing in late summer or early autumn.

Restio nutans is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained, sunny habitats.
