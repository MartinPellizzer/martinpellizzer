Restio equisetaceus

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal or sandy habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silvery-green hue.
