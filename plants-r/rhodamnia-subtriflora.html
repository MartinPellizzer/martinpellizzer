Rhodamnia subtriflora

Rhodamnia subtriflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. It is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.

Rhodamnia subtriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
