Renealmia geostachys

Renealmia geostachys, commonly known as the yellow spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor in the soil while absorbing nutrients.

Renealmia geostachys is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
