Ruellia kunthiana

Ruellia kunthiana, commonly known as the purple roselight, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant violet to purple flowers throughout the growing season. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Ruellia kunthiana thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
