Rosa moyesii f. rosea

Rosa moyesii f. rosea, commonly known as the pink moyes rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
