Rosa jasminoides

Rosa jasminoides, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Rosa jasminoides is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
