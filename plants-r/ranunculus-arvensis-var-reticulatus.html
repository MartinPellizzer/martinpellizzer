Ranunculus arvensis var. reticulatus

Ranunculus arvensis var. reticulatus, commonly known as the net-veined buttercup, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers with a distinctive net-like pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks. Its leaves are palmately divided and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

It is a herbaceous perennial that blooms from spring to early summer.
