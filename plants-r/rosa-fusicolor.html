Rosa fusicolor

Rosa fusicolor, commonly known as the yellow rose of China, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, thornless stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically single or semi-double, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa fusicolor is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil types.
