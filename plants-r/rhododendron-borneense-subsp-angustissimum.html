Rhododendron borneense subsp. angustissimum

Rhododendron borneense subsp.

angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Bornean rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Borneo. It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include robust stems and evergreen foliage, which contribute to its adaptability in high-altitude habitats.

This subspecies is of particular interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
