Rosa × waitziana var. leiostyla

Rosa × waitziana var. leiostyla, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering, with flowers that typically appear in clusters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season, often from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens and as a decorative plant.
