Rhynchanthera goyazensis

Rhynchanthera goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a vibrant shade of pink. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to retain moisture.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered critically endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
