Rubus × personatus

Rubus × personatus, commonly known as the European blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, dark purple to black drupes. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.

Its berries are edible and have been used in traditional cuisine and herbal remedies.
