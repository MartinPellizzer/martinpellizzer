Rhododendron exquisitum

Rhododendron exquisitum, commonly known as the exquisite rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pink and purple.

Rhododendron exquisitum thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in humid, temperate climates.
