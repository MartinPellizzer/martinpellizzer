Rhododendron dianthosmum

Rhododendron dianthosmum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron dianthosmum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
