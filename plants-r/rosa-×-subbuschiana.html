Rosa × subbuschiana

Rosa × subbuschiana, commonly known as the Subbuschiana rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Germany.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse flowering, typically producing clusters of deep pink to red flowers. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden cultivation and floral arrangements.
