Rhynchosia totta var. unifoliolata

Rhynchosia totta var. unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers with a prominent keel. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible beans.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
