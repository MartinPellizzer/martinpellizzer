Restio concolor

Restio concolor, commonly known as the smooth restio, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in dense spikes during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight waxy coating.
