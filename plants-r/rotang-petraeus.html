Rotang petraeus

Rotang petraeus, commonly known as the rock banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its large, spreading canopy and robust, fibrous roots. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in rocky or coastal environments, making it highly adaptable. Its heartwood is valued for its durability and is often used in furniture and construction.

The leaves are large, glossy, and emit a mild fragrance when crushed.
