Rosa micrantha var. ovata

Rosa micrantha var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has elliptical leaves with serrated edges and a compact growth habit. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
