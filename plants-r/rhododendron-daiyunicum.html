Rhododendron daiyunicum

Rhododendron daiyunicum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in dense clusters during the spring.

The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and is often found in forested areas.
