Rhododendron × candelabrum

Rhododendron × candelabrum, commonly known as the candleabra rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces leathery, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
