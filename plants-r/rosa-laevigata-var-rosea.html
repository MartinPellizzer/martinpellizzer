Rosa laevigata var. rosea

Rosa laevigata var. rosea, commonly known as the Chinese pink rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its soft, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with ovate to elliptical leaflets and a woody stem covered in thorns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This variety is valued for its delicate appearance and historical significance in Chinese horticulture.
