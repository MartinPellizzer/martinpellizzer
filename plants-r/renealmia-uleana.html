Renealmia uleana

Renealmia uleana, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to arrowroot.

Renealmia uleana is also valued for its ornamental appeal in tropical gardens.
