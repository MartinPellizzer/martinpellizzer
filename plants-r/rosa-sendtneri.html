Rosa sendtneri

Rosa sendtneri, commonly known as the Sendtner's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually thornless.

Rosa sendtneri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
