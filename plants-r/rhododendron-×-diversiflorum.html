Rhododendron × diversiflorum

Rhododendron × diversiflorum, commonly known as the California rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
