Rosa × doniana

Rosa × doniana, commonly known as the "Garden Rose" or "English Rose," is a hybrid rose species resulting from the cross between Rosa gallica and Rosa damascena.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Rosa × doniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
