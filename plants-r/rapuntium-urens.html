Rapuntium urens

Rapuntium urens, commonly known as stinkcup, is a flowering plant belonging to the genus Ranunculus in the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has a hairy stem and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Its flowers are usually solitary and surrounded by a cluster of sepals that resemble petals.

Rapuntium urens is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.
