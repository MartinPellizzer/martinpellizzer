Ruschia excedens

Ruschia excedens, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, spiky inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It is often cultivated for its striking floral display and drought tolerance.

Ruschia excedens prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
