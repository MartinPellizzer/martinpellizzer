Rubus sprengelii subsp. rothomagensis

Rubus sprengelii subsp.

rothomagensis, commonly known as the Rothomagensis raspberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves.

The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
