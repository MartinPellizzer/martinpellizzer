Rosa communis var. rousselii

Rosa communis var. rousselii, commonly known as the Rousselii rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its deep pink to crimson flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, aromatic foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in floral arrangements.
