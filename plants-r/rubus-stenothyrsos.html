Rubus stenothyrsos

Rubus stenothyrsos, commonly known as the narrow-thorned blackberry, is a species of brambles native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that ripen to a deep black color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas.
