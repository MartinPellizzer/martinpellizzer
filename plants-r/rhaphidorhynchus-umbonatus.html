Rhaphidorhynchus umbonatus

Rhaphidorhynchus umbonatus, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is often cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
