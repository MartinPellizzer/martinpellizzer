Ribes cynosbati f. inerme

Ribes cynosbati f. inerme, commonly known as the smooth gooseberry, is a variant of the gooseberry plant characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves.

It belongs to the genus Ribes within the family Grossulariaceae, which includes other shrubs and plants with edible berries.
