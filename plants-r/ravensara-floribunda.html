Ravensara floribunda

Ravensara floribunda, commonly known as the golden ravenala, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, palm-like appearance with large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its foliage. Ravensara floribunda thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
