Rosa cinnamomea f. umbratica

Rosa cinnamomea f. umbratica, commonly known as the shade-loving rose, is a compact, ornamental shrub with dark green, glossy foliage.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. This variety thrives in partial to full shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens or shaded borders. Its leaves remain evergreen throughout the year, providing year-round interest.

The plant is noted for its hardiness and ability to adapt to moist, well-drained soil conditions.
