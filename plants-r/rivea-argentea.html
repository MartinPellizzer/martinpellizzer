Rivea argentea

Rivea argentea, commonly known as silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.

Rivea argentea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
