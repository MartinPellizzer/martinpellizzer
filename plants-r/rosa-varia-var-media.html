Rosa varia var. media

Rosa varia var. media, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a hybrid species that exhibits a range of flowering characteristics.

It typically features multiple stems and can grow to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with dense, bushy foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and red. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges.

Rosa varia var. media is well-suited for garden cultivation and is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
