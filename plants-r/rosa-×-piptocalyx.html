Rosa × piptocalyx

Rosa × piptocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China and widely cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has dark green, glossy leaves with serrated edges. Its botanical features include horticultural variability, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
