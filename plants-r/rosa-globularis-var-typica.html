Rosa globularis var. typica

Rosa globularis var. typica, commonly known as the globe rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and thorny stems typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and container planting.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for both its beauty and its ability to attract pollinators.
