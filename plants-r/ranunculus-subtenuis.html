Ranunculus subtenuis

Ranunculus subtenuis, commonly known as the slenderleaf buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, palmate leaves and bright yellow flowers with numerous small petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many other buttercup species.

Ranunculus subtenuis is often found in meadows and along the edges of wetlands, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.
