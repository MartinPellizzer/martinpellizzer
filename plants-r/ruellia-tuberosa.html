Ruellia tuberosa

Ruellia tuberosa, commonly known as purple panic, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color and bloom in clusters.

Ruellia tuberosa is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often used in ornamental gardening.
