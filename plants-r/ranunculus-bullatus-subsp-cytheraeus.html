Ranunculus bullatus subsp. cytheraeus

Ranunculus bullatus subsp.

cytheraeus, commonly known as the Cytheraeus buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family.

The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of soft, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a striking yet relatively low-maintenance ornamental plant.
