Renanthera chanii

Renanthera chanii, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers, which resemble a slipper in shape. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Renanthera chanii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
