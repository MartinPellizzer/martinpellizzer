Racosperma nitidulum

Racosperma nitidulum, commonly known as the glossy acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in cylindrical spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is deep and strong, allowing it to thrive in dry and nutrient-poor soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
