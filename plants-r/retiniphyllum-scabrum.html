Retiniphyllum scabrum

Retiniphyllum scabrum, commonly known as the rough retiniphyllum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a rough, textured surface. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
