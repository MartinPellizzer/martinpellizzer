Ruschia lavisii

Ruschia lavisii, commonly known as the Karoo star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often strikingly colored species. Due to its hardy nature, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.
