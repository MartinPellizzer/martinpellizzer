Rosa gallica f. umbricola

Rosa gallica f. umbricola, commonly known as the dark pink garden rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa gallica.

It is characterized by its deep pink to almost black flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking flower color and fragrant aroma.
