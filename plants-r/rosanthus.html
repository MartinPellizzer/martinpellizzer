Rosanthus

Rosanthus, commonly known as the pink rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red petals and a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.

Rosanthus is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and has cultural significance in various regions.
