Rosa henryi var. glandulosa

Rosa henryi var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular rose, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves with glandular dots. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms.
