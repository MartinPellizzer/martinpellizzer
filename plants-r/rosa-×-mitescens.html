Rosa × mitescens

Rosa × mitescens, commonly known as the sweet autumn crocus, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and a short, hairy stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Despite its name, it is not related to true crocuses but is valued for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.
