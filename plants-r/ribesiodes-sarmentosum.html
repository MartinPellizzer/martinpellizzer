Ribesiodes sarmentosum

Ribesiodes sarmentosum, commonly known as the creeping honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense ground cover. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

Ribesiodes sarmentosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
