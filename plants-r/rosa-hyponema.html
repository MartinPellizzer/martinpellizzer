Rosa hyponema

Rosa hyponema, commonly known as the climbing rose, is a species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to climb using its tendrils. The plant produces fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the blooms.

Rosa hyponema is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
