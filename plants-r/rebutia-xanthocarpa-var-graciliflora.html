Rebutia xanthocarpa var. graciliflora

Rebutia xanthocarpa var. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered yellow-twigged rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a compact, branching stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight yellowish tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
