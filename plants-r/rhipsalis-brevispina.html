Rhipsalis brevispina

Rhipsalis brevispina, commonly known as the silver leaf cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is a climbing or trailing plant with thin, flexible stems that often have a silvery or grayish hue. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters during the winter months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
