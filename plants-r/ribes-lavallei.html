Ribes lavallei

Ribes lavallei, commonly known as the Lavalle's gooseberry, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Ribes.

It is native to South America, specifically found in the Andean regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are elliptical with serrated edges and are often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and appear in clusters, followed by yellowish-orange berries that are edible.
