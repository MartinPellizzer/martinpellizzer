Roemeria hybrida subsp. dodecandra

Roemeria hybrida subsp.

dodecandra, commonly known as the purple roemeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of striking violet to purple flowers.

The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is largely due to its vibrant floral display and unique foliage structure.
