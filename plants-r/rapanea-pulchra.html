Rapanea pulchra

Rapanea pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful evergreen, is a member of the Lauraceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, and it produces small, round berries that are typically dark red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
