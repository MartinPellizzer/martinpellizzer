Rhamnus schlechteri

Rhamnus schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's buckthorn, is a species of shrub native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, simple leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or woodland areas.

Rhamnus schlechteri is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
