Rabdosia taiwanensis

Rabdosia taiwanensis, commonly known as Taiwan lemongrass, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its essential oils are used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent.
