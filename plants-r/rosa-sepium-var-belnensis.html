Rosa sepium var. belnensis

Rosa sepium var. belnensis, commonly known as the Belnensian rose, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to specific regions of Europe.

It features dense, thorny stems and produces showy, pink to red flowers in late spring. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly serrated edge. This variety is valued for its compact growth habit and prolonged blooming period.

Botanically, it is closely related to other roses in the Rosa genus, sharing similar structural and reproductive characteristics.
