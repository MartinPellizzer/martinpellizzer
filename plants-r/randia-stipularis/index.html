Randia Stipularis

Randia stipularis is a tropical shrub known for its vibrant red flowers and medicinal properties. Native to the Caribbean and parts of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to treat various ailments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
