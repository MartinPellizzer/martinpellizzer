Ranunculus alexeenkoi

Ranunculus alexeenkoi, commonly known as the Caucasian buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with five to seven petals. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and hairy stems that bear alternate, palmately lobed leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
