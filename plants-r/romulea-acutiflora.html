Romulea acutiflora

Romulea acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a member of the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants.
