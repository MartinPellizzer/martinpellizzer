Rhagodia baccata var. linearis

Rhagodia baccata var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed prickly devil.

It is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of South Australia. The plant features linear, often spiny leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the warmer months.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
