Rondeletia cincta

Rondeletia cincta, commonly known as the silverleaf rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
