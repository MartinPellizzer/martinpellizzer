Rutidea nigerica

Rutidea nigerica, commonly known as the blackwood tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly Nigeria, and is characterized by its dense, dark bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Rutidea nigerica is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
