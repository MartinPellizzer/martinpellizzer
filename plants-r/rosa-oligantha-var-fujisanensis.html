Rosa oligantha var. fujisanensis

Rosa oligantha var. fujisanensis, commonly known as the Japanese rock rose, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, thorny stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its floral display.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
