Rosa caesia var. winchiana

Rosa caesia var. winchiana, commonly known as the White Garden Rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in clusters and have a delicate, almost translucent appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its fragrant blooms and compact growth habit.
