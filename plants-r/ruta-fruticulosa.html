Ruta fruticulosa

Ruta fruticulosa, commonly known as mountain rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

Ruta fruticulosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal remedies.
