Rosa willdenowii

Rosa willdenowii, commonly known as the Willdenow's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

Rosa willdenowii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
