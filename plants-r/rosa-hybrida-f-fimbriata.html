Rosa hybrida f. fimbriata

Rosa hybrida f. fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its distinctive fringed petals.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its foliage is dark green and provides a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.
