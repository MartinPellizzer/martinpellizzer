Rosa × salisii

Rosa × salisii, commonly known as the Salisii rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range from pink to deep red in color. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × salisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in the production of perfumes and essential oils.
