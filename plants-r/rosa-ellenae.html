Rosa ellenae

Rosa ellenae, commonly known as the Ellen's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly found in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with simple, alternate leaves and fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Rosa ellenae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
