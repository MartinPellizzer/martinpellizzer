Rhododendron idoneum

Rhododendron idoneum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, broad leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron idoneum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
