Rosa jundzillii var. pendulina

Rosa jundzillii var. pendulina, commonly known as the weeping Rosa jundzillii, is a unique variety of shrub rose native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its cascading, drooping branches that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in early summer, which add ornamental value to gardens. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the flowering stems.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
