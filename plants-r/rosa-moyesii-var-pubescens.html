Rosa moyesii var. pubescens

Rosa moyesii var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent moyes rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "pubescens.". It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
