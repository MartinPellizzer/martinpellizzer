Ruschia wittebergensis

Ruschia wittebergensis, commonly known as the Witteberg Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, typical of its arid habitat. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
