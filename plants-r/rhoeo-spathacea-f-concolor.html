Rhoeo spathacea f. concolor

Rhoeo spathacea f. concolor, commonly known as the Variegated Maranta, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South America.

It features striking, oval-shaped leaves with a unique coloration pattern, often displaying shades of silver, green, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is popular as a houseplant and is relatively easy to care for.
