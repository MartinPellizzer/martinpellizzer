Rosa × muscosa simplex

Rosa × muscosa simplex, commonly known as the simple muscosa rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, single-layered petals and a compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant typically produces fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × muscosa simplex is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
