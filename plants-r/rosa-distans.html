Rosa distans

Rosa distans, commonly known as the distant rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically growing in woodland areas and along stream banks. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, with five petals and numerous stamens. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a woody stem covered in thorns.

Rosa distans is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
