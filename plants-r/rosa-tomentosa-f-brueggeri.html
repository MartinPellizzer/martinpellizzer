Rosa tomentosa f. brueggeri

Rosa tomentosa f. brueggeri, commonly known as the Brueggeri rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to southern China.

It features dense, velvety gray-green foliage covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and hardy nature.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
