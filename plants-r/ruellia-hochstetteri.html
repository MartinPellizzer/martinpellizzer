Ruellia hochstetteri

Ruellia hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ruellia hochstetteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
