Rosa obtusifolia f. rectispina

Rosa obtusifolia f. rectispina, commonly known as the upright prickly rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental flowers and hardy nature.
