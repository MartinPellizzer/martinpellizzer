Rosa communis var. imitata

Rosa communis var. imitata, commonly known as the imitation rose, is a hybrid cultivar prized for its ornamental value.

It features semi-double to double flowers with a range of colors, often resembling the more common Rosa rugosa. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and cut flower arrangements.
