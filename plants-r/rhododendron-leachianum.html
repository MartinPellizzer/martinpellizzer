Rhododendron leachianum

Rhododendron leachianum, commonly known as the Leach's rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers in the spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

Rhododendron leachianum is also known for its hardiness and ability to adapt to a variety of environmental conditions.
