Ronnbergia germinyana

Ronnbergia germinyana, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Ronnbergia germinyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
