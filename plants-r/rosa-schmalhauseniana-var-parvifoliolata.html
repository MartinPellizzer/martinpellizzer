Rosa schmalhauseniana var. parvifoliolata

Rosa schmalhauseniana var. parvifoliolata, commonly known as the dwarf leaf shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, oval leaves with a sparse foliage structure, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to gardens with limited space.
