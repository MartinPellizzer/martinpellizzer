Rapanea ovalifolia

Rapanea ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage with oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for landscaping purposes.
