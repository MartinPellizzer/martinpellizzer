Renantherella histrionica

Renantherella histrionica, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers that resemble flames, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and is typically found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their strong, spicy fragrance.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
