Ruschia hallii

Ruschia hallii, commonly known as the desert star, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are often reduced to scales, and it thrives in full sun with minimal water.

Ruschia hallii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
