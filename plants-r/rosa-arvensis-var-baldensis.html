Rosa arvensis var. baldensis

Rosa arvensis var. baldensis, commonly known as the Bald Mountain rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
