Rhododendron chiliangense

Rhododendron chiliangense, commonly known as the Yunnan azalea, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in cool, humid climates at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Rhododendron chiliangense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
