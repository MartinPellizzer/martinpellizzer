It is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

Reinwardtia glabra is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
