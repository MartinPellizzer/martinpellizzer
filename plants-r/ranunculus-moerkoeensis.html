Ranunculus moerkoeensis

Ranunculus moerkoeensis, commonly known as the Moerkoean buttercup, is a rare flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, known for its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features delicate, palmate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat.
