Rebutia minuscula subsp. wessneriana

Rebutia minuscula subsp.

wessneriana, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America. It features a short, thick stem with small, rounded ribs and is typically green or blue-green in color.

The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
