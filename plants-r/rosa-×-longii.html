Rosa × longii

Rosa × longii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa × longii is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
