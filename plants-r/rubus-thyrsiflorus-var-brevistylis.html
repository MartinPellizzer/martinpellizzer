Rubus thyrsiflorus var. brevistylis

Rubus thyrsiflorus var. brevistylis, commonly known as the dwarf raspberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, thorny stems and produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant's leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets that are serrated and hairy. Its fruit, a small red berry, is edible and often used in jams and desserts.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.
