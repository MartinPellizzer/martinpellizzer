Rhododendron yangmingshanense

Rhododendron yangmingshanense, commonly known as the Yangming Mountain rhododendron, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and cool temperatures. Its botanical features include thick, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
