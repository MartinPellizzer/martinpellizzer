Rosa rubiginosa var. rotundifolia

Rosa rubiginosa var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are rounded and velvety, with a distinctive reddish-brown underside. This variety is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often used in xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
