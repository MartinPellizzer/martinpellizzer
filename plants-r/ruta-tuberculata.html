Ruta tuberculata

Ruta tuberculata, commonly known as mountain rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided and have a slightly aromatic, bitter scent.

Ruta tuberculata is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
