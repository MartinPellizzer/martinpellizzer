Rosa sayi

Rosa sayi, commonly known as the Say's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets.

Rosa sayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
