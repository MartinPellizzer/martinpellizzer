Rossioglossum grande var. aureum

Rossioglossum grande var. aureum, commonly known as the golden hibiscus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a prominent red center, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and ample sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
