Restrepia loefgrenii

Restrepia loefgrenii, commonly known as the Loefgren's restia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high elevations. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with several narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a slight fragrance.

Restrepia loefgrenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
