Restrepia pelyx

Restrepia pelyx, commonly known as the Pelyx orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to thrive in cool, humid environments.

Restrepia pelyx is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
