Rosa strictispina

Rosa strictispina, commonly known as the rigid spine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and the Tian Shan. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and thorny stems, which give it its common name. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five to seven petals, and appear in clusters.

Rosa strictispina is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
