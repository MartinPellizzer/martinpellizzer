Rubus idaeus var. microphyllus

Rubus idaeus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the miniature raspberry, is a variant of the red raspberry plant characterized by its smaller, more delicate leaves and fruits.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the United States, particularly in the southeastern region. The plant produces small, sweet berries that are often used in culinary applications. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and small spaces.

Botanically, it shares many traits with the common raspberry but exhibits distinct morphological differences.
