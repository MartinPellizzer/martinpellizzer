Rosa cinnamomea var. concolor

Rosa cinnamomea var. concolor, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces red, edible hips that persist through winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
