Rosa wichuraiana

Rosa wichuraiana, commonly known as the Wichuraiana rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant produces small, red, elliptical fruits called hips, which are edible and often used in culinary applications. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively disease-resistant.

Rosa wichuraiana prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil for optimal growth.
