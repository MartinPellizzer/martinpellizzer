Reaumuria tatarica

Reaumuria tatarica, commonly known as the Tatarian sandthrift, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, with a deep root system that allows it to survive in sandy and saline soils. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion.

Its medicinal properties have also been studied for their potential health benefits.
