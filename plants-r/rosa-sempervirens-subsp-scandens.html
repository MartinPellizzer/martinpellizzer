Rosa sempervirens subsp. scandens

Rosa sempervirens subsp.

scandens, commonly known as the evergreen climbing rose, is a member of the Rosaceae family. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that remains lush throughout the year.

The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It is a vigorous climber that can be trained on trellises or fences.

Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
