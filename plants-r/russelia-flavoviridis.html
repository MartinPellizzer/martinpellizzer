Russelia flavoviridis

Russelia flavoviridis, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a striking flowering shrub native to South America.

It features showy, tubular orange-yellow flowers that bloom profusely throughout the growing season. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Russelia flavoviridis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
