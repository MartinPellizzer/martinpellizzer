Ruellia lanceolata

Ruellia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
