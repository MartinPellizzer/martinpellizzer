Rosa × rouyana

Rosa × rouyana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is native to China and has been cultivated for centuries for its ornamental value. It produces fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, often from spring to autumn.

The leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.
