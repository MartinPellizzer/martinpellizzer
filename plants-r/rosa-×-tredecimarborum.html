Rosa × tredecimarborum

Rosa × tredecimarborum, commonly known as the White Sand Rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Rosa × tredecimarborum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its fragrant blooms.
