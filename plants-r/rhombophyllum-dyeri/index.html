Rhombophyllum Dyeri

Rhombophyllum dyeri is a striking succulent native to southern Africa, known for its unique, geometric leaf shapes. This plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the "ice plant" family. Its leaves are often described as rhombus-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other succulents. It is highly sought after by collectors for its vibrant colors and unusual form. Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant to cultivate.
