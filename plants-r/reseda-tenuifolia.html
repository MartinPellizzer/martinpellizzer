Reseda tenuifolia

Reseda tenuifolia, commonly known as slender reseda, is a low-growing annual plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Reseda tenuifolia is also known for its use in traditional herbal medicine.
