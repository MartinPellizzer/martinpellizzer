Reseda lutea var. mucronata

Reseda lutea var. mucronata, commonly known as yellowrocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Reseda lutea var. mucronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically in herbal medicine.
