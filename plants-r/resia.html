Resia

Resia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant.
