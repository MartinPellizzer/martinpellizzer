Rhagodia eremaea

Rhagodia eremaea, commonly known as the spinifex burr.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia. The plant is characterized by its spiny, succulent stems and small, yellow flowers that appear in spring. It is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and is often found in spinifex grasslands.

Rhagodia eremaea plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert animals.
