Rudbeckia klamathensis

Rudbeckia klamathensis, commonly known as Klamath Coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy yellow to orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush habitats. It produces composite flowers with yellow disk flowers surrounded by brown to reddish ray flowers.

Rudbeckia klamathensis is a valuable plant for pollinators and is often used in native plant gardens.
