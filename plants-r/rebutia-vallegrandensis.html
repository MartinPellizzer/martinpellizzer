Rebutia vallegrandensis

Rebutia vallegrandensis, commonly known as the Valleygrandensis Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, featuring succulent stems that store water.

Rebutia vallegrandensis is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
