Rajania sintenisii

Rajania sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to the high-altitude regions of Papua, Indonesia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display vibrant purple or pink hues, often with intricate patterns. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to its limited distribution and threats from habitat loss, Rajania sintenisii is considered endangered.
