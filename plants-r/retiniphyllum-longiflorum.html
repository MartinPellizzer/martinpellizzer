Retiniphyllum longiflorum

Retiniphyllum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered retiniphyl, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are opposite and oval, with a smooth texture and dark green coloration.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
