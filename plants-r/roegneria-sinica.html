Roegneria sinica

Roegneria sinica, commonly known as the Chinese wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is often found in moist, temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.

Roegneria sinica is also used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a forage crop.
