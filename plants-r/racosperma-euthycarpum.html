Racosperma euthycarpum

Racosperma euthycarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Racosperma euthycarpum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
