Rubus × pseudoprolongatus

Rubus × pseudoprolongatus, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, aggregate fruits that are a mix of blackberry and raspberry traits.

This hybrid is often found in moist, acidic soils in temperate regions.
