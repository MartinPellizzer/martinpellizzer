Rapuntium platycaulum

Rapuntium platycaulum, commonly known as the flat-leaved cress, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other members like mustard and cabbage. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are broad and slightly succulent, often with a peppery taste.

Rapuntium platycaulum is typically found in moist, disturbed soils along roadsides and in meadows.
