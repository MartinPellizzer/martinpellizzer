Ruta lamarmorae

Ruta lamarmorae, commonly known as the Armenian rue, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like citrus and rue. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
