Riencourtia oblongifolia var. angustifolia

Riencourtia oblongifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed riencourtia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories.
