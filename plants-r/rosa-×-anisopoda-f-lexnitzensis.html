Rosa × anisopoda f. lexnitzensis

Rosa × anisopoda f. lexnitzensis, commonly known as the Lexnitzensis rose, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is typically found in the mountainous regions of Central Europe. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are serrated and have a glossy appearance, while the stems are covered in fine, pale-colored hairs.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to cooler climates.
