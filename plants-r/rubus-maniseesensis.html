Rubus maniseesensis

Rubus maniseesensis, commonly known as the Maniseesensis raspberry.

This plant is a species of bramble native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. It belongs to the Rubus genus, which includes many species of shrubs with edible berries. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, aggregate fruits.

It is adapted to high-altitude environments and requires specific conditions for successful growth.
