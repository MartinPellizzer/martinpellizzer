Ruschia albiflora

Ruschia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered Ruschia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
