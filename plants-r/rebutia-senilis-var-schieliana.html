Rebutia senilis var. schieliana

Rebutia senilis var. schieliana, commonly known as the Schiel's old man cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded stem with a distinctive grayish-blue coloration and a dense covering of white, woolly hair. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
