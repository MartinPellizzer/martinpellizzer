Rhodanthe sterilescens

Rhodanthe sterilescens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of small, bright yellow flowers. The species belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is often found in coastal and sandy environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The plant is well adapted to withstand salt spray and is a popular choice for coastal gardens.
