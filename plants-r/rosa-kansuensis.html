Rosa kansuensis

Rosa kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu rose, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Rosa kansuensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
