Robiquetia schizogenia

Robiquetia schizogenia, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Robiquetia schizogenia is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
