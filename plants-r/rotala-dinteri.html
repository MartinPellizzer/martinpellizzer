Rotala dinteri

Rotala dinteri, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a compact aquatic plant native to tropical Africa.

It features whorled leaves that are typically bright green and can exhibit red or purple hues under certain light conditions. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually submerged, and it thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments. Rotala dinteri is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for pond and tank landscaping.
