Rosa luzoniensis

Rosa luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon rose, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its thorny stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated edge.

Rosa luzoniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
