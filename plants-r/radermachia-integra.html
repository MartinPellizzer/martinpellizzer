Radermachia integra

Radermachia integra, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Radermachia integra is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
