Renanthera ramuana

Renanthera ramuana, commonly known as the Ramuan orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which helps it store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
