Rosa × zajartakrivularis

Rosa × zajartakrivularis, commonly known as the Zajartakrivularis rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy blooms.

It typically produces large, semi-double to fully double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, depending on the specific cultivar. The plant features dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems typical of many rose species. It is well-suited for garden borders, containers, and shrub beds due to its ornamental value and moderate maintenance requirements.

Botanically, it is a result of crossbreeding between different Rosa species, showcasing genetic diversity and horticultural adaptability.
