Rosa × complicata f. pennina

Rosa × complicata f. pennina, commonly known as the Chinese rose or everblooming rose, is a hybrid species of rose that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a shrub with multiple stems and is notable for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a rich fragrance and a semi-double to fully double bloom structure.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its persistent flowering and hardy nature.
