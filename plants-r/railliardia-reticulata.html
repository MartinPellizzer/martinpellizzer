Railliardia reticulata

Railliardia reticulata, commonly known as the laceleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, lace-like foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, often with a delicate, veined pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Railliardia reticulata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
