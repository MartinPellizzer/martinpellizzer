Rosa chinensis f. viridiflora

Rosa chinensis f. viridiflora, commonly known as the Green-flowered Chinese Rose, is a unique cultivar of the common rose.

It is characterized by its vibrant green petals, which contrast strikingly with its deep red stamens and pistil. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and is native to China.

It typically blooms in spring and summer, producing clusters of fragrant flowers.
