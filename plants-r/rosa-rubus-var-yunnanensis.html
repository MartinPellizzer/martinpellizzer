Rosa rubus var. yunnanensis

Rosa rubus var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan raspberry, is a shrub native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it blooms in the spring.
