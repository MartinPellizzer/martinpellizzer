Reseda erecta var. funkii

Reseda erecta var. funkii, commonly known as the Funk's eryngo, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
