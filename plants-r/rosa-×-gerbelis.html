Rosa × gerbelis

Rosa × gerbelis, commonly known as the Gerbelis rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless growth habit and compact form, making it suitable for hedging and ornamental use. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to full blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in the spring. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop for the flowers.

Rosa × gerbelis is relatively disease-resistant and requires minimal maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
