Rhododendron malayanum var. pubens

Rhododendron malayanum var. pubens, commonly known as the hairy rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to the Philippines.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
