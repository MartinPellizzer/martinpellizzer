Rosa macrantha

Rosa macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and its ability to produce clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins and produces rose hips that are typically red or orange in color. Rosa macrantha thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its historical use in traditional Chinese medicine.
