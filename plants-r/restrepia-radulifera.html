Restrepia radulifera

Restrepia radulifera, commonly known as the radulifera orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or mountainous environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in racemes, and bloom during the cooler months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
