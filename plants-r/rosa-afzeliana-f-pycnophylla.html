Rosa afzeliana f. pycnophylla

Rosa afzeliana f. pycnophylla, commonly known as the dense-leaved rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
