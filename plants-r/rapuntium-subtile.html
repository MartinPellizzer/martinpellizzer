Rapuntium subtile

Rapuntium subtile, commonly known as the delicate buttercup, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy stem. The leaves are palmately divided, typically with three to five lobes, and are covered in fine hairs. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows.

This plant is often found in temperate regions and is known for its early spring blooming habit.
