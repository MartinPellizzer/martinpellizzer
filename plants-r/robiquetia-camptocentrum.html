Robiquetia camptocentrum

Robiquetia camptocentrum, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Robiquetia camptocentrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
