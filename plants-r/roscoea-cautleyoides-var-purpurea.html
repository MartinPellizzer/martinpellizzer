Roscoea cautleyoides var. purpurea

Roscoea cautleyoides var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed roscoea, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, orchid-like flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive, spurred lip. The plant has fleshy, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and floral display.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for shade gardens and woodland settings.
