Rosa micradena

Rosa micradena, commonly known as the small-flowered rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Rosa micradena typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
