Rosa communis var. ostensa

Rosa communis var. ostensa, commonly known as the Austrian rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa communis species.

It is characterized by its double flowers, which are typically pink to red in color, and its compact growth habit. The plant is native to Central Europe and thrives in temperate climates. It produces fragrant blooms throughout the summer months, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The leaves are dark green and serrated, providing a lush backdrop to its showy flowers.
