Rubus wagae

Rubus wagae, commonly known as the Wagae raspberry.

It is a species of bramble belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. Native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant produces small, red, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the regional flora and ecosystem.
