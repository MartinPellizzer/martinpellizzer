Rosa maximowicziana var. kokusanensis

Rosa maximowicziana var. kokusanensis, commonly known as the Japanese rose or kokusan rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, often covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
