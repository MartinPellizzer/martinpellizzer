Rosa × kordesii

Rosa × kordesii, commonly known as the Kordesii rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and widely cultivated for its ornamental value.

It features upright growth with thornless stems and is known for its abundant, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Rosa × kordesii is also valued for its resistance to common rose diseases, contributing to its popularity among gardeners.
