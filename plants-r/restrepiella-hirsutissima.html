Restrepiella hirsutissima

Restrepiella hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy restrepiella, is a rare and striking plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that range from purple to red. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
