Rosa solstitialis var. brusnikensis

Rosa solstitialis var. brusnikensis, commonly known as the Brusniky rose, is a variant of the summer rose species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
