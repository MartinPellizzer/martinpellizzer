Rosa × damascena coccinea

Rosa × damascena coccinea, commonly known as the crimson damask rose, is a hybrid species of the rose genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep pink to bright red. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. It produces fragrant blooms in late spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are often used in perfumery and culinary applications due to their rich scent and flavor.
