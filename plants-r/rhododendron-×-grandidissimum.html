Rhododendron × grandidissimum

Rhododendron × grandidissimum, commonly known as the giant rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing dense foliage.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
