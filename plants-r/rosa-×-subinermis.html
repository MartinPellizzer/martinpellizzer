Rosa × subinermis

Rosa × subinermis, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species that combines traits from various rose species.

It is characterized by its ability to produce flowers continuously throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant typically features thornless stems and large, fragrant blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its shape and promote continuous flowering.

This rose is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
