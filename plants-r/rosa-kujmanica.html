Rosa kujmanica

Rosa kujmanica, commonly known as the Kujman's rose, is a species native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals. Rosa kujmanica produces rose hips, which are the fruit of the plant and contain seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
