Ribes fedtschenkoi

Ribes fedtschenkoi, commonly known as the Fedtschenko gooseberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant features alternate, simple leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring. The species is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and fruit production.
