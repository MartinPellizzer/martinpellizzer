Relbunium hypocarpium var. incanum

Relbunium hypocarpium var. incanum, commonly known as the white-haired sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native environment.
