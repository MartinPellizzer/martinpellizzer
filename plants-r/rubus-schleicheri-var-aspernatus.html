Rubus schleicheri var. aspernatus

Rubus schleicheri var. aspernatus, commonly known as the aspernatus raspberry, is a shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
