Ranunculus Acinaciformis

Ranunculus acinaciformis is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its sharp, blade-like leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The plant's name translates to "spear-shaped," a reference to its distinctive foliage. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements.
