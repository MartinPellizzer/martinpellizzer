Russelia coccinea

Russelia coccinea, commonly known as the red-hot catwhiskers, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Russelia coccinea thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
