Rhododendron indicum var. ignescens

Rhododendron indicum var. ignescens, commonly known as the flame azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with a fiery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to bloom profusely in spring.
