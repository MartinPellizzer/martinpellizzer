Ruellia boliviana

Ruellia boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian ruellia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple tubular flowers. Ruellia boliviana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
