Rindera cyclodonta

Rindera cyclodonta, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers range in color from white to yellow.

Rindera cyclodonta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to full sun exposure.
