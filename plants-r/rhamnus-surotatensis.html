Rhamnus surotatensis

Rhamnus surotatensis, commonly known as the Surotatensis buckthorn, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, oval leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in forested or scrubland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
