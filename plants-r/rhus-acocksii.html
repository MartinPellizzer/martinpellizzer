Rhus acocksii

Rhus acocksii, commonly known as the Acocks' holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, glossy dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for certain bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
