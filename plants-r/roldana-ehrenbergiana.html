Roldana ehrenbergiana

Roldana ehrenbergiana, commonly known as the Desert Star, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warm season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
