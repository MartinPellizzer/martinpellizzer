Rosa × richardii

Rosa × richardii, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and profuse flowering, with blooms that can be single or double. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white, and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Rosa × richardii is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to different climates.
