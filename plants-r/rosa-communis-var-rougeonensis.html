Rosa communis var. rougeonensis

Rosa communis var. rougeonensis, commonly known as the Rougeon rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from France.

It is characterized by its deep red petals and strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing abundant flowers that are popular in gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for both its aesthetic appeal and historical significance.
