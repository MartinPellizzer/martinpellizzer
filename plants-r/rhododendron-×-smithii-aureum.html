Rhododendron × smithii aureum

Rhododendron × smithii aureum, commonly known as the golden flame rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that ranges from dark green to bronze in color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange to yellow, with a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its vibrant color and hardy nature, it is widely cultivated in gardens and public spaces.
