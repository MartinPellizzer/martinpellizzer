Ruellia barlerioides

Ruellia barlerioides, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia barlerioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
