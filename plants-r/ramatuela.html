Ramatuela

Ramatuela, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a waxy texture and a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
