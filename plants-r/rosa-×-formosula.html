Rosa × formosula

Rosa × formosula, commonly known as the Formosan rose, is a hybrid species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas.

Rosa × formosula is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in horticulture.
