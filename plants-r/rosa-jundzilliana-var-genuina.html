Rosa jundzilliana var. genuina

Rosa jundzilliana var. genuina, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features dark green, glossy foliage and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers with a soft, velvety texture. The plant has a strong, upright growth habit and is characterized by its dense, thorny stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
