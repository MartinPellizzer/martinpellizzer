Rosa obtusifolia var. subglabra

Rosa obtusifolia var. subglabra, commonly known as the woolly rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and broad, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its foliage is often covered in fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature in temperate climates.
