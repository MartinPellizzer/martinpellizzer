Ranunculus nudicaulis

Ranunculus nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, bright yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy stem that often grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three small, heart-shaped leaflets. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Ranunculus nudicaulis is also known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
