Rosa × reversa var. attenuata

Rosa × reversa var. attenuata, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant typically features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and shrubbery.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
