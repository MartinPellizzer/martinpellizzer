Rhynchosia dekindtii

Rhynchosia dekindtii, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain small, hard seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
