Rustia isernii

Rustia isernii, commonly known as the Isern's rustia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The small yellow flowers appear in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
