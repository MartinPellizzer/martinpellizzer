Rosa mollis f. pyriformis

Rosa mollis f. pyriformis, commonly known as the oval-leaf rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy beneath, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its growth habit is upright and bushy, making it suitable for small garden spaces or container planting.

The foliage remains evergreen, providing ornamental value throughout the year.
