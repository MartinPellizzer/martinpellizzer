Rhododendron Ponticum Var. Obtusum

Rhododendron ponticum var. obtusum is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Caucasus region. It is known for its dense foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. This variant is characterized by its broader leaves and more compact growth habit compared to other rhododendron species. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodlands and mountainous areas. Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes around the world.
