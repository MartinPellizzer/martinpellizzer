Rosa boursaultii

Rosa boursaultii, commonly known as the Boursault rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub with thorny stems and narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy plant.

Rosa boursaultii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
