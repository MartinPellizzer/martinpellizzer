Rosa fujisanensis var. setifera

Rosa fujisanensis var. setifera, commonly known as the Japanese mountain rose, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The leaves are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. This variety thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
