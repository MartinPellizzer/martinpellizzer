Rhododendron croceum

Rhododendron croceum, commonly known as the yellow rhododendron, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high elevations. The plant features showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

Rhododendron croceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
