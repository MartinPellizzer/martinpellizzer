Rosa × muennerstadtiensis

Rosa × muennerstadtiensis, commonly known as the Muennerstadt rose, is a hybrid shrub native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and as a cut flower.
