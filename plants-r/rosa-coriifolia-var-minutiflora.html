Rosa coriifolia var. minutiflora

Rosa coriifolia var. minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has thorny stems and elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its modest size.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
