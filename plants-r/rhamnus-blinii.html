Rhamnus blinii

Rhamnus blinii, commonly known as the Chinese buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.

The species is also used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions.
