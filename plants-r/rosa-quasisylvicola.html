Rosa quasisylvicola

Rosa quasisylvicola, commonly known as the mountain rose or the alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage.
