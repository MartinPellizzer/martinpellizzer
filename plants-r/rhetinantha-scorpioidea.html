Rhetinantha scorpioidea

Rhetinantha scorpioidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow and bloom during the summer months.

Rhetinantha scorpioidea is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
