Rosa × yezoensis

Rosa × yezoensis, commonly known as the Yezo rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rosa × yezoensis is also valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
