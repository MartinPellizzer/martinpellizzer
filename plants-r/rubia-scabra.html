Rubia scabra

Rubia scabra, commonly known as rough betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Rubia scabra is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
