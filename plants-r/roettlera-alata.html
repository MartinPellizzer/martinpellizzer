Roettlera alata

Roettlera alata, commonly known as the winged roettlera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Roettlera alata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
