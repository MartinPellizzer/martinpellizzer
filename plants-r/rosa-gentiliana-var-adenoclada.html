Rosa gentiliana var. adenoclada

Rosa gentiliana var. adenoclada, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, branched stems covered in dense, grayish-green foliage. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and alpine settings.
