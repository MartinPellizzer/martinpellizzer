Rosa × dumetorum subsp. leptotricha

Rosa × dumetorum subsp.

leptotricha, commonly known as the creeping rose, is a shrub-like plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems that often grow close to the ground.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

This subspecies is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed or ruderal habitats.
