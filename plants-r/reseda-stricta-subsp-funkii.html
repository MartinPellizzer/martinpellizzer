Reseda stricta subsp. funkii

Reseda stricta subsp.

funkii, commonly known as the narrowleaf reseda, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
