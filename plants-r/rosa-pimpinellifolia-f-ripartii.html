Rosa pimpinellifolia f. ripartii

Rosa pimpinellifolia f. ripartii, commonly known as the Alpine rose or rock rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are densely packed, providing a dense appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental appeal and adaptability to challenging garden conditions.
