Rosa macartnea

Rosa macartnea, commonly known as the Macartney rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China and is characterized by its fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Rosa macartnea is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in botanical history.
