Ruschia lisabeliae

Ruschia lisabeliae, commonly known as the Lisabel's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form with fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
