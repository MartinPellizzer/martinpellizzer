Racosperma neriifolium

Racosperma neriifolium, commonly known as the black-leaved acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in spikes, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Racosperma neriifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
