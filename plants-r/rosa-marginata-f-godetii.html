Rosa marginata f. godetii

Rosa marginata f. godetii, commonly known as the Godetii rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the summer. The plant has narrow, thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves that are covered in a fine layer of down. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
