Rhodiola yunnanensis subsp. forrestii

Rhodiola yunnanensis subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan rhodiola, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of China. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous climates. It is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas at elevations above 2,500 meters.

Due to its resilience and unique chemical composition, it is valued for its potential medicinal properties.
