Rubus × gabarinus

Rubus × gabarinus, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chinyensis (raspberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant produces aggregate fruits that are typically dark red to black in color, with a tart flavor. It has compound leaves with serrated edges and thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

This hybrid is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
