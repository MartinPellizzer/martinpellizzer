Rosa rubrifolia carniolica

Rosa rubrifolia carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan redleaf rose, is a species native to the Alpine regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which contrasts strikingly with its delicate pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a distinctive red coloration that intensifies in cooler temperatures.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
