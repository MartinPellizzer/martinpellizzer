Russelia flammea

Russelia flammea, commonly known as the flame rose or fire plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Russelia flammea is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
