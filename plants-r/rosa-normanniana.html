Rosa normanniana

Rosa normanniana, commonly known as the Norman rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It produces rose hips that are red and hairy when mature.

Rosa normanniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery.
