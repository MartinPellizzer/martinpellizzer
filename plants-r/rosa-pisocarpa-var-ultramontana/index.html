Rosa Pisocarpa Var. Ultramontana

Rosa pisocarpa var. ultramontana is a rare and striking species of rose native to the high altitudes of the Andes. Known for its vibrant, deep pink to red flowers, this variety captivates botanists and gardeners alike with its unique beauty. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. The plant's rugged appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among those seeking to grow challenging flora. Despite its allure, it remains relatively obscure, making it a sought-after specimen in specialized horticultural circles.
