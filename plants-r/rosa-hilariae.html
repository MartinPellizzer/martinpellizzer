Rosa hilariae

Rosa hilariae, commonly known as the Hilaria rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a striking contrast to the showy blooms.

Rosa hilariae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
