Rubus × brevithyrsus

Rubus × brevithyrsus, commonly known as the small-fruited blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, typical of the Rubus genus. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in fruit size and plant structure.
