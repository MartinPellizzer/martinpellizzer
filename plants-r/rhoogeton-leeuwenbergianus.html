Rhoogeton leeuwenbergianus

Rhoogeton leeuwenbergianus, commonly known as the Klein Karoo cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the semi-arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it highly sensitive to environmental changes.

Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
