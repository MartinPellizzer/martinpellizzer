Rosa × makinoana

Rosa × makinoana, commonly known as the Makino rose, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Rosa × makinoana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
