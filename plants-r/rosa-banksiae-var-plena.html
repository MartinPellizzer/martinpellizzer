Rosa banksiae var. plena

Rosa banksiae var. plena, commonly known as the Banksia rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its dense, double blooms that range in color from white to deep pink, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and broad, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and horticultural study.
