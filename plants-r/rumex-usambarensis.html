Rumex usambarensis

Rumex usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis dock, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in woodland and grassland habitats.

Rumex usambarensis is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
