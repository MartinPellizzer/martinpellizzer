Randia formosa var. nitida

Randia formosa var. nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf randia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs that contribute to its distinctive look. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique aesthetic and adaptability to various growing conditions.
