Rubus stephanandria

Rubus stephanandria, commonly known as the stephanandria raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or orange in color. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and it has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
