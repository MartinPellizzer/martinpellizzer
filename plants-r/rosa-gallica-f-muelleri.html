Rosa gallica f. muelleri

Rosa gallica f. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's gallica rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to Europe.

It features dense, thornless stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has deeply lobed, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Rosa gallica f. muelleri blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate floral display to gardens.
