Ribes burejense var. burejense

Ribes burejense var. burejense, commonly known as the Burejez gooseberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various species of shrubs and small trees commonly found in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated margin.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its potential use in traditional medicine.
