Rosa trachyphylla var. blondaeana

Rosa trachyphylla var. blondaeana, commonly known as the blondaeana rose, is a hybrid cultivar of the Rosa trachyphylla species.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which bloom in clusters and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
