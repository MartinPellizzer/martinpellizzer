Restio productus

Restio productus, commonly known as the slender restio, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Restio productus thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
