Rodgersia sambucifolia var. estrigosa

Rodgersia sambucifolia var. estrigosa, commonly known as the silver-leaf rodgersia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white to silver hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white to lavender flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, upright stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
