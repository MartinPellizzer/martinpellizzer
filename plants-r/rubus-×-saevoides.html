Rubus × saevoides

Rubus × saevoides, commonly known as the hybrid raspberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus idaeus (red raspberry) and Rubus strumosus (blackberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark red to black in color, and have a tart, juicy flavor. Its leaves are alternate, palmate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are thorny.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses in jams and desserts.
