Racosperma yirrkallense

Racosperma yirrkallense, commonly known as the Yirrkalla wattle, is a shrub native to the Northern Territory of Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor soils.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local fauna.
