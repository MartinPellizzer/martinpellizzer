Restrepia antennifera subsp. erythroxantha

Restrepia antennifera subsp.

erythroxantha, commonly known as the red-flowered restrepia, is a species of orchid native to South America. It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces striking red or reddish-pink flowers.

The plant typically has dark green, leathery leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled conditions.
