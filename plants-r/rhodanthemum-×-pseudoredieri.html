Rhodanthemum × pseudoredieri

Rhodanthemum × pseudoredieri, commonly known as the hybrid chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and orange. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a cut flower.

Rhodanthemum × pseudoredieri typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.
