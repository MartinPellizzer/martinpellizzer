Rosa stylosa var. gallicoides

Rosa stylosa var. gallicoides, commonly known as the Gallicoides rose, is a variant of the stylosa rose native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
