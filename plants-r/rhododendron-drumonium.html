Rhododendron drumonium

Rhododendron drumonium, commonly known as the dwarf rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually pink to red, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron drumonium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
