Rosa vosagiaca f. pusilla

Rosa vosagiaca f. pusilla, commonly known as the miniature shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, arching stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size.

Its botanical characteristics make it a prized addition to gardens seeking both ornamental value and hardiness.
