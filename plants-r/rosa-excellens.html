Rosa excellens

Rosa excellens, commonly known as the English rose, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy blooms that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. Rosa excellens features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves.

It produces hips, which are the fruit of the plant, often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
