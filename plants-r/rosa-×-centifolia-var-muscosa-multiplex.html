Rosa × centifolia var. muscosa-multiplex

Rosa × centifolia var. muscosa-multiplex, commonly known as the musky centifolia rose, is a hybrid rose prized for its fragrant, multicolored flowers.

It features a bushy growth habit with thorny stems and glossy, dark green foliage. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a distinctive musky scent that intensifies as they age. This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in perfumery due to its aromatic properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
