Rabdosia setschwanensis

Rabdosia setschwanensis, commonly known as Chinese blueweed, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Rabdosia setschwanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
