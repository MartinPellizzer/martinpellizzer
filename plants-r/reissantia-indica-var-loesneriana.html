Reissantia indica var. loesneriana

Reissantia indica var. loesneriana, commonly known as the Indian balsam, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
