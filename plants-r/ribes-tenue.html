Ribes tenue

Ribes tenue, commonly known as slender gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant produces small, yellowish-green berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn red in the fall.

Ribes tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for wildlife.
