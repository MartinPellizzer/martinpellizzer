Racosperma Mackeyanum

Racosperma mackeyanum is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the arid regions of northern Australia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique adaptations to survive in harsh, dry environments. This plant exhibits a distinctive growth habit, often forming compact shrubs with small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are typically pale or greenish, blending in with its surroundings to avoid herbivory. Despite its resilience, Racosperma mackeyanum remains poorly studied and is considered a botanical curiosity.
