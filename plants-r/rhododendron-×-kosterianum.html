Rhododendron × kosterianum

Rhododendron × kosterianum, commonly known as the Koster rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 2–4 meters and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its flowers range in color from white to deep pink, adding vibrant visual interest to gardens and natural landscapes.
