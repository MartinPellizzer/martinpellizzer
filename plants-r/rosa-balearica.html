Rosa balearica

Rosa balearica, commonly known as the Balearic rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region, particularly the Balearic Islands.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features upright, thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rosa balearica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its aromatic properties.
