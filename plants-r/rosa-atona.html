Rosa atona

Rosa atona, commonly known as the silky rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Rosa atona is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
