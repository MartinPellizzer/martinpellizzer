Rourea reticulata

Rourea reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined rourea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural collections in tropical regions.
