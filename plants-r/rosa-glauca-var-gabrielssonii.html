Rosa glauca var. gabrielssonii

Rosa glauca var. gabrielssonii, commonly known as the Gabrielsson's blue rose, is a rare and ornamental variant of the Rosa glauca species.

It is characterized by its pale blue to lavender-colored flowers, which are typically single or semi-double in form. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its rarity and unique coloration, it is highly valued by horticultural enthusiasts and collectors.
