Restio stenocoleus

Restio stenocoleus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural reserves.
