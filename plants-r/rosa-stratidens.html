Rosa stratidens

Rosa stratidens, commonly known as the mountain rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a red, hairy achene, which contains a single seed.

Rosa stratidens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
