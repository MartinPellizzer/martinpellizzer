Rubus menkei proles teretiusculus

Rubus menkei proles teretiusculus, commonly known as the slender blackberry.

It is a species of bramble belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its trailing growth habit and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. It is native to southeastern China and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated margins, and it is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
