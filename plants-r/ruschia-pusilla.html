Ruschia pusilla

Ruschia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf ruischia, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a woolly texture. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or rocky environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and rockeries.
