Rubus × assurgens var. belliatus

Rubus × assurgens var. belliatus, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, drupe-like berries that range in color from red to black. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
