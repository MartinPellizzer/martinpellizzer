Rhynchosia senna f. ovatis

Rhynchosia senna f. ovatis, commonly known as the broad-leaf senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its palmately compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets. The plant produces yellow, pea-like flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in open woodland or disturbed soils.

The seeds are enclosed in pod-like fruits and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.
