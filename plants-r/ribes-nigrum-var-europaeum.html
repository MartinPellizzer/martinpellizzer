Ribes nigrum var. europaeum

Ribes nigrum var. europaeum, commonly known as European blackcurrant, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It produces small, dark purple to black berries that are rich in antioxidants and used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant has alternate, simple leaves with serrated edges and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are an important food source for birds and wildlife.
