Rubus dichromophyllus

Rubus dichromophyllus, commonly known as the blackberry cane.

This plant is a species of bramble native to China, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black in color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
