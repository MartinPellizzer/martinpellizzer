Rhynchospora vulcani var. ecuadorensis

Rhynchospora vulcani var. ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian flamegrass, is a perennial grass native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its erect, flowering stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and wetlands.
