Rhacoma crossopetalum

Rhacoma crossopetalum, commonly known as the zigzag rhacoma, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
