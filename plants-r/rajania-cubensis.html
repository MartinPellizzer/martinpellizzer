Rajania cubensis

Rajania cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban starfruit, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a tropical shrub characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright orange, star-shaped fruits. The fruit is edible, with a juicy, sweet flesh that contains numerous seeds.

It is native to the Caribbean and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.
