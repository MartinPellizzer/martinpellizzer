Rosa reuteri var. glandulosa

Rosa reuteri var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, glossy leaves with small, glandular dots on the underside, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
