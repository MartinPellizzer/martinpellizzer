Ruta montbretii

Ruta montbretii, commonly known as mountain rue, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Ruta montbretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal remedies.
