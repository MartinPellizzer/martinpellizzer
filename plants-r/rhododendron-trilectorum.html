Rhododendron trilectorum

Rhododendron trilectorum, commonly known as the Hainan rhododendron, is a species native to the island of Hainan in China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the floral display.

Rhododendron trilectorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
