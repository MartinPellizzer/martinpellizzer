Rosanowia × hybrida

Rosanowia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid rose, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the crossbreeding of species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
