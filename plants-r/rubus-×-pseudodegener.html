Rubus × pseudodegener

Rubus × pseudodegener, commonly known as the blackberry dewberry.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have serrated edges.

The fruit, which is a berry, is often used in culinary applications and is valued for its flavor and nutritional content.
