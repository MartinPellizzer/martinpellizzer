Rosa canina var. dollineriana

Rosa canina var. dollineriana, commonly known as the Dollinerian rose, is a rare and cultivated variant of the dog rose.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers and thorny stems, typical of the Rosa canina species. The plant produces small, red, edible hips that are similar to those of the common dog rose. It is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance.

This variety is noted for its delicate fragrance and is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance.
