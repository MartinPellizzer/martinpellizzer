Ruschia robusta

Ruschia robusta, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or orange, often with a distinctive red or yellow stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Ruschia robusta is drought-tolerant and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its ornamental appeal.
