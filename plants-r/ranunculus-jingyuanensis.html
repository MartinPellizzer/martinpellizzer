Ranunculus jingyuanensis

Ranunculus jingyuanensis, commonly known as the Jingyuan buttercup, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features delicate, palmately divided leaves and produces flowers with five to eight petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
