Rhododendron stelligerum

Rhododendron stelligerum, commonly known as the alpine rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pink and purple.

Rhododendron stelligerum is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it plays a significant role in local ecosystems.
