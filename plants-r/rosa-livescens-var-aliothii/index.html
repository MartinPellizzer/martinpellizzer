Rosa Livescens Var. Aliothii

Rosa livescens var. aliothii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western china. it belongs to the rose family and is known for its elegant, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. this variant is distinguished by its unique leaf patterns and subtle color variations. due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
