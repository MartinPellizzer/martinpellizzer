Rosa lutetiana var. biserrata

Rosa lutetiana var. biserrata, commonly known as the French garden rose, is a variety of the Rosa lutetiana species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated edges that give it its botanical name. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticultural traditions.
