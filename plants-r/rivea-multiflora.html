Rivea multiflora

Rivea multiflora, commonly known as the yellow morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Rivea multiflora is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
