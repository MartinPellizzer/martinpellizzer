Rabdosia walkeri

Rabdosia walkeri, commonly known as the mountain rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
