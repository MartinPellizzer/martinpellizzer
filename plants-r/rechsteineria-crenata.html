Rechsteineria crenata

It is characterized by its unique rosette formation and distinctive, wavy-edged leaves. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and well-drained soil. Its flowering stems bear small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.
