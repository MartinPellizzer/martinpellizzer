Rosa mollis f. papyrifrons

Rosa mollis f. papyrifrons, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, which belongs to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with arching stems and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique texture and delicate blooms make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
