Rosa chloriprovincialis

Rosa chloriprovincialis, commonly known as the Chloriprovincial rose, is a rare and endemic species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pale green foliage and small, delicate pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native habitat.
