Rosa × lheritierana

Rosa × lheritierana, commonly known as the Lheritier rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It is native to France and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

Rosa × lheritierana prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
