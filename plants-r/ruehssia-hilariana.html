Ruehssia hilariana

Ruehssia hilariana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Ruehssia hilariana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
