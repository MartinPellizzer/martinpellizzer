Roscoea purpurea f. purpurea

Roscoea purpurea f. purpurea, commonly known as the purple roscoea, is a rhizomatous herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish-purple underside. The plant produces tall, upright spikes of showy, violet-pink flowers with prominent yellow stamens. It thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its unique foliage and floral display.
