Rosa brevistyla

Rosa brevistyla, commonly known as the short-styled rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three leaflets that are serrated along the edges.

Rosa brevistyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
