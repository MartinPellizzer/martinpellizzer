Rosa mollis f. latellipes

Rosa mollis f. latellipes, commonly known as the soft rose or velvet rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely textured, dark green foliage that is covered in a soft, velvety down, giving it its common name. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Rosa mollis f. latellipes prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
