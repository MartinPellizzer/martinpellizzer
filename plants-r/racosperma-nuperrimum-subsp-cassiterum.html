Racosperma nuperrimum subsp. cassiterum

Racosperma nuperrimum subsp.

cassiterum, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for native wildlife.
