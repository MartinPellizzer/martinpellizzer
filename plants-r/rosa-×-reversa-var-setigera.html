Rosa × reversa var. setigera

Rosa × reversa var. setigera, commonly known as the climbing pink or the pink climbing rose, is a hybrid rose species that exhibits a combination of characteristics from its parent species.

It is known for its vigorous growth habit, making it suitable for trellises or arches. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink in color, with a delicate fragrance. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
