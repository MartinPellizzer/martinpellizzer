Rhododendron × impeanum

Rhododendron × impeanum, commonly known as the Impean rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
