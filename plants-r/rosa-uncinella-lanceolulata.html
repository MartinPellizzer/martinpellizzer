Rosa uncinella lanceolulata

Rosa uncinella lanceolulata, commonly known as the lanceolate prickly rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its slender, thorned stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
