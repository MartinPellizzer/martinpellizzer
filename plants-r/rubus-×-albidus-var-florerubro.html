Rubus × albidus var. florerubro

Rubus × albidus var. florerubro, commonly known as the red-flowered blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple to black when mature. Its flowers are notable for their vibrant red color, which contrasts with the dark foliage.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique fruit characteristics.
