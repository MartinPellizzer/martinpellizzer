Rosa elliptica subvar. billetii

Rosa elliptica subvar. billetii, commonly known as the Billet's elliptic rose, is a shrub-like plant native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, often hairy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a compact growth habit. Its stems are covered in fine, glandular hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
