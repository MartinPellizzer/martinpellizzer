Restiaria parviflora

Restiaria parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered restiaria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal and inland regions. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, producing tiny, pale blue or white blooms.

Restiaria parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
