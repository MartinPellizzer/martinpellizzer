Rosa permixta

Rosa permixta, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a species native to the temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically produces single or double blooms, with petals that are often velvety and layered. Rosa permixta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
