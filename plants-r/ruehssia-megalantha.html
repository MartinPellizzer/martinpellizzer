Ruehssia megalantha

Ruehssia megalantha, commonly known as the giant ruehssia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
