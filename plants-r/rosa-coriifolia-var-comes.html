Rosa coriifolia var. comes

Rosa coriifolia var. comes, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, single or semi-double pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has slender, arching stems with thorns and dark green, serrated leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.

The foliage turns red in the fall, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.
