Rotang palembanicus

Rotang palembanicus, commonly known as the Palembang ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has a straight trunk with smooth, greyish-brown bark that is often fissured. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and lanceolate, with a glossy upper surface.

The flowers are small and yellowish, typically appearing in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.
