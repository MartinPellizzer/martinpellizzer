Rosa caninella var. luridella

Rosa caninella var. luridella, commonly known as the lurid rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
