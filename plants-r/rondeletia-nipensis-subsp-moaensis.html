Rondeletia nipensis subsp. moaensis

Rondeletia nipensis subsp.

moaensis, commonly known as the Moa Mountain rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, specifically Jamaica. It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in tropical regions.
