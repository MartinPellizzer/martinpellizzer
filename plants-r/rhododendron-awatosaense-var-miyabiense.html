Rhododendron awatosaense var. miyabiense

Rhododendron awatosaense var. miyabiense, commonly known as the Miyabiense Rhododendron, is a rare and unique species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value.

This variety is valued for its horticultural appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical research due to its limited distribution.
