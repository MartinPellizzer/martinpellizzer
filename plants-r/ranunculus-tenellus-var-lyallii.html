Ranunculus tenellus var. lyallii

Ranunculus tenellus var. lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a striking splash of color to its natural habitat.
