Romulea clusiana var. stenophylla

Romulea clusiana var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf romulea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and related plants. The plant produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
