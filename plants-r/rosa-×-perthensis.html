Rosa × perthensis

Rosa × perthensis, commonly known as the Western Australian rose, is a hybrid species native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suited to arid environments.

Rosa × perthensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and horticulture.
