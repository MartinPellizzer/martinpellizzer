Rhytiglossa lucida

Rhytiglossa lucida, commonly known as the shining leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow or greenish throat.

Rhytiglossa lucida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
