Rubus delicatus

Rubus delicatus, commonly known as the delicate raspberry, is a species of bramble native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its small, edible berries that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, and they bloom in the spring.
