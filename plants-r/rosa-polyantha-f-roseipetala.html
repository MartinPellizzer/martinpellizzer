Rosa polyantha f. roseipetala

Rosa polyantha f. roseipetala, commonly known as the pink-flowered shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub characterized by its dense growth habit and abundant blooms.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a flushed pink hue on the petals. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces hips that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
