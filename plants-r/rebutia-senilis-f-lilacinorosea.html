Rebutia senilis f. lilacinorosea

Rebutia senilis f. lilacinorosea, commonly known as the Lilac Rose Rebutia, is a hybrid cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, woolly hair, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of lilac and rose, typically during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a desert garden setting.
