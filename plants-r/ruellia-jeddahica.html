Ruellia jeddahica

Ruellia jeddahica, commonly known as the Jeddah ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces clusters of tubular, blue to purple flowers that bloom from spring to summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Ruellia jeddahica is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
