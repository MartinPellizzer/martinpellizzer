Rosa × reversa expansa

Rosa × reversa expansa, commonly known as the double-flowered pink rose, is a hybrid rose species renowned for its lush, full blooms.

It features large, showy flowers with multiple layers of petals, often in shades of pink or red. The plant is deciduous, shedding its leaves in autumn, and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small gardens or containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
