Rhodamnia trinervia var. spongiosa

Rhodamnia trinervia var. spongiosa, commonly known as the sponge-leaved rhodamnia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spongy, and velvety leaves that give it a unique texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
