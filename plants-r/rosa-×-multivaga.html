Rosa × multivaga

Rosa × multivaga, commonly known as the everblooming shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse, continuous flowering throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. Its foliage is dark green and dense, providing an attractive appearance throughout the year.

Rosa × multivaga is well-suited for borders, containers, and as a foundation plant in gardens.
