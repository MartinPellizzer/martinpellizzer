Ruellia treubiana var. minor

Ruellia treubiana var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it suitable for container planting and garden borders.
