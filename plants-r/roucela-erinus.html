Roucela erinus

Roucela erinus, commonly known as the ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions due to its water-storing tissues. It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant in dry landscapes.

Roucela erinus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
