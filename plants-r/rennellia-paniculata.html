Rennellia paniculata

Rennellia paniculata, commonly known as the Rennell island hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the South Pacific islands.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Rennellia paniculata thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and a subject of interest in botanic studies.
