Rosa fontanesii

Rosa fontanesii, commonly known as the Fontanesii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

Rosa fontanesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
