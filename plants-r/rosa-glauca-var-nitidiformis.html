Rosa glauca var. nitidiformis

Rosa glauca var. nitidiformis, commonly known as the glossy blushing rose, is a compact shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range from pale pink to deep red, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce repeat blooms throughout the summer.
