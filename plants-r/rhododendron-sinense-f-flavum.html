Rhododendron sinense f. flavum

Rhododendron sinense f. flavum, commonly known as the Chinese yellow rhododendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast strikingly with its dark green, leathery leaves. Native to China, this plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. The foliage is dense and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
