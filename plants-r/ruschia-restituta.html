Ruschia restituta

Ruschia restituta, commonly known as the Cape reed, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a powdery blue or white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
