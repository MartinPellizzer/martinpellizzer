Rosa sherardii var. cinerascens

Rosa sherardii var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens rose, is a variant of the shrub rose native to China.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its delicate appearance and fragrant blooms make it a valued ornamental plant.
