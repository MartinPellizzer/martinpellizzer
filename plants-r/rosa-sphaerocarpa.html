Rosa sphaerocarpa

Rosa sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with serrated edges and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Rosa sphaerocarpa is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its unique spherical fruit, which resembles a small ball, is another distinguishing botanical feature.
