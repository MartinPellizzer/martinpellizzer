Rosa × dumetorum f. platyphylla

Rosa × dumetorum f. platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf dog rose, is a horticultural form of the dog rose.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil.
