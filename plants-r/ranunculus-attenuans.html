Ranunculus attenuans

Ranunculus attenuans, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, yellow flowers with five to ten petals, typically appearing in early spring. The plant has a short, stout stem with trifoliate leaves that are palmately arranged. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
