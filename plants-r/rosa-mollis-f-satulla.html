Rosa mollis f. satulla

Rosa mollis f. satulla, commonly known as the satulla form of the soft rose, is a cultivar characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and delicate pink flowers.

It is a variation of Rosa mollis, a species native to the Mediterranean region. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are usually borne singly or in small clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for borders or container gardening.
