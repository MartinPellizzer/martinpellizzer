Ranunculus eustrigulosus

Ranunculus eustrigulosus, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a basal rosette of simple, alternate, and usually hairy leaves. Its flowers are yellow with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family.

It produces small, green, apple-shaped fruits that contain several seeds.
