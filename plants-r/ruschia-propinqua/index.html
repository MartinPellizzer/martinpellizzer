Ruschia Propinqua

Ruschia propinqua is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its succulent characteristics and vibrant blooms. This species is often found in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in arid environments. Its delicate pink to red flowers appear in spring, adding a splash of color to rocky landscapes. Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
