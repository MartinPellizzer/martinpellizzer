Rosa mollis f. contrahens

Rosa mollis f. contrahens, commonly known as the compact rose, is a compact, shrub-like form of the species Rosa mollis.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has dense, thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. It is native to regions of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small garden spaces and container growing.
