Rothmannia spathicalyx

Rothmannia spathicalyx, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are often bell-shaped and arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Rothmannia spathicalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
