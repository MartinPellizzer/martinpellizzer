Rosa sicula f. pedunculata

Rosa sicula f. pedunculata, commonly known as the Sicilian pink or Sicilian rose, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sicily.

It is a cultivar of Rosa sicula, characterized by its distinctive pink flowers and trailing growth habit. The plant typically produces fragrant, single or semi-double blooms in shades of pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
