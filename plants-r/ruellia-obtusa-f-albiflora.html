Ruellia obtusa f. albiflora

Ruellia obtusa f. albiflora, commonly known as the Whiteflower Ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, showy white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
