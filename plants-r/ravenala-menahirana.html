Ravenala menahirana

Ravenala menahirana, commonly known as the "Ravenala tree" or "Palm of the Forest," is a unique plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by bright red fruits containing edible seeds.

Its striking appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
