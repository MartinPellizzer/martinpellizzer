Rosa regelii

Rosa regelii, commonly known as the Regel's rose, is a species native to the high altitudes of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and is characterized by its thorny stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Rosa regelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
