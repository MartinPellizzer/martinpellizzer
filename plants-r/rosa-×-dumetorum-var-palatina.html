Rosa × dumetorum var. palatina

Rosa × dumetorum var. palatina, commonly known as the Palatinate rose, is a hybrid shrub native to central Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hedging and ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand colder climates, contributing to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
