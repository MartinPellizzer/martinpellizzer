Rudbeckia serotina f. viridiflora

Rudbeckia serotina f. viridiflora, commonly known as the greenflower latecone sunflower, is a horticultural form of the latecone coneflower.

It is characterized by its distinctive greenish-yellow flower heads, which contrast with the typical orange or yellow hues of the species. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in rough, hairy foliage. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, producing showy, composite inflorescences with yellow ray florets and a central disk.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
