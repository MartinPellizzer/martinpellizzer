Rytigynia concolor

Rytigynia concolor, commonly known as the smoothleaf rytigynia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rytigyniaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its smooth, elliptical leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks.

Rytigynia concolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
