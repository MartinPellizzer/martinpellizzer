Rubus yunanicus

Rubus yunanicus, commonly known as the Yunan raspberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are palmately compound, with serrated margins, and its flowers are white with five petals.

The fruit, which resembles a raspberry, is edible and has a tart flavor.
