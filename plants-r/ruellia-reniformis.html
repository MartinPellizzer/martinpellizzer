Ruellia reniformis

Ruellia reniformis, commonly known as the broad-leaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.

Ruellia reniformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
