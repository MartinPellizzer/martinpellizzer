Rapanea acrantha

Rapanea acrantha, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Rapanea acrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
