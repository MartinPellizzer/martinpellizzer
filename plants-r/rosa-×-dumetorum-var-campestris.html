Rosa × dumetorum var. campestris

Rosa × dumetorum var. campestris, commonly known as the field raspberry, is a hybrid shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.
