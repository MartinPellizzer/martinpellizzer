Rechsteineria aggregata f. tomentosa

Rechsteineria aggregata f. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly clustered rechsteineria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves covered in a fine, white tomentum, giving it a woolly appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
