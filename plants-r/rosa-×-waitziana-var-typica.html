Rosa × waitziana var. typica

Rosa × waitziana var. typica, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its evergreen shrub form and glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.

The variety is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and adaptability.
