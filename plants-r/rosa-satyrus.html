Rosa satyrus

Rosa satyrus, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rosa satyrus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
