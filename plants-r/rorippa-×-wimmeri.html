Rorippa × wimmeri

Rorippa × wimmeri, commonly known as the hybrid watercress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rorippa palustris and Rorippa islandica. This plant typically grows in moist or aquatic environments, such as ponds and wetlands. It has alternate, glossy, ovate leaves that are often submerged or floating.

The small white flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early summer.
