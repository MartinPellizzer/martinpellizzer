Romeroa

Romeroa, commonly known as silverweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

These plants are typically found in temperate regions and are characterized by their silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. Romeroa species are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive appearance and hardy nature.
