Reseda aucheri f. oblongifolia

Reseda aucheri f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed reseda, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to cultivated landscapes.
