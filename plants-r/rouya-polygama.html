Rouya polygama

Rouya polygama, commonly known as the many-flowered rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and pinnate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Rouya polygama is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
