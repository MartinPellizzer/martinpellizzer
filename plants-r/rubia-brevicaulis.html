Rubia brevicaulis

Rubia brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
