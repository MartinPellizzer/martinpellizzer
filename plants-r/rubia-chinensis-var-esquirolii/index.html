Rubia Chinensis Var. Esquirolii

Chinese rubia, scientifically known as Rubia chinensis var. esquirolii, is a rare and striking member of the Rubiaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this plant is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late spring. Its unique coloration and delicate structure make it a sought-after species among botanists and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, Chinese rubia is relatively obscure and faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure for future generations.
