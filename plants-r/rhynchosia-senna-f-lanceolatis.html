Rhynchosia senna f. lanceolatis

Rhynchosia senna f. lanceolatis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its lanceolate leaves, which are elongated and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typical of many legumes. It is native to tropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
