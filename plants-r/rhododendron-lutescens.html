Rhododendron lutescens

Rhododendron lutescens, commonly known as the yellow rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade.

Rhododendron lutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
