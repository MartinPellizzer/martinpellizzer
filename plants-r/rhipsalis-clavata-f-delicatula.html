Rhipsalis clavata f. delicatula

Rhipsalis clavata f. delicatula, commonly known as the delicate rhipsalis, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems covered in small, white spines. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
