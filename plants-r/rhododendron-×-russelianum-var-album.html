Rhododendron × russelianum var. album

Rhododendron × russelianum var. album, commonly known as the White Rhododendron, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and elliptical in shape.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
