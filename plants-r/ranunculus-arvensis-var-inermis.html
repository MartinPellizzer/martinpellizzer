Ranunculus arvensis var. inermis

Ranunculus arvensis var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth meadow buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist meadows, riverbanks, and other wetland habitats. The plant features palmately divided leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. It produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous small seeds.

Due to its delicate appearance and ecological significance, it is often found in conservation-focused habitats.
