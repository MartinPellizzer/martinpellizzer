Ruellia multisetosa

Ruellia multisetosa, commonly known as the hairy ruellia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Ruellia multisetosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
