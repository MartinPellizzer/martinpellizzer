Rosa bourgeauiana

Rosa bourgeauiana, commonly known as the Bourgeau rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Rosa bourgeauiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
