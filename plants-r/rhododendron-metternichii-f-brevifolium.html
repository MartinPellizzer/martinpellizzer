Rhododendron metternichii f. brevifolium

Rhododendron metternichii f. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved metternich rhododendron, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and clusters of deep pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings. Its foliage remains dense and attractive throughout the growing season, providing year-round visual interest.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
