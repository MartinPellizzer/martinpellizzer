Rudbeckia grandiflora var. grandiflora

Rudbeckia grandiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the bigleaf coneflower, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It features large, showy yellow petals surrounding a central disk of brown, cone-like flowers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically green and hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Rudbeckia grandiflora var. grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
