Roldana

Roldana, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Roldana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
