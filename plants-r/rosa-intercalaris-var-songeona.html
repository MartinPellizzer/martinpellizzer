Rosa intercalaris var. songeona

Rosa intercalaris var. songeona, commonly known as the Songeona rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its unique botanical features.

It is a climbing shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves that have serrated edges. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive double bloom structure. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
