Racosperma dissonum

Racosperma dissonum, commonly known as the sicklepod wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive sickle-shaped pods. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
