Rhododendron brookeanum var. extraneum

Rhododendron brookeanum var. extraneum, commonly known as the Philippine rhododendron, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom in spring. It thrives in humid, acidic soils and requires partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
