Rhaponticoides eriosiphon

Rhaponticoides eriosiphon, commonly known as the velvetleaf or woolly thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is native to regions of Europe and Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its foliage is covered in fine, white to grayish hairs, which give it a distinctive, velvety appearance.
