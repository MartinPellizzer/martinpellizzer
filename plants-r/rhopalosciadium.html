Rhopalosciadium

Rhopalosciadium, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its silver-gray, hairy leaves and small, violet flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is native to southern Africa and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
