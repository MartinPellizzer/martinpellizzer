Rosa × dumetorum suboxyphylla

Rosa × dumetorum suboxyphylla, commonly known as the alpine rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
