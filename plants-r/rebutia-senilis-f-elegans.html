Rebutia senilis f. elegans

Rebutia senilis f. elegans, commonly known as the silver moon cactus, is a fascinating succulent with unique botanical features.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its swollen, ribbed stems that store water. The plant typically has a short, thick caudex with a crown of small, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the axils of the leaves.

This variety is prized for its compact size and striking appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
