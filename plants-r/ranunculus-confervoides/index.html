Ranunculus Confervoides

Ranunculus confervoides is a rare and delicate aquatic plant known for its small, bright yellow flowers. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and shallow lakes. This species is characterized by its finely divided leaves and unique floral structure that resembles a cluster of tiny cups. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful conservation efforts. Its presence is an indicator of clean, unpolluted water in its habitat.
