Rubus × semiapiculatus

Rubus × semiapiculatus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that belongs to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, drupe-like berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruits.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
