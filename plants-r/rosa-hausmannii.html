Rosa hausmannii

Rosa hausmannii, commonly known as the Hausmann's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces showy pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a woody follicle containing several seeds, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Rosa hausmannii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
