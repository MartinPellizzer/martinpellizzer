Rinorea comorensis

Rinorea comorensis, commonly known as the Comoros rinorea, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale pink in color.

Rinorea comorensis is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
