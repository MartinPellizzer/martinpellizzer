Rosa agrestis var. vaccarii

Rosa agrestis var. vaccarii, commonly known as the Vaccarii wild rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically pink to red, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant produces hip fruits that are red and contain achenes, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
