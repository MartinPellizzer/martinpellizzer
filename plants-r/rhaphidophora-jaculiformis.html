Rhaphidophora jaculiformis

Rhaphidophora jaculiformis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shady environments with high air humidity. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
