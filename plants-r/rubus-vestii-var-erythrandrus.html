Rubus vestii var. erythrandrus

Rubus vestii var. erythrandrus, commonly known as the red-stemmed bramble, is a variant of the bramble genus Rubus.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural practices.
