Ryania speciosa var. subuliflora

Ryania speciosa var. subuliflora, commonly known as the needleleaf ryania, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces woody, spindle-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
