Rosa demetrii

Rosa demetrii, commonly known as the Demetrius rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, fragrant pink or red flowers. The plant produces oval, green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Rosa demetrii is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
