Rosa obtusifolia var. borreri

Rosa obtusifolia var. borreri, commonly known as the Borri rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with five petals and a strong, sweet fragrance. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
