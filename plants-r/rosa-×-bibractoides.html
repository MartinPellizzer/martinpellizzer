Rosa × bibractoides

Rosa × bibractoides, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species that combines traits from various Rosa species.

It is characterized by its continuous blooming cycle, producing flowers throughout the growing season. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Rosa × bibractoides is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
