Rubus laciniatus var. elegans

Rubus laciniatus var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant blackberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its finely lobed, dark green leaves and thorny stems. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that ripen to a deep black color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruits.
