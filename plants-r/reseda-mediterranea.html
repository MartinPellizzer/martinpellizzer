Reseda mediterranea

Reseda mediterranea, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features striking blue or purple ray flowers surrounded by spiny, leaf-like bracts. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Reseda mediterranea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically in dye production.
