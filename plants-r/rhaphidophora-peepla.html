Rhaphidophora peepla

Rhaphidophora peepla, commonly known as the black-veined arrowroot.

It is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with dark green veins, giving it its distinctive appearance. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental and edible plants.
