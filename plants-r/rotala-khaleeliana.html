Rotala khaleeliana

Rotala khaleeliana, commonly known as the Khaleel's water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright green, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically submerged or floating, depending on the water conditions. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in ponds and slow-moving streams.

Rotala khaleeliana is valued in aquatic gardening for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
