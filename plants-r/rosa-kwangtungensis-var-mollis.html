Rosa kwangtungensis var. mollis

Rosa kwangtungensis var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-hairy rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and hairy foliage, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant.
