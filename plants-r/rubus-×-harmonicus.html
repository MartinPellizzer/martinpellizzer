Rubus × harmonicus

Rubus × harmonicus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces edible berries, combining traits from both blackberries and raspberries. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces white to pale pink flowers in the spring. Its fruit is typically a mix of the size and flavor characteristics of its parent species.

It is often cultivated for its versatility in culinary uses and ornamental value.
