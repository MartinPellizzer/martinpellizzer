Rosa perusiana

Rosa perusiana, commonly known as the Peruvian rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa perusiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for both its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
