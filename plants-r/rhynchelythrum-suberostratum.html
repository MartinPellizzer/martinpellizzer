Rhynchelythrum suberostratum

Rhynchelythrum suberostratum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle.

This plant is a flowering species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny foliage and composite flower heads. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The leaves are narrow and often have a rough, hairy texture.

Its flowers are usually purple or pink, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
