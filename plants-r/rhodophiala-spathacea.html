Rhodophiala spathacea

Rhodophiala spathacea, commonly known as the flame violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy bracts that resemble petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhodophiala spathacea is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
