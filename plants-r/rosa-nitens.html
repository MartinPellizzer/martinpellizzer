Rosa nitens

Rosa nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Rosa nitens is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
