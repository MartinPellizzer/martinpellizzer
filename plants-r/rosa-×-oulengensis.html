Rosa × oulengensis

Rosa × oulengensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Rosa × oulengensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
