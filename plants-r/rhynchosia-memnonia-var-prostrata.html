Rhynchosia memnonia var. prostrata

Rhynchosia memnonia var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with stipules and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with stems that lie close to the ground. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural systems.

The seeds are edible and can be used in various culinary applications.
