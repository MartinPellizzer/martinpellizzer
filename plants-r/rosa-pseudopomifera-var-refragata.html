Rosa pseudopomifera var. refragata

Rosa pseudopomifera var. refragata, commonly known as the fragrant rose, is a rare and unique variant of the pseudopomifera rose species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fragrant flowers that emit a sweet, heady aroma. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil with partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with thorny stems typical of many rose varieties.

This botanical curiosity is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticulture.
