Rhodanthemum redieri var. sericeum

Rhodanthemum redieri var. sericeum, commonly known as the silky rhodanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The foliage is typically velvety and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

It is valued for its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
