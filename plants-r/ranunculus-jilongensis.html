Ranunculus jilongensis

Ranunculus jilongensis, commonly known as the Jilongensis buttercup, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves and produces small, cup-shaped flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
