Roupellia grata

Roupellia grata, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Roupellia grata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm climates.
