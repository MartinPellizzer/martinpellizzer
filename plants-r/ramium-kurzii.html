Ramium kurzii

Ramium kurzii, commonly known as the narrowleaf bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Ramium kurzii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
