Rubus pyramidalis microgene dentulifer

Rubus pyramidalis microgene dentulifer, commonly known as the small-fruited raspberry.

This plant is a species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
