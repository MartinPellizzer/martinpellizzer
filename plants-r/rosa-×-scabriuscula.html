Rosa × scabriuscula

Rosa × scabriuscula, commonly known as the roughleaf shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its rugged, thorny stems and small, oval leaves.

It produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the western United States and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, drought-tolerant shrub.
