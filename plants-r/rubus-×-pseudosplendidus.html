Rubus × pseudosplendidus

Rubus × pseudosplendidus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color, depending on the variety. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is valued for its hardiness and the flavor of its berries, which are often used in culinary applications.
