Rhynchosia venulosa

Rhynchosia venulosa, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces distinctive yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has compound leaves and develops pods that contain numerous seeds embedded in a hairy, spiny capsule. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.

Rhynchosia venulosa is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it an important plant in soil enrichment.
