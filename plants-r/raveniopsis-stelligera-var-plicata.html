Raveniopsis stelligera var. plicata

Raveniopsis stelligera var. plicata, commonly known as the zigzag papyrus, is a unique aquatic plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, pleated leaves that give it a distinctive zigzag appearance when viewed from above. The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments with soft, nutrient-rich substrates. Its leaves are typically dark green and can grow up to several feet in length, often floating on the water's surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems.
