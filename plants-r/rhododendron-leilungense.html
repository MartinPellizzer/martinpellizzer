Rhododendron leilungense

Rhododendron leilungense, commonly known as the Leilung rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron leilungense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
