Ribes × carrierei

Ribes × carrierei, commonly known as the hybrid currant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Ribes, which includes various species of gooseberries and currants. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and produces clusters of small, edible berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have serrated edges.

The flowers are usually pink or white, and the plant is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
