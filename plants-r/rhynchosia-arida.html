Rhynchosia arida

Rhynchosia arida, commonly known as the desert cowpea, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant is adapted to survive in harsh environments with its deep root system and succulent leaves. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rhynchosia arida is also valued for its edible seeds and has been used in traditional agriculture and medicine.
