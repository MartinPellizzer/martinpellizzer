Rubus hylonomus

Rubus hylonomus, commonly known as the Hylonomus raspberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it has thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
