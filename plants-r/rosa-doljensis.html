Rosa doljensis

Rosa doljensis, commonly known as the Doljana rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans, particularly in Romania and Serbia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, pinnate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer, which are followed by oval, red-orange hips.

Rosa doljensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
