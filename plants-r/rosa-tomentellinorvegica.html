Rosa tomentellinorvegica

Rosa tomentellinorvegica, commonly known as the Norwegian woolly rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly gray foliage and fragrant pink to red flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Scandinavia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
