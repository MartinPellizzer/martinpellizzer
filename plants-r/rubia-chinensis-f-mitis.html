Rubia chinensis f. mitis

Rubia chinensis f. mitis, commonly known as the Chinese mignonette or sweet violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, violet-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features slender stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
