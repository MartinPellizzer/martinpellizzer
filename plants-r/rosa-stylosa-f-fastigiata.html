Rosa stylosa f. fastigiata

Rosa stylosa f. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright form of the stylosa rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant with a dense, upright growth habit.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The foliage is dark green and glossy, with thorny stems that provide structural support. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It requires full sun to partial shade and regular pruning to maintain its shape and encourage flowering.
