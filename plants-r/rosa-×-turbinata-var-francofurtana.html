Rosa × turbinata var. francofurtana

Rosa × turbinata var. francofurtana, commonly known as the Frankfurt rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features deep pink, semi-double flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thorny stems and dark green, trifoliate leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
