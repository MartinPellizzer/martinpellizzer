Rosa × blomii

Rosa × blomii, commonly known as the Blomfield rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa bracteata and Rosa rugosa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a fragrant scent. The plant is deciduous, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Rosa × blomii is also valued for its hardiness and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season.
