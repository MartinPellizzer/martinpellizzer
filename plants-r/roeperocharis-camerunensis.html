Roeperocharis camerunensis

Roeperocharis camerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroon orchid, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils, while its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen.

Roeperocharis camerunensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
