Rottboellia compressa subvar. capensis

Rottboellia compressa subvar. capensis, commonly known as African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
