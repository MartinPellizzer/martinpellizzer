Rumex stenophyllus f. sachalinensis

Rumex stenophyllus f. sachalinensis, commonly known as the Sachalin dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Korean Peninsula and parts of eastern Russia.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with a waxy coating and prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Polygonaceae family.
