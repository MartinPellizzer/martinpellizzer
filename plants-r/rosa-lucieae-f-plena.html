Rosa lucieae f. plena

Rosa lucieae f. plena, commonly known as the full-blown form of the Lucie rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa lucieae species.

It is characterized by its abundant, double flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
