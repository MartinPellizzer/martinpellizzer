Restiaria hermanniifolia

Restiaria hermanniifolia, commonly known as the Hermann's restiaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
