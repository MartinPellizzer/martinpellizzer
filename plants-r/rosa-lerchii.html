Rosa lerchii

Rosa lerchii, commonly known as the Lerch's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated edges, and the plant has a woody stem covered in thorns.

Rosa lerchii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
