Rosa stylosa f. microphylloides

Rosa stylosa f. microphylloides, commonly known as the miniature leaf rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to China.

It is a naturally occurring form of Rosa stylosa, characterized by its smaller, more delicate leaves compared to the typical variety. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a prized addition to many botanical collections.
