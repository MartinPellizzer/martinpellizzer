Rompelia roseana

Rompelia roseana, commonly known as the rosean rompelia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Rompelia roseana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
