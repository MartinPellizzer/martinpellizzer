Rubia ruwenzoriensis

Rubia ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori balm, is a flowering plant native to the mountains of East Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rubia ruwenzoriensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional remedies.
