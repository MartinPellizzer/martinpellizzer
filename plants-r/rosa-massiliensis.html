Rosa massiliensis

Rosa massiliensis, commonly known as the Massilian rose, is a species of flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. Rosa massiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its historical use in perfumery and traditional medicine.
