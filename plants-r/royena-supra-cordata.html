Royena supra-cordata

Royena supra-cordata, commonly known as the broad-leaved royal fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping underground stems that help it spread. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, giving it a feathery appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
