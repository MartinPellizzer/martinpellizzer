Rosa × noisettiana f. manettii

Rosa × noisettiana f. manettii, commonly known as the Manettii rose, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits a unique combination of characteristics from its parent species.

It is known for its vigorous growth and ability to produce abundant, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically has thorny stems and dark green, glossy foliage that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for hedging or as a specimen plant due to its dense growth habit.

Botanically, it is classified as a cultivar within the Rosa genus, showcasing the diversity found in cultivated roses.
