Rubus pallidus f. pusillus

Rubus pallidus f. pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf raspberry.

It is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features small, pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a hairy surface, and the fruit is a small, tart berry.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
