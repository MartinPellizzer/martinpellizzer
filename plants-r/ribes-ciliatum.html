Ribes ciliatum

Ribes ciliatum, commonly known as the smoothcurrant, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by edible, red berries that ripen in late summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges and a soft, fuzzy texture.

Ribes ciliatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and wildlife.
