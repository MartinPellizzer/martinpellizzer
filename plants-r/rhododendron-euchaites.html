Rhododendron euchaites

Rhododendron euchaites, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia, including India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in humid and cool climates. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron euchaites is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a significant role in the ecosystems of its native habitats.
