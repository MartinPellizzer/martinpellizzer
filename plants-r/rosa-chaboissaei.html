Rosa chaboissaei

Rosa chaboissaei, commonly known as the Chaboissai rose, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink or red, with a pleasant fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Rosa chaboissaei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
