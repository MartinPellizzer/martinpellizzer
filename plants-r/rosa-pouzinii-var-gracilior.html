Rosa pouzinii var. gracilior

Rosa pouzinii var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender pink rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features narrow, arching stems with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
