Rosa spinosissima var. aculeatissima

Rosa spinosissima var. aculeatissima, commonly known as the prickly rose, is a variant of the spinosissima rose species.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for hedging or as a decorative plant.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species, often used in ornamental gardening.
