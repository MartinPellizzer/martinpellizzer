Rosa sphaeroidea

Rosa sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globe rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its spherical, tightly packed flower heads, which give it its common name. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are often hairy and serrated.

Rosa sphaeroidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
