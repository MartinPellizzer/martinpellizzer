Rebutia elegantula

Rebutia elegantula, commonly known as the Elegant Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It is a low-growing plant with a compact, globular stem that is often covered in dense, woolly hair. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Rebutia elegantula is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
