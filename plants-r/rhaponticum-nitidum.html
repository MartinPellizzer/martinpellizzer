Rhaponticum nitidum

Rhaponticum nitidum, commonly known as the slender rhapontic, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habit. The plant features narrow, upright stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
