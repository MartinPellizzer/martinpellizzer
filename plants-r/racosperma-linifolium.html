Racosperma linifolium

Racosperma linifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Racosperma linifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
