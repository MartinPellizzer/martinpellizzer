Rosa polyantha var. adenochaeta

Rosa polyantha var. adenochaeta, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces pink to white petals that are often double, giving it a showy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and continuous flowering make it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and container plantings.
