Rosa agrestis var. milenae

Rosa agrestis var. milenae, commonly known as the Milena wild rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. Its leaves are dark green, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Rosa agrestis var. milenae is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
