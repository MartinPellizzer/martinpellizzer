Rosa atropurpurascens

Rosa atropurpurascens, commonly known as the dark purple rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its deep purple to almost black flowers, which bloom in summer. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

Rosa atropurpurascens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
