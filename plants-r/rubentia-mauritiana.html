Rubentia mauritiana

Rubentia mauritiana, commonly known as the red-flowered Mauritius orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking red petals and intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the island's mountainous regions. Its unique coloration and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and requires careful preservation efforts.
