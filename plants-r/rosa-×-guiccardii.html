Rosa × guiccardii

Rosa × guiccardii, commonly known as the Guiccardini rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub, typically reaching heights of 1 to 3 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa × guiccardii is native to the Mediterranean region and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
