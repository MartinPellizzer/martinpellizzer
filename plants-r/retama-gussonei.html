Retama gussonei

Retama gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing drought tolerance and efficient water use. Its seed pods contain small, hard seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across the island's landscapes.

Retama gussonei plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
