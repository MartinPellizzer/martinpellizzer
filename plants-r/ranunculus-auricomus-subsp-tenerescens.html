Ranunculus auricomus subsp. tenerescens

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

tenerescens, commonly known as the softleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and bright yellow flowers with prominent, showy sepals.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its subspecies distinction is characterized by its softer, more succulent foliage compared to the typical form.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
