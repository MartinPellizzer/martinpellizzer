Rosa continens

Rosa continens, commonly known as the European rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Europe and western Asia and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers bloom in summer and are often used in perfumery and culinary applications.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and landscaping.
