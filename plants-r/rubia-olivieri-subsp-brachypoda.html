Rubia olivieri subsp. brachypoda

Rubia olivieri subsp.

brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of shrubs and herbs.

The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

This subspecies is often found in rocky or dry habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
