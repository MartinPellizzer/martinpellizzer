Rosa thuilleri

Rosa thuilleri, commonly known as the Thuillier rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and serrated, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Rosa thuilleri is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
