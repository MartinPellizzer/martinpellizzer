Rhododendron × bakeri

Rhododendron × bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high elevations. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clustered inflorescences during the spring.

Rhododendron × bakeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with acidic soil and partial shade.
