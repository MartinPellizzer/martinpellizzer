Rubus mallotifolius

Rubus mallotifolius, commonly known as the Chinese blackberry or red-black raspberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or black when mature. Its botanical features include a woody structure, hairy foliage, and a preference for moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
