Rosa × stylosoformis

Rosa × stylosoformis, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers.

It is a hybrid between Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia, resulting in a plant with a robust growth habit and thorny stems. The leaves are alternate, simple, and serrated, with a deep green color. It produces large, single or double blooms in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white.

This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a favored choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
