Ruellia kerrii

Ruellia kerrii, commonly known as Kerri's ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom from summer to fall. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ruellia kerrii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
