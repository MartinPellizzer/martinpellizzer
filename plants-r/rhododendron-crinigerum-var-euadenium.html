Rhododendron crinigerum var. euadenium

Rhododendron crinigerum var. euadenium, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its vibrant floral display.
