Ricinocarpus longistipularis

It is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its long, slender stipes and large, palmate leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in catkins, and it produces a toxic, milky sap that contains ricin.
