Rosa indica var. minima

Rosa indica var. minima, commonly known as the miniature tea rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces hips that are edible and ornamental. It is well-suited for container gardening and small garden spaces due to its compact size.

Rosa indica var. minima requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
