Ronnbergia drakeana

Ronnbergia drakeana, commonly known as the Drake's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, spider-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or high-altitude grasslands. Its leaves are long and grass-like, and the flowers are borne on a single, elongated inflorescence.

Ronnbergia drakeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
