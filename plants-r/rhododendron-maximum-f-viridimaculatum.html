Rhododendron maximum f. viridimaculatum

Rhododendron maximum f. viridimaculatum, commonly known as the Variegated Great Rhododendron, is a unique variant of the Great Rhododendron.

It features striking variegated leaves with a greenish-white mottling, which sets it apart from the typical solid green foliage. The plant is a broadleaf evergreen shrub that thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to red during the spring.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in shaded garden settings.
