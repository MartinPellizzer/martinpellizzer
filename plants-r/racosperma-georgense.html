Racosperma georgense

Racosperma georgense, commonly known as the Georgian raceme tree, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Georgia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. Racosperma georgense is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soils.
