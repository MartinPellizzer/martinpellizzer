Rebis gayana

Rebis gayana, commonly known as the red-eyed wonder, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and yellow stamens, which create a striking visual contrast. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rebis gayana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
