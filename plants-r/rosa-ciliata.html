Rosa ciliata

Rosa ciliata, commonly known as the silky rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silvery-white hairs on its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Rosa ciliata is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in hedges or as a decorative plant.
