Rumex microdon

Rumex microdon, commonly known as the small heartwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Rumex microdon is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
