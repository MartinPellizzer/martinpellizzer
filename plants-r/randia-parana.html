Randia parana

Randia parana, commonly known as parana rubber vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous small seeds.

Randia parana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
