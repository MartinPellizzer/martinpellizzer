Rosa cinnamomea f. microphylla

Rosa cinnamomea f. microphylla, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the cinnamon rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a velvety texture. The plant produces fragrant, single or double pink flowers with a subtle cinnamon scent. It is well-suited for container gardening and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
