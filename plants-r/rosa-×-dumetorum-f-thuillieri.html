Rosa × dumetorum f. thuillieri

Rosa × dumetorum f. thuillieri, commonly known as the alpine rose or thujifolia rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, often forming low shrubs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is well adapted to cold climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and alpine settings.
