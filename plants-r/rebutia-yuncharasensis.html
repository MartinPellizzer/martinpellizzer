Rebutia yuncharasensis

Rebutia yuncharasensis, commonly known as the Yunchara Rebutia, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is a low-growing plant with thick, ribbed stems that store water, typical of desert environments. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
