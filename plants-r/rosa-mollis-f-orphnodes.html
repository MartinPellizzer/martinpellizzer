Rosa mollis f. orphnodes

Rosa mollis f. orphnodes, commonly known as the soft rose with variegated foliage, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. The plant features a compact growth habit with hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. Its variegated foliage, often with white or cream markings, adds ornamental value to gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
