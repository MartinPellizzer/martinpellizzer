Rubus × semiamplifrons

Rubus × semiamplifrons, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces red, aggregate fruits that are typically used in culinary applications. The plant is native to North America and thrives in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both agriculture and ecological systems.
