Rosa rubrifolia var. livida

Rosa rubrifolia var. livida, commonly known as the dark-leaved redleaf rose, is a variant of the redleaf rose species characterized by its deep, almost black foliage.

This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is known for its vibrant, deep pink to crimson flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are dense and have a glossy texture, contributing to its ornamental value. It is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, requiring well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its unique foliage and showy blooms, it is often cultivated in gardens for both its aesthetic appeal and fragrance.
