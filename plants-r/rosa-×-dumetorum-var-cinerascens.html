Rosa × dumetorum var. cinerascens

Rosa × dumetorum var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a serrated margin and a hairy texture. This variety is often found in rocky or disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
