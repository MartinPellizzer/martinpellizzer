Rosa gallica var. agatha

Rosa gallica var. agatha, commonly known as the Agatha rose, is a historic variety of the Gallica rose.

It is characterized by its deep red, semi-double flowers and dark green, glossy foliage. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.

The Agatha rose is also known for its strong, sweet fragrance and is often used in perfumery.
