Rubus runssorensis f. umbrosus

Rubus runssorensis f. umbrosus, commonly known as the shadowy raspberry.

This plant is a flowering shrub native to specific regions in South America. It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, aggregate fruits. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
