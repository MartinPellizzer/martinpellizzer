Rosa × andegavensis var. dollineriana

Rosa × andegavensis var. dollineriana, commonly known as the Dollineriana rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse blooming of semi-double to double pink flowers. The plant typically features dark green, serrated leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
