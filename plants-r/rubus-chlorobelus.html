Rubus chlorobelus

Rubus chlorobelus, commonly known as the greenleaf raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to parts of eastern Asia, characterized by its distinctive greenish foliage. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically less tart than those of other raspberry species. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Rubus chlorobelus is often found in woodland areas and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
