Rubus adenothyrsus

Rubus adenothyrsus, commonly known as the alpine raspberry, is a species of bramble native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Botanically, it is notable for its ability to adapt to harsh alpine climates and its role in local ecosystems.
