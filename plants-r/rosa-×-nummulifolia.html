Rosa × nummulifolia

Rosa × nummulifolia, commonly known as the Nummulite Rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven elliptical leaflets with a glossy surface. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hedges and borders.
