Rupicapnos platycentra

Rupicapnos platycentra, commonly known as the flat-centre rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a drought-resistant structure.

Rupicapnos platycentra is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
