Rondeletia langlassei

Rondeletia langlassei, commonly known as the pink rondeletia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, and is well adapted to humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, moist climates.
