Rhodophiala rosea

Rhodophiala rosea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink or purple floral spikes. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the warmer months.

Rhodophiala rosea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
