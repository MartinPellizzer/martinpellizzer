Rosa cinnamomea var. plena

Rosa cinnamomea var. plena, commonly known as the Chinese double rose, is a variety of the Chinese rose that features double flowers with a rich, velvety texture.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage and produces rose-scented flowers that are highly valued in horticulture. It is a vigorous shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, with thorny stems and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

This variety is also noted for its ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
