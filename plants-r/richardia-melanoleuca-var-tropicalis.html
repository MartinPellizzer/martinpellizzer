Richardia melanoleuca var. tropicalis

Richardia melanoleuca var. tropicalis, commonly known as the tropical richardia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open forests.
