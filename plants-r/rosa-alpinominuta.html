Rosa alpinominuta

Rosa alpinominuta, commonly known as the alpine dwarf rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Alps.

It is a compact shrub with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and produces rose hips that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suited for alpine gardens.

Due to its delicate appearance and unique habitat, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
