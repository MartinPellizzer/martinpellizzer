Rosa urbica f. eulanceolata

Rosa urbica f. eulanceolata, commonly known as the urban rose, is a hybrid form of the common dog rose.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers with a slightly fragrant scent. The plant typically grows in urban environments, such as along sidewalks and in gardens, due to its adaptability to disturbed soils. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, while the stems are thorny.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging urban conditions.
