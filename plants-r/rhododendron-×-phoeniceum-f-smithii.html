Rhododendron × phoeniceum f. smithii

Rhododendron × phoeniceum f. smithii, commonly known as the Smith's flame azalea, is a hybrid evergreen shrub characterized by its striking, showy flowers.

It features dense, leathery foliage that provides a lush backdrop to its vibrant, orange-red blooms. The plant is native to the eastern United States, particularly in areas with acidic, well-drained soil. It thrives in partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural displays and ecological restoration projects.
