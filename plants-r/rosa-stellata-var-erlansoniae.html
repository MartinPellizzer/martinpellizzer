Rosa stellata var. erlansoniae

Rosa stellata var. erlansoniae, commonly known as the Erlandsong rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink, semi-double flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage and is known for its strong fragrance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
