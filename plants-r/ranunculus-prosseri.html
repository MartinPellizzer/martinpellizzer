Ranunculus prosseri

Ranunculus prosseri, commonly known as the Pseudostellaria ranunculus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, palmate leaves and showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive, hairy center. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. It is part of the Ranunculaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
