Rubus × kalaidae

Rubus × kalaidae, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (common blackberry) and Rubus idaeus (red raspberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters, with thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to white, and the fruit is a aggregate berry that can range in color from red to black.

This hybrid is valued for its hardy nature and the flavor of its edible fruits.
