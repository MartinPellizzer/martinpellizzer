Rubus × admixtus

Rubus × admixtus, commonly known as the hybrid raspberry or loganberry, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus idaeus (red raspberry) and Rubus chinensis (Chinese blackberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces berries that range in color from red to black, depending on the variety.

This hybrid is valued for its fruit quality and is cultivated for both commercial and ornamental purposes.
