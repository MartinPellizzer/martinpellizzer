Ribes micranthum

Ribes micranthum, commonly known as the smooth currant, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, oval leaves with a smooth texture and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in early spring. Its fruit is a small, edible berry that ripens to a red color in late summer.

Ribes micranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
