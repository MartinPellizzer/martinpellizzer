Ronnbergia petersii

Ronnbergia petersii, commonly known as the Peters' spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence that bears numerous small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Ronnbergia petersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
