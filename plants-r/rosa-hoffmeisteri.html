Rosa hoffmeisteri

Rosa hoffmeisteri, commonly known as the Hoffmeister's rose, is a species of shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape.

Rosa hoffmeisteri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
