Rosa camberiensis

Rosa camberiensis, commonly known as the Camberwell rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the United Kingdom and is characterized by its fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Rosa camberiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance in horticulture.
