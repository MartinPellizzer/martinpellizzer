Rosa × stenosepala

Rosa × stenosepala, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, red-orange hips that contain seeds.

Rosa × stenosepala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
