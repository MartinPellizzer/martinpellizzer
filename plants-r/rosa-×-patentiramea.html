Rosa × patentiramea

Rosa × patentiramea, commonly known as the patentiramea rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a shrub or small tree. The plant typically produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × patentiramea is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and relatively disease-resistant nature.
