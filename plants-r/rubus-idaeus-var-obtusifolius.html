Rubus idaeus var. obtusifolius

Rubus idaeus var. obtusifolius, commonly known as the European raspberry, is a variant of the common raspberry species.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces aggregate fruits composed of small, bright red drupes. It typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
