Rosa × odorata var. gigantea

Rosa × odorata var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant sweet briar rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its large, fragrant flowers and thorny stems.

It is native to the United States and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces pink to white blooms that are highly aromatic, often used in perfumery and horticulture. Its vigorous growth habit makes it suitable for hedging or as a decorative shrub.

However, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and prevent overgrowth.
