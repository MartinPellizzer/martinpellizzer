Ribesiodes obovatum

Ribesiodes obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on top. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its berries are red or orange and are edible, though often tart.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
