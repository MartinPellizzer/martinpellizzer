Rosa × muscosa florepallida

Rosa × muscosa florepallida, commonly known as the pale-flowered muscosa rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and compound leaves with ovate to elliptical leaflets. The flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a soft, fragrant scent. This plant is native to regions of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
