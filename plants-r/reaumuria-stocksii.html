Reaumuria stocksii

Reaumuria stocksii, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a low-growing shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Reaumuria stocksii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
