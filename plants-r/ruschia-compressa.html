Ruschia compressa

Ruschia compressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
