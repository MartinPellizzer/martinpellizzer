Rosa bella f. pallens

Rosa bella f. pallens, commonly known as the pale form of the beauty rose, is a cultivar characterized by its soft, pale pink blooms.

It is a hybrid tea rose that produces large, fragrant flowers with a delicate appearance. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
