Rhododendron nitidulum var. omeiense

Rhododendron nitidulum var. omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense Rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid climates. It is often found in forested areas at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
