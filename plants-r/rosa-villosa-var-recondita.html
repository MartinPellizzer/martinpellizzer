Rosa villosa var. recondita

Rosa villosa var. recondita, commonly known as the alpine rose or the alpine rock rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are hairy and grayish-green, providing a distinctive appearance.

Rosa villosa var. recondita is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and beauty.
