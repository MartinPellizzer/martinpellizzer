Rhododendron henanense subsp. lingbaoense

Rhododendron henanense subsp.

lingbaoense, commonly known as the Lingbao rhododendron, is a rare and endemic species native to China. It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic, well-drained soils. It prefers cool, humid climates and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
