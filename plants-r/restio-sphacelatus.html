Restio sphacelatus

Restio sphacelatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family, which is characteristic of the fynbos vegetation type. The plant is a perennial herb with slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in dense clusters. Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
