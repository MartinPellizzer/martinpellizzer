Ranunculus Clariflorus

Ranunculus clariflorus is a delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms and unique leaf structure. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in meadows and rocky outcrops, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat. Its flowers open in the morning and close by midday, a behavior known as nyctinasty. Due to habitat loss, Ranunculus clariflorus is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
