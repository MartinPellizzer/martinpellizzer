Ribes neglectum

Ribes neglectum, commonly known as the redcurrant bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It produces small, edible berries that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ribes neglectum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and fruiting habit.
