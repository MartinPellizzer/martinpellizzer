Rhododendron trichostomum var. ledoides

Rhododendron trichostomum var. ledoides, commonly known as the Ledoides rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features striking, broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
