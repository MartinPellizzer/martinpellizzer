Rabdosia albopilosa

Rabdosia albopilosa, commonly known as the white-haired rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem.

Rabdosia albopilosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
