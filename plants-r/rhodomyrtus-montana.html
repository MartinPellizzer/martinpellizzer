Rhodomyrtus montana

Rhodomyrtus montana, commonly known as the mountain rosemyrtle, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically ovate and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
