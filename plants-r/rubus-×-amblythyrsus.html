Rubus × amblythyrsus

Rubus × amblythyrsus, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves, which are typical of the Rubus genus. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas or along hedgerows.

Its botanical features make it both a useful ornamental plant and a valuable food source for wildlife.
