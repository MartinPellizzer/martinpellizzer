Ruellia ovata

Ruellia ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, depending on the species. Ruellia ovata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.
