Rubus × vestitifactus

Rubus × vestitifactus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark red to black in color. The plant has thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is valued for its culinary uses and ornamental appeal in gardens.
