Rhynchospora confinis

Rhynchospora confinis, commonly known as the narrowleaf beakspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Rhynchospora confinis thrives in wetland habitats, including marshes and swamps, where it plays a role in maintaining ecological balance.
