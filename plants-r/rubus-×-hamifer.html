Rubus × hamifer

Rubus × hamifer, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry hybrid, is a vigorous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chamaemorus (cloudberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The species exhibits thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. Its flowers are typically pink or white, and the fruit is a aggregate of small drupes, often dark red to black in color.

This plant is adapted to temperate climates and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
