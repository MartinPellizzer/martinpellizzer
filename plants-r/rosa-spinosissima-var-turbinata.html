Rosa spinosissima var. turbinata

Rosa spinosissima var. turbinata, commonly known as the Turbinated Bush Rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to southern Europe.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy underside and produces small, single flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its twisted, turbinated (turbinate) form, which gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative hedge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
