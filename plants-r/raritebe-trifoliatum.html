Raritebe trifoliatum

Raritebe trifoliatum, commonly known as the three-leaf wonder, is a rare flowering plant native to the temperate forests of southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.
