Rustia rubra

Rustia rubra, commonly known as the red rustia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of southern Africa. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, seed-filled capsules for reproduction.

Rustia rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
