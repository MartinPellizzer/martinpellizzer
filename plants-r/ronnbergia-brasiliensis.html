Ronnbergia brasiliensis

Ronnbergia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Ronnbergia brasiliensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
