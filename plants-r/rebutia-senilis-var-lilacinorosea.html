Rebutia senilis var. lilacinorosea

Rebutia senilis var. lilacinorosea, commonly known as the Pink Princess Cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a short, branching stem covered in dense, pale green to pinkish-white spines. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of pink, lilac, and rose, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
