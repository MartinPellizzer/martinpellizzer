Railliardia argentea

Railliardia argentea, commonly known as the silver bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Railliardia argentea thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.
