Rosa stylosa var. naias

Rosa stylosa var. naias, commonly known as the Naiad rose, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia.

It features dense, thornless stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive addition to subtropical gardens.
