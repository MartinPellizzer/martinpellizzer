Rosa kmetiana

Rosa kmetiana, commonly known as the Kmetiana rose, is a species native to the Balkan region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a slight fragrance. Rosa kmetiana produces hips, which are red, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
