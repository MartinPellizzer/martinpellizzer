Reseda attenuata

Reseda attenuata, commonly known as slender reseda, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its botanical features include a taproot system and a flowering period that typically occurs in spring.

Reseda attenuata is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
