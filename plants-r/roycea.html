Roycea

Roycea, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and elusive plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are small, leathery, and arranged in a compact, shrub-like form.
