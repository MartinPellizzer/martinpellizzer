Rosa florida

Rosa florida, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rosa florida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
