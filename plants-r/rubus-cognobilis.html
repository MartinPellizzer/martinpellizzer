Rubus cognobilis

Rubus cognobilis, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble in the family Rosaceae, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and is commonly found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

Its flowering period occurs in early summer, followed by fruiting in late summer.
