Rothia argentea

Rothia argentea, commonly known as silver rothia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.

Rothia argentea is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in both container and in-ground settings.
