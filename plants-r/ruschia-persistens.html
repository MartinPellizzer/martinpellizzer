Ruschia persistens

Ruschia persistens, commonly known as the evergreen Ruschia, is a perennial succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, grass-like leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
