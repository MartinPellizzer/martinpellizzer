Restio stenostachyus

Restio stenostachyus, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed restio, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue and are adapted for wind pollination.

Restio stenostachyus is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and drought tolerance.
