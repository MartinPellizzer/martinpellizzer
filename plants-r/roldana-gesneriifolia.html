Roldana gesneriifolia

Roldana gesneriifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf geranium, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and blooms, which are often used in landscaping for their visual appeal.

Roldana gesneriifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
