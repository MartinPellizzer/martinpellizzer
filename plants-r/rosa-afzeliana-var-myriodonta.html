Rosa afzeliana var. myriodonta

Rosa afzeliana var. myriodonta, commonly known as the African rose or myriodonta rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The species is also notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
