Rapanea fusca var. fasciculata

Rapanea fusca var. fasciculata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rapanea, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in whorls along the stems. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit.
