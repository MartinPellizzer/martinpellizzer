Rosa Rugosa Var. Regeliana

Rosa rugosa var. regeliana is a hardy shrub rose known for its rugged beauty and resilience. This variety is native to eastern Asia and thrives in coastal and sandy soils. It features dense, thorned stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant is popular for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its adaptability makes it a favorite among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, tough plants.
