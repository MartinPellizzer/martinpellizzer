Ruellia urens

Ruellia urens, commonly known as Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. Ruellia urens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various soil types.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
