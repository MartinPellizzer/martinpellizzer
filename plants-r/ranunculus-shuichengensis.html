Ranunculus shuichengensis

Ranunculus shuichengensis, commonly known as the Shuicheng buttercup, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers with five to eight petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the stems are usually hairy.
