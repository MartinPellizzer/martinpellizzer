Rubus apiculatus proles delicatus

Rubus apiculatus proles delicatus, commonly known as the delicate raspberry.

This plant is a species of bramble belonging to the Rubus genus, characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, edible fruits. It produces aggregate fruits composed of numerous drupelets, each with a tiny seed. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
