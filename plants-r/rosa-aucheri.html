Rosa aucheri

Rosa aucheri, commonly known as the Austrian rose, is a deciduous shrub native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features include simple, pinnate leaves and thornless stems.

Rosa aucheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
