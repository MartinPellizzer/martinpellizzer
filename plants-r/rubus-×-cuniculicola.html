Rubus × cuniculicola

Rubus × cuniculicola, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry or loganberry, is a perennial shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus pectoralis (raspberry), resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The plant produces large, dark purple to black fruits that are typically sweeter and less tart than its parent species. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
