Rosa bungeana

Rosa bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its botanical characteristics include a woody structure and the ability to thrive in temperate climates.

Rosa bungeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
