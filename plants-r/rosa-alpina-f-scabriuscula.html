Rosa alpina f. scabriuscula

Rosa alpina f. scabriuscula, commonly known as the Alpine rose.

This plant is a subspecies of the Alpine rose, characterized by its compact growth and dense, dark green foliage. It produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically serrated and have a slightly rough texture.

It is well adapted to rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
