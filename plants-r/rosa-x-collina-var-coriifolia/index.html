Rosa × Collina Var. Coriifolia

Coriifolia rose, scientifically known as Rosa × collina var. coriifolia, is a captivating hybrid rose prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature. This plant is a cross between Rosa collina and Rosa chinensis, combining the best traits of both parent species. Its foliage is uniquely shaped, with leaves that resemble small, heart-shaped leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in a variety of climates and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance. Whether used as a hedge, shrub, or specimen plant, Coriifolia rose adds both visual interest and a touch of elegance to any landscape.
