Ricinus communis var. purpurascens

Ricinus communis var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple leaf castor bean, is a variant of the castor oil plant characterized by its striking purple foliage.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant produces large, palmate leaves and distinctive spiny seed pods containing toxic seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
