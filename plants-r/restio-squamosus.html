Restio squamosus

Restio squamosus, commonly known as the silver restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. Restio squamosus is adapted to coastal conditions, showing resilience to salt spray and strong winds.
