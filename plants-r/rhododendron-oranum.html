Rhododendron oranum

Rhododendron oranum, commonly known as the Kashmiri rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in cool, shaded environments. Its flowers, which range from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Rhododendron oranum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
