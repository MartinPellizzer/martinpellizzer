Rhynchosia gorsii

Rhynchosia gorsii, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. Rhynchosia gorsii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
