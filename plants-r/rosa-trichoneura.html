Rosa trichoneura

Rosa trichoneura, commonly known as the rough-stemmed rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to parts of Asia, it is often found in woodland and shrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the rose genus.
