Rabdosia lophanthoides var. micrantha

Rabdosia lophanthoides var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
