Rosa pomponiana

Rosa pomponiana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and broad, dark green leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Rosa pomponiana is also cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
