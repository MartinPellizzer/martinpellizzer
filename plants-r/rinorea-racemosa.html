Rinorea racemosa

Rinorea racemosa, commonly known as the blue bell or white bell, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or white bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves, which are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Rinorea racemosa is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height.
