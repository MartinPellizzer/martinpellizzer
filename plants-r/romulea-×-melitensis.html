Romulea × melitensis

Romulea × melitensis, commonly known as the hybrid crown orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble orchids. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually a mix of yellow and orange, with a distinctive crown-like structure at the base.
