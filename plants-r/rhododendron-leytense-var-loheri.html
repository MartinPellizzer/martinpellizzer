Rhododendron leytense var. loheri

Rhododendron leytense var. loheri, commonly known as the Leyte rhododendron, is a rare and endemic species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is a shrub with dense, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
