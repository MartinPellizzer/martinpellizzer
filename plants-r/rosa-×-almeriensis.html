Rosa × almeriensis

Rosa × almeriensis, commonly known as the Almería rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Spain.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Rosa × almeriensis is also valued for its disease resistance and adaptability to various climates.
