Rhododendron websterianum var. websterianum

Rhododendron websterianum var. websterianum, commonly known as the Webster's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from pink to red.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
