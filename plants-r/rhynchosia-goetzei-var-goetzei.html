Rhynchosia goetzei var. goetzei

Rhynchosia goetzei var. goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's burr legume, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive pod-like fruits that enclose the seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
