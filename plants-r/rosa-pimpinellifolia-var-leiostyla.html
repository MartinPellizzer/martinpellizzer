Rosa pimpinellifolia var. leiostyla

Rosa pimpinellifolia var. leiostyla, commonly known as the meadow rose, is a compact shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
