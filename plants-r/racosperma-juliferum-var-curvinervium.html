Racosperma juliferum var. curvinervium

Racosperma juliferum var. curvinervium, commonly known as the narrow-veined racosperma, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and flowering habit. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that aids in pollination.
