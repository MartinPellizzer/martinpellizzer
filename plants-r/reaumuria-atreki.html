Reaumuria atreki

Reaumuria atreki, commonly known as the Atrek sandthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, thorned stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in steppe and desert landscapes.

Reaumuria atreki plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
