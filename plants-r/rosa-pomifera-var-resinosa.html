Rosa pomifera var. resinosa

Rosa pomifera var. resinosa, commonly known as the resinous rose, is a rare and unique variant of the pomifera rose species.

It is characterized by its distinctive resinous secretions that exude from its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance and aroma. The plant produces large, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires partial sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among botanists and horticultural enthusiasts.
