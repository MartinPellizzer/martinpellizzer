Ranunculus ovczinnikovii

Ranunculus ovczinnikovii, commonly known as the Ovczinnikov's buttercup, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features palmately divided leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky soils at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
