Rosa dumalis var. cariotii

Rosa dumalis var. cariotii, commonly known as the Cariotii rose, is a shrub-like species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its botanical structure includes compound leaves with serrated edges and multiple petals in its inflorescence.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil conditions.
