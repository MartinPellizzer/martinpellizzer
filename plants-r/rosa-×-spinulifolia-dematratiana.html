Rosa × spinulifolia dematratiana

Rosa × spinulifolia dematratiana, commonly known as the Dematratian rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features thorny stems and is characterized by its dense, spinose foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to the volcanic soils and mild climate of its native habitat.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
