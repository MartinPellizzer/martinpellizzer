Rostellularia procumbens f. alboflora

Rostellularia procumbens f. alboflora, commonly known as the white-flowered prostrate rostellularia, is a variant of the prostrate rostellularia species.

It is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit and distinctive white flowers. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This form is notable for its ornamental value due to its delicate white blooms.
