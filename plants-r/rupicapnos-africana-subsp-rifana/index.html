Rupicapnos Africana Subsp. Rifana

Rifana rupicapnos, scientifically known as Rupicapnos africana subsp. rifana, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat. Its unique foliage, featuring waxy, leathery leaves, makes it a standout in both wild and cultivated landscapes. Despite its ornamental value, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles compared to more widely recognized species. Growing in well-drained soils and full sun, it thrives in cooler climates and is a favorite among plant enthusiasts seeking rare botanical specimens.
