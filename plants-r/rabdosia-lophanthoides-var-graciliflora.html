Rabdosia lophanthoides var. graciliflora

Rabdosia lophanthoides var. graciliflora, commonly known as slender-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, with a distinctive blue or purple coloration.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
