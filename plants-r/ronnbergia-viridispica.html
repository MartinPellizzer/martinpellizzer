Ronnbergia viridispica

Ronnbergia viridispica, commonly known as the green-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ronnbergia viridispica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
