Ranunculus duriaei

Ranunculus duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features delicate, palmate leaves and produces small, yellow or orange flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
