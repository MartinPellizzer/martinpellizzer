Rosa jundzilliana var. subolida

Rosa jundzilliana var. subolida, commonly known as the Jundzilliana rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its deep pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. Its flowers have a strong, sweet fragrance and are often used in perfumery.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
