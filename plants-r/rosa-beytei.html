Rosa beytei

Rosa beytei, commonly known as the Beytei rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with five petals and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Rosa beytei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
