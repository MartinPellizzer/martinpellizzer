Rosa canina f. amansii

Rosa canina f. amansii, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a naturally occurring form of Rosa canina, distinguished by its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant features dense, thorned stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a prominent central pistil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its persistent flowering and hardy nature.
