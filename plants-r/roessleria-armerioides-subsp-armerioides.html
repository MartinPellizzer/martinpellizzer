Roessleria armerioides subsp. armerioides

Roessleria armerioides subsp.

armerioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed roessleria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands or along stream banks.

The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, white to pale pink flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
