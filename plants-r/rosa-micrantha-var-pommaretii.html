Rosa micrantha var. pommaretii

Rosa micrantha var. pommaretii, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering, with flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The plant produces small, oval leaves with serrated edges and has a woody stem structure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

This variety is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
