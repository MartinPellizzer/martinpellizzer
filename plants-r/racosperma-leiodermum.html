Racosperma leiodermum

Racosperma leiodermum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, often glabrous stems and bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or yellow-green, arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes.
