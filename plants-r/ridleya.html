Ridleya

Ridleya, commonly known as the Christmas tree fern, is a unique and striking plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Ridleya thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its spore-producing structures, called soruses, are located on the underside of its fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
