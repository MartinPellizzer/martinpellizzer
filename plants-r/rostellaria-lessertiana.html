Rostellaria lessertiana

Rostellaria lessertiana, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
