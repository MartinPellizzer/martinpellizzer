Rosa mollis f. ortalensis

Rosa mollis f. ortalensis, commonly known as the ortalensis rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa mollis species, characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental value.

It features semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has fragrant, medium-sized blooms and is known for its hardy nature, thriving in a variety of soil types. It is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to maintain.

The foliage is dark green and provides a nice contrast to the colorful flowers.
