Rosa systyla var. monsoniae

Rosa systyla var. monsoniae, commonly known as the Monson's shrub rose, is a compact, thornless shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage that provides an attractive backdrop to its blossoms. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and hardy nature.

The variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
