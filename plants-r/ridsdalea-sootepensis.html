Ridsdalea sootepensis

Ridsdalea sootepensis, commonly known as the Sootep orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring humid and shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and air.

The botanical features of Ridsdalea sootepensis make it a popular subject for horticultural study and cultivation.
