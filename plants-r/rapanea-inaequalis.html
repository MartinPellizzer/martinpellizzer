Rapanea inaequalis

Rapanea inaequalis, commonly known as the unequal rapanea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
