Rhododendron labolengense

Rhododendron labolengense, commonly known as the Labo rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, while its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences.

Rhododendron labolengense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
