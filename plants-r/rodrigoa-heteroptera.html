Rodrigoa heteroptera

Rodrigoa heteroptera, commonly known as the pink-flowered rock rose, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves and is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions.
