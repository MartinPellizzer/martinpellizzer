Ruellia carnea var. cinerea

Ruellia carnea var. cinerea, commonly known as silverleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is a perennial herb that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Ruellia carnea var. cinerea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
