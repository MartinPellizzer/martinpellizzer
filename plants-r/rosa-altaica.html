Rosa altaica

Rosa altaica, commonly known as the Altai rose, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Rosa altaica is also noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
