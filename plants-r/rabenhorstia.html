Rabenhorstia

Rabenhorstia, commonly known as the silver leaf cholla, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its branching stems covered in small, spiny segments. The plant has small, pale green leaves that are often mistaken for true leaves, though they are actually modified stems. Rabenhorstia thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations such as reduced foliage and water-storing stems.

Its unique botanical features make it well-suited for survival in harsh desert conditions.
