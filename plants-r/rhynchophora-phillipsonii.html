Rhynchophora phillipsonii

Rhynchophora phillipsonii, commonly known as the Phillipson's snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Helicarionidae and is characterized by its spiral shell and soft, moist body. The shell is typically conical and has a smooth surface with a slight spiral pattern. This species is found in tropical and subtropical regions, often in humid environments such as forests and gardens.
