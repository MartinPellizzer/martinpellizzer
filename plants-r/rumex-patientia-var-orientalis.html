Rumex patientia var. orientalis

Rumex patientia var. orientalis, commonly known as oriental dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern and eastern Europe.

It features broad, wavy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in the summer. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It is often found in wet meadows, along riverbanks, and in damp woodland areas.

Its leaves have been historically used in traditional medicine and as a vegetable.
