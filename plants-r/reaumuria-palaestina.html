Reaumuria palaestina

Reaumuria palaestina, commonly known as Palestine rosebay, is a shrub native to the arid regions of the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is well adapted to harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat. Its leaves are small, fleshy, and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Reaumuria palaestina is often found in rocky soils and is a hardy plant that thrives in full sun.
