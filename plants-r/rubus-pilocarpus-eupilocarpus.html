Rubus pilocarpus eupilocarpus

Rubus pilocarpus eupilocarpus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This plant is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. The plant is native to North America and thrives in temperate climates.

Its berries are often used in culinary applications and have a tart, slightly sweet flavor.
