Rosa contracta

Rosa contracta, commonly known as the Japanese rose or small rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Rosa contracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
