Rossioglossum splendens

Rossioglossum splendens, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Rossioglossum splendens is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
