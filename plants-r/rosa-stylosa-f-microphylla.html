Rosa stylosa f. microphylla

Rosa stylosa f. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature pink bush rose, is a compact shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, delicate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for container gardening or as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
