Ruellia praeclara

Ruellia praeclara, commonly known as the showy ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of blue, purple, and white. Ruellia praeclara is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
