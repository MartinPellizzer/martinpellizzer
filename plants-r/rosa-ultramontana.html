Rosa ultramontana

Rosa ultramontana, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, glossy foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Rosa ultramontana is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for alpine gardens and rockeries.
