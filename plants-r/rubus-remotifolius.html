Rubus remotifolius

Rubus remotifolius, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits called drupes, which are typically red when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Rubus remotifolius is often cultivated for its edible berries and is also valued in ecological restoration projects.
