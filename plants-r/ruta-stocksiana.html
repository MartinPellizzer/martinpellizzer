Ruta stocksiana

Ruta stocksiana, commonly known as the stock's rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and prefers full sun. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like citrus and rue.

Ruta stocksiana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
