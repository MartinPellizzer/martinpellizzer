Rubus × pseudovendeanus

Rubus × pseudovendeanus, commonly known as the blackberry raspberry, is a hybrid shrub in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that ripen to a dark red or black color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
