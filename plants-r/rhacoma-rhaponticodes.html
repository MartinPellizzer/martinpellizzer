Rhacoma rhaponticodes

Rhacoma rhaponticodes, commonly known as the mountain rhubarb, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Rhacoma rhaponticodes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
