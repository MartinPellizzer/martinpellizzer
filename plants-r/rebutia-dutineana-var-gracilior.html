Rebutia dutineana var. gracilior

Rebutia dutineana var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the spring and summer. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it drought-tolerant.

It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
