Restio nubigenus

Restio nubigenus, commonly known as the cloud restio, is a perennial grass-like plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in dune ecosystems.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
