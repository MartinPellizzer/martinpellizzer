Rosa mollis f. cinereobrunnea

Rosa mollis f. cinereobrunnea, commonly known as the cinereobrunnea form of the soft rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its pale pink to cream-colored flowers and fuzzy, grayish-brown foliage. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or shaded areas. Its stems are covered in a fine, woolly layer that gives it a distinctive appearance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
