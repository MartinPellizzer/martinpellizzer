Rosa × andegavensis var. superba

Rosa × andegavensis var. superba, commonly known as the Andegavensis rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in areas like Georgia and South Carolina. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers with a deep pink to red coloration, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
