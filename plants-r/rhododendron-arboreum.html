Rhododendron arboreum

Rhododendron arboreum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
