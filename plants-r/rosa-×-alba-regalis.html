Rosa × alba regalis

Rosa × alba regalis, commonly known as the White Queen Rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from Europe.

It is characterized by its large, fully double white flowers that are often adorned with a subtle pinkish blush. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thorny stems and dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its elegant appearance and is often used in ornamental gardens.
