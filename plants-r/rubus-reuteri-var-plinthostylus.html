Rubus reuteri var. plinthostylus

Rubus reuteri var. plinthostylus, commonly known as the slender blackberry, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are typically edible. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
