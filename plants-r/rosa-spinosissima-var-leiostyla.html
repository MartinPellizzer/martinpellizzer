Rosa spinosissima var. leiostyla

Rosa spinosissima var. leiostyla, commonly known as the silky prickly rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a rounded, red hip that contains numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
