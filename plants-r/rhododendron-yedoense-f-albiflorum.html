Rhododendron yedoense f. albiflorum

Rhododendron yedoense f. albiflorum, commonly known as the Yedoensis White Flowered Form, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
