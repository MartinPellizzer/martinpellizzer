Rosa × franzonii

Rosa × franzonii, commonly known as the Franzonii rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with petals that are semi-double to fully double in form. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × franzonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and disease resistance.
