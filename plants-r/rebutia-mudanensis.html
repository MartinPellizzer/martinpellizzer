Rebutia mudanensis

Rebutia mudanensis, commonly known as the Mudanensis Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative plant.
