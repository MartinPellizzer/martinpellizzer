Rubus micans var. litodon

Rubus micans var. litodon, commonly known as the silverleaf blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-tinged leaves and small, dark berries. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or woodland edges. Its leaves are usually elliptical with serrated edges and a dense covering of fine hairs.

The fruit, while edible, is often less flavorful compared to other blackberry varieties.
