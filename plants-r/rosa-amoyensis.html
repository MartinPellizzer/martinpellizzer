Rosa amoyensis

Rosa amoyensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, red fruits known as rose hips. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Rosa amoyensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
