Rumex kerneri

Rumex kerneri, commonly known as the Sierra rumex, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish coloration on the underside, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense mats.

The species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
