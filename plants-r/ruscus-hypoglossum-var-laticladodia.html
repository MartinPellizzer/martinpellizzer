Ruscus hypoglossum var. laticladodia

Ruscus hypoglossum var. laticladodia, commonly known as the broad-leaved evergreen euonym, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a compact and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits, which are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
