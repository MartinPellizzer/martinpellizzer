Ranunculus astrantiifolius var. polypetalus

Ranunculus astrantiifolius var. polypetalus, commonly known as the Polypetalus buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, palmate leaves with a hairy texture and bright yellow flowers that have numerous petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges or stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blossoms.
