Racosperma Neriifolium

Racosperma neriifolium is a unique flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its striking appearance. The plant features dark green leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid regions. This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance.
