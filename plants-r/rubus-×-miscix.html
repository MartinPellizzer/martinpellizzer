Rubus × miscix

Rubus × miscix, commonly known as the hybrid raspberry or blackberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruits can range from red to black in color.

This hybrid is prized for its flavor and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
