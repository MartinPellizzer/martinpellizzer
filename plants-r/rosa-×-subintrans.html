Rosa × subintrans

Rosa × subintrans, commonly known as the everblooming shrub rose, is a hybrid rose species that exhibits exceptional flowering characteristics.

It is known for its continuous bloom throughout the growing season, producing clusters of fragrant, semi-double to double pink or white flowers. The plant is a compact shrub with dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its shape and promote continuous flowering.

Rosa × subintrans is prized in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness across various climates.
