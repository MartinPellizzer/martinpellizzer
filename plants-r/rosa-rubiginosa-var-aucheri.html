Rosa rubiginosa var. aucheri

Rosa rubiginosa var. aucheri, commonly known as the Aucher's rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, pale pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, hairy rose hip that persists through the winter.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor, well-drained soils.
