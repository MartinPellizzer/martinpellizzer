Rhododendron calophyllum var. roseum

Rhododendron calophyllum var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum rhododendron, is a variant of the calophyllum rhododendron.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, with dense, leathery foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
