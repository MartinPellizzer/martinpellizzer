Rosa coriifolia var. heterotricha

Rosa coriifolia var. heterotricha, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets, and it has a woody, aromatic stem.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
