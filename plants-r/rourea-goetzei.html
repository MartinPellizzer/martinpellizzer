Rourea goetzei

Rourea goetzei, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its growth habit is compact, with branching that is often dense and bushy.

Rourea goetzei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
