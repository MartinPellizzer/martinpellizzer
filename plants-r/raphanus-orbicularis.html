Raphanus orbicularis

Raphanus orbicularis, commonly known as the globe radish, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy root that is typically white or yellow in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are arranged in clusters, and it has alternate, lobed leaves that are hairy in texture. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

Its growth habit and botanical features make it distinct from other members of the Brassica genus.
