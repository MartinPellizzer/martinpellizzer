Racosperma sclerophyllum var. teretiusculum

Racosperma sclerophyllum var. teretiusculum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often glandular and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in compact, spherical inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands.

The species is characterized by its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid environments.
