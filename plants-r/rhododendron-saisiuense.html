Rhododendron saisiuense

Rhododendron saisiuense, commonly known as the Saisiu rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while the flowers range in color from pink to red.

Rhododendron saisiuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as part of natural landscapes.
