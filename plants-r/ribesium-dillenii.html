Ribesium dillenii

Ribesium dillenii, commonly known as the silverleaf catchfly, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ribesium dillenii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is considered a hardy, low-maintenance plant for gardens.
