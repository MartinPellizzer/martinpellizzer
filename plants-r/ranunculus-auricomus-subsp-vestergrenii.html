Ranunculus auricomus subsp. vestergrenii

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

vestergrenii, commonly known as the Swedish buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe. It features delicate, yellow flowers with five petals that are often tinged with red or brown at the base.

The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are alternate along the stem. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Ranunculaceae family.
