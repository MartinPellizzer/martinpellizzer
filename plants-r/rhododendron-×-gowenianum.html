Rhododendron × gowenianum

Rhododendron × gowenianum, commonly known as the Gowenianum rhododendron, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant pink to red blooms.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
