Rosa × incanescens

Rosa × incanescens, commonly known as the everglast rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, thornless canes and compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a slight fragrance. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season.

Rosa × incanescens is prized for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
