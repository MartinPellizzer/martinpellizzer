Rodriguezia obtusifolia

Rodriguezia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Rodriguezia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It features large, broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are usually pink or red, with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses in warm climates.
