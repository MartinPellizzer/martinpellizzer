Rudgea sessilis subsp. sessilis

Rudgea sessilis subsp.

sessilis, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical shrub native to South America. It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape.
