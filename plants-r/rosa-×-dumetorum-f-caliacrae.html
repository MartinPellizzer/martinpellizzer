Rosa × dumetorum f. caliacrae

Rosa × dumetorum f. caliacrae, commonly known as the Cilician rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant is native to the eastern Mediterranean region, particularly Turkey and Cyprus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and seasonal blooming.
