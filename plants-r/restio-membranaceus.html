Restio membranaceus

Restio membranaceus, commonly known as the paper reed, is a member of the Restionaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and fibrous, strap-shaped leaves. Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
