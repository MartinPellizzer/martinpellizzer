Roepera reticulata

Roepera reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to yellowish flowers with intricate net-like venation on the petals. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as tropical forests.

Roepera reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
