Rhododendron simsii var. tawadae

Rhododendron simsii var. tawadae, commonly known as the Tawadae rhododendron, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage that provides a striking backdrop for its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The blooms, which range from pale pink to deep red, are typically borne in clusters and are highly fragrant. This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and shady borders due to its preference for acidic, well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in moderate climates.
