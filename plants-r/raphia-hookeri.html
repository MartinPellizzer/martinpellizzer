Raphia hookeri

Raphia hookeri, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a large, tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of wide, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that eventually develop into small, round fruits containing a single seed. Raphia hookeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional practices.

Its leaves are used for thatching and roofing in some regions.
