Roegneria shandongensis

Roegneria shandongensis, commonly known as the Shandong wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is often found in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and for erosion control.
