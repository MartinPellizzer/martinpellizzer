Rosa afzeliana f. inermis

Rosa afzeliana f. inermis, commonly known as the smooth rose, is a flowering plant native to regions of Asia, including China and India.

It is a hybrid form of Rosa afzeliana, characterized by its smooth, thornless stems and foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological applications.
