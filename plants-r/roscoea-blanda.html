Roscoea blanda

Roscoea blanda, commonly known as the slender roscoea, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features attractive, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces striking, orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Roscoea blanda thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and exotic plant.
