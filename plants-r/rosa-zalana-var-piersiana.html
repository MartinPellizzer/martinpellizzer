Rosa zalana var. piersiana

Rosa zalana var. piersiana, commonly known as the Piersiana rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, thornless stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The foliage is dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate drainage.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
