Rhynchosia totta var. elegantissima

Rhynchosia totta var. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant wild bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant bears pods that mature into hairy, woody capsules containing several seeds. It is characterized by its rapid growth habit and ability to thrive in disturbed or open habitats.

Botanically, it is notable for its ornamental value and potential use in traditional medicine.
