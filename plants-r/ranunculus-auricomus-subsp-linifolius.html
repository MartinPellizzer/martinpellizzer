Ranunculus auricomus subsp. linifolius

Ranunculus auricomus subsp.

linifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to western North America. It features showy, yellow flowers with five to seven petals, typically borne on slender, hairy stems.

The plant has palmate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
