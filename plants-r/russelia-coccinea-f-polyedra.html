Russelia coccinea f. polyedra

Russelia coccinea f. polyedra, commonly known as the variegated red russelia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom profusely in summer, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings, adding visual interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening or as a border plant in landscapes.
