Renanthera citrina var. sinica

Renanthera citrina var. sinica, commonly known as the Chinese violet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or cream in color, with a distinctive violet or purple lip. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its leaves are usually broad and strap-like. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
