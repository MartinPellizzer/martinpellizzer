Rhododendron × pictum

Rhododendron × pictum, commonly known as the hybrid rhododendron, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its showy, clustered flowers.

It is a cross between Rhododendron catawbiense and Rhododendron maximum, resulting in a plant with distinctive, often brightly colored blooms. The foliage is typically dense and leathery, providing an attractive evergreen presence. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a versatile choice for landscaping and horticultural collections.
