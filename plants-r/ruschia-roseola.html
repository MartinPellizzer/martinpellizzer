Ruschia roseola

Ruschia roseola, commonly known as the pink ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
