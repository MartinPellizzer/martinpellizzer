Rhytionanthos sphaericum

Rhytionanthos sphaericum, commonly known as the globe flower, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
