Rosa diversisepala

Rosa diversisepala, commonly known as the multibract rose, is a species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its botanical characteristics include bracteate inflorescences and thorny stems.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
