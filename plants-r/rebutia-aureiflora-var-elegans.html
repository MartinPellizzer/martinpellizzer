Rebutia aureiflora var. elegans

Rebutia aureiflora var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Rebutia, is a compact cactus native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, woolly spines that provide protection against herbivores and extreme weather. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
