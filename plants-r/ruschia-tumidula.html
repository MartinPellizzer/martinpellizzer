Ruschia tumidula

Ruschia tumidula, commonly known as the swollen Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, providing additional drought resistance.

Ruschia tumidula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
