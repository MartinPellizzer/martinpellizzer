Ruta cordata

Ruta cordata, commonly known as common rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly aromatic scent, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. Ruta cordata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for various health purposes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
