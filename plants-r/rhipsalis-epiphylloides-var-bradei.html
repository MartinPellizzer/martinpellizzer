Rhipsalis epiphylloides var. bradei

Rhipsalis epiphylloides var. bradei, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a unique epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems that often appear as a tangle of delicate, thread-like structures. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual contrast to its green stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
