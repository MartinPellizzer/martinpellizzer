Rubus cuifengensis

Rubus cuifengensis, commonly known as the Cuifeng blackberry, is a species of bramble native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is typically found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
