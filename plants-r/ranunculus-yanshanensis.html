Ranunculus yanshanensis

Ranunculus yanshanensis, commonly known as the Yanshan buttercup, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers with five to eight petals. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its leaves are palmately divided, and it produces yellow or orange-colored flowers.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
