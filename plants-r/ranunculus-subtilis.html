Ranunculus subtilis

Ranunculus subtilis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it is often found in association with other woodland flora.

Ranunculus subtilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
