Rhaphidophora calophylla var. violacea

Rhaphidophora calophylla var. violacea, commonly known as the violet-leafed calophylla, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves with a distinctive violet or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
