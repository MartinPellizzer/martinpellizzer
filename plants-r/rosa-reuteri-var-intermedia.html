Rosa reuteri var. intermedia

Rosa reuteri var. intermedia, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing a compact growth habit. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Rosa reuteri var. intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
