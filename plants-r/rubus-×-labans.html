Rubus × labans

Rubus × labans, commonly known as the blackberry-loganberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus loganobaccus (loganberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters, featuring thorny stems and trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are white or pale pink, and the fruit is a large, dark purple to black aggregate fruit with a sweet-tart flavor.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental value and culinary uses in both home and commercial settings.
