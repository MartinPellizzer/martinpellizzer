Rhamnus annonifolia

Rhamnus annonifolia, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are glossy and elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

Rhamnus annonifolia is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
