Rubus Colemanii Subsp. Halacsyi

Rubus colemanii subsp. halacsyi is a rare and elusive plant native to the arid regions of California. This subspecies belongs to the rose family and is known for its distinctive appearance and limited distribution. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats. Due to its restricted range and threats from habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical species.
