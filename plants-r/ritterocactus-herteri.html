Ritterocactus herteri

Ritterocactus herteri, commonly known as the Herter's ritterocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the tips of the stems. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
