Rosa mollis f. drimylopsis

Rosa mollis f. drimylopsis, commonly known as the Drimylopsis rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has finely textured, dark green foliage that is densely packed, giving it a lush appearance. It is typically evergreen, though it may lose some leaves in colder climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
