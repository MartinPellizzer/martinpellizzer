Rapanea yateensis

Rapanea yateensis, commonly known as the Yate's holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark blue berries.
