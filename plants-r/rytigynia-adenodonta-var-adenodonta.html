Rytigynia adenodonta var. adenodonta

Rytigynia adenodonta var. adenodonta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rytigynia, is a species native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, producing a pale yellow to cream color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in subtropical gardens for its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
