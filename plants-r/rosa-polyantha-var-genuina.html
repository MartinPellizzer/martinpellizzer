Rosa polyantha var. genuina

Rosa polyantha var. genuina, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features multiple stems with thorns and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms.

Rosa polyantha var. genuina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
