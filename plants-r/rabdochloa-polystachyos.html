Rabdochloa polystachyos

Rabdochloa polystachyos, commonly known as the clustered lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy perennial suitable for native gardens and erosion control.

Its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with soil bacteria also contributes to its ecological importance.
