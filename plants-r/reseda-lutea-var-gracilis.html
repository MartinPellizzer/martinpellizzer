Reseda lutea var. gracilis

Reseda lutea var. gracilis, commonly known as slender yellowrocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
