Racosperma lirellatum subsp. compressum

Racosperma lirellatum subsp.

compressum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia. It is characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems and small, trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, spherical clusters, which are typical of the Acacia genus. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compressed leaflets and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
