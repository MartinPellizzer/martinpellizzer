Rosa Multiflora F. Glabrescens

Rosa multiflora f. glabrescens is a rare and beautiful flowering plant known for its delicate pink blooms and compact growth habit. This cultivar is a variation of the multiflora rose, which is native to eastern Asia and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. The 'glabrescens' form is distinguished by its smoother, hairless stems and leaves, giving it a more refined appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens and landscapes. Due to its rarity and unique characteristics, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and horticulturists alike.
