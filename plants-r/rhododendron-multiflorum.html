Rhododendron multiflorum

Rhododendron multiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron multiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
