Rosa rugosa var. coruscans

Rosa rugosa var. coruscans, commonly known as the glittering rose, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rugged, thorny stems and dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and slightly serrated, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Rosa rugosa var. coruscans is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in various climates.
