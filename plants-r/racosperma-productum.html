Racosperma productum

Racosperma productum, commonly known as the broad-leafed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale yellow, arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes.

Racosperma productum is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
