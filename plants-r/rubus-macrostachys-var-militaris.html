Rubus macrostachys var. militaris

Rubus macrostachys var. militaris, commonly known as the military raspberry, is a species of bramble native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, edible red berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the flowers are usually white or pale pink.

The fruit is often used in local cuisines and has a tart, slightly sweet flavor.
