Rosa × misimensis

Rosa × misimensis, commonly known as the Misemensis rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Rosa × misimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
