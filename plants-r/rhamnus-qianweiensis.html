Rhamnus qianweiensis

Rhamnus qianweiensis, commonly known as the Qianwei privet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or black when mature. It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
