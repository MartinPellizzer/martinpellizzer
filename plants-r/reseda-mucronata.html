Reseda mucronata

Reseda mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy. Reseda mucronata is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in dry and sunny conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
