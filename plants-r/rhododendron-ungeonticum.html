Rhododendron ungeonticum

Rhododendron ungeonticum, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes, often in forested areas. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pink and red.

Rhododendron ungeonticum plays an important role in its native ecosystem, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
