Roebuckia lathamensis var. glabrata

Roebuckia lathamensis var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth roebuckia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, yellow-orange inflorescences. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, though the varietal name "glabrata" suggests a smoother texture compared to other varieties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
