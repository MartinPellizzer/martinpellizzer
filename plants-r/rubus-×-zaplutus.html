Rubus × zaplutus

Rubus × zaplutus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rubus ursinus (common blackberry) and Rubus idaeus (red raspberry). This plant typically produces dark red to black berries that are larger and more flavorful than its parent species. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and culinary uses.
