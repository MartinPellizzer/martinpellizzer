Rhododendron × sesterianum

Rhododendron × sesterianum, commonly known as the Sesterianum rhododendron, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, and prefers acidic, well-drained soils. It is native to regions in Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in cool, moist climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its vibrant floral display.
