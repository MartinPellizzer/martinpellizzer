Rosa communis var. aciphylloides

Rosa communis var. aciphylloides, commonly known as the aciphylloides rose, is a rare and unique variant of the common rose.

It is characterized by its unusual foliage, featuring leaves that are deeply lobed and resemble those of the aciphylleae, a group of plants with distinctive leaf structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, similar to other rose varieties.
