Roystonea jamaicana

Roystonea jamaicana, commonly known as the Jamaican royal palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Caribbean.

It features a slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark and produces large, feather-like leaves that form a crown at the top. The plant is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that can reach several meters in length, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

The Jamaican royal palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
