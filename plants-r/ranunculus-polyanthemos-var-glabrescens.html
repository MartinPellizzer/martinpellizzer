Ranunculus polyanthemos var. glabrescens

Ranunculus polyanthemos var. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous buttercup, is a variant of the polyanthemos buttercup.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate regions. The plant features showy, yellow flowers with numerous petals and a central cluster of stamens. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a smooth, glossy surface.

This variety is noted for its reduced pubescence compared to the typical form, giving it a more refined appearance.
