Rosa × embequeirouxi

Rosa × embequeirouxi, commonly known as the Embequeirouxi rose, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth and flowering.

This rose is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in the fragrance industry due to its aromatic properties.
