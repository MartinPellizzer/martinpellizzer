Rosa Velebitica

Rosa velebitica is a rare and beautiful species of rose native to the mountains of Croatia. This plant is known for its striking pink flowers and its unique habitat requirements. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil. Due to its limited range, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable botanical treasure.
