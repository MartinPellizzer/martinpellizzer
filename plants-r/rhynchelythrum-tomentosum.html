Rhynchelythrum tomentosum

Rhynchelythrum tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly rhynchelythrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
