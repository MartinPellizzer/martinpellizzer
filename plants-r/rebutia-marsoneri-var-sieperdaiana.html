Rebutia marsoneri var. sieperdaiana

Rebutia marsoneri var. sieperdaiana, commonly known as the Sieperdaian Rebutia, is a succulent belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, specifically Argentina. The plant features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is valued for its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
