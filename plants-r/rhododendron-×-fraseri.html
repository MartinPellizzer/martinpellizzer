Rhododendron × fraseri

Rhododendron × fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's rhododendron, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, leathery foliage that is typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and horticultural collections.
