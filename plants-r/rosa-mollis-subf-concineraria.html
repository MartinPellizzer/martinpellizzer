Rosa mollis subf. concineraria

Rosa mollis subf.

concineraria, commonly known as the soft rose or silky rose, is a botanical variant of the species Rosa mollis. It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers.

This subform is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant typically grows as a shrub with multiple stems and produces small, fragrant blooms.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural study.
