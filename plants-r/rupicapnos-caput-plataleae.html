Rupicapnos caput-plataleae

Rupicapnos caput-plataleae, commonly known as the flat-headed rupicapnos, is a member of the Berberidaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in woodland gardens.
