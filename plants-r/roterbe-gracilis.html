Roterbe gracilis

Roterbe gracilis, commonly known as the slender red bamboo, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. Its flowering stems produce small, clustered flowers that are often a deep red or orange hue.

Roterbe gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
