Rosa wichurana var. ampullicarpa

Rosa wichurana var. ampullicarpa, commonly known as the Ampullaria rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces hips that are orange-red in color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its beauty.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
