Rourea gudjuana

Rourea gudjuana, commonly known as the golden rourea, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Rourea gudjuana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid climates.
