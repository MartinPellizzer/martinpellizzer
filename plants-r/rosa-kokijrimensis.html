Rosa kokijrimensis

Rosa kokijrimensis, commonly known as the Kokijrim rose, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It prefers well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates with distinct seasonal changes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
