Rosa × dumetorum f. deseglisei

Rosa × dumetorum f. deseglisei, commonly known as the Deseglise's shrub rose, is a compact, thorny shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It is relatively hardy and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or rockeries.

This variety is also valued for its use in hedging and as a natural barrier due to its dense growth habit.
