Rosa micrantha var. insularum

Rosa micrantha var. insularum, commonly known as the island rose, is a shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
