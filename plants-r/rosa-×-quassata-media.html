Rosa × quassata media

Rosa × quassata media, commonly known as the shrub rose or the double shrub rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, compact foliage. The plant produces abundant clusters of double flowers, typically in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for hedges, borders, or as a specimen plant due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.

Rosa × quassata media prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth and flowering.
