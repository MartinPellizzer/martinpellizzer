Rhododendron rhododactylum

Rhododendron rhododactylum, commonly known as the snow lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences.

Rhododendron rhododactylum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
