Ranunculus sphaerospermus

Ranunculus sphaerospermus, commonly known as the spherical-seeded buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers with five petals and a distinctive, spherical seed capsule. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three deeply lobed leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
