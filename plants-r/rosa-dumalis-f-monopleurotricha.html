Rosa dumalis f. monopleurotricha

Rosa dumalis f. monopleurotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock rose, is a unique horticultural form of the Rosa dumalis species.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging environmental conditions.
