Ruta gilesii

Ruta gilesii, commonly known as the Indian rue, is a flowering plant native to South and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in herbal remedies.

Ruta gilesii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
