Remijia densiflora f. glabricalyx

Remijia densiflora f. glabricalyx, commonly known as the smooth-leafed remijia, is a botanical variant of the species Remijia densiflora.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its distinctive, glabrous (hairless) foliage. The leaves are typically lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and taxonomic studies.
