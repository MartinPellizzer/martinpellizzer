Rebutia leucanthema

Rebutia leucanthema, commonly known as the white-flowered rebutia, is a species of cactus native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It is a low-growing, spineless plant with a compact, rounded stem that often branches at the base. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Rebutia leucanthema is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
