Rosa Villosa Var. Submollis

Velvet rose, scientifically known as Rosa villosa var. submollis, is a striking shrub native to the western United States and parts of Canada. Its velvety, dark red blooms are a standout feature, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. With its fragrant flowers and lush foliage, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to any landscape.
