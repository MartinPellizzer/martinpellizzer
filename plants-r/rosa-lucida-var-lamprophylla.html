Rosa lucida var. lamprophylla

Rosa lucida var. lamprophylla, commonly known as the shining evergreen rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with a glossy, dark green appearance that remains throughout the year. The plant produces showy, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and year-round visual interest make it a valuable addition to landscape designs.
