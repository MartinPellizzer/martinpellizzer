Ravensara montana

Ravensara montana, commonly known as the mountain ravenwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. Ravensara montana is adapted to harsh, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its ornamental value and unique appearance make it a popular choice for coastal gardens and rockeries.
