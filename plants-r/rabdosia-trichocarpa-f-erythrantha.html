Rabdosia trichocarpa f. erythrantha

Rabdosia trichocarpa f. erythrantha, commonly known as the red-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant is distinguished by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
