Rosa cochinchinensis

Rosa cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, China, and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Rosa cochinchinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
