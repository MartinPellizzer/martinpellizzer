Rhododendron × nobleanum var. venustum

Rhododendron × nobleanum var. venustum, commonly known as the Veinstem rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters, with a compact and bushy habit. Its flowers range in color from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive veined appearance.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
