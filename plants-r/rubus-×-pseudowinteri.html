Rubus × pseudowinteri

Rubus × pseudowinteri, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are typically edible. It is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit production, it is cultivated in gardens and for commercial use.
