Rebutia xanthocarpa var. coerulescens

Rebutia xanthocarpa var. coerulescens, commonly known as the Blue-Fleshed Rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features compact, cylindrical stems that are typically blue-green in color, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
