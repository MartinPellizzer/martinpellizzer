Rouxia × rouxii

Rouxia × rouxii, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Rouxia splendens and Rouxia grandiflora.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia, characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features palmate leaves with a rough texture and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
