Rorippa peekelii

Rorippa peekelii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed watercress, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

The plant has alternate, linear leaves that are typically less than 2 cm in length, and it produces small, white or yellowish flowers in clusters.
