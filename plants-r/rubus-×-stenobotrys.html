Rubus × stenobotrys

Rubus × stenobotrys, commonly known as the evergreen blackberry.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and northern Baja California. The plant features thorny stems and trifoliate leaves, with white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit, a small, dark purple to black berry, is edible and has a tart flavor.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in coastal habitats.
