Rehsonia nipponica

Rehsonia nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese rehsonia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Rehsonia nipponica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
