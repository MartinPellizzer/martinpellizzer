Rutidea degemensis

Rutidea degemensis, commonly known as the Degem's burrbean, is a species of legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with well-drained soil.

Rutidea degemensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential nutritional value.
