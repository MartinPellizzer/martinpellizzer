Ribes polyanthes

Ribes polyanthes, commonly known as the yellow currant, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It produces small, yellow, edible berries that ripen in late summer. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Ribes polyanthes is valued for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local wildlife.
