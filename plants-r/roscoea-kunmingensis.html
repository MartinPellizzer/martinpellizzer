Roscoea kunmingensis

Roscoea kunmingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan turmeric, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric, and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vivid orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and are valued for their aromatic properties.

Roscoea kunmingensis is a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
