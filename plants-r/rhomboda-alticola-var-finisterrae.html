Rhomboda alticola var. finisterrae

Rhomboda alticola var. finisterrae, commonly known as the Canary Island rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid and sunny conditions of its native habitat.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and resilience in coastal environments.
