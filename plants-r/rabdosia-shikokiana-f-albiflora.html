Rabdosia shikokiana f. albiflora

Rabdosia shikokiana f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered rabdosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It is native to parts of East Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
