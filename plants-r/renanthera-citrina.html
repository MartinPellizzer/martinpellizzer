Renanthera citrina

Renanthera citrina, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers, which resemble a lady's slipper in shape. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Renanthera citrina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
