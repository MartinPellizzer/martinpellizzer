Rosa solanifolia

Rosa solanifolia, commonly known as the nightshade rose, is a unique plant that combines characteristics of both roses and nightshade plants.

It is a perennial shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a structure similar to traditional roses. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rosaceae family, which includes other roses, and is notable for its hybrid nature, blending traits from both the rose and nightshade families.
