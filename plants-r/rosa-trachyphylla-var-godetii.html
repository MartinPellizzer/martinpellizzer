Rosa trachyphylla var. godetii

Rosa trachyphylla var. godetii, commonly known as the Godetii rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
