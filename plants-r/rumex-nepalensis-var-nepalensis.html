Rumex nepalensis var. nepalensis

Rumex nepalensis var. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. Its flowers are small and greenish, arranged in clusters, and it produces triangular, edible seeds.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional cuisine.
