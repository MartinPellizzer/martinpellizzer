Rosularia adenotricha subsp. linearifolia

Rosularia adenotricha subsp.

linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaved rosebay, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
