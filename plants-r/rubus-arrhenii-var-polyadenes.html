Rubus arrhenii var. polyadenes

Rubus arrhenii var. polyadenes, commonly known as the red raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically pink to white, with five petals and numerous stamens. The fruit, a aggregate berry, is red and juicy, containing numerous small seeds.

It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is cultivated for its flavor and culinary uses.
