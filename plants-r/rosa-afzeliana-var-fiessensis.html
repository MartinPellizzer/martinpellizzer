Rosa afzeliana var. fiessensis

Rosa afzeliana var. fiessensis, commonly known as the Fiessent's rose, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is native to specific regions in Europe, particularly in the mountainous areas of the Alps. This plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers. The leaves are typically serrated and have a deep green color with a slightly hairy surface.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
