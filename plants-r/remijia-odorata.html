Remijia odorata

Remijia odorata, commonly known as the sweet sarsaparilla, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
