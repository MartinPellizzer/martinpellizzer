Rosa tomentosa var. mailleferi

Rosa tomentosa var. mailleferi, commonly known as the woolly rose or the French rose, is a cultivar of the Rosa tomentosa species.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale pink flowers and a fuzzy, grayish foliage. The plant is native to Europe and thrives in temperate climates. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and hedges.
