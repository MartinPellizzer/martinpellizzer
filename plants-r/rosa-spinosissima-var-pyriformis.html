Rosa spinosissima var. pyriformis

Rosa spinosissima var. pyriformis, commonly known as the Pyriform Rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring. Its stems are armed with sharp, thorny spines, which are characteristic of the Rosa spinosissima species.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
