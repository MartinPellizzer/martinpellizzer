Rubus × toussaintii

Rubus × toussaintii, commonly known as the blackberry- raspberry hybrid, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rubus ursinus (blackberry) and Rubus chinensis (raspberry), resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant typically has thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruit is a aggregate berry that can range in color from red to black.

This hybrid is valued for its fruit's flavor and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
