Reggeria

Reggeria, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that resemble foliage rather than true leaves. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically during the holiday season. Reggeria thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
