Rosa obtusifolia var. bergstedtii

Rosa obtusifolia var. bergstedtii, commonly known as Bergstedt's shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense, thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a mat-forming growth habit. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate gardens.
