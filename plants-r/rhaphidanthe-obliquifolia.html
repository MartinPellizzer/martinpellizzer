Rhaphidanthe obliquifolia

Rhaphidanthe obliquifolia, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its distinctive, often zigzagging stems.

Rhaphidanthe obliquifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover.
