Rosa kazarjanii

Rosa kazarjanii, commonly known as the Kazakh rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.

Rosa kazarjanii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its historical and cultural significance in the region.
