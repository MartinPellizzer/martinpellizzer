Racosperma maranoense

Racosperma maranoense, commonly known as the Maranoense Acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, bipinnate leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in dry, sandy soils.

The species is of interest in ecological studies due to its role in soil stabilization and potential medicinal properties.
