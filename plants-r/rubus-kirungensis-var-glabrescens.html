Rubus kirungensis var. glabrescens

Rubus kirungensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Kirungensis Raspberry, is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It features prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
