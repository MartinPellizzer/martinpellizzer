Ribes americanum f. mesochorum

Ribes americanum f. mesochorum, commonly known as the American gooseberry, is a cultivar of the gooseberry plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its small, tart berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is a deciduous shrub with compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in woodland edges and moist areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
