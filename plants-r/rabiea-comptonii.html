Rabiea comptonii

Rabiea comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and has adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Rabiea comptonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
