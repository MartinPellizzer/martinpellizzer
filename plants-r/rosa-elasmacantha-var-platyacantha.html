Rosa elasmacantha var. platyacantha

Rosa elasmacantha var. platyacantha, commonly known as the flat-leaf rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, woody stems with dense, thorny growth. The leaves are compound, consisting of several oval, serrated leaflets that are usually arranged in pairs. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and numerous stamens, blooming in summer.

This plant is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy ornamental species.
