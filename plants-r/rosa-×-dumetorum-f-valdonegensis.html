Rosa × dumetorum f. valdonegensis

Rosa × dumetorum f. valdonegensis, commonly known as the Valdonegros rose, is a botanical hybrid within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is typically found in mountainous regions of southern Europe. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, often with a deep red center. Its foliage is dark green and densely arranged, providing a lush appearance.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
