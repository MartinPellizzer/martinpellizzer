Rabdosia × inamii

Rabdosia × inamii, commonly known as the hybrid blue mistflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Rabdosia hirta and Rabdosia chinensis. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan, and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Rabdosia × inamii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
