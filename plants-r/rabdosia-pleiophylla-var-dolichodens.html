Rabdosia pleiophylla var. dolichodens

Rabdosia pleiophylla var. dolichodens, commonly known as the long-leaf rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a slight pubescence, while its flowers are small and clustered in whorls.

This variant is distinguished by its elongated leaves compared to the typical form of Rabdosia pleiophylla.
