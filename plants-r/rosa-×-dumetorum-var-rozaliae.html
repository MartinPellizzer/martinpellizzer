Rosa × dumetorum var. rozaliae

Rosa × dumetorum var. rozaliae, commonly known as Rozalia shrub rose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and compact shrub form, making it suitable for hedges or small garden borders. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
