Rosa sicula var. gracilens

Rosa sicula var. gracilens, commonly known as the Sicilian slender rose, is a variety of the Sicilian rose native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in pink or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its leaves are dark green and finely serrated, adding a delicate texture to its appearance.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its historical significance in horticultural traditions.
