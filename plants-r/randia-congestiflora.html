Randia congestiflora

Randia congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its most distinctive feature is its dense clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer.

Randia congestiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
