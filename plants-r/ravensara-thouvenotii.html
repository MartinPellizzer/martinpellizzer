Ravensara thouvenotii

Ravensara thouvenotii, commonly known as the New Caledonian ravenwood, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant has a distinctive bark that is often used in traditional medicine by the indigenous Kanak people.

Its fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed, and it plays an important role in the local ecosystem.
