Raphia dolichocarpa

Raphia dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-fruited raphia, is a large tropical palm native to central and western Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of many palm species. Its fruit is a long, slender fibrous capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential use in local crafts and construction.
