Rubus lasiotrichos var. blinii

Rubus lasiotrichos var. blinii, commonly known as the Blinii blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant native to eastern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy berries that ripen in late summer. The plant produces compound leaves with serrated edges and thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
