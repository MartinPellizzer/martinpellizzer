Rubus × luteipetalus

Rubus × luteipetalus, commonly known as the golden petal raspberry, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and compound leaves. Its fruit, a red or orange raspberry, is edible and often used in culinary applications.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
