Rosa tomentosa var. subsphaerica

Rosa tomentosa var. subsphaerica, commonly known as the woolly rose or spherical rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, grayish-white fuzzy hairs covering its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a leathery texture and a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
