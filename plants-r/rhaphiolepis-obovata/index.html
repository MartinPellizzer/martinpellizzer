Rhaphiolepis Obovata

Rhaphiolepis obovata is a versatile evergreen shrub prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Native to East Asia, it thrives in a variety of climates and soils, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its dense foliage and showy flowers make it both decorative and functional in landscaping. The plant's ability to tolerate pruning makes it ideal for hedges and topiaries. With its year-round appeal, Rhaphiolepis obovata remains a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
