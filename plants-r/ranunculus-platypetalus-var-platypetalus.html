Ranunculus platypetalus var. platypetalus

Ranunculus platypetalus var. platypetalus, commonly known as the flat-petaled buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features showy, yellow flowers with broad, flat petals that distinguish it from other ranunculus species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three deeply lobed leaflets, and the stems are hairy.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
