Ruschia purpureostyla

Ruschia purpureostyla, commonly known as the purple-styled Ruschia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially under stress or in full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. Its leaves are typically blue-gray, with a powdery coating that helps reduce water loss.

Ruschia purpureostyla is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
