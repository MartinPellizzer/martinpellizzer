Rosa mollidesiderata

Rosa mollidesiderata, commonly known as the velvetleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, woodland habitats. Its stems are thorny, and the flowers can range in color from white to pink and red.

Rosa mollidesiderata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
