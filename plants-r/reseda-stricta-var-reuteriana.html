Reseda stricta var. reuteriana

Reseda stricta var. reuteriana, commonly known as the narrowleaf reseda, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
