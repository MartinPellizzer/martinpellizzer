Rottboellia afzelii

Rottboellia afzelii, commonly known as African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Rottboellia afzelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
