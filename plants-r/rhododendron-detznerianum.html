Rhododendron detznerianum

Rhododendron detznerianum, commonly known as the Detzner's rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
