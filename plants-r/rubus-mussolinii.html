Rubus mussolinii

Rubus mussolinii, commonly known as the Mussolini blackberry, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, dark red berries that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has prickly stems and broad, trifoliate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its flowers are white and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.
