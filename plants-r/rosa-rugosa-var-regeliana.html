Rosa rugosa var. regeliana

Rosa rugosa var. regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's rugosa rose, is a shrub-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rugged, thorny stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. It is highly adaptable, thriving in coastal areas and well-drained soils.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and for erosion control.
