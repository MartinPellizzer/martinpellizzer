Rosa × laevigata

Rosa × laevigata, commonly known as the Chinese pink or everblooming rose, is a hybrid rose species native to China.

It is characterized by its deep pink to red flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant flowers and long blooming period.
