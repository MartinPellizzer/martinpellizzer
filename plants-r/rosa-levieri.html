Rosa levieri

Rosa levieri, commonly known as the Levieri rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub-like plant with upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and a fragrant scent. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a leathery texture.

This plant is well adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
