Rubia olivieri var. eburnea

Rubia olivieri var. eburnea, commonly known as the silky ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green or white, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its overall rugged appearance.

This species is often found in moist, woodland environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
