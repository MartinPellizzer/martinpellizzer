Rosa micrantha var. heterostyla

Rosa micrantha var. heterostyla, commonly known as the heterostyle rose, is a variation of the micrantha rose species.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with five to seven leaflets, and the stems are often covered in fine, white hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
