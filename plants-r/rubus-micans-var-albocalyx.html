Rubus micans var. albocalyx

Rubus micans var. albocalyx, commonly known as the white-flowered raspberry.

It is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its white or pale pink flowers, which distinguish it from the more typical red-flowered varieties. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to parts of Asia, particularly China. It produces small, tart berries similar to other raspberries, though its flavor profile may vary.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
