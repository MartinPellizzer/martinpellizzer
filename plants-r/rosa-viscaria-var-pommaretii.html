Rosa viscaria var. pommaretii

Rosa viscaria var. pommaretii, commonly known as the Pommaret's evergreen rose, is a compact, shrubby plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that remains evergreen throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers in spring and early summer. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and coastal landscapes.
