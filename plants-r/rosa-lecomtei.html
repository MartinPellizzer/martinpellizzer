Rosa lecomtei

Rosa lecomtei, commonly known as Lecomte's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the United States, specifically found in the southeastern states. The plant is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. Its flowers are pink to red in color, with five petals, and bloom in the spring.

The foliage is dark green and aromatic, with serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.
