Rubus ichangensis var. latifolius

Rubus ichangensis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf Chinese raspberry, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
