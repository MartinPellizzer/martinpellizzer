Rhaphiolepis bengalensis f. contracta

Rhaphiolepis bengalensis f. contracta, commonly known as the compact rhaphiolepis, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to India.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green color, providing a dense foliage effect. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for container gardening or as a hedge due to its controlled growth habit.

Its ornamental value lies in its dense foliage and seasonal floral display.
