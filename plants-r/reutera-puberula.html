Reutera puberula

Reutera puberula, commonly known as the silky reutera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth margin and a pubescent surface.

Reutera puberula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
