Rosa eglanteria f. pauciflora

Rosa eglanteria f. pauciflora, commonly known as the prickly rose, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, hairy rosehip that matures in autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
