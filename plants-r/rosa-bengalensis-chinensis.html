Rosa bengalensis chinensis

Rosa bengalensis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has a shrub-like structure with thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. Its botanical name reflects its geographical origin and species classification.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
