Rosa × maasii

Rosa × maasii, commonly known as the Maasai rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and dense, thorny stems. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa × maasii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
