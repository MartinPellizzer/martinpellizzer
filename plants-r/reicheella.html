Reicheella

Reicheella, commonly known as the "false orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their small, delicate flowers and are native to various regions in South America. Reicheella species typically grow in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. They have rhizomatous growth habits and produce showy, often fragrant blooms.
