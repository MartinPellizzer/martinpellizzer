Rubus holorhodos

Rubus holorhodos, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits, known as raspberries, which are typically red when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Rubus holorhodos is often found in woodland edges, thickets, and disturbed sites.
