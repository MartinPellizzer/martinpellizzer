Romulea rosea var. speciosa

Romulea rosea var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features elegant, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The flowers typically bloom in late summer, adding a touch of elegance to gardens. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a desirable addition to rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
