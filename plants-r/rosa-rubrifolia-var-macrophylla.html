Rosa rubrifolia var. macrophylla

Rosa rubrifolia var. macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf redleaf rose, is a hybrid rose species characterized by its distinctive foliage and floral features.

It is native to China and is known for its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are broad and leathery, often with a reddish hue, which contributes to its ornamental value. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its disease resistance and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
