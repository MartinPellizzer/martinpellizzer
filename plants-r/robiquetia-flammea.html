Robiquetia flammea

Robiquetia flammea, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a dramatic flair to its natural habitat. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or grayish tint. Its bulbs are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

Robiquetia flammea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
