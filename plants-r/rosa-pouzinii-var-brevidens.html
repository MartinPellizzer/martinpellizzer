Rosa pouzinii var. brevidens

Rosa pouzinii var. brevidens, commonly known as the short-stemmed shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features upright, thorny stems and is characterized by its small, fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and the plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness, thriving in full sun and requiring minimal water once established.
