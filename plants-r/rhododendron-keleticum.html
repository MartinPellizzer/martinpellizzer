Rhododendron keleticum

Rhododendron keleticum, commonly known as the Keletic rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron keleticum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in specific, moist conditions.
