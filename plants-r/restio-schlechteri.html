Restio schlechteri

Restio schlechteri, commonly known as the slender restio, is a grass-like plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale yellow flowers.
