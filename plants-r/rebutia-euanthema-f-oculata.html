Rebutia euanthema f. oculata

Rebutia euanthema f. oculata, commonly known as the "Eye of the Moon" cactus, is a unique hybrid belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and the presence of prominent, white tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant features a dense covering of spines, which are usually pale yellow to brown in color. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-white, blooming in late spring or early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
