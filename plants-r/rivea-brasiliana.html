Rivea brasiliana

Rivea brasiliana, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that open in the morning and close at dusk. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Rivea brasiliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
