Rumex sellowianus

Rumex sellowianus, commonly known as the Sellow's dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible rhubarb-like leaves. Rumex sellowianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
