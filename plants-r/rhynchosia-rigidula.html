Rhynchosia rigidula

Rhynchosia rigidula, commonly known as the rigid false pigeon pea, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, woody stems and trifoliate leaves.
