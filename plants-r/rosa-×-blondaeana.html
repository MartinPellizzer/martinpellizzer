Rosa × blondaeana

Rosa × blondaeana, commonly known as the French pink or the pink musk rose, is a hybrid shrub native to France.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Its flowers are often used in perfumery due to their delicate scent.

Rosa × blondaeana prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
