Rosa jundzilliana var. aspreticola

Rosa jundzilliana var. aspreticola, commonly known as the aspreticola rose, is a rare and ornamental shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dark green, velvety leaves and produces striking pink to red flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its rarity, it is often sought after by horticultural enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
