Rhopalocarpus alternifolius

Rhopalocarpus alternifolius, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its alternate, linear leaves that often exhibit a zigzag pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Rhopalocarpus alternifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
