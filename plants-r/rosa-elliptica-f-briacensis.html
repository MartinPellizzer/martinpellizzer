Rosa elliptica f. briacensis

Rosa elliptica f. briacensis, commonly known as the Briacensis rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the coastal regions of Brittany, France.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are elliptical, with a slightly serrated edge, and the plant has a dense, bushy growth habit. It is well adapted to sandy, coastal soils and can tolerate salt spray.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in maritime climates.
