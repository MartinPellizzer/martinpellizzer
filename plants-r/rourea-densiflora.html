Rourea densiflora

Rourea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered rourea, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Rourea densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
