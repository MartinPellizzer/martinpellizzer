Rabdosia longituba var. contracta

Rabdosia longituba var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often pubescent. Its flowers are typically lilac or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
