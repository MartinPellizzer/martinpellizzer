Rhodostachys urbaniana

Rhodostachys urbaniana, commonly known as the Urbanian rose.

It is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a serrated edge, and the stems are often covered in fine hairs.
