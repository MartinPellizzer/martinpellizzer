Rosa alpina var. latifolia

Rosa alpina var. latifolia, commonly known as the Alpine rose or broadleaf alpine rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia, typically growing in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are broad and lobed, often with a silvery underside, and it has a compact, shrub-like growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
