Riedelia macranthoides

Riedelia macranthoides, commonly known as the large-flowered riedelia, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red. Riedelia macranthoides thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticultural collections.
