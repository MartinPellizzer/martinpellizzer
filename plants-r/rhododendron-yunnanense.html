Rhododendron yunnanense

Rhododendron yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are often dark green and have a rough texture. Its flowers are showy, with five to seven petals and come in shades of pink, red, and white.

Rhododendron yunnanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
