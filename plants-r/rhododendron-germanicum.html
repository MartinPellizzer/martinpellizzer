Rhododendron germanicum

Rhododendron germanicum, commonly known as the German rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers in spring. The plant belongs to the Ericaceae family and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
