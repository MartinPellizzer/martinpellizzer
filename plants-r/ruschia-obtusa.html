Ruschia obtusa

Ruschia obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the warmer months. It is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
