Rosa × grovesii

Rosa × grovesii, commonly known as the San Diego rose, is a hybrid plant native to California.

It is characterized by its deep red to purple flowers and thorny stems. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal climates.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
