Ruschia orientalis

Ruschia orientalis, commonly known as the fireplant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
