Rauvolfia nukuhivensis

Rauvolfia nukuhivensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian rauvolfia, is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink.
