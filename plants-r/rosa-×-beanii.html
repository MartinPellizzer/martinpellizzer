Rosa × beanii

Rosa × beanii, commonly known as the Canadian rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and stipules, which are common in many rose species.

Rosa × beanii is native to Canada and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
