Rosa × dumetorum subf. gradskoensis

Rosa × dumetorum subf.

gradskoensis, commonly known as the Gradskoensis rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, often forming shrubs that are well-suited for ornamental use.

The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges.

This subspecies is native to the Balkan Peninsula and is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions.
