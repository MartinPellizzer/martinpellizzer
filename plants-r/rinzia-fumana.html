Rinzia fumana

Rinzia fumana, commonly known as the smoke rinzia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows in heathland and forest margins, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Rinzia fumana is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in coastal and subcoastal regions.
