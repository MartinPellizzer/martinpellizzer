Romulea bulbocodium var. debilis

Romulea bulbocodium var. debilis, commonly known as the slender romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive, spurred petal. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its bulbs are adapted for survival in arid conditions, allowing it to thrive in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
