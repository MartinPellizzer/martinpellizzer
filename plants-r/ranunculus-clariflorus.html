Ranunculus clariflorus

Ranunculus clariflorus, commonly known as the clearflower buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces showy, yellow flowers with five to eight petals. Its flowers are often accompanied by several sepals and numerous stamens.

Ranunculus clariflorus is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
