Racosperma grandifolium

Racosperma grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaved acacia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, open habitats. The plant features compound leaves with multiple small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
