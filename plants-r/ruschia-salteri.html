Ruschia salteri

Ruschia salteri, commonly known as the Salter's Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
