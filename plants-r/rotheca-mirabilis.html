Rotheca mirabilis

Rotheca mirabilis, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant grows as a shrub, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rotheca mirabilis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
