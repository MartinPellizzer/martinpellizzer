Rubus gremblichii

Rubus gremblichii, commonly known as the Gremblich raspberry, is a species of brambles native to South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and less-studied species within the Rubus genus.
