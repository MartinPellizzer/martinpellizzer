Rhaphidophora koidzumii

Rhaphidophora koidzumii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant features a rhizomatous stem system, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Rhaphidophora koidzumii thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil.
