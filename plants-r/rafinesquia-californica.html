Rafinesquia californica

Rafinesquia californica, commonly known as the California globemallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a tall, branching stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Rafinesquia californica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
