Rhamnus chiennanensis

Rhamnus chiennanensis, commonly known as the Chiennan boxthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy berries. It is typically found in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
