Rhaphidophora bonii

Rhaphidophora bonii, commonly known as the Bonii arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time.

Rhaphidophora bonii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
