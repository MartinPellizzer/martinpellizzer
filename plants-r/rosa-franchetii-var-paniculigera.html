Rosa franchetii var. paniculigera

Rosa franchetii var. paniculigera, commonly known as the Chinese rose or panicled Rosa, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant produces oval, yellowish fruits known as hips, which are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
