Rafnia capensis subsp. ovata

Rafnia capensis subsp.

ovata, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into woody, clustered fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and riverine forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
