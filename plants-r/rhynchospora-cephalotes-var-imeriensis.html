Rhynchospora cephalotes var. imeriensis

Rhynchospora cephalotes var. imeriensis, commonly known as the Imeriense spikegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of South America.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically brown or purple in color, arranged in spikelets along the stem.
