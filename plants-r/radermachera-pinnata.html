Radermachera pinnata

Radermachera pinnata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

Radermachera pinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
