Rosa granulata

Rosa granulata, commonly known as the granulate rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive granular texture on the petals.

Rosa granulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
