Reaumuria hypericoides f. hyrcanica

Reaumuria hypericoides f. hyrcanica, commonly known as the Hyrcanian reaumuria, is a succulent shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its adaptability to extreme climates makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and morphological traits compared to other forms of Reaumuria hypericoides.
