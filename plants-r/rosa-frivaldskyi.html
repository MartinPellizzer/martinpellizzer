Rosa frivaldskyi

Rosa frivaldskyi, commonly known as the Frivaldsky's rose, is a rare and critically endangered species native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate pink flowers and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils. Its limited distribution and habitat specificity make it highly vulnerable to environmental changes.

Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical species.
