Rosa rydbergii

Rosa rydbergii, commonly known as Rydberg's rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in areas of California and Nevada. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with thorny stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Rosa rydbergii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
