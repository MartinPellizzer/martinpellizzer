Ruschia brevicyma

Ruschia brevicyma, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
