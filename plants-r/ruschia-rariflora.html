Ruschia rariflora

Ruschia rariflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water. It produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers during the warmer months.

Due to its low water requirements and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
