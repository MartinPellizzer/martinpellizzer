Rosa cinnamomea var. multiplex

Rosa cinnamomea var. multiplex, commonly known as the Chinese pink or cinnamon rose, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its soft, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle cinnamon-like scent. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and fragrant aroma.
