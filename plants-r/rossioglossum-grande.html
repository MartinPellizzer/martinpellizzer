Rossioglossum grande

Rossioglossum grande, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, displaying vibrant colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture to reduce water loss.

Rossioglossum grande is a popular choice for collectors due to its ornamental value and unique floral display.
