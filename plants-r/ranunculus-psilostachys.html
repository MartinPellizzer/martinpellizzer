Ranunculus psilostachys

Ranunculus psilostachys, commonly known as the narrowleaf buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to regions of Asia, including China and Korea.

It features delicate, palmate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and the plant is noted for its ornamental value in gardens.

Ranunculus psilostachys is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
