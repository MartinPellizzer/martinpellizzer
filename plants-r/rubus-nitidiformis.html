Rubus nitidiformis

Rubus nitidiformis, commonly known as the shining formfruit raspberry.

It is a species of brambles native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges and a hairy texture.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or coastal areas.
