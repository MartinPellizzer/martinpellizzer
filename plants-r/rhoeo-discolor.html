Rhoeo discolor

Rhoeo discolor, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
