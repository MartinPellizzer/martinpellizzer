Ruellia subringens

Ruellia subringens, commonly known as the silverleaf ruellia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Ruellia subringens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
