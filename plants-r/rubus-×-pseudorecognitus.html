Rubus × pseudorecognitus

Rubus × pseudorecognitus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruit develops into a dark red or black berry.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and edible fruits, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
