Roegneria Czimganica Var. Glabrispicula

Roegneria czimganica var. glabrispicula is a lesser-known grass species native to specific regions of Central Asia. This variant is distinguished by its smooth, glabrous spikelets and compact growth habit. It thrives in dry, rocky environments where few plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution, it remains a subject of botanical interest and conservation concern. Understanding this plant offers insights into the adaptability of grasses in extreme climates.
