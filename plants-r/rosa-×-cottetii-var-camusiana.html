Rosa × cottetii var. camusiana

Rosa × cottetii var. camusiana, commonly known as the Camusiana rose, is a hybrid shrub native to France.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage, with dark green leaves that have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Rosa × cottetii var. camusiana prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
