Rosa reuteri f. morthieri

Rosa reuteri f. morthieri, commonly known as the Morthier's rose, is a compact, shrub-like species native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is characterized by its dense growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Rosa reuteri f. morthieri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
