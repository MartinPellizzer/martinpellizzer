Rabdosia silvatica

Rabdosia silvatica, commonly known as Silvatic Rabdosia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters.
