Rourea pallens

Rourea pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
