Rhododendron oresterum

Rhododendron oresterum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to China and is part of the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. It has moderate growth habits and can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

Rhododendron oresterum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
