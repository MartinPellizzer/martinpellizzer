Rubus hirtus proles kaltenbachii

Rubus hirtus proles kaltenbachii, commonly known as the Kaltenbach's bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in temperate climates and is typically found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
