Rosa × dumetorum var. schwarziana

Rosa × dumetorum var. schwarziana, commonly known as the Schwarziana rose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its strong, thorny stems and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant. It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
