Rubus perneggensis

Rubus perneggensis, commonly known as the Pernegg raspberry, is a species of bramble native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges and shrublands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
