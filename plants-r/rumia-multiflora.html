Rumia multiflora

Rumia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered rumia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Rumia multiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
