Rosa pomifera f. typica

Rosa pomifera f. typica, commonly known as the hairy rose or the Chinese rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa genus.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its hardy nature, it can thrive in a variety of climates and soil conditions.
