Ruta japonica

Ruta japonica, commonly known as Japanese rue, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnate leaves and small yellowish-green flowers. The plant contains compounds such as rutin and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal and pesticidal properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ruta japonica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.
