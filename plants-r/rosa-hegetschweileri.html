Rosa hegetschweileri

Rosa hegetschweileri, commonly known as the Hegetschweiler rose, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is known for its cold hardiness. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance.

Rosa hegetschweileri is also valued for its role in the cultivation of Rosa chinensis, a closely related species.
