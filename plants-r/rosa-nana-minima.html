Rosa nana minima

Rosa nana minima, commonly known as the miniature rock rose, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a reddish tinge. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and produces fragrant blooms throughout the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky soils and dry conditions, making it a hardy perennial.

Rosa nana minima is popular in rock gardens and alpine settings due to its ornamental value and resilience.
