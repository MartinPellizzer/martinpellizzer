Rosularia pisidica

Rosularia pisidica, commonly known as the Crimean rose.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Crimean Peninsula. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months.

Rosularia pisidica is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.
