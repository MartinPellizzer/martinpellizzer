Rosa × condaminea

Rosa × condaminea, commonly known as the Condaminea rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double to fully double flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. Its foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges.

Rosa × condaminea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and cold hardiness.
