Rhodospatha blanda subsp. melinonii

Rhodospatha blanda subsp.

melinonii, commonly known as the Melinon's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
