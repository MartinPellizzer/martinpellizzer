Rhynchosia burkei

Rhynchosia burkei, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive spiny pods that contain edible seeds. The plant thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Rhynchosia burkei is valued for its nutritional seeds and has been used in traditional agriculture and medicine.
