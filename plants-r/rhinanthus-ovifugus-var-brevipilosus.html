Rhinanthus ovifugus var. brevipilosus

Rhinanthus ovifugus var. brevipilosus, commonly known as the short-hairy yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.
