Ranunculus reniformis

Ranunculus reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features distinctive kidney-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils along stream banks and wet meadows. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ranunculus reniformis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
