Rubus nitidus f. ferocissimus

Rubus nitidus f. ferocissimus, commonly known as the fierce form of the glossy blackberry, is a variant of the Rubus genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and dark, glossy berries. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are highly sought after for their intense flavor. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
