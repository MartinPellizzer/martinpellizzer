Rosa sjuniki

Rosa sjuniki, commonly known as the Swedish rose, is a species native to northern Europe.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa sjuniki is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
