Ranunculus degenii f. glabratus

Ranunculus degenii f. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved buttercup, is a variant of the Ranunculus degenii species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

The f. glabratus form is distinguished by its smoother, less hairy foliage compared to the typical form.
