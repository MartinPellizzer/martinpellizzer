Rosa micrantha var. conferta

Rosa micrantha var. conferta, commonly known as the dense-flowered rose, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often appreciated in horticultural settings.
