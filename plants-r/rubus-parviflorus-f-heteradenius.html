Rubus parviflorus f. heteradenius

Rubus parviflorus f. heteradenius, commonly known as the small-flowered raspberry.

This plant is a flowering shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. The leaves are usually trifoliate, with serrated edges, and the flowers are white with five petals.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland edges or disturbed soils.
