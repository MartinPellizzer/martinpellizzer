Reseda microcarpa

Reseda microcarpa, commonly known as dwarf madder, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. Reseda microcarpa is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in dry, sunny conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
