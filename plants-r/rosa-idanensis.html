Rosa idanensis

Rosa idanensis, commonly known as the Idanensis rose, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to the arid and rocky conditions of its native habitat.

Rosa idanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
