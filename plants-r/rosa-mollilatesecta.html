Rosa mollilatesecta

Rosa mollilatesecta, commonly known as the soft-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lobed and have a soft, fuzzy texture, contributing to its unique appearance.

Rosa mollilatesecta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and mild fragrance.
