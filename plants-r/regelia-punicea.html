Regelia punicea

Regelia punicea, commonly known as the red regelia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and its ability to produce vibrant red flowers in late winter or early spring. The plant features thick, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. Regelia punicea thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and seasonal flowering.
