Rhododendron jasminiflorum var. oblongifolium

Rhododendron jasminiflorum var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the oblong-leaved jasmine rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink, often resembling jasmine blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
