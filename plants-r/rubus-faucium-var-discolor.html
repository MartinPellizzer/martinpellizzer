Rubus faucium var. discolor

Rubus faucium var. discolor, commonly known as the variable raspberry.

This variant is characterized by its distinctive discoloration in its foliage, which can range from light green to pale yellow. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of North America. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
