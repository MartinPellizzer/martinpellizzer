Rhodiola phariensis

Rhodiola phariensis, commonly known as the Tibetan rhodiola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. Rhodiola phariensis is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in Tibetan medicine.
