Rosa × schleicheri

Rosa × schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's rose, is a hybrid rose species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically reaches heights of 1 to 2 meters and has dark green, glossy leaves with serrated edges. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Rosa × schleicheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
