Rosa Spinosissima Var. Leiostyla

Rosa spinosissima var. leiostyla is a rare and striking member of the rose family known for its unique floral characteristics. This variety is distinguished by its pale pink petals and elongated, slender leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soil. Its spiny stems and compact growth make it a resilient plant, suitable for both ornamental and ecological purposes. Due to its limited distribution, it is often considered a rare and valuable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
