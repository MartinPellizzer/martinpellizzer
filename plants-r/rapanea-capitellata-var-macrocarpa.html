Rapanea capitellata var. macrocarpa

Rapanea capitellata var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited rapanea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that are followed by dark blue, fleshy fruits. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruiting structure.
