Rosa × Klasterskii

Rosa × klasterskii is a unique hybrid rose known for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature. This plant is a cross between Rosa rugosa and Rosa centifolia, combining the best traits of both parent species. It thrives in a variety of climates and is popular among gardeners for its fragrance and resilience. Its flowers come in shades of pink and red, adding beauty to gardens throughout the growing season. Despite its ornamental appeal, it also serves as a valuable resource for pollinators and wildlife.
