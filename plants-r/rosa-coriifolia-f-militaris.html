Rosa coriifolia f. militaris

Rosa coriifolia f. militaris, commonly known as the military rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin, and its flowers are typically pink to red, appearing in clusters along the stems. The plant is native to China and is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardy nature.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for garden landscapes.
