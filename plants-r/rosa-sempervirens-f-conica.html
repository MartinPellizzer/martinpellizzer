Rosa sempervirens f. conica

Rosa sempervirens f. conica, commonly known as the upright pink rose, is a compact, shrubby plant with dense, upright growth.

It features glossy, dark green foliage and produces showy, single or semi-double pink flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is a hardy species, suitable for garden borders or as a container plant.

Its conical shape and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
