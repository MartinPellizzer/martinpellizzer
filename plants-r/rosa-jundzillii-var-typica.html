Rosa jundzillii var. typica

Rosa jundzillii var. typica, commonly known as the Jundzillii rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the blooms.

It is a hardy shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
