Rhodax Orientalis

Rhodax orientalis is a rare and striking plant native to the eastern regions of the Mediterranean. It is known for its vibrant pink and white flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires full sun to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered in its natural range. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
