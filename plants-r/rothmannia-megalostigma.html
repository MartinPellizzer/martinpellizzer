Rothmannia megalostigma

Rothmannia megalostigma, commonly known as the giant rothmannia, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, featuring vibrant yellow petals with a distinctive red throat. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Rothmannia megalostigma thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
