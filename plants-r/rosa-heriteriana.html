Rosa heriteriana

Rosa heriteriana, commonly known as the Heritage Rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its shrub-like growth habit features thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves.

Rosa heriteriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
