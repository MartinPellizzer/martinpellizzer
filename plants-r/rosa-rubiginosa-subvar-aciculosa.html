Rosa rubiginosa subvar. aciculosa

Rosa rubiginosa subvar. aciculosa, commonly known as the thorny shrub rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant produces rose hips that are orange-red and contain a high concentration of vitamin C. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for hedging and ornamental planting.
