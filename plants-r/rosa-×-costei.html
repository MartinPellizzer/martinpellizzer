Rosa × costei

Rosa × costei, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, thornless stems and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. The plant is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, glossy leaves.

Rosa × costei is well-suited to temperate climates and is often used in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
