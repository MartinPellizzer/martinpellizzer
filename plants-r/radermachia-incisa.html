Radermachia incisa

Radermachia incisa, commonly known as the silver leaf radermachia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often ribbed and covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
