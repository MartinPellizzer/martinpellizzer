Rubus × prechtelsbaueri

Rubus × prechtelsbaueri, commonly known as the prechtelsbauer raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats. Its berries are usually red or yellow, containing multiple small seeds.

It is valued for both ornamental purposes and as a source of edible fruit in certain regions.
