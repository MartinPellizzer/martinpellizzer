Rosa austriaca f. calida

Rosa austriaca f. calida, commonly known as the Austrian rose, is a cold-hardy shrub native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is well adapted to temperate climates and can thrive in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping in cooler regions.
