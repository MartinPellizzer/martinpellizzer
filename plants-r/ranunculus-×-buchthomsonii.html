Ranunculus × buchthomsonii

Ranunculus × buchthomsonii, commonly known as the Buchthomson's buttercup, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features delicate, palmately lobed leaves and grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or alpine habitats.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
