Rosa jundzillii f. serrata

Rosa jundzillii f. serrata, commonly known as the serrated Rosa jundzillii, is a cultivar of the species Rosa jundzillii, which is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

This plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink and red. The leaves are compound, with serrated edges that give the plant its distinguishing feature. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in cooler climates.
