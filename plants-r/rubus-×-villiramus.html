Rubus × villiramus

Rubus × villiramus, commonly known as the hybrid blackberry raspberry.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces edible fruits with a unique combination of blackberry and raspberry characteristics. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and the flowers are pink to white in color.

The fruit develops from the flower and is usually harvested in late summer.
