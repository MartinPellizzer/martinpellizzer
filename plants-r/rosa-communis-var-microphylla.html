Rosa communis var. microphylla

Rosa communis var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature rose, is a compact shrub with small, delicate leaves and fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit, making it suitable for borders or containers. The plant produces pink, red, or white flowers throughout the growing season, adding color to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, flowering plant.
