Rhododendron haofui

Rhododendron haofui, commonly known as the Guangxi rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically the Guangxi region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Rhododendron haofui is often found in moist, acidic soils in mountainous areas, thriving in partial shade.
