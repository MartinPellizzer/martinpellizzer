Rosa polita

Rosa polita, commonly known as the smooth rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its smooth, thornless stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, compound, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Rosa polita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
