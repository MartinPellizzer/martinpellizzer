Rhododendron × watereri

Rhododendron × watereri, commonly known as the Himalayan rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with broad, leathery leaves that provide a lush green backdrop. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant floral displays.
