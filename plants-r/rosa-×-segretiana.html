Rosa × segretiana

Rosa × segretiana, commonly known as the Segretiana rose, is a hybrid species within the genus Rosa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep pink to crimson. The plant is a deciduous shrub, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. It originates from a cross between Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia, resulting in a plant with strong fragrance and good disease resistance.

This rose is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and is valued for its ornate blooms and hardy nature.
