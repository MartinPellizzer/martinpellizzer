Rungia laxiflora

Rungia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered rungia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
