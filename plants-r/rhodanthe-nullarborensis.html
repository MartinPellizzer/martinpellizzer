Rhodanthe nullarborensis

Rhodanthe nullarborensis, commonly known as the Nullarbor Banksia, is a flowering plant native to the southern coast of Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense cylindrical inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand strong coastal winds and salt spray.

Rhodanthe nullarborensis plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for native pollinators.
