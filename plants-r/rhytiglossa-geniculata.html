Rhytiglossa geniculata

Rhytiglossa geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and striking, colorful flowers that range from red to orange. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
