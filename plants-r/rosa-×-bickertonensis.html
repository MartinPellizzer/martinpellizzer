Rosa × bickertonensis

Rosa × bickertonensis, commonly known as the Bickerton's rose, is a hybrid species of rose native to the United Kingdom.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features thorny stems and pinnate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
