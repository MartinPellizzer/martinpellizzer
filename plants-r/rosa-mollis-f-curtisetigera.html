Rosa mollis f. curtisetigera

Rosa mollis f. curtisetigera, commonly known as the soft rose or cushion rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a slightly darker hue at the base. The plant has dense, upright growth with dark green, glossy leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a decorative plant in borders due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Rosa mollis f. curtisetigera is generally hardy and requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
