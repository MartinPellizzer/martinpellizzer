Rubroshorea Parvifolia

Rubroshorea parvifolia is a rare and striking plant known for its vivid red foliage and delicate pink flowers. Native to the dense, misty forests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ecological significance. Its small size and ornamental qualities make it a valuable addition to specialized gardens. Despite its beauty, Rubroshorea parvifolia remains under-studied and is at risk due to habitat loss and over-collection.
