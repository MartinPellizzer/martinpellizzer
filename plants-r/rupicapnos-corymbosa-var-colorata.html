Rupicapnos corymbosa var. colorata

Rupicapnos corymbosa var. colorata, commonly known as the colorata form of the evergreen shrub, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The foliage is typically dark green, with some cultivars displaying variegated leaf patterns. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
