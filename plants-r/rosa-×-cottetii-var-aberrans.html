Rosa × cottetii var. aberrans

Rosa × cottetii var. aberrans, commonly known as the Alpes-Rosa, is a hybrid rose species native to the Alps.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, deep red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces fragrant, semi-double blooms. It is well-suited for rock gardens and alpine settings due to its cold hardiness.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
