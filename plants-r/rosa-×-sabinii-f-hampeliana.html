Rosa × sabinii f. hampeliana

Rosa × sabinii f. hampeliana, commonly known as the Hampeliana rose, is a compact, floriferous hybrid rose with a dense growth habit.

It features dark green, glossy foliage and produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant is noted for its strong disease resistance and adaptability to various soil types. It blooms repeatedly throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vigorous growth and showy display of blooms.
