Rhododendron Kiusianum

Kiusi rhododendron, scientifically known as Rhododendron kiusianum, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Japan. It is celebrated for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to forested landscapes. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in the understory of temperate rainforests. With its dense foliage and showy blooms, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and horticultural collections. Due to its limited natural range, conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique species.
