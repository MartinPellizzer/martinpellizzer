Rosa medioxima

Rosa medioxima, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.

Rosa medioxima is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
