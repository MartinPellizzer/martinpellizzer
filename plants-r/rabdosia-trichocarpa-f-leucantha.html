Rabdosia trichocarpa f. leucantha

Rabdosia trichocarpa f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered rabdosia, is a variant of the species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy inflorescences. The plant typically features opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its visual appeal in gardens.
