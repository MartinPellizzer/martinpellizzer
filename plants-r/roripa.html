Roripa

Roripa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Rhamnaceae.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.
