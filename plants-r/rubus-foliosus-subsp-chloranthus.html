Rubus foliosus subsp. chloranthus

Rubus foliosus subsp.

chloranthus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered raspberry, is a shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, aggregate fruits.

The subspecies is distinguished by its yellowish flowers and slightly different leaf morphology compared to other Rubus species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
