Rhododendron × cunninghamii

Rhododendron × cunninghamii, commonly known as the Cunningham's rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
