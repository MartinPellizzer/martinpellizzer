Reichardia albanica

Reichardia albanica, commonly known as the Albanian daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Reichardia albanica is often found in open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.
