Rosa gallica var. baldensis

Rosa gallica var. baldensis, commonly known as the Baldens Rose, is a rare and ancient variety of the Gallica rose.

It is characterized by its deep red, semi-double flowers and its compact, shrub-like growth habit. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils. The plant is known for its strong fragrance and historical use in perfumery and medicinal applications.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical conservation efforts.
