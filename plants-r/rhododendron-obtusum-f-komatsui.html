Rhododendron obtusum f. komatsui

Rhododendron obtusum f. komatsui, commonly known as the Komatsui rhododendron, is a compact, broad-leaved evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a leathery texture and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.

The species is notable for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
