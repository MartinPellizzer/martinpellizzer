Rhododendron kaempferi var. komatsui

Rhododendron kaempferi var. komatsui, commonly known as the Komatsui azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are densely packed along the stems. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
