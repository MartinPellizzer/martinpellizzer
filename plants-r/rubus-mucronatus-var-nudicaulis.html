Rubus mucronatus var. nudicaulis

Rubus mucronatus var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender raspberry, is a shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Botanically, it is distinguished by its unique floral structure and the presence of bracts beneath its flowers.
