Rhododendron bellissimum

Rhododendron bellissimum, commonly known as the beautiful rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on the upper surface. Its flowers are showy, with five to seven petals, often in shades of pink or red.

Rhododendron bellissimum thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.
