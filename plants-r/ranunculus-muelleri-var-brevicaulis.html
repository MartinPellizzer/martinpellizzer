Ranunculus muelleri var. brevicaulis

Ranunculus muelleri var. brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features delicate, palmately divided leaves and showy, yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has a fibrous root system and is characterized by its short, compact stem.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Ranunculaceae family.
