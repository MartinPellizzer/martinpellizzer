Rosa livescens var. speciosa

Rosa livescens var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy evergreen rose, is a hybrid rose species native to South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which remains lush throughout the year, and its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It produces fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable addition to botanical gardens and cultivated landscapes.
