Rubus floribundus var. floribundus

Rubus floribundus var. floribundus, commonly known as the evergreen bramble, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruits are aggregate berries that ripen to red or black in summer.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
