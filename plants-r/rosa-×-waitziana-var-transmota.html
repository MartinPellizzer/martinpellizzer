Rosa × waitziana var. transmota

Rosa × waitziana var. transmota, commonly known as the Transmotal rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and hardy nature.
