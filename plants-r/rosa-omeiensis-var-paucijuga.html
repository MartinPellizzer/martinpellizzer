Rosa omeiensis var. paucijuga

Rosa omeiensis var. paucijuga, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the everblooming rose, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is well adapted to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
