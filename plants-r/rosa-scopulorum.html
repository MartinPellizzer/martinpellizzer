Rosa scopulorum

Rosa scopulorum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or coastal environments. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Rosa scopulorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
