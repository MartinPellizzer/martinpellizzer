Ruttya

Ruttya, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and milky sap. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its branching structure and small, inconspicuous flowers. Ruttya is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
