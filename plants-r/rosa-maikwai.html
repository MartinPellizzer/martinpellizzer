Rosa maikwai

Rosa maikwai, commonly known as the Maikwai rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Rosa maikwai thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
