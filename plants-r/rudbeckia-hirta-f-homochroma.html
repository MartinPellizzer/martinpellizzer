Rudbeckia hirta f. homochroma

Rudbeckia hirta f. homochroma, commonly known as the smooth coneflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and lack the hairy texture typical of the species. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and orange-red ray florets, blooming from late summer to early fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it suitable for borders, rock gardens, and wildflower mixes.
