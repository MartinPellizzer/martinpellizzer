Rhynchospora laxa var. minor

Rhynchospora laxa var. minor, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered flower spikes. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, acidic soils.
