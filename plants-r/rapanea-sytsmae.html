Rapanea sytsmae

Rapanea sytsmae, commonly known as the Sierra Madre holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Mexico, specifically the Sierra Madre Occidental. This plant is often found in shaded, moist habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native range.
