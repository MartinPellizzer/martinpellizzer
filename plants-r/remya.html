Remya

Remya, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible fruits resembling small, round berries. Remya thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden use.
