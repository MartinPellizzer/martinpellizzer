Rosa elliptica var. schulzei

Rosa elliptica var. schulzei, commonly known as the Schulze's elliptic rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features upright, thornless stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant pink flowers in late spring. The leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy green surface and serrated edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscaping.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in well-drained soil.
