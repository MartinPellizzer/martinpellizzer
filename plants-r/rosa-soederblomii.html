Rosa soederblomii

Rosa soederblomii, commonly known as the Soederblom rose, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa soederblomii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
