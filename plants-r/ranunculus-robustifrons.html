Ranunculus robustifrons

Ranunculus robustifrons, commonly known as the robust fronded buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to parts of southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant is characterized by its delicate, nodding yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy, glandular stem. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough, hairy texture that helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Ranunculus robustifrons is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique foliage.
