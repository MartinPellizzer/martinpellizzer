Rubus sikkimensis

Rubus sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim raspberry, is a species of bramble native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its prickly stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
