Rhaphiostylis zenkeri

Rhaphiostylis zenkeri, commonly known as the Zanzibar starflower, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in humid, coastal climates and is often found growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
