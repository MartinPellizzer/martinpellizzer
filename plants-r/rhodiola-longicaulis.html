Rhodiola longicaulis

Rhodiola longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed rhodiola, is a perennial plant native to the cold, mountainous regions of Asia.

Rhodiola longicaulis is valued for its adaptability to harsh conditions and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
