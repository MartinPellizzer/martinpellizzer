Rourea pervilleana

Rourea pervilleana, commonly known as the velvetleaf rourea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Rourea pervilleana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an attractive species for horticultural use in tropical and subtropical gardens.
