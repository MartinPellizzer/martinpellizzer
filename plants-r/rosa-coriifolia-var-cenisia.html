Rosa coriifolia var. cenisia

Rosa coriifolia var. cenisia, commonly known as the Chinese pink or pink sand rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink flowers, which bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has a compact growth habit with thorny stems and small, ovate leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and have a delicate, almost translucent appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens due to its unique floral display.
