Rapuntium flexuosum

Rapuntium flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible rapunculus, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in racemes, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.
