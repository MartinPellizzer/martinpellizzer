Riencourtia tenuifolia

Riencourtia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
