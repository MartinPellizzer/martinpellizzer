Rosa moschata var. rodnensis

Rosa moschata var. rodnensis, commonly known as the Rodnenskaya rose, is a rare and ancient variety of the musk rose.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, slightly hairy foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers with a pale pink to white coloration, often with a subtle musky scent. It is native to the Caucasus region and thrives in well-drained, fertile soils.

Due to its rarity and historical significance, it is highly valued by botanists and rose enthusiasts alike.
