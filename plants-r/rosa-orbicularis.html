Rosa orbicularis

Rosa orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaved rose, is a species native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular, or rounded, leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a subtle, sweet scent. Rosa orbicularis is a deciduous shrub that thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
