Rulingia magniflora

Rulingia magniflora, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular ornamental species in gardens and parks.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
