Rhynchospora kunthii var. composita

Rhynchospora kunthii var. composita, commonly known as the composite spikegrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of South America.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, acidic soils. The plant features a tall, slender stem with dense, spike-like clusters of small, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish or brownish and are arranged in compact inflorescences.

It plays an important ecological role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
