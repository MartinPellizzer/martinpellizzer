Rosa javorkae

Rosa javorkae, commonly known as the Java rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated edge.

Rosa javorkae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
