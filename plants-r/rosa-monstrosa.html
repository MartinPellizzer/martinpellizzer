Rosa monstrosa

Rosa monstrosa, commonly known as the monstrous rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves. Its botanical features include showy, fragrant blooms and a woody structure that supports its perennial nature.

Rosa monstrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
