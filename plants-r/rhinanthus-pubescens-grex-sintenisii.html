Rhinanthus pubescens grex sintenisii

Rhinanthus pubescens grex sintenisii, commonly known as the hairy yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially depends on host plants for water and nutrients. This species is of conservation interest due to its declining populations and specific habitat requirements.
