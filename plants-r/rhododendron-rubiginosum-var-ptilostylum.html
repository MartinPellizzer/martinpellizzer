Rhododendron rubiginosum var. ptilostylum

Rhododendron rubiginosum var. ptilostylum, commonly known as the woolly alpine rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps it withstand harsh alpine conditions. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats at high elevations. Its flowers are usually pink to reddish in color, appearing in clusters during the spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
