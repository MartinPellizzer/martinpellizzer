Rhodophiala bifida subsp. purpurea

Rhodophiala bifida subsp.

purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered rhodophiala, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family. It is a bulbous perennial plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.
