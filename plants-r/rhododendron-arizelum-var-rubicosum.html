Rhododendron arizelum var. rubicosum

Rhododendron arizelum var. rubicosum, commonly known as the Redstem Rhododendron, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
