Rabdosia kunmingensis

Rabdosia kunmingensis, commonly known as the Kunming rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, two-lipped flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Rabdosia kunmingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
