Rhododendron yuefengense

Rhododendron yuefengense, commonly known as the Yuefeng rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Ericaceae family and is adapted to acidic, well-drained soils. Its ornamental value is notable due to its vibrant floral displays, making it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

However, it is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to thrive.
