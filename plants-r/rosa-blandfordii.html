Rosa blandfordii

Rosa blandfordii, commonly known as the Blandford rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring thorny stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in spring and summer. Its botanical characteristics include simple, alternate leaves and a woody structure that allows it to thrive in dry, rocky environments.

Rosa blandfordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
