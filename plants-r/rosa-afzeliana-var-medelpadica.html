Rosa afzeliana var. medelpadica

Rosa afzeliana var. medelpadica, commonly known as the Medelpad rose, is a compact shrub native to southern Sweden and parts of Norway.

It features dark green, slightly hairy leaves and produces clusters of small, pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often found in moist, calcareous soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.

This variety is also noted for its historical use in traditional landscaping and horticultural practices.
