Ruschia modesta

Ruschia modesta, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
