Rhodax vinealis

Rhodax vinealis, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its vines can grow up to 10 meters long, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
