Romulea bulbocodium var. leichtliniana

Romulea bulbocodium var. leichtliniana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant produces small, rounded bulbs that serve as storage organs for nutrients. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
