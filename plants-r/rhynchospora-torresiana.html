Rhynchospora torresiana

Rhynchospora torresiana, commonly known as the Torresian flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist habitats, often forming dense stands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale green flowers. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively and colonize new areas.

Rhynchospora torresiana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
