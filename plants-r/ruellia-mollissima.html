Ruellia mollissima

Ruellia mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf ruellia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Ruellia mollissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
