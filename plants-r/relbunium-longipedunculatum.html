Relbunium longipedunculatum

Relbunium longipedunculatum, commonly known as the long peduncled bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

Relbunium longipedunculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
