Ranunculus parobscurans

Ranunculus parobscurans, commonly known as the dark-leaved buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with deeply lobed petals and a hairy stem. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a distinctive dark green coloration. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

This species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
