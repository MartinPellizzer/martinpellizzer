Rosa caesia f. decorosa

Rosa caesia f. decorosa, commonly known as the Decorosa shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, thornless stems and produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to white. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is well-suited for small gardens or containers due to its manageable size.

Rosa caesia f. decorosa is drought-tolerant and requires minimal pruning for optimal growth and blooming.
