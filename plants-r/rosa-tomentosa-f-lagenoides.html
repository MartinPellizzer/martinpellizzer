Rosa tomentosa f. lagenoides

Rosa tomentosa f. lagenoides, commonly known as the woolly rose or the fuzzy rose, is a cultivar of the species Rosa tomentosa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and is native to temperate regions of Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping and floral arrangements.
