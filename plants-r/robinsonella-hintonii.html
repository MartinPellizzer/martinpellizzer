Robinsonella hintonii

Robinsonella hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and native plant gardening.
