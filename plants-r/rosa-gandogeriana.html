Rosa gandogeriana

Rosa gandogeriana, commonly known as the Gandogerian rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated leaflets that provide a striking visual contrast.

Rosa gandogeriana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
