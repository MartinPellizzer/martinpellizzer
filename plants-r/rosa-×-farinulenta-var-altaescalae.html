Rosa × farinulenta var. altaescalae

Rosa × farinulenta var. altaescalae, commonly known as the Alpine rose, is a hybrid species native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
