Rochelia gracilis

Rochelia gracilis, commonly known as the slender rochelia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or riverine habitats.
