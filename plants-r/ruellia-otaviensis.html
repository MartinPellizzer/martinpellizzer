Ruellia otaviensis

Ruellia otaviensis, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.
