Rubia debilis

Rubia debilis, commonly known as the slender woodruff, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many flowering plants known for their ornamental value. The plant features delicate, aromatic leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Rubia debilis is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in herbal medicine.
