Reaumuria hypericoides var. angustifolia

Reaumuria hypericoides var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and linear, adapted to conserve water in dry environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its root system is extensive and deep, allowing it to access moisture from deeper soil layers.

This species is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is well-suited to harsh, desert-like conditions.
