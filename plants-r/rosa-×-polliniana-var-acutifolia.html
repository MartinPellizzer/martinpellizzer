Rosa × polliniana var. acutifolia

Rosa × polliniana var. acutifolia, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
