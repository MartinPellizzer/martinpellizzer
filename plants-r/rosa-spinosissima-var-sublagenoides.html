Rosa spinosissima var. sublagenoides

Rosa spinosissima var. sublagenoides, commonly known as the prickly rose or the thorny rose, is a variety of the Rosa spinosissima species.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers. The leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets that are typically oval and serrated. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
