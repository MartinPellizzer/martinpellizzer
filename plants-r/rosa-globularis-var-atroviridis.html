Rosa globularis var. atroviridis

Rosa globularis var. atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green globular rose, is a compact, shrub-like plant characterized by its rounded growth habit and dense foliage.

Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged in a unique, clustered pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
