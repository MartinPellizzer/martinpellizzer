Rubus schleicheri var. stylosus

Rubus schleicheri var. stylosus, commonly known as the slender raspberry.

It is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces edible, red berries that are similar in taste to regular raspberries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
