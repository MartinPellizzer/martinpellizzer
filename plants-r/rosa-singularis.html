Rosa singularis

Rosa singularis, commonly known as the rare white rose, is a unique species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its single, pure white petals and a subtle, delicate fragrance. This plant is native to a specific region in China and is highly valued for its ornamental beauty. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
