Rangium viridissimum

Rangium viridissimum, commonly known as the Emerald Spore Fern, is a rare and elusive plant species characterized by its vibrant green foliage and unique spore-producing structures.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in dense tropical rainforests. The plant's leaves are broad and leathery, with a waxy surface that aids in water retention. Its spores are released in a fine, green powder, which can travel long distances on the wind.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Rangium viridissimum is considered a conservation concern.
