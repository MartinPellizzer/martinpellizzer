Rupiphila tachiroei

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments. The plant has delicate, feathery fronds that are adapted to its harsh, high-altitude habitat. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, typical of many ferns.
