Rosa spinosissima florepleno

Rosa spinosissima florepleno, commonly known as the double-spined rose, is a species native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green and compound, consisting of multiple oval-shaped leaflets.

This rose is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
