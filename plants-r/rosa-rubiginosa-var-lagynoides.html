Rosa rubiginosa var. lagynoides

Rosa rubiginosa var. lagynoides, commonly known as the Lagynoides rose, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pink to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are small, red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to dry, rocky soils.
