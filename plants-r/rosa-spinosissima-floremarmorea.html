Rosa spinosissima floremarmorea

Rosa spinosissima floremarmorea, commonly known as the marble flower rose, is a rare and striking species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, mottled pink and white petals that resemble marble patterns, giving it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens due to its delicate and rare appearance.
