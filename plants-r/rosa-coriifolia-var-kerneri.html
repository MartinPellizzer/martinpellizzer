Rosa coriifolia var. kerneri

Rosa coriifolia var. kerneri, commonly known as the Kerneri cherry blossom rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, single-layered pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has thornless stems and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its early spring display.
