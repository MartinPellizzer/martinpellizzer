Rosa elliptica var. vaillantiana

Rosa elliptica var. vaillantiana, commonly known as the Vaillant's rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for garden cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
