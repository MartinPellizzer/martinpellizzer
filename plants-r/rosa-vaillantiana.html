Rosa vaillantiana

Rosa vaillantiana, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It features fragrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces red or orange hips in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Rosa vaillantiana is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
