Rhynchanthera kleinii

Rhynchanthera kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's rhynchanthera, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive lip that attracts pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid environment to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
