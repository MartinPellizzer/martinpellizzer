Rosa × marginata

Rosa × marginata, commonly known as the Marguerite rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves and produces rose-like blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

Rosa × marginata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and containers.
