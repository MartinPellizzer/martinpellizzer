Rosa dollineriana

Rosa dollineriana, commonly known as the Austrian rose, is a species native to the Alps and surrounding regions.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem covered in thorns and an extensive root system.

Rosa dollineriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
