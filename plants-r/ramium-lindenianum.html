Ramium lindenianum

Ramium lindenianum, commonly known as the white clover.

It is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Ramium lindenianum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which makes it an important plant in ecological and agricultural contexts.
