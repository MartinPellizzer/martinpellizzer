Roseodendron

Roseodendron, commonly known as the hybrid rose, is a flowering plant that combines the traits of roses and azaleas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically has glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Roseodendrons are popular in gardens for their ornamental value and fragrance.
