Rhododendron poukhanense var. plenum

Rhododendron poukhanense var. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered poukhan rhododendron, is a compact shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dense, leathery foliage that is dark green and glossy, providing a striking backdrop for its abundant blooms. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a rounded, plump shape that gives the variety its name. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade, making it well-suited for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
