Rosa pouzinii var. mauritii

Rosa pouzinii var. mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius rose, is a shrub native to the island of Mauritius.

It features fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and thorny stems, typical of many rose species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
