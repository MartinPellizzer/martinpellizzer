Robiquetia culicifera

Robiquetia culicifera, commonly known as the mosquito plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a notable species in botanical studies.
