Ruellia sylvaccola

Ruellia sylvaccola, commonly known as the Florida rainforest ruellia, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands and forests of southern Florida.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Ruellia sylvaccola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
