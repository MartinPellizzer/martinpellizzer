Rosa × rusticana

Rosa × rusticana, commonly known as the Rusticana rose, is a hybrid shrub rose native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and abundant production of large, semi-double to double pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and has dark green, leathery foliage. It is well-suited for garden use as a shrub or hedge due to its hardiness and disease resistance.

The Rosa × rusticana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and landscape purposes.
