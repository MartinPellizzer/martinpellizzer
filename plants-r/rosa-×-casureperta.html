Rosa × casureperta

Rosa × casureperta, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its continuous flowering throughout the growing season, which sets it apart from many other rose varieties. The plant typically produces fragrant, semi-double to double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.

This rose is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.
