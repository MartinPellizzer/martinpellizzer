Rungia camerunensis

Rungia camerunensis, commonly known as the African violet or Cameroon violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Rungia camerunensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
