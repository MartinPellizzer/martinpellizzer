Rosa nepalensis

Rosa nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves, typical of many rose species. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions of Nepal, India, and Bhutan.

Rosa nepalensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural and medicinal significance in traditional practices.
