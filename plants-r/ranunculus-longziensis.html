Ranunculus longziensis

Ranunculus longziensis, commonly known as the Yunnan buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy and often toxic species. The plant features delicate, palmately divided leaves and bright yellow flowers with numerous petals. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
