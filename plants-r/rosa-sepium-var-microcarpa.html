Rosa sepium var. microcarpa

Rosa sepium var. microcarpa, commonly known as the miniature rose or dwarf rose, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming repeatedly throughout the growing season. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a dense, bushy growth habit. It is well-suited for containers or small garden spaces due to its manageable size.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
