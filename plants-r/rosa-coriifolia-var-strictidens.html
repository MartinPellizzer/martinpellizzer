Rosa coriifolia var. strictidens

Rosa coriifolia var. strictidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved shrub rose, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, elliptical leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, semi-double pink flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its compact form and delicate floral display.
