Rubus pugionifer

Rubus pugionifer, commonly known as the swordleaf raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern North America, characterized by its distinctive sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the understory of forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
