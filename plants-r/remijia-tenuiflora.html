Remijia tenuiflora

Remijia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered remijia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer.
