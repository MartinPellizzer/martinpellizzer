Rosa balakhtensis

Rosa balakhtensis, commonly known as the Central Asian rose, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color. Rosa balakhtensis produces fragrant, single or semi-double flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
