Rosa coriifolia f. minutiflora

Rosa coriifolia f. minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature Chinese rose, is a compact shrub characterized by its small, clustered flowers.

It features thorned stems and elliptical, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals and a prominent central pistil. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in partial shade.

It is native to China and is often used in horticultural displays for its delicate appearance.
