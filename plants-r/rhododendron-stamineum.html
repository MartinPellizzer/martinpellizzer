Rhododendron stamineum

Rhododendron stamineum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Rhododendron stamineum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
