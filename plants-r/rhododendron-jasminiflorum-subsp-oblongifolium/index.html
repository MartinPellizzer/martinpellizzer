Rhododendron Jasminiflorum Subsp. Oblongifolium

Jasmine-flowered rhododendron, scientifically known as Rhododendron jasminiflorum subsp. oblongifolium, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is renowned for its fragrant, jasmine-like blooms that appear in late spring. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its showy flowers. With its unique scent and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike. Its beauty and rarity make it a noteworthy subject in the study of flora.
