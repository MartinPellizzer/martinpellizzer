Ranunculus centellifolius

Ranunculus centellifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, showy flowers with five to eight yellow petals and a central cluster of yellow stamens. The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, ornamental plant.
