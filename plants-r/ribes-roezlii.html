Ribes roezlii

Ribes roezlii, commonly known as the Roezli's gooseberry, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and alternate, ovate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
