Rosa dumalis f. kupcokii

Rosa dumalis f. kupcokii, commonly known as the Kupcokii rose, is a unique horticultural form of the shrub rose.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, semi-double pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features glossy, dark green foliage and thorny stems typical of the Rosa genus. It is well-suited for small garden spaces and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to temperate climates.
