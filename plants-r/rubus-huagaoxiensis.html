Rubus huagaoxiensis

Rubus huagaoxiensis, commonly known as the Huanggaoxiensis raspberry, is a species of shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested or mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
