Rhoicissus cuneifolia

Rhoicissus cuneifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded areas.

Its rapid growth makes it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential invasive species in some regions.
