Rhodorhiza perraudieri

Rhodorhiza perraudieri, commonly known as the Perraudier's rhododendron, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
