Rubus planus

Rubus planus, commonly known as the flatbed raspberry, is a shrub native to North America.

It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces yellowish-white flowers in the spring. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Rubus planus is an important food source for various wildlife species.
