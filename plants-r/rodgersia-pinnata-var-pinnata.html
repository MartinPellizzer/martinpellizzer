Rodgersia pinnata var. pinnata

Rodgersia pinnata var. pinnata, commonly known as the softleaf rodgersia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features large, palmately divided, dark green leaves that are soft and fuzzy to the touch. The plant produces showy, pale pink to white flowers on tall, sturdy stems in late summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded borders.
