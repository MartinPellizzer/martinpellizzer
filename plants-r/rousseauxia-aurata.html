Rousseauxia aurata

Rousseauxia aurata, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
