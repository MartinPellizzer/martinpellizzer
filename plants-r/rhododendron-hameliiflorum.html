Rhododendron hameliiflorum

Rhododendron hameliiflorum, commonly known as the Hameliiflorum rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features dense, broad leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
