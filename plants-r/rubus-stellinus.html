Rubus stellinus

Rubus stellinus, commonly known as the starfruit raspberry, is a species of bramble native to eastern North America.

It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or purple in color, with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and thorny stems typical of the Rubus genus. It thrives in woodland habitats and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

The fruit is edible, though some varieties may have a tart or slightly bitter flavor.
