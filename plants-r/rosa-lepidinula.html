Rosa lepidinula

Rosa lepidinula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.

Rosa lepidinula is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
