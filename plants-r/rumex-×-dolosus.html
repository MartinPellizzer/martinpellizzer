Rumex × dolosus

Rumex × dolosus, commonly known as the hybrid sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Rumex acetosa (common sorrel) and Rumex acetobatus (water sorrel). The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a reddish underside and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found near water bodies.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
