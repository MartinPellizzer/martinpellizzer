Rosa afzeliana var. transiens

Rosa afzeliana var. transiens, commonly known as the Transient Rosa or the Variable Rosa, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces fragrant pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have serrated edges, while its stems are often covered in thorns.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
