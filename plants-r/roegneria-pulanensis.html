Roegneria pulanensis

Roegneria pulanensis, commonly known as the Pulan grass, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, needle-like leaves. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Roegneria pulanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its reproductive structures include spikelets that bear both florets and bracts, typical of grasses.
