Rhododendron × kamatae

Rhododendron × kamatae, commonly known as the Kamata rhododendron, is a hybrid species native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Rhododendron × kamatae is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
