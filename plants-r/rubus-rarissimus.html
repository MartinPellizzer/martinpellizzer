Rubus rarissimus

Rubus rarissimus, commonly known as the rare raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, aggregate fruits. It is characterized by its sparse distribution and is considered a critically endangered species.

Botanically, it is part of the Rosaceae family, sharing traits with other Rubus species but distinguished by its rarity and unique habitat requirements.
