Rottlera moluccana

Rottlera moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan rottlera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellow or greenish hue.

Rottlera moluccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
