Rhynchanthera regnellii

Rhynchanthera regnellii, commonly known as the Regnell's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

This terrestrial herbaceous plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the understory of tropical forests. It features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The flowers are usually pale green to white with purple or brown markings, and they bloom during the wet season.

The plant is characterized by its lanceolate leaves and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.
