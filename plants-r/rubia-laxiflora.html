Rubia laxiflora

Rubia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender woodruff, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant features slender stems that often trail along the ground, producing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale blue. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy surface.

Rubia laxiflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
