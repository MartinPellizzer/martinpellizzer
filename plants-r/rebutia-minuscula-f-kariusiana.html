Rebutia minuscula f. kariusiana

Rebutia minuscula f. kariusiana, commonly known as the Kariusian Rebutia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often appearing in late spring or early summer. Its foliage is succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
