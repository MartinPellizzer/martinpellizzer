Rosa × spinulifolia

Rosa × spinulifolia, commonly known as the spinose rose, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has dark green, serrated leaves and produces rose hips that are orange-red in color. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

Rosa × spinulifolia prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
