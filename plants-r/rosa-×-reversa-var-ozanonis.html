Rosa × reversa var. ozanonis

Rosa × reversa var. ozanonis, commonly known as the Ozanonis rose, is a hybrid rose species originating from crosses between Rosa rugosa and Rosa × centifolia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, semi-double to double pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage. It is relatively hardy and adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
