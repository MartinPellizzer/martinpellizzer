Romulea rosea var. elegans

Romulea rosea var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant romulea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, waxy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique, cup-shaped blooms and compact growth habit.
