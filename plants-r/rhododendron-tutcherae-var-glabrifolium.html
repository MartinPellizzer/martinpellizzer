Rhododendron tutcherae var. glabrifolium

Rhododendron tutcherae var. glabrifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved tutchera rhododendron, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically leathery and have a smooth texture, which distinguishes it from other rhododendron varieties.
