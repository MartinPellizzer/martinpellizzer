Rosa oncophylla

Rosa oncophylla, commonly known as the giant rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red petals that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Rosa oncophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
