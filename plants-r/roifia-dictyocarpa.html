Roifia dictyocarpa

Roifia dictyocarpa, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical Africa. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically linear and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its habitat.

Roifia dictyocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
