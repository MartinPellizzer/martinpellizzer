Rudbeckia serotina f. novae-caesareae

Rudbeckia serotina f. novae-caesareae, commonly known as the New Caesarea rudbeckia, is a variant of the late rudbeckia, characterized by its striking floral display.

This perennial herb belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to specific regions of North America. It features large, showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of darker yellow to orange tubular flowers.
