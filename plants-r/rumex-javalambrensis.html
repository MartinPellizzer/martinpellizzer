Rumex javalambrensis

Rumex javalambrensis, commonly known as the Javalambrense sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Madagascar.

The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish hue on the underside. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in the spring.
