Rhododendron yungningense

Rhododendron yungningense, commonly known as the Yungning rhododendron, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Yungning region of Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous areas with high humidity and cool temperatures. The plant features broad, ovate leaves and produces clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers in the spring. Its botanical characteristics include a dense, compact growth habit and a preference for acidic, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its native habitat.
