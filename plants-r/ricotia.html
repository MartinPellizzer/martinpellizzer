Ricotia

Ricotia, commonly known as the common ricotia, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Brassicaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are annual or biennial herbs. The plants typically have simple, alternate leaves and produce small, white or yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Ricotia species are often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

They are sometimes considered weeds but are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
