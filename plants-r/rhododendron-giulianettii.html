Rhododendron giulianettii

Rhododendron giulianettii, commonly known as the Giulianetti rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in shaded garden environments.
