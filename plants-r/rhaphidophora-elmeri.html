Rhaphidophora elmeri

Rhaphidophora elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's arrowroot, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by spathiflorous inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Rhaphidophora elmeri is valued for its attractive foliage and ease of care in indoor settings.
