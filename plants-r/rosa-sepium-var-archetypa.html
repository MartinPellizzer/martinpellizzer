Rosa sepium var. archetypa

Rosa sepium var. archetypa, commonly known as the archetypal rose, is a rare and ancient variety of the rose species.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are dark green and serrated, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is highly valued for its historical significance and ornamental beauty in botanical gardens.
