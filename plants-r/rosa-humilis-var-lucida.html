Rosa humilis var. lucida

Rosa humilis var. lucida, commonly known as the shining shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellowish tinge. The plant has slender, thornless stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
