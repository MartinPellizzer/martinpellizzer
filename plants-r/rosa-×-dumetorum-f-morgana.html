Rosa × dumetorum f. morgana

Rosa × dumetorum f. morgana, commonly known as the Morgana rose, is a hybrid species belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to white. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its delicate blossoms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it a popular choice in gardens.
