Rosa mollis f. caliginosula

Rosa mollis f. caliginosula, commonly known as the misty rose, is a hybrid form of the silky rose, characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and pale pink to white flowers.

It is a compact shrub with multiple stems, often growing in a rounded or spreading habit. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a velvety texture and a pale underside, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to create a delicate, ethereal effect in the landscape.
