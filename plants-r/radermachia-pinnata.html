Radermachia pinnata

Radermachia pinnata, commonly known as the silver leaf radermachia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
