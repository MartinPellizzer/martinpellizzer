Rebutia einsteinii f. conoidea

Rebutia einsteinii f. conoidea, commonly known as the Einstein's Rebutia conoidea, is a compact, spineless cactus native to South America.

It features a rounded, globular stem that is typically greenish-gray in color, adorned with small, tuberculate growths. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which bloom at the tips of the stems. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
