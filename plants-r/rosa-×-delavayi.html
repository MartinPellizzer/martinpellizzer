Rosa × delavayi

Rosa × delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with five to seven leaflets.

Rosa × delavayi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
