Ruschia steingroeveri

Ruschia steingroeveri, commonly known as the Cape plume, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice among gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
