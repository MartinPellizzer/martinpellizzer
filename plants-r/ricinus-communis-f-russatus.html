Ricinus communis f. russatus

Ricinus communis f. russatus, commonly known as the Russian castor plant, is a variant of the castor oil plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with a deep green color and a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces spiny capsules that contain seeds used for oil extraction. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and economically valuable.
