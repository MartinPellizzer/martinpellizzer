Rhaphidophora sabit

Rhaphidophora sabit, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, narrow leaves with distinctive dark green to black stripes, giving it its striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Rhaphidophora sabit is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
