Russelia laciniata

Russelia laciniata, commonly known as the pink fairy fan, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to magenta flowers that bloom in dense, fan-like clusters. The plant features slender, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Russelia laciniata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its showy, long-lasting blooms.
