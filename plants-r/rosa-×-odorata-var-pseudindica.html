Rosa × odorata var. pseudindica

Rosa × odorata var. pseudindica, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a hybrid species within the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub with thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are borne singly or in clusters and are known for their long-lasting blooms.

The Chinese rose is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrance.
