Reseda lutea var. muelleri

Reseda lutea var. muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's yellowrocket, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers bloom in early spring, and it is often found in open habitats such as fields and coastal areas.

The variety muelleri is distinguished by its slightly different leaf shape and flower structure compared to the typical form of Reseda lutea.
