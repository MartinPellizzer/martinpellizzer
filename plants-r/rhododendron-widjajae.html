Rhododendron widjajae

Rhododendron widjajae, commonly known as the Java rhododendron, is a species native to Indonesia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in spring and are highly ornamental.

Rhododendron widjajae is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
