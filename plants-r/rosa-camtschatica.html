Rosa camtschatica

Rosa camtschatica, commonly known as the Altai rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Russia, Mongolia, and China. The plant produces showy, single or double pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with five to seven leaflets, and its stems are covered in thorns.

Rosa camtschatica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
