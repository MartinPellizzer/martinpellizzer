Ruschia senaria

Ruschia senaria, commonly known as the silver leaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking silver-gray foliage with a textured, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
