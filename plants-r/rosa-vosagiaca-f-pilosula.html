Rosa vosagiaca f. pilosula

Rosa vosagiaca f. pilosula, commonly known as the velvet rose, is a unique cultivar characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and fragrant, pale pink flowers.

It is a hybrid rose that exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. The plant is noted for its ornamental value, with its fuzzy leaves providing a tactile and visual contrast to its delicate blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its beauty and is often used in ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
