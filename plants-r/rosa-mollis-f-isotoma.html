Rosa mollis f. isotoma

Rosa mollis f. isotoma, commonly known as the soft rose or isotoma rose, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its fine, almost hairless leaves and its ability to thrive in sunny, well-drained conditions. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and special addition to botanical collections.
