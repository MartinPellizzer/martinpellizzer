Rabdosia maddenii

Rabdosia maddenii, commonly known as the slender rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
