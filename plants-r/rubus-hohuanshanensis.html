Rubus hohuanshanensis

Rubus hohuanshanensis, commonly known as the Hohuanshan bramble, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Hohuanshan region of Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it produces red or black berries that are edible.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
