Rungia arnottiana

Rungia arnottiana, commonly known as the red-flowered balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Brunelliaceae and is characterized by its striking red, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Rungia arnottiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
