Racosperma lanigerum var. gracilipes

Racosperma lanigerum var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed acacia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, often branched stems covered in a fine, white pubescence. The leaves are bipinnate, with small, linear leaflets that are usually pale green. It produces yellow flowers in globular or cylindrical inflorescences, which are attractive to pollinators.

The plant is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
