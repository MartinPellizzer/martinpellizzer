Ruschia prolongata

Ruschia prolongata, commonly known as the narrowleaf Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in small, pointed leaves. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance, it is well-suited for arid climates.
