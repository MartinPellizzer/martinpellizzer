Reseda Phyteuma F. Aragonensis

Reseda phyteuma f. aragonensis is a rare and lesser-known variant of the Reseda genus, native to the Iberian Peninsula. This plant is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical specimen from further decline.
