Ranunculus densiciliatus

Ranunculus densiciliatus, commonly known as the dense-ciliate buttercup, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features finely divided, pinnately arranged leaves that are covered in dense, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
