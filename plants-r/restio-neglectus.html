Restio neglectus

Restio neglectus, commonly known as the slender restio, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Restionaceae family, which includes many grass-like plants found in coastal and mountainous regions. Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
