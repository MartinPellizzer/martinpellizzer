Russelia villosa

Russelia villosa, commonly known as the velvet flower or silky russelia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates. Its unique appearance and ability to attract hummingbirds make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.

Russelia villosa is also noted for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
