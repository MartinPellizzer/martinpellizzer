Raddia Maculata

Raddia maculata is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, velvety petals and unique spotted leaves, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This plant thrives in cool, humid climates and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its beauty has made it a sought-after specimen in both horticulture and conservation efforts. Despite its allure, Raddia maculata remains under-studied, highlighting the need for further research and protection.
