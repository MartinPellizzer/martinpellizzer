Rosa cziragensis

Rosa cziragensis, commonly known as the Turk's Turban rose, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are often borne singly or in small clusters at the ends of the branches.

Rosa cziragensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
