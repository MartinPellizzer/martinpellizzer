Rodriguezia candida var. batemannii

Rodriguezia candida var. batemannii, commonly known as the Batemann's white rodriguezia, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that emerge from the base of these bracts, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
