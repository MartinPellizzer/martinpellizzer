Rosa celsii

Rosa celsii, commonly known as the Celsii rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, particularly in the Alps and Pyrenees. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Rosa celsii is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring thorny stems and dark green, serrated leaves.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
