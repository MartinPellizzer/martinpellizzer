Rosa gmelinii

Rosa gmelinii, commonly known as the Mongolian rose, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the cold climates of Mongolia and northern China, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces fragrant pink or white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are pinnate, with five to seven leaflets, and its stems are covered in thorns.

Rosa gmelinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
