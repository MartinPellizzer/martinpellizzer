Rostraria amblyantha

Rostraria amblyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rostraria, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, pale greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats and is often found in forest understories. Its unique rostrum-like structure on the flower spike distinguishes it from other orchid species.
