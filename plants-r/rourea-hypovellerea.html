Rourea hypovellerea

Rourea hypovellerea, commonly known as the velvet-leaf rourea, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
