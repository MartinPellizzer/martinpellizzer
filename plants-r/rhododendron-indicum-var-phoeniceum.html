Rhododendron indicum var. phoeniceum

Rhododendron indicum var. phoeniceum, commonly known as the Flame Rhododendron, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from deep crimson to orange-red, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green, and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its role in native ecosystems.
