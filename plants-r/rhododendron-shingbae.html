Rhododendron shingbae

Rhododendron shingbae, commonly known as the Shingba rhododendron, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron shingbae plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting local biodiversity and contributing to the region's unique flora.
