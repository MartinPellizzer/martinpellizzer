Rhinanthus × Digeneus

The rhinanthus × digeneus is a hybrid plant that combines features of two distinct species, rhinanthus minor and rhinanthus anthelios. It is commonly found in disturbed grasslands and open habitats across europe. This plant is known for its bright orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its hybrid nature makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more showy wildflowers.
