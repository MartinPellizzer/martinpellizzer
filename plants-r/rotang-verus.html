Rotang verus

Rotang verus, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody pods containing edible seeds. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction. Its bark and leaves are also utilized in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Rotang verus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various wildlife species.
