Rubus × pererythrostylus

Rubus × pererythrostylus, commonly known as the red-styled raspberry.

It is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus, characterized by its thorny stems and aggregate fruits. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is cultivated for its edible berries. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals and numerous stamens.

The fruit develops from the fertilized ovaries of the flower and is prized for its tart flavor.
