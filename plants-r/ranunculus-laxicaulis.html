Ranunculus laxicaulis

Ranunculus laxicaulis, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, nodding yellow flowers with five petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. The plant has a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardens and ecological restoration projects.
