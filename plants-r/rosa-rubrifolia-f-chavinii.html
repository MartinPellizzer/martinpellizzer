Rosa rubrifolia f. chavinii

Rosa rubrifolia f. chavinii, commonly known as the Chavinii rose, is a unique cultivar of the Rosa rubrifolia species.

It is characterized by its deep red foliage, which contrasts strikingly with its delicate pink to white flowers. This plant is typically shrub-like in form, with thorny stems and dense, aromatic leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and elegant floral display.
