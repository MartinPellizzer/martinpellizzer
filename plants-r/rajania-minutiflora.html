Rajania minutiflora

Rajania minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Rajania minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
