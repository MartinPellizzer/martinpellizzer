Rosa × reversa var. ellipsoidea

Rosa × reversa var. ellipsoidea, commonly known as the elliptic everblooming rose, is a hybrid rose species known for its continuous flowering habit.

It features large, semi-cupped blooms with a rich, velvety texture and a deep, intense red color. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, leathery foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
