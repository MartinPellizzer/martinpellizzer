Ribes leiobotrys

Ribes leiobotrys, commonly known as the narrowleaf currant, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Grossulariaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves. Its ornamental value and edible fruits make it a valuable plant in both horticulture and ecological settings.
