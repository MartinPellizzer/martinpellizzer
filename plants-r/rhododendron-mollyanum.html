Rhododendron mollyanum

Rhododendron mollyanum, commonly known as the Malayan rhododendron, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Rhododendron mollyanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid tropical climates.
