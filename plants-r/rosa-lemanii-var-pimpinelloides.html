Rosa lemanii var. pimpinelloides

Rosa lemanii var. pimpinelloides, commonly known as the alpine rose or mountain rose, is a species native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub with fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in alpine ecosystems.
