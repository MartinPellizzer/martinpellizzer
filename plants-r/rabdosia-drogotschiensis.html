Rabdosia drogotschiensis

Rabdosia drogotschiensis, commonly known as the Drogotschi's Rabdosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties make it of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
