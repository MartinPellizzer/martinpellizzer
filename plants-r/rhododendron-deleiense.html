Rhododendron deleiense

Rhododendron deleiense, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in shady, humid environments. It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known shrubs and flowering plants.

Rhododendron deleiense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
