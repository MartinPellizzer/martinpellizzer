Ranunculus cytheraeus

Ranunculus cytheraeus, commonly known as the Cythera buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, palmate leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. The plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some areas.
