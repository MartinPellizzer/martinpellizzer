Ryania speciosa var. tomentosa

Ryania speciosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the tomentosa ryania, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its hairy, grayish foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a distinctive, spiny capsule that contains numerous seeds.

The leaves and bark have been used traditionally in herbal medicine for their potential medicinal properties.
