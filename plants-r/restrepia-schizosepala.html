Restrepia schizosepala

Restrepia schizosepala, commonly known as the Schizosepala orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
