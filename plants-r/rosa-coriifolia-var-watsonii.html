Rosa coriifolia var. watsonii

Rosa coriifolia var. watsonii, commonly known as the Chinese shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features delicate, clustered flowers with five pink to red petals, often with a white or pale pink base. The plant has thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, semi-double flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
