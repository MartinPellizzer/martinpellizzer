Rhododendron heizhugouense

Rhododendron heizhugouense, commonly known as the Heizhugou rhododendron, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soil and high humidity. Its flowers range in color from pink to deep red, adding vibrant visual interest to its natural habitat.

Rhododendron heizhugouense is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
