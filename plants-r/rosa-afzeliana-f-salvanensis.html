Rosa afzeliana f. salvanensis

Rosa afzeliana f. salvanensis, commonly known as the Salvanense rose, is a botanical variant of the Rosa afzeliana species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, thorny stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the blooms.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
