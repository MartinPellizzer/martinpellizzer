Rosa abietina f. bruggeri

Rosa abietina f. bruggeri, commonly known as the Alpine rose or Brugger's rose, is a compact shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
