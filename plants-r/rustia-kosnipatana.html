Rustia kosnipatana

Rustia kosnipatana, commonly known as the mountain rustia, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine soils. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, which helps to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

Rustia kosnipatana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural environment.
