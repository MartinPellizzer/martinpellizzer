Rhynchanthera acuminata

Rhynchanthera acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-pointed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Rhynchanthera acuminata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.
