Ranunculus multistamineus

Ranunculus multistamineus, commonly known as the multistamine buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features showy, yellow flowers with numerous stamens, typically arranged in a bright, star-like pattern. The plant has a basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

The flowers bloom in spring and are pollinated primarily by insects.
