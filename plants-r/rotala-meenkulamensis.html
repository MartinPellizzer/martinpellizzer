Rotala meenkulamensis

Rotala meenkulamensis, commonly known as the Meenkulam water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to India.

It is characterized by its floating leaves, which are typically oval or rounded in shape. The plant produces small, green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens.
