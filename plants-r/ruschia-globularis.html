Ruschia globularis

Ruschia globularis, commonly known as the globe Ruschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, globular shape with dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
