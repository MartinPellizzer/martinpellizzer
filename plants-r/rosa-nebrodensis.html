Rosa nebrodensis

Rosa nebrodensis, commonly known as the Nebrodenses rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, pink to red flowers. The plant has pinnately lobed leaves and is typically found in rocky or limestone soils. Rosa nebrodensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

It is also noted for its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained conditions.
