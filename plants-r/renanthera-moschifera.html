Renanthera moschifera

Renanthera moschifera, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a sweet, spicy scent. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Renanthera moschifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
