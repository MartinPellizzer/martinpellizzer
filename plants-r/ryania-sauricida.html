Ryania sauricida

Ryania sauricida, commonly known as the ryania tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant is notable for its woody structure and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its fruits are capsule-like and contain numerous small seeds.
