Roemeria hybrida f. bivalvis

Roemeria hybrida f. bivalvis, commonly known as the bivalve form of the hybrid roemeria, is a botanical variant characterized by its distinctive two-lobed leaves.

The plant is a member of the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic compounds and often fern-like foliage. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands or stream banks.
