Romulea malenconiana var. gattefossei

Romulea malenconiana var. gattefossei, commonly known as the Gattefosse's romulea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
