Rubus × rudnensis

Rubus × rudnensis, commonly known as the rough blackberry, is a hybrid species within the Rubus genus.

It is native to eastern North America and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces aggregate fruits, which are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in woodland areas and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
