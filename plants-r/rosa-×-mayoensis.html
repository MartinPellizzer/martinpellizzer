Rosa × mayoensis

Rosa × mayoensis, commonly known as the Mayan rose, is a hybrid species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant has fragrant petals and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and historical significance, it is a valued addition to horticultural collections.
