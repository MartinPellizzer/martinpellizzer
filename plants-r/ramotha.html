Ramotha

Ramotha, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals and compact rosette growth habit. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect light to avoid leaf scorching. Its foliage is typically dark green with a waxy texture, complementing the colorful blooms.

Ramotha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
