Rhynchospora glomerata var. leptocarpa

Rhynchospora glomerata var. leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically reddish-brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in swamps, bogs, and along the edges of ponds.

The slender spikerush plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
