Rosa systyla var. typica

Rosa systyla var. typica, commonly known as the slender rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features slender, thornless stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Native to parts of Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
