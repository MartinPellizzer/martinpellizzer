Rhododendron obtusum f. tubiflorum

Rhododendron obtusum f. tubiflorum, commonly known as the broadleaf rhododendron, is a unique hybrid form within the Rhododendron genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive tubular shape that sets it apart from other rhododendron varieties. The plant is evergreen, featuring dense, leathery leaves that provide year-round foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
