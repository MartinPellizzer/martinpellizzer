Rosa communis var. mirifica

Rosa communis var. mirifica, commonly known as the Mirific Rose, is a rare and highly prized variant of the common rose.

It is characterized by its large, velvety petals that range in color from deep pink to almost crimson. This variety is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cool, temperate climates. The plant produces a strong, sweet fragrance, which is highly valued in perfumery and aromatherapy.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after specimen in both horticulture and botanical research.
