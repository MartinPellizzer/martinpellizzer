Rosa obtusifolia var. kanitzii

Rosa obtusifolia var. kanitzii, commonly known as the Kanitz's shrub rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thorny stems and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal areas due to its drought tolerance and hardiness.
