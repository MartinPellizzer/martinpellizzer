Risleya

Risleya, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen plant native to South Africa. The plant features long, arching leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Risleya is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in various light conditions.
