Rosa alpinoreuteri

Rosa alpinoreuteri, commonly known as the Alpine Reuter's rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
