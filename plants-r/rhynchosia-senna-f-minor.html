Rhynchosia senna f. minor

Rhynchosia senna f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers, which are typical of the legume family. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has small, rounded leaves and produces small pods that contain seeds.
