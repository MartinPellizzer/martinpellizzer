Ranunculus bulbosus var. leiopodus

Ranunculus bulbosus var. leiopodus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features a short rhizome and produces basal leaves that are heart-shaped with a smooth texture. Its flowers are yellow with five petals and are often accompanied by numerous stamens.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in wet conditions and its ornamental value in gardens.
