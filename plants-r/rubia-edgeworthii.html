Rubia edgeworthii

Rubia edgeworthii, commonly known as the Edgeworth's madder, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant features slender, branched stems and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The roots of Rubia edgeworthii contain compounds that have been historically used in traditional medicine.
