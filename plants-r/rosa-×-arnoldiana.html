Rosa × arnoldiana

Rosa × arnoldiana, commonly known as the Arnold rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant produces showy, semi-double to fully double blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems, contributing to its overall aesthetic appeal.
