Rosa polyantha var. glabrescens

Rosa polyantha var. glabrescens, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features numerous small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is well-suited for garden borders and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
