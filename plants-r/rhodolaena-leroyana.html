Rhodolaena leroyana

Rhodolaena leroyana, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate ornamental value. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round interest in the garden.

Rhodolaena leroyana is also valued for its drought tolerance and suitability for xeriscaping in arid regions.
