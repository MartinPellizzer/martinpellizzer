Rubus koehleri var. stenobotrys

Rubus koehleri var. stenobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf blackberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are typically edible. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
