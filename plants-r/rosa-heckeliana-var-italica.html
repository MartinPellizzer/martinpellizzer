Rosa heckeliana var. italica

Rosa heckeliana var. italica, commonly known as the Italian shrub rose, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, thorny stems and is characterized by its profuse display of semi-double, pink to coral-colored flowers. The foliage consists of dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges, providing a dense, attractive shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature, though it may require regular pruning to maintain its form.
