Rapanea philippensis

Rapanea philippensis, commonly known as the Philippine holly, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and bay trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture due to its hardy nature.
