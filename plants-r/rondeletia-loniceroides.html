Rondeletia loniceroides

Rondeletia loniceroides, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite or whorled in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, evergreen plant.
