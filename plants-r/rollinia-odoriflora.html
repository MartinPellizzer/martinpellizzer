Rollinia odoriflora

Rollinia odoriflora, commonly known as the fragrant starapple, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant produces a fruit that resembles a small apple, with a yellow or red skin and a juicy, sweet pulp.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
