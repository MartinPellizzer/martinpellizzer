Rosa mollis f. caerulifrons

Rosa mollis f. caerulifrons, commonly known as the sky-blue rose, is a unique cultivar of the rose plant characterized by its striking blue-pink flowers.

This variety is a form of Rosa mollis, a species native to China, and is prized for its unusual coloration. The flowers are typically borne in clusters and have a delicate, almost ethereal appearance. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double blooms that are highly sought after in horticulture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
