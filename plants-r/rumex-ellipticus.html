Rumex ellipticus

Rumex ellipticus, commonly known as narrowleaf dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that are often reddish in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are elongated, elliptical, and have a wavy margin with a prominent central vein.

Rumex ellipticus is often found in moist soils along roadsides, meadows, and streambanks.
