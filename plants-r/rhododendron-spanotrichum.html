Rhododendron spanotrichum

Rhododendron spanotrichum, commonly known as the Chinese rhododendron, is a species native to China and is part of the Ericaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils. The plant features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

Rhododendron spanotrichum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
