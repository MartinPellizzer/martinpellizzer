Rosa vanheurckiana var. barbata

Rosa vanheurckiana var. barbata, commonly known as the Barbata rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces fragrant, semi-double flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a rugged appearance and adaptability to various soil types.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
