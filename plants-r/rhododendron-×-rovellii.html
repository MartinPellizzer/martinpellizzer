Rhododendron × rovellii

Rhododendron × rovellii, commonly known as the Rovellii rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

Rhododendron × rovellii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
