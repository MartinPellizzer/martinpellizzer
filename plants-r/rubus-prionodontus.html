Rubus prionodontus

Rubus prionodontus, commonly known as the red raspberry.

It is a species of bramble native to eastern North America, characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Rubus prionodontus is valued for its edible fruits and its role in supporting local wildlife.
