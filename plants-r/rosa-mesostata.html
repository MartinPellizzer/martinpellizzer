Rosa mesostata

Rosa mesostata, commonly known as the shrub rose, is a species native to the eastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its flowers are usually pink to red in color. Rosa mesostata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
