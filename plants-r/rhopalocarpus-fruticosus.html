Rhopalocarpus fruticosus

Rhopalocarpus fruticosus, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and milky sap. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions and has small, simple leaves. It produces yellow flowers that are clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia.
