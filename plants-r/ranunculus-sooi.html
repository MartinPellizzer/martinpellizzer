Ranunculus sooi

Ranunculus sooi, commonly known as the Sooi buttercup, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Nepal.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many showy, often colorful flowers. The plant features delicate, palmate leaves and bright yellow petals that resemble those of other buttercup species. It typically grows in moist, woodland environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
