Rhynchosia ischnoclada

Rhynchosia ischnoclada, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

Rhynchosia ischnoclada is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a forage crop.
