Reseda phyteuma f. aragonensis

Reseda phyteuma f. aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragonese Reseda, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
