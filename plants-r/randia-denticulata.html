Randia denticulata

Randia denticulata, commonly known as the toothed randia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, which give it its common name. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

Randia denticulata is often found in moist, shady habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
