Rosa pilosiuscula

Rosa pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silky rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its soft, fuzzy foliage and fragrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive appearance.

The silky rose is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in various soil types.
