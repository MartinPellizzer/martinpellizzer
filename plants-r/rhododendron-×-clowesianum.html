Rhododendron × clowesianum

Rhododendron × clowesianum, commonly known as the Clowes rhododendron, is a hybrid shrub native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows to a height of 2–4 meters and features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
