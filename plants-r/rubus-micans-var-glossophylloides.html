Rubus micans var. glossophylloides

Rubus micans var. glossophylloides, commonly known as the glossy leaf raspberry, is a variant of the raspberry plant characterized by its distinctive glossy, dark green foliage.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically red or yellow in color, similar to other raspberry species. Its leaves are elliptical with a finely toothed margin, and the stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its potential use in horticulture.
