Rosa alpina debilis

Rosa alpina debilis, commonly known as the Alpine pink rose, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its slender stems and small, fragrant pink flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing delicate petals that are often described as having a soft, pastel hue. Rosa alpina debilis thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
