Opilia umbellulata var. marquesii

Opilia umbellulata var. marquesii, commonly known as the Marques' evergreen shrub, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in humid, well-drained soil. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and adaptability to maritime climates.
