Oxalis mira

Oxalis mira, commonly known as the golden buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, bead-like fruits that contain seeds. Oxalis mira thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
