Ossaea purpurascens

Ossaea purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed ossaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Ossaea purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
