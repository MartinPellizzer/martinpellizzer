Oxytropis pagobia

Oxytropis pagobia, commonly known as the Pagobian locoweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are typically pea-shaped and arranged in dense spikes.
