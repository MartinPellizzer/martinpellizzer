Oreocereus rettigii

Oreocereus rettigii, commonly known as the Rettig's night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its columnar stems and large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is known for its nocturnal blooming habit.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species.
