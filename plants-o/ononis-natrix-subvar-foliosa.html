Ononis natrix subvar. foliosa

Ononis natrix subvar. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal dunes and disturbed areas. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient and hardy plant in its native range.
