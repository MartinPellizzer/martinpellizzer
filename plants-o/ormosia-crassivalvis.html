Ormosia crassivalvis

Ormosia crassivalvis, commonly known as the broad-valved ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits. The plant exhibits a distinctive feature of having broad, leathery leaves with prominent veins. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in racemes, and attract various pollinators.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
