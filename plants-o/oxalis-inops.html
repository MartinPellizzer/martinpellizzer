Oxalis inops

Oxalis inops, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that features small, delicate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has a unique appearance with its translucent, almost ghostly leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Oxalis inops is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
