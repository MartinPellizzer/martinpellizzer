Orias

Orias, commonly known as the "spider plant," is a popular ornamental plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are followed by red berries, which contain the seeds. Orias is well adapted to a variety of growing conditions and is often used in indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a favored choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
