Onoseris altissima

Onoseris altissima, commonly known as the tall yellowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features a robust, branching stem that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its bright yellow flowers are clustered in dense, terminal heads, giving it a striking appearance.

The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.
