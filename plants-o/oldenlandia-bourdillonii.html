Oldenlandia bourdillonii

Oldenlandia bourdillonii, commonly known as the African star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Oldenlandia bourdillonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
