Obione flabellum

Obione flabellum, commonly known as the fan-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Obione flabellum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
