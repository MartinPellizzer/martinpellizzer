Ocotea tabascensis

Ocotea tabascensis, commonly known as the Mexican lauraceae, is a species of flowering plant in the laurel family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Mexico and Central America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are drupes, typically containing a single seed, and it plays a role in local ecosystems as a food source for various wildlife.
