Osmanthus aurantiacus

Osmanthus aurantiacus, commonly known as the orange-flowered osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, orange-red flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a flavoring in teas and confections.

Osmanthus aurantiacus thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.
