Othonna bainesii

Othonna bainesii, commonly known as the Baines' othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Othonna bainesii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
