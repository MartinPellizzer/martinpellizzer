Oberonia ensifolia

Oberonia ensifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive sword-shaped leaves, which are typically long and narrow, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in dense spikes. Oberonia ensifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
