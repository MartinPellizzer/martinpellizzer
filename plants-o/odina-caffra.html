Odina caffra

Odina caffra, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and velvety in texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Odina caffra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
