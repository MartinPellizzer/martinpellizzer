Oreopanax meiocephalus

Oreopanax meiocephalus, commonly known as the small-headed mountain arum, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking, spatulate inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
