Olinia macrophylla

Olinia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf olinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. Its flowers are small and clustered, often white or pale yellow, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

Olinia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
