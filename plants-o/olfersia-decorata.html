Olfersia decorata

Olfersia decorata, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are palmate, with five to seven lobes, and the stem is often covered in a thick, velvety coating.

Olfersia decorata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its culinary uses.
