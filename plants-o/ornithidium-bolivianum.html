Ornithidium bolivianum

Ornithidium bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian feather plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its unique, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Ornithidium bolivianum is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
