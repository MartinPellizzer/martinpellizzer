Oxytenanthera hosseusii

Oxytenanthera hosseusii, commonly known as the African grass or hosseusii grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is often found in open grasslands and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Oxytenanthera hosseusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
