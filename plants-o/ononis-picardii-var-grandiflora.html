Ononis picardii var. grandiflora

Ononis picardii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically growing in a basal rosette.

This species thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.
