Oreocharis argyreia var. angustifolia

Oreocharis argyreia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silverweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features a basal rosette of lanceolate, silver-hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
