Oncidium citrinum

Oncidium citrinum, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive lemon-like scent. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, clustered blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium citrinum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
