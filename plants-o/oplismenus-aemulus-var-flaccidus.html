Oplismenus aemulus var. flaccidus

Oplismenus aemulus var. flaccidus, commonly known as the Florida flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
