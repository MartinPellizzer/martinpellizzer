Oxycaryum cubense f. paraguayense

Oxycaryum cubense f. paraguayense, commonly known as the Paraguay palm, is a flowering plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is a small, ornamental palm native to South America, particularly found in Paraguay and Brazil. The plant features a slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown surface and produces clusters of small, white flowers. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of numerous narrow, linear leaflets.

This palm is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
