Oncidium luteopurpureum

Oncidium luteopurpureum, commonly known as the Purple-tinged Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium luteopurpureum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
