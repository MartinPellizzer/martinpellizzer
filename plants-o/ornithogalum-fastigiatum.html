Ornithogalum fastigiatum

Ornithogalum fastigiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Ornithogalum fastigiatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate floral display.
