Olearia platyphylla

Olearia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf daisy or silverleaf daisy, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance, with lance-shaped leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces showy white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often found in coastal and alpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Olearia platyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
