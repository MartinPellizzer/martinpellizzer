Oenanthe decumbens var. laciniata

Oenanthe decumbens var. laciniata, commonly known as the cutleaf water-dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The leaves are finely divided, with a distinctive lacy appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. It produces small, white or greenish flowers in umbels, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland edges.
