Oxalis repens f. speciosa

Oxalis repens f. speciosa, commonly known as the showy wood sorrel, is a variant of the common wood sorrel.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features trifoliate leaves with distinctive heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow or white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
