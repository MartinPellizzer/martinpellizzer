Orbeopsis gossweileri

Orbeopsis gossweileri, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are often clustered in a raceme and bloom during the summer months.

Orbeopsis gossweileri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
