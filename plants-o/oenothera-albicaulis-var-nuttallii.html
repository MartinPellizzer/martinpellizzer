Oenothera albicaulis var. nuttallii

Oenothera albicaulis var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's evening primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Oenothera albicaulis var. nuttallii is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
