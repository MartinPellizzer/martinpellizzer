Oncidium cucullatum var. phalaenopsis

Oncidium cucullatum var. phalaenopsis, commonly known as the Phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid species that combines traits from Oncidium cucullatum and Phalaenopsis.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
