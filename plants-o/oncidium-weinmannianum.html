Oncidium weinmannianum

Oncidium weinmannianum, commonly known as the Weinmannia orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, arranged in a raceme. Oncidium weinmannianum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
