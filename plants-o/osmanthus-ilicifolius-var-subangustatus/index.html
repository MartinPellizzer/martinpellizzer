Osmanthus Ilicifolius Var. Subangustatus

Sweet osmanthus, scientifically known as Osmanthus ilicifolius var. subangustatus, is a fragrant evergreen shrub native to southern China. It is prized for its small, golden-yellow flowers that emit a sweet, aromatic scent reminiscent of honey and citrus. The plant is commonly cultivated for both ornamental purposes and its use in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary applications. Its unique fragrance makes it a popular choice in gardens and tea blends. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native region.
