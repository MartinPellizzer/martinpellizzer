Olearia speciosa

Olearia speciosa, commonly known as the silver fern, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Olearia speciosa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
