Odina rubra

Odina rubra, commonly known as the red stem fern, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lush, green foliage that forms dense clumps. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Odina rubra is valued both for its aesthetic appeal and its role in traditional medicine.
