Octomeria gracilis var. crassifolia

Octomeria gracilis var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf octomeria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical species, adapted for water storage. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
