Oxalis zuccarinii

Oxalis zuccarinii, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant is characterized by its succulent stems and leaves, which help it store water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Oxalis zuccarinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
