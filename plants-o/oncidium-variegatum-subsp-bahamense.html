Oncidium variegatum subsp. bahamense

Oncidium variegatum subsp.

bahamense, commonly known as the Bahamian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean islands. It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
