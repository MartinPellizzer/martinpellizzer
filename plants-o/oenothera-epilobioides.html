Oenothera epilobioides

Oenothera epilobioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf evening primrose, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant has linear, slightly hairy leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Oenothera epilobioides is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
