Osmanthus zentaroanus f. aureus

Osmanthus zentaroanus f. aureus, commonly known as the golden osmanthus, is a rare and ornamental variant of the osmanthus plant.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. This cultivar is native to China and is prized for its aromatic fragrance and aesthetic appeal. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, and thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its flowers bloom in late autumn, making it a unique addition to gardens and landscapes.
