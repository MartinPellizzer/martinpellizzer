Olax gilletii

Olax gilletii, commonly known as the silverleaf olax, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered in terminal umbels. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and covered in a fine layer of whitish hairs.

Olax gilletii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
