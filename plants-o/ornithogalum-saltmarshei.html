Ornithogalum saltmarshei

Ornithogalum saltmarshei, commonly known as the salt marsh ornithogalum, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in saline or brackish environments, such as salt marshes and coastal dunes. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale green flowers. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it is adapted to withstand high salinity and periodic flooding.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
