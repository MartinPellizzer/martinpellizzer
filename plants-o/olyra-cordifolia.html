Olyra cordifolia

Olyra cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf olyra, is a tropical plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or white in color. Olyra cordifolia is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
