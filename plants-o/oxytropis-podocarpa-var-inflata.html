Oxytropis podocarpa var. inflata

Oxytropis podocarpa var. inflata, commonly known as the inflated bur pea, is a perennial legume native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces cylindrical, inflated pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in sagebrush communities.

Its botanical features make it an important component of arid ecosystem vegetation.
