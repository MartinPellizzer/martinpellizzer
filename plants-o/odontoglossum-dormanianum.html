Odontoglossum dormanianum

Odontoglossum dormanianum, commonly known as the Crimson Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments at high altitudes, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Odontoglossum dormanianum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
