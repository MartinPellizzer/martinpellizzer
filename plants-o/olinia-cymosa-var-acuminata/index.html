Olinia Cymosa Var. Acuminata

Cape honeysuckle, scientifically known as Olinia cymosa var. acuminata, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is prized for its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its rapid growth and easy maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in many regions. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive in certain areas if not properly managed.
