Origanum husnucan-baseri

Origanum husnucan-baseri, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, pale blue to violet flowers and woody stems. Its leaves are aromatic, with a strong, pungent scent, and are used in cooking and traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for Mediterranean climates.
