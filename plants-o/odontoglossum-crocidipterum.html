Odontoglossum crocidipterum

Odontoglossum crocidipterum, commonly known as the crocidipterum orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and the flowers are borne on tall, erect stems.

Odontoglossum crocidipterum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
