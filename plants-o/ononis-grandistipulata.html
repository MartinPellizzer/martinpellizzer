Ononis grandistipulata

Ononis grandistipulata, commonly known as the large-leafed sandwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats such as coastal dunes or rocky slopes. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are usually narrow and linear. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

Ononis grandistipulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh conditions.
