Oldenlandia purpurascens

Oldenlandia purpurascens, commonly known as the redstem spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and disturbed areas, where it spreads via rhizomes.

The flowers bloom throughout the year in some regions, making it a common sight in humid climates.
