Oreophila taraxacoides

Oreophila taraxacoides, commonly known as the mountain dandelion, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Oreophila taraxacoides plays a role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems and supports local pollinators.
