Osteospermum armatum

Osteospermum armatum, commonly known as the hardy African daisy, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features a sturdy, upright stem with narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers bloom in summer and are known for their ability to retain their color even when dried.

Osteospermum armatum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
