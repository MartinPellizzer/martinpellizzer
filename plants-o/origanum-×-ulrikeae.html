Origanum × ulrikeae

Origanum × ulrikeae, commonly known as the Ulrike's oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that are often gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Origanum × ulrikeae is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, as well as its ornamental appeal.
