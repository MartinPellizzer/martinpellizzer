Ouratea decrescens

Ouratea decrescens, commonly known as the white-flowered ouratea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
