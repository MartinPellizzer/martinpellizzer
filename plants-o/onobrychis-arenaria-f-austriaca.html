Onobrychis arenaria f. austriaca

Onobrychis arenaria f. austriaca, commonly known as the Austrian vetch, is a low-growing perennial legume native to Central Europe.

It features trifoliate leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in sustainable landscaping.
