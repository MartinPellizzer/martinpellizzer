Ooclinium capillare

Ooclinium capillare, commonly known as the slender ooclinium, is a rare and specialized aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like stems and small, clustered leaves that facilitate efficient nutrient absorption. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still water, often in shaded, nutrient-rich habitats. Its reproductive structures are unique, producing motile spores that aid in dispersal.
