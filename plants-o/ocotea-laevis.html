Ocotea laevis

Ocotea laevis, commonly known as the smooth ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically smooth and glossy. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elliptical, dark blue-black berries.

Ocotea laevis is often found in humid, shaded environments within lowland and montane forests.
