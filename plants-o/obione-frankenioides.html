Obione frankenioides

Obione frankenioides, commonly known as the coastal sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in sandy and well-drained soils, often found in dune environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming during the warmer months. It has adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in harsh coastal conditions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
