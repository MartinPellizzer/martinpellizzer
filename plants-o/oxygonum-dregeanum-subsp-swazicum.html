Oxygonum dregeanum subsp. swazicum

Oxygonum dregeanum subsp.

swazicum, commonly known as Swazi oxygonum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sandy soils.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
