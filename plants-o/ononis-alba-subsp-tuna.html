Ononis alba subsp. tuna

Ononis alba subsp.

tuna, commonly known as white restharrow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, white flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of Mediterranean flora and a subject of interest in ecological studies.
