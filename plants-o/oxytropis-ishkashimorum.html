Oxytropis ishkashimorum

Oxytropis ishkashimorum, commonly known as the Iskashim locoweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Oxytropis ishkashimorum is known for its toxic alkaloids, which can affect livestock that consume it.
