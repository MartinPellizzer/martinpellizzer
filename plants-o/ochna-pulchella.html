Ochna pulchella

Ochna pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful ochna, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its ornamental, palmate leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant fruit make it a visually striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
