Oonopsis foliosa var. foliosa

Oonopsis foliosa var. foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leafed oonopsis, is a species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
