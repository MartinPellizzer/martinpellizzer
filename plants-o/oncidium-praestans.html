Oncidium praestans

Oncidium praestans, commonly known as the showy oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored, fragrant flowers that bloom in a range of hues including yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are typically strap-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Oncidium praestans is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
