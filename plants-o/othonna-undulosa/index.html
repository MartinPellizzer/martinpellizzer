Othonna Undulosa

Othonna undulosa is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, undulating leaves that resemble waves. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care. Othonna undulosa is a great choice for gardeners looking to add texture and interest to their landscapes.
