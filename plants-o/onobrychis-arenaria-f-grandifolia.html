Onobrychis arenaria f. grandifolia

Onobrychis arenaria f. grandifolia, commonly known as the sand pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, pea-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and coastal dunes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
