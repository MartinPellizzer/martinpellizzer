Ornithogalum nallihanense

Ornithogalum nallihanense, commonly known as the Nallihan bulbous star.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa, characterized by its tuberous roots and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces star-shaped blooms during the summer months.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
