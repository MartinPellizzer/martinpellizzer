Orchis × menosii

Orchis × menosii, commonly known as the lesser twayblade, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Orchis × menosii is often found in association with other orchid species, showcasing its ecological adaptability.
