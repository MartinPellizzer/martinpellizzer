Oxalis polymorpha var. cauliflora

Oxalis polymorpha var. cauliflora, commonly known as the Christmas oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems, a feature known as cauliflory. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or yellow veining. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique floral display.
