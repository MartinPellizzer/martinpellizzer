Ocimum balansae

Ocimum balansae, commonly known as Balansae basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or white. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant properties.

Ocimum balansae is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
