Orostachys iwarenge f. aureomarginata

Orostachys iwarenge f. aureomarginata, commonly known as the golden-margin sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and leaves that are typically green with distinctive golden or yellow edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red, usually during the summer months. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
