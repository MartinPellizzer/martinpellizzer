Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. glabrum

Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem bearing a single, bell-shaped flower with six white to pale green petals. It blooms during the summer months, typically from November to February.

This species is often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops, thriving in well-drained soils.
