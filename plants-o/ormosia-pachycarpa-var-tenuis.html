Ormosia pachycarpa var. tenuis

Ormosia pachycarpa var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender-fruited ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant is noted for its dense, glossy foliage and is often found in lowland rainforests. It has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

The varietal form tenuis distinguishes it by its narrower, more elongated fruit pods compared to the typical variety.
