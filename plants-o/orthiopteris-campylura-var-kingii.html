Orthiopteris campylura var. kingii

Orthiopteris campylura var. kingii, commonly known as the King's cliff fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed cliff fern.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of North America. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rocky slopes and forest floors.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.
