Ormosia macrophylla

Ormosia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by woody, pod-like fruits containing flat, winged seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its durable timber.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in providing habitat for various wildlife species.
