Oenothera heterophylla

Oenothera heterophylla, commonly known as the variable evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.
