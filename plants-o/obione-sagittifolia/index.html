Obione Sagittifolia

Obione sagittifolia is a unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves that reflect its scientific name. This hardy species thrives in harsh environments with minimal water. Its ability to store moisture makes it a resilient choice for xeriscaping. Obione sagittifolia is both an ornamental and ecological asset in its native habitat.
