Ornithogalum urbanianum

Ornithogalum urbanianum, commonly known as the urbanian moonflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that open at night. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique nocturnal blooming habit.
