Orbignya dubia

Orbignya dubia, commonly known as the babassu palm, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of hard, woody fruits. Each fruit contains a single large seed, which is used for oil extraction.

The babassu palm plays an important ecological role in its habitat and is also valued for its economic potential.
