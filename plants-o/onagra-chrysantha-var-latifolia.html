Onagra chrysantha var. latifolia

Onagra chrysantha var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
