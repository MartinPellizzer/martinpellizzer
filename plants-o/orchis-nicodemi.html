Orchis nicodemi

Orchis nicodemi, commonly known as the Nicodemus orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are pollinated by bees and other insects, and it produces small, dark brown seeds.

Orchis nicodemi is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in shaded, semi-shaded environments.
