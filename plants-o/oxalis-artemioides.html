Oxalis artemioides

Oxalis artemioides, commonly known as the silverleaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves with a velvety texture and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Oxalis artemioides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
