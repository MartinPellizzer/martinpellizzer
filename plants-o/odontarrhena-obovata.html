Odontarrhena obovata

Odontarrhena obovata, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree with woody stems and thick, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is known for its milky sap, which is used in traditional medicine.

Odontarrhena obovata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
