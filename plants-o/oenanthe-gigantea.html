Oenanthe gigantea

Oenanthe gigantea, commonly known as giant water dropwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided and often have a distinctive reddish or purplish hue.

Oenanthe gigantea is typically found near water bodies, thriving in moist, calcareous soils.
