Oenanthe approximata

Oenanthe approximata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. The plant has slender stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. Its small, white or greenish flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Oenanthe approximata is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often found in nutrient-rich, waterlogged soils.
