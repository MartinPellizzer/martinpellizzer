Oncidium jamiesonii

Oncidium jamiesonii, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that resemble butterflies, with bright yellow petals and a contrasting green lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in varying environmental conditions.

Oncidium jamiesonii requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
