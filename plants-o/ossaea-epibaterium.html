Ossaea epibaterium

Ossaea epibaterium, commonly known as the epibatic moss, is a unique species of moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth pattern and its ability to form symbiotic relationships with certain bacteria. The plant's structure consists of leafy shoots and rhizoids that anchor it to surfaces such as rocks or soil. Its reproductive structures, known as capsules, release spores into the air for dispersal.

Ossaea epibaterium plays a vital role in maintaining soil moisture and supporting microbial communities in its habitat.
