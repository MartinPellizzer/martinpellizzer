Oxalis monophylla var. monophylla

Oxalis monophylla var. monophylla, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to many garden settings.
