Oryctina myrsinites

Oryctina myrsinites, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its growth habit is herbaceous, with a sprawling or upright form depending on environmental conditions.

Oryctina myrsinites is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
