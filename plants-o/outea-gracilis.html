Outea gracilis

Outea gracilis, commonly known as the slender bamboo or slender palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and pinnate leaves. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers.

Outea gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic qualities.
