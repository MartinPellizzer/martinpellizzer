Ouratea hassleriana

Ouratea hassleriana, commonly known as the red-twigged holly.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and bright red stems that become more prominent in winter. It produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
