Oberonia tiomanensis

It is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae, characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, with a distinctive labellum that sets it apart from other Oberonia species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in lowland rainforests.
