Otiophora angustifolia

Otiophora angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves that are typically narrow and leathery. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

Otiophora angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
