Osmundastrum cinnamomeum var. fokiense

Osmundastrum cinnamomeum var. fokiense, commonly known as the Japanese water fern, is a variant of the cinnamon fern.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia, particularly Japan and China. The plant features a tall, slender, spore-bearing frond that emerges from a creeping rhizome. Its leaves are dark green and have a distinctive, narrow, lance-shaped appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.
