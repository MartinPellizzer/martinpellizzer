Olmedia laurina

Olmedia laurina, commonly known as the laurel olive, is a flowering plant belonging to the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features aromatic, dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains seeds.

Olmedia laurina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in horticulture for its fragrance and foliage.
