Oenanthe exaltata

Oenanthe exaltata, commonly known as the tall water-dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often near water bodies such as streams and ponds. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a distinct veining pattern.

The small white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the summer months.
