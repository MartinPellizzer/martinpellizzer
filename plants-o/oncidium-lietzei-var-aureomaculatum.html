Oncidium lietzei var. aureomaculatum

Oncidium lietzei var. aureomaculatum, commonly known as the golden-spotted orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The leaves are usually broad and dark green, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
