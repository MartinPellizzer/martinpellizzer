Ornithogalum khuzestanicum

Ornithogalum khuzestanicum, commonly known as the Khuzestan star-of-Bethleham, is a perennial herb native to the Iranian plateau.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
