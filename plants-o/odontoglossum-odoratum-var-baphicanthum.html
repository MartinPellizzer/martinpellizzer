Odontoglossum odoratum var. baphicanthum

Odontoglossum odoratum var. baphicanthum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique scent.
