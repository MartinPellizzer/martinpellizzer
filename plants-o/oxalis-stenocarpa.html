Oxalis stenocarpa

Oxalis stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes and produces small, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodlands. Its fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Oxalis stenocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
