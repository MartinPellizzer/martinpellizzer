Oreosedum pedicellatum subsp. lusitanicum

Oreosedum pedicellatum subsp.

lusitanicum, commonly known as the Lusitanian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe. It typically features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color, adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable plant for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
