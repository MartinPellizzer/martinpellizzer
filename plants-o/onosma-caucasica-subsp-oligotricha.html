Onosma caucasica subsp. oligotricha

Onosma caucasica subsp.

oligotricha, commonly known as Caucasian narrow-leaved onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region. It is characterized by its slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
