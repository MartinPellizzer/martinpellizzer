Ostodes corniculata

Ostodes corniculata, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Ostodes corniculata thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species in specialized horticulture.
