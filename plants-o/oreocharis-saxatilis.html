Oreocharis saxatilis

Oreocharis saxatilis, commonly known as the rock oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a striking color contrast. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, demonstrating its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Oreocharis saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
