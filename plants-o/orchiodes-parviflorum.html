Orchiodes parviflorum

Orchiodes parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers arranged in a loose spike. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette.

Orchiodes parviflorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is prized for its delicate floral appearance.
