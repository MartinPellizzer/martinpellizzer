Oncostemum elephantipes

Oncostemum elephantipes, commonly known as the giant elephant's foot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its massive, swollen base that resembles an elephant's foot, which stores water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and typically green, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Oncostemum elephantipes is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering once established.
