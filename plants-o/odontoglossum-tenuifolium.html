Odontoglossum tenuifolium

Odontoglossum tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, which helps it store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to the cool, humid environments where it thrives.

Odontoglossum tenuifolium is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
