Otomeria linearifolia

Otomeria linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved otomeria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Otomeria linearifolia is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
