Oxalis dillenii var. piletorum

Oxalis dillenii var. piletorum, commonly known as the pileated oxalis, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, shield-shaped leaves that are often adorned with red veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
