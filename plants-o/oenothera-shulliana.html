Oenothera shulliana

Oenothera shulliana, commonly known as Shull's evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Oenothera shulliana is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.
