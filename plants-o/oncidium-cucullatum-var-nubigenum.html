Oncidium cucullatum var. nubigenum

Oncidium cucullatum var. nubigenum, commonly known as the Cloudy Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, racemose inflorescence bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers often have a subtle, sweet fragrance and bloom during the warmer months.

The variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
