Orchis latifolia var. dunensis

Orchis latifolia var. dunensis, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or meadow habitats. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers arranged in a loose spike, with three petals and a distinctive lip. Its leaves are broad and lanceolate, often with a waxy coating.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
