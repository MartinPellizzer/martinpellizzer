Oenothera biennis var. grandiflora

Oenothera biennis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Oenothera biennis var. grandiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
