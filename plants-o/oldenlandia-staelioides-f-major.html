Oldenlandia staelioides f. major

Oldenlandia staelioides f. major, commonly known as the large-leaved spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers bloom during the rainy season and are pollinated by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
