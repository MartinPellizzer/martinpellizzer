Ochna parviflora

Ochna parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered ochna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, hard, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed.

Ochna parviflora is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
