Ophryosporus foliolosus

Ophryosporus foliolosus, commonly known as the leafy spore plant, is a unique species within the family Ophryosporaceae.

It is characterized by its small, leaf-like structures that are actually modified sporophylls, which bear the spores. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures are highly specialized, with spores released in a controlled manner to enhance dispersal.

This plant plays a minor but ecologically significant role in nutrient cycling within its habitat.
