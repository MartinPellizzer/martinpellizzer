Oreopanax bonplandianus

Oreopanax bonplandianus, commonly known as the mountain sarsaparilla, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, leathery, palmate leaves that can grow up to 50 cm in diameter. It produces striking spadices with yellowish-green flowers, typical of the Araceae family.

Oreopanax bonplandianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments.
