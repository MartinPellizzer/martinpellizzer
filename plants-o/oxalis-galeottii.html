Oxalis galeottii

Oxalis galeottii, commonly known as the Cape mountain oxalis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover.

Oxalis galeottii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
