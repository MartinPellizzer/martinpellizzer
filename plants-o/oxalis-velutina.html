Oxalis velutina

Oxalis velutina, commonly known as velvet wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with a velvety texture and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a short, thick rhizome that helps it survive in various soil conditions. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
