Orchis kiraishiensis var. leucantha

Orchis kiraishiensis var. leucantha, commonly known as the Kiraishi orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are pale in color, with a distinctive white or pale pink hue, and it has lanceolate leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
