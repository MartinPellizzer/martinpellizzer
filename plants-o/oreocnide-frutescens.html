Oreocnide frutescens

Oreocnide frutescens, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, simple leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
