Osmanthus longissimus

Osmanthus longissimus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns red when mature.

Osmanthus longissimus is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and for the aromatic oils extracted from its flowers.
