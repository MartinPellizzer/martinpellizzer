Oncidium × denisoniae

Oncidium × denisoniae, commonly known as the Denison's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Oncidium × denisoniae requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
