Ornithogalum chloroleucum

Ornithogalum chloroleucum, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
