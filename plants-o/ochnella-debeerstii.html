Ochnella debeerstii

Ochnella debeerstii, commonly known as the Beaufort daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, leathery leaves and produces fruit that splits open to release its seeds.

Ochnella debeerstii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
