Ornithoglossum glaucum

Ornithoglossum glaucum, commonly known as the blue star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are often hairy as well. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Ornithoglossum glaucum is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
