Oxalis lechleri

Oxalis lechleri, commonly known as the "Dragon's Blood Tree," is a perennial shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is renowned for producing a dark red sap, which has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties. Its botanical features include a woody stem and a system of tuberous roots that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Oxalis lechleri plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various insect and bird species.
