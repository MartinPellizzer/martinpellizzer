Oncidium zebrinum

Oncidium zebrinum, commonly known as the zebrina orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with a distinctive striped pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium zebrinum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and striking appearance.
