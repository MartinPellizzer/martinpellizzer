Oxalis bela-vitoriae

Oxalis bela-vitoriae, commonly known as the Violet Oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant produces yellowish-orange blooms that are often tinged with red, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Oxalis bela-vitoriae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
