Ononis Natrix Subvar. Perusiana

Ononis natrix subvar. perusiana is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species native to specific regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the legume family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities and ecological importance. This subvariety is distinguished by its unique floral structure and adaptation to rocky, arid environments. Despite its limited distribution, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem. Understanding and conserving this plant is essential for maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
