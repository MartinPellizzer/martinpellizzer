Oncidium auroincarum

Oncidium auroincarum, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, showy inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that resemble torches, often with a contrasting red or orange lip. The plant has long, narrow leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to prevent root rot.

Oncidium auroincarum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
