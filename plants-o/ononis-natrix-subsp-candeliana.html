Ononis natrix subsp. candeliana

Ononis natrix subsp.

candeliana, commonly known as the candlestick sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically forms dense mats of tiny, rounded leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes. It has a compact growth habit with short, branching stems that give it a candle-like appearance.

This subspecies is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
