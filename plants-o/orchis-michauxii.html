Orchis michauxii

Orchis michauxii, commonly known as Michaux's orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded habitats and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems.

Orchis michauxii is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
