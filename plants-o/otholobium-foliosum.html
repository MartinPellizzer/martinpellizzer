Otholobium foliosum

Otholobium foliosum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed burr medic, is a perennial legume native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces distinctive burr-like seed pods. The plant is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and is used in rehabilitation of degraded lands.

Otholobium foliosum is also valued for its forage qualities and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
