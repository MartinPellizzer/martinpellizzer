Oldenlandia japonica

Oldenlandia japonica, commonly known as the Japanese spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Oldenlandia japonica is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
