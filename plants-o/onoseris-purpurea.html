Onoseris purpurea

Onoseris purpurea, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Onoseris purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
