Ornithogalum fuscescens

Ornithogalum fuscescens, commonly known as the purple star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale purple to white, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
