Oncidium echinatum var. brunleesianum

Oncidium echinatum var. brunleesianum, commonly known as the Brunlesian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences with vibrant yellow and orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting its growth habit.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
