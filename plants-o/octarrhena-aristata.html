Octarrhena aristata

Octarrhena aristata, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss. Its yellowish-green flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming during the cooler months.

It is well adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
