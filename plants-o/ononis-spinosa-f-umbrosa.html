Ononis spinosa f. umbrosa

Ononis spinosa f. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade-loving everlasting pea, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its low-growing habit and dense foliage.

It features small, pea-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This fern-like plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in shaded environments.
