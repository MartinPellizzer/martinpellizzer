Onopordum excelsum

Onopordum excelsum, commonly known as the giant thistle, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features large, spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a flowering stem with bracts that protect the flower heads.
