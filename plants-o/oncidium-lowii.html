Oncidium lowii

Oncidium lowii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern. The plant has aerial roots that help it climb and attach to trees in its natural habitat. Oncidium lowii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its flowers typically last several weeks, adding a vibrant touch to gardens or homes.
