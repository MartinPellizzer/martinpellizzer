Orthosiphon discolor

Orthosiphon discolor, commonly known as Java tea or Malabar skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical regions.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
