Onoseris costaricensis

Onoseris costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in tropical rainforests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
