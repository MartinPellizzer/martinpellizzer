Othonna cineraria

Othonna cineraria, commonly known as the ash-gray othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with silver-gray foliage that has a powdery coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
