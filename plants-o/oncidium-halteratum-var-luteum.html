Oncidium halteratum var. luteum

Oncidium halteratum var. luteum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape and a sweet, spicy fragrance. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in warm, humid environments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
