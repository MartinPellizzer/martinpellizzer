Ornithogalum sandrasicum

Ornithogalum sandrasicum, commonly known as the sandrasicum star-of-Bethleem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum sandrasicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
