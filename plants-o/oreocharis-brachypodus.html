Oreocharis brachypodus

Oreocharis brachypodus, commonly known as the short-rooted oreocharis, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
