Ocotea guianensis

Ocotea guianensis, commonly known as Guianese ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Venezuela, Colombia, and Guyana. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical berries containing seeds.

Ocotea guianensis is often found in moist, shaded environments within lowland rainforests.
