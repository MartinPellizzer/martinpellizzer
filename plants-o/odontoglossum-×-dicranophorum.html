Odontoglossum × dicranophorum

Odontoglossum × dicranophorum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum and Dicranophora species.

It is a herbaceous perennial with upright, branched stems that bear clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has elliptical, dark green leaves and produces pendulous, brightly colored blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its unique floral characteristics and adaptability to various growing conditions.
