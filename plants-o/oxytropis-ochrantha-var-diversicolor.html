Oxytropis ochrantha var. diversicolor

Oxytropis ochrantha var. diversicolor, commonly known as the Sierra buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.
