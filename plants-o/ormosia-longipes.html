Ormosia longipes

Ormosia longipes, commonly known as the long-peduncled ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

Ormosia longipes is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
