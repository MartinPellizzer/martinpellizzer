Ornithogalum umbellatum var. longebracteatum

Ornithogalum umbellatum var. longebracteatum, commonly known as the long bract star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems. The flowers are typically arranged in an umbel, giving the plant its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
