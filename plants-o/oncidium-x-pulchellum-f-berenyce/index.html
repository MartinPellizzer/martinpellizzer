Oncidium × Pulchellum F. Berenyce

Berenice orchid, scientifically known as Oncidium × pulchellum f. berenyce, is a captivating hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with ample sunlight. Its flowers, which range from yellow to orange with intricate markings, make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Easy to care for, it requires well-draining soil and regular watering to flourish. This unique orchid is not only a beautiful addition to any collection but also a symbol of resilience and natural beauty.
