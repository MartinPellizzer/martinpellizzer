Ornithogalum thunbergianulum

Ornithogalum thunbergianulum, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbous structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The flowers bloom in spring and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
