Ourouparia formosana

Ourouparia formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and purple. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and ornamental value.
