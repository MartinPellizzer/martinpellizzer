Odontonema lilacinum

Odontonema lilacinum, commonly known as the purple flame or violet flame, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features vibrant purple and pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to any garden. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contrasting beautifully with its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Odontonema lilacinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a container plant or in shaded garden areas.
