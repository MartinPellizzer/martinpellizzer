Orchiodes polygonoides

Orchiodes polygonoides, commonly known as the polygon-like orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive inflorescence that consists of a dense spike of flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, while its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a complex structure.

Orchiodes polygonoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
