Olearia kernotii

Olearia kernotii, commonly known as the Mount Cook daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually silver-gray and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Olearia kernotii is often found in rocky or open habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
