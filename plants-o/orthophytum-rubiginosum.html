Orthophytum rubiginosum

Orthophytum rubiginosum, commonly known as the red-stemmed pincushion cactus, is a striking succulent native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It features a dense cluster of cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically reddish-brown in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Orthophytum rubiginosum is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
