Olearia astroloba

Olearia astroloba, commonly known as the star-lobed daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage is covered in fine, silver-gray hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Olearia astroloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
