Osmanthus wilsonii

Osmanthus wilsonii, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Osmanthus wilsonii thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.
