Osmanthus scortechinii

Osmanthus scortechinii, commonly known as the Chinese osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its flowers are highly valued for their aromatic properties and are used in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary applications.

Osmanthus scortechinii is also cultivated as an ornamental plant for its ornate appearance and pleasant fragrance.
