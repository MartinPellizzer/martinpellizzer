Ononis baetica subsp. donanensis

Ononis baetica subsp.

donanensis, commonly known as the Iberian sand thorn, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal. It thrives in dry, sandy, or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are typically narrow and hairy. It has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in harsh, nutrient-poor environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
