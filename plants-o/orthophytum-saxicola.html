Orthophytum saxicola

Orthophytum saxicola, commonly known as the stone candelabra cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a succulent that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, exhibiting a compact, branching structure. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, and it has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water.

Orthophytum saxicola is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
