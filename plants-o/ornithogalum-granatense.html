Ornithogalum granatense

Ornithogalum granatense, commonly known as the granate star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive star shape. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its blooming period typically occurs in spring, adding a delicate beauty to gardens.
