Orthosiphon aristatus var. velteri

Orthosiphon aristatus var. velteri, commonly known as Java tea or *Plectranthus aristatus*, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, narrow lance-shaped leaves, and spikes of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.

The leaves are used in herbal teas and are believed to have diuretic properties.
