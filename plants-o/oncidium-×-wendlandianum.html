Oncidium × wendlandianum

Oncidium × wendlandianum, commonly known as the Wendland's Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a variety of floral forms and colors.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically showy, with intricate patterns and a range of hues from yellow to red.

The orchid requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to flourish.
