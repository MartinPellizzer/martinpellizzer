Oenothera cespitosa var. australis

Oenothera cespitosa var. australis, commonly known as the Australian evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Onagraceae family.
