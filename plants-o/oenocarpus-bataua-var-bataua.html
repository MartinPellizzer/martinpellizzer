Oenocarpus bataua var. bataua

Oenocarpus bataua var. bataua, commonly known as the bataua palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of large, elongated fruits that are typically green when mature. These fruits are encased in a thick, fibrous husk and contain a single, large seed.

The bataua palm is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local communities.
