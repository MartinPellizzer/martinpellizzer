Odontoglossum pescatorei var. germinyanum

Odontoglossum pescatorei var. germinyanum, commonly known as the Germinyanum Orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, lance-shaped leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, with inflorescences bearing multiple violet to purple blossoms. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
