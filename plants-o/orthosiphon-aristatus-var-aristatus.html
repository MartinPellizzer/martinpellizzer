Orthosiphon aristatus var. aristatus

Orthosiphon aristatus var. aristatus, commonly known as Java tea or *Plectranthus amboinicus*, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

This species is widely cultivated for its medicinal properties and ornamental value.
