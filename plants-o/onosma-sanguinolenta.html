Onosma sanguinolenta

Onosma sanguinolenta, commonly known as the bloody rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant has gray-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
