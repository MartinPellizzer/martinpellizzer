Onosma tricerosperma subsp. fastigiata

Onosma tricerosperma subsp.

fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved onosma, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, arranged in a compact, grass-like structure.

This subspecies is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh climates.
