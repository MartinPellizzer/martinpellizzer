Oenothera Lepida Var. Parviflora

Oenothera lepida var. parviflora is a rare and delicate wildflower native to the western united states. it is known for its small, pale yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. this variant of the evening primrose is often found in rocky soils and open habitats. its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival in the wild.
