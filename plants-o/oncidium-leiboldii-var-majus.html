Oncidium leiboldii var. majus

Oncidium leiboldii var. majus, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that typically display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
