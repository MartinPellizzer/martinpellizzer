Ononis vaticinatorum

Ononis vaticinatorum, commonly known as the prophet's thorn, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
