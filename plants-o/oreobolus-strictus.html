Oreobolus strictus

Oreobolus strictus, commonly known as the rigid sand sedge, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal dunes or inland saline habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish flowers arranged in spikelets.
