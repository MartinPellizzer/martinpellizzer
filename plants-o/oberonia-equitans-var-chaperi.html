Oberonia equitans var. chaperi

Oberonia equitans var. chaperi, commonly known as the Chaperi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a short rhizome and a single, often curved leaf. The flower spike produces a few showy, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in mountainous regions.

This variety is notable for its compact size and ornamental value in horticulture.
