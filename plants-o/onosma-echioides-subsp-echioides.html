Onosma echioides subsp. echioides

Onosma echioides subsp.

echioides, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Hoary Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry and sunny habitats.

The plant features slender, erect stems and small, hairy leaves that are often grayish or blue-green in color. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
