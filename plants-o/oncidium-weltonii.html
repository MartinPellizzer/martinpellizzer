Oncidium weltonii

Oncidium weltonii, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with showy, yellow flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Oncidium weltonii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements.
