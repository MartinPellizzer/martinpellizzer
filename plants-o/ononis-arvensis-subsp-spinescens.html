Ononis arvensis subsp. spinescens

Ononis arvensis subsp.

spinescens, commonly known as the spiny field cranesbill, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas, disturbed soils, and along roadsides.

The plant features slender, upright stems that are often covered in small, stiff hairs. Its flowers are pink to purple in color, arranged in loose clusters, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
