Oxytropis dinarica var. pseudourumovii

Oxytropis dinarica var. pseudourumovii, commonly known as the Dinaric oxytropis, is a perennial legume native to the Balkan region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to mountainous environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, compound leaves and produces yellowish to reddish flowers. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

This variant is distinguished by its specific morphological traits and geographical distribution.
