Orbivestus unionis

Orbivestus unionis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, metallic sheen leaves that reflect sunlight. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited to its harsh native environment.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
