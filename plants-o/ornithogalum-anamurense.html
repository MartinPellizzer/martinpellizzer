Ornithogalum anamurense

Ornithogalum anamurense, commonly known as the Anamur star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats.

Ornithogalum anamurense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
