Oldenlandia herbacea

Oldenlandia herbacea, commonly known as the common spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Oldenlandia herbacea is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
