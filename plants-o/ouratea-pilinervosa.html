Ouratea pilinervosa

Ouratea pilinervosa, commonly known as the silky ouratea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. Its ornamental value is notable due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
