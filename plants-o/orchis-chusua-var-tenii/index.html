Orchis Chusua Var. Tenii

Purple orchis, scientifically known as Orchis chusua var. tenii, is a rare and striking wildflower native to parts of Europe and Asia. This species belongs to the orchid family, known for its complex floral structures and diverse adaptations. Its vivid purple petals and unique morphology make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Found in woodland and meadow habitats, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Despite its beauty, the Purple orchis is threatened by habitat loss and climate change, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
