Oxylobium capitatum var. angustifolium

Oxylobium capitatum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed oxylobium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to cream-colored flowers, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and heathland environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
