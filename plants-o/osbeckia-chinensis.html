Osbeckia chinensis

Osbeckia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese osbeckia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Goodeniaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. Osbeckia chinensis is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
