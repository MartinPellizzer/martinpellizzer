Oberonia seidenfadenii

Oberonia seidenfadenii, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate structure typical of the Oberonia genus. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
