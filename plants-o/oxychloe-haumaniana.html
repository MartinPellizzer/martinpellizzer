Oxychloe haumaniana

Oxychloe haumaniana, commonly known as the Hauman's oxychloe, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a small, perennial herb characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish to pale blue flowers. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, disturbed soils.
