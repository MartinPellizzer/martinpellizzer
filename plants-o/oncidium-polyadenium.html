Oncidium polyadenium

Oncidium polyadenium, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Oncidium polyadenium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
