Orobus tuberosus var. major

Orobus tuberosus var. major, commonly known as the giant tuberous orobus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, tuberous roots that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
