Oleandra ejurana

Oleandra ejurana, commonly known as the red-flowered oleander, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth texture. Oleandra ejurana is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
