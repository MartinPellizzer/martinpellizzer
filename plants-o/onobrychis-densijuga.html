Onobrychis densijuga

Onobrychis densijuga, commonly known as the dense-jug vetch, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Onobrychis densijuga is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
