Ornithogalum amblyocarpum

Ornithogalum amblyocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help it survive dry periods and regenerate each growing season.

Ornithogalum amblyocarpum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soils.
