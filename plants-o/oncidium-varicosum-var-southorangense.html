Oncidium varicosum var. southorangense

Oncidium varicosum var. southorangense, commonly known as the South Orange orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features pendant, raceme-like inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly curled. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
