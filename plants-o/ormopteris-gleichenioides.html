Ormopteris gleichenioides

Ormopteris gleichenioides, commonly known as the Gleichenoid fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its long, narrow fronds that are typically divided into small, linear segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Ormopteris gleichenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
