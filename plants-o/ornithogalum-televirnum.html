Ornithogalum televirnum

Ornithogalum televirnum, commonly known as the silverleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant is adapted to Mediterranean climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
