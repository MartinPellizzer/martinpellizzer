Obione laciniata

Obione laciniata, commonly known as the lace fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a lacy appearance, making it visually striking. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its leaves are bioluminescent, emitting a faint glow at night, which aids in attracting nocturnal pollinators.

This unique characteristic makes Obione laciniata a fascinating subject for botanical study and a popular choice for specialized garden settings.
