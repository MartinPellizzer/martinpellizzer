Ononis variegata var. alleizettei

Ononis variegata var. alleizettei, commonly known as the Variegated Sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has a sprawling habit with hairy, succulent stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
