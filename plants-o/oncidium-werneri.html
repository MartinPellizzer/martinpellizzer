Oncidium werneri

Oncidium werneri, commonly known as the Werneri orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity. Oncidium werneri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its flowers can last several weeks, adding a vibrant touch to any collection.
