Ocotea estrellensis

Ocotea estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrella ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark blue berries.

Ocotea estrellensis is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its aromatic properties.
