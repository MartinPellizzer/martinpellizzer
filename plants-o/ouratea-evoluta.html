Ouratea evoluta

Ouratea evoluta, commonly known as the white star magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its showy, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
