Oryza Perennis Subsp. Barthii

Barth's perennial rice, scientifically known as Oryza perennis subsp. barthii, is a unique and promising crop that offers a sustainable alternative to traditional annual rice varieties. Unlike its annual counterparts, this plant can produce grain for multiple harvests without needing to be replanted each year. Native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia, it has garnered attention for its potential to reduce agricultural labor and resource use. Its ability to thrive in diverse conditions makes it a valuable candidate for climate-resilient farming. Researchers and farmers alike are exploring its role in enhancing food security and environmental sustainability.
