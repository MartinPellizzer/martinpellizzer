Ouratea longifolia var. microcalyx

Ouratea longifolia var. microcalyx, commonly known as the dwarf ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a small, smooth, spherical berry that turns red when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
