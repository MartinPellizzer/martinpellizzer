Orthophytum jabrense

Orthophytum jabrense, commonly known as the Jabrense Orthophytum, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, columnar growth habit and features a spiny, ribbed stem. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences at the stem tips. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
