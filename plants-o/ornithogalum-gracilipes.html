Ornithogalum gracilipes

Ornithogalum gracilipes, commonly known as the slender star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum gracilipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in garden settings.
