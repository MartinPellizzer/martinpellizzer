Ornithogalum luteum var. majus

Ornithogalum luteum var. majus, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features upright, narrow leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers in early summer. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
