Oenothera suzukiana

Oenothera suzukiana, commonly known as the Japanese evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

Oenothera suzukiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique flowering habit.
