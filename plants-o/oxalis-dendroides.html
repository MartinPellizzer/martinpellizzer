Oxalis dendroides

Oxalis dendroides, commonly known as the tree oxalis, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branched, woody stem with fleshy, alternate leaves that are often lobed or toothed. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow, pink, or white, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.
