Oxypetalum parviflorum var. parviflorum

Oxypetalum parviflorum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered oxypetalum, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a dark green color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
