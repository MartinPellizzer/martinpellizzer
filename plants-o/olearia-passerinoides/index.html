Olearia Passerinoides

Olearia passerinoides is a unique plant native to New Zealand, known for its distinctive silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. This shrub thrives in coastal and subalpine environments, adapting well to harsh weather conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is valued for its ornamental appeal. The plant's ability to withstand salt spray and strong winds makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Despite its beauty, Olearia passerinoides is also an important component of New Zealand's native ecosystems.
