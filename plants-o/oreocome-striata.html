Oreocome striata

Oreocome striata, commonly known as the striped oreocome, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. The plant thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
