Oncidium micranthum

Oncidium micranthum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a short, upright stem that bears several leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically arranged in a raceme. Oncidium micranthum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and elegant blooms make it a favored ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
