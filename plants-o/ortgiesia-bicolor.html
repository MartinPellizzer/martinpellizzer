Ortgiesia bicolor

Ortgiesia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly contrasting leaf colors, with one side green and the other white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
