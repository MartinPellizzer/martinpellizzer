Othonna retrorsa var. retrorsa

Othonna retrorsa var. retrorsa, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners in arid climates.
