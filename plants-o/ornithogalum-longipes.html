Ornithogalum longipes

Ornithogalum longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant is characterized by its elongated, slender stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum longipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
