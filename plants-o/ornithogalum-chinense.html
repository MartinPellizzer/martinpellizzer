Ornithogalum chinense

Ornithogalum chinense, commonly known as Chinese star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
