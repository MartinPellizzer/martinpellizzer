Obione hypoleuca

Obione hypoleuca, commonly known as the white orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant features delicate, white petals with a subtle fragrance, and its flowers are often clustered in small inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
