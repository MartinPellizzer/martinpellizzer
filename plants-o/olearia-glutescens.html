Olearia glutescens

Olearia glutescens, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, felt-like foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Olearia glutescens thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique texture and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
