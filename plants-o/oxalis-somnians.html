Oxalis somnians

Oxalis somnians, commonly known as the sleeping beauty, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is named for its unique behavior of closing its leaves at night, resembling a sleeping state. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Oxalis somnians is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
