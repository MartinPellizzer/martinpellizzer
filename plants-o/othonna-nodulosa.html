Othonna nodulosa

Othonna nodulosa, commonly known as the knobbed othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact foliage with distinctive knobby tubercles on its leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna nodulosa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
