Ornithogalum maculatum var. speciosum

Ornithogalum maculatum var. speciosum, commonly known as the spotted lily or white star lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers with a greenish tint. The plant is characterized by its showy inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
