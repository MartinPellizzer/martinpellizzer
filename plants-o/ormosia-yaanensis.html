Ormosia yaanensis

Ormosia yaanensis, commonly known as the Yaanensis ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Ormosia yaanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
