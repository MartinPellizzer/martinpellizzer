Oreopanax grandifolius

Oreopanax grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf oreopanax, is a tropical plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and valuable species in the study of tropical flora.
