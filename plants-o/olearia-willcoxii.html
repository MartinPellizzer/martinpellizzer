Olearia willcoxii

Olearia willcoxii, commonly known as the Stewart Island daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow daisy-like flowers. Olearia willcoxii is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is well-suited to maritime climates.
