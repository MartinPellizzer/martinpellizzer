Onopordum nezaketianum

Onopordum nezaketianum, commonly known as the Nezaketian thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to temperate climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important part of its local ecosystem.
