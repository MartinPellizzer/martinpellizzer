Oncidium oxystegium

Oncidium oxystegium, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive white lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Oncidium oxystegium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
