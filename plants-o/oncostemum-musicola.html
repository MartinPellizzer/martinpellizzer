Oncostemum musicola

Oncostemum musicola, commonly known as the musical orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests, where it thrives in the understory. Its unique floral structure includes specialized petals and sepals that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Oncostemum musicola is valued not only for its aesthetic beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
