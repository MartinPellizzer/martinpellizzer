Ornithogalum pendens

Ornithogalum pendens, commonly known as the hanging star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that hang downward from the stem. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a delicate touch to gardens.
