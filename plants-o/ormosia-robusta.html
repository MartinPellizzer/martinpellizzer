Ormosia robusta

Ormosia robusta, commonly known as the robust ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisine. Ormosia robusta is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its growth habit and botanical features make it an important species in its native ecosystems.
