Oncidium diceratum

Oncidium diceratum, commonly known as the showy oncidium, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms. The plant typically produces clusters of pendant, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Oncidium diceratum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
