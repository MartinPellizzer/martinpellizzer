Oberonia palmicola

Oberonia palmicola, commonly known as the palm leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique appearance, featuring a fleshy, palm-like pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
