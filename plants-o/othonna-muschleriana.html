Othonna muschleriana

Othonna muschleriana, commonly known as the Muschler's othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Othonna muschleriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
