Ornithogalum navaschinii

Ornithogalum navaschinii, commonly known as the Navaschinii star-of-Bethlem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a greenish tinge. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum navaschinii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
