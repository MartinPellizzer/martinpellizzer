Ossaea pulverulenta

Ossaea pulverulenta, commonly known as the desert sandflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the short rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Ossaea pulverulenta plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and supporting local wildlife.
