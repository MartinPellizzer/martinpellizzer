Odontonema pantasmense

Odontonema pantasmense, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Odontonema pantasmense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
