Ononis hispida var. pedunculiflora

Ononis hispida var. pedunculiflora, commonly known as the hairy bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems covered in fine, stiff hairs. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a rough texture due to the presence of glandular hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
