Ormosia integrifolia

Ormosia integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaf ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its simple, entire leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Ormosia integrifolia is valued for its hard wood and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
