Otholobium caffrum

Otholobium caffrum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardiness and attractive blooms.

Otholobium caffrum plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
