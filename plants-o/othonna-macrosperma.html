Othonna macrosperma

Othonna macrosperma, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a prominent midrib.

Othonna macrosperma is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, sunny climates.
