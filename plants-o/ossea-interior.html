Ossea interior

Ossea interior, commonly known as the interior ossea, is a rare and delicate plant species native to specific regions with high humidity and rich soil.

It features a unique structure with elongated, translucent leaves that allow for efficient light absorption. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the spring season. Its roots are highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it challenging to cultivate outside its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity, Ossea interior is often studied for its potential in ecological restoration and botanical research.
