Oxygonum junodii

Oxygonum junodii, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a distinctive, star-shaped appearance with fleshy, waxy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant has a short, thick stem with clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for arid environments.

Oxygonum junodii is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor and outdoor settings.
