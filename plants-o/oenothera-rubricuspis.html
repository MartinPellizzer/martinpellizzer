Oenothera rubricuspis

Oenothera rubricuspis, commonly known as the scarlet evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red to orange-yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Oenothera rubricuspis is part of the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is known for its nocturnal blooming habit.
