Oncidium sancti-pauli

Oncidium sancti-pauli, commonly known as the Paul's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendant-like inflorescences with vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium sancti-pauli is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
