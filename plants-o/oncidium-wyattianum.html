Oncidium wyattianum

Oncidium wyattianum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip, often arranged in a spike. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium wyattianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
