Onoseris hyssopifolia var. planifolia

Onoseris hyssopifolia var. planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its blue to purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variant is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
