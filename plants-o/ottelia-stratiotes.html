Ottelia stratiotes

Ottelia stratiotes, commonly known as water buttercup, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Ottelia stratiotes plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and stabilizing water surfaces.
