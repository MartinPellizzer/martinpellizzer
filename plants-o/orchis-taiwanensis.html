Orchis taiwanensis

Orchis taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, with a distinctive lip petal that aids in pollination.

Orchis taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
