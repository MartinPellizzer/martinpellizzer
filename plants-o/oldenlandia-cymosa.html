Oldenlandia cymosa

Oldenlandia cymosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Oldenlandia cymosa is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
