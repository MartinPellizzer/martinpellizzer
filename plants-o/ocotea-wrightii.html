Ocotea wrightii

Ocotea wrightii, commonly known as Wright's ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Central America and parts of Mexico, where it typically grows in humid, tropical climates. Ocotea wrightii is also valued for its wood, which is used in traditional crafts and construction.
