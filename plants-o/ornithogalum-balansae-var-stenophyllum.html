Ornithogalum balansae var. stenophyllum

Ornithogalum balansae var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a prominent central vein. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
