Onosma caucasica subsp. caucasica

Onosma caucasica subsp.

caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian heliotrope, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region. It features slender, ascending stems covered in fine, soft hairs.

The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a bluish-gray hue due to dense pubescence. Its flowers are typically purple or violet, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

This species thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
