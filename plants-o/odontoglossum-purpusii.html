Odontoglossum purpusii

Odontoglossum purpusii, commonly known as the Purple Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum purpusii is a popular choice for collectors due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
