Ochradenus randonioides var. glaber

Ochradenus randonioides var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth ochradenus, is a shrub native to arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in dry, sandy soils. Its leaves are typically smooth and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss.

This species is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
