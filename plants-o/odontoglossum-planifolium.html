Odontoglossum planifolium

Odontoglossum planifolium, commonly known as the flat-leaf orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and flat, contributing to its common name. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum planifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
