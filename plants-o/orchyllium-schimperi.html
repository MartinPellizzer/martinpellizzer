Orchyllium schimperi

Orchyllium schimperi, commonly known as the Schimper's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Africa, particularly found in regions of Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Orchyllium schimperi is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
