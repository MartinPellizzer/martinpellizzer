Orthophytum fosterianum var. estevesii

Orthophytum fosterianum var. estevesii, commonly known as the Estevess' orthophytum, is a striking succulent native to Brazil.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and resilience.
