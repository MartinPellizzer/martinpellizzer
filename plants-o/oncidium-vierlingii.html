Oncidium vierlingii

Oncidium vierlingii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive orange lip, which are typically arranged in a raceme. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in seasonal environments. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium vierlingii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
