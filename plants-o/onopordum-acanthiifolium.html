Onopordum acanthiifolium

Onopordum acanthiifolium, commonly known as the prickly centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Onopordum acanthiifolium is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid environments.
