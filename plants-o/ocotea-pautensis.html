Ocotea pautensis

Ocotea pautensis, commonly known as the Pautensis Ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Central and South America, particularly in regions of Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.

Ocotea pautensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
