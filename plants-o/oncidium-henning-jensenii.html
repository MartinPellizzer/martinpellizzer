Oncidium henning-jensenii

Oncidium henning-jensenii, commonly known as the Henning-Jensen's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting orchid blooms that typically appear in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Oncidium henning-jensenii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
