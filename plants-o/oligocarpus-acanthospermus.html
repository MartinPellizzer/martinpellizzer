Oligocarpus acanthospermus

Oligocarpus acanthospermus, commonly known as the spiny-seed oligocarpus, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. Its fruit is a dry, spiny capsule containing several seeds.

It is typically found in dry, sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
