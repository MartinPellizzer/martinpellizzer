Orbea paradoxa

Orbea paradoxa, commonly known as the paradox orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has fleshy, spiny stems and produces small, rounded leaves that help it store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Orbea paradoxa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
