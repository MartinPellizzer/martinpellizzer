Opuntia panellana

Opuntia panellana, commonly known as the Panellan prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems and spines, which are adaptations to its arid environment. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by red or purple fruits. Opuntia panellana is a perennial plant that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
