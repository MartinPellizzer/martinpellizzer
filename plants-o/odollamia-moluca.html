Odollamia moluca

Odollamia moluca, commonly known as the Molucca nut or Molucca chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to the Molucca Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The tree produces woody, spiny fruits that enclose edible seeds, which are used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
