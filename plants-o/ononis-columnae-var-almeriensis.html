Ononis columnae var. almeriensis

Ononis columnae var. almeriensis, commonly known as Almeria's columnar ononis, is a variant of the columnar ononis species native to southern Spain.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with slender, upright stems and small, pale green leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This plant is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
