Ononis subcordata subsp. cavanillesii

Ononis subcordata subsp.

cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' bedstraw, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, white or pale pink flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, sunny habitats. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive sub-cordate base.
