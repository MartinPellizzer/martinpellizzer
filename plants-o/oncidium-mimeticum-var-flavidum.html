Oncidium mimeticum var. flavidum

Oncidium mimeticum var. flavidum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers with a subtle scent. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
