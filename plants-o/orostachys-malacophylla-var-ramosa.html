Orostachys malacophylla var. ramosa

Orostachys malacophylla var. ramosa, commonly known as the branched stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Asia.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
