Otanthera setulosa

Otanthera setulosa, commonly known as the silky oatgrass, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features slender, grass-like stems with fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often have a soft, fuzzy texture.

Otanthera setulosa typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
