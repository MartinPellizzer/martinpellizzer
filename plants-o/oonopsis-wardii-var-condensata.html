Oonopsis wardii var. condensata

Oonopsis wardii var. condensata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers.
