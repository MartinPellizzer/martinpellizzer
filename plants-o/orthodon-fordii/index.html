Orthodon Fordii

Orthodon fordii is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. Known for its striking pink and white blooms, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the orchid family and is often referred to as the "Chinese orchid." Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of both scientific study and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Orthodon fordii remains elusive and challenging to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
