Ornithidium histrionicum

Ornithidium histrionicum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and botanical collections.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled greenhouse settings.
