Opuntia wiegandii

Opuntia wiegandii, commonly known as the Wiegand's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in sharp spines and glochids. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers, which are followed by edible, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing remarkable drought tolerance.

Its structure and adaptations make it well-suited for survival in harsh desert conditions.
