Oncidium halteratum var. fuscum

Oncidium halteratum var. fuscum, commonly known as the dark fuscum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, long-lasting inflorescences with flowers that range from yellow to orange, often with a dark brown or purple lip. The plant has wiry, leafless stems and is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
