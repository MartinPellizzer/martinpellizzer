Ornithidium tonsoniae

Ornithidium tonsoniae, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The botanical significance of Ornithidium tonsoniae lies in its unique adaptations and its role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
