Oxydectes lechleri

Oxydectes lechleri, commonly known as the caucho tree, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces a milky latex sap that is widely used in the rubber industry. The plant has a thick, fibrous bark that provides structural support and helps protect the tree from environmental stress. Its roots are extensive, allowing it to anchor firmly in the nutrient-poor soils of the rainforest.

The caucho tree plays a vital ecological role by providing habitat and contributing to the biodiversity of the Amazon ecosystem.
