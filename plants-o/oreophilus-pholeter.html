Oreophilus pholeter

It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in mountainous areas with rich, well-drained soil. The plant features a compact, herbaceous structure with broad, waxy leaves that help retain moisture. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically displaying shades of white and pale blue.
