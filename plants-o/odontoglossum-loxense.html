Odontoglossum loxense

Odontoglossum loxense, commonly known as the Loxense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in cloud forests, where it thrives in cool and humid conditions. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky substrates it inhabits.

Odontoglossum loxense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
