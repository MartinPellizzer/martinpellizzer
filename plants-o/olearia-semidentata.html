Olearia semidentata

Olearia semidentata, commonly known as the slender tree-ferns, is a species of tree-fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically light green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Olearia semidentata is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
