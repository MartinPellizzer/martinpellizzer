Othonna semicalva

Othonna semicalva, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Othonna semicalva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
