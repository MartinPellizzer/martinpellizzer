Oeosporangium yunnanense

Oeosporangium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan spore sac fern, is a rare and ancient fern species native to China.

It is characterized by its unique reproductive structures, specifically its ovoid spore sacs, which are clustered in specialized leaf bases. The plant exhibits a primitive morphology, with fertile fronds that bear spores in distinct, sac-like formations. Its botanical significance lies in its evolutionary position, offering insights into the early development of spore-producing plants.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
