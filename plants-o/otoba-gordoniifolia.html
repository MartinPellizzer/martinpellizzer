Otoba gordoniifolia

Otoba gordoniifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
