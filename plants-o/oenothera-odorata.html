Oenothera odorata

Oenothera odorata, commonly known as sweet fairy lantern, is a species of flowering plant in the evening primrose family.

It is native to the southeastern United States and is known for its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.

Oenothera odorata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
