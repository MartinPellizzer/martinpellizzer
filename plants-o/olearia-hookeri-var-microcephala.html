Olearia hookeri var. microcephala

Olearia hookeri var. microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed daisy bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly encountered in its natural habitat.
