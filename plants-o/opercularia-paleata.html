Opercularia paleata

Opercularia paleata, commonly known as the pale leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, pale green leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

Opercularia paleata is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its unique botanical characteristics.
