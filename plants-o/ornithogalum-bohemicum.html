Ornithogalum bohemicum

Ornithogalum bohemicum, commonly known as the Bohemian star-of-Bethlem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Southern Europe.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its bulbs are bulbous and store nutrients, allowing it to survive through winter dormancy.
