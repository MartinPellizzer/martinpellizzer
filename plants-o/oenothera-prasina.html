Oenothera prasina

Oenothera prasina, commonly known as the green evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Oenothera prasina is often found in sandy or disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, open habitats.
