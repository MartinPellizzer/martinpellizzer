Oncidium savegrensis

Oncidium savegrensis, commonly known as the Savegren's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Oncidium savegrensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
