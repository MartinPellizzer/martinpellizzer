Orthophytum minimum

Orthophytum minimum, commonly known as the dwarf feather cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to Brazil.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are covered in small, white spines. The plant produces delicate, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
