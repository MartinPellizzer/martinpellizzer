Osbeckia linearis var. moluccana

Osbeckia linearis var. moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan willow or slender osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Osbeckia linearis var. moluccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
