Opuntia alcerrecensis

Opuntia alcerrecensis, commonly known as the Sierra de las Nubes prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are typically green and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by edible fruits known as tunas. Opuntia alcerrecensis is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
