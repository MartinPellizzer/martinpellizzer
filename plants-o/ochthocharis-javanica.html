Ochthocharis javanica

Ochthocharis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Ochthocharis javanica thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
