Oncidium aurisasinorum

Oncidium aurisasinorum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, pendulous flowers that are typically yellow with greenish or brownish markings. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium aurisasinorum is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
