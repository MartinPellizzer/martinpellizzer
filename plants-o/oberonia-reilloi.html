Oberonia reilloi

Oberonia reilloi, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the forest floor.

Oberonia reilloi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
