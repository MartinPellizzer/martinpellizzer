Omonoia

Omonoia, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Omonoia is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardens in suitable climates.
