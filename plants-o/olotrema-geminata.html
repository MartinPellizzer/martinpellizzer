Olotrema geminata

Olotrema geminata, commonly known as the twinleaf orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It grows as an epiphytic plant, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments.

Olotrema geminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
