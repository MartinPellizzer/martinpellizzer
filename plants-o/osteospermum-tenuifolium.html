Osteospermum tenuifolium

Osteospermum tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disc florets. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with a central yellow disk, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Osteospermum tenuifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
