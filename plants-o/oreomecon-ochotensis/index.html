Oreomecon Ochotensis

Oreomecon ochotensis is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the cold climates of eastern Asia. It thrives in harsh environments such as tundra and alpine regions where few species can survive. This plant is known for its unique morphological features and medicinal potential. Despite its ecological importance, it remains poorly studied by scientists.
