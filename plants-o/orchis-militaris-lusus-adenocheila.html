Orchis militaris lusus adenocheila

Orchis militaris lusus adenocheila, commonly known as the Adenocheila Orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that typically display a combination of purple and white hues. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas with rich, humus-filled soil. Its reproductive strategy involves a symbiotic relationship with specific mycorrhizal fungi, which is crucial for its survival.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, this orchid is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
