Origanum album

Origanum album, commonly known as sweet marjoram, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, white or pink flowers and its slender, branched stems. The plant produces tiny, oval-shaped leaves that are fragrant and often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for herb gardens.

Origanum album is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional medicine.
