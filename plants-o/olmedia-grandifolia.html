Olmedia grandifolia

Olmedia grandifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf olmedia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Olmedia grandifolia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its suitability for xeriscaping and Mediterranean-style landscapes.
