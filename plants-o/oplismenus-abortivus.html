Oplismenus abortivus

Oplismenus abortivus, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in late summer, forming dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

Oplismenus abortivus is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and wet meadows.
