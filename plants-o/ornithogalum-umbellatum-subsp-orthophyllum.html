Ornithogalum umbellatum subsp. orthophyllum

Ornithogalum umbellatum subsp.

orthophyllum, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in the spring.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is often found in grasslands, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
