Ornithogalum orthophyllum subsp. kochii

Ornithogalum orthophyllum subsp.

kochii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features a bulbous stem with narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped white or pale blue flowers.

The plant is characterized by its showy inflorescence and is often found in grassy areas or rocky soils. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is adapted to dry, well-drained environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and floral arrangements.
