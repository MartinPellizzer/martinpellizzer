Odontoglossum connivens

Odontoglossum connivens, commonly known as the Christmas Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive greenish-white color and intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Odontoglossum connivens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a greenhouse setting.
