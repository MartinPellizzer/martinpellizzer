Olearia frostii

Olearia frostii, commonly known as the frost's daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has silver-gray foliage that reflects the harsh sunlight. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing white petals with a yellow center.

Olearia frostii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy and attractive appearance.
