Ononis columnae var. calycina

Ononis columnae var. calycina, commonly known as the Calycine Bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in loose clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks.
