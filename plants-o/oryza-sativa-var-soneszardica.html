Oryza sativa var. soneszardica

Oryza sativa var. soneszardica, commonly known as the Soneszardica rice, is a lesser-known variant of the rice species.

It is characterized by its unique grain morphology and growth habits, which differ from standard rice varieties. This variety is native to specific regions in Asia and is adapted to particular climatic conditions.
