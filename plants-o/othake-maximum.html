Othake maximum

Othake maximum, commonly known as the giant marsh grass, is a tall, perennial herb native to wetland areas in Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and tall, branched inflorescences that produce small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.

Its rapid growth makes it both a valuable forage plant and a potential invasive species in non-native environments.
