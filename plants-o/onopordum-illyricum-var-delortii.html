Onopordum illyricum var. delortii

Onopordum illyricum var. delortii, commonly known as the Delort's thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and wastelands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
