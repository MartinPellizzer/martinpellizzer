Ornithopus sativus var. roseus

Ornithopus sativus var. roseus, commonly known as rose-flowered salsify, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. Its root system consists of long, fleshy, white tuberous roots that are edible.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its historical use in cuisine.
