Osteocarpum acropterum var. acropterum

Osteocarpum acropterum var. acropterum, commonly known as the slender rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, linear to ovate leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in racemes, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often found in mountainous or coastal regions.
