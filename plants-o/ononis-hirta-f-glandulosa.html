Ononis hirta f. glandulosa

Ononis hirta f. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular hedgehogbean, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered with glandular hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
