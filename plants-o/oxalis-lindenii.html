Oxalis lindenii

Oxalis lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release its seeds. Oxalis lindenii thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
