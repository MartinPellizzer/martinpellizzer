Oncidium sathishkumarii

Oncidium sathishkumarii, commonly known as the Sathishkumari orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that display a vibrant array of colors, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are borne on racemes, often with a unique, intricate pattern that enhances its ornamental value.

This species is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
