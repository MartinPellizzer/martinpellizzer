Onosma erecta subsp. erecta

Onosma erecta subsp.

erecta, commonly known as the upright onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
