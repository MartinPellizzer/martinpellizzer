Othonna tenuissima

Othonna tenuissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, woody stem with dense, fleshy leaves that are narrow and linear, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
