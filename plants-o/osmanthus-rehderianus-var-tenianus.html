Osmanthus rehderianus var. tenianus

Osmanthus rehderianus var. tenianus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Osmanthus rehderianus var. tenianus is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic flowers.
