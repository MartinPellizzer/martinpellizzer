Oncoba brachyanthera

Oncoba brachyanthera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Oncoba brachyanthera is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems that help it access underground water.
