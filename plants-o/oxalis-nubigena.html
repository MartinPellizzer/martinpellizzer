Oxalis nubigena

Oxalis nubigena, commonly known as the cloud alpine oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or alpine environments. It has a short growing season and is adapted to cool, moist conditions.

Oxalis nubigena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific climate requirements.
