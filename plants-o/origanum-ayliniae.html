Origanum ayliniae

Origanum ayliniae, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic and often medicinal plants. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, containing essential oils that contribute to its strong scent.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean gardens.
