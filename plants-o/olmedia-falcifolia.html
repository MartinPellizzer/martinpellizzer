Olmedia falcifolia

Olmedia falcifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved olmedia, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Olmedia falcifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
