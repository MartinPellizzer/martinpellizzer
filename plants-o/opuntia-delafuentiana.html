Opuntia delafuentiana

Opuntia delafuentiana, commonly known as the Dela Fuente prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers, which are followed by edible, red-fleshed fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick stems and having a shallow root system.

Opuntia delafuentiana is also notable for its role in traditional medicine and as a source of food in certain regions.
