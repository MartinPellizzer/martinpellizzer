Oberonia toppingii

Oberonia toppingii, commonly known as the Topping's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizome and pseudobulbs. The plant produces a single, erect flower spike bearing several small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
