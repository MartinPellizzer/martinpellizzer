Ornithocephalus iridifolius

Ornithocephalus iridifolius, commonly known as the iridescence leaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, iridescent blue-green foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. The plant produces delicate, orchid-like flowers in shades of purple and white, which bloom during the cooler months. Its unique leaf structure and coloration are adaptations to its harsh, high-altitude environment.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is protected in its natural habitat.
