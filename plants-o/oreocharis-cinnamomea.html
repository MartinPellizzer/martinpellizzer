Oreocharis cinnamomea

Oreocharis cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its distinctive cinnamon-scented flowers, which are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It grows as an epiphytic or lithophytic plant, often found on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.

Oreocharis cinnamomea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fragrance and visual characteristics.
