Ornithogalum neurostegium subsp. neurostegium

Ornithogalum neurostegium subsp.

neurostegium, commonly known as the silverleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white flowers with a pale green center.

The plant is characterized by its subterranean bulbs, which store nutrients and allow it to survive in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens despite its potential to become invasive in certain regions.
