Oreodaphne glaberrima var. angustifolia

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
