Ornithogalum crinifolium

Ornithogalum crinifolium, commonly known as the laceleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, waxy, white or pale green leaves that give it a distinctive lace-like appearance. The plant produces tall, leafless stems bearing white, star-shaped flowers with a pleasant, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in gardens for its elegant appearance and seasonal blooms.
