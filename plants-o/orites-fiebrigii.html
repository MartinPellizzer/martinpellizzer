Orites fiebrigii

Orites fiebrigii, commonly known as the Cape fynbos orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
