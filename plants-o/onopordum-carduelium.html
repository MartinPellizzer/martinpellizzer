Onopordum carduelium

Onopordum carduelium, commonly known as the lesser thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Onopordum carduelium is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.
