Orthophytum santaritense

Orthophytum santaritense, commonly known as the Santa Rita grass.

It is a species of grass native to Brazil, specifically found in the state of Santa Catarina. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions.

Orthophytum santaritense is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
