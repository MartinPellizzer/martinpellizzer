Operculina brownii

Operculina brownii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from the leaf axils. Operculina brownii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
