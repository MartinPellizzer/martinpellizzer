Ottonia diversifolia f. grandifolia

Ottonia diversifolia f. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered ottonia, is a botanical variant of the ottonia genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and violet. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a robust root system adapted to moist, shaded environments. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
