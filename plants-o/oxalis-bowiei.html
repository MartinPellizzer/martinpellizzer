Oxalis bowiei

Oxalis bowiei, commonly known as the bowie oxalis, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves with a deep green color and vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, spherical fruit that splits open to release its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in many gardens.
