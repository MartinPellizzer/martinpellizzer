Olearia virgata var. implicita

Olearia virgata var. implicita, commonly known as the New Zealand frost flower, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is native to New Zealand and thrives in cool, temperate climates. It is particularly adapted to coastal environments, where it can tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
