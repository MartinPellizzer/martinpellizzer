Ormosia glazioviana

Ormosia glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziov's legume, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with Rhizobium bacteria in its root nodules.

Ormosia glazioviana is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in traditional crafts and furniture.
