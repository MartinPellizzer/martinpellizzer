Oncidium dentatum

Oncidium dentatum, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or green with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters. Oncidium dentatum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
