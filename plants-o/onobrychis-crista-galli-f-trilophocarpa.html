Onobrychis crista-galli f. trilophocarpa

Onobrychis crista-galli f. trilophocarpa, commonly known as the three-ridged goat's rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The fruit is a legume pod with three distinct ridges, giving it its specific epithet. This plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in dry, rocky soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
