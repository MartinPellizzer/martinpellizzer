Onosma mollis

Onosma mollis, commonly known as the soft onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Boraginaceae family.
