Onobrychis jahandiezii

Onobrychis jahandiezii, commonly known as the Persian clover, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for dry climates.
