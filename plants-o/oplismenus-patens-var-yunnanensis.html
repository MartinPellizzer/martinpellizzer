Oplismenus patens var. yunnanensis

Oplismenus patens var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, wiry leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
