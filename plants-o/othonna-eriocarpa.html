Othonna eriocarpa

Othonna eriocarpa, commonly known as the woolly othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna eriocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and resilience.
