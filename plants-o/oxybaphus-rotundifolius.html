Oxybaphus rotundifolius

Oxybaphus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved oxybaphus, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and rounded, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
