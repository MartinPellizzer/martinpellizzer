Oryctanthus asplundii

Oryctanthus asplundii, commonly known as the asplundii oxalis, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Oxalidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features slender, branching stems with heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are often hairy. Native to the Cape Province, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Oryctanthus asplundii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its bold, eye-catching blooms.
