Onobrychis tenoreana

Onobrychis tenoreana, commonly known as the Sicilian vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial legume that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Onobrychis tenoreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
