Oldenlandia mollis

Oldenlandia mollis, commonly known as silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Oldenlandia mollis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and brief flowering period.
