Oenanthe globulosa subsp. globulosa

Oenanthe globulosa subsp.

globulosa, commonly known as the globular water-dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils along stream banks and wet meadows.

The plant features a short, stout stem with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in early summer.

The species is characterized by its distinctive, globe-like inflorescence, which gives it its common name.
