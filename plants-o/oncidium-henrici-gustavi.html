Oncidium henrici-gustavi

Oncidium henrici-gustavi, commonly known as the Gustav Henrici orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters of yellow and green hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the air and soil.

Oncidium henrici-gustavi prefers bright, indirect light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
