Osmanthus aquifolium var. rotundifolius

Osmanthus aquifolium var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage with thick, dark green leaves that are rounded in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the fall, which emit a sweet, honey-like scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and aromatic blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
