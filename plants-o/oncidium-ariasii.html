Oncidium ariasii

Oncidium ariasii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers, which bloom in racemes and are often fragrant. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a dense layer of white pubescence.

Oncidium ariasii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
