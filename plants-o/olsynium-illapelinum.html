Olsynium illapelinum

Olsynium illapelinum, commonly known as the Illapelinum cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features a short, thick stem covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in summer, and are relatively showy.

Olsynium illapelinum is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal rainfall.
