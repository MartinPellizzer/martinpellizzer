Opuntia corotilla var. aurantiaciflora

Opuntia corotilla var. aurantiaciflora, commonly known as the Orange-flowered Prickly Pear, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flattened, fleshy stems that are covered in spines and are often green in color. The flowers of this variety are striking, with vibrant orange petals that bloom in the summer months. It produces edible fruits known as tunas, which are typically orange or red in color.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
