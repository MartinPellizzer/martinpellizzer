Oxalis lomana

Oxalis lomana, commonly known as the Lomana wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive veins, and its flowers are typically yellow with five petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Oxalis lomana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.

It is also noted for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
