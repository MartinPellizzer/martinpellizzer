Oncidium ballii

Oncidium ballii, commonly known as the ballii orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants rather than in soil.

Oncidium ballii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
