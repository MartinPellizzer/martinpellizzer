Orbea valida subsp. occidentalis

Orbea valida subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the Western orchid orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, adapting it to arid environments.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a strong fragrance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
