Origanum gracile

Origanum gracile, commonly known as slender oregano, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its compact size and fragrant foliage make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and culinary gardens.

It is also known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional remedies.
