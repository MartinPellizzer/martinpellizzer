Ourisia uniflora

Ourisia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower ourisia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch of color to the landscape.
