Oncidium heteranthum

Oncidium heteranthum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with elongated, strap-like leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, yellow-orange flowers that have a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium heteranthum is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
