Onosma tricerosperma subsp. catalaunica

Onosma tricerosperma subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan thimbleweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in Mediterranean climates.

The plant features slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
