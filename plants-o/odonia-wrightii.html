Odonia wrightii

Odonia wrightii, commonly known as Wright's odonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive blue-green hue. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale purple.

Odonia wrightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
