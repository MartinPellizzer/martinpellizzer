Onopordum mesatlanticum

Onopordum mesatlanticum, commonly known as the Atlantic thistle, is a perennial plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to salt-laden environments. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for small insects.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in coastal flora studies.
