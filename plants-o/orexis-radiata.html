Orexis radiata

Orexis radiata, commonly known as the radiant orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in vibrant shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has a slender, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

Orexis radiata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
