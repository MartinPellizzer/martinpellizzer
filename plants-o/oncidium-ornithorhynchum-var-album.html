Oncidium ornithorhynchum var. album

Oncidium ornithorhynchum var. album, commonly known as the White Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking white flowers with a subtle greenish tint, often arranged in clusters along the stem. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
