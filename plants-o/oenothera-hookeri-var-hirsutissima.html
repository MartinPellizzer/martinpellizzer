Oenothera hookeri var. hirsutissima

Oenothera hookeri var. hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it conserve moisture in arid environments.
