Orthanthe

Orthanthe, commonly known as the marsh gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is native to wetlands and marshy areas in Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Orthanthe is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in moist environments.
