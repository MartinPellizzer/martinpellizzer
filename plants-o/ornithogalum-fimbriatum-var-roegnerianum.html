Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. roegnerianum

Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. roegnerianum, commonly known as the roegnerianum star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The plant is characterized by its bulbous root system, which stores nutrients and water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
