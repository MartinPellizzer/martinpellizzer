Orchis lacera

Orchis lacera, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, erect stem that can reach up to 40 centimeters in height. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive hooded structure.

Orchis lacera produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
