Oreochrysum

Oreochrysum, commonly known as the golden cup, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Oreochrysum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
