Oncidium Barbatum Var. Ciliatum

Cinnamon spike orchid, scientifically known as Oncidium barbatum var. ciliatum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is prized for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that resemble cinnamon sticks, hence its common name. This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers. Whether displayed in a greenhouse or as a houseplant, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to any space.
