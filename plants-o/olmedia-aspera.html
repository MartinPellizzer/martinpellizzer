Olmedia aspera

Olmedia aspera, commonly known as the rough olmedia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Olmedia aspera is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in landscaping.
