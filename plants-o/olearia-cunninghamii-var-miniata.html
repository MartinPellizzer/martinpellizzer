Olearia cunninghamii var. miniata

Olearia cunninghamii var. miniata, commonly known as the silver mink, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost velvety appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like white flowers with yellow centers, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in temperate climates.
