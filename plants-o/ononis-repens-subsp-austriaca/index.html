Ononis Repens Subsp. Austriaca

Ononis repens subsp. austriaca is a hardy, low-growing plant native to central Europe. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in alpine and subalpine regions. This subspecies is known for its small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil, making it useful in erosion control. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
