Oxalis glabrata

Oxalis glabrata, commonly known as the smooth wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces heart-shaped leaves and small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its flowers bloom in spring, and it is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions.

Oxalis glabrata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in garden settings.
