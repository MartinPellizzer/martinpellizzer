Oberonia yunnanensis

Oberonia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan's orchid, is a species of orchid native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is a small epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in tropical and subtropical forest environments. The plant features a pseudobulb and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.

Oberonia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
