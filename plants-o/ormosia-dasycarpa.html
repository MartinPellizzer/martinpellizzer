Ormosia dasycarpa

Ormosia dasycarpa, commonly known as the dense-fruited ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Ormosia dasycarpa is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also known for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
