Onopordum × spinosissimum

Onopordum × spinosissimum, commonly known as the Spanish thistle or nodding thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sunny environments. It has a woody stem that becomes more pronounced with age, and its leaves are often covered in fine hairs.

Onopordum × spinosissimum is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
