Onosma hispida var. kashmirica

Onosma hispida var. kashmirica, commonly known as Kashmir thyme, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
