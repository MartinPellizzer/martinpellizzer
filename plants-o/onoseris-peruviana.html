Onoseris peruviana

Onoseris peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru and Bolivia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces small, achene-like seeds.
