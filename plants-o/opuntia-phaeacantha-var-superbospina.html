Opuntia phaeacantha var. superbospina

Opuntia phaeacantha var. superbospina, commonly known as the superb spine opuntia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in spines and glochids, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stems for survival in arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry climates.
