Opuntia inamoena var. flaviflora

Opuntia inamoena var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features flat, fleshy stems covered in spines and woolly hair, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunas, is edible and often used in local cuisines.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and drought tolerance.
