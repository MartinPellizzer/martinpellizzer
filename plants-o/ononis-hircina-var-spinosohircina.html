Ononis hircina var. spinosohircina

Ononis hircina var. spinosohircina, commonly known as the spiny hircine cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant features slender, hairy stems and distinctive, five-lobed leaves with a rough texture. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in rocky or gravelly soils.

This variety is characterized by its spiny bracts and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
