Oncidium lucayanum var. aureum

Oncidium lucayanum var. aureum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive golden hue, which are often adorned with dark markings. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences, and its leaves are strap-like and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
