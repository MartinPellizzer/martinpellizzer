Olotrema brunnea

Olotrema brunnea, commonly known as the brown leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, brownish pubescence. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color and are clustered in loose inflorescences.

Olotrema brunnea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
