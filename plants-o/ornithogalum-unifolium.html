Ornithogalum unifolium

Ornithogalum unifolium, commonly known as the single-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, tuberous stem and produces a single, broad, lance-shaped leaf. The plant is notable for its umbel of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum unifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
