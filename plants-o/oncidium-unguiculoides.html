Oncidium unguiculoides

Oncidium unguiculoides, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, spider-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium unguiculoides is popular among orchid growers for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
