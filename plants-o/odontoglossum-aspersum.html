Odontoglossum aspersum

Odontoglossum aspersum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces striking violet or purple flowers with a velvety texture. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Odontoglossum aspersum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure.
