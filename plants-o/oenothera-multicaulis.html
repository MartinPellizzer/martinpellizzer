Oenothera multicaulis

Oenothera multicaulis, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Oenothera multicaulis is often found in open areas such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
