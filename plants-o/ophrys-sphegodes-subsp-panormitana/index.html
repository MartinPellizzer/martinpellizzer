Ophrys Sphegodes Subsp. Panormitana

Ophrys sphegodes subsp. panormitana is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the Mediterranean region. This orchid is known for its unique mimicry, resembling a female wasp to attract male wasps for pollination. It thrives in rocky and limestone soils, often found in sunny, open habitats. Its delicate flowers display a striking blend of colors and patterns that aid in its deceptive pollination strategy. Despite its beauty, this subspecies faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
