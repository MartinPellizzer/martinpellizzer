Oxalis triangularis subsp. papilionacea

Oxalis triangularis subsp.

papilionacea, commonly known as the rainbow oxalis, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America. It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that resemble the petals of a flower, giving it a unique appearance.

The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or containers.

Its foliage and flowers add vibrant visual interest throughout the growing season.
