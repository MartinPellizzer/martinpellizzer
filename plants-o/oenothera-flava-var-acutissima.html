Oenothera flava var. acutissima

Oenothera flava var. acutissima, commonly known as the acuteleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

The plant is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
