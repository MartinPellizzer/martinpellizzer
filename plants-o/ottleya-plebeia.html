Ottleya plebeia

Ottleya plebeia, commonly known as the common ottleya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in open, sunny environments.

Ottleya plebeia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
