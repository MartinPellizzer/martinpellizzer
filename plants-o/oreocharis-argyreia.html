Oreocharis argyreia

Oreocharis argyreia, commonly known as the silver flower or silver bell, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that are predominantly white with a silvery-blue hue. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
