Origanum isthmicum

Origanum isthmicum, commonly known as Isthmic oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves, contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Origanum isthmicum is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
