Oncidium guttatum var. auriculatum

Oncidium guttatum var. auriculatum, commonly known as the Auriculate Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its flowers have a distinctive auriculate (ear-shaped) lip, which is a key identifying feature.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
