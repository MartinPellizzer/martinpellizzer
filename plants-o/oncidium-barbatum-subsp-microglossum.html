Oncidium barbatum subsp. microglossum

Oncidium barbatum subsp.

microglossum, commonly known as the miniature orchid or small-lipped orchid, is a species native to Central and South America. It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow to orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
