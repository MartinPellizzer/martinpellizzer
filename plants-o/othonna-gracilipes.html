Othonna gracilipes

Othonna gracilipes, commonly known as the slender leaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Othonna gracilipes is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
