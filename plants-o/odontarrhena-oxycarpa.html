Odontarrhena oxycarpa

Odontarrhena oxycarpa, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in dense clusters, which are borne on slender panicles. It is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Odontarrhena oxycarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
