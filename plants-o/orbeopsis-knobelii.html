Orbeopsis knobelii

Orbeopsis knobelii, commonly known as the knobelii orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
