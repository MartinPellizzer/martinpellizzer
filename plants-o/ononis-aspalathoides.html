Ononis aspalathoides

Ononis aspalathoides, commonly known as the dwarf crown vetch, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. The plant is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit, which helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Ononis aspalathoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in dry landscapes.
