Oligosporus macilentus

Oligosporus macilentus, commonly known as the slender spore grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that are typically green or pale in color. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical structure makes it an important species for ecological studies and habitat conservation.
