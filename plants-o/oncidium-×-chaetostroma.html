Oncidium × chaetostroma

Oncidium × chaetostroma, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a wide range of floral colors, including yellow, orange, and red.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Oncidium × chaetostroma is relatively easy to care for, requiring bright indirect light and well-draining potting mix.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in both indoor and outdoor gardening.
