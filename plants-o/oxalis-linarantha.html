Oxalis linarantha

Oxalis linarantha, commonly known as the pink oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces bead-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis linarantha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its delicate foliage and flowers.
