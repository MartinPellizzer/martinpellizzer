Oenothera siambonensis

Oenothera siambonensis, commonly known as the Siamese evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. Oenothera siambonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
