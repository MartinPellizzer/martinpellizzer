Osbeckia togoensis

Osbeckia togoensis, commonly known as Togo's goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a rough texture. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Osbeckia togoensis is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it plays a minor role in local ecosystems.
