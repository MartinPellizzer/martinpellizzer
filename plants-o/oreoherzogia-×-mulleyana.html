Oreoherzogia × mulleyana

Oreoherzogia × mulleyana, commonly known as the hybrid desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

The plant is a hybrid between Oreoherzogia pumila and Oreoherzogia macrantha, resulting in unique botanical features.
