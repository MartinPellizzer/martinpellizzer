Ononis natrix var. murcica

Ononis natrix var. murcica, commonly known as the Murcian sand thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in dry, open habitats. The plant features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
