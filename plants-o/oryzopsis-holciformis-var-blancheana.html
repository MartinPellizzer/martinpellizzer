Oryzopsis holciformis var. blancheana

Oryzopsis holciformis var. blancheana, commonly known as Blancheana grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically arranged in dense, compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
