Ogastemma Pusillum

Ogastemma pusillum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its small, pale yellow blooms that appear in late summer. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires specific environmental conditions to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Ogastemma pusillum is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and study this unique botanical species.
