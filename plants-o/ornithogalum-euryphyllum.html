Ornithogalum euryphyllum

Ornithogalum euryphyllum, commonly known as the broad-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum euryphyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
