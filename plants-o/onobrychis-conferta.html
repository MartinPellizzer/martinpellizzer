Onobrychis conferta

Onobrychis conferta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed burclover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces cylindrical, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Onobrychis conferta is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and forage production.
