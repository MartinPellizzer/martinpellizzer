Ornithidium serrulatum var. acuminatum

Ornithidium serrulatum var. acuminatum, commonly known as the acuminated feather fern, is a variety of the feather fern genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are finely divided and often have a serrated edge. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually light green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
