Ophiorrhiza Microcephala

Ophiorrhiza microcephala is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive small, rounded leaves and delicate white flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Despite its beauty, it remains understudied and is considered a species of conservation concern. Its ecological role and potential medicinal value make it an important subject for further research.
