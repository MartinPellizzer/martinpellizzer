Orobus dissitifolius

Orobus dissitifolius, commonly known as the scattered-leaved orobus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and appear in clusters during the spring.

Orobus dissitifolius is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
