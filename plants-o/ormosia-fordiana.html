Ormosia fordiana

Ormosia fordiana, commonly known as the Ford's ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
