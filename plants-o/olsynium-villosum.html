Olsynium villosum

Olsynium villosum, commonly known as the silky olsynium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Olsynium villosum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
