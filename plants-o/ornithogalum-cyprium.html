Ornithogalum cyprium

Ornithogalum cyprium, commonly known as Cyprus star-of-Bethlem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Ornithogalum cyprium is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
