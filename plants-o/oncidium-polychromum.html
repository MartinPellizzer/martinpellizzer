Oncidium polychromum

Oncidium polychromum, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that display a wide range of hues, including red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of showy, fragrant blooms that can last for several weeks.

Oncidium polychromum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
