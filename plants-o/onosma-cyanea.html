Onosma cyanea

Onosma cyanea, commonly known as the blue bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Onosma cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and delicate appearance.
