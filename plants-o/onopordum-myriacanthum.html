Onopordum myriacanthum

Onopordum myriacanthum, commonly known as the many-toothed thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its scientific name reflects the numerous teeth-like structures on its leaves.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
