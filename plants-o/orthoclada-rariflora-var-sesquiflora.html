Orthoclada rariflora var. sesquiflora

Orthoclada rariflora var. sesquiflora, commonly known as the rare-flowered orthoclada, is a unique species within the Orthocladaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral arrangement of leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces tiny, elongated fruits.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
