Oreiostachys producta

Oreiostachys producta, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and delicate, feathery panicles that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and airy flower heads.

Oreiostachys producta is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
