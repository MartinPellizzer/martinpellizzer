Osmanthus aquifolium var. purpureus

Osmanthus aquifolium var. purpureus, commonly known as the purple-leaf osmanthus, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its small, fragrant yellow flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture, while the plant produces small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its aromatic properties make it valuable in traditional Chinese medicine and perfumery.
