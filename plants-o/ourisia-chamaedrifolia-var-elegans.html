Ourisia chamaedrifolia var. elegans

Ourisia chamaedrifolia var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant ourisia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are densely arranged along its stems, giving it a compact, mat-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a subtle ornamental value to its habitat. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
