Oncidium warneri var. purpuratum

Oncidium warneri var. purpuratum, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that range in color from deep purples to rich reds. The plant typically features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are often arranged in racemes and are prized for their ornamental value in horticulture.

Oncidium warneri var. purpuratum requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
