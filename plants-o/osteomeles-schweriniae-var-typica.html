Osteomeles schweriniae var. typica

Osteomeles schweriniae var. typica, commonly known as the Schwerin's flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
