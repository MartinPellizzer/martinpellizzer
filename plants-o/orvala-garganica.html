Orvala garganica

Orvala garganica, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down.
