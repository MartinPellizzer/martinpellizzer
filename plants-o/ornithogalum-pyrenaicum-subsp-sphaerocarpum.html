Ornithogalum pyrenaicum subsp. sphaerocarpum

Ornithogalum pyrenaicum subsp.

sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the Pyrenean star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring.

The plant is characterized by its spherical, fleshy bulbs, which store nutrients and water. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This subspecies is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats.
