Oreobroma megarhizum

Oreobroma megarhizum, commonly known as the giant root oreobroma, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy roots that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.

Oreobroma megarhizum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
