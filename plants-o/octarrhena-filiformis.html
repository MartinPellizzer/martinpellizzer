Octarrhena filiformis

Octarrhena filiformis, commonly known as the slender sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
