Odontoglossum × pauwelsii

Odontoglossum × pauwelsii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum aequatoriale and Odontoglossum pusillum.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically white to pale pink, with a distinctive hooded lip and spurred structure.

It requires cool, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in greenhouse environments.
