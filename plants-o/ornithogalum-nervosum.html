Ornithogalum nervosum

Ornithogalum nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-veined star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
