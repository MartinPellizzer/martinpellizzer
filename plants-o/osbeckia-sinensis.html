Osbeckia sinensis

Osbeckia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Osbeckia sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
