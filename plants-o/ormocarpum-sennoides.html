Ormocarpum sennoides

Ormocarpum sennoides, commonly known as the Indian senna, is a flowering plant native to southern and eastern Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by distinctive seed pods that are elongated and ribbed. Its leaves are compound, with small leaflets arranged oppositely along the stems.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
