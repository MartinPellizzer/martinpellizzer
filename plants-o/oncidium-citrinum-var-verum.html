Oncidium citrinum var. verum

Oncidium citrinum var. verum, commonly known as the Lemon Spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, drooping inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that resemble spikes, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements.
