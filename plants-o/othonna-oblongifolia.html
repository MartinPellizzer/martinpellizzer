Othonna oblongifolia

Othonna oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved othonna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Othonna oblongifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
