Ostrowskia magnifica

Ostrowskia magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent ostrowski, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and vibrant purple petals that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, waxy leaves that are slightly serrated. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires partial shade to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
