Olearia argophylla

Olearia argophylla, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, felt-like hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a leathery texture.

Olearia argophylla is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
