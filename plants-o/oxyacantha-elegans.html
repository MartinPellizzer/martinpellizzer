Oxyacantha elegans

Oxyacantha elegans, commonly known as the elegant rosebay, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Oxyacantha elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
