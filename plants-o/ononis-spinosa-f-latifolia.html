Ononis spinosa f. latifolia

Ononis spinosa f. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. This variety is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical form of Ononis spinosa.
