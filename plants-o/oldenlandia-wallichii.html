Oldenlandia wallichii

Oldenlandia wallichii, commonly known as the silverleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oldenlandia wallichii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blooms.
