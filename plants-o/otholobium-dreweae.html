Otholobium dreweae

Otholobium dreweae, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal burr bush, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, thorny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces spherical pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Otholobium dreweae is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soils.
