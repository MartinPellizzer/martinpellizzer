Ortgiesia gracilis

Ortgiesia gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated flower spikes that bear small, fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall slender appearance.

Ortgiesia gracilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
