Ozanonia carniolica

Ozanonia carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Ozanonia carniolica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive flowers and hardy nature.
