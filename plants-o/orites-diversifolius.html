Orites diversifolius

Orites diversifolius, commonly known as the variable-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are diverse in shape and size, contributing to its common name.

Orites diversifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
