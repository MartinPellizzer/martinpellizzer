Orania macropetala

Orania macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, large, and showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Orania macropetala thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
