Orchidantha maxillarioides

Orchidantha maxillarioides, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle greenish tinge. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, fleshy roots and narrow, strap-like leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
