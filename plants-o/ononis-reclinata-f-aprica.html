Ononis reclinata f. aprica

Ononis reclinata f. aprica, commonly known as the alpine bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in alpine and arctic environments. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

This subspecies is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.
