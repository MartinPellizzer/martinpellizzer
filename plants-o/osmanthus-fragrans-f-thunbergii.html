Osmanthus fragrans f. thunbergii

Osmanthus fragrans f. thunbergii, commonly known as the fragrant osmanthus or sweet osmanthus, is a popular ornamental and aromatic shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that emit a sweet, spicy fragrance, particularly in the autumn. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its fragrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and the use of its flowers in teas and culinary applications.
