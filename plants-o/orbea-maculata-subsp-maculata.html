Orbea maculata subsp. maculata

Orbea maculata subsp.

maculata, commonly known as the spotted orbea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

The leaves are typically green with distinctive red or purple spots, which help in identifying the species. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
