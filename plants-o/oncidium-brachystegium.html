Oncidium brachystegium

Oncidium brachystegium, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive orange lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Oncidium brachystegium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
