Oligoneuron × maheuxii

Oligoneuron × maheuxii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Oligoneuron and Maheuxii species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structures and vibrant coloration. The plant typically features elongated, slender stems with clustered, showy flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic appeal.
