Ormenis mixta

Ormenis mixta, commonly known as the mixed ormenis, is a shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features small, aromatic leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Ormenis mixta is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
