Ouratea gillyana subsp. pachypoda

Ouratea gillyana subsp.

pachypoda, commonly known as the Brazilian holly or false holly, is a member of the Ochnaceae family. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles.

The plant is native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
