Oxalis suborbiculata

Oxalis suborbiculata, commonly known as the pink oxalis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Oxalidaceae family, characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and delicate blooms.
