Ophryosporus floribundus

Ophryosporus floribundus, commonly known as the flowering glasswort, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other brackish habitats.

Ophryosporus floribundus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
