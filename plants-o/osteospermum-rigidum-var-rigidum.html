Osteospermum rigidum var. rigidum

Osteospermum rigidum var. rigidum, commonly known as the hardstem osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, rigid stems and daisy-like flowers with blue or violet petals. The plant produces yellow-centered flower heads that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features include serrated leaves and a compact growth habit.
