Oncidium engelii

Oncidium engelii, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, known for its showy, long-lasting blooms. The plant produces inflorescences of fragrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. Oncidium engelii is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
