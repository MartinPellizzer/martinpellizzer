Ochna rufescens

Ochna rufescens, commonly known as the red-bark ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-barked trunk and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Ochna rufescens thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruiting display.
