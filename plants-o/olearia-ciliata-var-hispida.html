Olearia ciliata var. hispida

Olearia ciliata var. hispida, commonly known as the hispid daisy bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage covered in fine, bristly hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Olearia ciliata var. hispida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
