Oenothera tenella var. tenuifolia

Oenothera tenella var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, oval seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
