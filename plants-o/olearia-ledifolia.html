Olearia ledifolia

Olearia ledifolia, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Olearia ledifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
