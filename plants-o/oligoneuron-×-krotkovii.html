Oligoneuron × krotkovii

Oligoneuron × krotkovii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant with unique botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is a natural hybrid between Oligoneuron tenuifolium and Oligoneuron krotkovii. The plant is notable for its intricate floral structure, featuring showy, often fragrant blooms that attract pollinators. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and botanists alike.
