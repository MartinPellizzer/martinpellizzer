Orchis angustifolia f. densiflora

Orchis angustifolia f. densiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid with dense flower spikes, is a botanical variant of the narrow-leaved orchid.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. Its inflorescence is characterized by a dense arrangement of flowers along a single stem.

This subspecies is noted for its ecological significance in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
