Ornithogalum comptonii

Ornithogalum comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale green flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in spring and is adapted to dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens.
