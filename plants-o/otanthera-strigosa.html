Otanthera strigosa

Otanthera strigosa, commonly known as stiffleaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and swamps. Its leaves are linear and covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Otanthera strigosa produces showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall.
