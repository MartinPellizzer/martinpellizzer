Oxalis laxa var. rigida

Oxalis laxa var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its stems are somewhat woody and can become branched with age.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
