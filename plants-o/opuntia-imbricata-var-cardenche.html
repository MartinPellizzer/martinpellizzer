Opuntia imbricata var. cardenche

Opuntia imbricata var. cardenche, commonly known as the Cardenche prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features flattened, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunas, is edible and has a sweet, juicy flavor.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
