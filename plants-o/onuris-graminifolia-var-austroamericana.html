Onuris graminifolia var. austroamericana

Onuris graminifolia var. austroamericana, commonly known as the southern grassleaf fern, is a variant of the grassleaf fern native to the Americas.

It is a perennial herb with slender, creeping rhizomes that produce numerous small, lance-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically arranged in a rosette and have a grass-like appearance, hence the species name *graminifolia*. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings for its delicate foliage.
