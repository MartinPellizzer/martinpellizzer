Olearia arguta var. lanata

Olearia arguta var. lanata, commonly known as the woolly daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Olearia arguta var. lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
