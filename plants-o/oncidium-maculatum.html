Oncidium maculatum

Oncidium maculatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers with distinctive markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually fragrant and can bloom multiple times throughout the year.

Oncidium maculatum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
