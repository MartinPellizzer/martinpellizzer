Ononis broteroana var. grandiflora

Ononis broteroana var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered broteroan, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of showy, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
