Odostima obovata

Odostima obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed odostima, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, with yellow petals and a distinct scent.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
