Orbea variegata

Orbea variegata, commonly known as the variegated orchid vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom along the stems. It is a climbing plant that thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Orbea variegata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
