Odontoglossum cristatellum f. dayanum

Odontoglossum cristatellum f. dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique, spurred shape. The plant typically features dark green, lanceolate leaves and blooms in shades of violet and white, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
