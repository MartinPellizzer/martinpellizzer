Ormosia micrantha

Ormosia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Ormosia micrantha produces woody pods that contain several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.

The tree plays an important role in local ecosystems and is valued for its timber and ecological benefits.
