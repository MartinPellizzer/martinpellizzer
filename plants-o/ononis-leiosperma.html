Ononis leiosperma

Ononis leiosperma, commonly known as silky bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has thin, wiry stems and is characterized by its soft, silky leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Silky bedstraw is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its mild sedative properties.
