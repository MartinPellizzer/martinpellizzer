Oryctanthus ligustrinus

Oryctanthus ligustrinus, commonly known as the Cape yellow bell, is a striking South African plant characterized by its vibrant yellow, bell-shaped flowers.

Oryctanthus ligustrinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
