Oldenlandia assimilis

Oldenlandia assimilis, commonly known as the "Spider Plant" or "Air Plant," is a member of the Commelinaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, white or pale green flowers. The plant produces tiny, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its dispersal. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, hair-like structures.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
