Oscaria chinensis

Oscaria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese nettle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments. Oscaria chinensis is known for its stinging hairs, which can cause skin irritation upon contact.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
