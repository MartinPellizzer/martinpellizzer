Ocimum jamesii

Ocimum jamesii, commonly known as blue basil, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark blue to purple leaves and small, white to light purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its essential oils contain compounds like linalool and eugenol, which contribute to its strong fragrance.

Ocimum jamesii is valued for both ornamental purposes and traditional medicinal uses.
