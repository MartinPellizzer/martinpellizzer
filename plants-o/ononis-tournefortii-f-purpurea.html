Ononis tournefortii f. purpurea

Ononis tournefortii f. purpurea, commonly known as purple sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with small, fleshy leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant is native to Mediterranean regions and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal areas and dry, sunny environments.

Its ornamental value is due to its vibrant flower color and drought tolerance.
