Otomeria rupestris

Otomeria rupestris, commonly known as the rock otomeris, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Otomeria rupestris is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
