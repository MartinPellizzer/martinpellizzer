Oncidium ouricanense

Oncidium ouricanense, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. Oncidium ouricanense prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
