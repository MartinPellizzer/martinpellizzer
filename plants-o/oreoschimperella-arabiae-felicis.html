Oreoschimperella arabiae-felicis

Oreoschimperella arabiae-felicis, commonly known as the Arabian joy plant, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of the Middle East.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with fleshy, waxy leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-water conditions.
