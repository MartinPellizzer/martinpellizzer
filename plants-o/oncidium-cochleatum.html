Oncidium cochleatum

Oncidium cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often in clusters. Oncidium cochleatum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
