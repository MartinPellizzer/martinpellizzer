Oncidium retusum

Oncidium retusum, commonly known as the Spine Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and raceme of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with red or purple markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in dry conditions. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium retusum is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
