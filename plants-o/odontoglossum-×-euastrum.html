Odontoglossum × euastrum

Odontoglossum × euastrum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Odontoglossum orchids and other related species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structures and vibrant coloration. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and display a variety of hues, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
