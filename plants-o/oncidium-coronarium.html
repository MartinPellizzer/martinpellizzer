Oncidium coronarium

Oncidium coronarium, commonly known as the Crowned Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically yellow with reddish-brown markings, and they bloom in late winter to early spring.

Oncidium coronarium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
