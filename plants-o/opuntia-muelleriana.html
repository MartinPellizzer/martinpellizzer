Opuntia muelleriana

Opuntia muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its flattened, fleshy stems that are covered in spines and are often ribbed. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunah, is edible and contains a sweet, juicy pulp.

Opuntia muelleriana is adapted to arid environments and is drought-resistant.
