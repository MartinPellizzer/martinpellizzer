Origanum anglicum

Origanum anglicum, commonly known as English thyme, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and flowers that range from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its use in herbal medicine and as a culinary spice.

Origanum anglicum is also valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
