Ourisia muscosa

Ourisia muscosa, commonly known as the woolly ourisia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
