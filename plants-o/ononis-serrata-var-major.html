Ononis serrata var. major

Ononis serrata var. major, commonly known as the greater sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
