Odontoglossum angustatum var. heterosepalum

Odontoglossum angustatum var. heterosepalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its sepals and petals are often marked with contrasting hues, enhancing its ornamental value.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
