Oncidium longipes var. monophyllum

Oncidium longipes var. monophyllum, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features elongated, pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
