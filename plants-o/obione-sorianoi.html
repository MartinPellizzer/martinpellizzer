Obione sorianoi

Obione sorianoi, commonly known as the Sorianoi orchid, is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its flowering period typically occurs during the late spring and early summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
