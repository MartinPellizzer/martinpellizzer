Oxytropis torrentium

Oxytropis torrentium, commonly known as the torrent oxytropis, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
