Oxygonum dregeanum var. strictum

Oxygonum dregeanum var. strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf waterwheel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, pink to purple flowers in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are aromatic and have been used traditionally in some regions for medicinal purposes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
