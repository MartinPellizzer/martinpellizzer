Oryza abromeitiana

Oryza abromeitiana, commonly known as the dwarf ricegrass, is a species of grass native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a member of the Poaceae family and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with spikelets that are often covered in a dense layer of hairs.
