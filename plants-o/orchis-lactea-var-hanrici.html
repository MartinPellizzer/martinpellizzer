Orchis lactea var. hanrici

Orchis lactea var. hanrici, commonly known as the Hanrici's milk orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of Europe.

The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.
