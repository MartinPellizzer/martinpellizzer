Oxalis piottae

Oxalis piottae, commonly known as the golden oxalis, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that often have a striking green and white coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental appeal is enhanced by its ability to form dense, compact growth habits.
