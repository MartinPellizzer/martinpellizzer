Onobrychis balansae var. multiflora

Onobrychis balansae var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora burr vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
