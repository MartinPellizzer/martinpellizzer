Oreocharis primuloides var. albiflora

Oreocharis primuloides var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has a compact, herbaceous growth habit. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
