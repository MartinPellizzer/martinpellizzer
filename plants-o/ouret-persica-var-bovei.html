Ouret persica var. bovei

Ouret persica var. bovei, commonly known as the Bovei peach, is a variant of the peach tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, sweet fruits with a rich, aromatic flavor. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth and fruit production. Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and a flowering period in late spring.

The Bovei peach is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in both home gardens and commercial cultivation.
