Oliganthes corei

Oliganthes corei, commonly known as the Korean goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in forested areas and is known for its yellow-orange inflorescences. Oliganthes corei has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
