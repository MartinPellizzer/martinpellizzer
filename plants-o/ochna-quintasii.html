Ochna quintasii

Ochna quintasii, commonly known as the Quintas's ochna, is a small, deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by distinctive orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
