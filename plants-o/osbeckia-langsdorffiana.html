Osbeckia langsdorffiana

Osbeckia langsdorffiana, commonly known as the Langsdorff's willow herb, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features upright, branching stems covered with soft, hairy leaves. Its small, pink to purple flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Osbeckia langsdorffiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
