Odontonema callistachyum

Odontonema callistachyum, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange inflorescences that resemble flames, which appear during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are not only visually appealing but also attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
