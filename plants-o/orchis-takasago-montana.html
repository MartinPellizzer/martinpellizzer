Orchis takasago-montana

Orchis takasago-montana, commonly known as the Takasago mountain orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Orchis takasago-montana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
