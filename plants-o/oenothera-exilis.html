Oenothera exilis

Oenothera exilis, commonly known as slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Oenothera exilis is adapted to arid environments and has deep roots for water absorption.

It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in desert regions.
