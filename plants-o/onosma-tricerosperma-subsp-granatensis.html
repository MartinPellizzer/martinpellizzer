Onosma tricerosperma subsp. granatensis

Onosma tricerosperma subsp.

granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense Narrowleaf Hedge Mustard, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant has a taproot system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
