Onosma setosa f. granatensis

Onosma setosa f. granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense hedge mustard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as slopes and grasslands.
