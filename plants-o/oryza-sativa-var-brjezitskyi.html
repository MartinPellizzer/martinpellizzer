Oryza sativa var. brjezitskyi

Oryza sativa var. brjezitskyi, commonly known as the Brjezitsky rice, is a rare and less commonly studied variant of the rice plant.

It is characterized by its unique morphological features, including distinct grain morphology and growth habits compared to other rice varieties. This variety is native to specific regions in Asia and exhibits adaptability to particular environmental conditions. Its botanical significance lies in its potential for genetic diversity and possible contributions to rice breeding programs.
