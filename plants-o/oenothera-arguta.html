Oenothera arguta

Oenothera arguta, commonly known as prairie evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves.

Oenothera arguta is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
