Oberonia brunnea

Oberonia brunnea, commonly known as the brown orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. The plant has a slender, creeping rhizome with numerous aerial roots. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in a raceme, often with a distinctive dark brown or reddish coloration.

Oberonia brunnea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
