Ononis peyerimhoffii

Ononis peyerimhoffii, commonly known as the Peyer's whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often appearing in dense tufts.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
