Othonna amplexicaulis

Othonna amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and fleshy, often blue-green leaves that are clasping around the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Othonna amplexicaulis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and resilience.
