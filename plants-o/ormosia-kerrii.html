Ormosia kerrii

Ormosia kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's ormosia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Ormosia kerrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal blooms.
