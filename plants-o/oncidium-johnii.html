Oncidium johnii

Oncidium johnii, commonly known as the Showy Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features slender, often pendulous stems and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium johnii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
