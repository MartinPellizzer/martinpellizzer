Orychophragmus limprichtianus

Orychophragmus limprichtianus, commonly known as the limpricht's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in compact clusters. Native to arid regions of Central Asia, it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes.

Its small size and hardy nature make it a resilient species in challenging environments.
