Odontoglossum citrosmum var. roseum

Odontoglossum citrosmum var. roseum, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in its native environment.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
