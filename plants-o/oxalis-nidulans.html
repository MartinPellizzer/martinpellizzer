Oxalis nidulans

Oxalis nidulans, commonly known as the clustered wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodland areas. It produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds.

Oxalis nidulans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
