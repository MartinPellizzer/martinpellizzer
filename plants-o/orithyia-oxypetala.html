Orithyia oxypetala

Orithyia oxypetala, commonly known as the sharp-petaled orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped petals. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous areas. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Orithyia oxypetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
