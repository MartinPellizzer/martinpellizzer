Oldenlandia borrerioides

Oldenlandia borrerioides, commonly known as the silverleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Oldenlandia borrerioides is often found in rocky soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
