Oreorchis intermedia

Oreorchis intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate orchid, is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

Oreorchis intermedia is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
