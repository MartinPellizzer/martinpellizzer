Osa

Osa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a small evergreen shrub, often with a compact and bushy appearance. Osa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
