Onobrychis dielsii

Onobrychis dielsii, commonly known as the Diels' burr vetch, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Onobrychis dielsii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
