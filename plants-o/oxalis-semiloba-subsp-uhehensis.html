Oxalis semiloba subsp. uhehensis

Oxalis semiloba subsp.

uhehensis, commonly known as the Uhehensis wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa. It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration, and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow or orange.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and it has a fibrous root system. Oxalis semiloba subsp.

uhehensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
