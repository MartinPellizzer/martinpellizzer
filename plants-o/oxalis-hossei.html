Oxalis hossei

Oxalis hossei, commonly known as the purple heart oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features striking violet to purple heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months. Oxalis hossei thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and delicate blooms, which add a unique touch to any garden setting.
