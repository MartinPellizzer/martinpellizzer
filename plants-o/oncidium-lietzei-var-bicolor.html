Oncidium lietzei var. bicolor

Oncidium lietzei var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, two-toned flowers that typically feature a mix of yellow and green hues. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium lietzei var. bicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
