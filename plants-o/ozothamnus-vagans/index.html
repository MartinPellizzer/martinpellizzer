Ozothamnus Vagans

Ozothamnus vagans is a resilient shrub native to the arid regions of western australia. it thrives in harsh environments with limited water and intense sunlight. this plant is known for its compact growth and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. its ability to survive in poor soil makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration. despite its tough conditions, ozothamnus vagans adds beauty and ecological balance to its habitat.
