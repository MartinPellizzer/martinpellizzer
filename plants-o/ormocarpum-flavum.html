Ormocarpum flavum

Ormocarpum flavum, commonly known as the yellow orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Gulf Coast. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, growing in dense clusters.

The fruit is a unique, spiny capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
