Oreoloma

Oreoloma, commonly known as the bluebell or sweet vernal grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue or purple flower clusters that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Oreoloma species are often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
