Ortholobium chevalieri

Ortholobium chevalieri, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its small, oval leaves are often arranged in pairs, and it produces yellow to orange flowers that attract pollinators.

The plant is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
