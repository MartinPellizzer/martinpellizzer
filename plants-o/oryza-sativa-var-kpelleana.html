Oryza sativa var. kpelleana

Oryza sativa var. kpelleana, commonly known as the Kpelleana rice variety, is a traditional African rice species native to the savannas of West Africa.

It is characterized by its short stature, narrow leaves, and grains that are typically smaller and more elongated compared to other rice varieties. This variety is adapted to low-input agricultural systems and can thrive in diverse soil conditions, making it resilient to environmental stress. Its genetic diversity contributes to the broader understanding of rice evolution and adaptation.

It is also valued for its potential in sustainable agriculture and food security in local communities.
