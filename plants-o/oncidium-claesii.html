Oncidium claesii

Oncidium claesii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which often feature intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Oncidium claesii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to survive periods of drought.
