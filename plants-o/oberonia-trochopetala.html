Oberonia trochopetala

Oberonia trochopetala, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
