Oreorchis gracilis var. gracillima

Oreorchis gracilis var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a slender, upright stem that bears a single, narrow lanceolate leaf. The plant produces a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows.

Its botanical features make it a notable subject in the study of orchid morphology and pollination ecology.
