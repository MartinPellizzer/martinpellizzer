Oberonia kwangsiensis

Oberonia kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi Oberonia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly China.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. Its flowers are showy, with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip and a distinctive coloration pattern.

Oberonia kwangsiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
