Onobrychis bifera

Onobrychis bifera, commonly known as red bursaria, is a leguminous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and distinctive, spiny pods that contain edible seeds. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its flowers are usually pink or purple, adding ornamental value to the landscape.
