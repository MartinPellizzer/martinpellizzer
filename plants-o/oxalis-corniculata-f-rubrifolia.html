Oxalis corniculata f. rubrifolia

Oxalis corniculata f. rubrifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features bright red foliage, which distinguishes it from the typical green leaves of the species. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Oxalis corniculata f. rubrifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
