Oncidium sotoanum subsp. papalosmum

Oncidium sotoanum subsp.

papalosmum, commonly known as the Papalosmum orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive red lip.

The plant typically blooms during the dry season and prefers bright, indirect light with good air circulation. It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to thrive.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
