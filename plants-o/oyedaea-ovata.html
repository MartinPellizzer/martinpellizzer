Oyedaea ovata

Oyedaea ovata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in a compact rosette form, with the flowers emerging from the center. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oyedaea ovata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
