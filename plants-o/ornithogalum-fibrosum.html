Ornithogalum fibrosum

Ornithogalum fibrosum, commonly known as the fibrous star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its fibrous, bulb-like structures that store water and nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
