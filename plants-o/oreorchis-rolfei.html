Oreorchis rolfei

Oreorchis rolfei, commonly known as the Rolfe's green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem that bears several oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Oreorchis rolfei is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in well-drained soils.
