Ononis glabrescens var. filifolia

Ononis glabrescens var. filifolia, commonly known as silky sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June. The plant has slender, hairy stems and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its delicate appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
