Oncidium cultratum

Oncidium cultratum, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium cultratum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
