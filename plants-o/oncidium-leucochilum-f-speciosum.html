Oncidium leucochilum f. speciosum

Oncidium leucochilum f. speciosum, commonly known as the White-Lipped Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It features pendulous, raceme-like inflorescences with flowers that typically range from white to pale yellow, often with contrasting lip patterns. The plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
