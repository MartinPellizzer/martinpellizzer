Oreodaphne spectabilis

Oreodaphne spectabilis, commonly known as the showy oreodaphne, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and distinctive fruiting structures. The plant typically grows in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a glossy surface, and it produces small, clustered flowers followed by spherical berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
