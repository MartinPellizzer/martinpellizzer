Orchis viridis var. vaillantii

Orchis viridis var. vaillantii, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow, with a distinct lip that is often darker in color. This variety is often found in meadows and grasslands, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

It is a perennial plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
