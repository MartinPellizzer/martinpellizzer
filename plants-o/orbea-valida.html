Orbea valida

Orbea valida, commonly known as the silver star orbea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
