Oryza sativa var. parasepica

Oryza sativa var. parasepica, commonly known as the slender rice.

It is a variant of rice characterized by its narrow, elongated grains and reduced panicle structure. This variety is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant exhibits a short stature and is often cultivated for its unique grain morphology.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in genetic studies and rice breeding programs.
