Orthophytum grossiorum

Orthophytum grossiorum, commonly known as the roughleaf orthophytum, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, often in late summer or early autumn.
