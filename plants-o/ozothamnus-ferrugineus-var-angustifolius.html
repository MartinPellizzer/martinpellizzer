Ozothamnus ferrugineus var. angustifolius

Ozothamnus ferrugineus var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved golden bush, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, spherical clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
