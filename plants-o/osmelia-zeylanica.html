Osmelia zeylanica

Osmelia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon osmunda, is a species of fern native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are deeply divided and have a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Osmelia zeylanica plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
