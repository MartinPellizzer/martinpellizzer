Olax pauciflora

Olax pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered olax, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, with opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Olax pauciflora is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.
