Oxalis reclinata var. quinata

Oxalis reclinata var. quinata, commonly known as the pink oxalis, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often tinged with pink or red hues, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is widely cultivated in both gardens and as a houseplant.
