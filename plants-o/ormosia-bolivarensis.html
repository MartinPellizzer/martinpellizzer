Ormosia bolivarensis

Ormosia bolivarensis, commonly known as the Bolivian ormosia, is a species of leguminous tree native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals. Ormosia bolivarensis is valued for its hard wood and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
