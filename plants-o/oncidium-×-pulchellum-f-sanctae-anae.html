Oncidium × pulchellum f. sanctae-anae

Oncidium × pulchellum f. sanctae-anae, commonly known as the Santa Ana Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid with striking, long-lasting blooms.

It features pendulous inflorescences that produce clusters of fragrant, yellow and orange flowers with a contrasting red lip. The plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting mix to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
