Obione isatidea

Obione isatidea, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Obione isatidea is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including roadsides and fields.
