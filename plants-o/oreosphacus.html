Oreosphacus

Oreosphacus, commonly known as the groundsel, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. Oreosphacus species typically have hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed. They are native to various regions in Europe, Asia, and North America.
