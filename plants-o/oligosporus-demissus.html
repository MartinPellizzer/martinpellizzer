Oligosporus demissus

Oligosporus demissus, commonly known as the slender spore moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its small, elongated leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in length. The plant produces spores in capsules that are often borne on short, lateral branches. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Its simple morphology and spore-based reproduction are typical of many bryophyte species.
