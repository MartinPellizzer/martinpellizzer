Ononis serrata var. marmorata

Ononis serrata var. marmorata, commonly known as the marbled sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive marbled leaf patterns, which give it its common name. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or alpine environments. Its small white or pink flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging environments.
