Osbeckia aspera var. travancorica

Osbeckia aspera var. travancorica, commonly known as the Travancore osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a rough, pubescent surface. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the monsoon season.

It is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
