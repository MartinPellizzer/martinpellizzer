Oxytropis ningxiaensis

Oxytropis ningxiaensis, commonly known as Ningxia milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow or pale green pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.

Oxytropis ningxiaensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
