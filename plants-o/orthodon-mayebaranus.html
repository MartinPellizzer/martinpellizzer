Orthodon mayebaranus

Orthodon mayebaranus, commonly known as the mayebaran orchid, is a rare and unique species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated floral structure and vibrant, often colorful petals. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and specialized roots adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
