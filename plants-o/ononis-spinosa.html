Ononis spinosa

Ononis spinosa, commonly known as prickly restharrow, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems covered in stiff, spiny hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ononis spinosa is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
