Oberonia oligotricha

Oberonia oligotricha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale green. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few leaves and produces a single spike of flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
