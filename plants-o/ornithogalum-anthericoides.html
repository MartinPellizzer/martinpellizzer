Ornithogalum anthericoides

Ornithogalum anthericoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem base and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum anthericoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
