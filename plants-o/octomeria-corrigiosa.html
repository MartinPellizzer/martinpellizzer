Octomeria corrigiosa

Octomeria corrigiosa, commonly known as the rock fig, is a small shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Moraceae, which includes figs and mulberries, and is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, clustered flowers. Octomeria corrigiosa is often found growing on rocky outcrops and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
