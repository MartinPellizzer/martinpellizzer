Oncidium papilio f. latourae

Oncidium papilio f. latourae, commonly known as the Orange-Flowered Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features elongated, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that typically have a yellow base and orange or red markings. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

This orchid is popular among growers for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
