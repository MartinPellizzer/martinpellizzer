Olearia albida

Olearia albida, commonly known as silver olearia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to maritime climates. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Olearia albida is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators.
