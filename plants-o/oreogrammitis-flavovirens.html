Oreogrammitis flavovirens

Oreogrammitis flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright green to yellow-green coloration. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rock surfaces or in forest floors.

This moss is notable for its ability to tolerate desiccation, making it a resilient species in various habitats.
