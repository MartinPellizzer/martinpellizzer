Orbea marmorata

Orbea marmorata, commonly known as the marble orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble orchids. The plant features a short, fibrous root system and produces small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Orbea marmorata is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
