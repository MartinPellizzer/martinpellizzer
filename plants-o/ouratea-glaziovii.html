Ouratea glaziovii

Ouratea glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's ouratea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-barked trunk and dense, rounded crown. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
