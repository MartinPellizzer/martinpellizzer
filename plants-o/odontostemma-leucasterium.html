Odontostemma leucasterium

Odontostemma leucasterium, commonly known as the white-flowered spider orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure. The plant features showy, white to pale yellow flowers with long, slender petals and sepals. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in local ecosystems.
