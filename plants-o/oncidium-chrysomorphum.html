Oncidium chrysomorphum

Oncidium chrysomorphum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle green or brownish hue. The plant has strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly textured. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium chrysomorphum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
