Oxytropis kateninii

Oxytropis kateninii, commonly known as the Kateninii bur pea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to violet in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Oxytropis kateninii is also noted for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native range.
