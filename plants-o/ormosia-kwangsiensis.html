Ormosia kwangsiensis

Ormosia kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi ormosia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
