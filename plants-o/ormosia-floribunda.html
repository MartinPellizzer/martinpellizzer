Ormosia floribunda

Ormosia floribunda, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree also has a shallow root system, making it suitable for urban landscaping in suitable conditions.
