Osmanthus vaccinioides

Osmanthus vaccinioides, commonly known as the dwarf sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the fall. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its aromatic foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valued addition to gardens and landscapes.
