Osmanthus macrocarpus

Osmanthus macrocarpus, commonly known as the large-fruited osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture with a slightly serrated margin.

Osmanthus macrocarpus is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties, making it a popular plant in horticulture.
