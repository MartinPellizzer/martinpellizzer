Obione polyphylla

Obione polyphylla, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. Its fronds are typically light green and can grow to lengths of over 3 meters, contributing to its striking appearance.

Obione polyphylla plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's native forests, providing habitat and shelter for various wildlife species.
