Ochna harmandii

Ochna harmandii, commonly known as the Harmand's ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Ochna harmandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
