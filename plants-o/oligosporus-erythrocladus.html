Oligosporus erythrocladus

Oligosporus erythrocladus, commonly known as the red-stemmed spore plant, is a unique species within the Oligosporus genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive red-colored stems and small, clustered spores that are released during specific environmental conditions. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore structure is adapted for efficient dispersal, allowing it to colonize new areas rapidly.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex reproductive cycle and role in certain ecological systems.
