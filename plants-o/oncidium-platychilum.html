Oncidium platychilum

Oncidium platychilum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Oncidium platychilum is known for its ease of cultivation and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
