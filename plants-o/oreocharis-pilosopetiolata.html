Oreocharis pilosopetiolata

Oreocharis pilosopetiolata, commonly known as the hairy-leafed oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
