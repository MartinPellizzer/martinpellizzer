Orostachys spinosa

Orostachys spinosa, commonly known as the spiny orostachy, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Asia.

It features fleshy, spiny leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Orostachys spinosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
