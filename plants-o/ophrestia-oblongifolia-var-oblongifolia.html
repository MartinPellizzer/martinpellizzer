Ophrestia oblongifolia var. oblongifolia

Ophrestia oblongifolia var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ophrestia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Apiaceae family.
