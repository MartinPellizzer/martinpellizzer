Olearia nernstii

Olearia nernstii, commonly known as the Nernst's laceflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sunny, well-drained conditions and is often found in coastal areas.

Olearia nernstii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
