Orchis × flixensis

Orchis × flixensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Orchis morio and Orchis flixensis.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats across Europe. It has a sympodial growth habit with rhizomatous stems and lanceolate leaves.

Its flowers are often pollinated by bees and other insects, contributing to its ecological significance in its native range.
