Ornithogalum kuereanum

Ornithogalum kuereanum, commonly known as the Kurea lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant blooms with star-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers that have a distinctive, often fragrant scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is popular among gardeners interested in native flora.
