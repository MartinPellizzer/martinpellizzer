Otoglossum candelabrum

Otoglossum candelabrum, commonly known as the candlestick orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, candle-like inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Otoglossum candelabrum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
