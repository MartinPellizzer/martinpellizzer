Orchis papilionacea subsp. heroica

Orchis papilionacea subsp.

heroica, commonly known as the Heroic Butterfly Orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble butterflies, with a distinctive coloration of purple and white.

This subspecies is native to specific regions in southern Europe, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats. The plant has a complex root system and produces a single, erect stem with a few leaves.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, making it a notable species for botanical enthusiasts.
