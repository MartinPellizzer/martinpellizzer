Olearia phlogopappa var. subrepanda

Olearia phlogopappa var. subrepanda, commonly known as the New Zealand ice plant, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silver-gray, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to rocky soils and has a compact growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
