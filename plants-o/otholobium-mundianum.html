Otholobium mundianum

Otholobium mundianum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wattle, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant produces yellow globular flowers, which are typically clustered in spikes. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Otholobium mundianum is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
