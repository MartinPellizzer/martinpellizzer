Oryctanthus grandis

Oryctanthus grandis, commonly known as the giant sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody shrub form and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, needle-like leaves and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

Oryctanthus grandis is adapted to sandy soils and can withstand drought conditions due to its deep root system.
