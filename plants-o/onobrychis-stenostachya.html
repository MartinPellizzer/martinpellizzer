Onobrychis stenostachya

Onobrychis stenostachya, commonly known as narrow-stalked sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and is often found in dry, calcareous soils. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural and ecological restoration settings.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
