Otachyrium grandiflorum

Otachyrium grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant flameflower, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is native to Central and South America. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and covered in a fine layer of hairs. Its flowers are usually red or orange, with prominent yellow stamens that give it a fiery appearance.

Otachyrium grandiflorum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
