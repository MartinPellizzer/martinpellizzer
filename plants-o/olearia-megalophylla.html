Olearia megalophylla

Olearia megalophylla, commonly known as the giant silver fern, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, silvery-white, velvety leaves that are arranged in a distinctive fern-like pattern. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Olearia megalophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in New Zealand gardens.
