Ochlopoa annua subsp. pilantha

Ochlopoa annua subsp.

pilantha, commonly known as the pilantha annual, is a member of the Amaranthaceae family. It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its erect stem and lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.
