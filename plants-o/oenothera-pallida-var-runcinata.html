Oenothera pallida var. runcinata

Oenothera pallida var. runcinata, commonly known as the slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The leaves are linear and often have a slightly hairy texture. This species is part of the Onagraceae family and is known for its adaptability to arid environments.

It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.
