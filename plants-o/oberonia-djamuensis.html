Oberonia djamuensis

Oberonia djamuensis, commonly known as the Djamuensian orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the rainforests of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree bark or in the undergrowth of dense vegetation. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
