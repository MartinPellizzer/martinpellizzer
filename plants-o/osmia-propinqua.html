Osmia propinqua

Osmia propinqua, commonly known as the eastern squash bee, is a solitary ground-nesting bee species native to North America.

The female bee constructs its nest in the soil, lining the cells with a mixture of plant resins and secretions. Its specialized morphology and foraging behavior make it an important pollinator for both wild and cultivated plants.
