Oncidium ottonis

Oncidium ottonis, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent white lips, blooming during the summer months. Oncidium ottonis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ease of care and long-lasting blooms make it a favored ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
