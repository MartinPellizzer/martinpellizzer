Osmanthus collinus

Osmanthus collinus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the autumn season. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically harvested for their aromatic seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a slightly serrated edge.

Osmanthus collinus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its scent and medicinal properties.
