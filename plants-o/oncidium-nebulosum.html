Oncidium nebulosum

Oncidium nebulosum, commonly known as the Cloudy Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, long-lasting inflorescences with flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture from the air. Its flowers bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Oncidium nebulosum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
