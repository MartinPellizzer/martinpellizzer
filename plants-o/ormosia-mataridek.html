Ormosia mataridek

Ormosia mataridek, commonly known as the Mataridek tamarind.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with numerous small leaflets, and the flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow.

The fruit is a legume that splits open to release its seeds, contributing to its role in forest ecosystems.
