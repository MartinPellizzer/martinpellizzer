Odontoglossum reichenbachianum

Odontoglossum reichenbachianum, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that displays a range of hues, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are borne on tall, branched stems and are known for their intricate, often symmetrical structure.

Odontoglossum reichenbachianum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its vibrant, showy blooms.
