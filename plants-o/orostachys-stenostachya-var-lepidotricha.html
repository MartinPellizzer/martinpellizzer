Orostachys stenostachya var. lepidotricha

Orostachys stenostachya var. lepidotricha, commonly known as the silver leaf saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped growth pattern and silver-gray foliage due to a thick layer of trichomes. The plant produces small, pinkish flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in compact inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
