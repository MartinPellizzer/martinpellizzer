Oxytropis micans

Oxytropis micans, commonly known as the whispering pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets that contribute to its distinctive appearance.

Oxytropis micans is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
