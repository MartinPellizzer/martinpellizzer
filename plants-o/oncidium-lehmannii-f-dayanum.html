Oncidium lehmannii f. dayanum

Oncidium lehmannii f. dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This orchid is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
