Occultia fragrans

Occultia fragrans, commonly known as the Moonflower Vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet, intoxicating fragrance. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy green surface. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Its unique nocturnal blooming habit makes it a popular choice for evening gardens.
