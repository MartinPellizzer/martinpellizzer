Ononis balearica

Ononis balearica, commonly known as the Balearic whitlowgrass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, often hairy stems. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

Ononis balearica is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, showing resilience to harsh Mediterranean conditions.
