Oncidium wentworthianum

Oncidium wentworthianum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters of yellow and orange hues. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium wentworthianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
