Ononis natrix var. font-queri

Ononis natrix var. font-queri, commonly known as the Font-Queri bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, wiry stems that often lie close to the ground. It is often found in dry habitats such as rocky slopes and open woodlands.
