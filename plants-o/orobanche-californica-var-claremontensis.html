Orobanche californica var. claremontensis

Orobanche californica var. claremontensis, commonly known as the Claremont dwarf broomrape, is a parasitic flowering plant native to southern California.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, and its life cycle is closely tied to the seasonal availability of its host.
