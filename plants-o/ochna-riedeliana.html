Ochna riedeliana

Ochna riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's ochna, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Ochna riedeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
