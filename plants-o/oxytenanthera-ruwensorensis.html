Oxytenanthera ruwensorensis

Oxytenanthera ruwensorensis, commonly known as the Ruwensorensis grass, is a species of flowering plant in the Poaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Paraguay. This grass is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetland areas.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
