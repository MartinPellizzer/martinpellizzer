Oleandra australis

Oleandra australis, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fiery red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate, with elongated leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Oleandra australis is often planted for its vibrant floral display and can grow as a tree or shrub depending on pruning.
