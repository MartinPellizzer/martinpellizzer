Olax longifolia

Olax longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf olax, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Olax longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
