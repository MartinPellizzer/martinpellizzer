Ornithogalum wilmaniae

Ornithogalum wilmaniae, commonly known as the Wilman's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum wilmaniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
