Oxydectes regnelliana

Oxydectes regnelliana, commonly known as the red-flowered oxeye, is a striking perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Oxydectes regnelliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
