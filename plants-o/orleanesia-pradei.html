Orleanesia pradei

Orleanesia pradei, commonly known as the Prade's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its growth habit is generally compact, with erect stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Orleanesia pradei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
