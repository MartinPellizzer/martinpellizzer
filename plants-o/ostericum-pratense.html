Ostericum pratense

Ostericum pratense, commonly known as meadow ostericum, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered umbels of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. Its leaves are alternate, finely divided, and often have a slight aromatic scent.

Ostericum pratense is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
