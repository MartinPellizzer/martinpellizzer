Onosma drenowskii

Onosma drenowskii, commonly known as the Drenowsky's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, yellow to orange flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Onosma drenowskii is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
