Odontoglossum narthecioides

Odontoglossum narthecioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, pseudobulbous growth habit, producing a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant blooms with showy, yellowish-green to white flowers that have a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
