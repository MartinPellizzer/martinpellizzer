Oldenlandia callitrichoides

Oldenlandia callitrichoides, commonly known as the "Cape ivy," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season.

The species is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
