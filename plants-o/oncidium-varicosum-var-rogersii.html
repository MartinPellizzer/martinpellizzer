Oncidium varicosum var. rogersii

Oncidium varicosum var. rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers' orchid, is a hybrid orchid species originating from the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of long, pendant-like inflorescences bearing fragrant, yellowish-green to orange flowers. The plant typically thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. It requires regular watering and moderate fertilization to maintain optimal growth and blooming.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
