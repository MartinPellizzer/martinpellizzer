Odontonema cuspidatum

Odontonema cuspidatum, commonly known as the Brazilian flame or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features striking red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontonema cuspidatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
