Oncidium gutfreundianum

Oncidium gutfreundianum, commonly known as the Gutfreund's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues, often arranged in a raceme.

Oncidium gutfreundianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
