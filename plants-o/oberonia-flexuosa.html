Oberonia flexuosa

Oberonia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a dense spike, which are typically fragrant. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Oberonia flexuosa is valued for its unique morphology and is a subject of interest in botanical research and horticulture.
