Oxalis atrata

Oxalis atrata, commonly known as the velvetleaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodlands. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Oxalis atrata is also valued as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and floral display.
