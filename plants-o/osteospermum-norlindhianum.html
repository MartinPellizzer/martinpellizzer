Osteospermum norlindhianum

Osteospermum norlindhianum, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant features a compact growth habit with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, conical clusters, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Osteospermum norlindhianum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.
