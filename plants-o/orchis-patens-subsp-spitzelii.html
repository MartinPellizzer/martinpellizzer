Orchis patens subsp. spitzelii

Orchis patens subsp.

spitzelii, commonly known as the Spitzel's early orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with yellow markings.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces a single spike of flowers that bloom in early spring.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
