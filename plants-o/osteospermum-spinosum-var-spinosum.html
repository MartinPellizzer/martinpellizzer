Osteospermum spinosum var. spinosum

Osteospermum spinosum var. spinosum, commonly known as the spiny osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, which bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
