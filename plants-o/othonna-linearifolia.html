Othonna linearifolia

Othonna linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved othonna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Othonna linearifolia is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
