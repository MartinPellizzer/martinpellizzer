Onobrychis Melanotricha F. Minor

Onobrychis melanotricha f. minor is a rare and lesser-known variant of the common onobrychis plant. It is characterized by its unique dark green foliage and compact growth habit. This subspecies is primarily found in specific regions of southern Europe and the Mediterranean. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study. Its conservation status highlights the importance of preserving such rare plant forms.
