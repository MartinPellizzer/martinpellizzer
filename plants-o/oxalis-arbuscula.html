Oxalis arbuscula

Oxalis arbuscula, commonly known as the tree oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with a woody stem that resembles a small tree, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of five-petaled flowers in shades of white, pink, or red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis arbuscula is known for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
