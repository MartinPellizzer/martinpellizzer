Oxalis neaei

Oxalis neaei, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the wood sorrel family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

Oxalis neaei is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
