Ostinia oxyacantha

Ostinia oxyacantha, commonly known as the spiny acanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its dramatic appearance.

The plant also has historical uses in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
