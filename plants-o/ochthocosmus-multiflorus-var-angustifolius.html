Ochthocosmus multiflorus var. angustifolius

Ochthocosmus multiflorus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, five-petaled yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
