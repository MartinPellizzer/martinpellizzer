Orchis langei

Orchis langei, commonly known as the Lange's orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in Europe, particularly in the mountainous areas of Italy and Austria. The plant features a tall, slender stem with a single, showy floral spike that bears several pale purple or white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Orchis langei is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms, often relying on specific insect species for reproduction.
