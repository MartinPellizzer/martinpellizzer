Osteospermum moniliferum var. lanosum

Osteospermum moniliferum var. lanosum, commonly known as the woolly blue mist, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
