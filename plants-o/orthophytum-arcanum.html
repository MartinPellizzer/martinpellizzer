Orthophytum arcanum

Orthophytum arcanum, commonly known as the hidden leaf cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny stems and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its unique appearance and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Orthophytum arcanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
