Oxalis linearis var. alba

Oxalis linearis var. alba, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with white veins and small, delicate white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage and subtle floral display.

It is also noted for its ability to thrive in cooler climates and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
