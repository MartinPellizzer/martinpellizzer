Ocotea lindleyana

Ocotea lindleyana, commonly known as the fragrant ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Ocotea lindleyana is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
