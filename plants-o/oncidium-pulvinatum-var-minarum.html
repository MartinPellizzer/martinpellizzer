Oncidium pulvinatum var. minarum

Oncidium pulvinatum var. minarum, commonly known as the miniature Oncidium, is a small orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features compact, linear leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor growers.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
