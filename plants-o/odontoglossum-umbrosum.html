Odontoglossum umbrosum

Odontoglossum umbrosum, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark violet to almost black flowers, which are often speckled with white or yellow markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, and it produces showy, fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Odontoglossum umbrosum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
