Olearia ramulosa var. microphylla

Olearia ramulosa var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaf daisy bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens across the southern hemisphere.
