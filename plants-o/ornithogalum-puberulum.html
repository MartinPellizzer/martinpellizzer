Ornithogalum puberulum

Ornithogalum puberulum, commonly known as the woolly star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces a tall, flowering stem that bears a cluster of white, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Ornithogalum puberulum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
