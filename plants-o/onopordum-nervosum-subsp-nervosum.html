Onopordum nervosum subsp. nervosum

Onopordum nervosum subsp.

nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers.

The plant has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in a spherical head.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.
