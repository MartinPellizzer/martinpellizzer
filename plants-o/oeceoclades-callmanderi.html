Oeceoclades callmanderi

Oeceoclades callmanderi, commonly known as the Callmander's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, erect stem and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
