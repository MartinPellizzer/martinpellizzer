Oxyanthus speciosus var. pubescens

Oxyanthus speciosus var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy oxyanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
