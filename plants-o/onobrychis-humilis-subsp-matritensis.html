Onobrychis humilis subsp. matritensis

Onobrychis humilis subsp.

matritensis, commonly known as the Matritensis burr medic, is a low-growing perennial legume native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This subspecies is valued for its forage qualities and historical use in agriculture.
