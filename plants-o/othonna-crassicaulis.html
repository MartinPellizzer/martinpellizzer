Othonna crassicaulis

Othonna crassicaulis, commonly known as the broad-leaved othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is typically found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, where it forms dense, low-growing clumps.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping in regions with limited rainfall.
