Osyris abyssinica

Osyris abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian osyris, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Osyris abyssinica is adapted to thrive in poor soils and can withstand drought conditions.
