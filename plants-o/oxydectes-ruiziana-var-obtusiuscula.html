Oxydectes ruiziana var. obtusiuscula

Oxydectes ruiziana var. obtusiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and small, evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid climates.
