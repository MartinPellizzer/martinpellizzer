Osteospermum moniliferum

Osteospermum moniliferum, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with blue to violet petals. The plant produces a distinctive blue or purple center surrounded by white or pale blue ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Osteospermum moniliferum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
