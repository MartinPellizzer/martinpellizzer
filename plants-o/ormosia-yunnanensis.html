Ormosia yunnanensis

Ormosia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan ormosia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in subtropical and temperate regions, often in forested areas with well-drained soil. Its seeds are encased in pods and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ecological role in local ecosystems.

Ormosia yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
