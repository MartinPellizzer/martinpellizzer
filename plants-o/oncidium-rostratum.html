Oncidium rostratum

Oncidium rostratum, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange tips, arranged in racemes. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium rostratum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
