Orbea vibratilis

Orbea vibratilis, commonly known as the dancing orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble orchids. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Orbea vibratilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
