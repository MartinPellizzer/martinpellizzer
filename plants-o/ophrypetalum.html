Ophrypetalum

Ophrypetalum, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their velvety, fuzzy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. Ophrypetalum species are typically herbaceous, with upright stems and are often grown as houseplants due to their ornamental value. They prefer bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

The genus includes several species, such as Ophrypetalum grandiflorum, which is prized for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
