Othonna othonnites

Othonna othonnites, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking color and texture.

Othonna othonnites is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
