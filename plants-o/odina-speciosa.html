Odina speciosa

Odina speciosa, commonly known as the showy odina, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warm season. Odina speciosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
