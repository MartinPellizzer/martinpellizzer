Oryza sativa var. manilensis

Oryza sativa var. manilensis, commonly known as the Philippine rice, is a wild rice species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated grains and is typically found in wetland habitats. This variety is known for its genetic diversity and is considered an important resource for rice breeding programs. The plant has a tall, branched stem and produces small, white flowers.

It is adapted to tropical climates and plays a role in both ecological and agricultural systems.
