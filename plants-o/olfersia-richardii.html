Olfersia richardii

Olfersia richardii, commonly known as the Richard's orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive dark red or purple lip.

Olfersia richardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
