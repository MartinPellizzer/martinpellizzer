Ononis trifoliata

Ononis trifoliata, commonly known as three-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit with trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or rocky environments.

Ononis trifoliata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
