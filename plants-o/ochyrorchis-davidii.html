Ochyrorchis davidii

Ochyrorchis davidii, commonly known as the David's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears several oval, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, typically arranged in a loose spike.

Ochyrorchis davidii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
