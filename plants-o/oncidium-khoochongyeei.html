Oncidium khoochongyeei

Oncidium khoochongyeei, commonly known as the Khu Chong Yee orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium khoochongyeei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
