Oncidium lykaiosii

Oncidium lykaiosii, commonly known as the Lykaiosii orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the humid forests of Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and drawing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are spurred and have a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insects.

Oncidium lykaiosii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
