Oxymeris velutina

Oxymeris velutina, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the rainforests of West and Central Africa. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
