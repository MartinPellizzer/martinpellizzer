Oncidium odontochilum

Oncidium odontochilum, commonly known as the toothed lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive, tooth-like lip that gives it its scientific name. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Oncidium odontochilum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
