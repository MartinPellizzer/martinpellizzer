Olearia gunniana var. salicifolia

Olearia gunniana var. salicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree fern, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, silver-green fronds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. Its foliage is densely packed, giving it a lush, fern-like appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests and along stream banks.
