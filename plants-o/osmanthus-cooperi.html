Osmanthus cooperi

Osmanthus cooperi, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The sweet scent of its flowers makes it a favorite in gardens and for aromatic purposes.
