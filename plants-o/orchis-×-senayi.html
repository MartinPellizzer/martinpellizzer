Orchis × senayi

Orchis × senayi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural cross between Orchis morio and Orchis mascula.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that are usually purple or violet in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It has a fibrous root system and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Orchis × senayi is often found in the temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
