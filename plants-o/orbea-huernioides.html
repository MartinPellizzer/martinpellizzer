Orbea huernioides

Orbea huernioides, commonly known as the Hottentot's tears, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, pendant-like flowers that resemble tears. The plant produces orange to yellow flowers with a prominent, fleshy corona at the base. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Orbea huernioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
