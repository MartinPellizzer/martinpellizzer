Oberonia cathayana

Oberonia cathayana, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, often branched stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration ranging from greenish-white to pale yellow.

Oberonia cathayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
