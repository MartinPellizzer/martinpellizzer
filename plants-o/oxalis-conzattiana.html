Oxalis conzattiana

Oxalis conzattiana, commonly known as the rainbow oxalis, is a striking plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, triangular leaves with a unique pattern of red, orange, and yellow markings. The plant produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinctive yellow center. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
