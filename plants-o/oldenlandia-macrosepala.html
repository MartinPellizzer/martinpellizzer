Oldenlandia macrosepala

Oldenlandia macrosepala, commonly known as the large-sepaled spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers, which bloom in clusters along slender stems. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
