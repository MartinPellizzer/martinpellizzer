Onobrychis crinita

Onobrychis crinita, commonly known as silky senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with soft, silky hairs that give it its common name. Its flowers are yellow and appear in racemes, often blooming in late winter to early spring.

The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
