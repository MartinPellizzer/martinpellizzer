Oxalis venustula

Oxalis venustula, commonly known as the beautiful wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are heart-shaped and often tinged with red or purple veins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis venustula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blossoms.
