Ovilla globulariiflora

Ovilla globulariiflora, commonly known as the globe-shaped ovilla, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
