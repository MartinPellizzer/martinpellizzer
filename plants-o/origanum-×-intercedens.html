Origanum × intercedens

Origanum × intercedens, commonly known as Greek oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a strong, pungent scent due to its high concentration of essential oils. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or herb gardens.

Its ornamental value and culinary uses make it a popular choice among gardeners and cooks alike.
