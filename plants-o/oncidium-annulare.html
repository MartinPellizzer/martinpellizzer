Oncidium annulare

Oncidium annulare, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, often arranged in a raceme. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Oncidium annulare is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
