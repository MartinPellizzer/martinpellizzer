Oxalis europaea f. villicaulis

Oxalis europaea f. villicaulis, commonly known as the velvetleaf wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features trifoliate leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species in horticulture.
