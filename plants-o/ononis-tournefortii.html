Ononis tournefortii

Ononis tournefortii, commonly known as French rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to thrive in rocky and arid environments.

Ononis tournefortii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh conditions.
