Ochna cardiosperma

Ochna cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits containing a single, large, heart-shaped seed. Ochna cardiosperma is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and unique fruit structure.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
