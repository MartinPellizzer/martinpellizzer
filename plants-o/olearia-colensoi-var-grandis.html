Olearia colensoi var. grandis

Olearia colensoi var. grandis, commonly known as the large-leaved New Zealand daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow daisy-like flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Olearia colensoi var. grandis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
