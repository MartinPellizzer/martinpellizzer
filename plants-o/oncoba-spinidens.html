Oncoba spinidens

Oncoba spinidens, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are palmately lobed and may have a waxy coating, while its stems are covered in spines.

Oncoba spinidens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
