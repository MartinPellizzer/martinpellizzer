Orchis × perretii

Orchis × perretii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant resulting from the cross between Orchis morio and Orchis mascula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant produces striking purple or violet flowers with a distinctive lip, which is a characteristic feature of many orchid species. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Orchis × perretii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
