Onopordum arenarium

Onopordum arenarium, commonly known as the sand thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils. The plant features a tall, spiny stem with lobed, silvery-green leaves. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is characterized by its adaptability to arid environments and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
