Oreorchis parvula

Oreorchis parvula, commonly known as the small green orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow leaf and a cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its flowers are usually less than an inch in length and bloom in late spring to early summer.
