Oligostachyum glabrescens

Oligostachyum glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth leaved bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to white flowers, which are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine in some areas.
