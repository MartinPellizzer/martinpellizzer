Octerium

Octerium, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10-30 cm and prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Octerium is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
