Olax laxiflora

Olax laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered olax, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Olax laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
