Onosma decorticans

Onosma decorticans, commonly known as the naked root sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
