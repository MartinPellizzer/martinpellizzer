Osbeckia albiflora

Osbeckia albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower osbeckia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Osbeckia albiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
