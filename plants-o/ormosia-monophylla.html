Ormosia monophylla

Ormosia monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf ormosia, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its unique single leaf that is often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Ormosia monophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its distinctive foliage.
