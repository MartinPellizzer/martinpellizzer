Oncerum ciliatum

Oncerum ciliatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and prominent ciliate margins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Oncerum ciliatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
