Odontarrhena Serpentinicola

Odontarrhena serpentinicola is a rare and unique plant species that thrives in extreme environments. It is known for its ability to grow in serpentine soils, which are rich in heavy metals and poor in nutrients. This plant has adapted to harsh conditions through specialized physiological mechanisms. Its survival highlights the resilience of life in challenging ecosystems. Studying Odontarrhena serpentinicola offers insights into plant adaptation and ecological sustainability.
