Othonna dregeana

Othonna dregeana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, which bloom in spring. It has silver-green, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.

Othonna dregeana is well adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
