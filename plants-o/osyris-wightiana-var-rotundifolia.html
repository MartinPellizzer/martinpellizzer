Osyris wightiana var. rotundifolia

Osyris wightiana var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the Round-leaf Osyris, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found near water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
