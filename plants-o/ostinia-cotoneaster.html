Ostinia cotoneaster

Ostinia cotoneaster, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that turn red in the fall, adding visual interest to the landscape. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its compact growth and seasonal color changes.

Ostinia cotoneaster is relatively low maintenance and thrives in a variety of soil types.
