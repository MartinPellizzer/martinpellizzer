Oberna

Oberna, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, reddish-brown heartwood, which is highly valued for its durability and rich color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

The tree is often cultivated for its timber and is also used in traditional medicine.
