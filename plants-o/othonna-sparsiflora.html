Othonna sparsiflora

Othonna sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered othonna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and dense rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Othonna sparsiflora is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
