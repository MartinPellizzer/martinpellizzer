Oenothera megalantha

Oenothera megalantha, commonly known as the giant evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant produces large, hairy capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oenothera megalantha is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
