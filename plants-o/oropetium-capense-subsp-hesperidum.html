Oropetium capense subsp. hesperidum

Oropetium capense subsp.

hesperidum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically arranged in showy, flat-topped clusters.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Oropetium capense subsp.

hesperidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
