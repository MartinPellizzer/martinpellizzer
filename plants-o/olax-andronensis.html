Olax andronensis

Olax andronensis, commonly known as the Andronese olax, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showing a preference for sunny locations. Its flowering period occurs in the spring, and it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Olax andronensis is relatively drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid environments.
