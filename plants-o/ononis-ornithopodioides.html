Ononis ornithopodioides

Ononis ornithopodioides, commonly known as the bird's-foot trefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive cluster of three leaflets at the base, resembling a bird's foot. Its yellow flowers appear in spring and are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.
