Oerstedella centropetala

Oerstedella centropetala, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, with a compact rosette growth habit. The plant produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers with five petals that bloom in early spring. Its flowering stems are upright and bear the inflorescence at the center, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Oerstedella centropetala is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and borders.
