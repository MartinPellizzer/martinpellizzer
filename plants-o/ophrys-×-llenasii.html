Ophrys × llenasii

Ophrys × llenasii, commonly known as the Llenasii orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate flower structure, which often mimics the appearance of female insects to attract male pollinators. The plant typically grows in Mediterranean climates, thriving in well-drained soils and partial shade. Its flowers are usually small and showy, with specialized markings that enhance its mimicry.

This species is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its evolutionary adaptations and ornamental value.
