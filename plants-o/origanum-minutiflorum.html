Origanum minutiflorum

Origanum minutiflorum, commonly known as dwarf oregano, is a small, compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves and small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage, which emits a strong, pungent scent reminiscent of oregano. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and as a culinary herb.
