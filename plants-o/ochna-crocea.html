Ochna crocea

Ochna crocea, commonly known as the yellow ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glossy, dark green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ochna crocea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
