Ozothamnus coralloides

Ozothamnus coralloides, commonly known as the coral bush, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, branching structure and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. Ozothamnus coralloides thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
