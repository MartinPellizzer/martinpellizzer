Orthosia Rubens

Orthosia rubens is a striking member of the Apiaceae family known for its vibrant red flowers and unique appearance. This plant is native to regions of Europe and western Asia where it thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its biennial growth cycle allows it to flourish in the wild while also making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant's distinctive color and structure make it a favorite among botanists and horticultural enthusiasts. Its beauty and adaptability highlight its ecological and ornamental significance.
