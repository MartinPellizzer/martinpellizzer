Orixa japonica

Orixa japonica, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Orixa japonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
