Oxydectes julopsidium

Oxydectes julopsidium, commonly known as the yellow sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant is adapted to thrive in sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.
