Otostegia megastegia

Otostegia megastegia, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its hairy, broad leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.

Otostegia megastegia is known for its rapid growth and can become a weed in cultivated fields.
