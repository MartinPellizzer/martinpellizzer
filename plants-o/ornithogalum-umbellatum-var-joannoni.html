Ornithogalum umbellatum var. joannoni

Ornithogalum umbellatum var. joannoni, commonly known as the Joannon's star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial herb that prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
