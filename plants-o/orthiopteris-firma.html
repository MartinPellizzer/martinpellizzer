Orthiopteris firma

Orthiopteris firma, commonly known as the rigid sword fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The fronds are typically green and have a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant reproduces via spores and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.
