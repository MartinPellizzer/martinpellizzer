Ononis umbrosa

Ononis umbrosa, commonly known as shadowy sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers shady to partially shaded environments. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Ononis umbrosa is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
