Orbea sprengeri subsp. foetida

Orbea sprengeri subsp.

foetida, commonly known as the stinking orbea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in dry conditions, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
