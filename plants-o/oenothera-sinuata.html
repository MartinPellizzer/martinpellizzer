Oenothera sinuata

Oenothera sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, zigzagging stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Oenothera sinuata is a member of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.
