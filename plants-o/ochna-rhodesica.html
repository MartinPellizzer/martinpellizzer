Ochna rhodesica

Ochna rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodesian ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Ochna rhodesica is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
