Oreanthes fragilis

Oreanthes fragilis, commonly known as the fragile oreanthe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Oreanthes fragilis is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
