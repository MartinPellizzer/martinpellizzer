Odontostemma xerophilum

Odontostemma xerophilum, commonly known as the desert orchid, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in dry environments through its succulent leaves and deep root system. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Odontostemma xerophilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, low-water conditions.
