Oenothera mollissima var. genuina

Oenothera mollissima var. genuina, commonly known as the softstem evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
