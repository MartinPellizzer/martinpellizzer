Oenothera australis

Oenothera australis, commonly known as the Australian evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Oenothera australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other parts of the world for horticultural purposes.
