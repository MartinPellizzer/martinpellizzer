Oreocnide obovata var. obovata

Oreocnide obovata var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate oreocnide, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically shiny and dark green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany.
