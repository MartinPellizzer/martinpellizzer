Odontoglossum punctulatum

Odontoglossum punctulatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with white or yellow spots, and its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without being parasitic. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Odontoglossum punctulatum is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
