Ononis biflora subsp. maroccana

Ononis biflora subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, pale yellow flowers.

The plant has finely divided leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
