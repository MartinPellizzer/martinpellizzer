Osteospermum calendulaceum

Osteospermum calendulaceum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers surrounded by a series of white to pink ray florets. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a grayish-green hue.

The botanical features of Osteospermum calendulaceum make it a popular choice for garden cultivation due to its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
