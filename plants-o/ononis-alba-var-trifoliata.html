Ononis alba var. trifoliata

Ononis alba var. trifoliata, commonly known as white kidney vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

This species is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
