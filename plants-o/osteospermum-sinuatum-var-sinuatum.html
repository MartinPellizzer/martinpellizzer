Osteospermum sinuatum var. sinuatum

Osteospermum sinuatum var. sinuatum, commonly known as the Blue Frost or Blue Osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has a bushy growth habit with gray-green, fuzzy leaves that are slightly hairy to the touch. Its flowers consist of a central yellow disk surrounded by blue or violet ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
