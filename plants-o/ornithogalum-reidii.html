Ornithogalum reidii

Ornithogalum reidii, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a bulbous base with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant typically blooms in spring and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Ornithogalum reidii is also known for its ability to thrive in coastal environments.
