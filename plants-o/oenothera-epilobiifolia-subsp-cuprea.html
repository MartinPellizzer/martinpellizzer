Oenothera epilobiifolia subsp. cuprea

Oenothera epilobiifolia subsp.

cuprea, commonly known as the copperleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is characterized by its striking copper-colored foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening.

The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its stems are sturdy and slightly hairy.
