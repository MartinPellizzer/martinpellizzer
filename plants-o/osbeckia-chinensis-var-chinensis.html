Osbeckia chinensis var. chinensis

Osbeckia chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ecological value, providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a host plant for certain insect species.
