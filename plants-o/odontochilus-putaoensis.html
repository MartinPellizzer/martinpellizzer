Odontochilus putaoensis

Odontochilus putaoensis, commonly known as the Putao orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the northeastern regions of India and Myanmar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous forest areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
