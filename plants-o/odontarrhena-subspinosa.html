Odontarrhena subspinosa

Odontarrhena subspinosa, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Odontarrhena subspinosa is adapted to withstand drought conditions and has a shallow root system to maximize water absorption.
