Oenocarpus distichus

Oenocarpus distichus, commonly known as the breadnut tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Arecaceae, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The breadnut tree is valued for its nutritional content and has been used by indigenous communities for centuries.
