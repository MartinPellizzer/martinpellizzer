Olyra floribunda

Olyra floribunda, commonly known as the clustered flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright orange to red flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a slightly aromatic scent.

Olyra floribunda thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
