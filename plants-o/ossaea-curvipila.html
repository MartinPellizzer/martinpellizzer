Ossaea curvipila

Ossaea curvipila, commonly known as the slender lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom in late summer. Ossaea curvipila thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
