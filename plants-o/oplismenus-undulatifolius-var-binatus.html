Oplismenus undulatifolius var. binatus

Oplismenus undulatifolius var. binatus, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, three-petaled white flowers.

This species is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various wetland conditions.
