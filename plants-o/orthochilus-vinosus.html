Orthochilus vinosus

Orthochilus vinosus, commonly known as the vinous orthochilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive violet hue, contributing to its common name.

Orthochilus vinosus is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forest understories.
