Oryzopsis neesii

Oryzopsis neesii, commonly known as the slender false rice, is a grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist soils. Its flowering heads are small and spike-like, containing numerous tiny florets.

Oryzopsis neesii is often found in wetlands across North and South America, playing a role in local ecosystems.
