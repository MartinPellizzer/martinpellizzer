Orthoceras strictum f. viride

Orthoceras strictum f. viride, commonly known as the green-stemmed orthoceras, is a variant of the orthoceras plant characterized by its distinctively green stems.

This plant is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. It is a herbaceous perennial with narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. The flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and a subject of interest for plant enthusiasts.
