Orchis longicornu proles balearica

Orchis longicornu proles balearica, commonly known as the Balearic longhorn orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the Balearic Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a deep violet or purple hue with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in specific microhabitats, such as calcareous soils in open, sunny areas. Its floral structure includes a distinctive long, slender column, which is a key feature in orchid taxonomy.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
