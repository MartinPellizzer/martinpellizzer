Oncidium boliviense

Oncidium boliviense, commonly known as the Bolivian orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium boliviense is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
