Opuntia microdasys var. laevior

Opuntia microdasys var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth spineless prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to South Africa.

It features flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in short, pale spines that are less prominent than those of other Opuntia species. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, followed by edible, red-fleshed fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy pads.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food and traditional medicine.
