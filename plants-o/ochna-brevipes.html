Ochna brevipes

Ochna brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed ochna, is a small, deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 5 meters and has a dense, rounded crown. The plant features smooth, grayish-brown bark and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochna brevipes is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
