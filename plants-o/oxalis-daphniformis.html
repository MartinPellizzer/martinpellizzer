Oxalis daphniformis

Oxalis daphniformis, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silver-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, Oxalis daphniformis is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
