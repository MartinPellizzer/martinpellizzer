Olearia alpicola

Olearia alpicola, commonly known as the alpine daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features silver-gray foliage and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine regions with cool, moist conditions.

Olearia alpicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
