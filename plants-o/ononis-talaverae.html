Ononis talaverae

Ononis talaverae, commonly known as Talavera's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in dry, sunny environments. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and basal leaves.
