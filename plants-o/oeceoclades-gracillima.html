Oeceoclades gracillima

Oeceoclades gracillima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
