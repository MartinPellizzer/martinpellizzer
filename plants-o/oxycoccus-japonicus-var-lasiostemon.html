Oxycoccus japonicus var. lasiostemon

Oxycoccus japonicus var. lasiostemon, commonly known as the Japanese snowberry, is a low-growing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and often used in traditional cuisine. Its leaves are elliptical and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forested areas.
