Osmanthus rigidus

Osmanthus rigidus, commonly known as the rigid osmanthus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a member of the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers bloom in late autumn, producing a sweet, honey-like scent that attracts pollinators.

Osmanthus rigidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
