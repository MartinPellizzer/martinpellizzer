Orthothylax

Orthothylax, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Orthothylax species typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, meaning they attach themselves to trees or rocks for support rather than growing in soil.
