Ouratea flexuosa

Ouratea flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
