Odontorchis conica

Odontorchis conica, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
