Olax candida

Olax candida, commonly known as the white-flowered olax, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Olax candida is often found in woodland and savanna habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
