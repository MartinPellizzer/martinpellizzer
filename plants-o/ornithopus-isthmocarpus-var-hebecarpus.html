Ornithopus isthmocarpus var. hebecarpus

Ornithopus isthmocarpus var. hebecarpus, commonly known as the narrow-carpel sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and northern Baja California.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline, where it tolerates salt spray and strong winds. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.
