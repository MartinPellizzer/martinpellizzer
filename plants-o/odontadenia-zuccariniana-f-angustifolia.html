Odontadenia zuccariniana f. angustifolia

Odontadenia zuccariniana f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed false dandelion, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to its harsh environment.
