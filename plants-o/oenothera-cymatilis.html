Oenothera cymatilis

Oenothera cymatilis, commonly known as the sticky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and open areas.

Oenothera cymatilis is also known for its sticky resinous secretions, which give it its common name.
