Oncidium blanchetii

Oncidium blanchetii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments.

Oncidium blanchetii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
