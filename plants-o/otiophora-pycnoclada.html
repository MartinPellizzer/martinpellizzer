Otiophora pycnoclada

Otiophora pycnoclada, commonly known as the dense-leaved orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
