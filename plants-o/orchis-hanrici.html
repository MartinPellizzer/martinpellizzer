Orchis hanrici

Orchis hanrici, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of purple or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in woodland or meadow habitats. Its flowers are bilaterally symmetrical, with a distinctive lip that serves as a landing platform for pollinators.

Orchis hanrici is a perennial herb that blooms in late spring to early summer.
