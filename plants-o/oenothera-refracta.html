Oenothera refracta

Oenothera refracta, commonly known as the brittlestem evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.
