Onoseris corymbosa

Onoseris corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
