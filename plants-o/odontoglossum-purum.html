Odontoglossum purum

Odontoglossum purum, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Odontoglossum purum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
