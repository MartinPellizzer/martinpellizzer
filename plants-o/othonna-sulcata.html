Othonna sulcata

Othonna sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ribbed, and often zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Othonna sulcata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
