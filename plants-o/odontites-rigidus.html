Odontites rigidus

Odontites rigidus, commonly known as rigid toothgrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous grasslands and rocky slopes, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Odontites rigidus is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh alpine environments.
