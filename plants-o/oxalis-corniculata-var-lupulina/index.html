Oxalis Corniculata Var. Lupulina

Oxalis corniculata var. lupulina is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species that thrives in a variety of environments. Known for its unique leaf structure and vibrant flowers, it has captured the interest of botanists and gardeners alike. This variant of Oxalis is often mistaken for other similar species due to its striking appearance. It is native to parts of Asia and has been introduced to various regions around the world. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain areas, making it both a prized and potentially problematic plant.
