Orchis × csatoi

Orchis × csatoi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural cross between different orchid species.

It exhibits a range of morphological features, including tall, erect stems and showy, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are usually arranged in a spike and may have a distinctive lip or labellum.

Orchis × csatoi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
