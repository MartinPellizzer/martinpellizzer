Ottelia thorelii

Ottelia thorelii, commonly known as water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features rosettes of broad, waxy, spoon-shaped leaves that provide buoyancy and shade for aquatic life. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Ottelia thorelii thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It is often used in ornamental ponds and wetland restoration projects due to its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
