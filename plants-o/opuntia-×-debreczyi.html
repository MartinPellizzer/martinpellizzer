Opuntia × debreczyi

Opuntia × debreczyi, commonly known as the Hungarian prickly pear, is a hybrid cactus species native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its fruit, known as a tun, is edible and contains small, sweet seeds.

Opuntia × debreczyi is adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
