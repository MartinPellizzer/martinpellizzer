Onopordum rhodense

Onopordum rhodense, commonly known as the Rhodense thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, purple-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, silvery-tomentose leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Onopordum rhodense is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other thistle species.
