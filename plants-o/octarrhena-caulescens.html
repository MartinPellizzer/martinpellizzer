Octarrhena caulescens

Octarrhena caulescens, commonly known as the slender-stemmed caryophyllaceae, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, often with a slightly succulent texture.

Octarrhena caulescens is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
