Onosma echinidea

Onosma echinidea, commonly known as the spiny globe-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny, spherical leaves and tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Onosma echinidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
