Ormosia palembanica

Ormosia palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and traditional uses.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
