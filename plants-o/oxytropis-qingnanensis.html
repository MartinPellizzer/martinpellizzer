Oxytropis qingnanensis

Oxytropis qingnanensis, commonly known as the Guangxi milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Guangxi region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers.
