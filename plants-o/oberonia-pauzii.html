Oberonia pauzii

Oberonia pauzii, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. Oberonia pauzii thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland forests.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
