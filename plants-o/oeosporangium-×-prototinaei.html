Oeosporangium × prototinaei

Oeosporangium × prototinaei, commonly known as the hybrid spore-bearing plant, is a rare botanical hybrid that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species.

It belongs to the group of plants known for their unique reproductive structures, specifically the presence of specialized spore-producing organs called sporangia. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, where it can effectively carry out its reproductive cycle. Its sporangia are often clustered in dense formations, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is of particular interest to botanists studying plant evolution and hybridization.
