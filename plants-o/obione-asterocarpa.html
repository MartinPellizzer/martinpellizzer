Obione asterocarpa

Obione asterocarpa, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits, which are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is edible, with a sweet and tangy flavor, and is often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.

The tree is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its nutritional benefits.
