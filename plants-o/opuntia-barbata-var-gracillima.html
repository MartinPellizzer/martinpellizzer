Opuntia barbata var. gracillima

Opuntia barbata var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender spineless prickly pear, is a low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features flat, fleshy stems that are typically green and covered with small, soft spines called glochids. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunas, is edible and often used in culinary applications.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
