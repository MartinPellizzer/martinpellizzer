Oenanthe japonica

Oenanthe japonica, commonly known as Japanese water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, hollow stems and small, arrow-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are greenish-white and clustered in umbels, which are characteristic of the Apiaceae family.

Oenanthe japonica is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
