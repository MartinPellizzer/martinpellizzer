Ornithidium fulgens

Ornithidium fulgens, commonly known as the golden feather fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that display a vibrant golden hue. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and relies on its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from its host. Ornithidium fulgens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aesthetic contribution to indoor and outdoor green spaces.
