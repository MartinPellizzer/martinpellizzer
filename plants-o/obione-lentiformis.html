It is characterized by its flattened, ribbon-like thallus that forms a lens-shaped structure, typically less than 1 cm in width. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Obione lentiformis is often found in temperate regions and plays a minor role in soil stabilization and moisture retention.
