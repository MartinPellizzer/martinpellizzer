Orchis × tochniana

Orchis × tochniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Orchis morio and Orchis maculata.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers. The flowers are typically purple or violet with intricate patterns, and they bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its reproductive strategy involves insect pollination, and it is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
