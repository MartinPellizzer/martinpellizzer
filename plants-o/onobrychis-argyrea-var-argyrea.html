Onobrychis argyrea var. argyrea

Onobrychis argyrea var. argyrea, commonly known as silver vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-white foliage and trifoliate leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Silver vetch is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.
