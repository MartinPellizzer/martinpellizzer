Osteospermum incanum subsp. subcanescens

Osteospermum incanum subsp.

subcanescens, commonly known as the silver osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers with white petals.

The plant produces a distinctive, fluffy seed head that becomes prominent after flowering. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
