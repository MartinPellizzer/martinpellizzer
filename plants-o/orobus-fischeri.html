Orobus fischeri

Orobus fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's orobrush, is a small, flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Orobus fischeri is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
