Oldenlandia purpurea var. ciliolata

Oldenlandia purpurea var. ciliolata, commonly known as the purple false musk vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers, which are borne in clusters along the stems. The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
