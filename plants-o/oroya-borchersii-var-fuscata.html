Oroya borchersii var. fuscata

Oroya borchersii var. fuscata, commonly known as the dark oroya, is a variant of the oroya plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the Andes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a dark green to almost black coloration, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of purple or violet.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific humidity and light requirements.
