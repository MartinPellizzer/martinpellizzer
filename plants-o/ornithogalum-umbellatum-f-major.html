Ornithogalum umbellatum f. major

Ornithogalum umbellatum f. major, commonly known as the giant star-of-Bethlehem, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of white, star-shaped flowers, giving it its common name. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces large, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is noted for its showy blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
