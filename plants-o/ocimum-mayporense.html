Ocimum mayporense

Ocimum mayporense, commonly known as Mayporen basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique fragrance and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
