Ophrys × Delilloi

Ophrys × delilloi is a rare hybrid orchid that captivates with its intricate floral structure and unique mimicry. This plant is the result of crossbreeding between two species of Ophrys, known for their ability to resemble female insects. Its blossoms are designed to attract male wasps through a combination of color, scent, and shape. Found in specific regions of Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, Ophrys × delilloi is a subject of both botanical interest and conservation concern.
