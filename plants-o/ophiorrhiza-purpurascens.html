Ophiorrhiza purpurascens

Ophiorrhiza purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils, while its leaves are elliptical and glossy.

Ophiorrhiza purpurascens plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and supporting biodiversity in its habitat.
