Oberonia crassiuscula

Oberonia crassiuscula, commonly known as the thick-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with rich, well-drained soil.

Oberonia crassiuscula is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
