Odotalon lanceolata

Odotalon lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf odotalon, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Odotalon lanceolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
