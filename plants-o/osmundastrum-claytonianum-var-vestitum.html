Osmundastrum claytonianum var. vestitum

Osmundastrum claytonianum var. vestitum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique fern species characterized by its dense, moss-like appearance.

It features wiry, dark green fronds that are covered in a fine, velvety layer of trichomes, giving it a soft texture. This plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in arid, rocky environments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

The velvet fern is known for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient and distinctive member of the Osmunda genus.
