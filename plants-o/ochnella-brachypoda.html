Ochnella brachypoda

Ochnella brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed ochna, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Ochnella brachypoda is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
