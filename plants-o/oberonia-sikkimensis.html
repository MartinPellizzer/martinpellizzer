Oberonia sikkimensis

Oberonia sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim Oberonia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks in montane forests. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, typically featuring dark purple or brownish sepals and petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light conditions for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
