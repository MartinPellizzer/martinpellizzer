Oldenlandia proschii

Oldenlandia proschii, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces tiny, three-lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Oldenlandia proschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
