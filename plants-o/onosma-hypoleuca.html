Onosma hypoleuca

Onosma hypoleuca, commonly known as the white skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers clustered in loose racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
