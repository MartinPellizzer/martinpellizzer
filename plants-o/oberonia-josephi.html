Oberonia josephi

Oberonia josephi, commonly known as the Joseph's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically features a short rhizome with a few to several pseudobulbs, each bearing a single leaf. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Oberonia josephi is often found in shaded, moist forest environments, thriving in the understory of tropical rainforests.
