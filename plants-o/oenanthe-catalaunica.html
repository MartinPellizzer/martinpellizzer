Oenanthe catalaunica

Oenanthe catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalaunian hemlock, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters that attract pollinators like bees and flies.
