Oxytropis owerinii

Oxytropis owerinii, commonly known as the desert milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or pale purple, which bloom in early summer.
