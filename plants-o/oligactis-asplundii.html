Oligactis asplundii

Oligactis asplundii, commonly known as the asplundii dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow composite flowers. Oligactis asplundii is adapted to withstand drought conditions, with a deep root system that helps it survive in harsh, dry climates.
