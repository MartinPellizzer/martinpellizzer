Osteomeles resinosopunctata

Osteomeles resinosopunctata, commonly known as the resinous dot balm, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaves, which have a waxy texture and are often dotted with small, resinous glands. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Osteomeles resinosopunctata is valued both for its ecological role and its potential medicinal properties.
