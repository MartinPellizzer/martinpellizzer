Orinoquia yanomamica

Orinoquia yanomamica, commonly known as the Yanomami orchid, is a rare and endangered flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, colorful blooms that typically appear in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Its unique floral structure and pollination mechanisms make it an important subject of botanical study.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, conservation efforts are critical for its survival.
