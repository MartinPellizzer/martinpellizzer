Oenothera chamissonis

Oenothera chamissonis, commonly known as the coastal evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes or along shorelines.

Oenothera chamissonis is adapted to coastal environments with high salinity and strong winds.
