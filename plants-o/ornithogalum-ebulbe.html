Ornithogalum ebulbe

Ornithogalum ebulbe, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white, star-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its succulent foliage and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.

Ornithogalum ebulbe is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its historical and cultural significance.
