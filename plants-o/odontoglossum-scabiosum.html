Odontoglossum scabiosum

Odontoglossum scabiosum, commonly known as the Scabious orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its terrestrial growth habit, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Odontoglossum scabiosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
