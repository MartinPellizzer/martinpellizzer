Opuntia sphaerica var. unguispina

Opuntia sphaerica var. unguispina, commonly known as the black spine prickly pear, is a distinctive cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globose, flattened stems that are covered in dense, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its spines are typically black or dark purple, giving it its common name.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
