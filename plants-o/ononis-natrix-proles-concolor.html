Ononis natrix proles concolor

Ononis natrix proles concolor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.
