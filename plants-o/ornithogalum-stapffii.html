Ornithogalum stapffii

Ornithogalum stapffii, commonly known as the Stapf's star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum stapffii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
