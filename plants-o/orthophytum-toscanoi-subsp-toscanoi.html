Orthophytum toscanoi subsp. toscanoi

Orthophytum toscanoi subsp.

toscanoi, commonly known as the Toscana orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically produces inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and ease of care.
