Ouratea thyrsoidea

Ouratea thyrsoidea, commonly known as the red-flowered ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking, showy red flowers that bloom in terminal clusters. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, grayish bark and produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
