Ornithocephalus dunstervillei

Ornithocephalus dunstervillei, commonly known as the African orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude grasslands of southern Africa, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy orchid-like flower that blooms in spring. Its unique structure includes a specialized inflorescence with a distinctive, bird-like appearance, giving it its scientific name.
