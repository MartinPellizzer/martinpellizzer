Oxalis eckloniana var. montigena

Oxalis eckloniana var. montigena, commonly known as the mountain oxalis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple veining. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
