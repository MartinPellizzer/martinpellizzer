Oncidium guatemalense

Oncidium guatemalense, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.

Oncidium guatemalense requires well-draining soil and consistent watering to flourish.
