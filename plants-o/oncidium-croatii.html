Oncidium croatii

Oncidium croatii, commonly known as the Croatian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Balkan region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with red or purple spots.

Oncidium croatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
