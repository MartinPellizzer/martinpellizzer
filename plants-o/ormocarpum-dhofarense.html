Ormocarpum dhofarense

Ormocarpum dhofarense, commonly known as the Dhofar orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its distinctive orchid-like flowers, which are typically pink or purple in color. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique morphology, including its narrow, strap-shaped leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky substrates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
