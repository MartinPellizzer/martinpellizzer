Otanthera luzoniensis

Otanthera luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon aster, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of montane forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Otanthera luzoniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
