Othonna trinervia

Othonna trinervia, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Othonna trinervia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
