Oporinia autumnalis var. dentata

Oporinia autumnalis var. dentata, commonly known as the autumn fern, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that emerge in the fall and persist through winter. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are typically finely divided and have a soft, velvety texture.

Oporinia autumnalis var. dentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide year-round interest in gardens.
