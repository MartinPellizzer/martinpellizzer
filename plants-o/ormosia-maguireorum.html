Ormosia maguireorum

Ormosia maguireorum, commonly known as the Maguire's ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Ormosia maguireorum is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
