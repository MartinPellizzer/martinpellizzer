Orthosiphon wulfenioides var. wulfenioides

Orthosiphon wulfenioides var. wulfenioides, commonly known as Java tea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Orthosiphon wulfenioides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
