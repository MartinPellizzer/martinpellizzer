Oreorchis subcapitata

Oreorchis subcapitata, commonly known as the small-headed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in woodland and grassland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its flowers are characterized by a short, narrow labellum and a column that is distinctly divided.

Oreorchis subcapitata is a perennial species that blooms in late spring to early summer.
