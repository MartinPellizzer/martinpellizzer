Olea Laurifolia Var. Concolor

Olea laurifolia var. concolor is a unique species of evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California. It is known for its dense, dark green foliage and aromatic leaves that emit a subtle, pleasant scent. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its adaptability to harsh coastal conditions has earned it a place in both natural and cultivated landscapes. Despite its hardiness, it requires careful attention to irrigation to prevent root rot and ensure optimal growth.
