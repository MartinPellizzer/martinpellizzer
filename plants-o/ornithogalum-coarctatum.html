Ornithogalum coarctatum

Ornithogalum coarctatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous rootstock and produces upright, grass-like leaves that are narrow and slightly folded. The plant blooms with clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum coarctatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
