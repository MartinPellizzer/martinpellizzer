Oreocharis aurea var. cordato-ovata

Oreocharis aurea var. cordato-ovata, commonly known as the heart-shaped oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
