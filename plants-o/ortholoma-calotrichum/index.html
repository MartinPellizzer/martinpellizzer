Ortholoma Calotrichum

Ortholoma calotrichum is a rare and striking plant native to the coastal regions of California. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This species is often found in sandy soils along the ocean shoreline. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
