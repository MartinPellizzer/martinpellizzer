Onobrychis nikitinii

Onobrychis nikitinii, commonly known as Nikitin's burclover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Nikitin's burclover is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
