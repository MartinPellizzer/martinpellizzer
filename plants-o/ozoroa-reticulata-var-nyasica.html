Ozoroa reticulata var. nyasica

Ozoroa reticulata var. nyasica, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its pinnate leaves and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, hard, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in dry or semi-arid environments.
