Ochna pumila

Ochna pumila, commonly known as the dwarf ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ochna pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal conditions.
