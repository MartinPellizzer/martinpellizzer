Oldenlandia herbacea var. dolichosepala

Oldenlandia herbacea var. dolichosepala, commonly known as the slender-stalked spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale blue. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
