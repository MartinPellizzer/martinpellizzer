Oscularia ornata

Oscularia ornata, commonly known as the silver rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Oscularia ornata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
