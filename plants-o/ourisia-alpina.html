Ourisia alpina

Ourisia alpina, commonly known as the alpine ourisia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens with suitable conditions.
