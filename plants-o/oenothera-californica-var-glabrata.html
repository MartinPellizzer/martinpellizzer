Oenothera californica var. glabrata

Oenothera californica var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and grassland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
