Ozoroa reticulata subsp. foveolata

Ozoroa reticulata subsp.

foveolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ozoroa, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves that are often densely arranged along the stems.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats. Ozoroa reticulata subsp.

foveolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and foliage.
