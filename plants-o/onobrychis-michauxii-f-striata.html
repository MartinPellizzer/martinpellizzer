Onobrychis michauxii f. striata

Onobrychis michauxii f. striata, commonly known as the striped burr medic, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with a distinct white or yellow stripe running along the central vein. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in dense, globular inflorescences, followed by elongated, spiny pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in soil stabilization and forage production.
