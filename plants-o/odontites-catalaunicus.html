Odontites catalaunicus

Odontites catalaunicus, commonly known as the Catalonian toothed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions. It has a clump-forming habit and can reach a height of up to 30 centimeters.
