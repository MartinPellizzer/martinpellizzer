Origanum × adonidis

Origanum × adonidis, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a hybrid perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are often used in cooking and herbal remedies due to their distinctive fragrance and medicinal properties.

Origanum × adonidis is native to the Mediterranean region and is widely cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
