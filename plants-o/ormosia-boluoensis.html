Ormosia boluoensis

Ormosia boluoensis, commonly known as the Boluo ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its leaves are compound, with pinnate arrangements, and it produces small, clustered flowers.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
