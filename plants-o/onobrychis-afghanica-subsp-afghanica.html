Onobrychis afghanica subsp. afghanica

Onobrychis afghanica subsp.

afghanica, commonly known as Afghan goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

This subspecies is valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
