Oenothera serrulata var. oblanceolata

Oenothera serrulata var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
