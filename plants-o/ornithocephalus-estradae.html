Ornithocephalus estradae

Ornithocephalus estradae, commonly known as the Estrada's orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flower spikes and vibrant, colorful blooms that range from purple to pink. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocky surfaces, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are highly specialized, with intricate structures that facilitate pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, Ornithocephalus estradae is considered a vulnerable species.
