Ogiera triplinervis var. leiocarpa

Ogiera triplinervis var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-seeded ogiera, is a variant of the ogiera plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each leaflet having a fine, tri-nerved venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elliptical, smooth-seeded fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it distinct from other varieties within the Ogiera genus.
