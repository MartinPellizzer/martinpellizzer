Ornithogalum minimum var. spathaceum

Ornithogalum minimum var. spathaceum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum minimum var. spathaceum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
