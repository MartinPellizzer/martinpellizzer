Otanthera nicobarensis

Otanthera nicobarensis, commonly known as the Nicobar dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features basal leaves that are often hairy and have a broad, heart-shaped outline. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in a flat, disc-like structure typical of the dandelion genus.

Otanthera nicobarensis is adapted to coastal and sandy environments, showcasing resilience to saline conditions.
