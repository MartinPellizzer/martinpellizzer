Ocimum basilicum var. viridicrispum

Ocimum basilicum var. viridicrispum, commonly known as Creeping Basil, is a compact, low-growing variety of basil characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and vibrant green color.

It produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a decorative touch to gardens. This variety is highly aromatic, with a strong basil scent, making it popular for culinary use. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering to maintain its lush foliage.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for container gardening and ground cover in ornamental landscapes.
