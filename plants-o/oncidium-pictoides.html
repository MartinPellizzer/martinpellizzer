Oncidium pictoides

Oncidium pictoides, commonly known as the Painted Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, colorful flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings, resembling painted patterns. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium pictoides is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
