Oenothera numismatica

Oenothera numismatica, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its unique seed structure, which consists of a single, elongated seed enclosed in a papery capsule. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
