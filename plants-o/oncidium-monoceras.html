Oncidium monoceras

Oncidium monoceras, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive, butterfly-like shape. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms throughout the year. Oncidium monoceras thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
