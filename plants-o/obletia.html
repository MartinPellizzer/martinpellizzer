Obletia

Obletia, commonly known as the false hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are shrubs or small trees native to tropical and subtropical regions. Obletia species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant floral displays.
