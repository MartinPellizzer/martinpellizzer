Osteomeles ferruginea

Osteomeles ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty osteomele, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a reddish tinge. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.

Osteomeles ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
