Ononis tribracteata

Ononis tribracteata, commonly known as the three-bract ononis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Ononis tribracteata is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.
