Onychium Japonicum Subsp. Moupinense

Onychium japonicum subsp. moupinense is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its striking, velvety leaves and small, showy flowers. This subspecies is particularly valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited natural habitat and environmental threats, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
