Oncidium × monacranthum

Oncidium × monacranthum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers, which resemble the wings of a butterfly. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Oncidium × monacranthum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
