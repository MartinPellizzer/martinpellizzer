Oeoniella polystachys

Oeoniella polystachys, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow.
