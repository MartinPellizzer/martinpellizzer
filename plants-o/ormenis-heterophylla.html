Ormenis heterophylla

Ormenis heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf ormenis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves that can be either simple or compound depending on the environment. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Ormenis heterophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
