Oxalis virginea

Oxalis virginea, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a silvery sheen, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis virginea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
