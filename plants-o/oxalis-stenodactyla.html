Oxalis stenodactyla

Oxalis stenodactyla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three heart-shaped leaflets, and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Oxalis stenodactyla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
