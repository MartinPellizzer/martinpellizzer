Oncostemum macrophyllum

Oncostemum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf oncostemum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles, which are often borne on long, slender stalks. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded habitats.
