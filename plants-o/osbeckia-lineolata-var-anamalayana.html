Osbeckia lineolata var. anamalayana

Osbeckia lineolata var. anamalayana, commonly known as the Malabar osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces, and its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in racemes. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
