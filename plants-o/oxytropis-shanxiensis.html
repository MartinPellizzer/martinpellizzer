Oxytropis shanxiensis

Oxytropis shanxiensis, commonly known as Shanxi yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically found in the Shanxi province.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Oxytropis shanxiensis is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and plays a role in preventing soil erosion in its native environment.
