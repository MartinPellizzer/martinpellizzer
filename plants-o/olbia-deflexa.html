Olbia deflexa

Olbia deflexa, commonly known as the flexible olbia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems that often lie along the ground, forming a dense mat. Its leaves are elongated, slightly curled, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

The small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the late spring to early summer season.
