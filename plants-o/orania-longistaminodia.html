Orania longistaminodia

Orania longistaminodia, commonly known as the long-stamen orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated stamens that often extend beyond the petals. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
