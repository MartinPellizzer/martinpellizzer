Olearia virgata var. laxiflora

Olearia virgata var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender flowered snow bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. It has a low-growing, shrub-like habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural habitat.
