Ornithogalum ensifolium

Ornithogalum ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum ensifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
