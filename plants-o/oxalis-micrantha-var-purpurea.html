Oxalis micrantha var. purpurea

Oxalis micrantha var. purpurea, commonly known as purple wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple tinge, and small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shady garden settings.

Oxalis micrantha var. purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
