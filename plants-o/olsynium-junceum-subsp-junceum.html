Olsynium junceum subsp. junceum

Olsynium junceum subsp.

junceum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved olsynium, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in coastal areas and cliffs.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in dense spikes. It is adapted to withstand drought and salty air, making it a hardy species.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to harsh, arid environments.
