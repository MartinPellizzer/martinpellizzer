Oleandra benguetensis

Oleandra benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet oleander, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in open areas or along roadsides.

Oleandra benguetensis is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
