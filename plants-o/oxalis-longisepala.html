Oxalis longisepala

Oxalis longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepal oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or forest understories.

Oxalis longisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
