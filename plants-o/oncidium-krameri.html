Oncidium krameri

Oncidium krameri, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces fragrant blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Oncidium krameri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
