Olearia gunniana var. phlogopappa

Olearia gunniana var. phlogopappa, commonly known as the golden dandelion bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are borne in clustered heads. It is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
