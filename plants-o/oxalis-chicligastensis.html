Oxalis chicligastensis

Oxalis chicligastensis, commonly known as the red oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers. The plant produces edible tubers that are often used in traditional cuisine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Oxalis chicligastensis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties in some regions.
