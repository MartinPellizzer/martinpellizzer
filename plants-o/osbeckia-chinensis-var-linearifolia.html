Osbeckia chinensis var. linearifolia

Osbeckia chinensis var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved osbeckia, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that often branch near the base, and its leaves are elongated and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Osbeckia chinensis var. linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
