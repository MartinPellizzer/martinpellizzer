Oberonia japonica f. viridescens

Oberonia japonica f. viridescens, commonly known as the Emerald-stem Oberonia, is a rare and striking orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dark green, almost black, foliage that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This cultivar is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and rarity.
