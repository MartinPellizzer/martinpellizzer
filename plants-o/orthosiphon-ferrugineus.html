Orthosiphon ferrugineus

Orthosiphon ferrugineus, commonly known as Java tea or *Plectranthus ferrugineus*, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and fragrant scent. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Orthosiphon ferrugineus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
