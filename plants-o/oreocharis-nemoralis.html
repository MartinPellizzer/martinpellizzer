Oreocharis nemoralis

Oreocharis nemoralis, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates with distinct seasonal changes.

Due to its unique floral structure and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
