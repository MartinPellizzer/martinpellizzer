Ornithogalum breviscapum

Ornithogalum breviscapum, commonly known as the short-capped star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Ornithogalum breviscapum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
