Oenothera runcinata

Oenothera runcinata, commonly known as the smooth evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Oenothera runcinata is often found in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is a member of the Onagraceae family and is known for its nocturnal blooming habit.
