Oliganthemum filifolium

Oliganthemum filifolium, commonly known as the threadleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and thread-like, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
