Odontoglossum Sanguineum

Odontoglossum sanguineum is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. Known for its vibrant red and purple flowers, it captivates both botanists and gardeners alike. This species thrives in cool, humid environments with bright indirect light. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts. Proper care is essential to ensure its survival in cultivation.
