Oliveriana egregia

Oliveriana egregia, commonly known as the golden star plant, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and resemble small stars. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Oliveriana egregia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
