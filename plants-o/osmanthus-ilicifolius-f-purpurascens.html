Osmanthus ilicifolius f. purpurascens

Osmanthus ilicifolius f. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leaf osmanthus, is a cultivar of the osmanthus plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking purplish foliage, which provides a unique ornamental value in gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in the fall, which emit a sweet, honey-like scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for temperate climates.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and aromatic properties.
