Ononis hircina var. spinescens

Ononis hircina var. spinescens, commonly known as the spiny restharrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branching stems covered with stiff, spinose hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a rough texture, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in rock gardens or as a ground cover in dry environments.
