Oxalis dillenii subsp. filipes

Oxalis dillenii subsp.

filipes, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow or orange.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments. It is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable ornamental plant in gardens with well-drained soil.
