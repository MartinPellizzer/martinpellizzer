Orthochilus schaijesii

Orthochilus schaijesii, commonly known as the Schaijes' orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, preferring cool and humid conditions. Its flowering period occurs during the cooler months, and it is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
