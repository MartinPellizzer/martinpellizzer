Otholobium pubescens

Otholobium pubescens, commonly known as the silky burr bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in fynbos vegetation. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by hairy, spiny pods that contain seeds. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Otholobium pubescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in restoration projects due to its adaptability to harsh environments.
