Ormosia bopiensis

Ormosia bopiensis, commonly known as the Bopiensis Tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Ormosia bopiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
