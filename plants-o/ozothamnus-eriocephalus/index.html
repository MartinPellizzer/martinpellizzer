Ozothamnus Eriocephalus

Ozothamnus eriocephalus is a hardy shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its ability to withstand salt spray and strong winds makes it ideal for seaside landscapes. Ozothamnus eriocephalus is not only ornamental but also supports local wildlife, contributing to ecological balance.
