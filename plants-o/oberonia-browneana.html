Oberonia browneana

Oberonia browneana, commonly known as the Brown's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with yellow or brown markings. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Oberonia browneana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches in the canopy of tropical forests.
