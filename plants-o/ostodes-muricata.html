Ostodes muricata

Ostodes muricata, commonly known as the spiny ostode, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in small, sharp spines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Ostodes muricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
