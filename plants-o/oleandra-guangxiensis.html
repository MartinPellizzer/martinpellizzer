Oleandra guangxiensis

Oleandra guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi oleander, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks.

Oleandra guangxiensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
