Osmelia

Osmelia, commonly known as the nightshade plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, often bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant produces berries that contain toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals. Osmelia thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological study and horticultural research.
