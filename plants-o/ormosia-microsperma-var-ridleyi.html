Ormosia microsperma var. ridleyi

Ormosia microsperma var. ridleyi, commonly known as the ridley's ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces hard, woody pods that contain small, elliptical seeds. It is often found in lowland evergreen forests and is valued for its durable timber.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
