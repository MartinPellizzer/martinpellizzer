Oxyanthus molliramis

Oxyanthus molliramis, commonly known as the velvetleaf balsam, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Oxyanthus molliramis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
