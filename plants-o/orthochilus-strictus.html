Orthochilus strictus

Orthochilus strictus, commonly known as the narrowleaf orthochilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. This herbaceous plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, where it thrives in humid and cool conditions. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a smooth surface, and it produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
