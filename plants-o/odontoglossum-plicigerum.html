Odontoglossum plicigerum

Odontoglossum plicigerum, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns in shades of white and purple. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Odontoglossum plicigerum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and delicate beauty.
