Othonna speciosa

Othonna speciosa, commonly known as the showy othonna, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Othonna speciosa is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
