Osteospermum rigidum var. elegans

Osteospermum rigidum var. elegans, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features gray-green, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, composite flower heads with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in various climates.
