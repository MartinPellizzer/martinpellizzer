Oryza sativa var. ceylonica

Oryza sativa var. ceylonica, commonly known as the Ceylon rice, is a botanical variant of rice native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated grains and a distinctively fragrant aroma. The plant typically grows in tropical climates with high humidity and abundant rainfall. Its botanical features include a robust root system and panicle-like inflorescence.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and unique culinary properties.
