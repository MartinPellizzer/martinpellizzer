Oncidium turialbae

Oncidium turialbae, commonly known as the Turialba orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters.

Oncidium turialbae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
