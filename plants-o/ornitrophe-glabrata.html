Ornitrophe glabrata

Ornitrophe glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-barked orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, bark-like stems and striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the rainy season. Ornitrophe glabrata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
