Ophiopogon szechuanensis

Ophiopogon szechuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan spathiphyllum, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate regions.
