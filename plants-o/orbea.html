Orbea

Orbea, commonly known as the ice plant or snow plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae.

These plants are native to southern Africa and are characterized by their succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, often brightly colored flowers. Orbea species typically have a compact growth habit and are adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. They produce small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

The genus includes over 100 species, many of which are popular among succulent enthusiasts for their ornamental value.
