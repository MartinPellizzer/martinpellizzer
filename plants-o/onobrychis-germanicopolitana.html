Onobrychis germanicopolitana

Onobrychis germanicopolitana, commonly known as the Germanicopolitan burclover, is a perennial legume native to southeastern Europe.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spikes. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several rounded seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it ecologically significant in soil enrichment.
