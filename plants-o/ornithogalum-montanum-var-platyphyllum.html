Ornithogalum montanum var. platyphyllum

Ornithogalum montanum var. platyphyllum, commonly known as the broadleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a bulbous root system that stores nutrients for the growing season. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow star-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
