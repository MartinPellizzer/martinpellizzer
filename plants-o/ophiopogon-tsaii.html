Ophiopogon tsaii

Ophiopogon tsaii, commonly known as the T'ai mountain spathiphyllum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.
