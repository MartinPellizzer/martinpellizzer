Ononis variegata f. erioclada

Ononis variegata f. erioclada, commonly known as the variegated whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves, which often display a mix of green and white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale pink or white, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in sunny, dry conditions.
