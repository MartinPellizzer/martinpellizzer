Onobrychis saxatilis var. allionii

Onobrychis saxatilis var. allionii, commonly known as the alpine burr vetch, is a perennial legume native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh alpine environments. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are edible and have been traditionally used in various culinary and medicinal contexts.
