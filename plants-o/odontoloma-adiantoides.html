Odontoloma adiantoides

Odontoloma adiantoides, commonly known as the fern-like polypody, is a member of the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are divided into smaller, tooth-like segments, giving it its scientific name. It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Indian subcontinent.

Odontoloma adiantoides is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid forests.
