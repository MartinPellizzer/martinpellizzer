Onobrychis cornuta var. alba

Onobrychis cornuta var. alba, commonly known as white crowned vetch, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and trifoliate leaves with small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, curved pods that contain several seeds. It is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a valuable plant in both ornamental and agricultural settings.
