Ornithogalum Tenuifolium Var. Trichophyllum

Ornithogalum tenuifolium var. trichophyllum is a unique variant of the common star of Bethlehem plant. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. This plant is known for its striking white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, silky hair that gives it its name. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has cultural significance in some regions.
