Orchis euxina

Orchis euxina, commonly known as the red helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, adorned with lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple veins, blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Orchis euxina is notable for its ability to form dense colonies and is often found in nutrient-rich soils.
