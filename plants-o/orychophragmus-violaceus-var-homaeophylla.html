Orychophragmus violaceus var. homaeophylla

Orychophragmus violaceus var. homaeophylla, commonly known as the violet camellia, is a variant of the camellia species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is a shrub with dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown for its beautiful, long-lasting flowers.
