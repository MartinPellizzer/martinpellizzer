Orostachys japonica f. polycephala

Orostachys japonica f. polycephala, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. Native to Japan, it thrives in rocky or sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
