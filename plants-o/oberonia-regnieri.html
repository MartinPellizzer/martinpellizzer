Oberonia regnieri

Oberonia regnieri, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
