Oryzopsis purpurascens

Oryzopsis purpurascens, commonly known as the purple grass, is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that often display a bluish-green or purplish hue. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Oryzopsis purpurascens is adapted to drought conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
