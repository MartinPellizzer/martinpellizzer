Ornithidium hagsaterianum

Ornithidium hagsaterianum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique morphology includes elongated leaves with a waxy texture and clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
