Odontarrhena constellata

Odontarrhena constellata, commonly known as the star-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
