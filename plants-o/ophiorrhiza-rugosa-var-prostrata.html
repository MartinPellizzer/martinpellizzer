Ophiorrhiza rugosa var. prostrata

Ophiorrhiza rugosa var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate rough ophiorrhiza, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its prostrate or decumbent stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough or pubescent texture.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks.
