Osmanthus ilicifolius var. variegatus

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in the autumn, which are highly valued for their aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a visually appealing addition to any landscape.
