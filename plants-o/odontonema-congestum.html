Odontonema congestum

Odontonema congestum, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
