Odontites frutescens

Odontites frutescens, commonly known as the toothed bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has finely toothed leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is typically found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Odontites frutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
