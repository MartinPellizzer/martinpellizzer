Onopordum lanigerum

Onopordum lanigerum, commonly known as the woolly milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a taproot system and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Onopordum lanigerum is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
