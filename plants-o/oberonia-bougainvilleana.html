Oberonia bougainvilleana

Oberonia bougainvilleana, commonly known as the Bougainville orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and several fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink to purple bracts that enclose small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
