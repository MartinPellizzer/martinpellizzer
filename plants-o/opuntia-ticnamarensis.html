Opuntia ticnamarensis

Opuntia ticnamarensis, commonly known as the Ticnamarensis prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems known as cladodes. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers, which are typically followed by edible, fleshy fruits. Opuntia ticnamarensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing typical succulent features to store water.

Its structure and growth habits make it well-suited for survival in harsh, dry climates.
