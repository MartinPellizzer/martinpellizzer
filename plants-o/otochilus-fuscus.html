Otochilus fuscus

Otochilus fuscus, commonly known as the dark leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dark green to almost black foliage and produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Otochilus fuscus is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique leaf coloration and compact growth habit.
