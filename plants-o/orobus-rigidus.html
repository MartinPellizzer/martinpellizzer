Orobus rigidus

Orobus rigidus, commonly known as the rigid orobus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in the spring.

Its rigid stems and drought-resistant nature make it a resilient species in its native habitat.
