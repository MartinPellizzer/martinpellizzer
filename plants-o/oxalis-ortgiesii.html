Oxalis ortgiesii

Oxalis ortgiesii, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.

Oxalis ortgiesii is also known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a resilient addition to gardens.
