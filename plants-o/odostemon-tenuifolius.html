Odostemon tenuifolius

Odostemon tenuifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved odostemon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-white flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Odostemon tenuifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and floral display.
