Oxalis pseudocernua

Oxalis pseudocernua, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a silvery sheen, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, typically in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis pseudocernua is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
