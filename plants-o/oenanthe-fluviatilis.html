Oenanthe fluviatilis

Oenanthe fluviatilis, commonly known as the river bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils along riverbanks and wet meadows. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Oenanthe fluviatilis is known for its delicate appearance and its role in supporting local insect populations.
