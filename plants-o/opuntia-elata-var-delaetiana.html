Opuntia elata var. delaetiana

Opuntia elata var. delaetiana, commonly known as the Delaetian prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to the Canary Islands.

It features flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in clusters of small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
