Oncidium sphacelatum var. minus

Oncidium sphacelatum var. minus, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
