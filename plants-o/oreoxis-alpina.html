Oreoxis alpina

Oreoxis alpina, commonly known as the alpine oreodoxa, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Oreoxis alpina has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
