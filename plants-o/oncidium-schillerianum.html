Oncidium schillerianum

Oncidium schillerianum, commonly known as the Schiller's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings, arranged in a raceme. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in seasonal environments. Its leaves are strap-like and grassy, often growing in a dense cluster.

Oncidium schillerianum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
