Osbeckia grandiflora

Osbeckia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered osbeckia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Osbeckia grandiflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a desirable addition to native plant gardens.
