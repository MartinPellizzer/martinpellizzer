Oncostema sicula

Oncostema sicula, commonly known as the Sicilian onion grass, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. Oncostema sicula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
