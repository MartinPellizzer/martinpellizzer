Oxalis reclinata var. micromera

Oxalis reclinata var. micromera, commonly known as the small-leaf wood sorrel, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is characterized by delicate, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture.

Oxalis reclinata var. micromera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
