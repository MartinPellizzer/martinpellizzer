Ocotea panurensis

Ocotea panurensis, commonly known as the Panurensis Ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru. This evergreen tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its essential oils and has some traditional medicinal uses among indigenous communities.
