Ottelia cylindrica

Ottelia cylindrica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water plant, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. The plant has long, narrow leaves that float on the water surface, while its roots are submerged, anchoring it in the sediment. Ottelia cylindrica produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of aquatic ecosystems.
