Oberonia anceps

Oberonia anceps, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale pink, and are clustered in a spike.

Oberonia anceps has a complex root system and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
