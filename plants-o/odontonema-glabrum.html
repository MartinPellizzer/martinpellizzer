Odontonema glabrum

Odontonema glabrum, commonly known as the velvetleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Odontonema glabrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
