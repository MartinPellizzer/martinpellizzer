Otoglossum globuliferum

Otoglossum globuliferum, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, globular flower spikes that bear small, fragrant orchid flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Otoglossum globuliferum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
