Olearia consimilis

Olearia consimilis, commonly known as the silverweed tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Olearia consimilis is well adapted to the temperate climate of New Zealand and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
