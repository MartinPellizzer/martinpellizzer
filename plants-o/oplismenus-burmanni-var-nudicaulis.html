Oplismenus burmanni var. nudicaulis

Oplismenus burmanni var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender false nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and bear small, opposite leaves with a slightly heart-shaped base. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are usually hairless on the upper surface and may have a slightly rough texture.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
