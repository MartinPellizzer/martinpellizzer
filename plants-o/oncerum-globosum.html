Oncerum globosum

Oncerum globosum, commonly known as the globe onion, is a herbaceous plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Oncerum globosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils with full sun.

It is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
