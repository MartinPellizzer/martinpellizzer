Oncidium ostenianum

Oncidium ostenianum, commonly known as the Ostenian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flower spikes that often display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.

Oncidium ostenianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
