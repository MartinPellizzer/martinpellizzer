Oldenlandia mitrasacmoides

Oldenlandia mitrasacmoides, commonly known as the "Garden Trumpet" or "Cape Trumpet," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red base. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Oldenlandia mitrasacmoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
