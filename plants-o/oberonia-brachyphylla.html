Oberonia brachyphylla

Oberonia brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and typically produces small, greenish to yellowish flowers. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on the forest floor or along the bases of trees.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
