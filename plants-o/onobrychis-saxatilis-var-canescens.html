Onobrychis saxatilis var. canescens

Onobrychis saxatilis var. canescens, commonly known as the woolly goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
