Oryctanthus oerstedii

Oryctanthus oerstedii, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that produce vibrant orange-red flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
