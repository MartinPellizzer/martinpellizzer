Ononis spinosa subsp. caduca

Ononis spinosa subsp.

caduca, commonly known as the annual hedge parsley, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and waste areas.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, fern-like leaves that are alternate along the stem. Its flowers are white to pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom from spring to early summer.

The fruit is a dry, two-lobed capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
