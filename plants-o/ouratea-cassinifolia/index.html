Ouratea Cassinifolia

Ouratea cassinifolia is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its elegant, waxy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many plant enthusiasts worldwide.
