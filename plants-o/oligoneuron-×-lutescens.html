Oligoneuron × lutescens

Oligoneuron × lutescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered olive.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Oleaceae family, characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite leaves. It produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions with mild climates.
