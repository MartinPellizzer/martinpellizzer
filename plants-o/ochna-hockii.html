Ochna hockii

Ochna hockii, commonly known as the African holly, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically growing up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, clustered, and often yellow or orange in color, while the fruit is a capsule containing several seeds.

Ochna hockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
