Oxalis dumetorum

Oxalis dumetorum, commonly known as wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers resembling those of the genus Oxalis. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Oxalis dumetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
