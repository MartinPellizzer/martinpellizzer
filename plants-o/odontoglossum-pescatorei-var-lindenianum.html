Odontoglossum pescatorei var. lindenianum

Odontoglossum pescatorei var. lindenianum, commonly known as the Lindenburg orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It features vibrant, showy flowers with a distinctive violet and white coloration, often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant has elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity.
