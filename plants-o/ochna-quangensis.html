Ochna quangensis

Ochna quangensis, commonly known as the Quang Province ochna, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Ochna quangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
