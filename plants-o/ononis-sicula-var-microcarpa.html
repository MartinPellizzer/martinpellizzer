Ononis sicula var. microcarpa

Ononis sicula var. microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited sand rocket, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, prostrate stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland dry habitats.
