Oplismenus mollis

Oplismenus mollis, commonly known as soft grass or velvet grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and densely packed. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Oplismenus mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
