Ouratea ilicifolia

Ouratea ilicifolia, commonly known as the spiny ouratea, is a woody shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Ouratea ilicifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
