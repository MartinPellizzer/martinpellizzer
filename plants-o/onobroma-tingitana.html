Onobroma tingitana

Onobroma tingitana, commonly known as the Canary Island candleplant, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its tall, candle-like stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically small, opposite, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Onobroma tingitana is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
