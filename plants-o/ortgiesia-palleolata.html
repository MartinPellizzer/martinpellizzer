Ortgiesia palleolata

Ortgiesia palleolata, commonly known as the pale-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Native to tropical regions of South America, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a cluster of leaves and flowers.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
