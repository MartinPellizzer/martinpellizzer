Origanum cyrenaicum

Origanum cyrenaicum, commonly known as Cyprus oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, pale green leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its essential oils, rich in compounds like carvacrol and thymol, contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.

Origanum cyrenaicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
