Oxypetalum insigne var. nitidum

Oxypetalum insigne var. nitidum, commonly known as the glossy oxypetalum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
