Orchis × braunii

Orchis × braunii, commonly known as the Braun's twayblade, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Europe.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with two opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spike, typical of the twayblade genus. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows.

Orchis × braunii is primarily found in the British Isles and is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
