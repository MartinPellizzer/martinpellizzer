Ochnella mechowiana

Ochnella mechowiana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in warm climates.

Ochnella mechowiana is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to produce a profusion of blooms during the warmer months.
