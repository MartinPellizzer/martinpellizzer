Othake lindenii

Othake lindenii, commonly known as the golden lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Othake lindenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
