Oenothera macrantha

Oenothera macrantha, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oenothera macrantha thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often found in open woodlands, prairies, and along roadsides.
