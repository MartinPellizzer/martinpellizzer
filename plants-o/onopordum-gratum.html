Onopordum gratum

Onopordum gratum, commonly known as the showy thistle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking purple to pink composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has spiny leaves with a rough, hairy texture, and its stem is often covered in fine prickles. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Onopordum gratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
