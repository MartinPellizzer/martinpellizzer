Osbeckia saxatilis

Osbeckia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock osbeckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in harsh, cold environments. The plant features upright, branched stems covered with stiff, glandular hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Osbeckia saxatilis is adapted to low-nutrient conditions and is often found in mountainous or tundra habitats.
