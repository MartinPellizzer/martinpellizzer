Opuntia miquelii var. jilesii

Opuntia miquelii var. jilesii, commonly known as the Mexican prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in spines and hair-like structures called glochids. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its fruits, known as tunas, are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
