Ourisia cockayneana

Ourisia cockayneana, commonly known as the Stewart's ourisia, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes heaths and heathers, and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters and blooming in late summer to early autumn.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.
