Oreocome cicutaria

Oreocome cicutaria, commonly known as the common yellow cicutaria, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and reaches a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Oreocome cicutaria is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
