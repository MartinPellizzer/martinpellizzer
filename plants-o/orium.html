Orium

Orium, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stems. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of water. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The orium is also symbolically significant in many cultures, representing purity and enlightenment.
