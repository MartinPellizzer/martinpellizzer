Ortgiesia blumenavii var. alba

Ortgiesia blumenavii var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered ortgiesia, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
