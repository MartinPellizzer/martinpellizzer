Othonna disticha

Othonna disticha, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture, and its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
