Onuris oligosperma

Onuris oligosperma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into elongated, slender seed capsules. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Onuris oligosperma is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
