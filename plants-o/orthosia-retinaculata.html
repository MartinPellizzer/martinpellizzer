Orthosia retinaculata

Orthosia retinaculata, commonly known as the common orthosia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pale blue to purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and arranged alternately along the stem.

Orthosia retinaculata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.
