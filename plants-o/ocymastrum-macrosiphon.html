Ocymastrum macrosiphon

Ocymastrum macrosiphon, commonly known as the broad-siphon grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. Ocymastrum macrosiphon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its upright growth habit.
