Opuntia Luteispina

Yellow spine prickly pear, scientifically known as Opuntia luteispina, is a unique cactus native to the arid regions of North America. This species is characterized by its flat, spiny stems and vibrant yellow spines that give it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water, making it a resilient plant adapted to desert life. The plant is not only a hardy survivor but also plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its presence highlights the adaptability and ecological significance of cacti in extreme climates.
