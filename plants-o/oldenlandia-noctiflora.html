Oldenlandia noctiflora

Oldenlandia noctiflora, commonly known as night-flowering morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that open at night, typically in shades of white and purple. Its flowers are followed by capsule fruits that release tiny seeds.

Oldenlandia noctiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
