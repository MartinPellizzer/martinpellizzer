Oncidium gilvum

Oncidium gilvum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and requires bright, indirect light to thrive. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.

Oncidium gilvum is a pseudobulbous orchid that prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity.
