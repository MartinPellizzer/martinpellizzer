Ornithogalum langebergense

Ornithogalum langebergense, commonly known as the Langeberg star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant blooms in late winter to early spring, typically from September to November. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum langebergense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
