Ortachne rariflora

Ortachne rariflora, commonly known as the rare orchid of the genus Ortachne, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
