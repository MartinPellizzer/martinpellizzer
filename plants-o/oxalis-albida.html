Oxalis albida

Oxalis albida, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale green coloration. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Oxalis albida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
