Orostachys iwarenge var. chongsunensis

Orostachys iwarenge var. chongsunensis, commonly known as the Korean stonecress, is a perennial succulent native to Korea.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
