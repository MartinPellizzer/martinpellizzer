Otophora Furcata

Purple sea hare, scientifically known as Otophora furcata, is a fascinating marine gastropod found in tropical and subtropical waters. This species is notable for its striking violet and purple hues that shimmer under sunlight. With a soft, gelatinous body and a distinctive shell, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem. The purple sea hare is also recognized for its unique feeding habits and reproductive strategies. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both scientific study and underwater admiration.
