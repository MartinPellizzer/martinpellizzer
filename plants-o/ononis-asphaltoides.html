Ononis asphaltoides

Ononis asphaltoides, commonly known as the asphalt plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the plant is adapted to thrive in dry, rocky soils.

Ononis asphaltoides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and attractive floral display.
