Onobrychis sativa var. scardica

Onobrychis sativa var. scardica, commonly known as scarlet vetch, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines and as forage for livestock.
