Oxalis guessfeldtii

Oxalis guessfeldtii, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, bead-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis guessfeldtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
