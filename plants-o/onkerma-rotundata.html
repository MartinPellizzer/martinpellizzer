Onkerma rotundata

Onkerma rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of white and yellow, are often clustered in inflorescences.

Onkerma rotundata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
