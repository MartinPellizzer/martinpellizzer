Ornithogalum pullatum

Ornithogalum pullatum, commonly known as the purple star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale purple or white star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

Ornithogalum pullatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
