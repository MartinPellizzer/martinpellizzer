Oxygonum maculatum

Oxygonum maculatum, commonly known as the spotted dieffenbachia, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature white and green markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Oxygonum maculatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
