Orchis pseudocordigera

Orchis pseudocordigera, commonly known as the false heart orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears a few lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive hooded lip.

Orchis pseudocordigera is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
