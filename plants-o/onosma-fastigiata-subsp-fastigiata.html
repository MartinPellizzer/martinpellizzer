Onosma fastigiata subsp. fastigiata

Onosma fastigiata subsp.

fastigiata, commonly known as the upright onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features erect, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to horticultural collections.
