Obione pamparum

Obione pamparum, commonly known as the pampas lily, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of showy, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a common sight in open habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Obione pamparum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
