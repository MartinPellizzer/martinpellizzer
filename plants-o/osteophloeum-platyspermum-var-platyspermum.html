Osteophloeum platyspermum var. platyspermum

Osteophloeum platyspermum var. platyspermum, commonly known as the broad-seeded osteophloeum, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in inflorescences, and it produces distinctive, flat, broad pods as its fruit.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
