Oncidium tigrinum var. giganteum

Oncidium tigrinum var. giganteum, commonly known as the Tiger Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that typically display a combination of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium tigrinum var. giganteum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
