Othonna heterophylla

Othonna heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its diverse leaf morphology, which can vary from linear to broadly ovate. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by cylindrical, spiny fruits.

Othonna heterophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
