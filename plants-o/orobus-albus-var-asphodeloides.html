Orobus albus var. asphodeloides

Orobus albus var. asphodeloides, commonly known as the white orobus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
