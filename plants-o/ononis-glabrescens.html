Ononis glabrescens

Ononis glabrescens, commonly known as smooth bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pink or white flowers. The plant has hairy leaves that become glabrous as it matures, and its flowers are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
