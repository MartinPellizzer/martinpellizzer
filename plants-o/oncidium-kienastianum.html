Oncidium kienastianum

Oncidium kienastianum, commonly known as the Kienast's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its inflorescence consists of multiple blooms arranged in a spike, often with a distinctive lip.

Oncidium kienastianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
