Ornithogalum fimbriatum subsp. fimbriatum

Ornithogalum fimbriatum subsp.

fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers with fringed petals.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
