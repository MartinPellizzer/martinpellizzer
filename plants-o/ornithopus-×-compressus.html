Ornithopus × compressus

Ornithopus × compressus, commonly known as the narrowleaf sea rocket, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Ornithopus × compressus plays a role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
