Oncidium fasciferum

Oncidium fasciferum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and arching, strap-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow, butterfly-shaped flowers that are highly fragrant. Oncidium fasciferum is a sun-loving plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
