Ononis laxiflora

Ononis laxiflora, commonly known as slender flax or narrow-leaved ononis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in loose inflorescences, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Ononis laxiflora is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
