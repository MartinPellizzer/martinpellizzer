Ophiorrhiza oblanceolata

Ophiorrhiza oblanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mangrove, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rhizophoraceae.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, particularly in mangrove ecosystems. Its root system helps stabilize sediments and provides habitat for various marine and terrestrial organisms.
