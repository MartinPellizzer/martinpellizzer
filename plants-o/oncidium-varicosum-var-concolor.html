Oncidium varicosum var. concolor

Oncidium varicosum var. concolor, commonly known as the Concolor Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, long-lasting flowers that range in color from yellow to orange with red or purple markings. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.
