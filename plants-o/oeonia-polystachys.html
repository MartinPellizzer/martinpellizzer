Oeonia polystachys

Oeonia polystachys, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Oeonia polystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments.
