Origanum thymiflorum

Origanum thymiflorum, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are fragrant, with a strong lemony scent due to the presence of thymol and other essential oils.

Lemon thyme is often used in culinary applications and herbal remedies for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
