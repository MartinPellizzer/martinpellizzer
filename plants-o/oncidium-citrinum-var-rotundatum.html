Oncidium citrinum var. rotundatum

Oncidium citrinum var. rotundatum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green and slightly fleshy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive golden hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
