Olmedia erythrorhiza

Olmedia erythrorhiza, commonly known as the red-rooted olmedia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, particularly at the base. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Olmedia erythrorhiza is valued in gardens for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
