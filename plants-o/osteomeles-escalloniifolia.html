Osteomeles escalloniifolia

Osteomeles escalloniifolia, commonly known as the Escalloniifolia flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, flame-colored bracts that surround its small, clustered flowers. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that are often arranged in a feather-like pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree produces small, round fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals.
