Oenotrichia tripinnata

Oenotrichia tripinnata, commonly known as the three-parted hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically have five petals and a prominent central stigma. Oenotrichia tripinnata thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
