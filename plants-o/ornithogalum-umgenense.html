Ornithogalum umgenense

Ornithogalum umgenense, commonly known as the Umgeni star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a striking visual element to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes.
