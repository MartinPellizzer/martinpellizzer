Osbeckia gracilis

Osbeckia gracilis, commonly known as slender osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy as well. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Osbeckia gracilis is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.
