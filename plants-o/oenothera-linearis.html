Oenothera linearis

Oenothera linearis, commonly known as the linear evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Oenothera linearis is also noted for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, making it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
