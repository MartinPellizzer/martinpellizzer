Ouratea marginata

Ouratea marginata, commonly known as the marginated ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ouratea marginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
