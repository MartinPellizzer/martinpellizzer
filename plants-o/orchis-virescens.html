Orchis virescens

Orchis virescens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Orchis virescens is often found in woodland habitats, thriving in moist, shaded environments.

It is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
