Odonectis verticillata

Odonectis verticillata, commonly known as the spiral herb, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves around its stem, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Odonectis verticillata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
