Ornithogalum synanthifolium

Ornithogalum synanthifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Ornithogalum synanthifolium is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
