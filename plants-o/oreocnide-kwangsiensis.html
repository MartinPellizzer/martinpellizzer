Oreocnide kwangsiensis

Oreocnide kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi oreocnide, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly in the Guangxi region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are often white or pale yellow in color.

Oreocnide kwangsiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
