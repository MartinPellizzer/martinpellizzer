Oreopanax macrophyllus

Oreopanax macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf oreopanax, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forests.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
