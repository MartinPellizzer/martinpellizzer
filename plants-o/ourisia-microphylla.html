Ourisia microphylla

Ourisia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved ourisia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and densely packed, adapted to thrive in cool, moist environments.
