Oxytropis iskanderica

Oxytropis iskanderica, commonly known as Iskander's locoweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant is adapted to harsh, dry environments and often grows in rocky or sandy soils. It produces pods that contain seeds with a hard coat, which helps it survive in nutrient-poor conditions.

Oxytropis iskanderica is also known for its toxic properties, containing alkaloids that can be harmful to livestock.
