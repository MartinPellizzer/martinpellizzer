Oxytropis immersa

Oxytropis immersa, commonly known as the water bur.

It is a perennial legume native to the western United States, particularly found in moist, often saline soils. The plant has compound leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale blue or purple. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Oxytropis immersa plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and wildlife.
