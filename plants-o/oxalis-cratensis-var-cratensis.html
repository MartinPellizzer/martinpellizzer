Oxalis cratensis var. cratensis

Oxalis cratensis var. cratensis, commonly known as the Crimson Oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in spring. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with each leaflet having a pointed tip and a smooth texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.
