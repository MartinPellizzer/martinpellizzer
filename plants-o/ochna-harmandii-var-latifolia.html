Ochna harmandii var. latifolia

Ochna harmandii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ochna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
