Orbignya × teixeirana

Orbignya × teixeirana, commonly known as the golden cupuaçu, is a hybrid plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces a unique fruit with a hard outer shell and a sweet, aromatic pulp inside. Its fruit is valued for its high content of lipids, proteins, and antioxidants.
