Oligosporus argyreus

Oligosporus argyreus, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves with a silvery underside, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and abandoned fields.

Botanically, it belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and potatoes.
