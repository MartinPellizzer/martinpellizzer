Oberonia discolor

Oberonia discolor, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, pale green to white flowers that are often clustered in a loose inflorescence. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Oberonia discolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
