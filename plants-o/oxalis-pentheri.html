Oxalis pentheri

Oxalis pentheri, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis pentheri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
