Oxera neriifolia subsp. neriifolia

Oxera neriifolia subsp.

neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leaved oxera, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate rainforests. Oxera neriifolia subsp.

neriifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
