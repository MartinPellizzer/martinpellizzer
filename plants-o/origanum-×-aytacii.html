Origanum × aytacii

Origanum × aytacii, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, tubular flowers. The plant produces essential oils rich in thymol and carvacrol, contributing to its strong medicinal and culinary uses. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both horticultural and pharmaceutical applications.
