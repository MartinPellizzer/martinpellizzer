Oberonia werneri

Oberonia werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant has a compact, sympodial growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually pale yellow or greenish-white, arranged in a spike.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
