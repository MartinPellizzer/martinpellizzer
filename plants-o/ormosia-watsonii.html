Ormosia watsonii

Ormosia watsonii, commonly known as the Chinese tallow tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its rapid growth and attractive foliage.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions, posing a threat to native ecosystems.
