Otanthera montana

Otanthera montana, commonly known as the mountain blazingstar, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy to the touch. Its showy, yellow-orange flower heads bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Otanthera montana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to native gardens.
