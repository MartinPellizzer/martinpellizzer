Olea rigida

Olea rigida, commonly known as the rigid olive, is a species of flowering plant in the olive family, Oleaceae.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed. Olea rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to dry, rocky environments.

Its botanical features make it a unique and resilient species within the Oleaceae family.
