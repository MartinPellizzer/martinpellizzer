Orchis × dafnii

Orchis × dafnii, commonly known as the fairy orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Orchis × dafnii is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
