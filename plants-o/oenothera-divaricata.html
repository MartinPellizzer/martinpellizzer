Oenothera divaricata

Oenothera divaricata, commonly known as the spreading evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. Oenothera divaricata thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.
