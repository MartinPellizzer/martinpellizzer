Omalotheca hoppeana

Omalotheca hoppeana, commonly known as the Hoppe's ironwood, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. Its hard, woody structure makes it highly drought-tolerant and well adapted to harsh conditions.
