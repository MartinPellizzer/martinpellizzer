Orescia

Orescia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Orescia is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and modest floral display.
