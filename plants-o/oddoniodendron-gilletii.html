Oddoniodendron gilletii

Oddoniodendron gilletii, commonly known as the Cape rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features a woody stem with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
