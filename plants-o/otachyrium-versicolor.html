Otachyrium versicolor

Otachyrium versicolor, commonly known as the rainbow fern, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Its leaves exhibit a remarkable range of colors, from deep purples and blues to bright yellows and greens, depending on environmental conditions. The plant's foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability have made it a favored ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
