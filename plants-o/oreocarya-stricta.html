Oreocarya stricta

Oreocarya stricta, commonly known as the rigid oreocarya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. The plant features a slender, upright stem with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

Oreocarya stricta is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
