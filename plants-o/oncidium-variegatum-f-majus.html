Oncidium variegatum f. majus

Oncidium variegatum f. majus, commonly known as the Giant Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It features pendulous, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical habitats. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are dark green and often variegated with white or silver markings.
