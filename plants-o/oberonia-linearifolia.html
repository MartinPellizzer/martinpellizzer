Oberonia linearifolia

Oberonia linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Oberonia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its flowers are typically small and greenish-white, with a subtle fragrance.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions for successful cultivation.
