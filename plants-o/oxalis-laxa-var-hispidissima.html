Oxalis laxa var. hispidissima

Oxalis laxa var. hispidissima, commonly known as the hispid oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture, giving it its "hispid" characteristic. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage and the subtle beauty of its blooms.
