Ornithogalum dividens

Ornithogalum dividens, commonly known as the divided star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces upright, slender stems that bear clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from the base of the plant. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
