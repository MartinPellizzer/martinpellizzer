Ornithogalum strigosulum

Ornithogalum strigosulum, commonly known as the hairy star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

This plant is also known for its mild toxicity, making it important to handle it with care.
