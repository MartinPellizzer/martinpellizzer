Odontosoria melleri, commonly known as the Meller's harts-tongue fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Osmundaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, strap-like fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. Odontosoria melleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its unique foliage.
