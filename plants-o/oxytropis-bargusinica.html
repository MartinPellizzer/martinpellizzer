Oxytropis bargusinica

Oxytropis bargusinica, commonly known as Bargusin milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Oxytropis bargusinica is part of the legume family and has the ability to fix nitrogen, which can improve soil fertility.
