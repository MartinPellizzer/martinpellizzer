Ornithogalum narbonense var. brachystachys

Ornithogalum narbonense var. brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.
