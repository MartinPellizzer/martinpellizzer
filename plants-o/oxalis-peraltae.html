Oxalis peraltae

Oxalis peraltae, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery-white coating on the underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
