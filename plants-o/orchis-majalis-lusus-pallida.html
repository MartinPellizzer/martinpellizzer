Orchis majalis lusus pallida

Orchis majalis lusus pallida, commonly known as the pale lusus orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and it is known for its unique, low-growing habit.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered a threatened species in many areas.
