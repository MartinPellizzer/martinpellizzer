Oxalis callimarginata

Oxalis callimarginata, commonly known as the red oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with red margins and clusters of small, five-petaled white flowers. The plant belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis callimarginata is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
