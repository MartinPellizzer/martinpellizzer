Origanum ciliatum

Origanum ciliatum, commonly known as slender oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly for its essential oils.

Origanum ciliatum is also used in traditional herbal remedies for its antiseptic and digestive properties.
