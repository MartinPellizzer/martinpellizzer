Osmanthus aquifolium var. viridis-variegatus

Osmanthus aquifolium var. viridis-variegatus, commonly known as the variegated green osmanthus, is a unique variant of the osmanthus plant characterized by its striking variegated foliage.

This plant features lance-shaped leaves with a combination of green and white or yellow markings, creating an ornamental appearance. It is native to regions of Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the autumn, adding both visual and olfactory appeal.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use as a decorative shrub in gardens and landscapes.
