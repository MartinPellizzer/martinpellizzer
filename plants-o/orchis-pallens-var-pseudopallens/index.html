Orchis Pallens Var. Pseudopallens

Pallid orchis, scientifically known as Orchis pallens var. pseudopallens, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. This orchid species is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers and slender, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded meadows. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
