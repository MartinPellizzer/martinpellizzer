Odontoglossum principissa

Odontoglossum principissa, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, large, and showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, high-altitude environments. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink with a contrasting lip, and it blooms during the cooler months.

Odontoglossum principissa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
