Orthospermum unctuosum

Orthospermum unctuosum, commonly known as the woolly orthospermum, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Orthospermum unctuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
