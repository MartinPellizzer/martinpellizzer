Odontoglossum lapacense

Odontoglossum lapacense, commonly known as the Lapacense Orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, lance-shaped leaves and blooms with intricate, often violet or purple-colored flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
