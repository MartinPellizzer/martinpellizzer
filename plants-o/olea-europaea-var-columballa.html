Olea europaea var. columballa

Olea europaea var. columballa, commonly known as the Croatian olive.

It is a variant of the common olive tree, characterized by its compact growth habit and smaller leaves compared to the standard olive. This variety is native to the Adriatic region and is well adapted to the Mediterranean climate. The plant produces small, edible olives that are often used in local cuisines.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in coastal areas.
