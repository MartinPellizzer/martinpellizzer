Ormosia nitida

Ormosia nitida, commonly known as the slender ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Ormosia nitida is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
