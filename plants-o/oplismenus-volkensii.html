Oplismenus volkensii

Oplismenus volkensii, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that bear small, delicate, silver-green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Oplismenus volkensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its graceful appearance.
