Oldenburgia papionum

Oldenburgia papionum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and produces clusters of bright-colored blooms in shades of purple, pink, and white. Its ornamental value and ease of care have contributed to its widespread cultivation worldwide.
