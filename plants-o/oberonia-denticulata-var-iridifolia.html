Oberonia denticulata var. iridifolia

Oberonia denticulata var. iridifolia, commonly known as the Iridifolia orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces striking, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
