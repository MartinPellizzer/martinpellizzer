Orbea carnosa subsp. carnosa

Orbea carnosa subsp.

carnosa, commonly known as the Wooly Orbea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
