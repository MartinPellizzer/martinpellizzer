Oreocharis nemoralis var. nemoralis

Oreocharis nemoralis var. nemoralis, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale purple, which bloom during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are grass-like, arranged in a rosette, and are adapted to thrive in rocky and well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
