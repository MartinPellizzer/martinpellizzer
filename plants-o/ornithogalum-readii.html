Ornithogalum readii

Ornithogalum readii, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive red or purple stripe along the central vein. The plant is part of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
