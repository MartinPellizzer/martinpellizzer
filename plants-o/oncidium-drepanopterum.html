Oncidium drepanopterum

Oncidium drepanopterum, commonly known as the Sword Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green and white markings. Its flowers are typically yellow with red or orange markings, appearing in racemes during the rainy season.

Oncidium drepanopterum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
