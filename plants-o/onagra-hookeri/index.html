Onagra Hookeri

Hooker's onagra, scientifically known as Onagra hookeri, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. This species belongs to the evening primrose family, known for its showy, yellow-orange blooms that appear during the summer months. Despite its beauty, Hooker's onagra is critically endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this delicate species and preserve its ecological role. Its existence highlights the importance of biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
