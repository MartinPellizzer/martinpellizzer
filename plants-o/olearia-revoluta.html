Olearia revoluta

Olearia revoluta, commonly known as the silver mat, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Olearia revoluta is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
