Oxymitra discostigma

Oxymitra discostigma, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with waxy, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Oxymitra discostigma is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
