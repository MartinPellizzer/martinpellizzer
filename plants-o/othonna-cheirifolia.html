Othonna cheirifolia

Othonna cheirifolia, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Othonna cheirifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
