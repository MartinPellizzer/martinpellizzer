Omphalocarpum agglomeratum

Omphalocarpum agglomeratum, commonly known as the clustered burr grass, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, hairy, spiny fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
