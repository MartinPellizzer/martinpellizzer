Ocotea sassafras

Ocotea sassafras, commonly known as mountain cinnamon, is a flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Ocotea sassafras is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
