Oxytropis dehra-duni

Oxytropis dehra-duni, commonly known as the Dehra Dun oxytropis, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Oxytropis dehra-duni is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in nutrient-poor areas.
