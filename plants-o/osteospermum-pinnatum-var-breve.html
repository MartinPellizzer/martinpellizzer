Osteospermum pinnatum var. breve

Osteospermum pinnatum var. breve, commonly known as the dwarf blue osteospermum, is a compact, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features daisy-like flowers with blue to purple petals and a central yellow disk, blooming throughout the warmer months. The plant has upright, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and grayish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
