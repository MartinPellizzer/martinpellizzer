Onopordum × bilbilitanum

Onopordum × bilbilitanum, commonly known as the Bilbilitanum thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and showy purple or pink flower heads that resemble daisies. Native to the Canary Islands, this species thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its propagation.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
