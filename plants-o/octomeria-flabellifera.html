Octomeria flabellifera

Octomeria flabellifera, commonly known as the broad-leafed octomeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and emits a strong, spicy fragrance. Octomeria flabellifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
