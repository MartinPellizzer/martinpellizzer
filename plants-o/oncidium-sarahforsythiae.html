Oncidium sarahforsythiae

Oncidium sarahforsythiae, commonly known as Sarah Forsyth's Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with contrasting purple or red markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like, long, and somewhat fleshy, adapted to thrive in warm, humid environments.

Oncidium sarahforsythiae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its longevity and ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
