Oxalis preissiana

Oxalis preissiana, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the winter months. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis preissiana is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooms.
