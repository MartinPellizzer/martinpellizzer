Oreocallis

Oreocallis, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a genus of flowering plants native to the mountainous regions of South America.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Oreocallis species typically grow in high-altitude environments, where they adapt to cooler temperatures and well-drained soils.
