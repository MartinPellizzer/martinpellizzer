Onopordum jordanicola

Onopordum jordanicola, commonly known as Jordanian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of the Mediterranean region.

Onopordum jordanicola is often found in rocky habitats and can be a significant component of local flora in its native range.
