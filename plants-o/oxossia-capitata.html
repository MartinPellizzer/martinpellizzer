Oxossia capitata

Oxossia capitata, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Oxossia capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
