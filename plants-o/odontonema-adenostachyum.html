Odontonema adenostachyum

Odontonema adenostachyum, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its inflorescences are terminal and consist of clusters of bright, showy flowers that attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.

Odontonema adenostachyum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
