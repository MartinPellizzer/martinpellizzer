Oncidium brachyphyllum

Oncidium brachyphyllum, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features a compact growth habit with short, fleshy pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium brachyphyllum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
