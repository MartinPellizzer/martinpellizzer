Orchis × farquetii

Orchis × farquetii, commonly known as the Farquet's twayblade, is a hybrid orchid native to Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with a single spike of flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Orchis × farquetii is noted for its limited distribution and is considered a rare species in its native range.
