Oldenlandia Gracilis

Oldenlandia gracilis is a delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the rainy season. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its slender stems and intricate leaf patterns make it a popular choice for botanical gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species in the world of horticulture.
