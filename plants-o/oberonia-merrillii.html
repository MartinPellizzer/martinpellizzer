Oberonia merrillii

Oberonia merrillii, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Oberonia merrillii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its striking floral display.
