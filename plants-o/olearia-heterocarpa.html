Olearia heterocarpa

Olearia heterocarpa, commonly known as the New Zealand ice plant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in winter, attracting pollinators such as bees and insects. Its leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Olearia heterocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
