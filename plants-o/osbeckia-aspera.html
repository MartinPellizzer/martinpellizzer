Osbeckia aspera

Osbeckia aspera, commonly known as rough osbeckia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas. The plant has slender, branched stems covered in stiff, rough hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Osbeckia aspera is characterized by its alternate leaves, which are usually ovate to elliptic in shape.
