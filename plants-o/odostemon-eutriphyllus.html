Odostemon eutriphyllus

Odostemon eutriphyllus, commonly known as the giant leaved odostemon, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage is a deep green, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental leaves and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
