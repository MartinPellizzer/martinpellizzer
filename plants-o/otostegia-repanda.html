Otostegia repanda

Otostegia repanda, commonly known as the hairy spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, sticky hairs. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the spring.

Otostegia repanda is known for its adaptability to dry and rocky soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
