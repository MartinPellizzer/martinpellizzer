Oligomeris linifolia

Oligomeris linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique leaf structure and floral morphology make it an interesting subject for botanical study.

Oligomeris linifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in native landscapes.
