Osbeckia pumila

Osbeckia pumila, commonly known as the smooth osbeckia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It typically features prostrate stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Osbeckia pumila is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and along stream banks.
