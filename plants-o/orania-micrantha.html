Orania micrantha

Orania micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, small flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Orania micrantha produces small, greenish-white blooms that are often clustered together on slender stems.
