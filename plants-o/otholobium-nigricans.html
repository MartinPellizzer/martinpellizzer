Otholobium nigricans

Otholobium nigricans, commonly known as black wattle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, leathery pods. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
