Oberonia surigaensis

Oberonia surigaensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with prominent purple or brown markings.

Oberonia surigaensis is often found in moist, shaded forest understories, thriving in humid tropical environments.
