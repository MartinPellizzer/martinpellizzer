Orthophytum supthutii

Orthophytum supthutii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of bright yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Orthophytum supthutii is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
