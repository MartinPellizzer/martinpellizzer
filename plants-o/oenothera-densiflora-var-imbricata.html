Oenothera densiflora var. imbricata

Oenothera densiflora var. imbricata, commonly known as the imbricata evening primrose, is a species native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are alternate and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and branching.

The flowers are followed by capsule fruits that release seeds when mature.
