Oxytropis Campestris Subsp. Tiroliensis

Oxytropis campestris subsp. tiroliensis is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific alpine regions. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance. This subspecies thrives in rocky and well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature, making it a subject of interest for botanists. Conservation efforts are crucial due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to environmental changes.
