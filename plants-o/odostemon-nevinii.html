Odostemon nevinii

Odostemon nevinii, commonly known as the Nevin's goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and limestone areas.

Odostemon nevinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
