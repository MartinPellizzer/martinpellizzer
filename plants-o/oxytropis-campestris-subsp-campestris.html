Oxytropis campestris subsp. campestris

Oxytropis campestris subsp.

campestris, commonly known as yellow locoweed, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae. It is a perennial herb characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions.

The species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
