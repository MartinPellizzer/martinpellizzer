Onobrychis splendida

Onobrychis splendida, commonly known as the splendour burr or golden burr, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that produces distinctive yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is characterized by its spiny, pod-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Onobrychis splendida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
