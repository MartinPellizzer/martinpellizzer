Onosma briquetii

Onosma briquetii, commonly known as the Briquet's sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, pink to violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Onosma briquetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
