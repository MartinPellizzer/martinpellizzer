Oxalis blackii

Oxalis blackii, commonly known as the black oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis blackii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
