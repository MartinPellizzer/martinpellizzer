Oncidium × bluntii

Oncidium × bluntii, commonly known as the Blunt's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red-brown markings. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, showcasing its striking, long-lasting inflorescences. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Oncidium × bluntii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
