Oxalis ortgiesii subsp. tingoensis

Oxalis ortgiesii subsp.

tingoensis, commonly known as the Tingoensis wood sorrel, is a species native to South Africa. It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant with trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers.

The plant typically features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
