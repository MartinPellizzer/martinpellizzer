Odontostemma dawuense

Odontostemma dawuense, commonly known as the Dawu arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

Odontostemma dawuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in water gardens.
