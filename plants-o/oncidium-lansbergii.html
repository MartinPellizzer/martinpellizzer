Oncidium lansbergii

Oncidium lansbergii, commonly known as the Showy Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences with brightly colored, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees in its natural habitat.

Oncidium lansbergii requires bright, indirect light and moderate watering to thrive.
