Oxytropis × hybrida

Oxytropis × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid yellow globemallow, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of yellow to orange flowers.

The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Oxytropis × hybrida is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
