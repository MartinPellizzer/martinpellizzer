Ononis crispa

Ononis crispa, commonly known as the crinkled sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are characterized by their crinkled, wavy edges, which give it its common name.

Ononis crispa thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.
