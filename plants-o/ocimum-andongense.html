Ocimum andongense

Ocimum andongense, commonly known as Andong basil, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with a square stem and opposite leaves. Its leaves are often used in traditional Filipino cuisine and herbal medicine.

Ocimum andongense is valued for its distinctive fragrance and potential medicinal properties.
