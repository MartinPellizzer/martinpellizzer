Oxydectes Constricta

Oxydectes constricta is a small, cryptic parasite commonly found in the digestive tracts of various mammals. It belongs to the family Oxyuridae and is known for its ability to cause significant health issues in its hosts. This nematode is particularly prevalent in livestock, making it a major concern for farmers and veterinarians. Its life cycle involves both intermediate and definitive hosts, often involving grasses and other vegetation. Despite its tiny size, Oxydectes constricta plays a crucial role in the ecological balance of its environment.
