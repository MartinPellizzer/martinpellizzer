Orfilea multispicata

Orfilea multispicata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Orfilea multispicata is known for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
