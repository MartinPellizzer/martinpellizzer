Oxymeris dendroides

Oxymeris dendroides, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fruit that contains a single seed. Oxymeris dendroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
