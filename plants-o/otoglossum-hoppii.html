Otoglossum hoppii

Otoglossum hoppii, commonly known as the Hoppi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, stocky rhizome and produces a single, showy spike of flowers. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in grasslands or along forest margins.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
