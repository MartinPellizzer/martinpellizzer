Oenothera quadricolumnata

Oenothera quadricolumnata, commonly known as the four-column evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The seeds are enclosed in a capsule that splits open to release the seeds when mature.
