Ochna micrantha

Ochna micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a distinctive waxy coating.

Ochna micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
