Ocimum descampsii

Ocimum descampsii, commonly known as the African basil, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, ovate leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and thyme. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ocimum descampsii is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
