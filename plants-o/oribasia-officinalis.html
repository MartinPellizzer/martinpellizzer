Oribasia officinalis

Oribasia officinalis, commonly known as desert panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial grass that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in desert scrub and grassland ecosystems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal. It is drought-tolerant and has a deep root system that allows it to survive in poor soil conditions.

Oribasia officinalis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
