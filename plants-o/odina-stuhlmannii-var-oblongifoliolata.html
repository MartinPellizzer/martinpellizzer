Odina stuhlmannii var. oblongifoliolata

Odina stuhlmannii var. oblongifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed odina, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, and often lobed leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
