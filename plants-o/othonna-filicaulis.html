Othonna filicaulis

Othonna filicaulis, commonly known as the featherleaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, which bloom during the warmer months. Othonna filicaulis is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
