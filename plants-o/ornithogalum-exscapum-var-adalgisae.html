Ornithogalum exscapum var. adalgisae

Ornithogalum exscapum var. adalgisae, commonly known as the Adalgisa's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale blue star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
