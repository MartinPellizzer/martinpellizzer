Oncidium isopterum

Oncidium isopterum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its drooping, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of bright yellow, butterfly-like flowers. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium isopterum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
