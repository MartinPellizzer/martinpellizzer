Oncidium Putumayense

Putumayo orchid, scientifically known as Oncidium putumayense, is a striking member of the orchid family native to the humid rainforests of South America. This vibrant plant is celebrated for its showy, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors, from fiery reds to soft yellows. Thriving in warm, moist environments, it is often found growing on trees or in shaded undergrowth. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, the Putumayo orchid remains relatively rare in cultivation, adding to its allure and significance in the world of orchids.
