Ouratea saulensis

Ouratea saulensis, commonly known as the Brazilian holly or Brazilian ouratea, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in panicles, often appearing in spring.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
