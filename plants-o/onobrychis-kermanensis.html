Onobrychis kermanensis

Onobrychis kermanensis, commonly known as Kerman sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Kerman sainfoin is also valued for its forage qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
