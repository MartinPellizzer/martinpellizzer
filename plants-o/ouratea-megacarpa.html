Ouratea megacarpa

Ouratea megacarpa, commonly known as the giant ouratea, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy white or pale yellow flowers that are often fragrant. Its fruit is a large, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
