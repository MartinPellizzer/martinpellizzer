Odonia multiflora

Odonia multiflora, commonly known as the clustered broom, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Odonia multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
