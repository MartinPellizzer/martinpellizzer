Oncidium variegatum f. roseum

Oncidium variegatum f. roseum, commonly known as the Roseum Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety blooms that range from pale pink to deep rose hues. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous flowers that bloom sequentially over several weeks. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
