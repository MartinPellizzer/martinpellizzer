Oligosporus jussieanus

Oligosporus jussieanus, commonly known as the Jussiean spore plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, clustered spores rather than typical flowers or seeds. The plant's leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a waxy surface that aids in moisture retention. Its spores are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize moist, shaded environments effectively.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological specificity, it is considered a rare and poorly understood botanical specimen.
