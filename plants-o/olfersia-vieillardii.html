Olfersia vieillardii

Olfersia vieillardii, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like appearance, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Olfersia vieillardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
