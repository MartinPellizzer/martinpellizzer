Odontadenia cordigera

Odontadenia cordigera, commonly known as the heartleaf lily or mountain lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that produces showy, bell-shaped blooms during the summer months.

Odontadenia cordigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
