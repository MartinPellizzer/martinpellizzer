Onobrychis balansae

Onobrychis balansae, commonly known as the Balkan burr vetch, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Onobrychis balansae is valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
