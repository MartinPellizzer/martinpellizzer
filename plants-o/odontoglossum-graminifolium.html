Odontoglossum graminifolium

Odontoglossum graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaf orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with violet to purple flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
