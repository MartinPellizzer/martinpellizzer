Oenothera gayophytum f. diffusa

Oenothera gayophytum f. diffusa, commonly known as the diffuse evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the evening.
