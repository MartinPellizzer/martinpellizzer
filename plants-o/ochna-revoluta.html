Ochna revoluta

Ochna revoluta, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, white, spherical clusters of flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and greyish, becoming fissured with age.

Ochna revoluta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
