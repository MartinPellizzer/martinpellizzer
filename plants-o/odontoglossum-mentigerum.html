Odontoglossum mentigerum

Odontoglossum mentigerum, commonly known as the Mentiger Orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, cool environment.

Odontoglossum mentigerum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
