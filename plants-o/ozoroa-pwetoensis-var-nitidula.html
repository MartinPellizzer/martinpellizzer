Ozoroa pwetoensis var. nitidula

Ozoroa pwetoensis var. nitidula, commonly known as the glossy ozoroa, is a shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that have a waxy appearance, giving it a glossy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forest vegetation.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local ecosystem and a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
