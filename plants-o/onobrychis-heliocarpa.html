Onobrychis heliocarpa

Onobrychis heliocarpa, commonly known as the sunflower burclover, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in sunny, dry habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.
