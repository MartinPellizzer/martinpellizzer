Ornithogalum persicum var. elongatum

Ornithogalum persicum var. elongatum, commonly known as the Persian star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale blue flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
