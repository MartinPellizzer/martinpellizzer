Ormocarpopsis aspera

Ormocarpopsis aspera, commonly known as the rough-seeded orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with a rough, textured surface.

Ormocarpopsis aspera is primarily found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
