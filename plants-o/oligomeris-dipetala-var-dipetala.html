Oligomeris dipetala var. dipetala

Oligomeris dipetala var. dipetala, commonly known as the two-petaled olive, is a rare flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers with two petals. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, elliptical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and woodland habitats.
