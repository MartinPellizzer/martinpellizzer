Oncophyllum

Oncophyllum, commonly known as the false dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to Africa, with some extending into parts of Asia and Australia. They are generally herbaceous perennials, though some species may behave as annuals depending on the climate.
