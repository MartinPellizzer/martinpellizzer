Oxytropis oligantha var. glabra

Oxytropis oligantha var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth bur pea, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale yellow to reddish-brown. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it has small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

This species is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and plays a role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
