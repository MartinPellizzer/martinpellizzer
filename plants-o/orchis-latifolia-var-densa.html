Orchis latifolia var. densa

Orchis latifolia var. densa, commonly known as the dense broad-leaved orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows, woodlands, and stream sides. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, violet or purple flowers in late spring to early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of flowers, which is characteristic of the species.

Orchis latifolia var. densa is known for its ornamental value and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation sites.
