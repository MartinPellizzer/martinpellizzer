Oenothera eucharidium

Oenothera eucharidium, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Oenothera eucharidium thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as prairies and disturbed sites.

It is also valued for its oil-rich seeds, which have various industrial and medicinal applications.
