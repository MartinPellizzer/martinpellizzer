Odontostemma Weissianum

Odontostemma weissianum is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa. It belongs to the family Araceae, known for its unique floral structures and diverse species. This plant is distinguished by its striking, spatulate leaves and a distinctive inflorescence that resembles a shield. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its elusive habitat and limited distribution. Its conservation status is a growing concern as deforestation threatens its survival.
