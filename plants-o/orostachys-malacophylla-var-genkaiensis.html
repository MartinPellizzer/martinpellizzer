Orostachys malacophylla var. genkaiensis

Orostachys malacophylla var. genkaiensis, commonly known as the Genkai sandmat, is a low-growing succulent native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually pink or white.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
