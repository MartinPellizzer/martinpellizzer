Orbea × mutabilis

Orbea × mutabilis, commonly known as the variable orchid orchid.

This hybrid plant is a result of crossbreeding between Orbea rubra and Orbea variegata, leading to a range of color variations in its flowers. It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, showy blooms that can range from red to pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
