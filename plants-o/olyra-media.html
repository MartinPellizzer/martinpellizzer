Olyra media

Olyra media, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by delicate, grass-like foliage. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Olyra media is also known for its air-purifying properties, making it both ornamental and beneficial.
