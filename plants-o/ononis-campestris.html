Ononis campestris

Ononis campestris, commonly known as field scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, pinnately compound leaves. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually blue to purple in color, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
