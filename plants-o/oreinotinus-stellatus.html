Oreinotinus stellatus

Oreinotinus stellatus, commonly known as the star-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Oreinotinus stellatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
