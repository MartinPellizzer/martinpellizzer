Oberonia acaulis var. latipetala

Oberonia acaulis var. latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy rhizomes and erect, leafy stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in a spike, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and requires high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variant is prized for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
