Oldenlandia megistosiphon

Oldenlandia megistosiphon, commonly known as the large-flowered spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers with five petals, which are often accompanied by long, slender sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The species is notable for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
