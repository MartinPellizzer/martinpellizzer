Oenothera Filipes

Oenothera filipes, commonly known as the slender evening primrose, is a fascinating member of the Onagraceae family. This plant is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern united states and northern mexico. it is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and showy yellow flowers that bloom at dusk. the plant is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments with minimal water. its unique adaptations make it an important species in ecological studies and botanical research.
