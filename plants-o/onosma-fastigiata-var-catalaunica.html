Onosma fastigiata var. catalaunica

Onosma fastigiata var. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features erect, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow, linear, and often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, usually displaying shades of pink to purple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
