Ornithogalum brachystachys

Ornithogalum brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stalked star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous root system and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often mistaken for grasses or sedges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

Ornithogalum brachystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
