Ophrys akhdarensis

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green or brown, with intricate patterns that aid in attracting specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Ophrys akhdarensis is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native range.
