Oncidium quadrilobum

Oncidium quadrilobum, commonly known as the Four-lobed Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or brown markings. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium quadrilobum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
