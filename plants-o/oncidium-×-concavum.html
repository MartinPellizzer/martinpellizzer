Oncidium × concavum

Oncidium × concavum, commonly known as the Spurred Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, well-lit environments.

Oncidium × concavum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and long-lasting blooms.
