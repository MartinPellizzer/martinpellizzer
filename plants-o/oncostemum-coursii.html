Oncostemum coursii

Oncostemum coursii, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting throat. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and velvety in texture.
