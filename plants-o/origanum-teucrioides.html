Origanum teucrioides

Origanum teucrioides, commonly known as hardy oregano, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Origanum teucrioides is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
