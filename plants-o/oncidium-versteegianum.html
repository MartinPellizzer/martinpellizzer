Oncidium versteegianum

Oncidium versteegianum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting green or brown lip. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Oncidium versteegianum is also valued for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under proper conditions.
