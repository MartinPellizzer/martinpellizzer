Oldenlandia aridosa

Oldenlandia aridosa, commonly known as the desert spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky soils and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water in drought-prone habitats.

Oldenlandia aridosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
