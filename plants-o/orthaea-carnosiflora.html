Orthaea carnosiflora

Orthaea carnosiflora, commonly known as the fleshy-flowered orthaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
