Origanum purpurescens

Origanum purpurescens, commonly known as purple savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, pale green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its aromatic leaves and flowers are often used in cooking and herbal remedies.

Purple savory thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and herb gardens.
