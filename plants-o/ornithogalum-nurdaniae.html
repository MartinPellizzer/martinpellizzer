Ornithogalum nurdaniae

Ornithogalum nurdaniae, commonly known as the Nurdan's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
