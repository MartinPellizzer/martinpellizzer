Ophidion nekidapono

Ophidion nekidapono, commonly known as the Nekidapono snake orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, snake-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its flowers are usually a mix of green and yellow, with intricate patterns that resemble scales.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
