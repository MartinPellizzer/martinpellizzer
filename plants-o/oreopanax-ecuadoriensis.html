Oreopanax ecuadoriensis

Oreopanax ecuadoriensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian oreopanax, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its palmate leaves and woody stems. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Oreopanax ecuadoriensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
