Orthochilus Milnei

Orthochilus milnei is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America. Known for its unique, spurred petals and vibrant coloration, this plant has captivated botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests at high elevations. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered endangered by conservationists. This article explores the biology, ecology, and conservation challenges of this remarkable species.
