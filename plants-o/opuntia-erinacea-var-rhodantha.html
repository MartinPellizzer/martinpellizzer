Opuntia erinacea var. rhodantha

Opuntia erinacea var. rhodantha, commonly known as the crimson spine cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
