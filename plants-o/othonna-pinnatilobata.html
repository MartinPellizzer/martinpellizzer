Othonna pinnatilobata

Othonna pinnatilobata, commonly known as the narrow-lobed othonna, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and small, narrow leaves arranged in a pinnate pattern. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Othonna pinnatilobata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
