Ornitrophe pinnata

Ornitrophe pinnata, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that give it its common name, and its flowers are typically clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
