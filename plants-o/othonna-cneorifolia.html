Othonna cneorifolia

Othonna cneorifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for arid environments. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Othonna cneorifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
