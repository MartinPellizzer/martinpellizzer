Oreosedum monregalense

Oreosedum monregalense, commonly known as the Monregalense stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in the spring. Oreosedum monregalense is often found growing on rocky slopes and in well-drained soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
