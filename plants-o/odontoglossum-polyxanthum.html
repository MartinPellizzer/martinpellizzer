Odontoglossum polyxanthum

Odontoglossum polyxanthum, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Odontoglossum polyxanthum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
