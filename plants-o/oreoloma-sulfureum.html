Oreoloma sulfureum

Oreoloma sulfureum, commonly known as the sulfur yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and orange floral colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and feature a unique combination of vibrant hues.

Oreoloma sulfureum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
