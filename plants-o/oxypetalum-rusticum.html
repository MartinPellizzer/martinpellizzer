Oxypetalum rusticum

Oxypetalum rusticum, commonly known as the roughleaf oxypetalum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often rough to the touch. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, making it suitable for ground cover in shady or partially shaded areas.

Oxypetalum rusticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
