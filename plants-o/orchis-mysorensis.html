Orchis mysorensis

Orchis mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a perennial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by a rhizome and a rosette of basal leaves. The plant produces a single, erect flowering stem that bears a few to several showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
