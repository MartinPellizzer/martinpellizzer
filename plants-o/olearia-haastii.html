Olearia haastii

Olearia haastii, commonly known as the Haast's sunhemp, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features silvery-white, woolly leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, providing it with a unique appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Olearia haastii is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
