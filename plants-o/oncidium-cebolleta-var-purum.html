Oncidium cebolleta var. purum

Oncidium cebolleta var. purum, commonly known as the Cebolleta Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically blooms during the late spring to early summer season, adding vibrant color to gardens and indoor spaces. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
