Obione stipitata

Obione stipitata, commonly known as the stippled obione, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and flies.
