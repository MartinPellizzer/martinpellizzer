Origanum ehrenbergii

Origanum ehrenbergii, commonly known as desert oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and rocky environments. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong fragrance and medicinal properties.

Origanum ehrenbergii is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
