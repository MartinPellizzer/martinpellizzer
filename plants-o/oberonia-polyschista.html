Oberonia polyschista

Oberonia polyschista, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short rhizome with a cluster of fleshy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or golden in color, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
