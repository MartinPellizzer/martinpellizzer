Oleandra neriifolia

Oleandra neriifolia, commonly known as the white oleander, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Oleandra neriifolia is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

However, it is important to note that all parts of this plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
