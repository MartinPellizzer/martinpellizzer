Oxygonum delagoense var. strictum

Oxygonum delagoense var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved waterwheel plant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern around the stems. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
