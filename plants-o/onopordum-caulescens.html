Onopordum caulescens

Onopordum caulescens, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branching stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, seed-filled pods. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

Onopordum caulescens is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
