Oberonia sarasinorum

Oberonia sarasinorum, commonly known as the Sarasin's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
