Olfersia paleacea

Olfersia paleacea, commonly known as the white orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid rainforest environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Olfersia paleacea is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in traditional practices.
