Oberonia anguina

Oberonia anguina, commonly known as the snake orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and distinctive, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces small, intricate flowers with sepals and petals that are often dark purple or brown, resembling a snake's head. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

Oberonia anguina is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
