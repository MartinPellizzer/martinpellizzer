Ouret glabrata

Ouret glabrata, commonly known as the smooth ouret, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. Ouret glabrata is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
