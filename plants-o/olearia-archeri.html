Olearia archeri

Olearia archeri, commonly known as the mountain snowbell, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Olearia archeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
