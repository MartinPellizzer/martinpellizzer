Onopordum orientale

Onopordum orientale, commonly known as the oriental thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spiny, gray-green leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its robust structure and is often found in rocky or arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Onopordum orientale is both ornamental and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
