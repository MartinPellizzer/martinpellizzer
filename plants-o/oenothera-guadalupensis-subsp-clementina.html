Oenothera guadalupensis subsp. clementina

Oenothera guadalupensis subsp.

clementina, commonly known as the Guadalupe evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening.
