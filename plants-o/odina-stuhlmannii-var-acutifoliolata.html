Odina stuhlmannii var. acutifoliolata

Odina stuhlmannii var. acutifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved odina, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and lanceolate leaves with acute leaflets. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
