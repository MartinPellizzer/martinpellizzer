Ononis legitima

Ononis legitima, commonly known as the common whitlowgrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with a taproot and hairy stems that often lie prostrate on the ground. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Ononis legitima is adapted to dry, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.
