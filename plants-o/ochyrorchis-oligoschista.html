Ochyrorchis oligoschista

Ochyrorchis oligoschista, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces slender, upright stems with linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, arranged in a loose spike, and are pollinated by insects.

Ochyrorchis oligoschista is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
