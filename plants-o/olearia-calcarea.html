Olearia calcarea

Olearia calcarea, commonly known as the limestone daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils, often found on rocky or limestone outcrops. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Olearia calcarea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
