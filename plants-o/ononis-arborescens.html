Ononis arborescens

Ononis arborescens, commonly known as tree-like everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub-like perennial that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs, and it has a fibrous root system.

Ononis arborescens is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.
