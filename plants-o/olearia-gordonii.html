Olearia gordonii

Olearia gordonii, commonly known as the Kauri daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Olearia gordonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
