Ononis Glabrescens

Ononis glabrescens is a hardy flowering plant known for its resilience in challenging environments. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in alpine and arctic regions. This species produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its low-growing habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens and wildflower displays. Despite its delicate appearance, Ononis glabrescens is well adapted to survive in harsh climates.
