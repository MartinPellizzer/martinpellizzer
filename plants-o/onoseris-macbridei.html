Onoseris macbridei

Onoseris macbridei, commonly known as the MacBride's goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. The plant features yellow ray florets and yellow disk florets, giving it a bright, sun-like appearance. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Onoseris macbridei is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
