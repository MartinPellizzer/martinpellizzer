Otillis

Otillis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that bear vibrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, hence its common name. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, orange, and white.
