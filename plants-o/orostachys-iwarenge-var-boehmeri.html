Orostachys iwarenge var. boehmeri

Orostachys iwarenge var. boehmeri, commonly known as the Boermer's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, often blue-green leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and container gardening.
