Ochna apetala

Ochna apetala, commonly known as the white ochna, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough or hairy texture.

Ochna apetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
