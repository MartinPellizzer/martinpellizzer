Octas spicata

Octas spicata, commonly known as the spiky octopus plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions of South Africa.

It features striking, twisted, spiky stems that resemble the tentacles of an octopus, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its architectural form makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant.
