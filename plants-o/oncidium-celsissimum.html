Oncidium celsissimum

Oncidium celsissimum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in horticultural displays. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.

Oncidium celsissimum is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
