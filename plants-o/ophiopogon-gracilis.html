Ophiopogon gracilis

Ophiopogon gracilis, commonly known as the slender lilyturf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features grass-like foliage with narrow, dark green leaves that form dense clumps. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in late summer, arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Ophiopogon gracilis is valued for its hardiness and ability to form attractive ground covers.
