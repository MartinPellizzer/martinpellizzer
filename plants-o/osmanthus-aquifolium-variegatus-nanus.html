Osmanthus aquifolium variegatus-nanus

Osmanthus aquifolium variegatus-nanus, commonly known as the variegated dwarf osmanthus, is a compact shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and gold variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, which give way to orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
