Ornithogalum inclusum

Ornithogalum inclusum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, tuberous rhizome and produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is notable for its striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum inclusum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
