Oxydectes mollis

Oxydectes mollis, commonly known as the softleaf dandelion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features a basal rosette of soft, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Oxydectes mollis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
