Olearia lirata

Olearia lirata, commonly known as silverweed, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Olearia lirata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for native insects and birds.
