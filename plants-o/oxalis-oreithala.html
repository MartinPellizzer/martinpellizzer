Oxalis oreithala

Oxalis oreithala, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Oxalis oreithala is also noted for its adaptability to various climates, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
