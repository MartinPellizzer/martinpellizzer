Onagra erythrosepala

Onagra erythrosepala, commonly known as prairie evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in prairie and dry grassland habitats.

Onagra erythrosepala is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
