Ononis spinosa var. angustifolia

Ononis spinosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered with small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
