Oncidium × colnagoi

Oncidium × colnagoi, commonly known as the Colnago's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a variety of floral forms and colors.

It is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments with good air circulation. The plant typically produces pendulous racemes of flowers, which can range from yellow to orange and red hues. Oncidium × colnagoi is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and adaptability to different growing conditions.
