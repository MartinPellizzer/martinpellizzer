Odontoglossum constrictum f. pallens

Odontoglossum constrictum f. pallens, commonly known as the pale-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking pale yellow to white flowers with intricate patterns of dark markings. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, with erect, hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral appearance and relatively compact size.
