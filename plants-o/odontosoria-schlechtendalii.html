Odontosoria schlechtendalii

Odontosoria schlechtendalii, commonly known as the broad-leafed holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Osmundaceae.

It is characterized by its robust, upright fronds that are typically divided into broad, lance-shaped segments. The plant produces sporangia in clusters on the underside of its leaves, a feature typical of ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of forest ecosystems.
