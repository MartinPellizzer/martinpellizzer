Octadesmia elata

Octadesmia elata, commonly known as the slender waterwheel plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of narrow, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Octadesmia elata plays a role in providing habitat and oxygenation in aquatic ecosystems.
