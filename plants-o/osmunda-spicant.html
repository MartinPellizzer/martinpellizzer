Osmunda spicant

Osmunda spicant, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height.

The plant has a distinctive reproductive structure called a sorus, which contains the spores for propagation. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, allowing it to store nutrients and survive through harsh conditions.
