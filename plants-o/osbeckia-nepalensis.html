Osbeckia nepalensis

Osbeckia nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal willow herb, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, mountainous habitats. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Osbeckia nepalensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
