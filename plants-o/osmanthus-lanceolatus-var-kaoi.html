Osmanthus lanceolatus var. kaoi

Osmanthus lanceolatus var. kaoi, commonly known as the Kaoi osmanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its aromatic properties and ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its essential oils are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
