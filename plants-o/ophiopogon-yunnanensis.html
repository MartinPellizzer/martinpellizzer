Ophiopogon yunnanensis

Ophiopogon yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spathiphyllum, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in spathaceous inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ophiopogon yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
