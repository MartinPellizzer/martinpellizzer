Oxytropis nigrescens var. nigrescens

Oxytropis nigrescens var. nigrescens, commonly known as black locust, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compound leaves and yellow, pod-like fruits. The plant is native to parts of North America and is known for its hardy, drought-resistant nature. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer.

Its root system is deep and strong, allowing it to thrive in poor soil conditions.
