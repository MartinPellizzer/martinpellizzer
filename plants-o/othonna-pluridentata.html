Othonna pluridentata

Othonna pluridentata, commonly known as the many-toothed othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
