Orchiastrum pallidum

Orchiastrum pallidum, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family, which includes a wide variety of orchids. The plant produces small, pale-colored flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil in its natural habitat.
