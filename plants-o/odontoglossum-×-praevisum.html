Odontoglossum × praevisum

Odontoglossum × praevisum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum aemulans and Odontoglossum praevisum.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America, characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, thriving in the cooler mountainous climates of its native habitat. Its inflorescence consists of a spike bearing numerous flowers, each with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Odontoglossum × praevisum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under suitable conditions.
