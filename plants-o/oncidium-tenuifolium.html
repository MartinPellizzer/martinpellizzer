Oncidium tenuifolium

Oncidium tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive, intricate structure. The plant thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires good air circulation and moderate watering. It is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Oncidium tenuifolium is known for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year under proper conditions.
