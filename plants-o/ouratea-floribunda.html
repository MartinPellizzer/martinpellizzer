Ouratea floribunda

Ouratea floribunda, commonly known as the Brazilian beauty or white starwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light gray bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it an ornamental favorite. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
