Oxydectes tenuissima

Oxydectes tenuissima, commonly known as the slender marsh grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland environments in the eastern United States.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, exhibiting a low-growing habit with slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its rhizomatous growth system allows it to spread and form dense mats, which help stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
