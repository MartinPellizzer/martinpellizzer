Ononis maweana

Ononis maweana, commonly known as the African sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are usually succulent and adapted to arid environments, allowing it to conserve water efficiently.

Ononis maweana is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological resilience.
