Olax macrophylla

Olax macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf olax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Olacaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 20 centimeters in length. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by woody capsules containing seeds.

Olax macrophylla is often found in open woodlands and along forest edges, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
