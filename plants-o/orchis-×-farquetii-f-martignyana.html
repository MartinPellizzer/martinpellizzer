Orchis × farquetii f. martignyana

Orchis × farquetii f. martignyana, commonly known as the Martignyana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow-green lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, spurred flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
