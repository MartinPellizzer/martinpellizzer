Olax crassa

Olax crassa, commonly known as the rough olax, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Olax crassa is often found in dry, disturbed habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
