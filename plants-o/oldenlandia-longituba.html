Oldenlandia longituba

Oldenlandia longituba, commonly known as the slender spiderling, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
