Oxalis primulifolia

Oxalis primulifolia, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. It thrives in woodland habitats and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Oxalis primulifolia is often found in shaded areas and is known for its early blooming habit.
