Oberonia wappeana

Oberonia wappeana, commonly known as the Wapena orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the lowland rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with dark markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
