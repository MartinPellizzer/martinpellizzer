Onobrychis sintenisii

Onobrychis sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis' goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Onobrychis sintenisii is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
