Ononis nuristanica

Ononis nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan hedge mustard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species with distinctive four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters, and the plant produces small, round pods containing seeds.

Ononis nuristanica is valued for its resilience and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
