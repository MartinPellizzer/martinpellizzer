Oxalis darvalliana

Oxalis darvalliana, commonly known as the velvetleaf wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with a velvety texture and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread easily in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.

Oxalis darvalliana is also valued for its ability to thrive in humid climates and its role in ecological systems.
