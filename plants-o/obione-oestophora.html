Obione oestophora

Obione oestophora, commonly known as the purple orchid.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its striking violet and white petals. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, well-drained environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
