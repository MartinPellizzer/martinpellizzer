Ornduffia umbricola var. umbricola

Ornduffia umbricola var. umbricola, commonly known as the dark soil orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

It thrives in nutrient-poor, shaded forest floors, often growing in association with mycorrhizal fungi that aid in nutrient uptake. The plant features slender, dark green leaves and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive purple hue. Its unique adaptations to low-light conditions and poor soil make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
