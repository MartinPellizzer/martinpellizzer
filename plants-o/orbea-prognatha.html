Orbea prognatha

Orbea prognatha, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, which helps it survive in arid conditions.

Orbea prognatha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
