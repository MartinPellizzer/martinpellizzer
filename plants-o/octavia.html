Octavia

Octavia, commonly known as the octavia plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Octavia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, low-maintenance ornamental plants.
