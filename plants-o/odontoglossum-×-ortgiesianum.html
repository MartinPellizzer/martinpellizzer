Odontoglossum × ortgiesianum

Odontoglossum × ortgiesianum, commonly known as the orchid hybrid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Odontoglossum orchids, known for its vibrant and showy flowers. The plant typically produces large, colorful blooms with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by growers for its beauty and unique floral structure.
