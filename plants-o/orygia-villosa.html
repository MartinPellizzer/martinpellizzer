Orygia villosa

Orygia villosa, commonly known as the hairy orygia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Orygia villosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators, contributing to biodiversity in its native environment.
