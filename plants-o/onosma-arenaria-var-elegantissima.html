Onosma arenaria var. elegantissima

Onosma arenaria var. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant sand-verbena, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine, silky hairs, and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands, where it contributes to the local flora's biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
