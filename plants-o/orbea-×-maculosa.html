Orbea × maculosa

Orbea × maculosa, commonly known as the spotted orbea, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, often spiny stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to xeriscapes and succulent collections.
