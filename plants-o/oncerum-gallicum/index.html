Oncerum Gallicum

Oncerum gallicum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the humid forests of western Africa. Known for its striking appearance and unique ecological adaptations, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. This species belongs to the orchid family and exhibits fascinating reproductive strategies. Its delicate flowers and specialized root system make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, Oncerum gallicum faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
