Oenanthe globulosa subsp. kunzei

Oenanthe globulosa subsp.

kunzei, commonly known as the narrowleaf water-dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils along stream banks and wet meadows.

The plant features slender, aromatic stems and small, white or greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in wetland environments.
