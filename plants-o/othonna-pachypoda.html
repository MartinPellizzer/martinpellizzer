Othonna pachypoda

Othonna pachypoda, commonly known as the broad-leaved othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among gardeners in arid regions.
