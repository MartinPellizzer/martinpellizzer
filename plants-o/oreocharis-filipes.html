Oreocharis filipes

Oreocharis filipes, commonly known as the slender oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Oreocharis filipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
