Ornithogalum neurostegium subsp. eigii

Ornithogalum neurostegium subsp.

eigii, commonly known as the silverleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, linear leaves that are often silver-white in color, giving it its common name.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
