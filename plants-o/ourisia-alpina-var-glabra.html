Ourisia alpina var. glabra

Ourisia alpina var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth alpine ourisia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
