Ononis diffusa var. serratoides

Ononis diffusa var. serratoides, commonly known as the serrated sandwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to harsh environments and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
