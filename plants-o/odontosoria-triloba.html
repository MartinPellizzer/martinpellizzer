Odontosoria triloba

Odontosoria triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clustered fronds that have a distinctive three-lobed shape. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a rough texture, with serrated margins.

Odontosoria triloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
