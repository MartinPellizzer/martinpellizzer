Oncidium Sanguinolentum

Red oncidium, scientifically known as Oncidium sanguinolentum, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central and South America. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, long-lasting red flowers that resemble the color of blood, hence its dramatic common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. The Red oncidium is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its resilience and ability to bloom repeatedly. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
