Ornithogalum asphodeloides

Ornithogalum asphodeloides, commonly known as the asphodel lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
