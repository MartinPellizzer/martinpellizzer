Olax nana

Olax nana, commonly known as the dwarf olax, is a small shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, featuring compact foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are often white or pale pink, and it produces small, round berries that are edible.

Olax nana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.
