Onobrychis ebenoides f. albiflora

Onobrychis ebenoides f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered vetch, is a botanical variant of the species Onobrychis ebenoides.

It is a perennial legume native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
