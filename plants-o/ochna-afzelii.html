Ochna afzelii

Ochna afzelii, commonly known as the African buttertree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features smooth, greyish-brown bark that becomes fissured with age, and its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a hairy underside. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, woody, orange-red fruits.

Ochna afzelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
