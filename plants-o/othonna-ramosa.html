Othonna ramosa

Othonna ramosa, commonly known as the branched othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, branching stem structure with dense clusters of small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, hairy stalks. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna ramosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
