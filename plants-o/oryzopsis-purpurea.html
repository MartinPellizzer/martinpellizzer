Oryzopsis purpurea

Oryzopsis purpurea, commonly known as purple grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and striking purple to reddish tints on its inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Oryzopsis purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
