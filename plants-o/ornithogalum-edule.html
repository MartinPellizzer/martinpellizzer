Ornithogalum edule

Ornithogalum edule, commonly known as edible lily or white lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is notable for its edible bulbs, which are often consumed cooked or raw. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
