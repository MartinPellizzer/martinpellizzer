Onosma microcalyx

Onosma microcalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved onosma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that help it conserve water in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly areas.

Onosma microcalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
