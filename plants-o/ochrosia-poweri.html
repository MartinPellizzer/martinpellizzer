Ochrosia poweri

Ochrosia poweri, commonly known as the dwarf firecracker, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces striking, tubular, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

Ochrosia poweri is well adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
