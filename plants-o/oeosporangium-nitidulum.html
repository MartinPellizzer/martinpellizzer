Oeosporangium nitidulum

Oeosporangium nitidulum, commonly known as the smooth-sporangial fern, is a member of the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, evergreen fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a smooth, glossy texture. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are responsible for spore dispersal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations for efficient reproduction in moist, low-light conditions.
