Oncidium splendidum var. xanthinum

Oncidium splendidum var. xanthinum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green floral spikes, which bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip, typical of the Oncidium genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
