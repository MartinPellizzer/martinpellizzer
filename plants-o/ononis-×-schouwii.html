Ononis × schouwii

Ononis × schouwii, commonly known as the hybrid yellow sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, hairy stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in opposite pairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Ononis × schouwii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and unique floral display.
