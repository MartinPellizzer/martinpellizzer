Orobus laevigatus

Orobus laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is smooth and not fringed.

Orobus laevigatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
