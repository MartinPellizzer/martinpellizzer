Oplismenus benthamii

Oplismenus benthamii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by three-winged seeds. Oplismenus benthamii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in disturbed habitats.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and competitive nature.
