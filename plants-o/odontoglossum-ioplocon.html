Odontoglossum ioplocon

Odontoglossum ioplocon, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Odontoglossum ioplocon is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
