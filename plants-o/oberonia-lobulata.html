Oberonia lobulata

Oberonia lobulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring dark green, broad leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a unique, spurred lip.

Oberonia lobulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
