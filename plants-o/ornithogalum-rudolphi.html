Ornithogalum rudolphi

Ornithogalum rudolphi, commonly known as the African star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
