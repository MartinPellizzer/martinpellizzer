Oncidium aspecum

Oncidium aspecum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, butterfly-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with contrasting purple or red markings. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium aspecum is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
