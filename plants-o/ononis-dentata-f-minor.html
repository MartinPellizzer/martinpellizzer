Ononis dentata f. minor

Ononis dentata f. minor, commonly known as the lesser toothed cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has a mat-forming habit with hairy, branched stems and pinnately divided leaves. Its flowers are arranged in clusters and are pollinated primarily by insects.

The plant thrives in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
