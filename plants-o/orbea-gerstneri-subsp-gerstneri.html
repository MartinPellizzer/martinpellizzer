Orbea gerstneri subsp. gerstneri

Orbea gerstneri subsp.

gerstneri, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat.

The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces showy inflorescences that attract pollinators like bees and sunbirds.

This subspecies is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
