Osmanthus urceolatus

Osmanthus urceolatus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its ornamental value is largely due to its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.

It is often cultivated for its fragrance and used in traditional Chinese medicine.
