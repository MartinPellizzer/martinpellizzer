Ormenis africana

Ormenis africana, commonly known as the African barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ormenis africana is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
