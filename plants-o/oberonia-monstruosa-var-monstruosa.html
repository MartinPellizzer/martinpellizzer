Oberonia monstruosa var. monstruosa

Oberonia monstruosa var. monstruosa, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth. Its unique morphology and vibrant floral display make it a popular specimen in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
