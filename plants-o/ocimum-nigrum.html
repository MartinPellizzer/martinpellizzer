Ocimum nigrum

Ocimum nigrum, commonly known as black pepper.

It is a flowering plant native to India and belongs to the Lamiaceae family. The plant produces small, dark purple flowers and has aromatic, glandular leaves. Its fruit, called a peppercorn, is the dried, unripe fruit of the plant.

The essential oil of Ocimum nigrum is used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.
