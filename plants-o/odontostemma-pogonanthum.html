Odontostemma pogonanthum

Odontostemma pogonanthum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Odontostemma pogonanthum is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain areas.
