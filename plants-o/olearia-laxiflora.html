Olearia laxiflora

Olearia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender flaxflower, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silver-gray foliage and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with many slender, leafy branches. It is adapted to grow in sunny, well-drained soils, often found in coastal or inland scrub habitats.

Olearia laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
