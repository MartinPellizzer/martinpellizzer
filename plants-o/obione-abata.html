Obione abata

Obione abata, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often used in traditional medicine.

Obione abata thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.
