Ormosia tsangii

Ormosia tsangii, commonly known as the Chinese tallowtree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces pods containing several winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Ormosia tsangii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions for similar purposes.

However, it can become invasive in some areas, posing ecological challenges.
