Oncerum

Oncerum, commonly known as the golden cup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Oncerum typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance plants.
