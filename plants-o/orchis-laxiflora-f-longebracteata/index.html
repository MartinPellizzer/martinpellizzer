Orchis Laxiflora F. Longebracteata

Orchis laxiflora f. longebracteata is a rare and fascinating orchid species native to specific regions of Europe. This botanical variant is known for its distinctive floral morphology and ecological significance. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats, often found in woodland areas with rich soil. Its unique bract structure sets it apart from other members of the Orchis genus. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing conservation challenges.
