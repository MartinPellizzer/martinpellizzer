Orbivestus obionifolius

Orbivestus obionifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf starbloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves, which are covered in a fine, waxy coating to reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in rocky soil and access deep groundwater.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
