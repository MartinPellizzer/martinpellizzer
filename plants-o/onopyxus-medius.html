Onopyxus medius

Onopyxus medius, commonly known as the common tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters. The plant is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in dry conditions. It is often found along riverbanks and in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
