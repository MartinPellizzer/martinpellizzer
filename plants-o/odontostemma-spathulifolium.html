Odontostemma spathulifolium

Odontostemma spathulifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spathaceous bract. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
