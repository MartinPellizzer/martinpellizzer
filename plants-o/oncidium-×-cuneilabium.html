Oncidium × cuneilabium

Oncidium × cuneilabium, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a wide range of color variations, including yellow, orange, and red.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America and is known for its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Oncidium × cuneilabium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
