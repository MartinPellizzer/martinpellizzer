Oplismenus compositus var. loliaceus

Oplismenus compositus var. loliaceus, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
