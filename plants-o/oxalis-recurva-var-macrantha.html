Oxalis recurva var. macrantha

Oxalis recurva var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered wood sorrel, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive appearance.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil.
