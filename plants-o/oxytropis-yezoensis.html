Oxytropis yezoensis

Oxytropis yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

The plant produces small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting various pollinators. Oxytropis yezoensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
