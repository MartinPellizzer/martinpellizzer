Ophiobotrys zenkeri

Ophiobotrys zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's climbing fig, is a tropical vine native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
