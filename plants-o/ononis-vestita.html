Ononis vestita

Ononis vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a mat-forming habit, often found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are typically covered in a fine, velvety hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ononis vestita is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
