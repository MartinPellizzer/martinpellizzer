Odontostemma delavayi

Odontostemma delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's snake plant, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a suitable houseplant.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
