Oncidium harrisonianum

Oncidium harrisonianum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium harrisonianum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
