Orthosiphon iodocalyx

Orthosiphon iodocalyx, commonly known as Java tea or Malabar tea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically purple or pink in color, arranged in whorls along the stem.
