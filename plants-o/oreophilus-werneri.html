Oreophilus werneri

Oreophilus werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its elegant, spurred orchid blooms that typically range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
