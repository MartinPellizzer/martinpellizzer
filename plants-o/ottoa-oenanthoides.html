Ottoa oenanthoides

Ottoa oenanthoides, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with slender, branched stems and small, showy pink to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it blooms from late spring to early summer. Ottoa oenanthoides thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
