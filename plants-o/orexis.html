Orexis

The plant has a complex root system that allows it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils, and its leaves are covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Orexis produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom only during specific lunar phases, making it a subject of fascination for botanists.
