Onobrychis heterophylla

Onobrychis heterophylla, commonly known as the variable burr medic, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. Its leaves are alternate and compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

Onobrychis heterophylla is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and forage production.
