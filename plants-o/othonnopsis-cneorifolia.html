Othonnopsis cneorifolia

Othonnopsis cneorifolia, commonly known as the rock rose or coastal rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with woody stems and small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a whitish powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.

Othonnopsis cneorifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
