Orobus myrtifolius var. gracilis

Orobus myrtifolius var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf orobus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its growth habit is low and compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to well-drained soils.
