Otomeria micrantha

Otomeria micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered otomeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically has narrow, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Otomeria micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
