Oxygonum limbatum

Oxygonum limbatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed waterwheel, is a perennial aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving water and is often found in marshes and ponds.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
