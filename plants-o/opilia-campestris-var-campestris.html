Opilia campestris var. campestris

Opilia campestris var. campestris, commonly known as the field opilia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying shades of white or pale pink.

Opilia campestris var. campestris is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.
