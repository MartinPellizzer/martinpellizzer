Onobrychis sativa var. subinermis

Onobrychis sativa var. subinermis, commonly known as goat's rue, is a leguminous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant has a deep taproot system, allowing it to thrive in poor soil conditions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
