Ormosia velutina

Ormosia velutina, commonly known as velvet ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, glossy bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, followed by elongated pods containing flat, brown seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.

The tree is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
