Ornithochilus fuscus

Ornithochilus fuscus, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a slender, wiry stem with several ovate to lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-brown, and lack the typical showy colors of many orchids.

It is a rare and seldom-seen species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
