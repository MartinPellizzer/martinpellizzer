Oligochaetochilus bracteatus

Oligochaetochilus bracteatus, commonly known as the bracteate mossy orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

The plant has a unique growth habit, with its roots forming dense mats that help stabilize soil in its habitat. Its leaves are typically leathery and covered in fine hairs, which aid in moisture retention.
