Ossaea lima

Ossaea lima, commonly known as the lima moss or ocean moss, is a unique aquatic plant found in coastal regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching structure and its ability to thrive in saline environments. The plant's leaves are typically thin and ribbon-like, adapted for efficient water absorption. Ossaea lima plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal sediments and supporting marine biodiversity.

Its reproductive structures are specialized for dispersal by water currents, ensuring its survival in dynamic coastal ecosystems.
