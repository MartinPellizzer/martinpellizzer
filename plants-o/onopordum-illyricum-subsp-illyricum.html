Onopordum illyricum subsp. illyricum

Onopordum illyricum subsp.

illyricum, commonly known as the Illyrian thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It features a robust root system and erect, spiny stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height.

The plant produces large, purple to pink flower heads that are surrounded by stiff, bristly leaves. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
