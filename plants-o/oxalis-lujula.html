Oxalis lujula

Oxalis lujula, commonly known as the Lujula wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release its seeds. Oxalis lujula thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
