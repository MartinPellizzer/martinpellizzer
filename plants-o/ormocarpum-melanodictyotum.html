Ormocarpum melanodictyotum

Ormocarpum melanodictyotum, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
