Ozothamnus floribundus

Ozothamnus floribundus, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features silvery-gray foliage and produces small, yellow, dandelion-like flowers. It is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued as an ornamental plant.

Ozothamnus floribundus is adapted to coastal environments and can tolerate salt spray and wind.
