Oberonia rufilabris var. nepalensis

Oberonia rufilabris var. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepalese red-labied oberonia, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking red-brown labellum and elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories at high altitudes. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green, with a distinctive central lip.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
