Ormosia sericeolucida

Ormosia sericeolucida, commonly known as silky ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with silky, hairy surfaces, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in inflorescences that resemble spikes.

The fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
