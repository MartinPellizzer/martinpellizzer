Orobus baicalensis

Orobus baicalensis, commonly known as Baikal skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is valued in traditional Chinese medicine.
