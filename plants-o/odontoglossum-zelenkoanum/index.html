Odontoglossum Zelenkoanum

Odontoglossum zelenkoanum is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. This species is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts. Its unique beauty and delicate nature make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
