Osmites bellidiastrum

Osmites bellidiastrum, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in sandy, coastal soils and is often found in dune environments. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It has heart-shaped leaves and is adapted to withstand salt spray and wind.

Osmites bellidiastrum plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
