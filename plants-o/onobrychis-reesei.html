Onobrychis reesei

Onobrychis reesei, commonly known as the reese's burr vetch, is a leguminous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces hairy, triangular pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Onobrychis reesei is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
