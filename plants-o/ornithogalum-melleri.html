Ornithogalum melleri

Ornithogalum melleri, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

Ornithogalum melleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
