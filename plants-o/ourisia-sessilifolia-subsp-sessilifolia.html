Ourisia sessilifolia subsp. sessilifolia

Ourisia sessilifolia subsp.

sessilifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Ourisia, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in cool, humid climates.
