Origanum glaucum

Origanum glaucum, commonly known as blue germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils contribute to its use in herbal medicine and culinary applications.
