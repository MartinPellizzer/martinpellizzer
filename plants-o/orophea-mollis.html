Orophea mollis

Orophea mollis, commonly known as the silky orophea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
