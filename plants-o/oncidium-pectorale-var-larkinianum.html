Oncidium pectorale var. larkinianum

Oncidium pectorale var. larkinianum, commonly known as the Larkin's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and somewhat leathery in texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
