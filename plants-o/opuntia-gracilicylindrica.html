Opuntia gracilicylindrica

Opuntia gracilicylindrica, commonly known as the slender cylindrical prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in sharp, barbed spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, which are followed by edible, red-fleshed fruits. Opuntia gracilicylindrica is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its structure and growth habits make it well-suited for xeriscaping and sustainable landscaping in similar climates.
