Oldenlandia herbacea var. dolichantha

Oldenlandia herbacea var. dolichantha, commonly known as the slender blue mistflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in late summer to early autumn. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for shaded gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ethereal appearance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
