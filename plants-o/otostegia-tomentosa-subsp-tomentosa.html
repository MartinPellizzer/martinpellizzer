Otostegia tomentosa subsp. tomentosa

Otostegia tomentosa subsp.

tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly otostegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, white, woolly foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
