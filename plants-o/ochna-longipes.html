Ochna longipes

Ochna longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its woody structure and showy, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture, while its fruits are capsule-like and contain several seeds.

Ochna longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
