Ocotea gentryi

Ocotea gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are typically small drupes that contain a single seed.

Ocotea gentryi is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests, contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
