Oncidium × domingense

Oncidium × domingense, commonly known as the Domino Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Oncidium and Cattleya species.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in a raceme. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to flourish.

Oncidium × domingense is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
