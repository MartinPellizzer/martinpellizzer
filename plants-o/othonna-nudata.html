Othonna nudata

Othonna nudata, commonly known as the naked othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, giving it a unique appearance.

Othonna nudata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
