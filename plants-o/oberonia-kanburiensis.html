Oberonia kanburiensis

Oberonia kanburiensis, commonly known as the Kanburi orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and bark of its host.

Oberonia kanburiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
