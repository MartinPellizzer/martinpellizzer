Oriciopsis glaberrima

Oriciopsis glaberrima, commonly known as the African sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it is drought-tolerant, adapting well to arid environments.

Oriciopsis glaberrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with similar climates.
