Origanum × bilgilii

Origanum × bilgilii, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong medicinal and culinary uses.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes as well as for its aromatic properties.
