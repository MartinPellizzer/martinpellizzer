Oedina brevispicata

Oedina brevispicata, commonly known as the short-spurred orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white with a distinctive, short spur at the base of the petals.

Oedina brevispicata is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
