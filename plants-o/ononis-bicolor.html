Ononis bicolor

Ononis bicolor, commonly known as two-color sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its flowers are pink or purple and are arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Ononis bicolor is adapted to sandy and rocky soils, often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.
