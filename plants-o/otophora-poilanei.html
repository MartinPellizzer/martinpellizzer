Otophora poilanei

Otophora poilanei, commonly known as the African orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Otophora poilanei is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
