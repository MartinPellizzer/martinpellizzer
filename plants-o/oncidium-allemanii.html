Oncidium allemanii

Oncidium allemanii, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with intricate patterns resembling butterfly wings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium allemanii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
