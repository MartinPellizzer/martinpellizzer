Oenothera subacaulis

Oenothera subacaulis, commonly known as the slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Oenothera subacaulis is a member of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.
