Oenanthe javanica subsp. linearis

Oenanthe javanica subsp.

linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 cm wide.

The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and marshes.

Its root system is fibrous and can tolerate waterlogged soils, making it a common species in riparian zones.
