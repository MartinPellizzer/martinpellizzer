Onobrychis lasistanica

Onobrychis lasistanica, commonly known as the Lasista burclover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in dry and rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.
