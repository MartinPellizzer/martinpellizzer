Onosma gigantea var. hispida

Onosma gigantea var. hispida, commonly known as the hairy giant onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
