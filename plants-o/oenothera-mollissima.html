Oenothera mollissima

Oenothera mollissima, commonly known as the silky evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie and open woodland habitats.

Oenothera mollissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
