Oreopanax cheirophyllus

Oreopanax cheirophyllus, commonly known as the golden-leaf arrowroot, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
