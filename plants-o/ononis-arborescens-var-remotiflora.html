Ononis arborescens var. remotiflora

Ononis arborescens var. remotiflora, commonly known as the distant-flowered hedgehogbean, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a cylindrical pod containing several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
