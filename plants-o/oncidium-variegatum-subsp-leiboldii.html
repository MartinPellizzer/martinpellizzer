Oncidium variegatum subsp. leiboldii

Oncidium variegatum subsp.

leiboldii, commonly known as the Leibold's Oncidium, is a species of orchid native to South America. It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that typically appear in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
