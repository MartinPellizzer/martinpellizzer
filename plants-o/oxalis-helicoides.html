Oxalis helicoides

Oxalis helicoides, commonly known as the spiral wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique spiral growth pattern and ornamental foliage make it a visually appealing addition to gardens.
