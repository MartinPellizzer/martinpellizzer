Ormosia laxa

Ormosia laxa, commonly known as the slender ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Ormosia laxa is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
