Onosma limitanea var. parviflora

Onosma limitanea var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are pink to purple in color, with five petals and are arranged in dense clusters.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
