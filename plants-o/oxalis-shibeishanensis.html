Oxalis shibeishanensis

Oxalis shibeishanensis, commonly known as the Shibeishan wood sorrel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Shibeishan region of Sichuan Province. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oxalis shibeishanensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and floral display.
