Onoseris linifolia

Onoseris linifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it its common name.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
