Ornithidium scullianum

Ornithidium scullianum, commonly known as the Scullian's feathercup, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
