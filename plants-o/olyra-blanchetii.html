Olyra blanchetii

Olyra blanchetii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink.

Olyra blanchetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
