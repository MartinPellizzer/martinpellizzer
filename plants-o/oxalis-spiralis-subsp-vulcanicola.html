Oxalis spiralis subsp. vulcanicola

Oxalis spiralis subsp.

vulcanicola, commonly known as the volcanic oxalis, is a succulent plant native to volcanic regions. It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple.

The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh environments make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
