Ornithogalum pyrenaicum var. sphaerocarpum

Ornithogalum pyrenaicum var. sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the spherical bulb ornithogalum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces a spherical, fleshy bulb that serves as its primary storage organ. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a rosette at the base.
