Ornithogalum abyssinicum

Ornithogalum abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It features a short, underground rhizome and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and can thrive in full sun.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
