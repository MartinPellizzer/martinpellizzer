Oreocereus celsianus

Oreocereus celsianus, commonly known as the Celsianus cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the Canary Islands.

It is a columnar cactus with a tall, cylindrical stem that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

Oreocereus celsianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
