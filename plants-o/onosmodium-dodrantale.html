Onosmodium dodrantale

Onosmodium dodrantale, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found near water sources.

Onosmodium dodrantale is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
