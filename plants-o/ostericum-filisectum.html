Ostericum filisectum

Ostericum filisectum, commonly known as the threadleaf ostericum, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features delicate, narrow leaves that are often described as thread-like, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Ostericum filisectum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
