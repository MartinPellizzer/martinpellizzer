Osbeckia rosea

Osbeckia rosea, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to arid environments.

Osbeckia rosea is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited for xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
