Oncidium compressicaule

Oncidium compressicaule, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically arranged in a raceme. Oncidium compressicaule thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both horticultural and botanical studies.
