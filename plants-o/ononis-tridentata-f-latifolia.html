Ononis tridentata f. latifolia

Ononis tridentata f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hedgehogbean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are broader than those of the typical species. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by legumes containing several seeds. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
