Ochna fischeri

Ochna fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's ochna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its elliptical leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are capsules that release small, winged seeds when mature.

Ochna fischeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
