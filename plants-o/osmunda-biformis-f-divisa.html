Osmunda biformis f. divisa

Osmunda biformis f. divisa, commonly known as the divided water fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive fronds.

It features bipinnate leaves that are divided into two distinct parts, giving it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments, often found near water bodies. Its rhizomes are extensively branched and help in anchoring the plant in wet soils.

The fronds are typically green and can reach lengths of up to several feet, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
