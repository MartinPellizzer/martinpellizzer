Oyedaea boliviana

Oyedaea boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky soil in which it grows.

Oyedaea boliviana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
