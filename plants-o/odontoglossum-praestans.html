Odontoglossum praestans

Odontoglossum praestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems that bear numerous showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically violet to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often lighter in hue. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking floral display.
