Oncidium pescatorei

Oncidium pescatorei, commonly known as the Pescatore's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features pendant, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow to orange in color. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium pescatorei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
