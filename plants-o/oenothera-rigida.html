Oenothera rigida

Oenothera rigida, commonly known as the rigid evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect, stiff stems and yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oenothera rigida is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
