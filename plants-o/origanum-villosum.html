Origanum villosum

Origanum villosum, commonly known as woolly oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in culinary applications for its aromatic leaves, which have a mild oregano flavor.

Origanum villosum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential antimicrobial properties.
