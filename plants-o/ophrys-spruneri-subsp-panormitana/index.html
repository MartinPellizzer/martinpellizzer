Ophrys Spruneri Subsp. Panormitana

Ophrys spruneri subsp. panormitana is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the Mediterranean region. This orchid is known for its unique floral structure and mimicry of female wasps, which plays a crucial role in its pollination strategy. Despite its ecological importance, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival in the wild. This article explores its biology, habitat, and conservation status.
