Origanum × dumanii

Origanum × dumanii, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. It is widely used in culinary applications for its pungent, herbaceous flavor.

Additionally, it has medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
