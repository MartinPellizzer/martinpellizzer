Otostegia fruticosa

Otostegia fruticosa, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, compact growth with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, round fruits. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Otostegia fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
