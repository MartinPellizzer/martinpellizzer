Oncidium macropetalum var. chrysopterum

Oncidium macropetalum var. chrysopterum, commonly known as the golden wing orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, pendulous flowers with golden-yellow petals and a distinctive orange lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
