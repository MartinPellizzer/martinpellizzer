Odontarrhena tavolarae

Odontarrhena tavolarae, commonly known as the Tavolara orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Sardinia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in rocky, coastal habitats and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
