Operculina tuberosa

Operculina tuberosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Operculina tuberosa is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
