Ocotea oblonga

Ocotea oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed.
