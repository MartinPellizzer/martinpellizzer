Opuntia platyacantha var. gracilior

Opuntia platyacantha var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender flat-tongue cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems that are typically green and covered in small spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
