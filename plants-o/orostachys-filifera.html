Orostachys filifera

Orostachys filifera, commonly known as the threadleaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to arid regions of Asia.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically thread-like in appearance, adapted to store water. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or white flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its low-growing habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive and hardy ornamental plant.
