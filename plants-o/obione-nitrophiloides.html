Obione nitrophiloides

It is characterized by its small, closely packed leaves that form dense mats on moist, nitrogen-rich soils. Its reproductive structures, called gametangia, are typically located on the underside of the leaves.

Obione nitrophiloides plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
