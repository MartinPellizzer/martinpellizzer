Onopordum magrebiense

Onopordum magrebiense, commonly known as the Maghreb thistle, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in harsh conditions.

Its flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
