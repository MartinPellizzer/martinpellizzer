Otophora pycnostachys

Otophora pycnostachys, commonly known as the dense-stalked orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense arrangement of flowering stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Otophora pycnostachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
