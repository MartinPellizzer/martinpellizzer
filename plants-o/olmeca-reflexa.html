Olmeca reflexa

Olmeca reflexa, commonly known as the Mexican reflexed orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with reflexed petals that give it its common name.

Olmeca reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
