Odontoglossum insleayi var. splendens

Odontoglossum insleayi var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Toothed Orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers with a distinctive toothed lip. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces showy, violet to purple blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
