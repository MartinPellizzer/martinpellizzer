Oenotrichia Tenera

Oenotrichia tenera is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom during the short rainy season. This species thrives in rocky soils and is often found in isolated pockets of vegetation. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
