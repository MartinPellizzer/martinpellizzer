Oxalis candida

Oxalis candida, commonly known as white wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each leaflet having a pale green to white coloration. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Oxalis candida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
