Olsynium floribundum

Olsynium floribundum, commonly known as the showy milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats. It produces small, cylindrical seed pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Olsynium floribundum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing nectar for pollinators in its ecosystem.
