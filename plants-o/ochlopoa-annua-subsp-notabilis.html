Ochlopoa annua subsp. notabilis

Ochlopoa annua subsp.

notabilis, commonly known as the slender annual grass, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides.

The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

The flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, characteristic of the grass family Poaceae.
