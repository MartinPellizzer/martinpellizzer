Odontoglossum × owenianum

Odontoglossum × owenianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchids.

It is native to the Andes Mountains in South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it prefers well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
