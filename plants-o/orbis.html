Orbis

Orbis, commonly known as the globe flower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, globe-shaped inflorescences that consist of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and thrives in partial to full shade. Orbis species are often found in woodland areas and are valued for their ornamental appeal.

The flowers bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
