Osteospermum fruticosum

Osteospermum fruticosum, commonly known as the hardy osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub-like perennial that produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features silver-green foliage and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Osteospermum fruticosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
