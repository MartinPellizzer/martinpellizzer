Opuntia platyacantha f. deflexispina

Opuntia platyacantha f. deflexispina, commonly known as the broad-leafed prickly pear with flexed spines, is a unique variant of the prickly pear cactus.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems that are often ribbed and covered in clusters of spines. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are modified into flat, fleshy stems, which store water for survival in arid environments.

This variety is native to Mexico and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
