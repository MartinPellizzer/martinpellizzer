Orites fragrans

Orites fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features strap-shaped leaves and produces showy, often white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Orites fragrans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
