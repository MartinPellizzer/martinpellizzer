Odontoglossum × elegantius

Odontoglossum × elegantius, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as purple, pink, and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its native high-altitude environment. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Odontoglossum × elegantius requires cool temperatures, high humidity, and bright, indirect light to flourish.
