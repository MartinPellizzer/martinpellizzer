Ononis spinosa subsp. maritima

Ononis spinosa subsp.

maritima, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa. It is a low-growing perennial with silver-blue foliage and spiny, cylindrical stems.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers in racemes during the summer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to salty, windy environments.

Its ornamental value and ability to stabilize coastal dunes make it an important plant in maritime ecosystems.
