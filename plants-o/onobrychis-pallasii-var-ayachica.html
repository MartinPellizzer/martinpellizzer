Onobrychis pallasii var. ayachica

Onobrychis pallasii var. ayachica, commonly known as Ayachica burr clover, is a perennial legume native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces hairy, spiny pods that contain small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in erosion control and as a forage plant.
