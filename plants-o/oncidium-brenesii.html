Oncidium brenesii

Oncidium brenesii, commonly known as the Brenes orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and glossy. Oncidium brenesii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
