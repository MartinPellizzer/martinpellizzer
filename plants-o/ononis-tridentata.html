Ononis tridentata

Ononis tridentata, commonly known as three-toothed cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with creeping stems that form dense mats. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are typically lobed or toothed.

Ononis tridentata is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in harsh, sunny environments.
