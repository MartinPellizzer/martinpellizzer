Oxydectes subglabra

Oxydectes subglabra, commonly known as the smooth oxydectes, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves that are typically smooth and lanceolate in shape. The flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, featuring tubular corollas with two lips, and are often a vibrant pink or purple color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
