Oxalis fourcadei

Oxalis fourcadei, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blossoms.

Oxalis fourcadei is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
