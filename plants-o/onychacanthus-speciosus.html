Onychacanthus speciosus

Onychacanthus speciosus, commonly known as the showy onychacanthus, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy bracts that often resemble petals, though they are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting display.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
