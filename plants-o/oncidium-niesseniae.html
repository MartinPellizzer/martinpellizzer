Oncidium niesseniae

Oncidium niesseniae, commonly known as the Niessen's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in seasonal environments. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in shaded, moist habitats.

Oncidium niesseniae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
