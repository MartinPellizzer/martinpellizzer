Ooia

Ooia, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a tropical species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a subtle fragrance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ooia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
