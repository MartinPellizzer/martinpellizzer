Oxalis calligera

Oxalis calligera, commonly known as the curly oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate veins. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis calligera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
