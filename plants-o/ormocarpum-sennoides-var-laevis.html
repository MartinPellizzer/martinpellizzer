Ormocarpum sennoides var. laevis

Ormocarpum sennoides var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth senna, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain the seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
