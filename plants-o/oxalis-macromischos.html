Oxalis macromischos

Oxalis macromischos, commonly known as the giant wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with five petals. The plant produces small, round berries that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oxalis macromischos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
