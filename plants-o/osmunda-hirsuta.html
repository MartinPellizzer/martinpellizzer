Osmunda hirsuta

Osmunda hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy sword fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant has a distinctive rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are divided into segments with a hairy texture, giving it its common name.

Osmunda hirsuta thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
