Osbertia heleniastrum

Osbertia heleniastrum, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in alpine or rocky habitats.

Osbertia heleniastrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
