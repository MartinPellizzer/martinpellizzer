Odontarrhena floribunda

Odontarrhena floribunda, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, white or pale blue flowers are arranged in dense clusters, giving it a star-like appearance.

Odontarrhena floribunda is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
