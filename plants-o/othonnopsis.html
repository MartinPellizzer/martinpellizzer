Othonnopsis

Othonnopsis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, adding visual interest to its appearance. Othonnopsis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
