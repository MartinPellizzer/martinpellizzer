Ouratea Vellozoi

Ouratea vellozoi is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. It is known for its elegant, waxy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native habitat.
