Ononis cenisia var. subaristata

Ononis cenisia var. subaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, branching stems and small, often blue or violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes.

This variety is noted for its resilience to harsh conditions and its role in stabilizing sandy soils.
