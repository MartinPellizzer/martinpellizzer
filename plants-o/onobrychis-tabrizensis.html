Onobrychis tabrizensis

Onobrychis tabrizensis, commonly known as Tabriz burr clover, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are also a food source for various wildlife species.
