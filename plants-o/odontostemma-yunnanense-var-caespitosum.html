Odontostemma yunnanense var. caespitosum

Odontostemma yunnanense var. caespitosum, commonly known as the Yunnan tussock gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its clustered, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
