Oncidium hassleri

Oncidium hassleri, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy coating.

Oncidium hassleri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
