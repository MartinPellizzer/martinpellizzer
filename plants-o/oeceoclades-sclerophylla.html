Oeceoclades sclerophylla

Oeceoclades sclerophylla, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
