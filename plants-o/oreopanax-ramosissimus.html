Oreopanax ramosissimus

Oreopanax ramosissimus, commonly known as the mountain sarsaparilla, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit with multiple branching stems. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that are often red or yellow when mature.

Oreopanax ramosissimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
