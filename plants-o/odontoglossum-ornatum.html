Odontoglossum ornatum

Odontoglossum ornatum, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vivid colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its high-altitude, often cool and humid environment. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces numerous small, delicate flowers in clusters.

Odontoglossum ornatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
