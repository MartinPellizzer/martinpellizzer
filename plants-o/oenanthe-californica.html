Oenanthe californica

Oenanthe californica, commonly known as California water dropwort, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as streambanks and forest floors. The plant features slender, hollow stems and small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a mild, somewhat peppery scent.

The species is characterized by its aquatic-like appearance and its ability to thrive in wetland habitats.
