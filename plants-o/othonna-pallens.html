Othonna pallens

Othonna pallens, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Othonna pallens thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.
