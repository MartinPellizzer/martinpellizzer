Orbea taitica

Orbea taitica, commonly known as the Taiti orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of tubular, orange to red flowers. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

Orbea taitica is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
