Ormosia glauca

Ormosia glauca, commonly known as the blue ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of pale blue or white flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Ormosia glauca is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
