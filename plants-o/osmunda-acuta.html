Osmunda acuta

Osmunda acuta, commonly known as the sensitive fern, is a species of fern native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are sensitive to environmental changes, hence its common name. The plant has a unique life cycle involving both a sporophyte and a gametophyte stage. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots, which help anchor it in moist, shaded habitats.

Osmunda acuta thrives in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp environments, contributing to the ecological diversity of these areas.
