Oncidium uhlii

Oncidium uhlii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with green or red streaks. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium uhlii is popular among orchid growers for its relatively easy care requirements and long-lasting blooms.
