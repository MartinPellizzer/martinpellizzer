Odyendea

Odyendea, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture. Odyendea species are popular houseplants due to their ability to thrive in indoor conditions with moderate light and regular watering.

They are also valued for their ornamental beauty and ease of care.
