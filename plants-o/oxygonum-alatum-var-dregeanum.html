Oxygonum alatum var. dregeanum

Oxygonum alatum var. dregeanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf morning glory, is a tropical climbing plant characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves.

It produces striking trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant is native to Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
