Oxyanthus natalensis

Oxyanthus natalensis, commonly known as Natal oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxyanthus natalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
