Orchis × loreziana nothosubsp.

loreziana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Orchis morio and Orchis lutea. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe, particularly the British Isles.

The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive lip, typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
