Ouratea latepaniculata

Ouratea latepaniculata, commonly known as the panicle ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are alternate and consist of several elliptical or ovate leaflets. The plant produces showy, panicle-like inflorescences that are typically white or pale pink, attracting various pollinators. Its flowers are followed by small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
