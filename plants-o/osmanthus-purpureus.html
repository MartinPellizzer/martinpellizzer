Osmanthus purpureus

Osmanthus purpureus, commonly known as the purple osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically harvested in the winter months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, dark green surface.

Osmanthus purpureus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic foliage and flowers.
