Oncidium arietinum

Oncidium arietinum, commonly known as the Shrimp Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can last several weeks, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Oncidium arietinum is relatively easy to care for, making it suitable for both novice and experienced growers.
