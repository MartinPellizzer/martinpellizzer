Octamyrtus pleiopetala var. arfakensis

Octamyrtus pleiopetala var. arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfakensis octamyrtus, is a rare shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of primary forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
