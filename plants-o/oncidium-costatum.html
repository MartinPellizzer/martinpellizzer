Oncidium costatum

Oncidium costatum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium costatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
