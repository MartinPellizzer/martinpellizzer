Oncidium maculatum var. parviflorum

Oncidium maculatum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or brown markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like, green, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix for optimal growth.
