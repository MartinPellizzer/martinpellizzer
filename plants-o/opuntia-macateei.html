Opuntia macateei

Opuntia macateei, commonly known as the Huastec prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems that are covered in small spines and glochids. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Opuntia macateei is well adapted to arid environments and plays an important role in local ecosystems.
