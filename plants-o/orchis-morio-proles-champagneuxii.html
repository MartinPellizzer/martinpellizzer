Orchis morio proles champagneuxii

Orchis morio proles champagneuxii, commonly known as the Champagneux's Early Purple Orchid, is a rare and distinctive orchid species native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a yellow-green lip. The plant typically grows in woodland habitats, thriving in moist, shaded environments with well-drained soil. It is known for its delicate, spurred flowers and is often found in small, scattered populations.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in several European countries.
