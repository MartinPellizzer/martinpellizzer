Onobrychis tournefortii f. concolor

Onobrychis tournefortii f. concolor, commonly known as the smooth bartsia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bright yellow flowers clustered in racemes. The leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
