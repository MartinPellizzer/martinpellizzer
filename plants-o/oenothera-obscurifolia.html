Oenothera obscurifolia

Oenothera obscurifolia, commonly known as the dark-leaved evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant is part of the Onagraceae family and is known for its showy, fragrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
