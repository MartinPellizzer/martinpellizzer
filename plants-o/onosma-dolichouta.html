Onosma dolichouta

Onosma dolichouta, commonly known as the long-stemmed sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branched stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Onosma dolichouta thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
