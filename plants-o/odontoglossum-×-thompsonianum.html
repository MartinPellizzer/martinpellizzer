Odontoglossum × thompsonianum

Odontoglossum × thompsonianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value.

It is a hybrid between Odontoglossum orchids, typically featuring vibrant, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, high-altitude environments. It has fleshy, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous flowers.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized orchid growing conditions.
