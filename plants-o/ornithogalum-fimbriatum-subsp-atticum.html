Ornithogalum fimbriatum subsp. atticum

Ornithogalum fimbriatum subsp.

atticum, commonly known as the Attic Star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is adapted to rocky and dry soils, often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system.
