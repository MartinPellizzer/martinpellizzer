Osmanthus decorus

Osmanthus decorus, commonly known as the sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its ornamental value comes from both its aromatic blooms and its decorative foliage.

Osmanthus decorus is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
