Osmanthus × burkwoodii

Osmanthus × burkwoodii, commonly known as the Burkwood osmanthus, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn, typically from October to November. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and showy floral display.
