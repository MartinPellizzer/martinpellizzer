Orchadocarpa

Orchadocarpa, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family, which includes a wide variety of orchids. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.
