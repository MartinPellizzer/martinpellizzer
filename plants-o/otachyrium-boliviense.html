Otachyrium boliviense

Otachyrium boliviense, commonly known as the Bolivian feather grass, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate inflorescences that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
