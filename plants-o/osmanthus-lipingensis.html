Osmanthus lipingensis

Osmanthus lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping osmanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small yellow to golden flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The Liping osmanthus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its aromatic flowers used in tea and perfumery.
