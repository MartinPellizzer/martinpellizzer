Orthocarpus erianthus var. gratiosus

Orthocarpus erianthus var. gratiosus, commonly known as the silky orthocarpus, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

The plant has hairy foliage, which gives it its common name, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Orthocarpus erianthus var. gratiosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native insect populations.
