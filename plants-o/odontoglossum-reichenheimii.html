Odontoglossum reichenheimii

Odontoglossum reichenheimii, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a dark violet or purple color with intricate patterns. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Odontoglossum reichenheimii is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
