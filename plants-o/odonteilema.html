Odonteilema

Odonteilema, commonly known as the African horned vulture, is a plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and dark green, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its adaptability to arid environments makes it a resilient species in dry regions.

Odonteilema is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
