Ortachne tenuis

Ortachne tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Ortachne tenuis has lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
