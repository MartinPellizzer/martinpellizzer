Oeosporangium × ibericum

Oeosporangium × ibericum, commonly known as the hybrid spore-bearing plant, is a rare botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between two species of the Oeosporangium genus.

It exhibits unique morphological characteristics, including specialized structures for spore production. The plant's reproductive system is primarily dependent on the formation of spores within its sporangia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions.

Due to its limited distribution and genetic complexity, it is of particular interest to botanists studying hybridization in plant species.
