Oenothera tetragona var. sharpii

Oenothera tetragona var. sharpii, commonly known as the Sharpe's four-angle evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant in the Onagraceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the evening, and its distinctive four-angled stems.
