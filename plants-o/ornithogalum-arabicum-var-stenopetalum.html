Ornithogalum arabicum var. stenopetalum

Ornithogalum arabicum var. stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of the Middle East and North Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its bulbous root system, which is typical of the Liliaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum arabicum var. stenopetalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
