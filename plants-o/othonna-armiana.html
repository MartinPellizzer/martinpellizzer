Othonna armiana

Othonna armiana, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
