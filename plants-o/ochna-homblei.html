Ochna homblei

Ochna homblei, commonly known as the African star flower, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and a smooth, bark-like stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Ochna homblei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
