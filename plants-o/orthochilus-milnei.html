Orthochilus milnei

Orthochilus milnei, commonly known as the Milne's rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
