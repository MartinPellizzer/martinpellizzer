Oncidium × bockemuhliae

Oncidium × bockemuhliae, commonly known as the Bockemuhlia orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines the characteristics of its parent species, Oncidium and Bockemuhlia.

It is known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically has pseudobulbs and is native to regions with warm, humid climates. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
