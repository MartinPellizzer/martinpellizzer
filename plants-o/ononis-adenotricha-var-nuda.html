Ononis adenotricha var. nuda

Ononis adenotricha var. nuda, commonly known as the naked sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites. The plant has small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers with five petals. It is characterized by its reduced trichomes, giving it a smoother appearance compared to other varieties.

This species is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
