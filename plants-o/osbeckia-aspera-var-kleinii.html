Osbeckia aspera var. kleinii

Osbeckia aspera var. kleinii, commonly known as the rough osbeckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to regions of Asia, including China and Mongolia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features upright stems covered with stiff, hairy structures, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Osbeckia aspera var. kleinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
