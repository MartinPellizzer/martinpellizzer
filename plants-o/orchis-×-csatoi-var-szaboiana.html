Orchis × csatoi var. szaboiana

Orchis × csatoi var. szaboiana, commonly known as the Szabó's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Carpathian Basin.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a prominent labellum and spurred column. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats. Its flowers are usually a mix of violet and white, with a distinct fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
