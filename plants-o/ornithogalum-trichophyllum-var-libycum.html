Ornithogalum trichophyllum var. libycum

Ornithogalum trichophyllum var. libycum, commonly known as the Libyan star-of-Bethleem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant appearance.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
