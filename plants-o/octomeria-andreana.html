Octomeria andreana

Octomeria andreana, commonly known as the Andean octomeris, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Octomeria andreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
