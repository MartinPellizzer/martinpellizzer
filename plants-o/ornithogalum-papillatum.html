Ornithogalum papillatum

Ornithogalum papillatum, commonly known as the rough-leafed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, rough-textured foliage and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
