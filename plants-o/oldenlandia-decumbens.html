Oldenlandia decumbens

Oldenlandia decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of blue or purple, typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.
