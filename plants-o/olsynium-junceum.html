Olsynium junceum

Olsynium junceum, commonly known as the narrowleaf false nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Olsynium junceum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
