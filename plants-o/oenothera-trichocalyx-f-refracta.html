Oenothera trichocalyx f. refracta

Oenothera trichocalyx f. refracta, commonly known as the fragrant evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Oenothera, which includes various species of evening primroses. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
