Odontarrhena bertolonii

Odontarrhena bertolonii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of Madagascar. It has strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, violet-colored blooms.

Odontarrhena bertolonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
