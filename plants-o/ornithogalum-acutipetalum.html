Ornithogalum acutipetalum

Ornithogalum acutipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has linear, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern around the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum acutipetalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
