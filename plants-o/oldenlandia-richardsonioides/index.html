Oldenlandia Richardsonioides

Oldenlandia richardsonioides is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. This species is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along riverbanks. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental degradation.
