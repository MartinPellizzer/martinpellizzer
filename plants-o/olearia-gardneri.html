Olearia gardneri

Olearia gardneri, commonly known as the Stewart Island daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its white to pale yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the temperate climate of Stewart Island. It has a compact, shrub-like form with narrow, silvery-green leaves.

Olearia gardneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
