Ornithidium stenophyllum

Ornithidium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
