Ouratea osaensis

Ouratea osaensis, commonly known as the Osa mountain ouratea, is a species of flowering plant native to the Osa Peninsula in Costa Rica.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within the tropical rainforests.

Due to its restricted range and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
