Oyedaea trachyphylla

Oyedaea trachyphylla, commonly known as the rough-leaved oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to dry and well-drained conditions.
