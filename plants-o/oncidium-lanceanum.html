Oncidium lanceanum

Oncidium lanceanum, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a striking display of color and fragrance.

Oncidium lanceanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
