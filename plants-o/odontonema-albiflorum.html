Odontonema albiflorum

Odontonema albiflorum, commonly known as the whiteflower odontonema, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical structure includes a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement, making it a distinctive member of the Acanthaceae family.
