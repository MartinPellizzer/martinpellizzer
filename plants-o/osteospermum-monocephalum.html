Osteospermum monocephalum

Osteospermum monocephalum, commonly known as the single-headed osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It typically grows in well-drained soil and thrives in full sun.

Osteospermum monocephalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
