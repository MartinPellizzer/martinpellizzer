Oxalis caprina

Oxalis caprina, commonly known as the rock oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which resemble those of clover, and its clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Oxalis caprina is also valued for its resilience and low maintenance requirements.
