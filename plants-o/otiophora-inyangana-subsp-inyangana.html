Otiophora inyangana subsp. inyangana

Otiophora inyangana subsp.

inyangana, commonly known as the Inyangana lichen, is a member of the family Verrucariaceae. It is a crustose lichen that typically grows on rocky substrates in subtropical and tropical regions.

The thallus is tightly adherent, with a dark brown to black coloration. It lacks distinct lobes and has a granular appearance.

This lichen is often found in shaded, humid environments and is used in traditional medicine by local communities.
