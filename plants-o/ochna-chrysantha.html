Ochna chrysantha

Ochna chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow ochna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ochna chrysantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
