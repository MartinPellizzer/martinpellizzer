Othonna quinquedentata

Othonna quinquedentata, commonly known as the five-toothed othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
