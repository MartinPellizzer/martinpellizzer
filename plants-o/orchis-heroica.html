Orchis heroica

Orchis heroica, commonly known as the Hero's Orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are usually purple or violet in color, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with yellow or white. This species thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it a notable subject in botanical studies.
