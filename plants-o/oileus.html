Oileus

Oileus, commonly known as the oil plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has palmate leaves and produces capsules as its fruiting body. Oileus species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

They are sometimes cultivated for their ornamental value and for the oils extracted from their seeds.
