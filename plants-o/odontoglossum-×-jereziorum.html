Odontoglossum × jereziorum

Odontoglossum × jereziorum, commonly known as the Jerez orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments with high humidity, preferring well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
