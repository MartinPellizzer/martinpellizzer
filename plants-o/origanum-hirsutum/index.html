Origanum Hirsutum

Origanum hirsutum, commonly known as hairy oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. This plant is prized for its aromatic leaves and its ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils. It is often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine due to its strong flavor and potential health benefits. Origanum hirsutum is also valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature. Its presence in gardens and wild areas highlights its ecological and cultural significance.
