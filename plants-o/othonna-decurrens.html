Othonna decurrens

Othonna decurrens, commonly known as the narrowleaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Othonna decurrens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience.
