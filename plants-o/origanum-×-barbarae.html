Origanum × barbarae

Origanum × barbarae, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a perennial herb native to Turkey and the Balkans.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Origanum, which includes various types of oregano and marjoram. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
