Oncidium lietzei

Oncidium lietzei, commonly known as the Lietze's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in a spike. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium lietzei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
