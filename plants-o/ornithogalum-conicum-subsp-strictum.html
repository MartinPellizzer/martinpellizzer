Ornithogalum conicum subsp. strictum

Ornithogalum conicum subsp.

strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features slender, upright stems that bear narrow, linear leaves arranged in a rosette.

The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils in woodland or open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
