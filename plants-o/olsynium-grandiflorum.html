Olsynium grandiflorum

Olsynium grandiflorum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a sprawling, bushy habit. Its leaves are opposite, linear to lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Olsynium grandiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
