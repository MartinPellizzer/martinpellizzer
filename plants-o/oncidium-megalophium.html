Oncidium megalophium

Oncidium megalophium, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, with a vibrant mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Oncidium megalophium is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
