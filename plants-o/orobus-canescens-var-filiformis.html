Orobus canescens var. filiformis

Orobus canescens var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender orobus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its distinctive, grass-like appearance.

This variety is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
