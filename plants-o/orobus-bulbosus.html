Orobus bulbosus

Orobus bulbosus, commonly known as the bulbous orobus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its bulbous root system allows it to store water and nutrients, making it drought-tolerant.

Orobus bulbosus is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral.
