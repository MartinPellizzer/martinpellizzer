Onosma erecta var. pubiflora

Onosma erecta var. pubiflora, commonly known as the pubescent onosma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its common name.

This species is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
