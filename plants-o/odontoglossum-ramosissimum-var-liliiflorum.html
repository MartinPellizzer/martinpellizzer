Odontoglossum ramosissimum var. liliiflorum

Odontoglossum ramosissimum var. liliiflorum, commonly known as the lace orchid, is a species native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers with intricate, lace-like petals. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments with cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and have a delicate, fragrant scent.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
