Oncoba subtomentosa

Oncoba subtomentosa, commonly known as the silky tree or silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Oncoba subtomentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
