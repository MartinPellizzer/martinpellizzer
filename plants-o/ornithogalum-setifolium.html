Ornithogalum setifolium

Ornithogalum setifolium, commonly known as the thread-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners.
