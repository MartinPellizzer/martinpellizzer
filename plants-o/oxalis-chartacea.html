Oxalis chartacea

Oxalis chartacea, commonly known as the shield leaf wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate foliage and flowering habit.

Oxalis chartacea prefers partial to full shade and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
