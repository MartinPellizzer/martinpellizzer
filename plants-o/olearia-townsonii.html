Olearia townsonii

Olearia townsonii, commonly known as the New Zealand flax or mountain flax, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and alpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in forest margins and rocky habitats, and it plays a role in supporting local wildlife.

Olearia townsonii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been introduced to other parts of the world.
