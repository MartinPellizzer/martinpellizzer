Oncidium jarmilae

Oncidium jarmilae, commonly known as the Jarmila orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for epiphytic growth on trees or rocks.

Oncidium jarmilae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
