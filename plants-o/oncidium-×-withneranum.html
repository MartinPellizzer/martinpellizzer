Oncidium × withneranum

Oncidium × withneranum, commonly known as the Withner orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various Oncidium species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in a range of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces racemes of flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium × withneranum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
