Osbeckia crinita

Osbeckia crinita, commonly known as the silky osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate climates. The plant features soft, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Osbeckia crinita is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

It is also used in traditional medicine by some indigenous communities.
