Oreocharis longifolia var. multiflora

Oreocharis longifolia var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora orchid, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and has slender, branching stems. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
