Ornithogalum drakensbergense

Ornithogalum drakensbergense, commonly known as the Drakensberg star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to the Drakensberg Mountains in South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions with cool, moist conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
