Omentaria

Omentaria, commonly known as the "garden omentaria," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features showy, colorful blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically has broad, lance-shaped leaves and grows as an upright shrub or small tree. Its flowers are often clustered in panicles, adding to its ornamental value.

Omentaria thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.
