Oxalis caerulea

Oxalis caerulea, commonly known as the blue oxalis, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, and it produces small, triangular seed pods. Oxalis caerulea thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and delicate foliage.
