Odontonema flagellum

Odontonema flagellum, commonly known as the weeping willow fern, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features delicate, cascading fronds that give it a flowing, almost ethereal appearance. The plant has feather-like leaves that are typically bright green, with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Odontonema flagellum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique visual appeal and air-purifying qualities.
