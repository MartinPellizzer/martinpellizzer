Orchis angustifolia f. lehnertii

Orchis angustifolia f. lehnertii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale green or yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. This orchid is typically found in moist, calcareous soils, often in woodland or meadow habitats.
