Osmites dentata

Osmites dentata, commonly known as the toothed osmite, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months. Osmites dentata is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems that help it access groundwater.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
