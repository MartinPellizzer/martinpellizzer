Oxalis elegans

Oxalis elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated as an ornamental. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Oxalis elegans is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
