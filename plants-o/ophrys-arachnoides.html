Ophrys arachnoides

Ophrys arachnoides, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, which closely resembles a spider, aiding in its pollination strategy. The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats. Its flowers are usually pale green or brown, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of an insect.

Ophrys arachnoides is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats across southern Europe.
