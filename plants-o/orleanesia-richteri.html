Orleanesia richteri

Orleanesia richteri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Orleanesia richteri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
