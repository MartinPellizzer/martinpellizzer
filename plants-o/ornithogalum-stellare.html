Ornithogalum stellare

Ornithogalum stellare, commonly known as star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its bulbous underground stem, which serves as a storage organ. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassy areas and rocky outcrops.

The flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.
