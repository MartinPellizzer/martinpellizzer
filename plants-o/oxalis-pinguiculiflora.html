Oxalis pinguiculiflora

Oxalis pinguiculiflora, commonly known as the buttercup oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oxalis pinguiculiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
