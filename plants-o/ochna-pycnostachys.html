Ochna pycnostachys

Ochna pycnostachys, commonly known as the dense-stamen ochna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptic or ovate in shape. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, with showy yellow petals that attract pollinators.

The fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
