Oncidium brienianum var. rufida-maculis

Oncidium brienianum var. rufida-maculis, commonly known as the Rufidemaculated Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of yellow and orange. Its blooms are adorned with distinctive red or orange maculations, giving it its varietal name.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
