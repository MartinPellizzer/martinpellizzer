Oenothera godetia

Oenothera godetia, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its stems are erect and branching.

Oenothera godetia is also known for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
