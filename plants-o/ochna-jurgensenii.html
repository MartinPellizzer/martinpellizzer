Ochna jurgensenii

Ochna jurgensenii, commonly known as Jurgensen's ochna, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Ochna jurgensenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
