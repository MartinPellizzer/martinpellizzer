Ornithogalum gildenhuysii

Ornithogalum gildenhuysii, commonly known as the Gildenhuys' star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant is characterized by its underground bulb, which serves as a storage organ for nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum gildenhuysii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
