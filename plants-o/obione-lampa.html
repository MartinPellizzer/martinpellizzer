Obione lampa

Obione lampa, commonly known as the nightshade vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a bright red berry that contains several seeds, often used in traditional medicine.
