Opuntia microdasys var. pallida

Opuntia microdasys var. pallida, commonly known as the pale prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to the Mediterranean region.

It features flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in clusters of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunaf, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
