Oncidium hedyosmum

Oncidium hedyosmum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, yellow and green flowers resembling butterflies. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Oncidium hedyosmum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
