Osmanthus dinggyensis

Osmanthus dinggyensis, commonly known as the Yunnan osmanthus, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aromatic properties.

Osmanthus dinggyensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of essential oils.
