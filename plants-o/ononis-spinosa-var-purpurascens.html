Ononis spinosa var. purpurascens

Ononis spinosa var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are pink or purple, often with five petals. Native to arid and rocky habitats, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

This variant is distinguished by its vibrant floral coloration and is often found in Mediterranean climates.
