Ornithogalum longicollum

Ornithogalum longicollum, commonly known as the long-necked star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Ornithogalum longicollum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
