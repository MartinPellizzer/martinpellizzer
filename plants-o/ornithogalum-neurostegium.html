Ornithogalum neurostegium

Ornithogalum neurostegium, commonly known as the silverleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a silvery or grayish bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in gardens for its elegant appearance.
