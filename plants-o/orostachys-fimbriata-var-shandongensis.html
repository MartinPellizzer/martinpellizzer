Orostachys fimbriata var. shandongensis

Orostachys fimbriata var. shandongensis, commonly known as the Shandong stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and fimbriated (fringed) leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a notable species in the Crassulaceae family.
