Ornithogalum dregeanum

Ornithogalum dregeanum, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, or green. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The flowers typically bloom in spring or early summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to the landscape.
