Ononis mauritii

Ononis mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

Ononis mauritii is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils, often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
