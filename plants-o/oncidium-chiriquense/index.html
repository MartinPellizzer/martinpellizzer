Oncidium Chiriquense

Oncidium chiriquense is a tropical orchid native to Central America. It is known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements. Oncidium chiriquense is a great choice for both novice and experienced growers.
