Orthophytum rafaelii

Orthophytum rafaelii, commonly known as the Rafael's orthophytum, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Orthophytum rafaelii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
