Oldenlandia halaei

Oldenlandia halaei, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, green berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its foliage is covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
