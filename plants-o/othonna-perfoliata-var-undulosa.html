Othonna perfoliata var. undulosa

Othonna perfoliata var. undulosa, commonly known as the undulating othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, undulating, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique leaf morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
