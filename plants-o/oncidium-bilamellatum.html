Oncidium bilamellatum

Oncidium bilamellatum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs and produces pendulous inflorescences with vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Oncidium bilamellatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
