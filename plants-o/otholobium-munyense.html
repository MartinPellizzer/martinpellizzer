Otholobium munyense

Otholobium munyense, commonly known as the Munyense burr bush, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume features such as compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its small, hairy leaves and yellow flowers are typical of the genus Otholobium.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in degraded areas.
