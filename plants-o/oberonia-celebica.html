Oberonia celebica

Oberonia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces a cluster of leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in a spike, and it typically blooms during the wet season.

Oberonia celebica is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
