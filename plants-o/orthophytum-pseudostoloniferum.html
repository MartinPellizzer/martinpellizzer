Orthophytum pseudostoloniferum

Orthophytum pseudostoloniferum, commonly known as the Stoloniferous Orthophytum, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and the presence of stolons, which are horizontal stems that can root and form new plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spiny appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique growth pattern and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
