Omphalea ankaranensis

Omphalea ankaranensis, commonly known as the Madagascar moonseed, is a climbing shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, spiny fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, lowland areas.
