Ononis pubescens subsp. paniculata

Ononis pubescens subsp.

paniculata, commonly known as the hairy honesty, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant produces cylindrical, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its hairy foliage and showy flowers make it a notable species in its native habitat.
