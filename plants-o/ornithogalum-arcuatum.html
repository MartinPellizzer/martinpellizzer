Ornithogalum arcuatum

Ornithogalum arcuatum, commonly known as the bowstring lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
