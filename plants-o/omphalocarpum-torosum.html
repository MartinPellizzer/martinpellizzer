Omphalocarpum torosum

Omphalocarpum torosum, commonly known as the Torosum burrweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to dry conditions. The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
