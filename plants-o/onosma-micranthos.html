Onosma micranthos

Onosma micranthos, commonly known as the narrow-flowered sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.
