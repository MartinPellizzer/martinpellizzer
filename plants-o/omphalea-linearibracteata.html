Omphalea linearibracteata

Omphalea linearibracteata, commonly known as the narrow-bract euphorbia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant is characterized by its simple, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded areas.
