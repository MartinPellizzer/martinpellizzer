Oplismenus undulatifolius

Oplismenus undulatifolius, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Oplismenus undulatifolius is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
