Onobrychis tommasinii f. italica

Onobrychis tommasinii f. italica, commonly known as Italian burr medic, is a perennial leguminous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil improvement and forage production.
