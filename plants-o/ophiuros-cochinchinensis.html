Ophiuros cochinchinensis

Ophiuros cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese starfish plant, is a rare and unique species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves that resemble the arms of a starfish, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually a vibrant green, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Despite its striking appearance, it is not commonly cultivated due to its specific habitat requirements and limited distribution.
