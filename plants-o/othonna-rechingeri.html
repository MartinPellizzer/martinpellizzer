Othonna rechingeri

Othonna rechingeri, commonly known as the silver bush, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, adapted to store water in its thick leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna rechingeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
