Ouratea nigroviolacea

Ouratea nigroviolacea, commonly known as the black-violet ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and striking violet to purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.
