Odontostemma glandulosum

Odontostemma glandulosum, commonly known as the sticky root plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Odontostemma glandulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
