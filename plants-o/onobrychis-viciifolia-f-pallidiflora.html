Onobrychis viciifolia f. pallidiflora

Onobrychis viciifolia f. pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers arranged in racemes. The plant has a woody rootstock and is typically found in dry, rocky soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically in traditional herbal medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for erosion control in arid environments.
