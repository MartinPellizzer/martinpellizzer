Olearia elliptica

Olearia elliptica, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often found growing in coastal and forested areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Olearia elliptica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
