Osmia macrodon

Osmia macrodon, commonly known as the large carpenter bee, is a species of solitary bee native to North America.

It is characterized by its robust body, metallic green or blue coloration, and a distinctive, elongated face. The plant it pollinates includes a variety of flowering plants, particularly those with open, shallow blossoms. Osmia macrodon plays a vital role in pollination, contributing to the reproduction of many plant species.

Its foraging behavior and preference for certain flowers make it an important pollinator in its ecosystem.
