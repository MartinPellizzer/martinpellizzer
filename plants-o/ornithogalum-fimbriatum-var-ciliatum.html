Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. ciliatum

Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. ciliatum, commonly known as the fringed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped, hairy leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive fringed appearance. The plant grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
