Ochna buettneri

Ochna buettneri, commonly known as the African star, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features elliptical, glossy green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochna buettneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
