Oxymeris polystachya

Oxymeris polystachya, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in dense, terminal clusters. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several pairs of elliptical leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Oxymeris polystachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
