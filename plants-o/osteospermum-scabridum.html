Osteospermum scabridum

Osteospermum scabridum, commonly known as the rough osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. The plant has rough, hairy leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
