Ononis capitata

Ononis capitata, commonly known as white sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Ononis capitata is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it tolerates harsh environmental conditions.
