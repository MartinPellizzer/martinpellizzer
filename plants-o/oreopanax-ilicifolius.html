Oreopanax ilicifolius

Oreopanax ilicifolius, commonly known as the holly-leaf oreopanax, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with leathery, dark green leaves that have a holly-like appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are arranged alternately along the stems and are often adorned with small, sharp spines.

Oreopanax ilicifolius is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
