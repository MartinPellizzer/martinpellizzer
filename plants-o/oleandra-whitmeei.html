Oleandra whitmeei

Oleandra whitmeei, commonly known as the white oleander, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Oleandra whitmeei is also known for its adaptability to various climates and its ability to bloom throughout the year under favorable conditions.
