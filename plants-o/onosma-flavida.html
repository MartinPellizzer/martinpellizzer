Onosma flavida

Onosma flavida, commonly known as the yellow onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Onosma flavida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
