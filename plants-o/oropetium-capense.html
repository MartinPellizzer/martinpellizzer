Oropetium capense

Oropetium capense, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Oropetium capense is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in coastal areas.
