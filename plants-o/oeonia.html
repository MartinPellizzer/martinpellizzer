Oeonia

Oeonia, commonly known as the marsh woundwort, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

These plants are typically herbaceous and often found in wet or marshy environments. They are characterized by their hairy stems and showy blue or purple flowers. Oeonia species are known for their medicinal properties and have been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
