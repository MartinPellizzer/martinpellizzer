Orostachys iwarenge f. magna

Orostachys iwarenge f. magna, commonly known as the giant orostachys, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the growing season, typically in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners in dry climates.
