Oserya flagelliformis

Oserya flagelliformis, commonly known as the giant fan-leaf tree, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in diameter. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Oserya flagelliformis is known for its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in moist, humid environments.
