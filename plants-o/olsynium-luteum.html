Olsynium luteum

Olsynium luteum, commonly known as the yellow olsynium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Olsynium luteum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
