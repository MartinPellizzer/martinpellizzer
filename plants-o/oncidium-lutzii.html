Oncidium lutzii

Oncidium lutzii, commonly known as the Lutz's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding substrate.

Oncidium lutzii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
