Ornithogalum oxypetalum

Ornithogalum oxypetalum, commonly known as the white star lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
