Oncidium brachyandrum

Oncidium brachyandrum, commonly known as the short-armed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with red-brown markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Oncidium brachyandrum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
