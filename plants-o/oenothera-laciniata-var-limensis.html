Oenothera laciniata var. limensis

Oenothera laciniata var. limensis, commonly known as the Limens leafy evening primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, lobed leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers emit a mild fragrance and attract nocturnal pollinators such as moths.

Oenothera laciniata var. limensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
