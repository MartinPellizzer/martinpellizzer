Oncidium spectatissimum var. cinctum

Oncidium spectatissimum var. cinctum, commonly known as the Zebra Orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that display a vibrant mix of yellow and green hues with distinctive dark markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
