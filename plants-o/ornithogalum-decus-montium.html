Ornithogalum decus-montium

Ornithogalum decus-montium, commonly known as the mountain star-of-Bethleem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountainous regions, where it adapts well to cool and dry conditions.

The flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
