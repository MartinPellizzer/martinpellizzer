Otophora multijuga

Otophora multijuga, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows from a fibrous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Otophora multijuga is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
