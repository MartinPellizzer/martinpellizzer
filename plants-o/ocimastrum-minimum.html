Ocimastrum minimum

Ocimastrum minimum, commonly known as the small ocima, is a small shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its tiny, opposite leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in rocky or arid habitats, thriving in full sun.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
