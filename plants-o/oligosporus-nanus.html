Oligosporus nanus

Oligosporus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf spore plant, is a small, herbaceous species native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Sporaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique spore-producing structures. The plant produces minute, spherical spores that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Oligosporus nanus is often found in moist, shaded environments, thriving in humid climates.
