Ozoroa argyrochrysea

Ozoroa argyrochrysea, commonly known as the silver-leafed tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves, which are covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Ozoroa argyrochrysea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
