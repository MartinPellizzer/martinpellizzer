Ochyrorchis splendens

Ochyrorchis splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Sun-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which resemble sunflowers, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the poor soils of its native environment.

Ochyrorchis splendens is a perennial herb that produces showy, fragrant blooms during the spring and summer months.
