Ochna richardsiae

Ochna richardsiae, commonly known as Richards' ochna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ochnaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda. The plant is characterized by its attractive, glossy, elliptical leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Ochna richardsiae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
