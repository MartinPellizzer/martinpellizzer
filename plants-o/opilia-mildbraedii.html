Opilia mildbraedii

Opilia mildbraedii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale pink with a yellow center. The plant grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Opilia mildbraedii is a perennial herb with linear leaves and produces its flowers on tall, erect stems.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
