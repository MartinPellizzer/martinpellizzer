Ostrya multinervis

Ostrya multinervis, commonly known as the Chinese hemlock, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its finely toothed leaves and small, reddish-brown cones. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. Its bark is smooth and gray, with a distinctive flaky texture.
