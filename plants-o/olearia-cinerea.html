Olearia cinerea

Olearia cinerea, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Olearia cinerea is a perennial shrub that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in native plant gardens.
