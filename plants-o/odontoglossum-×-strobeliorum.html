Odontoglossum × strobeliorum

Odontoglossum × strobeliorum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum orchids, resulting in a unique combination of traits.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are showy, with a complex structure that includes a spurred lip and vibrant colors such as pink, purple, and white.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
