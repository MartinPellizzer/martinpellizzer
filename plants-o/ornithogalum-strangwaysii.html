Ornithogalum strangwaysii

Ornithogalum strangwaysii, commonly known as the red-flowered star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant blooms that appear in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
