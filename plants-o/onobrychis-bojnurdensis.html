Onobrychis bojnurdensis

Onobrychis bojnurdensis, commonly known as the Bojnurd clover, is a perennial legume native to Iran.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh climates. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions.

Onobrychis bojnurdensis is also valued for its potential in forage and soil stabilization.
