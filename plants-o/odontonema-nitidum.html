Odontonema nitidum

Odontonema nitidum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Odontonema nitidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
