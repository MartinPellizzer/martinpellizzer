Ononis natrix subvar. clausoniana

Ononis natrix subvar. clausoniana, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing, perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and sandy soils in Europe.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has a branching stem system with opposite, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in coastal areas or along dry slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, arid environments.
