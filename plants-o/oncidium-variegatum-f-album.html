Oncidium variegatum f. album

Oncidium variegatum f. album, commonly known as the White Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America. The plant typically features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
