Oncidium buchtienoides

Oncidium buchtienoides, commonly known as the Buchtienoides orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are strap-like, medium green, and often have a waxy texture.

Oncidium buchtienoides thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.
