Osbeckia stellata var. stellata

Osbeckia stellata var. stellata, commonly known as the starflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Osbeckia stellata var. stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
