Oreogrammitis nipponica

Oreogrammitis nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese wall fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, showcasing its adaptability to rocky substrates. Its spores are produced in sori that are clustered along the margins of the fronds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
