Ornithogalum ciliiferum

Ornithogalum ciliiferum, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped white or pale blue flowers. The plant is characterized by its fibrous roots and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms during the summer months and is adapted to arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
