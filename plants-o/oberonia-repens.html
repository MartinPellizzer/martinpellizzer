Oberonia repens

Oberonia repens, commonly known as the creeping oberonia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing plant with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Oberonia repens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
