Othonna alata

Othonna alata, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna alata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
