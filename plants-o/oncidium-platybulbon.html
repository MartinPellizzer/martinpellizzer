Oncidium platybulbon

Oncidium platybulbon, commonly known as the flat bulb orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its clustered pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring and summer, producing blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Oncidium platybulbon is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored plant among orchid enthusiasts.
