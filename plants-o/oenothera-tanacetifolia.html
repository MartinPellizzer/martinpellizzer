Oenothera tanacetifolia

Oenothera tanacetifolia, commonly known as golden-rod nightshade, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and prairies.
