Orbea sprengeri subsp. ogadensis

Orbea sprengeri subsp.

ogadensis, commonly known as the Ogaden orbea, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of East Africa. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, reducing water loss.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
