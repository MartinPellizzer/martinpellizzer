Ovieda

Ovieda, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus within the family Cistaceae. The plant is characterized by its evergreen shrub form and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. Ovieda thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it adaptable to rocky and arid environments.

Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
