Osmanthus forrestii

Osmanthus forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant belongs to the Oleaceae family and is valued for its aromatic properties. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Osmanthus forrestii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in traditional Chinese medicine.
