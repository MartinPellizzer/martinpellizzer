Ornithogalum schoenlandii

Ornithogalum schoenlandii, commonly known as the silverleaf star of Jordan, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, slender stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
