Oncidium javieri

Oncidium javieri, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features pendant, raceme-like inflorescences with brightly colored, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Oncidium javieri is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
