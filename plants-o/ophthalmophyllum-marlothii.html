Ophthalmophyllum marlothii

Ophthalmophyllum marlothii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, showy blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Ophthalmophyllum marlothii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
