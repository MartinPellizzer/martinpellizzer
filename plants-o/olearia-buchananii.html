Olearia buchananii

Olearia buchananii, commonly known as the mountain dandelion, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. The plant features silver-gray foliage and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Olearia buchananii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
