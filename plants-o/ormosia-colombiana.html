Ormosia colombiana

Ormosia colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian ormosia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in humid, shaded forest environments.

Ormosia colombiana is valued for its ornamental potential and has been studied for its ecological and possibly medicinal properties.
