Oxalis illapelina

Oxalis illapelina, commonly known as the Illapel oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis illapelina is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
