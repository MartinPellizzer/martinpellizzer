Osteospermum psammophilum

Osteospermum psammophilum, commonly known as the sand-plain osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking blue to violet flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Osteospermum psammophilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
