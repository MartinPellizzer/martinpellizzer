Oncostemum buxifolium

Oncostemum buxifolium, commonly known as the boxleaf oncostemum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves that resemble those of boxwood. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in horticulture.
