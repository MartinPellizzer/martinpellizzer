Odontocarya tamoides var. paupera

Odontocarya tamoides var. paupera, commonly known as the slender leaf lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in humid, temperate environments.

It features a pale green to yellowish thallus with a smooth, leathery texture. The plant exhibits a distinctive, elongated leaf-like structure with a central midrib. It is often found growing on trees, rocks, or soil in shaded areas.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for environmental conditions and its contribution to ecological diversity.
