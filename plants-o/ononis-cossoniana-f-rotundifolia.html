Ononis cossoniana f. rotundifolia

Ononis cossoniana f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed whitlow grass, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers. This plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry and sunny habitats. It is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of North Africa.
