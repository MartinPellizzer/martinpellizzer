Oryzopsis kingii

Oryzopsis kingii, commonly known as King's grass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Oryzopsis kingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.
