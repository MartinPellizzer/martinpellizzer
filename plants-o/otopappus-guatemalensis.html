Otopappus guatemalensis

Otopappus guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan goatweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster.

Otopappus guatemalensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
