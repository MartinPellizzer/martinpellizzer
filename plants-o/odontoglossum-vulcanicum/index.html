Odontoglossum Vulcanicum

Odontoglossum vulcanicum is a rare and striking orchid native to the volcanic regions of Central America. It thrives in high-altitude environments where temperatures fluctuate dramatically between day and night. Known for its vibrant, often iridescent flowers, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Its unique adaptations allow it to survive in harsh, nutrient-poor soils. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
