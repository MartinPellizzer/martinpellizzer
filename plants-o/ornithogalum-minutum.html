Ornithogalum minutum

Ornithogalum minutum, commonly known as the little star-of-Bethlehem, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, often growing in sandy soils. It blooms during the winter months, adding a touch of elegance to its natural habitat.

Ornithogalum minutum is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
