Opuntia gomei

Opuntia gomei, commonly known as the Gomei prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in sharp spines and woolly areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Opuntia gomei is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
