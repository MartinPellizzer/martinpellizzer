Oncidium maculatum var. psittacinum

Oncidium maculatum var. psittacinum, commonly known as the Golden Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a striking combination of vibrant yellow and green hues.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often marked with subtle patterns.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
