Onosma stellulata var. kubanica

Onosma stellulata var. kubanica, commonly known as the Kuban starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, glandular hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.
