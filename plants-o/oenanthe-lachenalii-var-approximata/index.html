Oenanthe Lachenalii Var. Approximata

Lachenal's water dropwort, scientifically known as Oenanthe lachenalii var. approximata, is a rare and elusive aquatic plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in cold, clear streams and lakes, where it forms dense clusters of slender, ribbed stems. Its small, white flowers bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to its aquatic habitat. Despite its beauty, the plant is highly sensitive to environmental changes and pollution. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique species from extinction.
