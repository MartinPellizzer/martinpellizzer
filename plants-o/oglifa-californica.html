Oglifa californica

Oglifa californica, commonly known as California oglifa, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Oglifa californica is adapted to coastal climates and prefers well-drained soils.
