Ornithogalum neurostegium var. ciliatum

Ornithogalum neurostegium var. ciliatum, commonly known as the woolly cowslip, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are broad, lanceolate, and often covered with fine, hairy structures, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
