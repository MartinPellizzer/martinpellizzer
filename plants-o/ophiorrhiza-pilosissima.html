Ophiorrhiza pilosissima

Ophiorrhiza pilosissima, commonly known as the silky ophiorrhiza, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Ophiorrhiza pilosissima is noted for its adaptability to wet conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
