Oldenlandia Sieberi Var. Congesta

Blue mistflower, scientifically known as Oldenlandia sieberi var. congesta, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the southeastern United States. It is prized for its delicate, blue-violet flowers that appear in late summer and early fall. This perennial herb thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Its unique appearance and ecological value make it a popular choice for native gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant among the general public.
