Omalotheca alpigena

Omalotheca alpigena, commonly known as the alpine omalotheca, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Omalotheca alpigena is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh mountain conditions.
