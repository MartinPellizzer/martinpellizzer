Osmanthus didymopetalus

Osmanthus didymopetalus, commonly known as the Chinese osmanthus, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is widely cultivated for its aromatic flowers used in perfumes and traditional Chinese medicine.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns red when mature.
