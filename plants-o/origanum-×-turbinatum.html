Origanum × turbinatum

Origanum × turbinatum, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant is valued for its pungent fragrance and is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
