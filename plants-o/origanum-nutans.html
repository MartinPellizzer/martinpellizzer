Origanum nutans

Origanum nutans, commonly known as leaning oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and small, pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its aromatic and medicinal properties.

Origanum nutans is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
