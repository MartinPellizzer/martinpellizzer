Onopordum × bilbilitanum var. viride

Onopordum × bilbilitanum var. viride, commonly known as the green thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, greenish-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

The varietal name 'viride' refers to its distinctive green hue, distinguishing it from other varieties of the species.
