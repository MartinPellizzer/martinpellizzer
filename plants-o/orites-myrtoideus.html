Orites myrtoideus

Orites myrtoideus, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces showy pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are generally narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Orites myrtoideus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
