Oncidium microchilum

Oncidium microchilum, commonly known as the miniature lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically blooms in late spring or early summer, and its flowers are often a mix of yellow, green, and white. Oncidium microchilum prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favored species among orchid enthusiasts.
