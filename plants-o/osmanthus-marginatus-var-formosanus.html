Osmanthus marginatus var. formosanus

Osmanthus marginatus var. formosanus, commonly known as the Taiwan osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a margin that is often slightly wavy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
