Oncidium vulcanicum

Oncidium vulcanicum, commonly known as the Vulcan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to regions of South America.

It features a compact growth habit with pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium vulcanicum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
