Onobrychis schuschajensis

Onobrychis schuschajensis, commonly known as the Schuschajev's vetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
