Onobrychis melanotricha var. villosa

Onobrychis melanotricha var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
