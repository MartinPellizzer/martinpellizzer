Ocotea atirrensis

Ocotea atirrensis, commonly known as the Atirrensis Ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, particularly in regions of Brazil and Peru. Its wood is valued for its fragrance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Ocotea atirrensis plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
