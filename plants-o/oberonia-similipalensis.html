Oberonia similipalensis

Oberonia similipalensis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
