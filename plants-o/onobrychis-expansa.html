Onobrychis expansa

Onobrychis expansa, commonly known as broadleaf burclover, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woods, pastures, and disturbed areas. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Additionally, it is a valuable forage plant for livestock due to its high nutritional value.
