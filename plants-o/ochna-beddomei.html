Ochna beddomei

Ochna beddomei, commonly known as the Beddome's ochna, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Ochna beddomei is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
