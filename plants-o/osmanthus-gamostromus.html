Osmanthus gamostromus

Osmanthus gamostromus, commonly known as the Chinese osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the autumn season. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically dark red when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green upper surface.

Osmanthus gamostromus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is widely cultivated for its aromatic flowers and medicinal properties.
