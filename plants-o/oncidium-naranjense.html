Oncidium naranjense

Oncidium naranjense, commonly known as the Orange-flowered Orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers with a distinctive yellow throat, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium naranjense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
