Onopordum illyricum var. giganteum

Onopordum illyricum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, spiny leaves and showy purple to pink flower heads that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It has a deep taproot system, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
