Othonna tagetes

Othonna tagetes, commonly known as the golden othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with fleshy, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
