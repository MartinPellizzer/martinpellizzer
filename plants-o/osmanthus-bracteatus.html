Osmanthus bracteatus

Osmanthus bracteatus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain a single seed. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture.

Osmanthus bracteatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aromatic flowers.
