Osteospermum monstrosum

Osteospermum monstrosum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like blooms with vibrant yellow centers and blue or purple petals. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, with gray-green, fuzzy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring through early autumn.
