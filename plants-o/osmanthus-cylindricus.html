Osmanthus cylindricus

Osmanthus cylindricus, commonly known as the cylindrical osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the autumn season. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically orange or red in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Osmanthus cylindricus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aromatic foliage and flowers.
