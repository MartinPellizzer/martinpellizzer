Ocotea Bijuga

Ocotea bijuga is a lesser-known tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America. It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes well-known species like avocado and cinnamon. This plant is valued for its aromatic wood and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of growing interest among botanists and conservationists. Despite its potential, Ocotea bijuga remains under-researched and threatened by habitat loss.
