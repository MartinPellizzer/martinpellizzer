Ononis candeliana

Ononis candeliana, commonly known as candleplant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, yellow flowers resembling candles, hence its common name. The plant has hairy, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to xeriscaped gardens.
