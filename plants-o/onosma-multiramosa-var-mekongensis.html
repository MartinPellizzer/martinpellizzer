Onosma multiramosa var. mekongensis

Onosma multiramosa var. mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong onosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
