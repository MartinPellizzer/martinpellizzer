Oldenlandia sphaerocarpa

Oldenlandia sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, round, green seed pods that contain numerous tiny, hairy seeds. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its overall appearance.
