Oncidium echinophorum

Oncidium echinophorum, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant inflorescences with multiple flowers in a raceme, often in shades of yellow and green. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees in its natural habitat.

Oncidium echinophorum requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
