Onosmodium occidentale var. sylvestre

Onosmodium occidentale var. sylvestre, commonly known as the western sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branched stems and small, pale blue to lavender flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
