Ochna verticillata

Ochna verticillata, commonly known as the clustered ochna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Ochna verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
