Oxypetalum erectum f. genuinum

Oxypetalum erectum f. genuinum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved oxypetalum, is a rare and unique botanical variant within the Oxypetalum genus.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, adding to its ornamental appeal. This form is distinguished by its more compact and refined structure compared to the typical species.

It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments.
