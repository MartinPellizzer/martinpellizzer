Olyra filiformis

Olyra filiformis, commonly known as the slender lily, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Olyra filiformis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its graceful form and delicate flowers.
