Ozothamnus argophyllus

Ozothamnus argophyllus, commonly known as silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has a compact growth habit and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
