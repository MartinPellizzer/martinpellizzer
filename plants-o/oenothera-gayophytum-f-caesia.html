Oenothera gayophytum f. caesia

Oenothera gayophytum f. caesia, commonly known as the blue evening primrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that open in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
