Oberonia cuneata

Oberonia cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Oberonia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. Oberonia cuneata thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
