Oxytropis tatarica

Oxytropis tatarica, commonly known as Tatarian milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to temperate regions of Eurasia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow or greenish-yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in dry meadows, rocky slopes, and disturbed soils.

Oxytropis tatarica is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
