Olearia steetzii

Olearia steetzii, commonly known as the Stewart's daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal and alpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, providing a distinctive texture to the plant.

Olearia steetzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
