Otacanthus azureus

Otacanthus azureus, commonly known as the blue flame cactus, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue and purple spines, which provide both protection and a unique aesthetic. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of blue and violet, often during the cooler months. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments.

Otacanthus azureus is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and eye-catching appearance.
