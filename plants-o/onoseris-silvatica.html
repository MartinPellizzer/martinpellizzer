Onoseris silvatica

Onoseris silvatica, commonly known as the forest aster, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. The plant features upright stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowers are small, yellowish to brownish, and are arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Onoseris silvatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
