Oryctanthus ruficaulis var. latifolius

Oryctanthus ruficaulis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved red-stemmed orchid, is a South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
