Ornithogalum unifolium subsp. plurifolium

Ornithogalum unifolium subsp.

plurifolium, commonly known as the many-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features a short, underground bulb and produces a single, erect stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant blooms with clusters of star-shaped, white or pale pink flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
