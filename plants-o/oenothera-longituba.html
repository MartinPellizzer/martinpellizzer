Oenothera longituba

Oenothera longituba, commonly known as the tall evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Oenothera longituba is often found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.
