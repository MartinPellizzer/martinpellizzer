Orchis morio f. robustior

Orchis morio f. robustior, commonly known as the robust greater orchid, is a variant of the greater orchid native to Europe.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in woodland and grassland habitats. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

The species is known for its robust growth and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
