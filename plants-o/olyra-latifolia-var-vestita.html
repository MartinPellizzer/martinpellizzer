Olyra latifolia var. vestita

Olyra latifolia var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet-leafed olyra, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing species within the Malvaceae family.
