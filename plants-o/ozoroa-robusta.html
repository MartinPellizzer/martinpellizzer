Ozoroa robusta

Ozoroa robusta, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ozoroa robusta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
