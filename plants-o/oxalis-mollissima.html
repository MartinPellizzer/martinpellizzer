Oxalis mollissima

Oxalis mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the wood sorrel family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Native to South America, it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Oxalis mollissima is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
