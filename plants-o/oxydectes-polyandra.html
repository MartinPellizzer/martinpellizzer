Oxydectes polyandra

Oxydectes polyandra, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its rapid growth makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.

However, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
