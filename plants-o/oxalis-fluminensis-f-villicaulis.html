Oxalis fluminensis f. villicaulis

Oxalis fluminensis f. villicaulis, commonly known as the velvet oxalis, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color, often adorned with intricate patterns of lighter green. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate white or pink flowers throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage, which is often more showy than its modest blooms.
