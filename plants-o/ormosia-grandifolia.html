Ormosia grandifolia

Ormosia grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters at the base of the leaves. Its fruits are woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds.

Ormosia grandifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
