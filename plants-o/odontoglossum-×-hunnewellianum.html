Odontoglossum × hunnewellianum

Odontoglossum × hunnewellianum, commonly known as the Hunnewell hybrid orchid, is a hybrid orchid resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchid species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often feature a vibrant mix of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its native habitat. Its botanical features include long, strap-like leaves and a complex, branched inflorescence.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated by growers for its striking floral display.
