Ozothamnus tephrodes

Ozothamnus tephrodes, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, and its flowers are small, yellow, and dandelion-like. It produces a capsule containing numerous seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Ozothamnus tephrodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
