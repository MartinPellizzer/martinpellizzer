Othonna chromochaeta

Othonna chromochaeta, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Othonna chromochaeta is typically found in rocky or coastal areas, where it demonstrates a degree of drought tolerance.
