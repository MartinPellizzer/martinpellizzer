Oenanthe pinnata

Oenanthe pinnata, commonly known as the clustered water dropwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. Its leaves are often finely toothed, and the flowers are arranged in dense umbels.
