Otites wolgensis

Otites wolgensis, commonly known as the Wolgen's otite, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow. Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
