Orbea conjuncta

Orbea conjuncta, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with upright stems and narrow leaves. Its flowers are often pink or purple and are arranged in racemes.

Orbea conjuncta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
