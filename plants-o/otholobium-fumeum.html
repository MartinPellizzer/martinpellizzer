Otholobium fumeum

Otholobium fumeum, commonly known as the smoke bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its fluffy, pale yellow to greenish flowers that resemble smoke. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Otholobium fumeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
