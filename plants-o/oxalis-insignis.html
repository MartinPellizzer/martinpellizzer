Oxalis insignis

Oxalis insignis, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veined pattern, and it produces small, bead-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Oxalis insignis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
