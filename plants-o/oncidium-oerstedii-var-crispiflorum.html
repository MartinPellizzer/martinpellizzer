Oncidium oerstedii var. crispiflorum

Oncidium oerstedii var. crispiflorum, commonly known as the Crispiflorum Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and slightly wavy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
