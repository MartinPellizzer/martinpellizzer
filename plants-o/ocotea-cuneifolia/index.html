Ocotea Cuneifolia

Ocotea cuneifolia is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America. It is known for its aromatic leaves and is commonly found in humid, lowland forests. This plant belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. Its unique shape and ecological importance make it a notable species in its habitat. Ocotea cuneifolia is valued for its wood and has cultural significance in some regions.
