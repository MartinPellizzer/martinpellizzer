Osmanthus scortechinii var. oblongus

Osmanthus scortechinii var. oblongus, commonly known as the Chinese osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The fruit, which is a small, dark red drupe, is also used in culinary and medicinal applications.
