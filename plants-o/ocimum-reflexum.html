Ocimum reflexum

Ocimum reflexum, commonly known as wild basil, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs such as mint and oregano. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a fragrant, slightly minty aroma. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically violet or purple in color.

Ocimum reflexum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
