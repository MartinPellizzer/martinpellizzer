Ormosia mexicana

Ormosia mexicana, commonly known as Mexican ormosia, is a species of leguminous tree native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Ormosia mexicana is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
