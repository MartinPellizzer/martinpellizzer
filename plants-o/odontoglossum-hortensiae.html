Odontoglossum hortensiae

Odontoglossum hortensiae, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often grown as a terrestrial orchid in cultivation.

Odontoglossum hortensiae is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and moisture conditions.
