Ochna gambleoides

Ochna gambleoides, commonly known as the Natal ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy berries that are a key feature of its botanical appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ochna gambleoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
