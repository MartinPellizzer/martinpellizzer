Onosma fastigiata subsp. mauretanica

Onosma fastigiata subsp.

mauretanica, commonly known as the Mauretanian upright onosma, is a perennial herb native to North Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
