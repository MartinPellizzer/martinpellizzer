Ononis ramosissima var. arganietorum

Ononis ramosissima var. arganietorum, commonly known as the Arganietum Ononis, is a variant of the flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in semi-arid habitats.
