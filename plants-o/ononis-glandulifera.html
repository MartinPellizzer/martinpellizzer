Ononis glandulifera

Ononis glandulifera, commonly known as the stickyweed or glandular onon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with small, lance-shaped leaves covered in glandular hairs that produce a sticky substance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming from spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and inland areas.

Ononis glandulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
