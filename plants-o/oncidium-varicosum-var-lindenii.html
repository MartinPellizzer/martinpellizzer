Oncidium varicosum var. lindenii

Oncidium varicosum var. lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting its growth habit.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
