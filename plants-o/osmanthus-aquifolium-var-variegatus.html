Osmanthus aquifolium var. variegatus

Osmanthus aquifolium var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated holly osmanthus, is a decorative shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
