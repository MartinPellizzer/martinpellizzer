Oncidium palaciosii

Oncidium palaciosii, commonly known as the Palacios orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red markings. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its environment. Oncidium palaciosii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
