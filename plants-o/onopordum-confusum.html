Onopordum confusum

Onopordum confusum, commonly known as the Mediterranean thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spiny leaves with a grayish-green color and produces purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its robust stem and is often found in rocky or dry soils. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions.

Onopordum confusum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
