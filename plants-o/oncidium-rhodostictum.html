Oncidium rhodostictum

Oncidium rhodostictum, commonly known as the red-streaked orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves and striking, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The flowers feature a combination of yellow, orange, and red hues, with a distinctive red streak running down the center of each petal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
