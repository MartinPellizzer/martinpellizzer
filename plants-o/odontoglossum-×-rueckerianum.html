Odontoglossum × rueckerianum

Odontoglossum × rueckerianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum aestivalium and Odontoglossum lindenii.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant features tall, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, violet, and white. Its inflorescence is a raceme of resupinate, spurred orchid flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
