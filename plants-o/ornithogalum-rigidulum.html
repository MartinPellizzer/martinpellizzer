Ornithogalum rigidulum

Ornithogalum rigidulum, commonly known as the rigid lily of the valley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Ornithogalum rigidulum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
