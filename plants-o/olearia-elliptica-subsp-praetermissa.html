Olearia elliptica subsp. praetermissa

Olearia elliptica subsp.

praetermissa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved iceplant, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-gray pubescence.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in coastal environments and is well adapted to salty air and strong winds. Olearia elliptica subsp.

praetermissa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
