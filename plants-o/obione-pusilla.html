Obione pusilla

Obione pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf tamarisk.

This small shrub is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It has small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Obione pusilla is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient conditions.
