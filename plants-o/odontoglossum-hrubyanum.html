Odontoglossum hrubyanum

Odontoglossum hrubyanum, commonly known as the Hrubyan's orchid, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, producing a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
