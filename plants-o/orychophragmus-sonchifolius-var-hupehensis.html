Orychophragmus sonchifolius var. hupehensis

Orychophragmus sonchifolius var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal medicine.
