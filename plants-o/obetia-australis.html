Obetia australis

Obetia australis, commonly known as the Australian rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodlands or heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white, green, or pale yellow.

Obetia australis is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in areas with limited rainfall.
