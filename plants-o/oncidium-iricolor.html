Oncidium iricolor

Oncidium iricolor, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Oncidium iricolor thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

It is popular among orchid growers for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
