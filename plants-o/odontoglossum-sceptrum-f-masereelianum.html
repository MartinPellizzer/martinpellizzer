Odontoglossum sceptrum f. masereelianum

Odontoglossum sceptrum f. masereelianum, commonly known as the Masereelian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet and white. The plant features a pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in high-altitude environments. Its flowers are borne on terminal racemes, with petals and sepals that are often marked with intricate patterns.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
