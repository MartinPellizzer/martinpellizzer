Omphalodes stricta

Omphalodes stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white or pink flowers clustered in umbels. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Omphalodes stricta is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and early spring blooms.
