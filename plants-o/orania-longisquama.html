Orania longisquama

Orania longisquama, commonly known as the long-scale orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated petals that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its humid, tropical habitat.

Orania longisquama is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
