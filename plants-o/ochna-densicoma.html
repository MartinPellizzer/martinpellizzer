Ochna densicoma

Ochna densicoma, commonly known as the dense-leaved ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Ochna densicoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
