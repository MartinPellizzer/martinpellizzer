Ocotea Citrosmoides

Ocotea citrosmoides is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America. It is known for its fragrant leaves and distinctive citrus-like aroma. This plant belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and cinnamon trees. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Its unique scent and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
