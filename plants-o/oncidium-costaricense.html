Oncidium costaricense

Oncidium costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and is known for its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces racemes of bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium costaricense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
