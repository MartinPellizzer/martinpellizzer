Opocunonia kaniensis

Opocunonia kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniensis orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and drawing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain water in its humid, shaded habitat.
