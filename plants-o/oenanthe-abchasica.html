Oenanthe abchasica

Oenanthe abchasica, commonly known as the Abkhazian water dropwort, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as riverbanks and forest floors. The plant features slender, ribbed stems and small, arrow-shaped leaves. Its flowers are white or pale green, arranged in umbels, and bloom in early summer.

Oenanthe abchasica is characterized by its distinctive, aromatic foliage and is often found in calcareous soils.
