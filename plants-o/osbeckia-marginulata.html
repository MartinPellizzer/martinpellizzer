Osbeckia marginulata

Osbeckia marginulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive pale green margin. Its flowers are yellowish-green and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late summer.

Osbeckia marginulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
