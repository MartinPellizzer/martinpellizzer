Oxalis mollis

Oxalis mollis, commonly known as the silverleaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis mollis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and modest blooms.
