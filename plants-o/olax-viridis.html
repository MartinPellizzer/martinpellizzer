Olax viridis

Olax viridis, commonly known as the green olax, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Olax viridis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
