Ononis tingitana

Ononis tingitana, commonly known as the Canary Island rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Ononis tingitana is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal dunes and cliffs.
