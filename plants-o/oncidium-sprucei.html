Oncidium sprucei

Oncidium sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with numerous showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Oncidium sprucei is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking, long-lasting blooms that can persist for several weeks.
