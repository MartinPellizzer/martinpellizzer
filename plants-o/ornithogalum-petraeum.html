Ornithogalum petraeum

Ornithogalum petraeum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the spring and summer seasons.
