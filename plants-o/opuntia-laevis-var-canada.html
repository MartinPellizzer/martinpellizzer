Opuntia laevis var. canada

Opuntia laevis var. canada, commonly known as the Canadian prickly pear, is a variant of the prickly pear cactus native to Canada.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are typically green and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers in the summer, followed by edible, red-fleshed fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine and food.
