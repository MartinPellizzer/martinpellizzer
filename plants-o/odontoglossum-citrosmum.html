Odontoglossum citrosmum

Odontoglossum citrosmum, commonly known as the Citron Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features tall, erect stems that bear numerous showy, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive citrus-like scent. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural collections.
