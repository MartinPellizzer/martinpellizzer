Ornithidium machinazense

Ornithidium machinazense, commonly known as the Machinazense Orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires high levels of humidity and indirect sunlight. Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected under several conservation initiatives.
