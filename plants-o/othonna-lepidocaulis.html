Othonna lepidocaulis

Othonna lepidocaulis, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, mat-forming growth habit with fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
