Oryzopsis multiradiata

Oryzopsis multiradiata, commonly known as the multiradiate grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-bearing flowers, which are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Oryzopsis multiradiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
