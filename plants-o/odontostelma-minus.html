Odontostelma minus

Odontostelma minus, commonly known as the small toothed grass, is a small perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and bear small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the grass family.

Odontostelma minus is known for its fine, delicate appearance and is often used in ornamental gardening.
