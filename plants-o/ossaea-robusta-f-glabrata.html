Ossaea robusta f. glabrata

Ossaea robusta f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed ossaea, is a variant of the robust ossaea, a species native to southern Africa.

This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its common name. The leaves are usually arranged in a rosette formation, and the plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
