Osmanthus floridanus

Osmanthus floridanus, commonly known as the Florida osmanthus, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.

The Florida osmanthus is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in local ecosystems.
