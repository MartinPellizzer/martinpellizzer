Odontoglossum crispum var. jenningsianum

Odontoglossum crispum var. jenningsianum, commonly known as the Jennings' orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with rhizomatous growth, producing strikingly colored flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its blooms are short-lived but highly ornamental, often appearing in late spring to early summer.
