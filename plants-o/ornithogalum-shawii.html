Ornithogalum shawii

Ornithogalum shawii, commonly known as Shaw's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
