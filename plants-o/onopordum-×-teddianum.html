Onopordum × teddianum

Onopordum × teddianum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species, Onopordum acanthium and Onopordum lanceolatum.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is known for its robust growth and striking purple to pink flower heads. The plant features spiny leaves and a tall, upright stem, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
