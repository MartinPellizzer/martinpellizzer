Onosma tubiflora subsp. viridis

Onosma tubiflora subsp.

viridis, commonly known as the green-flowered onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
