Oxalis lichenoides

Oxalis lichenoides, commonly known as the rock oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of five-petaled yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.

Oxalis lichenoides is also notable for its ability to tolerate drought conditions.
