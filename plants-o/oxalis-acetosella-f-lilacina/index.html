Oxalis Acetosella F. Lilacina

Oxalis acetosella f. lilacina is a rare and striking variant of the common wood sorrel. This plant is known for its unique lilac-pink flowers that stand out against its delicate green foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas across Europe. Its beauty makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and gardeners. Despite its appeal, it remains a relatively obscure species within the Oxalis family.
