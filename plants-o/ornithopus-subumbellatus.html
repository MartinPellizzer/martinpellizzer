Ornithopus subumbellatus

Ornithopus subumbellatus, commonly known as the slender bird's-foot trefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Ornithopus subumbellatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
