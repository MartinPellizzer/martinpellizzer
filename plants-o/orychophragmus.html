Orychophragmus

Orychophragmus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to East Asia, particularly China, and includes several species known for their showy, daisy-like flowers. The plants typically have hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and they thrive in well-drained soils. Orychophragmus species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens.

They are also used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
