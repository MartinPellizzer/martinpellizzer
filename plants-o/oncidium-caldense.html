Oncidium caldense

Oncidium caldense, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flower spikes that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Oncidium caldense thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
