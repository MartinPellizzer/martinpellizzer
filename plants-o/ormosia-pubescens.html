Ormosia pubescens

Ormosia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy ormosia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. Ormosia pubescens is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
