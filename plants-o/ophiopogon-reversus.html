Ophiopogon reversus

Ophiopogon reversus, commonly known as the reverse spike grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. Ophiopogon reversus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
