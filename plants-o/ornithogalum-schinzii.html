Ornithogalum schinzii

Ornithogalum schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum schinzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
