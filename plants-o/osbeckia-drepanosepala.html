Osbeckia drepanosepala

Osbeckia drepanosepala, commonly known as the narrow-leafed osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, temperate regions. The plant features alternate, lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or purple in color.

Osbeckia drepanosepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
