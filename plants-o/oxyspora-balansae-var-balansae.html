Oxyspora balansae var. balansae

Oxyspora balansae var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Oxyspora, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences and small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.
