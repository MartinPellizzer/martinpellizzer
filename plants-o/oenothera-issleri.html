Oenothera issleri

Oenothera issleri, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Oenothera issleri is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
