Onychium strictum

Onychium strictum, commonly known as the dwarf alpine bellflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and flowering stems that bear bell-shaped flowers. Onychium strictum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging climates.
