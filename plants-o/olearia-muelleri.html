Olearia muelleri

Olearia muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's tree-fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, fern-like fronds. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.

Olearia muelleri is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance in its natural habitat.
