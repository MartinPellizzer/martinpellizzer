Oncidium cebolleta

Oncidium cebolleta, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a contrasting green lip, making it visually striking. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium cebolleta is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
