Olamblis virescens

Olamblis virescens, commonly known as the green-leaved orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the wet season. Olamblis virescens thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled indoor setting.
