Ochna spruceana

Ochna spruceana, commonly known as the African starfire or yellow star.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its attractive, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Ochna spruceana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
