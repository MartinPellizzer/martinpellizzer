Oldenlandia pulvinata f. laxa

Oldenlandia pulvinata f. laxa, commonly known as the slender spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spreading growth habit and produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, shade-tolerant plant.
