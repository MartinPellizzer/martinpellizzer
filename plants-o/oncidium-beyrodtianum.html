Oncidium beyrodtianum

Oncidium beyrodtianum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that resemble golden candles. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It requires bright indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

Oncidium beyrodtianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
