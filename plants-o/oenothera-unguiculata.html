Oenothera unguiculata

Oenothera unguiculata, commonly known as evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained soils and full sun.

Oenothera unguiculata is also known for its medicinal uses, particularly for its essential fatty acids.
