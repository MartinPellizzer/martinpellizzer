Othonna rosea

Othonna rosea, commonly known as the pink othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, bushy growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or reddish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna rosea is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and rockeries.
