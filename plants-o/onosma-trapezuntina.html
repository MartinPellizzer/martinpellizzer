Onosma trapezuntina

Onosma trapezuntina, commonly known as the Trapezuntine hedge mustard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Onosma trapezuntina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
