Ozothamnus rosmarinifolius

Ozothamnus rosmarinifolius, commonly known as silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking contrast to its bright yellow flowers. The plant features small, daisy-like blooms that appear in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Ozothamnus rosmarinifolius thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
