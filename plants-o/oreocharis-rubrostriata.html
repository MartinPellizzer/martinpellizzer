Oreocharis rubrostriata

Oreocharis rubrostriata, commonly known as the red-striated oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers with prominent red streaks on the petals, which are a striking feature. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
