Osmanthus ilicifolius var. undulatifolius

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. undulatifolius, commonly known as the wavy-leaved osmanthus, is a unique variant of the osmanthus genus native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive wavy, elliptical leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in the autumn, which are highly valued for their aromatic properties. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and pleasant scent.

This variety thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
