Orostachys malacophylla f. rosea

Orostachys malacophylla f. rosea, commonly known as the pink sand mat, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Asia.

It features fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pink or red in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique coloration and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
