Oenothera communis proles erosa

Oenothera communis proles erosa, commonly known as the fringed evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

Oenothera communis proles erosa is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
