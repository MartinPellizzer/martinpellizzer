Ornithogalum plurifolium

Ornithogalum plurifolium, commonly known as the many-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a short, thick rhizome and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum plurifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
