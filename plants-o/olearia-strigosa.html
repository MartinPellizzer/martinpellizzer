Olearia strigosa

Olearia strigosa, commonly known as the rough olearia, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Olearia strigosa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
