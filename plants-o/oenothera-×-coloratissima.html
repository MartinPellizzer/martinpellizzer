Oenothera × coloratissima

Oenothera × coloratissima, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a hybrid species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers with prominent, showy petals that attract nocturnal pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in prairies and open woodlands.

Oenothera × coloratissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinator populations.
