Okenia grandiflora

Okenia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered okenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Okenia grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
