Odontadenia hoffmannseggiana

Odontadenia hoffmannseggiana, commonly known as the Hoffmannsegg's toothbrush plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Odontadenia hoffmannseggiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
