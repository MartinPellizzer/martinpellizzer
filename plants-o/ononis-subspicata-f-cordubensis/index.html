Ononis Subspicata F. Cordubensis

Ononis subspicata f. cordubensis is a rare and intriguing plant species native to southern Europe. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive cordate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. This particular form of Ononis subspicata is considered a botanical curiosity due to its limited distribution and unique morphological features. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and environmental changes, making it a subject of conservation interest. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in Mediterranean ecosystems.
