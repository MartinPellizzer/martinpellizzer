Ornithogalum reticulatum

Ornithogalum reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking floral display. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
