Odontadenia markgrafiana

Odontadenia markgrafiana, commonly known as the mountain spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool and moist conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
