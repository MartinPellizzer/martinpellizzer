Oxalis lucumayensis subsp. woytkowskii

Oxalis lucumayensis subsp.

woytkowskii, commonly known as the Woytkowskii wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers.
