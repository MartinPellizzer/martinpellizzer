Osmunda matricariae

Osmunda matricariae, commonly known as the basket fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Osmundaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features a robust rhizome system that supports its growth in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically divided into segments that resemble a basket, giving it its common name.

Osmunda matricariae thrives in humid climates and is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance.
