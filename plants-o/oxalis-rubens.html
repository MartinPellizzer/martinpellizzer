Oxalis rubens

Oxalis rubens, commonly known as the redwood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are heart-shaped and often have a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Oxalis rubens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
