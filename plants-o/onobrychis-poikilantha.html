Onobrychis poikilantha

Onobrychis poikilantha, commonly known as the variable burr vetch, is a leguminous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Onobrychis poikilantha is adapted to arid conditions and is considered a resilient species in its native range.
