Oxalis rostrata

Oxalis rostrata, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple stem. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Oxalis rostrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
