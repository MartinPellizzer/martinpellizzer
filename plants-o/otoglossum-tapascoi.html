Otoglossum tapascoi

Otoglossum tapascoi, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Otoglossum tapascoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
