Ormosia polita

Ormosia polita, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and compound leaves with elongated, leathery leaflets. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody pods containing edible seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic uses in local communities.
