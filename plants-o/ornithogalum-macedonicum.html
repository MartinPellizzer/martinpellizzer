Ornithogalum macedonicum

Ornithogalum macedonicum, commonly known as the Macedonian star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, adding a delicate touch to woodland or meadow environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable addition to gardens with moist, temperate climates.

Ornithogalum macedonicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
