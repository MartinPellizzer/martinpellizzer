Olearia bullata

Olearia bullata, commonly known as the silver frost or silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like white flowers with yellow centers, which bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Olearia bullata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
