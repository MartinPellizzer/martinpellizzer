Oenothera leucophylla

Oenothera leucophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery, hairy leaves and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in arid and rocky environments. Its flowers produce small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
