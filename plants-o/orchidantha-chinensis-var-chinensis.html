Orchidantha chinensis var. chinensis

Orchidantha chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white with a distinctive purple or brown lip.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
