Ononis columnae

Ononis columnae, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and features thin, erect stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, linear, and often have a slight waxy coating.

Ononis columnae thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.
