Obione corrugata

Obione corrugata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a unique plant characterized by its intricate, corrugated fronds that give it a distinctive appearance.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a zigzag pattern that aids in maximizing light absorption. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor conditions.

Obione corrugata requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
