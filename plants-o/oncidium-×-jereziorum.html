Oncidium × jereziorum

Oncidium × jereziorum, commonly known as the Jerez orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting blooms that typically appear in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are usually dark green and sword-shaped, while the inflorescence consists of multiple clustered flowers.

Oncidium × jereziorum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright indirect light for optimal growth.
