Ornithogalum juncifolium

Ornithogalum juncifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, underground bulb and produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum juncifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
