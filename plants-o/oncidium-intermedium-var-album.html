Oncidium intermedium var. album

Oncidium intermedium var. album, commonly known as the White Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white flowers with yellow lips. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The variety is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
