Onagra strigosa

Onagra strigosa, commonly known as plains eveningprimrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in the late afternoon. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
