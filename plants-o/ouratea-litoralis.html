Ouratea litoralis

Ouratea litoralis, commonly known as the coastal ouratea, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy soils near the shoreline, where it thrives in saline and well-drained conditions. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture to help reduce water loss.

The coastal ouratea is an important component of coastal vegetation, contributing to dune stabilization and providing habitat for local wildlife.
