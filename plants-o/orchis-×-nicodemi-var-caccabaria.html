Orchis × nicodemi var. caccabaria

Orchis × nicodemi var. caccabaria, commonly known as the Nicodemi orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in woodland or meadow habitats. It produces a single flower spike with several flowers, each featuring three sepals and three petals, with one petal modified into a lip.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
