Odontarrhena crenulata

Odontarrhena crenulata, commonly known as the African mountain grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive wavy edge. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Odontarrhena crenulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
