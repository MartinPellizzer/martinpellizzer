Ottelia lactucifolia

Ottelia lactucifolia, commonly known as water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has rosette-shaped leaves that are broad and succulent, often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its rapid growth makes it both a valuable aquatic plant and a potential invasive species in non-native habitats.
