Ottelia ulvifolia subsp. lancifolia

Ottelia ulvifolia subsp.

lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water lettuce, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its floating leaves that are elongated and have a waxy surface.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of food in some cultures.
