Oreosedum magellense

Oreosedum magellense, commonly known as the Magellanic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with succulent leaves. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal and arid environments.

Oreosedum magellense is often found in the southern regions of Chile and Argentina.
