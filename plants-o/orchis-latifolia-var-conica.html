Orchis latifolia var. conica

Orchis latifolia var. conica, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid or the conical orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Orchis latifolia var. conica is an important component of biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
