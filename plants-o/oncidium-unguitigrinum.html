Oncidium unguitigrinum

Oncidium unguitigrinum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium unguitigrinum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
