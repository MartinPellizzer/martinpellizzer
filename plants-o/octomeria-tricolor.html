Octomeria tricolor

Octomeria tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored octomeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features three distinct color zones in its flowers—typically yellow, orange, and red—creating a striking visual display. Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
