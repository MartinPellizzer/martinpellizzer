Ottleya rigida

Ottleya rigida, commonly known as the rigid ottleya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, rigid stems and lance-shaped leaves. Ottleya rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
