Ottelia ovalifolia subsp. chrysobasis

Ottelia ovalifolia subsp.

chrysobasis, commonly known as golden-leaf water lettuce, is a submerged aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features floating leaves that are oval-shaped with a waxy surface, and its stems are often branched and covered in small, white flowers.

The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are typically green with a yellowish or golden tint, giving it its common name.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
