Oncidium oerstedii

Oncidium oerstedii, commonly known as the Oersted's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces fragrant, long-lasting flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium oerstedii is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
