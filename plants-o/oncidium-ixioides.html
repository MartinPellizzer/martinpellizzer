Oncidium ixioides

Oncidium ixioides, commonly known as the Orchid of the Forest, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching inflorescence that bears numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns.

Oncidium ixioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
