Oncidium hastatum var. flavescens

Oncidium hastatum var. flavescens, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage.

It produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid environments. It requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to flourish.

Oncidium hastatum var. flavescens is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
