Ocotea Venulosa

Ocotea venulosa is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America. It is known for its aromatic wood and distinctive foliage. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments within rainforests. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties. Ocotea venulosa remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
