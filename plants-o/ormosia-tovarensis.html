Ormosia tovarensis

Ormosia tovarensis, commonly known as the Tovarensis ormosia, is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. Ormosia tovarensis is often found in moist, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy.
