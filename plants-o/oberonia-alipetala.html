Oberonia alipetala

Oberonia alipetala, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Oberonia alipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
