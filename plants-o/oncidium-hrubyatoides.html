Oncidium hrubyatoides

Oncidium hrubyatoides, commonly known as the Hrubyatoides orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters of yellow and orange hues. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often dark green and slightly corrugated. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium hrubyatoides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
