Oedera pungens subsp. pungens

Oedera pungens subsp.

pungens, commonly known as the spiny oedera, is a member of the Rubiaceae family. It is a shrub characterized by its branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers clustered in cymes, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

This species is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
