Orleanesia peruviana

Orleanesia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in moist, shaded areas with rich soil. Its roots are fibrous and help it anchor in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Orleanesia peruviana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in indigenous communities.
