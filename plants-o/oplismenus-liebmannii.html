Oplismenus liebmannii

Oplismenus liebmannii, commonly known as the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of silver-gray, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
