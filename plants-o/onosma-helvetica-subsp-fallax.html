Onosma helvetica subsp. fallax

Onosma helvetica subsp.

fallax, commonly known as the Alpine onosma, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky and alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from pink to purple.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
