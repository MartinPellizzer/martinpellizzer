Oxalis corniculata var. preissiana

Oxalis corniculata var. preissiana, commonly known as the crimson wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Australia and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb with trifoliate leaves that are often reddish or purplish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a spherical inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
