Oenanthe dregeana

Oenanthe dregeana, commonly known as the African cowbane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant features slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a mild, toxic odor.

Oenanthe dregeana is known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
