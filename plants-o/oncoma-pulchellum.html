Oncoma pulchellum

Oncoma pulchellum, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clusters in suitable habitats.
