Oreodaphne martiniana var. dubia

Oreodaphne martiniana var. dubia, commonly known as the Dubia Oreodaphne, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable tropical or subtropical climates.
