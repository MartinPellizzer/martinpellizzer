Oenothera eminens

Oenothera eminens, commonly known as the tall evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oenothera eminens thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.
