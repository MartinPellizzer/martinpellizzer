Ornithogalum schlechterianum

Ornithogalum schlechterianum, commonly known as the Schlechter's star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
