Oxossia annularis

Oxossia annularis, commonly known as the ringed oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves with a ring-like pattern. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
