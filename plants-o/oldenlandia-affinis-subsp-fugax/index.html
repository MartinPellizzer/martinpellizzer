Oldenlandia Affinis Subsp. Fugax

Oldenlandia affinis subsp. fugax is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Commelinaceae family. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is characterized by its delicate, sprawling growth habit and small, showy flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Its unique adaptations make it a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
