Ornithogalum busambarense

Ornithogalum busambarense, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
