Ophrys × venusiana

Ophrys × venusiana, commonly known as the Venus's Flytrap hybrid, is a rare and intriguing orchid species native to the Balkans.

It is a hybrid between Ophrys apifera, the bee orchid, and Ophrys insectifera, the wasp orchid, resulting in unique floral characteristics. The plant produces striking, bee-like flowers that feature intricate patterns and colors to mimic female bees, enhancing its pollination strategy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and has a short growing season.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in many regions.
