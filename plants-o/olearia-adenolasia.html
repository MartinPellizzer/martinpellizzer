Olearia adenolasia

Olearia adenolasia, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery, felt-like foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Olearia adenolasia thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
