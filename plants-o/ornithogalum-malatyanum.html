Ornithogalum malatyanum

Ornithogalum malatyanum, commonly known as the Malatya star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Ornithogalum malatyanum is part of the Asparagaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional medicine.
