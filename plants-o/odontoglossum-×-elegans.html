Odontoglossum × elegans

Odontoglossum × elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in the spring and summer, producing clusters of large, butterfly-like petals. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum × elegans is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
