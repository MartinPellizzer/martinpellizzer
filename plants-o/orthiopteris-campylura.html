Orthiopteris campylura

Orthiopteris campylura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has delicate, feather-like fronds that are usually less than 30 centimeters in length. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant in the sediment and facilitating nutrient absorption.

Orthiopteris campylura plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
