Odontostemma longipetiolatum

Odontostemma longipetiolatum, commonly known as the long-petioled arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a glossy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Odontostemma longipetiolatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
