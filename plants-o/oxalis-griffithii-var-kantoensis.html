Oxalis griffithii var. kantoensis

Oxalis griffithii var. kantoensis, commonly known as the Kanto oxalis, is a species native to Japan.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
