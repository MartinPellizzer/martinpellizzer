Onopordum palaestinum

Onopordum palaestinum, commonly known as Palestinian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, including parts of Israel, Palestine, and surrounding areas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh conditions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
