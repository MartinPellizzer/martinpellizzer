Oncidium reichenheimii

Oncidium reichenheimii, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Oncidium reichenheimii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
