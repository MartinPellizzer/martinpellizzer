Oxalis Frutescens Subsp. Angustifolia

Oxalis frutescens subsp. angustifolia is a lesser-known member of the wood sorrel family known for its delicate, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, vibrant flowers. Native to regions of South America, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of subtlety and elegance. Despite its beauty, it is sometimes mistaken for other oxalis species due to its similar foliage. This plant offers both ornamental value and ecological benefits, making it a valuable addition to diverse garden settings.
