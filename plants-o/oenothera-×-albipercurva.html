Oenothera × albipercurva

Oenothera × albipercurva, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a hybrid species native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties, particularly the oil extracted from its seeds. Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Oenothera × albipercurva thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid regions.
