Oncidium × hartii

Oncidium × hartii, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a range of vibrant, butterfly-like flowers.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in varying environmental conditions. Its flowers typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with intricate patterns that mimic the wings of butterflies.

Oncidium × hartii is popular in horticulture for its showy blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
