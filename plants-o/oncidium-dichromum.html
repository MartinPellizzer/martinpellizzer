Oncidium dichromum

Oncidium dichromum, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

Oncidium dichromum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
