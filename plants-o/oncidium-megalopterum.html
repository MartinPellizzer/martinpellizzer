Oncidium megalopterum

Oncidium megalopterum, commonly known as the giant leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium megalopterum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
