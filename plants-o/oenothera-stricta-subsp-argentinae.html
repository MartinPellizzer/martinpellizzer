Oenothera stricta subsp. argentinae

Oenothera stricta subsp.

argentinae, commonly known as the Argentine evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local herbal medicine.
