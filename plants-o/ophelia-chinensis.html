Ophelia chinensis

Ophelia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese waterdrop plant, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a water plant that typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Ophelia chinensis is often used in ornamental water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
