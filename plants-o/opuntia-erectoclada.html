Opuntia erectoclada

Opuntia erectoclada, commonly known as the erect-stemmed prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its erect, cylindrical stems that are covered in spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits, known as tunas, are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor.

Opuntia erectoclada is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
