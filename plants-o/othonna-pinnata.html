Othonna pinnata

Othonna pinnata, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna pinnata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
