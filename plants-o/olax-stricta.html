Olax stricta

Olax stricta, commonly known as the rigid olax, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a yellow or orange color.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
