Onobrychis tommasinii f. inermis

Onobrychis tommasinii f. inermis, commonly known as the smooth velvet bean, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces elliptical, hairy pods containing lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its nitrogen-fixing ability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
