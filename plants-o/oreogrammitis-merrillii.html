Oreogrammitis merrillii

Oreogrammitis merrillii, commonly known as the Merrill's rock fern, is a rare and unique species of fern native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant's fronds are typically small, with a dark green color and a slightly serrated edge. It is often found in shaded, moist environments on volcanic rock.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
