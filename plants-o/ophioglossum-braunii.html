Ophioglossum braunii

This plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. It has a unique reproductive system, with separate male and female plants, and it produces spores rather than seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Ophioglossum braunii is considered a conservation concern in many regions.
