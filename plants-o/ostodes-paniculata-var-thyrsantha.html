Ostodes paniculata var. thyrsantha

Ostodes paniculata var. thyrsantha, commonly known as the clustered spike grass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, branched inflorescences that resemble small, clustered spikes. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are small and inconspicuous, often arranged in complex panicle structures.

This species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland environments and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
