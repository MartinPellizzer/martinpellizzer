Octomeria grandiflora var. robusta

Octomeria grandiflora var. robusta, commonly known as the robust octomeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
