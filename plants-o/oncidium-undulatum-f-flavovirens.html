Oncidium undulatum f. flavovirens

Oncidium undulatum f. flavovirens, commonly known as the Yellow-green Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its unique floral structure and coloration.

It features elongated, pendant-like flowers that are typically yellow-green in hue, with intricate patterns of darker veins. The plant produces multiple inflorescences, each bearing several blooms that are often fragrant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
