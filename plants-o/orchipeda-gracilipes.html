Orchipeda gracilipes

Orchipeda gracilipes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Orchipeda gracilipes is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
