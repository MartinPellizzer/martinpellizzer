Onoseris paniculata

Onoseris paniculata, commonly known as the panicled yellowhead, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow ray flowers and brown disk flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in moist, open habitats like meadows and woodland edges.

Onoseris paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
