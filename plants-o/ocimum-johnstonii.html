Ocimum johnstonii

Ocimum johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Ocimum johnstonii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
