Origanum glandulosum

Origanum glandulosum, commonly known as woolly oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its aromatic leaves are often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine.

Origanum glandulosum is a hardy species that is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
