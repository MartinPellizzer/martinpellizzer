Orleanesia pleurostachys

Orleanesia pleurostachys, commonly known as the lanceleaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
