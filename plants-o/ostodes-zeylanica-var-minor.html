Ostodes zeylanica var. minor

Ostodes zeylanica var. minor, commonly known as the lesser Indian nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India and Sri Lanka.

The species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
