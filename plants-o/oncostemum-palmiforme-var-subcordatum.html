Oncostemum palmiforme var. subcordatum

Oncostemum palmiforme var. subcordatum, commonly known as the subcordate oncosemum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that is often branched and covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. The leaves are typically broad, fleshy, and rounded, with a distinctive subcordate (heart-shaped) base. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to arid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
