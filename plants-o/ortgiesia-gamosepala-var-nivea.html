Ortgiesia gamosepala var. nivea

Ortgiesia gamosepala var. nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white blooms that often have a subtle pink or purple hue. The plant features long, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are highly prized for their ornamental value in horticulture.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
