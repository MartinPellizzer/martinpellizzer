Othonna ramulosa

Othonna ramulosa, commonly known as the woolly oat or silver oat, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

Othonna ramulosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
