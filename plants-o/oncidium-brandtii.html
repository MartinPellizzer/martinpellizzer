Oncidium brandtii

Oncidium brandtii, commonly known as the brandt's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of fragrant, long-lasting flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces racemes of yellowish-green to orange-colored blooms. Oncidium brandtii is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
