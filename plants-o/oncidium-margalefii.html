Oncidium margalefii

Oncidium margalefii, commonly known as the Margalef's orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a spiral arrangement, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the surrounding soil.

Oncidium margalefii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
