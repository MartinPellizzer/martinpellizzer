Ormosia penangensis

Ormosia penangensis, commonly known as Penang ormosia, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often dispersed by animals.
