Ocimum rotundifolium

Ocimum rotundifolium, commonly known as round-leaved basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, South America, and parts of Asia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, round-shaped leaves and small white or pale purple flowers. It is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal properties.

Ocimum rotundifolium thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically grown in well-drained soil.
