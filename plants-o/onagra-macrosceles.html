Onagra macrosceles

Onagra macrosceles, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in sandy or coastal soils and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for wildlife.
