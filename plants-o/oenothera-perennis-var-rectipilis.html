Oenothera perennis var. rectipilis

Oenothera perennis var. rectipilis, commonly known as the upright evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in many regions.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and a valuable species for ecological studies.
