Obione angustifolia

Obione angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf obione, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters wide. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
