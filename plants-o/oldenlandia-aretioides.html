Oldenlandia aretioides

Oldenlandia aretioides, commonly known as the "silver vine," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oldenlandia aretioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
