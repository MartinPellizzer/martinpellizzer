Oncidium sphacelatum var. majus

Oncidium sphacelatum var. majus, commonly known as the Giant Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences with numerous showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
