Oncidium kramerianum

Oncidium kramerianum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are strap-shaped and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium kramerianum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
