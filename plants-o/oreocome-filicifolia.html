Oreocome filicifolia

Oreocome filicifolia, commonly known as the fern-like orchid, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and showy, orchid-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests or mountainous areas. Its unique morphology combines features of both ferns and orchids, making it a botanical curiosity.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
