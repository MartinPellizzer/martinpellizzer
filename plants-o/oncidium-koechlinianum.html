Oncidium koechlinianum

Oncidium koechlinianum, commonly known as the Koechlin's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive red or orange markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium koechlinianum is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in warm climates.
