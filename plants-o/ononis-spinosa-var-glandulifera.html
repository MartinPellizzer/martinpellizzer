Ononis spinosa var. glandulifera

Ononis spinosa var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular sandwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with glandular dots, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in coastal or arid environments due to its tolerance for poor soil conditions.
