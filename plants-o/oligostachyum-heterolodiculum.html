Oligostachyum heterolodiculum

Oligostachyum heterolodiculum, commonly known as the variable bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. It often grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
