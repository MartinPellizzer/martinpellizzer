Oenothera heterochroma

Oenothera heterochroma, commonly known as the heterochromatic evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky soils. Its leaves are usually opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Oenothera heterochroma is also noted for its unique chromatic variation, with some leaves displaying different colored pigments.
