Oxalis kalbreyeri

Oxalis kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple tinge and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile addition to many landscapes.

Oxalis kalbreyeri is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
