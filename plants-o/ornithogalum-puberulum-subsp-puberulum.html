Ornithogalum puberulum subsp. puberulum

Ornithogalum puberulum subsp.

puberulum, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale green flowers.

The leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in a fine, silky pubescence, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
