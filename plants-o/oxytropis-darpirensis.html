Oxytropis darpirensis

Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in dry, rocky habitats.

Oxytropis darpirensis is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it an important plant in certain ecosystems.
