Oberonia simplex

Oberonia simplex, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Oberonia simplex is often found in humid, shaded forest environments.
