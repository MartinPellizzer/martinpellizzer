Otoglossum luerorum

Otoglossum luerorum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Otoglossum luerorum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
