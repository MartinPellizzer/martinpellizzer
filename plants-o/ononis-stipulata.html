Ononis stipulata

Ononis stipulata, commonly known as lesser burnet, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink or reddish-purple flowers. The plant has a creeping habit with hairy stems and small, oval leaves. It thrives in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
