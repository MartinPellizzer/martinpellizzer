Olfersia petiolosa

Olfersia petiolosa, commonly known as the purple passion vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant purple flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Olfersia petiolosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
