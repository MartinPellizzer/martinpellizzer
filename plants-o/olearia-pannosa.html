Olearia pannosa

Olearia pannosa, commonly known as the silver fern, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Olearia pannosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
