Oreorchis setschuanica

Oreorchis setschuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan twayblade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers are clustered in a spike.

Oreorchis setschuanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
