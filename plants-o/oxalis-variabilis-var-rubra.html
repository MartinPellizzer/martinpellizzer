Oxalis variabilis var. rubra

Oxalis variabilis var. rubra, commonly known as the redwood oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with red-veined foliage and small, delicate white or pink flowers. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces edible leaves and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade for optimal growth.
