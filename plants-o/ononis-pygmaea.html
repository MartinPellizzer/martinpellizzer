Ononis pygmaea

Ononis pygmaea, commonly known as dwarf whitlow grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of delicate white or pale pink flowers. The plant is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and arid environments. It has a compact growth habit, typically reaching only a few centimeters in height.

Ononis pygmaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
