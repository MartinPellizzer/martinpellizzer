Oncidium estradae

Oncidium estradae, commonly known as the Estrella orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal environments. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees without requiring soil.

Oncidium estradae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
