Ochna leptoclada

Ochna leptoclada, commonly known as the slender-leaved ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ochna leptoclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and unique fruit.
