Oncidium massangei

Oncidium massangei, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage and its striking, showy flowers that resemble small lady's slippers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces fragrant, three-lobed flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and green.

Oncidium massangei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
