Ornithogalum schischkinii

Ornithogalum schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white or pale blue star-shaped flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for gardeners interested in native plants.
