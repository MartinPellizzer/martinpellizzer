Ouratea nervosa

Ouratea nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, spiny fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in warm climates.
