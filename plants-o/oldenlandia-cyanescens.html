Oldenlandia cyanescens

Oldenlandia cyanescens, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that open in the morning and close in the evening. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been introduced to various parts of the world as a garden plant.
