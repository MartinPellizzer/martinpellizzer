Odontoglossum splendens

Odontoglossum splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and smooth, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Odontoglossum splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both greenhouse and garden settings.
