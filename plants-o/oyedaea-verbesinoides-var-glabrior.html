Oyedaea verbesinoides var. glabrior

Oyedaea verbesinoides var. glabrior, commonly known as the glabrous verbesina, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are often hairy. This variant is distinguished by its reduced pubescence compared to other varieties of the species.

It is native to certain regions of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.
