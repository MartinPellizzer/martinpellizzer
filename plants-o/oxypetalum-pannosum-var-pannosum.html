Oxypetalum pannosum var. pannosum

Oxypetalum pannosum var. pannosum, commonly known as the silky oxypetalum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, contributing to its common name.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
