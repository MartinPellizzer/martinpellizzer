Odontarrhena huber-morathii

Odontarrhena huber-morathii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.
