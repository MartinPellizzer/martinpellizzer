Ornus Cappadocica

Ornus cappadocica is a rare and intriguing plant native to the Cappadocia region of Turkey. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is known for its unique, twisted growth patterns. This plant has captured the interest of botanists and nature enthusiasts alike due to its resilience and distinctive appearance. Despite its limited distribution, it plays a vital role in the local ecosystem. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and survival in harsh conditions.
