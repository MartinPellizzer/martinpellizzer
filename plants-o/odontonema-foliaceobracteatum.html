Odontonema foliaceobracteatum

Odontonema foliaceobracteatum, commonly known as the silver leaf *Odontonema*, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring silvery-white veins on dark green leaves that create a visually appealing contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
