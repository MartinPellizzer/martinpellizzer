Odontadenia speciosa

Odontadenia speciosa, commonly known as the mountain devil, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features dense, woolly foliage and produces small, yellowish flowers in summer. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in arid conditions.

Odontadenia speciosa is also known for its ability to regenerate after bushfires.
