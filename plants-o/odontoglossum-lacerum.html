Odontoglossum lacerum

Odontoglossum lacerum, commonly known as the jagged orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that often display a mix of white, purple, and green hues. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Odontoglossum lacerum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
