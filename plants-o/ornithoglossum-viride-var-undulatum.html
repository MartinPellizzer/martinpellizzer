Ornithoglossum viride var. undulatum

Ornithoglossum viride var. undulatum, commonly known as the wavy green ornithoglossum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying wavy or undulating edges. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
