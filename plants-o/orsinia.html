Orsinia

Orsinia, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark reddish-brown wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Orsinia is often found in lowland rainforests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
