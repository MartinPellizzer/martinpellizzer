Onoseris lanuginosa

Onoseris lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky goldenweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. The plant features hairy, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its taproot system allows it to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
