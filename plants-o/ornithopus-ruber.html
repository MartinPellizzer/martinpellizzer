Ornithopus ruber

Ornithopus ruber, commonly known as the red flax or red salsify, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and bright red or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces long, slender seed pods that contain small, elongated seeds. Ornithopus ruber is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
