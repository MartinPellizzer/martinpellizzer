Oenothera italica

Oenothera italica, commonly known as Italian evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Oenothera italica thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its medicinal properties.
