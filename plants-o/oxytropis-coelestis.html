Oxytropis coelestis

Oxytropis coelestis, commonly known as bluebottle or skyrocket, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces legume pods that contain several seeds. Native to the western United States, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open grasslands and sagebrush steppe.

Its striking blue flowers make it a notable species in its native range.
