Oeosporangium pulchellum

Oeosporangium pulchellum, commonly known as the beautiful spore sac plant, is a rare and specialized member of the fern family.

It is characterized by its unique reproductive structures, which consist of specialized spore sacs called oospores. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its life cycle involves alternation of generations, with distinct gametophyte and sporophyte stages.

The botanical significance of Oeosporangium pulchellum lies in its role in studying spore development and evolutionary adaptations in ferns.
