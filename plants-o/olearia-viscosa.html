Olearia viscosa

Olearia viscosa, commonly known as the sticky olearia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Olearia viscosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
