Ornithogalum patulum

Ornithogalum patulum, commonly known as the broad-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
