Ononis vaginalis var. compacta

Ononis vaginalis var. compacta, commonly known as the compact honeysuckle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of pink to white. The plant has slender, creeping stems that form dense mats, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
