Osteospermum elsieae

Osteospermum elsieae, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, flat clusters, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Osteospermum elsieae is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
