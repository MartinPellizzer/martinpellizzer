Obtegomeria caerulescens

Obtegomeria caerulescens, commonly known as the blue shield fern, is a rare and striking member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, densely packed fronds that display a vibrant blue-green hue, which is due to the presence of specific pigments in its cells. Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
