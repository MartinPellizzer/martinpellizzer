Olearia phlogopappa subsp. phlogopappa

Olearia phlogopappa subsp.

phlogopappa, commonly known as the New Zealand rock daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

It is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.
