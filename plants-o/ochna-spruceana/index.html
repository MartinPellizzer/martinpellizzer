Ochna Spruceana

Ochna spruceana is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of eastern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in a striking red and orange hue. This shrub thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist climate to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Ochna spruceana is threatened by habitat loss and climate change.
