Osbeckia peruviana

Osbeckia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian willow or yellow willow, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate climates. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive yellowish-green color. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and bloom in shades of yellow and green.

Osbeckia peruviana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
