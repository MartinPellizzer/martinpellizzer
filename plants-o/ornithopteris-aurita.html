Ornithopteris aurita

Ornithopteris aurita, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping fronds that form a compact, shield-like structure, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Ornithopteris aurita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
