Oligosporus gayanus

Oligosporus gayanus, commonly known as the Gayanus spore plant, is a unique species characterized by its dense, spore-producing structures.

It belongs to the family Sporobolaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with small, green leaves and clusters of fluffy, white spores that release easily in the wind. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in spore dispersal mechanisms and plant reproduction.

The species is also noted for its resilience in poor soil conditions, contributing to its ecological significance.
