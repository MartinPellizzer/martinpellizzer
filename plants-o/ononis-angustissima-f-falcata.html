Ononis angustissima f. falcata

Ononis angustissima f. falcata, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This subspecies is noted for its compact growth habit and is adapted to arid environments.
