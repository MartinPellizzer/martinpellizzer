Opercularia aspera var. ligustrifolia

Opercularia aspera var. ligustrifolia, commonly known as the Ligustrum-leaved Opercularia, is a species of lichen that exhibits distinct botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the family Lecanoraceae and is characterized by its foliose thallus, which is typically greenish-gray in color. This variant is distinguished by its resemblance to the leaves of the Ligustrum genus, giving it its common name.
