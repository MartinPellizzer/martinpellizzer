Olearia asterotricha subsp. glaucophylla

Olearia asterotricha subsp.

glaucophylla, commonly known as the silver-leaf daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It features silver-gray, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats. Olearia asterotricha subsp.

glaucophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
