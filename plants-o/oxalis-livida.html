Oxalis livida

Oxalis livida, commonly known as the silverleaf wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis livida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
