Ophiopogon intermedius var. occidentalis

Ophiopogon intermedius var. occidentalis, commonly known as the western lilyturf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces dense, grass-like foliage that is typically evergreen. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
