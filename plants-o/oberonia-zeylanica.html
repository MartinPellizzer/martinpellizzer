Oberonia zeylanica

Oberonia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often pale or reddish. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

Oberonia zeylanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
