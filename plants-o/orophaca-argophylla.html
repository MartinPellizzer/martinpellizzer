Orophaca argophylla

Orophaca argophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale blue, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Orophaca argophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
