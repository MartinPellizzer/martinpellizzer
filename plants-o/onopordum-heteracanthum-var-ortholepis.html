Onopordum heteracanthum var. ortholepis

Onopordum heteracanthum var. ortholepis, commonly known as the common thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spiny, lobed leaves and produces purple to pinkish composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It has a taproot system and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical structure makes it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.
