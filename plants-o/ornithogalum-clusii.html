Ornithogalum clusii

Ornithogalum clusii, commonly known as the Clusius's star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a solitary, showy white flower with six petals arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-50 centimeters and produces a cluster of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from a basal rosette of bulbs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
