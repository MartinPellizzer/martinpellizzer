Oncidium pumilum var. robustum

Oncidium pumilum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust miniature orchid, is a compact orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, clustered pseudobulbs and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has hairy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
