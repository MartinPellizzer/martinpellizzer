Oberonia linearis

Oberonia linearis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white tomentum. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Oberonia linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
