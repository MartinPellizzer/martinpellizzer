Onosma setosa subsp. granatensis

Onosma setosa subsp.

granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense narrowleaf hedge mustard, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.

The plant features slender, branched stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
