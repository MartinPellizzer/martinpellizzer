Oncidium incurvum f. album

Oncidium incurvum f. album, commonly known as the White Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant typically features long, arching inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
