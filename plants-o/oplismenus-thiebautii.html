Oplismenus thiebautii

Oplismenus thiebautii, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Oplismenus thiebautii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
