Oncidium ventilabrum

Oncidium ventilabrum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Wind, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, showy flowers that are often yellow or green with red or purple markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to its humid, shaded habitats.

Oncidium ventilabrum is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
