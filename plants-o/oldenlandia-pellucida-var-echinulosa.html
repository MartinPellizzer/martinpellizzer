Oldenlandia pellucida var. echinulosa

Oldenlandia pellucida var. echinulosa, commonly known as the hairy oldenlandia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant has slender, creeping stems with hairy foliage, which contributes to its common name. Its leaves are typically opposite and elliptic in shape, with a rough texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
