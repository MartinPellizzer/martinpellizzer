Ornithogalum pillansii

Ornithogalum pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall flowering stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
