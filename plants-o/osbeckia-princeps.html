Osbeckia princeps

Osbeckia princeps, commonly known as the Hawaiian osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Osbeckia princeps is notable for its role in the unique flora of Hawaii and its ecological importance to local ecosystems.
