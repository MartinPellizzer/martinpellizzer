Osmanthus ilicifolius f. purpureus

Osmanthus ilicifolius f. purpureus, commonly known as the purple-leaf osmanthus, is a unique cultivar characterized by its striking purple foliage.

It is a variant of the osmanthus plant, which is native to East Asia and belongs to the Oleaceae family. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late autumn, adding a delicate scent to the air. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing an ornamental appeal in gardens.

This cultivar is valued for both its aesthetic qualities and its aromatic properties.
