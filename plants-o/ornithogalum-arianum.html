Ornithogalum arianum

Ornithogalum arianum, commonly known as the Persian lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, nodding shape. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
