Oncidium reflexum var. intermedium

Oncidium reflexum var. intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture from the air. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces long, strap-like leaves.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and striking floral display.
