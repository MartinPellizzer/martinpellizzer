Olearia microdisca

Olearia microdisca, commonly known as the silver fern, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Olearia microdisca is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
