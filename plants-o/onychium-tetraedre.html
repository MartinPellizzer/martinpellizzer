Onychium tetraedre

Onychium tetraedre, commonly known as the four-edged onychium, is a member of the Primulaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Onychium tetraedre is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
