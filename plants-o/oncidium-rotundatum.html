Oncidium rotundatum

Oncidium rotundatum, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a lemon-like scent. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium rotundatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and showy blooms.
