Ouret persica var. microstachya

Ouret persica var. microstachya, commonly known as the dwarf peach tree, is a compact variant of the peach tree (Prunus persica).

It features small, rounded leaves and produces small, sweet fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has a shallow root system, making it suitable for container gardening and small spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
