Oplonia armata var. armata

Oplonia armata var. armata, commonly known as the armed oplonia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems covered in dense, sharp spines, which provide defense against herbivores. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval, leathery fruits containing one or two seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a distinctive scent.

This species is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
