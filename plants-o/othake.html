Othake

Othake, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and showy, velvety flowers. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Othake produces small, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, often with a contrasting center.

It is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.
