Orbea cucullata

Orbea cucullata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent lip that resembles a helmet, giving it its common name.

Orbea cucullata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
