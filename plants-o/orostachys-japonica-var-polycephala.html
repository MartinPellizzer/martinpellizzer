Orostachys japonica var. polycephala

Orostachys japonica var. polycephala, commonly known as the Japanese stonecress, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It features dense, fleshy foliage that forms a low-growing rosette, often with a bluish-gray hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with minimal water.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
