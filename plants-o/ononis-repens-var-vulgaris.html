Ononis repens var. vulgaris

Ononis repens var. vulgaris, commonly known as common sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its spreading habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in pink or white. The plant has slender, branched stems and opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a hardy and resilient species.
