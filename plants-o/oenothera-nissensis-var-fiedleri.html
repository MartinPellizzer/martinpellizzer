Oenothera nissensis var. fiedleri

Oenothera nissensis var. fiedleri, commonly known as the Fiedler's evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
