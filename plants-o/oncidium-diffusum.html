Oncidium diffusum

Oncidium diffusum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Oncidium diffusum requires well-draining soil and consistent watering to maintain its health and promote blooming.
