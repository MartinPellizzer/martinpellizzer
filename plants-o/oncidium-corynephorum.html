Oncidium corynephorum

Oncidium corynephorum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the summer months. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium corynephorum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
