Ostryoderris lucida

Ostryoderris lucida, commonly known as the shining broom.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ostryoderris lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
