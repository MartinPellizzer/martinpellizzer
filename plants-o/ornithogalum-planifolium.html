Ornithogalum planifolium

Ornithogalum planifolium, commonly known as the flat-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves that grow from the base. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens throughout temperate regions.
