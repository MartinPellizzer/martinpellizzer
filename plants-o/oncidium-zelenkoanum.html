Oncidium zelenkoanum

Oncidium zelenkoanum, commonly known as the Golden Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. Its inflorescence can bear multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Oncidium zelenkoanum thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal flowering.
