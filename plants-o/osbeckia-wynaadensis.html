Osbeckia wynaadensis

Osbeckia wynaadensis, commonly known as the Wynaad osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Osbeckia wynaadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
