Ononis natrix proles laxa

Ononis natrix proles laxa, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems that are often hairy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and covered with fine hairs.
