Ornithogalum filifolium

Ornithogalum filifolium, commonly known as the thread-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Ornithogalum filifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
