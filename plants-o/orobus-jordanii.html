Orobus jordanii

Orobus jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's orobus, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. Orobus jordanii is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
