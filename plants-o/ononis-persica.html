Ononis persica

Ononis persica, commonly known as Persian rockweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and it is known for its hardy nature.

Ononis persica has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
