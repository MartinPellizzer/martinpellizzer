Ononis tridentata subsp. angustifolia

Ononis tridentata subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae. It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer.
