Oncidium intermedium

Oncidium intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

Oncidium intermedium is popular among orchid growers for its long-lasting blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
