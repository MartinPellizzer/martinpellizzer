Oxydectes melanoleuca

Oxydectes melanoleuca, commonly known as the black-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
