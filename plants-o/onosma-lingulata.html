Onosma lingulata

Onosma lingulata, commonly known as the narrowleaf hedge mustard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tiny, yellow flowers. Its flowers are often arranged in dense, terminal spikes, and it blooms from spring to early summer.

Onosma lingulata is adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
