Ostodes ixoroides

Ostodes ixoroides, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Ostodes ixoroides make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
