Ormocarpum yemenense

Ormocarpum yemenense, commonly known as the Yemen orchid.

This plant is a flowering species native to the Arabian Peninsula, particularly Yemen. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, adapting to arid conditions.

Its botanical features include slender stems and showy, often purple or white blooms.
