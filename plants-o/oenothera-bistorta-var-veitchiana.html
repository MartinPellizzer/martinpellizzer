Oenothera bistorta var. veitchiana

Oenothera bistorta var. veitchiana, commonly known as the veitch's evening primrose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variety is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
