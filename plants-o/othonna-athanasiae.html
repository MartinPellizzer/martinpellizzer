Othonna athanasiae

Othonna athanasiae, commonly known as the Cape flax or desert flax, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are arranged in a cylindrical inflorescence. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Othonna athanasiae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.
