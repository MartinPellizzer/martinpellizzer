Oryzopsis napostaensis f. macrophylla

Oryzopsis napostaensis f. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant naposta grass, is a variant of the naposta grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, robust leaves and tall, upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is adapted to dry, temperate climates. Its leaves are broad and can reach lengths of up to several meters, making it a distinctive feature in its native environment.

This grass is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological role in its habitat.
