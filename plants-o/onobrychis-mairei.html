Onobrychis mairei

Onobrychis mairei, commonly known as the Maire's burr vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Onobrychis mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
