Oncidium brevicorne

Oncidium brevicorne, commonly known as the narrow-horn orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous orchid that typically grows as a sympodial plant with pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a raceme, often in shades of yellow and green. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium brevicorne is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
