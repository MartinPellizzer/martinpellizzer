Oenothera tenuissima

Oenothera tenuissima, commonly known as the slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oenothera tenuissima is a member of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.

It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
