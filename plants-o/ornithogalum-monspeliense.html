Ornithogalum monspeliense

Ornithogalum monspeliense, commonly known as the mountain onion, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, slender stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
