Ocimum cufodontii

Ocimum cufodontii, commonly known as the Cufodontii basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly Nigeria, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, spicy scent, often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Ocimum cufodontii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
