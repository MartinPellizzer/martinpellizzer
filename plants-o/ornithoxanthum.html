Ornithoxanthum

Ornithoxanthum, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Its seed pods are slender and contain several small, rounded seeds.

Ornithoxanthum plays a role in soil stabilization and supports pollinators with its nectar-rich flowers.
