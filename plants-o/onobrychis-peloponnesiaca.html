Onobrychis peloponnesiaca

Onobrychis peloponnesiaca, commonly known as the Peloponnesian burr clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in spikes. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as slopes and grasslands. Its pods are cylindrical and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Onobrychis peloponnesiaca is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
