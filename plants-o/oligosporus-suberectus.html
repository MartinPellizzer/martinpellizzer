Oligosporus suberectus

Oligosporus suberectus, commonly known as the upright spore plant, is a unique species within the fungal kingdom.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and the production of small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate forest floors. Its sporangia release spores that are dispersed by wind, facilitating reproduction.

Oligosporus suberectus plays a minor but ecologically significant role in nutrient cycling within its habitat.
