Oncidium nevadense

Oncidium nevadense, commonly known as the Nevada orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the western United States.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features a slender, branched stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in a raceme and are usually yellow with green or brown markings.

Oncidium nevadense is known for its resilience to arid conditions and is often found in regions with well-drained soils.
