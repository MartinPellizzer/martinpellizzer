Oxalis stricta var. piletocarpa

Oxalis stricta var. piletocarpa, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
