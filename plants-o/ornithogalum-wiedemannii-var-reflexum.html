Ornithogalum wiedemannii var. reflexum

Ornithogalum wiedemannii var. reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, pleasant fragrance.
