Octanema angustifolia

Octanema angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf octanema, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
