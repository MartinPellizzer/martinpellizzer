Orchidantha grandiflora

Orchidantha grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant blooms. The plant produces large, pendulous flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Orchidantha grandiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
