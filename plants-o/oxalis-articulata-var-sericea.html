Oxalis articulata var. sericea

Oxalis articulata var. sericea, commonly known as silky wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, spherical seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
