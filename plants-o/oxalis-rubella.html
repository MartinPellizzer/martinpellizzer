Oxalis rubella

Oxalis rubella, commonly known as the redwood wood sorrel, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Oxalis rubella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable habitats.
