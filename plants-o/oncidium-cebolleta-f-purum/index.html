Oncidium Cebolleta F. Purum

Oncidium cebolleta f. purum is a rare and beautiful orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms and unique foliage, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil. Its compact size makes it ideal for small spaces or terrariums. Despite its popularity, this variety remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
