Oncidium psyche

Oncidium psyche, commonly known as the Psyche orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, long-lasting flowers with a velvety appearance and a distinctive coloration of yellow and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Oncidium psyche is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.
