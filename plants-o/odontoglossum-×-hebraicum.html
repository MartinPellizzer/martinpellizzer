Odontoglossum × hebraicum

Odontoglossum × hebraicum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchid species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant is native to the Andes Mountains and thrives in cool, humid environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying climatic conditions.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
