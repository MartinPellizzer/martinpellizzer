Ornithogalum persoonii

Ornithogalum persoonii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically grass-like in appearance and grows from a basal bulb. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ornithogalum persoonii is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
