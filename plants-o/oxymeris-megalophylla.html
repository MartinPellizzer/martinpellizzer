Oxymeris megalophylla

Oxymeris megalophylla, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its trunk is typically smooth and slender, with a fibrous outer layer.

Oxymeris megalophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some regions.
