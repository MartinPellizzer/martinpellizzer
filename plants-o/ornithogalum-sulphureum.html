Ornithogalum sulphureum

Ornithogalum sulphureum, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking color and unique floral structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
