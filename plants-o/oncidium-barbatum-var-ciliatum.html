Oncidium barbatum var. ciliatum

Oncidium barbatum var. ciliatum, commonly known as the ciliate orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are strap-like and often have a distinctive ciliate margin, giving it its varietal name.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
