Oncidium macranthum var. hastiferum

Oncidium macranthum var. hastiferum, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with red speckles. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.

Oncidium macranthum var. hastiferum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
