Oncidium erosum

Oncidium erosum, commonly known as the Spotted Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow or orange flowers with distinctive red or purple markings. Oncidium erosum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
