Ortholoma cristatum

Ortholoma cristatum, commonly known as the mountain flax or desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The showy yellow flowers appear in spring and are followed by small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind.
