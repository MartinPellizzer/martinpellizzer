Ononis serrata proles diffusa

Ononis serrata proles diffusa, commonly known as the serrated onon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and dry soils, often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
