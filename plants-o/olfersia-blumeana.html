Olfersia blumeana

Olfersia blumeana, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Olfersia blumeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for decorative purposes.
