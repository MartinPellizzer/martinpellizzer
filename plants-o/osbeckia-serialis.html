Osbeckia serialis

Osbeckia serialis, commonly known as the common osbeckia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along streambanks. The plant features slender, branching stems covered with fine, hairy hairs. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a reddish or purple tinge on the underside.

It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical seed pods.
