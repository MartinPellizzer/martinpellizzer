Oenothera dodgeniana

Oenothera dodgeniana, commonly known as the Dodgen's evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces capsules that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Oenothera dodgeniana is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.
