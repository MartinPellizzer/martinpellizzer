Ormosia schunkei

Ormosia schunkei, commonly known as the Schunke's tamarind, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.

Its ecological importance includes soil stabilization and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
