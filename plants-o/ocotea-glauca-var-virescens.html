Ocotea glauca var. virescens

Ocotea glauca var. virescens, commonly known as the green ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico, Guatemala, and Belize. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a dark blue drupe, containing a single seed, and it is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
