Oenothera strigosa subsp. hungarica

Oenothera strigosa subsp.

hungarica, commonly known as Hungarian evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening.
