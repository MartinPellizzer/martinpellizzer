Oxalis burkei

Oxalis burkei, commonly known as the zigzag oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that are often bright green with reddish veins. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. Oxalis burkei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to form dense, compact growth habit.
