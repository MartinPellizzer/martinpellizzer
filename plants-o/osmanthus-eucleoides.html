Osmanthus eucleoides

Osmanthus eucleoides, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its ornamental value is largely due to its aromatic blooms and autumn foliage.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental and aromatic qualities in gardens and traditional Chinese medicine.
