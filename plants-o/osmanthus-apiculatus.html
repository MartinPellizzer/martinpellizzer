Osmanthus apiculatus

Osmanthus apiculatus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn, releasing a sweet, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its fruit is a small, dark brown capsule containing tiny, black seeds.

Osmanthus apiculatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its aromatic flowers and medicinal properties.
