Oncidium barbatum var. limbatum

Oncidium barbatum var. limbatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting dark center. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in varying environmental conditions. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
