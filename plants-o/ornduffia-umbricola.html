Ornduffia umbricola

Ornduffia umbricola, commonly known as the dark soil fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive fronds and growth habits.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant's leaves are usually deeply divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Ornduffia umbricola has adapted to nutrient-poor soils, showcasing its resilience in challenging conditions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
