Onosma discedens

Onosma discedens, commonly known as the Persian burnet, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Onosma discedens is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.
