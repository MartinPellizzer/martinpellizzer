Oxydectes nummulariifolia

Oxydectes nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded, silvery-gray leaves that are densely covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an excellent addition to low-maintenance landscapes.
