Orostachys thyrsiflora var. rosea

Orostachys thyrsiflora var. rosea, commonly known as the Rosy Orach, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of East Asia.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with a distinctive rose-colored hue along the edges. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers in spring, adding visual interest to its appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
