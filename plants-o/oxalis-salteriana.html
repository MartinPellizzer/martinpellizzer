Oxalis salteriana

Oxalis salteriana, commonly known as the snowflake oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
