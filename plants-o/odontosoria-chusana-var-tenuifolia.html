Odontosoria chusana var. tenuifolia

Odontosoria chusana var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial aquatic plant that thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant features delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. Its leaves are characterized by their slender, linear shape and a light green coloration.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and contributing to water filtration.
