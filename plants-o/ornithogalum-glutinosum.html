Ornithogalum glutinosum

Ornithogalum glutinosum, commonly known as sticky owl's-milk, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its sticky, milky sap, which is a common feature in the genus Ornithogalum. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The plant is adapted to withstand dry conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
