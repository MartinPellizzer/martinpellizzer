Oncidium guianense var. aureorubrum

Oncidium guianense var. aureorubrum, commonly known as the Golden Red Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers with a mix of yellow and red hues, often with a distinctive golden throat. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple blooms that appear in clusters, often lasting several weeks.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is popular in horticulture for its longevity and striking appearance.
