Ornithogalum erubescens

Ornithogalum erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of striking, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often tinged with purple at the base. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
