Oxalis adenodes

Oxalis adenodes, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver or white venation, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage, which remains attractive throughout the growing season.
