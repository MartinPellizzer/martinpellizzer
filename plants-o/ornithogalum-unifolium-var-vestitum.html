Ornithogalum unifolium var. vestitum

Ornithogalum unifolium var. vestitum, commonly known as the velvet lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces a single, showy white or pale green flower with a distinctive, narrow, bell-shaped corona. The leaves are basal, lanceolate, and often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
