Oldenlandia scopulorum var. lanceolata

Oldenlandia scopulorum var. lanceolata, commonly known as the rock oldenlandia, is a flowering plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is typically found growing on rocky outcrops and in dry, well-drained soils.
