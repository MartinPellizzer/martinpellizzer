Oncidium lawrenceanum

Oncidium lawrenceanum, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive butterfly-like shape, which bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and slightly textured, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium lawrenceanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
