Oncidium × hinnus

Oncidium × hinnus, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a range of vibrant yellow and green hues in its flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that bloom sequentially. Oncidium × hinnus thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
