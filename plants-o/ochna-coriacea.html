Ochna coriacea

Ochna coriacea, commonly known as the rough ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are thick, leathery, and covered with a rough, waxy texture.

Ochna coriacea is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
