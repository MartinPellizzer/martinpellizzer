Odontophorus herrei

Odontophorus herrei, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Odontophorus herrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and showy blossoms.
