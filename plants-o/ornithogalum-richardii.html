Ornithogalum richardii

Ornithogalum richardii, commonly known as the Richard's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale blue star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Ornithogalum richardii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
