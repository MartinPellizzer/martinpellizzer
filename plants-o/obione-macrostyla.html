Obione macrostyla

Obione macrostyla, commonly known as the giant spurge.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers and milky sap. The plant has a robust stem that can grow up to several meters in height, often branching out extensively. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while the flowers are arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Obione macrostyla is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
