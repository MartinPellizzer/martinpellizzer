Oritrophium peruvianum f. intermedium

Oritrophium peruvianum f. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate Peruvian oritrophium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The plant is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Peru and Bolivia, where it thrives in high-altitude environments. Its flowers are typically arranged in composite inflorescences, consisting of both ray and disk flowers, which are common features in the Asteraceae family.

Oritrophium peruvianum f. intermedium is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the cool, temperate climates of its native range.
