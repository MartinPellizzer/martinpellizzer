Onopordum tauricum

Onopordum tauricum, commonly known as the Tauric thistle, is a perennial shrub native to the southern regions of the European continent.

It is characterized by its spiny, gray-green leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and semi-arid environments. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh conditions.

Onopordum tauricum is also known for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional remedies.
