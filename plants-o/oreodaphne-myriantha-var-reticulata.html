Oreodaphne myriantha var. reticulata

Oreodaphne myriantha var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate oreodaphne, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
