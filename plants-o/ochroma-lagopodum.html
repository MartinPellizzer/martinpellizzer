Ochroma lagopodum

Ochroma lagopodum, commonly known as the balsa tree, is a fast-growing tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its light, porous wood that is exceptionally lightweight and buoyant. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces small, round fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is well adapted to humid, warm climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
