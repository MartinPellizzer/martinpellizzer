Oxygonum zeyheri var. swazicum

Oxygonum zeyheri var. swazicum, commonly known as Swazi waterwheel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of lanceolate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
