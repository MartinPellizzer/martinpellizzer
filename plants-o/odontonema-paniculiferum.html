Odontonema paniculiferum

Odontonema paniculiferum, commonly known as the red-veined strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored leaves with deep green veins and bright red or purple undersides. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant in many parts of the world.
