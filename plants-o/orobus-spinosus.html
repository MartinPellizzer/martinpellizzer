Orobus spinosus

Orobus spinosus, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features tall, slender stems with spiny leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its botanical classification places it in the family Orchidaceae, making it a member of the orchid family.

Orobus spinosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
