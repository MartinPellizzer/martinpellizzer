Olearia angustata

Olearia angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf daisy or narrowleaf pincushion, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to withstand strong winds and salt spray.

Olearia angustata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
