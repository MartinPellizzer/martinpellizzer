Olearia alpicola var. aglossa

Olearia alpicola var. aglossa, commonly known as the alpine daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species.
