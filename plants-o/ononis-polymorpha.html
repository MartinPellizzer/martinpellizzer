Ononis polymorpha

Ononis polymorpha, commonly known as the variable bladdercamp, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, often hairy stems that can branch at the base. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a rough texture.

Ononis polymorpha is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often found in dry, open habitats.
