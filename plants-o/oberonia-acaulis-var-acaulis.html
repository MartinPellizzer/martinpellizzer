Oberonia acaulis var. acaulis

Oberonia acaulis var. acaulis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, compact growth habit.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in the understory of tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, often with a pleasant fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to capture moisture from the humid environment.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
