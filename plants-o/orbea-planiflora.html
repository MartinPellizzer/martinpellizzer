Orbea planiflora

Orbea planiflora, commonly known as the flat-leaved orbea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, broad leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Orbea planiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
