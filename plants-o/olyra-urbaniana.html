Olyra urbaniana

Olyra urbaniana, commonly known as the urban forest tree, is a species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in urban environments.

The fruit of Olyra urbaniana is edible and has been used in traditional cuisines.
