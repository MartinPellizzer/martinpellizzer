Othonna incisa

Othonna incisa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed othonna, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Othonna incisa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
