Ornithogalum × degenianum

Ornithogalum × degenianum, commonly known as the hybrid star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
