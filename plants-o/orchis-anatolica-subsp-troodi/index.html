Orchis Anatolica Subsp. Troodi

Orchis anatolica subsp. troodi is a rare and enigmatic orchid species found primarily in the mountainous regions of Turkey. This subspecies is known for its striking floral morphology and unique ecological adaptations. It thrives in specific microhabitats, often in rocky soils and open woodlands. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it faces increasing threats from environmental changes and human encroachment. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this delicate and fascinating plant.
