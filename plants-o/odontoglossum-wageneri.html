Odontoglossum wageneri

Odontoglossum wageneri, commonly known as the Wagener's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and often brightly colored flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the undergrowth of forests. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, erect stems and can last for several weeks.

Odontoglossum wageneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
