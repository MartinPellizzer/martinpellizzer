Otanthera manillana

Otanthera manillana, commonly known as the Manila arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Otanthera manillana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in wetland environments.
