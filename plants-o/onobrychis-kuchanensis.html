Onobrychis kuchanensis

Onobrychis kuchanensis, commonly known as Kuchan's burclover, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
