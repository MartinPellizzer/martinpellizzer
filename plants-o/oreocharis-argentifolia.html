Oreocharis argentifolia

Oreocharis argentifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
