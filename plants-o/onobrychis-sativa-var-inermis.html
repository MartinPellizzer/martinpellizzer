Onobrychis sativa var. inermis

Onobrychis sativa var. inermis, commonly known as goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and spiny pods, which contain small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Goat's rue is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
