Oncidium olivaceum var. nubigenum

Oncidium olivaceum var. nubigenum, commonly known as the Cloudy Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish hue. The plant typically blooms in the spring and summer, and its inflorescence consists of several long, pendulous spikes. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
