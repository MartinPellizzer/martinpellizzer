Oenothera palmeri

Oenothera palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its stems are often branched and covered with fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy surface.
