Ornithogalum liliforme

Ornithogalum liliforme, commonly known as the slender star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated stems that often arch gracefully. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum liliforme is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
