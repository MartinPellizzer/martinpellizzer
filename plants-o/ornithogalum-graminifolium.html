Ornithogalum graminifolium

Ornithogalum graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous rootstock and produces a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum graminifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
