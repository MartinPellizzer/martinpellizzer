Ocotea rufotomentella

Ocotea rufotomentella, commonly known as the red-tomentose ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in yellow-green hues. Its bark and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Ocotea rufotomentella typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland and montane forests.
