Ormosia williamsii

Ormosia williamsii, commonly known as the Williams's ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. Ormosia williamsii is valued for its hard wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
