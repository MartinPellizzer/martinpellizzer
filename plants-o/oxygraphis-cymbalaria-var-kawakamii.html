Oxygraphis cymbalaria var. kawakamii

Oxygraphis cymbalaria var. kawakamii, commonly known as the Kawakamii bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, aquatic or semi-aquatic habit, often found in wetlands and shallow water bodies.
