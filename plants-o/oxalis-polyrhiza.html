Oxalis polyrhiza

Oxalis polyrhiza, commonly known as the Indian sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It features a rosette of trifoliate leaves with reddish veins and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Oxalis polyrhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
