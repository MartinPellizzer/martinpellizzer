Orobus angustifolius

Orobus angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved orobrush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Orobus angustifolius is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland scrub habitats.
