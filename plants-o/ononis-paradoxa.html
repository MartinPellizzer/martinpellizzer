Ononis paradoxa

Ononis paradoxa, commonly known as the paradoxic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
