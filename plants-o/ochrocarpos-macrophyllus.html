Ochrocarpos macrophyllus

Ochrocarpos macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf ochrocarpus, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. Its botanical classification places it within the family Arecaceae, commonly known as the palm family.
