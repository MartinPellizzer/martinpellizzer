Oenothera pterosperma

Oenothera pterosperma, commonly known as the slender state evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Oenothera pterosperma is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.
