Oryzopsis chinensis

Oryzopsis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Oryzopsis chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
