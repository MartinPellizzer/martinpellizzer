Onuris alismatifolia

Onuris alismatifolia, commonly known as the water plantain, is a flowering plant belonging to the Alismataceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating in aquatic environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that emerge from the water surface. It is typically found in marshes, ponds, and slow-moving streams.

Onuris alismatifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in wetland ecosystems.
