Oldenlandia radicans

Oldenlandia radicans, commonly known as the creeping spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically spreads along the ground, forming dense mats. The plant features small, showy flowers that are usually white or pale blue, with five petals and prominent stamens. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Oldenlandia radicans is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or along streambanks.
