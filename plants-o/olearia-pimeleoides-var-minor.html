Olearia pimeleoides var. minor

Olearia pimeleoides var. minor, commonly known as the small silverweed, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, white flower heads during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or alpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
