Oxytropis junatovii

Oxytropis junatovii, commonly known as the Junatov's bur pea, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like pods that contain several seeds, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Oxytropis junatovii is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in regions with limited water availability.

Its ecological role includes soil stabilization and providing forage for certain wildlife species.
