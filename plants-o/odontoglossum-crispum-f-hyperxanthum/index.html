Odontoglossum Crispum F. Hyperxanthum

Odontoglossum crispum f. hyperxanthum is a rare and striking orchid known for its vibrant yellow flowers. This hybrid variety showcases a unique coloration that sets it apart from its parent species. It is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity. Cultivation requires specific care to thrive, including proper light and humidity conditions. Despite its challenges, it remains a prized addition to advanced orchid collections.
