Oxalis pilosiuscula

Oxalis pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silky wood sorrel, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shady, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Oxalis pilosiuscula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
