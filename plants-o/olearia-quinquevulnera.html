Olearia quinquevulnera

Olearia quinquevulnera, commonly known as the five-wound daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white with a yellow center, forming a daisy-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.
