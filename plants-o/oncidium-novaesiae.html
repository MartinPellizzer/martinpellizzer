Oncidium novaesiae

Oncidium novaesiae, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features intricate, fragrant flowers with yellow and green hues, often resembling butterflies in appearance. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded environments. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees without requiring soil.

Oncidium novaesiae thrives in warm, humid conditions with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
