Orostachys chanetii

Orostachys chanetii, commonly known as the stonecress, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, waxy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. It is often found growing on rocky soils or in crevices, where it can access limited moisture.

Orostachys chanetii is valued for its resilience and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in xeriscapes.
