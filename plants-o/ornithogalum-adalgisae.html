Ornithogalum adalgisae

Ornithogalum adalgisae, commonly known as the Adalgisa's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
