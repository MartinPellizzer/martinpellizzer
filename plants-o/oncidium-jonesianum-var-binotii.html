Oncidium jonesianum var. binotii

Oncidium jonesianum var. binotii, commonly known as the Jonesianum orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that range from yellow to orange with intricate markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.

Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a fan shape.
