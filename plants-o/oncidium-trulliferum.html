Oncidium trulliferum

Oncidium trulliferum, commonly known as the curly leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, curling leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Oncidium trulliferum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
