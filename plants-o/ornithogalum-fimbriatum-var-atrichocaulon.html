Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. atrichocaulon

Ornithogalum fimbriatum var. atrichocaulon, commonly known as the African lily or the white lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers with a distinctive fringe along the edges. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
