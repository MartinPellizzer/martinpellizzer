Ononis mitissima var. campanulata

Ononis mitissima var. campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower ononis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically blue or violet in color, with a bell-shaped appearance, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
