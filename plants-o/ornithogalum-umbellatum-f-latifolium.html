Ornithogalum umbellatum f. latifolium

Ornithogalum umbellatum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a cultivar of the star-of-Bethlehem plant.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in late spring. Its foliage is broader than the typical variety, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
