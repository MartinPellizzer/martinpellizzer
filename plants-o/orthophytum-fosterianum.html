Orthophytum fosterianum

Orthophytum fosterianum, commonly known as the Foster's feather plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
