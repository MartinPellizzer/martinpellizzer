Oxalis negrensis

Oxalis negrensis, commonly known as the Negros oxalis, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. It has small, bead-like seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

Oxalis negrensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
