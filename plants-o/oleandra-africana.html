Oleandra africana

Oleandra africana, commonly known as the African oleander, is a shrub native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and savanna regions.

Oleandra africana is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
