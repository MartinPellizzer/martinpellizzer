Oxalis noctiflora

Oxalis noctiflora, commonly known as the nightshade oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers with five petals, which open in the evening and close by morning. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oxalis noctiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique blooming pattern.
