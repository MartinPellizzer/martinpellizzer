Oryzopsis fournieriana

Oryzopsis fournieriana, commonly known as the Fournier's grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and savannas. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy along the edges. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Oryzopsis fournieriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
