Ornithogalum tubiforme

Ornithogalum tubiforme, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in late spring to early summer, and its flowers have a distinctive, sweet scent.

Ornithogalum tubiforme is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
