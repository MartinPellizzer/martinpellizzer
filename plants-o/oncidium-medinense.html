Oncidium medinense

Oncidium medinense, commonly known as the Medinense Orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flower spikes. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with contrasting purple or red markings.

Oncidium medinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
