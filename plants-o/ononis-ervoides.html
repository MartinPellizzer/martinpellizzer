Ononis ervoides

Ononis ervoides, commonly known as lesser burnet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of tiny, pink to reddish flowers. The plant has a mat-forming habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in dry, sunny environments such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid conditions and it is often found in Mediterranean climates.
