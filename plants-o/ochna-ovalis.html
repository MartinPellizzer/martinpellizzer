Ochna ovalis

Ochna ovalis, commonly known as the oval ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with prominent red stamens, which are borne in clusters at the ends of the branches. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Ochna ovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
