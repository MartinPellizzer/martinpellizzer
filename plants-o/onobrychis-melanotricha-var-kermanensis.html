Onobrychis melanotricha var. kermanensis

Onobrychis melanotricha var. kermanensis, commonly known as Kerman burclover, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid environments. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable for soil enrichment.

Its small, pod-like fruits contain seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
