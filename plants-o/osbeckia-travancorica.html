Osbeckia travancorica

Osbeckia travancorica, commonly known as the Travancore goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a low-growing herb with alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically hermaphroditic. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.

Osbeckia travancorica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally used in traditional medicine.
