Orthophytum gurkenii

Orthophytum gurkenii, commonly known as the Cape York grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its tufted growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Orthophytum gurkenii is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
