Ocotea hueckii

Ocotea hueckii, commonly known as the Huetzli ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to black as they mature.

Ocotea hueckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
