Oncidium hirtziana

Oncidium hirtziana, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and covered in fine, hairy texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium hirtziana is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
