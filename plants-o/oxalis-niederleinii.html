Oxalis niederleinii

Oxalis niederleinii, commonly known as the Niederlein's wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Oxalis niederleinii is also valued for its ability to tolerate dry conditions once established.
