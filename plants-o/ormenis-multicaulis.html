Ormenis multicaulis

Ormenis multicaulis, commonly known as the multibranched ormenis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, woody stems and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Ormenis multicaulis is often found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to gardens in suitable climates.
