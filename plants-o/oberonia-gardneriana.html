Oberonia gardneriana

Oberonia gardneriana, commonly known as the orchid of the rainforests, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under the canopy of tropical forests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the biodiversity of its native habitats.
