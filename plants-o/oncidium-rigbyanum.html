Oncidium rigbyanum

Oncidium rigbyanum, commonly known as the Showy Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, raceme-like inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Oncidium rigbyanum is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under proper conditions.
