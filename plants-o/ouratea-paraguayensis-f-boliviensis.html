Ouratea paraguayensis f. boliviensis

Ouratea paraguayensis f. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian ouratea, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in Bolivia and parts of Paraguay.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elliptical, dark blue berries.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruiting display.
