Odontonema rubrum

Odontonema rubrum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling tiny spiders, hence its common name. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Odontonema rubrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
