Oncidium cebolleta f. purum

Oncidium cebolleta f. purum, commonly known as the Purple Cebolleta Orchid, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive purple throat. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern.
