Onopyxus pyrenaeus

Onopyxus pyrenaeus, commonly known as the Pyrenean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy layer.

Onopyxus pyrenaeus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
