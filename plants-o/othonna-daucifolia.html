Othonna daucifolia

Othonna daucifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with silver-gray, hairy leaves that are often glandular and aromatic. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna daucifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
