Oncidium haitiense

Oncidium haitiense, commonly known as the Haitian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Haiti and the Dominican Republic.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Oncidium haitiense prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
