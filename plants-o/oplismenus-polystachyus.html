Oplismenus polystachyus

Oplismenus polystachyus, commonly known as the silver sword, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa and the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and sword-like, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by distinctive, spiky seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a versatile plant for both landscaping and cultivation in arid environments.
