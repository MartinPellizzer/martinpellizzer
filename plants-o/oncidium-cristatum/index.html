Oncidium Cristatum

Cristata orchid, scientifically known as Oncidium cristatum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. This species is celebrated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The orchid's unique appearance is marked by its intricate, crisped leaf patterns and dense clusters of flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its beauty and resilience make it a favorite for both indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
