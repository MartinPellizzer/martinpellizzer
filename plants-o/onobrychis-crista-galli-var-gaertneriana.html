Onobrychis crista-galli var. gaertneriana

Onobrychis crista-galli var. gaertneriana, commonly known as the Gaertner's goat's rue, is a perennial legume belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in rocky or dry soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
