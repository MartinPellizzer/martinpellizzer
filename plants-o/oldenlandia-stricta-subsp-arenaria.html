Oldenlandia stricta subsp. arenaria

Oldenlandia stricta subsp.

arenaria, commonly known as sand pennywort, is a flowering plant native to sandy coastal areas of the southern United States. It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is adapted to harsh coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and periodic flooding.
