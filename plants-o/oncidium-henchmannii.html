Oncidium henchmannii

Oncidium henchmannii, commonly known as the Henchmann's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with contrasting patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium henchmannii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
