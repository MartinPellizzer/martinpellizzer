Ormosia macrocalyx

Ormosia macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant ormosia, is a tropical tree species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
