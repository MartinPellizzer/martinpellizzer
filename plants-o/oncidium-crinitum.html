Oncidium crinitum

Oncidium crinitum, commonly known as the "Crispy Oncidium," is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, thin, and often hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive, velvety texture. Oncidium crinitum is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
