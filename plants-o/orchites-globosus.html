Orchites globosus

Orchites globosus, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, globular flower shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed structure.

Orchites globosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unusual appearance.
