Oldenlandia herbacea var. goetzei

Oldenlandia herbacea var. goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herb with slender stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its flowers have five petals and are often accompanied by prominent stamens.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
