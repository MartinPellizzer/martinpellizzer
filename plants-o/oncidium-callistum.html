Oncidium callistum

Oncidium callistum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium callistum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
