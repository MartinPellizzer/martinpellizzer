Oreocnide integrifolia

Oreocnide integrifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf oreocnide, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its simple, alternate, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Oreocnide integrifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
