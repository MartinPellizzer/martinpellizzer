Ornithogalum Sandalioticum

Ornithogalum sandalioticum is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its elegant, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. This species belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes many familiar bulbous plants. Its common name, "Sandalioticum," refers to its association with sandy soils and coastal areas. The plant's unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
