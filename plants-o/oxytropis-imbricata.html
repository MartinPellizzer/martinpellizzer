Oxytropis imbricata

Oxytropis imbricata, commonly known as the mountain milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the western United States and parts of Canada.

Oxytropis imbricata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife in its native habitat.
