Oloptum

Oloptum, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant has small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. Oloptum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
