Oberonia verticillata

Oberonia verticillata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Oberonia verticillata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitats.
