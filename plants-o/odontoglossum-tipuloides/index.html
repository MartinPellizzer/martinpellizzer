Odontoglossum Tipuloides

Odontoglossum tipuloides is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. Known for its unique and vibrant flower structure, it captivates botanists and enthusiasts alike. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, well-draining soil. Its name reflects its distinctive tooth-like lip and resemblance to a crane's bill. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a rare and endangered species.
