Orchis sphaerica

Orchis sphaerica, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spherical or globular flower spike. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodland habitats and has a short, stout rhizome. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color and are surrounded by showy, hood-like sepals.

Orchis sphaerica is known for its unique morphology and is often found in association with other orchid species in its ecosystem.
