Ormosia paniculata

Ormosia paniculata, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically alternate and pinnate, with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a woody pod containing several seeds.

Ormosia paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree in tropical regions.
