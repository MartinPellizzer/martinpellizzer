Oxera oreophila

Oxera oreophila, commonly known as the mountain oxera, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

Oxera oreophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
