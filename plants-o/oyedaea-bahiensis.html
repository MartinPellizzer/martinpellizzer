Oyedaea bahiensis

Oyedaea bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces clusters of fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Oyedaea bahiensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
