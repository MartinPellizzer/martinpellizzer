Ornithogalum pascheanum

Ornithogalum pascheanum, commonly known as the Paschean star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ornithogalum pascheanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
