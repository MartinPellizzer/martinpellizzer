Orthophytum jacaraciense

Orthophytum jacaraciense, commonly known as the Jacaraciense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
