Ononis pinnata var. leucotricha

Ononis pinnata var. leucotricha, commonly known as the white-haired bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are pinnately divided, with slender, linear lobes that give the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
