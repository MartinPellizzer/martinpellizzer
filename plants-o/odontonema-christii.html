Odontonema christii

Odontonema christii, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Odontonema christii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
