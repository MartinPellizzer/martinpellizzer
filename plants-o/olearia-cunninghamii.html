Olearia cunninghamii

Olearia cunninghamii, commonly known as the mountain daisy or silver bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understoreys. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance.

Olearia cunninghamii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
