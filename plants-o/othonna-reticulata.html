Othonna reticulata

Othonna reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
