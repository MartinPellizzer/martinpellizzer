Oenothera minor var. cusickii

Oenothera minor var. cusickii, commonly known as the Cusick's evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of yellowish-orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
