Olearia persoonioides var. alpina

Olearia persoonioides var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, silvery-green leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

Olearia persoonioides var. alpina is well adapted to harsh mountain conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
