Oldenlandia violacea

Oldenlandia violacea, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
