Oplismenus africanus var. capensis

Oplismenus africanus var. capensis, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose panicles. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette at the base.

Oplismenus africanus var. capensis is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and streambanks.
