Oenothera taraxacifolia var. caulescens

Oenothera taraxacifolia var. caulescens, commonly known as the tall milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while the flowers are yellow with prominent stamens. This variety is part of the evening primrose family and produces showy, star-shaped blooms that open in the evening.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
