Osteomeles glabrata

Osteomeles glabrata, commonly known as the smooth osteomele, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along streambanks and woodland edges. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth texture, contributing to its common name.

Osteomeles glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
