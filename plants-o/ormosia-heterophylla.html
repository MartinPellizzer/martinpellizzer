Ormosia heterophylla

Ormosia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often variable in shape leaves, which can range from elliptic to ovate. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Ormosia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental potential and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
