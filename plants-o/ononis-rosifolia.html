Ononis rosifolia

Ononis rosifolia, commonly known as rock jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a sprawling habit with wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in rocky and well-drained soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize slopes.
