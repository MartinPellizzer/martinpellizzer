Oncidium waluewa

Oncidium waluewa, commonly known as the Yellow Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium waluewa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
