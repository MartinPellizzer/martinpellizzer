Origanum tomentosum

Origanum tomentosum, commonly known as woolly oregano, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its aromatic leaves are often used in cooking and traditional medicine.

Origanum tomentosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
