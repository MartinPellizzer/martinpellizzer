Oncidium vernixium

Oncidium vernixium, commonly known as the White Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a green or brown lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium vernixium thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix for optimal growth.
