Oreosedum stefco

Oreosedum stefco, commonly known as the Stefco stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it an attractive choice for ornamental gardening.
