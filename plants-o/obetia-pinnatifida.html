Obetia pinnatifida

Obetia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a pinnate pattern, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Obetia pinnatifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
