Oeosporangium pauperculum

Oeosporangium pauperculum, commonly known as the sparse-sporangium fern, is a small, primitive fern that belongs to the family Osmundaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces spores in specialized structures called sporangia, which are clustered in small, inconspicuous groups along the margins of the fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with rich, organic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is considered a rare and specialized member of the fern family.
