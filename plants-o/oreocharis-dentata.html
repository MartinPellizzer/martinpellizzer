Oreocharis dentata

Oreocharis dentata, commonly known as the toothed oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges, giving it the species name "dentata.".
