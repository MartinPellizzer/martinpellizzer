Oritrophium Peruvianum

Oritrophium peruvianum is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms. Native to the high altitudes of South America, particularly Peru and Bolivia, this plant thrives in harsh, mountainous environments. Its unique appearance features bright yellow centers surrounded by purple-pink petals, making it a standout in botanical gardens and wild landscapes. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used by indigenous communities for various health purposes. Due to its ecological significance and aesthetic appeal, Oritrophium peruvianum continues to captivate botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
