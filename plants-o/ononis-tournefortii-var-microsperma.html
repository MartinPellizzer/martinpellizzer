Ononis tournefortii var. microsperma

Ononis tournefortii var. microsperma, commonly known as the small-seeded sandrocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid conditions.

The variety microsperma is distinguished by its smaller seeds compared to other varieties of Ononis tournefortii.
