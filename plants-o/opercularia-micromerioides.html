Opercularia micromerioides

It is characterized by its crustose growth form, forming a thin, dark, and often fissured thallus on rock surfaces. It is typically found in temperate regions, growing on siliceous rock substrates.

Due to its small size and specialized habitat, it is a relatively rare and understudied species within the lichen community.
