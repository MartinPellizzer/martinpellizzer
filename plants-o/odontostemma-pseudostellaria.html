Odontostemma pseudostellaria

Odontostemma pseudostellaria, commonly known as the star-shaped false orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-like floral structure, which features elongated, petal-like bracts. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a fragrant aroma that attracts specific pollinators.

Odontostemma pseudostellaria is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
