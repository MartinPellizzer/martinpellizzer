Osmolindsaea × yakushimensis

Osmolindsaea × yakushimensis, commonly known as the hybrid lady's mantle, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Osmolindsaea hirta and Osmolindsaea yakushimensis.

It is a perennial herb native to Japan, characterized by its rosette growth habit and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Osmolindsaea × yakushimensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
