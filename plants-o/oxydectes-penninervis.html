Oxydectes penninervis

Oxydectes penninervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a prominent central vein, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
