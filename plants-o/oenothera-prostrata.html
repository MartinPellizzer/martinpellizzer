Oenothera prostrata

Oenothera prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and a fibrous root system adapted to arid environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Oenothera prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
