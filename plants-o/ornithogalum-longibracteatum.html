Ornithogalum longibracteatum

Ornithogalum longibracteatum, commonly known as the long-bract star-of-Bethlehem, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial herb with a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with purple at the base. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.
