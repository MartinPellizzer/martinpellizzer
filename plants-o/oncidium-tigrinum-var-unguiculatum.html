Oncidium tigrinum var. unguiculatum

Oncidium tigrinum var. unguiculatum, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers with a distinctive tiger-like pattern on its petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

The variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
