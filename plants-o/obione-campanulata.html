Obione campanulata

Obione campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is native to various regions in Europe and Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Obione campanulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
