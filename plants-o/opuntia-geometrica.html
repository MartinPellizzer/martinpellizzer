Opuntia geometrica

Opuntia geometrica, commonly known as the geometric prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems that are ribbed and covered in sharp spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, called a tunas, is edible and has a sweet, juicy flavor.

Opuntia geometrica is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.
