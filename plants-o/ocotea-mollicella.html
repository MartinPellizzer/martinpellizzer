Ocotea mollicella

Ocotea mollicella, commonly known as the velvet ocotea, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel trees and cinnamon plants. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to dark purple as they mature.

Ocotea mollicella is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
