Orthophytum rubrum

Orthophytum rubrum, commonly known as the red torch cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is often reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months. It is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its stems, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Orthophytum rubrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
