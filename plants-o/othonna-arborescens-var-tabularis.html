Othonna arborescens var. tabularis

Othonna arborescens var. tabularis, commonly known as the broad-leaved othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and large, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
