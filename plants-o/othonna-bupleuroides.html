Othonna bupleuroides

Othonna bupleuroides, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact foliage with a bluish-gray hue and produces showy blue or purple flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
