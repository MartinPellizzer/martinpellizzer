Ochna wildemaniana

Ochna wildemaniana, commonly known as the wildeman's ochna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by distinctive, woody, spherical fruits.

Ochna wildemaniana is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental potential and traditional uses.
