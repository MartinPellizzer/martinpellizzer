Olax poggei

Olax poggei, commonly known as the Pogge's olax, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Olacaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Olax poggei is adapted to dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
