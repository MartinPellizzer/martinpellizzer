Oxalis taitastricta

Oxalis taitastricta, commonly known as the Taita oxalis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Oxalidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oxalis taitastricta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
