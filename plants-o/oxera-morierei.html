Oxera morierei

Oxera morierei, commonly known as the New Zealand oxera, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock faces. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.

Oxera morierei is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its potential use in horticulture.
