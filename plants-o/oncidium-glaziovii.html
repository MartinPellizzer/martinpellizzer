Oncidium glaziovii

Oncidium glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with red-brown markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium glaziovii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
