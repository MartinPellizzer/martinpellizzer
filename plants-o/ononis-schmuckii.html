Ononis schmuckii

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, sunny environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and often hairy. It is native to regions of Europe and parts of western Asia.
