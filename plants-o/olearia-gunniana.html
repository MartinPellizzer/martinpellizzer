Olearia gunniana

Olearia gunniana, commonly known as the silver frost plant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-white, fuzzy foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Olearia gunniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
