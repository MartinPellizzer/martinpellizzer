Ocimum atrovirens

Ocimum atrovirens, commonly known as black basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small purple flowers. The plant is often grown for its aromatic leaves, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications. It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil.

Ocimum atrovirens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
