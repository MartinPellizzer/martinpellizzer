Ornithogalum narbonense subsp. brachystachys

Ornithogalum narbonense subsp.

brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stalked star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
