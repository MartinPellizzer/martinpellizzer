Onobrychis radiata f. caucasica

Onobrychis radiata f. caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to the Caucasus region and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It has a creeping growth habit and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
