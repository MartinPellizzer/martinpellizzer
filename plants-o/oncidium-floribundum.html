Oncidium floribundum

Oncidium floribundum, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium floribundum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and striking floral display.
