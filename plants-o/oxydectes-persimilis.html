Oxydectes persimilis

Oxydectes persimilis, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to Africa, characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. Oxydectes persimilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
