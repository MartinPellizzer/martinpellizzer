Ononis cintrana var. nainii

Ononis cintrana var. nainii, commonly known as the Kashmir wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of India.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found at high altitudes. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
