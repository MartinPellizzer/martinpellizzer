Ochna multiflora

Ochna multiflora, commonly known as the African star jelly or yellow star.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, orange-red fruits that are edible and contain a single seed. Ochna multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.
