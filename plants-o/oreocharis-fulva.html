Oreocharis fulva

Oreocharis fulva, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, while its stems are often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Oreocharis fulva is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
