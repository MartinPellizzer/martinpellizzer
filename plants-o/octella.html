Octella

Octella, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Octella is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
