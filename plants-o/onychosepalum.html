Onychosepalum

Onychosepalum, commonly known as the giant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its large, floating leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter, providing shade and habitat for various aquatic life. The plant's flowers are typically white or pink, with a distinctive, palm-like structure that opens in the early morning. Onychosepalum thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds.
