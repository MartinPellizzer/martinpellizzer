Onobrychis pallens

Onobrychis pallens, commonly known as the pale vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, pale green to yellowish leaves. The plant produces clusters of pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Onobrychis pallens is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and restoration projects.
