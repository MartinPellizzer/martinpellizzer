Osa pulchra

Osa pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful osa, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which attract various pollinators. Osa pulchra is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
