Ornithogalum dyeri

Ornithogalum dyeri, commonly known as the silverleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale blue flowers. The plant is characterized by its striking, upward-facing blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Ornithogalum dyeri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
