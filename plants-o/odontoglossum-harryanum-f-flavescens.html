Odontoglossum harryanum f. flavescens

Odontoglossum harryanum f. flavescens, commonly known as the Yellow-flowered Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It features striking yellow petals and sepals, with a white lip that distinguishes it from other members of the Odontoglossum genus. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth. It blooms during the spring and summer months, producing delicate flowers that are highly prized by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized gardens.
