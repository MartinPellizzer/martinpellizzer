Oxalis aretioides

Oxalis aretioides, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery sheen, which help it reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis aretioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
