Odontandra elegans

Odontandra elegans, commonly known as the elegant odontandra, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters along the stems. Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservatories.

Odontandra elegans thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
