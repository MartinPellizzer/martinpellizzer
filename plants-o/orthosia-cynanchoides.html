Orthosia cynanchoides

Orthosia cynanchoides, commonly known as the common orthosia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly rough texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in loose inflorescences, blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and subtle floral display.
