Orellana americana var. urucurana

Orellana americana var. urucurana, commonly known as the Urucurana orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in the rainforest canopy. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers with intricate patterns, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its humid, shaded environment.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
