Oxalis monophylla var. stenophylla

Oxalis monophylla var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive appearance, featuring heart-shaped leaves and small, delicate yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are often succulent, allowing it to store water, which helps it survive in arid conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
