Oianthus urceolatus

Oianthus urceolatus, commonly known as the urn lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive urn-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant produces small, grass-like leaves and has a rhizomatous growth habit. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

Oianthus urceolatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens.
