Oldenlandia cynanchica

Oldenlandia cynanchica, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, blue-violet flowers during the summer months. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
