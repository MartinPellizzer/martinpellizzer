Origanum foliosum

Origanum foliosum, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has creeping stems and narrow, aromatic leaves that emit a fresh, lemony scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its culinary and medicinal uses are valued for its aromatic properties and antiseptic qualities.
