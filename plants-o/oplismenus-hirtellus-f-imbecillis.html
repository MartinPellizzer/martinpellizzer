Oplismenus hirtellus f. imbecillis

Oplismenus hirtellus f. imbecillis, commonly known as the dwarf chain fern, is a compact, low-growing fern that exhibits unique morphological characteristics.

It features delicate, linear fronds that are shorter and more densely packed compared to the typical species, giving it a more compact appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a subtle, fern-like texture.

This fern is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
