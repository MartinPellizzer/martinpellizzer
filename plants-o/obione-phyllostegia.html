Obione phyllostegia

Obione phyllostegia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweeds and related plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense inflorescences and are pollinated by various insects.

Obione phyllostegia is known for its milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the Apocynaceae family.
