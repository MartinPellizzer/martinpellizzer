Oncidium oxyceras

Oncidium oxyceras, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually strap-like and lance-shaped, providing a lush backdrop for its showy blooms.

Oncidium oxyceras is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements.
