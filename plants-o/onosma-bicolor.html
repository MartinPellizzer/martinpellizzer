Onosma bicolor

Onosma bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored flowers, which typically feature a white base with a purple or violet upper lip. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Onosma bicolor is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
