Ornithogalum tenuifolium subsp. robustum

Ornithogalum tenuifolium subsp.

robustum, commonly known as the robust lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces umbels of white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant is characterized by its robust growth and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. It has a bulbous root system that stores nutrients, supporting its growth in various soil types.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
