Orophaca barrii

Orophaca barrii, commonly known as the Barri's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Orophaca barrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
