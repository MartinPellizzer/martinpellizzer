Orostachys japonica var. erubescens

Orostachys japonica var. erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that turn reddish in full sun. The plant produces small, bright red flowers during the summer months, adding vibrant color to its appearance. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
