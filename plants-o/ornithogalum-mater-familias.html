Ornithogalum mater-familias

Ornithogalum mater-familias, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for the plant's growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
