Ochna Guianensis

Ochna guianensis is a striking tropical plant native to South America, known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique foliage. It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is often found in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features palmate leaves with a glossy texture and clusters of bright yellow or orange blooms that attract pollinators. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, Ochna guianensis remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
