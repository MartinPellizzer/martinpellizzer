Onobrychis subacaulis f. grandiflora

Onobrychis subacaulis f. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered bursaria, is a perennial legume native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a cylindrical pod containing several seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
