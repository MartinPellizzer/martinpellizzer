Oxalis fabifolia

Oxalis fabifolia, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features trifoliate leaves, each composed of three heart-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Oxalis fabifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
