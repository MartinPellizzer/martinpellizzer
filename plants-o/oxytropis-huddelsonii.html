Oxytropis huddelsonii

Oxytropis huddelsonii, commonly known as Huddelson's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in arid and semi-arid regions.

The plant produces yellow or pale yellow pea-like flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Oxytropis huddelsonii is often found in sagebrush steppe and grassland habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.
