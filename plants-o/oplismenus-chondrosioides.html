Oplismenus chondrosioides

Oplismenus chondrosioides, commonly known as the silver thread or cat's tail plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, arranged in dense clusters. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily, forming dense mats in moist, shaded environments.

Oplismenus chondrosioides is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
