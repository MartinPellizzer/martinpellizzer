Othonna retrorsa var. spektakelensis

Othonna retrorsa var. spektakelensis, commonly known as the spectacle plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
