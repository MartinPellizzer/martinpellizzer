Oxytropis Nana Var. Fallax

Oxytropis nana var. fallax is a small, mat-forming legume native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Its resilience makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
