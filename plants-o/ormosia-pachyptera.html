Ormosia pachyptera

Ormosia pachyptera, commonly known as the thick-leaf ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces pod fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often found in lowland forests.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine, though its botanical study remains limited compared to more widely researched species.
