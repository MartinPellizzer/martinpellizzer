Oxytropis schensiensis

Oxytropis schensiensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. Oxytropis schensiensis is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

It is valued for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation and as a potential forage plant.
