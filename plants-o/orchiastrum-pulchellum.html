Orchiastrum pulchellum

Orchiastrum pulchellum, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors and intricate patterns. Native to tropical regions, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears multiple blooms, each with three sepals and three petals.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a pollinator attractant and its ornamental value in horticulture.
