Oreopanax nitidus

Oreopanax nitidus, commonly known as the glossy oreopanax, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
