Ossaea pulchra

Ossaea pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Ossaea pulchra typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and resilience.
