Oncidium spectabile

Oncidium spectabile, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, long-lasting flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal climates. Its leaves are typically broad and grass-like, arranged in a fan shape.

Oncidium spectabile is popular in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
