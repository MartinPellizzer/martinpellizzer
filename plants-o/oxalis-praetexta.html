Oxalis praetexta

Oxalis praetexta, commonly known as the Crimson Oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or orange vein pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis praetexta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and delicate blooms.
