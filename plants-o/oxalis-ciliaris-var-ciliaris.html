Oxalis ciliaris var. ciliaris

Oxalis ciliaris var. ciliaris, commonly known as the silverleaf oxalis, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery-white border, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to both garden and indoor settings.
