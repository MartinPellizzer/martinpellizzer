Onosma pilosa

Onosma pilosa, commonly known as the hairy onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Onosma pilosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
