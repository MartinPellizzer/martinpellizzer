Oeceoclades flavescens

Oeceoclades flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The flowers are small and clustered, with a yellowish hue, and are often borne on the upper part of the plant. This orchid prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
