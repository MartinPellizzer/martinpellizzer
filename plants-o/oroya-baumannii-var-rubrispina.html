Oroya baumannii var. rubrispina

Oroya baumannii var. rubrispina, commonly known as the red-spined oroya, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red spines that cover its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an important species in its native habitat.
