Onobrychis Tournefortii

Onobrychis tournefortii is a hardy legume native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient plant for challenging landscapes. Known for its feathery foliage and delicate pink or white flowers, it adds a graceful touch to natural settings. This plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its nitrogen-fixing abilities. Its adaptability and beauty make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated gardens.
