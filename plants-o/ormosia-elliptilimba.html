Ormosia elliptilimba

Ormosia elliptilimba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several elliptical, flat seeds.

Ormosia elliptilimba is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
