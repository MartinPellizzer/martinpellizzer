Osmorhiza longistylis var. villicaulis

Osmorhiza longistylis var. villicaulis, commonly known as the velvet root.

This variant is characterized by its hairy, or villosely textured, stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other varieties of the species. It is a member of the Apiaceae family, known for its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its root system is particularly notable for its thick, fleshy roots, which are often used in traditional herbal medicine.
