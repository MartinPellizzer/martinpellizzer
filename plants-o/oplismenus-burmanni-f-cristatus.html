Oplismenus burmanni f. cristatus

Oplismenus burmanni f. cristatus, commonly known as the crested iron plant, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its unique, rosette-like foliage.

The leaves are typically a vibrant green, with a textured surface and a waxy coating that gives them a distinctive appearance. One of its most notable features is the presence of a central crest or spine, which adds to its visual appeal. It is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
