Oberonia austroyunnanensis

Oberonia austroyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Oberonia, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing in the understory of dense forests. Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and its elusive nature, Oberonia austroyunnanensis remains a challenging plant to study and cultivate.
