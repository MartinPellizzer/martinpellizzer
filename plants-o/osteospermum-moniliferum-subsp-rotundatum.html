Osteospermum moniliferum subsp. rotundatum

Osteospermum moniliferum subsp.

rotundatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red.

The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval or rounded in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to gardens in temperate climates.
