Osteospermum foveolatum

Osteospermum foveolatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with silver-gray foliage and produces fluffy, spherical seed heads that are distinctive in appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
