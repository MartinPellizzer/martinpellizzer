Onobrychis psoraleifolia var. pleiophylla

Onobrychis psoraleifolia var. pleiophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved burr medic, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with broad, ovate leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant is characterized by its distinctive burr-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its forage quality and potential in ecological restoration projects.
