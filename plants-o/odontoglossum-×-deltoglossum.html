Odontoglossum × deltoglossum

Odontoglossum × deltoglossum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum and Deltoglossum species.

It is a tropical orchid characterized by its striking, showy flowers with vibrant colors and intricate patterns. The plant typically features sympodial growth, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
