Odontadenia grandiflora

Odontadenia grandiflora, commonly known as the giant toothed lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is a perennial herb with tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive toothed structure on the petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Odontadenia grandiflora thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
