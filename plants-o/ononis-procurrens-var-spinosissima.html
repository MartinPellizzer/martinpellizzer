Ononis procurrens var. spinosissima

Ononis procurrens var. spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has creeping stems covered in spiny tubercles, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.
