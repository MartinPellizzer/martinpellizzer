Otochilus fragrans

Otochilus fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually elongated and lance-shaped, with a waxy surface.

Otochilus fragrans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
