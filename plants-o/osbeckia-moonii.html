Osbeckia moonii

Osbeckia moonii, commonly known as the moon's flower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small herbaceous plant with delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Osbeckia moonii is often found in forest floors and along stream banks, where it contributes to the understory vegetation.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in New Zealand's flora.
