Ostericum viridiflorum var. praeteritum

Ostericum viridiflorum var. praeteritum, commonly known as the ancient green-flowered ostericum, is a rare and delicate perennial plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It features finely divided, grass-like foliage and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in early summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas with rich, acidic soil. Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and historical presence in botanical records.
