Onobrychis hypargyrea

Onobrychis hypargyrea, commonly known as silver vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Silver vetch is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and erosion control.
