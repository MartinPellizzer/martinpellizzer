Ornithogalum leichtlinii

Ornithogalum leichtlinii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale blue star-shaped flowers. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and ability to bloom in late winter or early spring.
