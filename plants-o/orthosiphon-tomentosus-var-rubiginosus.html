Orthosiphon tomentosus var. rubiginosus

Orthosiphon tomentosus var. rubiginosus, commonly known as the red-stemmed cat's whiskers, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The varietal name *rubiginosus* refers to the reddish coloration of its stems and leaves.
