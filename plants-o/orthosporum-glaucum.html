Orthosporum glaucum

Orthosporum glaucum, commonly known as the bluegrass spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with three stamens and a single pistil.

Orthosporum glaucum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
