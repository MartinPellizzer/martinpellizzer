Oenothera contorta var. flexuosa

Oenothera contorta var. flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, zigzagging stems and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional medicine.
