Olearia Viscidula

Olearia viscidula is a unique shrub native to New Zealand's subantarctic islands. It is known for its dense, sticky foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its resilience to harsh weather makes it an important part of its local ecosystem. Despite its tough exterior, Olearia viscidula plays a vital role in supporting native wildlife.
