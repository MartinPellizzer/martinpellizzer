Osbeckia stellata

Osbeckia stellata, commonly known as the starflower or starweed, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Osbeckia stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
