Odontoglossum × guttatum

Odontoglossum × guttatum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant with a complex botanical profile.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is a natural hybrid between Odontoglossum australis and Odontoglossum regelii. The plant is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns and a unique structure that includes a prominent lip. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This hybrid is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
