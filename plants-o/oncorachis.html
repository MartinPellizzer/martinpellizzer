Oncorachis

Oncorachis, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are native to coastal regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. Oncorachis species are characterized by their striking blue or purple ray flowers and silver-gray foliage. They typically grow in sandy or rocky soils and are adapted to maritime climates.
