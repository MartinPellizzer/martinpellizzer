Ornithogalum echinospermum

Ornithogalum echinospermum, commonly known as the spiny-seeded star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale blue star-shaped flowers. The plant's most distinctive feature is its spiny, spherical seed capsules, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum echinospermum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
