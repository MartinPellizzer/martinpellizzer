Oreotelia glauca

Oreotelia glauca, commonly known as the bluebark tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, pale blue flowers. Oreotelia glauca is adapted to harsh environments, including high altitudes and saline soils.
