Ossaea boekei

Ossaea boekei, commonly known as the Boeke's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Ossaea boekei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
