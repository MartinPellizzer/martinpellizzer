Oncidium reisii

Oncidium reisii, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings, resembling the wings of a butterfly. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its roots are adapted for absorption from the air. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium reisii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and long-lasting blooms.
