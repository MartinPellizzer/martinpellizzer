Olearia stellulata var. canescens

Olearia stellulata var. canescens, commonly known as the silver olearia, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features grayish-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting native pollinators. It is typically found in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
