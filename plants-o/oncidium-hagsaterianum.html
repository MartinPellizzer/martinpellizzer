Oncidium hagsaterianum

Oncidium hagsaterianum, commonly known as the Hagsater orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of yellow and green hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium hagsaterianum requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.
