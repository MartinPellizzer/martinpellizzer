Ornithogalum gambosanum

Ornithogalum gambosanum, commonly known as the African star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
