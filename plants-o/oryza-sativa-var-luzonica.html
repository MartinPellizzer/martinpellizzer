Oryza sativa var. luzonica

Oryza sativa var. luzonica, commonly known as Luzon rice, is a variety of rice native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect plant structure and small, fragrant panicles. The grains are typically long and slender, with a slightly waxy texture. This variety is well adapted to wetland conditions and is often cultivated in lowland rice fields.

It is valued for its aromatic qualities and is a staple food crop in the region.
