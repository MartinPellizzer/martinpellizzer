Oncinotis nitida

Oncinotis nitida, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and pond edges, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in dense colonies.

Oncinotis nitida plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
