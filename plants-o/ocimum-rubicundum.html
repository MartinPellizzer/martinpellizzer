Ocimum rubicundum

Ocimum rubicundum, commonly known as red sage or red basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and clusters of small, red flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ocimum rubicundum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
