Orthophytum disjunctum var. variegatum

Orthophytum disjunctum var. variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Orthophytum, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with green and white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows as a compact rosette, with leaves that are broad and slightly recurved. It produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
