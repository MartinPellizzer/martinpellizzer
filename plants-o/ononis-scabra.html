Ononis scabra

Ononis scabra, commonly known as common whitlowgrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, along roadsides, and in rocky areas.

Ononis scabra has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
