Oenothera × ligerica

Oenothera × ligerica, commonly known as the hybrid evening primrose, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Oenothera × ligerica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
