Oncidium mimeticum

Oncidium mimeticum, commonly known as the mimic orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are showy, with a variety of colors, and often resemble other orchid species, hence its common name.

Oncidium mimeticum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
