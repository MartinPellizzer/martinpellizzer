Odontoglossum × lawrenceanum

Odontoglossum × lawrenceanum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum australinum and Odontoglossum lawrenceanum.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces multiple leaves from the base. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers with bright colors, typically in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum × lawrenceanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
