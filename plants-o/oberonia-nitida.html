Oberonia nitida

Oberonia nitida, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It has a short, fleshy stem and produces aerial roots that help it absorb moisture from the air.

Oberonia nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
