Olearia phlogopappa var. angustifolia

Olearia phlogopappa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in harsh, rocky environments. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
