Odontostemma omeiense

Odontostemma omeiense, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocky substrates or in forest understories. Its flowering period occurs in the spring, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
