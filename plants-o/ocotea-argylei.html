Ocotea argylei

Ocotea argylei, commonly known as the Argyle's ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, cinnamon, and avocado. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and lance-shaped leaves with a fragrant aroma. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark purple berries.

Ocotea argylei is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.
