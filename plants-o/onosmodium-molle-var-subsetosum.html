Onosmodium molle var. subsetosum

Onosmodium molle var. subsetosum, commonly known as the hairy onosmodium, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in the early spring. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
