Ophrys kamtschatica

Ophrys kamtschatica, commonly known as the Kamchatka bee orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Russian Far East and parts of eastern Siberia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually yellow or brownish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by male bees.
