Odontarrhena marschalliana

Odontarrhena marschalliana, commonly known as the marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in humid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

Odontarrhena marschalliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
