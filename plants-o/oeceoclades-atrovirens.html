Oeceoclades atrovirens

Oeceoclades atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with high light exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by hobbyists.
