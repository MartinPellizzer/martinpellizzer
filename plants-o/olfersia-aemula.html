Olfersia aemula

Olfersia aemula, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers that resemble torches, with a distinctive red throat. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often covered in a waxy coating.

Olfersia aemula is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in greenhouses and gardens for its vibrant, showy blooms.
