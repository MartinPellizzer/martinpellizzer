Oxalis acromelaena

Oxalis acromelaena, commonly known as the purple heart oxalis, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant purple or reddish hue. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with five petals, typically in shades of white or pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis acromelaena is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
