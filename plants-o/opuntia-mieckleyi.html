Opuntia mieckleyi

Opuntia mieckleyi, commonly known as the Mexican prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in spines and glochids, which are small, barbed bristles. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunas, is edible and has a sweet, juicy flavor.

Opuntia mieckleyi is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
