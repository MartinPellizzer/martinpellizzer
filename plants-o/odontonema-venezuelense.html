Odontonema venezuelense

Odontonema venezuelense, commonly known as the Venezuelan flame bean, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange tubular flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Odontonema venezuelense is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
