Odontium rubrum var. simplex

Odontium rubrum var. simplex, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety red petals with a contrasting white lip, making it a visually appealing species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowering season occurs during the warmer months, producing clusters of fragrant blooms.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and relatively easy care requirements.
