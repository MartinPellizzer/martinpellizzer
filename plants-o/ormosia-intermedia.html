Ormosia intermedia

Ormosia intermedia, commonly known as the yellow ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a fibrous, aromatic bark and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth surface with a glossy appearance.

Ormosia intermedia is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in local medicine and crafts.
