Ostrya virginiana subsp. guatemalensis

Ostrya virginiana subsp.

guatemalensis, commonly known as Guatemalan hop-hornbeam, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to Central America. It belongs to the Betulaceae family, which includes birches and alders, and is characterized by its finely toothed leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree produces small, winged samaras that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.
