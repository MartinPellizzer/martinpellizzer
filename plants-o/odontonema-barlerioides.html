Odontonema barlerioides

Odontonema barlerioides, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Odontonema barlerioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
