Oenothera rubra

Oenothera rubra, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces yellow, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Oenothera rubra typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.

It is a herbaceous perennial with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.
