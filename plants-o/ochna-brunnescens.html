Ochna brunnescens

Ochna brunnescens, commonly known as the yellow ochna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features smooth, grayish-brown bark and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochna brunnescens is often found in rocky or wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
