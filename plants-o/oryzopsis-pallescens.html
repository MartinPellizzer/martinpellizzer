Oryzopsis pallescens

Oryzopsis pallescens, commonly known as the gray bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grayish-blue foliage and is often found in prairie and savanna habitats. The plant has a clumping growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, which helps it withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Oryzopsis pallescens plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
