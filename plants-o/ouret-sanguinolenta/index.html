Ouret Sanguinolenta

Ouret sanguinolenta is a rare and striking plant known for its vivid red blooms and unique growth patterns. Found primarily in specific high-altitude regions, it thrives in harsh environmental conditions. Its name, meaning "blood-red" in Latin, reflects the intense color of its flowers. Despite its beauty, the plant remains elusive and is often difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
