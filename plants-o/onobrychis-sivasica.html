Onobrychis sivasica

Onobrychis sivasica, commonly known as Sivasica burr clover, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native region.
