Onobrychis gracilis subsp. gracilis

Onobrychis gracilis subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as slender vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets.

The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
