Oncidium bifrons

Oncidium bifrons, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers, which resemble the wings of a butterfly. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium bifrons is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
