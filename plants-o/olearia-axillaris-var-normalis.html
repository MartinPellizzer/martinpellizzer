Olearia axillaris var. normalis

Olearia axillaris var. normalis, commonly known as the Silver Tussock, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Olearia axillaris var. normalis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
