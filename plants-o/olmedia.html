Olmedia

Olmedia, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its seeds are encased in a pod and can remain dormant in the soil for several years.

Olmedia is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
