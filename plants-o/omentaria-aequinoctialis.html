Omentaria aequinoctialis

Omentaria aequinoctialis, commonly known as the equinox orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom during the equinoxes. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often near streams or in dense undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
