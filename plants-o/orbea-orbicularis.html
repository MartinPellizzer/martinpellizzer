Orbea orbicularis

Orbea orbicularis, commonly known as the round-orbed orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, orbicular-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a robust rhizome and produces dense clusters of blooms during the warmer months. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

Orbea orbicularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
