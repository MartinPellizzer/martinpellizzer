Osmoglossum egertonii

Osmoglossum egertonii, commonly known as the Egerton's grass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often clustered at the base of the plant.
