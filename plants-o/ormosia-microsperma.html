Ormosia microsperma

Ormosia microsperma, commonly known as the small-seeded ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow. Its fruit is a legume pod containing minute, elongated seeds.

Ormosia microsperma is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
