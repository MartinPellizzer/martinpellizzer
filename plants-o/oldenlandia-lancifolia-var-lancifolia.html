Oldenlandia lancifolia var. lancifolia

Oldenlandia lancifolia var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows as an annual herb with slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for garden use in suitable climates.
