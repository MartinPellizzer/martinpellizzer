Onobrychis cyri

Onobrychis cyri, commonly known as the Cyprus burclover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Onobrychis cyri is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
