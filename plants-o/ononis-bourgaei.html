Ononis bourgaei

Ononis bourgaei, commonly known as the Mediterranean bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features wiry, branching stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in loose spikes, typically blooming from spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, linear to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

Ononis bourgaei is adapted to rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
