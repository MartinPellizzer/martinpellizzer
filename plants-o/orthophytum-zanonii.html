Orthophytum zanonii

Orthophytum zanonii, commonly known as the Zanonii orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Orthophytum zanonii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
