Oldenlandia rosulata var. rosulata

Oldenlandia rosulata var. rosulata, commonly known as the Rosulate Oldenlandia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit, with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
