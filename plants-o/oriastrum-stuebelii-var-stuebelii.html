Oriastrum stuebelii var. stuebelii

Oriastrum stuebelii var. stuebelii, commonly known as the Stuebel's flax.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its gray-green foliage and produces showy yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
