Oncidium verrucosum

Oncidium verrucosum, commonly known as the疣状万代兰, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees or other surfaces without soil.

Oncidium verrucosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
