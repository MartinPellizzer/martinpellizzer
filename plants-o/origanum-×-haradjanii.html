Origanum × haradjanii

Origanum × haradjanii, commonly known as Greek oregano, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are widely used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces pink to purple flowers, which bloom in summer and attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, contributing to its strong scent and therapeutic properties.
