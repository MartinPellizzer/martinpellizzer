Ozoroa marginata

Ozoroa marginata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves with a distinctive white margin. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. Ozoroa marginata prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for those seeking a low-maintenance, aesthetically pleasing plant.
