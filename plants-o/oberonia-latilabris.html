Oberonia latilabris

Oberonia latilabris, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive broad labellum. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oberonia latilabris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
