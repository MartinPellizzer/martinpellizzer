Osmundastrum claytonianum var. pilosum

Osmundastrum claytonianum var. pilosum, commonly known as the silky fern, is a variant of the Christmas fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, evergreen fronds that remain green throughout the year. The fronds are divided into narrow, overlapping segments that give the plant a lush, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's rhizomes are robust, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies over time.
