Orbis truncata

Orbis truncata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year under proper care. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Orbis truncata is known for its ability to adapt to a variety of environmental conditions, enhancing its appeal as a decorative plant.
