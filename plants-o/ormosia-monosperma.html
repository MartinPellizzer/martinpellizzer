Ormosia monosperma

Ormosia monosperma, commonly known as the single-seeded ormosia, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Ormosia monosperma is valued for its timber and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
