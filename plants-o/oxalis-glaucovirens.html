Oxalis glaucovirens

Oxalis glaucovirens, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white coating, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Oxalis glaucovirens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
