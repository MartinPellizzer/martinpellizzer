Odontoglossum myanthum

Odontoglossum myanthum, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen leaves.

Odontoglossum myanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
