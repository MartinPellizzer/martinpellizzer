Oncidium crispum var. limbatum

Oncidium crispum var. limbatum, commonly known as the Orchid with Crinkled Leaves, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of yellow and orange hues. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often crinkled or wavy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
