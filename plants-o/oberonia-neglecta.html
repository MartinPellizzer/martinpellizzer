Oberonia neglecta

Oberonia neglecta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as undergrowth in forests. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Oberonia neglecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
