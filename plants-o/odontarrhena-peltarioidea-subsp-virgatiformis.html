Odontarrhena peltarioidea subsp. virgatiformis

Odontarrhena peltarioidea subsp.

virgatiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed odontarrhena, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads.

This subspecies is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in coastal and inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.
