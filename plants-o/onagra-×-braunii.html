Onagra × braunii

Onagra × braunii, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a cross between Helianthus annuus and Helianthus tuberosus.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and robust, upright stems. The plant produces both male and female flowers, with the female flowers developing into the edible seeds that are rich in oil. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its roots are fibrous and deep-reaching.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential agricultural uses.
