Onopordum intermedium

Onopordum intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Its flowering stems are usually branched and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Onopordum intermedium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
