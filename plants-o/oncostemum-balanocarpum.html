Oncostemum balanocarpum

Oncostemum balanocarpum, commonly known as the yellow banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and sprawling canopy. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture, while its bark is thick and fissured.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various species.
