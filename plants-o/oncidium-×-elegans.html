Oncidium × elegans

Oncidium × elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America. The plant typically produces pendulous spikes of colorful, fragrant blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Oncidium × elegans thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor cultivation and as a container plant.
