Onobrychis sirjaevii

Onobrychis sirjaevii, commonly known as the Sirjaev's goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Additionally, it is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which enriches the soil.
