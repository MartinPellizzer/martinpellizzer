Oreosedum nevadense

Oreosedum nevadense, commonly known as the Sierra foothill stonecrop, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Oreosedum nevadense thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
