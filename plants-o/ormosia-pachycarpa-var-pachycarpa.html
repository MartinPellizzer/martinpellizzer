Ormosia pachycarpa var. pachycarpa

Ormosia pachycarpa var. pachycarpa, commonly known as the thick-fruited ormosia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody, globose fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical forests, growing in well-drained soils.

Ormosia pachycarpa var. pachycarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
