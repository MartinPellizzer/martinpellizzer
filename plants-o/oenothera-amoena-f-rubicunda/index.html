Oenothera Amoena F. Rubicunda

Red evening primrose, scientifically known as Oenothera amoena f. rubicunda, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the United States. This perennial species is renowned for its vibrant red petals that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. The plant's unique coloration and ecological role make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated environments. Its delicate yet resilient nature has earned it a special place in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts.
