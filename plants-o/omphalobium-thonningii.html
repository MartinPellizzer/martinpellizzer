Omphalobium thonningii

Omphalobium thonningii, commonly known as the African burr medic, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive burr-like seed pods that aid in seed dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Omphalobium thonningii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
