Oxalis zamorana

Oxalis zamorana, commonly known as the Zamorana wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces tiny, bead-like fruits that contain several seeds. Oxalis zamorana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and delicate plant.
