Oncidium gardneri var. praestans

Oncidium gardneri var. praestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces numerous racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees in its natural habitat.

It requires bright light, moderate temperatures, and good air circulation for optimal growth.
