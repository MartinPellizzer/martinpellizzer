Ochrosia mariannensis var. crassicarpa

Ochrosia mariannensis var. crassicarpa, commonly known as the thick-fruited ochrosia, is a tropical shrub native to the island of Guam.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. Ochrosia mariannensis var. crassicarpa is often found in dry to moist forest environments and is of interest for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
