Onoseris fraterna

Onoseris fraterna, commonly known as the Fraternally Blushing Daisy, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the family. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
