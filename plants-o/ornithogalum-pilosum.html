Ornithogalum pilosum

Ornithogalum pilosum, commonly known as the silky star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its hairy texture, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Ornithogalum pilosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
