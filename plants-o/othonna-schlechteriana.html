Othonna schlechteriana

Othonna schlechteriana, commonly known as the Schlechter's othonna, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Othonna schlechteriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
