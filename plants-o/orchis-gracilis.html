Orchis gracilis

Orchis gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender stem with a single or few flowers, each with a distinctive pouch-shaped lip. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing nutrients from the soil.

Orchis gracilis is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
