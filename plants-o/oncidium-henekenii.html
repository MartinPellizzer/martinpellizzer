Oncidium henekenii

Oncidium henekenii, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, raceme-like inflorescences with showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with green or brown markings. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium henekenii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
