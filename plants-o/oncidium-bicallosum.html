Oncidium bicallosum

Oncidium bicallosum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers, which are often clustered in racemes. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Oncidium bicallosum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
