Oxalis elongata

Oxalis elongata, commonly known as the spurred oxalis, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tubular leaves that resemble the shape of a heart or a spur, and it produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, often displaying vibrant color changes in response to light.

Oxalis elongata is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
