Oncidium stenoglossum

Oncidium stenoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms in summer, with flowers that have narrow, elongated petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium stenoglossum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
