Oreorchis patens

Oreorchis patens, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces tall, erect flowering stems with clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often more brightly colored.

Oreorchis patens is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
