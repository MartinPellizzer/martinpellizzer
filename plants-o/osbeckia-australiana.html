Osbeckia australiana

Osbeckia australiana, commonly known as the Australian willow-herb, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate environments. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in other damp habitats.

Osbeckia australiana plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
