Oxytenanthera macrothyrsus

Oxytenanthera macrothyrsus, commonly known as the giant panic grass, is a tall, robust grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, open panicles and long, narrow leaves that can reach several meters in height. The plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas, grasslands, and along roadsides. Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil, making it an important plant in ecological restoration projects.

Oxytenanthera macrothyrsus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential agricultural uses.
