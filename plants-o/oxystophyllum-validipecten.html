Oxystophyllum validipecten

Oxystophyllum validipecten, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.
