Othake canescens

Othake canescens, commonly known as the hairy othake, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.
