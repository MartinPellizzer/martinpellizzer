Ouratea reticulata var. angustifolia

Ouratea reticulata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly serrated margin. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
