Olearia phlogopappa subsp. gunniana

Olearia phlogopappa subsp.

gunniana, commonly known as the Northern River Daisy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in damp, shady environments such as stream banks and forest margins. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and arranged alternately along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
