Ophrys × Gauthieri Subsp. Fraresiana

Ophrys × gauthieri subsp. fraresiana is a rare and fascinating orchid species found primarily in the Mediterranean region. This orchid is known for its unique appearance, featuring intricate floral structures that mimic the shapes and colors of female insects. Its hybrid origin highlights the complex evolutionary relationships among orchid species. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into both botanical diversity and ecological conservation.
