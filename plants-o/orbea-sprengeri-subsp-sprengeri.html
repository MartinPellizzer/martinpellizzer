Orbea sprengeri subsp. sprengeri

Orbea sprengeri subsp.

sprengeri, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and ability to bloom reliably make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
