Odontoglossum glonerianum

Odontoglossum glonerianum, commonly known as the Gloriosa orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum glonerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
