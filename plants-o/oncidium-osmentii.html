Oncidium osmentii

Oncidium osmentii, commonly known as the Orange Sunrise orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a mix of orange, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in seasonal environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, often blooming in late spring or early summer.

Oncidium osmentii is valued in horticulture for its long-lasting blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
