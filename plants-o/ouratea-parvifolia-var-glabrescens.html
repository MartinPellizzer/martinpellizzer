Ouratea parvifolia var. glabrescens

Ouratea parvifolia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf ouratea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
