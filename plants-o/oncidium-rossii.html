Oncidium rossii

Oncidium rossii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellow flowers with a distinctive golden throat. Oncidium rossii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
