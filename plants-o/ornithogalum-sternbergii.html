Ornithogalum sternbergii

Ornithogalum sternbergii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. During the flowering season, it bears clusters of white or pale pink star-shaped flowers that are highly ornamental. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its attractive appearance and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
