Oreophilus nummularius

Oreophilus nummularius, commonly known as the silverleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Oreophilus nummularius thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
