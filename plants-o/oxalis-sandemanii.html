Oxalis sandemanii

Oxalis sandemanii, commonly known as the sandemanii wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is a succulent, adapted to arid environments, with fleshy stems and leaves that store water. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Oxalis sandemanii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
