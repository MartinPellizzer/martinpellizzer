Oenothera bipartita

Oenothera bipartita, commonly known as the two-colored evening primrose, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow and orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds. Oenothera bipartita is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and prairies.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
