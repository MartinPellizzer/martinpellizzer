Oreocarya suffruticosa var. arenicola

Oreocarya suffruticosa var. arenicola, commonly known as the sandstone buckthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils, often found on rocky or sandy slopes. Its fruit is a small, capsule that contains several seeds.

This variety is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
