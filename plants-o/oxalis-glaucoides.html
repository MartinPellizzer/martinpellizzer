Oxalis glaucoides

Oxalis glaucoides, commonly known as the blue oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with a bluish-green hue and small, delicate flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage and the fleeting beauty of its blooms.
