Onohualcoa seleri

Onohualcoa seleri, commonly known as the Seler's onohualcoa, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is characterized by broad, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
