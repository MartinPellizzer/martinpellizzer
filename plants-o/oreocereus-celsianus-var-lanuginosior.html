Oreocereus celsianus var. lanuginosior

Oreocereus celsianus var. lanuginosior, commonly known as the woolly moon cactus, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a well-developed root system adapted to arid environments.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
