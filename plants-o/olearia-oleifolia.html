Olearia oleifolia

Olearia oleifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, daisy-like white flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Olearia oleifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
