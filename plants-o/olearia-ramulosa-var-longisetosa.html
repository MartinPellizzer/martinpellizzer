Olearia ramulosa var. longisetosa

Olearia ramulosa var. longisetosa, commonly known as the long-hairy daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant has densely hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in its native environment. It typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to maritime conditions.

Olearia ramulosa var. longisetosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.
