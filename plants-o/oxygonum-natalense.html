Oxygonum natalense

Oxygonum natalense, commonly known as Natal spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, arching stems with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Oxygonum natalense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
