Oldenlandia valida

Oldenlandia valida, commonly known as the silverleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, small white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Oldenlandia valida is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
