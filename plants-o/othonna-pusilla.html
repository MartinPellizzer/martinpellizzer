Othonna pusilla

Othonna pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf othonna, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, succulent leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in terminal spikes. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Othonna pusilla is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
