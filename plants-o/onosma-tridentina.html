Onosma tridentina

Onosma tridentina, commonly known as the three-toothed onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, with five petals and a prominent central tube.

Onosma tridentina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
