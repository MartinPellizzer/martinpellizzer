Orchis cernua

Orchis cernua, commonly known as the nodding orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodlands and meadows. The plant features a single, upright stem with a few leaves and a single, drooping flower spike. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Orchis cernua is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, damp environments.
