Oryza sativa var. philippensis

Oryza sativa var. philippensis, commonly known as the Philippine rice.

This variety is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its short stature and compact grain structure. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and warm temperatures. The plant typically has a fibrous root system and produces panicles that bear small, white flowers.

Its grains are generally smaller than those of the common rice varieties and are often used in local cuisines.
