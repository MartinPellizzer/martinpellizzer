Ochna coccinea

Ochna coccinea, commonly known as the red ochna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red, showy flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochna coccinea is often found in rocky or wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
