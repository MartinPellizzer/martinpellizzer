Oddoniodendron reitsmarum

Oddoniodendron reitsmarum, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Oddoniodendron reitsmarum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
