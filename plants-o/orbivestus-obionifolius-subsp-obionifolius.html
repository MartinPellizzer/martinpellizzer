Orbivestus obionifolius subsp. obionifolius

Orbivestus obionifolius subsp.

obionifolius, commonly known as the obionifolius orchid, is a species native to specific regions in South America. It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of tightly clustered, showy flowers arranged in a spiral pattern.

The plant produces small, fragrant blossoms that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
