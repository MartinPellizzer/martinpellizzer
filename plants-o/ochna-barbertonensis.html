Ochna barbertonensis

Ochna barbertonensis, commonly known as the Barberton oxeye, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and emits a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and inland gardens.

Ochna barbertonensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
