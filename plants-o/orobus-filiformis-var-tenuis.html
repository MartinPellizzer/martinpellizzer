Orobus filiformis var. tenuis

Orobus filiformis var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender orobus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats, often in dry or semi-arid regions. The plant features slender, erect stems that are covered in fine hairs, and its small, white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months. It has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
