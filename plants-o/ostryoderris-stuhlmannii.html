Ostryoderris stuhlmannii

Ostryoderris stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

Ostryoderris stuhlmannii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
