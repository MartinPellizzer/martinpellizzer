Onobrychis picta

Onobrychis picta, commonly known as the painted vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features trifoliate leaves with distinctive patterns, and its yellow flowers are borne in clusters. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Onobrychis picta is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
