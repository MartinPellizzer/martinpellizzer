Otochlamys eckloniana

Otochlamys eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually dark green and sword-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
