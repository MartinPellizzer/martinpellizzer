Onosma cinerea

Onosma cinerea, commonly known as the grey thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green, woolly foliage and tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Onosma cinerea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
