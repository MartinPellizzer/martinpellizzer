Oncidium foveatum

Oncidium foveatum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences and brightly colored flowers, often featuring yellow and green hues. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in varying conditions.

Oncidium foveatum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
