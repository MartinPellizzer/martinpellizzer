Oberonia rhizoides

Oberonia rhizoides, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained, acidic soil.

Oberonia rhizoides is noted for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
