Oreiostachys schlechteri

Oreiostachys schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that are typically arranged in a panicle-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
