Ornithogalum graecum var. ciliolatum

Ornithogalum graecum var. ciliolatum, commonly known as the Greek star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in spring. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its delicate foliage and is sometimes used in floral arrangements.
