Ouret congesta

Ouret congesta, commonly known as the clustered ouret, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Ouret congesta is often found growing in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline.

It is adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.
