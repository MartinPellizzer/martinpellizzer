Ochrosia thiollierei

Ochrosia thiollierei, commonly known as the Madagascar star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Ochrosia thiollierei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
