Oncidium ayabacanum

Oncidium ayabacanum, commonly known as the Ayabacan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium ayabacanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
