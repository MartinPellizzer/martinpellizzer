Ornithogalum striatum

Ornithogalum striatum, commonly known as the striped star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its striking, striped leaves and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Ornithogalum striatum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
