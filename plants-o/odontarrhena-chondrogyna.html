Odontarrhena chondrogyna

Odontarrhena chondrogyna, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Odontarrhena chondrogyna is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
