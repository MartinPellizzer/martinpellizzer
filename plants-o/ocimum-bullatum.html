Ocimum bullatum

Ocimum bullatum, commonly known as the Purple Basil or Chocolate Basil, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking purple to violet-colored leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale purple, adding visual interest to gardens. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications for their unique flavor and aroma.

Ocimum bullatum thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
