Oldenlandia hymenophylla

Oldenlandia hymenophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf or velvet leaf, is a delicate, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
