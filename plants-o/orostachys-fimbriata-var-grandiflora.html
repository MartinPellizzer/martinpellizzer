Orostachys fimbriata var. grandiflora

Orostachys fimbriata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Japanese stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
