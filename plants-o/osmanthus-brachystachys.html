Osmanthus brachystachys

Osmanthus brachystachys, commonly known as the small-stemmed osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its fruit is a capsule containing tiny, spherical seeds.

Osmanthus brachystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic foliage and blooms.
