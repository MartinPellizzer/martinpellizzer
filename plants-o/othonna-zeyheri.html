Othonna zeyheri

Othonna zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's othonna, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features succulent, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Othonna zeyheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.
