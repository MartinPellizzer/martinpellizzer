Oncidium phylloglossum

Oncidium phylloglossum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

Oncidium phylloglossum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
