Oudemansia javensis

Oudemansia javensis, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oudemansia javensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
