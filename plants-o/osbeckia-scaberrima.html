Osbeckia scaberrima

Osbeckia scaberrima, commonly known as rough osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas, particularly found in regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features hairy, branched stems and alternate, ovate leaves with a rough, scabrous texture. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds.

Osbeckia scaberrima is known for its adaptability to harsh environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
