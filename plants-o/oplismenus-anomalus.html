Oplismenus anomalus

Oplismenus anomalus, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its flowering spikes produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked.

Oplismenus anomalus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
