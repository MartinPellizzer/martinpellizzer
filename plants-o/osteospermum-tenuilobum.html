Osteospermum tenuilobum

Osteospermum tenuilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats and prefers well-drained soils.
