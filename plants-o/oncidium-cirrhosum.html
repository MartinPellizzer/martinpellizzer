Oncidium cirrhosum

Oncidium cirrhosum, commonly known as the curly lip orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and produces racemes of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent, curling lips. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Oncidium cirrhosum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
