Oberonia pachyrachis

Oberonia pachyrachis, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Oberonia pachyrachis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
