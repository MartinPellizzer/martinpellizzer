Orchis morio var. gigas

Orchis morio var. gigas, commonly known as the giant early purple orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland areas. It has a tall, slender stem with a single spike of flowers.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
