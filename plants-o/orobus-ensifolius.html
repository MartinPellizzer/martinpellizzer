Orobus ensifolius

Orobus ensifolius, commonly known as the swordleaf orobus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Orobus ensifolius thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
