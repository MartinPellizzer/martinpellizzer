Origanum orega

Origanum orega, commonly known as wild oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale blue or purple flowers and aromatic, fuzzy leaves that emit a strong, pungent scent. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to their high content of essential oils.

Origanum orega is valued for its hardy nature and ability to attract beneficial insects to gardens.
