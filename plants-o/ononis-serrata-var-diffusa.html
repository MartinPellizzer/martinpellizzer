Ononis serrata var. diffusa

Ononis serrata var. diffusa, commonly known as the serrated ononis, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, pink to purple flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
