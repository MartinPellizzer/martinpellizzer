Ornithochilus moretonii

Ornithochilus moretonii, commonly known as the Moreton Bay Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the subtropical regions of eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and coastal areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy spike of pale green to yellowish flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Ornithochilus moretonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
