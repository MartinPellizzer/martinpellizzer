Ochna hackarsii

Ochna hackarsii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Ochna hackarsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
