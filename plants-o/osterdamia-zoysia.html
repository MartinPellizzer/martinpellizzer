Osterdamia zoysia

Osterdamia zoysia, commonly known as zoysia grass, is a warm-season grass species native to Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It is characterized by its fine texture, dense growth habit, and ability to form a thick, resilient turf. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically light to medium green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Osterdamia zoysia is often used in lawns, sports fields, and erosion control due to its durability and aesthetic appeal.
