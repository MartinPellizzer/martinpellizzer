Oncostemum microsphaerum

Oncostemum microsphaerum, commonly known as the small sphere scale.

It is a species of scale insect belonging to the family Coccidae, characterized by its small, rounded body covered in a waxy coating. The plant is typically found on the stems and leaves of various host plants, where it feeds on plant sap. Its botanical significance lies in its role as a potential pest in horticultural and agricultural settings.

Due to its cryptic appearance, it is often difficult to identify without specialized examination.
