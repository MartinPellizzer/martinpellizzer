Oncidium kalbreyerianum

Oncidium kalbreyerianum, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow to orange with red or brown markings. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium kalbreyerianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
