Oryza sativa var. alaotrana

Oryza sativa var. alaotrana, commonly known as the Alaotran rice, is a subspecies of rice native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and small, fragrant grains. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces panicles that bear numerous small, white flowers.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and is an important crop in local agriculture.
