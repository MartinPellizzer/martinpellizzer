Odontarrhena tortuosa subsp. heterophylla

Odontarrhena tortuosa subsp.

heterophylla, commonly known as the spiral-leaved odontarrhena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves along the stem, which gives it its common name.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
