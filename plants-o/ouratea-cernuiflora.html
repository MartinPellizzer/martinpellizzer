Ouratea cernuiflora

Ouratea cernuiflora, commonly known as the hanging ouratea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its drooping, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
