Osbeckia rostrata var. marginulata

Osbeckia rostrata var. marginulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved osbeckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have distinct margins. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is characterized by its adaptability to arid conditions and is often found in disturbed soils.
