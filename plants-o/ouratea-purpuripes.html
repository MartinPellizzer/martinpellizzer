Ouratea purpuripes

Ouratea purpuripes, commonly known as the purple-leaf ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a striking purple underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The leaves are typically leathery and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
