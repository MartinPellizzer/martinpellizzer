Orthophytum foliosum

Orthophytum foliosum, commonly known as the leafy orthophytum, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers during the growing season, which are clustered in a showy inflorescence. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Orthophytum foliosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
