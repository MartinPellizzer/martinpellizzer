Ornithidium donaldeedodii

Ornithidium donaldeedodii, commonly known as the Moonlight Orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale blue petals that emit a faint luminescence in the evening, a trait attributed to its unique bioluminescent properties. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires a specific symbiotic relationship with certain mycorrhizal fungi to flourish. Its flowers bloom only during the full moon, making it a subject of fascination for botanists and horticulturists alike.

Due to its limited habitat and endangered status, conservation efforts are underway to protect this botanical marvel.
