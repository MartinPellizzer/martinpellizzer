Ornithogalum insigne

Ornithogalum insigne, commonly known as the showy star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ornithogalum insigne is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
