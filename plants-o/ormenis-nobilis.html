Ormenis nobilis

Ormenis nobilis, commonly known as the noble ormenis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Ormenis nobilis is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
