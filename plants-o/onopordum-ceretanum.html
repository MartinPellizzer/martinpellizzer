Onopordum ceretanum

Onopordum ceretanum, commonly known as the Mediterranean thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Its inflorescence consists of a cluster of small, tubular flowers surrounded by bracts.

Onopordum ceretanum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
