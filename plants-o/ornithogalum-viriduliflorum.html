Ornithogalum viriduliflorum

Ornithogalum viriduliflorum, commonly known as the green-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
