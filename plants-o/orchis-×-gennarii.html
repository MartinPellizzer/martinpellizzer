Orchis × gennarii

Orchis × gennarii, commonly known as the Gennari orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Orchis morio and Orchis mascula.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, particularly found in moist grasslands and woodlands. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with intricate patterns, typical of the Orchidaceae family. It has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
