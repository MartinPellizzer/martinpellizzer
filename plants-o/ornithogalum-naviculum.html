Ornithogalum naviculum

Ornithogalum naviculum, commonly known as the boat lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant's leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum naviculum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
