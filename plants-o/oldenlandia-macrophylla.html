Oldenlandia macrophylla

Oldenlandia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often accompanied by long, slender bracts. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
