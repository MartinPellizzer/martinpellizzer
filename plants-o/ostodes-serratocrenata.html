Ostodes serratocrenata

Ostodes serratocrenata, commonly known as the serrated crested grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive serrated edge, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small mammals.
