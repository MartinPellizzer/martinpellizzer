Orostachys maximowiczii

Orostachys maximowiczii, commonly known as the stonecress, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of East Asia.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers during the growing season, typically in summer. It is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Orostachys maximowiczii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
