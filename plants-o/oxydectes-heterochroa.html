Oxydectes heterochroa

Oxydectes heterochroa, commonly known as the variegated forest fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright green and yellow variegated fronds that add visual interest to its foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with each leaflet bearing a unique color pattern.

Oxydectes heterochroa thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
