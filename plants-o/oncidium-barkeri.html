Oncidium barkeri

Oncidium barkeri, commonly known as the Barker's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.

Oncidium barkeri is known for its long-lasting blooms and ability to bloom multiple times a year under proper conditions.
