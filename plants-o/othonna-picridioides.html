Othonna picridioides

Othonna picridioides, commonly known as the yellow-leaf othonna, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellowish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Othonna picridioides is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
