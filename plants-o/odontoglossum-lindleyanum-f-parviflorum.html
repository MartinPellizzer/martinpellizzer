Odontoglossum lindleyanum f. parviflorum

Odontoglossum lindleyanum f. parviflorum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate foliage.

It originates from the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
