Ornithogalum pluttulum

Ornithogalum pluttulum, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for the plant's growth. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
