Odontoglossum coronarium var. miniatum

Odontoglossum coronarium var. miniatum, commonly known as the miniature crowned orchid, is a miniature orchid species native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that resemble miniature versions of the typical Odontoglossum coronarium. The plant produces showy, violet or purple flowers with a distinctive crown-like structure on the lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
