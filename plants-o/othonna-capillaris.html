Othonna capillaris

Othonna capillaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved othonna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Othonna capillaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
