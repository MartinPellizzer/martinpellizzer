Odontochilus Brevistylis Subsp. Candidus

Odontochilus brevistylis subsp. candidus is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique plant is known for its striking white flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high levels of indirect light. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
