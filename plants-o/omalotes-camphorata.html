Omalotes camphorata

Omalotes camphorata, commonly known as the camphor tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark that contain camphor. The plant typically grows as a tall, evergreen tree with smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, white flowers followed by dark, oval berries.

Omalotes camphorata is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
