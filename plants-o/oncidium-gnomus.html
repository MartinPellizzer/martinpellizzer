Oncidium gnomus

Oncidium gnomus, commonly known as the "Dwarf Orchid," is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, white, and orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. Oncidium gnomus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a desirable addition to indoor gardens and greenhouse collections.
