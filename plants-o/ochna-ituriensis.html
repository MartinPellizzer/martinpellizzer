Ochna ituriensis

Ochna ituriensis, commonly known as the Ituri ochna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

Ochna ituriensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native environment.
