Oreiostachys pullei

Oreiostachys pullei, commonly known as the Chinese grass or Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of grass species. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
