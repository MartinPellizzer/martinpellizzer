Oncidium toachicum

Oncidium toachicum, commonly known as the Tachica orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, adapted to its moist, shaded habitat.

Oncidium toachicum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
