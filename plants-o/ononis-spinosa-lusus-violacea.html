Ononis spinosa lusus violacea

Ononis spinosa lusus violacea, commonly known as the violet spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Capparaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to violet, which give it its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, typical of many species in the Capparaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
