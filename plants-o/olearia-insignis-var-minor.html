Olearia insignis var. minor

Olearia insignis var. minor, commonly known as the small silverwood, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
