Odontoglossum × claesianum

Odontoglossum × claesianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum raphanifolium and Odontoglossum claesus.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andes Mountains in South America. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum × claesianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
