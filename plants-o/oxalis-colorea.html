Oxalis colorea

Oxalis colorea, commonly known as the rainbow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from pink to red and yellow. The plant features trifoliate leaves, each leaflet being heart-shaped and often adorned with a red or purple vein. Oxalis colorea thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
