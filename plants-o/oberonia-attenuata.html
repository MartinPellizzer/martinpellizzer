Oberonia attenuata

Oberonia attenuata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often clustered at the base of the stem.

Oberonia attenuata is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
