Onobrychis luristanica

Onobrychis luristanica, commonly known as the Luristan burr vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in semi-arid and temperate climates. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Onobrychis luristanica is also valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
