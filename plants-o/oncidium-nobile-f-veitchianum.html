Oncidium nobile f. veitchianum

Oncidium nobile f. veitchianum, commonly known as the Veitch's Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. The plant produces pendulous spikes of fragrant, yellow-orange blossoms with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
