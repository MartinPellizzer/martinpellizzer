Ozothamnus alpinus

Ozothamnus alpinus, commonly known as alpine sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features gray-green, fuzzy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Ozothamnus alpinus is adapted to cold climates and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy species in harsh environments.
