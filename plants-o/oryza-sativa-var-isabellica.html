Oryza sativa var. isabellica

Oryza sativa var. isabellica, commonly known as Isabellica rice, is a traditional rice variety native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated grains and a distinct aroma. This variety is typically grown in lowland wet rice fields and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth. Isabellica rice is valued for its unique flavor and is often used in traditional Filipino dishes.

Its cultivation is closely tied to the cultural and agricultural heritage of the region.
