Oncidium tenuirostre

Oncidium tenuirostre, commonly known as the narrow-beak orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Oncidium tenuirostre is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
