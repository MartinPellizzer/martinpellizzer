Odontonema coccineum

Odontonema coccineum, commonly known as the scarlet lily or red lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for.

Odontonema coccineum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
