Oncidium cucullatum var. dayanum

Oncidium cucullatum var. dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
