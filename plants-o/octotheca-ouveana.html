Octotheca ouveana

Octotheca ouveana, commonly known as the Ouvean starflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, glossy leaves that are oval in shape. Its flowers typically appear in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils under partial to full sun.
