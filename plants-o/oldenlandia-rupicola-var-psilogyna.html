Oldenlandia rupicola var. psilogyna

Oldenlandia rupicola var. psilogyna, commonly known as the rock oldenlandia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
