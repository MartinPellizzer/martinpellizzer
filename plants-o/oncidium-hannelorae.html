Oncidium hannelorae

Oncidium hannelorae, commonly known as the Hannelore orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Oncidium hannelorae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
