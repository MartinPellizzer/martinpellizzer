Oxydectes draconopsis

Oxydectes draconopsis, commonly known as the dragon's breath orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescence that resembles a dragon's breath. The plant produces vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warm season. Its unique morphology and vivid colors make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
