Olearia gunniana var. angustifolia

Olearia gunniana var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved daisy bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Olearia gunniana var. angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
