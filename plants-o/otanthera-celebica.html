Otanthera celebica

Otanthera celebica, commonly known as the Celebes aster, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in wetlands and swampy areas, adapting to its humid tropical environment. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk, and it produces small, winged seeds for wind dispersal.
