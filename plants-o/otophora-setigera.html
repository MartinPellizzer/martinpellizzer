Otophora setigera

Otophora setigera, commonly known as the slender lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive thallus structure with a crusty upper layer and a more loosely structured lower layer. The plant exhibits a pale green to yellowish coloration, which helps it absorb sunlight for photosynthesis. It is often found on tree trunks and rocks in temperate regions.

Otophora setigera plays a vital role in nutrient cycling and serves as a habitat for various microorganisms.
