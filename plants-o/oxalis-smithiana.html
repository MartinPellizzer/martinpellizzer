Oxalis smithiana

Oxalis smithiana, commonly known as the purple oxalis, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often tinged with purple or red hues. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, white, or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Oxalis smithiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
