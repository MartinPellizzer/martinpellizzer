Ornithocephalus numenius

Ornithocephalus numenius, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stems above the water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often floating on the surface of ponds or lakes. Its roots are submerged, absorbing nutrients from the aquatic environment.

The sacred lotus is also known for its symbolic significance in various cultures and religious traditions.
