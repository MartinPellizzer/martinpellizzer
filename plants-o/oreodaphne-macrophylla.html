Oreodaphne macrophylla

Oreodaphne macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed oreodaphne, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
