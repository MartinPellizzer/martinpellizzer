Origanum × balearicum

Origanum × balearicum, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that emit a distinct lemony scent when crushed. The plant produces pink to purple flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its culinary and medicinal uses are valued for its essential oils and antiseptic properties.
