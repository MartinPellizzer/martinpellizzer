Orbea wissmannii var. eremastrum

Orbea wissmannii var. eremastrum, commonly known as the Eremastrum orbea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically green and may turn reddish under intense sunlight.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
