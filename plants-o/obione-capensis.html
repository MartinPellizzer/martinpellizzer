Obione capensis

Obione capensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Obione capensis is also valued for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
