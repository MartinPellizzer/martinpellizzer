Ottochloa arnottiana

Ottochloa arnottiana, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to brown flowers.

Ottochloa arnottiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
