Oldenlandia Lapeyrousei

Oldenlandia lapeyrousei is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant, star-shaped blooms that often display a mix of white, yellow, and red hues. This species thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
