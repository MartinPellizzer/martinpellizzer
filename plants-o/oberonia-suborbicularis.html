Oberonia suborbicularis

Oberonia suborbicularis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
