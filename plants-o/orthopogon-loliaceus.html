Orthopogon loliaceus

Orthopogon loliaceus, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Orthopogon loliaceus is primarily a herbaceous perennial, exhibiting a growth habit that allows it to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
