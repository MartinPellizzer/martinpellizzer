Oplopanax horridus var. brevilobus

Oplopanax horridus var. brevilobus, commonly known as the short-lobed ginseng, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of North America.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately compound leaves with short lobes. Due to overharvesting, it is also considered a threatened species in some regions.
