Olearia populifolia

Olearia populifolia, commonly known as the silver tree, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its silvery-gray, woolly leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. Olearia populifolia is adapted to coastal environments and is often found in dunes or on rocky shores.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in New Zealand's flora.
