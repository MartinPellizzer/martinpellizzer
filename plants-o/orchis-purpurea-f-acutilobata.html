Orchis purpurea f. acutilobata

Orchis purpurea f. acutilobata, commonly known as the sharp-lobed purple orchid, is a botanical variant of the common purple orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped with sharp lobes, giving it its specific epithet.

This orchid is known for its showy blooms and is often found in the temperate regions of Europe.
