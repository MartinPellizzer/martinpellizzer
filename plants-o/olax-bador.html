Olax bador

Olax bador, commonly known as the African olive, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. Due to its hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
