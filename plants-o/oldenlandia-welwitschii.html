Oldenlandia welwitschii

Oldenlandia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
