Ormocarpum coronilloides

Ormocarpum coronilloides, commonly known as the silverleaf orache, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white or silver hairs, giving it its common name. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

Ormocarpum coronilloides is known for its drought tolerance and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
