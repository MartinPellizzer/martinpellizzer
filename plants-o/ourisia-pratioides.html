Ourisia pratioides

Ourisia pratioides, commonly known as the common ourisia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features wiry, ascending stems with opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its hardy nature and ability to attract pollinators.
