Olfersia splendens

Olfersia splendens, commonly known as the golden torch or golden torch orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow inflorescence that resembles a torch. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Olfersia splendens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique appearance.
