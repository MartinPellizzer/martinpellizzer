Ormosia euneura

Ormosia euneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate with several pairs of narrow, elliptical leaflets. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in racemes, and the plant produces leguminous pods that contain several seeds.

Ormosia euneura is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
