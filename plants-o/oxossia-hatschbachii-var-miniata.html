Oxossia hatschbachii var. miniata

Oxossia hatschbachii var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature oxossia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, intricate flower structure, with a distinctive coloration pattern of red and white markings. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters, showcasing a complex arrangement of petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
