Oenothera communis f. suaveolens

Oenothera communis f. suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
