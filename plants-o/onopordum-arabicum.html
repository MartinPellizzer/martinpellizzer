Onopordum arabicum

Onopordum arabicum, commonly known as the desert thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss. Its yellow flowers appear in late summer and are followed by spiny, globe-shaped fruits.

Onopordum arabicum is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
