Osteospermum trigonospermum

Osteospermum trigonospermum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like blooms. The plant features a sturdy, upright stem with narrow, gray-green leaves. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and yellow, with a distinctive central disk.

Osteospermum trigonospermum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and resilience in various climates.
