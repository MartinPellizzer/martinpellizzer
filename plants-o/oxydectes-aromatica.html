Oxydectes aromatica

Oxydectes aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant dune grass, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune ecosystems. Its aromatic properties make it notable for its use in traditional herbal remedies.

Oxydectes aromatica plays a crucial role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion.
