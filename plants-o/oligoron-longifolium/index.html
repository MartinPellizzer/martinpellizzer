Oligoron Longifolium

Oligoron longifolium is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia. Known for its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pale blue blooms, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This plant is often found growing in shaded forest understories where it receives filtered sunlight. Due to habitat loss and overcollection, Oligoron longifolium is now classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species for future generations.
