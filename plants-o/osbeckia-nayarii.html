Osbeckia nayarii

Osbeckia nayarii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.

Osbeckia nayarii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying xerophytic adaptations.
