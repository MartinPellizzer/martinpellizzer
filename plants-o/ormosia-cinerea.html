Ormosia cinerea

Ormosia cinerea, commonly known as the pale ormosia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The tree produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its bark and leaves have been utilized for their medicinal properties in various indigenous cultures.

Ormosia cinerea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
