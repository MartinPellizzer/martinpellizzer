Oerstedella ornata

Oerstedella ornata, commonly known as the ornate orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate flower structure with a prominent, spurred lip and vividly colored petals. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect light. Its flowers bloom in clusters, adding a decorative and ecological value to its habitat.

Oerstedella ornata is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
