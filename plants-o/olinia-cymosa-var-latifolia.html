Olinia cymosa var. latifolia

Olinia cymosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved olinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Its botanical features make it an attractive species for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
