Olearia phlogopappa var. brevipes

Olearia phlogopappa var. brevipes, commonly known as the slender daisy bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is typically found in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
