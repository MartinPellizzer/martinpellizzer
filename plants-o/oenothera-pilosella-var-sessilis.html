Oenothera pilosella var. sessilis

Oenothera pilosella var. sessilis, commonly known as the sessile evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The sessile evening primrose is also noted for its ability to attract nocturnal pollinators like moths.
