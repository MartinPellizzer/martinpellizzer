Oxalis nivea

Oxalis nivea, commonly known as the snowflake oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and trifoliate leaves that resemble clover. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a subtle elegance to its foliage.

Oxalis nivea is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
