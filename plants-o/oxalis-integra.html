Oxalis integra

Oxalis integra, commonly known as the purple heart oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, vibrant purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Oxalis integra is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and prolonged blooming period.
