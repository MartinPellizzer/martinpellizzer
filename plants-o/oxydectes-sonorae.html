Oxydectes sonorae

Oxydectes sonorae, commonly known as the Sonoran dune lily, is a flowering plant native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, with deep roots that help it survive drought conditions.

Oxydectes sonorae plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dunes and providing nectar for pollinators.
