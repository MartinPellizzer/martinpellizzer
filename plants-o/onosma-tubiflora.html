Onosma tubiflora

Onosma tubiflora, commonly known as the woolly flaxflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to dry, coastal climates.

Onosma tubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
