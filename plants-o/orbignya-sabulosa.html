Orbignya sabulosa

Orbignya sabulosa, commonly known as the Brazilian palm or the sand palm, is a tropical plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, multi-stemmed trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, round fruits that contain a single seed.
