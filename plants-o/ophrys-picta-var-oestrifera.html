Ophrys picta var. oestrifera

Ophrys picta var. oestrifera, commonly known as the painted orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
