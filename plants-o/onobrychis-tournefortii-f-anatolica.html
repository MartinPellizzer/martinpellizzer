Onobrychis tournefortii f. anatolica

Onobrychis tournefortii f. anatolica, commonly known as Anatolian burr vetch, is a perennial legume native to Anatolia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and grassland habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
