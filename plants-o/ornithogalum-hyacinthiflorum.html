Ornithogalum hyacinthiflorum

Ornithogalum hyacinthiflorum, commonly known as the hyacinth-flowered star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale blue flowers resembling hyacinths. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking floral display during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
