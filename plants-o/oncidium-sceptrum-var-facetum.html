Oncidium sceptrum var. facetum

Oncidium sceptrum var. facetum, commonly known as the Orchid with a Crown, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its rhizome is pseudobulbous, and it produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant blooms.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
