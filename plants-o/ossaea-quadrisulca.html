Ossaea quadrisulca

Ossaea quadrisulca, commonly known as the four-ribbed lily, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Liliaceae.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive four-ribbed structure on the inner part of the petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy texture.

Ossaea quadrisulca is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
