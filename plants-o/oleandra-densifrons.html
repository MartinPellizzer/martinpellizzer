Oleandra densifrons

Oleandra densifrons, commonly known as the dense-fruited oleander, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Oleandra densifrons is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
