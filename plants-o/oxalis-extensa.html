Oxalis extensa

Oxalis extensa, commonly known as the broadleaf wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically appearing in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Oxalis extensa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
