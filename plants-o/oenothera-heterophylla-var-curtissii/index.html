Oenothera Heterophylla Var. Curtissii

Oenothera heterophylla var. curtissii is a unique variant of the evening primrose known for its striking floral display. Native to the southwestern United States, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves vary in shape, adapting to different ecological conditions, which makes it a fascinating subject for botanical study. The plant blooms at night, attracting moths and other nocturnal pollinators. Its resilience and beauty make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
