Ortgiesia coelestis var. acutifolia

Ortgiesia coelestis var. acutifolia, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
