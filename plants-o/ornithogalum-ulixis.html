Ornithogalum ulixis

Ornithogalum ulixis, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its bulbs are adapted for storage of nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Ornithogalum ulixis is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
