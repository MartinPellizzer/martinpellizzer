Ophiorrhiza rugosa var. angustifolia

Ophiorrhiza rugosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or coastal soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its adaptability to saline environments makes it an important species in coastal ecosystems.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
