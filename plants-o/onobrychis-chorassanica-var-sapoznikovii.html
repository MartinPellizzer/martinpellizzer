Onobrychis chorassanica var. sapoznikovii

Onobrychis chorassanica var. sapoznikovii, commonly known as the Central Asian vetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.
