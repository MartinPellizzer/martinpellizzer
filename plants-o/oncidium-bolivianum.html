Oncidium bolivianum

Oncidium bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features long, pendant-like flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or orange markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually sword-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Oncidium bolivianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
