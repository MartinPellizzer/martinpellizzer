Ocotea beekmanii

Ocotea beekmanii, commonly known as the Beekman's ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface. The fruit is a small, dark-colored drupe that contains a single seed, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
