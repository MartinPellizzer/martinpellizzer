Ornithogalum rogersii

Ornithogalum rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers' star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a central bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Ornithogalum rogersii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
