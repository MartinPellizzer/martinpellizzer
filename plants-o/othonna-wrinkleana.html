Othonna wrinkleana

Othonna wrinkleana, commonly known as the wrinkled othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and clustered, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna wrinkleana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
