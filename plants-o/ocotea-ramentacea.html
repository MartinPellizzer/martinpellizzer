Ocotea ramentacea

Ocotea ramentacea, commonly known as the silky ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are glossy and have a distinctive silky texture, while its flowers are small and clustered.

The tree is valued for its timber and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
