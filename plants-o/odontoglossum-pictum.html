Odontoglossum pictum

Odontoglossum pictum, commonly known as the painted orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, colorful flowers with intricate patterns. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically borne on tall, erect stems and bloom in late spring or early summer.

Odontoglossum pictum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
