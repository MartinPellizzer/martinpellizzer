Ononis columnae var. aggregata

Ononis columnae var. aggregata, commonly known as the clustered bladder campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and produces small, pink or white flowers. The plant features fleshy, rounded leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is native to parts of Europe and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
