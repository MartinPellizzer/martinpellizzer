Oricia trifoliolata

Oricia trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, well-drained environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they are divided into three distinct leaflets, giving it a unique appearance.

Oricia trifoliolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
