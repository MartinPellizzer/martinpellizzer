Oenanthe diversifolia

Oenanthe diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaved water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, stream banks, and damp woodlands. The plant features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a distinctive veining pattern.

The small white or greenish flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in late spring to early summer.
