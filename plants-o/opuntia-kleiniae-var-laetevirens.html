Opuntia kleiniae var. laetevirens

Opuntia kleiniae var. laetevirens, commonly known as the green opuntia, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features flat, fleshy pads covered in fine, pale spines that help reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its vibrant green color and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
