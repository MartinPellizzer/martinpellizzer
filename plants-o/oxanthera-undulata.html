Oxanthera undulata

Oxanthera undulata, commonly known as the wavy sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in coastal or inland areas with well-drained conditions.

Oxanthera undulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid environments.
