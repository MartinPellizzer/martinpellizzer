Osmanthus myrtifolius

Osmanthus myrtifolius, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its ornamental value is largely due to its aromatic blooms and vibrant foliage.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental and aromatic qualities in gardens and landscapes.
