Olyra cordifolia var. scabriuscula

Olyra cordifolia var. scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-leaved olyra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, rough-textured leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
