Oyedaea jahnii

Oyedaea jahnii, commonly known as the Jahnii orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and vibrant hues that attract pollinators.

Oyedaea jahnii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
