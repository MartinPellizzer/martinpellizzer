Orbicularia phyllanthoides

Orbicularia phyllanthoides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in winter, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which provides some protection against intense sunlight.

Orbicularia phyllanthoides is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
