Onopordum candidum

Onopordum candidum, commonly known as the white thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust stem with spiny leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, white to pale purple flower heads that are surrounded by bracts. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a woolly texture.

Onopordum candidum is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
