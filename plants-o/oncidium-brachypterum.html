Oncidium brachypterum

Oncidium brachypterum, commonly known as the Short-winged Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white markings. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, supporting the plant's structure and water storage.

Oncidium brachypterum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
