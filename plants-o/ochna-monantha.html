Ochna monantha

Ochna monantha, commonly known as the African coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling coral in appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Ochna monantha is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
