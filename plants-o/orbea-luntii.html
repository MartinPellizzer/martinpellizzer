Orbea luntii

Orbea luntii, commonly known as the Luntii orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
