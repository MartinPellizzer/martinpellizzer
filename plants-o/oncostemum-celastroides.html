Oncostemum celastroides

Oncostemum celastroides, commonly known as the creeping sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
