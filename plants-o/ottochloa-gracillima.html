Ottochloa gracillima

Ottochloa gracillima, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a species of grass native to South America, particularly found in Brazil. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It has slender, wiry stems and small, narrow leaves.

Ottochloa gracillima is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
