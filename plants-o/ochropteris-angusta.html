Ochropteris angusta

Ochropteris angusta, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
