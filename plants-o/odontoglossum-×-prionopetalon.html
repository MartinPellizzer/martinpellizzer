Odontoglossum × prionopetalon

Odontoglossum × prionopetalon, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum and Prionopetalon species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, typically in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
