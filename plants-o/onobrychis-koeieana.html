Onobrychis koeieana

Onobrychis koeieana, commonly known as Koeie's burr vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It is often found in grassland and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in both ecological and potential agricultural contexts.
