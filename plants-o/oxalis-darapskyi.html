Oxalis darapskyi

Oxalis darapskyi, commonly known as the star oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves with reddish veins, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Oxalis darapskyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
