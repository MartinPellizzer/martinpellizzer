Oreothyrsus

Oreothyrsus, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring erect stems and showy, often fragrant flowers. Oreothyrsus species are known for their ability to adapt to harsh environments, often growing in full sun.
