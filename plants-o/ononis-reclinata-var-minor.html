Ononis reclinata var. minor

Ononis reclinata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically features slender stems that are covered in fine, white hairs, and it produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters. The leaves are narrow and opposite, often with a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

This plant is known for its ability to tolerate dry conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
