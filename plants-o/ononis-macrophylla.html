Ononis macrophylla

Ononis macrophylla, commonly known as broadleaf sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Ononis macrophylla is often used in rock gardens and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
