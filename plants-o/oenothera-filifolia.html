Oenothera filifolia

Oenothera filifolia, commonly known as silky evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

Oenothera filifolia is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
