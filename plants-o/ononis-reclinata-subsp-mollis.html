Ononis reclinata subsp. mollis

Ononis reclinata subsp.

mollis, commonly known as soft sandspur, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage.

The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually hairy and fleshy, adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils.

It is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
