Oleandra urbani

Oleandra urbani, commonly known as the urban oleander, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming throughout the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Oleandra urbani is a shrub that can grow up to several meters tall, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

However, it is important to note that all parts of this plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
