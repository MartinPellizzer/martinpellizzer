Orogenia linearifolia

Orogenia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf mountain plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to high-altitude regions of South America, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its growth habit is typically low and sprawling, with stems that often root at the nodes.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
