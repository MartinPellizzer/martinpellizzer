Oxalis campestris

Oxalis campestris, commonly known as wood sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Oxalidaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is native to Europe, Asia, and Africa, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Oxalis campestris is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
