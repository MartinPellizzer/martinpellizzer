Oberonia carnosa

Oberonia carnosa, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a pseudobulb structure that helps store water and nutrients.

The flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.
