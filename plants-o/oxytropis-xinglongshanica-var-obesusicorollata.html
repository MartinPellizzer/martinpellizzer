Oxytropis xinglongshanica var. obesusicorollata

Oxytropis xinglongshanica var. obesusicorollata, commonly known as the Xinglongshan locoweed, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
