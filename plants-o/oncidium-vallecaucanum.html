Oncidium vallecaucanum

Oncidium vallecaucanum, commonly known as the Vallecaucan orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent purple markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium vallecaucanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
