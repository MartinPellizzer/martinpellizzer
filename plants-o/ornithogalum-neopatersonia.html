Ornithogalum neopatersonia

Ornithogalum neopatersonia, commonly known as the Paterson's lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial bulbous plant that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
