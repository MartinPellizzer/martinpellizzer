Oncidium unicorne var. laetum

Oncidium unicorne var. laetum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers with a distinctive orange lip. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its blooming cycle typically occurs during the spring and summer months.
