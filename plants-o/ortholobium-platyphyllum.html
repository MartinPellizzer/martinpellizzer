Ortholobium platyphyllum

Ortholobium platyphyllum, commonly known as the flat-leaf burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a distinctive flat or slightly folded appearance.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, producing legume pods that contain several seeds.
