Ornithogalum tenuifolium subvar. glabrum

Ornithogalum tenuifolium subvar. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its subspecies name. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
