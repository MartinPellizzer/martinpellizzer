Olax austrosinensis

Olax austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese olax, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its ecological role in its native habitat.
