Oldenlandia caespitosa var. lanceolata

Oldenlandia caespitosa var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate spurge or slender spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features slender, creeping stems that form dense mats, with small, lance-shaped leaves arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in spikes, producing a distinctive appearance.

It is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
