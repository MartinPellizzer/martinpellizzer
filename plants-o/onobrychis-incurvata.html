Onobrychis incurvata

Onobrychis incurvata, commonly known as the curled burr vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, curved pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Onobrychis incurvata is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
