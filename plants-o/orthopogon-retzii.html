Orthopogon retzii

Orthopogon retzii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in dense clusters.
