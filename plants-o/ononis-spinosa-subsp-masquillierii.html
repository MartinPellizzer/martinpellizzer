Ononis spinosa subsp. masquillierii

Ononis spinosa subsp.

masquillierii, commonly known as the rock cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, sunny environments.

The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, and its leaves are narrow and hairy. It has a compact growth habit with a fibrous root system that helps it survive in harsh conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid landscapes.
