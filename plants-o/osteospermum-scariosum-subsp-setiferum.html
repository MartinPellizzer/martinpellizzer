Osteospermum scariosum subsp. setiferum

Osteospermum scariosum subsp.

setiferum, commonly known as the woolly osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance contribute to its ornamental value.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more robust growth and thicker leaves compared to other members of the species.
