Ozoroa nitida

Ozoroa nitida, commonly known as the slender sand thorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.

Ozoroa nitida is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
