Onuris spegazziniana

Onuris spegazziniana, commonly known as the Spegazzini's onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, spherical bulbs that are often used in traditional medicine. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from the base.
