Ormenis eriolepis

Ormenis eriolepis, commonly known as the hairy ormenis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in the spring. Ormenis eriolepis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
