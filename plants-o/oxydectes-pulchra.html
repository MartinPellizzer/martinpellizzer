Oxydectes pulchra

Oxydectes pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oxydectes pulchra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
