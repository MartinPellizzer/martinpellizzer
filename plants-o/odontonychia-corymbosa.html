Odontonychia corymbosa

Odontonychia corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a corymbous inflorescence. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Odontonychia corymbosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in rainforests.
