Onobrychis tournefortii f. leiocarpa

Onobrychis tournefortii f. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-seeded bur clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces smooth, round pods that contain several seeds, distinguishing it from other forms of the species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment and sustainable agriculture.
