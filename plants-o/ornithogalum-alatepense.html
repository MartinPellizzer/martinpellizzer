Ornithogalum alatepense

Ornithogalum alatepense, commonly known as the winged star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale green flowers. The plant's bulbs are often used in traditional medicine, though they contain toxic compounds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a striking addition to botanical collections.
