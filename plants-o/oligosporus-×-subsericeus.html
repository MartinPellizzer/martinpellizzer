Oligosporus × subsericeus

Oligosporus × subsericeus, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant with unique botanical features.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically exhibits a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually borne in racemes and display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and orange.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
