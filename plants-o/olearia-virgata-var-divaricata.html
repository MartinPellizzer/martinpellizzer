Olearia virgata var. divaricata

Olearia virgata var. divaricata, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate salt spray.
