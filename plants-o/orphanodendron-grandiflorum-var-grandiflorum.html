Orphanodendron grandiflorum var. grandiflorum

Orphanodendron grandiflorum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered orphanodendron, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a woody stem and produces leaves that are broad and ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
