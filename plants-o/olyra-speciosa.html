Olyra speciosa

Olyra speciosa, commonly known as the showy olyra, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.
