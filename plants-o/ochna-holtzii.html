Ochna holtzii

Ochna holtzii, commonly known as the African holly, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with subtle veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ochna holtzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
