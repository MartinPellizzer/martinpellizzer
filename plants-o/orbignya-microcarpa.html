Orbignya microcarpa

Orbignya microcarpa, commonly known as the babassu palm, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered fruits. Its seeds are encased in hard, woody shells and are used to produce oil.

The babassu palm is valued for its oil, which is used in cosmetics and industry.
