Ornithogalum polyphlebium

Ornithogalum polyphlebium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous rootstock and produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Ornithogalum polyphlebium is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
