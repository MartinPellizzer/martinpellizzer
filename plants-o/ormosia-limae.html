Ormosia limae

Ormosia limae, commonly known as the Java orchid tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Ormosia limae belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both ornamental horticulture and ecological restoration projects.
