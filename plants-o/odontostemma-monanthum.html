Odontostemma monanthum

Odontostemma monanthum, commonly known as the single-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
