Ornithogalum synadelphicum

Ornithogalum synadelphicum, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
