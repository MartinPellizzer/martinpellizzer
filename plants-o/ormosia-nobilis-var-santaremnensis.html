Ormosia nobilis var. santaremnensis

Ormosia nobilis var. santaremnensis, commonly known as the Santarem ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It produces hard, woody pods containing seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by animals.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
