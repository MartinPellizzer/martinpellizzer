Oenothera nocturna

Oenothera nocturna, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Oenothera nocturna thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
