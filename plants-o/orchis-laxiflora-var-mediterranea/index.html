Orchis Laxiflora Var. Mediterranea

Common mediterranean orchid, scientifically known as Orchis laxiflora var. mediterranea, is a striking and rare flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. This orchid thrives in rocky soils and open habitats, often found in sunlit meadows and coastal cliffs. Its delicate pink or white flowers bloom in late spring, adding a touch of elegance to the landscape. With its unique appearance and ecological significance, it plays an important role in local biodiversity. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
