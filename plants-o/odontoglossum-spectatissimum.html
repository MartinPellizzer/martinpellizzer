Odontoglossum spectatissimum

Odontoglossum spectatissimum, commonly known as the showy odontoglossum, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often covered in a fine layer of pubescence.

Odontoglossum spectatissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
