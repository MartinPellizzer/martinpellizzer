Oncidium tarquiense

Oncidium tarquiense, commonly known as the Tarqui orchid, is a species native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium tarquiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
