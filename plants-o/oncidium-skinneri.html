Oncidium skinneri

Oncidium skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often in clusters. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium skinneri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
