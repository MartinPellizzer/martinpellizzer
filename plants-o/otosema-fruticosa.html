Otosema fruticosa

Otosema fruticosa, commonly known as the fragrant shrub, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are aromatic and contain essential oils, making it valuable in traditional medicine.

Otosema fruticosa thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
