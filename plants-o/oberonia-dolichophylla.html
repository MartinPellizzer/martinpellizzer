Oberonia dolichophylla

Oberonia dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf Oberonia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features slender, dark green leaves that are elongated and often curved. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.
