Ornithogalum ponticum

Ornithogalum ponticum, commonly known as the European star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow star-shaped flowers in the spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum ponticum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
