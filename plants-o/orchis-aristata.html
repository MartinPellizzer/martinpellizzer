Orchis aristata

Orchis aristata, commonly known as the showy orchis, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. The plant features a tall, erect stem with a single, showy flower that is usually purple or violet in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral along the stem.

Orchis aristata is characterized by its intricate floral structure and is a member of the Orchidaceae family.
