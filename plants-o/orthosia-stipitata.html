Orthosia stipitata

Orthosia stipitata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color.

Orthosia stipitata is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other woodland flora.
