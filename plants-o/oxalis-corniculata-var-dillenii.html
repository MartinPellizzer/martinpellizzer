Oxalis corniculata var. dillenii

Oxalis corniculata var. dillenii, commonly known as the Dillenii wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage and flowers are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in partial shade.
