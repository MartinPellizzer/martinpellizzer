Odontoglossum × eugenes

Odontoglossum × eugenes, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Odontoglossum and Eugenia species.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
