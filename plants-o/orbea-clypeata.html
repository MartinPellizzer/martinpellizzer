Orbea clypeata

Orbea clypeata, commonly known as the shield flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. Orbea clypeata is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
