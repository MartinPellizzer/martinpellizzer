Ochrosia microcalyx

Ochrosia microcalyx, commonly known as the dwarf pomegranate, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
