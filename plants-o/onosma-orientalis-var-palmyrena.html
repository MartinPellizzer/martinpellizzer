Onosma orientalis var. palmyrena

Onosma orientalis var. palmyrena, commonly known as the Palmyrene hyssop, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. Its small, tubular flowers are typically violet or blue, arranged in dense spikes at the tips of the branches.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
