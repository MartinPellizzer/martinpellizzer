Olearia phlogopappa var. microcephala

Olearia phlogopappa var. microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed daisy bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Olearia phlogopappa var. microcephala is also noted for its resilience to coastal conditions and salt spray.
