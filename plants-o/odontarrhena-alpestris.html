Odontarrhena alpestris

Odontarrhena alpestris, commonly known as the Alpine toothbrush, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its leaves are linear and grass-like. It is well adapted to cold climates and can survive in high-altitude conditions with low temperatures and strong winds.
