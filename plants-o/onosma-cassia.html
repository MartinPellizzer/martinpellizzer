Onosma cassia

Onosma cassia, commonly known as yellow sandthistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Onosma cassia is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

It has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
