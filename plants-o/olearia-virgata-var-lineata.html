Olearia virgata var. lineata

Olearia virgata var. lineata, commonly known as the silver-leaf daisy or silver fern, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety foliage and daisy-like white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
