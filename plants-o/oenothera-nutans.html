Oenothera nutans

Oenothera nutans, commonly known as the nodding evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.

Oenothera nutans is also known for its ability to attract nocturnal pollinators like moths.
