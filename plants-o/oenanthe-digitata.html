Oenanthe digitata

Oenanthe digitata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water dropwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often submerged or floating in shallow water. It is typically found in wetlands, stream margins, and other moist environments.
