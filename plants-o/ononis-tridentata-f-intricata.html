Ononis tridentata f. intricata

Ononis tridentata f. intricata, commonly known as the intricately branched sandspur, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments. Its intricate branching pattern and adaptability to harsh conditions make it a notable species in its native range.

The subspecies f. intricata is distinguished by its more complex and elaborate floral and vegetative structures compared to the typical form.
