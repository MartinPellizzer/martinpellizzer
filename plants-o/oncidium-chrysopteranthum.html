Oncidium chrysopteranthum

Oncidium chrysopteranthum, commonly known as the golden wing orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium chrysopteranthum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
