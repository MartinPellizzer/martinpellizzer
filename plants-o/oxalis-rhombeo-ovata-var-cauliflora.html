Oxalis rhombeo-ovata var. cauliflora

Oxalis rhombeo-ovata var. cauliflora, commonly known as the cauliflower oxalis, is a striking plant characterized by its unique, clustered inflorescence.

It features bright yellow flowers that resemble miniature cauliflower, emerging from the base of the plant. The leaves are heart-shaped with a distinctive reddish-purple coloration, adding to its ornamental appeal. It is a perennial herb native to South Africa, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
