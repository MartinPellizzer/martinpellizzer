Osbeckia lebrunii

Osbeckia lebrunii, commonly known as the slender osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate regions. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Osbeckia lebrunii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
