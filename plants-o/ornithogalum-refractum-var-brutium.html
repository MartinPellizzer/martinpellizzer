Ornithogalum refractum var. brutium

Ornithogalum refractum var. brutium, commonly known as the Brute's ornithogalum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of pale green to white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
