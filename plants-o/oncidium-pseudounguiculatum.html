Oncidium pseudounguiculatum

Oncidium pseudounguiculatum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Forest, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Oncidium pseudounguiculatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
