Oudneya zygarrhena

Oudneya zygarrhena, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that resemble bells, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.

Oudneya zygarrhena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
