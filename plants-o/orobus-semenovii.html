Orobus semenovii

Orobus semenovii, commonly known as the Semenov's orobus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates. Its small, pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it has compound leaves with stipules.
