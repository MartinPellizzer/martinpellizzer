Oplismenus hildebrandtii

Oplismenus hildebrandtii, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking evergreen perennial native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of broad, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces striking blue or purple flower spikes in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a standout in both garden and landscape settings.
