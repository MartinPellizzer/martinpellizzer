Ononis broteroana

Ononis broteroana, commonly known as the broteroan whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat.

Ononis broteroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
