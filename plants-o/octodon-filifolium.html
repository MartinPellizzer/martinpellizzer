Octodon filifolium

Octodon filifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental value. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Due to its specific habitat, it is not commonly cultivated outside its native range.
