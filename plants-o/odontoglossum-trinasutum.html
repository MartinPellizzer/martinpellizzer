Odontoglossum trinasutum

Odontoglossum trinasutum, commonly known as the three-ridged orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colorful flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, preferring cool and humid environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and velvety, while its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky substrates it inhabits.

Odontoglossum trinasutum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
