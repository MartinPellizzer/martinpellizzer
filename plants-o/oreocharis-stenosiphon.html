Oreocharis stenosiphon

Oreocharis stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan province, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip petal that aids in pollination. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
