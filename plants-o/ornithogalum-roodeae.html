Ornithogalum roodeae

Ornithogalum roodeae, commonly known as the Roodea star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a bulbous root system and produces striking, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Ornithogalum roodeae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
