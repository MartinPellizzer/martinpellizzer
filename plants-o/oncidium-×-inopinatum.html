Oncidium × inopinatum

Oncidium × inopinatum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers, which resemble the wings of a butterfly. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, fragrant blooms that appear in clusters.

Oncidium × inopinatum requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
