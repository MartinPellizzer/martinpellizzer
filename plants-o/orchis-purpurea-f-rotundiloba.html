Orchis purpurea f. rotundiloba

Orchis purpurea f. rotundiloba, commonly known as the purple orchid with rounded leaves, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its distinctive floral morphology.

It features a tall, erect stem with dense clusters of violet or purple flowers, often with a yellow-green keel. The leaves are broad and ovate, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This form is a natural variant of Orchis purpurea, exhibiting slight morphological differences in leaf shape and flower structure.

It thrives in woodland habitats and is valued for its beauty in horticultural collections.
