Onopordum × delortii

Onopordum × delortii, commonly known as the hybrid everlasting, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Onopordum acanthium and Onopordum lappaceum, resulting in a plant with distinctive, long-lasting composite flowers. The plant features silver-gray foliage and produces yellow-orange flower heads that remain intact for extended periods, making it popular in dried floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in native gardens.
