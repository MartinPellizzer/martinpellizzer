Ormocarpum discolor

Ormocarpum discolor, commonly known as the pale orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Gulf Coast. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale purple or white. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture.

The fruit is a unique, inflated capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
