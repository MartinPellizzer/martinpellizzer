Oxalis ostenii

Oxalis ostenii, commonly known as the golden oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis ostenii is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
