Ozothamnus tesselatus

Ozothamnus tesselatus, commonly known as the Australian daisy or coastal daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 20 to 50 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern, and are covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Ozothamnus tesselatus is adapted to coastal environments and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
