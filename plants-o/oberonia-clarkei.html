Oberonia clarkei

Oberonia clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in a spike, often with a faintly fragrant scent.

Oberonia clarkei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
