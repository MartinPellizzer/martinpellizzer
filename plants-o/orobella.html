Orobella

Orobella, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces long, pendulous clusters of flowers that resemble tassels. Orobella thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
