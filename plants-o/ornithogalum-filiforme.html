Ornithogalum filiforme

Ornithogalum filiforme, commonly known as the slender star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its elongated, thread-like stems that often arch gracefully. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Ornithogalum filiforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
