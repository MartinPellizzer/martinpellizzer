Opuntia mesacantha var. cymochila

Opuntia mesacantha var. cymochila, commonly known as the Cymochila prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features flattened, fleshy stems that are covered in spiny areoles, typical of the Opuntia genus. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, a large, fleshy berry, is edible and often used in traditional cuisine.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
