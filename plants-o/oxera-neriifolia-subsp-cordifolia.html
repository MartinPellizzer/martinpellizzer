Oxera neriifolia subsp. cordifolia

Oxera neriifolia subsp.

cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf oxera, is a perennial shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Oxera neriifolia subsp.

cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
