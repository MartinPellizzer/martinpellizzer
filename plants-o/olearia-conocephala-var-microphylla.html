Olearia conocephala var. microphylla

Olearia conocephala var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leafed rock heath, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
