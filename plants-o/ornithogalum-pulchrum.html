Ornithogalum pulchrum

Ornithogalum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette around the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ornithogalum pulchrum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
