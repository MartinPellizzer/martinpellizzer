Olearia magniflora

Olearia magniflora, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, while its stems are covered in fine hairs.

Olearia magniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
