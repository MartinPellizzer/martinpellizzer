Oreodaphne splendens

Oreodaphne splendens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a reddish or bronze tint on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Oreodaphne splendens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
