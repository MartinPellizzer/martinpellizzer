Oncidium gloriosum var. flaviflorum

Oncidium gloriosum var. flaviflorum, commonly known as the Yellow-flowered Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, elongated pseudobulbs that support its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers with contrasting green sepals and petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
