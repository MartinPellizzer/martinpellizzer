Ornithogalum praetextum

Ornithogalum praetextum, commonly known as the spotted lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive blue-green coloration. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear white or pale pink flowers with a central greenish hue. Its flowers are typically arranged in umbels and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
