Ornithogalum simile

Ornithogalum simile, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ornithogalum simile is also known for its toxic properties, containing compounds that can be harmful if ingested.
