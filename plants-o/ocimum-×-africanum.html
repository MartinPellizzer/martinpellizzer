Ocimum × africanum

Ocimum × africanum, commonly known as the African basil, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and compact growth habit, often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. The plant typically has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ocimum × africanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
