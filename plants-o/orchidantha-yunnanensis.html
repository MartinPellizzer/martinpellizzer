Orchidantha yunnanensis

Orchidantha yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan orchidantha, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing blooms that range in color from white to pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
