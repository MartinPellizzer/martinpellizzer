Odontochilus brevistylis subsp.

brevistylis, commonly known as the short-styled odontochilus, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

The flowers are typically pale green or white, with intricate, lip-like structures that aid in pollination by specific insect species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.
