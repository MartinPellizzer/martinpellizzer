Olearia montana

Olearia montana, commonly known as the mountain daisy or silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and showy white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers.

Olearia montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
