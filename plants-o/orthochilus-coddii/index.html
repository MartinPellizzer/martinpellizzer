Orthochilus Coddii

Orthochilus coddii is a rare and elusive orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America. This delicate plant thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity. Its unique flower structure and vibrant colors make it a subject of fascination for botanists and enthusiasts alike. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
