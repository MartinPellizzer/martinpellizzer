Ononis viscosa f. cavanillesii

Ononis viscosa f. cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' stickyweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, green, ovate leaves with a distinctive sticky coating on the upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Ononis genus, valued for its ornamental and ecological roles.
