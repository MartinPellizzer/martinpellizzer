Oxalis dendroides f. longiscapa

Oxalis dendroides f. longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed wood sorrel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched, woody stem that is often covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside. Its flowers are typically yellow with five petals and are arranged in clusters.

It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
