Ormosia hosiei

Ormosia hosiei, commonly known as the Chinese tallow tree, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. Ormosia hosiei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its rapid growth and attractive foliage.
