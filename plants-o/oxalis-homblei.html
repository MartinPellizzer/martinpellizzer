Oxalis homblei

Oxalis homblei, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery sheen, which give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
