Obetia radula

Obetia radula, commonly known as the slime orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a short rhizome with a single, erect stem bearing a single, showy flower. Its flowers are usually greenish or brownish with a distinctive, fleshy labellum.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
