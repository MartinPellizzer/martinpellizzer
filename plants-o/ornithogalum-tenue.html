Ornithogalum tenue

Ornithogalum tenue, commonly known as the slender star-of-Bethlem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is often found in woodland and rocky areas, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Ornithogalum tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
