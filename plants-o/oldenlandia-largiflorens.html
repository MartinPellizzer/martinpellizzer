Oldenlandia largiflorens

Oldenlandia largiflorens, commonly known as the large-flowered spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring slender, branching stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often in summer, and it is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shady environments.

Oldenlandia largiflorens is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal remedies.
