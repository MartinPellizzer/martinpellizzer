Oxalis bullulata

Oxalis bullulata, commonly known as the snowflake oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, and it produces small, rounded fruit capsules that contain seeds. Oxalis bullulata thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
