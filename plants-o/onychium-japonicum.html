Onychium japonicum

Onychium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese onychium, is a flowering plant native to Japan and parts of Korea.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants such as buckwheat and sorrel. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, and it has slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Onychium japonicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.
