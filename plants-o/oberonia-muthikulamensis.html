Oberonia muthikulamensis

Oberonia muthikulamensis, commonly known as the Muthikulam orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or among leaf litter. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil.

Oberonia muthikulamensis is a rare and endemic species, highlighting the biodiversity of the Western Ghats' ecosystems.
