Odontoglossum coronarium var. dayanum

Odontoglossum coronarium var. dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in cool, humid environments at high altitudes. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive violet or purple coloration and a unique lip structure.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
