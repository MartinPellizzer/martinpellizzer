Odontoglossum × wendlandianum

Odontoglossum × wendlandianum, commonly known as the Wendlandian orchid, is a hybrid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a unique, spurred lip. Its leaves are typically broad and dark green, and it prefers well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its elegant blooms.
