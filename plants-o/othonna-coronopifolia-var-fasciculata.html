Othonna coronopifolia var. fasciculata

Othonna coronopifolia var. fasciculata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
