Oxytropis Nothoxys

Oxytropis nothoxys is a rare and striking legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This perennial plant is known for its unique, spiny pods and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its ecological importance and ornamental value make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
