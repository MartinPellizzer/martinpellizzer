Ononis hirta

Ononis hirta, commonly known as hairy cinquefoil, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. The plant has a creeping rootstock and produces small, yellowish flowers with five petals. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, and the plant is known for its hardy nature.

Ononis hirta is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
