Ophrys Apifera F. Fulvofusca

Ophrys apifera f. fulvofusca is a rare and fascinating orchid known for its striking mimicry of female wasps. This plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in specific habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its unique appearance, with golden-brown markings, helps it attract male wasps for pollination. The orchid's intricate floral structure plays a crucial role in its reproductive strategy. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes.
