Oncidium crispum var. lionetianum

Oncidium crispum var. lionetianum, commonly known as the Lionetianum orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, long-lasting flowers that typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, arranged in a raceme. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often crinkled or wavy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
