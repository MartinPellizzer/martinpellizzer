Ochna caudata

Ochna caudata, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Ochna caudata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
