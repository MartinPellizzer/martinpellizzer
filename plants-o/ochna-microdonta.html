Ochna microdonta

Ochna microdonta, commonly known as the dwarf ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Ochna microdonta is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
