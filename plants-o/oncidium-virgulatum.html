Oncidium virgulatum

Oncidium virgulatum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, raceme-like inflorescences with showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly fleshy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium virgulatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
