Odontoglossum × kraenzlinii

Odontoglossum × kraenzlinii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchids.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Andes, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant features showy, often fragrant flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. Its leaves are typically broad and dark green, adapted to capture sunlight in its native habitat.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
