Oxytropis nuristanica

Oxytropis nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan oxytropis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It is adapted to thrive in high-altitude environments with poor soil conditions.

Oxytropis nuristanica is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
