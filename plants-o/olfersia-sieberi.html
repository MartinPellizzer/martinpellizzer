Olfersia sieberi

Olfersia sieberi, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps retain moisture.

The flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
