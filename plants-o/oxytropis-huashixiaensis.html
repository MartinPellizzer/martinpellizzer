Oxytropis huashixiaensis

Oxytropis huashixiaensis, commonly known as the Hua Shixia legume, is a perennial herb native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, contributing to the alpine flora of its habitat. It thrives in high-altitude environments with poor soil, showcasing its adaptability to extreme conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
