Oreocnide obovata var. mucronata

Oreocnide obovata var. mucronata, commonly known as the blunt-tipped oreocnide, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, ovate to obovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small and clustered, with white or pale yellow petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.
