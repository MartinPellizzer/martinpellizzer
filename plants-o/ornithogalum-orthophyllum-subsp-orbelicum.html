Ornithogalum orthophyllum subsp. orbelicum

Ornithogalum orthophyllum subsp.

orbelicum, commonly known as the mountain star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped white flowers during the summer months.

The plant is characterized by its underground bulbous structures, which store nutrients and water. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
