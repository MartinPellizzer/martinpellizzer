Oberonia zimmermanniana

Oberonia zimmermanniana, commonly known as the orchid of the rainforests, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has a sympodial growth habit. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in the undergrowth of dense forest canopies.
