Othonna trifida

Othonna trifida, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. Othonna trifida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for dry garden environments.
