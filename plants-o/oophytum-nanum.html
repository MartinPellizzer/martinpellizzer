Oophytum nanum

Oophytum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf oophytum, is a small, terrestrial plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. The plant features a rosette of fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of yellow, orange, or red, making it an attractive addition to gardens.

Oophytum nanum is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
