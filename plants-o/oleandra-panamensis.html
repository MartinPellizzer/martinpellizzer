Oleandra panamensis

Oleandra panamensis, commonly known as the Panama oleander, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Oleandra panamensis is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
