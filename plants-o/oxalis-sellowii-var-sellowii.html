Oxalis sellowii var. sellowii

Oxalis sellowii var. sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.

It is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread easily in suitable conditions.
