Oenothera biennis var. nutans

Oenothera biennis var. nutans, commonly known as the nodding evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening and close by dawn. The plant typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves are typical of the genus Oenothera.

The seeds are enclosed in a capsule that splits open to release the small, winged seeds.
