Orthosia misera

Orthosia misera, commonly known as the small orthosia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in dry, open habitats.

Orthosia misera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
