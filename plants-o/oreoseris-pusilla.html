Oreoseris pusilla

Oreoseris pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf oreoseris, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Oreoseris pusilla is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand dry conditions.
