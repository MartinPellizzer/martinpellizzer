Orania ferruginea

Orania ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive reddish-brown sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late spring or early summer.

Orania ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
