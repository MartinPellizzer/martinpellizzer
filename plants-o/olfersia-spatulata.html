Olfersia spatulata

Olfersia spatulata, commonly known as the spoonleaf orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Olfersia spatulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable climate.
