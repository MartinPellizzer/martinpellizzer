Osmolindsaea odorata

Osmolindsaea odorata, commonly known as the fragrant fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that emit a subtle, pleasant aroma. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, typical of many fern species.

Osmolindsaea odorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fragrance.
