Oncidium guianense f. album

Oncidium guianense f. album, commonly known as the white Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America, particularly the Amazon basin. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are known for their elegant, fragrant appearance.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
