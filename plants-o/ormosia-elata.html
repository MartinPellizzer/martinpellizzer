Ormosia elata

Ormosia elata, commonly known as the giant sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by dark blue to black berries. Ormosia elata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.

It plays a role in local ecosystems and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
