Ornithogalum zebrinum

Ornithogalum zebrinum, commonly known as the zebra lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive purple or red stripe. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
