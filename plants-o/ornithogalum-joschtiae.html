Ornithogalum joschtiae

Ornithogalum joschtiae, commonly known as the Joschti's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous root system and produces striking, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
