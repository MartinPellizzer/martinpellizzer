Ornithogalum tardans

Ornithogalum tardans, commonly known as the late-flowering star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
