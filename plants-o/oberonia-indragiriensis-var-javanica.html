Oberonia indragiriensis var. javanica

Oberonia indragiriensis var. javanica, commonly known as the Javan Oberonia, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Indonesia.

It features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, and it thrives in high humidity and indirect light.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
