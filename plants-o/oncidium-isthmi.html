Oncidium isthmi

Oncidium isthmi, commonly known as the Isthmian orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Oncidium isthmi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
