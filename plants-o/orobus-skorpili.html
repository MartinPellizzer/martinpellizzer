Orobus skorpili

Orobus skorpili, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, saucer-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features a slender, branching stem and hairy leaves that are typically alternate in arrangement. Its seed pods are elongated and contain numerous small, hard seeds.

Orobus skorpili is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.
