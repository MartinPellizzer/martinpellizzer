Ochna pulchra f. integra

Ochna pulchra f. integra, commonly known as the smooth ochna, is a rare and striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth and unbroken, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typical of the Ochnaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
