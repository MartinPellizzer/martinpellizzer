Oncidium Pectorale Var. Caloglossum

Pectoral oncidium, scientifically known as Oncidium pectorale var. caloglossum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. This variant is distinguished by its vibrant, waxy flowers that display a unique blend of colors, ranging from deep purples to soft yellows. Its foliage is broad and sword-like, contributing to its dramatic appearance in both cultivated and natural settings. The plant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively uncommon species, making it a sought-after addition to specialized collections.
