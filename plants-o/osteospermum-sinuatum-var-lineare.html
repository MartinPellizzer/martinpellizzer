Osteospermum sinuatum var. lineare

Osteospermum sinuatum var. lineare, commonly known as the linear osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. The leaves are linear and slightly hairy, giving the plant a unique appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
