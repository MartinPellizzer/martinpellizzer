Osbeckia chinensis var. angustifolia

Osbeckia chinensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf osbeckia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, angular stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture, while the roots are fibrous and used in traditional medicine.

This species is often found in moist, rocky soils in mountainous regions.
