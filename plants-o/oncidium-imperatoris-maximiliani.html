Oncidium imperatoris-maximiliani

Oncidium imperatoris-maximiliani, commonly known as the Emperor orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to South America.

It features showy, colorful inflorescences with long, pendulous flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or red markings. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium imperatoris-maximiliani is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
