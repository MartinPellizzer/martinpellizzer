Olax egleri

Olax egleri, commonly known as the Egeria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Olax egleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in indoor settings.
