Origanum glandulosum var. elongatum

Origanum glandulosum var. elongatum, commonly known as the slender lemon thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving it a distinctive texture and scent. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This variety is valued for its culinary uses and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
