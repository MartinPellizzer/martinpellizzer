Oncidium crispum var. grandiflorum

Oncidium crispum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the orchid with large flowers, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features long, narrow leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its flowers bloom in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
