Oxalis riedelii

Oxalis riedelii, commonly known as the red oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and showy pink or red flowers. The plant produces small, bead-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oxalis riedelii thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and delicate blooms.
