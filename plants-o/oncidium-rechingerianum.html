Oncidium rechingerianum

Oncidium rechingerianum, commonly known as the Rechinger's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are strap-shaped, firm, and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Oncidium rechingerianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
