Oreocarya suffruticosa

Oreocarya suffruticosa, commonly known as the mountain buckthorn, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by dark blue berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Oreocarya suffruticosa is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
