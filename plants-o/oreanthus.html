Oreanthus

Oreanthus, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Oreanthus species are characterized by their delicate, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.
