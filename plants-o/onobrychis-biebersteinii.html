Onobrychis biebersteinii

Onobrychis biebersteinii, commonly known as the yellow bur clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Onobrychis biebersteinii is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
