Origanum comptum

Origanum comptum, commonly known as narrowleaf oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are used in cooking and traditional medicine for their pungent, minty flavor.

Origanum comptum is a hardy plant that is often cultivated in gardens for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
