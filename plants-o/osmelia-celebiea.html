Osmelia celebiea

Osmelia celebiea, commonly known as the Celebes osmunda, is a species of aquatic fern native to the island of Celebes in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that form dense mats on the water surface. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps anchor it in shallow waters. Its spores are released into the air, allowing for dispersal to new habitats.

Osmelia celebiea plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
