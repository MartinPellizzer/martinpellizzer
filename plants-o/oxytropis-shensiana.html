Oxytropis shensiana

Oxytropis shensiana, commonly known as Chinese locoweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and grasslands.

Oxytropis shensiana is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.
