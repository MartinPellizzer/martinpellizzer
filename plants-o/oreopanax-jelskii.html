Oreopanax jelskii

Oreopanax jelskii, commonly known as the Jelskii's sycogate, is a tropical plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a climbing shrub that can reach heights of up to 10 meters, characterized by its pinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and often consumed by local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to grow in rocky or mountainous terrain.
