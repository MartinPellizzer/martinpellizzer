Osbeckia manillana

Osbeckia manillana, commonly known as the Manila ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features upright stems covered with soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or purple in color.

Osbeckia manillana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
