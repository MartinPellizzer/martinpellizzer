Odontoglossum robustum

Odontoglossum robustum, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a species native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Odontoglossum robustum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
