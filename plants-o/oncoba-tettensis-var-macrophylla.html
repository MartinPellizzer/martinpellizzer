Oncoba tettensis var. macrophylla

Oncoba tettensis var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf oncoba, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an attractive species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
