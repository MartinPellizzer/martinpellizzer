Ochnella densicoma

Ochnella densicoma, commonly known as the dense-leaved ochna, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage, with leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Ochnella densicoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
