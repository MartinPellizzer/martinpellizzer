Oncidium × wilckeanum

Oncidium × wilckeanum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species renowned for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, yellow flowers that often have red or orange markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

Oncidium × wilckeanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and striking floral display.
