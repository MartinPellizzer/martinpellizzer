Oxalis roselata

Oxalis roselata, commonly known as the rose of Sharon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veined pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Oxalis roselata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
