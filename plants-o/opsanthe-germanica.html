Opsanthe germanica

Opsanthe germanica, commonly known as the German opsin.

This plant is a flowering species native to specific regions of Europe. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The leaves are narrow and elongated, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and borders.
