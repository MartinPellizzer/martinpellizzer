Orchis papilionacea var. nivea

Orchis papilionacea var. nivea, commonly known as the white helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its pale green to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in woodland or grassland habitats, thriving in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and the plant produces a single spike of flowers.

The white helleborine is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
