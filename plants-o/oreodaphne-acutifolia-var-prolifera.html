Oreodaphne acutifolia var. prolifera

Oreodaphne acutifolia var. prolifera, commonly known as the clustered oreodaphne, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage. The plant features elliptical, glossy leaves with a pointed tip and a smooth, leathery texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark, spherical berries.

This variant is noted for its dense growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in tropical gardens.
