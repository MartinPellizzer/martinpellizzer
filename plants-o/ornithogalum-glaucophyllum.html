Ornithogalum glaucophyllum

Ornithogalum glaucophyllum, commonly known as the blue star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, waxy, blue-green leaves that form a dense, compact cluster. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue star-shaped flowers during the spring or early summer. It is often found in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
