Ononis Cintrana Var. Zaiana

Cintranzaian ononise, scientifically known as Ononis cintrana var. zaiana, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. This variant of the Ononis species is distinguished by its striking violet-blue petals and compact growth habit. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and limestone outcrops. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
