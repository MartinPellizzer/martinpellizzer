Oncidium ornithopodum

Oncidium ornithopodum, commonly known as the Bird's Foot Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous flower spikes with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium ornithopodum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
