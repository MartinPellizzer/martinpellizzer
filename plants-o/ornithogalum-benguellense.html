Ornithogalum benguellense

Ornithogalum benguellense, commonly known as the Benguella star-of-Bethleem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
