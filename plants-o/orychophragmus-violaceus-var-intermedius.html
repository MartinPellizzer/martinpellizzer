Orychophragmus violaceus var. intermedius

Orychophragmus violaceus var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate Chinese violet, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features basal leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in late spring to early summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. Its flowers have five petals and are arranged in racemes, adding ornamental value to gardens.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
