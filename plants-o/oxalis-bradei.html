Oxalis bradei

Oxalis bradei, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. Oxalis bradei thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
