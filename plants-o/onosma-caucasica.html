Onosma caucasica

Onosma caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are generally linear and gray-green in color, adapted to reduce water loss.

Onosma caucasica is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
