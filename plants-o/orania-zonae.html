Orania zonae

Orania zonae, commonly known as the Zonal Plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive zonation, where different colored bands appear on its leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Orania zonae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
