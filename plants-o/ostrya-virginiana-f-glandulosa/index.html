Ostrya Virginiana F. Glandulosa

Ostrya virginiana f. glandulosa is a unique variant of the American hemlock, distinguished by its glandular features. This form exhibits a more compact growth habit compared to its typical counterpart. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant's distinctive leaf and stem structures make it a subject of interest for botanists. Its rarity and ecological significance highlight its importance in natural ecosystems.
