Otoglossum brevifolium var. spathulatum

Otoglossum brevifolium var. spathulatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a unique coloration pattern, typically featuring a deep purple or violet hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
