Onopordum

Onopordum, commonly known as thistle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their spiny leaves and purple or yellow flowers that resemble daisies. Onopordum species are typically perennial herbs, often found in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. They have a widespread distribution across Europe, Asia, and North Africa.
