Ornithogalum pyrenaicum var. stachyoides

Ornithogalum pyrenaicum var. stachyoides, commonly known as the Pyrenean star-of-Bethleem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or limestone soils. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
