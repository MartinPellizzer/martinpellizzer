Oncidium plicigerum

Oncidium plicigerum, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pleated leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium plicigerum is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
