Ononis natrix subsp. angustissima

Ononis natrix subsp.

angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and sandy soils in Europe. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early summer.

The plant has a mat-forming habit with wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves. It is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in limestone areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
