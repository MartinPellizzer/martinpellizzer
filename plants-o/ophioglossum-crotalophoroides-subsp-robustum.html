Ophioglossum crotalophoroides subsp. robustum

Ophioglossum crotalophoroides subsp.

The plant has a short, stout rhizome with a single, lanceolate, and often hairy leaf. Its spores are produced in sporangia that are clustered in a distinctive, tongue-like structure called a sporophyll.
