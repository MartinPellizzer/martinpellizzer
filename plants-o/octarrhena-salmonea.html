Octarrhena salmonea

Octarrhena salmonea, commonly known as the red sandras.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Arabian Peninsula. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered pink or red flowers that bloom during the spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments.

Octarrhena salmonea is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping.
