Ornithogalum maculatum

Ornithogalum maculatum, commonly known as the spotted lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, pale green leaves with a distinctive red or purple stripe running down the center. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum maculatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
