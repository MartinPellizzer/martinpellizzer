Ornithogalum jacobi

Ornithogalum jacobi, commonly known as Jacob's lantern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, underground rhizome and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Ornithogalum jacobi is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
