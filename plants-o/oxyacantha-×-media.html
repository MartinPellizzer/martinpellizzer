Oxyacantha × media

Oxyacantha × media, commonly known as the common scarlet gooseberry, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, bright red berries that are edible when ripe. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers are white and appear in early summer, followed by the development of its showy fruit in late summer.

Oxyacantha × media is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in hedgerows.
