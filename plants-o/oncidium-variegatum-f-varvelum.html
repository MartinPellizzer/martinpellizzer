Oncidium variegatum f. varvelum

Oncidium variegatum f. varvelum, commonly known as the Varvel's Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid prized for its unique and colorful foliage.

This cultivar is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and yellow patterns. It is a compact plant that typically grows to a height of about 12 to 18 inches. The plant produces fragrant, yellow-orange flowers in a raceme, which bloom during the spring and summer months.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health and vibrancy.
