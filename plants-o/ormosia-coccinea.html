Ormosia coccinea

Ormosia coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its bark is thick and rough. Ormosia coccinea is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

It also has a long history of use in traditional medicine.
