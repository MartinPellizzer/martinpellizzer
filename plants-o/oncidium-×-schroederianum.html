Oncidium × schroederianum

Oncidium × schroederianum, commonly known as the Schröder's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species renowned for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, brightly colored flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences that can last several weeks. It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.

Oncidium × schroederianum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular but moderate watering.
