Oeosporangium tinaei

Oeosporangium tinaei, commonly known as the tinaei spore plant, is a unique species characterized by its specialized reproductive structures.

It belongs to the fungal kingdom and is notable for its distinctive arrangement of spore-producing cells called sporangia. The plant exhibits a complex life cycle involving both asexual and sexual reproduction stages. Its sporangia are typically clustered in a specific pattern, which aids in spore dispersal.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Oeosporangium tinaei is of interest to mycologists and conservationists alike.
