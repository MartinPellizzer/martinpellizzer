Ononis serrata var. baetica

Ononis serrata var. baetica, commonly known as the Iberian sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. This plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas. It is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant.

The species plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
