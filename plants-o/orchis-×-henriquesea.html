Orchis × henriquesea

Orchis × henriquesea, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Orchis morio and Orchis purpurea.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe, particularly in the UK and France. The plant produces tall flowering stems with showy, violet or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, calcareous soils.

The hybrid is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
