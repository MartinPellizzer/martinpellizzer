Oxybaphus diffusus

Oxybaphus diffusus, commonly known as the spreading oxybaphus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, branching stems that often spread horizontally along the ground. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the cooler months.

Oxybaphus diffusus is known for its resilience to drought and its ability to thrive in marginal environments.
