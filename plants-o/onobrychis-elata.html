Onobrychis elata

Onobrychis elata, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its pods are slender, linear, and contain several seeds.

Onobrychis elata is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
