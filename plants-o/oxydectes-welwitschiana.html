Oxydectes welwitschiana

Oxydectes welwitschiana, commonly known as the Welwitschia mirabilis, is a unique and ancient plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is one of the oldest living seed plants, with a lineage dating back over 100 million years. The plant features two large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, which are adapted to the arid environment. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater sources.

The plant also produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is pollinated by wind and insects.
