Ornithogalum transcaucasicum

Ornithogalum transcaucasicum, commonly known as the Caucasian star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum transcaucasicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
