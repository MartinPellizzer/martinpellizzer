Olfersia alata

Olfersia alata, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a slender, climbing stem with wing-like structures on its leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Olfersia alata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
