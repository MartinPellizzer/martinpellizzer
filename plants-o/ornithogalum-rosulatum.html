Ornithogalum rosulatum

Ornithogalum rosulatum, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant blooms with clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that appear in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and early blooming habit.
