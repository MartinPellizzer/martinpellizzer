Odontoglossum × chestertonii

Odontoglossum × chestertonii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum lindenii and Odontoglossum ravenii.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that produce a single leaf per node. The plant features showy, violet to purple flowers with long, narrow petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Odontoglossum × chestertonii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
