Odontonema tubaeforme

Odontonema tubaeforme, commonly known as the pink flame or pink bell plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking pink to magenta flowers in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
