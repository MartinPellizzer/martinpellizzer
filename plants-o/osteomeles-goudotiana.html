Osteomeles goudotiana

Osteomeles goudotiana, commonly known as the Goudot's flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy, and its bark is smooth with a reddish hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

Osteomeles goudotiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
