Oberonia jenkinsiana

Oberonia jenkinsiana, commonly known as the Jenkins' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing structure. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with reddish-brown markings. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Oberonia jenkinsiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
