Oreophilus montis-rotundi

Oreophilus montis-rotundi, commonly known as the mountain-rounded orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often growing on rocky outcrops or in shaded forest clearings. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its blooming season typically occurs during the spring and early summer months.
