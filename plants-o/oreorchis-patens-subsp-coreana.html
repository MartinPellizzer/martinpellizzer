Oreorchis patens subsp. coreana

Oreorchis patens subsp.

coreana, commonly known as the Korean twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers.

Native to Korea, it typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and stream sides. The plant has a short rhizome and produces flowers in late spring to early summer.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
