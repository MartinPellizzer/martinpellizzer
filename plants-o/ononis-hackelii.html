Ononis hackelii

Ononis hackelii, commonly known as Hackel's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Ononis hackelii thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.
