Opuntia anacantha var. utkilio

Opuntia anacantha var. utkilio, commonly known as the spiny hedgehog cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems covered in sharp spines and small, woolly areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its structure and features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
