Ophioglossum concinnum

Ophioglossum concinnum, commonly known as the northern sword fern, is a rare and ancient fern species native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped fronds that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a unique reproductive structure called a sporangium, which produces spores for propagation. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
