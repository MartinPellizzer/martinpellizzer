Oncidium × charlesworthii

Oncidium × charlesworthii, commonly known as the Charlesworth orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various Oncidium species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with good air circulation.

Oncidium × charlesworthii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and showy blooms.
