Odontoglossum tigrinum

Odontoglossum tigrinum, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in cool, humid environments at high altitudes. The plant features a pseudobulb with a rosette of dark green, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow and green markings that resemble a tiger's stripes.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
