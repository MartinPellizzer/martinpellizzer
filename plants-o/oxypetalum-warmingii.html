Oxypetalum warmingii

Oxypetalum warmingii, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the evergreen rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with dark green, glossy leaves. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their aromatic and therapeutic properties.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its medicinal applications.
