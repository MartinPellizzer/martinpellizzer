Origanum boissieri

Origanum boissieri, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its essential oils, rich in compounds like thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Origanum boissieri is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
