Oxypetalum polyanthum

Oxypetalum polyanthum, commonly known as the Chinese rose or Rosa chinensis, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring thorny stems and dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom in spring and summer, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Oxypetalum polyanthum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
