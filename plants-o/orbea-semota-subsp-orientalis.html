Orbea semota subsp. orientalis

Orbea semota subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the Eastern Orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a succulent shrub characterized by its cylindrical stems and clusters of tubular, pale yellow to orange flowers.

The leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in arid or semi-arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
