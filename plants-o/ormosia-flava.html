Ormosia flava

Ormosia flava, commonly known as golden ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Ormosia flava is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.

Its growth habit and botanical features make it an important species in tropical ecosystems.
