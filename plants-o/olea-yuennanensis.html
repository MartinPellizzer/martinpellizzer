Olea yuennanensis

Olea yuennanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan olive, is a species of evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are edible but not widely consumed. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, Olea yuennanensis is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
