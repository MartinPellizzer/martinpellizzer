Ouratea podocarpa

Ouratea podocarpa, commonly known as the red-leaf ouratea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and striking red or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
