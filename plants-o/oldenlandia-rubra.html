Oldenlandia rubra

Oldenlandia rubra, commonly known as redleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink or red. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, three-lobed fruits that contain tiny seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.
