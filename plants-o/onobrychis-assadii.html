Onobrychis assadii

Onobrychis assadii, commonly known as the Assad's goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has bipinnate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to survive in harsh environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils with low moisture availability.
