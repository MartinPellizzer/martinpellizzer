Osteospermum scariosum

Osteospermum scariosum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Osteospermum scariosum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
