Orthodon hirtus

Orthodon hirtus, commonly known as the hairy orthodon, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Orthodon hirtus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
