Opuntia rhodantha var. spinosior

Opuntia rhodantha var. spinosior, commonly known as the red prickly pear, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are typically red or pink in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its spines are more pronounced and longer compared to other Opuntia species.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
