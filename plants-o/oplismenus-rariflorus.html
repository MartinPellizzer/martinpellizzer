Oplismenus rariflorus

Oplismenus rariflorus, commonly known as the slender false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along streambanks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.
