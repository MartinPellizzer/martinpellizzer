Oxalis maxonii

Oxalis maxonii, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that resemble those of common oxalis species, with three heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Oxalis maxonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowers.
