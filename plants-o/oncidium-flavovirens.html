Oncidium flavovirens

Oncidium flavovirens, commonly known as the Yellow-green Oncidium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive golden lip. It is known for its relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Oncidium flavovirens thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
