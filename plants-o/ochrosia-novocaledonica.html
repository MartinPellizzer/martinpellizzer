Ochrosia novocaledonica

Ochrosia novocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia ochrosia, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia in the South Pacific.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland forest areas.

Ochrosia novocaledonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
