Onopordum × godronii

Onopordum × godronii, commonly known as the Godron's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in rocky and dry soils. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and wastelands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens but can be invasive in certain environments.
