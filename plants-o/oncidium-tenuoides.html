Oncidium tenuoides

Oncidium tenuoides, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear numerous small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in various environmental conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium tenuoides is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
