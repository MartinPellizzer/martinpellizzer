Ornithogalum arabicum var. hipponense

Ornithogalum arabicum var. hipponense, commonly known as the Egyptian star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features strap-like, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
