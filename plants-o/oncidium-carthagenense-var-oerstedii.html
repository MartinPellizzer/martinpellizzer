Oncidium carthagenense var. oerstedii

Oncidium carthagenense var. oerstedii, commonly known as the Oersted's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a fan shape, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
