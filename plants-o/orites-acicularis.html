Orites acicularis

Orites acicularis, commonly known as the needleleaf orite, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Orites acicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
