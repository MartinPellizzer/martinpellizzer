Oncidium callacallaense

Oncidium callacallaense, commonly known as the Callacalla orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow and orange petals. Oncidium callacallaense prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
