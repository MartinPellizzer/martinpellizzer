Ornithogalum filicaule

Ornithogalum filicaule, commonly known as the broad-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often broader than those of other Ornithogalum species. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring or early summer. It grows in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant is adapted to tolerate dry conditions and is often found in well-drained soils.
