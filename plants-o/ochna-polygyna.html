Ochna polygyna

Ochna polygyna, commonly known as the many-flowered ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Ochna polygyna is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
