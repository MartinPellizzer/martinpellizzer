Oligosporus changaicus

Oligosporus changaicus, commonly known as the Changai spore plant, is a rare and unique species belonging to the fungal-like group of organisms.

It is characterized by its intricate spore-producing structures that are adapted for dispersal in specific microhabitats. The plant exhibits a complex life cycle involving both sexual and asexual reproduction stages. Its morphology includes a network of filaments that form a dense, spore-rich matrix.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered an important subject for botanical research.
