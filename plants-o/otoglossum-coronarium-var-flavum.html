Otoglossum coronarium var. flavum

Otoglossum coronarium var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow crowned orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green striped petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in the wild.
