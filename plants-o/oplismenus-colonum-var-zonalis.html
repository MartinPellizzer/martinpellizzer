Oplismenus colonum var. zonalis

Oplismenus colonum var. zonalis, commonly known as the zonal geranium, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it popular for use in hanging baskets and containers. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The zonal geranium is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions.
