Orchis × jamainii

Orchis × jamainii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Orchis morio and Orchis anthropophora.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant produces showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

This hybrid is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
