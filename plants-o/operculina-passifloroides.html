Operculina passifloroides

Operculina passifloroides, commonly known as the pink passion vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers, which are often clustered in inflorescences. The plant belongs to the Passifloraceae family and is known for its showy, complex floral structures. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Operculina passifloroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
