Onobrychis melanotricha var. melanotricha

Onobrychis melanotricha var. melanotricha, commonly known as blackstem burr vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with dark green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by cylindrical, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
