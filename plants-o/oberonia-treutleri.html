Oberonia treutleri

Oberonia treutleri, commonly known as the Treutler's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Oberonia treutleri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
