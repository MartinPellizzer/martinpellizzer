Oyedaea huilensis

Oyedaea huilensis, commonly known as the Huiltepec cactus, is a species native to the highlands of Oaxaca, Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, pale green stems and sparse, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and early summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
