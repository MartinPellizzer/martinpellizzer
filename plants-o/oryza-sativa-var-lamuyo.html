Oryza sativa var. lamuyo

Oryza sativa var. lamuyo, commonly known as the Philippine rice.

It is a variety of rice native to the Philippines and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically reaches a height of about 100-120 cm and has long, narrow leaves. Its grains are small and fragrant, with a distinct aroma that sets it apart from other rice varieties.

This variety is well adapted to tropical climates and is often cultivated in lowland rice fields.
