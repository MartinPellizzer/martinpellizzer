Oreocnide pilosula

Oreocnide pilosula, commonly known as the hairy oreocnide, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in mountainous areas with high humidity. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale yellow.

Oreocnide pilosula is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
