Odontostemma longistylum var. longistylum

Odontostemma longistylum var. longistylum, commonly known as the long-styled odontostemma, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its elongated, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of tropical flora and plant morphology.
