Oftia

Oftia, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often surrounded by green bracts. Its leaves are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette.

Oftia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
