Octomeria micrantha

Octomeria micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered octomeria, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly found in Brazil and Paraguay. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
