Oxalis rosea var. simplex

Oxalis rosea var. simplex, commonly known as the pink buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pinkish hue and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Oxalis rosea var. simplex is known for its ease of care and ability to spread through rhizomes.
