Oenothera pilosella

Oenothera pilosella, commonly known as the silky evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Oenothera pilosella is a member of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses.
