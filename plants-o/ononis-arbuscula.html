Ononis arbuscula

Ononis arbuscula, commonly known as the creeping sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its mat-forming growth habit with small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers, typically blooming in spring. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.
