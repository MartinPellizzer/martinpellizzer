Olbia micans

Olbia micans, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
