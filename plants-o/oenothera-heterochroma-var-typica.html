Oenothera heterochroma var. typica

Oenothera heterochroma var. typica, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
