Otholobium holosericeum

Otholobium holosericeum, commonly known as the silky otholobium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, hard seeds enclosed in pods, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
