Olsynium junceum subsp. depauperatum

Olsynium junceum subsp.

depauperatum, commonly known as the slender rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in moist conditions.

This subspecies is characterized by its reduced floral structures and is often found in association with other aquatic or semi-aquatic vegetation.
