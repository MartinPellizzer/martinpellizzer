Onopordum hasankeyfense

Onopordum hasankeyfense, commonly known as the Hasankeyf thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
