Ourisia macrophylla subsp. robusta

Ourisia macrophylla subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the giant ourisia, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 10 cm in length.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in damp, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of New Zealand's native flora.
