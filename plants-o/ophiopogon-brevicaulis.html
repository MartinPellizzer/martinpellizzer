Ophiopogon brevicaulis

Ophiopogon brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-leaved spathiphyllum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.
