Oenanthe × boissieui

Oenanthe × boissieui, commonly known as the hybrid water dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. It is native to regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
