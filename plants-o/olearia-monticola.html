Olearia monticola

Olearia monticola, commonly known as the mountain dandelion, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its woolly, grey-green foliage. The plant produces small, dandelion-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Olearia monticola typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils.

It is a hardy species that is often found in open habitats at high elevations.
