Oxalis barrelieri

Oxalis barrelieri, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white coating. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Oxalis barrelieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate flowers.
