Oenanthe silaifolia f. lanceolata

Oenanthe silaifolia f. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate water dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, white to greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. Its stems are often hairy and can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

The species is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
