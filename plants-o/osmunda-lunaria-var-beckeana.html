Osmunda lunaria var. beckeana

Osmunda lunaria var. beckeana, commonly known as the Beck's moonwort, is a fern species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically dark green and finely divided. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.
