Orchis tephrosanthos var. cercopitheca

Orchis tephrosanthos var. cercopitheca, commonly known as the Cercopitheca Orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions in Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats. Its morphological features include long, slender stems and lanceolate leaves.
