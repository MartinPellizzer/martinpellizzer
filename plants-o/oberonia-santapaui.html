Oberonia santapaui

Oberonia santapaui, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
