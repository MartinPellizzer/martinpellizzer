Oncidium jimenezii

Oncidium jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features slender, often branched pseudobulbs and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or orange with darker markings, and they bloom in clusters during the dry season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
