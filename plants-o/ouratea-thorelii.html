Ouratea thorelii

Ouratea thorelii, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, white, flame-like inflorescences that bloom during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in tropical and subtropical climates.
