Orania timikae

Orania timikae, commonly known as the Timika orchid, is a rare and endemic flowering plant native to the highlands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on tree trunks or rocky surfaces. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns of purple and white, and it produces a unique type of seed capsule.

Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, Orania timikae is considered a vulnerable species.
