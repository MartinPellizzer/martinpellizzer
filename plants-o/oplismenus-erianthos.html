Oplismenus erianthos

Oplismenus erianthos, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in a dense layer of silvery-white hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered with a fine, silky coating of hairs.

Oplismenus erianthos is typically found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and streambanks.
