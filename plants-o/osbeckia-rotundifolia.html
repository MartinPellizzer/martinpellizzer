Osbeckia rotundifolia

Osbeckia rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf osbeckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features rounded, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and are typically pink to purple in color.

Osbeckia rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
