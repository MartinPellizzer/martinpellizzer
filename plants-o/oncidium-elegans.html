Oncidium elegans

Oncidium elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white markings. Oncidium elegans is known for its relatively easy care requirements and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
