Oberonia affinis

Oberonia affinis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features a slender stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, often clustered in a loose inflorescence.

Oberonia affinis is characterized by its unique pseudobulbs and is often found in epiphytic or lithophytic habitats.
