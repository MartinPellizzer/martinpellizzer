Orbea ubomboensis

Orbea ubomboensis, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a flowering plant that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a trailing vine, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Orbea ubomboensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
