Orbea ciliata

Orbea ciliata, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight. It is drought-tolerant and often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
