Olea microcarpa

Olea microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited olive, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of North Africa. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. Its fruit is a small, oval drupe that ripens to a dark purple color.

Olea microcarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
