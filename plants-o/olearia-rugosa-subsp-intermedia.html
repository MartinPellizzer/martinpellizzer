Olearia rugosa subsp. intermedia

Olearia rugosa subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the silverweed tree, is a shrub-like plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking visual contrast.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its unique appearance and hardiness.
