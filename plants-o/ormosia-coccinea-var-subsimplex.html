Ormosia coccinea var. subsimplex

Ormosia coccinea var. subsimplex, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are typically woody and branched. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
