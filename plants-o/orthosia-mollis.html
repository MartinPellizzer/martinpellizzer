Orthosia mollis

Orthosia mollis, commonly known as the slender orthosia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale blue flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Orthosia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
