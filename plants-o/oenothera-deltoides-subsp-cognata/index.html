Oenothera Deltoides Subsp. Cognata

Oenothera deltoides subsp. cognata, also known as the desert evening primrose, is a striking member of the Onagraceae family native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. This plant is renowned for its vibrant yellow flowers that open in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths. It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh desert environments. The plant's resilience and beauty make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens. Its ecological importance supports local wildlife and contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat.
