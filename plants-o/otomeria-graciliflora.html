Otomeria graciliflora

Otomeria graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered otomeria, is a small shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Otomeria graciliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
