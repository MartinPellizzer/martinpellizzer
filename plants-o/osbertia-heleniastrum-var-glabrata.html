Osbertia heleniastrum var. glabrata

Osbertia heleniastrum var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth osbertia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, hairless surface, giving the plant its varietal name "glabrata.". This species is native to parts of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ease of care.
