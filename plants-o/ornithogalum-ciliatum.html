Ornithogalum ciliatum

Ornithogalum ciliatum, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, hairy, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with silver. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum ciliatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
