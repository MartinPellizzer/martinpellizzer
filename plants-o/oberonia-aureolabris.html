Oberonia aureolabris

Oberonia aureolabris, commonly known as the golden-labied orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking golden-yellow labellum with a contrasting greenish-blue center. The plant typically features narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Oberonia aureolabris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
