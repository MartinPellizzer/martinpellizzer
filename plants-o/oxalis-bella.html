Oxalis bella

Oxalis bella, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Oxalidaceae family, known for its calcium oxalate crystals in the leaves. Oxalis bella thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
