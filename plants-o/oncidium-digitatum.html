Oncidium digitatum

Oncidium digitatum, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with brightly colored flowers. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Oncidium digitatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
