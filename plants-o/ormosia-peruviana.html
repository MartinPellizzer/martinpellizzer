Ormosia peruviana

Ormosia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Ormosia peruviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
