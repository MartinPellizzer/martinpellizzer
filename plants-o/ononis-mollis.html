Ononis mollis

Ononis mollis, commonly known as white sand veronica, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, arranged in a basal rosette, and are covered with a fine pubescence.

Ononis mollis thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
