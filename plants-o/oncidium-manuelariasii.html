Oncidium manuelariasii

Oncidium manuelariasii, commonly known as the Manuelarias orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Oncidium manuelariasii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
