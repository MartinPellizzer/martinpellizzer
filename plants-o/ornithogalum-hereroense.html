Ornithogalum hereroense

Ornithogalum hereroense, commonly known as the Herero star-of-Bethleham, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a short, stout stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
