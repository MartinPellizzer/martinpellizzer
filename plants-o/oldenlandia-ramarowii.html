Oldenlandia ramarowii

Oldenlandia ramarowii, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
