Operculina Coptica

Operculina coptica is a striking member of the Nightshade family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to parts of Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and well-drained soil. Its unique features include a distinctive, hood-like structure called an operculum that protects the flower bud. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. With proper care, Operculina coptica can become a stunning addition to any garden or indoor space.
