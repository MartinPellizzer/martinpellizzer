Orthopichonia lacourtiana

Orthopichonia lacourtiana, commonly known as the lacourtian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically features elliptical, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Orthopichonia lacourtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
