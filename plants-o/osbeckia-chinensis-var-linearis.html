Osbeckia chinensis var. linearis

Osbeckia chinensis var. linearis, commonly known as the slender osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species characterized by its linear, narrow leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems, and it produces small, elliptical seeds.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
