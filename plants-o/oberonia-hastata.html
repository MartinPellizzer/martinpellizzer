Oberonia hastata

Oberonia hastata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in rich, humus-filled soil. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and a cluster of fleshy, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale green, and are arranged in a loose spike.

Oberonia hastata is notable for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
