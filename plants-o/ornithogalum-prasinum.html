Ornithogalum prasinum

Ornithogalum prasinum, commonly known as the green star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum prasinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
