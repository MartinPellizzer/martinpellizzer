Oxalis haedulipes

Oxalis haedulipes, commonly known as the golden oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and delicate blooms.

Oxalis haedulipes prefers partial to full sun and is relatively low maintenance in suitable climates.
