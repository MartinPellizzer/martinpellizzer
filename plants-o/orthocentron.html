Orthocentron

Orthocentron, commonly known as the golden spike grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves with a distinctive golden hue along the midrib, giving it its common name. Orthocentron typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
