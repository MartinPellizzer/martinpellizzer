Ornitrophe spicata

Ornitrophe spicata, commonly known as the spiky feather plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, feather-like inflorescences.

It belongs to the family Ornitropheaceae and is native to the arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that reduce water loss, adapting it to its harsh environment. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a striking contrast of colors.

Ornitrophe spicata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
