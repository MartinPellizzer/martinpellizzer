Oxera brevicalyx

Oxera brevicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved oxera, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along its stems. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which are often borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shady or semi-shaded environments.

Oxera brevicalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
