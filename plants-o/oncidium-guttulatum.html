Oncidium guttulatum

Oncidium guttulatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, spike-like inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and often have a waxy texture. Oncidium guttulatum is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
