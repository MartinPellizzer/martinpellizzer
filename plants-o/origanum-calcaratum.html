Origanum calcaratum

Origanum calcaratum, commonly known as rock savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, adding a pungent, slightly bitter flavor to dishes.

Additionally, it is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
