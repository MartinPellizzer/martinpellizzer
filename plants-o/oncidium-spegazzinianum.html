Oncidium spegazzinianum

Oncidium spegazzinianum, commonly known as the Spegazzini's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are strap-shaped and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

Oncidium spegazzinianum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
