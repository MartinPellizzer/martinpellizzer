Oxalis spodiophylla var. microphylla

Oxalis spodiophylla var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature wood sorrel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with reddish veins, and it produces delicate yellow flowers with five petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its compact growth and attractive foliage.

Oxalis spodiophylla var. microphylla is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
