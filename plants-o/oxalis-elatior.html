Oxalis elatior

Oxalis elatior, commonly known as the spurred oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It prefers partial shade to full sun and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Oxalis elatior is known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding color to gardens and landscapes.
