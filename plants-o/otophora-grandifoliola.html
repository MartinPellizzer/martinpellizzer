Otophora grandifoliola

Otophora grandifoliola, commonly known as the large-leaf otophora, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a waxy surface and serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Otophora grandifoliola thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.
