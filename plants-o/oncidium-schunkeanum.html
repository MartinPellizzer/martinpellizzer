Oncidium schunkeanum

Oncidium schunkeanum, commonly known as the Schunke's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with white and yellow markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is noted for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Oncidium schunkeanum is popular among orchid growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
