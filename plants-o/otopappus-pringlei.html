Otopappus pringlei

Otopappus pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's pappus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing, herbaceous structure with narrow, gray-green leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and are adapted to attract pollinators in its harsh environment.
