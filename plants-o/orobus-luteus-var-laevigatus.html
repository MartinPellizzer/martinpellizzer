Orobus luteus var. laevigatus

Orobus luteus var. laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth yellow orobus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are smooth and often hairy, with small, yellowish-green leaves arranged oppositely along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
