Osodendron altissimum subsp. altissimum

Osodendron altissimum subsp.

altissimum, commonly known as the tall osodendron, is a species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage.

The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in terminal panicles, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
