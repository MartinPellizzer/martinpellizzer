Opuntia horstii

Opuntia horstii, commonly known as the Mexican prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in small, sharp spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunas, is edible and has a sweet, succulent taste.

Opuntia horstii is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of food.
