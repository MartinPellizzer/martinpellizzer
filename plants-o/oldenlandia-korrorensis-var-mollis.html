Oldenlandia korrorensis var. mollis

Oldenlandia korrorensis var. mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf strobilanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both ecological and horticultural settings.
