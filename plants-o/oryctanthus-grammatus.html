Oryctanthus grammatus

Oryctanthus grammatus, commonly known as the honeycomb lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns resembling a honeycomb. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Oryctanthus grammatus is known for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
