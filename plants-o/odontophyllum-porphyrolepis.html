Odontophyllum porphyrolepis

Odontophyllum porphyrolepis, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking red stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
