Ozoroa homblei

Ozoroa homblei, commonly known as the Hombli's lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, typically displaying vibrant pink or red hues. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
