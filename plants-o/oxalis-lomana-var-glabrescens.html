Oxalis lomana var. glabrescens

Oxalis lomana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage is usually a deep green, and it produces yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months.

Oxalis lomana var. glabrescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
