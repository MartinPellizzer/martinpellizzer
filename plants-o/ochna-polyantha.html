Ochna polyantha

Ochna polyantha, commonly known as the red-flowered ochna, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and striking clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochna polyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
