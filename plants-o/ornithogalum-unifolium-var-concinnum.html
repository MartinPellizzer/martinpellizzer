Ornithogalum unifolium var. concinnum

Ornithogalum unifolium var. concinnum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The leaves are narrow, linear, and often slightly curled, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
