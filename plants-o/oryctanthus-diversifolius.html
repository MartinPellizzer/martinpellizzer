Oryctanthus diversifolius

Oryctanthus diversifolius, commonly known as the mountain zinnia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, varying in shape and size across different individuals.

Oryctanthus diversifolius is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
