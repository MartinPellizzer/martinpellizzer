Oriastrum sphaeroidale

Oriastrum sphaeroidale, commonly known as globe mallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves and stems, which help it retain water in arid environments. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Oriastrum sphaeroidale is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.
