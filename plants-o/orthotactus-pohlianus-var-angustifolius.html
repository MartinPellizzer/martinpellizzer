Orthotactus pohlianus var. angustifolius

Orthotactus pohlianus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orthotactus, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is a mat-forming plant with a distinctive, flat, leafy structure that resembles a cushion. The leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a slightly undulating margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or soil in temperate regions.
