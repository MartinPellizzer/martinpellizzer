Oxalis perennans

Oxalis perennans, commonly known as the everlasting oxalis, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental value.

Oxalis perennans is also notable for its ability to produce seed capsules that can remain on the plant for an extended period.
