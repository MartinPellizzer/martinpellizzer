Ononis thomsonii var. ayachica

Ononis thomsonii var. ayachica, commonly known as the Ayachica sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in desert scrub habitats.
