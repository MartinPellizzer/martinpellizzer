Oxalis canaliculata var. canaliculata

Oxalis canaliculata var. canaliculata, commonly known as the buttonweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves.

It features small, five-petaled flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it both an ornamental plant and a potential weed in various environments.
