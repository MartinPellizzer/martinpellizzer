Oyedaea lanceolata

Oyedaea lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf orchid, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to specific regions in Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. It is often found in coastal or mountainous areas with a Mediterranean climate.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
