Oncidium luridum var. henchmannii

Oncidium luridum var. henchmannii, commonly known as the Henchmann's Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid that showcases the diversity within the Oncidium genus.
