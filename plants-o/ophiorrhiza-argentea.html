Ophiorrhiza argentea

Ophiorrhiza argentea, commonly known as silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
