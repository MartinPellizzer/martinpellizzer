Oncidium bustosii

Oncidium bustosii, commonly known as the Ecuadorian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of Ecuador and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences. The plant typically produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Oncidium bustosii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
