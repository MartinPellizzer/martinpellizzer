Oncidium praenitens

Oncidium praenitens, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers, which resemble a lady's slipper in appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium praenitens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
