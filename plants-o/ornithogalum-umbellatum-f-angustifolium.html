Ornithogalum umbellatum f. angustifolium

Ornithogalum umbellatum f. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a botanical variant of the star-of-Bethlehem plant.

It is a bulbous perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
