Oncostemum bojerianum

Oncostemum bojerianum, commonly known as the Bojer's onion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous stem and clustered, grass-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in various regions with a Mediterranean climate.
