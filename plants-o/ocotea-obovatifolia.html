Ocotea obovatifolia

Ocotea obovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically broad and ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by dark blue or black berries.

Ocotea obovatifolia is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
