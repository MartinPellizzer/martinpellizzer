Origanum thymus

Origanum thymus, commonly known as thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and flowers that range from white to pink. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties. Thyme is often used in cooking and traditional medicine due to its essential oils.

Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
