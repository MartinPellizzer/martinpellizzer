Othonna ciliata

Othonna ciliata, commonly known as the woolly othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance.

Othonna ciliata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and drought tolerance.
