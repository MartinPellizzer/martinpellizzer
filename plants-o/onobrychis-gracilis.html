Onobrychis gracilis

Onobrychis gracilis, commonly known as slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, creeping stems with small, pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are pale yellow and appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Onobrychis gracilis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
