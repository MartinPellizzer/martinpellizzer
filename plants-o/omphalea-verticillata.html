Omphalea verticillata

Omphalea verticillata, commonly known as the spiral leaf physostegia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-arranged leaves that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Omphalea verticillata is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
