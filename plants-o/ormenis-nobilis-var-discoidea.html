Ormenis nobilis var. discoidea

Ormenis nobilis var. discoidea, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a unique variant of the orchid species Ormenis nobilis.

This plant is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
