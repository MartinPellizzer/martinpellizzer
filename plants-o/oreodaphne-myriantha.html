Oreodaphne myriantha

Oreodaphne myriantha, commonly known as the glossy tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically opposite or whorled. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release flat, winged seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
