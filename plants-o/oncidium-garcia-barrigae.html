Oncidium garcia-barrigae

Oncidium garcia-barrigae, commonly known as the Garcia-Barriga orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, adapted to the humid, shaded environments where it grows.

Oncidium garcia-barrigae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
