Ornithidium pendens

Ornithidium pendens, commonly known as the hanging feather plant, is a unique member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that resemble delicate feathers, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
