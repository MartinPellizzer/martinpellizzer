Ochna chilversii

Ochna chilversii, commonly known as the Chilvers ochna, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with dark green, glossy leaves. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are often eaten by birds and small mammals.

Ochna chilversii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
