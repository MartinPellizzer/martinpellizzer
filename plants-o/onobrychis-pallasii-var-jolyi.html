Onobrychis pallasii var. jolyi

Onobrychis pallasii var. jolyi, commonly known as the Joly's goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, spindly stems and trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.
