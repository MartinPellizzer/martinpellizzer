Obione semibaccata

Obione semibaccata, commonly known as the slender waxleaf.

It is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Obione semibaccata prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates.
