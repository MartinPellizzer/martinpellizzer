Ornithogalum monophyllum subsp. monophyllum

Ornithogalum monophyllum subsp.

monophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of narrow, linear leaves.

The plant blooms with white, star-shaped flowers that are arranged in a raceme, typically during the summer months. Its leaves and flowers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.
