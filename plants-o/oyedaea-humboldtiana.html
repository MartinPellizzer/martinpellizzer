Oyedaea humboldtiana

Oyedaea humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt's oyedaea, is a rare and unique plant native to the coastal regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery fuzz. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand strong sea breezes and salt spray.
