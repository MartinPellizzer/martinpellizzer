Ononis tridentata f. dentata

Ononis tridentata f. dentata, commonly known as the toothed bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically oval or lance-shaped with serrated edges, giving the plant its "toothed" appearance. This plant is generally low-growing and serves as a food source for various insects and small animals.
