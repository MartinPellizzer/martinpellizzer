Osmanthus bambusifolius

Osmanthus bambusifolius, commonly known as bamboo-leaf osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like leaves that are elongated and slightly serrated. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in clusters, which are highly valued for their aromatic properties. These flowers are typically harvested before they open to preserve their scent.

Osmanthus bambusifolius is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in traditional Chinese medicine.
