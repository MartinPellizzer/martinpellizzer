Oncerum rupestre

Oncerum rupestre, commonly known as the rock heather, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, evergreen habit. The plant typically features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Oncerum rupestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
