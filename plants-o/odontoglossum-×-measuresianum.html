Odontoglossum × measuresianum

Odontoglossum × measuresianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Odontoglossum orchids.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions of the Andes, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its native habitat.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
