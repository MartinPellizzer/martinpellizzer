Oxytropis pallens

Oxytropis pallens, commonly known as the pale locoweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain seeds.
