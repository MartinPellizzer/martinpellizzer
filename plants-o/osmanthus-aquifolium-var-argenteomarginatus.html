Osmanthus aquifolium var. argenteomarginatus

Osmanthus aquifolium var. argenteomarginatus, commonly known as the silver-margin osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with striking white or silver margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late autumn, which are often used in perfumes and traditional medicine. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and aromatic blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
