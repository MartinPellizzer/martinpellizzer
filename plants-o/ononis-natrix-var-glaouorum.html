Ononis natrix var. glaouorum

Ononis natrix var. glaouorum, commonly known as the Algerian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.
