Orchis gigantea

Orchis gigantea, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Europe.

This plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting large, showy flowers. The flowers are typically violet or purple in color, with a unique structure that includes a prominent lip petal. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Orchis gigantea is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
