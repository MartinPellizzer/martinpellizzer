Oldenlandia hispida

Oldenlandia hispida, commonly known as the hairy oldenlandia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, white or yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oldenlandia hispida is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
