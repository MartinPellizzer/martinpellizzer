Oncidium gracile

Oncidium gracile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and delicate, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in a raceme. Oncidium gracile prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and good air circulation to thrive.
