Oxalis casta

Oxalis casta, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-blue hue, often adorned with red veins. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis casta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
