Orontium majus var. album

Orontium majus var. album, commonly known as the white water lily, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It features broad, white, floating leaves that are often tinged with pink at the base. The plant produces showy, white flowers with yellow stamens that bloom in early summer. Its roots are rhizomatous, allowing it to spread through vegetative propagation.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, contributing to aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
