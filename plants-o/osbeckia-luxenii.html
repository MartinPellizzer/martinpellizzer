Osbeckia luxenii

Osbeckia luxenii, commonly known as the woolly osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features hairy, silver-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Osbeckia luxenii is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine regions.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in its native habitat.
