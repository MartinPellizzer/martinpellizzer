Oxalis pennelliana

Oxalis pennelliana, commonly known as the pink oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink or red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a heart-shaped leaflet, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Oxalis pennelliana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
