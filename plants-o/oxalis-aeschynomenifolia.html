Oxalis aeschynomenifolia

Oxalis aeschynomenifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white leaves with a velvety texture and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.

Oxalis aeschynomenifolia is known for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
