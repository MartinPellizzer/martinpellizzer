Othonna sedifolia

Othonna sedifolia, commonly known as the Woolly Othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
