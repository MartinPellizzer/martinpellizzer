Osmundopteris cicutaria

Osmundopteris cicutaria, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It is native to wetland habitats and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive rhizome system that helps it anchor and absorb nutrients from the waterlogged soil. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.

Osmundopteris cicutaria plays an important ecological role in aquatic and riparian ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
