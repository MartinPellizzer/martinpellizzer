Oxybaphus bodinii

Oxybaphus bodinii, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually purple or violet, with a tubular shape and showy petals.

Oxybaphus bodinii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
