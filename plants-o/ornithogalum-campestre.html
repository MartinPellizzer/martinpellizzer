Ornithogalum campestre

Ornithogalum campestre, commonly known as the meadow bell or star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers during late spring to early summer. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
