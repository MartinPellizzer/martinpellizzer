Oreocereus trollii var. tenuior

Oreocereus trollii var. tenuior, commonly known as the slender moon cactus, is a striking cactus species native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hair. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the top of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a favored plant among cactus enthusiasts.
