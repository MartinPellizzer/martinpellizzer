Olea sphaerica

Olea sphaerica, commonly known as the globe olive, is a rare and unique species native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense, evergreen foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Olea sphaerica is adapted to arid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in traditional medicine.
