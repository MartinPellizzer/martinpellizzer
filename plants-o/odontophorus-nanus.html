Odontophorus nanus

Odontophorus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Odontophorus nanus is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in terrariums or greenhouses.
