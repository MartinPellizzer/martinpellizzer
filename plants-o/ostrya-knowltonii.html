Ostrya knowltonii

Ostrya knowltonii, commonly known as the Mexican hop-hornbeam, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family, which includes birches, alders, and hickories. Ostrya knowltonii is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to a variety of elevational ranges.
