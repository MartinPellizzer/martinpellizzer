Oxalis kollmannii

Oxalis kollmannii, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage, which remains attractive throughout the growing season.
