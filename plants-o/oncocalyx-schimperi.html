Oncocalyx schimperi

Oncocalyx schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian orchid, is a perennial herb native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces a single spike of flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Oncocalyx schimperi is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny areas at higher elevations.

Its ornamental value has made it a subject of interest for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
