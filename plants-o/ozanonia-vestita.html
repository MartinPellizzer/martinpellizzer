Ozanonia vestita

Ozanonia vestita, commonly known as the smooth bryum, is a small, mat-forming moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its dense, greenish-gray shoots and small, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and not ribbed. The plant produces elongated capsules that are often covered in a thin, transparent calyptra. Ozanonia vestita is commonly found in forest floors, rock crevices, and along streams, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling.

Its simple morphology and adaptability make it a common component of temperate and boreal ecosystems.
