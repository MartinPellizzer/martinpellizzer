Oenothera argentinae

Oenothera argentinae, commonly known as the Argentine evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Oenothera, which includes many species of evening primroses. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Oenothera argentinae is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in supporting local pollinators.
