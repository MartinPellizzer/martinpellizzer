Oxalis ornithopus

Oxalis ornithopus, commonly known as the bird's beak oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bird-like beak-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Oxalis ornithopus is often grown as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
