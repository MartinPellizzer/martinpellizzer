Orchis prosteveniella

Orchis prosteveniella, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Heath Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Europe.

It is a perennial herb with a slender, upright stem that bears a single spike of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it typically grows in calcareous soils in woodland or heathland habitats. Orchis prosteveniella is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

It is a protected species in many European countries due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
