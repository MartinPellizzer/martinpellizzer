Othake roseum var. robustum

Othake roseum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust othake rose, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has a woody stem with thick, glossy leaves that are arranged in an alternate pattern. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
