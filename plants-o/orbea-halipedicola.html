Orbea halipedicola

Orbea halipedicola, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are often pink or red in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Orbea halipedicola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
