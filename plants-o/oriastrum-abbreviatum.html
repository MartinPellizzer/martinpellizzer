Oriastrum abbreviatum

Oriastrum abbreviatum, commonly known as the shortleaf globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive globular shape.
