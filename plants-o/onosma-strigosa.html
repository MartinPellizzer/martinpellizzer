Onosma strigosa

Onosma strigosa, commonly known as the woolly onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, gray-green leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Onosma strigosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
