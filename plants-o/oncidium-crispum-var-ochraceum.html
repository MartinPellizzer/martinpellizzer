Oncidium crispum var. ochraceum

Oncidium crispum var. ochraceum, commonly known as the Orange Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with red or brown markings. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
