Oenothera tarijensis

Oenothera tarijensis, commonly known as the Tarijensis evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. Oenothera tarijensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
