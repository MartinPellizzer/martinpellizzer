Oldenlandia richardsonioides var. gracilis

Oldenlandia richardsonioides var. gracilis, commonly known as slender oldenlandia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is distinguished by its more delicate appearance compared to other varieties of Oldenlandia.
