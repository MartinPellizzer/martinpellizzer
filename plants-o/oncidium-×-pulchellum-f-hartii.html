Oncidium × pulchellum f. hartii

Oncidium × pulchellum f. hartii, commonly known as the Hartii Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species with striking, long-lasting blooms.

It features pendulous, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting mix to flourish.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
