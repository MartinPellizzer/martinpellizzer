Ononis laxiflora f. elegans

Ononis laxiflora f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters along the stems. The plant has hairy, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid conditions, allowing it to retain moisture efficiently. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
