Ozoroa pulcherrima

Ozoroa pulcherrima, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods that contain flat, fibrous seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Ozoroa pulcherrima is well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
