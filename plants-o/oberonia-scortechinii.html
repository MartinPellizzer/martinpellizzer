Oberonia scortechinii

Oberonia scortechinii, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, with its roots anchored in the bark of host trees. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in a rosette formation.

Oberonia scortechinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
