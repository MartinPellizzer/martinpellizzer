Obeliscaria tagetes

Obeliscaria tagetes, commonly known as the Mexican aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of 30 to 100 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and upright.

Obeliscaria tagetes is native to Mexico and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
