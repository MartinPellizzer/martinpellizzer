Oenothera biennis f. grandiflora

Oenothera biennis f. grandiflora, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

The f. grandiflora form is notable for its larger floral display compared to the typical species.
