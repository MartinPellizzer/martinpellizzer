Ononis cossoniana f. robusta

Ononis cossoniana f. robusta, commonly known as the robust ononis, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
