Oliverella bussei

Oliverella bussei, commonly known as the African olive, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family, which includes other olive-like plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits.

Oliverella bussei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
