Oxydectes muricata

Oxydectes muricata, commonly known as the spiked dwarf lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features vibrant, showy flowers with a distinctive spiky appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually a mix of red, orange, and yellow, creating a striking visual contrast.

Oxydectes muricata is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
