Ortgiesia coelestis var. albomarginata

Ortgiesia coelestis var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that feature a deep purple or violet hue with distinctive white margins. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
