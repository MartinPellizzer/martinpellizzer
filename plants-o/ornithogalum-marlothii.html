Ornithogalum marlothii

Ornithogalum marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's star of Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, and its bulbs are used for propagation.

Ornithogalum marlothii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
