Opuntia atroviridis f. parviflora

Opuntia atroviridis f. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered black prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark green, spiny stems and small, clustered flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its fleshy pads store water, allowing it to survive in low-moisture climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
