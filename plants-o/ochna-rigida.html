Ochna rigida

Ochna rigida, commonly known as the rigid ochna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, fleshy fruits that are yellow when mature. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Ochna rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
