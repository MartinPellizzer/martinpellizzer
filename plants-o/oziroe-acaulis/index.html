Oziroe Acaulis

Oziroe acaulis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Madagascar. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring bright yellow flowers that stand out against its green foliage. Despite its beauty, this plant is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered. Its survival depends on the preservation of its natural habitat. Studying Oziroe acaulis offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of Madagascar's ecosystems.
