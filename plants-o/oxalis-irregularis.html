Oxalis irregularis

Oxalis irregularis, commonly known as the irregular oxalis, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a desirable addition to both ornamental and botanical collections.
