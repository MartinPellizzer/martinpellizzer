Ornithogalum kilicii

Ornithogalum kilicii, commonly known as the Turkish bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Turkey.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale blue flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its resilience. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
