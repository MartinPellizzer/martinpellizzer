Oeonia brauniana var. sarcanthoides

Oeonia brauniana var. sarcanthoides, commonly known as the scarlet bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable plant for both ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
