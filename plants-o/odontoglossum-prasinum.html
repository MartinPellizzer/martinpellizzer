Odontoglossum prasinum

Odontoglossum prasinum, commonly known as the Emerald Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant green foliage, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and greenish-white, with a subtle fragrance.

Odontoglossum prasinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
