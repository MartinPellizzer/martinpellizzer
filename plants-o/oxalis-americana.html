Oxalis americana

Oxalis americana, commonly known as wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb with trifoliate leaves that resemble those of clover. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Oxalis americana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
