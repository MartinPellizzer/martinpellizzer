Oldenlandia silvatica

Oldenlandia silvatica, commonly known as the forest spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with five petals and a prominent central spur.

Oldenlandia silvatica is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed or nutrient-rich soils.
