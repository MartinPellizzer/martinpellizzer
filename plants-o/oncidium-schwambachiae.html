Oncidium schwambachiae

Oncidium schwambachiae, commonly known as the Schwanbach orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that display a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are arranged in a raceme and often feature a prominent lip that is distinctly shaped.

Oncidium schwambachiae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
