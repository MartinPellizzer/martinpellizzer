Oncocalyx bolusii

Oncocalyx bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's orchid, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique floral structure includes a spurred lip, which is a distinguishing feature of many orchid species.

Oncocalyx bolusii is also noted for its ability to tolerate dry conditions, making it a resilient choice for gardens in arid climates.
