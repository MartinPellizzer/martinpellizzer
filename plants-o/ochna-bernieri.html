Ochna bernieri

Ochna bernieri, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance. Ochna bernieri thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
