Odontites recordonii

Odontites recordonii, commonly known as the Alpine toothplant, is a rare and alpine species native to the Pyrenees and the southern Alps.

It is a herbaceous plant with small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Odontites recordonii thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes.
