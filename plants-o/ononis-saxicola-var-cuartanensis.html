Ononis saxicola var. cuartanensis

Ononis saxicola var. cuartanensis, commonly known as the Cuartana rockcress, is a flowering plant species native to specific regions of southern Europe.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and distinctive seed pods. This variety thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
