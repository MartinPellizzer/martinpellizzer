Ormosia semicastrata f. litchiifolia

Ormosia semicastrata f. litchiifolia, commonly known as the Litchi-leaved Ormosia, is a variant of the Ormosia semicastrata species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, litchi-like leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This ornamental plant is valued for its unique foliage and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
