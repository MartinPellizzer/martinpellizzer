Oxalis tenuicaulis

Oxalis tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a bright green color and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. Its stems are slender and hairy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Oxalis tenuicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
