Othonna burttii

Othonna burttii, commonly known as the Cape goldbush, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Othonna burttii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
