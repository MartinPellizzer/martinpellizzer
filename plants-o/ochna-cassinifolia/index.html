Ochna Cassinifolia

Ochna cassinifolia is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive foliage. This plant thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in regions with mild climates and moderate rainfall. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The species is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for local wildlife. Despite its beauty, Ochna cassinifolia remains relatively unknown to many gardeners outside its native range.
