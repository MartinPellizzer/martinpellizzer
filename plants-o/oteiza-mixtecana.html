Oteiza mixtecana

Oteiza mixtecana, commonly known as the Mixtecan oteiza, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Oaxaca, Mexico.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, often covered in a fine pubescence.

Oteiza mixtecana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
