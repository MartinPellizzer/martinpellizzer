Osmanthus yunnanensis

Osmanthus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the autumn season. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aromatic properties.

Osmanthus yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
