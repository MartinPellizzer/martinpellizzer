Oenothera uniflora

Oenothera uniflora, commonly known as evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Oenothera uniflora is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
