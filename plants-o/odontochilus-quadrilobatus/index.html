Odontochilus Quadrilobatus

Odontochilus quadrilobatus is a rare and fascinating orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique floral structure and striking appearance, this plant has captured the interest of botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and conservation status of Odontochilus quadrilobatus.
