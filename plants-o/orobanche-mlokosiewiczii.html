Orobanche mlokosiewiczii

Orobanche mlokosiewiczii, commonly known as the Mlokosiewicz's broomrape, is a parasitic flowering plant that lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on host plants for nutrition.

The plant produces small, often inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale in color.
