Oncidium × brandtii

Oncidium × brandtii, commonly known as the Yellow Lady's Slipper, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from its parent species.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive pouch-like lip. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in varying environmental conditions.

Oncidium × brandtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow with proper care.
