Oxypetalum alpinum var. alpinum

Oxypetalum alpinum var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine oxypetalum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its ability to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Oxypetalum alpinum var. alpinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable climatic conditions.
