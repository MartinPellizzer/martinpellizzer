Olax hypoleuca var. hypoleuca

Olax hypoleuca var. hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered olax, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
