Orthophytum toscanoi

Orthophytum toscanoi, commonly known as the Toscana grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Orthophytum toscanoi is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive grasslands.
