Ornithogalum procerum

Ornithogalum procerum, commonly known as the slender star-of-Bethlem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum procerum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
