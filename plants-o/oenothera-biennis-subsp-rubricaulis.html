Oenothera biennis subsp. rubricaulis

Oenothera biennis subsp.

rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America. It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and large, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine.
