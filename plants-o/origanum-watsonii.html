Origanum watsonii

Origanum watsonii, commonly known as Watson's oregano, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Origanum watsonii is also a popular ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage.
