Olearia canescens subsp. canescens

Olearia canescens subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its silvery, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas. Olearia canescens subsp.

canescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
