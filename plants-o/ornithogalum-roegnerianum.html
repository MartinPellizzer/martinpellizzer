Ornithogalum roegnerianum

Ornithogalum roegnerianum, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, arranged in a cluster at the top of a slender stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
