Ornithogalum perpulchrum

Ornithogalum perpulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, leafless stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
