Oligomeris frutescens

Oligomeris frutescens, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its ability to produce vibrant, showy flowers in a variety of colors. The plant typically has velvety, dark green leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Oligomeris frutescens is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
