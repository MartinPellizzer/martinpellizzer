Osbeckia ornata

Osbeckia ornata, commonly known as the showy osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its showy, yellow flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Osbeckia ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
