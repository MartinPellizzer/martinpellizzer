Ochna cyanophylla

Ochna cyanophylla, commonly known as the blue-leaf ochna, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often accompanied by red or orange bracts. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Ochna cyanophylla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
