Oplismenus africanus var. simplex

Oplismenus africanus var. simplex, commonly known as African mountain grass, is a perennial herb native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
