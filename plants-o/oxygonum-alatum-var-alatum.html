Oxygonum alatum var. alatum

Oxygonum alatum var. alatum, commonly known as the winged spider plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, wing-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
