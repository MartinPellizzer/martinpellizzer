Origanum cilicicum

Origanum cilicicum, commonly known as Cyprus oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its essential oils, rich in compounds like carvacrol, contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Origanum cilicicum is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
