Ormosia nobilis

Ormosia nobilis, commonly known as the noble ormosia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.
