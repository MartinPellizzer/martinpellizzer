Oligloron zeylanica

Oligloron zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise, is a small evergreen shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble the true star anise. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark, waxy berries that contain aromatic seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a fragrant, slightly oily texture.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for its antispasmodic and carminative effects.
