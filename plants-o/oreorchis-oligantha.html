Oreorchis oligantha

Oreorchis oligantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

Oreorchis oligantha has a short rhizome and produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.
