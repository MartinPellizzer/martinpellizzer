Oncidium baueri

Oncidium baueri, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium baueri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
