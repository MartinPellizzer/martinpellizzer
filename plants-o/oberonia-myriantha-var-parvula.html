Oberonia myriantha var. parvula

Oberonia myriantha var. parvula, commonly known as the small-leafed Oberonia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic or lithophytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features compact, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It is characterized by its short, erect inflorescence and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
