Oberonia sinuosa

Oberonia sinuosa, commonly known as the Sinuous Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and green. Its flowers are characterized by their unique, sinuous shape, which gives the species its common name.

Oberonia sinuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
