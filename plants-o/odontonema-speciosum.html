Odontonema speciosum

Odontonema speciosum, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Odontonema speciosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
