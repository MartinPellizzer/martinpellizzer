Oxygonum dregeanum var. lobophyllum

Oxygonum dregeanum var. lobophyllum, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-white, fuzzy foliage that provides a striking contrast to its dark green stems. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy fruits that are edible for birds and small mammals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to coastal and Mediterranean climates.
