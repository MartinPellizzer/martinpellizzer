Onopordum × arenesi

Onopordum × arenesi, commonly known as the hybrid Spanish thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Onopordum × arenesi is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
