Odontoglossum brevifolium var. weberbauerianum

Odontoglossum brevifolium var. weberbauerianum, commonly known as the Weberbauer's short-leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant features compact, rhizomatous growth with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns of purple and green.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
