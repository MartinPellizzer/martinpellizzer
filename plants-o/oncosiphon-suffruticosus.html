Oncosiphon suffruticosus

Oncosiphon suffruticosus, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and attractive blooms.

Oncosiphon suffruticosus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
