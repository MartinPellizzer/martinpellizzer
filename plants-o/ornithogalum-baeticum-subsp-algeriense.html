Ornithogalum baeticum subsp. algeriense

Ornithogalum baeticum subsp.

algeriense, commonly known as Algerian star-of-Bethlem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and strap-like leaves.

The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
