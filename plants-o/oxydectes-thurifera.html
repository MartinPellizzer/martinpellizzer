Oxydectes thurifera

Oxydectes thurifera, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that emit a sweet, intoxicating aroma.
