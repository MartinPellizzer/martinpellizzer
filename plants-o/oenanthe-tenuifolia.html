Oenanthe tenuifolia

Oenanthe tenuifolia, commonly known as slender water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as stream banks and marshy areas. The plant features slender, erect stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in the summer months.

Oenanthe tenuifolia is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
