Ononis aragonensis var. reuteri

Ononis aragonensis var. reuteri, commonly known as the Aragon bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in open habitats such as slopes and coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the legume family, Fabaceae.
