Ozothamnus Cordatus

Blue everlasting, scientifically known as Ozothamnus cordatus, is a hardy perennial plant native to Australia. It is prized for its striking blue-gray foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This versatile plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable. Whether used in gardens or as a wildflower, Blue everlasting adds a unique and lasting beauty to any setting.
