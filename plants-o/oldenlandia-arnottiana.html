Oldenlandia arnottiana

Oldenlandia arnottiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf or the silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
