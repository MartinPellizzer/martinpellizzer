Ornithogalum Uitenhagense

Ornithogalum uitenhagense is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its elegant, star-shaped flowers. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands. This species is popular among gardeners for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements. Its blooms typically appear in spring and can last for several weeks. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its toxic properties.
