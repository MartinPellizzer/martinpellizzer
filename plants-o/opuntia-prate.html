Opuntia prate

Opuntia prate, commonly known as the prairie prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to North America.

It is characterized by its flat, spineless pads and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in prairies and disturbed soils. Its pads are edible and can be used in various culinary applications.

Opuntia prate plays an important ecological role by providing food and shelter for native wildlife.
