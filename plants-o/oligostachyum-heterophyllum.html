Oligostachyum heterophyllum

Oligostachyum heterophyllum, commonly known as the broadleaf bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles.

Oligostachyum heterophyllum is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to different environmental conditions.
