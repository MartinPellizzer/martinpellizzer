Oncidium brevilabrum

Oncidium brevilabrum, commonly known as the short-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its flowers typically have three sepals and three petals, with the lip being short and distinctive.

Oncidium brevilabrum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
