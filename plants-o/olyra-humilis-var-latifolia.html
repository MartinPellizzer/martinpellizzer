Olyra humilis var. latifolia

Olyra humilis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved olyra, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers and broad, elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
