Oxalis eckloniana var. sonderi

Oxalis eckloniana var. sonderi, commonly known as the Sonder's wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with reddish veins and produces clusters of small, yellow, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage and flowers are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Oxalis eckloniana var. sonderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
