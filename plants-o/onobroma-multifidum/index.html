Onobroma Multifidum

Onobroma multifidum is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers, it is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. This species thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats. Its distinctive leaves, which are deeply lobed and sometimes feather-like, add to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, Onobroma multifidum is relatively rare and requires specific conditions to flourish.
