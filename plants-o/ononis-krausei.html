Ononis krausei

Ononis krausei, commonly known as Krause's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features hairy, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry slopes.

Ononis krausei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy environments.
