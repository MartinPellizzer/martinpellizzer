Olearia conocephala

Olearia conocephala, commonly known as the mountain dandelion, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in coastal and mountainous regions, often growing in well-drained soils.

Olearia conocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native insect populations.
