Ornithopus sativus subvar. monarthrus

Ornithopus sativus subvar. monarthrus, commonly known as the slender rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, branching stems that bear small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant has pinnately divided leaves with a hairy surface, and its seeds are small and elongated. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
