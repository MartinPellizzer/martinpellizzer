Oleandra geniculata

Oleandra geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag oleander, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Oleandra geniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
