Orbea cooperi

Orbea cooperi, commonly known as the cooper's orbea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, often blue-green leaves and clusters of tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Orbea cooperi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
