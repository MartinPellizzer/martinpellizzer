Ochna congesta

Ochna congesta, commonly known as the African starburst tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble starbursts. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy coating. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Ochna congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
