Oreopanax robustus

Oreopanax robustus, commonly known as the robust mountain arum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are often surrounded by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
