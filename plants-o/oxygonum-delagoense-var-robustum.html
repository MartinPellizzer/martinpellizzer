Oxygonum delagoense var. robustum

Oxygonum delagoense var. robustum, commonly known as the robust oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
