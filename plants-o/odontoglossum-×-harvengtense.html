Odontoglossum × harvengtense

Odontoglossum × harvengtense, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchid species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant produces showy, colorful flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. It has strap-like leaves that are typically broad and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
