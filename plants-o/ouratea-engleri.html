Ouratea engleri

Ouratea engleri, commonly known as the Engler's ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
