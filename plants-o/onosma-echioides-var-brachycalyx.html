Onosma echioides var. brachycalyx

Onosma echioides var. brachycalyx, commonly known as the narrow-calyx horsegram, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its seeds are enclosed in narrow, tubular pods, which are characteristic of the species.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
