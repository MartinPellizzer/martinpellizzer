Oncidium desertorum var. alborubrum

Oncidium desertorum var. alborubrum, commonly known as the Reddish Oncidium, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting orchid flowers that range from pink to red in color. The plant produces showy, spurred petals and a distinctive, three-lobed lip, typical of the Oncidium genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
