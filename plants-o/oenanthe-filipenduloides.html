Oenanthe filipenduloides

Oenanthe filipenduloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and damp meadows. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped.
