Osmanthus longispermus

Osmanthus longispermus, commonly known as the sweet osmanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, small yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its aromatic flowers used in teas and traditional Chinese medicine.

Osmanthus longispermus thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
