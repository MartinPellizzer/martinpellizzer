Olea laperrinei

Olea laperrinei, commonly known as the Madagascar olive, is a small evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family, which includes other olive species. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, fleshy fruits.

Olea laperrinei is adapted to the dry, arid conditions of its native habitat, making it a resilient and unique member of the olive family.
