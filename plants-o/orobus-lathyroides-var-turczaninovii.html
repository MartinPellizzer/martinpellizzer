Orobus lathyroides var. turczaninovii

Orobus lathyroides var. turczaninovii, commonly known as the Turczaninov's orobus, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to drought conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying shades of pink or purple.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
