Oxygonum alatum

Oxygonum alatum, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged leaves and pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are high in protein and essential nutrients. Its botanical features include compound leaves with leaflets arranged in a pinnate pattern.

Oxygonum alatum thrives in warm, humid climates and is valued for both its agricultural and ornamental qualities.
