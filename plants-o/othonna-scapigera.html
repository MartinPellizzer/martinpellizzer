Othonna scapigera

Othonna scapigera, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Othonna scapigera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
