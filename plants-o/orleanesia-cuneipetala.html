Orleanesia cuneipetala

Orleanesia cuneipetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive petal structure that aids in pollination by specific insects.

Orleanesia cuneipetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
