Opuntia backebergii

Opuntia backebergii, commonly known as the Backeberg prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in spines and are often ribbed. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Opuntia backebergii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
