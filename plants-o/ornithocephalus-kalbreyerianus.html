Ornithocephalus kalbreyerianus

Ornithocephalus kalbreyerianus, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb structure with elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are showy, with a unique coloration pattern that includes shades of purple and white, and they bloom in late summer.
