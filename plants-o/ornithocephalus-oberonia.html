Ornithocephalus oberonia

Ornithocephalus oberonia, commonly known as the Oberonia orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often hairy growth habit. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, yellow, or orange, depending on the species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
