Ocotea cymosa

Ocotea cymosa, commonly known as the fragrant ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Ocotea cymosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
