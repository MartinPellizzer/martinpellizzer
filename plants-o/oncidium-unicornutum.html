Oncidium unicornutum

Oncidium unicornutum, commonly known as the unicorn orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flower spikes that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium unicornutum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
