Onopordum × humile var. turolense

Onopordum × humile var. turolense, commonly known as the Turolense thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in local flora and a subject of ecological study.
