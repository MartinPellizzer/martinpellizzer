Ononis reclinata proles mollis

Ononis reclinata proles mollis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, prostrate stems covered in fine, soft hairs, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along paths.
