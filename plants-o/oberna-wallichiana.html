Oberna wallichiana

Oberna wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers and slender, woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are small, ovate, and often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Oberna wallichiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
