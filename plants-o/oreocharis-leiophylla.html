Oreocharis leiophylla

Oreocharis leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaf oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that often display a range of colors. The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is appreciated for its aesthetic value in horticulture.
