Oxalis bridgesii

Oxalis bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridgestone wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive appearance.

Oxalis bridgesii is also valued for its ability to thrive in rocky or dry environments.
