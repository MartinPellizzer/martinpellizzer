Olea subrotunda

Olea subrotunda, commonly known as the rounded olive, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often slightly hairy. It produces small yellow flowers followed by small, spherical fruits.

Olea subrotunda is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
