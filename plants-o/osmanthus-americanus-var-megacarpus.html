Osmanthus americanus var. megacarpus

Osmanthus americanus var. megacarpus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn, producing a distinctive sweet scent. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are dark green and elliptical in shape. Its fruits are small, woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the use of its flowers in teas and culinary applications.
