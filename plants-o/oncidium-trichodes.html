Oncidium trichodes

Oncidium trichodes, commonly known as the Orchid with Trichodes, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or red markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in seasonal environments. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat.

Oncidium trichodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
