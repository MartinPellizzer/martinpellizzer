Onobrychis elegans

Onobrychis elegans, commonly known as slender burr clover, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, burr-like seed pods that aid in seed dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Onobrychis elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
