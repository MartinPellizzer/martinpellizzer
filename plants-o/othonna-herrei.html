Othonna herrei

Othonna herrei, commonly known as the Cape foveolata, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant features dense, fleshy foliage that stores water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically borne in compact clusters and bloom in the spring.

Othonna herrei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
