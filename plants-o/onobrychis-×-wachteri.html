Onobrychis × wachteri

Onobrychis × wachteri, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable forage crop. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and agriculturally significant.
