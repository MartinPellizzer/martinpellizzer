Oxalis breviscapa

Oxalis breviscapa, commonly known as the broad-capselle oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the flowers are usually yellow or orange with five petals.

Oxalis breviscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
