Olearia fulgens

Olearia fulgens, commonly known as the golden tree daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

Olearia fulgens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
