Odontites longiflorus subsp. lateritius

Odontites longiflorus subsp.

lateritius, commonly known as the laterite odontite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves.

The plant produces delicate, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or lateritic soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
