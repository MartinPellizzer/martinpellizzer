Onobrychis psoraleifolia

Onobrychis psoraleifolia, commonly known as the yellow burr medic, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces hairy, triangular pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Onobrychis psoraleifolia is valued for its forage qualities and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
