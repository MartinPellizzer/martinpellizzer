Oreopanax compactus

Oreopanax compactus, commonly known as the compact umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded canopy of dark green, leathery leaves that give it a compact and bushy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by clustered, orange-yellow fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Oreopanax compactus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
