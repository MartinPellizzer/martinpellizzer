Olearia gunniana var. flavescens

Olearia gunniana var. flavescens, commonly known as the golden flax-leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
