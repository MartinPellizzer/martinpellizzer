Ochyrorchis Gilbertii

Ochyrorchis gilbertii is a rare and enigmatic orchid native to the mountainous regions of New Guinea. This delicate flowering plant is known for its striking violet and white petals that resemble a ghostly apparition. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in dense, shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical treasure from threats like deforestation and climate change.
