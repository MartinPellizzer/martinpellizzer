Orobus sessilifolius var. ovalifolius

Orobus sessilifolius var. ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaf milkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The leaves are oval-shaped, sessile, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically pink to purple in color, attracting various pollinators.

This plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
