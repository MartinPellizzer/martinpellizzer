Oliganthes milleri

Oliganthes milleri, commonly known as the Miller's oliganthes, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

Oliganthes milleri is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems that help it access groundwater.
