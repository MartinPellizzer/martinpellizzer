Oncosporum granulatum

Oncosporum granulatum, commonly known as the granular oncosporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, granular texture on the upper surface. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Oncosporum granulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various insects and wildlife.
