Osbeckia perangusta

Osbeckia perangusta, commonly known as the narrowleaf osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, temperate environments. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Osbeckia perangusta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
