Olax glabriflora

Olax glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered olax, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are borne in clusters at the ends of branches.

Olax glabriflora is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
