Oxalis linearis

Oxalis linearis, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern and produces small, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.

Oxalis linearis is also valued for its ability to add a touch of greenery to gardens and indoor spaces.
