Obetia madagascariensis

Obetia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of the island. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the rich, organic soil.

Obetia madagascariensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
