Osbeckia glabrata

Osbeckia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth osbeckia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features upright, branched stems covered with smooth, glabrous foliage. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in clusters.

Osbeckia glabrata is often found in wetland ecosystems and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects.
