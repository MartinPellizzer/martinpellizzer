Opuntia palmadora

Opuntia palmadora, commonly known as the Mexican fire cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems that are covered in small, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by bright red fruits. Opuntia palmadora is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its thick stems.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
