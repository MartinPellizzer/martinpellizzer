Oenanthe nodiflora

Oenanthe nodiflora, commonly known as the clustered water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland edges. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, clustered white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Oenanthe nodiflora is often found in wetlands and is considered a characteristic species of riparian habitats.
