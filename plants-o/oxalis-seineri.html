Oxalis seineri

Oxalis seineri, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Oxalidaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, bead-like seed pods that contain several seeds. Oxalis seineri thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
