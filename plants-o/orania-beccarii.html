Orania beccarii

Orania beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in the humid and shaded understory. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it often forms symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
