Oreopanax superoerstedianus

Oreopanax superoerstedianus, commonly known as the highland arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with white spadices surrounded by brightly colored bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
