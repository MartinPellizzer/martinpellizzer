Orchis champagneuxii var. grandis

Orchis champagneuxii var. grandis, commonly known as the Grand Orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a mix of purple, white, and yellow hues. The plant is native to specific regions in Europe, where it thrives in moist, calcareous soils. Its growth habit includes a tall, upright stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves.
