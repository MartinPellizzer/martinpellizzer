Ononis subspicata var. grandiflora

Ononis subspicata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal areas and dry habitats. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces showy, white or pale pink flowers in early spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize sandy soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
