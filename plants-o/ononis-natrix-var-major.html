Ononis natrix var. major

Ononis natrix var. major, commonly known as the greater sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, usually from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils.
