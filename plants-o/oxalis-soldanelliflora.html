Oxalis soldanelliflora

Oxalis soldanelliflora, commonly known as the mountain oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, clover-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal.

Oxalis soldanelliflora is also valued for its ability to thrive in cooler climates and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
