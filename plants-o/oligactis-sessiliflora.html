Oligactis sessiliflora

Oligactis sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered oligactis, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and dark green coloration. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, with tubular corollas that open into five lobes.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
