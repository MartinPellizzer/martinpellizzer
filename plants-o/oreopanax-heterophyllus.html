Oreopanax heterophyllus

Oreopanax heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable-leaf sarsaparilla, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which vary significantly along its stem, a feature that gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
