Oncidium astranthum

Oncidium astranthum, commonly known as the star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium astranthum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
