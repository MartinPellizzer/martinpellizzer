Olearia thomsonii

Olearia thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's tree-ferns, is a species of tree-fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a unique reproductive system, producing both male and female spores on the same plant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Olearia thomsonii plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's native ecosystems.
