Olearia rufa

Olearia rufa, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Olearia rufa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
