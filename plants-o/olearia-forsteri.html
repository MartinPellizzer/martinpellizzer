Olearia forsteri

Olearia forsteri, commonly known as the New Zealand iceplant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features silver-gray foliage with small, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss in its coastal habitat. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in clusters and blooming in spring.

Olearia forsteri is well adapted to harsh environments, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils along coastal areas.
