Oenothera biennis subsp. suaveolens

Oenothera biennis subsp.

suaveolens, commonly known as showy evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America. It features showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening and close by dawn.

The plant has hairy, branching stems and heart-shaped, toothed leaves. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This subspecies is often found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.
