Oenothera candicans

Oenothera candicans, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Oenothera candicans is often found in open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.
