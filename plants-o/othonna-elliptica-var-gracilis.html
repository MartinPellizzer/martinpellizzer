Othonna elliptica var. gracilis

Othonna elliptica var. gracilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
