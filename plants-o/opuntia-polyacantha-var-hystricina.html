Opuntia polyacantha var. hystricina

Opuntia polyacantha var. hystricina, commonly known as the red spine cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its spines are long and reddish, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This cactus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique look and hardy nature.
