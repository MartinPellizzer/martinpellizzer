Odontostemma littledalei

Odontostemma littledalei, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature a unique coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or along riverbanks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
