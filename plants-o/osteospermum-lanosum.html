Osteospermum lanosum

Osteospermum lanosum, commonly known as the Woolly Osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, woolly stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
