Orchis × alfredi-fuchsii

Orchis × alfredi-fuchsii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Orchis mascula and Orchis morio.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces a single, elongated flower spike with several flowers arranged along its length. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, typical of many orchid species.

This hybrid is often found in meadows and woodland edges, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
