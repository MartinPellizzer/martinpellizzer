Oncidium obovatum

Oncidium obovatum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Oncidium obovatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

It prefers indirect light and well-draining potting mix to prevent root rot.
