Ornithopus glaucus

Ornithopus glaucus, commonly known as silver flax or blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy blue-violet flowers.
