Ossaea laxivenula var. grandifolia

Ossaea laxivenula var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered ossaea, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale blue to violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Poaceae family.
