Oncidium remotiflorum

Oncidium remotiflorum, commonly known as the remote-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces racemes of small, showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium remotiflorum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
