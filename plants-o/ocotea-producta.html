Ocotea producta

Ocotea producta, commonly known as the white ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.

The tree can reach heights of up to 20 meters and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
