Olfersia acrocarpa

Olfersia acrocarpa, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble torches, with a distinctive golden hue and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy spike with multiple flowers arranged in a spiral.

Olfersia acrocarpa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.
