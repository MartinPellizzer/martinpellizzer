Oeceoclades rauhii

Oeceoclades rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting a few leaves and a single flower spike.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
