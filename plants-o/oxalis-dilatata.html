Oxalis dilatata

Oxalis dilatata, commonly known as the spurred oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside and produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening for their vibrant colors and unique appearance.

Oxalis dilatata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
