Ochyrorchis macrantha

Ochyrorchis macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are relatively large for an orchid, with prominent sepals and petals.

Ochyrorchis macrantha is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
