Ortegia

Ortegia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a leathery texture.

Ortegia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils.
