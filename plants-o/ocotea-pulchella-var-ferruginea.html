Ocotea pulchella var. ferruginea

Ocotea pulchella var. ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in subtropical and tropical gardens.
