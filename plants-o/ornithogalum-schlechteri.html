Ornithogalum schlechteri

Ornithogalum schlechteri, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
