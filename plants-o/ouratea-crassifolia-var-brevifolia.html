Ouratea crassifolia var. brevifolia

Ouratea crassifolia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved ouratea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of forests. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are usually less than 5 cm in length. Its flowers are pale green to white, with a distinctive, elongated calyx.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
