Oxalis dreyerae

Oxalis dreyerae, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple coloration, often adorned with white veins. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal blooming.
