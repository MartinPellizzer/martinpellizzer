Oxydectes exuberans

Oxydectes exuberans, commonly known as the vibrant flameleaf plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that display a vivid red or orange hue, especially under bright light. Its showy, tubular flowers, which range from pink to purple, bloom in clusters and attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
