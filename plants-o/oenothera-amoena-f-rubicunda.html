Oenothera amoena f. rubicunda

Oenothera amoena f. rubicunda, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange-yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Oenothera amoena f. rubicunda is often found in open areas such as prairies and disturbed sites.
