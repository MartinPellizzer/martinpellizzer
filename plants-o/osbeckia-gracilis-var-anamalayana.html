Osbeckia gracilis var. anamalayana

Osbeckia gracilis var. anamalayana, commonly known as the slender osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on the upper parts of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Osbeckia gracilis var. anamalayana is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate foliage and flowering display.
