Othonna cyclophylla

Othonna cyclophylla, commonly known as the Cape wheelcactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, segmented stems that resemble a wheel, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
