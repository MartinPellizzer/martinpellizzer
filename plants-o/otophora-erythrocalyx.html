Otophora erythrocalyx

Otophora erythrocalyx, commonly known as the red-calyx orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually borne on erect stems and feature a unique combination of red and yellow hues.

Otophora erythrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
