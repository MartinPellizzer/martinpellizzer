Odontosoria chinensis

Odontosoria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese tooth fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
