Oncidium ehrenbergii

Oncidium ehrenbergii, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, yellow flowers with a distinctive orange lip, often resembling butterflies in flight. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium ehrenbergii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
