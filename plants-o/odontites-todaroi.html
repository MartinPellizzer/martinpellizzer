Odontites todaroi

Odontites todaroi, commonly known as the alpine toothplant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped, hairy leaves. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Odontites todaroi is adapted to cold climates and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
