Osmanthus americanus subsp. megacarpus

Osmanthus americanus subsp.

megacarpus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a shrub or small tree native to China. It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that are typically orange to red in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its aromatic flowers and edible fruits.
