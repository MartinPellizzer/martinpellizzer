Othonna petiolaris

Othonna petiolaris, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Othonna, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
