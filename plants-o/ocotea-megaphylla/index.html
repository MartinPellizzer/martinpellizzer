Ocotea Megaphylla

Ocotea megaphylla is a tropical evergreen tree native to the wet forests of Central and South America. It is known for its aromatic leaves and distinctive dark bark that shimmers in the rainforest. This species plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity and maintaining forest ecosystems. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Despite its ecological importance, Ocotea megaphylla faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
