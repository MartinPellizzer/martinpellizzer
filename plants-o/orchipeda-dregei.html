Orchipeda dregei

Orchipeda dregei, commonly known as the Red-stemmed Orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that are often reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers with white markings, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Orchipeda dregei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
