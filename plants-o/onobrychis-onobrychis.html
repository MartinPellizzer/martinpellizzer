Onobrychis onobrychis

Onobrychis onobrychis, commonly known as goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides. It has a deep taproot system that allows it to thrive in poor soil conditions.

The plant is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
