Olearia muricata

Olearia muricata, commonly known as the rough leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its woolly, silver-green foliage. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Olearia muricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
