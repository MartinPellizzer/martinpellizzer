Ononis alba var. tuna

Ononis alba var. tuna, commonly known as white sand everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
