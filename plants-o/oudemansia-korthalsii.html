Oudemansia korthalsii

Oudemansia korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals' orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant possesses a complex root system that helps it thrive in the humid, shaded environments of its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Oudemansia korthalsii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its rarity and unique appearance.
