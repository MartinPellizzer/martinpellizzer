Ornithocephalus dasyrhizus

Ornithocephalus dasyrhizus, commonly known as the hairy root orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy roots and compact, often densely packed growth habit. The plant typically features small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a subject of interest in botanical research and conservation efforts.
