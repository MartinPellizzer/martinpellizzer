Oplismenus psilostachys

Oplismenus psilostachys, commonly known as narrowleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale purple to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. Its fibrous root system helps stabilize soil, making it ecologically important in wetland ecosystems.

Oplismenus psilostachys is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging wet conditions.
