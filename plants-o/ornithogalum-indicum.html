Ornithogalum indicum

Ornithogalum indicum, commonly known as the Indian star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Asia.

It features erect, slender stems that bear clusters of white, star-shaped flowers resembling a cluster of stars. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum indicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
