Osteospermum volkensii

Osteospermum volkensii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with silver-green foliage that adds visual interest. Its flowers bloom in summer and are often used in gardens for their ornamental value.

Osteospermum volkensii is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
