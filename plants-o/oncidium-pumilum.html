Oncidium pumilum

Oncidium pumilum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot. Oncidium pumilum is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its appeal among orchid enthusiasts.
