Onosma iranica

Onosma iranica, commonly known as Iranian hedge mustard, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a rough texture. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and limited water availability.

Onosma iranica plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
