Oberonia formosana f. viridiflora

Oberonia formosana f. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered form of the Formosan Oberonia, is a rare orchid species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are usually elliptical and dark green, with a glossy surface.

This form is distinguished by its vibrant, almost fluorescent green floral bracts, which set it apart from other variants of Oberonia formosana.
