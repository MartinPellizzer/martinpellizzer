Otanema latifolia var. retusa

Otanema latifolia var. retusa, commonly known as the broadleaf flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It features striking, tubular red bracts that surround small, yellow flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
