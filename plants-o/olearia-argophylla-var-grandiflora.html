Olearia argophylla var. grandiflora

Olearia argophylla var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
