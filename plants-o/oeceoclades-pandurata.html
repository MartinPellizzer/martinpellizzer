Oeceoclades pandurata

Oeceoclades pandurata, commonly known as the pandurata orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a rosette of linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowering stem produces a cluster of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive pandurata-like shape.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
