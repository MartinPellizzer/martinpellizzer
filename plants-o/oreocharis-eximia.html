Oreocharis eximia

Oreocharis eximia, commonly known as the Yunnan lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of white or pale pink petals that are adorned with intricate patterns. Its leaves are long and grass-like, forming a rosette at the base of the plant.

Oreocharis eximia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
