Odollamia manghas

Odollamia manghas, commonly known as the Indian milkweed, is a shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, oval-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. Odollamia manghas is also known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for various pollinators.
