Oncidium phyllochilum

Oncidium phyllochilum, commonly known as the dark leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Oncidium phyllochilum requires well-draining potting mix and moderate light to thrive.
