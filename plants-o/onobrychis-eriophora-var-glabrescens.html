Onobrychis eriophora var. glabrescens

Onobrychis eriophora var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth everlasting pea, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with stipules and is characterized by its glabrous (hairless) appearance, especially on the stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
