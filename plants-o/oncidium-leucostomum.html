Oncidium leucostomum

Oncidium leucostomum, commonly known as the white-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that typically feature white petals with green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant blossoms that bloom sequentially over several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium leucostomum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
