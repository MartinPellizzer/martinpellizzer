Ononis filicaulis

Ononis filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Ononis filicaulis is a low-growing perennial that thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
