Oxalis orbiculata

Oxalis orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaf wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or yellowish pattern, and small, five-petaled flowers that range from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Oxalis orbiculata is also valued for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a resilient addition to gardens.
