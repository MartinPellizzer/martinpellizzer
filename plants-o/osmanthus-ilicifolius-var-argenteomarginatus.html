Osmanthus ilicifolius var. argenteomarginatus

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. argenteomarginatus, commonly known as silver-margin osmanthus, is a variant of the osmanthus plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers and dark green leaves with distinctive silver or white margins. The plant is evergreen, typically growing as a shrub or small tree, and thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade. Its ornamental value comes from its aromatic foliage and showy blooms, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.

The variety is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of fragrant oils.
