Oenothera hazeliae var. parviflora

Oenothera hazeliae var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant in the Onagraceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
