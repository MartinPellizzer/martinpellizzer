Ochna staudtii

Ochna staudtii, commonly known as the Staudt's ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochna staudtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
