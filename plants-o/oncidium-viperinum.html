Oncidium viperinum

Oncidium viperinum, commonly known as the viper orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that resemble small, elongated spikes. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in two rows along the stem. Oncidium viperinum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
