Ormosia acuta

Ormosia acuta, commonly known as the sharp-pointed ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are sometimes used in traditional cuisines. Ormosia acuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitats.
