Oxytenanthera thwaitesii var. densa

Oxytenanthera thwaitesii var. densa, commonly known as the dense thwaites grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
