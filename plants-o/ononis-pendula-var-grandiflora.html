Ononis pendula var. grandiflora

Ononis pendula var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually succulent and arranged in a basal rosette.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
