Ornithogalum ciliatifolium

Ornithogalum ciliatifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves with finely toothed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
