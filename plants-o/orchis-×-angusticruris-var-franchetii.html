Orchis × angusticruris var. franchetii

Orchis × angusticruris var. franchetii, commonly known as the Franchet's narrow-leg orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and striking, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, while the flowers are borne on tall, erect spikes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
