Ottelia acuminata var. lunanensis

Ottelia acuminata var. lunanensis, commonly known as the Lunan waterplant, is a aquatic plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant has slender, branching stems that often float on the water surface. Its leaves are small, elliptical, and usually have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.
