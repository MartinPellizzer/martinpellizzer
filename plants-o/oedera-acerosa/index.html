Oedera Acerosa

Oedera acerosa is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its unique, spiny leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red and purple. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain. Due to its limited habitat and fragile ecosystem, Oedera acerosa is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
