Othonna natalensis

Othonna natalensis, commonly known as Natal Othonna, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in color. It produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in summer.

Othonna natalensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
