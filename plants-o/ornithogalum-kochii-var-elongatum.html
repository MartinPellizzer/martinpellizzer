Ornithogalum kochii var. elongatum

Ornithogalum kochii var. elongatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its elongated stems, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum kochii var. elongatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
