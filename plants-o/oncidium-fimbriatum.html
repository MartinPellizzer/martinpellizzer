Oncidium fimbriatum

Oncidium fimbriatum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful, and long-lasting flower spikes that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. Oncidium fimbriatum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
