Oberonia integerrima

Oberonia integerrima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and usually yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive spurred lip.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
