Onopordum libanoticum

Onopordum libanoticum, commonly known as the Lebanon thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, spiny stems and dark green, heart-shaped leaves with rough textures. The plant produces yellow flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an adaptable and resilient species in its native habitat.
