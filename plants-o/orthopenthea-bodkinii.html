Orthopenthea bodkinii

Orthopenthea bodkinii, commonly known as the Bodkin's orthopenthea, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pentagonal-shaped leaves that display a vibrant mix of green and purple hues. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its growth habit is primarily shrub-like, with a compact form and dense foliage.
