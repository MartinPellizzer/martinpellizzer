Oligostachyum wuyishanicum

Oligostachyum wuyishanicum, commonly known as the Wuyi mountain bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China, specifically the Wuyi Mountains region, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to white flowers.

Oligostachyum wuyishanicum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in subtropical gardens.
