Oryza barthii

Oryza barthii, commonly known as Barth's rice, is a species of grass native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is closely related to cultivated rice species. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 meter and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in a thin hull and are often used in traditional African cuisines.

Oryza barthii is also studied for its potential in agricultural research and genetic diversity.
