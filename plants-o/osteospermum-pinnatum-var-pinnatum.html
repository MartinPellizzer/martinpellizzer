Osteospermum pinnatum var. pinnatum

Osteospermum pinnatum var. pinnatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy leaves that are often arranged in a pinnate pattern. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscaping.

The botanical features of this species include its unique seed heads, which are composed of tightly packed, woody follicles.
