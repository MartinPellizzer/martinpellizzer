Odontoglossum × staurastrum

Odontoglossum × staurastrum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Odontoglossum orchids and staurastrum species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy floral structures. The plant typically produces vibrant, colorful flowers with a unique combination of traits inherited from its parent species. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and well-draining soil.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex hybrid genetics and ornamental value in horticulture.
