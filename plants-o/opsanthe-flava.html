Opsanthe flava

Opsanthe flava, commonly known as the yellow opsanthe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its slender stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
