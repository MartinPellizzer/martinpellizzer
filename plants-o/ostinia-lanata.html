Ostinia lanata

Ostinia lanata, commonly known as the woolly ostinia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Ostinia lanata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
