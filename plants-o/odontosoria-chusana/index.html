Odontosoria Chusana

Chusan brake, scientifically known as Odontosoria chusana, is a rare and delicate fern native to eastern Asia. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its unique fronds display a striking pattern of serrated edges and intricate vein structures. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable plant for future generations.
