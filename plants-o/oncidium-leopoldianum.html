Oncidium leopoldianum

Oncidium leopoldianum, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium leopoldianum is popular among orchid growers for its long-lasting blooms and distinctive lemony fragrance.
