Orthanthella aucheri

Orthanthella aucheri, commonly known as the Armenian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and erect.

Orthanthella aucheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
