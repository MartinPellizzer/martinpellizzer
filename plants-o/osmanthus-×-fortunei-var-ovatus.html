Osmanthus × fortunei var. ovatus

Osmanthus × fortunei var. ovatus, commonly known as the sweet osmanthus or golden osmanthus, is a flowering shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, small yellow to golden orange flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, round, dark brown fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.
