Oncidium × murrellianum

Oncidium × murrellianum, commonly known as the Murrell's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium × murrellianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
