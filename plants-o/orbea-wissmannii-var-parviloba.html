Orbea wissmannii var. parviloba

Orbea wissmannii var. parviloba, commonly known as the dwarf orbea, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
