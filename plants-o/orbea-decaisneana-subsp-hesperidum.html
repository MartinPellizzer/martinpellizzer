Orbea decaisneana subsp. hesperidum

Orbea decaisneana subsp.

hesperidum, commonly known as the Cape horned orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact, evergreen shrub form with narrow, leathery leaves.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific care to flourish.
