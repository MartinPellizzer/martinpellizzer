Oetosis isoetifolia

Oetosis isoetifolia, commonly known as the slender spike moss, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the family Isoetaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often spirally arranged around its stem. This plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and wetlands. Its root system helps stabilize sediment and improve water quality by filtering impurities.

Oetosis isoetifolia plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
