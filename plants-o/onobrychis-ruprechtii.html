Onobrychis ruprechtii

Onobrychis ruprechtii, commonly known as Ruprecht's vetch, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Onobrychis ruprechtii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
