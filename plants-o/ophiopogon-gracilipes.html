Ophiopogon gracilipes

Ophiopogon gracilipes, commonly known as the slender leaved spathiphyllum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features dense, grass-like foliage with narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically enclosed in a leaf-like bract, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ophiopogon gracilipes is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
