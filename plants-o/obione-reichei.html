Obione reichei

Obione reichei, commonly known as the rare desert orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pale pink or white flowers that bloom during the brief summer months. The plant has adapted to arid environments with a deep root system and reduced leaf surface to minimize water loss. Its growth is slow, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Obione reichei is considered endangered in many areas.
