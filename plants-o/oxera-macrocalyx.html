Oxera macrocalyx

Oxera macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered oxera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features a sprawling growth habit with woody stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Oxera macrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
