Olearia divaricata

Olearia divaricata, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, felt-like foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and temperate gardens.

Olearia divaricata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
