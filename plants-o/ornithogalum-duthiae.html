Ornithogalum duthiae

Ornithogalum duthiae, commonly known as the Duthia's star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale green, star-shaped flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. Its bulbs are used in traditional medicine and horticulture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
