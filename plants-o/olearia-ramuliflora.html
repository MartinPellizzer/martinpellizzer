Olearia ramuliflora

Olearia ramuliflora, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, which contribute to its unique texture and appearance.

Olearia ramuliflora is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
