Oncidium pulchellum

Oncidium pulchellum, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has parallel-veined, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium pulchellum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
