Odontites vernus subsp. vernus

Odontites vernus subsp.

vernus, commonly known as the early toothworts, is a small perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in a spike.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations to its habitat.
