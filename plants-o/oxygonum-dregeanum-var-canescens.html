Oxygonum dregeanum var. canescens

Oxygonum dregeanum var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late summer to early autumn. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance and resilience.
