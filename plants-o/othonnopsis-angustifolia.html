Othonnopsis angustifolia

Othonnopsis angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and the ability to store water in its fleshy stems.

Othonnopsis angustifolia is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
