Oenothera spiralis var. linearis

Oenothera spiralis var. linearis, commonly known as the linear evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses.

This variety is noted for its unique spiral growth pattern and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
