Ononis arvensis subsp. maritima

Ononis arvensis subsp.

maritima, commonly known as seaside hedge-parsley, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. It has a low-growing habit and produces small, round fruits that disperse by wind.

This subspecies is adapted to salt-laden winds and is an important component of coastal vegetation.
