Oreocereus neocelsianus var. villosissimus

Oreocereus neocelsianus var. villosissimus, commonly known as the Woolly Crowned Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems covered in dense, white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming at night. It is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

This cactus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique morphology.
