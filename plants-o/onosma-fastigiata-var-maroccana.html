Onosma fastigiata var. maroccana

Onosma fastigiata var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan upright onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Morocco.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and long blooming period.
