Octomeria lithophila

Octomeria lithophila, commonly known as the rock octomeris, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and fleshy, spiny leaves that help it store water. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops, adapting to arid and well-drained environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in collections of succulents.
