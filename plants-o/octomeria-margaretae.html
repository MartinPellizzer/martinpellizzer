Octomeria margaretae

Octomeria margaretae, commonly known as the Margaretae octomeria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Paraguay. The plant is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Octomeria margaretae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
