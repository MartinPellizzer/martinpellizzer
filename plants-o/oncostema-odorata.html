Oncostema odorata

Oncostema odorata, commonly known as the fragrant grass or sweet grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant is notable for its aromatic leaves, which emit a sweet, pleasant scent when crushed. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically arranged in panicles, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Oncostema odorata is often used in traditional medicine and for its ornamental value in gardens.
