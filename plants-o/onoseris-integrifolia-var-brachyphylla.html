Onoseris integrifolia var. brachyphylla

Onoseris integrifolia var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-leaved yellowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It features yellow ray florets and is often found in association with other native flora in its ecosystem.

Its short, broad leaves and distinctive floral structure make it a notable species in regional botanical studies.
