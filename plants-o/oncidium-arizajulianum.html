Oncidium arizajulianum

Oncidium arizajulianum, commonly known as the Arizajulian orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in a spike. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium arizajulianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
