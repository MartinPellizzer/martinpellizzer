Omphalogramma viola-grandis

Omphalogramma viola-grandis, commonly known as the large violet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers, which are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
