Orchis tephrosanthos

Orchis tephrosanthos, commonly known as the dark purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that range in color from purple to pale pink. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing a single flower per stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Orchis tephrosanthos is often found in woodland and meadow habitats, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
