Orobus tuberosus var. angustifolius

Orobus tuberosus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tuberous orobus, is a perennial herb native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
