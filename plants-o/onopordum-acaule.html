Onopordum acaule

Onopordum acaule, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and spiny leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Onopordum acaule is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
