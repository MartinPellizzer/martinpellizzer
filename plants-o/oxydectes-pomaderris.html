Oxydectes pomaderris

Oxydectes pomaderris, commonly known as the pink mallee, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to Western Australia.

Oxydectes pomaderris is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in dry conditions.
