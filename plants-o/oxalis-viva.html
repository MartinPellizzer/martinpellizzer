Oxalis viva

Oxalis viva, commonly known as the liveforever or liveforever plant, is a member of the Oxalidaceae family.

It is a low-growing, perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant produces bright yellow blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis viva is noted for its ability to survive in arid conditions, showcasing its resilience and adaptability.
