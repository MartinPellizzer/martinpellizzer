Onobrychis kemulariae

Onobrychis kemulariae, commonly known as the Kemularia burr clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces distinctive bur-like seed pods that contain several small, rounded seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
