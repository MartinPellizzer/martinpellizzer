Ononis columnae subsp. minutissima

Ononis columnae subsp.

minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf thimbleweed, is a small perennial herb native to the western United States. It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy and covered in small, white to pale pink flowers.

The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid environments and contribute to its ecological significance in native plant communities.
