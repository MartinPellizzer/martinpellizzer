Oenothera biformiflora var. cruciata

Oenothera biformiflora var. cruciata, commonly known as the cruciform evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.
