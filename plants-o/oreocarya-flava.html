Oreocarya flava

Oreocarya flava, commonly known as the yellow oreocarya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Oreocarya flava is a perennial species, known for its resilience in harsh alpine environments.
