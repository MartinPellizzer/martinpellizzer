Olearia erubescens

Olearia erubescens, commonly known as the pink frost.

It is a shrub native to New Zealand, characterized by its dense, silvery foliage and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is well adapted to coastal environments. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in windy and salty conditions.

Olearia erubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging climates.
