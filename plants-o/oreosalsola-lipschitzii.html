Oreosalsola lipschitzii

Oreosalsola lipschitzii, commonly known as the Ecuadorian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.
