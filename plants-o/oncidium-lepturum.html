Oncidium lepturum

Oncidium lepturum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and long, pendulous flower spikes. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive lip. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Oncidium lepturum is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
