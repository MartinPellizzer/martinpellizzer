Orthaea paniculata

Orthaea paniculata, commonly known as the panicled orthaea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, branched inflorescences. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, which are often adorned with bracts. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

Orthaea paniculata is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
