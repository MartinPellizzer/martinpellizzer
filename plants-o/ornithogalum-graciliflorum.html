Ornithogalum graciliflorum

Ornithogalum graciliflorum, commonly known as slender-flowered star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often mistaken for grasses or sedges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum graciliflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
