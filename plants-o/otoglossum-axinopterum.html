Otoglossum axinopterum

Otoglossum axinopterum, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive hooded lip that attracts pollinators.

Otoglossum axinopterum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.
