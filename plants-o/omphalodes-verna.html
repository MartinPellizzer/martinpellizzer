Omphalodes verna

Omphalodes verna, commonly known as the spring everlasting, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, branching stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its delicate appearance and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
