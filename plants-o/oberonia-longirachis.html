Oberonia longirachis

Oberonia longirachis, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a slender, wiry stem that can grow up to several meters in length, often with a few nodes along its length. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

The flowers are small and clustered, typically in a spike-like arrangement, with a unique structure that distinguishes it from other orchid species.
