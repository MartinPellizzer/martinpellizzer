Orthophytum buranhense

Orthophytum buranhense, commonly known as the Buranhene stone plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or grayish hue. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

Orthophytum buranhense is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
