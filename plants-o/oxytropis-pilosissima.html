Oxytropis pilosissima

Oxytropis pilosissima, commonly known as the silky locoweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as sagebrush steppe and grasslands. Oxytropis pilosissima is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
