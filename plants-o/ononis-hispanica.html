Ononis hispanica

Ononis hispanica, commonly known as Spanish sandparsley, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branched stems and small, often hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

Ononis hispanica is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
