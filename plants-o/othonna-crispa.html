Othonna crispa

Othonna crispa, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Othonna crispa is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
