Ocotea santamartae

Ocotea santamartae, commonly known as the Santa Marta ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Andes mountain range in Colombia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, cinnamon, and avocado. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed.

Ocotea santamartae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
