Oxymeris acutiflora

Oxymeris acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered oxymeris, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. Oxymeris acutiflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
