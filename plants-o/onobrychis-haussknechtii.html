Onobrychis haussknechtii

Onobrychis haussknechtii, commonly known as the Haussknecht's burclover, is a perennial legume native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Its seed pods are hairy and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
