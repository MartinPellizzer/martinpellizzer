Origanum barcense

Origanum barcense, commonly known as Barcense oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, dark green leaves and clusters of pale blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its essential oils, rich in compounds like thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong fragrance and medicinal properties.

Origanum barcense is often used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
