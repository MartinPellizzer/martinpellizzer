Oncidium sotoanum f. albiflorum

Oncidium sotoanum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the Whiteflower Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its striking white floral display.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that bloom in succession. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.
