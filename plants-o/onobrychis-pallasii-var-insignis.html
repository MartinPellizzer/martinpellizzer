Onobrychis pallasii var. insignis

Onobrychis pallasii var. insignis, commonly known as the Pallas's vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish to reddish pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
