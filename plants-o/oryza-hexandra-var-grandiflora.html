Oryza hexandra var. grandiflora

Oryza hexandra var. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant rice plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall stature, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, making it visually striking. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

This variety is primarily cultivated for ornamental purposes rather than grain production.
