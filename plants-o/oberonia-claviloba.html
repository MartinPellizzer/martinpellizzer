Oberonia claviloba

Oberonia claviloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
