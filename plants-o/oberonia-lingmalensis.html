Oberonia lingmalensis

Oberonia lingmalensis, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
