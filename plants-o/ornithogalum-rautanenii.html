Ornithogalum rautanenii

Ornithogalum rautanenii, commonly known as the silverleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The flowers typically bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
