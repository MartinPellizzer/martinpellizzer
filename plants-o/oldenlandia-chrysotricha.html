Oldenlandia chrysotricha

Oldenlandia chrysotricha, commonly known as golden thread, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are often accompanied by long, slender bracts. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually have a hairy texture.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
