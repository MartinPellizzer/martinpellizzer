Oenothera claviformis var. purpurascens

Oenothera claviformis var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purplestem evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
