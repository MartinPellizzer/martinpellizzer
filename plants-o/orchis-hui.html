Orchis hui

Orchis hui, commonly known as the Chinese twayblade, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale purple or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Orchis hui has a short flowering period, usually in late spring to early summer.

Its structure and growth habits make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
