Oncidium × mulus

Oncidium × mulus, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a range of vibrant floral colors, often including shades of yellow, orange, and red.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. Oncidium × mulus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit variable growth habits and flower characteristics depending on the parent species.
