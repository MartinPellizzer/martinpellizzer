Ocimum basilicum var. chamaeleonticum

Ocimum basilicum var. chamaeleonticum, commonly known as the Variegated Lemon Basil, is a hybrid basil variety that exhibits unique foliage patterns.

Its leaves are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and sometimes gold, creating an attractive visual display. This plant is a compact shrub that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of lavender or white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The leaves are often used in culinary applications, though they are less common than traditional basil varieties due to their milder flavor.
