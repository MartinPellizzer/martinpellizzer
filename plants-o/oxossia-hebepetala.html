Oxossia hebepetala

Oxossia hebepetala, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Oxossia hebepetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
