Ormosia villamilii

Ormosia villamilii, commonly known as the Philippine ormosia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often found in lowland to montane forests and is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
