Onobrychis iranensis

Onobrychis iranensis, commonly known as Iranian burr medic, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps it thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It is often used for soil stabilization and as a forage crop due to its hardy nature.

Its seeds are small and typically enclosed in a hairy, pod-like structure.
