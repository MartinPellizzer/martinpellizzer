Oberonia gracilipes

Oberonia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
