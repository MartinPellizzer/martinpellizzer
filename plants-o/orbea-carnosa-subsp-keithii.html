Orbea carnosa subsp. keithii

Orbea carnosa subsp.

keithii, commonly known as the Keith's orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments.

The plant produces striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other flowering plants.

Its succulent nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
