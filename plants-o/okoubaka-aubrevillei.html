Okoubaka aubrevillei

Okoubaka aubrevillei, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in hard, spiny pods that split open when mature.

Okoubaka aubrevillei is adapted to thrive in disturbed soils and can often be found in agricultural areas.
