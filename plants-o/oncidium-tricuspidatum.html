Oncidium tricuspidatum

Oncidium tricuspidatum, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, long-lasting flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or brown markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like, narrow, and often covered in a waxy coating.

Oncidium tricuspidatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
