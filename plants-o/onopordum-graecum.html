Onopordum graecum

Onopordum graecum, commonly known as Greek thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust stem with spiny leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by spiky, purple bracts. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its latex sap is milky and can cause skin irritation.
