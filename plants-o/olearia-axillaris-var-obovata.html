Olearia axillaris var. obovata

Olearia axillaris var. obovata, commonly known as the silver fern, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
