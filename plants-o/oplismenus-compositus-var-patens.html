Oplismenus compositus var. patens

Oplismenus compositus var. patens, commonly known as the spreading sensitiveplant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, curling when disturbed. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread make it a popular choice for ground cover in shaded garden settings.
