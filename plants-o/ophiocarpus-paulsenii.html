Ophiocarpus paulsenii

Ophiocarpus paulsenii, commonly known as the Paulsen's grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Ophiocarpus paulsenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
