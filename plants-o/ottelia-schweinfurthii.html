Ottelia schweinfurthii

Ottelia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the swamp buttercup, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, marshes, and wetlands. The plant features floating leaves with a waxy surface and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its rhizomes help it spread and establish in wet environments.

Ottelia schweinfurthii plays a role in water filtration and provides habitat for aquatic organisms.
