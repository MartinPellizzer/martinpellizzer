Origanum majorana var. tenuifolium

Origanum majorana var. tenuifolium, commonly known as slender leaf marjoram, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry environments.

Its essential oils, rich in compounds like carvacrol, contribute to its use in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
