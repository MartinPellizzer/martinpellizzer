Oreocharis cordato-ovata

Oreocharis cordato-ovata, commonly known as the heart-shaped arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
