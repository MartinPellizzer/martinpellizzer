Oldenlandia leptoneura

Oldenlandia leptoneura, commonly known as the slenderleaf moonviolet, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.
