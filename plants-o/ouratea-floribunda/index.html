Ouratea Floribunda

Ouratea floribunda is a striking evergreen shrub native to the humid lowland forests of South America. Known for its elegant, clustered white or pale pink flowers, it adds a delicate beauty to any garden. This plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded landscapes. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its blooms. With its graceful appearance and relatively easy care requirements, Ouratea floribunda is a favored ornamental plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
