Oxytropis transversa

Oxytropis transversa, commonly known as the Transverse milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Oxytropis transversa is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
