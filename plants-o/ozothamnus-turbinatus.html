Ozothamnus turbinatus

Ozothamnus turbinatus, commonly known as the Turbinated Daisy or Turbinated Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sandy environments.

Ozothamnus turbinatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
