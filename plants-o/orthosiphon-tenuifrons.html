Orthosiphon tenuifrons

Orthosiphon tenuifrons, commonly known as slender-leaved horsegram, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.
