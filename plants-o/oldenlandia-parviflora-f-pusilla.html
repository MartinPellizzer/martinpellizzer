Oldenlandia parviflora f. pusilla

Oldenlandia parviflora f. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf small-flowered oldenlandia, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, often blooming during the warmer months. The plant has slender, branching stems and hairy leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
