Obione graciliflora

Obione graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered obione, is a herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soils in partial shade. Its reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, and it is often found in moist woodland habitats.

Obione graciliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
