Oglifa soyerii

Oglifa soyerii, commonly known as the rare desert orchid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in the early spring. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Oglifa soyerii is considered an endangered species.
