Oxygonum dregeanum subsp. lanceolatum

Oxygonum dregeanum subsp.

lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features slender, upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
