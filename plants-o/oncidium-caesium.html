Oncidium caesium

Oncidium caesium, commonly known as the yellow oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Oncidium caesium is popular among orchid growers for its ease of care and striking floral display.
