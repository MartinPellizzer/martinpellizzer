Oxalis neocaledonica

Oxalis neocaledonica, commonly known as the golden star oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Caledonia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellow central vein and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and delicate floral display.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
