Oncidium castaneum

Oncidium castaneum, commonly known as the cinnamon orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly fleshy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Oncidium castaneum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
