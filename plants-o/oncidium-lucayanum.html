Oncidium lucayanum

Oncidium lucayanum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features showy, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in horticultural displays. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium lucayanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
