Oncidium trifurcatum

Oncidium trifurcatum, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. Oncidium trifurcatum is a pseudobulbous orchid that requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
