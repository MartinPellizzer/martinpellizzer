Orobus asphodeloides

Orobus asphodeloides, commonly known as the Cape asphodel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Orobus asphodeloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
