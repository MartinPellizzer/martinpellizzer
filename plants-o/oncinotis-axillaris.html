Oncinotis axillaris

Oncinotis axillaris, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and forest edges. The plant features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, yellow flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the summer months.

Oncinotis axillaris produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
