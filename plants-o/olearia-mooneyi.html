Olearia mooneyi

Olearia mooneyi, commonly known as the Mooney's daisybush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in compact heads, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Olearia mooneyi is often found in coastal and rocky habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
