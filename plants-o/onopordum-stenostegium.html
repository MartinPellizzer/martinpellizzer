Onopordum stenostegium

Onopordum stenostegium, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its flowering period occurs from late summer to early autumn, and it produces small, achene fruits that are dispersed by wind.

Onopordum stenostegium is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
