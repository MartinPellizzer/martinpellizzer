Ornithogalum multifolium

Ornithogalum multifolium, commonly known as the many-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, underground rhizome and produces striking, star-shaped white flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum multifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
