Osmitopsis pinnatifida subsp. pinnatifida

Osmitopsis pinnatifida subsp.

pinnatifida, commonly known as the slender leaf osmitopsis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
