Osteospermum hamiltonii

Osteospermum hamiltonii, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces showy composite flowers with yellow centers and blue to violet ray florets.

Osteospermum hamiltonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
