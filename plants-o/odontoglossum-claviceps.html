Odontoglossum claviceps

Odontoglossum claviceps, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in cool, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip and vibrant colors that range from yellow to orange.

Odontoglossum claviceps is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
