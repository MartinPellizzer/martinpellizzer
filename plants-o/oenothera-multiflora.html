Oenothera multiflora

Oenothera multiflora, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces numerous small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Oenothera multiflora is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
