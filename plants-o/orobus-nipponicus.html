Orobus nipponicus

Orobus nipponicus, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are often pale pink or white, with a distinctive lip petal that aids in pollination.

Orobus nipponicus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
