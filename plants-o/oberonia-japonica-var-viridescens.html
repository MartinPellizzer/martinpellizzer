Oberonia japonica var. viridescens

Oberonia japonica var. viridescens, commonly known as the Emerald Orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to Japan.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves with a striking emerald-green color. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
