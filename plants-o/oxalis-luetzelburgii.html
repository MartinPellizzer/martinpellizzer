Oxalis luetzelburgii

Oxalis luetzelburgii, commonly known as the purple oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often surrounded by distinctive purple bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis luetzelburgii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
