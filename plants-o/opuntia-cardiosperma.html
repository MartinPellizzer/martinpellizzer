Opuntia cardiosperma

Opuntia cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in spines and are often segmented. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers, which are followed by fruit that contains seeds enclosed in a hardened, heart-shaped structure. Opuntia cardiosperma is adapted to arid environments and has a slow growth rate.

It is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and resilience.
