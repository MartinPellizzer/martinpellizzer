Ornithogalum turbinatum var. madrasicum

Ornithogalum turbinatum var. madrasicum, commonly known as the Madrasic bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
