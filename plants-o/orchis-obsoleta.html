Orchis obsoleta

Orchis obsoleta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a single, erect stem that can reach up to 60 centimeters in height. Its flowers are pale purple to white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Orchis obsoleta is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
