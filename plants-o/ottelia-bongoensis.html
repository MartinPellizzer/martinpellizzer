Ottelia bongoensis

Ottelia bongoensis, commonly known as the Bongo's water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to parts of Africa.

It has broad, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy layer, which helps it retain moisture in its aquatic environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its roots are adapted for anchoring in shallow water, and it spreads primarily through vegetative reproduction.

Ottelia bongoensis plays a role in providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and can contribute to water filtration in its ecosystem.
