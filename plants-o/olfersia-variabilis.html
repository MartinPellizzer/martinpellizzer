Olfersia variabilis

Olfersia variabilis, commonly known as the variable olfersia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in rainforests.
