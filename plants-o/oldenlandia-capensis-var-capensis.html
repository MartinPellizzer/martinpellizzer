Oldenlandia capensis var. capensis

Oldenlandia capensis var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape spider flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like inflorescences composed of small white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. It produces small, oval-shaped berries that are blue or black when mature.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
