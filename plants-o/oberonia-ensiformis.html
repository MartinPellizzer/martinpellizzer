Oberonia ensiformis

Oberonia ensiformis, commonly known as the sword-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, sword-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in a spike. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Oberonia ensiformis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.

It is often found in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids in forest understories.
