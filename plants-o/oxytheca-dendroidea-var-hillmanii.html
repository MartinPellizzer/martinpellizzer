Oxytheca dendroidea var. hillmanii

Oxytheca dendroidea var. hillmanii, commonly known as the Hillman's tree heath, is a shrub native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
