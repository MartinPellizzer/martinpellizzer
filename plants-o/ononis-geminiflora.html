Ononis geminiflora

Ononis geminiflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and rocky habitats in the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in dry environments.

Ononis geminiflora plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is often found in desert and coastal dunes.
