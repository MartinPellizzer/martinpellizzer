Oustropis

Oustropis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. Oustropis species are typically small, compact plants that thrive in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight. They are popular houseplants due to their ease of care and ornamental value.

The flowers are borne on long, slender stems and often last for several weeks.
