Ornithogalum rubrum

Ornithogalum rubrum, commonly known as the red belladonna lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a red or purple tinge. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
