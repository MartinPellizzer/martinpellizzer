Oncidium variegatum var. purpureum

Oncidium variegatum var. purpureum, commonly known as the Purple Orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, and it produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
