Orobus tuberosus var. prostratus

Orobus tuberosus var. prostratus, commonly known as the prostrate tuberous orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features a rhizomatous root system that produces tubers, which are used for propagation. The plant typically grows in a prostrate or sprawling manner, with thin, wiry stems that lie close to the ground. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white and pale pink.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
