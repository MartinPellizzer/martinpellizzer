Oncidium santanderianum

Oncidium santanderianum, commonly known as the Santander orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Oncidium santanderianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
