Olyra loretensis

Olyra loretensis, commonly known as the Loretto palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Olyra loretensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils with full sunlight.
