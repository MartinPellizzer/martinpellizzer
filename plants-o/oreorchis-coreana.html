Oreorchis coreana

Oreorchis coreana, commonly known as the Korean twayblade, is a flowering plant native to Korea and parts of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has a short flowering period, usually in late spring to early summer.

Oreorchis coreana is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
