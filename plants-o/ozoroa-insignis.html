Ozoroa insignis

Ozoroa insignis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stem is erect and often branched.

Ozoroa insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
