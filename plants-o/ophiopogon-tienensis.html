Ophiopogon tienensis

Ophiopogon tienensis, commonly known as the Tien Mountains spathiphyllum, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. Ophiopogon tienensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
