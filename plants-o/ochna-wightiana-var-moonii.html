Ochna wightiana var. moonii

Ochna wightiana var. moonii, commonly known as Moon's ochna, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree with simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
