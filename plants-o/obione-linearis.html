Obione linearis

Obione linearis, commonly known as the slender obione, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or coastal landscapes. Its growth habit is herbaceous, with the stems dying back in winter and regrowing in spring.

Obione linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its refined appearance.
