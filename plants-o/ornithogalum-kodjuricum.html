Ornithogalum kodjuricum

Ornithogalum kodjuricum, commonly known as the Kodjuric bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale green flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
