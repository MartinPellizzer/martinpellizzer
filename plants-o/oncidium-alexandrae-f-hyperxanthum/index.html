Oncidium Alexandrae F. Hyperxanthum

Oncidium alexandrae f. hyperxanthum is a captivating orchid known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms. This hybrid variety showcases striking yellow and orange hues that make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. Its unique coloration and ease of care have made it a favorite for both beginners and experienced growers. This plant exemplifies the beauty and diversity of the orchid family.
