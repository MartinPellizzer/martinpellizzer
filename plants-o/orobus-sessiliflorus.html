Orobus sessiliflorus

Orobus sessiliflorus, commonly known as the sessile-flower orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a spike. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Orobus sessiliflorus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or forested areas.
