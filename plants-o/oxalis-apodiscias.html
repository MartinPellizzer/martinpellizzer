Oxalis apodiscias

Oxalis apodiscias, commonly known as the silverleaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis apodiscias is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
