Ononis leucotricha

Ononis leucotricha, commonly known as white-haired sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and rocky environments in the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white or pale pink flowers clustered in racemes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Ononis leucotricha is adapted to withstand drought and has a deep root system to access moisture.
