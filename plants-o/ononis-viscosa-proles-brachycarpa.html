Ononis viscosa proles brachycarpa

Ononis viscosa proles brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-seeded hedge parsley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in early spring, followed by the distinctive short, rounded pods.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and is often found in disturbed or marginal habitats.
