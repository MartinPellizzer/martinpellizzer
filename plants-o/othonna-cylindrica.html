Othonna cylindrica

Othonna cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical othonna, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

Othonna cylindrica prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
