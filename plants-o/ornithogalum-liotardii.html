Ornithogalum liotardii

Ornithogalum liotardii, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ornithogalum liotardii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
