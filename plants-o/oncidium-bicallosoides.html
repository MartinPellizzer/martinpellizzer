Oncidium bicallosoides

Oncidium bicallosoides, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences that bear bright yellow to orange flowers with contrasting purple markings. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium bicallosoides is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
