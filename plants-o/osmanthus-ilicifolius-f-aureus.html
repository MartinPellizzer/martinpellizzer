Osmanthus ilicifolius f. aureus

Osmanthus ilicifolius f. aureus, commonly known as the golden osmanthus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn a striking golden hue in the autumn. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange, which bloom in late autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique foliage and seasonal color changes make it a visually appealing addition to any landscape.
