Oncidium strictum

Oncidium strictum, commonly known as the stiff orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in a raceme. Oncidium strictum is a pseudobulbous orchid that requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
