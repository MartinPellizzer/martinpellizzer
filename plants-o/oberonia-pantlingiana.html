Oberonia pantlingiana

Oberonia pantlingiana, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its leaves are usually elongated and leathery, while its roots are adapted for epiphytic growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
