Opilia macrocarpa

Opilia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited opilia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Opilia macrocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
