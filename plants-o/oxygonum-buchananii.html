Oxygonum buchananii

Oxygonum buchananii, commonly known as the Buchanan's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant produces showy blooms during the early morning hours, which close by midday. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Oxygonum buchananii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
