Otonychium imbricatum

Otonychium imbricatum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Otonychium imbricatum is adapted to harsh alpine environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained conditions.
