Oberonia scytophylla

Oberonia scytophylla, commonly known as the long-spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with elongated spurs that can reach up to 5 centimeters in length. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with reddish or brownish markings.

Oberonia scytophylla is often found growing in shaded, moist undergrowth, and it plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.
