Orbea maculata subsp. rangeana

Orbea maculata subsp.

rangeana, commonly known as the spotted orbea, is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is native to southern Africa. It is a flowering plant characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically covered in a waxy coating.
