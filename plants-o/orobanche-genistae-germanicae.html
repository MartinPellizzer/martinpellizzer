Orobanche genistae-germanicae

Orobanche genistae-germanicae, commonly known as the yellow broom broom.

This parasitic plant belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and lacks chlorophyll, relying entirely on its host for nutrients. It typically infests plants in the legume family, such as Genista species. The plant produces small, yellow or orange flowers and has a highly reduced structure with no true leaves.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
