Orania disticha

Orania disticha, commonly known as the two-branched orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive two-branched inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Orania disticha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental species within the orchid family.
