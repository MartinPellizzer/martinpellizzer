Oxalis uittienii

Oxalis uittienii, commonly known as the pink oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and showy, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.

Oxalis uittienii is a perennial herb that prefers full sun to partial shade.
