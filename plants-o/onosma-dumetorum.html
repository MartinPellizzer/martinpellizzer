Onosma dumetorum

Onosma dumetorum, commonly known as wild thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and sage. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Onosma dumetorum thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Its essential oils contain compounds with potential antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.
