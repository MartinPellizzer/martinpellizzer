Oberonia brachystachys

Oberonia brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the forest floor.

This species is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
