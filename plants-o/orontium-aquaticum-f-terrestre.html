Orontium aquaticum f. terrestre

Orontium aquaticum f. terrestre, commonly known as the terrestrial water shield, is a rare variant of the water shield plant.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often floating or partially submerged. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom in early summer.

The species is notable for its adaptation to both aquatic and terrestrial conditions, making it a unique example of a plant with a hybrid growth habit.
