Olearia asterotricha

Olearia asterotricha, commonly known as the New Zealand frostflower, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage covered in fine, star-shaped trichomes, giving it a frost-like appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Olearia asterotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
