Obione cryatallina

Obione cryatallina, commonly known as the crystal leaf plant, is a striking species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its translucent, waxy leaves that reflect light in a shimmering manner. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern, enhancing its visual appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
