Oenothera globularis

Oenothera globularis, commonly known as the globular evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant in the Onagraceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
