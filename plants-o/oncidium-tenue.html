Oncidium tenue

Oncidium tenue, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Oncidium tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
