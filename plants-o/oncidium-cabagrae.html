Oncidium cabagrae

Oncidium cabagrae, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that have a distinctive, intricate pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, lasting several weeks, and are highly fragrant.

Oncidium cabagrae is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
