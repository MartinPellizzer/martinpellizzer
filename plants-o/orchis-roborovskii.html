Orchis roborovskii

Orchis roborovskii, commonly known as the Roborovskii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive, showy orchid flower that typically blooms in late spring or early summer. The plant features a robust, upright stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height, adorned with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, with a unique, spurred structure that aids in pollination.

Orchis roborovskii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
