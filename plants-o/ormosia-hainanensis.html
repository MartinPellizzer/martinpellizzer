Ormosia hainanensis

Ormosia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan ormosia, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain small, dark brown seeds. Ormosia hainanensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.

It plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
