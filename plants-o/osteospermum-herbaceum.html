Osteospermum herbaceum

Osteospermum herbaceum, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces daisy-like flowers with blue to purple petals and a yellow center. The plant has a sprawling growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Osteospermum herbaceum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
