Odontoglossum naevium

Odontoglossum naevium, commonly known as the speckled orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are broad and dark green, often with a velvety texture.

Odontoglossum naevium is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral morphology.
