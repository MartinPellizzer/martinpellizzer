Origanum × welwitschii

Origanum × welwitschii, commonly known as the Cape garlic, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a strong garlic or onion scent. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its unique fragrance and ornamental value make it a popular choice in gardens and herbal applications.
