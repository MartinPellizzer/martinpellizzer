Onosma tricerosperma subsp. atlantica

Onosma tricerosperma subsp.

atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic onosma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens.

This subspecies is adapted to dry conditions and exhibits a compact growth habit.
