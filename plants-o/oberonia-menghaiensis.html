Oberonia menghaiensis

Oberonia menghaiensis, commonly known as the Menghai orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomatous root system helps it anchor in the soil and absorb nutrients.

Oberonia menghaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
