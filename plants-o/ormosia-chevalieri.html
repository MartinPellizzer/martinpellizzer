Ormosia chevalieri

Ormosia chevalieri, commonly known as the Malayan ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain elliptical seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Ormosia chevalieri is valued for its hard, heavy timber and is also studied for its potential ecological roles in forest ecosystems.
