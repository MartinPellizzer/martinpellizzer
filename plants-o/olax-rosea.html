Olax rosea

Olax rosea, commonly known as the pink olax, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Olax rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
