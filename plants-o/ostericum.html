Ostericum

Ostericum, commonly known as sweet cicely, is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces aromatic seeds that are often used in culinary applications. Native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Ostericum is valued for its fragrant foliage and its historical use in herbal medicine.
