Ochthocosmus lemaireanus var. candidus

Ochthocosmus lemaireanus var. candidus, commonly known as the white-flowered star grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive feature is the white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
