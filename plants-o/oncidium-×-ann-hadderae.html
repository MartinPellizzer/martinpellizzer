Oncidium × ann-hadderae

Oncidium × ann-hadderae, commonly known as the Hybrid Oncidium, is a tropical orchid species that exhibits a variety of flower colors, including yellow, orange, and red.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features long, arching stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and are known for their vibrant, showy appearance.

Oncidium × ann-hadderae requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
