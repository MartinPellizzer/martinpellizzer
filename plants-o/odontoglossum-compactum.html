Odontoglossum compactum

Odontoglossum compactum, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring a short, stout rhizome and a dense cluster of leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a spurred lip and a vibrant color palette, often including shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the warmer months.

Odontoglossum compactum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
