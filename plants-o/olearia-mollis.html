Olearia mollis

Olearia mollis, commonly known as the silverweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and silver-velvet foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, are small and yellow, forming dense clusters that attract pollinators.

Olearia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
