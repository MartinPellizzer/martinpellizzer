Osmanthus obovatifolius

Osmanthus obovatifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaf osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its lanceolate to ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces fragrant yellowish-white flowers in clusters during the autumn season, which are highly valued for their aromatic properties. Osmanthus obovatifolius is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a versatile plant in horticulture.
