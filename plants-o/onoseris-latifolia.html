Onoseris latifolia

Onoseris latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and meadows. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer.

Onoseris latifolia is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
