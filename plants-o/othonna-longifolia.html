Othonna longifolia

Othonna longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf othonna, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy stems and clusters of small, blue-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are followed by small, round fruits. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Othonna longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in xeriscapes.
