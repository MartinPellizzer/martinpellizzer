Oldenlandia corymbosa var. tereticaulis

Oldenlandia corymbosa var. tereticaulis, commonly known as the slender false spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or white. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs. This species is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in humid, sunny environments.
