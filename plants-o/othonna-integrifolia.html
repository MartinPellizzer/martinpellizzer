Othonna integrifolia

Othonna integrifolia, commonly known as the Cape rose or African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with opposite, fleshy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
