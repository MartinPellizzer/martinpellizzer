Oncidium graminifolium var. holochilum

Oncidium graminifolium var. holochilum, commonly known as the green-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant appearance and long-lasting blooms.

The variety holochilum is distinguished by its more vibrant green foliage compared to other varieties of Oncidium graminifolium.
