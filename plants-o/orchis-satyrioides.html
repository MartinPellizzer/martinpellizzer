Orchis satyrioides

Orchis satyrioides, commonly known as the satyr orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in woodland and meadow habitats. The plant features a tall, erect stem with a single spike of violet to purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged along the stem, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Orchis satyrioides is known for its delicate appearance and is often appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens.
