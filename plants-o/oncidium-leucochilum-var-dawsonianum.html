Oncidium leucochilum var. dawsonianum

Oncidium leucochilum var. dawsonianum, commonly known as the Dawson's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with white and green markings. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
