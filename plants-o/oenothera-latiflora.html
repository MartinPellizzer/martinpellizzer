Oenothera latiflora

Oenothera latiflora, commonly known as the showy evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Oenothera latiflora is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
