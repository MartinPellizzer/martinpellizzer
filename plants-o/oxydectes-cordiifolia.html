Oxydectes cordiifolia

Oxydectes cordiifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
