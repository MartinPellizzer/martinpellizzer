Oncidium jurgensenianum

Oncidium jurgensenianum, commonly known as the Jurgensen's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences with numerous fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Oncidium jurgensenianum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
