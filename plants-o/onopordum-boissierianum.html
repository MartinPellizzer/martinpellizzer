Onopordum boissierianum

Onopordum boissierianum, commonly known as the Boissier's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust stem with spiny leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by distinctive spiny seed heads. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.

Onopordum boissierianum is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
