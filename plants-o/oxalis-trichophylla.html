Oxalis trichophylla

Oxalis trichophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
