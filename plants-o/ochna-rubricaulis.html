Ochna rubricaulis

Ochna rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown stems and dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are woody capsules that split open to release small, hairy seeds.

Ochna rubricaulis is often found in rocky or coastal habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
