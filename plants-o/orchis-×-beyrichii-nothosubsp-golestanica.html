Orchis × beyrichii nothosubsp. golestanica

Orchis × beyrichii nothosubsp.

golestanica, commonly known as the Golestan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive greenish-yellow lip.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its morphology includes tall, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves and a single, showy flower per stem.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
