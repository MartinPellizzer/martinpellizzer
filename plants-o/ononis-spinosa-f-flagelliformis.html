Ononis spinosa f. flagelliformis

Ononis spinosa f. flagelliformis, commonly known as the flagelliform wild pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and slender, thread-like stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as coastal dunes or disturbed areas.

This variant is notable for its distinctive morphology and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
