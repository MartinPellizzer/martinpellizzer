Ononis cirtensis

Ononis cirtensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved restharrow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ononis cirtensis is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and is commonly used in dry garden landscapes.
