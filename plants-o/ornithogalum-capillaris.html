Ornithogalum capillaris

Ornithogalum capillaris, commonly known as the slender star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Ornithogalum capillaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
