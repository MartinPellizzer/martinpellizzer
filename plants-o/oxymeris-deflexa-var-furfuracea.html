Oxymeris deflexa var. furfuracea

Oxymeris deflexa var. furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy oxymeris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often pubescent, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid climates.
