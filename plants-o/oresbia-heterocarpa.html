Oresbia heterocarpa

Oresbia heterocarpa, commonly known as the purple milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy purple flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Oresbia heterocarpa plays a role in nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
