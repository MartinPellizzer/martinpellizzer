Ochnella afzelii

Ochnella afzelii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange-red, fleshy berries. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.

Ochnella afzelii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
