Orthophytum toscanoi subsp. atropurpureum

Orthophytum toscanoi subsp.

atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple-leafed orthophytum, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep purple or reddish hue, especially under bright sunlight.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
