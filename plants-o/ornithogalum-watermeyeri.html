Ornithogalum watermeyeri

Ornithogalum watermeyeri, commonly known as the Watermeyeri star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water storage. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a resilient and visually striking addition to dry climate gardens.
