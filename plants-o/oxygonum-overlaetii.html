Oxygonum overlaetii

Oxygonum overlaetii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Oxygonum overlaetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
