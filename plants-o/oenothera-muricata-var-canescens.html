Oenothera muricata var. canescens

Oenothera muricata var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its seeds rich in essential fatty acids.
