Ouratea magdalenae

Ouratea magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalen's ouratea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the humid forests of southern Mexico, particularly in the states of Oaxaca and Chiapas. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

The species is relatively rare and is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological and horticultural traits.
