Oldenlandia cyperoides

Oldenlandia cyperoides, commonly known as the bluetail or blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue-violet flowers arranged in spikes. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it is often found in wet soils along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
