Oldenlandia sphaerocephala

Oldenlandia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globehead spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces distinctive, spherical seed capsules that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Oldenlandia sphaerocephala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
