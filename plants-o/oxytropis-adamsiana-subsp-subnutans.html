Oxytropis adamsiana subsp. subnutans

Oxytropis adamsiana subsp.

subnutans, commonly known as the nodding milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to central Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow or pale yellow flowers in clusters. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in stabilizing sandy soils.
