Oncidium helenae

Oncidium helenae, commonly known as the Helen's orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting blooms. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Oncidium helenae thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
