Oxalis urbaniana

Oxalis urbaniana, commonly known as the urban oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces bright yellow blooms that are often tinged with red or orange, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis urbaniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
