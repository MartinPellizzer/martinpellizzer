Ocotea williamsii

Ocotea williamsii, commonly known as Williams' ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico, Guatemala, and Costa Rica. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are followed by spherical, dark blue berries.

Ocotea williamsii is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
