Ononis pusilla var. calycina

Ononis pusilla var. calycina, commonly known as the small onyx or dwarf onyx, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is well adapted to rocky and dry environments. It is often found in gravelly soils and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Its botanical features make it a desirable plant for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
