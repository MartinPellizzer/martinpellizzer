Ornithogalum distans

Ornithogalum distans, commonly known as the distant star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, and its blooming period typically occurs in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
