Omentaria capensis

Omentaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape omentaria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Omentaria capensis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous environments.
