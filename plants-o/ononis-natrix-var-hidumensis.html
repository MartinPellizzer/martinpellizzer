Ononis natrix var. hidumensis

Ononis natrix var. hidumensis, commonly known as the Hidumensian sandspurry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

This variety is often found in disturbed or coastal habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
