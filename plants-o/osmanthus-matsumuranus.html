Osmanthus matsumuranus

Osmanthus matsumuranus, commonly known as the Japanese osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, dark brown fruits that contain a single seed. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Osmanthus matsumuranus is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
