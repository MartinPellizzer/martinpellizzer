Ottelia balansae

Ottelia balansae, commonly known as the African water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. Ottelia balansae thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It is often used in water gardens and can help in water purification by absorbing nutrients.
