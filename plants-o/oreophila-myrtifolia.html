Oreophila myrtifolia

Oreophila myrtifolia, commonly known as the mountain myrtle.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This species is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
