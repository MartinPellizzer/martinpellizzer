Oubanguia laurifolia

Oubanguia laurifolia, commonly known as the African laurifolia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Oubanguia laurifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
