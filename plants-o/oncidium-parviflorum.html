Oncidium parviflorum

Oncidium parviflorum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow blooms that are often adorned with a dark lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium parviflorum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and ornamental value.
