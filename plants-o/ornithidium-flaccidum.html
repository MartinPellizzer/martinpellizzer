Ornithidium flaccidum

Ornithidium flaccidum, commonly known as the flaccid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant has a slender, upright stem with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and pale green, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
