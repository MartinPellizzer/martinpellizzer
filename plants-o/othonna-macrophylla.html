Othonna macrophylla

Othonna macrophylla, commonly known as the giant othonna, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
