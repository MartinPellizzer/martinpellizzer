Oryzopsis ovata

Oryzopsis ovata, commonly known as the oval grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. Oryzopsis ovata is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
