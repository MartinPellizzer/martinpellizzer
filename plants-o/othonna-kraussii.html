Othonna kraussii

Othonna kraussii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features silver-gray foliage with woolly textures, which help it conserve moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Othonna kraussii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
