Oncidium decipiens

Oncidium decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Oncidium decipiens is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its long-lasting blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
