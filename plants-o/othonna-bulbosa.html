Othonna bulbosa

Othonna bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, bulb-like stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna bulbosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
