Osteospermum pinnatipartitum

Osteospermum pinnatipartitum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It produces cylindrical flower heads with showy ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular flowers.

Osteospermum pinnatipartitum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for gardens and containers.
