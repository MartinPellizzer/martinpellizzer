Orbexilum latifolia

Orbexilum latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf orbexilum, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. Orbexilum latifolia typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade.
