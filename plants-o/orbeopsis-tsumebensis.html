Orbeopsis tsumebensis

Orbeopsis tsumebensis, commonly known as the Tsumeb orchid, is a rare and unique plant native to Namibia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its floral structure includes intricate patterns and vibrant colors, which attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
