Ornithogalum uitenhagense

Ornithogalum uitenhagense, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its susceptibility to fungal diseases.
