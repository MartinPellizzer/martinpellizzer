Oleandra archboldii

Oleandra archboldii, commonly known as the Archbold oleander, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth margin.

Oleandra archboldii is adapted to coastal and sandy soils, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
