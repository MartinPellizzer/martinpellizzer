Oncidium albicans

Oncidium albicans, commonly known as the White Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in a raceme. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium albicans is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
