Ormosia mekongensis

Ormosia mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, round seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Ormosia mekongensis is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
