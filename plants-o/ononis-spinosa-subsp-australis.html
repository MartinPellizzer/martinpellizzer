Ononis spinosa subsp. australis

Ononis spinosa subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian sand everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a low-growing perennial herb with wiry stems and small, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It is adapted to sandy and gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant.

This subspecies is often found in coastal and inland areas with well-drained soils.
