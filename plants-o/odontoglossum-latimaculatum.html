Odontoglossum latimaculatum

Odontoglossum latimaculatum, commonly known as the broad-spotted orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant features a pseudobulb with a rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique color pattern. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with dark purple or brown markings.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
