Oncidium olivaceum var. macrochilum

Oncidium olivaceum var. macrochilum, commonly known as the Orchid with Long Lip, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
