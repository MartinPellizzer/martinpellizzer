Olearia arguta

Olearia arguta, commonly known as silverweed, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silver-gray foliage due to dense covering of fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Olearia arguta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
