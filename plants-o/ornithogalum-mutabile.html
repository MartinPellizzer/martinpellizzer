Ornithogalum mutabile

Ornithogalum mutabile, commonly known as the variable star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, purple, and even red, depending on the variety. The plant typically blooms in spring and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its variable flower colors, it is also known as the variable star-of-Bethlehem.
