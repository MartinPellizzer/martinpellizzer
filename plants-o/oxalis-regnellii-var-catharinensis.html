Oxalis regnellii var. catharinensis

Oxalis regnellii var. catharinensis, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage is often variegated with white or yellow markings, adding visual interest.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
