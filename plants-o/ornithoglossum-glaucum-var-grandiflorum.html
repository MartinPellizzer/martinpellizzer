Ornithoglossum glaucum var. grandiflorum

Ornithoglossum glaucum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the blue-flowered lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
