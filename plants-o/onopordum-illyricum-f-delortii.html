Onopordum illyricum f. delortii

Onopordum illyricum f. delortii, commonly known as the Delort's thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a variant of the common thistle, characterized by its distinctive purple to pink flower heads and spiny leaves. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky habitats. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

The flower heads are typically visited by various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance in its native range.
