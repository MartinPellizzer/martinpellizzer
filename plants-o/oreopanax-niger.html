Oreopanax niger

Oreopanax niger, commonly known as black oreopanax, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright yellow spadices surrounded by large, showy white bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Oreopanax niger is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique foliage and dramatic floral displays.
