Oldenlandia lancifolia var. grandiflora

Oldenlandia lancifolia var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
