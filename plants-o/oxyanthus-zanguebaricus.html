Oxyanthus zanguebaricus

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Oxyanthus zanguebaricus is also valued for its resilience to salt spray and wind, which contributes to its ecological importance in coastal ecosystems.
