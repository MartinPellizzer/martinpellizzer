Otoglossum chitaraquense

Otoglossum chitaraquense, commonly known as the Chitaraquense orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy floral displays. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Otoglossum chitaraquense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
