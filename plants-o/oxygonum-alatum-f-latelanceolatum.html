Oxygonum alatum f. latelanceolatum

Oxygonum alatum f. latelanceolatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves.

This plant belongs to the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of the Americas. Its foliage is typically broad and lanceolate, with a strikingly veined pattern that enhances its ornamental value. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a houseplant.
