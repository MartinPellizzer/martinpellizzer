Olfersia undulata

Olfersia undulata, commonly known as the undulating orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, wavy leaf patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Olfersia undulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
