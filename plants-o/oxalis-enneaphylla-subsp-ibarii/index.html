Oxalis Enneaphylla Subsp. Ibarii

Oxalis enneaphylla subsp. ibarii is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that bloom in spring. This subspecies is particularly valued for its ornamental qualities and unique foliage patterns. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Careful cultivation and conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival in the wild.
