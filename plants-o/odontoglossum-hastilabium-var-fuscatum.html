Odontoglossum hastilabium var. fuscatum

Odontoglossum hastilabium var. fuscatum, commonly known as the dark-leaved odontoglossum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
