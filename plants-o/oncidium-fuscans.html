Oncidium fuscans

Oncidium fuscans, commonly known as the brown orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish or brownish tinge. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Oncidium fuscans is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
