Onopordum cyprium

Onopordum cyprium, commonly known as Cyprus thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, spiny stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, covered with rough, grayish-green leaves. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers with purple centers, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, prickly texture.

Onopordum cyprium is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and arid environments.
