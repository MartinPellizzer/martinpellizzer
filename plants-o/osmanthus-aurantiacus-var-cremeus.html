Osmanthus aurantiacus var. cremeus

Osmanthus aurantiacus var. cremeus, commonly known as the Cream Osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.
