Onobrychis talagonica

Onobrychis talagonica, commonly known as the Talagonica burr clover, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Onobrychis talagonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in dry habitats.
