Orthosiphon rubicundus var. hainanensis

Orthosiphon rubicundus var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan orthosiphon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, hairy stems, and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale purple to blue, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the summer months.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
