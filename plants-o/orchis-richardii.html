Orchis richardii

Orchis richardii, commonly known as Richard's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in woodland and grassland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Orchis richardii is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
