Oncidium urophyllum f. flavum

Oncidium urophyllum f. flavum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits striking yellow and green coloration.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescence with numerous flowers. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
