Orchis spathulata

Orchis spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears several broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more prominently marked.

Orchis spathulata is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
