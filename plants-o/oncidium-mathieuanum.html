Oncidium mathieuanum

Oncidium mathieuanum, commonly known as the Mathieu's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has long, strap-like leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity. It is a pseudobulbous orchid, meaning it has swollen stems that store water and nutrients.

Oncidium mathieuanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of care and vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
