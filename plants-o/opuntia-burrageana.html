Opuntia burrageana

Opuntia burrageana, commonly known as the Burrage prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Opuntia burrageana is well adapted to arid environments and plays an important role in local ecosystems.
