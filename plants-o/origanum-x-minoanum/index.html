Origanum × Minoanum

Origanum × minoanum is a hybrid herb known for its aromatic leaves and vibrant flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is commonly used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its strong scent and health benefits. Its compact growth makes it ideal for gardens and containers. With its historical significance and versatility, it remains a valued plant in both traditional and modern settings.
