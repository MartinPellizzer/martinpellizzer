Orbea inodora

Orbea inodora, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with erect, hairy stems and linear leaves. Orbea inodora produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique fragrance and beauty.
