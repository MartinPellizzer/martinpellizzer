Orthosiphon villosus

Orthosiphon villosus, commonly known as hairy horsegram, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often glandular. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color.
