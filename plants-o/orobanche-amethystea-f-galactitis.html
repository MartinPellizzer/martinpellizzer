Orobanche amethystea f. galactitis

Orobanche amethystea f. galactitis, commonly known as the pink winter barren, is a parasitic flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host plants for nutrients, typically parasitizing members of the Fabaceae family. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, which are often used for ornamental purposes. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
