Odotalon tricuspidata

Odotalon tricuspidata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a rare and distinctive flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a slender, upright stem with a single, showy flower. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces a delicate, fragrant bloom during the spring season.
