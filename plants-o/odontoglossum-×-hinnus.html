Odontoglossum × hinnus

Odontoglossum × hinnus, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchid species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
