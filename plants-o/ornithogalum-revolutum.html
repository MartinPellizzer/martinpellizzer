Ornithogalum revolutum

Ornithogalum revolutum, commonly known as the rolling leaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, rolled leaves that give it its common name, and it produces striking white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs, which store nutrients for the plant's growth.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
