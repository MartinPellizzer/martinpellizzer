Oncidium macropetalum var. fuscopetalum

Oncidium macropetalum var. fuscopetalum, commonly known as the dark-petaled orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers with dark purple to brown petals and a contrasting yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value.

The variety is distinguished by its darker coloration compared to the typical form of Oncidium macropetalum.
