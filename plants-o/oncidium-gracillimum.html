Oncidium gracillimum

Oncidium gracillimum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. Oncidium gracillimum is known for its ease of cultivation and is often grown as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
