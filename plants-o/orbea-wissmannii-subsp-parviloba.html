Orbea wissmannii subsp. parviloba

Orbea wissmannii subsp.

parviloba, commonly known as the dwarf orbea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in small, rounded leaves that help it store water.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
