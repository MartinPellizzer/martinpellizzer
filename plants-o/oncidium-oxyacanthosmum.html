Oncidium oxyacanthosmum

Oncidium oxyacanthosmum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with red or brown markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in conditions with irregular rainfall.

Oncidium oxyacanthosmum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
