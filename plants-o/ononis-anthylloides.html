Ononis anthylloides

Ononis anthylloides, commonly known as the small-flowered bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
