Ornanthes mannifera

Ornanthes mannifera, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often sought after by horticulturists and conservationists.
