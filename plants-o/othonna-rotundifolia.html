Othonna rotundifolia

Othonna rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf othonna, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded or oval in shape, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
