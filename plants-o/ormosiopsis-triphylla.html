Ormosiopsis triphylla

Ormosiopsis triphylla, commonly known as the three-leaf orchid, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant features three leaf-like structures that are often mistaken for true leaves, though they are actually modified stems. Its flowers, which are typically small and delicate, bloom in clusters and are often pale green or white in color.

Ormosiopsis triphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
