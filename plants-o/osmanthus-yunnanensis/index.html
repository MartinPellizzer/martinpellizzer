Osmanthus Yunnanensis

Osmanthus yunnanensis is a fragrant evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. It is known for its small, golden-yellow flowers that emit a sweet, aromatic scent. The plant is highly valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits. Its ornamental value has also made it a popular choice in horticulture. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains relatively obscure compared to other osmanthus species.
