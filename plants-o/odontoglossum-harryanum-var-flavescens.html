Odontoglossum harryanum var. flavescens

Odontoglossum harryanum var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals, which contrast with its greenish-blue lip. Native to the Andean regions of South America, this plant thrives in high-altitude cloud forests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
