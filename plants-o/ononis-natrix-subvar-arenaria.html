Ononis natrix subvar. arenaria

Ononis natrix subvar. arenaria, commonly known as sand vetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and sandy environments.

It is characterized by its low, sprawling growth habit and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes or inland sandy areas.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for various insects.
