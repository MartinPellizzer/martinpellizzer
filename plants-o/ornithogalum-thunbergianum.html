Ornithogalum thunbergianum

Ornithogalum thunbergianum, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that form a rosette near the ground. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
