Olax thouarsiana

Olax thouarsiana, commonly known as the Madagascar olive, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually elliptic in shape.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.
