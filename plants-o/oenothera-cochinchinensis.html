Oenothera cochinchinensis

Oenothera cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Oenothera cochinchinensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
