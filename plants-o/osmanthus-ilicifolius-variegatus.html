Osmanthus ilicifolius variegatus

Osmanthus ilicifolius variegatus, commonly known as the variegated fragrant wood, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring white and yellow markings on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the autumn, which emit a sweet, honey-like scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and aromatic blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
