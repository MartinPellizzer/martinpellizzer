Onobrychis collina f. gracillima

Onobrychis collina f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender field vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often found in the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal use.
