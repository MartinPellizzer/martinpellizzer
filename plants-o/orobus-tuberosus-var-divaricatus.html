Orobus tuberosus var. divaricatus

Orobus tuberosus var. divaricatus, commonly known as the zigzag goldenroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, adding ornamental value to the plant.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
