Odontostemma nigricans var. zhenkangense

Odontostemma nigricans var. zhenkangense, commonly known as the Zhenkang spider plant, is a unique variant of the spider plant species native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender leaves that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
