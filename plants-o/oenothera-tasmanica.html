Oenothera tasmanica

Oenothera tasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Oenothera tasmanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
