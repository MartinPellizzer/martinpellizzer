Onychium chinense

Onychium chinense, commonly known as Chinese onychium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible roots. The plant features upright, branched stems and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Onychium chinense is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a traditional medicinal plant.
