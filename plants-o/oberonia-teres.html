Oberonia teres

Oberonia teres, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, creeping rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive hooded labellum. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, Oberonia teres is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
