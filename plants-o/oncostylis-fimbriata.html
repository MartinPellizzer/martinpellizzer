Oncostylis fimbriata

Oncostylis fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed leaf sedge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

Oncostylis fimbriata is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its adaptability to various wetland habitats.
