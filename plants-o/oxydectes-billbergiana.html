Oxydectes billbergiana

Oxydectes billbergiana, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by white ray florets, blooming throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for xeriscaping and garden borders.
