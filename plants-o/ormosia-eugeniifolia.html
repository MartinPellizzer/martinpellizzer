Ormosia eugeniifolia

Ormosia eugeniifolia, commonly known as the yellow tamarind, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate with 5 to 9 pairs of leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or yellowish-green in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Ormosia eugeniifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
