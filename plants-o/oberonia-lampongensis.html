Oberonia lampongensis

Oberonia lampongensis, commonly known as the Lampong orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
