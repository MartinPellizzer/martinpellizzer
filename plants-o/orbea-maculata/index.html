Orbea Maculata

Orbea maculata is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family known for its vibrant, tubular flowers. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique spotted leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. It requires minimal water and well-draining soil to flourish. This plant is not only beautiful but also adds a touch of exotic flair to any garden or indoor space.
