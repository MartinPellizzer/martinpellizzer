Ostodes muricata var. minor

Ostodes muricata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser spiny hibiscus, is a tropical shrub characterized by its woody stems and distinctive spiny leaves.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom throughout the warm season. Its leaves are typically ovate with serrated edges and bear small, sharp spines along the veins.
