Osmanthus caudatus

Osmanthus caudatus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, dark brown fruits that contain a single seed. Osmanthus caudatus is often cultivated for its aromatic foliage and ornamental value.

It thrives in temperate climates and is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a spice.
