Orthosporum multifidum

Orthosporum multifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orthosporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, deeply lobed leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences and bloom in early spring.

Orthosporum multifidum is known for its fibrous roots and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
