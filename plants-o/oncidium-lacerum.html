Oncidium lacerum

Oncidium lacerum, commonly known as the Shining Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green and white patterns. The plant produces striking, pendulous flower spikes with clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange blossoms. Oncidium lacerum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
