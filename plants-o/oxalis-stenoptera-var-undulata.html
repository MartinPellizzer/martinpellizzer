Oxalis stenoptera var. undulata

Oxalis stenoptera var. undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf wood sorrel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with wavy margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, delicate yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage, which remains attractive throughout the growing season.
