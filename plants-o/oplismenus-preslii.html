Oplismenus preslii

Oplismenus preslii, commonly known as the silver hair veldtgrass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Oplismenus preslii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
