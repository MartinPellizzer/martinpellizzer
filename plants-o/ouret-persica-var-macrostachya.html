Ouret persica var. macrostachya

Ouret persica var. macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stalked peach.

This variant is a botanical variation of the peach, characterized by its distinctive floral structure and growth habits. It typically features elongated floral stalks and a compact growth form, which can make it visually striking in gardens. The plant produces edible fruits similar to the common peach, though they may vary in size and flavor.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and potential for fruit production.
