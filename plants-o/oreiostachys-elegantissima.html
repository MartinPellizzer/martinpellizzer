Oreiostachys elegantissima

Oreiostachys elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, arching stems that bear delicate, feathery inflorescences, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest in gardens.

Oreiostachys elegantissima is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in wetland environments.
