Oncidium cervantesii

Oncidium cervantesii, commonly known as the Cervantes orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a short, stocky rhizome and pseudobulbs that support its growth. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. Its foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

Oncidium cervantesii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
