Otoglossum brachypterum

Otoglossum brachypterum, commonly known as the narrow-winged orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, narrow petals. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
