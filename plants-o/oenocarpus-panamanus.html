Oenocarpus panamanus

Oenocarpus panamanus, commonly known as the Panama palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions with tropical climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its fruit, known as a "breadfruit," is edible and has been traditionally used by indigenous communities for food and other purposes.
