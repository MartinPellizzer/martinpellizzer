Ochnella buechneri

Ochnella buechneri, commonly known as the Buechner's ochna, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
