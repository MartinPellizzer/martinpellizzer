Ocotea mosenii

Ocotea mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii Ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel trees and cinnamon plants. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Ocotea mosenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and ecological significance.
