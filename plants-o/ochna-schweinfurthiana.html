Ochna schweinfurthiana

Ochna schweinfurthiana, commonly known as the African star glory, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and unique fruiting structures.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
