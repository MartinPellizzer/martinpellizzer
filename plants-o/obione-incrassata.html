Obione incrassata

Obione incrassata, commonly known as the thick-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, dark green leaves that are thick and leathery in texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Obione incrassata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
