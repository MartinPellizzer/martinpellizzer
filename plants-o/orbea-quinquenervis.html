Orbea quinquenervis

Orbea quinquenervis, commonly known as the five-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its arid environment.

Orbea quinquenervis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
