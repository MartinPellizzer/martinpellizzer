Ochna Superba

Ochna superba is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, known for its vibrant yellow flowers and glossy green leaves. This plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often found in coastal regions and rocky outcrops. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Ochna superba is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African healing practices. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its health and longevity.
