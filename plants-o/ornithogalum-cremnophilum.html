Ornithogalum cremnophilum

Ornithogalum cremnophilum, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, preferring well-drained conditions. It produces a bulbous root system that stores nutrients and water.

Ornithogalum cremnophilum is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to withstand drought and cold temperatures.
