Ormosia macrodisca

Ormosia macrodisca, commonly known as the large-leaved ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Ormosia macrodisca is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and potential medicinal properties.
