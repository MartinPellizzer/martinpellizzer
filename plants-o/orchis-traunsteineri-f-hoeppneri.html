Orchis traunsteineri f. hoeppneri

Orchis traunsteineri f. hoeppneri, commonly known as the Traunsteiner's orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in many areas.
