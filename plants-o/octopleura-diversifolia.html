Octopleura diversifolia

Octopleura diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaved octopleura, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that vary in color from deep green to reddish tones. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
