Orbea verrucosa subsp. fucosa

Orbea verrucosa subsp.

fucosa, commonly known as the Orange Tree or Cape Orange, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
