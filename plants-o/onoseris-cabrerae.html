Onoseris cabrerae

Onoseris cabrerae, commonly known as the Canary Islands thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, herbaceous stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish composite flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
