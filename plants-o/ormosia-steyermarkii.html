Ormosia steyermarkii

Ormosia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the wet forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in humid, lowland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
