Oxytropis halleri var. korabensis

Oxytropis halleri var. korabensis, commonly known as the Korab oxytropis, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high elevations.
