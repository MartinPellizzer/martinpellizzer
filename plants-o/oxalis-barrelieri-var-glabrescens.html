Oxalis barrelieri var. glabrescens

Oxalis barrelieri var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaf wood sorrel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a creeping rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread across the forest floor.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and vibrant floral display.
