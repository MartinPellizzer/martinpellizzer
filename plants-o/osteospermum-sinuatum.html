Osteospermum sinuatum

Osteospermum sinuatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with white petals and a central yellow disk, which attract pollinators. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Osteospermum sinuatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
