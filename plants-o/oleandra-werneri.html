Oleandra werneri

Oleandra werneri, commonly known as the Werneri oleander, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Oleandra werneri is evergreen and thrives in warm, sunny climates, making it a popular ornamental plant.

However, it is important to note that all parts of this plant are toxic due to the presence of cardiac glycosides.
