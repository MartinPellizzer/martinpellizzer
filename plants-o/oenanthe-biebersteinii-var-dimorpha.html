Oenanthe biebersteinii var. dimorpha

Oenanthe biebersteinii var. dimorpha, commonly known as the two-form water dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.
