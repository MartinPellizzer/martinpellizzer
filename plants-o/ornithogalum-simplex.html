Ornithogalum simplex

Ornithogalum simplex, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale green star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its showy inflorescence, which resembles a star, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum simplex is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
