Ocimum vanderystii

Ocimum vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It is often grown for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other basil species.

Ocimum vanderystii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
