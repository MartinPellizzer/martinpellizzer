Ornithogalum × wildtii

Ornithogalum × wildtii, commonly known as the hybrid star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of star-like white flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy bulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

The hybrid is a result of crossbreeding between species of the Ornithogalum genus, showcasing genetic diversity within the group.
