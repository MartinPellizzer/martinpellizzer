Oncidium guttatum var. morrenii

Oncidium guttatum var. morrenii, commonly known as the Morren's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

This variety is known for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
