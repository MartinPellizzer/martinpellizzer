Ophthalmophyllum acutum

Ophthalmophyllum acutum, commonly known as the sharp-leaved eyeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Ophthalmophyllum acutum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
