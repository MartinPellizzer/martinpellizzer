Ononis insignis

Ononis insignis, commonly known as the common bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, creeping stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pale pink or white, with five petals, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Ononis insignis is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in open woodlands, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
