Osmanthus integrifolius

Osmanthus integrifolius, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The leaves are simple, entire, and often have a glossy dark green appearance. The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
