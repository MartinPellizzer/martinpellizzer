Ornithogalum dictaeum

Ornithogalum dictaeum, commonly known as the mountain onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum dictaeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
