Oncidium rutkisii

Oncidium rutkisii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Oncidium rutkisii is prized for its vibrant color and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
