Oxalis fenestrata

Oxalis fenestrata, commonly known as the window leaf oxalis, is a striking plant characterized by its unique, fenestrated (window-like) leaves.

Native to South Africa, it belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its delicate, heart-shaped foliage with intricate leaf perforations. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a touch of color to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical features make it both a visually appealing and relatively low-maintenance plant for gardeners.
