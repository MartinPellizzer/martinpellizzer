Ophiopogon sinensis

Ophiopogon sinensis, commonly known as Chinese grass or black root, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are followed by dark purple berries. Its roots are extensively used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ophiopogon sinensis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
