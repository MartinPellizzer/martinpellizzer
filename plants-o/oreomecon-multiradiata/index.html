Oreomecon Multiradiata

Oreomecon multiradiata is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of western China. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring delicate, radiating petals that resemble a sunburst. This species thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Oreomecon multiradiata faces increasing threats to its survival.
