Osmitopsis Afra

Osmitopsis afra is a rare and fascinating succulent native to South Africa. It is known for its unique, star-shaped leaves that resemble tiny satellites orbiting the plant. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice among collectors and enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Osmitopsis afra remains a relatively obscure species in the world of botany.
