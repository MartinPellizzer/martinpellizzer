Oreosedum tenellum

Oreosedum tenellum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to low-maintenance gardens.
