Olearia pinifolia

Olearia pinifolia, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Olearia pinifolia is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
