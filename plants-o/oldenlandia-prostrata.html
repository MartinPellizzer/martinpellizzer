Oldenlandia prostrata

Oldenlandia prostrata, commonly known as creeping mosquito plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with prostrate stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in wetlands and along stream banks.
