Orchiastrum porrifolium

Orchiastrum porrifolium, commonly known as the garlic leaf orchid, is a unique plant with distinctive botanical features.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family, which includes a wide variety of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its broad, garlic-scented leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Orchiastrum porrifolium thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.
