Olax hypoleuca

Olax hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered olax, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a pale green color. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Olax hypoleuca is known for its adaptability to saline and arid conditions, making it a hardy plant in coastal ecosystems.
