Ochlopoa bifida

Ochlopoa bifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which bloom in the early morning. Ochlopoa bifida is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
