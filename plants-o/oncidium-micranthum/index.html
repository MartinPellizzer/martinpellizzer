Oncidium Micranthum

Common oncidium, scientifically known as Oncidium micranthum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America. This plant is prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors including yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Oncidium micranthum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a favorite in botanical gardens and home collections alike.
