Ostericum crassum

Ostericum crassum, commonly known as the broad-leaved ostericum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early summer.

Ostericum crassum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
