Olearia gunniana var. brevipes

Olearia gunniana var. brevipes, commonly known as the Tasmanian dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as heaths and grasslands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in its native region.
