Odontoglossum hauensteinii

Odontoglossum hauensteinii, commonly known as the Hauenstein's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces pendulous, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Odontoglossum hauensteinii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
