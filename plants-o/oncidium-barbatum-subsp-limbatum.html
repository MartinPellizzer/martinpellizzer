Oncidium barbatum subsp. limbatum

Oncidium barbatum subsp.

limbatum, commonly known as the Spotted Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America. It features pendulous, branched inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red.

The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
