Oncidium stenotis

Oncidium stenotis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces racemes of bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns. Oncidium stenotis thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
