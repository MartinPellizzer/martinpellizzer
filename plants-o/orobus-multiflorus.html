Orobus multiflorus

Orobus multiflorus, commonly known as the many-flowered orobus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and the plant has a slender, erect stem.

Orobus multiflorus is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
