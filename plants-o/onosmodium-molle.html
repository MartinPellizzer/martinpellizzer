Onosmodium molle

Onosmodium molle, commonly known as the silky senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its pinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Botanically, it is notable for its medicinal uses and ornamental value.
