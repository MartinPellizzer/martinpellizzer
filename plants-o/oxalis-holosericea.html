Oxalis holosericea

Oxalis holosericea, commonly known as the silky oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, often adorned with red or orange markings. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis holosericea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
