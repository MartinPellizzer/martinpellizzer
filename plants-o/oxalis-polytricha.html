Oxalis polytricha

Oxalis polytricha, commonly known as the coral oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, coral-like inflorescences that resemble miniature flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves with reddish veins and produces small, yellow flowers clustered in a spherical shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis polytricha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
