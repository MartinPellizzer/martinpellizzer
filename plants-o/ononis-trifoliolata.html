Ononis trifoliolata

Ononis trifoliolata, commonly known as three-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with numerous stems that root at the nodes. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, and are often covered in fine hairs.

Ononis trifoliolata thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
