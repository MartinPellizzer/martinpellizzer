Oenothera dubia f. latifolia

Oenothera dubia f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf evening primrose, is a variant of the evening primrose species native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, typically growing in dry, sandy soils. Its flowers produce small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique blooming habit.
