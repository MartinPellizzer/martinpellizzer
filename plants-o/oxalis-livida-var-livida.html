Oxalis livida var. livida

Oxalis livida var. livida, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves with a waxy texture and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in various garden settings.

Oxalis livida var. livida is also valued for its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
