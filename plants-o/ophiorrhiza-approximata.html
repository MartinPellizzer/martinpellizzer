Ophiorrhiza approximata

Ophiorrhiza approximata, commonly known as the Indian bean tree, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are composed of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Ophiorrhiza approximata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
