Oenothera alba

Oenothera alba, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces elongated, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Oenothera alba is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in sandy or well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects.
