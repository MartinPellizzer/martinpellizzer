Oncidium minaxoides

Oncidium minaxoides, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent white lips. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, adding ornamental value to gardens and greenhouses.

Oncidium minaxoides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
