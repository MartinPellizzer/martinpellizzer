Oncidium meirax

Oncidium meirax, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage and its striking, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their long-lasting color and fragrance.

Oncidium meirax requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
