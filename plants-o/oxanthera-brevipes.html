Oxanthera brevipes

Oxanthera brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed oxalid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Oxalidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers.
