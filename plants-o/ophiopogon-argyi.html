Ophiopogon argyi

Ophiopogon argyi, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Japanese grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia, characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments. The plant features rhizomatous growth, allowing it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Ophiopogon argyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
