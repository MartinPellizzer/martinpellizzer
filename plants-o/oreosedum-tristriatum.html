Oreosedum tristriatum

Oreosedum tristriatum, commonly known as the three-striped stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to succulent collections.
