Oxyanthus speciosus subsp. globosus

Oxyanthus speciosus subsp.

globosus, commonly known as the globular oxyanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
