Oxytropis adamsiana subsp. janensis

Oxytropis adamsiana subsp.

janensis, commonly known as the Janenskaya oxytropis, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
