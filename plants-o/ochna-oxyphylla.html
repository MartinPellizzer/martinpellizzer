Ochna oxyphylla

Ochna oxyphylla, commonly known as the African star jelly or yellow star jelly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with five petals, which are arranged in a star-like pattern. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Ochna oxyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
