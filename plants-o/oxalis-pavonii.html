Oxalis pavonii

Oxalis pavonii, commonly known as the rainbow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, colorful flowers that range from pink to violet. The plant belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis pavonii is also noted for its ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
