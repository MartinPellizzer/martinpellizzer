Orbea albocastanea

Orbea albocastanea, commonly known as the white-flowered orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, featuring thick, fleshy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Orbea albocastanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
