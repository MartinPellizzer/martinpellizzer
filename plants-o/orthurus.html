Orthurus

Orthurus, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Orthurus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

It is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
