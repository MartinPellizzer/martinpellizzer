Osterdamia liukiuensis

Osterdamia liukiuensis, commonly known as the Okinawa daisies, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats, showing a preference for well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, and it blooms from late spring to early autumn.

Osterdamia liukiuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
