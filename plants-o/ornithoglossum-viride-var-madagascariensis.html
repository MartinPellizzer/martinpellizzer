Ornithoglossum viride var. madagascariensis

Ornithoglossum viride var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar green orchid, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, orchid-like inflorescence. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinct, tongue-shaped spathe.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
