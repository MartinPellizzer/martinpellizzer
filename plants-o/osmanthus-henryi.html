Osmanthus henryi

Osmanthus henryi, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Osmanthus henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aromatic blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade to full sun.
