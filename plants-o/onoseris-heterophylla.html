Onoseris heterophylla

Onoseris heterophylla, commonly known as the variableleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a sprawling growth habit. Its leaves are variable in shape, ranging from basal to stem leaves, which is reflected in its scientific name.

The flowers are yellow, with a central disk that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
