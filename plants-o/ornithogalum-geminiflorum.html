Ornithogalum geminiflorum

Ornithogalum geminiflorum, commonly known as the double-flowered star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive central yellow spot. The plant produces two rows of small, white flowers on each scape, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Ornithogalum geminiflorum is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
