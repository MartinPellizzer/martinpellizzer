Orianthera centralis

Orianthera centralis, commonly known as the central starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Orianthera centralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
