Otomeria oculata

Otomeria oculata, commonly known as the eye-leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, eye-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are succulent and can store water, adapting it to arid environments.

Otomeria oculata is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking foliage.
