Oftia jasminum

Oftia jasminum, commonly known as the sweet shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a strong, sweet scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Oftia jasminum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and visual appeal.
