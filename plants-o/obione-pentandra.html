Obione pentandra

Obione pentandra, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Obione pentandra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
