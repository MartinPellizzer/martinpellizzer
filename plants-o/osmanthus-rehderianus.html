Osmanthus rehderianus

Osmanthus rehderianus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are highly prized for their aromatic properties and are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a flavoring in teas and confections.

Osmanthus rehderianus thrives in well-drained soil and requires a temperate climate with mild winters.
