Ornithogalum serotinum

Ornithogalum serotinum, commonly known as the late-flowering star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer or autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.
