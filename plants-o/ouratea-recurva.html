Ouratea recurva

Ouratea recurva, commonly known as the silverleaf ouratea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its ornamental value is due to its compact growth habit and striking foliage.

It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and requires well-drained soil.
