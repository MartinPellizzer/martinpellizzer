Ocotea aureotomentosa

Ocotea aureotomentosa, commonly known as golden-tomentose ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Colombia, Ecuador, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are covered in a dense layer of golden-colored pubescence. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

Ocotea aureotomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
