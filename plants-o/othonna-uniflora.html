Othonna uniflora

Othonna uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower othonna, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces a single, showy flower per stem, usually in shades of white or pale yellow. It is adapted to arid conditions and can tolerate salt spray, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Othonna uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
