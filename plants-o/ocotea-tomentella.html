Ocotea tomentella

Ocotea tomentella, commonly known as the velvet ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and aromatic leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

Ocotea tomentella is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its ecological and potential medicinal significance.
