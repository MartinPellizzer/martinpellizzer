Ornithogalum macranthum

Ornithogalum macranthum, commonly known as the giant star-of-Bethlehem, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
