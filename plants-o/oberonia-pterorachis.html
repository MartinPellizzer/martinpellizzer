Oberonia pterorachis

Oberonia pterorachis, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with dark purple petals and a yellow lip, which are highly fragrant. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Oberonia pterorachis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
