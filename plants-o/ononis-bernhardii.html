Ononis bernhardii

Ononis bernhardii, commonly known as the hardstem whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to thrive in dry, rocky environments.
