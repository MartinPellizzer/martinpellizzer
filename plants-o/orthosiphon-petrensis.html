Orthosiphon petrensis

Orthosiphon petrensis, commonly known as the Malabar senna, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.
