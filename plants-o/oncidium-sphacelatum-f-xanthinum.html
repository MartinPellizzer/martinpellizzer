Oncidium sphacelatum f. xanthinum

Oncidium sphacelatum f. xanthinum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its vibrant yellow and green coloration.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
