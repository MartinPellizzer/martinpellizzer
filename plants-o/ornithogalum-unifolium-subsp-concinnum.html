Ornithogalum unifolium subsp. concinnum

Ornithogalum unifolium subsp.

concinnum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features a bulbous stem that produces a single, narrow lance-shaped leaf.

The plant blooms with clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland plantings.
