Oenothera viminea var. parviflora

Oenothera viminea var. parviflora, commonly known as the slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
