Osbeckia chinensis var. lancifolia

Osbeckia chinensis var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved osbeckia, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Osbeckia chinensis var. lancifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
