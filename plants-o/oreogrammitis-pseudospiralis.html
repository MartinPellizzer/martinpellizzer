Oreogrammitis pseudospiralis

Oreogrammitis pseudospiralis, commonly known as the spiral moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green to blackish-green foliage. This moss is known for its resilience to desiccation and is commonly found in temperate and arctic regions.
