Odostemon lanceolatus

Odostemon lanceolatus, commonly known as the lanceleaf odostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct, somewhat rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in umbels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Odostemon lanceolatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
