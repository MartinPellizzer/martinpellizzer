Oncidium leiboldii

Oncidium leiboldii, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features bright, colorful flowers that resemble lemon slices, often in shades of yellow and green. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium leiboldii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
