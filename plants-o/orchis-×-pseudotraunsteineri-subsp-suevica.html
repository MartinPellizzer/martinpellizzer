Orchis × pseudotraunsteineri subsp. suevica

Orchis × pseudotraunsteineri subsp.

suevica, commonly known as the Suevic orchid, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a mix of characteristics from its parent species. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to parts of Central and Eastern Europe.

The plant typically features tall, slender stems with a single spike of purple or violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinctive veining pattern.

This subspecies is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
