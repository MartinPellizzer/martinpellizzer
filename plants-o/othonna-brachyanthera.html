Othonna brachyanthera

Othonna brachyanthera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved othonna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Othonna brachyanthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
