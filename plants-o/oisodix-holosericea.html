Oisodix holosericea

Oisodix holosericea, commonly known as the silky spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Oisodix holosericea is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
