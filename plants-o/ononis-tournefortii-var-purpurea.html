Ononis tournefortii var. purpurea

Ononis tournefortii var. purpurea, commonly known as purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
