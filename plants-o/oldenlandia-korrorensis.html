Oldenlandia korrorensis

Oldenlandia korrorensis, commonly known as the mountain spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Oldenlandia korrorensis is often found in cloud forests and is adapted to the harsh environmental conditions of its native range.
