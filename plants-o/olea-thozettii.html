Olea thozettii

Olea thozettii, commonly known as the African olive, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. Olea thozettii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for dry climates.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
