Onoclea sensibilis var. genuina

Onoclea sensibilis var. genuina, commonly known as the sensitive fern, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features a rhizomatous stem system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, delicate appearance.

The plant is sensitive to environmental changes, hence its common name, and plays an important role in forest ecosystems.
