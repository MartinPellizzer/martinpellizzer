Olearia adenophora

Olearia adenophora, commonly known as the silverweed tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Olearia adenophora is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
