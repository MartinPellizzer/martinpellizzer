Othonna surculosa

Othonna surculosa, commonly known as the woolly bush or mountain dandelion, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, seed-bearing structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Othonna surculosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
