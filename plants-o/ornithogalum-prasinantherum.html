Ornithogalum prasinantherum

Ornithogalum prasinantherum, commonly known as the green-flowered star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, underground bulb from which narrow, grass-like leaves emerge. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with six petals, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is noted for its unique coloration and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
