Ornithogalum vandermerwei

Ornithogalum vandermerwei, commonly known as the white bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
