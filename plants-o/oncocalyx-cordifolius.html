Oncocalyx cordifolius

Oncocalyx cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Oncocalyx cordifolius is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
