Ophiorrhiza recurvipetala

Ophiorrhiza recurvipetala, commonly known as the recurved petal vine, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This plant is often found in moist, shaded habitats in tropical regions of Southeast Asia.
