Ottelia ovalifolia

Ottelia ovalifolia, commonly known as water lentil or floating heart, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its floating leaves that are oval-shaped and have a waxy surface to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. Ottelia ovalifolia is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.

Its unique structure allows it to thrive in aquatic environments while contributing to water oxygenation.
