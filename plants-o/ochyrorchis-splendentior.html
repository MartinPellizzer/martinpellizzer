Ochyrorchis splendentior

Ochyrorchis splendentior, commonly known as the Splendid Sun-orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which are adorned with intricate patterns resembling sunbursts. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in open forest or heathland environments. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, usually from late autumn to early spring.

Ochyrorchis splendentior is noted for its unique morphology and is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
