Ononis spinosa var. microphylla

Ononis spinosa var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf hedgehog bean, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, spiny leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. Its small, oblong leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry, well-drained conditions.
