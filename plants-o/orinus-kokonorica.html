Orinus kokonorica

Orinus kokonorica, commonly known as the Tibetan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Tibet and neighboring areas.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. Orinus kokonorica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
