Ornithogalum pulchellum

Ornithogalum pulchellum, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of star-shaped, white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum pulchellum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
