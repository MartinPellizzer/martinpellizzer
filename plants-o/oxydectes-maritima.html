Oxydectes maritima

Oxydectes maritima, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-blue foliage. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found along shores and cliffs. Its unique morphology helps it withstand harsh maritime conditions.

Oxydectes maritima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
