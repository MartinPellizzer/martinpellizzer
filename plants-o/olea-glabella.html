Olea glabella

Olea glabella, commonly known as the smooth olive, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its simple, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy on both sides. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, spherical fruits. Olea glabella thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
