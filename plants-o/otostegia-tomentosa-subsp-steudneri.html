Otostegia tomentosa subsp. steudneri

Otostegia tomentosa subsp.

steudneri, commonly known as the woolly otostegia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and drought tolerance.
