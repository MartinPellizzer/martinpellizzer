Ornithogalum giganteum

Ornithogalum giganteum, commonly known as the giant star of Bethlehem, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that resemble stars, blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
