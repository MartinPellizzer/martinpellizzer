Oenanthe dielsii subsp. stenophylla

Oenanthe dielsii subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of China. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous areas and forest understories.

The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its small, white flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to other subspecies within the Oenanthe dielsii complex.
