Odontoglossum vulcanicum

Odontoglossum vulcanicum, commonly known as the Volcanic Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers with intricate patterns, often resembling a volcanic landscape, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or volcanic soils, thriving in cool, humid environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its harsh, mountainous habitat.

Odontoglossum vulcanicum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and unique appearance.
