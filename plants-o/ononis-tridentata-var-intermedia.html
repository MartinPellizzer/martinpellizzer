Ononis tridentata var. intermedia

Ononis tridentata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, often hairy stems that spread along the ground. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, trifoliate, and have a distinctive trifid (three-lobed) shape.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, streambanks, and woodland edges.
