Oplonia Minor Var. Vestita

Oplonia minor var. vestita is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring small, clustered leaves and pale yellow flowers. This variant stands out due to its distinct leaf patterns and subtle coloration. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in botanical studies. Its conservation status highlights the need for further research and protection.
