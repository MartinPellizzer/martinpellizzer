Oligosporus pamiricus

Oligosporus pamiricus, commonly known as the Pamir sporegrass, is a rare and specialized plant found in the high-altitude regions of the Pamir Mountains.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing structure and unique adaptations to extreme environmental conditions. The plant produces small, spore-like structures that aid in its reproductive strategy in harsh climates. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs to reduce water loss.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Poaceae, showcasing its close relation to other grass species.
