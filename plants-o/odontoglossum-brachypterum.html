Odontoglossum brachypterum

Odontoglossum brachypterum, commonly known as the short-winged orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer. The plant features a complex inflorescence with numerous flowers arranged in a raceme, and its leaves are broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Odontoglossum brachypterum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
