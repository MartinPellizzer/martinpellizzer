Oligoron

Oligoron, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species native to arid regions.

It features silvery, velvety leaves that help reduce water loss through transpiration. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry conditions.

Oligoron is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
