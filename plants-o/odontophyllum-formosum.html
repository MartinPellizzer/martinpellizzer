Odontophyllum formosum

Odontophyllum formosum, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid or the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Odontophyllum formosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
