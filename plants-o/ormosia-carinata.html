Ormosia carinata

Ormosia carinata, commonly known as the ridged ormosia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Ormosia carinata is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
