Oxymeris glandulifera

Oxymeris glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular oxymeris, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant's leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its stems are often woody and branched. Oxymeris glandulifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.

It is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
