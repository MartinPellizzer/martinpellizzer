Ornithogalum gulnariense

Ornithogalum gulnariense, commonly known as the Gulnariense Star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or limestone soils. It is adapted to temperate climates and requires well-drained conditions to thrive.

Ornithogalum gulnariense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
