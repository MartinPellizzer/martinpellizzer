Orithyia heterophylla

Orithyia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant exhibits notable morphological variation, with leaves that can differ in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
