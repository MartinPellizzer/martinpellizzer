Ottonia colliculorum

Ottonia colliculorum, commonly known as the mountain helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a compact rosette of dark green, hairy leaves and produces striking, hooded flowers with a distinctive greenish-yellow color. Ottonia colliculorum is notable for its ability to grow in poor soil conditions and its preference for cool, humid climates.

It is often found in forested areas and is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
