Ophiopogon fooningensis

Ophiopogon fooningensis, commonly known as the Fooning grass-leaf yam, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its rhizomes are often harvested for their medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Ophiopogon fooningensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and therapeutic uses.
