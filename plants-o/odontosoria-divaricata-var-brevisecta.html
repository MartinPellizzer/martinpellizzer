Odontosoria divaricata var. brevisecta

Odontosoria divaricata var. brevisecta, commonly known as the short-stemmed tooth fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant, often found on rocks or in shaded forest floors. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in shaded gardens.
