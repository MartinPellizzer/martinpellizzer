Onosma echioides subsp. fastigiata

Onosma echioides subsp.

fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs.

The leaves are narrow, linear, and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green coloration. Its flowers are typically purple or violet, arranged in dense spikes at the ends of the stems.

This species thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
