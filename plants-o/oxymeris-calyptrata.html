Oxymeris calyptrata

Oxymeris calyptrata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a fibrous, woody trunk and compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
