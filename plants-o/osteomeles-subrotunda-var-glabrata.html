Osteomeles subrotunda var. glabrata

Osteomeles subrotunda var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-flowered osteomeles, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous small, flat seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
