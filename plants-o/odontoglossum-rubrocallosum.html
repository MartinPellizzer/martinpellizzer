Odontoglossum rubrocallosum

Odontoglossum rubrocallosum, commonly known as the red-collared orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, thriving in cool and humid environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant coloration make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
