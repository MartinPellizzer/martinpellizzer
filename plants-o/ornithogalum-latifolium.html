Ornithogalum latifolium

Ornithogalum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a resilient addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
