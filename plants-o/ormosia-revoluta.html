Ormosia revoluta

Ormosia revoluta, commonly known as the broad-leafed ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, ovate leaflets that provide dense foliage. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in racemes, with a distinctive yellow or greenish hue.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
