Oleandra duidao

Oleandra duidao, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oleandra duidao is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
