Osmanthus ilicifolius var. rotundifolius

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf sweet osmanthus, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late autumn. The leaves are oval-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface. This variety is prized for its aromatic oils used in traditional Chinese medicine and perfumery.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight for optimal growth.
