Ochyrorchis ensifolia

Ochyrorchis ensifolia, commonly known as the sword-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a strong, spicy fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ochyrorchis ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
