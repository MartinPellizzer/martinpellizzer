Ornithogalum flaccidum

Ornithogalum flaccidum, commonly known as the flaccid star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground storage organs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
