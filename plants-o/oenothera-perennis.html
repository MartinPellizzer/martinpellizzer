Oenothera perennis

Oenothera perennis, commonly known as evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Oenothera perennis is also notable for its medicinal uses, particularly in the treatment of skin conditions and inflammation.
