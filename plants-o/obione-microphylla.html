Obione microphylla

Obione microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved obione, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its drought tolerance.

The plant's compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
