Orchis Maculata Subsp. Macrostachys

Orchis maculata subsp. macrostachys is a striking member of the orchid family known for its vibrant purple and yellow petals. This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist woodland areas. Its unique floral structure and eye-catching coloration make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The plant blooms in late spring, adding a splash of color to the natural landscape. Despite its beauty, it is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
