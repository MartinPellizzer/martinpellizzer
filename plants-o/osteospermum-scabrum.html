Osteospermum scabrum

Osteospermum scabrum, commonly known as the rough osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy, glandular leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are showy, with yellow centers surrounded by blue to violet ray florets.

Osteospermum scabrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
