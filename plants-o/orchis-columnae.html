Orchis columnae

Orchis columnae, commonly known as the columned orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers arranged in a loose spike, with a distinctive column-like structure in the center of each flower. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Orchis columnae is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
