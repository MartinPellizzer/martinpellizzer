Oberonia mindorensis

Oberonia mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Oberonia mindorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
