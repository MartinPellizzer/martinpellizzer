Ononis natrix var. clausoniana

Ononis natrix var. clausoniana, commonly known as the narrowleaf bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually narrow and lance-shaped. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences and are typically pink or pale purple in color.

It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and along woodland edges.
