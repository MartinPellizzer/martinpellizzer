Ozoroa obovata var. obovata

Ozoroa obovata var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed ozoroa, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features simple, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth margin and a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive species within the Rubiaceae family.
