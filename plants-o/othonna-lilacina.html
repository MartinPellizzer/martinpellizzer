Othonna lilacina

Othonna lilacina, commonly known as the lilac ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to lilac flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Othonna lilacina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
