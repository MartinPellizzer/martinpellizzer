Oenothera × heiniana

Oenothera × heiniana, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a hybrid species native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Oenothera × heiniana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
