Odontites tenuifolius

Odontites tenuifolius, commonly known as slender-leaved toothwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
