Oberonioides maingayi

Oberonioides maingayi, commonly known as the African orchid, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its elegant, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along riverbanks. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, while its inflorescence bears a cluster of showy, colorful flowers.

Oberonioides maingayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
