Onopordum prjachinii

Onopordum prjachinii, commonly known as the Przhevalsk's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
