Octamyrtus elegans

Octamyrtus elegans, commonly known as the elegant myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Octamyrtus elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
