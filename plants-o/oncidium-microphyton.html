Oncidium microphyton

Oncidium microphyton, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, meaning it grows in successive shoots from the base. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium microphyton is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
