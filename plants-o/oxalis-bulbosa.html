Oxalis bulbosa

Oxalis bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant blooms with delicate, five-petaled flowers that are typically pale yellow or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
