Oncidium microglossum

Oncidium microglossum, commonly known as the miniature tongue orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Oncidium microglossum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of cultivation and ornamental value.
