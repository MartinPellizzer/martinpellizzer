Oncidium gravesianum

Oncidium gravesianum, commonly known as the Showy Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of yellow and orange hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium gravesianum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
