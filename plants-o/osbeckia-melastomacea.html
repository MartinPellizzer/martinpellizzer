Osbeckia melastomacea

Osbeckia melastomacea, commonly known as the purplestem sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. Osbeckia melastomacea plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
