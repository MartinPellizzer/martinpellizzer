Obesia serrulata

Obesia serrulata, commonly known as the serrated obesia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, ovate leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Obesia serrulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
