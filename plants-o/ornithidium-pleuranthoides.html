Ornithidium pleuranthoides

Ornithidium pleuranthoides, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained conditions and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
