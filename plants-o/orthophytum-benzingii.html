Orthophytum benzingii

Orthophytum benzingii, commonly known as the Benzing's feather plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and its long, narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces striking red or orange inflorescences that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Orthophytum benzingii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
