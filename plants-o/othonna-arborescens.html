Othonna arborescens

Othonna arborescens, commonly known as the tree othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features a woody stem with dense, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Othonna arborescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
