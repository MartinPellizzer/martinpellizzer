Octopleura loeselioides var. grandiflora

Octopleura loeselioides var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered octopleura, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are often pink or red in hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
