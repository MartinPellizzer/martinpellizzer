Ononis viscosa var. clausonis

Ononis viscosa var. clausonis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal and dune habitats.
