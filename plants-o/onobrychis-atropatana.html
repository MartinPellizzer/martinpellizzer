Onobrychis atropatana

Onobrychis atropatana, commonly known as the dark purple vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep violet. Its root system has nitrogen-fixing capabilities, making it valuable for soil enrichment.

The seeds are typically enclosed in narrow, pod-like fruits that dehisce to release the seeds.
