Olearia stellulata var. quercifolia

Olearia stellulata var. quercifolia, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in dense clusters during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well adapted to coastal environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
