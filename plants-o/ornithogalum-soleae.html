Ornithogalum soleae

Ornithogalum soleae, commonly known as the solea lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum soleae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
