Orania subdisticha

Orania subdisticha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orania, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a distichous pattern. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Orania subdisticha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
