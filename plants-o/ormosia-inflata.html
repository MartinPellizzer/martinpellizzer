Ormosia inflata

Ormosia inflata, commonly known as the inflated ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, inflated pods that resemble small balloons. The plant has compound leaves with several leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like or small tree-like, depending on the environment.

Ormosia inflata is valued for its ornamental features and has traditional uses in some regions.
