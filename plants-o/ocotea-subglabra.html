Ocotea subglabra

Ocotea subglabra, commonly known as the smooth ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces aromatic, egg-shaped berries.

Ocotea subglabra is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
