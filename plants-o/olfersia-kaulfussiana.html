Olfersia kaulfussiana

Olfersia kaulfussiana, commonly known as the Christmas star or star anise, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped leaves and has a woody stem with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its fruit, which resembles a star, is the primary part used in culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its fragrant seeds.
