Ornithogalum monarchos

Ornithogalum monarchos, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous underground stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum monarchos is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
