Oplismenus mollissimus

Oplismenus mollissimus, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by winged seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some regions.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural areas.
