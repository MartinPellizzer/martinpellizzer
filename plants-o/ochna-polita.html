Ochna polita

Ochna polita, commonly known as the white ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ochna polita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
