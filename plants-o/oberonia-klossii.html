Oberonia klossii

Oberonia klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Indonesia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, creeping rhizome and produces compact, leafy pseudobulbs. The plant has elliptic to ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and arranged in a raceme, often in shades of white, yellow, or pink.

Oberonia klossii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates for optimal growth.
