Oleandra ornata

Oleandra ornata, commonly known as the ornate oleander, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. Oleandra ornata is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
