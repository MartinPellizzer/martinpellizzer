Oxalis pretoensis

Oxalis pretoensis, commonly known as Black Oxalis, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis pretoensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
