Otamplis vitifolia

Otamplis vitifolia, commonly known as the grapeleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Otamplis vitifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
