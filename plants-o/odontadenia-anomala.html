Odontadenia anomala

Odontadenia anomala, commonly known as the Cape York devil's backbone, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom during the wet season, typically from November to March. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.
