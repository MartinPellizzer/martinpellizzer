Oxytropis borealis var. sulphurea

Oxytropis borealis var. sulphurea, commonly known as yellow burr medic, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in cold, dry environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by spiny, pod-like fruits. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and plays a role in stabilizing coastal and tundra ecosystems.
