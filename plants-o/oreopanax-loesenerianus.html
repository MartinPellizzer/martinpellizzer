Oreopanax loesenerianus

Oreopanax loesenerianus, commonly known as the mountain sarsaparilla, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length, with long, slender leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by woody, spiny fruits containing several seeds. Oreopanax loesenerianus thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in mountainous regions.
