Oncidium carthagenense var. swartzii

Oncidium carthagenense var. swartzii, commonly known as the Swartz's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, showy inflorescences with brightly colored, fragrant flowers that typically range from yellow to orange. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
