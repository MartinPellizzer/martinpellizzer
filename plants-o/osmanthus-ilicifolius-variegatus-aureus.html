Osmanthus ilicifolius variegatus-aureus

Osmanthus ilicifolius variegatus-aureus, commonly known as the golden osmanthus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring a mix of yellow, white, and green hues on its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in late autumn, which are highly valued for their sweet, honey-like scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

Its unique appearance and aromatic blooms make it a sought-after addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
