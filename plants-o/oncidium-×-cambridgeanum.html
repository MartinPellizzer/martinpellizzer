Oncidium × cambridgeanum

Oncidium × cambridgeanum, commonly known as the Cambridge orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and grassy, often arranged in a fan shape.

Oncidium × cambridgeanum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
