Oberonia pulchrebracteata

Oberonia pulchrebracteata, commonly known as the beautiful bract orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that are often more prominent than the actual flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Oberonia pulchrebracteata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
