Oncidium victor

Oncidium victor, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, butterfly-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium victor requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
