Olea yuennanensis var. xeromorpha

Olea yuennanensis var. xeromorpha, commonly known as the Yunnan olive, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by olive-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and has a moderate growth rate.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is often used in xeriscaping and as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
