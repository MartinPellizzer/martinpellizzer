Ottleya argyraea

Ottleya argyraea, commonly known as the silverleaf ottleya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Ottleya argyraea is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
