Oxydectes subcomosa

Oxydectes subcomosa, commonly known as the silky leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
