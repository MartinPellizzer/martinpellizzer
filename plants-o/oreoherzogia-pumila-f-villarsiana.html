Oreoherzogia pumila f. villarsiana

Oreoherzogia pumila f. villarsiana, commonly known as the dwarf desert dandelion, is a small flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America.

The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is adapted to harsh desert conditions, with deep roots that help it survive in sandy soils.
