Olearia virgata

Olearia virgata, commonly known as the silver tree, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Olearia virgata is well adapted to coastal environments and is often found in sandy soils along the shores of New Zealand.
