Orychophragmus hupehensis

Orychophragmus hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
