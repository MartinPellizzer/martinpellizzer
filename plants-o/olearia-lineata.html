Olearia lineata

Olearia lineata, commonly known as the silver tuft or silver mist, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Olearia lineata thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
