Orchiodes rubicundum

Orchiodes rubicundum, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red lips and greenish-yellow petals, which make it visually distinctive. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually short-lived but highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

Orchiodes rubicundum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
