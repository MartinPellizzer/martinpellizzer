Osmanthus heterophyllus var. bibracteatus

Osmanthus heterophyllus var. bibracteatus, commonly known as the Chinese osmanthus or sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant has opposite, elliptic leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties in traditional Chinese horticulture.
