Ornithogalum pearsonii

Ornithogalum pearsonii, commonly known as the pearson's star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum pearsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
