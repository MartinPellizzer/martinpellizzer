Orchis Quadripunctata F. Albiflora

Orchis quadripunctata f. albiflora is a striking variant of the four-spotted orchid known for its unique white floral display. This rare and elegant plant thrives in specific alpine and montane habitats across Europe. Its delicate, pale blooms contrast sharply with the typical purple hues of its parent species. The plant's beauty makes it a subject of fascination for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile and endangered flora.
