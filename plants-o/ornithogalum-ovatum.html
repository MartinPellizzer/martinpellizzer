Ornithogalum ovatum

Ornithogalum ovatum, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
