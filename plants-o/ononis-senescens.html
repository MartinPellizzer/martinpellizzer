Ononis senescens

Ononis senescens, commonly known as old man's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, pinnate, and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Ononis senescens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in rocky or sandy habitats.
