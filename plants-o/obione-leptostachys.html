Obione leptostachys

Obione leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
