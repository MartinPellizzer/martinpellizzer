Ononis rosea

Ononis rosea, commonly known as rose dogbane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
