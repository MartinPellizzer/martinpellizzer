Odontoglossum nebulosum var. guttatum

Odontoglossum nebulosum var. guttatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with distinctive markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
