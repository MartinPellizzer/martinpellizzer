Oenothera picensis subsp. bonariensis

Oenothera picensis subsp.

bonariensis, commonly known as the Argentine evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the genus Oenothera, which includes various species of evening primroses.

This subspecies is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that typically bloom in the evening. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
