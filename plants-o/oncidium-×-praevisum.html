Oncidium × praevisum

Oncidium × praevisum, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green floral displays, which resemble the wings of a butterfly. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Oncidium × praevisum requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
