Olearia velutina

Olearia velutina, commonly known as velvet olearia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. Its flowers are small and white, clustered in dense inflorescences, and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Olearia velutina is often found in coastal and forested areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
