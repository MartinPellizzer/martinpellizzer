Orchidantha chinensis

Orchidantha chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces a spike of small, clustered blossoms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Orchidantha chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
