Olyra obliquifolia

Olyra obliquifolia, commonly known as the zigzag umbrella tree, is a unique species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence that consists of multiple clusters of small white flowers. The plant has compound leaves with a zigzag pattern along the midrib, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, tropical conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Olyra obliquifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and unusual foliage.
