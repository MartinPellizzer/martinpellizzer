Ornithogalum merxmuelleri

Ornithogalum merxmuelleri, commonly known as the silver bulbous plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale green flowers. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

Ornithogalum merxmuelleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
