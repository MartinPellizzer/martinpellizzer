Ornithopus compressus f. longicornis

Ornithopus compressus f. longicornis, commonly known as the long-beak salsify, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces yellow flowers in elongated racemes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
