Orania oreophila

Orania oreophila, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires a cool, moist environment to flourish. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Orania oreophila is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
