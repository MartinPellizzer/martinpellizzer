Oreosyce triangularis

Oreosyce triangularis, commonly known as the triangular orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, triangular-shaped leaves and vibrant, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
