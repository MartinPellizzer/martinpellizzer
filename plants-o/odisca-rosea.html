Odisca rosea

Odisca rosea, commonly known as the rose-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep rose. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, with a smooth texture.

Odisca rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
