Oxydectes nigroviridis

Oxydectes nigroviridis, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and its distinctive dark purple veins. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a subtle greenish tint. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Oxydectes nigroviridis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
