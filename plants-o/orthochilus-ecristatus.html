Orthochilus ecristatus

Orthochilus ecristatus, commonly known as the smooth-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers with a smooth lip. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool and moist conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
