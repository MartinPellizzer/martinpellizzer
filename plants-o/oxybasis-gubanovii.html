Oxybasis gubanovii

Oxybasis gubanovii, commonly known as the Gubanovii goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

Oxybasis gubanovii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its resilience in difficult climates.
