Onobrychis melanotricha f. robusta

Onobrychis melanotricha f. robusta, commonly known as the robust burr clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are followed by hairy, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and disturbed sites.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
