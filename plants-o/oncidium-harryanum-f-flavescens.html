Oncidium harryanum f. flavescens

Oncidium harryanum f. flavescens, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its vibrant yellow and green coloration.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
