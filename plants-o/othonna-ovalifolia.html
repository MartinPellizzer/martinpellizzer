Othonna ovalifolia

Othonna ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved Othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Othonna ovalifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
