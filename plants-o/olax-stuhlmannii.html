Olax stuhlmannii

Olax stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Stuhlmann's olax, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
