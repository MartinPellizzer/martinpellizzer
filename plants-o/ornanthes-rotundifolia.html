Ornanthes rotundifolia

Ornanthes rotundifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments.
