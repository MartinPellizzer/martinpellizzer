Onoseris trinervis

Onoseris trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. Its leaves are alternate, three-veined, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow with a central disk, and the plant produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind.
