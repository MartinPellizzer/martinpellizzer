Oncidium panduriforme

Oncidium panduriforme, commonly known as the panduriform orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with dark markings. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium panduriforme is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
