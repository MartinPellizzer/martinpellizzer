Odontites luteus var. brachystemon

Odontites luteus var. brachystemon, commonly known as the yellow toothbrush plant, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated in gardens.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
