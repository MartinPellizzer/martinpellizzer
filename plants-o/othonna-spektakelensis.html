Othonna spektakelensis

Othonna spektakelensis, commonly known as the spectacle plant, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color, with a unique, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna spektakelensis is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid conditions.
