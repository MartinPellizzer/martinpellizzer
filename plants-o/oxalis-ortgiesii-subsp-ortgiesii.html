Oxalis ortgiesii subsp. ortgiesii

Oxalis ortgiesii subsp.

ortgiesii, commonly known as the Cape buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
