Oxydectes ambanivoulensis

Oxydectes ambanivoulensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spider orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption.
