Ononis repens subsp. australis

Ononis repens subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Australia. It features slender, creeping stems that root at the nodes, forming dense mats on sandy or gravelly soils.

The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in clusters during spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

It is often found in coastal dunes and disturbed sandy habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environments.
