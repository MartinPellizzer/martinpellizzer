Ornithogalum seineri

Ornithogalum seineri, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its flowers are often borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum seineri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
