Orchis cubitalis

Orchis cubitalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few leaves and a single spike of flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive hooded lip.

Orchis cubitalis is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
