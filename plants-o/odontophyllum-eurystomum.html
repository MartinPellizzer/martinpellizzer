Odontophyllum eurystomum

Odontophyllum eurystomum, commonly known as the broad-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Odontophyllum eurystomum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
