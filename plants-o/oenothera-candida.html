Oenothera candida

Oenothera candida, commonly known as the white evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Oenothera candida is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
