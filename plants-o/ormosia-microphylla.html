Ormosia microphylla

Ormosia microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf ormosia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elliptical, leathery fruits. Ormosia microphylla is often found in shaded, moist forest environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
