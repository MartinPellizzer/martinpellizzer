Oudemansia obversa

Oudemansia obversa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinctive silver or white underside.

Oudemansia obversa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
