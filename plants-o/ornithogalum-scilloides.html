Ornithogalum scilloides

Ornithogalum scilloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of star-shaped, white flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often mistaken for grasses or sedges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Ornithogalum scilloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
