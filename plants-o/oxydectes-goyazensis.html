Oxydectes goyazensis

Oxydectes goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis orchid, is a rare and unique species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
