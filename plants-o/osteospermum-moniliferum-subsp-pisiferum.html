Osteospermum moniliferum subsp. pisiferum

Osteospermum moniliferum subsp.

pisiferum, commonly known as the Cape fleur or blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to landscapes.
