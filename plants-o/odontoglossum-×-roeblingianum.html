Odontoglossum × roeblingianum

Odontoglossum × roeblingianum, commonly known as the Roebling's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that often display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant typically grows at high elevations, thriving in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its native habitat.

Odontoglossum × roeblingianum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and adaptability in cultivation.
