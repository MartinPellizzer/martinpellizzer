Ornitrophe schmidelia

Ornitrophe schmidelia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Its unique morphology includes tubular leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a striking addition to specialized botanical collections.
