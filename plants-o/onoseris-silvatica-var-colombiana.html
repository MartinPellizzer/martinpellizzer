Onoseris silvatica var. colombiana

Onoseris silvatica var. colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian forest aster, is a perennial herb native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. The plant features erect, hairy stems that bear alternate, lanceolate leaves with serrated edges. Its showy, daisy-like flowers are yellow with a central disc, blooming in late summer to early autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators in its native habitat.
