Ononis syriaca

Ononis syriaca, commonly known as Syrian catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with slender stems and small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to arid environments and suitable for use in xeriscaping.
