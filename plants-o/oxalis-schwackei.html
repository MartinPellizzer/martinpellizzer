Oxalis schwackei

Oxalis schwackei, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis schwackei is also noted for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense mats in suitable conditions.
