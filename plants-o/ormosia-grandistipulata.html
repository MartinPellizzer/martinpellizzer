Ormosia grandistipulata

Ormosia grandistipulata, commonly known as the large-leaf ormosia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which consist of multiple leaflets arranged along a central axis. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elongated, woody pods containing several seeds. Ormosia grandistipulata is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
