Othonna rufibarbis

Othonna rufibarbis, commonly known as the red-haired othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, which are borne on slender, branching stems. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Othonna rufibarbis is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appearance and low water requirements.
