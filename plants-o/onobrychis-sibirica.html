Onobrychis sibirica

Onobrychis sibirica, commonly known as Siberian clover, is a perennial legume native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Onobrychis sibirica is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural settings.
