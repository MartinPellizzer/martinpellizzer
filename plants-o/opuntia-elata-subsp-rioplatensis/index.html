Opuntia Elata Subsp. Rioplatensis

Palo de palo, scientifically known as Opuntia elata subsp. rioplatensis, is a resilient cactus native to South America. This species thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions with its unique morphology. Its flat, spineless stems serve as water storage structures and provide shade for desert animals. The plant is also valued for its edible fruits and traditional medicinal uses among local communities. Palo de palo plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
