Ornithogalum magnificum

Ornithogalum magnificum, commonly known as the magnolia lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and produces narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
