Ononis angustissima subsp. falcata

Ononis angustissima subsp.

falcata, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, often succulent stems that are typically covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in pairs along the stems.

This subspecies is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, thriving in dry, sunny environments.
