Onobrychis ebenoides f. hybrida

Onobrychis ebenoides f. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural and ecological restoration contexts.

The hybrid form exhibits variability in growth habit and flower color compared to its parent species.
