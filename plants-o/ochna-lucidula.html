Ochna lucidula

Ochna lucidula, commonly known as the shining ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by orange-red berries. Ochna lucidula thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal flowers.
