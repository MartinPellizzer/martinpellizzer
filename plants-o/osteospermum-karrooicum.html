Osteospermum karrooicum

Osteospermum karrooicum, commonly known as the Karoo osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue to purple daisy-like flowers. The plant has gray-green, hairy leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds in its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Osteospermum karrooicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
