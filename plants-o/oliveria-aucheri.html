Oliveria aucheri

Oliveria aucheri, commonly known as silverleaf germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Oliveria aucheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract beneficial insects.
