Oreodaphne citrosmoides var. reticulata

Oreodaphne citrosmoides var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate oreodaphne, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptic leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
