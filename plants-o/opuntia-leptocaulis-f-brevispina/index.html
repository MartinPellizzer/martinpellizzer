Opuntia Leptocaulis F. Brevispina

Opuntia leptocaulis f. brevispina is a unique cactus species known for its compact and spiny growth habit. Native to the arid regions of North America, it thrives in harsh environments with minimal water. Its short, stout stems are covered in dense clusters of small, stiff spines. This variety is prized for its resilience and ornamental value in both gardens and collections. It offers a fascinating example of adaptation to extreme climates.
