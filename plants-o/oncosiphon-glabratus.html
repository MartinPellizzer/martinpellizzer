Oncosiphon glabratus

Oncosiphon glabratus, commonly known as the smooth senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
