Opuntia Erinacea Var. Aurea

Opuntia erinacea var. aurea is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its vibrant yellow stems and clusters of bright orange flowers, it stands out among other cacti. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for desert gardens. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
