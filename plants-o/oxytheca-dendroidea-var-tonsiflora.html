Oxytheca dendroidea var. tonsiflora

Oxytheca dendroidea var. tonsiflora, commonly known as the woolly tree heath, is a shrub-like plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
