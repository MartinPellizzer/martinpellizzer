Olax macrocalyx

Olax macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered olax, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Olax macrocalyx is often found in woodland and forest margins, where it thrives in partial to full sunlight.
