Onopordum murcicum

Onopordum murcicum, commonly known as the Murcian thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and purple or pink flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant has deeply lobed, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and wastelands.

Onopordum murcicum is a perennial species that can grow up to 2 meters in height, making it a striking feature in its native environment.
