Otholobium pictum

Otholobium pictum, commonly known as the painted bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they consist of three leaflets, and the plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.
