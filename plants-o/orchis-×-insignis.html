Orchis × insignis

Orchis × insignis, commonly known as the showy orchis, is a hybrid species within the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear numerous showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and is found across much of Europe. Its flowers have a distinct three-lobed lip, which is a key feature in orchid identification.

Orchis × insignis is a perennial herb that thrives in nutrient-rich soils and is often associated with other orchid species in mixed populations.
