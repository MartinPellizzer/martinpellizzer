Obetia ficifolia

Obetia ficifolia, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue or purple flowers in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in well-drained soils.

Obetia ficifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
