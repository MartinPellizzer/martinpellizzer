Ochna atropurpurea var. natalitia

Ochna atropurpurea var. natalitia, commonly known as Natal ochna, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves that are often reddish-purple in color, especially when young. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
