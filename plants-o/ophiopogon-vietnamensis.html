Ophiopogon vietnamensis

Ophiopogon vietnamensis, commonly known as Vietnamese spathiphyllum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ophiopogon vietnamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
