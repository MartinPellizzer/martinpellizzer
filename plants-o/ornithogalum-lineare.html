Ornithogalum lineare

Ornithogalum lineare, commonly known as the linear star-of-Bethlem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, making it a notable addition to gardens.

Ornithogalum lineare is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
