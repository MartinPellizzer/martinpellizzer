Oenothera muricata subsp. parviflora

Oenothera muricata subsp.

parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered evening primrose, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a herbaceous perennial with yellow flowers that bloom in the evening.

The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsules containing numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
