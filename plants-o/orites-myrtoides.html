Orites myrtoides

Orites myrtoides, commonly known as the silver myrtle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, pale green, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in spring.

Orites myrtoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
