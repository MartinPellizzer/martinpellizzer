Oxygonum dregeanum subsp. dregeanum

Oxygonum dregeanum subsp.

dregeanum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. It is adapted to thrive in full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.

The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.
