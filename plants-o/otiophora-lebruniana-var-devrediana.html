Otiophora lebruniana var. devrediana

Otiophora lebruniana var. devrediana, commonly known as the Devred's rock cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.
