Oxleya xanthoxyla

Oxleya xanthoxyla, commonly known as the yellow-wood tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale yellow bark and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its fruits are woody capsules containing several winged seeds.

Oxleya xanthoxyla is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
