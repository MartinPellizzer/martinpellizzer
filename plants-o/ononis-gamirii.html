Ononis gamirii

Ononis gamirii, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its root system is adapted to stabilize sandy soils, making it a valuable plant in ecological restoration projects.

Ononis gamirii is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, showcasing its resilience and adaptability.
