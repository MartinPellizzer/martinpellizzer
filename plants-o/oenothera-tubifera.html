Oenothera tubifera

Oenothera tubifera, commonly known as the yellow evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. Oenothera tubifera is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid environments.
