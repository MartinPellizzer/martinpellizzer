Otites sendtneri

Otites sendtneri, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grassland habitats. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It has a short, underground rhizome and narrow, grass-like leaves.

Otites sendtneri is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
