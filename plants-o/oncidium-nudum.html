Oncidium nudum

Oncidium nudum, commonly known as the naked orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, branching growth habit and its ability to produce multiple flowers along the stems. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Oncidium nudum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
