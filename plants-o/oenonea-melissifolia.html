Oenonea melissifolia

Oenonea melissifolia, commonly known as the honeyleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its leaves are used in traditional herbal medicine for their soothing properties.

Oenonea melissifolia is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
