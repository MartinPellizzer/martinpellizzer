Odontocarya krukoviana

Odontocarya krukoviana, commonly known as the Krukovian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it an interesting subject for botanical study and cultivation.
