Osteospermum moniliferum subsp. canescens

Osteospermum moniliferum subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the silverhead osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its silvery-white, fuzzy foliage and produces daisy-like yellow flowers with dark centers.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative garden plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
