Orbeopsis

Orbeopsis, commonly known as the starfruit or carambola, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Averrhoa within the family Malvaceae. The plant produces a distinctive five-lobed fruit that turns yellow when ripe. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy surface.

Orbeopsis is valued both for its edible fruit and its ornamental appeal.
