Orobus luteus var. orientalis

Orobus luteus var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental yellow orobus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in temperate climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its vibrant blooms.

Its seeds are also used in traditional herbal medicine for their potential health benefits.
