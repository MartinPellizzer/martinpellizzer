Oxalis zeyheri

Oxalis zeyheri, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Oxalis zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
