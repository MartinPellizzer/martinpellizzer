Otomeria volubilis

Otomeria volubilis, commonly known as the climbing otomeria, is a perennial vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Otomeria volubilis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
