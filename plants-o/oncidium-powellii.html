Oncidium powellii

Oncidium powellii, commonly known as the yellow butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences with clusters of yellow and green flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and requires bright, indirect light to thrive. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.

Oncidium powellii is often grown in pots with well-draining soil and benefits from regular watering and good air circulation.
