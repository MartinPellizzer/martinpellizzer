Onagra muricata

Onagra muricata, commonly known as the rough nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and small, dark purple berries that ripen in the fall. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Due to its toxic berries, it should be handled with care.
