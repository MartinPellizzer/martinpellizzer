Ornithogalum fimbriatum subsp. gracilipes

Ornithogalum fimbriatum subsp.

gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, linear leaves that are often tinged with a pale green or bluish hue.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers with six petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
