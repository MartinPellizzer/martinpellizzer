Osodendron altissimum

Osodendron altissimum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows up to several meters in height, making it one of the tallest orchid species. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

Osodendron altissimum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.
