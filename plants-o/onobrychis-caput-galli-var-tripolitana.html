Onobrychis caput-galli var. tripolitana

Onobrychis caput-galli var. tripolitana, commonly known as the Tripolitan crown vetch, is a perennial legume native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in reclamation projects.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil fertility.
