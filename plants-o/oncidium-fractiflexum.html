Oncidium fractiflexum

Oncidium fractiflexum, commonly known as the Flexile Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, racemose inflorescence that bears numerous fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, anchoring itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Oncidium fractiflexum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
