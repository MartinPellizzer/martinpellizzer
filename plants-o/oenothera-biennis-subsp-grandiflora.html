Oenothera biennis subsp. grandiflora

Oenothera biennis subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as showy evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America. It features showy, orange-yellow flowers that open in the evening and close by morning.

The plant has hairy, branched stems and lance-shaped, hairy leaves. It produces capsules containing numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
