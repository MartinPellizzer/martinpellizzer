Orvala

Orvala, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions. Its flowers come in a wide range of colors, including purple, blue, white, and pink.
