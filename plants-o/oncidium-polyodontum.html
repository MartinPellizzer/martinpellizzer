Oncidium polyodontum

Oncidium polyodontum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Forest, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with intricate, tooth-like markings on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments with good air circulation.

Oncidium polyodontum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
