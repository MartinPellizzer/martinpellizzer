Octomeria zygoglossa

Octomeria zygoglossa, commonly known as the velvetleaf octomeria, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
