Ochna kibbiensis

Ochna kibbiensis, commonly known as the Kibbi ochna, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display vibrant red or orange hues. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. Ochna kibbiensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
