Orbea miscella

Orbea miscella, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, are usually pink or purple and have a distinctive tubular shape.

Orbea miscella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
