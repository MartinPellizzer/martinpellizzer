Oberonia miniata

Oberonia miniata, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Oberonia miniata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
