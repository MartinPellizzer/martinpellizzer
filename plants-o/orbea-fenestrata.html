Orbea fenestrata

Orbea fenestrata, commonly known as the window leaf orbea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and distinctive leaves that have a translucent or window-like appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Orbea fenestrata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
