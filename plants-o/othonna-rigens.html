Othonna rigens

Othonna rigens, commonly known as the rigid othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy foliage that is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
