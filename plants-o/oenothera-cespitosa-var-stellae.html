Oenothera cespitosa var. stellae

Oenothera cespitosa var. stellae, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
