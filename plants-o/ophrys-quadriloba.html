Ophrys quadriloba

Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture, and it produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers in late spring to early summer.

Ophrys quadriloba is primarily found in Europe and is known for its specialized relationship with specific pollinators.
