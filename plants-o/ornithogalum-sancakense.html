Ornithogalum sancakense

Ornithogalum sancakense, commonly known as the Turkish star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its bulbs are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Ornithogalum sancakense thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
