Oncidium fuscopetalum

Oncidium fuscopetalum, commonly known as the brown-petaled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, linear leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating. The plant produces pendulous racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with brown markings, blooming during the dry season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Oncidium fuscopetalum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
