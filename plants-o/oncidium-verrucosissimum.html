Oncidium verrucosissimum

Oncidium verrucosissimum, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features long, narrow leaves and has a complex root system adapted for epiphytic growth. Oncidium verrucosissimum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
