Ochna pisiformis

Ochna pisiformis, commonly known as the African star fruit or yellow star.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers and elliptical, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

Ochna pisiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruit.
