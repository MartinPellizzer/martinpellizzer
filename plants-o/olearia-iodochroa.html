Olearia iodochroa

Olearia iodochroa, commonly known as the silver frost plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a frost-covered appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its overall appearance. Olearia iodochroa thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique coloration and compact growth habit.
