Ornithogalum nelsonii

Ornithogalum nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's star-of-Bethlem, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that resemble a cluster of stars, hence its common name. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to thrive in arid conditions and is typically found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Ornithogalum nelsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
