Orchis palmata

Orchis palmata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear clusters of showy, violet or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Orchis palmata is often found in nutrient-rich soils and is a common indicator species in temperate regions.
