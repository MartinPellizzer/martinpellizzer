Oncidium × coradinei

Oncidium × coradinei, commonly known as the coral orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from Oncidium and Cymbidium.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, with strap-like leaves and a branched inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium × coradinei is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
