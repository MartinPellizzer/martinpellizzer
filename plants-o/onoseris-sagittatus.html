Onoseris sagittatus

Onoseris sagittatus, commonly known as the arrowhead dandelion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets. Onoseris sagittatus is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
