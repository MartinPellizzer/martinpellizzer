Otopappus aschenbornii

Otopappus aschenbornii, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

The plant features a basal rosette of gray-green, woolly leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are yellow and resemble those of a dandelion, blooming in late spring to early summer.
