Orostachys komarovii

Orostachys komarovii, commonly known as the stonecress or desert stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Orostachys komarovii is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand extreme temperatures and drought conditions.
