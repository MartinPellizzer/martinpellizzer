Olax humilis

Olax humilis, commonly known as the small olax, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, simple leaves that are usually opposite or alternate in arrangement. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and they are clustered in inflorescences.

Olax humilis is known for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is often used in traditional medicine.
