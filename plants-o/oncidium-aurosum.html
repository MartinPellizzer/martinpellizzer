Oncidium aurosum

Oncidium aurosum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow and green flowers that resemble a cluster of golden candles, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in varying environmental conditions. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat.

Oncidium aurosum requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
