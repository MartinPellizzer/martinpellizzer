Osmanthus fordii

Osmanthus fordii, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and have a leathery texture. Its flowers are highly valued for their aromatic properties and are often used in perfumes and traditional Chinese medicine.

Osmanthus fordii thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
