Oncidium ornithorhynchum var. albiflorum

Oncidium ornithorhynchum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the White-flowered Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a fan shape, and it produces pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
