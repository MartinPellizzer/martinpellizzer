Ornithogalum unifolium f. plurifolium

Ornithogalum unifolium f. plurifolium, commonly known as the many-leaved star-of-Bethleem, is a botanical variant of the star-of-Bethleem plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive, clustered inflorescence of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variant is often cultivated for its elegant appearance and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
