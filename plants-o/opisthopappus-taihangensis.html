Opisthopappus taihangensis

Opisthopappus taihangensis, commonly known as the Taihang mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and it produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
