Ornithogalum clavatum

Ornithogalum clavatum, commonly known as the clustered star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous base with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is part of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape.
