Ochna thyrsoidea

Ochna thyrsoidea, commonly known as the yellow ochna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in terminal or axillary clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry woodland or savanna habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
