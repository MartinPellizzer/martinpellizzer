Orthophytum santosianum

Orthophytum santosianum, commonly known as the Santosian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the Atlantic Forest region. It has strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with colorful, fragrant blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture, though it remains relatively rare in the wild.
