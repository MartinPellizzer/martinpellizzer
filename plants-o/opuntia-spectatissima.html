Opuntia spectatissima

Opuntia spectatissima, commonly known as the showy prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and covered in sharp spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Opuntia spectatissima is well adapted to arid environments and plays an important role in local ecosystems.
