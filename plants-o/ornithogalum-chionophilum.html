Ornithogalum chionophilum

Ornithogalum chionophilum, commonly known as the snow-loving star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the cooler months. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a notable plant in seasonal gardens.

Ornithogalum chionophilum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
