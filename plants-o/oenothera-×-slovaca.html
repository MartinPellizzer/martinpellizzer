Oenothera × slovaca

Oenothera × slovaca, commonly known as the Slovak evening primrose, is a hybrid species native to Central Europe.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a hairy texture.

Oenothera × slovaca is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, showcasing adaptability to various environmental conditions.
