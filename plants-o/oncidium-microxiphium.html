Oncidium microxiphium

Oncidium microxiphium, commonly known as the narrow sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Oncidium microxiphium prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
