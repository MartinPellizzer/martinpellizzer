Oenanthe fistulosa var. tabernaemontana

Oenanthe fistulosa var. tabernaemontana, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Water-dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its small, white flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic, often pungent odor and contains various bioactive compounds in its leaves and roots.
