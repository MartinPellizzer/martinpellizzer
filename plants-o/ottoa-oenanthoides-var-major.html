Ottoa oenanthoides var. major

Ottoa oenanthoides var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered ottoa, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant features slender, branched stems that rise from a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are white to pale yellow, arranged in compound umbels, and bloom in late spring to early summer.
