Oncidium saintlegerianum

Oncidium saintlegerianum, commonly known as the Saint Leger orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with long, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, requiring regular watering and moderate humidity to thrive.
