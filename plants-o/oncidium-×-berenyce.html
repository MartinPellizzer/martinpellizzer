Oncidium × berenyce

Oncidium × berenyce, commonly known as the Berenyce orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from its parent species, Oncidium × surinamense and Oncidium × harrisonianum.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences with clusters of fragrant, brightly colored flowers, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Oncidium × berenyce is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
