Osyris lanceolata

Osyris lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf osyris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is often found in wetlands and estuarine environments, where it thrives in brackish water conditions.

Osyris lanceolata is a hardy species that plays a role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
