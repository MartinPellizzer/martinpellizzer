Ornithogalum tempskyanum

Ornithogalum tempskyanum, commonly known as the Tempskyan star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and somewhat succulent. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
