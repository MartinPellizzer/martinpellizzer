Ophioglossum Vulgatum Subsp. Lusitanicum

Ophioglossum vulgatum subsp. lusitanicum is a rare and ancient fern found in parts of Europe and North Africa. It is known for its distinctive, narrow leaves and unique reproductive structures. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often associated with limestone soils. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserve this fascinating relic of the plant kingdom.
