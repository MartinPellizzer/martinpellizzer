Othonna primulina

Othonna primulina, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that reflects light and reduces water loss. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna primulina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
