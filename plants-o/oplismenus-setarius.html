Oplismenus setarius

Oplismenus setarius, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Oplismenus setarius is also valued as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
