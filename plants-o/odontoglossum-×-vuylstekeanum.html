Odontoglossum × vuylstekeanum

Odontoglossum × vuylstekeanum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Odontoglossum ramosum and Odontoglossum himalaicum.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Andes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, with intricate patterns and a fragrant scent.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
