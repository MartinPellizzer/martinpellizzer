Osmanthus francii

Osmanthus francii, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Osmanthus francii is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
