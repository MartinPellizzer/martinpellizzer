Oxalis fluminensis f. pallidiflora

Oxalis fluminensis f. pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered water oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple hue, and its flowers are pale yellow with five petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate partial shade.

Oxalis fluminensis f. pallidiflora is also known for its ability to spread through runners, making it a versatile ground cover in gardens.
