Orbea abayensis

Orbea abayensis, commonly known as the Abyssinian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, aiding in water retention.

Orbea abayensis is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and beautiful blooms.
