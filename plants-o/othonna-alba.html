Othonna alba

Othonna alba, commonly known as the white othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, white or pale green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
