Opuntia stanlyi var. peeblesiana

Opuntia stanlyi var. peeblesiana, commonly known as Peebles' prickly pear, is a cactus species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, spineless pads and vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its pads are edible when fully mature and can be used in culinary applications.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
