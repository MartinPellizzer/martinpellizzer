Othonna lessingii

Othonna lessingii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of blue-gray, woolly leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid habitat. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Othonna lessingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
