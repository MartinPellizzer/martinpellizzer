Othonna quinqueradiata

Othonna quinqueradiata, commonly known as the five-rayed othonna, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and dense clusters of small, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
