Oldenlandia stellarioides

Oldenlandia stellarioides, commonly known as starry oldenlandia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
