Ophryosporus ovatus

Ophryosporus ovatus, commonly known as the ovate spore plant, is a unique species within the Ophryosporaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ovular spore sacs that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or damp soil. Its spores are dispersed through wind, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
