Oberonia pedicellata

Oberonia pedicellata, commonly known as the orchid orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.
