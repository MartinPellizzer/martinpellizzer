Ononis Repens Var. Austriaca

Ononis repens var. austriaca is a lesser-known yet resilient plant native to alpine regions of Central Europe. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This variant of Ononis repens is distinguished by its compact growth and unique floral characteristics. Often overlooked in favor of more showy flora, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its subtle beauty and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
