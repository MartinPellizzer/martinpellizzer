Ophrys × jeanpertii

Ophrys × jeanpertii, commonly known as the Jeanpertii orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Ophrys, which is known for its mimicry of female insects to attract male pollinators. The plant produces intricate, bee-like flowers that are highly specialized for pollination through sexual deception. It typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
