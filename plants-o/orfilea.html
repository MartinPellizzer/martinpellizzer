Orfilea

Orfilea, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that resemble small lanterns. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warm months, producing clusters of tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Orfilea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
