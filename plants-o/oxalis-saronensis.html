Oxalis saronensis

Oxalis saronensis, commonly known as the pink oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pinkish hue. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, often resembling those of the genus Oxalis. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal blooming.
