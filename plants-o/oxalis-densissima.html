Oxalis densissima

Oxalis densissima, commonly known as the dense oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leafy foliage and clusters of small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, and it produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Oxalis densissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
