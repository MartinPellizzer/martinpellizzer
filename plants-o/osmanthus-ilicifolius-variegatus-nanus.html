Osmanthus ilicifolius variegatus-nanus

Osmanthus ilicifolius variegatus-nanus, commonly known as the variegated dwarf osmanthus, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces fragrant, golden-yellow flowers in the autumn, which are highly valued for their aromatic properties. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for container gardening.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in horticulture.
