Onobrychis iranshahrii

Onobrychis iranshahrii, commonly known as the Iranian burr vetch, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry regions.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
