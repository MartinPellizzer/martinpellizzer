Ononis serrata var. intermedia

Ononis serrata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to arid and coastal environments.
