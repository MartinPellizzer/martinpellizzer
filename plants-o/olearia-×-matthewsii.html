Olearia × matthewsii

Olearia × matthewsii, commonly known as the Matthes' daisy, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom in spring and early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Olearia × matthewsii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for coastal or garden landscapes.
