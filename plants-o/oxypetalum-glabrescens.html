Oxypetalum glabrescens

Oxypetalum glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-flowered oxypetalum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
