Odontadenia nitida var. oblongifolia

Odontadenia nitida var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed toothed bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that have serrated margins. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, often with a distinctive toothed pattern on the calyx. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
