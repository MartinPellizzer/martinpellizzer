Oncodia glumacea

Oncodia glumacea, commonly known as the spiny grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and stiff, spiny leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the late spring to early summer. Oncodia glumacea thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
