Olsynium nigricans

Olsynium nigricans, commonly known as the black-flowered olsynium, is a striking perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a dark green hue. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from deep purple to black, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Olsynium nigricans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
