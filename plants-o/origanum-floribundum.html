Origanum floribundum

Origanum floribundum, commonly known as sweet marjoram, is a flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its aromatic, small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its essential oils contribute to its use in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
