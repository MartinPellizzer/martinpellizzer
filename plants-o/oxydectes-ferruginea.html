Oxydectes ferruginea

Oxydectes ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a rusty-red or reddish-brown pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest undergrowth.

Oxydectes ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
