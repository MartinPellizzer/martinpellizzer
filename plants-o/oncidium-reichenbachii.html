Oncidium reichenbachii

Oncidium reichenbachii, commonly known as the Yellow Butterfly Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, butterfly-like flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the previous ones. Its roots are typically aerial, allowing it to grow on trees or other structures in its natural habitat.

Oncidium reichenbachii requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
