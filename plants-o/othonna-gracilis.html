Othonna gracilis

Othonna gracilis, commonly known as the slender othonna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence. Its leaves are linear and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Othonna gracilis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
