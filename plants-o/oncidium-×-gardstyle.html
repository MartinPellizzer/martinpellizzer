Oncidium × gardstyle

Oncidium × gardstyle, commonly known as the garden orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various Oncidium species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically has long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of blossoms that bloom sequentially over several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium × gardstyle is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
