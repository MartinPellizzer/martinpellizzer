Olearia avicenniifolia

Olearia avicenniifolia, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white pubescent foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in coastal and rocky habitats, often growing in well-drained soils.

Olearia avicenniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
