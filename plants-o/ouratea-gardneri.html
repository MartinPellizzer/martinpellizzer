Ouratea gardneri

Ouratea gardneri, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Ouratea gardneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal flowering.
