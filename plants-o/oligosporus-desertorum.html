Oligosporus desertorum

Oligosporus desertorum, commonly known as the desert spore plant, is a rare and resilient species adapted to arid environments.

It possesses a compact, succulent structure with thick, waxy leaves that minimize water loss. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in dense formations. Its root system is extensive and deep, allowing it to access moisture from underground water sources.

Due to its unique adaptations, it plays a crucial role in stabilizing desert soils and supporting specialized desert ecosystems.
