Oenothera × britannica

Oenothera × britannica, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Oenothera × britannica is also noted for its medicinal uses, particularly for its oil-rich seeds.
