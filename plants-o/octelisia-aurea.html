Octelisia aurea

Octelisia aurea, commonly known as the golden octelisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface.

Octelisia aurea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
