Osyris alba

Osyris alba, commonly known as the white osyris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Osyris alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
