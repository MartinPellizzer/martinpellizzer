Oxytropis kansuensis

Oxytropis kansuensis, commonly known as Kansu milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to arid and semi-arid regions of China.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit, trifoliate leaves, and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces pods containing several seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in sandy or gravelly soils.

Oxytropis kansuensis is often found in steppe and desert steppe ecosystems, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
