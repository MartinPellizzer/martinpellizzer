Ochna lucens

Ochna lucens, commonly known as the golden ochna, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Ochna lucens is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
