Olgasis

Olgasis, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features showy, vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting green or yellow throat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a basal rosette.

Olgasis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
