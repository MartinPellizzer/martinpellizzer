Ornithogalum haworthioides

Ornithogalum haworthioides, commonly known as the Haworth's star-of-Bethlehem, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa, characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to many gardens.
