Ononis picta var. clausonis

Ononis picta var. clausonis, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has hairy, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
