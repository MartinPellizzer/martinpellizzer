Oncidium wentworthianum var. tenue

Oncidium wentworthianum var. tenue, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to South America.

It features slender, upright pseudobulbs that produce a single, slender stem with a few elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, arranged in a raceme. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
