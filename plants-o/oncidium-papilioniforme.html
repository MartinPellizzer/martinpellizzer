Oncidium papilioniforme

Oncidium papilioniforme, commonly known as the butterfly orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with red and purple markings. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium papilioniforme is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
