Ophiopogon stenophyllus

Ophiopogon stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lilyturf, is a perennial evergreen plant native to East Asia.

It features grass-like, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in dense, tufted clusters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers in early summer, followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.

Ophiopogon stenophyllus is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and its low maintenance requirements.
