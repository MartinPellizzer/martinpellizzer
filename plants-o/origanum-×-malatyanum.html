Origanum × malatyanum

Origanum × malatyanum, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. It is widely used in culinary applications and traditional medicine due to its high concentration of essential oils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both gardens and herbal collections.
