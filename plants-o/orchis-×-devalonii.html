Orchis × devalonii

Orchis × devalonii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Orchis morio and Orchis anthropophora.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant produces showy, violet or purple flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with darker pigments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils, and it is often found in regions of Europe.

Due to its hybrid nature, Orchis × devalonii exhibits a range of morphological variations.
