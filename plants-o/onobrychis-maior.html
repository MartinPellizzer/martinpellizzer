Onobrychis maior

Onobrychis maior, commonly known as the greater burclover, is a perennial legume native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Onobrychis maior is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
