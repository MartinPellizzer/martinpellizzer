Oncidium altissimum

Oncidium altissimum, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces numerous small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs.

Oncidium altissimum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
