Orbea rogersii

Orbea rogersii, commonly known as the Rogers' orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Orbea rogersii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
