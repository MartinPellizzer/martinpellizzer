Orobanche camphorosmae

Orobanche camphorosmae, commonly known as the camphorosma broomrape, is a parasitic flowering plant that lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host for nutrients.

The plant typically attaches to the roots of host plants such as Camphorosma species, forming a symbiotic relationship.
