Oenothera primiveris subsp. caulescens

Oenothera primiveris subsp.

caulescens, commonly known as the slender evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
