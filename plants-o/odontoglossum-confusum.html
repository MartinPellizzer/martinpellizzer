Odontoglossum confusum

Odontoglossum confusum, commonly known as the confused tooth-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces racemes of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, tooth-like lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Odontoglossum confusum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
