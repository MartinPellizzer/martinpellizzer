Oenanthe aquatica

Oenanthe aquatica, commonly known as water dropwort, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland and aquatic environments.

It has slender, erect stems that often grow in water or very moist soil. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clustered umbels, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Oenanthe aquatica is often found in marshes, streambanks, and other freshwater habitats.
