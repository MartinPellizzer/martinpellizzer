Oncidium crassidactylum

Oncidium crassidactylum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on its petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oncidium crassidylum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
