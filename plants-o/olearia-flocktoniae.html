Olearia flocktoniae

Olearia flocktoniae, commonly known as the Flockton's silver bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Olearia flocktoniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its hardy and low-maintenance nature.
