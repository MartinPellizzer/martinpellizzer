Oberna nivea

Oberna nivea, commonly known as the white snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Oberna nivea thrives in well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
