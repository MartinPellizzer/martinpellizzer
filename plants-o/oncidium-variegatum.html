Oncidium variegatum

Oncidium variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting markings. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Oncidium variegatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
