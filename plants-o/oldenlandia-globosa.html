Oldenlandia globosa

Oldenlandia globosa, commonly known as the globular spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive globular inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, white to purple flowers clustered in a rounded shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and swampy areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Oldenlandia globosa is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
