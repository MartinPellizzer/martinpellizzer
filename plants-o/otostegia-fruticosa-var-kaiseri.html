Otostegia fruticosa var. kaiseri

Otostegia fruticosa var. kaiseri, commonly known as the Kaiseri rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, displaying small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
