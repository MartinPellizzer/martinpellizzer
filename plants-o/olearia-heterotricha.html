Olearia heterotricha

Olearia heterotricha, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, which contribute to its unique texture and appearance.

Olearia heterotricha is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
