Otoglossum dayanum

Otoglossum dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Otoglossum dayanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
