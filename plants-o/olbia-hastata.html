Olbia hastata

Olbia hastata, commonly known as the clustered milkweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to reddish-orange flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its milky sap is a distinguishing feature, and it serves as a host plant for several butterfly species.

Olbia hastata typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
