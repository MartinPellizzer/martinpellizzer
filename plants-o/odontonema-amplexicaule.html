Odontonema amplexicaule

Odontonema amplexicaule, commonly known as the clustered strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Odontonema amplexicaule thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
