Onopordum tauricum subsp. humile

Onopordum tauricum subsp.

humile, commonly known as the Turkish thistle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia. It features silver-gray, woolly foliage and produces pale purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant is characterized by its robust root system and ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a hardy, drought-tolerant species.
