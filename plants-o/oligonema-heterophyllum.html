Oligonema heterophyllum

Oligonema heterophyllum, commonly known as the variable leaved daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Oligonema heterophyllum is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
