Oxalis enneaphylla subsp. ibarii

Oxalis enneaphylla subsp.

ibarii, commonly known as the Ibaria oxalis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.
