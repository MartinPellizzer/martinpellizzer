Ozothamnus thyrsoideus

Ozothamnus thyrsoideus, commonly known as silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
