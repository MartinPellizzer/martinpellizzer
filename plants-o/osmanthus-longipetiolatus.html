Osmanthus longipetiolatus

Osmanthus longipetiolatus, commonly known as the long petiole osmanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with long petioles, giving it its common name. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
