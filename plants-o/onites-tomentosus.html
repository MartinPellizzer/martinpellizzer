Onites tomentosus

Onites tomentosus, commonly known as the wooly onite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Onites tomentosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
