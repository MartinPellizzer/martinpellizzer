Onosma petiolata

Onosma petiolata, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, and pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
