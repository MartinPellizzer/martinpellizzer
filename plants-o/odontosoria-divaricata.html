Odontosoria divaricata

Odontosoria divaricata, commonly known as the variable spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its creeping rhizomes and delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in many fern species.

Odontosoria divaricata is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
