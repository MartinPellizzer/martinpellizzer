Ornithogalum conicum var. strictum

Ornithogalum conicum var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped white flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens and borders.
