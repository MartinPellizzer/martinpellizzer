Odontium rubrum

Odontium rubrum, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that resemble flames, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

Odontium rubrum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
