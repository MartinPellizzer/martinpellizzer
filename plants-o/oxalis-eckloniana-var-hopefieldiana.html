Oxalis eckloniana var. hopefieldiana

Oxalis eckloniana var. hopefieldiana, commonly known as the Hopefield oxalis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red or maroon coloration, often with a contrasting white or yellow margin. The plant produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to pale red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and prolonged flowering period.
