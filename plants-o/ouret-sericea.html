Ouret sericea

Ouret sericea, commonly known as silverleaf tree or *Pterocarpus erinaceus*, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, silver-gray bark and pinnate leaves with soft, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The tree is valued for its durable wood and has traditional medicinal uses in various African cultures.
