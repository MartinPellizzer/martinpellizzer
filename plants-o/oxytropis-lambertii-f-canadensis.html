Oxytropis lambertii f. canadensis

Oxytropis lambertii f. canadensis, commonly known as Canadian milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to North America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

This variety is notable for its role in soil stabilization and as a food source for native wildlife.
