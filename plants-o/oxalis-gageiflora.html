Oxalis gageiflora

Oxalis gageiflora, commonly known as the golden oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns and produces clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is part of the Oxalidaceae family, known for its calcium oxalate crystals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
