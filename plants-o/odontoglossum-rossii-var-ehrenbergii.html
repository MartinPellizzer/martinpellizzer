Odontoglossum rossii var. ehrenbergii

Odontoglossum rossii var. ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Ehrenberg's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers with intricate patterns of white and green. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in high-altitude cloud forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
