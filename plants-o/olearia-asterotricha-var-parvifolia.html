Olearia asterotricha var. parvifolia

Olearia asterotricha var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved ice plant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in compact clusters, and bloom during the spring and summer months.

It is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils.
