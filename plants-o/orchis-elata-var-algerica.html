Orchis elata var. algerica

Orchis elata var. algerica, commonly known as the Algerian early orchid, is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, adorned with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers in late spring to early summer, arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in open woodlands or meadows.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is appreciated by botanists and gardeners alike.
