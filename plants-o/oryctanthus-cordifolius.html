Oryctanthus cordifolius

Oryctanthus cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Oryctanthus cordifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
