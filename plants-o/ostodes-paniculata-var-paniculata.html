Ostodes paniculata var. paniculata

Ostodes paniculata var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicled ostodes, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces small, dry fruits called samaras, which are dispersed by wind. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical climates.
