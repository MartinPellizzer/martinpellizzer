Ornithogalum autumnulum

Ornithogalum autumnulum, commonly known as the autumn lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing clusters of white or pale pink flowers with six tepals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant, bell-shaped flowers that attract pollinators.
