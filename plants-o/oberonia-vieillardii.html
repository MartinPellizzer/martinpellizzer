Oberonia vieillardii

Oberonia vieillardii, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid and cool conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often fragrant. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

Oberonia vieillardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
