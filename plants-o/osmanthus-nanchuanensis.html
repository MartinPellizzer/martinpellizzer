Osmanthus nanchuanensis

Osmanthus nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn, releasing a sweet, honey-like scent. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a flavoring in teas and confections.

Osmanthus nanchuanensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
