Oreochloa elegans

Oreochloa elegans, commonly known as the slender grass or elegant grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands. Its flowering spikes are slender and bear small, spikelet-like flowers.

Oreochloa elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
