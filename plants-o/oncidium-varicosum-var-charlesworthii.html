Oncidium varicosum var. charlesworthii

Oncidium varicosum var. charlesworthii, commonly known as the Charlesworth's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting its growth habit.

The flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
