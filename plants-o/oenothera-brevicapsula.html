Oenothera brevicapsula

Oenothera brevicapsula, commonly known as the short-capsule evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, dry fruits known as capsules, which contain numerous seeds. Oenothera brevicapsula is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
