Osmoxylon simplicifolium

Osmoxylon simplicifolium, commonly known as the simple-leaf osmoxylon, is a rare flowering plant native to the temperate forests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers clustered in terminal panicles, which bloom during the late spring to early summer season. Its root system is fibrous and shallow, allowing it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
