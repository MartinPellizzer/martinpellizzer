Onosma sharifii

Onosma sharifii, commonly known as the Persian spring gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair, while its stems are often branched and upright.

Onosma sharifii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
