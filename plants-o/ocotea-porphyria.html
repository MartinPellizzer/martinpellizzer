Ocotea porphyria

Ocotea porphyria, commonly known as the purple ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico, Guatemala, and Honduras. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a distinct scent when crushed. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

Ocotea porphyria is often found in humid, shaded environments within lowland tropical forests.
