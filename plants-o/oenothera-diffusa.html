Oenothera diffusa

Oenothera diffusa, commonly known as evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds enclosed in a capsule. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Oenothera diffusa is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
