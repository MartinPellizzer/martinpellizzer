Oncidium clowesii

Oncidium clowesii, commonly known as the Clowes's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or red markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its roots are fibrous and often grow in a loose, airy manner to facilitate air circulation.

Oncidium clowesii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
