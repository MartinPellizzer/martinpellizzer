Ononis crotalarioides var. rubricaulis

Ononis crotalarioides var. rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, often reddish stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands, rocky slopes, and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Rosaceae family.
