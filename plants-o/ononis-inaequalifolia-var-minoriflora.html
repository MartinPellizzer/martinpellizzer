Ononis inaequalifolia var. minoriflora

Ononis inaequalifolia var. minoriflora, commonly known as the lesser floricole ononis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branched stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand dry conditions.
