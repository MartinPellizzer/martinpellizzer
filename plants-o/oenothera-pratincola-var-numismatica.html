Oenothera pratincola var. numismatica

Oenothera pratincola var. numismatica, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties, particularly the oils derived from its seeds. Its growth habit includes a sprawling or ascending stem, often with a mat-forming structure.
