Oenothera biennis var. vulgaris

Oenothera biennis var. vulgaris, commonly known as evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies, roadsides, and disturbed areas.

Oenothera biennis var. vulgaris is also valued for its oil-rich seeds, which have various medicinal and industrial applications.
