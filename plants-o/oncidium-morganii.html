Oncidium morganii

Oncidium morganii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with greenish sepals and a distinctive, narrow spur. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture from the air. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their long-lasting beauty, often lasting several weeks.

Oncidium morganii requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
