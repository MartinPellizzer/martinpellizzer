Ostericum sieboldii var. microphyllum

Ostericum sieboldii var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved Japanese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Ostericum sieboldii var. microphyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
