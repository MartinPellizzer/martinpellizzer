Osteospermum zeyheri

Osteospermum zeyheri, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has dark green, narrow leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
