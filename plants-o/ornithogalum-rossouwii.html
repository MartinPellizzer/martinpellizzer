Ornithogalum rossouwii

Ornithogalum rossouwii, commonly known as the Rossouw's star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, strap-like leaves and produces a tall, erect stem bearing white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its bulbous underground stem, which stores nutrients and water. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum rossouwii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
