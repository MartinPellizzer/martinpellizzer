Oleandra longipes

Oleandra longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed oleander, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. It is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
