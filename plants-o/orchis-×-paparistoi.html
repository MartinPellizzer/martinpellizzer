Orchis × paparistoi

Orchis × paparistoi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Orchis morio and Orchis mascula.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It has a robust rhizome system that helps it establish in various soil types.

Botanically, it is classified as a member of the Orchidaceae family and exhibits typical orchid morphology with bilateral symmetry.
