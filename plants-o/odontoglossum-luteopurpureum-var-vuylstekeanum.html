Odontoglossum luteopurpureum var. vuylstekeanum

Odontoglossum luteopurpureum var. vuylstekeanum, commonly known as the Purple-lipped Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and yellow floral coloration, with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the late spring to early summer season.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
