Onosma fastigiata var. numidica

Onosma fastigiata var. numidica, commonly known as the Numidian upright oniongrass, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant gardens.
