Onosma heterophylla

Onosma heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from ovate to lanceolate, depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense spikes, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Onosma heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
