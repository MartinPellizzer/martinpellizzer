Ononis Pendula Subsp. Broussonetii

Ononis pendula subsp. broussonetii is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species that thrives in rocky and arid environments. This subspecies is characterized by its delicate, cascading growth habit and pale green foliage. It is native to regions in southern Europe and parts of North Africa, where it adapts well to harsh climatic conditions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity. Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
