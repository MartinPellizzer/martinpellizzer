Oberonia wightiana var. nilgirensis

Oberonia wightiana var. nilgirensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri Oberonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South India, particularly the Nilgiri and Western Ghats. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, intricate flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette.

This orchid is often found in moist, shaded environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
