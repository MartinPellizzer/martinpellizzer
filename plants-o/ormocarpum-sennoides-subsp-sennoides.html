Ormocarpum sennoides subsp. sennoides

Ormocarpum sennoides subsp.

sennoides, commonly known as the Indian senna, is a shrub native to the Indian subcontinent. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by cylindrical pods containing flat, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
