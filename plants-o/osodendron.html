Osodendron

Osodendron, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their vibrant, showy flowers and are often cultivated for their ornamental value. Osodendron species typically have thick, fleshy leaves and are adapted to thrive in humid, warm climates. They are popular houseplants due to their ability to bloom year-round under proper care.

The genus includes several species, such as Osodendron simplex, which is widely appreciated for its beauty and ease of maintenance.
