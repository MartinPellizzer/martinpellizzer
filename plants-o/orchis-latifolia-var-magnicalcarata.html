Orchis latifolia var. magnicalcarata

Orchis latifolia var. magnicalcarata, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid variant with a large spurred lip, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its robust, herbaceous growth and features broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, erect flowering stems with numerous showy, violet to purple flowers. Its unique floral structure includes a prominent, spurred lip that serves as a pollination aid.

This variant is typically found in moist, calcareous habitats across Europe.
