Orbea semitubiflora

Orbea semitubiflora, commonly known as the narrow-tubed orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Orbea semitubiflora is a perennial herb that produces showy blooms during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
