Oberonia longitepala

Oberonia longitepala, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences. The plant features elongated, slender stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Oberonia longitepala thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches in rainforests.
