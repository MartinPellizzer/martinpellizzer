Obione taltalensis

Obione taltalensis, commonly known as the Taltal orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the coastal regions of northern Chile.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in arid environments, adapting to the saline and sandy soils of its habitat. Its roots are specialized to absorb moisture efficiently, allowing it to survive in harsh conditions.
