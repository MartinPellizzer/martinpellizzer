Ornithogalum rubescens

Ornithogalum rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, red or pink flowers in early spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum rubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
