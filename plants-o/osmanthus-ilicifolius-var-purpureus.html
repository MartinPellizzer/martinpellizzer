Osmanthus ilicifolius var. purpureus

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. purpureus, commonly known as the purple leaf osmanthus, is a variant of the osmanthus plant characterized by its striking purple foliage.

This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the autumn, adding a subtle scent to the air. The leaves are typically narrow and leathery, providing a unique texture to the plant's appearance.

While it is not as widely cultivated as other osmanthus varieties, it is appreciated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal interest.
