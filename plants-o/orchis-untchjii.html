Orchis untchjii

Orchis untchjii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to certain regions of Europe.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a complex root system and relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
