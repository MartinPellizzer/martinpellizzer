Orchis × jailae

Orchis × jailae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Orchis morio and Orchis purpurea.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with a distinctive violet or purple flower spike. The flowers are typically showy, with three sepals and three petals, and are arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows.

The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and lance-shaped leaves.
