Orthochilus Ecristatus F. Flavus

Orthochilus ecristatus f. flavus is a rare and striking orchid known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique growth habits. Native to specific regions in South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich soil. Its distinct appearance makes it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from threats like habitat loss and climate change.
