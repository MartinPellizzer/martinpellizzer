Orchis incarnata var. trifurca

Orchis incarnata var. trifurca, commonly known as the three-lobed lady's tresses, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and slender, flowering stems. The plant produces a spike of small, white to pale purple flowers that are arranged in a loose, trichas-like pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows.

This orchid is often found in association with other orchid species and is considered a rare and specialized plant in its native range.
