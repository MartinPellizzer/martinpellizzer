Orthophytum lanuginosum

Orthophytum lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly orthophytum, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Its leaves are often blue-green in color and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Orthophytum lanuginosum is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental appeal.
