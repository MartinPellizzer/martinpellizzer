Odontoglossum × scottii

Odontoglossum × scottii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum aphyllum and Odontoglossum × scottii.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andes Mountains of South America. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Odontoglossum × scottii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
