Olyra juruana

Olyra juruana, commonly known as the Juruana tree, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. The leaves are large, simple, and alternate, often with a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

The plant is valued for its wood, which is used in construction and furniture making.
