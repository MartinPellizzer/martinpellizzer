Oritrophium Blepharophyllum

Oritrophium blepharophyllum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers and unique, hairy leaves that help it survive in harsh desert conditions. This plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in crevices where moisture can be retained. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare and beautiful species.
