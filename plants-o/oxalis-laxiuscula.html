Oxalis laxiuscula

Oxalis laxiuscula, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and small, delicate flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, round, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Oxalis laxiuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
