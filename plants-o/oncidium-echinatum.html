Oncidium echinatum

Oncidium echinatum, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium echinatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
