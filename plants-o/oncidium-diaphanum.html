Oncidium diaphanum

Oncidium diaphanum, commonly known as the Transparent Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a subtle elegance to gardens and indoor spaces.

Oncidium diaphanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
