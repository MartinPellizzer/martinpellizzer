Oenothera magellanica var. chubutensis

Oenothera magellanica var. chubutensis, commonly known as the Chubut evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to the Patagonian region of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Oenothera magellanica var. chubutensis is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, contributing to local biodiversity.
