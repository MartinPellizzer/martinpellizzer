Ostenia

Ostenia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Ostenia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
