Oberonia laxa

Oberonia laxa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a compact, rhizomatous structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faintly fragrant scent.

Oberonia laxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
