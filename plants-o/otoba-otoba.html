Otoba otoba

Otoba otoba, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvet tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by fruit resembling a capsule. Otoba otoba is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and traditional contexts.
