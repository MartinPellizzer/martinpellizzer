Oncidium lucasianum

Oncidium lucasianum, commonly known as the Lucasian orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with distinct sepals and petals, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Oncidium lucasianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
