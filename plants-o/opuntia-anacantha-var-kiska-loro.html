Opuntia anacantha var. kiska-loro

Opuntia anacantha var. kiska-loro, commonly known as the Kiska Loro cactus, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a spineless opuntia with flat, fleshy stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
