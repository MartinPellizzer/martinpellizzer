Odontoglossum powellii

Odontoglossum powellii, commonly known as Powell's orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Odontoglossum powellii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
