Onobrychis grandis var. grandis

Onobrychis grandis var. grandis, commonly known as the tall vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and forage.
