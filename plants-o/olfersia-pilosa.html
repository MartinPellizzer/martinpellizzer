Olfersia pilosa

Olfersia pilosa, commonly known as the silky orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features long, narrow leaves with a waxy texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Olfersia pilosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
