Ocimum hians var. microphyllum

Ocimum hians var. microphyllum, commonly known as dwarf holy basil, is a compact variant of the holy basil plant.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers, which are followed by small, round seeds. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and use in culinary applications.
