Onagra chrysantha var. parviflora

Onagra chrysantha var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered sunflower, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to North America.

It is a herbaceous annual plant with yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are alternately arranged. Its flowers consist of ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets, typical of the sunflower genus.
