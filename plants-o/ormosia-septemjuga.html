Ormosia septemjuga

Ormosia septemjuga, commonly known as the seven-leaved ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with seven leaflets, giving it its common name, and produces small, pod-like fruits. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

The species also plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
