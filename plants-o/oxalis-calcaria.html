Oxalis calcaria

Oxalis calcaria, commonly known as the mountain oxalis, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves with a distinctive white or pale green margin, and it produces vibrant yellow flowers with five petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to arid landscapes.

Oxalis calcaria is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
