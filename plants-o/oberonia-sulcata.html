Oberonia sulcata

Oberonia sulcata, commonly known as the warty orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive warty stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking violet or purple flowers with a unique, wrinkled appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Oberonia sulcata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
