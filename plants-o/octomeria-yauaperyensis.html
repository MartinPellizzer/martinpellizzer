Octomeria yauaperyensis

Octomeria yauaperyensis, commonly known as the Yauapey palm, is a rare and endemic species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, slender palm with a single trunk and pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white flowers, followed by black, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
