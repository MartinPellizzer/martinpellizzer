Odontotrichum paucicapitatum

Odontotrichum paucicapitatum, commonly known as the few-headed toothed grass, is a small annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and features slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Odontotrichum paucicapitatum is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as fields and coastal areas.
