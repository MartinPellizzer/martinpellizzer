Oncidium velutinum var. purpureum

Oncidium velutinum var. purpureum, commonly known as the Purple Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
