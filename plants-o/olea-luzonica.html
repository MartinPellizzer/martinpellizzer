Olea luzonica

Olea luzonica, commonly known as the Luzon olive, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family, which includes other olive species. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by drupes that are often used in traditional medicine.

Olea luzonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
