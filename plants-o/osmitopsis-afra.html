Osmitopsis afra

Osmitopsis afra, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale blue flower heads. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Osmitopsis afra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
