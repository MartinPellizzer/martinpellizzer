Ononis hirta var. cirtensis

Ononis hirta var. cirtensis, commonly known as the Cretan spurge, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, with small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.

The species is relatively drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to arid environments.
