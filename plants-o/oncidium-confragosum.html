Oncidium confragosum

Oncidium confragosum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red lip. It is noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, provided they are well-draining.

Oncidium confragosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
