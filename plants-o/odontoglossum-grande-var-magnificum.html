Odontoglossum grande var. magnificum

Odontoglossum grande var. magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It features striking, large, showy flowers with vibrant purple and yellow hues, often with intricate patterns. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in high-altitude, cool climates. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
