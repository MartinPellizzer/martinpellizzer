Oleandra micrantha

Oleandra micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered oleander, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes other ornamental and medicinal plants. Oleandra micrantha is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and can grow up to several meters in height.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
