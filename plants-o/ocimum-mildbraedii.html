Ocimum mildbraedii

Ocimum mildbraedii, commonly known as the Cape gentian.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family, native to South Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a fragrant aroma.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
