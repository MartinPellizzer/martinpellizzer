Ortachne

Ortachne, commonly known as the false oat, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. Ortachne is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its growth habit makes it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
