Origanum webbianum

Origanum webbianum, commonly known as mountain oregano, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, grey-green leaves and clusters of pale blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its essential oils, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Origanum webbianum is also an important plant in traditional herbal medicine.
