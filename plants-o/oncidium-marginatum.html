Oncidium marginatum

Oncidium marginatum, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, racemose inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that have a distinctive lemon scent. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Oncidium marginatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
