Oncidium calyptostalix

Oncidium calyptostalix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a spike that can range in color from yellow to orange and red. Its leaves are typically narrow and strap-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shady environments.

Oncidium calyptostalix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
