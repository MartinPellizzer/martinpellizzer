Oberonia filaris

Oberonia filaris, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. The plant has a short, upright stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often arranged in a spike, and are pollinated by insects.

Oberonia filaris is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
