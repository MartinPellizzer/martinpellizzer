Osyris spinescens

Osyris spinescens, commonly known as the spiny osyris, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits that contain several seeds. Osyris spinescens thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
