Oryzopsis napostaensis f. brachyphylla

Oryzopsis napostaensis f. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed naposta grass, is a variant of the naposta grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter, more densely packed inflorescences compared to the typical form. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh conditions. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a tufted growth pattern.

This form is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphological traits and ecological adaptations.
