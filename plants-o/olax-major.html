Olax major

Olax major, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, featuring thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
