Oncidium examinans

Oncidium examinans, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers, which often feature a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Oncidium examinans requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
