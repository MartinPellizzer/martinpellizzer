Odontoglossum myrianthum

Odontoglossum myrianthum, commonly known as the orchid with the many flowers, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
