Oxytropis nigrescens var. lonchopoda

Oxytropis nigrescens var. lonchopoda, commonly known as the purple sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the western United States.

The plant produces small, pea-like pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy species in harsh environments.
