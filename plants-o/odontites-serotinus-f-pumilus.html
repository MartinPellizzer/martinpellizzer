Odontites serotinus f. pumilus

Odontites serotinus f. pumilus, commonly known as the dwarf fern, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered fronds that typically grow close to the ground. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called soruses on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
