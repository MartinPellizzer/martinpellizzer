Orleania

Orleania, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Orleania has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in sunny environments.
