Onopordum illyricum proles delortii

Onopordum illyricum proles delortii, commonly known as the Delort's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust, upright growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural areas.
