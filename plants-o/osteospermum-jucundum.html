Osteospermum jucundum

Osteospermum jucundum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. It is typically shrub-like, with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Osteospermum jucundum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
