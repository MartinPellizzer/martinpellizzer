Oncidium × jamaicense

Oncidium × jamaicense, commonly known as the Jamaican orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Oncidium × jamaicense thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
