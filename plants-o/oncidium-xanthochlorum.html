Oncidium xanthochlorum

Oncidium xanthochlorum, commonly known as the Yellow Chlorine Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with green and yellow markings, which are typically borne on branched inflorescences. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium xanthochlorum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
