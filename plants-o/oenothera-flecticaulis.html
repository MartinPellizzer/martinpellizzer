Oenothera flecticaulis

Oenothera flecticaulis, commonly known as the zigzag evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. Oenothera flecticaulis is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption.

It is also noted for its ability to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.
