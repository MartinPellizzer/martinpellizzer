Othonna plantaginea

Othonna plantaginea, commonly known as the silverleaf othonna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance and helps it reflect sunlight. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Othonna plantaginea is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental value.
