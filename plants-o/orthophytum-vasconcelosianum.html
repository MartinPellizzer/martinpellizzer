Orthophytum vasconcelosianum

Orthophytum vasconcelosianum, commonly known as the Vasconcelosianum orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red lip, which are borne on erect inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
