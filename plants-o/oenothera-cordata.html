Oenothera cordata

Oenothera cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and open woodlands. Its seeds are enclosed in capsule-like fruits that split open to release the seeds.

Oenothera cordata is also known for its medicinal properties, with its oils used in traditional and modern herbal remedies.
