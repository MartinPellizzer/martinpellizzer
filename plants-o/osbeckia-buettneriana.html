Osbeckia buettneriana

Osbeckia buettneriana, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, showy purple or pink flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, such as deserts and mountain ranges.

Osbeckia buettneriana is adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperatures, making it a hardy species in harsh climates.
