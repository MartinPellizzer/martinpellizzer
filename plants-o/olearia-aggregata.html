Olearia aggregata

Olearia aggregata, commonly known as the silverweed or silver olearia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features silvery-white, fuzzy leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine environments.

Olearia aggregata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
