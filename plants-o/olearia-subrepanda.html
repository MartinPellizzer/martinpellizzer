Olearia subrepanda

Olearia subrepanda, commonly known as the silverweed tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in dense, fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a leathery texture.

Olearia subrepanda is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
