Olearia asterotricha subsp. asterotricha

Olearia asterotricha subsp.

asterotricha, commonly known as the silver tussock plant, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a compact growth habit with wiry stems and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its botanical features include hairy leaves and a distinctive, tufted appearance that gives it its common name.
