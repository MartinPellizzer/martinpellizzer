Orescia montana

Orescia montana, commonly known as the mountain orescia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, often branching towards the upper portion. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a hairy texture, and are arranged alternately along the stem.

Orescia montana is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
