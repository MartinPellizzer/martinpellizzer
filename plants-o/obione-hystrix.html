Obione hystrix

Obione hystrix, commonly known as the spiny hystrix, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive spiny foliage.

It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant's leaves are covered in sharp, protective spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in shades of yellow and green, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Obione hystrix is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions, making it a resilient choice for landscaping in certain climates.
