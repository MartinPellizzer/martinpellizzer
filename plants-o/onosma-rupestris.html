Onosma rupestris

Onosma rupestris, commonly known as the rock onosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, pink to purple flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
