Ononis pinnata var. rosifolia

Ononis pinnata var. rosifolia, commonly known as pink sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, ascending stems covered in small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has finely divided, pinnately arranged leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in arid and semi-arid environments.
