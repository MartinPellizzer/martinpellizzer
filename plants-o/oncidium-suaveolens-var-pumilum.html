Oncidium suaveolens var. pumilum

Oncidium suaveolens var. pumilum, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the small fragrant orchid, is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces pendulous, fragrant flower spikes with bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months and prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its small size and delicate appearance make it a striking addition to any collection of orchids.
