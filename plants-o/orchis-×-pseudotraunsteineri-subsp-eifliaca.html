Orchis × pseudotraunsteineri subsp. eifliaca

Orchis × pseudotraunsteineri subsp.

eifliaca, commonly known as the Eifel orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect flowering stems.

The plant produces showy, violet to purple flowers with a distinct lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is typically found in woodland habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
