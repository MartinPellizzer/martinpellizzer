Onobrychis Argentea Subsp. Africana

Onobrychis argentea subsp. africana is a lesser-known but ecologically significant legume native to parts of Africa. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and distinctive pod-like fruits. Despite its hardy nature, it remains underappreciated in both botanical and agricultural circles. Its potential for soil enrichment and as a forage crop makes it a valuable subject for further study.
