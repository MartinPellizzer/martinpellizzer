Onobrychis intermedia

Onobrychis intermedia, commonly known as red brome, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelike inflorescences. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, triangular seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Onobrychis intermedia is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and has been used in traditional medicine.
