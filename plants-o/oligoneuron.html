Oligoneuron

Oligoneuron, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its botanical classification places it in the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family.

Oligoneuron is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
