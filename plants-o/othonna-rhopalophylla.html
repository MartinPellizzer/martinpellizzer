Othonna rhopalophylla

Othonna rhopalophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which helps it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It has a compact growth habit and is often grown as an ornamental plant in rock gardens.

Othonna rhopalophylla thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
