Ornithidium cordyline

Ornithidium cordyline, commonly known as the sacred bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

Ornithidium cordyline thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
