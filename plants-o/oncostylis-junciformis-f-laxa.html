Oncostylis junciformis f. laxa

Oncostylis junciformis f. laxa, commonly known as the loose-flowered rush, is a botanical variant of the species Oncostylis junciformis.

It is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, typically found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, grass-like stems and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Its leaves are linear and often have a slight sheen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This form is valued in horticulture for its adaptability and aesthetic qualities in water gardens and wetland landscapes.
