Osteospermum macrocarpum

Osteospermum macrocarpum, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with blue or purple petals. The plant produces showy, composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Osteospermum macrocarpum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various climates.
