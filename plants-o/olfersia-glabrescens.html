Olfersia glabrescens

Olfersia glabrescens, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, fleshy, red fruits. Its fruits are edible and have a tangy, sour flavor, often used in local cuisines.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
