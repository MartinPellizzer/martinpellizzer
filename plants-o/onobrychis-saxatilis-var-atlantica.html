Onobrychis saxatilis var. atlantica

Onobrychis saxatilis var. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic bursaria, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, followed by distinctive bur-like seed pods. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
