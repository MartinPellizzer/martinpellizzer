Oncidium rupestre

Oncidium rupestre, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing on tree trunks or cliffs. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

Oncidium rupestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
