Oncidium × haematochilum

Oncidium × haematochilum, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from Oncidium and Haematochilum.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often red-tipped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical and subtropical habitats. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Oncidium × haematochilum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its showy blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
