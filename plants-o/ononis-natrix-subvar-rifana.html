Ononis natrix subvar. rifana

Ononis natrix subvar. rifana, commonly known as the Rif spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North Africa and the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and hairy, while its stems are slender and often prostrate.
