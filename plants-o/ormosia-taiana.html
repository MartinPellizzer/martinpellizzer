Ormosia taiana

Ormosia taiana, commonly known as the Taiana ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Ormosia taiana is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist, lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the island's unique flora.
