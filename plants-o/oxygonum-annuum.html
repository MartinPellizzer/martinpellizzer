Oxygonum annuum

Oxygonum annuum, commonly known as the common morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms that open in the morning. It thrives in warm climates and is often grown for its ornamental value.

Oxygonum annuum is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread quickly in suitable conditions.
