Oscularia major

Oscularia major, commonly known as the desert star, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of the Americas.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season, which attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Oscularia major is known for its resilience and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for desert landscaping.
