Oreojuncus monanthos

Oreojuncus monanthos, commonly known as the single-flowered rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color. The plant produces a single, spike-like inflorescence at the top of the stem, bearing small, greenish flowers. Oreojuncus monanthos thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, contributing to its resemblance to other rush species.
