Ornithogalum deltoideum

Ornithogalum deltoideum, commonly known as the delta lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale blue flowers with a distinctive, bell-shaped structure. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky environments and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
