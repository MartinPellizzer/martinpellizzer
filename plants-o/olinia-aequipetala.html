Olinia aequipetala

Olinia aequipetala, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Olinia aequipetala is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
