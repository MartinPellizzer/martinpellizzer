Othonna hallii

Othonna hallii, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna hallii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
