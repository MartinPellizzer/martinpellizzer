Orthophytum mello-barretoi

Orthophytum mello-barretoi, commonly known as the Mello-Barreto's orthophytum, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warm season, which are typically borne in clusters. It is well adapted to arid environments, featuring spines that provide protection and reduce water loss.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
