Onopordum laconicum

Onopordum laconicum, commonly known as the Laconic thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny stems and large, silvery-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy purple or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Onopordum laconicum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
