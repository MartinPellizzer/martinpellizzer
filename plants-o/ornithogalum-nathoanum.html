Ornithogalum nathoanum

Ornithogalum nathoanum, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale green flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum nathoanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
