Ostryocarpus racemosus

Ostryocarpus racemosus, commonly known as the clustered grassleaf, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is native to regions of southern Africa, including South Africa and Namibia.

Ostryocarpus racemosus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
