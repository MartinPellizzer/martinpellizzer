Odostemon andrieuxii

Odostemon andrieuxii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, daisy-like inflorescences, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Odostemon andrieuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
