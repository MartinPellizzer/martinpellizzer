Ormocarpum keniense

Ormocarpum keniense, commonly known as Kenyan orchid bean, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators.

The fruit, a legume, contains seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its survival in harsh conditions.
