Oxalis decaphylla

Oxalis decaphylla, commonly known as the buttercup oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are typical of the Oxalis genus. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Oxalis decaphylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
