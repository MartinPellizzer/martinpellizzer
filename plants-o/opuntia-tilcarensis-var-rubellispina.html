Opuntia tilcarensis var. rubellispina

Opuntia tilcarensis var. rubellispina, commonly known as the red-spined prickly pear, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are covered in spines, with the spines of this variety being a distinctive reddish color. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by edible, red fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
