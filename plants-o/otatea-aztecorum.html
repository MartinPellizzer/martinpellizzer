Otatea aztecorum

Otatea aztecorum, commonly known as the Mexican fern, is a species of tree in the family Cyatheaceae.

It is native to Mexico and parts of Central America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Otatea aztecorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
