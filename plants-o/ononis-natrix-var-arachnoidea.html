Ononis natrix var. arachnoidea

Ononis natrix var. arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-hair ononis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and its leaves are typically trifoliate, meaning they are divided into three leaflets.

It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as hillsides and grasslands.
