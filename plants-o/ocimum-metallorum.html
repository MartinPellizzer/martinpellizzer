Ocimum metallorum

Ocimum metallorum, commonly known as silver leaf basil, is a member of the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its silver-white undersides on its lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that attract pollinators.

Ocimum metallorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.
