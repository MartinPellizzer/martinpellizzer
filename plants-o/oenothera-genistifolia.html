Oenothera genistifolia

Oenothera genistifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and the flowers have four petals that open at dusk.
