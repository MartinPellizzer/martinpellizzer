Odontarrhena Muralis

Odontarrhena muralis is a rare and intriguing plant native to southern Africa. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is often found growing on walls and cliffs. Despite its small size, it possesses strikingly unique floral structures. Its scientific name reflects its dental-like appearance and its association with walls. This plant remains a subject of fascination for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
