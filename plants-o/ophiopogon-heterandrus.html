Ophiopogon heterandrus

Ophiopogon heterandrus, commonly known as the Japanese forest grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

Ophiopogon heterandrus is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
