Ornithogalum algeriense subsp. baeticum

Ornithogalum algeriense subsp.

baeticum, commonly known as the Baetican star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers.
