Oncidium posadaroides

Oncidium posadaroides, commonly known as the Posada's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often yellow or greenish-yellow flowers with contrasting purple or brown markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, supporting its evergreen foliage.

Oncidium posadaroides is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
