Oxalis peduncularis var. peduncularis

Oxalis peduncularis var. peduncularis, commonly known as the spurred oxalis, is a species native to South America.

It is a flowering plant that produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and showy blooms.
