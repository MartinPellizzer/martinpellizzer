Orchis sthenanthera

Orchis sthenanthera, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and are arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Orchis sthenanthera is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils in woodland or meadow habitats.
