Oberonia japenensis

Oberonia japenensis, commonly known as the Japanese Oberonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescences and strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, adapted to low-light conditions.

Oberonia japenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
