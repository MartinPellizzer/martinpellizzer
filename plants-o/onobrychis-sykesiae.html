Onobrychis sykesiae

Onobrychis sykesiae, commonly known as the Sykes' vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. Its seed pods are slender and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Onobrychis sykesiae is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
