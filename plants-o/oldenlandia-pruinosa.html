Oldenlandia pruinosa

Oldenlandia pruinosa, commonly known as the silverleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers bloom throughout the year, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Oldenlandia pruinosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
