Oncidium angustisegmentum

Oncidium angustisegmentum, commonly known as the narrow-segmented orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive three-lobed lip and are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Oncidium angustisegmentum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
