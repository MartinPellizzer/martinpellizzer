Oxytropis trautvetteri

Oxytropis trautvetteri, commonly known as the Trautvetter's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, often at high elevations.
