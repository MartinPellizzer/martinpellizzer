Oeosporangium elegans

Oeosporangium elegans, commonly known as the elegant spore-bearing plant, is a rare and specialized fern-like organism found in tropical regions.

It is characterized by its unique reproductive structures called oesporangia, which are responsible for producing spores. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near water bodies. Its leaves are usually elongated and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
