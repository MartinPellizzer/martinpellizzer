Oldenlandia herbacea var. uniflora

Oldenlandia herbacea var. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower carpet grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming during the wet season. The plant has fibrous roots and a sprawling growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
