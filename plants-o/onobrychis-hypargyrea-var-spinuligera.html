Onobrychis hypargyrea var. spinuligera

Onobrychis hypargyrea var. spinuligera, commonly known as the silver burclover, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green trifoliate leaves. The plant produces cylindrical, spiny pods that contain small, dark brown seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed sites.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for soil stabilization and as a forage species.
