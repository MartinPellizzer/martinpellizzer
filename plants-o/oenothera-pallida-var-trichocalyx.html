Oenothera pallida var. trichocalyx

Oenothera pallida var. trichocalyx, commonly known as the pale evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and pale yellow flowers that open in the evening. The species is known for its ecological importance as a food source for various pollinators and its role in stabilizing soil in disturbed areas.
