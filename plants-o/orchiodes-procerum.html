Orchiodes procerum

Orchiodes procerum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, gracefully curved flower spikes that bear numerous small, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and cloud forests. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a dense rosette at the base.

The flowers, which range in color from white to pale yellow, are highly specialized with intricate structures adapted for pollination by specific insect species.
