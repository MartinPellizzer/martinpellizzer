Ononis tridentata var. intricata

Ononis tridentata var. intricata, commonly known as the intricate sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by rounded, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three lobes that are often hairy and slightly lobed.

This variety is noted for its intricate leaf patterns, which give it its common name.
