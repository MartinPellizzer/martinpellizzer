Orchis nanhutashanensis

Orchis nanhutashanensis, commonly known as the Nanhutashan orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in nutrient-rich soils. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and hues of purple and white, making it visually striking.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
