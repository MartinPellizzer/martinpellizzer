Osteospermum australe

Osteospermum australe, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Osteospermum australe is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
