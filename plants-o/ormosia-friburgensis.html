Ormosia friburgensis

Ormosia friburgensis, commonly known as the Friburgensis legume, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its dense foliage and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Ormosia friburgensis is also valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in construction and furniture.
