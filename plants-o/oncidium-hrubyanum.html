Oncidium hrubyanum

Oncidium hrubyanum, commonly known as the Hrubyan's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species, which stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, adapted to capture sunlight in its natural habitat.

Oncidium hrubyanum is popular among orchid growers for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
