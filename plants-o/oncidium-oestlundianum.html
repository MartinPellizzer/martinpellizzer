Oncidium oestlundianum

Oncidium oestlundianum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often covered in a fine, fuzzy coating.

Oncidium oestlundianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
