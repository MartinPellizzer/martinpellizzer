Onopordum nervosum var. glomeratum

Onopordum nervosum var. glomeratum, commonly known as the clustered thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray, woolly foliage with spiny margins and produces dense clusters of purple to pink flowers. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species adapted to Mediterranean climates.
