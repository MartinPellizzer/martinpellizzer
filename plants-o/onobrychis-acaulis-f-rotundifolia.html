Onobrychis acaulis f. rotundifolia

Onobrychis acaulis f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved sickle vetch, is a variant of the slender vetch.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and rounded, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean regions.

This form is notable for its horticultural interest and use in ornamental gardening.
