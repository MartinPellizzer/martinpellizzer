Osteomeles pyracantha

Osteomeles pyracantha, commonly known as the Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and produces clusters of small, orange-red berries that are edible when ripe. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Osteomeles pyracantha is also valued for its ability to attract birds and its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
