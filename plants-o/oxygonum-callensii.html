Oxygonum callensii

Oxygonum callensii, commonly known as the dwarf water hyacinth, is a floating aquatic plant native to South America.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are typically light green to bluish in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in hue. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
