Ononis Cristata Var. Mairei

Ononis cristata var. mairei is a rare and distinctive plant species known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance. Native to specific regions in southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and coastal environments where it adapts to harsh conditions. This variant of Ononis cristata is characterized by its intricate, helmet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile habitats.
