Oberonia kaernbachiana

Oberonia kaernbachiana, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the undergrowth of rainforests. Its rhizome is fibrous, and it produces small, ovate leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Oberonia kaernbachiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
