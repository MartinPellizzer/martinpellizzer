Olearia ramulosa var. rigida

Olearia ramulosa var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid daisy bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract native pollinators.
