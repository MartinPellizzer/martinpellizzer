Orchis × ligustica

Orchis × ligustica, commonly known as the Italian twayblade, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, narrow spike of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in association with other orchid species. Orchis × ligustica is a perennial herb that produces a single, erect flowering stem.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
