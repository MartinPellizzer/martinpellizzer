Ornithogalum boissieri

Ornithogalum boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its bulbs are underground storage organs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum boissieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
