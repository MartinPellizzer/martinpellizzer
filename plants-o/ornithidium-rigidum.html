Ornithidium rigidum

Ornithidium rigidum, commonly known as the rigid feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Ornithidium rigidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
