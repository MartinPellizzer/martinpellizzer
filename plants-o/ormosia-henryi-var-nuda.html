Ormosia henryi var. nuda

Ormosia henryi var. nuda, commonly known as the naked ormosia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, bean-like pods.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
