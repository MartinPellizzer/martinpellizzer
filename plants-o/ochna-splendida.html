Ochna splendida

Ochna splendida, commonly known as the splendour ochna, is a striking shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple center. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain smooth, dark seeds. Ochna splendida thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its colorful blooms.
