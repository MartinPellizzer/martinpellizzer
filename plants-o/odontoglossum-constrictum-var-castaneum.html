Odontoglossum constrictum var. castaneum

Odontoglossum constrictum var. castaneum, commonly known as the red-flowered odontoglossum, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red or orange flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments at high elevations, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are dark green, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
