Origanum × lirium

Origanum × lirium, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, and its leaves are covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant is valued for its citrusy aroma, which comes from the essential oils present in its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and culinary use.

Origanum × lirium is also known for its hardiness and ability to attract beneficial insects like bees and butterflies.
