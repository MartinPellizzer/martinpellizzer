Ormosia sumatrana

Ormosia sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
