Operculina pteropus

Operculina pteropus, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a fleshy, orange-red fruit that contains numerous seeds. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Operculina pteropus is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional uses in herbal medicine.
