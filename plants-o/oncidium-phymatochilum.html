Oncidium phymatochilum

Oncidium phymatochilum, commonly known as the "Phytochilum orchid," is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, featuring pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendant inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Oncidium phymatochilum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
