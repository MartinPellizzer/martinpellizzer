Ophiorrhiza trichocarpos var. glabra

Ophiorrhiza trichocarpos var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, glabrous stems and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth, and is often found in lowland areas. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem, while the flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

This species is notable for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity and its potential use in traditional medicine.
