Othonnopsis marocana

Othonnopsis marocana, commonly known as Moroccan stonecress, is a small flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Othonnopsis marocana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
