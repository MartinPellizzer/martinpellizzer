Oenothera communis f. parviflora

Oenothera communis f. parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This variety is notable for its shorter stature compared to the typical Oenothera communis.
