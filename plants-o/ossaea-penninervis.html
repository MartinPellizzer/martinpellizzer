Ossaea penninervis

Ossaea penninervis, commonly known as the slender-veined grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves with fine, parallel veins. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spike that bears small, pale green flowers.

Ossaea penninervis is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and low maintenance requirements.
