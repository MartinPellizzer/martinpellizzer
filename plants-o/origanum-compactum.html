Origanum compactum

Origanum compactum, commonly known as compact oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its aromatic foliage is used in cooking and herbal remedies.

Origanum compactum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
