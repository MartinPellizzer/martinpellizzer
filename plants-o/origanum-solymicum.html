Origanum solymicum

Origanum solymicum, commonly known as Syrian oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its essential oils, rich in thymol and carvacrol, contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

It is often used in culinary and traditional herbal applications.
