Ochna oconnorii

Ochna oconnorii, commonly known as the O'Connor's ochna, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually red or orange in hue, with prominent yellow stamens. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ochna oconnorii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
