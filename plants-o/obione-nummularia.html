Obione nummularia

Obione nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, round leaves that are typically silver or gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or summer.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
