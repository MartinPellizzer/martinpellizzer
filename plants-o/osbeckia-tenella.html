Osbeckia tenella

Osbeckia tenella, commonly known as the slender osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of less than 10 inches. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Osbeckia tenella is often found in dry meadows and along rocky slopes.
