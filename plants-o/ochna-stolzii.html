Ochna stolzii

Ochna stolzii, commonly known as the yellow ochna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Ochna stolzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
