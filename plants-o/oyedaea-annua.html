Oyedaea annua

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue, purple, or white. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its vines can grow rapidly, often climbing supports or sprawling along the ground.
