Odontonema microphyllus

Odontonema microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaf velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with high humidity and well-drained soil.
