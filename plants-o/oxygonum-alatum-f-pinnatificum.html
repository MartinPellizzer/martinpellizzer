Oxygonum alatum f. pinnatificum

Oxygonum alatum f. pinnatificum, commonly known as the broadleaf winged bean, is a variant of the winged bean plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, large, heart-shaped leaves that are often winged along the edges. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds and can grow up to several meters in height. Its foliage is typically green with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.
