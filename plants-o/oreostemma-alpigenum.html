Oreostemma alpigenum

Oreostemma alpigenum, commonly known as the alpine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant grows in rocky or alpine habitats, often in well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, hairy coating, which helps it retain moisture in harsh environments.
