Oeosporangium pteridioides subsp. reichsteinii

Oeosporangium pteridioides subsp.

reichsteinii, commonly known as the Reichstein's water fern, is a rare and specialized member of the fern family. It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble those of true water ferns.

The plant exhibits a unique reproductive structure, with sporangia arranged in a distinctive circular pattern on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in temperate regions with high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
