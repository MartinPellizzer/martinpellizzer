Oxalis orbicularis

Oxalis orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Oxalis orbicularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
