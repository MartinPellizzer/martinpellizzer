Oberonia dolichostachys

Oberonia dolichostachys, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, gracefully arching stems. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and delicate blooms.
