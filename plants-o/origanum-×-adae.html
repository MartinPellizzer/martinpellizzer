Origanum × adae

Origanum × adae, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a hybrid perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage is often used in culinary applications and herbal remedies due to its distinctive fragrance.

Origanum × adae is valued for its ornamental appeal and versatility in both garden and culinary settings.
