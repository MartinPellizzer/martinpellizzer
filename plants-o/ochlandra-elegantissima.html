Ochlandra elegantissima

Ochlandra elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The botanical features of Ochlandra elegantissima make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
