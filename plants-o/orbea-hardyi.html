Orbea hardyi

Orbea hardyi, commonly known as the hardy orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant produces showy blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Orbea hardyi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
