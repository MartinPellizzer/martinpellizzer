Othonna leptophylla

Othonna leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

Othonna leptophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
