Oxalis haworthii

Oxalis haworthii, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, velvety silver-green leaves with a waxy texture, and its flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. It is known for its ability to tolerate drought and its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Oxalis haworthii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
