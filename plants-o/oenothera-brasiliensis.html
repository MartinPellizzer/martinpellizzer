Oenothera brasiliensis

Oenothera brasiliensis, commonly known as Brazilian evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Oenothera, which includes various species of evening primroses. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Oenothera brasiliensis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
