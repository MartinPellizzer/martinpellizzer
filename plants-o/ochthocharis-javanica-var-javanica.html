Ochthocharis javanica var. javanica

Ochthocharis javanica var. javanica, commonly known as the Javanese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
