Oreocarya cristata

Oreocarya cristata, commonly known as the Sierra Lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, recurved petals that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments, thriving at high elevations. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette near the ground.

Oreocarya cristata is known for its resilience to harsh climates and is often found in mountainous regions.
