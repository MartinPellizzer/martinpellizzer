Otosema macrophylla

Otosema macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf otosema, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Otosema macrophylla thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
