Oncidium ascendens

Oncidium ascendens, commonly known as the ascending orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Oncidium ascendens thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
