Ocotea puberula var. arechavaletae

Ocotea puberula var. arechavaletae, commonly known as the Arechavaleta's ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically dark purple when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
