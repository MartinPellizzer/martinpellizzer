Orrhopygium caudatum

Orrhopygium caudatum, commonly known as the long-tailed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Orrhopygium caudatum is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
