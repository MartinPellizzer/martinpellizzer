Osbeckia zeylanica var. decandra

Osbeckia zeylanica var. decandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Osbeckia zeylanica var. decandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
