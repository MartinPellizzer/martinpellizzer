Oenothera × pseudocernua

Oenothera × pseudocernua, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a hybrid perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening and close by dawn. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, oil-rich seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Oenothera × pseudocernua is also valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
