Odontarrhena discolor

Odontarrhena discolor, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in open, sunny habitats. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Odontarrhena discolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
