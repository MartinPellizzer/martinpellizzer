Oxalis rhombeo-ovata subsp. sustenta

Oxalis rhombeo-ovata subsp.

sustenta, commonly known as the Cape iron cross, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features distinctive, shield-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a prominent central vein.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers with five petals, typically in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil. Oxalis rhombeo-ovata subsp.

sustenta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
