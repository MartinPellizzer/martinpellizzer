Olamblis laxa

Olamblis laxa, commonly known as the slender lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Olamblis laxa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
