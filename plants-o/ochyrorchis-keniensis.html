Ochyrorchis keniensis

Ochyrorchis keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and grasslands. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and ecological adaptations.
