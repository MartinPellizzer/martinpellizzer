Osmodium nigrum

Osmodium nigrum, commonly known as the black osmodium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are a distinctive feature of the species. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.

Osmodium nigrum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
