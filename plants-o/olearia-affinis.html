Olearia affinis

Olearia affinis, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Olearia affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
