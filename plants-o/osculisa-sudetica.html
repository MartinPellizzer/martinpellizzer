Osculisa sudetica

Osculisa sudetica, commonly known as the Sudeten lily, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the flowers are usually white or pale pink with yellow stamens.

Osculisa sudetica is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
