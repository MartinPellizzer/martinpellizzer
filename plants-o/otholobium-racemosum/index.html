Otholobium Racemosum

Otholobium racemosum is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its modest size, Otholobium racemosum holds cultural and ecological significance in its native regions.
