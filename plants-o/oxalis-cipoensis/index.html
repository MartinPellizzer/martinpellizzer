Oxalis Cipoensis

Oxalis cipoensis is a unique and lesser-known member of the wood sorrel family. Native to the high altitudes of the Andes, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its delicate foliage and vibrant flowers make it a striking addition to any garden. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. This article explores the characteristics, care, and significance of Oxalis cipoensis.
