Onistis

Onistis, commonly known as the goldenroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its high content of iridoids and flavonoids. Onistis typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
