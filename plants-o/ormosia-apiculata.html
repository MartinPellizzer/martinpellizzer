Ormosia apiculata

Ormosia apiculata, commonly known as the mountain ash or red ash, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Ormosia apiculata is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.
