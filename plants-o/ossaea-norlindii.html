Ossaea norlindii

Ossaea norlindii, commonly known as the Norwegian mountain grass, is a perennial grass species native to the high altitudes of Scandinavia.

It thrives in cold, arid environments and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, including strong winds and low temperatures.

Ossaea norlindii plays a vital role in stabilizing soil in alpine ecosystems.
