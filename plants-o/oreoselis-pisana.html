Oreoselis pisana

Oreoselis pisana, commonly known as the Mediterranean sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. Oreoselis pisana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
