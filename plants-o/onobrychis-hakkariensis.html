Onobrychis hakkariensis

Onobrychis hakkariensis, commonly known as Hakkari burr vetchy, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant is typically found in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its leaves are trifoliate, typical of many legumes.

The seeds are encased in a hairy pod, which contributes to its distinctive appearance.
