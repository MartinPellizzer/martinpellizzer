Ononis hispida var. glabrescens

Ononis hispida var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its pink or white flowers and succulent leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny conditions. Its leaves are generally smooth and glabrous, which is a distinguishing feature of the variety.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
