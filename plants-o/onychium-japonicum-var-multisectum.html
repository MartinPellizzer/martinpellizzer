Onychium japonicum var. multisectum

Onychium japonicum var. multisectum, commonly known as the Japanese onychium, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and flowering structures. The plant features striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often deeply divided, giving it a fern-like appearance. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically display shades of pink or red.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to moist, shaded environments.
