Odontochilus degeneri

Odontochilus degeneri, commonly known as the Degener's toothed orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and distinctive toothed lip, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
