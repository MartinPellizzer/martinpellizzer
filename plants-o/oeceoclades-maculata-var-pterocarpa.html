Oeceoclades maculata var. pterocarpa

Oeceoclades maculata var. pterocarpa, commonly known as the spotted moth orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, velvety leaves with a distinctive mottled green and silver pattern. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a speckled appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
