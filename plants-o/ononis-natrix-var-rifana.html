Ononis natrix var. rifana

Ononis natrix var. rifana, commonly known as the Rif sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically features wiry, sprawling stems covered with small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers in early spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
