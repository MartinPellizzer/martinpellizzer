Oncidium × maderoanum

Oncidium × maderoanum, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a range of botanical characteristics typical of the Oncidium genus.

It features showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical habitats. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, providing a lush, green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Oncidium × maderoanum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
