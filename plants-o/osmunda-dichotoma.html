Osmunda dichotoma

Osmunda dichotoma, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a large, perennial fern native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has a distinctive rhizome system that supports its extensive growth. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small, oval-shaped pinnae.

Osmunda dichotoma thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands.
