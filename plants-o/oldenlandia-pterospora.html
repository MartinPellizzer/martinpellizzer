Oldenlandia pterospora

Oldenlandia pterospora, commonly known as the winged spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across various habitats. It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
