Ornithopus perpusillus var. elongatus

Ornithopus perpusillus var. elongatus, commonly known as the slender fairy wand, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, feathery inflorescences that resemble small wands, typically blooming in shades of pale blue or purple. The plant has fine, linear leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery bloom, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
