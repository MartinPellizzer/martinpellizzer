Ornithogalum namibiense

Ornithogalum namibiense, commonly known as the Namibian star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It features a rosette of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the rainy season, typically in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Ornithogalum namibiense is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
