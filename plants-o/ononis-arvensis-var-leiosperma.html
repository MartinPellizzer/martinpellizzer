Ononis arvensis var. leiosperma

Ononis arvensis var. leiosperma, commonly known as the smooth-spurred bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, smooth-seeded fruits, which contribute to its classification as a member of the Caryophyllaceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically utilized in traditional herbal remedies.
