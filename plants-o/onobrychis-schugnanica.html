Onobrychis schugnanica

Onobrychis schugnanica, commonly known as the Schugnanica burclover, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish inflorescences that appear in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in harsh conditions.

Onobrychis schugnanica is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and ecological restoration projects.
