Odontoglossum hennisii

Odontoglossum hennisii, commonly known as the Hennis's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in cool, humid environments at high elevations. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers, which are usually showy with intricate patterns. Its flowers are often pale green or yellow with purple markings, and it blooms during the cooler months.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
