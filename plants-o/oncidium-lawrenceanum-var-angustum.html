Oncidium lawrenceanum var. angustum

Oncidium lawrenceanum var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
