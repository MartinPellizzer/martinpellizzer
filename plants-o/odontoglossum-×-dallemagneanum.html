Odontoglossum × dallemagneanum

Odontoglossum × dallemagneanum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Odontoglossum orchid species.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
