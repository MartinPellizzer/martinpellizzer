Occultia

Occultia, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a rare and enigmatic species found in remote, shaded forest environments.

It possesses delicate, translucent leaves that appear almost ethereal under indirect light. The plant's flowers are small and pale, often blending seamlessly with its surroundings, which may contribute to its elusive reputation. Occultia thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soil and requires high humidity to flourish.

Despite its beauty, it remains poorly understood due to its scarcity and the difficulty of studying it in its natural habitat.
