Oncidium guttatum var. dodgsonii

Oncidium guttatum var. dodgsonii, commonly known as the Dodgson's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features pendant, racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
