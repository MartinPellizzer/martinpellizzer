Ophiopogon bodinieri

Ophiopogon bodinieri, commonly known as the Bodinier lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish bloom. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Ophiopogon bodinieri is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and modest floral display.
