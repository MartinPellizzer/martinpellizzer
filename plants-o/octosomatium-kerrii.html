Octosomatium kerrii

Octosomatium kerrii, commonly known as the African orchid or Kerri's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as yellow and orange. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories.

Botanically, it features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
