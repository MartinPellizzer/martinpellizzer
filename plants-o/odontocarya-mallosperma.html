Odontocarya mallosperma

Odontocarya mallosperma, commonly known as the African sandflea plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Odontocarya mallosperma is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
