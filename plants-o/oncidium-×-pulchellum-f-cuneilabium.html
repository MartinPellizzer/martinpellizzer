Oncidium × pulchellum f. cuneilabium

Oncidium × pulchellum f. cuneilabium, commonly known as the cuneilabium orchid, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.

It features pendulous inflorescences with showy, often yellow or orange-colored blooms that have a distinctive, spurred lip. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are broad and slightly falcate, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is a tropical orchid that thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
