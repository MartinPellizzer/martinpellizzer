Oenothera albicaulis

Oenothera albicaulis, commonly known as the whitestem evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Oenothera albicaulis is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

It is a member of the Onagraceae family, which includes other evening primroses and related species.
