Onobrychis humilis subsp. humilis

Onobrychis humilis subsp.

humilis, commonly known as lesser burr medic, is a low-growing perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features trifoliate leaves with stipules and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant is characterized by its cylindrical, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky habitats.

This species is valued for its forage quality and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
