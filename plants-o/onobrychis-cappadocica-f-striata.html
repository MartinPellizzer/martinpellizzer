Onobrychis cappadocica f. striata

Onobrychis cappadocica f. striata, commonly known as the striated goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely striated leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or arid habitats. It has a creeping growth habit and produces small, hard pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
