Oncidium janeirense

Oncidium janeirense, commonly known as the Janeire's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Oncidium janeirense is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
