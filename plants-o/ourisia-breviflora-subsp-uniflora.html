Ourisia breviflora subsp. uniflora

Ourisia breviflora subsp.

uniflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ourisia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, often in alpine or subalpine environments.

The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers in the spring. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched, and it has a compact growth habit.

This species is adapted to cold, harsh climates and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
