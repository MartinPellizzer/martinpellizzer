Orchis militaris var. perplexans

Orchis militaris var. perplexans, commonly known as the confused military orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers that display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant typically grows in woodland habitats, thriving in moist, shaded environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its flowers are designed to attract specific pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species in many regions.
