Oxyanthus swynnertonii var. breviflora

Oxyanthus swynnertonii var. breviflora, commonly known as the Swynnerton's oxyanthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of pink and white, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its popularity among horticultural enthusiasts.
