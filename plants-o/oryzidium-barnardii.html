Oryzidium barnardii

Oryzidium barnardii, commonly known as the Cape York grass.

This plant is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa, particularly found in the Cape Province. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It plays a minor role in local ecosystems but is valued for its resilience and ability to stabilize soil in degraded areas.
