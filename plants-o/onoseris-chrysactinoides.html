Onoseris chrysactinoides

Onoseris chrysactinoides, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of tubular disk florets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its drought tolerance.

Onoseris chrysactinoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
