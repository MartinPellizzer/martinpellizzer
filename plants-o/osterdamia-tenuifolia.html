Osterdamia tenuifolia

Osterdamia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in open, sunny habitats. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.

Osterdamia tenuifolia is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its low maintenance requirements.
