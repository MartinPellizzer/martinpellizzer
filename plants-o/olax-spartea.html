Olax spartea

Olax spartea, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces woody, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Olax spartea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
