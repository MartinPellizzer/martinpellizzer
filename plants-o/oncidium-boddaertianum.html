Oncidium boddaertianum

Oncidium boddaertianum, commonly known as the Boddaert's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences that can be quite long. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium boddaertianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
