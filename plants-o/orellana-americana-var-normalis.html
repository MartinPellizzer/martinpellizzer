Orellana americana var. normalis

Orellana americana var. normalis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high elevations. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and air.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
