Oncerum glareosum

Oncerum glareosum, commonly known as the golden torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow inflorescence, which resembles a torch and is highly ornamental. Its leaves are large, elongated, and often have a glossy texture.

Oncerum glareosum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
