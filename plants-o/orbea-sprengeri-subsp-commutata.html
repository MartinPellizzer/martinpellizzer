Orbea sprengeri subsp. commutata

Orbea sprengeri subsp.

commutata, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments.

The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive orange throat, which bloom in summer. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements. Orbea sprengeri subsp.

commutata thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.
