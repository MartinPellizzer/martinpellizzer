Orbea deflersiana

Orbea deflersiana, commonly known as the Zulu orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its showy, pendulous blooms that resemble orchids. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive purple throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Orbea deflersiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
