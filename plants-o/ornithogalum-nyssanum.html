Ornithogalum nyssanum

Ornithogalum nyssanum, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem that produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Ornithogalum nyssanum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
