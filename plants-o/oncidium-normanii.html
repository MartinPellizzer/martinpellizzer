Oncidium normanii

Oncidium normanii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive white lip, often blooming in clusters during the summer months. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in seasonal climates. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees or other surfaces without soil.

Oncidium normanii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its showy blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
