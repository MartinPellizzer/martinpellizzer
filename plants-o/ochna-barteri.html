Ochna barteri

Ochna barteri, commonly known as the Natal orchid or Natal ochna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that split open to release its seeds. Ochna barteri thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
