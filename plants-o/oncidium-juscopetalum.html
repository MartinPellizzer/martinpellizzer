Oncidium juscopetalum

Oncidium juscopetalum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a yellow or orange hue. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which helps it store water and nutrients, making it adaptable to various growing conditions. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Oncidium juscopetalum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
