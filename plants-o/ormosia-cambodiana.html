Ormosia cambodiana

Ormosia cambodiana, commonly known as Cambodian ormosia, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Cambodia and parts of Vietnam.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Ormosia cambodiana is also valued for its durable wood, which has been historically used in construction and furniture-making.
