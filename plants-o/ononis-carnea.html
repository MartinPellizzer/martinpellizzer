Ononis carnea

Ononis carnea, commonly known as red sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a bluish-gray hue.

Ononis carnea thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as dunes and coastal areas.
