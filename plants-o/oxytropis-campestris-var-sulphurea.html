Oxytropis campestris var. sulphurea

Oxytropis campestris var. sulphurea, commonly known as yellow bur pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the Oxytropis genus. This variant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides, where it contributes to soil stabilization through its nitrogen-fixing abilities.
