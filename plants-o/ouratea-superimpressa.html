Ouratea superimpressa

Ouratea superimpressa, commonly known as the dwarf ouratea, is a species of flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in terminal inflorescences, adding a delicate appearance to the plant.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
