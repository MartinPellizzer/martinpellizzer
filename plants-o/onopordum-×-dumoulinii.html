Onopordum × dumoulinii

Onopordum × dumoulinii, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Onopordum acanthium and Onopordum nervosum, resulting in a plant with distinctive spiny leaves and purple to pink composite flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a robust root system and can thrive in poor soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

Its ornamental value is notable, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
