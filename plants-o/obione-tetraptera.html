Obione tetraptera

Obione tetraptera, commonly known as the four-winged grass.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its name refers to the four-winged seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across different areas.
