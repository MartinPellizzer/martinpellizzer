Ouratea myrioneura

Ouratea myrioneura, commonly known as the white sylph, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elongated, lance-shaped leaves.
