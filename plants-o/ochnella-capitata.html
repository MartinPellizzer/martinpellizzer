Ochnella capitata

Ochnella capitata, commonly known as the red-flowered ochna, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in dense clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Ochnella capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
