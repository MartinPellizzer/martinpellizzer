Osbeckia multiflora

Osbeckia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered osbeckia, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its showy, yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

Osbeckia multiflora is adapted to moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.
