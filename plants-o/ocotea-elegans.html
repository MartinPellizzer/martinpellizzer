Ocotea elegans

Ocotea elegans, commonly known as the elegant ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its small, yellowish flowers are followed by elliptical, dark blue-black berries.

Ocotea elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant foliage.
