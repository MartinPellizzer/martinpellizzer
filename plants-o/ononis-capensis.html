Ononis capensis

Ononis capensis, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Ononis capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
