Ornithogalum uluense

Ornithogalum uluense, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of star-shaped, white flowers. The plant is characterized by its nocturnal blooming habit, with flowers opening at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and unique flowering pattern.
