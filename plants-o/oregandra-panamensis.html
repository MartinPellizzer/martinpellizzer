Oregandra panamensis

Oregandra panamensis, commonly known as Panama oregano, is a flowering plant native to Central America, particularly Panama.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to violet in color. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Oregandra panamensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
