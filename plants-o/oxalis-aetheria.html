Oxalis aetheria

Oxalis aetheria, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often tinged with silver or white, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
