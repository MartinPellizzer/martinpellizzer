Ormostema

Ormostema, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a genus of aquatic plants belonging to the family Nymphaeaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, floating flowers that open in the morning and close at night. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to repel water. Ormostema species are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Asia and Africa.
