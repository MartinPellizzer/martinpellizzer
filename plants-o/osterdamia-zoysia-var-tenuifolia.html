Osterdamia zoysia var. tenuifolia

Osterdamia zoysia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf zoysia, is a grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

However, it is less common in cultivation compared to other zoysia varieties.
