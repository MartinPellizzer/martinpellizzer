Oakes-amesia

Oakes-amesia, commonly known as the silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. Oakes-amesia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
