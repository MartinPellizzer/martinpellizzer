Ophrys tetraloniae var. posidonia

Ophrys tetraloniae var. posidonia, commonly known as the Posidonia orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that enhance its mimicry.
