Oxalis smallii

Oxalis smallii, commonly known as the smallii wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial species that produces attractive foliage and blooms throughout the spring and early summer.

Oxalis smallii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
