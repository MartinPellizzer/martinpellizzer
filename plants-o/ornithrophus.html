The plant is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and it thrives in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils. Ornithrophus is often found in grasslands, meadows, and along roadsides, and it plays a role in nitrogen fixation due to its symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.
