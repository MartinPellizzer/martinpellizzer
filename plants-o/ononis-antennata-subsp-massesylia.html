Ononis antennata subsp. massesylia

Ononis antennata subsp.

massesylia, commonly known as the Massiliensian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
