Ononis atlantica

Ononis atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils.

Ononis atlantica is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and harsh maritime conditions.
