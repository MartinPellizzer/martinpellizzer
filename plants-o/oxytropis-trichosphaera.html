Oxytropis trichosphaera

Oxytropis trichosphaera, commonly known as the hairy globemallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and pinnately compound leaves with small, hairy leaflets. Oxytropis trichosphaera is often found in disturbed sites and is adapted to drought conditions due to its deep root system and hairy foliage.
