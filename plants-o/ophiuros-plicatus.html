Ophiuros plicatus

Ophiuros plicatus, commonly known as the pleated sea star, is a species of sea star belonging to the family Ophiuridae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pleated or ridged skin that gives it a unique appearance. This species is typically found in shallow coastal waters, where it thrives on rocky substrates. Ophiuros plicatus has a central disc with short, flexible arms that help it move and feed on small organisms.

Its ecological role includes contributing to the biodiversity of marine environments and serving as prey for various predators.
