Oenothera prismatica

Oenothera prismatica, commonly known as the prismatic evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that open in the evening and are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

Oenothera prismatica is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
