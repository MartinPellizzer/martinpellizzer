Oenothera cinerea

Oenothera cinerea, commonly known as silverleaf evening primrose, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its medicinal and ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
