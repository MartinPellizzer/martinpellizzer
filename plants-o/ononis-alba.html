Ononis alba

Ononis alba, commonly known as white sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Ononis alba thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
