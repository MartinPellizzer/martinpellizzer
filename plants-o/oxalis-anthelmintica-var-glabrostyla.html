Oxalis anthelmintica var. glabrostyla

Oxalis anthelmintica var. glabrostyla, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes and small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
