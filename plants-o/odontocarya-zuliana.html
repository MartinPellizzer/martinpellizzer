Odontocarya zuliana

Odontocarya zuliana, commonly known as the Zulian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Odontocarya zuliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
