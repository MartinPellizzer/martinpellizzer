Othonna albicaulis

Othonna albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its grey-green, cylindrical stems that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Othonna albicaulis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
