Ornithogalum polymorphum

Ornithogalum polymorphum, commonly known as the variable star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its bulbs are adapted for storage and can remain dormant during dry periods.

Ornithogalum polymorphum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
