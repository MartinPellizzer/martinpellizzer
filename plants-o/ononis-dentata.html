Ononis dentata

Ononis dentata, commonly known as the toothed cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodland or along streambanks. The plant features a creeping rootstock with erect, hairy stems and small, palmately divided leaves. Its flowers are yellow, with five petals, and appear in early summer.

Ononis dentata is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
