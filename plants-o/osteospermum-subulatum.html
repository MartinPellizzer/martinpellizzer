Osteospermum subulatum

Osteospermum subulatum, commonly known as the needlestem osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and compact growth habit.

Osteospermum subulatum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
