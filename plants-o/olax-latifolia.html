Olax latifolia

Olax latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf olax, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually broad and ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in racemes, and can be white, pink, or red in color.

Olax latifolia is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
