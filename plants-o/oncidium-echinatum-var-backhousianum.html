Oncidium echinatum var. backhousianum

Oncidium echinatum var. backhousianum, commonly known as the Backhouse's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal environments. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often covered in a waxy coating.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of growing conditions.
