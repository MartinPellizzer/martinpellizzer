Oenothera boothii subsp. condensata

Oenothera boothii subsp.

condensata, commonly known as the denseleaf evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States. It is characterized by its clustered, narrow leaves and yellow, tubular flowers that bloom at night.
