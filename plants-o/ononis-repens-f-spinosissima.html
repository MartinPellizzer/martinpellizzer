Ononis repens f. spinosissima

Ononis repens f. spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems, which are often covered in small, spiny protrusions. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to thrive in sandy or gravelly soils.

It is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
