Ocotea lindbergii

Ocotea lindbergii, commonly known as the Lindberg's ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland rainforests.

Ocotea lindbergii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
