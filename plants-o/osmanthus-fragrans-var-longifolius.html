Osmanthus fragrans var. longifolius

Osmanthus fragrans var. longifolius, commonly known as the fragrant osmanthus or sweet osmanthus, is a species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, round, orange-colored fruits that are edible and used in traditional Chinese cuisine. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctive aroma.

This variety is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
