Oncidium zikanianum

Oncidium zikanianum, commonly known as the Zikanianum orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting blooms. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Oncidium zikanianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
