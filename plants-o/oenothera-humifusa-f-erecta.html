Oenothera humifusa f. erecta

Oenothera humifusa f. erecta, commonly known as the upright evening primrose, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features upright, branching stems that grow up to 1 meter tall, with lance-shaped, hairy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that open in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths. Its flowers are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
