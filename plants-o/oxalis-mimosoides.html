Oxalis mimosoides

Oxalis mimosoides, commonly known as the mimic buttercup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the buttercup. The plant produces small, rounded fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Oxalis mimosoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
