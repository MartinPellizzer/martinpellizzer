Othonna pillansii

Othonna pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' othonna, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, basal rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which bloom in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil and minimal water.

Othonna pillansii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
