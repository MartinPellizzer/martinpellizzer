Oncidium labiatum

Oncidium labiatum, commonly known as the Lip-shaped Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous, linear pseudobulbs that support its showy, fragrant flowers. The inflorescence consists of multiple racemes, each bearing bright yellow to orange flowers with distinctive lip-shaped petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
