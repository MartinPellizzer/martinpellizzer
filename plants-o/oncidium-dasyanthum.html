Oncidium dasyanthum

Oncidium dasyanthum, commonly known as the showy oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Oncidium dasyanthum requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
