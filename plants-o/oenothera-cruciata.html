Oenothera cruciata

Oenothera cruciata, commonly known as the cross-leaved evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive cross-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
