Obione halimus

Obione halimus, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are small, leathery, and often covered in fine hairs.

Obione halimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and gardening.
