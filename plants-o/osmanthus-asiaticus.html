Osmanthus asiaticus

Osmanthus asiaticus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule containing aromatic seeds.

Osmanthus asiaticus is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and for the production of osmanthus tea and essential oils.
