Onobrychis gracilis var. bulgarica

Onobrychis gracilis var. bulgarica, commonly known as the Bulgarian narrowleaf burr medic, is a perennial legume native to southeastern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its forage quality and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
