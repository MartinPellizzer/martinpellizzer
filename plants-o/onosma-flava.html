Onosma flava

Onosma flava, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Onosma flava is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
