Oberonia ritaii

Oberonia ritaii, commonly known as the Hainan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose cluster and are often pollinated by insects.

Oberonia ritaii is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of conservation interest.
