Oyedaea reticulata

Oyedaea reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate net-like vein patterns on its petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air, and it thrives in warm, humid climates with high rainfall.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
