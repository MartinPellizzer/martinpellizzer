Odontosoria madagascariensis

Odontosoria madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar tooth fern, is a species of fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble a toothbrush. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocky or tree trunks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce small, lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green.
