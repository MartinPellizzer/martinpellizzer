Ornithogalum heterocuspe

Ornithogalum heterocuspe, commonly known as the star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, pale green leaves that are narrow and linear in shape. The plant produces clusters of white or pale blue flowers with six petals, resembling a star, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum heterocuspe is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
