Onopordum majorii

Onopordum majorii, commonly known as the Greater Cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces yellow flowers in late summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green and have a rough, hairy texture.

Onopordum majorii is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
