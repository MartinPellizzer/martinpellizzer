Osteospermum hyoseroides

Osteospermum hyoseroides, commonly known as the blue osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
