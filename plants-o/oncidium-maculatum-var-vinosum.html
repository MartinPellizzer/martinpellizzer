Oncidium maculatum var. vinosum

Oncidium maculatum var. vinosum, commonly known as the Crimson Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, dark red to purple flowers with intricate markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, warm environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in low-light conditions.

The flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a mix of colors that range from deep crimson to maroon.
