Oxalis geniculata

Oxalis geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and trifoliate leaves, which give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis geniculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
