Othonna retrorsa

Othonna retrorsa, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its silver-gray, woolly leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs that help reduce water loss and reflect sunlight.

Othonna retrorsa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
