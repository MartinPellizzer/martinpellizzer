Osmanthus bibracteatus

Osmanthus bibracteatus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The sweet fragrance of its flowers is highly valued in traditional Chinese culture and is often used in teas and perfumes.
