Odontoglossum biberianum

Odontoglossum biberianum, commonly known as the Biberian orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in cool, humid environments at high elevations. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

Odontoglossum biberianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
