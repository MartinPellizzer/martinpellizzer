Ophrys × Delfuocoi

Ophrys × delfuocoi is a rare hybrid orchid that combines the unique traits of its parent species. It is known for its striking mimicry, often resembling female wasps to attract male pollinators. This plant thrives in specific alpine environments where its delicate flowers can flourish. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its existence highlights the intricate relationships between plants and their pollinators in natural ecosystems.
