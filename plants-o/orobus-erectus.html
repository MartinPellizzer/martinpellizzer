Orobus erectus

Orobus erectus, commonly known as the upright spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial herb with erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent spur, and it blooms during the spring and summer months.

Orobus erectus is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
