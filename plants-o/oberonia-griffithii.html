Oberonia griffithii

Oberonia griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in fine, silky hairs.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
