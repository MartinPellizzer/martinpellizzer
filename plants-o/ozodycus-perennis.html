Ozodycus perennis

Ozodycus perennis, commonly known as the everlasting flower, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to produce numerous small, showy flowers throughout the growing season. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several months under favorable conditions. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Ozodycus perennis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
