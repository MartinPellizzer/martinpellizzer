Ouratea subscandens

Ouratea subscandens, commonly known as the climbing ouratea, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Ochnaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, white to pale yellow flowers. The flowers are clustered in umbels and are often visited by various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
