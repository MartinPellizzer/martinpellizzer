Oligopholis

Oligopholis, commonly known as the narrowleaf yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and adaptable species suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
