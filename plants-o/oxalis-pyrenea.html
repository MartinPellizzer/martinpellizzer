Oxalis pyrenea

Oxalis pyrenea, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-pink flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxalis pyrenea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
