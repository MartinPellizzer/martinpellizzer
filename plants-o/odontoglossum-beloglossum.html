Odontoglossum beloglossum

Odontoglossum beloglossum, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces a single or few upright, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically violet or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with yellow or green.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
