Osmanthus ilicifolius f. rotundifolius

Osmanthus ilicifolius f. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf fragrant olive, is a cultivar of the osmanthus plant characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves.

It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that emit a sweet, aromatic fragrance, particularly in the autumn. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its unique foliage and fragrance make it a popular choice for both ornamental and aromatic purposes.
