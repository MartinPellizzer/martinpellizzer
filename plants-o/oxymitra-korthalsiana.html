Oxymitra korthalsiana

Oxymitra korthalsiana, commonly known as the Korthals' oxymitra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.
