Onotrophe oleracea

Onotrophe oleracea, commonly known as the edible mustard plant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants and cruciferous vegetables. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, serrated, and edible, often used in traditional cuisines.
