Oenothera rosea

Oenothera rosea, commonly known as the rose evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Oenothera rosea is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
