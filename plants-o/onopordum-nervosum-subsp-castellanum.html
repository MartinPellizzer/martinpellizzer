Onopordum nervosum subsp. castellanum

Onopordum nervosum subsp.

castellanum, commonly known as the Castellano thistle, is a perennial shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features silver-gray, woolly leaves with a distinctive central vein, and its flowers are typically yellow with purple centers.

The plant produces spiny, globular heads of flowers that are surrounded by bracts. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in Mediterranean flora.
