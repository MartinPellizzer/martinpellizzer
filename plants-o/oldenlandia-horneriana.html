Oldenlandia horneriana

Oldenlandia horneriana, commonly known as the silverleaf false musk, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom during the wet season.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
