Othonna filifolia

Othonna filifolia, commonly known as the hairleaf othonna, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.

Othonna filifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
