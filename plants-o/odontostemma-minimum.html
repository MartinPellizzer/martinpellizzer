Odontostemma minimum

Odontostemma minimum, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes plants like calla lilies and peace lilies. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family.

Odontostemma minimum is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
