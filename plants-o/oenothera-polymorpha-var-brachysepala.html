Oenothera polymorpha var. brachysepala

Oenothera polymorpha var. brachysepala, commonly known as the short-sepaled evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening.
