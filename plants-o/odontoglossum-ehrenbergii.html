Odontoglossum ehrenbergii

Odontoglossum ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Ecuadorian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This herbaceous plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. The flowers are typically violet or purple in color, with a white or yellow throat.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
