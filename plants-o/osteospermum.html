Osteospermum

Osteospermum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers with bright colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The leaves are typically narrow and often covered in fine hairs. Osteospermum species are herbaceous perennials that thrive in full sun and well-drained soil.

They are popular in gardens for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
