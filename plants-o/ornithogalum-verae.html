Ornithogalum verae

Ornithogalum verae, commonly known as the true star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is also cultivated for horticultural and pharmaceutical purposes.
