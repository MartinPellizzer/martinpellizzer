Orbea lutea

Orbea lutea, commonly known as the yellow orbea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, Orbea lutea is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
