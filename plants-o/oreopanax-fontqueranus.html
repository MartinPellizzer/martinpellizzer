Oreopanax fontqueranus

Oreopanax fontqueranus, commonly known as the Fontqueran's arrowleaf, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
