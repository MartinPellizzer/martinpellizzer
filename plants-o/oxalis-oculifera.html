Oxalis oculifera

Oxalis oculifera, commonly known as the eye-leaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that resemble eyes, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.

Oxalis oculifera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
