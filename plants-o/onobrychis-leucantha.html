Onobrychis leucantha

Onobrychis leucantha, commonly known as white vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

White vetch is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
