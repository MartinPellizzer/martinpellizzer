Ormopterum turcomanicum

Ormopterum turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan rock-cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed, with a distinctive appearance that aids in its identification. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Ormopterum turcomanicum plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat due to its deep root system.
