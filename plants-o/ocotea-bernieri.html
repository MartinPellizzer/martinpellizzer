Ocotea bernieri

Ocotea bernieri, commonly known as the yellow sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and ornamental value. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, spherical berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a fragrant, resinous scent.

Ocotea bernieri is valued in horticulture for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in shaded to partially sunny conditions.
