Oncidium brienianum var. sulphureum

Oncidium brienianum var. sulphureum, commonly known as the Sulphur Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers with contrasting orange and brown markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting its evergreen foliage.

The flowers bloom in clusters, often lasting several weeks, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
