Oncidium caucanum

Oncidium caucanum, commonly known as the caucanum orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulbous structure with linear leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically yellow to orange with contrasting purple or brown markings, and they bloom sequentially along the spike. This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright indirect light for optimal growth.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
