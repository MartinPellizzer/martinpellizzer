Osmanthus ilicifolius var. bibracteatus

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. bibracteatus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The leaves are dark green, elliptical, and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, oval berries that are edible but not commonly consumed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
