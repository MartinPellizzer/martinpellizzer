Odontostemma weissianum var. weissianum

Odontostemma weissianum var. weissianum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar and shelter for various wildlife.
