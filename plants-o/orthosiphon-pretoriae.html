Orthosiphon pretoriae

Orthosiphon pretoriae, commonly known as the Pretoria siphon, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically pink or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stems.
