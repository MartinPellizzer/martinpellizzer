Ouratea subscandens var. surinamensis

Ouratea subscandens var. surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam ouratea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant features compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
