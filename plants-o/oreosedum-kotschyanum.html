Oreosedum kotschyanum

Oreosedum kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kotschyan's stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in dry climates.
