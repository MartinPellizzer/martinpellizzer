Onopordum meridionale

Onopordum meridionale, commonly known as the Southern Thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has deeply lobed, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Onopordum meridionale is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
