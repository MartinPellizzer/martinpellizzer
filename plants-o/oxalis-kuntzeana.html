Oxalis kuntzeana

Oxalis kuntzeana, commonly known as the velvetleaf wood sorrel, is a species of flowering plant in the Oxalidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Oxalis kuntzeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
