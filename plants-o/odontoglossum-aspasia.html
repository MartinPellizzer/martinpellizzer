Odontoglossum aspasia

Odontoglossum aspasia, commonly known as the Aspasia orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves and tall, erect stems that bear clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate, spurred petals and showy appearance. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Odontoglossum aspasia is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
