Ornithocephalus torresii

Ornithocephalus torresii, commonly known as the Torresii orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The orchid produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.
