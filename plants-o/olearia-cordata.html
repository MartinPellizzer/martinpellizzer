Olearia cordata

Olearia cordata, commonly known as the silver mat or silver bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Olearia cordata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
