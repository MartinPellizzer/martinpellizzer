Orchis patens var. fallax

Orchis patens var. fallax, commonly known as the autumn orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, often with a few leaves along the stem. The flowers are typically pale green to brownish in color, with a distinct lip petal that is usually darker and more prominent. This variety is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist meadows and woodland edges.

It blooms in late summer to early autumn, making it a notable species in the orchid flora of its habitat.
