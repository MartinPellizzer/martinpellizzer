Oxalis stellata var. stellata

Oxalis stellata var. stellata, commonly known as the star oxalis, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage and flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal.

Oxalis stellata var. stellata is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
