Odontonema dubiosum

Odontonema dubiosum, commonly known as the Brazilian flame vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and can grow as a trailing or climbing vine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
