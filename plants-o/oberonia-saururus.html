Oberonia saururus

Oberonia saururus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a raceme. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Oberonia saururus thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in the understory of tropical forests.
