Onobrychis davisii

Onobrychis davisii, commonly known as Davis' goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.

Davis' goat's rue is known for its drought tolerance and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
