Osmanthus ilicifolius var. purpurascens

Osmanthus ilicifolius var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf osmanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late autumn. The leaves are elliptical, with a leathery texture and a distinctive purplish hue, especially in the autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and the aromatic scent it emits during flowering.
