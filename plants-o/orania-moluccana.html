Orania moluccana

Orania moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a complex inflorescence with multiple flowers arranged along a spathaceous bract. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, tropical environment in which it thrives.

Orania moluccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
