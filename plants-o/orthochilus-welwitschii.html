Orthochilus welwitschii

Orthochilus welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's orchid, is a unique orchid species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to the arid environments where it thrives.

Orthochilus welwitschii is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
