Ornithogalum crispum

Ornithogalum crispum, commonly known as the curly lily or the crested lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often curled or crinkled, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, umbellate clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, often fragrant scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ornithogalum crispum is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
