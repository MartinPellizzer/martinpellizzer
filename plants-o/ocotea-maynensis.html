Ocotea maynensis

Ocotea maynensis, commonly known as the Mayan cinnamon or Mayan laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Belize. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark and leaves have been traditionally used in local medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ocotea maynensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense tropical forests.
