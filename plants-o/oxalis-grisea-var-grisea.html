Oxalis grisea var. grisea

Oxalis grisea var. grisea, commonly known as the silver leaf oxalis, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage and prolonged blooming period.
