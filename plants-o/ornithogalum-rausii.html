Ornithogalum rausii

Ornithogalum rausii, commonly known as the white star lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulb, which stores nutrients for growth. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
