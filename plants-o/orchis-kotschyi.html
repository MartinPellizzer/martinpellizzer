Orchis kotschyi

Orchis kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high elevations, often in areas with limited moisture. The plant produces a single, erect stem bearing a spike of flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple. Its flowers have a distinct three-lobed lip, a characteristic feature of many orchid species.

Orchis kotschyi is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique and delicate appearance.
