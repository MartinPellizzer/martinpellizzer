Ocotea hilariana

Ocotea hilariana, commonly known as the Hilarian's ocotea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico and Costa Rica. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves, which emit a pleasant scent when crushed. Ocotea hilariana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance and fragrance.
