Othonna cacalioides

Othonna cacalioides, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, with narrow, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
