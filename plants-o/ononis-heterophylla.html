Ononis heterophylla

Ononis heterophylla, commonly known as the variable bladderleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with slender, often hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to summer. Its leaves are typically alternate and vary in shape, contributing to its common name.

Ononis heterophylla is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
