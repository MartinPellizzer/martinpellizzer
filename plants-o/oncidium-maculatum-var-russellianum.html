Oncidium maculatum var. russellianum

Oncidium maculatum var. russellianum, commonly known as the Russell's Oncidium, is a flowering orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting orchid blooms that typically feature a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
