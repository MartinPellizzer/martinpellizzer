Orchis cyclochila

Orchis cyclochila, commonly known as the cyclochila orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces a spike of pale yellow to greenish flowers with a unique, recurved lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows.

Orchis cyclochila is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.
