Oxalis petrophila

Oxalis petrophila, commonly known as the rock oxalis, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of five-petaled yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats. Oxalis petrophila is adapted to mild, moist climates and is sensitive to frost and drought.
