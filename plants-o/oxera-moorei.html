Oxera moorei

Oxera moorei, commonly known as the Moore's oxera, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Oxera moorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
