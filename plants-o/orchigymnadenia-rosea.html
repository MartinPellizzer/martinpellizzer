Orchigymnadenia rosea

Orchigymnadenia rosea, commonly known as the pink fairy orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features delicate, pink or rose-colored flowers that bloom in spring, usually in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a short, underground rhizome.

This species is noted for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
