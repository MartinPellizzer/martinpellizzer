Olyra davidseana

Olyra davidseana, commonly known as the Davidseana olive, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture, while its fruit is a capsule containing small, oily seeds.

Olyra davidseana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
