Oenothera gigas

Oenothera gigas, commonly known as the giant evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. Oenothera gigas is a perennial herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
