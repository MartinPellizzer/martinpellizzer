Odontoglossum tenue

Odontoglossum tenue, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces a single, erect stem. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Odontoglossum tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate beauty.
