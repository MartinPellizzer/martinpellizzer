Ononis natrix var. saaniana

Ononis natrix var. saaniana, commonly known as the Saanica bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are arranged in dense clusters along the stems, blooming from late spring to early summer.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
