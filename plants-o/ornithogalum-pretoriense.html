Ornithogalum pretoriense

Ornithogalum pretoriense, commonly known as the Pretoria star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer seasons. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid environments.
