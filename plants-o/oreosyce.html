Oreosyce

Oreosyce, commonly known as the chocolate orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its distinctive, fragrant flowers that resemble chocolate. Its flowers are typically dark brown or purple, with a velvety texture.

Oreosyce is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in horticultural settings for its unique appearance.
