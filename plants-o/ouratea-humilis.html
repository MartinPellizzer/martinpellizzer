Ouratea humilis

Ouratea humilis, commonly known as the dwarf ouratea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features dense, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
