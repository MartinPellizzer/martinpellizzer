Obione kochiana

Obione kochiana, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.
