Oncidium unicolor

Oncidium unicolor, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically borne in clusters on long, drooping inflorescences. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, providing structural support.

Oncidium unicolor is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
