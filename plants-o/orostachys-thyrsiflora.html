Orostachys thyrsiflora

Orostachys thyrsiflora, commonly known as the mountain feather grass, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Asia.

It features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal rainfall.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
