Ophiorrhiza japonica var. amamiana

Ophiorrhiza japonica var. amamiana, commonly known as the Amami orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the Amami Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or rocky crevices.
