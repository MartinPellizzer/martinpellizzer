Onopix

Onopix, commonly known as the blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are finely divided and resemble those of other members of the carrot family. Onopix is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

It is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
