Oncidium rhynchanthum

Oncidium rhynchanthum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly curved. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium rhynchanthum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
