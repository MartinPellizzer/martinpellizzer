Ononis variegata f. oranensis

Ononis variegata f. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranensis variety of variable onyx, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves with contrasting colors, often featuring white or yellow markings against a green background. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
