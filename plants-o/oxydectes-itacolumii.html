Oxydectes itacolumii

Oxydectes itacolumii, commonly known as the Itacolumi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant produces small, green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
