Oxalis acetosella f. rhodantha

Oxalis acetosella f. rhodantha, commonly known as the redwood wood sorrel, is a variant of the common wood sorrel.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red. It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
