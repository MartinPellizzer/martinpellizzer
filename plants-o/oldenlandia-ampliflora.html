Oldenlandia ampliflora

Oldenlandia ampliflora, commonly known as the broadleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
