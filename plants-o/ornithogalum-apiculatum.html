Ornithogalum apiculatum

Ornithogalum apiculatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbs that store nutrients for growth. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens or woodland settings.
