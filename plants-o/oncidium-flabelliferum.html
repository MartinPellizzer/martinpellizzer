Oncidium flabelliferum

Oncidium flabelliferum, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium flabelliferum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
