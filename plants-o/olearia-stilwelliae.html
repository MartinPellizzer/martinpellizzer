Olearia stilwelliae

Olearia stilwelliae, commonly known as the Stewart Island daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, often in sandy or rocky soils. It has silver-gray foliage and is adapted to the temperate climate of its native habitat.

Olearia stilwelliae is a hardy species that is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
