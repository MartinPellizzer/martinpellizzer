Osteospermum pisiferum var. canescens

Osteospermum pisiferum var. canescens, commonly known as the silver osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers with white to pale yellow petals and a prominent central disk. The plant features silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators.

Osteospermum pisiferum var. canescens is typically grown in gardens or as a potted plant due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
