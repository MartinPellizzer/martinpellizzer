Orostachys limuloides

Orostachys limuloides, commonly known as the turtle tail plant, is a succulent native to arid regions of East Asia.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.

Orostachys limuloides is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal watering, making it a low-maintenance plant for gardeners.
