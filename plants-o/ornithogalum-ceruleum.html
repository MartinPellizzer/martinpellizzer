Ornithogalum ceruleum

Ornithogalum ceruleum, commonly known as the blue star lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette around the base. Its bulbs are adapted for storage and can remain dormant during dry periods.

Ornithogalum ceruleum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
