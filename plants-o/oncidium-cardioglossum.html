Oncidium cardioglossum

Oncidium cardioglossum, commonly known as the "Heartleaf Oncidium," is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium cardioglossum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
