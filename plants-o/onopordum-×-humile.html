Onopordum × humile

Onopordum × humile, commonly known as the dwarf milk thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads that resemble thistles. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin, a common trait among species in its genus.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in roadside areas and can be invasive in certain environments.
