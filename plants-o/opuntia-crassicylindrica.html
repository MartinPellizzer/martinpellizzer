Opuntia crassicylindrica

Opuntia crassicylindrica, commonly known as the thick-stemmed prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, cylindrical stems that are covered in clusters of spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its fruit, known as a tunas, is edible and often used in culinary applications.

Opuntia crassicylindrica is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
