Oncidium racemosum

Oncidium racemosum, commonly known as the "Chinese Orchid," is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemose inflorescences, bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are strap-shaped, alternate, and often covered in a waxy cuticle.

Oncidium racemosum is popular in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
