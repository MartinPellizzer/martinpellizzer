Ophioprason fistulosum

Ophioprason fistulosum, commonly known as the slender pipe-root.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, tubular roots and its ability to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
