Oncidium holochrysum

Oncidium holochrysum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its bright yellow flowers and strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their vibrant color and long-lasting display.

Oncidium holochrysum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
