Oxytropis Campestris Subsp. Rishiriensis

Oxytropis campestris subsp. rishiriensis is a rare and unique plant species found exclusively on the island of Rishiri in Japan. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its striking purple flowers that bloom in early summer. This subspecies is adapted to the harsh, rocky coastal environments of its native habitat. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of isolated island ecosystems.
