Oberonia triangularis

Oberonia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped pseudobulbs and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat specificity, Oberonia triangularis is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
