Oxytropis neorechingeriana

Oxytropis neorechingeriana, commonly known as the Neorechinger's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is adapted to thrive in poor soils and can tolerate drought conditions.
