Osteospermum pyrifolium

Osteospermum pyrifolium, commonly known as the fireleaf osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and autumn. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purplish hue along the edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Osteospermum pyrifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
