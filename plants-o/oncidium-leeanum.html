Oncidium leeanum

Oncidium leeanum, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive lemon-like scent. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Oncidium leeanum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
