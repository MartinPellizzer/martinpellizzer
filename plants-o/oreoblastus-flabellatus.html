Oreoblastus flabellatus

Oreoblastus flabellatus, commonly known as the flabellate orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that often feature intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Oreoblastus flabellatus is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and unique botanical features.
