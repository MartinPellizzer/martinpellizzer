Oropetium capense var. hesperidum

Oropetium capense var. hesperidum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Oropetium capense var. hesperidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
