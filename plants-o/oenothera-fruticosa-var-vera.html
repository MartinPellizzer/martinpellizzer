Oenothera fruticosa var. vera

Oenothera fruticosa var. vera, commonly known as the silverleaf evening primrose, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
