Oxalis reflexa

Oxalis reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and small, delicate flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded woodland environments.

Oxalis reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
