Oenothera contorta var. divaricata

Oenothera contorta var. divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, zigzagging stems and yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
