Orostachys aggregata var. boehmeri

Orostachys aggregata var. boehmeri, commonly known as the stonecrop or desert stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It features compact, rosette-forming foliage with fleshy, often blue-green leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for both outdoor cultivation and indoor environments.
