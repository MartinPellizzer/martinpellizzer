Oplopanax elatus

Oplopanax elatus, commonly known as Asian ginseng, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters and red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded, moist forest environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is highly valued in traditional herbal medicine.
