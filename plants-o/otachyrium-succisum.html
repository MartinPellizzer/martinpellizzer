Otachyrium succisum

Otachyrium succisum, commonly known as the succulent orchid, is a unique plant characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and delicate, often fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to certain regions with arid or semi-arid climates. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soil, adapting to environments where water is scarce. Its leaves are thick and waxy, helping to retain moisture, while its flowers are usually small and clustered.

Otachyrium succisum is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging growing conditions.
