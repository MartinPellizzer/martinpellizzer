Odontoloma blumeanum

Odontoloma blumeanum, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking blue flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It grows as an epiphyte, typically found on trees in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique coloration and role in local ecosystems.
