Othonna munita

Othonna munita, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Othonna munita is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
