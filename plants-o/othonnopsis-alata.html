Othonnopsis alata

Othonnopsis alata, commonly known as the winged dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of fleshy, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Othonnopsis alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in arid gardens.
