Origanum vulgare f. glabrescens

Origanum vulgare f. glabrescens, commonly known as sweet marjoram, is a fragrant perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and often used in cooking and herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring dry, Mediterranean climates.

This cultivar is valued for its mild, sweet scent and culinary versatility.
