Ornithoxanthum liotardii

Ornithoxanthum liotardii, commonly known as the yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Ornithoxanthum liotardii is a member of the legume family and has the ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
