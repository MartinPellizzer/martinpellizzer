Oncidium lucayanum var. purpureum

Oncidium lucayanum var. purpureum, commonly known as the Purple Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features long, arching inflorescences with clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
