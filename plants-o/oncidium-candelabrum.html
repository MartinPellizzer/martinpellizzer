Oncidium candelabrum

Oncidium candelabrum, commonly known as the candlestick orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact, branching stem with numerous pseudobulbs that support its growth. The plant produces clusters of pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, resembling candlesticks. Oncidium candelabrum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
