Origanum hirtum

Origanum hirtum, commonly known as curly mint or woolly oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fuzzy leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its leaves are often used in cooking and traditional medicine for their pungent, minty scent.

Origanum hirtum is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
