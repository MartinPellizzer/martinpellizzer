Oncocalyx ugogensis

Oncocalyx ugogensis, commonly known as the Uganda flame flower, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Uganda, and is characterized by its striking, flame-like orange-red flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and emits a strong, sweet scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Oncocalyx ugogensis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
