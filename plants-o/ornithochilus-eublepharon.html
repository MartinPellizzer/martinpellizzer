Ornithochilus eublepharon

Ornithochilus eublepharon, commonly known as the bird's eye orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with a single, narrow leaf. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with prominent, showy sepals and petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
