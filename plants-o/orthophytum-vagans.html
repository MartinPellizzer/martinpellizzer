Orthophytum vagans

Orthophytum vagans, commonly known as the wandering featherplant, is a succulent native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves that have a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Orthophytum vagans is often cultivated for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
