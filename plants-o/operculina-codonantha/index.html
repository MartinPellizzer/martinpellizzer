Operculina Codonantha

Operculina codonantha is a fascinating flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red and orange. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
