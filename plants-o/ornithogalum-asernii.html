Ornithogalum asernii

Ornithogalum asernii, commonly known as the asernii star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum asernii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
