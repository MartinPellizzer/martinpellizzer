Ononis inaequifolia

Ononis inaequifolia, commonly known as unequal-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, and its leaves are characterized by their irregular shape and serrated edges. Ononis inaequifolia is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats, where it is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
