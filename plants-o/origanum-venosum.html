Origanum venosum

Origanum venosum, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, and its leaves are thin, aromatic, and often covered in a fine, vein-like pattern. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its essential oils contain thymol and carvacrol, contributing to its strong lemony scent and medicinal properties.

Origanum venosum is widely used in culinary applications and traditional herbal remedies.
