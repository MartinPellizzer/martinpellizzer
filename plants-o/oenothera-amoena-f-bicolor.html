Oenothera amoena f. bicolor

Oenothera amoena f. bicolor, commonly known as the pink and white evening primrose, is a striking perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, bi-colored flowers that open in the evening, featuring pink petals with a white center. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its blooming period occurs in late summer to early autumn, adding a dramatic floral display to landscapes.
