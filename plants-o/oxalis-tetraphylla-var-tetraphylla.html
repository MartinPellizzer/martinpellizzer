Oxalis tetraphylla var. tetraphylla

Oxalis tetraphylla var. tetraphylla, commonly known as the four-leaf oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with four lobes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or white, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to form dense mats.
