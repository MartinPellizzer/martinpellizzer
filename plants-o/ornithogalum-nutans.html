Ornithogalum nutans

Ornithogalum nutans, commonly known as the nodding cowslip, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers that hang downward. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and blooms during the summer months. It is adapted to well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum nutans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
