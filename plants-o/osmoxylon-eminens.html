Osmoxylon eminens

Osmoxylon eminens, commonly known as the tall osmoxylon, is a member of the Santalaceae family and is characterized by its woody structure and climbing habit.

Its roots are known to form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, enhancing nutrient absorption.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, Osmoxylon eminens is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
