Odontoglossum platycheilum

Odontoglossum platycheilum, commonly known as the flat-lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high elevations. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive flat lip. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and they bloom during the cooler months.

Odontoglossum platycheilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
