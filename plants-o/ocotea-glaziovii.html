Ocotea glaziovii

Ocotea glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's ocotea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by elliptical, dark purple berries. Its wood is valued for its fine grain and durability, often used in furniture and construction.

Ocotea glaziovii plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
