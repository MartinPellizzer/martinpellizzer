Olearia axillaris var. linearis

Olearia axillaris var. linearis, commonly known as the linear leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its foliage and floral structure reflect adaptations to its native environment, making it a distinctive species in the Olearia genus.
