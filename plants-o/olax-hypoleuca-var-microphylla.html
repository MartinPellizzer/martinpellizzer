Olax hypoleuca var. microphylla

Olax hypoleuca var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved olax, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
