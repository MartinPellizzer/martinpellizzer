Ononis viscosa var. breviflora

Ononis viscosa var. breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered hedge parsley, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant has hairy, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to arid environments and suitable for use in xeriscaping.
