Ornithogalum eigii

Ornithogalum eigii, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

Ornithogalum eigii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
