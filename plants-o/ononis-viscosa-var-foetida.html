Ononis viscosa var. foetida

Ononis viscosa var. foetida, commonly known as stinkcup, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in dry, sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy. It is often found in open habitats such as roadsides, wastelands, and disturbed areas.

The species is known for its strong, unpleasant odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom.
