Othonna revoluta

Othonna revoluta, commonly known as the Cape spekboom, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a white, powdery coating. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Othonna revoluta is also noted for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its resilience to drought.
