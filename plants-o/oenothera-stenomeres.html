Oenothera stenomeres

Oenothera stenomeres, commonly known as narrowleaf evening primrose, is a species of flowering plant in the Onagraceae family.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Oenothera stenomeres is often found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.
