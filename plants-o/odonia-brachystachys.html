Odonia brachystachys

Odonia brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It typically grows in dense clusters, with slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which are usually greenish or brownish in hue. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance as a member of the Cyperaceae family.

Odonia brachystachys plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms.
