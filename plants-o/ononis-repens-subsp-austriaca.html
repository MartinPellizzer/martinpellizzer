Ononis repens subsp. austriaca

Ononis repens subsp.

austriaca, commonly known as the Austrian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its prostrate habit, with small, oval leaves arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

This subspecies is adapted to harsh environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological resilience.
