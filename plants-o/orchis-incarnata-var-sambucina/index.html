Orchis Incarnata Var. Sambucina

Orchis incarnata var. sambucina is a rare and delicate orchid native to specific regions of europe. it belongs to the orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and coloration. this variant stands out due to its striking purple and white petals that resemble those of the hawthorn flower. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
