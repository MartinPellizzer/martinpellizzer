Oplismenus cubensis

Oplismenus cubensis, commonly known as Cuban ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive silver-green coloration. Its small, clustered flowers are typically white or pale pink, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

Oplismenus cubensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
