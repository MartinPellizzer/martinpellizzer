Oschatzia Saxifraga

Oschatzia saxifraga is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Europe. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in crevices where other plants struggle to survive. Known for its striking violet-blue petals and compact size, this species is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Its unique habitat and limited distribution make it a subject of ecological interest. Despite its beauty, Oschatzia saxifraga remains under threat from habitat loss and climate change.
