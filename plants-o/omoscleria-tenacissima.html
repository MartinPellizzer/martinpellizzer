Omoscleria tenacissima

Omoscleria tenacissima, commonly known as the hardy rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Omoscleria tenacissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
