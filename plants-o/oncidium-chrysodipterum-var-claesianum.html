Oncidium chrysodipterum var. claesianum

Oncidium chrysodipterum var. claesianum, commonly known as the Golden Lip Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers with red-brown lip and green sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces multiple flower spikes during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
