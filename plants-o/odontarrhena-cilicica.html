Odontarrhena cilicica

Odontarrhena cilicica, commonly known as the Cilicia senna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Odontarrhena cilicica is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
