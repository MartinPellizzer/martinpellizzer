Oryza sativa var. cyclina

Oryza sativa var. cyclina, commonly known as the Japonica rice variety, is a rice plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its shorter grain and lower amylose content compared to other rice varieties. This variety is well adapted to colder climates and is often cultivated in regions with shorter growing seasons. Its botanical features include a compact plant structure and a high yield potential under suitable conditions.

It plays an important role in traditional rice farming and culinary practices in its native regions.
