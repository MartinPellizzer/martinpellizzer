Ornithogalum aestivum

Ornithogalum aestivum, commonly known as the summer snowflake, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in the summer months. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its underground bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum aestivum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
