Orobus setifolius

Orobus setifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved orobus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in scrublands and coastal areas.

Orobus setifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
