Oxalis mathewsii

Oxalis mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews oxalis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oxalidaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Oxalis mathewsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate foliage and unique floral structure.

It is relatively low maintenance and suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
