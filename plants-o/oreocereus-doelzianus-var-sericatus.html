Oreocereus doelzianus var. sericatus

Oreocereus doelzianus var. sericatus, commonly known as the silver column cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
