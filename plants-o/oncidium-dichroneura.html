Oncidium dichroneura

Oncidium dichroneura, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral structures, which resemble a lady's slipper, hence its common name. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. Oncidium dichroneura thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
