Onosma maculata

Onosma maculata, commonly known as the spotted fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant features hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive spotted or mottled appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Onosma maculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
