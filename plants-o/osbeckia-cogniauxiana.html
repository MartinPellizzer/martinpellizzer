Osbeckia cogniauxiana

Osbeckia cogniauxiana, commonly known as the dwarf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a compact, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pale blue to purple, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Osbeckia cogniauxiana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
