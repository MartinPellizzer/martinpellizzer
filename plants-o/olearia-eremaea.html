Olearia eremaea

Olearia eremaea, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in spring and early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Olearia eremaea is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystem.
