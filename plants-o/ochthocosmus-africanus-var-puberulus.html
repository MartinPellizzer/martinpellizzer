Ochthocosmus africanus var. puberulus

Ochthocosmus africanus var. puberulus, commonly known as the African orchid or the velvet orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
