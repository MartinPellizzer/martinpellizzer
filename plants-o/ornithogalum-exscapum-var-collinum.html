Ornithogalum exscapum var. collinum

Ornithogalum exscapum var. collinum, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces striking bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in spring, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

It is also sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
