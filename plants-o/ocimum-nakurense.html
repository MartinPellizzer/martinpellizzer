Ocimum nakurense

Ocimum nakurense, commonly known as Nakuren basil, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal properties.

Ocimum nakurense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
