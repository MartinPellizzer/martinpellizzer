Oliveria bruguieri

Oliveria bruguieri, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a distinctive serrated edge.

Oliveria bruguieri is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
