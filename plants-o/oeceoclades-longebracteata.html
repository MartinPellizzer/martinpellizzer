Oeceoclades longebracteata

Oeceoclades longebracteata, commonly known as the long-bract orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with elongated bracts that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom during the rainy season.
