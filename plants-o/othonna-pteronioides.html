Othonna pteronioides

Othonna pteronioides, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often adorned with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Othonna pteronioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
