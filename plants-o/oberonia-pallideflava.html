Oberonia pallideflava

Oberonia pallideflava, commonly known as the pale-flowered Oberonia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with several narrow, leathery leaves. Its flowers are pale yellow to cream-colored, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to mimic its natural habitat.
