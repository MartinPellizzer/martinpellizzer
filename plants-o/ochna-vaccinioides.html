Ochna vaccinioides

Ochna vaccinioides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are red when mature, containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Ochna vaccinioides is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
