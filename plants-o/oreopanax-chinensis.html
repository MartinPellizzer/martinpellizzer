Oreopanax chinensis

Oreopanax chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese mountain aralia, is a flowering plant native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically palmately arranged with serrated margins. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
