Onobrychis nabelekii

Onobrychis nabelekii, commonly known as the Nabelek's vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

Onobrychis nabelekii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
