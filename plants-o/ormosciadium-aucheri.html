Ormosciadium aucheri

Ormosciadium aucheri, commonly known as the Armenian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
