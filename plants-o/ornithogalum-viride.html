Ornithogalum viride

Ornithogalum viride, commonly known as green star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of pale green, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
