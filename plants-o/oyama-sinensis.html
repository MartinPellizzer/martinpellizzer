Oyama sinensis

Oyama sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese mountain bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained, moist soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Oyama sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
