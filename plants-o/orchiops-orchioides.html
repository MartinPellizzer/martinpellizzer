Orchiops orchioides

Orchiops orchioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive violet or purple coloration. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
