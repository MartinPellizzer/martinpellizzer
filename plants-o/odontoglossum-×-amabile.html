Odontoglossum × amabile

Odontoglossum × amabile, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Odontoglossum aemulum and Odontoglossum strictum.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the Andes Mountains of South America. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Odontoglossum × amabile is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
