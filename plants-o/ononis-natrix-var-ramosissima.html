Ononis natrix var. ramosissima

Ononis natrix var. ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and produces small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or rocky habitats.

This variant is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
