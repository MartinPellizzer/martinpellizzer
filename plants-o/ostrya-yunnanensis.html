Ostrya yunnanensis

Ostrya yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan hop-hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and catkin-like flowers. The tree produces small, winged nuts that are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.

Ostrya yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
