Oxydectes spica

Oxydectes spica, commonly known as the spike dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and a single, elongated flower head that develops into a fluffy seed sphere. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Oxydectes spica is typically found in dry, open habitats such as fields and roadsides.
