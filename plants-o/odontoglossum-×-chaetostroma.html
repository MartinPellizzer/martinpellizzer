Odontoglossum × chaetostroma

Odontoglossum × chaetostroma, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Odontoglossum and Chaetostroma species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers with intricate petal structures. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, adapted to thrive in cool, humid environments.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
