Oncostemum nervosum f. sambiranense

Oncostemum nervosum f. sambiranense, commonly known as the Sambiranense Oncostemum, is a rare and unique variant of the Oncostemum nervosum species.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf venation and slightly different foliage structure compared to the typical form. This plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying plant morphology and regional biodiversity.
