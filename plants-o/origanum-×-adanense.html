Origanum × adanense

Origanum × adanense, commonly known as Turkish oregano, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with narrow, gray-green leaves that are densely packed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils, rich in carvacrol and thymol, contribute to its use in both culinary and medicinal applications.
