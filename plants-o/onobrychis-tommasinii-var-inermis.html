Onobrychis tommasinii var. inermis

Onobrychis tommasinii var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and the plant has a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is distinguished by its lack of thorns, setting it apart from other varieties of Onobrychis.
