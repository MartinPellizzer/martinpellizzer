Orchis latifolia var. hocquartiana

Orchis latifolia var. hocquartiana, commonly known as the Hocquart's broad-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodlands and meadows. It is a perennial herb that blooms in late spring to early summer.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture and botanical gardens.
