Ouratea parviflora var. granulosa

Ouratea parviflora var. granulosa, commonly known as the granulate ouratea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with a leathery texture and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
