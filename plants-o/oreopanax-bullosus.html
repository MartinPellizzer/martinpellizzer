Oreopanax bullosus

Oreopanax bullosus, commonly known as the balloon fig, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed or serrated. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round, fleshy fruits that resemble small balloons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
