Oncidium barbatum var. johnianum

Oncidium barbatum var. johnianum, commonly known as the John's Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with red or brown markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.
