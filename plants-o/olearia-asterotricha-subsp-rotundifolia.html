Olearia asterotricha subsp. rotundifolia

Olearia asterotricha subsp.

rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf daisy, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It features silver-gray foliage with rounded, leathery leaves that are densely covered in fine, white hairs.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
