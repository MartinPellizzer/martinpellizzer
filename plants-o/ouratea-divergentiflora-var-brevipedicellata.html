Ouratea divergentiflora var. brevipedicellata

Ouratea divergentiflora var. brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed ouratea, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, round berries. Its bark is smooth and grayish in color, and it has a moderate growth rate.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
