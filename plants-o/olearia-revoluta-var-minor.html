Olearia revoluta var. minor

Olearia revoluta var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaved New Zealand daisy, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features silver-gray, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a coastal plant.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
