Oberonia katakiana

Oberonia katakiana, commonly known as the Katakian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in montane forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive violet or purple coloration.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
