Oncidium hernandezii

Oncidium hernandezii, commonly known as the Yellow Oncidium, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow or yellow-green flowers with prominent, showy lips. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium hernandezii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
