Ormosia venezolana

Ormosia venezolana, commonly known as the Venezuelan ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America, particularly Venezuela and neighboring regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, tropical forests.

Ormosia venezolana is valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used historically in construction and craftsmanship.
