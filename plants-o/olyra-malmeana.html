Olyra malmeana

Olyra malmeana, commonly known as the Malme's olive, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features simple, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
