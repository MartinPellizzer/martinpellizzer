Onobrychis michauxii f. concolor

Onobrychis michauxii f. concolor, commonly known as the smooth vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with a smooth, glossy texture and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated, hairy pods containing several elliptical seeds. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as slopes and roadsides.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and erosion control.
