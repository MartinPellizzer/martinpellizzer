Oncidium × amictum

Oncidium × amictum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which often have a subtle green or brownish hue. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces numerous racemes of fragrant flowers.

Oncidium × amictum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
