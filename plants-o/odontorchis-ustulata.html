Odontorchis ustulata

Odontorchis ustulata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and North Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands, heathlands, and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are pale yellow with distinct purple spots and are arranged in a loose spike.

Odontorchis ustulata is a short-lived perennial, often flowering in its second year.
