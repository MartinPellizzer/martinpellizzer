Onobrychis tournefortii var. leiocarpa

Onobrychis tournefortii var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

The variety leiocarpa is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves.
