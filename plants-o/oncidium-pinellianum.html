Oncidium pinellianum

Oncidium pinellianum, commonly known as the Pinellian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces racemes of flowers that can last for several weeks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Oncidium pinellianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
