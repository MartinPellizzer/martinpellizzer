Onosma cornuta

Onosma cornuta, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like bracts that surround the flower cluster. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Onosma cornuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.
