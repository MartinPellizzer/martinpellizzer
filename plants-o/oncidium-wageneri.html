Oncidium wageneri

Oncidium wageneri, commonly known as the Wageners Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-shaped and grass-like, often arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Oncidium wageneri prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
