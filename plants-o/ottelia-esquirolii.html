Ottelia esquirolii

Ottelia esquirolii, commonly known as the African water plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its floating leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in a waxy layer to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Ottelia esquirolii thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in aquatic ecosystems and a subject of study in botany.
