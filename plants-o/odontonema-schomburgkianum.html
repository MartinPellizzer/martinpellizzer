Odontonema schomburgkianum

Odontonema schomburgkianum, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with slightly serrated edges and a rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Odontonema schomburgkianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
