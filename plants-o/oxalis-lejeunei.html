Oxalis lejeunei

Oxalis lejeunei, commonly known as the mountain oxalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins and small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are edible and have a tangy, sour flavor, making it a popular plant in some culinary traditions.

Oxalis lejeunei is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
