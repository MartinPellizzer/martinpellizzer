Olgaea talassica

Olgaea talassica, commonly known as the sea olive, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers and leathery, evergreen leaves. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its root system helps stabilize sandy soils, contributing to dune formation.

Olgaea talassica is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
