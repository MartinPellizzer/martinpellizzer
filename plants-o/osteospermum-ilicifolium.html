Osteospermum ilicifolium

Osteospermum ilicifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf osteospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers with purple centers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
