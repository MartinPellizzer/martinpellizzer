Ornithogalum ruthenicum

Ornithogalum ruthenicum, commonly known as the Ruthenian star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, stout rhizome and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ornithogalum ruthenicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
