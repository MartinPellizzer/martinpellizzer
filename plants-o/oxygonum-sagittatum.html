Oxygonum sagittatum

Oxygonum sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Oxygonum sagittatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
