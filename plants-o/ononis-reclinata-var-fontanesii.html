Ononis reclinata var. fontanesii

Ononis reclinata var. fontanesii, commonly known as the Fontanesii sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and coastal environments in the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs, and its leaves are narrow, linear, and often blue-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy and rocky substrates.
