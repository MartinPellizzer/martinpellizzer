Ochna schomburgkii

Ochna schomburgkii, commonly known as the African star glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Ochna schomburgkii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
