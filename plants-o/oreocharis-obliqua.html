Oreocharis obliqua

Oreocharis obliqua, commonly known as the narrow-leafed oreocharis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it blooms during the spring season.
