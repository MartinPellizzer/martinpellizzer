Oenothera cardiophylla var. splendens

Oenothera cardiophylla var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Evening Primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to xeriscaped gardens and a valuable species for ecological studies.
