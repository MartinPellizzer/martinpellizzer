Ornithogalum ulophyllum

Ornithogalum ulophyllum, commonly known as the white star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers with six petals. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Ornithogalum ulophyllum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its elegant floral display.
