Onychium × matsumotoi

Onychium × matsumotoi, commonly known as the hybrid onychium, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural collections.
