Ornithogalum griseum

Ornithogalum griseum, commonly known as the pale star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces clusters of star-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its bulbs are toxic and should be handled with care.

Ornithogalum griseum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
