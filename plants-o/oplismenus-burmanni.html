Oplismenus burmanni

Oplismenus burmanni, commonly known as the silver fern, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white undersides on its lance-shaped leaves, which contrast with its dark green upper surfaces. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Oplismenus burmanni is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
