Onoseris macrophylla

Onoseris macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf sunflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy yellow composite flowers with prominent brown centers, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

Its botanical structure supports pollinators and contributes to ecological diversity in its habitat.
