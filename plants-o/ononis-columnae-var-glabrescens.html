Ononis columnae var. glabrescens

Ononis columnae var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems that are covered in fine, soft hairs that become less prominent as the plant matures. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
