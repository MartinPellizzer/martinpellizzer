Oncidium mixturum

Oncidium mixturum, commonly known as the Orchid Mixtura, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in a raceme. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Oncidium mixturum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
