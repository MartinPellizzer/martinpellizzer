Onobrychis radiata var. aestivalis

Onobrychis radiata var. aestivalis, commonly known as the early vetch, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
