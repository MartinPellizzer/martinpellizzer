Onopordum elongatum

Onopordum elongatum, commonly known as the tall thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, purple or pinkish composite flower heads. The plant has lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Onopordum elongatum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
