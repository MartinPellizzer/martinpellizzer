Odontites Longiflorus

Odontites longiflorus is a striking member of theApiaceae family known for its delicate, elongated flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture.
