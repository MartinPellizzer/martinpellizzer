Orobanche elatior subsp. elatior

Orobanche elatior subsp.

elatior, commonly known as the greater yellow witch. It is a root-parasitic plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family, lacking chlorophyll and relying entirely on host plants for nutrients.

The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and can parasitize a variety of herbaceous plants.

Its life cycle involves germinating in response to chemical signals released by host roots.
