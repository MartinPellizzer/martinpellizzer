Oncidium peltiforme

Oncidium peltiforme, commonly known as the shield orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Oncidium peltiforme thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
