Ossaea krugii

Ossaea krugii, commonly known as the Krug's ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water and withstand harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise rugged appearance. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in rocky or sandy soils.

Ossaea krugii is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
