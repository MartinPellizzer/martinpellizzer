Ormosia esquirolii

Ormosia esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's tallowtree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitats.
