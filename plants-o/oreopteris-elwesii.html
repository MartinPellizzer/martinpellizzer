Oreopteris elwesii

Oreopteris elwesii, commonly known as the Elwes' wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces both sterile and fertile fronds. Its leaves are pinnately divided with a distinctive, serrated margin.

Oreopteris elwesii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
