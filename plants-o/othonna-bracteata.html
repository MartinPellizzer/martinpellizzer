Othonna bracteata

Othonna bracteata, commonly known as the silver leaf othonna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine, dense hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Othonna bracteata is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
