Onychopetalum lanceolatum

Onychopetalum lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf onychopetalum, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Onychopetalum lanceolatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
