Orthophytum striatifolium

Orthophytum striatifolium, commonly known as the Striate Leaf Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often striped or streaked with contrasting colors. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
