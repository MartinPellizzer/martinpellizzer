Orixa japonica f. glabrifolia

Orixa japonica f. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved oriental hibiscus, is a variant of the oriental hibiscus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in temperate climates.
