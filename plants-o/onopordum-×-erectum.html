Onopordum × erectum

Onopordum × erectum, commonly known as the tall thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and spiny leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces purple to pink flower heads composed of numerous small florets. It is native to the Mediterranean region but has been introduced to other parts of the world.

Onopordum × erectum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
