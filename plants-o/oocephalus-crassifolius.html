Oocephalus crassifolius

Oocephalus crassifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved oocephalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and large, broad leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It is native to regions of South America, particularly in the Andes, where it thrives in temperate to subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
