Onopordum murbeckii

Onopordum murbeckii, commonly known as the California thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features a robust taproot system and upright, spiny stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, purple-to-pink composite flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are deeply lobed, with a rough, hairy texture, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Onopordum murbeckii is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
