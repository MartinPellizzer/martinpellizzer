Oreanthes rotundifolius

Oreanthes rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved oreanthes, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves and produces showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
