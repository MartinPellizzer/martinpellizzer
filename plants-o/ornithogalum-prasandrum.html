Ornithogalum prasandrum

Ornithogalum prasandrum, commonly known as the white star lily, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial herb with a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland or shady areas and is native to southern Africa. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.
