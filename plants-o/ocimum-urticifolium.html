Ocimum urticifolium

Ocimum urticifolium, commonly known as Indian borage, is a herbaceous plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and basil. The plant is characterized by its hairy, serrated leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ocimum urticifolium is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
