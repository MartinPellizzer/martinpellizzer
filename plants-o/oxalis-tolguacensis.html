Oxalis tolguacensis

Oxalis tolguacensis, commonly known as the Tolguacensis wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow or orange. The plant is characterized by its succulent stems and leaves, which help it store water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Oxalis tolguacensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in gardens.
