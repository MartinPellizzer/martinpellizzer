Oncidium confusum

Oncidium confusum, commonly known as the confused orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, racemose inflorescence that bears numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with contrasting purple or brown markings, making them visually striking.

Oncidium confusum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
