Oncidium bolivianense

Oncidium bolivianense, commonly known as the Bolivian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters of yellow and green hues. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Oncidium bolivianense is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
