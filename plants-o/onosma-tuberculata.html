Onosma tuberculata

Onosma tuberculata, commonly known as the wrinkled honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Onosma tuberculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
