Ormosia poilanei

Ormosia poilanei, commonly known as the Malagasy tamarind tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are sometimes used in local cuisines. Ormosia poilanei is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in traditional crafts and construction.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
