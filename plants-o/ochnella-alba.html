Ochnella alba

Ochnella alba, commonly known as the white ochna, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Ochnella alba is characterized by its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
