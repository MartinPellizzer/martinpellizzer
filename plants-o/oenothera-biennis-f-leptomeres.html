Oenothera biennis f. leptomeres

Oenothera biennis f. leptomeres, commonly known as the slender-stemmed evening primrose, is a botanical variant of the evening primrose species.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. This form is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as prairies and roadsides. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
