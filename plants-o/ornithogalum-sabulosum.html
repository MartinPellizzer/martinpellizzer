Ornithogalum sabulosum

Ornithogalum sabulosum, commonly known as the sand lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows from a bulbous stem. It prefers well-drained sandy soils and full sun to partial shade.

Ornithogalum sabulosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
