Ornithopus isthmocarpus var. africanus

Ornithopus isthmocarpus var. africanus, commonly known as the African sea holly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and distinctive, spiny seed pods that resemble small horns. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
