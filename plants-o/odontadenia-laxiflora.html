Odontadenia laxiflora

Odontadenia laxiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring.

Odontadenia laxiflora is adapted to withstand drought and is often found in open habitats such as shrublands and grasslands.
