Oldenlandia nitida

Oldenlandia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, delicate white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, often hairy stems and elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Oldenlandia nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blooms.
