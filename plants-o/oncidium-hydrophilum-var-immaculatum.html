Oncidium hydrophilum var. immaculatum

Oncidium hydrophilum var. immaculatum, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
