Oreocnide frutescens subsp. nivea

Oreocnide frutescens subsp.

nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered oreocnide, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Euphorbiaceae family.
