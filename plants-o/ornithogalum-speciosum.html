Ornithogalum speciosum

Ornithogalum speciosum, commonly known as the showy star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features striking, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that form a basal rosette, and its stems are often tinged with purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ornithogalum speciosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
