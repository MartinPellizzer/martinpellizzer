Ormosia basilanensis

Ormosia basilanensis, commonly known as the Basilan ormosia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Ormosia basilanensis is valued for its hard wood and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the regional flora.
