Oryza alta

Oryza alta, commonly known as the tall wild rice, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, broad leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Oryza alta is often found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and riverbanks.

It plays an important ecological role in supporting biodiversity and is also valued for its potential as a food source.
