Ornithogalum vallis-gratiae

Ornithogalum vallis-gratiae, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in the spring. The plant belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and fragrance.
