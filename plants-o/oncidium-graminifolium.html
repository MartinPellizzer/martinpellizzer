Oncidium graminifolium

Oncidium graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Oncidium graminifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
