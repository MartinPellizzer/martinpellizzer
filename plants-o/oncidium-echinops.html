Oncidium echinops

Oncidium echinops, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive spiky appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Oncidium echinops is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
