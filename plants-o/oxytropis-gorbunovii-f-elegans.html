Oxytropis gorbunovii f. elegans

Oxytropis gorbunovii f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, pinnate leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, arranged in clusters, and are attractive to pollinators such as bees.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
