Oreocarya fulvocanescens var. nitida

Oreocarya fulvocanescens var. nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf oreocarya, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, such as cliffs and slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often curling slightly at the edges.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color.
