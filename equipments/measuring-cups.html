measuring cups

Apothecary measuring cups are specialized tools designed to accurately measure small quantities of ingredients in the apothecary trade.

They typically feature a series of engraved lines representing different increments of measurement, such as drams, scruples, and grains. Apothecaries use these cups to prepare customized remedies and treatments, requiring precise measurements to ensure efficacy and safety. The cups often have a pouring lip for easy transfer of ingredients and a sturdy design to withstand frequent use.

By using apothecary measuring cups, practitioners can maintain the highest standards of quality in their work.
