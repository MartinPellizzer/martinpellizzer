measuring cups

Apothecary measuring cups are specialized kitchen tools designed for precise measurement of ingredients in traditional and modern apothecary applications.

These cups are typically made of glass or ceramic and feature engraved graduations to measure ingredients in units of weight, such as drams, ounces, or grams. Apothecaries use these measuring cups to accurately prepare potions, elixirs, and other medicinal concoctions. The precise measurements ensure consistency and quality in the final product.

By using apothecary measuring cups, apothecaries can maintain the highest standards of their craft.
