mortars

Apothecary mortars are stone or ceramic vessels used in traditional medicine and alchemy for mixing and grinding medicinal ingredients.

Apothecaries rely on these mortars to combine and prepare various potions, elixirs, and powders. The mortars' smooth surfaces allow for efficient grinding and mixing of powders and herbs, reducing the risk of contamination. The durability of mortars makes them ideal for heavy use in apothecary settings.

By using mortars, apothecaries can create precise and consistent mixtures for their remedies.
