mortars

Apothecary mortars are traditional grinding and mixing tools used by apothecaries to prepare medicinal concoctions.

These mortars, typically made of ceramic, marble, or wood, consist of a bowl-shaped container and a pestle used to grind and mix various ingredients. Apothecaries rely on mortars to create customized remedies, as they allow for precise control over the texture and consistency of the final product. The use of mortars ensures the quality and potency of medicinal preparations.

By using mortars, apothecaries can effectively combine and process ingredients to create effective treatments.
