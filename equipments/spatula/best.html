An apothecary spatula is a specialized tool designed for measuring and mixing ingredients in the preparation of medicinal compounds.

Apothecaries use this spatula to accurately scoop and transfer ingredients, ensuring precise measurements and consistency in their formulations. Its flat, broad blade and calibrated markings allow for precise measurements, making it an essential tool in the apothecary's workspace. The spatula's durability and ease of cleaning also make it a practical choice for frequent use.

By using an apothecary spatula, apothecaries can maintain the high standards of accuracy and quality required in their work.
