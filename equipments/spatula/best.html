11 Best Spatulas

An apothecary spatula is a specialized tool used by apothecaries and pharmacists to measure and handle small quantities of powders, liquids, and solids in their work.

This spatula typically features a flat, broad blade and a slender handle, allowing for precise control and manipulation of materials. Apothecaries use this spatula to accurately dispense medication, mix potions, and measure ingredients with high precision. Its design also prevents spills and contamination, ensuring a clean and safe working environment.

By using an apothecary spatula, professionals can maintain the highest standards of quality and accuracy in their work.
