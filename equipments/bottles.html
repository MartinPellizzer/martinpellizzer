Apothecary bottles are glass containers designed specifically for storing and dispensing medicinal preparations.

Apothecaries, traditional practitioners of herbal medicine, use these bottles to store and sell their remedies. The unique design of the bottles features a narrow neck and a rounded body, making it easy to label and pour liquids. This functionality allows apothecaries to accurately measure and dispense their medicines to customers.

The use of apothecary bottles promotes a professional and traditional approach to healthcare.
