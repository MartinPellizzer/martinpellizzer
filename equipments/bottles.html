bottles

Apothecary bottles are specialized containers designed for storing and dispensing medicinal liquids and powders.

These bottles are typically made of glass or ceramic materials and feature unique shapes and caps that allow for precise measurements and easy pouring. Apothecaries use these bottles to store and dispense medicinal preparations, such as tinctures, elixirs, and ointments, in a controlled and sanitary environment. The distinctive shape and cap of apothecary bottles also help to prevent contamination and ensure the accurate measurement of ingredients.

This ensures the quality and safety of medicinal preparations.
