7 Best Double Boilers

A double boiler for apothecaries is a specialized cooking vessel consisting of two stacked pots, used to gently heat ingredients without direct heat.

This setup allows apothecaries to melt waxes, dissolve resins, and mix oils without risk of scorching or burning. By controlling the temperature, apothecaries can create precise blends for various applications, such as making medicinal salves or perfumes. The double boiler also helps to preserve the integrity of sensitive ingredients, ensuring their potency and effectiveness.

This tool is essential for apothecaries who require precise temperature control in their craft.
