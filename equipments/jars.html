jars

Apothecary jars are glass containers used to store and display herbal remedies, potions, and other medicinal concoctions.

Apothecaries use these jars to preserve and showcase their handmade products, making them a staple in traditional and modern herbalism. The jars are often decorative and come in various sizes, allowing apothecaries to present their offerings in a visually appealing way. The glass containers also protect the products from light and air, preserving their potency and shelf life.

By using apothecary jars, apothecaries can showcase their craftsmanship and expertise in creating unique and effective remedies.
