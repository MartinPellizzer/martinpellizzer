7 Best Measuring Cups

An apothecary measuring cup is a precise measuring tool used in the preparation of medicinal potions and remedies.

It allows apothecaries to accurately measure ingredients in traditional units such as drachms, scruples, and ounces. This product is essential for apothecaries as it enables them to maintain the precise balance of ingredients required for effective treatments. By using an apothecary measuring cup, apothecaries can ensure the quality and consistency of their medicinal products.

The use of such a cup helps to maintain the integrity and safety of the medicinal preparations.
