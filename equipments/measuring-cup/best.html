9 Best Measuring Cups

An apothecary's measuring cup is a specialized tool used to accurately measure liquids and powders in traditional medicine and herbalism.

Apothecaries rely on this tool to prepare and dispense medicines, ensuring precise dosages and consistency. The measuring cup is typically marked with gradations in apothecary weights, such as drams, scruples, and ounces, allowing apothecaries to measure ingredients with precision. This tool is essential for apothecaries to maintain the quality and efficacy of their remedies.

By using an apothecary's measuring cup, apothecaries can ensure the accuracy and reliability of their work.
