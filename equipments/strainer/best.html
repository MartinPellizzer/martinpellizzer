An apothecary strainer is a specialized tool designed for measuring and filtering ingredients in herbal remedies and medicinal concoctions.

Apothecaries use it to accurately measure and strain powders, liquids, and plant extracts with precision. This strainer features small holes or perforations to remove impurities and ensure the purity of the ingredients. Its use is crucial in apothecary practices for creating consistent and high-quality remedies.

By using a strainer, apothecaries can maintain the integrity and potency of their herbal preparations.
