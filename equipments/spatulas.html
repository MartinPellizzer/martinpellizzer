Apothecary spatulas are specialized tools used by apothecaries to measure and mix ingredients in the creation of medicinal potions and elixirs.

These spatulas typically feature a rounded or oval-shaped head and a long handle, allowing for precise control and manipulation of ingredients. Apothecaries rely on these spatulas to accurately measure the quantities of herbs, oils, and other substances required for their recipes. By using a consistent and precise method, apothecaries can ensure the quality and efficacy of their products.

This specialized tool has been an essential component of apothecary work for centuries.
