pestles

A pestle for apothecaries is a traditional tool used to grind and mix ingredients in the preparation of medicinal remedies.

Apothecaries use pestles in conjunction with mortars, which are shallow bowls made of stone or ceramic, to create homemade medicines and potions. The pestle allows for precise control over the texture and consistency of the final product. By grinding ingredients together, apothecaries can create potent remedies that are tailored to individual needs.

This ancient tool remains an essential component of traditional apothecary practice.
