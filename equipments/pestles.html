pestles

Pestles for apothecaries are handheld tools used to grind and mix medicinal ingredients into various concoctions.

Apothecaries rely on pestles to extract the active properties from herbs, plants, and other substances. The pestle is typically paired with a mortar, a bowl-shaped container, to facilitate the grinding process. By using a pestle, apothecaries can efficiently create customized remedies with precise measurements and blends.

This traditional tool remains essential in the practice of apothecary, allowing for the creation of tailored medicinal preparations.
