lid grips

Lid grips for apothecaries are small, rubberized pads that attach to the lids of apothecary jars to provide a secure grip.

Apothecaries use these grips to prevent the lids from slipping off during use or transport, which can lead to spills and contamination. The grips also help to reduce the risk of injury from sharp edges or broken glass. By providing a stable lid, apothecary grips enable apothecaries to work safely and efficiently.

They are a simple yet effective solution for apothecaries who value precision and safety in their work.
