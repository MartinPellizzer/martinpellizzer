lid grips

Lid grips for apothecaries are small, non-slip covers that attach to the lids of apothecary jars, containers, or vials.

These grips provide a secure grip, preventing accidental drops or spills. Apothecaries use lid grips to maintain a safe working environment, especially when handling fragile or heavy containers filled with valuable or sensitive materials. The grips also help reduce the risk of injury from broken glass or sharp edges.

By using lid grips, apothecaries can ensure a secure and stable work surface.
