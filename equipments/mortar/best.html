11 Best Mortars

Mortar for apothecaries is a traditional tool used in the preparation of medicines and remedies.

It consists of two parts: a bowl or container and a pestle, a grinding tool used to mix and crush ingredients. Apothecaries use this mortar to combine and process various substances, such as herbs, spices, and minerals, to create medicinal compounds. The mortar allows for the precise measurement and mixing of ingredients, ensuring consistency and quality in the final product.

By using a mortar, apothecaries can create customized remedies tailored to individual needs.
