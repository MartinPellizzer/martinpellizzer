9 Best Mortars

Apothecary's mortar is a traditional, handheld grinding instrument used by apothecaries to mix and blend medicinal ingredients.

It typically consists of a bowl-shaped container made of ceramic, stone, or other durable materials. Apothecaries use mortar to combine and grind various substances, such as herbs, spices, and minerals, into fine powders or pastes. This process is essential for creating medicinal preparations, including potions, ointments, and pills.

By using mortar, apothecaries can accurately mix and blend ingredients to create effective and consistent medicinal products.
