Linosyris glabrata

Linosyris glabrata, commonly known as the smooth linosyris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Linosyris glabrata is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
