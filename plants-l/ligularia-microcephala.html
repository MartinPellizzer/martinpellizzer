Ligularia microcephala

Ligularia microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf cowleaf, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive white underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens as a decorative plant.
