Lysimachia campestris

Lysimachia campestris, commonly known as yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

Lysimachia campestris thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
