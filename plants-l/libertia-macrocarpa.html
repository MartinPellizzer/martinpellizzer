Libertia macrocarpa

Libertia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant libertia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces large, fleshy seed pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and striking foliage.
