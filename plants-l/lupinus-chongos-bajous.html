Lupinus chongos-bajous

Lupinus chongos-bajous, commonly known as the Chongos-bajous lupine, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant typically features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Lupinus chongos-bajous is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or open areas with adequate sunlight.
