Liparis commelinoides

Liparis commelinoides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in areas with moist, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in gardens and greenhouses.
