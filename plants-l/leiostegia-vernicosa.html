Leiostegia vernicosa

Leiostegia vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy shield fern, is a rare and delicate plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that give it a striking visual appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on tree trunks or rocky surfaces. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white in color, and it produces spores rather than seeds.

Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered a conservation concern.
