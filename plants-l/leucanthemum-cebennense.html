Leucanthemum cebennense

Leucanthemum cebennense, commonly known as the white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with white petals surrounding a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Leucanthemum cebennense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a rock garden plant.
