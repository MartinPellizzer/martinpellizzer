Leptodermis diffusa var. umbellata

Leptodermis diffusa var. umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella leaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

The leaves are usually broad and leathery, contributing to its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
