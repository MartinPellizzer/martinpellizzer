Lannea schweinfurthii var. stuhlmannii

Lannea schweinfurthii var. stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African wild olive, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

The species is valued for its hardiness and traditional uses, including medicinal applications.
