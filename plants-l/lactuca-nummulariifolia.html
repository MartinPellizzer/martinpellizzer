Lactuca nummulariifolia

Lactuca nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety leaves that are shaped like small coins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sunny conditions.

Lactuca nummulariifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
