Ligularia latipes

Ligularia latipes, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often green with a reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog gardens.
