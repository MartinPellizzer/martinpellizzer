Laelia tibicinis

Laelia tibicinis, commonly known as the Tibicini orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, allowing it to survive in its harsh environment. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Laelia tibicinis is valued for its resilience and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
