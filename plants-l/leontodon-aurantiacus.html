Leontodon aurantiacus

Leontodon aurantiacus, commonly known as the red leontodon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces elongated flower heads.

Leontodon aurantiacus is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it is often found in temperate regions.
