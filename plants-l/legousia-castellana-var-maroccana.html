Legousia castellana var. maroccana

Legousia castellana var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the rocky regions of North Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in racemes, adding ornamental value to its habitat.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is often found in arid and semi-arid environments.
