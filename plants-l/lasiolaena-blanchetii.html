Lasiolaena blanchetii

Lasiolaena blanchetii, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feather-like inflorescences that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has fibrous roots and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Lasiolaena blanchetii is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
