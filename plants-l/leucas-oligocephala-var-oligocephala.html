Leucas oligocephala var. oligocephala

Leucas oligocephala var. oligocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed leucas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
