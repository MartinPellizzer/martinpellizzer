Lampranthus stayneri

Lampranthus stayneri, commonly known as the Stayner's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers during the rainy season, adding a striking visual contrast. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
