Licaria cannella subsp. angustata

Licaria cannella subsp.

angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf licaria, is a perennial shrub native to parts of South America. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of *Licaria cannella*.
