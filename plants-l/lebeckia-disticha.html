Lebeckia disticha

Lebeckia disticha, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking a striking yet low-maintenance plant.
