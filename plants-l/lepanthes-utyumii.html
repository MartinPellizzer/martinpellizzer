Lepanthes utyumii

Lepanthes utyumii, commonly known as the Yutu orchid, is a species of orchid native to the humid forests of Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. Its flowers are often short-lived but highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and moths.

Lepanthes utyumii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
