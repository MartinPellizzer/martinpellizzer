Lathyrus parreiszii

Lathyrus parreiszii, commonly known as the Parrezii pea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces clusters of pea-like flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its pods contain small, edible seeds that are used in traditional cuisines.

Lathyrus parreiszii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to rocky and arid environments.
