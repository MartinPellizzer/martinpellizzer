Lyonia alainii

Lyonia alainii, commonly known as the Alain's lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems.

Lyonia alainii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories on the island of Maui.
