Lawsonia alba var. miniata

Lawsonia alba var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature henna plant, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a ornamental species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and pleasant fragrance, it is valued in both horticultural and traditional contexts.
