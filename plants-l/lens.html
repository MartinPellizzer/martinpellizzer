Lens

Lens, commonly known as the lentil, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its lens-shaped seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowering stems bear small, pea-like flowers that eventually develop into pods containing the edible seeds.

The botanical structure of the lentil includes a fibrous root system and compound leaves with trifoliate arrangements.
