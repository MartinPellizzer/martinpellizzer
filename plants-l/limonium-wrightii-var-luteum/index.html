Limonium Wrightii Var. Luteum

Limonium wrightii var. luteum is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms. Native to the southwestern United States, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. This perennial species is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Its unique color and resilience make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens. Understanding its care and growing conditions can enhance its beauty in both natural and cultivated settings.
