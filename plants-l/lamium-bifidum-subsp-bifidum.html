Lamium bifidum subsp. bifidum

Lamium bifidum subsp.

bifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or purple midrib and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces capsules as its fruit, containing several small, dark seeds. Lamium bifidum subsp.

bifidum is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
