Luxemburgia leitonii

Luxemburgia leitonii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped center, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season, and are highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, Luxemburgia leitonii is a subject of conservation efforts to protect its dwindling population.
