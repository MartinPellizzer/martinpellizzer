Limonium gummiferum var. corymbulosum

Limonium gummiferum var. corymbulosum, commonly known as the clustered sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
