Lysimachia alternifolia

Lysimachia alternifolia, commonly known as the alternateleaf chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Asia and parts of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features alternate, oval-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
