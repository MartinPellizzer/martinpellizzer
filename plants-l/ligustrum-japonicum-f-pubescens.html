Ligustrum japonicum f. pubescens

Ligustrum japonicum f. pubescens, commonly known as the Japanese privet with pubescent leaves, is a variant of the Japanese privet characterized by its hairy foliage.

This plant is a shrub native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinct appearance. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by dark blue berries.

The plant is relatively easy to care for and thrives in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
