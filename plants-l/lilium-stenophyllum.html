Lilium stenophyllum

Lilium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily, is a species of flowering plant in the lily family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the western United States, particularly California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Lilium stenophyllum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
