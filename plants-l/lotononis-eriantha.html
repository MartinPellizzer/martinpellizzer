Lotononis eriantha

Lotononis eriantha, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit with a basal rosette of leaves and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
