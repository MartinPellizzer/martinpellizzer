Lilium canadense var. hartwegii

Lilium canadense var. hartwegii, commonly known as the Hartweg's Canada lily, is a variant of the Canada lily native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands. The plant features showy, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink to reddish in color. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces berries that contain the seeds.

This variant is distinguished by its unique floral characteristics and geographic distribution.
