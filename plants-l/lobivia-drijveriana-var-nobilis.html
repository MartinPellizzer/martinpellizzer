Lobivia drijveriana var. nobilis

Lobivia drijveriana var. nobilis, commonly known as the Drijver's lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically short and cylindrical, reducing surface area to minimize water loss.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
