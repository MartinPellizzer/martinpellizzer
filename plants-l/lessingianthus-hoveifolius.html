Lessingianthus hoveifolius

Lessingianthus hoveifolius, commonly known as the Hovei leafless sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually small and often reduced, giving it a unique appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
