Lessertia rigida var. marina

Lessertia rigida var. marina, commonly known as the marine lessertia, is a rare variant of the lessertia rigida species.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to store water, making it drought-resistant. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are often a deep green color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and ability to withstand harsh maritime conditions.
