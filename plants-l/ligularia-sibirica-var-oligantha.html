Ligularia sibirica var. oligantha

Ligularia sibirica var. oligantha, commonly known as the Siberian ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin and a dark green center. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a panicle inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in cool, humid climates.
