Lomatolepis nudicaulis

Lomatolepis nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lomatoglossum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its simple, linear leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.
