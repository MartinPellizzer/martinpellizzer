Leucas altissima

Leucas altissima, commonly known as the tall leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Leucas altissima is typically found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.
