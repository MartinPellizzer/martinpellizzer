Ligustrum australianum

Ligustrum australianum, commonly known as the Australian privet, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin.

Ligustrum australianum is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
