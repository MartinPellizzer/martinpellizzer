Liparis delicatula

Liparis delicatula, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or streaked.

Liparis delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
