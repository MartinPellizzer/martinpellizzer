Linaria pamirica

Linaria pamirica, commonly known as the Pamir Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the plant family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It is often found in the Pamir Mountains and surrounding areas, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
