Layia munzii

Layia munzii, commonly known as the Munz's golden aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in California and Nevada. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Layia munzii is a perennial herb that is well adapted to arid environments.
