Leucadendron spissifolium subsp. natalense

Leucadendron spissifolium subsp.

natalense, commonly known as Natal candle bush, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more compact growth habit compared to other members of the species.
