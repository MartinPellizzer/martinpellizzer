Laelia praestans var. candida

Laelia praestans var. candida, commonly known as the white laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to produce multiple flower spikes throughout the growing season. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its flowers are notable for their large, white petals and a pale yellow lip, creating a striking and elegant appearance.

This variety is prized for its beauty and hardiness, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
