Limonium ramosissimum

Limonium ramosissimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, making it a hardy perennial.

Limonium ramosissimum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
