Lotus creticus var. collinus

Lotus creticus var. collinus, commonly known as the Crete lotus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features palmate leaves with a distinctive lobed shape and produces showy pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in Mediterranean horticulture.
