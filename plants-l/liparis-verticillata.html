Liparis verticillata

Liparis verticillata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
