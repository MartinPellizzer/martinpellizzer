Lysimachia candida var. depauperata

Lysimachia candida var. depauperata, commonly known as the slender yellow loosestrife, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, trailing stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in wetland gardens.
