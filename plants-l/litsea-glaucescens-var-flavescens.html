Litsea glaucescens var. flavescens

Litsea glaucescens var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered litsea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, aromatic bark and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in panicles, typically blooming in the spring.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that ripens in the fall, making it an important species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
