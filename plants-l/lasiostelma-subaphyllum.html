Lasiostelma subaphyllum

Lasiostelma subaphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in the spring. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry garden settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to native plant collections.
