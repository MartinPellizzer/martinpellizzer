Leea speciosa

Leea speciosa, commonly known as the showy leea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptic to ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Leea speciosa is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
