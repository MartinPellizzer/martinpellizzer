Latania commersonii

Latania commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's fan palm, is a striking evergreen tree native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits, which are an important food source for local wildlife. Its adaptability to coastal environments makes it a resilient species in arid and saline conditions.

Latania commersonii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
