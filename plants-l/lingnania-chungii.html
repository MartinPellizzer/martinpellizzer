Lingnania chungii

Lingnania chungii, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by drupes that are typically red or black when mature.

Due to its unique fragrance and slow growth, it is highly valued in traditional medicine and as a specialty wood.
