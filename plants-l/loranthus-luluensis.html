Loranthus luluensis

Loranthus luluensis, commonly known as the Lulu's mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of host trees, primarily in tropical and subtropical regions.

Loranthus luluensis plays a role in the ecosystem by providing food for birds and other animals that consume its fruits.
