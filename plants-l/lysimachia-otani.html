Lysimachia otani

Lysimachia otani, commonly known as the Japanese chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features small, delicate leaves that are typically oval or rounded with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Lysimachia otani is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
