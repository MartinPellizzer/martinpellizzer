Ligularia amplectens

Ligularia amplectens, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, with a distinctive arrowhead shape at the base. The plant thrives in shallow water, often found in ponds and wetlands, and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in the summer.

Ligularia amplectens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to help stabilize aquatic environments.
