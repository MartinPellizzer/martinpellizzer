Lantana concinna

Lantana concinna, commonly known as the common lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lantana concinna is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
