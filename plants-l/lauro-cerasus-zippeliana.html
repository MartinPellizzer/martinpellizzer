Lauro-cerasus zippeliana

Lauro-cerasus zippeliana, commonly known as the Zippeliana cherry laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, red berries. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

The species is relatively hardy and adaptable to various soil types.
