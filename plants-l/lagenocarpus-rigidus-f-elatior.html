Lagenocarpus rigidus f. elatior

Lagenocarpus rigidus f. elatior, commonly known as the glossy leaf hibiscus, is a variant of the hibiscus species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a glossy appearance, and its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms make it a popular choice for tropical landscaping.
