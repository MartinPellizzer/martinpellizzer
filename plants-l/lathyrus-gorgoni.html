Lathyrus gorgoni

Lathyrus gorgoni, commonly known as the Gorgon pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender, trailing stems with small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, composed of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Lathyrus gorgoni is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance and ability to bloom in late spring.
