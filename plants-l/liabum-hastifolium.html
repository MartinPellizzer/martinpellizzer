Liabum hastifolium

Liabum hastifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved liabum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, often arranged in inflorescences at the base of the leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Liabum hastifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
