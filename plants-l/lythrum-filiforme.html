Lythrum filiforme

Lythrum filiforme, commonly known as narrowleaf purpleweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes other species such as the common lythrum. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats like meadows and wetlands. It produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer.

Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.
