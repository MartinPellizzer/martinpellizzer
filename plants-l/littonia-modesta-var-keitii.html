Littonia modesta var. keitii

Littonia modesta var. keitii, commonly known as the Keitii variant of the modest lily, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure for optimal growth.
