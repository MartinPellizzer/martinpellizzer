Lotus garcinii

Lotus garcinii, commonly known as the African lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, edible aril. The African lotus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and cultural significance in various regions.
