Licuala spectabilis

Licuala spectabilis, commonly known as the giant palm or giant leaved palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its stems are typically unbranched and can grow quite tall, sometimes reaching heights of over ten meters.

The botanical features of Licuala spectabilis make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical gardens.
