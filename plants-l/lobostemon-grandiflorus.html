Lobostemon grandiflorus

Lobostemon grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

Lobostemon grandiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
