Lilium leichtlinii var. tigrinum

Lilium leichtlinii var. tigrinum, commonly known as the tigrine lily, is a rare and striking variant of the leichtlinii lily.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bold orange and red streaks on the white petals, creating a tiger-like pattern. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It produces tall, slender stems with large, showy flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
