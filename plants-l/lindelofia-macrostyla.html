Lindelofia macrostyla

Lindelofia macrostyla, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow blooms. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
