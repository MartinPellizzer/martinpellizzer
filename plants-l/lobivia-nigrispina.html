Lobivia nigrispina

Lobivia nigrispina, commonly known as the Black-spined Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a gray-green color and distinctive black spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Lobivia nigrispina is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
