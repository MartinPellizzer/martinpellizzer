Linum clevelandii

Linum clevelandii, commonly known as Cleveland's flax, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax plants. The plant is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Cleveland's flax is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in indigenous plant use.
