Leucopogon morrisonii

Leucopogon morrisonii, commonly known as Morrison's heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
