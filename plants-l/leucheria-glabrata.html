Leucheria glabrata

Leucheria glabrata, commonly known as the smooth leucheria, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or greenish-white, and are clustered in loose inflorescences.

Leucheria glabrata is often found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
