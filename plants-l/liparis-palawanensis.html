Liparis palawanensis

Liparis palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found exclusively in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
