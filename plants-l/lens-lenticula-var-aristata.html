Lens lenticula var. aristata

Lens lenticula var. aristata, commonly known as the slender lens bean, is a variant of the common lens bean, characterized by its unique morphological features.

It is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. The plant produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are usually oval and slightly hairy.

This variety is valued not only for its nutritional content but also for its adaptability to various soil conditions.
