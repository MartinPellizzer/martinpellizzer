Laelia itambana

Laelia itambana, commonly known as the Itambana orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral displays. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in seasonal environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with intricate patterns, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Laelia itambana thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
