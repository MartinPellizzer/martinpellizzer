Lambertia propinqua

Lambertia propinqua, commonly known as the dwarf lambertia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of bright red flowers that appear in winter. The plant has small, glossy, dark green leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is well adapted to coastal environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Lambertia propinqua is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and resilience.
