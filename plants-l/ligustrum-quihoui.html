Ligustrum quihoui

Ligustrum quihoui, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
