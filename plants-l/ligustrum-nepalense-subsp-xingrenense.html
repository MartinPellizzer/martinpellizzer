Ligustrum nepalense subsp. xingrenense

Ligustrum nepalense subsp.

xingrenense, commonly known as the Xingren privet, is a shrub native to China. It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of white flowers.

The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
