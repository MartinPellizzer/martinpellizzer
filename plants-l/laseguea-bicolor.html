Laseguea bicolor

Laseguea bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features striking blue or violet flowers with white markings, typically arranged in racemes. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.
