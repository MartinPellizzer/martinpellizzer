Lantana frutilla var. obtusifolia

Lantana frutilla var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The leaves are typically broad and oval-shaped, with a slightly waxy texture. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil types.

It is also known for its resistance to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice in landscaping.
