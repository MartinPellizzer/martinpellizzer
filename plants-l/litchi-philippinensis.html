Litchi philippinensis

Litchi philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine litchi, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It produces small, round fruits with a thick, red outer rind and a white, juicy, seed-filled interior. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, often with a glossy green surface.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and the unique flavor of its fruit.
