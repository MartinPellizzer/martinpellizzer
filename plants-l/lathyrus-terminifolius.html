Lathyrus terminifolius

Lathyrus terminifolius, commonly known as the slender pea or the mountain pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, often found in mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features slender, upright stems and trifoliate leaves, with small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several small, angular seeds.

Lathyrus terminifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
