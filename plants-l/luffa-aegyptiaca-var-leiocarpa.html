Luffa aegyptiaca var. leiocarpa

Luffa aegyptiaca var. leiocarpa, commonly known as sponge gourd, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, fibrous, and spongy fruit, which is harvested when immature and used as a natural sponge. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has heart-shaped leaves with tendrils that aid in climbing.
