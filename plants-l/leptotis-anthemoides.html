Leptotis anthemoides

Leptotis anthemoides, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
