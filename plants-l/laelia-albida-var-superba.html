Laelia albida var. superba

Laelia albida var. superba, commonly known as the white laelia, is a striking orchid native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals and a prominent, often pinkish lip that distinguishes it from other laelia species. This variety thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with ample sunlight and well-drained soil. Its flowers typically bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a prized addition to both greenhouse and outdoor collections.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
