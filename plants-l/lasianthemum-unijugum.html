Lasianthemum unijugum

Lasianthemum unijugum, commonly known as the single-juged bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has opposite, lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
