Lonicera caprifolium var. ruberrima

Lonicera caprifolium var. ruberrima, commonly known as the redflower honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its vibrant red flowers and woody stems.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is native to eastern North America. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
