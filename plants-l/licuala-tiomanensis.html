Licuala tiomanensis

Licuala tiomanensis, commonly known as the Tioman palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit of Licuala tiomanensis is small and typically contains a single seed, making it an important species for both ecological and potential horticultural purposes.
