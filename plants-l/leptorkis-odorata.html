Leptorkis odorata

Leptorkis odorata, commonly known as the sweet-smelling orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Leptorkis odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
