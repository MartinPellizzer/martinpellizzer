Laurus nobilis var. lanceolata

Laurus nobilis var. lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved laurel, is a variant of the sweet bay laurel.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. The leaves are elongated and narrow, with a glossy dark green surface and a subtle aromatic scent. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in culinary and medicinal applications.
