Laelia anceps var. percivaliana

Laelia anceps var. percivaliana, commonly known as the Percival's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often intensely colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically produces a single or few flower spikes, each bearing several blooms with intricate patterns and a pleasant fragrance. It thrives in cool, bright environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
