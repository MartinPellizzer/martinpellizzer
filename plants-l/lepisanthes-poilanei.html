Lepisanthes poilanei

Lepisanthes poilanei, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
