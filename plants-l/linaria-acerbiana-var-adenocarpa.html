Linaria acerbiana var. adenocarpa

Linaria acerbiana var. adenocarpa, commonly known as the腺果马利筋, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its fruit is a cylindrical capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
