Laserpitium latifolium

Laserpitium latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It features basal leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, with hairy surfaces.

The showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
