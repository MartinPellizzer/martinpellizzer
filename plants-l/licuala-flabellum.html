Licuala flabellum

Licuala flabellum, commonly known as the fan palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The trunk is typically short and stout, with a smooth, grayish-brown surface.
