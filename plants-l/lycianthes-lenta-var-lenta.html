Lycianthes lenta var. lenta

Lycianthes lenta var. lenta, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico and Central America. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple. Its berries are orange-red when ripe and contain toxic compounds, making them unsuitable for human consumption.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
