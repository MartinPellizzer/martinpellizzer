Lapeirousia serrulata

Lapeirousia serrulata, commonly known as the serrated orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the fynbos biome.

Lapeirousia serrulata is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
