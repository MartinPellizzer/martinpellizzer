Laserpitium alpinum

Laserpitium alpinum, commonly known as alpine hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, dry conditions. It produces bright yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

Laserpitium alpinum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
