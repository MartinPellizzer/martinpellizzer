Lasianthus lucidus

Lasianthus lucidus, commonly known as the glossy lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

Lasianthus lucidus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
