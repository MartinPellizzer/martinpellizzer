Lannea rubra var. rubra

Lannea rubra var. rubra, commonly known as the red lannea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas and along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
