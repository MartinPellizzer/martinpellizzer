Lachenalia lanceifolia var. maculata

Lachenalia lanceifolia var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive spots or streaks. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of tubular, often brightly colored flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts and gardeners.
