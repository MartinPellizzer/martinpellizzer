Ligularia ucrainica

Ligularia ucrainica, commonly known as the Ukrainian ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Eastern Europe.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration and a serrated margin. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Ligularia ucrainica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in garden settings.
