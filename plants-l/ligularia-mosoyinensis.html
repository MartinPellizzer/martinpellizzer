Ligularia mosoyinensis

Ligularia mosoyinensis, commonly known as the Mosoyin ligularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
