Litsea akoensis var. chitouchiaoensis

Litsea akoensis var. chitouchiaoensis, commonly known as the Chitouchiao litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in parts of China, Vietnam, and Laos. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers.
