Lonicera glabrata

Lonicera glabrata, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its berries, which are red and edible, mature in the fall and are a food source for birds.

Lonicera glabrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
