Lepanthes ramonensis

Lepanthes ramonensis, commonly known as the Ramon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, typically featuring a single, short rhizome. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Lepanthes ramonensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a subject of interest in botanical research due to its unique floral morphology.
