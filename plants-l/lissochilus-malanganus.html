Lissochilus malanganus

Lissochilus malanganus, commonly known as the Malaganus orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant thrives in cool, humid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within the orchid family.
