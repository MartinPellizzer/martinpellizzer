Litsea aban-gibotii

Litsea aban-gibotii, commonly known as the Philippine litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, blue-black berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in tropical rainforests.
