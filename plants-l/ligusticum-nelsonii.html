Ligusticum nelsonii

Ligusticum nelsonii, commonly known as Nelson's angelica, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-like flower clusters.

Its flowers are small and white, arranged in dense, umbrella-shaped inflorescences, and it blooms in late summer to early fall.
