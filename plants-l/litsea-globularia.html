Litsea globularia

Litsea globularia, commonly known as the globular laurier-rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to red as it matures.
