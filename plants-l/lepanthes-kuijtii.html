Lepanthes kuijtii

Lepanthes kuijtii, commonly known as the Kuijt's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Lepanthes kuijtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
