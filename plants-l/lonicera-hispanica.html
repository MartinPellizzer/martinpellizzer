Lonicera hispanica

Lonicera hispanica, commonly known as Spanish honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera hispanica is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
