Lastrea patula

Lastrea patula, commonly known as the slender lastrea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, two-lipped flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its succulent-like leaves and compact size make it suitable for container cultivation and rock gardens.
