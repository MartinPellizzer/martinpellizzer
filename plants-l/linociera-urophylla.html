Linociera urophylla

Linociera urophylla, commonly known as the silky linociera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a faintly fragrant scent. Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Linociera urophylla thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
