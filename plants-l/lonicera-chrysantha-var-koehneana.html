Lonicera chrysantha var. koehneana

Lonicera chrysantha var. koehneana, commonly known as the Koehne's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and wildlife value.
