Lellingeria cantarensis

Lellingeria cantarensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that display vibrant colors, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Lellingeria cantarensis is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists due to its ornamental value and ecological significance.
