Lasianthus rabilii

Lasianthus rabilii, commonly known as the Philippine flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red-orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine pubescence.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
