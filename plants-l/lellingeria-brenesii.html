Lellingeria brenesii

Lellingeria brenesii, commonly known as the Guanacaste tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The tree produces yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

The plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine and as a source of timber.
