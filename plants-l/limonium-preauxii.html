Limonium preauxii

Limonium preauxii, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium preauxii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
