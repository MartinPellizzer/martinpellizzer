Lasianthus hiiranensis

Lasianthus hiiranensis, commonly known as the Hiranensis flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
