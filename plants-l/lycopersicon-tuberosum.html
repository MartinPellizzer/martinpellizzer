Lycopersicon tuberosum

Lycopersicon tuberosum, commonly known as the tomato, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and alternate leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped. The plant produces vibrant red fruits that are rich in lycopene and other nutrients. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular crop in agriculture worldwide.
