Lomaria petiolaris

Lomaria petiolaris, commonly known as the slender leaf lomaria, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a lanceolate shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lomaria petiolaris is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
