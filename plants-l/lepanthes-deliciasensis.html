Lepanthes deliciasensis

Lepanthes deliciasensis, commonly known as the Delicias orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single flower spike with several small blooms.

Lepanthes deliciasensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
