Lasiandra velutina

Lasiandra velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf lasiandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying shades of yellow or green.

Lasiandra velutina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
