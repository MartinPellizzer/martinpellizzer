Lotus fulgurans

Lotus fulgurans, commonly known as the flame lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive red or orange center. The plant thrives in shallow water and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life.

The bloom period typically occurs during the warmer months, making it a popular attraction in botanical gardens.
