Lantana salzmannii

Lantana salzmannii, commonly known as the Cape lantana, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Lantana salzmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
