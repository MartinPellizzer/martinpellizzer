Lepidium spinescens

Lepidium spinescens, commonly known as the spiny cress, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes.

The fruit is a dry, segmented capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
