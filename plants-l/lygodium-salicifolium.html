Lygodium salicifolium

Lygodium salicifolium, commonly known as the salicifolium water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Lygodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green and covered in fine hairs. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth.

The plant's spores are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
