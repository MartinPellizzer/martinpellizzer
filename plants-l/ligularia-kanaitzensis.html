Ligularia kanaitzensis

Ligularia kanaitzensis, commonly known as the Kanaitz Mountain Ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream sides and forest floors.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species for ecological and horticultural studies.
