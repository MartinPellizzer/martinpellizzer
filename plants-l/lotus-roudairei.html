Lotus roudairei

Lotus roudairei, commonly known as the pink lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink flowers that bloom in the early morning and close in the heat of the day. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life.

The lotus is also noted for its symbolic significance in various cultures, representing purity and enlightenment.
