Lantana fiebrigii var. puberulenta

Lantana fiebrigii var. puberulenta, commonly known as the rough lantana, is a flowering shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has hairy, rough-textured leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
