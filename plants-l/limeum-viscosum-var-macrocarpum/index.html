Limeum Viscosum Var. Macrocarpum

Limeum viscosum var. macrocarpum is a unique variant of the limeum plant known for its distinctive features. This plant is characterized by its thick, sticky leaves that give it a unique texture. It thrives in humid environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. Its large, fleshy fruits are a notable feature that sets it apart from other limeum species. Due to its rarity, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
