Lyonia ekmanii

Lyonia ekmanii, commonly known as the Alaska lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine, white hairs.

Lyonia ekmanii thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
