Lindera gambleana var. floribunda

Lindera gambleana var. floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese sweetwood, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
