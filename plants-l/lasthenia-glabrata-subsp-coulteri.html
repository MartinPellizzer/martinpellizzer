Lasthenia glabrata subsp. coulteri

Lasthenia glabrata subsp.

coulteri, commonly known as Coulter's stickyseed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and finely divided leaves.
