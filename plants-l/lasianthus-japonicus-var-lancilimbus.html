Lasianthus japonicus var. lancilimbus

Lasianthus japonicus var. lancilimbus, commonly known as the long-leaved Japanese lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
