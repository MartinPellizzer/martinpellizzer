Limnophila dubia

Limnophila dubia, commonly known as the water thyme.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, typically found in wetland environments. The plant features small, green, thread-like leaves that float on the water surface. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Limnophila dubia is often used in aquatic gardens and can contribute to water purification in natural ecosystems.
