Lepanthes electilis

Lepanthes electilis, commonly known as the electric orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive white and green coloration. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Lepanthes electilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
