Laelia schultzei

Laelia schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's orchid, is a species native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and blooms with intricate, colorful flowers that can range from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Laelia schultzei is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
