Lagoseris robusta

Lagoseris robusta, commonly known as the robust sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a mat-forming habit, characterized by its dense, sprawling growth. The plant features narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Lagoseris robusta is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
