Leiphaimos brachyloba f. cyanea

Leiphaimos brachyloba f. cyanea, commonly known as the blue-leaved leiphaimos, is a unique variant of the leiphaimos genus native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue-green foliage, which is due to a high concentration of chlorophyll and anthocyanin pigments. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
