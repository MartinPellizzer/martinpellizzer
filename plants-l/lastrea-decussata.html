Lastrea decussata

Lastrea decussata, commonly known as the clustered rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. The plant has a woody stem with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
