Leea javanica

Leea javanica, commonly known as the Java leea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Leeaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

The flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in spring or early summer.
