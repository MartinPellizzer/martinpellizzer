Limonium pachynense

Limonium pachynense, commonly known as the Cyprus sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the island of Cyprus.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. Limonium pachynense thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in coastal and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
