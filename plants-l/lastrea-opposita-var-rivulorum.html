Lastrea opposita var. rivulorum

Lastrea opposita var. rivulorum, commonly known as the river lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
