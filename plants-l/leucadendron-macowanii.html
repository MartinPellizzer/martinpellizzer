Leucadendron macowanii

Leucadendron macowanii, commonly known as the Silver Tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, velvety foliage that gives it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, adding visual interest during the flowering season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
