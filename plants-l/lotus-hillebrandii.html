Lotus hillebrandii

Lotus hillebrandii, commonly known as the Hillebrand's lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often floating or partially submerged. Its rhizomes grow along the bottom of water bodies, anchoring the plant and facilitating nutrient absorption.

Lotus hillebrandii is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
