Linaria weilleri var. vera

Linaria weilleri var. vera, commonly known as the Mexican toadflax, is a perennial flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Linaria weilleri var. vera thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.
