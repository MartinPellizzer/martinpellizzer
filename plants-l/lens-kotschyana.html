Lens kotschyana

Lens kotschyana, commonly known as the Persian lentil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds. The plant produces yellow flowers and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in arid and semi-arid climates and is adapted to poor soil conditions.

The seeds are rich in protein and have been used as a traditional food source in various regions.
