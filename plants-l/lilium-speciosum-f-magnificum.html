Lilium speciosum f. magnificum

Lilium speciosum f. magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent lily, is a striking hybrid cultivar of the species Lilium speciosum.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, displaying a range of colors from white to deep red. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
