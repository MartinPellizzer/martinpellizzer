Lonicera nummulariifolia var. cordata

Lonicera nummulariifolia var. cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf honeysuckle, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its growth habit is low and spreading, making it suitable for ground cover or as a border plant.

The foliage remains attractive throughout the year, offering year-round ornamental value in gardens.
