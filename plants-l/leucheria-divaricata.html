Leucheria divaricata

Leucheria divaricata, commonly known as the variable leucheria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
