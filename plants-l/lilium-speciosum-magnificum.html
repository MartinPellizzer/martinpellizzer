Lilium speciosum magnificum

Lilium speciosum magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent showy lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, with petals that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces sword-shaped, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming in late summer to early autumn.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
