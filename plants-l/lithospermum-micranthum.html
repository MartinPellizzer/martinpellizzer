Lithospermum micranthum

Lithospermum micranthum, commonly known as small-flowered lithosperm, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern North America.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and showy blue to violet flowers. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
