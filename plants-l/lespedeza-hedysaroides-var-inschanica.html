Lespedeza hedysaroides var. inschanica

Lespedeza hedysaroides var. inschanica, commonly known as the Chinese lespeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which attract pollinators like bees.

Its fibrous root system helps prevent soil erosion, making it a valuable plant for ecological restoration.
