Lorentzia saltenia var. angustifolia

Lorentzia saltenia var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf lorentzia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and showy blooms.
