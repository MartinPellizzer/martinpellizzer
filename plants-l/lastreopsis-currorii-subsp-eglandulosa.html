Lastreopsis currorii subsp. eglandulosa

Lastreopsis currorii subsp.

eglandulosa, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lastreopsis, is a member of the Apiaceae family. It is characterized by its smooth, non-glandular leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in umbels.
