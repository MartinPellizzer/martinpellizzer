Linochilus lacunosus

Linochilus lacunosus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
