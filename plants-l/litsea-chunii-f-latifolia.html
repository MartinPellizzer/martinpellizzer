Litsea chunii f. latifolia

Litsea chunii f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved litsea, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, Vietnam, and the Philippines.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
