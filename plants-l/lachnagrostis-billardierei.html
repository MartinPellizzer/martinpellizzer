Lachnagrostis billardierei

Lachnagrostis billardierei, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in wet or damp environments, such as swampy areas and margins of water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny flowers. It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is often found in coastal or brackish habitats.

Lachnagrostis billardierei plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
