Ludisia otletae

Ludisia otletae, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in the winter season. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ludisia otletae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
