Laelia anceps var. crawshayana

Laelia anceps var. crawshayana, commonly known as the Crawshay's laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often bicolor flowers that range from yellow to orange with purple or red markings. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical and subtropical climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal blooming.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
