Ligustrum yesoense

Ligustrum yesoense, commonly known as the Yesso privet, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, dark berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum yesoense is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
