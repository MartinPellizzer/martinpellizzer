Lilium kanahirae

Lilium kanahirae, commonly known as the Japanese tiger lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers with dark red speckles. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, strap-like leaves. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help the plant survive winter dormancy.

Lilium kanahirae thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
