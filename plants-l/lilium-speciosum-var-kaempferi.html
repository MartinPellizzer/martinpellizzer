Lilium speciosum var. kaempferi

Lilium speciosum var. kaempferi, commonly known as the Korean tiger lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to Korea.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a distinctive black blotch at the base. The plant produces upright, strap-like leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
