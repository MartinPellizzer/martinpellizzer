Listera yunnanensis

Listera yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan basketflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are heart-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and branching.

Listera yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
