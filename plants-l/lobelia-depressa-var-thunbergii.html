Lobelia depressa var. thunbergii

Lobelia depressa var. thunbergii, commonly known as Thunberg's lobelia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features small, tubular blue to violet flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has hairy, wrinkled leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
