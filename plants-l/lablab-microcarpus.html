Lablab microcarpus

Lablab microcarpus, commonly known as small-seeded lablab, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, edible seeds that are used in various traditional cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.

Lablab microcarpus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
