Ligustrum ibota f. angustifolium

Ligustrum ibota f. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical species, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and adaptability to various soil conditions.

The leaves are evergreen, providing year-round foliage in suitable climates.
