Lowryanthus rubens

Lowryanthus rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered lowryanthus, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a small shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small, bright red flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in understory layers of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
