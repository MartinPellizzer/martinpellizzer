Lampranthus stenopetalus

Lampranthus stenopetalus, commonly known as the firecracker flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its vibrant flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, adding both beauty and ecological value to gardens.
