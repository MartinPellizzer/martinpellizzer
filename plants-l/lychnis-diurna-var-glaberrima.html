Lychnis diurna var. glaberrima

Lychnis diurna var. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous sunflower.

This variant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, distinguishing it from other varieties of Lychnis diurna. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom during the day, hence its common name. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
