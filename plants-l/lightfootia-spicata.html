Lightfootia spicata

Lightfootia spicata, commonly known as the spiky pincushion plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, spherical inflorescence. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spiky heads, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Lightfootia spicata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
