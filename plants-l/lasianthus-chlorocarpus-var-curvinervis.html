Lasianthus chlorocarpus var. curvinervis

Lasianthus chlorocarpus var. curvinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined lasianthus, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with prominent, curved veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
