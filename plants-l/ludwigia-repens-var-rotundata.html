Ludwigia repens var. rotundata

Ludwigia repens var. rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically bright red or orange, depending on water conditions and nutrient levels. This variety thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for aquariums and pond landscaping.

The plant's vibrant coloration and ease of care contribute to its popularity among aquatic enthusiasts.
