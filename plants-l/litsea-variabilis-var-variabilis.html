Litsea variabilis var. variabilis

Litsea variabilis var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It produces small, white flowers followed by elliptical, reddish-brown fruits.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
