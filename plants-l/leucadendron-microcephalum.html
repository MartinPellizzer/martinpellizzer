Leucadendron microcephalum

Leucadendron microcephalum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-blue foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
