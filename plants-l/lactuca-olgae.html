Lactuca olgae

Lactuca olgae, commonly known as the Corsican endive, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and lettuces. The plant is characterized by its curly, dark green leaves that are often used in salads and cooking. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Lactuca olgae is also known for its mild bitterness, which contributes to its unique flavor profile.
