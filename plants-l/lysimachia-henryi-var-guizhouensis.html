Lysimachia henryi var. guizhouensis

Lysimachia henryi var. guizhouensis, commonly known as the Guizhou yellowthread, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
