Lycianthes ferruginea var. firmior

Lycianthes ferruginea var. firmior, commonly known as the red-berried nightshade, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2-5 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture with prominent veins.

The plant is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes.
