Lachemilla cyanea

Lachemilla cyanea, commonly known as the blue moss cushion, is a low-growing evergreen plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically blue or purple and are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Lachemilla cyanea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in rocky or alpine settings.
