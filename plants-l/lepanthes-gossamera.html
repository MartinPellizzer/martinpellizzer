Lepanthes gossamera

Lepanthes gossamera, commonly known as the silver star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem and produces delicate, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive silvery-white color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Lepanthes gossamera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
