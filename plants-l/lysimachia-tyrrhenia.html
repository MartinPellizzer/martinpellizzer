Lysimachia tyrrhenia

Lysimachia tyrrhenia, commonly known as the Tyrrhenian chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has thin, spreading stems and hairy leaves, which help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia tyrrhenia is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and ability to spread easily.
