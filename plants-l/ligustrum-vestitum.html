Ligustrum vestitum

Ligustrum vestitum, commonly known as the European privet, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. Ligustrum vestitum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and adaptability to various soil types.

It is also used in hedges and as a decorative element in gardens.
