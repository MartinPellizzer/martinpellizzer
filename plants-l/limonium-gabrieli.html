Limonium gabrieli

Limonium gabrieli, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silvery-gray, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Limonium gabrieli is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
