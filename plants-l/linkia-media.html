Linkia media

Linkia media, commonly known as the dwarf red hibiscus, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Linkia media is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
