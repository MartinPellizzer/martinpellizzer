Laelia rubescens

Laelia rubescens, commonly known as the red laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in high-altitude, cool environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Laelia rubescens is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
