Lathyrus sphaericus

Lathyrus sphaericus, commonly known as the globe pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, inflated pods that resemble small globes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Lathyrus sphaericus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
