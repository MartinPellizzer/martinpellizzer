Lepanthes aperta

Lepanthes aperta, commonly known as the open lemur orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in a rosette, and the flowers are characterized by their unique, spurred shape.

Lepanthes aperta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
