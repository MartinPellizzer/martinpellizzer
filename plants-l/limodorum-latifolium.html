Limodorum latifolium

Limodorum latifolium, commonly known as broad-leaved lily of the valley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features attractive, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver along the edges. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Limodorum latifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
