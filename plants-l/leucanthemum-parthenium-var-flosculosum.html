Leucanthemum parthenium var. flosculosum

Leucanthemum parthenium var. flosculosum, commonly known as the white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant grows in a compact, bushy form, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, often covered in fine white down.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
