Ligularia tenuicaulis

Ligularia tenuicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or pale margin, giving it an elegant appearance. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture and light conditions.
