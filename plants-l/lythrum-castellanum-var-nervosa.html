Lythrum castellanum var. nervosa

Lythrum castellanum var. nervosa, commonly known as narrow-veined lythrum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes plants such as the common lythrum. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins, and clusters of pink to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
