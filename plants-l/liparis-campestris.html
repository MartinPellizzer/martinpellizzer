Liparis campestris

Liparis campestris, commonly known as the field orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, usually pink or purple, and arranged in a loose spike.

Liparis campestris is characterized by its simple, non-fragrant blossoms and is often found in disturbed soils.
