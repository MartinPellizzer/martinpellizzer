Lobelia cliffortiana var. xalapensis

Lobelia cliffortiana var. xalapensis, commonly known as the Xalapa lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often in mountainous regions. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
