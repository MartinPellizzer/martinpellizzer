Larrea Fruticosa

Larrea fruticosa, commonly known as the desert sage, is a hardy shrub native to the arid regions of the Americas. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and extreme temperatures. Its distinctive yellow-green foliage and fragrant resin make it easily recognizable. The plant has been used for centuries by indigenous peoples for medicinal and practical purposes. Today, it remains a symbol of resilience in the face of desert challenges.
