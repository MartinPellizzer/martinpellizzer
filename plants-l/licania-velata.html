Licania velata

Licania velata, commonly known as the silverleaf licania, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. Licania velata is typically found in moist, shaded areas of tropical rainforests in Central and South America.
