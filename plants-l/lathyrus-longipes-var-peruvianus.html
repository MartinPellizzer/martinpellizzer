Lathyrus longipes var. peruvianus

Lathyrus longipes var. peruvianus, commonly known as the Peruvian grass pea, is a climbing legume native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features slender, twining stems with small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. The plant produces elongated pods containing several angular, yellowish seeds. It is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Due to its nutritional value, it has been traditionally used as a food source in certain indigenous communities.
