Lachenalia unifolia var. wrightii

Lachenalia unifolia var. wrightii, commonly known as the Wright's single-flowered lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower that is usually pink or purple in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
