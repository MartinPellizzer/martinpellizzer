Lomaria polymorpha

Lomaria polymorpha, commonly known as the variable lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often have a silvery or grayish hue.

Lomaria polymorpha is known for its adaptability and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
