Licania leptostachya var. crassifolia

Licania leptostachya var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved licania, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The varietal form, crassifolia, is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical species.
