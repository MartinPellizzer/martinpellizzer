Liparis vestita subsp. seidenfadenii

Liparis vestita subsp.

seidenfadenii, commonly known as the Seidenfaden's velvet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This subspecies is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia.
