Lepanthes jimenezii

Lepanthes jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with intricate patterns. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are broad and ovate, providing a lush green backdrop to its delicate blooms.

Lepanthes jimenezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical studies due to its unique floral structure.
