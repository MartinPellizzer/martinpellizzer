Leucadendron humifusum

Leucadendron humifusum, commonly known as the coastal leucadendron, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and conical or spherical flower heads, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds. It is often used in coastal landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and stabilizing dune ecosystems.
