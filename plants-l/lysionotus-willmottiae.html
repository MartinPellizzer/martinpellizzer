Lysionotus willmottiae

Lysionotus willmottiae, commonly known as the Willmott's sun-orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of New Guinea.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its small, brightly colored flowers that resemble miniature sunflowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a short flowering period during the warmer months. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and limited natural propagation, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
