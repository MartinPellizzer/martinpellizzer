Limodorum ventricosum

Limodorum ventricosum, commonly known as the hooded lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, hooded flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces a tall flowering stem. Limodorum ventricosum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
