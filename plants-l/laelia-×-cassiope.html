Laelia × cassiope

Laelia × cassiope, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Brassia species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Laelia × cassiope is popular among orchid growers for its hardiness and ornamental value.
