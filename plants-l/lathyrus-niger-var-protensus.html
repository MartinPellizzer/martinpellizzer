Lathyrus niger var. protensus

Lathyrus niger var. protensus, commonly known as black vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its tall, erect stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its fruit is a pod containing several elliptical seeds.

Lathyrus niger var. protensus is often grown as a cover crop or for its ornamental value in gardens.
