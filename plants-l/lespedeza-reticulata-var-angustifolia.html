Lespedeza reticulata var. angustifolia

Lespedeza reticulata var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Chinese clover, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender, trifoliate leaves with a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
