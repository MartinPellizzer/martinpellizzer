Lobivia hoffmanniana

Lobivia hoffmanniana, commonly known as the Hoffmann's lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and desert gardens.
