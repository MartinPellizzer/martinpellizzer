Ligularia microcardia

Ligularia microcardia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, deeply lobed leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in umbrella-like clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value in garden settings.
