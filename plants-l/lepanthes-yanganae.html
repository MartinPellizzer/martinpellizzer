Lepanthes yanganae

Lepanthes yanganae, commonly known as the Yanganae orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree branches in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Lepanthes yanganae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
