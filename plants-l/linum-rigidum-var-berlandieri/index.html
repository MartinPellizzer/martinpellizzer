Linum Rigidum Var. Berlandieri

Linum rigidum var. berlandieri is a rare and striking plant native to the southwestern united states. it belongs to the linaceae family and is known for its distinctive blue or purple flowers. this variant is often found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability. its common name, desert flax, reflects its habitat and appearance. despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in botanical studies.
