Lespedeza juncea var. inschanica

Lespedeza juncea var. inschanica, commonly known as Chinese strawsedge, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its botanical features make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
