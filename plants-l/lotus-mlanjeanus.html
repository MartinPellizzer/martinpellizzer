Lotus mlanjeanus

Lotus mlanjeanus, commonly known as the Malanje lotus, is a flowering plant native to the montane grasslands of eastern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has bipinnate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats at moderate elevations.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
