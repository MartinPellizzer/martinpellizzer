Leucadendron tortum var. inflexum

Leucadendron tortum var. inflexum, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces woody, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun exposure.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
