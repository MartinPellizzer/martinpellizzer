Linaria striata var. tristis

Linaria striata var. tristis, commonly known as the weedy toadflax, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gardens.
