Lisianthius volubilis

Lisianthius volubilis, commonly known as the climbing lisianthium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant blooms.
