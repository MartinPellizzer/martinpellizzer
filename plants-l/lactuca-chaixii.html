Lactuca chaixii

Lactuca chaixii, commonly known as Chinese endive, is a species of lettuce native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and rosette-forming leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly bitter taste. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. Lactuca chaixii is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which are used in various culinary applications.

It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
