Lonicera guillonii

Lonicera guillonii, commonly known as the Guillon's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but generally not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.

Lonicera guillonii is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
