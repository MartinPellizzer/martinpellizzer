Leptospermum eriocalyx

Leptospermum eriocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pink flowers. The plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It produces small, edible fruits that are used by various wildlife species.

The plant is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
