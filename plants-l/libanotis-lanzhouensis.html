Libanotis lanzhouensis

Libanotis lanzhouensis, commonly known as the Lanzhou bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically found in the Lanzhou region.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
