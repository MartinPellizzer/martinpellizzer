Linociera verrucosa

Linociera verrucosa, commonly known as the rough bark tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, fissured bark that gives it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
