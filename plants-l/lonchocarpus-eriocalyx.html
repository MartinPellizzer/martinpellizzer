Lonchocarpus eriocalyx

Lonchocarpus eriocalyx, commonly known as the red sand mat, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance.
