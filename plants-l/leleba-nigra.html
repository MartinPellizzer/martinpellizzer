Leleba nigra

Leleba nigra, commonly known as the black leleba, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from deep purple to white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Leleba nigra is considered a vulnerable species.
