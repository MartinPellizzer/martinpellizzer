Licania vaupesiana

Licania vaupesiana, commonly known as the Amazonian evergreen tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon Basin. The plant is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.
