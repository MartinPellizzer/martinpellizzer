Lessingianthus regis

Lessingianthus regis, commonly known as the royal lessingia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, brightly colored ray florets that surround a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
