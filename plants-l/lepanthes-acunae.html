Lepanthes acunae

Lepanthes acunae, commonly known as the Acuna's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors, attracting various pollinators.

Lepanthes acunae is appreciated for its ornamental value and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
