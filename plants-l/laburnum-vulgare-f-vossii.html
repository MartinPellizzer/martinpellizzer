Laburnum vulgare f. vossii

Laburnum vulgare f. vossii, commonly known as the Voss's golden chain, is a cultivar of the laburnum tree characterized by its striking yellow flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are clustered in drooping chains, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The cultivar is noted for its vibrant color and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
