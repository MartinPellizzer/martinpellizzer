Lipusa multiflora var. lucida

Lipusa multiflora var. lucida, commonly known as the brightleaf lipusa, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that exhibits a striking metallic sheen under sunlight. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and year-round interest make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
