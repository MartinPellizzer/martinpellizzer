Lysimachia solaniflora

Lysimachia solaniflora, commonly known as the flowering yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Lysimachia solaniflora thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.
