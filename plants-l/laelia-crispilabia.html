Laelia crispilabia

Laelia crispilabia, commonly known as the wrinkled laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive crinkled appearance. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and moderate humidity.

Laelia crispilabia is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
