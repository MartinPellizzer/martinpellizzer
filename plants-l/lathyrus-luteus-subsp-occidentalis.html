Lathyrus luteus subsp. occidentalis

Lathyrus luteus subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the western yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
