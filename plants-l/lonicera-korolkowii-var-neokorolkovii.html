Lonicera korolkowii var. neokorolkovii

Lonicera korolkowii var. neokorolkovii, commonly known as the Neokorolkovii honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer, attracting birds and other wildlife.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
