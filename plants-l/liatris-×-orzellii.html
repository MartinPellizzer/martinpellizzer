Liatris × orzellii

Liatris × orzellii, commonly known as the Orzell's blazing star, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescences composed of numerous purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as prairies, wetlands, and woodland edges. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem.

Liatris × orzellii is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
