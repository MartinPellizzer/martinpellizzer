Lysimachia ovalifolia

Lysimachia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf chickweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia ovalifolia is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
