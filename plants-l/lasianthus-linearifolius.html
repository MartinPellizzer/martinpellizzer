Lasianthus linearifolius

Lasianthus linearifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are often pink or purple, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

The species is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
