Lebeckia linearis

Lebeckia linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces daisy-like yellow flowers with a prominent central disk. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in coastal and inland regions.

Lebeckia linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
