Litsea griffithii

Litsea griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's lime or Indian lime, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, yellow-orange fruits that are edible. Litsea griffithii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrant foliage and attractive fruit.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
