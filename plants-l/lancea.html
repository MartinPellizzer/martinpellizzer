Lancea

Lancea, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically produces small, daisy-like flowers that are often yellow or white in color. Lancea species are native to various regions in the Northern Hemisphere, including Europe, Asia, and North America.

They are often found in moist, woodland environments and are valued for their ornamental appeal.
