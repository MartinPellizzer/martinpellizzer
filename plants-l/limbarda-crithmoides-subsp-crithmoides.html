Limbarda crithmoides subsp. crithmoides

Limbarda crithmoides subsp.

crithmoides, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen foliage and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
