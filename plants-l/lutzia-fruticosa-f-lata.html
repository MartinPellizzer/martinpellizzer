Lutzia fruticosa f. lata

Lutzia fruticosa f. lata, commonly known as the broadleaf lutzia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a glossy appearance, while its fruit is a small, fleshy berry.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
