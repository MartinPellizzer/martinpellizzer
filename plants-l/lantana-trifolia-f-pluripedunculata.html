Lantana trifolia f. pluripedunculata

Lantana trifolia f. pluripedunculata, commonly known as the multibranched lantana, is a flowering shrub native to the Americas.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular ornamental plant, though it can be invasive in some regions.
