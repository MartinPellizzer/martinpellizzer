Lonicera loureiroi var. major

Lonicera loureiroi var. major, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains small, black seeds.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has been used in traditional medicine.
