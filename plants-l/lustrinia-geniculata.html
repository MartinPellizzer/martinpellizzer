Lustrinia geniculata

Lustrinia geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

Lustrinia geniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to trail or climb in gardens and greenhouses.
