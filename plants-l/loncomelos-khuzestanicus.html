Loncomelos khuzestanicus

Loncomelos khuzestanicus, commonly known as the Khuzestan tamarisk, is a shrub native to the southwestern regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Tamaricaceae family and is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitat and is of interest for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
