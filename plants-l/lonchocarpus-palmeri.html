Lonchocarpus palmeri

Lonchocarpus palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's urd.

It is a climbing legume native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Jamaica and Cuba. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers and elongated, woody pods that contain toxic seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is notable for its chemical compounds, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
