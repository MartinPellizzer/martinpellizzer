Lotononis lanceolata

Lotononis lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lotonon, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
