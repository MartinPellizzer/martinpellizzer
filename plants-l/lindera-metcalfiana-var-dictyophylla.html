Lindera metcalfiana var. dictyophylla

Lindera metcalfiana var. dictyophylla, commonly known as the Chinese wild cinnamon, is a variant of the Lindera metcalfiana species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, elliptical berries that are typically orange-red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
