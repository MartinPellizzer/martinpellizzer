Lemurella virescens

Lemurella virescens, commonly known as the green lemur plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking emerald-green foliage, which is often variegated with shades of white and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in humid environments.

Lemurella virescens is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
