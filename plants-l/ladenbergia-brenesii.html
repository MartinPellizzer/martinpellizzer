Ladenbergia brenesii

Ladenbergia brenesii, commonly known as the Guanacaste tree, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind and animals.

The plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
