Limonium drepanostachyum

Limonium drepanostachyum, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Sea Holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, often found on sandy or rocky shores. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Limonium drepanostachyum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in saline conditions.
