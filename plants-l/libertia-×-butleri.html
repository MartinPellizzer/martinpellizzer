Libertia × butleri

Libertia × butleri, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical classification highlights its hybrid origin, combining traits from both parent species.
