Lindenbergia nigrescens

Lindenbergia nigrescens, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, dark purple to black berries that mature in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface.

Lindenbergia nigrescens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
