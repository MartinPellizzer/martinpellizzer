Limonium tenellum

Limonium tenellum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has succulent leaves and stems, adapted to withstand salty and windy conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.

Limonium tenellum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh coastal climates.
