Lastrea nephrodioides

Lastrea nephrodioides, commonly known as the kidneyleaf.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tinge and have a waxy surface to reduce water loss. It produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
