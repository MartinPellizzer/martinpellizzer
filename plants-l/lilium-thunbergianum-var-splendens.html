Lilium thunbergianum var. splendens

Lilium thunbergianum var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange with a yellow throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan shape around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and vibrant color.
