Leptoglossis tenuis

Leptoglossis tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid bee.

This plant is a type of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae. It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators.

It plays an important role in the ecological systems of its habitat by supporting biodiversity and contributing to the pollination of other plant species.
