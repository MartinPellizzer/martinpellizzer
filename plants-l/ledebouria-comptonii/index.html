Ledebouria Comptonii

Ledebouria comptonii is a rare and striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, often twisted leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires periods of dormancy to flourish. Its flowers, which appear in spring, are typically pale green or yellow. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a prized addition to many succulent collections.
