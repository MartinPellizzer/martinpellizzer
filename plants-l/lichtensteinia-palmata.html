Lichtensteinia palmata

Lichtensteinia palmata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Lichtensteinia palmata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
