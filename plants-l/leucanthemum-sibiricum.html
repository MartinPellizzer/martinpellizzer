Leucanthemum sibiricum

Leucanthemum sibiricum, commonly known as Siberian oxeye, is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia and parts of Europe.

It features showy white petals with a yellow center, forming daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for landscaping purposes.
