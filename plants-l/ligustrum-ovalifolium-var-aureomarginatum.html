Ligustrum ovalifolium var. aureomarginatum

Ligustrum ovalifolium var. aureomarginatum, commonly known as the golden-margin privet, is a variant of the privet shrub characterized by its distinctive golden-yellow margins on the leaves.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in a variety of soil types and light conditions. The plant has small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by dark blue berries that are attractive to birds. Its dense growth habit makes it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental landscaping.

The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a glossy appearance, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.
