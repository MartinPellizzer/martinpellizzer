Leea unifoliolata

Leea unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are usually clustered at the base of the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Leea unifoliolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
