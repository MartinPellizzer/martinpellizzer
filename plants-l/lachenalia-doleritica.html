Lachenalia doleritica

Lachenalia doleritica, commonly known as the rock lachenalia, is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions. It typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Lachenalia doleritica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
