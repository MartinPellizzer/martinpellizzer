Lepisorus Leiopteris

Lepisorus leiopteris is a unique fern species known for its delicate, lace-like fronds. Native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. This plant is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks, showcasing its adaptability to epiphytic life. Its intricate leaf patterns make it a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
