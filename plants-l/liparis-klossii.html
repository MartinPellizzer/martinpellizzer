Liparis klossii

Liparis klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually elongated and strap-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Liparis klossii is considered a rare and endangered species.
