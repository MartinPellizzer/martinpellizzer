Lilium jinfushanense

Lilium jinfushanense, commonly known as the Jinfushan lily, is a rare and striking species native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing vibrant pink or red petals with a white throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
