Ligularia ghatsukupa

Ligularia ghatsukupa, commonly known as the Ghatukupa ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
