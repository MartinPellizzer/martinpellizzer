Lotus haydonii

Lotus haydonii, commonly known as the yellow lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces distinctive, spherical seed pods. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
