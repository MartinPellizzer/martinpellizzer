Lonicera calvescens

Lonicera calvescens, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often aromatic leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange, which bloom in spring. Its berries are red or orange and contain seeds, contributing to its ornamental value.

Lonicera calvescens is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
