Laburnum Alpinum

Laburnum alpinum is a striking member of the Fabaceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its slender, drooping branches create a graceful appearance in gardens. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
