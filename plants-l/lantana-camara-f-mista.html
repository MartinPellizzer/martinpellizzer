Lantana camara f. mista

Lantana camara f. mista, commonly known as the mista lantana, is a hybrid form of the common lantana.

It is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of the Americas, characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, requiring careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
