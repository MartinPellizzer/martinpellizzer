Lycaste hirtzii

Lycaste hirtzii, commonly known as the Hirtz's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms in the spring, with inflorescences that can reach several inches in length. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Lycaste hirtzii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
