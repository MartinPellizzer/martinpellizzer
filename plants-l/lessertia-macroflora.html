Lessertia macroflora

Lessertia macroflora, commonly known as the giant star fern, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, making it a dramatic addition to any garden or indoor space. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes.

Lessertia macroflora is also valued for its ability to support local biodiversity by providing habitat for small insects and birds.
