Laelia × sanderae

Laelia × sanderae, commonly known as the Sanderae orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in cool, humid environments with good air circulation and partial shade.

Laelia × sanderae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
