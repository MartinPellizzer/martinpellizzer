Ligularia purdomii

Ligularia purdomii, commonly known as the Chinese ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are borne in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas along streams or in rocky crevices.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its unique foliage and floral display.
