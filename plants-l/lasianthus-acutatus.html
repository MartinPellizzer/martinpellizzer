Lasianthus acutatus

Lasianthus acutatus, commonly known as the acute-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Lasianthus acutatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
