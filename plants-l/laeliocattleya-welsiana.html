Laeliocattleya welsiana

Laeliocattleya welsiana, commonly known as the Welsiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from Laelia and Cattleya orchids.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in warm and humid environments. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of pink, white, and purple hues. It produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less-than-ideal conditions.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
