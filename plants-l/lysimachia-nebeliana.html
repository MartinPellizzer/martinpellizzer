Lysimachia nebeliana

Lysimachia nebeliana, commonly known as the mist flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Lysimachia nebeliana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
