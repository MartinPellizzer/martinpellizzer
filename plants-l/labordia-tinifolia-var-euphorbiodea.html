Labordia tinifolia var. euphorbiodea

Labordia tinifolia var. euphorbiodea, commonly known as the "Cape coral tree," is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It features striking, coral-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
