Limonium Kaschgaricum

Limonium kaschgaricum is a striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its clusters of small, bright blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This species thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and lesser-known member of the Limonium family.
