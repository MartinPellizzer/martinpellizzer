Lopezia langmaniae

Lopezia langmaniae, commonly known as the Langman's lupine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and pea-like flowers.
