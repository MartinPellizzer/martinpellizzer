Lindsaea schultzei

Lindsaea schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's spikemoss, is a species of vascular fern belonging to the family Lindsaeaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems covered with small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in dense, spike-like formations along the stems. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and reproductive structures make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
