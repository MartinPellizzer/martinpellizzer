Lantana leprieuri

Lantana leprieuri, commonly known as the Florida lantana, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring dense, aromatic foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often changing as the blooms mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Lantana leprieuri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
