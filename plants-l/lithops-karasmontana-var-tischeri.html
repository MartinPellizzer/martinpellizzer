Lithops karasmontana var. tischeri

Lithops karasmontana var. tischeri, commonly known as the Karoo lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance with two fleshy, paired leaves that resemble stones. The plant stores water in its thick, fleshy leaves, which are adapted to its arid environment. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
