Lepisorus Chinensis

Lepisorus chinensis is a unique fern species native to parts of Asia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. This plant is known for its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and intricate frond structure. Its ability to absorb moisture from the air makes it well adapted to moist habitats. Lepisorus chinensis is not only an ecological indicator species but also holds cultural significance in traditional medicine.
