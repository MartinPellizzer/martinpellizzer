Languas diversifolia

Languas diversifolia, commonly known as the variable-leaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that vary in shape and size across different parts of the plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Languas diversifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
