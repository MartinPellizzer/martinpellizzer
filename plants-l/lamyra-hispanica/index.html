Lamyra Hispanica

Lamyra hispanica is a rare and fascinating plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This small herb is characterized by its delicate white flowers and finely divided leaves. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
