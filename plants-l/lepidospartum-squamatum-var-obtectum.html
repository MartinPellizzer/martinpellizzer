Lepidospartum squamatum var. obtectum

Lepidospartum squamatum var. obtectum, commonly known as silverleaf rockrose, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that is covered in fine, scale-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
