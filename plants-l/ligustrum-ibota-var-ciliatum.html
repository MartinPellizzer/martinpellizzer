Ligustrum ibota var. ciliatum

Ligustrum ibota var. ciliatum, commonly known as the silky privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and usually elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silky texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue-black berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
