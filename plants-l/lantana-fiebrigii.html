Lantana fiebrigii

Lantana fiebrigii, commonly known as the Cape lantana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana fiebrigii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
