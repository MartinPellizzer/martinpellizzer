Litsea membranacea

Litsea membranacea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are often aromatic. It produces small, white flowers followed by edible, fragrant berries.

Litsea membranacea is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
