Leptorhynchos scaber

Leptorhynchos scaber, commonly known as the rough-leaved leptorhynchos, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
