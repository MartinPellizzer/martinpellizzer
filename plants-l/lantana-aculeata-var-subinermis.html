Lantana aculeata var. subinermis

Lantana aculeata var. subinermis, commonly known as the smooth lantana, is a flowering shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and runners make it an invasive species in many areas.
