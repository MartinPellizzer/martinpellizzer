Leucas stricta

Leucas stricta, commonly known as narrow-leaved leucas, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually narrow and slightly hairy. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in whorled clusters along the stem.

It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, and it is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
