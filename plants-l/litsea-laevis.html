Litsea laevis

Litsea laevis, commonly known as the smooth litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea laevis is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
