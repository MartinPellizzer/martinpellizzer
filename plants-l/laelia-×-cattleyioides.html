Laelia × cattleyioides

Laelia × cattleyioides, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Cattleya species, resulting in a plant with hybrid vigor.

It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a branching pattern with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
