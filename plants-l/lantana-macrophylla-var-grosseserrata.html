Lantana macrophylla var. grosseserrata

Lantana macrophylla var. grosseserrata, commonly known as the broad-leaf lantana, is a flowering shrub native to Australia.

It features dense, aromatic foliage with dark green, ovate leaves that have coarsely toothed margins. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in full sun make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
