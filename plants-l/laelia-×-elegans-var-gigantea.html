Laelia × elegans var. gigantea

Laelia × elegans var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant laelia, is a hybrid orchid species originating from crosses between Laelia and other orchid genera.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 cm in diameter, often displaying vibrant colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, thriving in humid, shaded environments with good air circulation. Its pseudobulbs are robust, and it produces long, arching inflorescences that bear multiple blooms.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to cultivation under proper care.
