Lens nigricans var. tenorei

Lens nigricans var. tenorei, commonly known as the Sicilian lentil, is a variant of the common lentil native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, oval-shaped seeds that are slightly smaller than typical lentils. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in well-drained soils and temperate climates. It produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing the edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and traditional use in Mediterranean cuisine.
