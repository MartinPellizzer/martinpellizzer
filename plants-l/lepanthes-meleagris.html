Lepanthes meleagris

Lepanthes meleagris, commonly known as the spotted violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet petals with distinctive black spots, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the wet season.

Lepanthes meleagris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
