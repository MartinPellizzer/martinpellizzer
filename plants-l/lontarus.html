Lontarus

Lontarus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, velvety petals. The plant typically produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, white, and blue. Lontarus thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ease of care and long-lasting blooms.
