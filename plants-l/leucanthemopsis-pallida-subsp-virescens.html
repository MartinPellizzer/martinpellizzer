Leucanthemopsis pallida subsp. virescens

Leucanthemopsis pallida subsp.

virescens, commonly known as the green-leaved white marguerite, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its showy, white ray florets surrounding yellow disc flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its darker green foliage compared to the typical pale green of the main species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
