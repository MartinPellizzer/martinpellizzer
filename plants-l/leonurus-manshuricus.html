Leonurus manshuricus

Leonurus manshuricus, commonly known as the Siberian hedgehog.

This perennial herb is native to regions of eastern Asia, including parts of Russia and China. It features a hairy stem and lance-shaped leaves, with small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
