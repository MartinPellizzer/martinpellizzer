Limonium multiforme

Limonium multiforme, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and is often found growing in sandy or rocky soils near the shore. Limonium multiforme is a perennial species that thrives in salty, well-drained environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
