Lonicera denudata var. johnstonii

Lonicera denudata var. johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, twining stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange, which bloom in late spring. Its berries are red or black and are an important food source for birds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens and natural habitats.
