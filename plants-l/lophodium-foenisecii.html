Lophodium foenisecii

Lophodium foenisecii, commonly known as the common lichen of the genus Lophodium, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive, leaf-like thallus with a pale green to yellowish color and a finely wrinkled surface. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provide nutrients through photosynthesis. It is often found on tree trunks, stumps, and rocks in temperate regions.

Its slow growth and long lifespan make it an indicator species for environmental health.
