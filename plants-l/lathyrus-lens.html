Lathyrus lens

Lathyrus lens, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The seeds, which are enclosed in pods, are small and often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.
