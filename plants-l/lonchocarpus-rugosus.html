Lonchocarpus rugosus

Lonchocarpus rugosus, commonly known as the rough legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by distinctive, rough-textured pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
