Limonium × erectiflorum

Limonium × erectiflorum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and showy, spike-like flower heads that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in sunny, well-drained conditions. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding a striking visual element to coastal landscapes.

Limonium × erectiflorum is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
