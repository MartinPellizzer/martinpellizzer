Lebeckia pauciflora

Lebeckia pauciflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lebeckia, is a South African native plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

Lebeckia pauciflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
