Leucas neuflizeana

Leucas neuflizeana, commonly known as the Neuflizean everlasting, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is often cultivated for its long-lasting flowers and adaptability to various soil conditions.
