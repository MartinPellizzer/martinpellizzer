Leontodon atlantica

Leontodon atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic leontodon, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the western Mediterranean.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along cliffs and dunes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a rough texture.

It is adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong coastal winds.
