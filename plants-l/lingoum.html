Lingoum

Lingoum, commonly known as the Japanese ginger or *Zingiber mioga*, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers like turmeric and cardamom. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, with a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Lingoum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has culinary uses in some traditional cuisines.
