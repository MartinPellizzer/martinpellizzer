Lysimachia saganeitensis

Lysimachia saganeitensis, commonly known as the Japanese yellow water primrose, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, preferring moist soil conditions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic settings.
