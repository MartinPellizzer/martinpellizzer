Lysimachia glaucescens

Lysimachia glaucescens, commonly known as blue-eyed Mary, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, star-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Lysimachia glaucescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
