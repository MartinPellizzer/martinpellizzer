Licuala lanuginosa

Licuala lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The silky palm is valued for its ornamental qualities and has also been used in traditional medicine.
