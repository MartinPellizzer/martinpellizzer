Lysimachia clementsoniana

Lysimachia clementsoniana, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features small, showy flowers that are typically violet or white in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lysimachia clementsoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
