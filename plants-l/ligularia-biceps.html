Ligularia biceps

Ligularia biceps, commonly known as the giant leopard plant, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive dark green color and prominent veins. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, spire-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking dramatic, shade-tolerant plants.
