Lobelia lavendulacea

Lobelia lavendulacea, commonly known as the lavender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Lobelia lavendulacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
