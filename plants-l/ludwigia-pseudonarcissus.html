Ludwigia pseudonarcissus

Ludwigia pseudonarcissus, commonly known as the golden Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season, attracting various pollinators. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in aquascaping due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Ludwigia pseudonarcissus is also known for its ability to purify water and support aquatic ecosystems.
