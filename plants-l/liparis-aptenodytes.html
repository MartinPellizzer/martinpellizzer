Liparis aptenodytes

Liparis aptenodytes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose spike, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
