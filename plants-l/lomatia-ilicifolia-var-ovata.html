Lomatia ilicifolia var. ovata

Lomatia ilicifolia var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed lomatia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a valued species in native gardens and conservation efforts.
