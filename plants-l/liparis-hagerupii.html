Liparis hagerupii

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soils. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single, showy flower per stem.
