Lactuca stenocephala

Lactuca stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its upright stem and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a compact cluster at the top of the stem. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Lactuca stenocephala is not commonly cultivated for food but is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
