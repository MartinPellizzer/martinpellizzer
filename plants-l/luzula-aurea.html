Luzula aurea

Luzula aurea, commonly known as golden wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with yellow or golden hues.
