Loasa laxa

Loasa laxa, commonly known as the slender loasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Loasa laxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
