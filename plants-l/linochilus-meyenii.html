Linochilus meyenii

Linochilus meyenii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Linochilus meyenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
