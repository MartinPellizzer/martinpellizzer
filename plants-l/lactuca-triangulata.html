Lactuca triangulata

Lactuca triangulata, commonly known as the triangular lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its triangular leaves that are typically lobed and have a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Lactuca triangulata is not typically cultivated for food but is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
