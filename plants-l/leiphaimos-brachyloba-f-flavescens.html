Leiphaimos brachyloba f. flavescens

Leiphaimos brachyloba f. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered leiphaimos, is a unique variant of the leiphaimos genus.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and its distinctive yellow floral display. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, adding to its ornamental appeal.

While relatively rare, it is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and unusual appearance.
