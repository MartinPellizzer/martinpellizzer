Litsea montana

Litsea montana, commonly known as mountain litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits.

Litsea montana is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
