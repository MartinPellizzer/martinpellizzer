Lonicera bracteolaris

Lonicera bracteolaris, commonly known as the bracteolaris honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are red berries that mature in late summer, providing food for birds and small mammals.

Lonicera bracteolaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
