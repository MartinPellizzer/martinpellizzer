Litsea pallens

Litsea pallens, commonly known as the white-flowered litsea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in panicles, producing a subtle fragrance.

Litsea pallens is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
