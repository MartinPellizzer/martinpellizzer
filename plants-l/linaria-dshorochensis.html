Linaria dshorochensis

Linaria dshorochensis, commonly known as the Dshoroch Mountain snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It produces small, elongated seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation across its native habitat.

Linaria dshorochensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
