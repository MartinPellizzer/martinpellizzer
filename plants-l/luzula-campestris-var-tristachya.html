Luzula campestris var. tristachya

Luzula campestris var. tristachya, commonly known as the tufted wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense colonies, making it a common component of wetland and forest understory vegetation.
