Languas globosa

Languas globosa, commonly known as the globe lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, globe-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
