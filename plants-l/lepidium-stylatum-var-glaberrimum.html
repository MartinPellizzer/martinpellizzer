Lepidium stylatum var. glaberrimum

Lepidium stylatum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth wall peppergrass, is a flowering plant species native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops such as cabbage and broccoli. The plant is characterized by its glabrous (smooth) stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically alternate and pinnately divided, with a distinctive appearance that aids in its identification.

This variant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showcasing its adaptability to various environments.
