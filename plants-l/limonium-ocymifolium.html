Limonium ocymifolium

Limonium ocymifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, thriving in sandy or rocky soils. It produces small, spherical seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements.

Limonium ocymifolium is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to tolerate salty and windy conditions.
