Lysimachia microcarpa

Lysimachia microcarpa, commonly known as dwarf yellow mosquito plant, is a low-growing herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has slender, trailing stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia microcarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
