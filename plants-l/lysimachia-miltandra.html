Lysimachia miltandra

Lysimachia miltandra, commonly known as the narrowleaf chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia miltandra is often found in the understory of forests and is known for its ability to spread via stolons.
