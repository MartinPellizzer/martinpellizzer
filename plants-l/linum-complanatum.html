Linum complanatum

Linum complanatum, commonly known as flat flax, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their showy blue or white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in loose racemes, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale blue.

Linum complanatum is adapted to moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight.
