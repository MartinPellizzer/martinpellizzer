Lasianthus obliquinervis var. simizui

Lasianthus obliquinervis var. simizui, commonly known as the Simizu's lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia.
