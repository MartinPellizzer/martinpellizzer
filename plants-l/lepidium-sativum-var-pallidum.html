Lepidium sativum var. pallidum

Lepidium sativum var. pallidum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a biennial or annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has alternate, pinnately compound leaves with linear to lanceolate leaflets. Its seeds are small, oval, and typically yellow or brown in color.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
