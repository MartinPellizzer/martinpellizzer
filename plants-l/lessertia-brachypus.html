Lessertia brachypus

Lessertia brachypus, commonly known as the dwarf African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slightly woolly texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
