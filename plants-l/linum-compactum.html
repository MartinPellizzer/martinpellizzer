Linum compactum

Linum compactum, commonly known as the compact flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, blue or purple flowers, typically in clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species.

Linum compactum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in textile production.
