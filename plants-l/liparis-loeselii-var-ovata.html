Liparis loeselii var. ovata

Liparis loeselii var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces racemes of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
