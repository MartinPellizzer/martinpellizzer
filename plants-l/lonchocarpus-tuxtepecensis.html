Lonchocarpus tuxtepecensis

Lonchocarpus tuxtepecensis, commonly known as Tuxtepec legume, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which contributes to soil fertility. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in disturbed habitats such as forest edges and rocky areas.

Due to its ecological importance, it is sometimes used in reforestation efforts.
