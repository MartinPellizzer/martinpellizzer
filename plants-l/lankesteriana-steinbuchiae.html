Lankesteriana steinbuchiae

Lankesteriana steinbuchiae, commonly known as the Steinbuch's flame orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting specific pollinators such as bees and moths.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
