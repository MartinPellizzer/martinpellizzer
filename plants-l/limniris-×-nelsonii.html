Limniris × nelsonii

Limniris × nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's cypress.

It is a hybrid evergreen conifer native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of scale-like leaves that are dense and dark green, providing a striking visual contrast.

Limniris × nelsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and adaptability.
