Linochilus cinerascens subsp. cinerascens

Linochilus cinerascens subsp.

cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-gray orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, pale green to ash-gray flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
