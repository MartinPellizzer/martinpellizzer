Limnanthemum hastatum

Limnanthemum hastatum, commonly known as the water starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, often with a yellow center. The plant thrives in moist or aquatic environments, such as wetlands and marshes. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting aquatic biodiversity.
