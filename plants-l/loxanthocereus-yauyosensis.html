Loxanthocereus yauyosensis

Loxanthocereus yauyosensis, commonly known as the Yauyos cholla, is a cacti species native to Peru.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which attract various pollinators. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
