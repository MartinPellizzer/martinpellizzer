Lathyrus magellanicus var. ovalifolius

Lathyrus magellanicus var. ovalifolius, commonly known as the Magellanic vetch, is a perennial legume native to South America.

It features slender, climbing stems that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The leaves are pinnate, with oval-shaped leaflets that give it its specific epithet. Its small, pea-like flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
