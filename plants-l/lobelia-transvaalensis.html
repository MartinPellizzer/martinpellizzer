Lobelia transvaalensis

Lobelia transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal lobelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia transvaalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
