Lobivia nealeana var. purpureiflora

Lobivia nealeana var. purpureiflora, commonly known as the Purpleflowered Lobivia, is a compact cacti species native to the arid regions of South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense white or grayish spines, which help protect it from intense sunlight and herbivores. The plant produces striking clusters of deep purple flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and gardeners in dry climates.
