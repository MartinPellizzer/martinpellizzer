Liatris × creditonensis

Liatris × creditonensis, commonly known as the hybrid blazing star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spires of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Liatris × creditonensis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
