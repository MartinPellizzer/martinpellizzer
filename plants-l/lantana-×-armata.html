Lantana × armata

Lantana × armata, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features vibrant clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and vegetative means.
