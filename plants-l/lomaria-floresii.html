Lomaria floresii

Lomaria floresii, commonly known as the Flores lomaria, is a rare flowering plant native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance.
