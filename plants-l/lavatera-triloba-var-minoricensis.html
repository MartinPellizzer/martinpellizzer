Lavatera triloba var. minoricensis

Lavatera triloba var. minoricensis, commonly known as the Minorca lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Minorca.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring silvery-gray foliage with a woolly texture. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
