Litsea variabilis var. tonkinensis

Litsea variabilis var. tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and southern China. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The fruit is a drupe, typically containing a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
