Leandra calvescens

Leandra calvescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are broad and velvety, giving it its common name.

Leandra calvescens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
