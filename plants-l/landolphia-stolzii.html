Landolphia stolzii

Landolphia stolzii, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a woody vine that can grow up to several meters in length, with thick, often hairy stems. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange in color, which attract pollinators such as birds and bats. Its leaves are usually large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Landolphia stolzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
