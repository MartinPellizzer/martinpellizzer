Lightfootia tenella var. fasciculata

Lightfootia tenella var. fasciculata, commonly known as the fasciculate lightfootia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge, arranged in tight clusters along the stems. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
