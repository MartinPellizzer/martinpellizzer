Lysimachia caliginis

Lysimachia caliginis, commonly known as the foggy moss or cloud moss, is a low-growing perennial plant native to moist, shaded environments in temperate regions.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid conditions and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
