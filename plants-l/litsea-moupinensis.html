Litsea moupinensis

Litsea moupinensis, commonly known as the Moupin litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries.

Litsea moupinensis is often found in humid, shaded environments within montane forests.
