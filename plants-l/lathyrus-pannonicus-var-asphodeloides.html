Lathyrus pannonicus var. asphodeloides

Lathyrus pannonicus var. asphodeloides, commonly known as the asphodeloid falsebrome, is a perennial flowering plant native to southeastern Europe.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink. The plant typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils, and it has a distinctive, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves.
