Leuzea annae-bentiae

Leuzea annae-bentiae, commonly known as the Cape York leuzea, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to the hot, dry climate of its native habitat.

Leuzea annae-bentiae is often found in coastal dunes and is an important part of the local flora in its ecosystem.
