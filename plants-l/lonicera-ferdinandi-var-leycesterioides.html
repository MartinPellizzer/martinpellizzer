Lonicera ferdinandi var. leycesterioides

Lonicera ferdinandi var. leycesterioides, commonly known as the lemon honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators.
