Lysimachia linearifolia

Lysimachia linearifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia linearifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and ability to spread through rhizomes.
