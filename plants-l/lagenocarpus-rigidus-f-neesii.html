Lagenocarpus rigidus f. neesii

Lagenocarpus rigidus f. neesii, commonly known as the Nees's rigid thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features robust, spiny stems and large, rough-textured leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. Its flowers are yellow and appear in dense clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
