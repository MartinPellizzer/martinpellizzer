Lacinaria spicata var. foliacea

Lacinaria spicata var. foliacea, commonly known as the leafy lacinaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
