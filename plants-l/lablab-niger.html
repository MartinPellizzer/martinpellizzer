Lablab niger

Lablab niger, commonly known as hyacinth bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces clusters of violet or blue flowers. It thrives in warm climates and is often grown as a cover crop or for its edible parts.

Lablab niger is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, improving fertility.
