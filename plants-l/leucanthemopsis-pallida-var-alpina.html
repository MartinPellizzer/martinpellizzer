Leucanthemopsis pallida var. alpina

Leucanthemopsis pallida var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine white ragwort, is a perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a compact growth habit with narrow, linear leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
