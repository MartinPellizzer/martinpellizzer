Ledenbergia macrantha

Ledenbergia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered ledenbergia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Ledenbergia macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
