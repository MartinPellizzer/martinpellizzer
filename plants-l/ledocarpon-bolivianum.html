Ledocarpon bolivianum

Ledocarpon bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian leucanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaf structures. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and shrublands, thriving in cool, temperate climates. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.
