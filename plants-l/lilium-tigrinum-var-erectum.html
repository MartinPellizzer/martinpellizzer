Lilium tigrinum var. erectum

Lilium tigrinum var. erectum, commonly known as the tiger lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to eastern North America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, often with dark markings resembling a tiger's stripes. The plant produces sword-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is known for its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
