Lachenalia pallida var. coerulescens

Lachenalia pallida var. coerulescens, commonly known as the blue-flowered lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, and it produces striking blue or violet flowers in the spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
