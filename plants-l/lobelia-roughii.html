Lobelia roughii

Lobelia roughii, commonly known as roughleaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
