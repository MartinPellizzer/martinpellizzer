Lagerstroemia ovalifolia var. minor

Lagerstroemia ovalifolia var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf crape myrtle, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green, providing dense foliage throughout the growing season. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

Its compact size makes it suitable for smaller garden spaces and container planting.
