Lophiolepis × nezaketiae

Lophiolepis × nezaketiae, commonly known as the hybrid grass or Nezaketia grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, which are often wind-pollinated.

Lophiolepis × nezaketiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
