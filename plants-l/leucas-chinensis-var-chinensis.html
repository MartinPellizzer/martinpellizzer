Leucas chinensis var. chinensis

Leucas chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese leucas, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a pale blue or purple hue.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
