Lyonia libanensis

Lyonia libanensis, commonly known as the Lebanon lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Lyonia libanensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
