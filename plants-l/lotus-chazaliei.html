Lotus chazaliei

Lotus chazaliei, commonly known as the Malayan lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, muddy environments such as marshes and riverbanks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers bloom in summer and are followed by pods that contain several seeds.
