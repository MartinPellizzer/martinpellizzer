Lotus hintoniorum

Lotus hintoniorum, commonly known as the Hinton's lotus, is a rare and lesser-known species within the legume family.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers, which bloom on tall, erect stems. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate climates. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a botanical curiosity and subject of ecological study.
