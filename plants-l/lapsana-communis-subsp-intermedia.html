Lapsana communis subsp. intermedia

Lapsana communis subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate common cudweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is a low-growing herb with slender stems and small, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the aster family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in various habitats.
