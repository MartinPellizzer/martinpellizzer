Logania centaurium

Logania centaurium, commonly known as the red milkweed or red bower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting a variety of pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are typically hairy and erect. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant's unique appearance and ecological value make it a notable species in its native habitat.
