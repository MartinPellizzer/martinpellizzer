Laurus psychotrioides

Laurus psychotrioides, commonly known as the Mexican laurel or Mexican bay leaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive scent and potential medicinal properties.
