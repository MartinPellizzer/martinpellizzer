Licuala micholitzii var. leeana

Licuala micholitzii var. leeana, commonly known as the Leeana palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests. Its stems are stout and covered in a fibrous sheath, and it produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
