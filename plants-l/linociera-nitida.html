Linociera nitida

Linociera nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers, which are typically borne in clusters. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Linociera nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
