Lobelia polyodon

Lobelia polyodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy blue to violet flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Lobelia polyodon is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
