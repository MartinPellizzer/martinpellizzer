Linaria gangitis

Linaria gangitis, commonly known as the New Zealand dandelion, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the plant family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
