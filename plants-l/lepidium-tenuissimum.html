Lepidium tenuissimum

Lepidium tenuissimum, commonly known as slender wall pepper, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces tiny, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on slender stems. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Lepidium tenuissimum is known for its peppery scent and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
