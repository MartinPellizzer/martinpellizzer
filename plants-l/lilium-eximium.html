Lilium eximium

Lilium eximium, commonly known as the Chinese lily, is a striking perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting its large, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Lilium eximium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
