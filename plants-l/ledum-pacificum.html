Ledum pacificum

Ledum pacificum, commonly known as alpine rose or Sitka rose, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and appear in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Ledum pacificum thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
