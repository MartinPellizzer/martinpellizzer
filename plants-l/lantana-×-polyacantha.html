Lantana × polyacantha

Lantana × polyacantha, commonly known as the orange lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its flowering stems are usually hairy and bear the inflorescences at the tips.

Lantana × polyacantha is valued in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.
