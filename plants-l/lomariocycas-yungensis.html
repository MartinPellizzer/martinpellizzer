Lomariocycas yungensis

It belongs to the family Cycadaceae and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to a subtropical climate.
