Lomelosia austroaltaica

Lomelosia austroaltaica, commonly known as the Altai lomelosia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climatic conditions. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. Its fibrous roots help it retain moisture in dry soils, while its compact growth form aids in resisting wind erosion.

Lomelosia austroaltaica is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
