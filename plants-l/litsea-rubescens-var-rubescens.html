Litsea rubescens var. rubescens

Litsea rubescens var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines.
