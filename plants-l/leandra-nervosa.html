Leandra nervosa

Leandra nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Leandra nervosa is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
