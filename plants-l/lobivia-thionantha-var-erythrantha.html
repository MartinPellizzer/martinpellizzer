Lobivia thionantha var. erythrantha

Lobivia thionantha var. erythrantha, commonly known as the Red-flowered Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the growing season, which attract pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
