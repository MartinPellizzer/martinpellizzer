Lachenalia patentissima

Lachenalia patentissima, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that store water and nutrients. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

Lachenalia patentissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
