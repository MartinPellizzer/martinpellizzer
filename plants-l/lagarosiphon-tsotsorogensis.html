Lagarosiphon tsotsorogensis

Lagarosiphon tsotsorogensis, commonly known as the Natal water hyacinth, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its floating leaves and showy blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to aquatic environments, with roots that anchor it in shallow water. It plays a role in water ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.

However, it can become invasive in some regions, outcompeting native flora.
