Lepanthes gabriellae

Lepanthes gabriellae, commonly known as the Gabrielle's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within the canopy of forests. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthes gabriellae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
