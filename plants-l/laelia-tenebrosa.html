Laelia tenebrosa

Laelia tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark laelia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
