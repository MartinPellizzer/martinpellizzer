Lysimachia pterantha var. baoxingensis

Lysimachia pterantha var. baoxingensis, commonly known as the Baoxing yellowjacket, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The species features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
