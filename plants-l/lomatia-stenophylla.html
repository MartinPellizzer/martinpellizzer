Lomatia stenophylla

Lomatia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lomatia, is an evergreen shrub native to the wet tropical regions of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. Lomatia stenophylla is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rainforest environments.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
