Lobivia lateritia var. kupperiana

Lobivia lateritia var. kupperiana, commonly known as the Kupperiana Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green or blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It flowers with yellowish or pale orange blooms during the spring and summer months.

Due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
