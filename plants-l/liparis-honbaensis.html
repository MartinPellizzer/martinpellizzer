Liparis honbaensis

Liparis honbaensis, commonly known as the Honba orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it has a relatively short flowering period.

Liparis honbaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
