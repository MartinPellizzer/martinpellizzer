Lychnis densonii

Lychnis densonii, commonly known as the Sierra lousewort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in rocky, alpine environments. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lychnis densonii is adapted to harsh, high-altitude conditions and plays a role in its local ecosystem.
