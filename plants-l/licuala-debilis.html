Licuala debilis

Licuala debilis, commonly known as the slender palm or dwarf palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to two meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
