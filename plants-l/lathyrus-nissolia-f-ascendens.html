Lathyrus nissolia f. ascendens

Lathyrus nissolia f. ascendens, commonly known as the ascending nissolia pea, is a botanical variant of the species Lathyrus nissolia.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and delicate, pea-like flowers. The plant typically features trifoliate leaves with a slender stem, and its flowers range in color from pale pink to white.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or woodland areas, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
