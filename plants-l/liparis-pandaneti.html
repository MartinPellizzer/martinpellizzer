Liparis pandaneti

Liparis pandaneti, commonly known as the pandan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near the forest floor. Its leaves are strap-shaped and can reach lengths of up to 30 cm, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Liparis pandaneti is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to certain cultivation conditions.
