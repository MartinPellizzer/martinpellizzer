Leucadendron adscendens

Leucadendron adscendens, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces conical, yellowish flower heads that are highly ornamental and attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for coastal gardens and xeriscaping.
