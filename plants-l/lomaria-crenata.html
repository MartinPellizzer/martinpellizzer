Lomaria crenata

Lomaria crenata, commonly known as the serrated lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, usually ranging in color from white to pale pink.

Lomaria crenata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
