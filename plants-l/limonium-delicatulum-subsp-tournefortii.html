Limonium delicatulum subsp. tournefortii

Limonium delicatulum subsp.

tournefortii, commonly known as the pink sand veronica, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers bloom in early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
