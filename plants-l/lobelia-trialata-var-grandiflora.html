Lobelia trialata var. grandiflora

Lobelia trialata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the showy lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and prefers moist, well-drained soils. It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
