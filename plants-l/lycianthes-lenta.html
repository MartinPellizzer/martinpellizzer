Lycianthes lenta

Lycianthes lenta, commonly known as the slow-growing nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically green berries that turn red when ripe.

The leaves are usually oval-shaped with a rough texture and can vary in size.
