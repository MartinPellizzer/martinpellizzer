Lavandula stricta f. conferta

Lavandula stricta f. conferta, commonly known as the compact lavender, is a compact, shrub-like form of the narrowleaf lavender.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces small, fragrant purple flowers in summer. This variety is characterized by its tightly packed growth habit, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.
