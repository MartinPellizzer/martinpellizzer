Limoniastrum ouarglense

Limoniastrum ouarglense, commonly known as the Moroccan tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a low, spreading growth habit with woody stems and narrow, linear leaves. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
