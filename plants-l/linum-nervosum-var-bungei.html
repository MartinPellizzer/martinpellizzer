Linum nervosum var. bungei

Linum nervosum var. bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's flax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and have a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically blue to purple, with five petals and a distinctive central yellow spot.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
