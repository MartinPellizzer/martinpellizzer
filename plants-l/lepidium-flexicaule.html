Lepidium flexicaule

Lepidium flexicaule, commonly known as slender cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, herbaceous habit and produces small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. The plant has alternate, pinnately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a dry, segmented capsule that contains several small, rounded seeds.

Lepidium flexicaule is typically found in moist, disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
