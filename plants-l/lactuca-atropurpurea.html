Lactuca atropurpurea

Lactuca atropurpurea, commonly known as purple lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its dark purple leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

The leaves are edible and have a mild, slightly bitter taste, making them a popular ingredient in salads.
