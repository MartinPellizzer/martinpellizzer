Leptorkis arnoglossophylla

Leptorkis arnoglossophylla, commonly known as the red-veined nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems covered in fine, stinging hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, with prominent red veins that contrast with their green coloration.
