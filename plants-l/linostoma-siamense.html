Linostoma siamense

Linostoma siamense, commonly known as the Siam orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Linostoma siamense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
