Lepidium danielsii

Lepidium danielsii, commonly known as the Daniel's cress, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine regions.
