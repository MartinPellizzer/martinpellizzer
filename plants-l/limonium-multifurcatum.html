Limonium multifurcatum

Limonium multifurcatum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant thrives in coastal environments and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils. It produces small, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Limonium multifurcatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
