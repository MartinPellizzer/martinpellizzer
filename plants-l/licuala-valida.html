Licuala valida

Licuala valida, commonly known as the golden palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in inflorescences, which are typically borne on the lower part of the stem.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
