Laelia lobata

Laelia lobata, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically has long, narrow leaves and produces inflorescences with several showy blooms. Laelia lobata is adapted to grow in rocky or well-drained soil, often in high-altitude environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
