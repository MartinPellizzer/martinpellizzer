Lightfootia tenella var. montana

Lightfootia tenella var. montana, commonly known as the mountain lightfootia, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss.

Lightfootia tenella var. montana is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
