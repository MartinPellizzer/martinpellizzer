Leptorkis distans

Leptorkis distans, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. Native to tropical regions of South America, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's root system is specialized for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in both horticultural and botanical studies.
