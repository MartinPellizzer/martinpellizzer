Lobularia nummularia

Lobularia nummularia, commonly known as silver dollar plant, is a low-growing annual or biennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, rounded, silvery-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a mat-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
