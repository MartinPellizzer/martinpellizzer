Ligustrum neilgherrense

Ligustrum neilgherrense, commonly known as the Nilgiri privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It typically grows in moist, shaded areas at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and are followed by small, dark berries.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
