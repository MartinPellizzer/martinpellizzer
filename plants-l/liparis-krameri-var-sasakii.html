Liparis krameri var. sasakii

Liparis krameri var. sasakii, commonly known as the Sasakii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces slender, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive yellowish-green to brownish coloration.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
