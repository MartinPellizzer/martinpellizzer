Limonium byzacium

Limonium byzacium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Limonium byzacium is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
