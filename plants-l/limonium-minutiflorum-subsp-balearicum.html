Limonium minutiflorum subsp. balearicum

Limonium minutiflorum subsp.

balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly the Balearic Islands. It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.

The plant thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are adapted to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
