Limonia × aurantiifolia

Limonia × aurantiifolia, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit, a small, round orange-like citrus fruit, is typically sour and used in culinary applications.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm climates.
