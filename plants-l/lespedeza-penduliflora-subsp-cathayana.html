Lespedeza penduliflora subsp. cathayana

Lespedeza penduliflora subsp.

cathayana, commonly known as Chinese stragglerbush, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, woodland areas and thrives in partial to full shade.

The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
