Lasianthus dalatensis

Lasianthus dalatensis, commonly known as the Dalat beauty, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface.
