Lysimachia Nemorum Subsp. Azorica

Azorean enchanter's nightshade, scientifically known as Lysimachia nemorum subsp. azorica, is a rare and captivating plant native to the Azores archipelago. This unique subspecies thrives in the moist, shaded environments of mountainous forests and rocky crevices. Its striking violet flowers and delicate foliage make it a standout among the flora of the region. Despite its common name, it is not toxic and holds cultural significance in local traditions. The plant's limited distribution highlights the importance of conservation efforts to protect this botanical gem.
