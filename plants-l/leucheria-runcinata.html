Leucheria runcinata

Leucheria runcinata, commonly known as the slender leucheria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, grassy areas and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry conditions.

Leucheria runcinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its subtle beauty.
