Legousia speculum-veneris f. polypiflora

Legousia speculum-veneris f. polypiflora, commonly known as the Venus's Looking Glass, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, glassy, bell-shaped flowers that resemble a mirror, giving it its evocative common name. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for rock gardens and shaded borders.
