Liparis phyllocardia var. minor

Liparis phyllocardia var. minor, commonly known as the small heart orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
