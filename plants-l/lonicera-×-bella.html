Lonicera × bella

Lonicera × bella, commonly known as the yellow honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans.

Lonicera × bella is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
