Lobostemon thymeloideus

Lobostemon thymeloideus, commonly known as the fragrant thyme-leaved lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that emit a strong, herbaceous scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lobostemon thymeloideus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and fragrant properties.
