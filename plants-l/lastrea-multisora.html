Lastrea multisora

Lastrea multisora, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy leaves that are often aromatic. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract birds and insects, contributing to its ecological significance.
