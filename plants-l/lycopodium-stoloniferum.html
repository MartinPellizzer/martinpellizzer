Lycopodium stoloniferum

Lycopodium stoloniferum, commonly known as the running clubmoss, is a member of the Lycopodiaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen plant characterized by its creeping stems that form dense mats. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and has sporangia that release spores for reproduction. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky habitats.

Lycopodium stoloniferum is often found in temperate regions and plays a role in soil stabilization and ecosystem diversity.
