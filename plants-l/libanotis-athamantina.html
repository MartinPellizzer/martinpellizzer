Libanotis athamantina

Libanotis athamantina, commonly known as the Persian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

Libanotis athamantina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
