Ligularia kojimae

Ligularia kojimae, commonly known as the Japanese ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers that appear in the summer months, adding a striking visual element to gardens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
