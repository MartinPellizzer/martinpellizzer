Lactuca alpestris

Lactuca alpestris, commonly known as alpine lettuce, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its rosette of serrated, bluish-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers.

Lactuca alpestris is also known for its milky sap, which is characteristic of the genus Lactuca.
