Laportea sinensis

Laportea sinensis, commonly known as Chinese iron plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiky yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is typically found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Laportea sinensis is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
