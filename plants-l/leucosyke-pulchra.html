Leucosyke pulchra

Leucosyke pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with numerous showy blooms that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Leucosyke pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
