Lotus lourdes-santiagoi

Lotus lourdes-santiagoi, commonly known as the Santiago lotus, is a rare and unique species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a central yellow flower, which bloom during the rainy season. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
