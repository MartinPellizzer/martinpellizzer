Leonotis grandis

Leonotis grandis, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that resemble firecrackers, hence its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value.
