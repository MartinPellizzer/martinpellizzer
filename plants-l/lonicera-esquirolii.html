Lonicera esquirolii

Lonicera esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early spring. Its berries are red or orange and contain small seeds.

Lonicera esquirolii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
