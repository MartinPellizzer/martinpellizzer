Licuala whitmorei

Licuala whitmorei, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of over 5 meters. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to withstand high humidity and strong winds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional crafts.
