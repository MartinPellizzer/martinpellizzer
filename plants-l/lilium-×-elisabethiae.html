Lilium × elisabethiae

Lilium × elisabethiae, commonly known as the Elizabeth Lily, is a hybrid cultivar of the lily family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant is native to the United States and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.
