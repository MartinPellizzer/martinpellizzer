Leschenaultia striata

Leschenaultia striata, commonly known as the striped leschenaultia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink or purple. Leschenaultia striata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and extended blooming period.
