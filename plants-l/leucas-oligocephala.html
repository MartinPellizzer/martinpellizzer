Leucas oligocephala

Leucas oligocephala, commonly known as narrow-headed leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Leucas oligocephala is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
