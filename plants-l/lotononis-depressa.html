Lotononis depressa

Lotononis depressa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of violet, purple, and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers well-drained conditions. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in partial shade and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a favored choice for both gardeners and botanists studying African flora.
