Lavatera triloba f. minoricensis

Lavatera triloba f. minoricensis, commonly known as the Minorca lavatera, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It features soft, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, and its flowers are showy, with five petals in shades of pink to white. The plant produces fluffy, gray seed heads that persist into winter, adding interest to the landscape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to Mediterranean gardens.
