Lindera sinensis

Lindera sinensis, commonly known as Chinese wild ginger, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Lindera sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
