Lantana stricta

Lantana stricta, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The leaves are typically opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in a variety of habitats including disturbed soils and coastal areas.

The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
