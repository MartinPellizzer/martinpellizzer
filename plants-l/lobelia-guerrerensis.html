Lobelia guerrerensis

Lobelia guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerense Lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with hairy surfaces.
