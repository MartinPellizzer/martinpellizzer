Lachnagrostis chamissonis

Lachnagrostis chamissonis, commonly known as Chamisson's hairgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like growths. The plant has fine, hair-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are often inconspicuous. It thrives in moist, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or dune environments.

Lachnagrostis chamissonis is adapted to withstand harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
