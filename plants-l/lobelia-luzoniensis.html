Lobelia luzoniensis

Lobelia luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountain slopes. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.
