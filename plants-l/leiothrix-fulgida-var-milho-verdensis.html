Leiothrix fulgida var. milho-verdensis

Leiothrix fulgida var. milho-verdensis, commonly known as the Portuguese flame violet, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Azores.

It features vibrant, flame-like flowers with a deep red to orange hue, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
