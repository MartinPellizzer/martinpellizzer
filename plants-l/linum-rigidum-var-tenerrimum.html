Linum rigidum var. tenerrimum

Linum rigidum var. tenerrimum, commonly known as the slender flax or narrow-flowered flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it produces capsules as its fruit.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
