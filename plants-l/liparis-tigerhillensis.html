Liparis tigerhillensis

Liparis tigerhillensis, commonly known as the Tiger Hill Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Nepal.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments at high altitudes. Its flowers are often pale yellow or greenish with intricate patterns, adapting to its alpine habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
