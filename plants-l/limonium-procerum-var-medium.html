Limonium procerum var. medium

Limonium procerum var. medium, commonly known as the slender sea holly, is a coastal plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shorelines. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium procerum var. medium is well adapted to salty and windy environments, making it a hardy ornamental plant.
