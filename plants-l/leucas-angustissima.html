Leucas angustissima

Leucas angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows as a small shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
