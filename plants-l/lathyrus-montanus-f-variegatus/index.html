Lathyrus Montanus F. Variegatus

Lathyrus montanus f. variegatus is a striking variant of the mountain pea plant known for its unique leaf patterns. This plant features striking variegation that adds visual interest to any garden or natural setting. It thrives in cooler climates and is often found in rocky or woodland environments. Its delicate flowers add a touch of elegance to the landscape. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in changing environmental conditions.
