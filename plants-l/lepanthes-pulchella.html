Lepanthes pulchella

Lepanthes pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette-like formation.

Lepanthes pulchella is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
