Lithospermum flexuosum

Lithospermum flexuosum, commonly known as flexible lithosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies. The plant features slender, ascending stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its small, violet to purple flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and it produces distinctive, red or orange-red berries that contain the seeds.

Lithospermum flexuosum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its potential healing benefits.
