Lactuca fenzlii

Lactuca fenzlii, commonly known as Fenzli's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a composite inflorescence. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Lactuca fenzlii is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves, though it is less commonly consumed than other lettuce varieties.
