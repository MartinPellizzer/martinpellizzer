Lecanorchis cerina f. albida

Lecanorchis cerina f. albida, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale white to cream-colored flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, relying on aerial roots for attachment and nutrient absorption.
