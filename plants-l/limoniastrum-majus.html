Limoniastrum majus

Limoniastrum majus, commonly known as the desert star or blue star, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, bright blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, covered in a dense layer of fine hairs that help reduce water loss.

Limoniastrum majus is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
