Liparis jamaicensis

Liparis jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. It has a short rhizome and produces flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Liparis jamaicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
