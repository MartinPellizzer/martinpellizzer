Listrostachys thonneriana

Listrostachys thonneriana, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It features attractive, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
