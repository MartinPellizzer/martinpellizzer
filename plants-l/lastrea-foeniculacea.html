Lastrea foeniculacea

Lastrea foeniculacea, commonly known as the Cape fennel.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
