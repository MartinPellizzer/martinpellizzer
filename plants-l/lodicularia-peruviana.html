Lodicularia peruviana

Lodicularia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant produces showy white or pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots anchor it in shallow waters, while its stems support the foliage.

Lodicularia peruviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in freshwater ecosystems.
