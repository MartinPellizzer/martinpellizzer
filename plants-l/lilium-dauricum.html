Lilium dauricum

Lilium dauricum, commonly known as the Daurian lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, grass-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its bulbous root system is adapted for storing nutrients and water.

Lilium dauricum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
