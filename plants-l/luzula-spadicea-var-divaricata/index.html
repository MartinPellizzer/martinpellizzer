Luzula Spadicea Var. Divaricata

Luzula spadicea var. divaricata is a striking member of the plant family Poaceae, known for its unique and elegant appearance. This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its foliage features a distinctive blue-green hue that adds a cool contrast to its surroundings. The plant produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring or early summer. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species among botanists and gardeners alike.
