Leucas somalensis

Leucas somalensis, commonly known as Somali leucas, is a flowering plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Somalia and neighboring countries.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows as a shrub with slender, erect stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom during the rainy season.
