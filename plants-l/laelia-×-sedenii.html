Laelia × sedenii

Laelia × sedenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Brassia species.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the high Andes of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It is known for its hardiness and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.

Botanically, it features pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves, typical of many orchid genera.
