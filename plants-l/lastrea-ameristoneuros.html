Lastrea ameristoneuros

Lastrea ameristoneuros, commonly known as the American stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to North America.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to full sun exposure.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
