Litsea zeylanica var. rubrinervia

Litsea zeylanica var. rubrinervia, commonly known as the red-veined litsea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves with prominent red veins, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
