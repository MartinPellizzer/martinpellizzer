Liparis formosana

Liparis formosana, commonly known as the Formosa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its flowering period occurs during the spring and early summer months.

Liparis formosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
