Ludwigia sedioides

Ludwigia sedioides, commonly known as the dwarf Ludwigia, is a compact aquatic plant native to South America.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tinge, especially under strong lighting. The plant produces tiny, yellowish flowers that are often submerged, making it a popular choice for planted aquariums. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers slightly acidic to neutral water.

Ludwigia sedioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in freshwater environments.
