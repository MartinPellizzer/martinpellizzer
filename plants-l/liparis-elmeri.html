Liparis elmeri

Liparis elmeri, commonly known as Elmer's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive appearance, featuring a short, erect stem and a single, showy flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flower is usually greenish-yellow with a yellow lip.

Liparis elmeri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
