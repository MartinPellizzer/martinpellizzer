Leptochilus heudelotii

Leptochilus heudelotii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Leptochilus heudelotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
