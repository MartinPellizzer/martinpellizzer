Litsea baviensis var. szemaois

Litsea baviensis var. szemaois, commonly known as the Baviensis Sze Maois Lime, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round, yellow fruits. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental horticulture and ecological studies.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary and secondary forests.
