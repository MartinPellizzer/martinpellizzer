Lindera wardii

Lindera wardii, commonly known as the Chinese wild laurustin, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy green upper surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by dark purple berries.

Lindera wardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal interest.
