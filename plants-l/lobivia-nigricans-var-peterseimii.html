Lobivia nigricans var. peterseimii

Lobivia nigricans var. peterseimii, commonly known as the Peterseim Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a ribbed stem with prominent areoles, from which it produces short, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
