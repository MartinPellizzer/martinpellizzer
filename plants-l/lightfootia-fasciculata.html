Lightfootia fasciculata

Lightfootia fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered lightfoot.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are typically opposite and elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface.

Lightfootia fasciculata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
