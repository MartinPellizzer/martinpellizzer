Lythrum flexicaule

Lythrum flexicaule, commonly known as slender lythrum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes plants such as willows and pomegranates. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Lythrum flexicaule is often found in moist soils along streambanks and in wet meadows.
