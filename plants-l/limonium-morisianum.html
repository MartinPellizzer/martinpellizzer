Limonium morisianum

Limonium morisianum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features silvery, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in coastal environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in sandy or rocky coastal areas.

Limonium morisianum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and resilience to coastal conditions.
