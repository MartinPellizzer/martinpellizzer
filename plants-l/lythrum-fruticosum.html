Lythrum fruticosum

Lythrum fruticosum, commonly known as redroot lythrum, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of showy pink or red flowers. The plant has fibrous roots that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in summer.

Lythrum fruticosum is often found in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist soils.
