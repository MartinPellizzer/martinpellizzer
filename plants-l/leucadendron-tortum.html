Leucadendron tortum

Leucadendron tortum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces striking, cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Leucadendron tortum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
