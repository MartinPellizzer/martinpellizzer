Leucanthemum glaucophyllum var. esterellense

Leucanthemum glaucophyllum var. esterellense, commonly known as the Esterellense ragwort, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features gray-green foliage and yellow flowers with white ray florets, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in mountainous regions. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
