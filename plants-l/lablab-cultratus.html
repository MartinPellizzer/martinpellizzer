Lablab cultratus

Lablab cultratus, commonly known as the sickle bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, sickle-shaped pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, curved pods containing edible seeds. Lablab cultratus is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are rich in protein and minerals.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial cover crop.
