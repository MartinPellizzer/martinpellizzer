Liparis minahassae

Liparis minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
