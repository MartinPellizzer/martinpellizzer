Lapeirousia ramosissima

Lapeirousia ramosissima, commonly known as the Cape yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the fynbos biome. It produces slender, branching stems and has narrow, grass-like leaves.

Lapeirousia ramosissima is often found in coastal areas and is known for its resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
