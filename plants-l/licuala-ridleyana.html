Licuala ridleyana

Licuala ridleyana, commonly known as the Ridley's palm, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. The plant has a short trunk and produces cones rather than flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
