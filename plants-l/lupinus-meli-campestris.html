Lupinus meli-campestris

Lupinus meli-campestris, commonly known as the Campestris lupine, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. The plant produces spiny, cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing ability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
