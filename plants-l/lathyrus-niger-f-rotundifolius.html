Lathyrus niger f. rotundifolius

Lathyrus niger f. rotundifolius, commonly known as the black vetch, is a cultivar of the vetch plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and has a climbing habit with tendrils. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing addition to gardens and landscapes.
