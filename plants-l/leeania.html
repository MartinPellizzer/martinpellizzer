Leeania

Leeania, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern United States. The plant is characterized by its blue or violet flowers and silver-green leaves with a velvety texture. Leeania thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and ability to tolerate salt spray make it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
