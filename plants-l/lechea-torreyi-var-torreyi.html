Lechea torreyi var. torreyi

Lechea torreyi var. torreyi, commonly known as the Torrey's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss.

The plant is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
