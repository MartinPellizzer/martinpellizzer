Linaria multicaulis f. messanensis

Linaria multicaulis f. messanensis, commonly known as the Messanese toadflax, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers in racemes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is valued for its compact growth habit and long-lasting floral display in garden settings.
