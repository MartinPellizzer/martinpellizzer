Leucadendron pubigerum

Leucadendron pubigerum, commonly known as the silver coin bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in landscaping.
