Luzula ostenii

Luzula ostenii, commonly known as the alpine woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in panicle-like clusters during the summer months.

Luzula ostenii is adapted to cold, rocky environments and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous regions.
