Laeliopsis chinensis

Laeliopsis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese laelia, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laeliopsis chinensis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
