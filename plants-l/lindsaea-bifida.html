Lindsaea bifida

Lindsaea bifida, commonly known as the split-leaf tillandsia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often divided, giving it its common name. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers.

Lindsaea bifida is adapted to grow on trees, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
