Ligularia tatewakii

Ligularia tatewakii, commonly known as the Japanese ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces yellow-orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts, creating a striking visual contrast. Ligularia tatewakii thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas along stream banks.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and can be grown in garden settings with proper moisture and light conditions.
