Lepeostegeres centiflorus

Lepeostegeres centiflorus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, erect stem that supports its showy, daisy-like flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow with a central yellow disk. The plant produces numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a single, large inflorescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
