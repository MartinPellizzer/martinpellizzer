Listera tenuis

Listera tenuis, commonly known as slender basketwort, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Listera tenuis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is known for its ornamental value in woodland gardens.
