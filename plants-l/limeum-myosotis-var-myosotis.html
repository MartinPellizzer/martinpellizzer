Limeum myosotis var. myosotis

Limeum myosotis var. myosotis, commonly known as the common forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy, and it grows in a variety of habitats including meadows, woodlands, and wetlands. It is often found in moist soils and can thrive in partial to full shade.

The species is also known for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
