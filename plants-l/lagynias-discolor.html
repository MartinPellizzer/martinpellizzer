Lagynias discolor

Lagynias discolor, commonly known as the variable leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often colorful and variable leaf patterns. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
