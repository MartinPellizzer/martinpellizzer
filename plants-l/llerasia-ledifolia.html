Llerasia ledifolia

Llerasia ledifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous species that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers with a distinctive spurred shape. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its complex inflorescence and specialized pollination mechanisms.
