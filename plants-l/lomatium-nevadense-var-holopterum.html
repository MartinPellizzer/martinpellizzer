Lomatium nevadense var. holopterum

Lomatium nevadense var. holopterum, commonly known as the Sierra lomatium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and umbel-like flower clusters. The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high elevations, where it thrives in well-drained soils. This variant is distinguished by its slightly different leaf structure compared to other lomatium species.
