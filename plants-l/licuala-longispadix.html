Licuala longispadix

Licuala longispadix, commonly known as the long-spadix licuala, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with a long, spadix surrounded by colorful bracts. Its growth habit is typically erect, with a slender trunk and a crown of broad leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
