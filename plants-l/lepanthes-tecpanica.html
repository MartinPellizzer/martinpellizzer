Lepanthes tecpanica

Lepanthes tecpanica, commonly known as the Tecpanica orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of tropical forests. The plant features delicate, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, showy lips. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the forest floor.

Lepanthes tecpanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
