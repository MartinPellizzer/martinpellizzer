Lonicera ciliosa f. depressa

Lonicera ciliosa f. depressa, commonly known as the low-growing honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and white. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
