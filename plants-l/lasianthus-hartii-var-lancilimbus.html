Lasianthus hartii var. lancilimbus

Lasianthus hartii var. lancilimbus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are often pale blue or purple and are arranged in whorls along the stems.
