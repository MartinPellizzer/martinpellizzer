Limonium scabrum var. corymbulosum

Limonium scabrum var. corymbulosum, commonly known as the clustered sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, tolerating salty air and sandy soils. Its leaves are linear and gray-green, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
