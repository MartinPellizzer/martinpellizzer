Lepidium desvauxii

Lepidium desvauxii, commonly known as the desert cress, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species of mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
