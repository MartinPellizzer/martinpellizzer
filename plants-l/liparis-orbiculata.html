Liparis orbiculata

Liparis orbiculata, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rosette of fleshy, oval leaves that form a dense, compact structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appeal make it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
