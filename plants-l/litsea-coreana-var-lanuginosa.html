Litsea coreana var. lanuginosa

Litsea coreana var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, lanuginous (fuzzy) leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elliptical, dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
