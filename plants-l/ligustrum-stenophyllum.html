Ligustrum stenophyllum

Ligustrum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum stenophyllum is also valued for its use in hedges and as a landscape plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
