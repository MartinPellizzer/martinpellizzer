Liquidambar multinervis

Liquidambar multinervis, commonly known as the Chinese sweet gum, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Aceraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that split open to release seeds, a feature typical of the genus Liquidambar. Its leaves turn vibrant shades of red and yellow in autumn, making it a popular ornamental tree.

The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in furniture and construction.
