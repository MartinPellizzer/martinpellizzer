Lomatogonium floribundum

Lomatogonium floribundum, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Lomatogonium floribundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
