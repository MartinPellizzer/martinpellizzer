Lomatia ilicifolia var. integrifolia

Lomatia ilicifolia var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth lomatia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal regions.
