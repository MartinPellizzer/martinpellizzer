Lomaria longifolia

Lomaria longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in spikes, often in shades of pink or purple. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape designs.
