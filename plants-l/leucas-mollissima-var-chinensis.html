Leucas mollissima var. chinensis

Leucas mollissima var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese leucas, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
