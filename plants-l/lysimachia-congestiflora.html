Lysimachia congestiflora

Lysimachia congestiflora, commonly known as golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a low-growing perennial with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically forms dense mats and has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
