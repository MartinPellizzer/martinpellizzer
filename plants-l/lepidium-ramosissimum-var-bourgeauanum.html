Lepidium ramosissimum var. bourgeauanum

Lepidium ramosissimum var. bourgeauanum, commonly known as the California wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with numerous branched stems that bear small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds enclosed in a hard, capsule-like fruit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
