Lemaireocereus chende

Lemaireocereus chende, commonly known as the Chende cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its dense, spiny stems that grow upright. The plant produces small, white flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and adapting to its harsh environment.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
