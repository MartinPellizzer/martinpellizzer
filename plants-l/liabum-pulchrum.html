Liabum pulchrum

Liabum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful liabum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Liabum pulchrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
