Lythrum tenellum

Lythrum tenellum, commonly known as narrowleaf rosemary.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in the summer months.

Lythrum tenellum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils.
