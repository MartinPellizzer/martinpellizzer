Leucas beddomei

Leucas beddomei, commonly known as the Beddome's leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
