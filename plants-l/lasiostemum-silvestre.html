Lasiostemum silvestre

Lasiostemum silvestre, commonly known as wild morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant produces edible seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Lasiostemum silvestre is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
