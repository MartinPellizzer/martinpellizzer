Liparis minutiflora

Liparis minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a short flowering period.

Liparis minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
