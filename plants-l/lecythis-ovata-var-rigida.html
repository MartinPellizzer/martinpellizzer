Lecythis ovata var. rigida

Lecythis ovata var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid lecythis, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a single large seed surrounded by a sticky, aromatic pulp. The bark is thick and often has a reddish or brownish color, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

This species is valued for its hard, durable wood and its role in local ecosystems.
