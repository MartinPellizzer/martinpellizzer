Lantana tomasii

Lantana tomasii, commonly known as the Tomasi's lantana, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange. The plant produces opposite, simple leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct serrated edge. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Lantana tomasii is also known for its ability to attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
