Liparis kwangtungensis

Liparis kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern China.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a faint fragrance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
