Lastrea oreopteris

Lastrea oreopteris, commonly known as the rock fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive, creeping rhizome with small, lance-shaped fronds. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
