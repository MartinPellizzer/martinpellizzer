Lachenalia orchioides var. glaucina

Lachenalia orchioides var. glaucina, commonly known as the blue-flowered lachenalia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette, and its bulbs are adapted to store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
