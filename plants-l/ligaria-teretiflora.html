Ligaria teretiflora

Ligaria teretiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered ligaria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers. Ligaria teretiflora is of interest to botanists due to its adaptive features and ecological role in its native environment.
