Lobelia carens

Lobelia carens, commonly known as the mountain lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Appalachian Mountains.

It produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of down.

Lobelia carens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
