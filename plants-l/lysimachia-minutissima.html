Lysimachia minutissima

Lysimachia minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf yellow-eyed grass, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features tiny, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has slender, creeping stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its compact size and delicate appearance make it a desirable plant for naturalized gardens and conservation areas.
