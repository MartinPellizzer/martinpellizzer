Libanothamnus spectabilis

Libanothamnus spectabilis, commonly known as the showy thamnus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.
