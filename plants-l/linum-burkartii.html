Linum burkartii

Linum burkartii, commonly known as the Burkart's flax, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax known for their showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, producing a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Linum burkartii is adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments.
