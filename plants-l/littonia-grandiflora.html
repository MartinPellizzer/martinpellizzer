Littonia grandiflora

Littonia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora littonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Littonia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
