Leucojum vernale

Leucojum vernale, commonly known as the spring snowflake, is a bulbous plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It produces delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Leucojum vernale is often cultivated for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
