Lobivia chrysantha subsp. jajoana

Lobivia chrysantha subsp.

jajoana, commonly known as the yellow lobivia, is a compact, flowering cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It features a short, ribbed stem with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months.

The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and moderate temperatures.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts and desert gardens.
