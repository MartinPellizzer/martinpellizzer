Lonicera rubra

Lonicera rubra, commonly known as red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically reddish-brown beneath and dark green above. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in the fall, attracting various birds and small mammals.

Lonicera rubra is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
