Legendrea mollissima

Legendrea mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Legendrea mollissima is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks.
