Linaria genistifolia

Linaria genistifolia, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, racemose inflorescences.

Linaria genistifolia thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
