Linaria scenoreina

Linaria scenoreina, commonly known as the Narrowleaf toadflax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Linaria scenoreina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
