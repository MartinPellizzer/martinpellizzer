Lilium colchicum

Lilium colchicum, commonly known as the meadow lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, topped with showy, white or pale pink flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Lilium colchicum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
