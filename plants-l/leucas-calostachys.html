Leucas calostachys

Leucas calostachys, commonly known as the yellow leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Leucas calostachys is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
