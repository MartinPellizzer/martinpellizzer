Lonchocarpus crucisrubierae

Lonchocarpus crucisrubierae, commonly known as the Trinidadian coral bean, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, coral-like flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.
