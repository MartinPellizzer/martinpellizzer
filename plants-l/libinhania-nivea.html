Libinhania nivea

Libinhania nivea, commonly known as the snowflake orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, star-shaped flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its blooming period typically occurs during the late spring and early summer months.

Due to its limited habitat and endangered status, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
