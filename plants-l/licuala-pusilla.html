Licuala pusilla

Licuala pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf palm, is a small, compact palm native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, unbranched stem with a crown of narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is popular as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
