Lycopodium dendroideum f. strictum

Lycopodium dendroideum f. strictum, commonly known as the compact clubmoss, is a unique fern ally characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that form a tight rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its sporophytic structures, including strobili, are typically clustered at the base of the plant.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
