Lepicephalus rigidus

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Lepicephalus rigidus plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
