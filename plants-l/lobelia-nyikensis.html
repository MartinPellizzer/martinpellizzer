Lobelia nyikensis

Lobelia nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyikeni lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia nyikensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
