Lavandula bipinnata var. subnuda

Lavandula bipinnata var. subnuda, commonly known as the slender lavender, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces spikes of purple or pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal uses.
