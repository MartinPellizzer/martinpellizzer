Lantana cujabensis f. albiflora

Lantana cujabensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered lantana, is a variant of the lantana genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The flowers of this plant are typically white or pale yellow, which distinguishes it from other lantana species. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
