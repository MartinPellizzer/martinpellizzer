Limodorum tuberosum f. albiflorum

Limodorum tuberosum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered yellow violets, is a rare and striking variant of the yellow violet.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with its dark green, heart-shaped leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a tuberous root system and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring.

This cultivar is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique coloration.
