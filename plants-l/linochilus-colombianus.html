Linochilus colombianus

Linochilus colombianus, commonly known as the Colombian linochilus, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in cloud forests where humidity is high and temperatures are cool.

Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the nutrient-poor soils of its habitat, and it plays a role in the ecological balance of its native ecosystem.
