Litsea mathuataensis

Litsea mathuataensis, commonly known as the Philippine lauraceae, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its aromatic, simple leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red when mature, containing one or two seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.
