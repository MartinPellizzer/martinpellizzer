Lacinaria leptostachya

Lacinaria leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed lacinia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
