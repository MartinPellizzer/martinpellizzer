Lespedeza aitchisonii

Lespedeza aitchisonii, commonly known as Aitchison's lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pink to reddish-purple flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration in degraded landscapes.
