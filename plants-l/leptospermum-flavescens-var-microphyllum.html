Leptospermum flavescens var. microphyllum

Leptospermum flavescens var. microphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the myrtle family (Myrtaceae) and is known for its aromatic foliage. It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often in heathlands and open forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
