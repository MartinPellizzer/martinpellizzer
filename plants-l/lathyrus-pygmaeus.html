Lathyrus pygmaeus

Lathyrus pygmaeus, commonly known as the dwarf pea or pygmy pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Lathyrus pygmaeus is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
