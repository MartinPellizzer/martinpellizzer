Leonotis myricifolia

Leonotis myricifolia, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
