Lepanthes ciliicampa

Lepanthes ciliicampa, commonly known as the Campa slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pouch-like flower structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small and have a delicate, intricate design with vibrant colors.

Lepanthes ciliicampa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
