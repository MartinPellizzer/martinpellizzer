Licania microcarpa

Licania microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited licania, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Licania microcarpa is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
