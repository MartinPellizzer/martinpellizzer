Lecanorchis ohwii

Lecanorchis ohwii, commonly known as the Japanese ghost orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to Japan.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a single, slender stem and a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy, white to pale pink flower with a distinctive hooded labellum. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
