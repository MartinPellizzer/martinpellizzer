Leptodactylon californicum var. glandulosum

Leptodactylon californicum var. glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular leathery fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to California.

This variant is distinguished by its glandular structures, which contribute to its unique appearance and ecological adaptations.
