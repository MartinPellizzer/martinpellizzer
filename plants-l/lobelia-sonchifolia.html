Lobelia sonchifolia

Lobelia sonchifolia, commonly known as the common lobelia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, serrated leaves and produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Lobelia sonchifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
