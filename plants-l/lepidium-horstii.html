Lepidium horstii

Lepidium horstii, commonly known as the Andean cress, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Lepidium horstii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
