Lychnis × media

Lychnis × media, commonly known as the common garden pink, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Lychnis × media is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
