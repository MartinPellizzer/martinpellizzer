Leucadendron pyramidale

Leucadendron pyramidale, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, tree-like structure and distinctive silvery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that appear in cone-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
