Leionema rotundifolium

Leionema rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leafed leionema, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, rounded, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Leionema rotundifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
