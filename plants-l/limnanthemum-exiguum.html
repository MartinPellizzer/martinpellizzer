Limnanthemum exiguum

Limnanthemum exiguum, commonly known as the small water starwort, is a flowering plant native to wetland and aquatic environments in South America.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white flowers and narrow, floating leaves. The plant typically grows in shallow water or along the edges of ponds and lakes. Its roots help stabilize soil and prevent erosion in aquatic habitats.

Limnanthemum exiguum plays a role in supporting aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
