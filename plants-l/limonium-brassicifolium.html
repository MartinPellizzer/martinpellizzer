Limonium brassicifolium

Limonium brassicifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often growing in sandy or gravelly habitats. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, and it is known for its hardy, drought-tolerant nature.
