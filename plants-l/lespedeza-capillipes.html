Lespedeza capillipes

Lespedeza capillipes, commonly known as slender lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in open woodlands and along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a fine, hairy texture.

Lespedeza capillipes is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
