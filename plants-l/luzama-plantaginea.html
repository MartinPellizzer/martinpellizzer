Luzama plantaginea

Luzama plantaginea, commonly known as the velvetleaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
