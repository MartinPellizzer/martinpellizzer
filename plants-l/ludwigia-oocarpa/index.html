Ludwigia Oocarpa

Red ludwigia, scientifically known as Ludwigia oocarpa, is a vibrant aquatic plant native to South America. It is prized for its striking red foliage and is commonly used in aquariums to add color and texture. This species thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires regular water changes to maintain its health. Its growth is often accelerated with the addition of CO2 and liquid fertilizers. Overall, Red ludwigia is a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
