Limonia corymbosa

Limonia corymbosa, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Limonia corymbosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
