Lobivia cardenasiana

Lobivia cardenasiana, commonly known as the Cardenasian lobivia, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in its harsh desert environment.

Lobivia cardenasiana is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
