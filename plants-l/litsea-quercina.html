Litsea quercina

Litsea quercina, commonly known as the sweet chestnut or Chinese sweet chestnut, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Litsea quercina thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
