Lacinaria ligulistylis

Lacinaria ligulistylis, commonly known as the slender-styled lacinaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
