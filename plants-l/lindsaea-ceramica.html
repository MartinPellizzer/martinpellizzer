Lindsaea ceramica

Lindsaea ceramica, commonly known as the Ceramica fern, is a species of epiphytic fern belonging to the family Lindsaeaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the islands of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a delicate, feather-like frond structure with numerous small, lance-shaped pinnae. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in montane forests.

Lindsaea ceramica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
