Linum keniense

Linum keniense, commonly known as Kenyan flax, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of blue or violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or woodland habitats.

Linum keniense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential in traditional medicine.
