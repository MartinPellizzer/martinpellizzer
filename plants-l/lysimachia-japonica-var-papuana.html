Lysimachia japonica var. papuana

Lysimachia japonica var. papuana, commonly known as the Papuan chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bright yellow flowers clustered in racemes. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in regions with humid climates.
