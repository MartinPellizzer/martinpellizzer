Lobelia erinus f. elata

Lobelia erinus f. elata, commonly known as the trailing lobelia or skyflower, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom profusely throughout the growing season. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it ideal for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, adding texture to garden landscapes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, requiring regular watering during dry periods.
