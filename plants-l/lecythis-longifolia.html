Lecythis longifolia

Lecythis longifolia, commonly known as the longpod soursop, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and distinctive, elongated fruits that contain a single large seed. The plant belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
