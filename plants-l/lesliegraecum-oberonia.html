Lesliegraecum oberonia

Lesliegraecum oberonia, commonly known as the Oberonia orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines, where it typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and a single or few pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in inflorescences that can be either racemose or spicate, and they exhibit a range of colors from white to pink and yellow.

Lesliegraecum oberonia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
