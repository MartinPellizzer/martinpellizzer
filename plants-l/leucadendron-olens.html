Leucadendron olens

Leucadendron olens, commonly known as the silver leaf candle bush, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white foliage and vibrant yellow-orange flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Leucadendron olens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
