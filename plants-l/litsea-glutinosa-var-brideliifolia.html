Litsea glutinosa var. brideliifolia

Litsea glutinosa var. brideliifolia, commonly known as the Brideliifolia litsea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, red berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.

The species is also noted for its role in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
