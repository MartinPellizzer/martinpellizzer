Lipozygis erubescens

Lipozygis erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered lipozygis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the rainy season.

Lipozygis erubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
