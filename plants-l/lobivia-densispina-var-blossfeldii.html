Lobivia densispina var. blossfeldii

Lobivia densispina var. blossfeldii, commonly known as the Blossfeld's dense-spined lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, sharp spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in desert gardens.
