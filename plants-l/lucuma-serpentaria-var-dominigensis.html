Lucuma serpentaria var. dominigensis

Lucuma serpentaria var. dominigensis, commonly known as the Dominican lucuma, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
