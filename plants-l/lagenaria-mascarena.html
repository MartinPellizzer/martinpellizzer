Lagenaria mascarena

Lagenaria mascarena, commonly known as the African bottle gourd, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces large, thick-walled fruits that can be used as containers or for culinary purposes. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, while its vines can grow quite long.

The fruit's rapid growth and unique shape make it a notable species in both horticulture and botany.
