Lowryanthus

Lowryanthus, commonly known as the false mallow, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It consists of several species native to South America, particularly in regions of Brazil and Argentina. Lowryanthus species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant floral displays.
