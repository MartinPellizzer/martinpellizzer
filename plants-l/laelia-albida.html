Laelia albida

Laelia albida, commonly known as the white laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a pale green to yellowish-green lip. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions with good air circulation. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized and bloom in late winter to early spring.

Laelia albida is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
