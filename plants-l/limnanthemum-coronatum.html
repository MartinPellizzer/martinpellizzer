Limnanthemum coronatum

Limnanthemum coronatum, commonly known as the crown vine or crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and its stems are woody with thorn-like projections. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or near water sources.

The flowers are pollinated by insects, and the plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
