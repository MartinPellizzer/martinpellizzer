Linaria bordiana

Linaria bordiana, commonly known as the border toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, elongated seed pods.

Linaria bordiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
