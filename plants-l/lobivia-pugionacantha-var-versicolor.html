Lobivia pugionacantha var. versicolor

Lobivia pugionacantha var. versicolor, commonly known as the Rainbow Lobivia, is a compact, leafy cactus native to southern Africa.

It features striking, colorful flowers that range from yellow to red, often with contrasting markings. The plant has a short, cylindrical stem with ribbed structure and produces small, spiny leaves along its body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its unique coloration and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
