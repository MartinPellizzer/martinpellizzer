Lespedeza maximowiczii

Lespedeza maximowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese struthium or red lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Lespedeza maximowiczii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone regions.
