Lonicera tatarinowii

Lonicera tatarinowii, commonly known as the Tatarian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its berries are red or orange and contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds.

Lonicera tatarinowii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
