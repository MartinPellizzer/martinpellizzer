Leucocarpum oleaster

Leucocarpum oleaster, commonly known as the white-fruit olive, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oleaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, white, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture.

The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
