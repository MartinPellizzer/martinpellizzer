Lepanthes nebulina

Lepanthes nebulina, commonly known as the misty orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that often display a soft, mist-like appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, adapted to capture limited light.

Lepanthes nebulina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
