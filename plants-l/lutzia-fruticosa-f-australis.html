Lutzia fruticosa f. australis

Lutzia fruticosa f. australis, commonly known as the Australian lutzia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a textured appearance to the plant.
