Leucas usagarensis

Leucas usagarensis, commonly known as Indian borage, is a flowering plant native to regions of India and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and aromatic. Its flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in whorled clusters.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
