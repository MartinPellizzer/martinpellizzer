Linum tmoleum

Linum tmoleum, commonly known as yellow flax, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces delicate yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. Its seeds are small and oval, often used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil.

Linum tmoleum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in textile production.
