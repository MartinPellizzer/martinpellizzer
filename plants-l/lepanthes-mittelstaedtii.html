Lepanthes mittelstaedtii

Lepanthes mittelstaedtii, commonly known as the Mittelstaedt's slipper orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate, slipper-shaped petals and sepals. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

Lepanthes mittelstaedtii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
