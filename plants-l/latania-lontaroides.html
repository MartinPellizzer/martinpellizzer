Latania lontaroides

Latania lontaroides, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, compound leaves. The plant can grow up to 20 meters in height, making it one of the tallest palms in the world. Its leaves are arranged in a fan shape and are often used in ornamental horticulture.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
