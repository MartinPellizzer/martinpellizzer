Lysimachia arvensis subsp. platyphylla

Lysimachia arvensis subsp.

platyphylla, commonly known as broadleaf yellowthread, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.

The plant has creeping stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread across the ground. Its leaves are elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
