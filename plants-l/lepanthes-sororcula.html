Lepanthes sororcula

Lepanthes sororcula, commonly known as the "Coral Pink Pterostigma Orchid," is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, coral-pink flowers with a distinctive dark purple pterostigma. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Lepanthes sororcula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
