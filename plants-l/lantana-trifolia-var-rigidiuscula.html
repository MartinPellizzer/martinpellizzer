Lantana trifolia var. rigidiuscula

Lantana trifolia var. rigidiuscula, commonly known as rigid stem lantana, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, red, and purple, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental use in landscaping.
