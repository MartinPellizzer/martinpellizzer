Lycoris rosea

Lycoris rosea, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often evergreen, and its bulbs are used in traditional medicine. Lycoris rosea typically blooms in the fall, adding a dramatic visual element to gardens with its vibrant, torch-like flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
