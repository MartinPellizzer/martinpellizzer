Lepanthes orbiculata

Lepanthes orbiculata, commonly known as the orbiculate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Lepanthes orbiculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
