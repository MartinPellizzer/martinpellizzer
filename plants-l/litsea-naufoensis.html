Litsea naufoensis

Litsea naufoensis, commonly known as the Naufoan-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round berries.

Litsea naufoensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant properties.
