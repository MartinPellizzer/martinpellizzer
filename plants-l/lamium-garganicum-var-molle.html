Lamium garganicum var. molle

Lamium garganicum var. molle, commonly known as soft betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically bright green, and it produces clusters of small, two-lipped, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
