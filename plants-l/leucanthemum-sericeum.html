Leucanthemum sericeum

Leucanthemum sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white foliage and daisy-like flowers with yellow centers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Leucanthemum sericeum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
