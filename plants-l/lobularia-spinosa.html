Lobularia spinosa

Lobularia spinosa, commonly known as the spiny lobularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and hairy, often spiny leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Lobularia spinosa is typically an annual or biennial plant, blooming in spring or early summer.
