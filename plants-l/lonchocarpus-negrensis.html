Lonchocarpus negrensis

Lonchocarpus negrensis, commonly known as the Negros legume, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in lowland areas and is adapted to tropical climates. It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of alkaloids in its seeds.

However, its conservation status remains uncertain due to habitat loss and limited research.
