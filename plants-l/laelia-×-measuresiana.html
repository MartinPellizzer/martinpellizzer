Laelia × measuresiana

Laelia × measuresiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
