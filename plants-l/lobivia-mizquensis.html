Lobivia mizquensis

Lobivia mizquensis, commonly known as the Mizquense Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
