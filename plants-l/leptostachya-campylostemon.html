Leptostachya campylostemon

Leptostachya campylostemon, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense spike, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
