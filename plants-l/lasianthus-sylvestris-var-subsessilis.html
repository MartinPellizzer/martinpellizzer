Lasianthus sylvestris var. subsessilis

Lasianthus sylvestris var. subsessilis, commonly known as the slender-leaved lasianthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
