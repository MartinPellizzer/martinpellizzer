Lobelia columnae

Lobelia columnae, commonly known as the columnar lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright, columnar growth habit, which gives it a unique and compact appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, often clustered in dense spikes. Lobelia columnae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
