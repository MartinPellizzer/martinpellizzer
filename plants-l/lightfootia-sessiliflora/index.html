Lightfootia Sessiliflora

Mexican flame tree, scientifically known as Lightfootia sessiliflora, is a striking tropical tree native to Central America and parts of the Caribbean. It is renowned for its vibrant orange and red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a fiery display during the dry season. The tree's rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in warm climates. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits have earned it a place in both natural habitats and urban environments. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive in non-native regions.
