Limonium halophilum

Limonium halophilum, commonly known as sea helene, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in saline environments, showcasing its halophytic nature. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are adapted to withstand salt spray and coastal winds.

Limonium halophilum is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
