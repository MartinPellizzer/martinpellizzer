Laserpitium pseudomeum

Laserpitium pseudomeum, commonly known as the slender hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It blooms from late spring to early summer, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitats.
