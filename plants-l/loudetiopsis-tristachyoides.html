Loudetiopsis tristachyoides

Loudetiopsis tristachyoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dune grass, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along dunes and coastal plains. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white and arranged in clusters.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dune systems and preventing erosion.
