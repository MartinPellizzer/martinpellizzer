Lortetia holosericea

Lortetia holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lortetia holosericea is also known for its fragrant blooms, which attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
