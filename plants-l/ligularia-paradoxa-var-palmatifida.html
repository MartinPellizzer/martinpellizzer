Ligularia paradoxa var. palmatifida

Ligularia paradoxa var. palmatifida, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, giving it a dramatic, tropical appearance. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are clustered in dense, upright inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a focal point in shaded landscapes.

Its foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, making it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
