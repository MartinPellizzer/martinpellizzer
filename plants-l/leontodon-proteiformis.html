Leontodon proteiformis

Leontodon proteiformis, commonly known as the variable dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are often described as having a variable shape, which contributes to its common name.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
