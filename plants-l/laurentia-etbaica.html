Laurentia etbaica

Laurentia etbaica, commonly known as the Etbaic laurel, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
