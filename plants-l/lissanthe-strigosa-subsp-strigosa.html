Lissanthe strigosa subsp. strigosa

Lissanthe strigosa subsp.

strigosa, commonly known as the rough lissanthe, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving the plant its characteristic rough texture.

The flowers are typically pale blue to purple and are arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
