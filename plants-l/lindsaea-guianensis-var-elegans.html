Lindsaea guianensis var. elegans

Lindsaea guianensis var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant lindsaea, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
