Lonicera × bella f. atrorosea

Lonicera × bella f. atrorosea, commonly known as the dark rose honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its striking floral display.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically a combination of dark rose and white hues, arranged in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It is native to China and thrives in USDA hardiness zones 6 through 9.

This ornamental plant is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
