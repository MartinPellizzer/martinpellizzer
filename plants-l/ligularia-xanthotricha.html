Ligularia xanthotricha

Ligularia xanthotricha, commonly known as the golden-haired ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green hue and a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
