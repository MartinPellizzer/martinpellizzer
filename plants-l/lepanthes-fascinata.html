Lepanthes fascinata

Lepanthes fascinata, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often feature intricate patterns and vibrant hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and arranged in a rosette, aiding in water retention.

Lepanthes fascinata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
