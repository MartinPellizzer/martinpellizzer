Lycaste rossiana var. mattogrossensis

Lycaste rossiana var. mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Mattogrossense Lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support broad, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically in late winter to early spring. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is a sought-after species among advanced orchid growers.
