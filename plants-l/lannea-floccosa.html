Lannea floccosa

Lannea floccosa, commonly known as the woolly lannea, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly white or grayish pubescence covering its stems and leaves. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

Lannea floccosa is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
