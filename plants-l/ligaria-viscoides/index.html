Ligaria Viscoides

Ligaria viscoides, scientifically known as Ligaria viscoides, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of South America. This unique species belongs to the Asteraceae family, commonly known as the sunflower family. Its distinctive appearance, featuring bright yellow flowers and velvety leaves, makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Ligaria viscoides thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates. Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it has gained attention in both horticulture and ecological studies.
