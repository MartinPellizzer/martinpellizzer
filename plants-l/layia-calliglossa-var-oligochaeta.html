Layia calliglossa var. oligochaeta

Layia calliglossa var. oligochaeta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are linear and often covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with dark centers.
