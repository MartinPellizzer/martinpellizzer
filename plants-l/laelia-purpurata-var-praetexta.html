Laelia purpurata var. praetexta

Laelia purpurata var. praetexta, commonly known as the Purple-streaked Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and its striking, velvety purple and green foliage. The flowers are typically large, fragrant, and come in a range of colors from white to deep purple, with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires good air circulation and moderate light.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
