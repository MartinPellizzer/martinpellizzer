Lycopodium stellae-polaris

Lycopodium stellae-polaris, commonly known as the starry clubmoss, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Lycopodiaceae family.

This plant thrives in cool, moist environments such as shaded forest floors or rocky crevices.
