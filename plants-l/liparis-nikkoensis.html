Liparis nikkoensis

Liparis nikkoensis, commonly known as the Nikko orchid, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a short flowering period in late spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
