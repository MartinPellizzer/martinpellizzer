Lychnis frigida

Lychnis frigida, commonly known as the alpine campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae) and is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense clusters of pink or white flowers. Lychnis frigida is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to grow in full sun.
