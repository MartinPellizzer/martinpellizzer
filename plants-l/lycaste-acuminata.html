Lycaste acuminata

Lycaste acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, warm environments. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lycaste acuminata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
