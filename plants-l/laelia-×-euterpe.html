Laelia × euterpe

Laelia × euterpe, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Euterpe species, resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics.

It typically features vibrant, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a range of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in humid, well-lit environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying conditions.

Laelia × euterpe is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
