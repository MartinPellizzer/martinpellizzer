Lycopersicon esculentum subsp. galenii

Lycopersicon esculentum subsp.

galenii, commonly known as the Galen's tomato, is a botanical variant of the domesticated tomato. It is characterized by its small, round fruits that typically range in color from red to orange.

This subspecies is native to South America and has been cultivated for its unique flavor and nutritional profile. It shares many genetic traits with the common tomato but exhibits distinct morphological features.

The plant is often grown in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
