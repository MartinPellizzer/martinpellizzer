Lycianthes hylophila

Lycianthes hylophila, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its distinctive purple leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some areas.
