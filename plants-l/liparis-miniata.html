Liparis miniata

Liparis miniata, commonly known as the miniature orchid or dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its compact size and attractive blooms, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
