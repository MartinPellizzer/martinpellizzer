Lastrea filix-mas var. abbreviata

Lastrea filix-mas var. abbreviata, commonly known as the compact snake plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with a subtle white stripe running along the midrib. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with moderate sunlight.

This variety is prized for its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.
