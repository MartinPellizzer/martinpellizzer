Lessertia perennans var. polystachya

Lessertia perennans var. polystachya, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
