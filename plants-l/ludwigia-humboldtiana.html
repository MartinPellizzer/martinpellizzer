Ludwigia humboldtiana

Ludwigia humboldtiana, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange-colored leaves, which can vary in intensity depending on light exposure and nutrient levels. This species thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in marshes and ponds. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The plant has a branching stem system and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green.
