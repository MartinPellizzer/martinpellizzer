Lonicera wulingensis

Lonicera wulingensis, commonly known as the Wuling honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera wulingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
