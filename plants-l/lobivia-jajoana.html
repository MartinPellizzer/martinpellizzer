Lobivia jajoana

Lobivia jajoana, commonly known as the Jajoan Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines, which provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its succulent leaves are modified into spines, allowing it to store water efficiently in its stem.

Lobivia jajoana is well adapted to harsh desert environments, making it a resilient and hardy plant.
