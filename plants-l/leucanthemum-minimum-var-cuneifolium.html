Leucanthemum minimum var. cuneifolium

Leucanthemum minimum var. cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed oxeye daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, white flowers with yellow centers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow and slightly toothed, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
