Lasianthus schmidtii

Lasianthus schmidtii, commonly known as Schmidt's lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
