Lactuca undulata

Lactuca undulata, commonly known as the wavy lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its undulating, wavy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca undulata is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils.
