Listera cordata f. rubescens

Listera cordata f. rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed basket lily, is a rare and striking variant of the basket lily species.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodland environments, preferring well-drained, acidic soils. Its distinctive red stems and leaves make it a notable ornamental plant in botanic gardens.

This form is a fascinating example of natural variation within the Listera genus.
