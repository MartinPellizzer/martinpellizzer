Leucopogon crassiflorus

Leucopogon crassiflorus, commonly known as the broad-flowered heath, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

Leucopogon crassiflorus is often found in heathlands and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils.
