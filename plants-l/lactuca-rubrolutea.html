Lactuca rubrolutea

Lactuca rubrolutea, commonly known as the red and yellow lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves that are typically red or orange in color, with a yellowish underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

Lactuca rubrolutea is often found in dry, rocky soils and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
