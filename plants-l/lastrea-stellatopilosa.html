Lastrea stellatopilosa

Lastrea stellatopilosa, commonly known as the star-pilose lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine, star-shaped hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its leaves are typically small and sparsely arranged, contributing to its overall compact form.

Lastrea stellatopilosa is well adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xerophytic gardens.
