Lilium fresnense

Lilium fresnense, commonly known as the Mexican tiger lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange and red striped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stem is upright and bears the showy flowers.

Lilium fresnense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
