Lychnitis sinuata

Lychnitis sinuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and slightly serrated, often with a waxy surface.

Lychnitis sinuata is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
