Lisianthius longifolius var. cordifolius

Lisianthius longifolius var. cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaved lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to native plant collections.
