Limonium kardamylii

Limonium kardamylii, commonly known as the Greek sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds, typical of the Caryophyllaceae family. Limonium kardamylii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
