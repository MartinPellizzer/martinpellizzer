Lobelia fluviatilis

Lobelia fluviatilis, commonly known as river lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as riverbanks and marshes. The plant features whorled leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. Its fibrous root system helps stabilize soil in aquatic habitats.

Lobelia fluviatilis is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
