Leptodermis gracilis var. gracilis

Leptodermis gracilis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leathery fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, leathery leaves that are usually arranged in a compact rosette. The leaves are typically elongated and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
