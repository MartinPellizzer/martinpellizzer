Lunaria cantoniensis

Lunaria cantoniensis, commonly known as the Chinese moonseed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces distinctive spherical seed pods that turn blue when mature, resembling small moons. These pods contain small, smooth seeds that are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
