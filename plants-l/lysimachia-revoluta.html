Lysimachia revoluta

Lysimachia revoluta, commonly known as the hairy yellow loosestrife, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures. Its showy, yellow flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Lysimachia revoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
