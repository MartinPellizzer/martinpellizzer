Landolphia pyramidata

Landolphia pyramidata, commonly known as the African Crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are usually alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.
