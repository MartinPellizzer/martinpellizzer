Lepanthes sanjuanensis

Lepanthes sanjuanensis, commonly known as the San Juan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring white and green hues. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Lepanthes sanjuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
