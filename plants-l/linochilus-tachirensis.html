Linochilus tachirensis

Linochilus tachirensis, commonly known as the Tachira lily, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Venezuela.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features strap-like leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
