Lobelia flaccida var. stricta

Lobelia flaccida var. stricta, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
