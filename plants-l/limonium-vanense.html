Limonium vanense

Limonium vanense, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, which helps reduce water loss.

Limonium vanense is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
