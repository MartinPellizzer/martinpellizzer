Lasiopera tenuifolia

Lasiopera tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to Africa, particularly found in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Lasiopera tenuifolia is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
