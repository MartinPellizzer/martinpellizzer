Lycaste macrophylla var. plana

Lycaste macrophylla var. plana, commonly known as the broad-leafed lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
