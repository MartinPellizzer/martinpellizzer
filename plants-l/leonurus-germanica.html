Leonurus germanica

Leonurus germanica, commonly known as European hedgehog or lesser hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
