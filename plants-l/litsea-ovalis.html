Litsea ovalis

Litsea ovalis, commonly known as the oval leaf litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces berries that are typically orange-red when ripe, containing several seeds.

Litsea ovalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
