Lobelia cardinalis f. rosea

Lobelia cardinalis f. rosea, commonly known as the cardinal flower rosea form, is a striking variant of the cardinal flower native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to rose-red flowers, which contrast beautifully with its deep green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in the garden.
