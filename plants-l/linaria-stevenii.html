Linaria stevenii

Linaria stevenii, commonly known as the Steven's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Linaria in the family Plantaginaceae. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Linaria stevenii thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes.
