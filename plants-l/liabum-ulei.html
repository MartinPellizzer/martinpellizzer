Liabum ulei

Liabum ulei, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety hair, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Liabum ulei is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain regions.
