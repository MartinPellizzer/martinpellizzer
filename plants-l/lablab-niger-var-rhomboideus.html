Lablab niger var. rhomboideus

Lablab niger var. rhomboideus, commonly known as the broadleaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and cylindrical, hairy pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is often grown for its protein-rich seeds, which are used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable crop in sustainable agricultural systems.
