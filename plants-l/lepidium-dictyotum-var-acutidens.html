Lepidium dictyotum var. acutidens

Lepidium dictyotum var. acutidens, commonly known as the sharp-leafed cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual plant characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the mustard family. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

Its botanical features make it a useful species for ecological studies and soil health assessments.
