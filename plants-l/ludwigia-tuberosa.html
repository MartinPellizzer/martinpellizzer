Ludwigia tuberosa

Ludwigia tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has smooth, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Ludwigia tuberosa is also cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display.
