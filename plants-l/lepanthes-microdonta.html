Lepanthes microdonta

Lepanthes microdonta, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Lepanthes microdonta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
