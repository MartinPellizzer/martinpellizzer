Lapeirousia cyanescens

Lapeirousia cyanescens, commonly known as the blue orchid of the Western Cape, is a member of the Iridaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive, often twisted shape. It blooms during the winter months, typically from July to September.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species.
