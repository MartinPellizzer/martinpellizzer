Lotus filicaulis

Lotus filicaulis, commonly known as the fan-leaved lotus, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, fan-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom above the water. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Lotus filicaulis thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.
