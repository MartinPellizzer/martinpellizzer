Laurus heteranthera

Laurus heteranthera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved laurel, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like bay laurel. The plant is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe, containing a single seed.

Laurus heteranthera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
