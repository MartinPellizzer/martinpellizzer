Ludwigia Glandulosa Var. Typica

Ludwigia glandulosa var. typica is a striking aquatic plant known for its vibrant red and orange foliage. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving rivers and ponds. This plant is popular among aquarium hobbyists for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. Its unique coloration changes with light and nutrient levels, making it a favorite for planted tank displays. Despite its beauty, it requires careful maintenance to prevent overgrowth and maintain water quality.
