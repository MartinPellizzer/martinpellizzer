Lacmellea gracilis

Lacmellea gracilis, commonly known as the slender mossy orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
