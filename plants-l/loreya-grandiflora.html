Loreya grandiflora

Loreya grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered lorea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Loreya grandiflora thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
