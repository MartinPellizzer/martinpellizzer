Lychnis × intermedia

Lychnis × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid carnation or garden carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the carnation family, Caryophyllaceae.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 20–60 cm, featuring erect, often branched stems. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soil in full sun.

Lychnis × intermedia is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
