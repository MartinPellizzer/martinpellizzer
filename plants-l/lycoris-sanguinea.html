Lycoris sanguinea

Lycoris sanguinea, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange in color, blooming in the autumn. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge after the flowers have faded. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Lycoris sanguinea is also noted for its ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil.
