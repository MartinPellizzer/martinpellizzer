Ligusticum linearilobum var. lanceolatum

Ligusticum linearilobum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into dry, ribbed fruits.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower leaf lobes compared to the typical form of Ligusticum linearilobum.
