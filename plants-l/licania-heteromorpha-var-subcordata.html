Licania heteromorpha var. subcordata

Licania heteromorpha var. subcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf licania, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The leaves are typically glabrous and have a smooth texture, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits that are edible.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
