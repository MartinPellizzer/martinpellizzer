Laelia hookeri

Laelia hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and blooms with intricate, colorful flowers that can range from white to pink and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laelia hookeri is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
