Linaria aequitriloba subsp. fragilis

Linaria aequitriloba subsp.

fragilis, commonly known as the fragrant toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers.
