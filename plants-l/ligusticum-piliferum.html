Ligusticum piliferum

Ligusticum piliferum, commonly known as the hairy ligusticum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

Ligusticum piliferum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassy areas.
