Luziola caroliniana

Luziola caroliniana, commonly known as Carolina lantern, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Luziola caroliniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
