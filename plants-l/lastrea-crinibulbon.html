Lastrea crinibulbon

Lastrea crinibulbon, commonly known as the curly hair cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem covered in dense, white, hair-like spines that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
