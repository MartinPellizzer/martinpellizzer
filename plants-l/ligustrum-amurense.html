Ligustrum amurense

Ligustrum amurense, commonly known as Amur privet, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruits are dark purple berries that contain seeds.

Ligustrum amurense is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
