Lampranthus altistylus

Lampranthus altistylus, commonly known as the tall-stemmed ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
