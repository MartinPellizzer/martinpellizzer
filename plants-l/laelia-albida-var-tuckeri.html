Laelia albida var. tuckeri

Laelia albida var. tuckeri, commonly known as the Tucker's white laelia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers with a subtle greenish tint and a pleasant, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming compact clumps with fleshy, dark green leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
