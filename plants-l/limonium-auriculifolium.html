Limonium auriculifolium

Limonium auriculifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-gray, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Limonium auriculifolium is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
