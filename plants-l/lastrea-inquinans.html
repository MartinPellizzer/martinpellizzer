Lastrea inquinans

Lastrea inquinans, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or arid environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color, blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
