Limosella aquatica var. aquatica

Limosella aquatica var. aquatica, commonly known as marsh sandwort, is a flowering plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in coastal areas and freshwater marshes.
