Lilium meleagris

Lilium meleagris, commonly known as the checkered lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan. The plant is characterized by its striking, spotted or checkered petals that range in color from white to pink and red. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help it survive in its native habitat.

The checkered lily is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
