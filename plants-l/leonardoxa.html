Leonardoxa

Leonardoxa, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dazzling display during the flowering season. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces long, slender pods that contain seeds.

Leonardoxa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
