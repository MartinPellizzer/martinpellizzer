Lactuca pulchella

Lactuca pulchella, commonly known as the pretty lettuce or slender lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae).

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its delicate, hairless leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 cm.

Its seeds are small and contain a milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the genus Lactuca.
