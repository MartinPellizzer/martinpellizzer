Lobelia arabidea

Lobelia arabidea, commonly known as the Arabian lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are opposite and often hairy, providing some protection against desiccation.

Lobelia arabidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
