Lysimachia henryi var. henryi

Lysimachia henryi var. henryi, commonly known as the Chinese yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to China.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
