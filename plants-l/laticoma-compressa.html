Laticoma compressa

Laticoma compressa, commonly known as the broadleaf laticoma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are typically broad and compressed in appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the spring and summer months. Laticoma compressa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its latex-rich sap has been historically used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
