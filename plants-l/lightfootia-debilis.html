Lightfootia debilis

Lightfootia debilis, commonly known as the slender lightfoot.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its delicate, trailing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Lightfootia debilis is often cultivated as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.
