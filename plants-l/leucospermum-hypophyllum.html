Leucospermum hypophyllum

Leucospermum hypophyllum, commonly known as the mountain fire or yellow starling, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions, characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often thriving in full sun.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
