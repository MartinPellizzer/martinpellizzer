Leucaena brandegeei

Leucaena brandegeei, commonly known as the silky serissa, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.

However, it can be invasive in some areas if not managed properly.
