Lesquerella Ludoviciana Var. Arenosa

Lesquerella ludoviciana var. arenosa is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. It thrives in harsh, sandy environments where few other plants can survive. This variant is known for its unique adaptations that allow it to conserve water and withstand extreme temperatures. Despite its resilience, it remains under threat due to habitat loss and climate change. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into ecological survival strategies in desert ecosystems.
