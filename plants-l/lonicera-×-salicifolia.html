Lonicera × salicifolia

Lonicera × salicifolia, commonly known as the silver honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage.

It produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its growth habit includes both upright and trailing forms, making it versatile for different garden settings.
