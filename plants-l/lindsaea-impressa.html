Lindsaea impressa

Lindsaea impressa, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate, branching fronds that often exhibit a zigzag pattern. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a distinctive, slightly dimpled texture on the underside. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.

Lindsaea impressa is valued for its ornamental appearance and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
