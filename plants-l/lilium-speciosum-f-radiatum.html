Lilium speciosum f. radiatum

Lilium speciosum f. radiatum, commonly known as the radiate lily, is a striking hybrid cultivated for its ornamental value.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive radiating pattern of petals. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that emerge from a rhizomatous root system.

This cultivar is prized for its vibrant colors and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
