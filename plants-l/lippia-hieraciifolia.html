Lippia hieraciifolia

Lippia hieraciifolia, commonly known as silverleaf lippia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in dry, rocky habitats.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb, adding a citrusy flavor to dishes.
