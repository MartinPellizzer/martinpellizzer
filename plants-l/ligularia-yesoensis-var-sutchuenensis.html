Ligularia yesoensis var. sutchuenensis

Ligularia yesoensis var. sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside, often marked with prominent veins. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers in the summer, arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas along riverbanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
