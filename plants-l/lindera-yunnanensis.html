Lindera yunnanensis

Lindera yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan loranthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province, and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species.
