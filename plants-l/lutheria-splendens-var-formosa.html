Lutheria splendens var. formosa

Lutheria splendens var. formosa, commonly known as the golden lutheria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
