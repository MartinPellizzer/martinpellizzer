Leplaea mangenotiana

Leplaea mangenotiana, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
