Liparis montana var. montana

Liparis montana var. montana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, leafy stems and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the acidic soil in which it thrives.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
