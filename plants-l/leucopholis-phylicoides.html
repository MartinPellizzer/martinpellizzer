Leucopholis phylicoides

Leucopholis phylicoides, commonly known as the silverleaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Leucopholis phylicoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
