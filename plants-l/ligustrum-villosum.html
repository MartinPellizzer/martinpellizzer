Ligustrum villosum

Ligustrum villosum, commonly known as the silky privet, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. Ligustrum villosum is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in various soil types.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental gardens.
