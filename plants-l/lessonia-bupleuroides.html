Lessonia bupleuroides

Lessonia bupleuroides, commonly known as the Chilean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Chile.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
