Luvunga linearis

Luvunga linearis, commonly known as the slender luvunga, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the wet season. Luvunga linearis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
