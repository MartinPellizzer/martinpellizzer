Lycaste lasioglossa var. flava

Lycaste lasioglossa var. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, warm environments. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
