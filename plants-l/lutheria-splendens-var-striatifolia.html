Lutheria splendens var. striatifolia

Lutheria splendens var. striatifolia, commonly known as the striped lutheria, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with some varieties displaying subtle striping or variegation. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated in gardens or as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
