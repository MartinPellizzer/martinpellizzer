Lycaste longisepala

Lycaste longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepal lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Lycaste longisepala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
