Lobelia gaudichaudii var. koolauensis

Lobelia gaudichaudii var. koolauensis, commonly known as Koolau lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features showy, tubular blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
