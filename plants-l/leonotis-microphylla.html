Leonotis microphylla

Leonotis microphylla, commonly known as the blanket flower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features bright orange to yellow tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in xeriscaping.
