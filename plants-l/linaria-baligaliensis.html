Linaria baligaliensis

Linaria baligaliensis, commonly known as the Baligal yellowtop, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Linaria in the family Plantaginaceae. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Linaria baligaliensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
