Limnanthemum nymphoides

Limnanthemum nymphoides, commonly known as the water starflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy coating to help retain moisture.

Limnanthemum nymphoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens.
