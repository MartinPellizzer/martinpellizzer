Lupinus angustifolius subsp. linifolius

Lupinus angustifolius subsp.

linifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaf lupin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, erect stems and pinnately compound leaves with narrow, linear leaflets.

The plant produces spiny, cylindrical pods that contain small, bean-like seeds. It is adapted to calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
