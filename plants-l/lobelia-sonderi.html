Lobelia sonderi

Lobelia sonderi, commonly known as the Cape lobelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Lobelia sonderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
