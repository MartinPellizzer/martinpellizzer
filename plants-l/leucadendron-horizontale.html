Leucadendron horizontale

Leucadendron horizontale, commonly known as the horizontal leucadendron, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that grow horizontally along the stems, creating a unique, cascading appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flower heads, which are surrounded by specialized bracts that add to its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
