Lobelia sichuanensis

Lobelia sichuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Lobelia sichuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
