Lomelosia minoana subsp. minoana

Lomelosia minoana subsp.

minoana, commonly known as the Minoan lomelosia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Crete. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellowish.

This subspecies is of particular interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological adaptations.
