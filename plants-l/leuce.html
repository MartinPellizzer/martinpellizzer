Leuce

Leuce, commonly known as white hellebore, is a perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a basal rosette of dark green, velvety leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
