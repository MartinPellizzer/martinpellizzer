Licuala bissula

Licuala bissula, commonly known as the Bissul bamboo palm, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a palm-like appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens. Its foliage is typically a deep green, and it produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds.

Licuala bissula is adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive.
