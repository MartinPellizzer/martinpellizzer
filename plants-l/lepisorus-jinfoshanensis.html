Lepisorus jinfoshanensis

Lepisorus jinfoshanensis, commonly known as the Jinfoshan maidenhair fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are short and thick, supporting the growth of its intricate foliage.

Lepisorus jinfoshanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
