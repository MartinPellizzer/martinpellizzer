Lotus siliquosus var. glaber

Lotus siliquosus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, glabrous (hairless) foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The smooth lotus is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
