Laelia gloriosa

Laelia gloriosa, commonly known as the glorious orchid, is a species native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces large, colorful blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. Laelia gloriosa is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
