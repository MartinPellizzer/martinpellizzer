Leucanthemum redieri

Leucanthemum redieri, commonly known as the redleaf leucanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy white to pale yellow ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. Its foliage is notable for its reddish or purplish tinge, which distinguishes it from other members of the genus.

Leucanthemum redieri typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
