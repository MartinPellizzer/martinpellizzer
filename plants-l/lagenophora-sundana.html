Lagenophora sundana

Lagenophora sundana, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Lagenophora sundana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
