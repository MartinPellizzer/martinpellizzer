Lantana lippioides

Lantana lippioides, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Lantana lippioides is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
