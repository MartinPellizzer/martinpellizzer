Liparis graciliscapa

Liparis graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, while its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns.

Liparis graciliscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
