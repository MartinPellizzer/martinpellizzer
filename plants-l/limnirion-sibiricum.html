Limnirion sibiricum

Limnirion sibiricum, commonly known as Siberian waterweed, is a aquatic plant native to the cold regions of Siberia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as lakes and rivers. The plant has long, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, while its roots anchor it to the substrate. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small fish and invertebrates.

Limnirion sibiricum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
