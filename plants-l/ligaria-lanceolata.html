Ligaria lanceolata

Ligaria lanceolata, commonly known as the Chilean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

Ligaria lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
