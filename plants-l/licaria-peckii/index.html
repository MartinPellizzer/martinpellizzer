Licaria Peckii

Licaria peckii is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the cloud forests of Central America. This flowering plant belongs to the Malvaceae family, known for its diverse and often showy blooms. Its delicate pink and white petals make it a striking addition to any botanical collection. Despite its beauty, Licaria peckii remains little known outside of specialized horticultural circles.
