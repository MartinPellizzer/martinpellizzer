Lieberkuhna bracteata

Lieberkuhna bracteata, commonly known as the golden bract fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, golden-yellow bracts that surround the spore-bearing structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are pinnately divided and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

The botanical features of Lieberkuhna bracteata make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to botanical gardens.
