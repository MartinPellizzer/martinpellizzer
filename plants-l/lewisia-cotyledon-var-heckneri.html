Lewisia cotyledon var. heckneri

Lewisia cotyledon var. heckneri, commonly known as the Heckner's cushion plant, is a succulent perennial native to the western United States.

It features dense, fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
