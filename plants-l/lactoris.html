Lactoris

Lactoris, commonly known as the milkweed plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its milky sap, which is often toxic and can cause skin irritation. The plant typically has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful flowers. Lactoris species are native to various regions, including North America, Africa, and parts of Asia.
