Leucanthemum Pallens Subsp. Discoideum

White daisy, scientifically known as Leucanthemum pallens subsp. discoideum, is a delicate flowering plant native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its pure white petals and a yellow center, resembling a miniature sun. This species thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Its simple yet elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a natural look. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more showy blooms.
