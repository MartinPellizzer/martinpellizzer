Lilium rubescens

Lilium rubescens, commonly known as the red lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces bulbous underground stems.

Lilium rubescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
