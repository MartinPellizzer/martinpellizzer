Lonicera kawakamii

Lonicera kawakamii, commonly known as the Kawakamii honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly glossy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
