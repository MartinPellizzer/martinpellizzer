Ligularia pterodonta

Ligularia pterodonta, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking yellowish-white spadices surrounded by a green spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ligularia pterodonta is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
