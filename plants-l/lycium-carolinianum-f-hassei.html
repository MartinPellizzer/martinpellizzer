Lycium carolinianum f. hassei

Lycium carolinianum f. hassei, commonly known as the Carolina wolfberry, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible and often used in local culinary traditions. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
