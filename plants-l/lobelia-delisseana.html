Lobelia delisseana

Lobelia delisseana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a contrasting yellow or white throat. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia delisseana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.
