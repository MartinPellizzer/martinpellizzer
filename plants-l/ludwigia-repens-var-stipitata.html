Ludwigia repens var. stipitata

Ludwigia repens var. stipitata, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, ribbon-like leaves that are often bright red or orange, depending on light conditions. The plant thrives in moderate to high light environments and is popular in aquariums for its vibrant coloration. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear above the water surface.

This variant is known for its ease of care and ability to grow rapidly in suitable conditions.
