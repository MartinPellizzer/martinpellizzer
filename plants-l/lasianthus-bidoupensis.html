Lasianthus bidoupensis

Lasianthus bidoupensis, commonly known as the Bidoup mountain gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Vietnam.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lasianthus bidoupensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
