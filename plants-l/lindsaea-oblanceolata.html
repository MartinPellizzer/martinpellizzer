Lindsaea oblanceolata

Lindsaea oblanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often slightly curved leaves that form a rosette-like structure. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, which are followed by bright red or orange berries. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
