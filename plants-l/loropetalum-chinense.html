Loropetalum chinense

Loropetalum chinense, commonly known as Chinese loropetalum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are followed by ornamental, red or orange berries. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a glossy surface and a reddish or purple hue in the fall.

Loropetalum chinense is valued in landscaping for its vibrant seasonal color and adaptability to various soil conditions.
