Lespedeza dunnii

Lespedeza dunnii, commonly known as the southern lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Lespedeza dunnii is often found in woodlands, thickets, and along streambanks, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects.
