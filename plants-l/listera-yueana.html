Listera yueana

Listera yueana, commonly known as the Chinese basketwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Listera yueana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
