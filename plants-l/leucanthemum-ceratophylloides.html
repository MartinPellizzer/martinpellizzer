Leucanthemum ceratophylloides

Leucanthemum ceratophylloides, commonly known as the woolly leucanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow-centered white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
