Lespedeza × intermixta

Lespedeza × intermixta, commonly known as the hybrid lespedeza, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb native to eastern North America, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in open woodlands and disturbed sites, thriving in well-drained soils. Lespedeza × intermixta is notable for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
