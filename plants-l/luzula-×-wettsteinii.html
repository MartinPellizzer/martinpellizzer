Luzula × wettsteinii

Luzula × wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's wood rush, is a hybrid species within the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a spike of small, greenish flowers that are often clustered near the top of the stem.

Luzula × wettsteinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging, wet conditions.
