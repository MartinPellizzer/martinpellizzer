Lithophragma micrantha

Lithophragma micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf strawberry plant, is a flowering perennial native to the western United States.

Lithophragma micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
