Lacinaria scariosa var. perusta

Lacinaria scariosa var. perusta, commonly known as the scaly lacinaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, scaly leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is covered in a dense layer of fine, scaly structures that provide protection against environmental stress.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
