Luzula luzuloides var. erythranthema

Luzula luzuloides var. erythranthema, commonly known as the red-flowered wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear small, spike-like inflorescences with striking red or purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue.

This variety is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
