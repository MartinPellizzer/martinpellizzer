Lepidotrichilia sambiranensis

Lepidotrichilia sambiranensis, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found exclusively in Madagascar.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, where it relies on specific symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
