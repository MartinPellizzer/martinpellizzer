Lepanthes effusa

Lepanthes effusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests. Lepanthes effusa belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural enthusiasts and botanical studies.
