Ladenbergia dissimiliflora

Ladenbergia dissimiliflora, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
