Ligularia hodgsonii

Ligularia hodgsonii, commonly known as the giant leopard plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white to yellowish margin, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Ligularia hodgsonii is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and can grow up to 2 meters in height.
