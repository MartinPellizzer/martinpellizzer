Luzula × sichuanensis

Luzula × sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan wood rush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and is native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
