Linociera nilotica

Linociera nilotica, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces a milky sap, which is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

Linociera nilotica is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
