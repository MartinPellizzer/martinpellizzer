Limonium estevei

Limonium estevei, commonly known as the Spanish sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, often found on sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is well adapted to salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.

Limonium estevei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in coastal landscaping.
