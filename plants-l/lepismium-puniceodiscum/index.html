Lepismium Puniceodiscum

Lepismium puniceodiscum is a striking member of the nightshade family known for its vibrant, flame-like flowers. Native to the Andes mountains, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. The plant's foliage is dense and evergreen, adding texture to any garden setting. With proper care, it can bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
