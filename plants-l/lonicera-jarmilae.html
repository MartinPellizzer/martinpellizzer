Lonicera jarmilae

Lonicera jarmilae, commonly known as the Jarmilae honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds but not typically for humans. Lonicera jarmilae thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
