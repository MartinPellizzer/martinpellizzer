Lonicera adenocarpa

Lonicera adenocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
