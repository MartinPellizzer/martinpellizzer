Ligustrum rosmarinifolium

Ligustrum rosmarinifolium, commonly known as sweet box, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are often described as resembling rosemary leaves. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Ligustrum rosmarinifolium is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
