Lepanthes spadariae

Lepanthes spadariae, commonly known as the Spadaria Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive spadix-like structure. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees and other plants without being parasitic. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Lepanthes spadariae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
