Leucas wightiana

Leucas wightiana, commonly known as silverleaf leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and medicinal properties.

Leucas wightiana is also used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for its purported health benefits.
