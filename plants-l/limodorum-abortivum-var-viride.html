Limodorum abortivum var. viride

Limodorum abortivum var. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered larkspur, is a rare and delicate species native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers, which are usually a pale green or white in color, giving it its common name.
