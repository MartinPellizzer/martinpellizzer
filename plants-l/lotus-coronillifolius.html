Lotus coronillifolius

Lotus coronillifolius, commonly known as the crownleaf lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy underside. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Lotus coronillifolius is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
