Lygistum glandulosum

Lygistum glandulosum, commonly known as the sticky nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which contribute to its sticky texture. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Lygistum glandulosum is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
