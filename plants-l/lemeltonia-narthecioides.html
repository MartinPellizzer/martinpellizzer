Lemeltonia narthecioides

Lemeltonia narthecioides, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and showy, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in its native range.
