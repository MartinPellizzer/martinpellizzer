Lepidium abyssinicum

Lepidium abyssinicum, commonly known as Ethiopian cress, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard species. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and edible leaves that have a peppery flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Lepidium abyssinicum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
