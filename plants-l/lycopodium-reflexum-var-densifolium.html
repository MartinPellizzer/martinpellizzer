Lycopodium reflexum var. densifolium

Lycopodium reflexum var. densifolium, commonly known as the dense-leaved clubmoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like leaves that form tight, compact clusters along its stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its sporophytes are small and herbaceous, with sporangia developing on the underside of the leaves.

This variety is notable for its robust growth and adaptability to various soil types, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
