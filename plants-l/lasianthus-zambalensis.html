Lasianthus zambalensis

Lasianthus zambalensis, commonly known as Zambal's lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.
