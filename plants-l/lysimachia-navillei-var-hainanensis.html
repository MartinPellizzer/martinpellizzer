Lysimachia navillei var. hainanensis

Lysimachia navillei var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan yellowthread, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, creeping growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers.
