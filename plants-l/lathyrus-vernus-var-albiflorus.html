Lathyrus vernus var. albiflorus

Lathyrus vernus var. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered pea vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several kidney-shaped seeds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
