Lepanthes casasae

Lepanthes casasae, commonly known as the Casasae Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Lepanthes casasae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
