Lantana viburnoides var. kisi

Lantana viburnoides var. kisi, commonly known as the Kisi lantana, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and showy flower clusters.
