Lobivia duursmaiana

Lobivia duursmaiana, commonly known as the Duursma's lobivia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit. The plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring. Lobivia duursmaiana is well adapted to arid environments, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its slow growth rate and minimal care requirements make it an ideal plant for succulent enthusiasts.
