Lasiagrostis splendens

Lasiagrostis splendens, commonly known as the splendid grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Lasiagrostis splendens prefers full sun and is relatively low maintenance once established.
