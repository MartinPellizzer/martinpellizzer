Laelia × mylamiana

Laelia × mylamiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure, typical of many orchid genera. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
