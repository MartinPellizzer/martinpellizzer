Lachnaea elegans

Lachnaea elegans, commonly known as the elegant lachnaea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lachnaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its growth habit is usually low and mat-forming, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Lachnaea elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
