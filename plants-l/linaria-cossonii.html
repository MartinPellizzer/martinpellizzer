Linaria cossonii

Linaria cossonii, commonly known as the Cosson's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Linaria cossonii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
