Ladenbergia lucens

Ladenbergia lucens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sunlight for optimal growth.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
