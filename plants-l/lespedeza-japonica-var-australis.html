Lespedeza japonica var. australis

Lespedeza japonica var. australis, commonly known as Australian lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has been introduced to other regions for erosion control and as a forage plant.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil fertility.
