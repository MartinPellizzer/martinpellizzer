Laserpitium panax

Laserpitium panax, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to yellow composite flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in dry, sunny habitats. It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
