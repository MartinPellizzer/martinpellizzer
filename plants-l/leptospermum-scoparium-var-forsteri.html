Leptospermum scoparium var. forsteri

Leptospermum scoparium var. forsteri, commonly known as the silver mat-rush, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are densely arranged along its stems, giving it a mat-like appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red or orange berries. It is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, often thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh conditions.
