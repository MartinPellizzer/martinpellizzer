Landolphia lanceolata

Landolphia lanceolata, commonly known as the African arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in humid, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes milkweeds.
