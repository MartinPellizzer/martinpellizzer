Lipochaeta lavarum var. skottsbergii

Lipochaeta lavarum var. skottsbergii, commonly known as the Skottsberg's lipochaeta, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Bolivia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in temperate to subtropical climates.
