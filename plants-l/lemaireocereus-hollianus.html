Lemaireocereus hollianus

Lemaireocereus hollianus, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in the winter months. The plant has flattened, segmented stems that are often used for propagation. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique blooming cycle makes it a popular ornamental plant in homes and gardens.
