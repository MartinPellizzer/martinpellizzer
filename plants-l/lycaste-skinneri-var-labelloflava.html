Lycaste skinneri var. labelloflava

Lycaste skinneri var. labelloflava, commonly known as the Orange-Flowered Lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
