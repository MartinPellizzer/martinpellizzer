Ladenbergia physophora

Ladenbergia physophora, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often fissured. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
