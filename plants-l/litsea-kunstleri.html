Litsea kunstleri

Litsea kunstleri, commonly known as the Malayan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when mature.

Litsea kunstleri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
