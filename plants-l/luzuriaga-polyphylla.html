Luzuriaga polyphylla

Luzuriaga polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved wintergreen, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and possesses aromatic, slightly medicinal leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Luzuriaga polyphylla thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
