Linosyris stricta

Linosyris stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf linosyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Linosyris stricta thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
