Lemaireocereus cartwrightianus

Lemaireocereus cartwrightianus, commonly known as the Cartwright's night blooming cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces large, fragrant, white flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique flowering behavior and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens.
