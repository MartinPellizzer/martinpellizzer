Lepanthes catlingii

Lepanthes catlingii, commonly known as the Catling's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are delicate, with white petals and sepals, and a distinctive yellow lip.

Lepanthes catlingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
