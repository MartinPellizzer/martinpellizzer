Leea thorelii

Leea thorelii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Leea thorelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
