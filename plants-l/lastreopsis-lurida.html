Lastreopsis lurida

Lastreopsis lurida, commonly known as the slender lastreopsis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flower heads that appear in clusters. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a creeping growth habit and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
