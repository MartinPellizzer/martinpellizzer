Leucas moluccoides

Leucas moluccoides, commonly known as the Moluccan leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal uses.
