Legousia Julianii

Legousia julianii is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to a small region in southern Europe. It is known for its striking violet-blue blossoms that bloom in late spring. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly understood by botanists due to its limited distribution. The plant thrives in rocky, sun-exposed areas and is often overlooked in the wild. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this unique species from habitat loss and climate change.
