Lasthenia obtusifolia var. bridgesii

Lasthenia obtusifolia var. bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridgess' stickyhead, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a sticky texture.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.
