Licuala kiahii

Licuala kiahii, commonly known as the Kiahii palm, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, slender palm with a straight trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as thatching material in some cultures.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
