Leucanthemum vulgare var. eliasii

Leucanthemum vulgare var. eliasii, commonly known as the Eliasii marguerite, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 18 inches, with upright stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
