Ludwigia erigata

Ludwigia erigata, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, making it a popular choice for aquatic gardens and ponds. Its leaves are elongated and often have a reddish hue, adding visual interest to its appearance.

Ludwigia erigata is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.
