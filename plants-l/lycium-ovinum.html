Lycium ovinum

Lycium ovinum, commonly known as the goji berry plant, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It produces small, red, berry-like fruits that are rich in antioxidants and nutrients. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its leaves are small, oval, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lycium ovinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for both its berries and medicinal properties.
