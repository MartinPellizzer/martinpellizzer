Lonicera missionis

Lonicera missionis, commonly known as the Mission honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It has opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Lonicera missionis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging climates.
