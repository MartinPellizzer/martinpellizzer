Lobelia mexicana

Lobelia mexicana, commonly known as Mexican whorled lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and often have a slightly hairy texture.
