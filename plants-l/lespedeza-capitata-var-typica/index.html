Lespedeza Capitata Var. Typica

Lespedeza capitata var. typica is a small, herbaceous plant native to eastern asia. it is known for its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. this species thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade. it is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value. its compact size makes it ideal for rock gardens and borders.
