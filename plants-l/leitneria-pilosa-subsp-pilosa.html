Leitneria pilosa subsp. pilosa

Leitneria pilosa subsp.

pilosa, commonly known as the hairy leitneria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures. Its small, white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the rainy season.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
