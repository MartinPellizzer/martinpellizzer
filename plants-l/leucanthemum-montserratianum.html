Leucanthemum montserratianum

Leucanthemum montserratianum, commonly known as the Montserrat daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features showy white ray florets surrounding a central disc of yellow-orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Leucanthemum montserratianum is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
