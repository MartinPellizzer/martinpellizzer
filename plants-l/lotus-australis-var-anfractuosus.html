Lotus australis var. anfractuosus

Lotus australis var. anfractuosus, commonly known as the rugged lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features palmately compound leaves with serrated margins and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its roots are fibrous and capable of fixing nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
