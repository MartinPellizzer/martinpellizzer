Lomelosia Kurdica

Kurdish lomelosia, scientifically known as Lomelosia kurdica, is a rare and unique plant species native to the mountainous regions of Kurdistan. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. This plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and small, pale flowers that bloom in the spring. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered an endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical treasure for future generations.
