Lavatera × columbretensis

Lavatera × columbretensis, commonly known as the hybrid lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2–5 meters. The plant produces showy, fluffy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a soft, hairy texture.

Lavatera × columbretensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
