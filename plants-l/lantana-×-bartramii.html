Lantana × bartramii

Lantana × bartramii, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is part of the Verbenaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various soil types and climates. It produces small, oval berries that are often consumed by birds, aiding in seed dispersal.

Lantana × bartramii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
