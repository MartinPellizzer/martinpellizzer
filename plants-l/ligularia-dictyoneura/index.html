Ligularia Dictyoneura

Dictyoneura ligularia, scientifically known as Ligularia dictyoneura, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is renowned for its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach impressive sizes, often spanning over a foot in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in woodland areas and along stream banks. Its vibrant green foliage provides a dramatic visual impact in gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that this plant is toxic to both humans and animals if ingested.
