Linanthus laxus

Linanthus laxus, commonly known as the slender flax or desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Linanthus laxus is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats, where it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
