Lepanthes aguirrei

Lepanthes aguirrei, commonly known as the Aguirre's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, showy blooms in shades of white and pink.

Lepanthes aguirrei is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
