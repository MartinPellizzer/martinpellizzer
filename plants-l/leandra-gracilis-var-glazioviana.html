Leandra gracilis var. glazioviana

Leandra gracilis var. glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziov's tree fern, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Central and South America.

The varietal form glazioviana is distinguished by its slightly different frond structure and coloration compared to the typical variety of Leandra gracilis.
