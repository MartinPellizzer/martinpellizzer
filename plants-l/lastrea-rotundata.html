Lastrea rotundata

Lastrea rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
