Lonicera × mirallesii

Lonicera × mirallesii, commonly known as the Miralles honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, usually yellow or orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its berries are small, red, and edible, providing food for birds and other wildlife.

Lonicera × mirallesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
