Leighia lanceolata

Leighia lanceolata, commonly known as the orchid mantis, is a unique plant that combines the features of an orchid and a mantis.

It is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Colombia. The plant has striking, elongated flowers that resemble the slender body of a praying mantis. Its inflorescence consists of multiple pale green to white flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern.

Leighia lanceolata is known for its unusual appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
