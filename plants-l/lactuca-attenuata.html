Lactuca attenuata

Lactuca attenuata, commonly known as the slender lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its thin, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Lactuca attenuata is not typically cultivated for food but is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
