Leighia uniflora

Leighia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces a single, delicate flower that is typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
