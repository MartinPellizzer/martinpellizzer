Laricorchis Rigida

Laricorchis rigida is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often growing in association with coniferous forests. This plant is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring dense, grass-like leaves and striking, spurred flowers. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Studying Laricorchis rigida offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and the importance of preserving biodiversity.
