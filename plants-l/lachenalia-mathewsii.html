Lachenalia mathewsii

Lachenalia mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews' lachenalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
