Lobelia mearnsii

Lobelia mearnsii, commonly known as Mearns' lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia mearnsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
