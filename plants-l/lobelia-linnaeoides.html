Lobelia linnaeoides

Lobelia linnaeoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia linnaeoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
