Leucas suffruticosa

Leucas suffruticosa, commonly known as Indian borage, is a flowering plant native to South Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features hairy, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

It is often found in moist, open habitats such as fields and forest edges.
