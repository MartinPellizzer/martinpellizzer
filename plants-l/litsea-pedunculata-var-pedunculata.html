Litsea pedunculata var. pedunculata

Litsea pedunculata var. pedunculata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
