Listrostachys gabonensis

Listrostachys gabonensis, commonly known as the Gabon bristlepod, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bristled pods. The plant typically grows in moist, forested areas and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, yellowish flowers.
