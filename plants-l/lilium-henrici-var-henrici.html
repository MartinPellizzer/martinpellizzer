Lilium henrici var. henrici

Lilium henrici var. henrici, commonly known as the Henrici lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
