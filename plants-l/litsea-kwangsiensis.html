Litsea kwangsiensis

Litsea kwangsiensis, commonly known as Guangxi litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color.

Litsea kwangsiensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
