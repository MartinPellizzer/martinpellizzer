Lachnostachys verbascifolia var. paniculata

Lachnostachys verbascifolia var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicled mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, erect stems covered in soft, velvety leaves that are gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their mild laxative properties.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
