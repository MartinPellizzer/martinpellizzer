Leucas alluaudii

Leucas alluaudii, commonly known as the Madagascar leucas, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in dry, rocky areas and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat.

Leucas alluaudii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
