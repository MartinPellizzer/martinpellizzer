Lasianthus taiheizanensis

Lasianthus taiheizanensis, commonly known as the Taiheizan hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Japan, specifically the mountainous regions of Taiheizan. The plant is characterized by its showy, pentagonal flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface depending on the variety.
