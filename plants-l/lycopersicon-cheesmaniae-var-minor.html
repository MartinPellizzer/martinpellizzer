Lycopersicon cheesmaniae var. minor

Lycopersicon cheesmaniae var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf cherry tomato, is a compact variety of tomato plant native to Mexico.

It features small, round fruits that are typically red when ripe, though some cultivars may exhibit yellow or orange hues. The plant has a bushy growth habit with hairy leaves and stems, typical of the Solanaceae family. It is valued for its early maturity and is often grown in home gardens for its flavorful, juicy fruits.

Due to its size, it is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces.
