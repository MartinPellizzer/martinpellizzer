Lepachys peduncularis var. picta

Lepachys peduncularis var. picta, commonly known as the painted pincushion, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The leaves are typically green with red or purple markings, adding to its ornamental appeal. The flowers, which appear in clusters, are usually yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
