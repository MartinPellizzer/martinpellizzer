Lamourouxia parayana

Lamourouxia parayana, commonly known as the Parayana orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.
