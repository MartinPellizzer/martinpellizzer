Laportea Cuneata

Laportea cuneata is a unique plant native to the southeastern United States. It is commonly known as the arrowleaf or arrowhead plant due to its distinctive leaf shape. This herbaceous perennial thrives in moist, shady environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture. Laportea cuneata is not only an ornamental plant but also plays an important role in local ecosystems.
