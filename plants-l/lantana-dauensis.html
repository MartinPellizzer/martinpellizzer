Lantana dauensis

Lantana dauensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese lantana, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly rough texture.

Lantana dauensis is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
