Larryleachia cactiformis var. cactiformis

Larryleachia cactiformis var. cactiformis, commonly known as the Cactiform Cactus, is a unique succulent plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features a cylindrical stem that resembles a cactus, covered in spiny, tuberculate structures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but striking. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and adapting it to arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
