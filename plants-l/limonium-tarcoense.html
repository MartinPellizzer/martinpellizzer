Limonium tarcoense

Limonium tarcoense, commonly known as the Tarcoan sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant is often found in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers bloom in summer.

Limonium tarcoense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
