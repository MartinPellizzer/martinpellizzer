Lobelia simsii

Lobelia simsii, commonly known as the Cape lobelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
