Liriope graminifolia var. praealba

Liriope graminifolia var. praealba, commonly known as the white lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern North America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and grow in dense clumps. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded areas.

Liriope graminifolia var. praealba is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
