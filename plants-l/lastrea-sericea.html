Lastrea sericea

Lastrea sericea, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It has a slow growth rate and is known for its longevity, sometimes living for several decades.

The silver leaf tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
