Lycopersicon esculentum provar. subviride

Lycopersicon esculentum provar. subviride, commonly known as the green tomato plant, is a variant of the domesticated tomato species.

It is characterized by its foliage that tends to have a lighter green hue compared to the standard tomato plant. The leaves are typically larger and more elongated, giving the plant a distinct appearance. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value rather than for fruit production.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
