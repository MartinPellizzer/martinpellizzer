Lens montbretii

Lens montbretii, commonly known as the Montbretii lens, is a species of legume native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds that are often used in culinary applications. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white, and it produces pods that contain the edible seeds.

Lens montbretii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
