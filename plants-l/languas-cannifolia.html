Languas cannifolia

Languas cannifolia, commonly known as the cannabidiol plant, is a flowering plant in the Cannabaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small greenish-white flowers that develop into spherical, woody fruits containing seeds. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its chemical composition, it has attracted interest for both medicinal and industrial applications.
