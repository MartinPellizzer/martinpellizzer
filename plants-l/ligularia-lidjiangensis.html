Ligularia lidjiangensis

Ligularia lidjiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a disc-like cluster, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
