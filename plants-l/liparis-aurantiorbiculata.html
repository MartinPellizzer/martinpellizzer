Liparis aurantiorbiculata

Liparis aurantiorbiculata, commonly known as the orange-eyed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers with a bright orange lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant coloration make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
