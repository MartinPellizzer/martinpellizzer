Limonium braunii

Limonium braunii, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores. Limonium braunii is a perennial species that blooms in summer, adding vibrant color to coastal landscapes.

Its ability to tolerate harsh maritime conditions makes it an important part of coastal flora.
