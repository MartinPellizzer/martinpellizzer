Lepidium angustissimum

Lepidium angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf rocket, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, green, edible leaves that are often used in culinary applications. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Lepidium angustissimum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
