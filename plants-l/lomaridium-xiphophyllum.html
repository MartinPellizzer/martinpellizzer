Lomaridium xiphophyllum

Lomaridium xiphophyllum, commonly known as the swordleaf lomaridium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.
