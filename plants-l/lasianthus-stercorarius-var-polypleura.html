Lasianthus stercorarius var. polypleura

Lasianthus stercorarius var. polypleura, commonly known as the broad-leafed stercorarius, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are often violet or purple in color, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
