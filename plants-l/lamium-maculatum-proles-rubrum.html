Lamium maculatum proles rubrum

Lamium maculatum proles rubrum, commonly known as the red-veined sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins, which give it its striking appearance. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a colorful, low-maintenance plant.
