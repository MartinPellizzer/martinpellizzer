Limonium minutum

Limonium minutum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in sandy or saline soils. It is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

Limonium minutum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
