Lisianthius ovatifolius

Lisianthius ovatifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaf lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its ovate, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
