Lonicera tomentella var. conaensis

Lonicera tomentella var. conaensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and natural habitats.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
