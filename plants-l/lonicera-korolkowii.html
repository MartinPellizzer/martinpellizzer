Lonicera korolkowii

Lonicera korolkowii, commonly known as the korolkowii honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early summer. Its berries are red and edible, though not commonly consumed by humans.

Lonicera korolkowii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
