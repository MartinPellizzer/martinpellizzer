Lycaste × victoriarum

Lycaste × victoriarum, commonly known as the Victoria lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
