Laelia × schilleriana var. splendens

Laelia × schilleriana var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its large, fragrant blooms that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other surfaces in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its botanical features include pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients, and long, strap-like leaves that support its growth.
