Lobelia reflexa

Lobelia reflexa, commonly known as the nodding lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches. The plant features whorled leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The genus Lobelia is known for its showy flowers and medicinal properties, though Lobelia reflexa is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal.
