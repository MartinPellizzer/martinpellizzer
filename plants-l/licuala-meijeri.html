Licuala meijeri

Licuala meijeri, commonly known as the golden palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are often a striking golden-yellow color. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family and is typically found in humid, lowland areas. Its leaves are broad and can grow up to several feet in diameter, making it a visually dramatic plant.

Licuala meijeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
