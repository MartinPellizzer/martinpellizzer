Laburnum rochelii

Laburnum rochelii, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in spring. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Laburnum rochelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soils.
