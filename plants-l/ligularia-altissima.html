Ligularia altissima

Ligularia altissima, commonly known as the giant ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often with a distinctive reddish or purple margin. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are arranged in a spire-like cluster. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Due to its impressive foliage and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic statement plant.
