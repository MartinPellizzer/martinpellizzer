Lepanthes guerrerensis

Lepanthes guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrero orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Lepanthes guerrerensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
