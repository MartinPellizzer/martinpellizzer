Ligularia fischeri var. fischeri

Ligularia fischeri var. fischeri, commonly known as the giant cowleaf, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage is notable for its striking coloration and texture, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
