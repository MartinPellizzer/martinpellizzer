Lobostemon viridiargenteus

Lobostemon viridiargenteus, commonly known as the silver-green lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale green to yellowish-white. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its foliage rather than its flowers.

Lobostemon viridiargenteus prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor or greenhouse cultivation.
