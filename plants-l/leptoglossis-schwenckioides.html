Leptoglossis schwenckioides

Leptoglossis schwenckioides, commonly known as the Schwenck's gnat orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with a unique structure, featuring elongated petals and a specialized labellum. It is typically found growing on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
