Lepidium affghanum

Lepidium affghanum, commonly known as Afghan wallcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its seed pods are elongated and contain numerous small, rounded seeds.

Lepidium affghanum is native to regions of Central and South Asia, where it thrives in dry, mountainous areas.
