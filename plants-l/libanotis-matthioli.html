Libanotis matthioli

Libanotis matthioli, commonly known as the Mediterranean bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a purple or pink throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it adapts well to dry conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
