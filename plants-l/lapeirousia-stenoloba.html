Lapeirousia stenoloba

Lapeirousia stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the winter months, making it a rare and sought-after ornamental plant.
