Lepidium montanum subsp. tenellum

Lepidium montanum subsp.

tenellum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It typically features small, yellowish or greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are often narrow and linear.

The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Lepidium montanum subsp.

tenellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
