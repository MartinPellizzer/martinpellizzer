Lastrea subdimorpha

Lastrea subdimorpha, commonly known as the Cape rose or coastal rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water conservation.

It is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.
