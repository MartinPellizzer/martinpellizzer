Liparis tripartita

Liparis tripartita, commonly known as the Three-lobed Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests or rocky crevices. Its roots are often tuberous, which help it store moisture and nutrients.

Liparis tripartita is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
