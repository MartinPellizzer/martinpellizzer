Lunania dentata

Lunania dentata, commonly known as the toothed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has toothed leaves and often grows in rocky or sandy soils. It typically blooms in the summer months and is often found in coastal areas.

Lunania dentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
