Licuala malajana

Licuala malajana, commonly known as the Malayan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as rainforests and swamps. Its trunk is short and robust, often covered with a fibrous sheath.

The species is valued for its ornamental foliage and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
