Lophiaris × haematochila

Lophiaris × haematochila, commonly known as the blood-flowered lophiaris, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of succulents. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries for its vibrant floral display.
