Leucojum hyemale var. flore-roseum

Leucojum hyemale var. flore-roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered snowflake, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Europe.

It produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from the base of the bulb. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation in garden settings.
