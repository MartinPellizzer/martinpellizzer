Lastrea melanopus

Lastrea melanopus, commonly known as the black starfish plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
