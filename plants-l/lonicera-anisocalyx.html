Lonicera anisocalyx

Lonicera anisocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are red berries that mature in the fall and are edible for birds and some mammals.

Lonicera anisocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
