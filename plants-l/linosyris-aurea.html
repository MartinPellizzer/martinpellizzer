Linosyris aurea

Linosyris aurea, commonly known as the golden linosyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Linosyris aurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
