Lycianthes medusocalyx

Lycianthes medusocalyx, commonly known as the Medusa calyx tomato, is a species native to Central America.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by orange to red berries containing several seeds. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and the stems are hairy.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in some traditional medicinal practices.
