Leonurus wutaishanicus

Leonurus wutaishanicus, commonly known as the Wutaishan horse-leek, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small purple or pink flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
