Lacistema lucidum

Lacistema lucidum, commonly known as the shining nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.
