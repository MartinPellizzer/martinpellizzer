Lithocardium corymbosum var. lineatum

Lithocardium corymbosum var. lineatum, commonly known as the lined lithocardium, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and features narrow, linear leaves that are often variegated with white or pale green markings. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
