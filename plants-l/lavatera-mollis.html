Lavatera mollis

Lavatera mollis, commonly known as the common lavatera, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture with a slight pubescence.

Lavatera mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its visual impact.
