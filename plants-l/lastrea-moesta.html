Lastrea moesta

Lastrea moesta, commonly known as the wandering jew, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
