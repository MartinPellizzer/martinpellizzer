Lobelia oreas

Lobelia oreas, commonly known as the alpine lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Appalachian Mountains.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often in clusters. Lobelia oreas is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable component of alpine ecosystems.
