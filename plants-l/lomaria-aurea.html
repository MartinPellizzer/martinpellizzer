Lomaria aurea

Lomaria aurea, commonly known as the golden lomaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat woody.

Lomaria aurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
