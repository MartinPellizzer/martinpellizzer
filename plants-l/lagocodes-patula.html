Lagocodes patula

Lagocodes patula, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically silver or grayish in appearance, covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature, attracting various bird species.

Lagocodes patula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
