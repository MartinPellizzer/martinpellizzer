Leucadendron tomentosum

Leucadendron tomentosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
