Litsea longipes

Litsea longipes, commonly known as the long-peduncled litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. Litsea longipes is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
