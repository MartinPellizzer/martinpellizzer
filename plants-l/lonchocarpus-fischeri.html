Lonchocarpus fischeri

Lonchocarpus fischeri, commonly known as Fischer's legume, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant is notable for its striking yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its seeds are enclosed in hard, woody pods, which contribute to its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
