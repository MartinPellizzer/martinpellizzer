Lobelia fervens

Lobelia fervens, commonly known as the fiery lobelia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wetland restoration projects.
