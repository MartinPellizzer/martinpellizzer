Lennea modesta

Lennea modesta, commonly known as the slender sandmat, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit with narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in summer. Its succulent leaves and stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

Lennea modesta is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and limited rainfall.
