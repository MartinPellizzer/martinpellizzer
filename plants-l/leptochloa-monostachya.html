Leptochloa monostachya

Leptochloa monostachya, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces a single, slender spike of tiny flowers, which is typical of its genus. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Leptochloa monostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.
