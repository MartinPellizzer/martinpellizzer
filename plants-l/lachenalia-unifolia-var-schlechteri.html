Lachenalia unifolia var. schlechteri

Lachenalia unifolia var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's single-flowered lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a single, upright flower stem that bears a cluster of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
