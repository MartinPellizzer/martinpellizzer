Lourea Obcordata

Lourea obcordata is a striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to South America, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with high humidity and warm temperatures. Its unique heart-shaped leaves and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal, it requires well-drained soil and indirect sunlight to flourish. This plant not only adds beauty to gardens but also plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
