Lathyrus pannonicus subsp. varius

Lathyrus pannonicus subsp.

varius, commonly known as the variable vetch, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Europe. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are an important food source for various wildlife species.
