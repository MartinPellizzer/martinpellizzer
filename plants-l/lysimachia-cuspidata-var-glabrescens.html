Lysimachia cuspidata var. glabrescens

Lysimachia cuspidata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
