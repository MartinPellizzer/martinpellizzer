Langloisia

Langloisia, commonly known as the bluebell of the genus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and riverbanks. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often forming a dense basal rosette.
