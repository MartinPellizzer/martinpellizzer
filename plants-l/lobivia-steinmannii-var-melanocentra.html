Lobivia steinmannii var. melanocentra

Lobivia steinmannii var. melanocentra, commonly known as the Black Centered Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, cylindrical stem with a dark, almost black center, contrasting with its lighter green or blue-gray outer surface. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
