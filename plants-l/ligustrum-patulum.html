Ligustrum patulum

Ligustrum patulum, commonly known as the broadleaf privet, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum patulum is also used in horticulture for hedging and as a foundation plant.
