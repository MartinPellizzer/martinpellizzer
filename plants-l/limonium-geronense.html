Limonium geronense

Limonium geronense, commonly known as Geronese sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Limonium geronense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
