Lophosoria frigida

Lophosoria frigida, commonly known as the cold-loving orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a loose cluster, often blooming in late summer or early autumn. Lophosoria frigida is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.
