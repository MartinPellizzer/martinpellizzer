Lagerstroemia floribunda var. floribunda

Lagerstroemia floribunda var. floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that range in color from red to orange, which appear before the actual small white flowers. The plant has smooth, grayish-brown bark that peels in thin layers, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions and its seasonal display of vibrant foliage.
