Lasiorrhiza menana

Lasiorrhiza menana, commonly known as the red sand ratplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its succulent species adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are fleshy and covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
