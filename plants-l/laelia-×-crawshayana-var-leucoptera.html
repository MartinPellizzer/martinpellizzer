Laelia × crawshayana var. leucoptera

Laelia × crawshayana var. leucoptera, commonly known as the white-winged laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescences and large, showy flowers with a distinctive white-veined pattern. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs and is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that help it retain moisture.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
