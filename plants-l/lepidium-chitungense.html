Lepidium chitungense

Lepidium chitungense, commonly known as the Chitungwe cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and economically important plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and its finely divided, fern-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.
