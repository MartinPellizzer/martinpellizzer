Lilium brownii var. odorum

Lilium brownii var. odorum, commonly known as the Chinese lily or the fragrant lily, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
