Leptochilus heteroclitus var. linnaeanus

Leptochilus heteroclitus var. linnaeanus, commonly known as the Linnaean sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

The plant belongs to the family Asteraceae and is known for its ability to thrive in coastal dunes and rocky shores.
