Lisianthius obtusifolius var. constrictus

Lisianthius obtusifolius var. constrictus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly curled, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid conditions.
