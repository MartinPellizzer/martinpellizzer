Lobularia maritima var. rosula-venti

Lobularia maritima var. rosula-venti, commonly known as the coastal lobularia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to tolerate salty air and sandy soils. It thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky shores.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal ecosystems.
