Lotus bollei var. argenteus

Lotus bollei var. argenteus, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, waxy leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
