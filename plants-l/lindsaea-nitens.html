Lindsaea nitens

Lindsaea nitens, commonly known as the slender lindsaea, is a member of the fern family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, tropical environments. The plant has delicate, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and produce spores for propagation.

Lindsaea nitens is often found in shaded, moist areas of rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
