Lyonia rubiginosa subsp. stahlii

Lyonia rubiginosa subsp.

stahlii, commonly known as the Stahli's lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.

The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of local ecosystems and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
