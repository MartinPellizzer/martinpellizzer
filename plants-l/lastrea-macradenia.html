Lastrea macradenia

Lastrea macradenia, commonly known as the Cape fynbos, is a flowering plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in nutrient-poor, well-drained soils typical of fynbos vegetation.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the region's unique biodiversity.
