Listera ovata f. densiflora

Listera ovata f. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered basket lily, is a horticultural form of the basket lily species.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is well-suited for woodland gardens and prefers moist, shaded environments.
