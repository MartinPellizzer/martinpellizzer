Linum medium var. texanum

Linum medium var. texanum, commonly known as Texas flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in dry, open habitats.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators and are used in native plant gardens for their ornamental value.
