Lygodesmia pumila

Lygodesmia pumila, commonly known as the small leaved lygodesmia, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas or on rocky substrates.

Lygodesmia pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its dense, compact growth habit.
