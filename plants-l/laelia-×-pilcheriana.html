Laelia × pilcheriana

Laelia × pilcheriana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceola.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in cool, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

The hybrid is known for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
