Lychnophora diamantinana

Lychnophora diamantinana, commonly known as diamondleaf or Brazilian diamondleaf, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple.

Lychnophora diamantinana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
