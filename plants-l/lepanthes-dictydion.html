Lepanthes dictydion

Lepanthes dictydion, commonly known as the silver-tipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a delicate appearance, featuring a slender stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellowish-green lip that sets it apart from other orchid species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable plant in botanical collections.
