Lantana lundiana

Lantana lundiana, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
