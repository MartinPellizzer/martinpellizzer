Leiothrix argyroderma

Leiothrix argyroderma, commonly known as the silver-leafed fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive silvery-white undersides on its leaves.

The plant features delicate, feather-like fronds that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, contributing to its unique appearance.
