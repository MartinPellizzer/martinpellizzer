Lepachys columnaris var. tagetes

Lepachys columnaris var. tagetes, commonly known as the Mexican flame vine, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in long, cascading clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

This variety is often used in hanging baskets or as a climbing plant due to its vigorous growth habit.
