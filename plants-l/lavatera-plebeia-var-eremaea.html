Lavatera plebeia var. eremaea

Lavatera plebeia var. eremaea, commonly known as the desert lavatera, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southwestern Australia.

It features soft, woolly leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to poor soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
