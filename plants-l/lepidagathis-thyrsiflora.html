Lepidagathis thyrsiflora

Lepidagathis thyrsiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of yellow flowers, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
