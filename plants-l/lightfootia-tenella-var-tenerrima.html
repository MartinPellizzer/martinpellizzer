Lightfootia tenella var. tenerrima

Lightfootia tenella var. tenerrima, commonly known as the silver mohair plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The leaves are typically silver-gray in color, with a fuzzy texture that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
