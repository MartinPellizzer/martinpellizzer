Leucas oligocephala subsp. tenuifolia

Leucas oligocephala subsp.

tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved leucas, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grassy areas and rocky soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink, blooming in spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
