Littaea rezlii

Littaea rezlii, commonly known as the Rezlii's rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, evergreen leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for coastal landscapes.

Littaea rezlii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid climates.
