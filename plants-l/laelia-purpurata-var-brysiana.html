Laelia purpurata var. brysiana

Laelia purpurata var. brysiana, commonly known as the Brysiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink floral displays, which are highly prized in horticulture. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous flower spikes that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
