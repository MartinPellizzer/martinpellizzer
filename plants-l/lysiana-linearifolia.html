Lysiana linearifolia

Lysiana linearifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and bloom in early spring.

Lysiana linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
