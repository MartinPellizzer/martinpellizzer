Limonia leptostachya

Limonia leptostachya, commonly known as the sour orange or Indian lime, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are typically sour but can be used in culinary applications. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Limonia leptostachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils.
