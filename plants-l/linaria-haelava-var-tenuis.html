Linaria haelava var. tenuis

Linaria haelava var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
