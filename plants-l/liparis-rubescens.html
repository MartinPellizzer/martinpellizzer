Liparis rubescens

Liparis rubescens, commonly known as the red orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and green flower spikes, which often feature intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually small but numerous, forming dense clusters along the stem.

Liparis rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
