Lactuca brevirostris

Lactuca brevirostris, commonly known as the short-nosed lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its upright growth habit and pale green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Lactuca brevirostris is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
