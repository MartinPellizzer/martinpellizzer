Lasiobema championii

Lasiobema championii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The botanical features of Lasiobema championii make it well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates.
