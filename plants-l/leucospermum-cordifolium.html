Leucospermum cordifolium

Leucospermum cordifolium, commonly known as the firewheel plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red, flame-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in the Proteaceae family.
