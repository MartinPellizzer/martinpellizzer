Lissochilus anjoanensis

Lissochilus anjoanensis, commonly known as the Anjoanensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and growth habits. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. It features slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
