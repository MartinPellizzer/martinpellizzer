Lactuca spicata var. multifida

Lactuca spicata var. multifida, commonly known as the curly endive, is a cultivated variety of the lettuce genus Lactuca.

It is characterized by its crisp, dark green leaves that are curled and fringed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is typically grown for its edible leaves, which have a slightly bitter taste. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often used in salads and as a garnish due to its unique texture and flavor.
