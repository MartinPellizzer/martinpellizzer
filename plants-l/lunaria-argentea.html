Lunaria argentea

Lunaria argentea, commonly known as silver moons.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its biennial growth habit and distinctive seed pods. The plant produces spherical, translucent seed pods that turn from green to white as they mature. These pods contain small, black seeds that are often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Lunaria argentea is native to Europe and western Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
