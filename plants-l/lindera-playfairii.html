Lindera playfairii

Lindera playfairii, commonly known as Chinese lantern bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, orange-red berries that resemble lanterns, hence its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a fragrant, aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Lindera playfairii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
