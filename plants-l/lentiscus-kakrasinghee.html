Lentiscus kakrasinghee

Lentiscus kakrasinghee, commonly known as the Indian mastic tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It is often found in dry, rocky areas and is valued for its aromatic resins and medicinal properties.
