Lysiphyllum winitii

Lysiphyllum winitii, commonly known as the Chinese sea holly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are composed of ray florets and disk florets.

Lysiphyllum winitii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
