Lochmocydia ramentacea

Lochmocydia ramentacea, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
