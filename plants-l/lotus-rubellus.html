Lotus rubellus

Lotus rubellus, commonly known as the red lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

The red lotus is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in various cultures.
