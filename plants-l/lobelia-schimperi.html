Lobelia schimperi

Lobelia schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
