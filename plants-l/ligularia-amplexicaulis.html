Ligularia amplexicaulis

Ligularia amplexicaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clustered inflorescences during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to wetland gardens and bog landscapes.
