Limonium reticulatum

Limonium reticulatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the western Pacific.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. Limonium reticulatum is a perennial species that typically blooms in summer, adding vibrant color to coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.
