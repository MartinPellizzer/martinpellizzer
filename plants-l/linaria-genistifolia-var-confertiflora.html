Linaria genistifolia var. confertiflora

Linaria genistifolia var. confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered yellow flower.

This variant is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe, characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of bright yellow, spurred flowers. The leaves are linear and slightly hairy, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and easy care requirements.
