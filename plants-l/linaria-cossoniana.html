Linaria cossoniana

Linaria cossoniana, commonly known as the Cosson's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Linaria cossoniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
