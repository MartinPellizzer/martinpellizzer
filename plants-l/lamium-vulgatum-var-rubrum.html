Lamium vulgatum var. rubrum

Lamium vulgatum var. rubrum, commonly known as red deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple-tinted, especially along the veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers with two lips, typically in shades of pink or red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its attractive foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
