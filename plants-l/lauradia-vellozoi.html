Lauradia vellozoi

Lauradia vellozoi, commonly known as the Vellozoi laurel, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and avocado. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, round berries that are typically dark blue or black when mature.

Lauradia vellozoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
