Lasinia reticulata

Lasinia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features feathery, finely divided fronds that are often covered in a network of fine veins, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is valued for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
