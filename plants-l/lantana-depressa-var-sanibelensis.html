Lantana depressa var. sanibelensis

Lantana depressa var. sanibelensis, commonly known as Sanibel lantana, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Florida and the Caribbean.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils, often found in coastal dunes and wetlands. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Sanibel lantana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
