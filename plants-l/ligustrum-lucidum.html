Ligustrum lucidum

Ligustrum lucidum, commonly known as glossy privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum lucidum is also valued for its ability to thrive in urban environments.
