Lonicera splendens

Lonicera splendens, commonly known as the golden honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it suitable for various garden settings.
