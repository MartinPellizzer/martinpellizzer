Lightfootia indica

Lightfootia indica, commonly known as the Indian lightfoot.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, pale green leaves. It produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Lightfootia indica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
