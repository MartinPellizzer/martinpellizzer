Lippia fruticosa

Lippia fruticosa, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small blue or purple flowers. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.

Lippia fruticosa is also used in traditional herbal remedies for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
