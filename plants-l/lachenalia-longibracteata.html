Lachenalia longibracteata

Lachenalia longibracteata, commonly known as the Longibracteate Lachenalia, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, grass-like foliage and produces tall, branched stems with clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers. The flowers typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, or red, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. This species is well-suited to rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique morphology and colorful blooms make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
