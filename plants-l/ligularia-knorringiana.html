Ligularia knorringiana

Ligularia knorringiana, commonly known as the Himalayan ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
