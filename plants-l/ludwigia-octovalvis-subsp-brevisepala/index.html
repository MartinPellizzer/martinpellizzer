Ludwigia Octovalvis Subsp. Brevisepala

Ludwigia octovalvis subsp. brevisepala is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking red and green coloration. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in the Amazon basin. This subspecies is prized by aquarium hobbyists for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for foreground planting in planted tanks. With proper lighting and nutrients, it can flourish and enhance the visual appeal of any aquatic setup.
