Ligularia cremanthodioides

Ligularia cremanthodioides, commonly known as the cremanthodioides ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence, which attracts various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
