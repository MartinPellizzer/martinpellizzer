Ligularia jacquemontiana

Ligularia jacquemontiana, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive white margin, and it produces yellow-orange flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas. It belongs to the Araceae family and is valued for its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.

Ligularia jacquemontiana is a hardy plant that can grow up to 2 meters tall, making it a striking addition to aquatic or bog gardens.
