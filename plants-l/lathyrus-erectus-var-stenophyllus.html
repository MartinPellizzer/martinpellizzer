Lathyrus erectus var. stenophyllus

Lathyrus erectus var. stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pea, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its pods contain edible seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
