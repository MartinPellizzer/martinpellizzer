Ligusticum pimpinellifolium

Ligusticum pimpinellifolium, commonly known as scarlet eggplant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or purple in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

However, it is not commonly used for culinary purposes.
