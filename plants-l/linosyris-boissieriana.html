Linosyris boissieriana

Linosyris boissieriana, commonly known as the Boissier's linosyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and is adapted to rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as slopes and gravelly areas.

Linosyris boissieriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
