Lepanthes tungurahuae

Lepanthes tungurahuae, commonly known as the Tungurahua orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns on their petals. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive greenish or yellowish lip.

Lepanthes tungurahuae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
