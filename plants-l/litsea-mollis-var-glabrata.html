Litsea mollis var. glabrata

Litsea mollis var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth litsea, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, smooth-textured leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.
