Linum humile

Linum humile, commonly known as small flax, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It produces delicate blue or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Linum humile is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky soils.
