Lactuca viminea subsp. viminea

Lactuca viminea subsp.

viminea, commonly known as slender lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Compositae). It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are followed by small, fluffy seeds. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
