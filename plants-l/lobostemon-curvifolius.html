Lobostemon curvifolius

Lobostemon curvifolius, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiraling leaves that form a compact rosette shape. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Lobostemon curvifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
