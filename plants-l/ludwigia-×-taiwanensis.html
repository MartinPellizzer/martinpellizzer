Ludwigia × taiwanensis

Ludwigia × taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan Ludwigia, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange coloration, which becomes more intense under strong lighting and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Ludwigia × taiwanensis is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ease of care.
