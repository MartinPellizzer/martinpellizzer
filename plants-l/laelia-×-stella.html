Laelia × stella

Laelia × stella, commonly known as the Stella orchid, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant features compact, sympodial growth with dense clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces long, arching inflorescences with numerous blooms.

Laelia × stella is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
