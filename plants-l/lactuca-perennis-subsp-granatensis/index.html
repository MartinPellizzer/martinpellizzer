Lactuca Perennis Subsp. Granatensis

Lactuca perennis subsp. granatensis is a rare and lesser-known member of the daisy family. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is characterized by its compact growth and striking blue to purple flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Understanding its ecology and cultivation can help preserve this unique species.
