Lindera bokorensis

Lindera bokorensis, commonly known as the Bokorensis spicebush, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are small, dark berries that mature in the fall and are an important food source for local wildlife.

Lindera bokorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
