Limonium nogalense

Limonium nogalense, commonly known as the Galician sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Limonium nogalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in coastal landscaping.
