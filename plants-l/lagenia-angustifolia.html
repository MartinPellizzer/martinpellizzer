Lagenia angustifolia

Lagenia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated seed pods that turn red as they mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Lagenia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
