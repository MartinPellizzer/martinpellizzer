Lactuca prenanthoides

Lactuca prenanthoides, commonly known as the slender lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has a short life cycle and is often found in disturbed soils.
