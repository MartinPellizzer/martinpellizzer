Ludia erosifolia

Ludia erosifolia, commonly known as the sharpleaf larkspur, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that have a serrated edge. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the cooler months.

Ludia erosifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
