Lellingeria prionodes

Lellingeria prionodes, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in forest understories and along stream banks.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
