Laelia × irrorata var. gaskelliana

Laelia × irrorata var. gaskelliana, commonly known as the Gaskell's laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are broad, dark green, and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
