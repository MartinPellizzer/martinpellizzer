Lepanthes anisoloba

Lepanthes anisoloba, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Lepanthes anisoloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
