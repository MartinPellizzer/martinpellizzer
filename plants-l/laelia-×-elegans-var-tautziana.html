Laelia × elegans var. tautziana

Laelia × elegans var. tautziana, commonly known as the Tautziana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a combination of traits from its parent species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to the humid highlands of South America, where it thrives in shaded, well-drained environments. Its pseudobulbs are robust, and it produces multiple inflorescences throughout the growing season.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
