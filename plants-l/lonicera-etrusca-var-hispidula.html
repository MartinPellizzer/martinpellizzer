Lonicera etrusca var. hispidula

Lonicera etrusca var. hispidula, commonly known as the Tuscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. Its red berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky areas.
