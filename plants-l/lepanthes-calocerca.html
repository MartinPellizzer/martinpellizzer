Lepanthes calocerca

Lepanthes calocerca, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often display vibrant hues of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Lepanthes calocerca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
