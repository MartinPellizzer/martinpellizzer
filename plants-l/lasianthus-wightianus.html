Lasianthus wightianus

Lasianthus wightianus, commonly known as the Wight's lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth surface and a pointed tip.
