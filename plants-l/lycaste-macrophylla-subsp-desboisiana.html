Lycaste macrophylla subsp. desboisiana

Lycaste macrophylla subsp.

desboisiana, commonly known as the Desboisiana lycaste, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Mexico. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

The plant has thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing structural support and aiding in water retention.

This subspecies is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
