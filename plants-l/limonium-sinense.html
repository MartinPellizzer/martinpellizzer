Limonium sinense

Limonium sinense, commonly known as Chinese sea holly, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Plumbagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Limonium sinense thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its cultivation in various horticultural settings.
