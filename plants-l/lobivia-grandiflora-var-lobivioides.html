Lobivia grandiflora var. lobivioides

Lobivia grandiflora var. lobivioides, commonly known as the Lobivia-like Lobivia, is a compact, spineless cactus native to central South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring. The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to well-drained, rocky soils. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
