Lepanthes pseudomucronata

Lepanthes pseudomucronata, commonly known as the narrow-lobed slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes pseudomucronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
