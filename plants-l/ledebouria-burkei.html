Ledebouria burkei

Ledebouria burkei, commonly known as the Burke's bulbous lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous base and strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
