Lyperia integerrima

Lyperia integerrima, commonly known as the rigid lyperia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellowish.

Lyperia integerrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid conditions.
