Lycopersicon commutatum

Lycopersicon commutatum, commonly known as the yellow tomato, is a species of nightshade plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its yellow or orange fruit, which typically ripens to a deep red color. The plant produces small, hairy leaves and has a sprawling growth habit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.

However, it is less commonly grown for commercial tomato production compared to other tomato species.
