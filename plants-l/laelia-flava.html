Laelia flava

Laelia flava, commonly known as the yellow laelia, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and strap-like leaves. This orchid thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and ample sunlight. It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.

Laelia flava is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
