Lantana camara

Lantana camara, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are often aromatic. Lantana camara is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is also noted for its invasive potential in many regions outside its native range.
