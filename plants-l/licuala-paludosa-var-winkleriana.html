Licuala paludosa var. winkleriana

Licuala paludosa var. winkleriana, commonly known as the swamp pandanus, is a variant of the swamp pandanus species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and swampy areas, where it can tolerate waterlogged soils. Its leaves are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside, with a rough texture.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in some regions.
