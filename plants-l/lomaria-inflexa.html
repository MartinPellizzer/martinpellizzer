Lomaria inflexa

Lomaria inflexa, commonly known as the narrowleaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in racemes, often displaying shades of pink or white.

Lomaria inflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
