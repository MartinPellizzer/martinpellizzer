Lambertia formosa

Lambertia formosa, commonly known as the yellow lambertia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in dense, spherical clusters during the summer months. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Lambertia formosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like birds and bees.
