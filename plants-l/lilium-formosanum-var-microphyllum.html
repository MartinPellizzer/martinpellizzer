Lilium formosanum var. microphyllum

Lilium formosanum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the Formosan dwarf lily, is a rare and delicate species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its compact size and unique floral structure make it a distinctive member of the Liliaceae family.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
