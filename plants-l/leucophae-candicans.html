Leucophae candicans

Leucophae candicans, commonly known as the candlelight orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique, candle-like inflorescence.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. The plant features elongated, white flowers with a subtle glow, which is due to the reflective properties of its petals. It thrives in warm, moist environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
