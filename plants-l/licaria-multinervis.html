Licaria multinervis

Licaria multinervis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Licaria multinervis is often found in moist, shaded environments in tropical and subtropical regions.
