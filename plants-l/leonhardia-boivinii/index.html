Leonhardia Boivinii

Leonhardia boivinii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the highlands of Ecuador. It is known for its striking white petals and unique, star-shaped blooms that appear in late summer. This species thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this beautiful and rare botanical gem.
