Ligularia sagitta

Ligularia sagitta, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of yellow or orange.

Ligularia sagitta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden areas or as a houseplant.
