Lipschitziella auriculata

Lipschitziella auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate lipschitziella, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive auriculate base, giving it its common name.
