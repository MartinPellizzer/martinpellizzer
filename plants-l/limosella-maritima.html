Limosella maritima

Limosella maritima, commonly known as sea sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in dunes and tidal zones. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with four petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Limosella maritima is an important species for stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
