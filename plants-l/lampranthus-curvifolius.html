Lampranthus curvifolius

Lampranthus curvifolius, commonly known as the zigzag firecracker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and vibrant colors make it a striking addition to any landscape.
