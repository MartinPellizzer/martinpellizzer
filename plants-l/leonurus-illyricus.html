Leonurus illyricus

Leonurus illyricus, commonly known as the lesser hedgehog plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color, arranged in whorls along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Leonurus illyricus is often found in dry, rocky soils and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
