Liparia tecta

Liparia tecta, commonly known as the woolly lipara, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale purple.

Liparia tecta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
