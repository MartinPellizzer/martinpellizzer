Lotus holosericeus

Lotus holosericeus, commonly known as the velvet lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple petals and a soft, velvety texture on its leaves and flowers. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its unique, recurved petals that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The lotus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in various Asian traditions.
