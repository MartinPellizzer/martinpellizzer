Lonicera vaccinium

Lonicera vaccinium, commonly known as the common honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in a variety of soil types and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lonicera vaccinium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and hedgerows.
