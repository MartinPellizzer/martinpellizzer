Logfia Heterantha

Bluebell, scientifically known as Logfia heterantha, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. With its delicate, bell-shaped blooms that range from pale blue to deep violet, it is a popular ornamental species in gardens and natural habitats. This plant thrives in woodland environments, often carpeting the forest floor with a vibrant display of color in spring. Its unique structure and elegant appearance make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Logfia heterantha is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity.
