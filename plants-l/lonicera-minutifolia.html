Lonicera minutifolia

Lonicera minutifolia, commonly known as the dwarf honeysuckle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and white. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Lonicera minutifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
