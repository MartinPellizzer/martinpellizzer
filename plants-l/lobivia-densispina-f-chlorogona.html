Lobivia densispina f. chlorogona

Lobivia densispina f. chlorogona, commonly known as the chlorogona form of the dense-spined lobivia, is a rare and striking cactus native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of bright green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts in controlled environments.
