Licuala mustapana

Licuala mustapana, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as a natural fiber source.

The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
