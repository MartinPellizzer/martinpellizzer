Lobostemon stachydeus

Lobostemon stachydeus, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. It typically grows in dry, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
