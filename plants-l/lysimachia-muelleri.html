Lysimachia muelleri

Lysimachia muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Australia.

It features small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has slender, creeping stems and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Lysimachia muelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native garden landscapes.
