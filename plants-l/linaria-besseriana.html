Linaria besseriana

Linaria besseriana, commonly known as the Balkan toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features slender stems and clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant adaptations.

Linaria besseriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.
