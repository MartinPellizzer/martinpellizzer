Lobelia sulawesiensis

Lobelia sulawesiensis, commonly known as the Sulawesi lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories, and thrives in humid tropical climates. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture.
