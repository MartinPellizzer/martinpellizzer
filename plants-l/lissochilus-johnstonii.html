Lissochilus johnstonii

Lissochilus johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
