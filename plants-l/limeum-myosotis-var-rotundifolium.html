Limeum myosotis var. rotundifolium

Limeum myosotis var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the rounded-leaved forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in clusters. The leaves are rounded and succulent, giving the plant a unique, almost fleshy appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming, delicate blossoms.
