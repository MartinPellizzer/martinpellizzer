Lathyrus variabilis f. ovalifolius

Lathyrus variabilis f. ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaf sweet pea, is a botanical variant of the sweet pea species.

It is characterized by its distinctive oval-shaped leaves, which set it apart from the typical lanceolate leaves of the species. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pea-like flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
