Lycopodioides Longicuspis

Lycopodioides longicuspis is a rare and ancient plant species belonging to the lycophyte group. It is characterized by its unique spore-bearing structures and primitive morphology. Found in specific, often remote habitats, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying this plant offers insights into early plant evolution and ecological adaptation.
