Leonotis Ebracteata

Leonotis ebracteata, also known as the red devil's bit, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red tubular flowers and hairy stems, making it a visually appealing addition to gardens. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in rocky or dry environments. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that it can be toxic to livestock and pets.
