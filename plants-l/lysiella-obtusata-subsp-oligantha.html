Lysiella obtusata subsp. oligantha

Lysiella obtusata subsp.

oligantha, commonly known as the slender lysiella, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and open woodlands.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and limited branching.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
