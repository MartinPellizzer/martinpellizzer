Lepanthes amicitiae

Lepanthes amicitiae, commonly known as the friendship orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Lepanthes amicitiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
