Leonotis longidens

Leonotis longidens, commonly known as the blanket flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

Leonotis longidens is also cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
