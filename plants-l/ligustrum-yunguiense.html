Ligustrum yunguiense

Ligustrum yunguiense, commonly known as the Yungui ligustrum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It is often found in moist, shady environments and can grow up to 3 meters tall.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warmer climates.
