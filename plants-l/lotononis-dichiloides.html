Lotononis dichiloides

Lotononis dichiloides, commonly known as the two-lobed lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
