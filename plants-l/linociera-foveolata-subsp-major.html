Linociera foveolata subsp. major

Linociera foveolata subsp.

major, commonly known as the large-flowered linociera, is a species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
