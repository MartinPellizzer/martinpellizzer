Lysimachia debilis

Lysimachia debilis, commonly known as slender yellow-eyed grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia debilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.
