Litosanthes pseudolucida

Litosanthes pseudolucida, commonly known as the glossy leaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with glossy, elliptical leaves that have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
