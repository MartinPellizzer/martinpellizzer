Liparis suborbicularis

Liparis suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are broad and oval, often with a waxy texture, and it has a short, sturdy rhizome.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
