Lemaireocereus marginatus var. gemmatus

Lemaireocereus marginatus var. gemmatus, commonly known as the Moon Cactus, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, globular stems that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
