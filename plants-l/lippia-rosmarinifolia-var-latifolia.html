Lippia rosmarinifolia var. latifolia

Lippia rosmarinifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf lippia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features aromatic, narrow to broad lance-shaped leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in ornamental gardening.

Its essential oils are valued for their aromatic and medicinal properties.
