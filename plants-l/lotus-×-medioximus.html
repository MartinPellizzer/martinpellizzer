Lotus × medioximus

Lotus × medioximus, commonly known as the hybrid lotus, is a cross between *Nelumbo nucifera* and *Nelumbo lutea*.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats, such as ponds and lakes, where it forms dense stands. Its leaves are large, circular, and float on the water surface, while its roots anchor in the mud.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in various parts of Asia for centuries.
