Lycaste colleyi

Lycaste colleyi, commonly known as the Colley's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with dark markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Lycaste colleyi is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
