Leucanthemum laciniatum

Leucanthemum laciniatum, commonly known as the Shining Leucanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy white to pale yellow flowers with a central yellow disk, surrounded by narrow, lobed leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is finely cut, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

It is often cultivated for its attractive blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
