Lycaste mezae

Lycaste mezae, commonly known as the Mezae lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lycaste mezae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
