Lantana cuneatifolia

Lantana cuneatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, orange, and red. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight serration along the edges. This plant is part of the Verbenaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
