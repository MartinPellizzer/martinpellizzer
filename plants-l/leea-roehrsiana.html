Leea roehrsiana

Leea roehrsiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its slender, evergreen shrub form. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.

Leea roehrsiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
