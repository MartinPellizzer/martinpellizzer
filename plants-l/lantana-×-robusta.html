Lantana × robusta

Lantana × robusta, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are usually lance-shaped. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Lantana × robusta is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
