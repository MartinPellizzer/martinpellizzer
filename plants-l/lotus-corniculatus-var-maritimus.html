Lotus corniculatus var. maritimus

Lotus corniculatus var. maritimus, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes and along shorelines. It is a hardy perennial that is well adapted to saline and windy conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and ecological restoration projects.
