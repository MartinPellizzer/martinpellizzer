Leonurus indicus

Leonurus indicus, commonly known as Indian hedgehog, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, clustered in whorls along the stems.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
