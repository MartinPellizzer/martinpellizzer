Litobrochia kleiniana

Litobrochia kleiniana, commonly known as the Klein's rock lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Litobrochia kleiniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
