Lindneria

Lindneria, commonly known as the glasswing fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, translucent leaves that resemble glass, giving it its common name. These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Central and South America. Lindneria species typically grow as herbaceous perennials, often found in moist, shaded environments.

They produce small, clustered flowers that are usually green or white in color.
