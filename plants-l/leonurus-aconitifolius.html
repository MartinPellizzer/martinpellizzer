Leonurus aconitifolius

Leonurus aconitifolius, commonly known as the lesser hedgehog urinary bladder, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, kidney-shaped leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found along roadsides or in dry meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
