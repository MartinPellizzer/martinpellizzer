Lactuca patersonii

Lactuca patersonii, commonly known as Paterson's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly California, and thrives in dry, rocky environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has a rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves. It is often found in chaparral and coastal sage scrub habitats.

Lactuca patersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
