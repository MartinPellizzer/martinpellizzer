Lepismium cruciforme var. cavernosum

Lepismium cruciforme var. cavernosum, commonly known as the cave nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually alternate and ovate, with a velvety texture.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
