Laccodiscus ferrugineus

Laccodiscus ferrugineus, commonly known as the rusty laccodiscus, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
