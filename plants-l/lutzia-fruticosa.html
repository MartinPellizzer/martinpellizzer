Lutzia fruticosa

Lutzia fruticosa, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Indian Ocean.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height, featuring dense, woody stems and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. Lutzia fruticosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.
