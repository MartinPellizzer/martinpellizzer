Lithospermum croceum

Lithospermum croceum, commonly known as yellow lithosperm, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

Lithospermum croceum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
