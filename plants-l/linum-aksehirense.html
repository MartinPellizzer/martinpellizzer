Linum aksehirense

Linum aksehirense, commonly known as the Aksehir flax, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its seeds are used for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.

Linum aksehirense is a rare species, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
