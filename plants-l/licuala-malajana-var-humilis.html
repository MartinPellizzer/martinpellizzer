Licuala malajana var. humilis

Licuala malajana var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf palm or small-leaf palm, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a compact, low-growing palm with slender, clustered leaves that are typically less than a meter in length. The plant features a short, unbranched trunk and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage. Its leaves are palmately divided, giving it a unique, feathery appearance.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.
