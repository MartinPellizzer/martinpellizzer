Lepanthes focalis

Lepanthes focalis, commonly known as the focal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant has a short, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive, hooded lip.

Lepanthes focalis is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
