Lapeirousia calycina

Lapeirousia calycina, commonly known as the Orange Rain lily, is a striking South African bulbous perennial belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers with a distinctive yellow center, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, star-shaped blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Lapeirousia calycina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in rock gardens and containers.
