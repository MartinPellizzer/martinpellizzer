Linanthus oblanceolatus

Linanthus oblanceolatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf linanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the tribe Lennoeae within the sunflower family, Asteraceae. The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Linanthus oblanceolatus is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, where it adapts well to dry conditions.
