Lepanthes bradei

Lepanthes bradei, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with intricate patterns.

Lepanthes bradei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty.
