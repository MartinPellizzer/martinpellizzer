Lysimachia ovoidea

Lysimachia ovoidea, commonly known as the egg-shaped chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are oval-shaped and slightly hairy, with a waxy texture.

Lysimachia ovoidea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
