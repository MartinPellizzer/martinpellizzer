Lobelia alata var. angustifolia

Lobelia alata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the slender cardinal flower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and striking tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. This variety is often found in moist, woodland habitats, thriving in partial to full shade.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
