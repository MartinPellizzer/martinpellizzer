Leandra fallacissima

Leandra fallacissima, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces fragrant blooms in various colors.

Leandra fallacissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
