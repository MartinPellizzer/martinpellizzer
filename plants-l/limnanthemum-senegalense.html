Limnanthemum senegalense

Limnanthemum senegalense, commonly known as the Senegalese water mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy white or pink flowers. It is often found near water bodies and is adapted to moist environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
