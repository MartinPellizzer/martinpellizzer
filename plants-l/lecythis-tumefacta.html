Lecythis tumefacta

Lecythis tumefacta, commonly known as the swollen lecythium, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces a distinctive fruit with a thick, spongy rind and a creamy, edible pulp. The tree is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological and horticultural study.
