Lastrea eriocarpa

Lastrea eriocarpa, commonly known as the woolly lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white, woolly hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
