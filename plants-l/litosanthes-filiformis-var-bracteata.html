Litosanthes filiformis var. bracteata

Litosanthes filiformis var. bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate litosanthes, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
