Linum rzedowskii

Linum rzedowskii, commonly known as the Rzedowskii flax, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and linear leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
