Ligularia tussilaginea

Ligularia tussilaginea, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive central boss. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
