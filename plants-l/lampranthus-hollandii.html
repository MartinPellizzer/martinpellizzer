Lampranthus hollandii

Lampranthus hollandii, commonly known as the Orange Sunset.

It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa. This succulent plant features vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are fleshy and cylindrical, adapted for water storage.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
