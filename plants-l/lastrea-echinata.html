Lastrea echinata

Lastrea echinata, commonly known as the spiny lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny leaves, which help it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
