Listrostachys tenerrima

Listrostachys tenerrima, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that reduce water loss and help it survive in harsh conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom in spring.

It is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
