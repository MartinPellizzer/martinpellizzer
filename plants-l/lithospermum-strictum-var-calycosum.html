Lithospermum strictum var. calycosum

Lithospermum strictum var. calycosum, commonly known as the narrowleaf lithosperm, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, which bloom in the summer months. Its roots contain compounds that have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

The species is often found in rocky or dry habitats, demonstrating its adaptability to arid environments.
