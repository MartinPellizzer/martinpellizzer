Lobelia reinwardtiana var. reinwardtiana

Lobelia reinwardtiana var. reinwardtiana, commonly known as the Cape lobelia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it typically grows in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
