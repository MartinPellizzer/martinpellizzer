Lonicera harmsii

Lonicera harmsii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The flowers are followed by orange-red berries that contain numerous seeds. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
