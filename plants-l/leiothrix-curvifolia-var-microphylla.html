Leiothrix curvifolia var. microphylla

Leiothrix curvifolia var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a feathery texture, typically growing to a height of no more than 30 centimeters. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are densely packed, giving it a lush, full appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
