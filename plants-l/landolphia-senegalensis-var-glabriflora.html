Landolphia senegalensis var. glabriflora

Landolphia senegalensis var. glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous floral parts, as indicated by its varietal name. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
