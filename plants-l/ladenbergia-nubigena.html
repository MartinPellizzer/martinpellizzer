Ladenbergia nubigena

Ladenbergia nubigena, commonly known as the cloud-like legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, pinnate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
