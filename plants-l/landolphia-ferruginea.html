Landolphia ferruginea

Landolphia ferruginea, commonly known as the red-eyed African morning glory, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers with a contrasting dark red or black eye. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are often dispersed by animals. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Landolphia ferruginea is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in some cultures.
