Lepanthes caveroi

Lepanthes caveroi, commonly known as the Cervera's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender stem with several ovate, dark green leaves. Its flowers are delicate, with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip.

Lepanthes caveroi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
