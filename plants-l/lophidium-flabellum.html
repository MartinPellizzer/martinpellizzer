Lophidium flabellum

Lophidium flabellum, commonly known as the fan-leaved lophidium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Lophidium flabellum is valued both for its ornamental appearance and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
