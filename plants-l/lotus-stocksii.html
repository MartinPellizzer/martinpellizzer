Lotus stocksii

Lotus stocksii, commonly known as the white lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals and a central yellow or pinkish stamen. The plant typically grows in still water, such as ponds and lakes, and has a buoyant, spongy root system. Its flowers bloom in the early morning and close by midday, a behavior known as nyctinasty.

The lotus is also noted for its ability to purify water and is often used in traditional medicine.
