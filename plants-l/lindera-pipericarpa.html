Lindera pipericarpa

Lindera pipericarpa, commonly known as spicebush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its opposite, simple, and aromatic leaves that emit a peppery scent when crushed. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in early spring, followed by dark purple berries that are edible for birds.

Lindera pipericarpa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
