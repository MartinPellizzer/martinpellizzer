Lebeckia longipes

Lebeckia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
