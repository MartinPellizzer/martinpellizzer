Litsea thunbergii

Litsea thunbergii, commonly known as Chinese huckleberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that give way to small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
