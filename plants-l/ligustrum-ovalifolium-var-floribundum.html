Ligustrum ovalifolium var. floribundum

Ligustrum ovalifolium var. floribundum, commonly known as the fragrant privet, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, black berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
