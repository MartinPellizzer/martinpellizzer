Lespedeza × simulata

Lespedeza × simulata, commonly known as Chinese stragglerbine, is a perennial legume native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Lespedeza × simulata is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to fix nitrogen and stabilize soil.
