Lewisia cantelovii

Lewisia cantelovii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal ranges of California and the Sierra Nevada mountains.

It is a member of the Montiaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pink to purple flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
