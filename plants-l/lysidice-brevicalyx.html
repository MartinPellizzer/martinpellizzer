Lysidice brevicalyx

Lysidice brevicalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved lythrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes other species such as the common lythrum. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
