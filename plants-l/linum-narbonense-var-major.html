Linum narbonense var. major

Linum narbonense var. major, commonly known as the Greater Flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax plants. This variety is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and historical studies.
