Lathyrus tropicalandinus

Lathyrus tropicalandinus, commonly known as the tropical sweet pea, is a climbing legume native to South America.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces vibrant, pea-like flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant is characterized by its long, slender stems and its ability to climb using tendrils. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The seeds are edible and can be used in various culinary applications.
