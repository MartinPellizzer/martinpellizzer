Lithothamnus buxioides

Lithothamnus buxioides, commonly known as the rock holly, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and inland areas with mild climates. Its leaves are typically leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its pale yellow or white flowers.

Lithothamnus buxioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
