Lindsaea stricta var. microphylla

Lindsaea stricta var. microphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features small, linear to lanceolate leaves that are often densely packed along the stems. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing sporangia, are typically found on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in humid environments.
