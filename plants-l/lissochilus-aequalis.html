Lissochilus aequalis

Lissochilus aequalis, commonly known as the equal orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Peru and Bolivia. This orchid typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool and humid conditions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of white and purple.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
