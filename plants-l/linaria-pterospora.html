Linaria pterospora

Linaria pterospora, commonly known as the featherleaf toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant features narrow, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. Linaria pterospora typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
