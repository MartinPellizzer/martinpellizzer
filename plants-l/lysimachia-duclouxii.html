Lysimachia duclouxii

Lysimachia duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Primulaceae family.
