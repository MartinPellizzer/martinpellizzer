Lafoensia petiolata

Lafoensia petiolata, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading canopy and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
