Lankesteriana caudatipetala

Lankesteriana caudatipetala, commonly known as the long-petaled flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with long, slender petals that give it its scientific name. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
