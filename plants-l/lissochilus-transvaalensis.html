Lissochilus transvaalensis

Lissochilus transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
