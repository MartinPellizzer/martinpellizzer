Laurentia michelii var. subacaulis

Laurentia michelii var. subacaulis, commonly known as the dwarf laurentia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
