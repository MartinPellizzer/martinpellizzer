Lepanthes neooreophilus

Lepanthes neooreophilus, commonly known as the Neoreophilus orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthes neooreophilus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
