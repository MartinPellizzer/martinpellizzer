Liliastrum liliforme

Liliastrum liliforme, commonly known as the slender lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and slender, often slightly curved stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
