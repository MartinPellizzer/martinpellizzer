Leucothoe breviflora

Leucothoe breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered leucothoe, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often have a reddish tinge in the fall.

Leucothoe breviflora is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and modest floral display.
