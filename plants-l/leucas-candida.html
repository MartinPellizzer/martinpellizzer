Leucas candida

Leucas candida, commonly known as white leucas, is a herbaceous plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
