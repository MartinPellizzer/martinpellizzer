Lonchocarpus urucu

Lonchocarpus urucu, commonly known as urucu or red bean tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods containing dark red, edible seeds that are rich in nutrients. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional use in local cuisines.

Additionally, it has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of various bioactive compounds.
