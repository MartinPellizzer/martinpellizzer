Leonuroides

Leonuroides, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Leonuroides is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered an invasive species in some areas.
