Ledebouria pardalota

Ledebouria pardalota, commonly known as the red-leaved bulbous plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
