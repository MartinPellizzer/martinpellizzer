Luzula × bogdanii

Luzula × bogdanii, commonly known as the bog sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and spike-like inflorescences. This species typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as bogs, marshes, and along stream banks. Its rhizomes help it spread and establish dense colonies in suitable habitats.

The plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
