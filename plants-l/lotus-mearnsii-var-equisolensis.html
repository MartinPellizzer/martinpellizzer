Lotus mearnsii var. equisolensis

Lotus mearnsii var. equisolensis, commonly known as the Evergreen lotus, is a variant of the Mearns' lotus.

It is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

The botanical characteristics of this variety include its evergreen foliage and its ability to withstand drought conditions.
