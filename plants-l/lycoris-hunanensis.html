Lycoris hunanensis

Lycoris hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan lycoris, is a species of flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and white flowers that bloom in the autumn. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces bulbous structures that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable ornamental plant for gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
