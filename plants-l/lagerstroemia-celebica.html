Lagerstroemia celebica

Lagerstroemia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes crepe myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its showy, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and turn red in autumn, adding to its ornamental value.

It is often cultivated for its attractive flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
