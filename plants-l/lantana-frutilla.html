Lantana frutilla

Lantana frutilla, commonly known as the red lantana, is a flowering shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, yellow, and pink, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Lantana frutilla is also known for its ability to attract birds and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.
