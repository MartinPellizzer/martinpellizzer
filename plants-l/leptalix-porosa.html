Leptalix porosa

Leptalix porosa, commonly known as the slender lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive, thin, leaf-like structure with a pale green to yellowish-green thallus. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provide nutrients through photosynthesis. It is often found on decaying wood or rock surfaces in temperate regions.

Due to its delicate nature, it is sensitive to environmental changes and air pollution.
