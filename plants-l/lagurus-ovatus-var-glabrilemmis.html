Lagurus ovatus var. glabrilemmis

Lagurus ovatus var. glabrilemmis, commonly known as the silky salsify, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats. Its unique, silky appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its edible roots, which have a mild, nutty flavor.
