Lysimachia lychnoides

Lysimachia lychnoides, commonly known as lesser bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia lychnoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
