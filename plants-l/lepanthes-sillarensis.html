Lepanthes sillarensis

Lepanthes sillarensis, commonly known as the Sillares orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or among leaf litter.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in the biodiversity of high-altitude ecosystems.
