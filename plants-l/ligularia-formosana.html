Ligularia formosana

Ligularia formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a prominent central disk, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Ligularia formosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
