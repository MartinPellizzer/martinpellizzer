Limonium aureum var. dielsianum

Limonium aureum var. dielsianum, commonly known as the golden sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, adapting well to saline environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
