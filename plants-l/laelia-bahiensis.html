Laelia bahiensis

Laelia bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its pseudobulbs are covered in a dense layer of roots. Laelia bahiensis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
