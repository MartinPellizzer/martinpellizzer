Lapathum hydrolapathum

Lapathum hydrolapathum, commonly known as water speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.
