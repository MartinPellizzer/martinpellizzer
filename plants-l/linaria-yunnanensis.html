Linaria yunnanensis

Linaria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan toadflax, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the genus Linaria in the family Plantaginaceae. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions.
