Lepanthes eumeces

Lepanthes eumeces, commonly known as the red-bellied night orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has a delicate, epiphytic growth habit, often growing on trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes eumeces is known for its nocturnal blooming period, attracting specific pollinators such as moths.
