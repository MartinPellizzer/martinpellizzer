Lasianthus glabrescens

Lasianthus glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its common name. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in spikes.

Lasianthus glabrescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
