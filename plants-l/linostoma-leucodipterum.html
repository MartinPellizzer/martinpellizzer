Linostoma leucodipterum

Linostoma leucodipterum, commonly known as the white-winged dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, silvery-green leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. Its fruit is a small, hairy achene that is dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread across dry, open habitats.

Linostoma leucodipterum is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
