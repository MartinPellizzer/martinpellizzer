Lessingianthus ibitipocensis

Lessingianthus ibitipocensis, commonly known as the Ibitipocas sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a prominent central disk. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
