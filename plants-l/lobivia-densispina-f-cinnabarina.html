Lobivia densispina f. cinnabarina

Lobivia densispina f. cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-spined lobivia, is a compact, spine-tipped cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, reddish-brown spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique coloration and compact form make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
