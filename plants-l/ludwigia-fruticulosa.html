Ludwigia fruticulosa

Ludwigia fruticulosa, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Ludwigia fruticulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
