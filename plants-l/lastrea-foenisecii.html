Lastrea foenisecii

Lastrea foenisecii, commonly known as the sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss.
