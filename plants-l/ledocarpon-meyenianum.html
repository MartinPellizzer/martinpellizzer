Ledocarpon meyenianum

Ledocarpon meyenianum, commonly known as the meyenianum leucanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence, featuring white ray florets and yellow disk florets. Native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, it thrives in temperate climates. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
