Lonicera trichosantha var. trichosantha

Lonicera trichosantha var. trichosantha, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is characterized by its ability to climb using tendrils, and it bears red berries that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
