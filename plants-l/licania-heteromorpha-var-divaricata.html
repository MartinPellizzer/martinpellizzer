Licania heteromorpha var. divaricata

Licania heteromorpha var. divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag licania, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems that twist and turn as it climbs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, winged fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
