Laelia angereri

Laelia angereri, commonly known as the Angereri orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru.

It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces tall, slender stems with several lance-shaped leaves and bears clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its bloom period typically occurs during the cooler months, and it is appreciated for its ornamental value in cultivation.

Laelia angereri is relatively easy to grow under the right conditions, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
