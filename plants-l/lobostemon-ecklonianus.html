Lobostemon ecklonianus

Lobostemon ecklonianus, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

The species is also valued for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
