Limeum fenestratum var. exalatum

Limeum fenestratum var. exalatum, commonly known as the fenestrated lime, is a striking plant characterized by its distinctive, fenestrated (window-like) leaves.

The leaves are typically large, with intricate perforations that give the plant its unique appearance. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, warm climates. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
