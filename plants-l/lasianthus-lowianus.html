Lasianthus lowianus

Lasianthus lowianus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and shaded landscapes.
