Leptidium erubescens

Leptidium erubescens, commonly known as the redstem sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops and weeds. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. Its small, white or pink flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the spring.
