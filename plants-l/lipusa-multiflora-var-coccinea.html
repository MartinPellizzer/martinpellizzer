Lipusa multiflora var. coccinea

Lipusa multiflora var. coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered lipusa, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual appeal to gardens. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
