Luzula hieronymi

Luzula hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymi's wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Native to temperate regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
