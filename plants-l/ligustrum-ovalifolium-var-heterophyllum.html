Ligustrum ovalifolium var. heterophyllum

Ligustrum ovalifolium var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the California privet, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It has a dense, compact growth habit and is often used as a hedge or ornamental plant.

Its foliage is evergreen in mild climates but may drop leaves in colder regions.
