Laburnum ramentaceum

Laburnum ramentaceum, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, releasing small, winged seeds for dispersal.
