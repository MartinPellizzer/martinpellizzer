Lambertia uniflora

Lambertia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower lambertia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy red flowers that appear in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has woody stems and produces small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and rocky habitats.

Lambertia uniflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
