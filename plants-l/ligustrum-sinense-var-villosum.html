Ligustrum sinense var. villosum

Ligustrum sinense var. villosum, commonly known as the hairy privet, is a shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile ornamental plant.

Due to its adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
