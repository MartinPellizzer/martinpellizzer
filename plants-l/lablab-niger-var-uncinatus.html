Lablab niger var. uncinatus

Lablab niger var. uncinatus, commonly known as the hairy lablab, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its pods contain edible seeds that are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.

The hairy lablab is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.
