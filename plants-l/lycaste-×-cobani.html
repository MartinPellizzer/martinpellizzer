Lycaste × cobani

Lycaste × cobani, commonly known as the cobani lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Lycaste meyenii and Lycaste laeta.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Lycaste × cobani is relatively easy to care for, making it a suitable option for both novice and experienced growers.
