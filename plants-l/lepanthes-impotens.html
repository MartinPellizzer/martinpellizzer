Lepanthes impotens

Lepanthes impotens, commonly known as the feeble orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and delicate, usually white or pale green, with intricate patterns.

Lepanthes impotens is noted for its unique morphology and is often found in montane cloud forests.
