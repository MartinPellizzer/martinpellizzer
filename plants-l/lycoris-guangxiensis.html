Lycoris guangxiensis

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its bulbs are used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty.
