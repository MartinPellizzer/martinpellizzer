Limonium rungsii

Limonium rungsii, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in saline and well-drained soils, often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. Limonium rungsii is a perennial species that blooms in late summer to early autumn.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add color to seaside landscapes.
