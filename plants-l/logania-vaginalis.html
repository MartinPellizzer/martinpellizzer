Logania vaginalis

Logania vaginalis, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
