Lessertia flexuosa

Lessertia flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, twisting fronds that give it a distinctive spiral appearance. The plant has a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread along the forest floor. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Lessertia flexuosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
