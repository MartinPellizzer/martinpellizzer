Lepidium capense

Lepidium capense, commonly known as African cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, edible leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Lepidium capense is valued for its peppery flavor and is used in various culinary and traditional medicinal applications.
