Lindmania serrulata var. reducta

Lindmania serrulata var. reducta, commonly known as the red-tipped lindmania, is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with distinctive red or orange markings along the edges. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts that surround small, white flowers. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for tropical indoor gardens.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
