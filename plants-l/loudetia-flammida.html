Loudetia flammida

Loudetia flammida, commonly known as the flame grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and bright red or orange bracts that give it its striking appearance. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in full sun. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in dense spikes, while the leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Loudetia flammida is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
