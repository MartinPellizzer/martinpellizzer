Laburnum laburnum lusus pendulum

Laburnum laburnum lusus pendulum, commonly known as the weeping laburnum, is a striking ornamental shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its cascading branches that bear clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers, resembling golden curtains. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its foliage turns a vibrant yellow in autumn, adding seasonal interest to the garden.
