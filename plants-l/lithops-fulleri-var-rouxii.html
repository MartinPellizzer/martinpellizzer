Lithops fulleri var. rouxii

Lithops fulleri var. rouxii, commonly known as the "Cape Town Lithops," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pair of fleshy, leaf-like structures that store water and nutrients. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically green with a pale or reddish tinge, and they change color as the plant matures.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
