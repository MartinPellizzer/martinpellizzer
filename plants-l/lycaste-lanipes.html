Lycaste lanipes

Lycaste lanipes, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Lycaste lanipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
