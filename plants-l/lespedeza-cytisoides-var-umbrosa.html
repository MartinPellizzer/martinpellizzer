Lespedeza cytisoides var. umbrosa

Lespedeza cytisoides var. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in woodland areas and moist, shaded environments, often forming dense mats or clumps. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellow to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lespedeza cytisoides var. umbrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
