Lapathum britannicum

Lapathum britannicum, commonly known as the British burnet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the United Kingdom.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom from June to August. Lapathum britannicum is also known for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
