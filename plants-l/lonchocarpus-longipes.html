Lonchocarpus longipes

Lonchocarpus longipes, commonly known as the longipes legume, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, elongated pods that contain its seeds. The plant is native to regions in Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, tropical forests. Its flowers are typically yellow or green, and it produces a strong, sometimes toxic odor.

Due to its potential toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
