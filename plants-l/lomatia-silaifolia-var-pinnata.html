Lomatia silaifolia var. pinnata

Lomatia silaifolia var. pinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lomatia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnate leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
