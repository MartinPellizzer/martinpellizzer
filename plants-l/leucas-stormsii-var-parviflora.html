Leucas stormsii var. parviflora

Leucas stormsii var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered leucas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
