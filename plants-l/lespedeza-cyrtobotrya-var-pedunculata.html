Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. pedunculata

Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. pedunculata, commonly known as the slender lespedeza, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, upright clusters of pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets and a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or open fields.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
