Lasianthus membranaceoideus

Lasianthus membranaceoideus, commonly known as the membranous lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in whorls, displaying vibrant colors such as pink or purple.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
