Lavatera meonantha

Lavatera meonantha, commonly known as the small-flowered lavatera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are ovate and often have a slightly serrated edge, while the flowers bloom in clusters.

Lavatera meonantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
