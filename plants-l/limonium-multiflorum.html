Limonium multiflorum

Limonium multiflorum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spikes of small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for coastal gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in sandy or rocky habitats.

Limonium multiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salty air and wind.
