Linderniella ugandensis

Linderniella ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda lindernia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
