Leucojum tingitanum

Leucojum tingitanum, commonly known as the Canary Island snowflake, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Canary Islands.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Leucojum tingitanum is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread in suitable conditions.
