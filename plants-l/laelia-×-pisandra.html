Laelia × pisandra

Laelia × pisandra, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Laelia and Brassia species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted for efficient photosynthesis and water retention.

Laelia × pisandra is prized in horticulture for its vibrant colors and ease of cultivation.
