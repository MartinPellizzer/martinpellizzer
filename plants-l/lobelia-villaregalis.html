Lobelia villaregalis

Lobelia villaregalis, commonly known as the Mexican whorled lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features whorled leaves and produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Lobelia villaregalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
