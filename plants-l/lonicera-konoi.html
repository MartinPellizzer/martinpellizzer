Lonicera konoi

Lonicera konoi, commonly known as the Japanese honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit, with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Lonicera konoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
