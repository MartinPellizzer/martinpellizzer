Lasianthus konchurangensis

Lasianthus konchurangensis, commonly known as the Konchurang mountain gentian, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments at elevations above 1,500 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
