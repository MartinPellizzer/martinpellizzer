Lepidium Montanum Var. Canescens

Lepidium montanum var. canescens is a lesser-known yet resilient plant species found in high-altitude regions. It thrives in rocky and arid environments where few other plants can survive. This variant is characterized by its grayish foliage and compact growth habit. Its seeds are small and adapted to disperse in harsh conditions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
