Linospadix apetiolatus

Linospadix apetiolatus, commonly known as the velvet spathiphyllum, is a tropical plant characterized by its unique botanical features.

It belongs to the Araceae family, known for its distinctive inflorescence structure. The plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
