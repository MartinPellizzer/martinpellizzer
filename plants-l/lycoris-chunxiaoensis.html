Lycoris chunxiaoensis

Lycoris chunxiaoensis, commonly known as the Chunxiao lycoris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and white flowers that bloom in the autumn season. The plant features strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, and it thrives in well-drained, moist soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is found in woodland and shaded garden environments.
