Laelia anceps var. williamsii

Laelia anceps var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high altitudes of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically blooms during the winter months and prefers cool, bright conditions with moderate humidity. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in drier environments.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
