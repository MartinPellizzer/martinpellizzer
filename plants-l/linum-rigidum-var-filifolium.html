Linum rigidum var. filifolium

Linum rigidum var. filifolium, commonly known as the slender flax or filifolium flax, is a variant of the flax plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic value in native plant gardens.
