Lavatera micans

Lavatera micans, commonly known as the silverleaf lavatera, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features silvery-white, fluffy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Lavatera micans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
