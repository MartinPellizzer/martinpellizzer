Lasianthus tentaculatus

Lasianthus tentaculatus, commonly known as the threadleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
