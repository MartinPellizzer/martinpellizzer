Listera Cordata

Listera cordata is a delicate perennial plant known for its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of white flowers. Native to woodland areas in eastern North America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in shaded understories where it competes with other shade-tolerant flora. Its unique leaf structure and flowering pattern make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners. Listera cordata plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and biodiversity.
