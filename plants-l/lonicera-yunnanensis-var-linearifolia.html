Lonicera yunnanensis var. linearifolia

Lonicera yunnanensis var. linearifolia, commonly known as the Yunnan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged opposite each other along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, usually yellow or white, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
