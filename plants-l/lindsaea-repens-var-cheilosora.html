Lindsaea repens var. cheilosora

Lindsaea repens var. cheilosora, commonly known as the creeping lindsaea, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with long, narrow leaves that often form a dense carpet on the forest floor. Its botanical features make it an important component of understory vegetation in humid forests.
