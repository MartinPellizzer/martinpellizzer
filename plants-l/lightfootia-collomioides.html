Lightfootia collomioides

Lightfootia collomioides, commonly known as the collomioides tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and have a waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, typically appearing in yellow or greenish-yellow hues.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed enclosed in a hard, woody shell.
