Lepanthes blumenavii

Lepanthes blumenavii, commonly known as the Blumenavi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vivid colors such as red, orange, or yellow. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its terrestrial growth habit, often found in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size and are borne on slender stems.

Lepanthes blumenavii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique and colorful blooms.
