Loxothysanus pedunculatus

Loxothysanus pedunculatus, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often with a waxy surface to reduce water loss.

The orchid's root system is specialized, with mycorrhizal associations that aid in nutrient absorption from the soil.
