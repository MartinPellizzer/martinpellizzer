Ligularia anoleuca

Ligularia anoleuca, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple underside, which adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its striking foliage, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
