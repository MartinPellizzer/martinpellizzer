Lindsaea orbiculata

Lindsaea orbiculata, commonly known as the orbiculate spikemoss, is a species of fern ally native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth form. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. Its sporangia are clustered in dense, spike-like structures at the tips of the stems.

Lindsaea orbiculata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
