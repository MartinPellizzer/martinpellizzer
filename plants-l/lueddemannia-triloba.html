It is a foliose lichen, meaning it has a leaf-like structure with a distinct upper and lower surface. The thallus is typically pale green to yellowish in color, and it often grows on bark, soil, or rock surfaces. It is characterized by its three-lobed lobes and a lack of distinct medulla.
