Lisianthius congestus

Lisianthius congestus, commonly known as the clustered lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense clusters of small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to its harsh habitat.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of botanical interest.
