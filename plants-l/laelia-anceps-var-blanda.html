Laelia anceps var. blanda

Laelia anceps var. blanda, commonly known as the soft laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the spring and summer months, and its pseudobulbs are clustered and covered in a velvety sheath. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and relative ease of cultivation.
