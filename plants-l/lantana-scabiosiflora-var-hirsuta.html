Lantana scabiosiflora var. hirsuta

Lantana scabiosiflora var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy lantana, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has hairy foliage and stems, which give it its varietal name "hirsuta.". It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
