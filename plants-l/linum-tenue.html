Linum tenue

Linum tenue, commonly known as slender flax, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, delicate blue or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a soft, hairy texture.

Linum tenue is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant.
