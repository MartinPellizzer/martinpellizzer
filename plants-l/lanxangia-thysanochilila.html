Lanxangia thysanochilila

Lanxangia thysanochilila, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are small but striking, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered threatened and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
