Leucadendron sabulosum

Leucadendron sabulosum, commonly known as the coastal leucadendron, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking, cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Leucadendron sabulosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
