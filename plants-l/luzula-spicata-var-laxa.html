Luzula spicata var. laxa

Luzula spicata var. laxa, commonly known as the loose spike sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Luzula spicata var. laxa is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in natural landscapes.
