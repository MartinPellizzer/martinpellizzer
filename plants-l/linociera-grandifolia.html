Linociera grandifolia

Linociera grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered linociera, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with a slight fragrance, which bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, often dispersed by wind.

Linociera grandifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
