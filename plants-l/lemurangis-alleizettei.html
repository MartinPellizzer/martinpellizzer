Lemurangis alleizettei

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, thriving in the understory of dense rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the rich, organic soil of its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Lemurangis alleizettei is considered a critically endangered species.
