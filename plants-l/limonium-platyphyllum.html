Limonium platyphyllum

Limonium platyphyllum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bract-like structures that surround the flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and saline environments.

Limonium platyphyllum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
