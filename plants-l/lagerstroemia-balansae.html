Lagerstroemia balansae

Lagerstroemia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae crepe myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that display vibrant red, pink, or purple hues during the flowering season. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by elongated, woody fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth margin and a glossy upper surface.

Lagerstroemia balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal color display.
