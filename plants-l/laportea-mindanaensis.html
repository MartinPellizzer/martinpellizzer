Laportea mindanaensis

Laportea mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a composite head. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
