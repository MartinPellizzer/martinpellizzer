Libocedrus formosana

Libocedrus formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan incense cedar, is a coniferous tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Cupressaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. Its wood is highly valued for its fragrance and durability, often used in traditional craftsmanship.
