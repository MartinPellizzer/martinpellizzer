Lepidium stylosum

Lepidium stylosum, commonly known as the slender wallcress, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine environments. Its small, white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Lepidium stylosum is characterized by its narrow leaves and compact growth habit, making it a distinctive yet subtle addition to its natural habitat.
