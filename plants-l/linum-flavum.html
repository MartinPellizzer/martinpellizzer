Linum flavum

Linum flavum, commonly known as the yellow flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically tubular in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Linum flavum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its seeds.
