Lilium lancifolium melpomene

Lilium lancifolium melpomene, commonly known as the Melpomene lily, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant red and white petals.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

However, it is important to note that some species of Lilium can be invasive in certain regions.
