Lepanthes menatoi

Lepanthes menatoi, commonly known as the Menato's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that resemble small slippers, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with intricate patterns, and it has a short, fleshy stem.

Lepanthes menatoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
