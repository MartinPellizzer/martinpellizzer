Liparis gibbosa

Liparis gibbosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Liparis gibbosa is valued for its unique morphology and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
