Lobelia diastateoides

Lobelia diastateoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia diastateoides is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
