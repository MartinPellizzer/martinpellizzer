Lonchocarpus guatemalensis var. jurgensenii

Lonchocarpus guatemalensis var. jurgensenii, commonly known as the Guatemalan sensitive plant, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its bipinnate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical climates. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
