Linaria haenseleri

Linaria haenseleri, commonly known as the desert snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Linaria haenseleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
