Limonium fausti

Limonium fausti, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its blue or purple flower heads that resemble small holly berries, surrounded by soft, feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and sandy soils, showing a high tolerance for saline conditions. Limonium fausti is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its flowers bloom in summer, adding a striking visual element to coastal landscapes.
