Liriosma macrophylla

Liriosma macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved liriosma, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. Liriosma macrophylla thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, humid climates.
