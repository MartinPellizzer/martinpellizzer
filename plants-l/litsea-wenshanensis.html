Litsea wenshanensis

Litsea wenshanensis, commonly known as Wenshan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Wenshan region of Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small, white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Litsea wenshanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
