Lobelia siphilitica var. densiflora

Lobelia siphilitica var. densiflora, commonly known as dense-flowered blue lobelia, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features upright, branched stems covered in dense clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is notable for its abundant floral display and ecological value to pollinators.
