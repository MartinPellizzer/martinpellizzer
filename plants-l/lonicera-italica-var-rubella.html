Lonicera italica var. rubella

Lonicera italica var. rubella, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for hedging and ornamental gardening.
