Litsea xanthophylla

Litsea xanthophylla, commonly known as the yellow-flowered litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers and aromatic leaves that emit a citrus-like scent. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
