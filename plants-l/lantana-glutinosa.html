Lantana glutinosa

Lantana glutinosa, commonly known as sticky lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant has sticky hairs on its stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Lantana glutinosa is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
