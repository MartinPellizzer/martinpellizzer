Litsea deccanensis

Litsea deccanensis, commonly known as the Deccan litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and bay trees, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, oval-shaped berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a distinct aromatic scent.

Litsea deccanensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
