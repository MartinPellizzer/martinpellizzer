Licuala modesta

Licuala modesta, commonly known as the dwarf palm or small-leaf palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant features a short, unbranched stem with a crown of narrow, feather-like leaves that are typically less than a meter in length. Its flowers are small and clustered in spadices, and it produces edible, sweet-smelling fruits that are consumed by various animals.

Licuala modesta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to different growing conditions.
