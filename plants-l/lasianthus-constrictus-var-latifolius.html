Lasianthus constrictus var. latifolius

Lasianthus constrictus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
