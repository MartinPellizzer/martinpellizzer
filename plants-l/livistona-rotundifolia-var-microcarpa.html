Livistona rotundifolia var. microcarpa

Livistona rotundifolia var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf palm or microcarpa palm, is a compact, slow-growing palm native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a short, stout trunk with a dense crown of narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white inflorescences that bear tiny, clustered fruits. Its leaves are typically rounded or elliptical in shape, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
