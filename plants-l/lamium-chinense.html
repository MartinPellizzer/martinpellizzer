Lamium chinense

Lamium chinense, commonly known as Chinese dead-nettle, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lamium chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
