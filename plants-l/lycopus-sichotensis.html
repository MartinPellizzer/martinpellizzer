Lycopus sichotensis

Lycopus sichotensis, commonly known as Sichuan bugleweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It produces small, blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem.

Lycopus sichotensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.
