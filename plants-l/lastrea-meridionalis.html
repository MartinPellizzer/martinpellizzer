Lastrea meridionalis

Lastrea meridionalis, commonly known as the Canary Islands rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and showy, pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat. It is often found growing on coastal cliffs and in sunny, open areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
