Luzula × borreri

Luzula × borreri, commonly known as the hybrid green moor grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces spikelets that are typically green to purplish in color, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, heaths, and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
