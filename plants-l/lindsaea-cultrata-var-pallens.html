Lindsaea cultrata var. pallens

Lindsaea cultrata var. pallens, commonly known as the pale lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically pale green in color. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
