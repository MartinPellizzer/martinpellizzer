Landolphia fragrans

Landolphia fragrans, commonly known as the sweet-scented star vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, honey-like aroma. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Landolphia fragrans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
