Lilium dauricum var. furuyanum

Lilium dauricum var. furuyanum, commonly known as the Japanese lily or Daurian lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to East Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or mountainous areas. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

The flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
