Luzula sylvatica f. altissima

Luzula sylvatica f. altissima, commonly known as the tall wood rush, is a tall, herbaceous perennial belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. This cultivar is notable for its height, often reaching up to 1.5 meters, making it a striking addition to shaded gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add vertical interest in woodland settings.
