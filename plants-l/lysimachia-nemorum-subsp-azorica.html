Lysimachia nemorum subsp. azorica

Lysimachia nemorum subsp.

azorica, commonly known as Azorean yellow loosestrife, is a herbaceous plant native to the Azores. It is characterized by its creeping growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in dense inflorescences.

The leaves are typically oval to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
