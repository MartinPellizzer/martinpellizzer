Lightfootia namaquana

Lightfootia namaquana, commonly known as the Namaqua daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Lightfootia namaquana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
