Liabum umbellatum

Liabum umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its clustered, umbrella-like inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Liabum umbellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
