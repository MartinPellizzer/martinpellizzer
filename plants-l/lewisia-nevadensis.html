Lewisia nevadensis

It belongs to the Montiaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
