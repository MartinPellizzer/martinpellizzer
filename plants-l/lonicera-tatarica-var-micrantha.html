Lonicera tatarica var. micrantha

Lonicera tatarica var. micrantha, commonly known as the Tatarian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
