Lasianthus oculus-cati

Lasianthus oculus-cati, commonly known as the cat's eye plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, eye-like flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are borne in whorled clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal in gardens and horticultural displays.
