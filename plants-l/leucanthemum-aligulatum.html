Leucanthemum aligulatum

Leucanthemum aligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved oxeye daisy, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its white ray flowers surrounding a yellow central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Leucanthemum aligulatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate.
