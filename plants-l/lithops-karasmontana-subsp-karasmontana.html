Lithops karasmontana subsp. karasmontana

Lithops karasmontana subsp.

karasmontana, commonly known as the Karas Mountains lithops, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a pair of rounded, stone-like structures.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

This subspecies is notable for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
