Laelia marcaliana

Laelia marcaliana, commonly known as the Marcaliana orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers. Its blossoms are usually pink to purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Laelia marcaliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
