Lindsaea copelandii

Lindsaea copelandii, commonly known as the Copeland's spikemoss, is a member of the family Lindsaeaceae.

It is a small, evergreen fern-like plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a creeping rhizome with erect, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its spore-bearing structures, called sporangia, are arranged in clusters on the underside of the leaves.

Lindsaea copelandii is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in areas with high humidity and partial shade.
