Ligularia carpathica

Ligularia carpathica, commonly known as the Carpathian ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
