Lysimachia rubida

Lysimachia rubida, commonly known as red sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia rubida is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
