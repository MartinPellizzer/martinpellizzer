Latrienda multiflora

Latrienda multiflora, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Latrienda multiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
