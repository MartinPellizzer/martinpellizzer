Lilium fauriei

Lilium fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese lily, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped, dark green leaves. Lilium fauriei is a perennial herbaceous plant that blooms in the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural cultivation.
