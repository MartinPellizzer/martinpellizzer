Linum keniense var. aberdaricum

Linum keniense var. aberdaricum, commonly known as Aberdaric flax, is a variant of the flax species native to Kenya.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers, which bloom in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in semi-arid regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential agricultural uses.
