Lens culinaris var. villosa

Lens culinaris var. villosa, commonly known as hairy lentil, is a variant of the common lentil characterized by its hairy foliage and pod structure.

It is an annual legume native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are typically brown or yellow in color. It has a fibrous root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, enhancing soil fertility.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and nutritional content, making it a useful crop in sustainable agriculture.
