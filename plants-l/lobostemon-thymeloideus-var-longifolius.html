Lobostemon thymeloideus var. longifolius

Lobostemon thymeloideus var. longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved lobostemon, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
