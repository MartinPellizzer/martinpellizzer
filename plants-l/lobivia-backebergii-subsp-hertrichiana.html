Lobivia backebergii subsp. hertrichiana

Lobivia backebergii subsp.

hertrichiana, commonly known as the Hertrich's lobivia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit.

The plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water, and it produces yellow flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This subspecies is prized by collectors for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
