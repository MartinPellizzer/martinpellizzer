Lithospermum diffusum var. suave

Lithospermum diffusum var. suave, commonly known as sweet lithospermum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

The roots of this plant contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
