Lacinaria scariosa var. praecellens

Lacinaria scariosa var. praecellens, commonly known as the showy lace fern, is a striking ornamental fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, feathery fronds with a distinctive, lace-like appearance that adds a refined elegance to gardens. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its foliage is often adorned with a fine, scaly texture that gives it a unique visual appeal.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in woodland or water garden settings.
