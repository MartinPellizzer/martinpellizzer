Limonium Angustatum

Limonium angustatum is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant, spiky blooms that often appear in coastal and sandy environments. This species belongs to the Plumbagaceae family and is native to regions such as southern Europe and northern Africa. Its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant landscaping. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it ideal for rock gardens and coastal gardens. With its unique appearance and hardy nature, Limonium angustatum is both an ornamental and ecological asset in diverse landscapes.
