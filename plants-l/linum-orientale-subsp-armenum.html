Linum orientale subsp. armenum

Linum orientale subsp.

armenum, commonly known as Armenian flax, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue or purple flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats. It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
