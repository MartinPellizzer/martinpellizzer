Lycium colorans

Lycium colorans, commonly known as the colorful boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries that are edible for humans and wildlife. Lycium colorans thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant seasonal color changes.
