Liparis robustior

Liparis robustior, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense clusters of flowers. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Liparis robustior is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
