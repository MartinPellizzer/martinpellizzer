Liparis plicata var. kawakamii

Liparis plicata var. kawakamii, commonly known as the Kawakamii pleated orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique pleated leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
