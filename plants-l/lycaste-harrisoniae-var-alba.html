Lycaste harrisoniae var. alba

Lycaste harrisoniae var. alba, commonly known as the white lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its pale blooms.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
