Lasiospora tuberosa

Lasiospora tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous lasiospora, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its tuberous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
