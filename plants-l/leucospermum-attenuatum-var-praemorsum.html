Leucospermum attenuatum var. praemorsum

Leucospermum attenuatum var. praemorsum, commonly known as the yellow starfish, is a striking South African shrub characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and needle-like leaves.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. The plant produces dense clusters of star-shaped blooms, which are particularly showy during the winter months. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its resilience to coastal conditions.
