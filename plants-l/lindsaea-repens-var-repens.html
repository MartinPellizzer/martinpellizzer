Lindsaea repens var. repens

Lindsaea repens var. repens, commonly known as the creeping lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with rhizomes that spread along the surface of trees or rocks. The leaves are typically elongated and covered in fine, soft hairs, which help in moisture retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
