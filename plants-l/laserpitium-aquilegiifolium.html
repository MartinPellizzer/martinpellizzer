Laserpitium aquilegiifolium

Laserpitium aquilegiifolium, commonly known as the heartleaf grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy habitats and is often found in disturbed soils. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
