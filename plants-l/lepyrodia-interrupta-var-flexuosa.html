Lepyrodia interrupta var. flexuosa

Lepyrodia interrupta var. flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag or mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in a dense, flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions.

Its unique growth pattern and vibrant floral display make it a notable species in its native habitat.
