Leucadendron hirtum

Leucadendron hirtum, commonly known as the Silver Curled Conebush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy foliage and striking conical flower heads that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and unique appearance.

Leucadendron hirtum plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ecological and aesthetic contributions.
