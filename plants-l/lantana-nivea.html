Lantana nivea

Lantana nivea, commonly known as silver lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, requiring careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
