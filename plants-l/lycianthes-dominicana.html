Lycianthes dominicana

Lycianthes dominicana, commonly known as the Dominican nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, with five petals and a distinctive calyx.

The fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
