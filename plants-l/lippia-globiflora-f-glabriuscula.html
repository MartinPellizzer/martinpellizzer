Lippia globiflora f. glabriuscula

Lippia globiflora f. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lippia, is a compact shrub native to Mexico.

It features small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage.
