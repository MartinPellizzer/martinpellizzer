Lomaria nigra

Lomaria nigra, commonly known as black lomaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Lomaria nigra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
