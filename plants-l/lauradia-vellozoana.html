Lauradia vellozoana

Lauradia vellozoana, commonly known as the Vellozoa laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its leaves are glossy and lance-shaped.

Lauradia vellozoana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
