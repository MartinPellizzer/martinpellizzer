Litsea litseifolia

Litsea litseifolia, commonly known as the white litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic foliage.
