Lastrea torresiana

Lastrea torresiana, commonly known as the Torresiana ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or autumn. It is a low-growing shrub that forms dense mats, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions makes it a popular choice for landscaping in Mediterranean climates.
