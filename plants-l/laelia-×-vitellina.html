Laelia × vitellina

Laelia × vitellina, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that often display a mix of colors such as white, pink, and yellow. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in varying environmental conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
