Lamarkia

Lamarkia, commonly known as the lamarkia plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that often come in vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Lamarkia plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.
