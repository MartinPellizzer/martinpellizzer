Lobbia dependens

Lobbia dependens, commonly known as the hanging milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems that often hang from trees or shrubs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lobbia dependens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and unique growth habit.
