Ligularia coreana

Ligularia coreana, commonly known as the Korean ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Korea.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a spire-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
