Llanosia

Llanosia, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woody stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has adapted to harsh conditions with thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Llanosia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its ecological importance lies in its role as a habitat and food source for local wildlife.
