Lithoon sieversianum

Lithoon sieversianum, commonly known as the Sievers' lithoon, is a species of red algae found primarily in coastal marine environments.

It thrives in rocky substrates and is often found in shallow, tide pool areas where it can absorb nutrients from the water. The plant's structure consists of a thallus that is typically bright red or purple in color, which helps it absorb sunlight for photosynthesis. Lithoon sieversianum plays a vital role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
