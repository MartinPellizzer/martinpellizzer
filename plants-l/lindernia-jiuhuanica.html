Lindernia jiuhuanica

Lindernia jiuhuanica, commonly known as the Hainan lindernia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species with potential ecological significance.
