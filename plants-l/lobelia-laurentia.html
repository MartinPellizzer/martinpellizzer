Lobelia laurentia

Lobelia laurentia, commonly known as the Laurentian lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often found in shaded areas, thriving in partial to full shade conditions.

Lobelia laurentia is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
