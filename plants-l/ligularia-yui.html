Ligularia yui

Ligularia yui, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
