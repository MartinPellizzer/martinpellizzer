Linaria spartea f. meonantha

Linaria spartea f. meonantha, commonly known as the meonantha linaria, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems that produce clusters of delicate, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive spurred shape. The foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
