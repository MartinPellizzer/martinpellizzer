Lotus digitatus

Lotus digitatus, commonly known as the finger lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, paddle-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate, finger-like veins. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental ponds and water gardens.
