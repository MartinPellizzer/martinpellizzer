Litsea procera

Litsea procera, commonly known as the tall litsea or mountain litsea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, red berries containing seeds. Litsea procera is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
