Lavatera assurgentiflora subsp. dendroidea

Lavatera assurgentiflora subsp.

dendroidea, commonly known as the tree lavatera, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa. It is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has palmately lobed leaves and can grow up to 5 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for local wildlife.
