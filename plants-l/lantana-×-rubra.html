Lantana × rubra

Lantana × rubra, commonly known as the red lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the warmer regions of the Americas.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, opposite leaves and its ability to thrive in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
