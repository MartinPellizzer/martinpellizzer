Limonia flavicans

Limonia flavicans, commonly known as the yellow lime or golden lime, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, yellow flowers and edible, citrus-like fruits. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture, while the fruit is typically small, round, and has a yellowish-orange color.

Limonia flavicans is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
