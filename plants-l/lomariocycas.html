Lomariocycas

Lomariocycas, commonly known as the African cycad, is a genus of cycads native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their stout, unbranched stems and large, pinnately compound leaves. Lomariocycas species are typically slow-growing and can live for hundreds of years, making them important components of their ecosystems.
