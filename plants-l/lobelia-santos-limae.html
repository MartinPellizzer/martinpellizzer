Lobelia santos-limae

Lobelia santos-limae, commonly known as the Santos-Limae lobelia, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are opposite and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lobelia santos-limae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral structure.
