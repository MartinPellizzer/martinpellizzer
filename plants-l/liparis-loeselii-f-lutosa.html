Liparis loeselii f. lutosa

Liparis loeselii f. lutosa, commonly known as the Lutosa orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its botanical features include delicate, lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its rarity and beauty.
