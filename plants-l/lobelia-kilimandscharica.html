Lobelia kilimandscharica

Lobelia kilimandscharica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro lobelia, is a striking flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia kilimandscharica is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
