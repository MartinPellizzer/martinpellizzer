Lithocarpus Ternaticupulus Var. Arisanensis

Lithocarpus ternaticupulus var. arisanensis is a rare and intriguing species of evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan. It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chinkapins, and is known for its hard, durable wood. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and small, cup-shaped fruits that mature to a dark brown color. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in dense, shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern and a subject of botanical interest.
