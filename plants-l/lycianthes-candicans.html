Lycianthes candicans

Lycianthes candicans, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America. The plant features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name.

The fruit is a small, yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.
