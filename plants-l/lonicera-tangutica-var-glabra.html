Lonicera tangutica var. glabra

Lonicera tangutica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that are typically hairless, giving it its varietal name "glabra.". The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a smooth texture.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
