Limonium caprariense

Limonium caprariense, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring sandy or gravelly soils. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is often found in dune environments.

Limonium caprariense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
