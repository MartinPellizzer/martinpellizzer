Lepanthes deuteroleonii

Lepanthes deuteroleonii, commonly known as the Deuteroleon's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red streaks.

Lepanthes deuteroleonii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
