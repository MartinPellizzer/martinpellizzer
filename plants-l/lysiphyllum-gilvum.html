Lysiphyllum gilvum

Lysiphyllum gilvum, commonly known as the golden sea fan, is a species of soft coral found in shallow tropical waters.

It is characterized by its branching, fan-like structure and vibrant yellow coloration. The plant's polyps are typically small and arranged in a dense, feathery pattern. It thrives in clear, warm seawater with good light penetration.

Lysiphyllum gilvum plays an important role in reef ecosystems by providing habitat for various marine organisms.
