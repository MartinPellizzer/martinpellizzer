Leonotis mollissima

Leonotis mollissima, commonly known as the red lantana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of tubular red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and sandy areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
