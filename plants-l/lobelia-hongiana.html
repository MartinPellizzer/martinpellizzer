Lobelia hongiana

Lobelia hongiana, commonly known as the Hong Kong lobelia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
