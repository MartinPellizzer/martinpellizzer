Lindmania navioides

Lindmania navioides, commonly known as the Navio's mace, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers followed by spadices, which are often used in traditional medicine.
