Laelia heidii

Laelia heidii, commonly known as the Heidii orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its mountainous habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Laelia heidii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
