Lytoneuron crenulans

Lytoneuron crenulans, commonly known as the serrated leaf vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and restoration projects.
