Laelia regnellii

Laelia regnellii, commonly known as the Regnell's orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with intricate patterns and a pleasant fragrance.

Laelia regnellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
