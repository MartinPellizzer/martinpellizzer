Lobelia gladiaria

Lobelia gladiaria, commonly known as the swordleaf lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is often found in swampy areas.

Lobelia gladiaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for pollinators.
