Llagunoa nitida var. glabra

Llagunoa nitida var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth llagunoa, is a shrub native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is often aromatic, and it thrives in well-drained soils with ample sunlight.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to dry conditions.
