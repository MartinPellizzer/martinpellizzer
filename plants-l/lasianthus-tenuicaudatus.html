Lasianthus tenuicaudatus

Lasianthus tenuicaudatus, commonly known as the slender-tailed lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers.
