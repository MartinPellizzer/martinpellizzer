Lemonanthemum

Lemonanthemum, commonly known as lemon thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a lemony scent and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage is often used in culinary applications, adding a zesty flavor to dishes.

Lemonanthemum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
