Ledebouria confusa

Ledebouria confusa, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a species of bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Ledebouria confusa is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
