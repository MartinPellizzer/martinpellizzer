Lepeostegeres beccarii

Lepeostegeres beccarii, commonly known as the beccarii orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that display a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.
