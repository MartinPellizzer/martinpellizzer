Lepismium Chrysocarpum

Lepismium chrysocarpum is a rare and captivating member of the nightshade family known for its striking yellow fruits. Native to the high Andes of Peru, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments at high altitudes. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of fascination for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and conservation status of this remarkable species.
