Lobivia walterspielii

Lobivia walterspielii, commonly known as the Walter's lobivia, is a small, slow-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and protected species.
