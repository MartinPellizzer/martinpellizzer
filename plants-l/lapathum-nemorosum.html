Lapathum nemorosum

Lapathum nemorosum, commonly known as the wood betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodland areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Lapathum nemorosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and natural habitats.
