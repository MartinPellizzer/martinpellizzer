Lagenophora sinuosa

Lagenophora sinuosa, commonly known as the sinuous bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and slightly notched, contributing to its unique appearance.

Lagenophora sinuosa is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
