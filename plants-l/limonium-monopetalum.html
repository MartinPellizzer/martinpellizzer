Limonium monopetalum

Limonium monopetalum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, round seed capsules. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

Limonium monopetalum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
