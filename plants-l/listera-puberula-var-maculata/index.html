Listera Puberula Var. Maculata

Spotted wintergreen, scientifically known as Listera puberula var. maculata, is a delicate woodland herb that thrives in cool, moist environments. Its striking appearance features small, heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture and distinctive white or pale green spots. This plant is often found in shaded forest floors, where it plays a vital role in the ecosystem by supporting pollinators and contributing to soil health. Despite its modest size, Spotted wintergreen is a symbol of resilience and quiet beauty in the natural world. Its unique features make it a favorite among botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
