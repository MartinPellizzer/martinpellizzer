Linostylis fasciculiflora

Linostylis fasciculiflora, commonly known as the clustered-flowered orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms that are often arranged in racemes or fascicles. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Linostylis fasciculiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
