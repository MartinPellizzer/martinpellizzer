Lepanthes stewartiorum

Lepanthes stewartiorum, commonly known as Stewart's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale green with intricate patterns. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.

Lepanthes stewartiorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
