Limeum obovatum

Limeum obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed lime, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lanceolate to obovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in loose clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
