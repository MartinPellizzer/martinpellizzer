Lysionotus pauciflorus

Lysionotus pauciflorus, commonly known as the few-flowered lythrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its foliage is often lance-shaped with a smooth texture.

Lysionotus pauciflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
