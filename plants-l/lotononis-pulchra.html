Lotononis pulchra

Lotononis pulchra, commonly known as the purple star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are arranged in a spike-like cluster.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
