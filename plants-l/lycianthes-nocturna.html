Lycianthes nocturna

Lycianthes nocturna, commonly known as nightshade tomato, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom at night, hence its common name. Its fruit is a small, orange-red berry that ripens in the evening.
