Limniris robusta

Limniris robusta, commonly known as the robust water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with showy petals that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Limniris robusta plays an important role in water ecosystems by helping to control algae growth and improving water quality.
