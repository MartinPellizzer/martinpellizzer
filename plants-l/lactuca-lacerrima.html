Lactuca lacerrima

Lactuca lacerrima, commonly known as the hairy lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showing a preference for temperate climates.
