Laelia anceps subsp. anceps

Laelia anceps subsp.

anceps, commonly known as the Andes orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes. It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and typically produces multiple flower spikes.

The flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple or reddish markings, and they bloom during the cooler months. It prefers cool, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a hybrid for its ornamental value.

The plant has a pseudobulb structure and is known for its resilience in cultivation.
