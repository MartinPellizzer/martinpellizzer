Lavatera triloba subsp. minoricensis

Lavatera triloba subsp.

minoricensis, commonly known as the Minorca lavatera, is a shrub native to the Balearic Islands. It features showy, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in summer.

The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that are typically lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native region.
