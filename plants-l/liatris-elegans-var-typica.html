Liatris elegans var. typica

Liatris elegans var. typica, commonly known as the elegant blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous narrow, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and prairies. Its showy blooms, which range in color from pink to purple, attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
