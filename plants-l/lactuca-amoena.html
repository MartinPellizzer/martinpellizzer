Lactuca amoena

Lactuca amoena, commonly known as the handsome lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is characterized by its rosette of broad, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Lactuca amoena is often found in meadows, fields, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its historical use in herbal medicine is noted for its potential soothing properties.
