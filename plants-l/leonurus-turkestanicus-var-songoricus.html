Leonurus turkestanicus var. songoricus

Leonurus turkestanicus var. songoricus, commonly known as the Songor hare's foot, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant features small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in whorls along the upper stem. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape, contributing to its unique appearance.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
