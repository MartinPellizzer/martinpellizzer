Leobordea longiflora

Leobordea longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered leobordea, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Leobordea longiflora is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
