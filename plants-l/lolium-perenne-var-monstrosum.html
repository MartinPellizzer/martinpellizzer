Lolium perenne var. monstrosum

Lolium perenne var. monstrosum, commonly known as the giant ryegrass, is a variant of perennial ryegrass characterized by its notably larger size and robust growth habit.

It exhibits enhanced tillering and longer, more vigorous leaves compared to standard ryegrass varieties. This plant is often cultivated for its high forage value and rapid establishment in agricultural settings. Botanically, it possesses a fibrous root system and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a valuable species in both pasture and turfgrass management.
