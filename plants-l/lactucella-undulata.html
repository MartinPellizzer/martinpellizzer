Lactucella undulata

Lactucella undulata, commonly known as the wavy lettuce, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, wavy-edged leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Lactucella undulata is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
