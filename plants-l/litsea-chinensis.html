Litsea chinensis

Litsea chinensis, commonly known as Chinese laurustin, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, dark purple berries.

Litsea chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some uses in traditional medicine.
