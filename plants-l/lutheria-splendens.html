Lutheria splendens

Lutheria splendens, commonly known as the splendour lutheria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Lutheria splendens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for containers or as a border plant in gardens.
