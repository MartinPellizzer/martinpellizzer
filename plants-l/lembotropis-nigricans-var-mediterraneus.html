Lembotropis nigricans var. mediterraneus

Lembotropis nigricans var. mediterraneus, commonly known as the Mediterranean lembotropis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
