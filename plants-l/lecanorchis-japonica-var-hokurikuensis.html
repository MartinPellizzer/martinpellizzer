Lecanorchis japonica var. hokurikuensis

Lecanorchis japonica var. hokurikuensis, commonly known as the Hokuriku orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid native to Japan.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and a single, lanceolate leaf. The plant produces a spike of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
