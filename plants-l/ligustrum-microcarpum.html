Ligustrum microcarpum

Ligustrum microcarpum, commonly known as the small-fruited privet, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by small, dark berries. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and as a hedge plant.

Ligustrum microcarpum is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various soil types.
