Lavatera olbia var. intermedia

Lavatera olbia var. intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid lavatera, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, semi-double flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in summer. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
