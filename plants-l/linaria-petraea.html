Linaria petraea

Linaria petraea, commonly known as the rock snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are tubular in shape, resembling small snapdragons. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Linaria petraea is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal areas and dry slopes.
