Lysimachia montana

Lysimachia montana, commonly known as mountain chickweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lysimachia montana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
