Ligusticum catalaunicum

Ligusticum catalaunicum, commonly known as the Catalan lovage, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots, parsley, and celery, and is characterized by its aromatic, often fern-like foliage. Ligusticum catalaunicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
