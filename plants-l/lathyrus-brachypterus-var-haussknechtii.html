Lathyrus brachypterus var. haussknechtii

Lathyrus brachypterus var. haussknechtii, commonly known as the dwarf vetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its compact growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep violet. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and ecological restoration projects.
