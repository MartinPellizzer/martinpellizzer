Lespedeza elegans

Lespedeza elegans, commonly known as silky lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink to reddish-purple, arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Silky lespedeza is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.
