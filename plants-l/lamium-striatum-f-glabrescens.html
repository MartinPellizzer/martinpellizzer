Lamium striatum f. glabrescens

Lamium striatum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped, glabrous (smooth) leaves with a distinctive purple underside, and it produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and early spring blooms.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
