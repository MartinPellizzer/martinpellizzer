Lunania piperoides

Lunania piperoides, commonly known as the pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, white or pale purple flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
