Lomatia fraxinifolia

Lomatia fraxinifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lomatia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in dense clusters, which are followed by woody, cylindrical pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Lomatia fraxinifolia is also noted for its medicinal uses by Indigenous Australian communities.
