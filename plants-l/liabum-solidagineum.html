Liabum solidagineum

Liabum solidagineum, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-green pea-like flowers. The plant produces lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

Liabum solidagineum is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
