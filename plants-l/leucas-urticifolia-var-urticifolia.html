Leucas urticifolia var. urticifolia

Leucas urticifolia var. urticifolia, commonly known as Indian nettle or false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves with fine hairs, giving it a rough texture. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom in the summer months.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
