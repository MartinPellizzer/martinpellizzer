Leucogenes

Leucogenes, commonly known as the whiteflower orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Leucogenes is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
