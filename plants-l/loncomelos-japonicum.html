Loncomelos japonicum

Loncomelos japonicum, commonly known as Japanese mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or woodland habitats and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Loncomelos japonicum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
