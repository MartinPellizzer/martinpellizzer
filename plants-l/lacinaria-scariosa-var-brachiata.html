Lacinaria scariosa var. brachiata

Lacinaria scariosa var. brachiata, commonly known as the bracted lacinaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
