Lycaste cinnabarina

Lycaste cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-brown lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with inflorescences that can reach several inches in length. Its leaves are dark green, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

Lycaste cinnabarina is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
