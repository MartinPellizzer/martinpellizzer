Leucadendron globosum

Leucadendron globosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, spherical flower heads that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
