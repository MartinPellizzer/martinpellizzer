Linum munbyanum

Linum munbyanum, commonly known as Munby's flax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes other species of flax, and is characterized by its delicate, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a slender, erect stem with narrow leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Linum munbyanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
