Lens hohenackeri

Lens hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's lens, is a rare and ancient legume species.

It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds and distinctive pod structure. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is adapted to specific ecological niches. Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves and a short, erect growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
