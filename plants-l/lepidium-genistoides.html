Lepidium genistoides

Lepidium genistoides, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and compound leaves that resemble those of the genista shrubs. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Lepidium genistoides is often found in disturbed areas and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
