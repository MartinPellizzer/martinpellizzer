Lecythis angustifolia

Lecythis angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lecythis, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a single, large, oily seed. Its bark and seeds have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.

Lecythis angustifolia plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
