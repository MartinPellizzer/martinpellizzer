Liparis brunnescens

Liparis brunnescens, commonly known as the brown orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dark brown to reddish-brown flower spikes. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Liparis brunnescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
