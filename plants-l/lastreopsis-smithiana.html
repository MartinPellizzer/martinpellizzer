Lastreopsis smithiana

Lastreopsis smithiana, commonly known as the New Zealand rock fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in rocky or shaded environments. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense, clustered fronds. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a leathery texture.

It is native to New Zealand and is adapted to thrive in moist, cool climates.
