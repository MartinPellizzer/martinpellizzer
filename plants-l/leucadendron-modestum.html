Leucadendron modestum

Leucadendron modestum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces conical or cylindrical flower heads that range in color from yellow to orange, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Leucadendron modestum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
