Linum moroderorum

Linum moroderorum, commonly known as the Moroder's flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their showy blue or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of blue or purple.

Linum moroderorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
