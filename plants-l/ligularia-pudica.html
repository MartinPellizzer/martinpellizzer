Ligularia pudica

Ligularia pudica, commonly known as the slender ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland gardens and bog plantings.
