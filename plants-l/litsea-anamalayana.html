Litsea anamalayana

Litsea anamalayana, commonly known as the Malayan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
