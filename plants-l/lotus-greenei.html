Lotus greenei

Lotus greenei, commonly known as the Sierra Madre lotus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments. The plant features palmate leaves with a waxy surface, and its flowers are usually pink or purple in color. Lotus greenei is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a unique ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
