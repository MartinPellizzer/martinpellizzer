Limonium otolepis

Limonium otolepis, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Limonium otolepis is a member of the Plumbagaceae family and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
