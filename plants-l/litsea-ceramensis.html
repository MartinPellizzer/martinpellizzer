Litsea ceramensis

Litsea ceramensis, commonly known as the Ceram litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a glossy green surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, red berries that are edible for some bird species.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
