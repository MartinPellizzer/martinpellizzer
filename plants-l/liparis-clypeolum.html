Liparis clypeolum

Liparis clypeolum, commonly known as the flat-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive rosette of broad, flat leaves that form a dense mat. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
