Lobelia obscura

Lobelia obscura, commonly known as the dark lobelia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia obscura is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
