Laelia × gottoana

Laelia × gottoana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and a species of Cattleya.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with moderate humidity.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to cultivation.
