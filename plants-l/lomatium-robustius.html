Lomatium robustius

Lomatium robustius, commonly known as the giant lomatium, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in umbels. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Lomatium robustius thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and open woodlands.
