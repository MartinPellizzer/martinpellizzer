Lithops annae

Lithops annae, commonly known as the "Fairy Thorn," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance, with two fleshy leaves that resemble stones. The plant stores water in its thick, fleshy leaves, allowing it to survive in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months.

Lithops annae is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
