Linum lewisii

Linum lewisii, commonly known as Lewis flax, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes species known for their showy blue or white flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer.

Linum lewisii is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
