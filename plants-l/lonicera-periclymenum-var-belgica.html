Lonicera periclymenum var. belgica

Lonicera periclymenum var. belgica, commonly known as the Belgian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its red berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local ecosystems.
