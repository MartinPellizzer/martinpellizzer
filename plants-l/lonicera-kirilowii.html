Lonicera kirilowii

Lonicera kirilowii, commonly known as the Kirilow honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera kirilowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and wildlife benefits.
