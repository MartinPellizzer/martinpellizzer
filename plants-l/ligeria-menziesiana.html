Ligeria menziesiana

Ligeria menziesiana, commonly known as the Menzies' ligeria, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ligeria menziesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
