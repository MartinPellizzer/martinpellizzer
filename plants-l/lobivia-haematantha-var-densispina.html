Lobivia haematantha var. densispina

Lobivia haematantha var. densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined lobivia, is a succulent plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense, spiny appearance. The plant features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles, and bloom during the warmer months.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or cactus collections.
