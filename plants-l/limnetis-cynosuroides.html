Limnetis cynosuroides

Limnetis cynosuroides, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of aquatic plant that thrives in freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that float on the water's surface. The plant has a complex rhizome system that helps it anchor in muddy or sandy substrates. Its reproductive structures are typically found in the water, contributing to its spread through spore dispersal.

Limnetis cynosuroides plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to various aquatic organisms.
