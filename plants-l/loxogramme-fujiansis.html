Loxogramme fujiansis

Loxogramme fujiansis, commonly known as the Fujian water fern, is a rare aquatic fern native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are divided into small, lobed segments. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and streams. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Loxogramme fujiansis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in aquatic ecosystems.
