Loddigesia

Loddigesia, commonly known as the "London plane tree," is a species of tree native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that turn a striking golden color in the autumn. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, which are followed by winged fruits that disperse seeds by wind. Loddigesia is highly adaptable and thrives in urban environments, making it a popular choice for city landscaping.

Its robust nature and ability to tolerate pollution make it a valuable addition to public spaces.
