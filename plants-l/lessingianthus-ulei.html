Lessingianthus ulei

Lessingianthus ulei, commonly known as the Brazilian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
