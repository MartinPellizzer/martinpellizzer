Leontodon calvatus

Leontodon calvatus, commonly known as the smooth leontodon, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are basal, narrow, and often hairy, while its flowering stems bear a single flower at the top.

Leontodon calvatus is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
