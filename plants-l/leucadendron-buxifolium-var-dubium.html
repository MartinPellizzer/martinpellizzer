Leucadendron buxifolium var. dubium

Leucadendron buxifolium var. dubium, commonly known as the silver leaf sugar bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in conical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The leaves are leathery and have a compact, bushy growth habit.
