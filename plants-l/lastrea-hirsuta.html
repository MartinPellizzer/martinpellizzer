Lastrea hirsuta

Lastrea hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
