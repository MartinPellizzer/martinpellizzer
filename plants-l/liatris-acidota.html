Liatris acidota

Liatris acidota, commonly known as the eastern blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear showy, pink to purple flower heads. Liatris acidota is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
