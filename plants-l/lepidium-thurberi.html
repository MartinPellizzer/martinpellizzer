Lepidium thurberi

Lepidium thurberi, commonly known as Thurber's cress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has small, white or yellowish flowers and produces small, round seed pods.

Its leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed, and often covered in fine hairs.
