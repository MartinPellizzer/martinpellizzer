Lantana bullata

Lantana bullata, commonly known as the silver lantana, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana bullata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
