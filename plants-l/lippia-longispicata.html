Lippia longispicata

Lippia longispicata, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
