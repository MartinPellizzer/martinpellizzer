Linaria origanifolia var. rodriguezii

Linaria origanifolia var. rodriguezii, commonly known as Rodriguez's throatwort, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive throat-like marking. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
