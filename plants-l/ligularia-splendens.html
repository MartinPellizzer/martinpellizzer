Ligularia splendens

Ligularia splendens, commonly known as the golden shield, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green with a distinctive yellow margin. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a border plant.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, shade-tolerant plants.
