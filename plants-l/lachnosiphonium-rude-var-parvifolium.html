Lachnosiphonium rude var. parvifolium

Lachnosiphonium rude var. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved lachnosiphonium, is a rare and lesser-known fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are typically shorter and narrower than those of the typical variety. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests or along stream banks.
