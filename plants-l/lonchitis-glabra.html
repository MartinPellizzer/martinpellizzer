Lonchitis glabra

Lonchitis glabra, commonly known as the smooth bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It is characterized by its simple, branching stems and small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces tiny, green flowers that are typically clustered in spikes near the water's surface.
