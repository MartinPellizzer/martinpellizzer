Liparis exilis

Liparis exilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Liparis exilis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and exhibits typical orchid morphology with specialized pollination mechanisms.
