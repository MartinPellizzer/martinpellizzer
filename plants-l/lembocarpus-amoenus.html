Lembocarpus amoenus

Lembocarpus amoenus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its aesthetic appeal.
