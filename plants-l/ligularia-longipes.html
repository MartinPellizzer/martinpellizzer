Ligularia longipes

Ligularia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and prominent veins. The plant produces tall, slender stems that can reach several feet in height, supporting its showy yellow-orange flowers. These flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Ligularia longipes thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland habitats.
