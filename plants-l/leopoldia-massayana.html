Leopoldia massayana

Leopoldia massayana, commonly known as the Massayana orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground.
