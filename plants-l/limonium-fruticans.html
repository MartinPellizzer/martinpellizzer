Limonium fruticans

Limonium fruticans, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flower heads and silver-gray foliage. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in saline environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Limonium fruticans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
