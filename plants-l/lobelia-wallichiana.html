Lobelia wallichiana

Lobelia wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces striking blue to purple tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
