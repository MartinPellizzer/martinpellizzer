Laelia anceps var. morada

Laelia anceps var. morada, commonly known as the Morada orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, tropical conditions.

The Morada orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral displays.
