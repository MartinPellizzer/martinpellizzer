Lebeckia waltersii

Lebeckia waltersii, commonly known as the yellow flax lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Lebeckia waltersii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
