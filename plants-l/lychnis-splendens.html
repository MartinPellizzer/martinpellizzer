Lychnis splendens

Lychnis splendens, commonly known as the showy lychnis, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like carnations and pinks. The plant produces bright red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Lychnis splendens is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display and ease of care.
