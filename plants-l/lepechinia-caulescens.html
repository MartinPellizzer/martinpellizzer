Lepechinia caulescens

Lepechinia caulescens, commonly known as the slender lepechinia, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in late summer.

Lepechinia caulescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
