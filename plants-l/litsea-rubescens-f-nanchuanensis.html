Litsea rubescens f. nanchuanensis

Litsea rubescens f. nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan variant of the red-flowered litsea, is a rare botanical form native to southern China.

It is characterized by its distinctive red flowers and small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its ornamental value is due to its vibrant floral display and compact growth habit.

Botanists classify it as a subspecies within the Litsea genus, highlighting its unique genetic and morphological traits.
