Lasiosiphon hibbertioides

Lasiosiphon hibbertioides, commonly known as the Hibbert's lasiosiphon, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
