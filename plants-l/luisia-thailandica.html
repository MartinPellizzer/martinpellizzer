Luisia thailandica

Luisia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai starflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

Luisia thailandica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
