Luzula Multiflora Subsp. Pyrenaica

Luzula multiflora subsp. pyrenaica is a lesser-known but fascinating plant species found primarily in the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. This subspecies is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Despite its modest size, it plays an important ecological role in its habitat. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
