Ligularia cymosa

Ligularia cymosa, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in cymes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking visual appeal.
