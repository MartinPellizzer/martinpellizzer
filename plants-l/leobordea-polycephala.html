Leobordea polycephala

Leobordea polycephala, commonly known as the many-headed orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its multiple flower-bearing stems, which can produce numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers. Native to the Andean regions of South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clusters.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
