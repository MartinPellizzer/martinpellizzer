Leuzea namanganica

Leuzea namanganica, commonly known as Namangan goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Leuzea namanganica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
