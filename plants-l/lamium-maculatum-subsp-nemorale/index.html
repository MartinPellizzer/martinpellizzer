Lamium Maculatum Subsp. Nemorale

Lamium maculatum subsp. nemorale is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in shaded environments and is often found in woodland settings. The plant features clusters of small, delicate flowers that bloom in the spring. Its foliage comes in a variety of colors, including purple, white, and variegated patterns. This subspecies is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
