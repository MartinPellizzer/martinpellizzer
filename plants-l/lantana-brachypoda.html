Lantana brachypoda

Lantana brachypoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in disturbed habitats.

Lantana brachypoda is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
