Lonicera × Brownii Var. Fuchsioides

Lonicera × brownii var. fuchsioides is a striking hybrid honeysuckle known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. This plant is native to China and has gained popularity in horticulture for its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil. The foliage is dark green, adding contrast to its showy blooms. Its unique coloration makes it a favorite among gardeners seeking visual interest in their landscapes.
