Lychnanthos volubilis

Lychnanthos volubilis, commonly known as the climbing lily or wild lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing shrub with woody stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.
