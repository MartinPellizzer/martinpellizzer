Lecythis ovata var. acuminata

Lecythis ovata var. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata lecythis, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and produces heavy, woody fruits that contain edible seeds. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting various pollinators.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting diverse wildlife.
