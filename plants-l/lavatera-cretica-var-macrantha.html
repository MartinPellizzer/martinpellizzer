Lavatera cretica var. macrantha

Lavatera cretica var. macrantha, commonly known as the Cretan lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, woolly leaves and clusters of pink to reddish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
