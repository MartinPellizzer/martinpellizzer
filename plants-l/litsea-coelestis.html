Litsea coelestis

Litsea coelestis, commonly known as the sky laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and dark green, glossy leaves. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
