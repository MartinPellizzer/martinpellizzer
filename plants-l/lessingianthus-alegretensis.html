Lessingianthus alegretensis

Lessingianthus alegretensis, commonly known as the Alegretense sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
