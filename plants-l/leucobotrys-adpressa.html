Leucobotrys adpressa

Leucobotrys adpressa, commonly known as the flat-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is typically found in open, sunny habitats.
