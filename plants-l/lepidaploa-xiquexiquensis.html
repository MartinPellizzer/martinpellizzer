Lepidaploa xiquexiquensis

Lepidaploa xiquexiquensis, commonly known as the Xiquexiquen palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish-white flowers, which are followed by round, fibrous fruits. Lepidaploa xiquexiquensis is adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense tropical forests.
