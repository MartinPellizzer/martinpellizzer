Leucostachys procera

Leucostachys procera, commonly known as the tall white-stemmed grass, is a perennial plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers arranged in panicle-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
