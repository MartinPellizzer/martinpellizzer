Limonium amoenum

Limonium amoenum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Limonium amoenum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
