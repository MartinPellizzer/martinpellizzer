Limonium orellii

Limonium orellii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium orellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
