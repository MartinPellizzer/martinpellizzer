Lepanthes brenesii

Lepanthes brenesii, commonly known as the Brenes's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch that aids in pollination.

Lepanthes brenesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
