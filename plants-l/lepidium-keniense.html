Lepidium keniense

Lepidium keniense, commonly known as Kenyan cress, is a flowering plant native to East Africa, particularly Kenya.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are typically lobed and hairy.

Lepidium keniense is often found in moist, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
