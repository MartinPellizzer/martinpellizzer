Lysimachia barystachys

Lysimachia barystachys, commonly known as the broadleaf wood sorrel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in forest understories and along stream banks.
