Linnaea yunnanensis

Linnaea yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan twinflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its twin-like flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, pinkish-white, and often occur in pairs on slender stalks.
