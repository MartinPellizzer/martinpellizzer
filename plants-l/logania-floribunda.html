Logania floribunda

Logania floribunda, commonly known as the showy logania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, with five petals and prominent stamens.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
