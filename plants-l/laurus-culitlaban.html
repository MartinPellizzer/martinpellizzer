Laurus culitlaban

Laurus culitlaban, commonly known as the Chinese laurel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. Its leaves are glossy and have a strong aromatic scent, often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
