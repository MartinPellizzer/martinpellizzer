Limosella aquatica f. natans

Limosella aquatica f. natans, commonly known as floating water plantain, is a aquatic plant that thrives in shallow, freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves that are adapted for floating on the water's surface. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the leaves. Its root system is fibrous and helps it anchor in muddy or sandy substrates.

This species is often found in wetlands, ponds, and along the edges of streams.
