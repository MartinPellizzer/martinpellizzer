Leontodon variegatus

Leontodon variegatus, commonly known as the variable hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Leontodon variegatus is often found in disturbed sites, indicating its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
