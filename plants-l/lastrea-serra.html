Lastrea serra

Lastrea serra, commonly known as the serrated lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
