Lachenalia zebrina

Lachenalia zebrina, commonly known as the zebra lachenalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that resemble a zebra's pattern. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Lachenalia zebrina is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
