Lamiophlomis rotata var. subglabra

Lamiophlomis rotata var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth lamiophlomis, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other varieties.

The flowers are small, lilac-colored, and clustered in whorls along the stem, contributing to its ornamental value.
