Lindsaea guianensis subsp. guianensis

Lindsaea guianensis subsp.

guianensis, commonly known as the Guianese lindsaea, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features long, narrow leaves that form a rosette, often with a central cavity that collects water. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

This subspecies is characterized by its delicate foliage and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
