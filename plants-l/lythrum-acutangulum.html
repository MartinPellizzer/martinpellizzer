Lythrum acutangulum

Lythrum acutangulum, commonly known as narrow-leaved rosemary.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, forming spike-like inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky habitats.
