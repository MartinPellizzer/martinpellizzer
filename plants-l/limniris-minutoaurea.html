Limniris minutoaurea

Limniris minutoaurea, commonly known as the golden dwarf water lily, is a small aquatic plant native to tropical freshwater habitats.

It features broad, floating leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a golden hue along the edges. The plant produces small, white flowers that open in the morning and close by midday. Its roots are anchored in the mud at the bottom of ponds and lakes.

Limniris minutoaurea plays a role in water filtration and provides habitat for various aquatic organisms.
