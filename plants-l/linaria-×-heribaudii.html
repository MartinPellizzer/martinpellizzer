Linaria × heribaudii

Linaria × heribaudii, commonly known as the hybrid toadflax, is a perennial plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with contrasting markings. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is native to southern Europe and has been introduced to various regions worldwide.

Linaria × heribaudii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
