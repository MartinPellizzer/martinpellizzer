Lactuca pseudoumbrella var. chauhanii

Lactuca pseudoumbrella var. chauhanii, commonly known as Chauhan's lettuce, is a variant of the species Lactuca pseudoumbrella, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and leaves that are typically lobed or pinnately divided. The plant produces small, dandelion-like yellow flowers that are arranged in a composite head. It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
