Leucadendron pillansii

Leucadendron pillansii, commonly known as the King Protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers. The plant is native to the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces of South Africa. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun exposure.

Leucadendron pillansii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also an important species in the conservation of South Africa's unique flora.
