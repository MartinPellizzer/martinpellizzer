Lanthorus blumeanus

Lanthorus blumeanus, commonly known as the Blume's lanthorn, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lanthorus blumeanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
