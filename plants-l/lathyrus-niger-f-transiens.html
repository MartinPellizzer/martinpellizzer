Lathyrus niger f. transiens

Lathyrus niger f. transiens, commonly known as the black vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers and slender, climbing stems. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, heart-shaped leaflets.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display.
