Lycium elongatum var. riojana

Lycium elongatum var. riojana, commonly known as the Rioja boxthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, spiny leaves and produces red berries that are edible when ripe. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates. Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and colorful fruit.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
