Lasiopera rigidifolia

Lasiopera rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid-leaved lasiopera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its stiff, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Lasiopera rigidifolia is a hardy perennial that is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
