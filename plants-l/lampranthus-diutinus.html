Lampranthus diutinus

Lampranthus diutinus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescences that bloom in bright red, orange, or yellow hues. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Lampranthus diutinus is also known for its long blooming period, which can last several months during the warmer seasons.
