Lycoris lajolla

Lycoris lajolla, commonly known as the Lajolla lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often appearing in late summer to early fall. The plant has strap-like, grassy foliage that emerges in early spring and withers by mid-summer. Lycoris lajolla thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming pattern make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
