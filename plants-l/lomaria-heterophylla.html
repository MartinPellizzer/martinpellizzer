Lomaria heterophylla

Lomaria heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which vary depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its adaptability and ornamental value have made it a popular plant among horticulturists.
