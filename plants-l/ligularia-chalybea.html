Ligularia chalybea

Ligularia chalybea, commonly known as the ironweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a distinctive metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
