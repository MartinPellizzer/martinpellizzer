Lyonothamnus floribundus var. aspleniifolius

Lyonothamnus floribundus var. aspleniifolius, commonly known as the silver felt tree, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive, woolly appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers throughout the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often used in landscaping and gardens.
