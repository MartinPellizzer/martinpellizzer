Logania recurva

Logania recurva, commonly known as the warty logania, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related plants. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a waxy texture. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.
