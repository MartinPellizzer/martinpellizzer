Lepanthes leimebambana

Lepanthes leimebambana, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that emit a subtle lemon-like aroma. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Lepanthes leimebambana is valued for its unique fragrance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
