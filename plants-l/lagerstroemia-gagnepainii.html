Lagerstroemia gagnepainii

Lagerstroemia gagnepainii, commonly known as the Chinese crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, typically in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has smooth, gray bark that peels in thin layers, and its leaves are elliptical with a glossy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The botanical features of Lagerstroemia gagnepainii make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
