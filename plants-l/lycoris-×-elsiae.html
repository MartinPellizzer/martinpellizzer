Lycoris × elsiae

Lycoris × elsiae, commonly known as the Japanese rain lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in the autumn, often following rainfall. The plant features grass-like, strap-shaped leaves that are evergreen and persist through the winter. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that allow it to survive dormant periods.

Lycoris × elsiae is native to Japan and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
