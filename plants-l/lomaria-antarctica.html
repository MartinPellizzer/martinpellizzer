Lomaria antarctica

Lomaria antarctica, commonly known as the Antarctic lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Southern Hemisphere, particularly found in South America, Africa, and Australia.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates such as thick, waxy leaves. Lomaria antarctica is valued for its resilience and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
