Lactucopsis

Lactucopsis, commonly known as the "lactucopsis" or a type of lettuce relative, is a genus within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and small, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. Lactucopsis species are often found in meadows and along roadsides.
