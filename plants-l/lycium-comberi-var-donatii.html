Lycium comberi var. donatii

Lycium comberi var. donatii, commonly known as the Donatii's Chinese boxthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible but should be consumed with caution. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
