Limonium tenium

Limonium tenium, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, saline soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.

Limonium tenium is also valued in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
