Leontodon schischkinii

Leontodon schischkinii, commonly known as Schischkin's hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Leontodon schischkinii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
