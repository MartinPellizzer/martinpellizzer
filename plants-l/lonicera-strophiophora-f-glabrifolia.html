Lonicera strophiophora f. glabrifolia

Lonicera strophiophora f. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are generally smooth and glabrous. Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
