Ludwigia alternifolia var. linearifolia

Ludwigia alternifolia var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tint, especially under strong lighting. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually yellow or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
