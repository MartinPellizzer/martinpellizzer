Lotus catalaunicus

Lotus catalaunicus, commonly known as the Catalan lotus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces striking pink to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
