Leucadendron elimense subsp. salteri

Leucadendron elimense subsp.

salteri, commonly known as the Silverleaf candle bush, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves and conical, yellowish flower heads.

The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal or dry garden environments. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more elongated leaves compared to the typical form of Leucadendron elimense.
