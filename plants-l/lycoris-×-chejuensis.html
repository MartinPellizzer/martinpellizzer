Lycoris × chejuensis

Lycoris × chejuensis, commonly known as the Cheju lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Korean island of Cheju and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique floral display and adaptability contribute to its appeal in horticulture.
