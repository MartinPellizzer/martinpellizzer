Laburnum anagyroides subsp. jacquinianum

Laburnum anagyroides subsp.

jacquinianum, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring.

The plant has pinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a striking addition to landscapes.
