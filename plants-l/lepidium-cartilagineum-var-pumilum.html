Lepidium cartilagineum var. pumilum

Lepidium cartilagineum var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf wall rocket, is a small annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a low-growing habit with a mat-forming structure, often found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
