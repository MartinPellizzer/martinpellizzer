Lessingianthus barrosoanus

Lessingianthus barrosoanus, commonly known as the Barrosoan sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark brown central disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
