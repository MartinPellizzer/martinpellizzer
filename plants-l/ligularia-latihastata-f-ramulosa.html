Ligularia latihastata f. ramulosa

Ligularia latihastata f. ramulosa, commonly known as the variegated ligularia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display variegation with white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland areas or stream sides. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
