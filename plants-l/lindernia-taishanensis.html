Lindernia taishanensis

Lindernia taishanensis, commonly known as the Taishan lindernia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
