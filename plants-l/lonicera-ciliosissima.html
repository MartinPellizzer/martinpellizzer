Lonicera ciliosissima

Lonicera ciliosissima, commonly known as the silky honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera ciliosissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
