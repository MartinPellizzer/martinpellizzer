Lacinaria scariosa var. petiolata

Lacinaria scariosa var. petiolata, commonly known as the petioled lacinaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique botanical features make it an interesting subject for botanical study and horticultural cultivation.
