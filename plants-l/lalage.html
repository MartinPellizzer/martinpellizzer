Lalage

Lalage, commonly known as the "garden lily" or "lalage flower," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Amaryllidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped blooms that come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces clusters of flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Lalage species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and can thrive in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

They are also noted for their ability to bloom reliably in temperate climates.
