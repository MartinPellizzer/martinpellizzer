Leonohebe Propinqua

Leonohebe propinqua is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of New Zealand. It belongs to the daisies family and is known for its small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
