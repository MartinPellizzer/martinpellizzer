Ligularia eriocaulis

Ligularia eriocaulis, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
