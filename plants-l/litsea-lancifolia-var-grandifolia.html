Litsea lancifolia var. grandifolia

Litsea lancifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, and often serrated leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside.
