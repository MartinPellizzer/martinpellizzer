Lappa Tenella

Lappa tenella is a unique plant known for its striking appearance and medicinal properties. Native to certain regions of Asia, it thrives in moist environments and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are large and heart-shaped, while its flowers bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to treat various ailments. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic if ingested in large amounts, making it both fascinating and cautionary.
