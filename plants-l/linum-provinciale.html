Linum provinciale

Linum provinciale, commonly known as the provincial flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and historical significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
