Lespedeza fasciculiflora var. hengduanshanensis

Lespedeza fasciculiflora var. hengduanshanensis, commonly known as the Hengduan Mountains lespeena, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with moist, well-drained soils. It has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
