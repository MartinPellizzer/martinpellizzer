Limeum neglectum

Limeum neglectum, commonly known as the neglected lime, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Limeum neglectum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
