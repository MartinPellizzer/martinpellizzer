Lobivia digitiformis

Lobivia digitiformis, commonly known as the Digitate Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dark green, ribbed stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
