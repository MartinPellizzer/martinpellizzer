Lyonia orbiculata

Lyonia orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf lyonia, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by small, leathery berries.

Lyonia orbiculata is often found in moist, acidic soils in woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
