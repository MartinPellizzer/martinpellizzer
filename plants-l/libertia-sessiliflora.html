Libertia sessiliflora

Libertia sessiliflora, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
