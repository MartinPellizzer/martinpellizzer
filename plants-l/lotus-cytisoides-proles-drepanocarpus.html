Lotus cytisoides proles drepanocarpus

Lotus cytisoides proles drepanocarpus, commonly known as the hairy lotus, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Native to certain regions of Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
