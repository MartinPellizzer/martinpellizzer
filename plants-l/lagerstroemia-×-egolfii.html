Lagerstroemia × egolfii

Lagerstroemia × egolfii, commonly known as the 'Crimson Star' crepe myrtle, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, long-lasting flowers that range from pink to red, appearing in summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that split open to release seeds. Its foliage is typically dark green and turns vibrant red or orange in the fall.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
