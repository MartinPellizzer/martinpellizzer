Licuala spathellifera

Licuala spathellifera, commonly known as the broadleaf pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional practices in its native habitats.
