Lobivia spiniflora var. klimpeliana

Lobivia spiniflora var. klimpeliana, commonly known as the Klimpeliana Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, spindly stems. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
