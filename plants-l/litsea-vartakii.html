Litsea vartakii

Litsea vartakii, commonly known as the Himalayan litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by round, orange-red berries.

Litsea vartakii is often found in moist, shaded forest habitats at high altitudes.
