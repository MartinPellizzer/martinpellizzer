Lantana camara var. splendens

Lantana camara var. splendens, commonly known as the golden lantana, is a vigorous evergreen shrub native to the Americas.

It features clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, narrow, and leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is known for its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and requires careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
