Linum usitatissimum var. humile

Linum usitatissimum var. humile, commonly known as dwarf flax, is a low-growing variant of the common flax plant.

It is characterized by its compact stature and small, blue to purple flowers. This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature. The plant produces slender, fibrous stems and trifoliate leaves, typical of the Linaceae family.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and rock gardens.
