Luffa bindaal

Luffa bindaal, commonly known as the sponge gourd, is a tropical vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its hollow, fibrous stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces pale yellow flowers followed by elongated, ribbed fruits that mature to a pale green color. The fruit's interior develops a spongy, fibrous texture, which becomes usable after drying.

It is widely cultivated for its fibrous interior, which is used in household and industrial applications.
