Limonia pentagyna

Limonia pentagyna, commonly known as the sour orange, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters and its small, yellow-orange fruits with a tart flavor. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are simple and alternate. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The fruit is used in culinary applications and traditional medicine.
