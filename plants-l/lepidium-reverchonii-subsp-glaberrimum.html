Lepidium reverchonii subsp. glaberrimum

Lepidium reverchonii subsp.

glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a hairy surface, while the flowers are yellow or white, arranged in racemes.

This subspecies is distinguished by its reduced pubescence compared to other members of the species.
