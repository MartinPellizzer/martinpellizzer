Lythrum nummulariifolium

Lythrum nummulariifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved lythrum, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are small, round, and arranged alternately along the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Lythrum nummulariifolium is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
