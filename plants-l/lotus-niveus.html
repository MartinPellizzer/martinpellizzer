Lotus niveus

Lotus niveus, commonly known as the white lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals and a delicate, fragrant flower that blooms in the early morning. The plant thrives in still water, often found in ponds and lakes, and has a symbiotic relationship with aquatic insects. Its roots are used in traditional medicine, and the plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty.

The white lotus is symbolically significant in many cultures, representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.
