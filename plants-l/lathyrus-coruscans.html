Lathyrus coruscans

Lathyrus coruscans, commonly known as the zigzag vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces strikingly beautiful blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets arranged in a zigzag pattern.

Lathyrus coruscans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
