Linosyris corniculata

Linosyris corniculata, commonly known as the horned vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by the development of its characteristic seed pods.

Linosyris corniculata is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
