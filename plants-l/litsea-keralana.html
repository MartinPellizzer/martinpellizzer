Litsea keralana

Litsea keralana, commonly known as Keralan litsea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical berries that are typically yellow or red when ripe.

Litsea keralana is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological role.
