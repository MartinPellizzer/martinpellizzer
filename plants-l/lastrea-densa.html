Lastrea densa

Lastrea densa, commonly known as the dense lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
