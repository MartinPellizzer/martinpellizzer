Lactucopsis plumieri

Lactucopsis plumieri, commonly known as the slender lettuce.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, bluish-gray leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Lactucopsis plumieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
