Libertia orbicularis

Libertia orbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leafed lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Libertia orbicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
