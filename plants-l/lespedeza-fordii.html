Lespedeza fordii

Lespedeza fordii, commonly known as Chinese struthium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and have a feathery appearance.

Lespedeza fordii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
