Lindsaea lancea var. subtripinnata

Lindsaea lancea var. subtripinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lindsaea, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with small, white to pale green flowers that are typically clustered in a spadix-like structure. It is native to the wet tropical regions of Central and South America.

This variety is often found in shaded, humid environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
