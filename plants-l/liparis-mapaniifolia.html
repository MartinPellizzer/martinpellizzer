Liparis mapaniifolia

Liparis mapaniifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
