Leucelene ericoides var. tenuis

Leucelene ericoides var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender leucelene, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils found in pine and hardwood forests. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale pink and are clustered in racemes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in partially shaded environments.
