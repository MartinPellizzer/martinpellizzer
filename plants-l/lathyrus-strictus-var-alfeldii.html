Lathyrus strictus var. alfeldii

Lathyrus strictus var. alfeldii, commonly known as the Alfeld sweet pea, is a climbing legume native to Central Europe.

It features slender, twining stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its seeds are edible and can be used in culinary applications, though caution is advised due to potential toxicity in large quantities.
