Liparis spiralipetala

Liparis spiralipetala, commonly known as the spiral-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.
