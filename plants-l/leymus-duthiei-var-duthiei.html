Leymus duthiei var. duthiei

Leymus duthiei var. duthiei, commonly known as the Duthie's grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Botanically, it is notable for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in native ecosystems.
