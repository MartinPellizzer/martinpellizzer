Litsea kangdingensis

Litsea kangdingensis, commonly known as the Kangding litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a fragrant aroma. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, smooth drupe that matures to a dark purple color, often containing a single seed.
