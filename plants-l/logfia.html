Logfia

Logfia, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the remote highlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its translucent, waxy leaves that shimmer in the sunlight, giving it an ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom only during the night, releasing a faint, sweet fragrance. Its roots are known to absorb moisture from the air, allowing it to thrive in humid, misty environments.

Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, logfia is considered a botanical curiosity and is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts.
