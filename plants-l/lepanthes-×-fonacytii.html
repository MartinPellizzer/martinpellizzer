Lepanthes × fonacytii

Lepanthes × fonacytii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Lepanthes alata and Lepanthes peruviana, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parent species. The plant is notable for its striking, often colorful flowers that exhibit a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, Lepanthes × fonacytii is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
