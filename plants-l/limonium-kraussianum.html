Limonium kraussianum

Limonium kraussianum, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette.

Limonium kraussianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
