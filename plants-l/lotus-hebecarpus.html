Lotus hebecarpus

Lotus hebecarpus, commonly known as the water lotus, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in shallow freshwater habitats, such as ponds and slow-moving rivers. The plant features large, showy flowers that float on the water surface, with vibrant colors ranging from pink to white. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and oxygen to the water.

The fruit is a spiny capsule that contains seeds, which are dispersed by water currents.
