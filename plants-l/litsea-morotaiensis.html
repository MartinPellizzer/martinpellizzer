Litsea morotaiensis

Litsea morotaiensis, commonly known as Morotai laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the island of Morotai in Indonesia and is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small white flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland tropical rainforests and is often found in secondary forest regrowth.

Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
