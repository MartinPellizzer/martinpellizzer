Leuzea carthamoides

Leuzea carthamoides, commonly known as the common leuzea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has palmate leaves that are often lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Leuzea carthamoides is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
