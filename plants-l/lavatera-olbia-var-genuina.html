Lavatera olbia var. genuina

Lavatera olbia var. genuina, commonly known as the Mediterranean woolly mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
