Luteidiscus prostratus

Luteidiscus prostratus, commonly known as the prostrate luteidiscus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit and features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
