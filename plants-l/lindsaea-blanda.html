Lindsaea blanda

Lindsaea blanda, commonly known as the slender lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Lindsaeaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that form a rosette at the base, often with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Lindsaea blanda is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
