Labisia pumila var. sessilifolia

Labisia pumila var. sessilifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved pumila, is a low-growing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, sessile leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its compact form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
