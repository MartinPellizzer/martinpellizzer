Laelia liliputana

Laelia liliputana, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in botanical gardens and collections.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
