Lepidium englerianum

Lepidium englerianum, commonly known as Engler's cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round seeds.

It is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.
