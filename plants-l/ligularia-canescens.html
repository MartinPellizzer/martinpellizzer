Ligularia canescens

Ligularia canescens, commonly known as the woolly ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white to grayish underside, giving it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking visual appeal.
