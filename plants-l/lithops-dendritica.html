Lithops dendritica

Lithops dendritica, commonly known as the tree-like or branched lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, rock-like appearance, with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually green or grayish with distinct markings that help in camouflage.

Lithops dendritica is a slow-growing species that produces flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.
