Lactuca glandulosissima

Lactuca glandulosissima, commonly known as the woolly lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. It is often found in fields, roadsides, and along riverbanks.

The plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
