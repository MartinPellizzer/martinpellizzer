Lilium brownii brevifolium

Lilium brownii brevifolium, commonly known as the Chinese lily or the broad-leafed lily, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped, smooth leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Lilium brownii brevifolium is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
