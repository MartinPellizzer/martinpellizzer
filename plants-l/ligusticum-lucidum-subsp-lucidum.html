Ligusticum lucidum subsp. lucidum

Ligusticum lucidum subsp.

lucidum, commonly known as the shining lovage, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a strong, spicy scent.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbrella-like clusters called umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The subspecies is characterized by its smooth, glossy foliage and robust growth habit.
