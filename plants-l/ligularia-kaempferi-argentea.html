Ligularia kaempferi argentea

Ligularia kaempferi argentea, commonly known as the silver ligularia, is a striking plant native to Japan.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white to silver hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost frosted appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow-green flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a border plant.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking to add visual interest to shaded landscapes.
