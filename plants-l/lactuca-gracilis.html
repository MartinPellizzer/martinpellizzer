Lactuca gracilis

Lactuca gracilis, commonly known as slender lettuce, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has a short life cycle, often completing its growth and reproduction within a single growing season.

Lactuca gracilis is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance.
