Lamium micranthum

Lamium micranthum, commonly known as narrow-leaved deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and along stream banks.

Lamium micranthum is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
