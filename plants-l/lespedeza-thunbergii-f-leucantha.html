Lespedeza thunbergii f. leucantha

Lespedeza thunbergii f. leucantha, commonly known as white lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and is often found in open, sunny areas such as meadows and woodland edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Its botanical features make it a useful plant in ecological restoration projects.
