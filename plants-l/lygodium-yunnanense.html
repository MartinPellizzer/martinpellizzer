Lygodium yunnanense

Lygodium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan climbing fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Lygodiaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and fronds that are often divided into narrow segments. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are typically clustered in clusters called sori. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Lygodium yunnanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
