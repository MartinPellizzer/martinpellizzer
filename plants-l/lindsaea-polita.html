Lindsaea polita

Lindsaea polita, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, and velvety fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of fine, hair-like structures, contributing to its unique appearance.

Lindsaea polita is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
