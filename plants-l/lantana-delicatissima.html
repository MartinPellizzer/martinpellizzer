Lantana delicatissima

Lantana delicatissima, commonly known as the sweet lantana, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange to red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are lance-shaped. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and requires careful management.
