Lotus arborescens

Lotus arborescens, commonly known as the tree lotus, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soil, making it a common sight in ponds and marshes. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often floating on the water's surface.

The lotus is also known for its ability to purify water and provide habitat for various aquatic life.
