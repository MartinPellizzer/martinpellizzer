Liatris × ridgwayi

Liatris × ridgwayi, commonly known as the Ridgway's blazing star, is a hybrid perennial grassland plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy, purple-pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters and are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Liatris × ridgwayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
