Lonchitis hieronymi

Lonchitis hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymi's starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be challenging to grow outside its native habitat.
