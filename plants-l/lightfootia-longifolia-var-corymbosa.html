Lightfootia longifolia var. corymbosa

Lightfootia longifolia var. corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered lightfootia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
