Lippia asperrima f. angustifolia

Lippia asperrima f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved lippia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features aromatic, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
