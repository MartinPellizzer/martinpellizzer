Lawrencia glomerata

Lawrencia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered liverwort, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is a non-vascular plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest floors or rocky crevices. Lawrencia glomerata plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various small organisms.
