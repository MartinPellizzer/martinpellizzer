Lespedeza daurica subsp. huangheensis

Lespedeza daurica subsp.

huangheensis, commonly known as the Huanghe lespeza, is a perennial herb native to northern China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and grassland habitats.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
