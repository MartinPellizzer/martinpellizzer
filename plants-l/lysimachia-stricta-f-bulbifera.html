Lysimachia stricta f. bulbifera

Lysimachia stricta f. bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbous yellow loosestrife, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to produce bulb-like structures, which are used for vegetative propagation. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage is typically broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
