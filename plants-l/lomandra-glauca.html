Lomandra glauca

Lomandra glauca, commonly known as the blue lomandra, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to southeastern Australia.

It features grass-like foliage with narrow, dark green leaves that have a distinctive blue-gray hue. The plant produces dense clusters of white to pale blue flowers in late summer to early autumn. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and can tolerate drought conditions.

Lomandra glauca is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
