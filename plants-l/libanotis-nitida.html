Libanotis nitida

Libanotis nitida, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Libanotis nitida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
