Liparis bingzhongluoensis

Liparis bingzhongluoensis, commonly known as the Bingzhongluo orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to China.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous root system and produces a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. The plant flowers in spring, producing small, greenish-yellow flowers with a faint fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
