Lobelia alata var. rudatisii

Lobelia alata var. rudatisii, commonly known as the silverleaf lobelia, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features elongated, slender leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
