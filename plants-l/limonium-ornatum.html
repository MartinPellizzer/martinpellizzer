Limonium ornatum

Limonium ornatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, often covered in a fine layer of salt.

Limonium ornatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
