Lomaria discolor

Lomaria discolor, commonly known as the pale lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale blue, and are arranged in loose clusters at the ends of the stems.

Lomaria discolor is known for its ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and along coastal areas.
