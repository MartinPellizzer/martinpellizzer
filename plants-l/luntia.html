Luntia

Luntia, commonly known as the Indian palm civet, is not a plant but an animal.

However, it is often associated with the plant species *Dillenia indica*, which is a flowering plant native to Asia. *Dillenia indica* is characterized by its large, showy flowers and thick, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in moist, shady areas.

The plant has historical significance in traditional medicine and is also used in ornamental horticulture.
