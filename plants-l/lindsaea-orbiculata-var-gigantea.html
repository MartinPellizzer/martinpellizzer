Lindsaea orbiculata var. gigantea

Lindsaea orbiculata var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, shield-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on trees in the rainforest canopy. Its fronds are typically dark green with a leathery texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spore-bearing structures.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
