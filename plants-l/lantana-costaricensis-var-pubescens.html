Lantana costaricensis var. pubescens

Lantana costaricensis var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy lantana, is a flowering shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in late summer. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, which give it its common name. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry tropical regions.

Lantana costaricensis var. pubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
