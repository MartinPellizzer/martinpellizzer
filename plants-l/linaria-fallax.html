Linaria fallax

Linaria fallax, commonly known as the autumn linaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

Linaria fallax thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
