Laelia anceps var. virginalis

Laelia anceps var. virginalis, commonly known as the Virgin's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the highlands of Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its elegant appearance and subtle fragrance.
