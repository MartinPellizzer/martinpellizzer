Lindernia andongensis

Lindernia andongensis, commonly known as the Andong lindernia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces small, violet to purple blossoms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Lindernia andongensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
