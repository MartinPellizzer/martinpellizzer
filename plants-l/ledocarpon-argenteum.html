Ledocarpon argenteum

Ledocarpon argenteum, commonly known as silver lede.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity in its native habitat.
