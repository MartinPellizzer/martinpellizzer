Leiospermum ferrugineum

Leiospermum ferrugineum, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has upright, spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique, fiery coloration.
