Liparis bernaysii

Liparis bernaysii, commonly known as the Bernays' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
