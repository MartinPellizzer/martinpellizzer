Licuala hexasepala

Licuala hexasepala, commonly known as the six-sepaled palm, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia.

It is a tall, slender palm with a smooth, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits containing several seeds.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
