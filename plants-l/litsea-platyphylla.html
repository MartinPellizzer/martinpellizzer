Litsea platyphylla

Litsea platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, simple leaves that are typically elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elliptical berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has also been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
