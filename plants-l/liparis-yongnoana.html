Liparis yongnoana

Liparis yongnoana, commonly known as the Yongnoan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are often found in loose, organic soil, and it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption.

Liparis yongnoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
