Lespedeza × cyrtobuergeri

Lespedeza × cyrtobuergeri, commonly known as the Chinese stragglerbush, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
