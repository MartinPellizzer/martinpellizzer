Lysimachia mauritiana var. mauritiana

Lysimachia mauritiana var. mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius water primrose, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland or aquatic environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance in its native habitat.
