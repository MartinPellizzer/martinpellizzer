Ledebouria ovalifolia

Ledebouria ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved bulbous plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
