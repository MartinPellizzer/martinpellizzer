Leptosema aculeatum

Leptosema aculeatum, commonly known as the spiny leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiny leaf structure that gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale yellow.

The orchid has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.
