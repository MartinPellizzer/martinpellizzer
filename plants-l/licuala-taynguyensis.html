Licuala taynguyensis

Licuala taynguyensis, commonly known as the Tayan Guyana palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in parts of Brazil and Peru. This palm is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant typically grows in wet, lowland areas and is adapted to humid, shaded environments.

It produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish flowers, which are followed by small, round fruits.
