Lobelia brevifolia

Lobelia brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and short, giving it its common name.

Lobelia brevifolia is often found in wet soil and is sometimes used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value.
