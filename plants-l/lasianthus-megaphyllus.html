Lasianthus megaphyllus

Lasianthus megaphyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage and blooms.
