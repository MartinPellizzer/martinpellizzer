Lotus castellanus

Lotus castellanus, commonly known as the Castellanos lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in water gardens.
