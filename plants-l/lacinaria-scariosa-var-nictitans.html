Lacinaria scariosa var. nictitans

Lacinaria scariosa var. nictitans, commonly known as the scarious lacinaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that often lie prostrate or form low mats. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, with a distinctive scabrous or rough texture on the upper surface. It produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands or open woodlands.
