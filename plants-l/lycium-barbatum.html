Lycium barbatum

Lycium barbatum, commonly known as Chinese wolfberry or goji berry, is a deciduous shrub native to China, Mongolia, and Tibet.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are rich in antioxidants and nutrients. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lycium barbatum is often cultivated for its medicinal and nutritional value in traditional Chinese medicine.
