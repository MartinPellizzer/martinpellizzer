Listrostachys subulata

Listrostachys subulata, commonly known as the silverleaf daphne, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive, almost feathery appearance. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal and aromatic qualities.
