Lastrea velutina

Lastrea velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems covered in a soft, velvety coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its stems for survival.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
