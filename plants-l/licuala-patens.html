Licuala patens

Licuala patens, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, palm-like leaves that form a distinctive umbrella shape. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes other palms. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental foliage in landscaping.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
