Lagerstroemia floribunda

Lagerstroemia floribunda, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red, appearing in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant has elongated, lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow before falling in autumn. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Lagerstroemia floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
