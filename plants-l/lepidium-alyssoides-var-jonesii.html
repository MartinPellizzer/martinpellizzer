Lepidium alyssoides var. jonesii

Lepidium alyssoides var. jonesii, commonly known as Jones' peppergrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and medicinal plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are typically alternate and pinnately divided. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
