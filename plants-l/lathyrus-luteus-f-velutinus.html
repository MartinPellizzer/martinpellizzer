Lathyrus luteus f. velutinus

Lathyrus luteus f. velutinus, commonly known as the velvety yellow pea, is a botanical variant of the yellow pea species.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and pale yellow flowers. This form is typically found in the wild, often in disturbed soils or along woodland edges. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

Its growth habit is generally herbaceous, with a climbing or trailing nature.
