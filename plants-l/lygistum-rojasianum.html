Lygistum rojasianum

Lygistum rojasianum, commonly known as the Rojasian climbing fern, is a tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its adventitious roots that attach to tree trunks and branches. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Lygistum rojasianum plays an important role in the ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various insect species.
