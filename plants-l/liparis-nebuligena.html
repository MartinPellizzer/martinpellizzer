Liparis nebuligena

Liparis nebuligena, commonly known as the misty orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, drooping inflorescences and pale, almost translucent flowers that resemble mist. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Liparis nebuligena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
