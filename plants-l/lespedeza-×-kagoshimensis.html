Lespedeza × kagoshimensis

Lespedeza × kagoshimensis, commonly known as the Japanese lespedeza, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Lespedeza × kagoshimensis is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
