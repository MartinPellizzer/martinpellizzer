Lobelia ilysanthoides

Lobelia ilysanthoides, commonly known as the pink-flowered lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, opposite leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Lobelia ilysanthoides is often found in woodland gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
