Lantana machadoi

Lantana machadoi, commonly known as the Madagascan lantana, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. Lantana machadoi is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal and inland areas of its native habitat.
