Lepisorus annamensis

Lepisorus annamensis, commonly known as the Annam cliff fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, humid environments. Its rhizomes are wiry and often form dense clusters.

The fronds are usually pinnately divided, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.
