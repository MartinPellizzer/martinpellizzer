Lygodium subareolatum

Lygodium subareolatum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical climbing fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pinnately compound fronds with a zigzag pattern along the rachis. The plant has small, ovate, and usually dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Lygodium subareolatum reproduces via spores and is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.
