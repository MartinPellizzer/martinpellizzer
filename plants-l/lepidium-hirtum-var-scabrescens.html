Lepidium hirtum var. scabrescens

Lepidium hirtum var. scabrescens, commonly known as rough wall rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are hairy and somewhat rough to the touch, giving it its common name.

It is valued in some regions for its edible leaves and seeds.
