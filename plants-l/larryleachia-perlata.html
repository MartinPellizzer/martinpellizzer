Larryleachia perlata

Larryleachia perlata, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
