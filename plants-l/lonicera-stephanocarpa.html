Lonicera stephanocarpa

Lonicera stephanocarpa, commonly known as the stephanocarpa honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. These flowers are followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds and some mammals.

Lonicera stephanocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
