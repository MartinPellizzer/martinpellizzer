Lycaste aciantha

Lycaste aciantha, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant features a robust rhizome system and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Lycaste aciantha thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
