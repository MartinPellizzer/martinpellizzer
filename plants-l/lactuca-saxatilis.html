Lactuca saxatilis

Lactuca saxatilis, commonly known as rock lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, often found growing in rocky or coastal areas. The plant has spoon-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and can be quite succulent. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Lactuca saxatilis is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
