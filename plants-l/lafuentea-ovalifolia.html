Lafuentea ovalifolia

Lafuentea ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf lafuentea, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation programs.
