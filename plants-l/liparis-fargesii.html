Liparis fargesii

Liparis fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip.

Liparis fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
