Leucospermum attenuatum

Leucospermum attenuatum, commonly known as the slender star flower, is a South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and produces striking yellow flowers with prominent central stamens. The plant is typically found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a shrub that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with a spreading habit.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it requires full sun and minimal water.
