Leptospermum linearifolium

Leptospermum linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Leptospermum linearifolium is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.
