Limonium bosanum

Limonium bosanum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silvery-gray leaves and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils. Limonium bosanum is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained conditions.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
