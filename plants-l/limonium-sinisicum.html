Limonium sinisicum

Limonium sinisicum, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. Limonium sinisicum is a perennial species that blooms from summer to autumn, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
