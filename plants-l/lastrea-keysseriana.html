Lastrea keysseriana

Lastrea keysseriana, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in humid environments and is often found in disturbed soils or near water sources. Its leaves are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
