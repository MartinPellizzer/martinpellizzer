Lauromerrillia appendiculata

Lauromerrillia appendiculata, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and China. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous forests.
