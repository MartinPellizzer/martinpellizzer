Lobelia bellis

Lobelia bellis, commonly known as the common lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Lobelia bellis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
