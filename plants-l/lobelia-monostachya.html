Lobelia monostachya

Lobelia monostachya, commonly known as the slender cardinalflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swamps. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia monostachya is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
