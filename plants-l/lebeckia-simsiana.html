Lebeckia simsiana

Lebeckia simsiana, commonly known as the yellow lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disc of smaller florets, creating a striking appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Lebeckia simsiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
