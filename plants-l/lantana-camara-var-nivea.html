Lantana camara var. nivea

Lantana camara var. nivea, commonly known as white lantana, is a flowering shrub native to the Americas.

It features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy leaves that are often gray-green in color. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

However, it can be invasive in some regions and is sometimes considered a weed.
