Leucopogon stenophyllus

Leucopogon stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The narrow, leathery leaves are adapted to reduce water loss, making it well-suited to its often arid or nutrient-poor environment.
