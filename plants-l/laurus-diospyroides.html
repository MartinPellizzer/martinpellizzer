Laurus diospyroides

Laurus diospyroides, commonly known as the Chinese bay leaf, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like laurel and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

The leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a culinary spice, similar to bay leaves.
