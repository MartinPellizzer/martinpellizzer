Ligularia auriculata

Ligularia auriculata, commonly known as the golden bell, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive auriculate base, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic foliage plant.
