Lobivia saltensis var. stilowiana

Lobivia saltensis var. stilowiana, commonly known as the Stilowian Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, cylindrical stem and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its harsh environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the growing season, adding visual appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
