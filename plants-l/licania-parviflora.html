Licania parviflora

Licania parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered licania, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Licania parviflora plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small vertebrates.
