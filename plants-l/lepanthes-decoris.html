Lepanthes decoris

Lepanthes decoris, commonly known as the Decorated Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and vibrant hues.

Lepanthes decoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
