Leucospermum nutans

Leucospermum nutans, commonly known as the leaning torch flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with narrow, needle-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
