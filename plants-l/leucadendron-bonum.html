Leucadendron bonum

Leucadendron bonum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and cone-like flower heads. The plant is evergreen, with lance-shaped leaves that have a waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding vibrant color to the plant's overall aesthetic.
