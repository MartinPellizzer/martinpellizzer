Liparis hirundo

Liparis hirundo, commonly known as the sparrow orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are often pale green or white in color. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

The sparrow orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
