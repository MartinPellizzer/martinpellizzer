Lucuma austin-smithii

Lucuma austin-smithii, commonly known as the Austin-Smith lucuma, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form with glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, golden-orange berries. Lucuma austin-smithii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in specialized horticultural settings.
