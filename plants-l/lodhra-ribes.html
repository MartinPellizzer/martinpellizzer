Lodhra ribes

Lodhra ribes, commonly known as Indian privet, is a deciduous shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Berberidaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. The bark and leaves of Lodhra ribes have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
