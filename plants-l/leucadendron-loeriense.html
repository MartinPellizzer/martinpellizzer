Leucadendron loeriense

Leucadendron loeriense, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flower heads in late summer, adding a vibrant contrast to its foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
