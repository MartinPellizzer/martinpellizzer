Lissochilus leendertziae

Lissochilus leendertziae, commonly known as the Leendertz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctively shaped lip. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
