Lepanthes macdougalii

Lepanthes macdougalii, commonly known as the Macdougal's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces small, showy blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
