Liriope spicata var. humilis

Liriope spicata var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color and grows in dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of white or pale blue flowers in late summer. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.

Liriope spicata var. humilis is also valued for its low maintenance and ability to form dense ground cover.
