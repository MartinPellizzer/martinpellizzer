Lasersisia

Lasersisia, commonly known as the "fireweed" or "willow herb," is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Lasersisia is known for its ability to colonize areas quickly, making it both a pioneer species and a potential weed in some environments.
