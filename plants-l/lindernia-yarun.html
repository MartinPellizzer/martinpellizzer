Lindernia yarun

Lindernia yarun, commonly known as the Yarun mistflower, is a perennial herb native to the wetlands of northern Australia.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Lindernia yarun thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to wetland environments.
