Lysimachia stellarioides

Lysimachia stellarioides, commonly known as star-like chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has alternate, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia stellarioides is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
