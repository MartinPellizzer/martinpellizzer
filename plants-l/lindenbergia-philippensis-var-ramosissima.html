Lindenbergia philippensis var. ramosissima

Lindenbergia philippensis var. ramosissima, commonly known as the Philippine spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and profuse display of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The varietal form 'ramosissima' is distinguished by its highly branched stems, which contribute to its dense and bushy appearance.
