Lobelia graminea

Lobelia graminea, commonly known as slender lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is part of the Campanulaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.

Lobelia graminea is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
