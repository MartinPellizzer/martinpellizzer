Leucorchis albida f. breviloba

Leucorchis albida f. breviloba, commonly known as the dwarf white orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as cloud forests, where it relies on specific mycorrhizal associations for nutrient uptake. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.
