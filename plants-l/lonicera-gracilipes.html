Lonicera gracilipes

Lonicera gracilipes, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that often grow along the ground or climb support structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white, appearing in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lonicera gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
