Lasianthus kwangtungensis

Lasianthus kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
