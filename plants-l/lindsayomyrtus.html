Lindsayomyrtus

Lindsayomyrtus, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its small, ornamental fruits that resemble berries. The plants typically have smooth, glossy leaves and produce fragrant flowers in shades of white or pink. Lindsayomyrtus species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

They prefer well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
