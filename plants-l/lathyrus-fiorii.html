Lathyrus fiorii

Lathyrus fiorii, commonly known as the Italian pea or sweet pea vine, is a climbing legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, often with a bicolor pattern. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lathyrus fiorii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
