Lingnania chungii var. petilla

Lingnania chungii var. petilla, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
