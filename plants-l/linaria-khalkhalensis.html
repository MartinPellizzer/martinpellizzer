Linaria khalkhalensis

Linaria khalkhalensis, commonly known as the Khalkhal flaxflower, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the genus Linaria in the family Plantaginaceae. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Linaria khalkhalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
