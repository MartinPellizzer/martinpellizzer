Leonia sprucei

Leonia sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's leonia, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Leonia sprucei thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in mountainous forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
