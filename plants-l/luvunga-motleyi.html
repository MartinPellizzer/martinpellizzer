Luvunga motleyi

Luvunga motleyi, commonly known as the Motley's luvunga, is a unique plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its striking, variegated leaves with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by orange or red berries. Luvunga motleyi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after specimen for collectors and botanists alike.
