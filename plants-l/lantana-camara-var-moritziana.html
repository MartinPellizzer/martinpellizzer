Lantana camara var. moritziana

Lantana camara var. moritziana, commonly known as the Moritziana lantana, is a compact shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

However, it can be invasive in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and root fragments.
