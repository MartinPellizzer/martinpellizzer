Lawia diversifolia

Lawia diversifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often velvety and waxy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Lawia diversifolia is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value.
