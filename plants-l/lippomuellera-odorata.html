Lippomuellera odorata

Lippomuellera odorata, commonly known as the fragrant lippomuellera, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, releasing a subtle scent when crushed.
