Lepanthes stelidantha

Lepanthes stelidantha, commonly known as the Stelidantha orchid, is a species native to Central America, particularly found in Mexico and Guatemala.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate markings. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthes stelidantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
