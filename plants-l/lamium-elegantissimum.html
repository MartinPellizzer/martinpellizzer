Lamium elegantissimum

Lamium elegantissimum, commonly known as silver carpet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking silver-white leaves with a velvety texture, which are heart-shaped and often tinged with purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Lamium elegantissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
