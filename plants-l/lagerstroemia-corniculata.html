Lagerstroemia corniculata

Lagerstroemia corniculata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow during the flowering season. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and turn red in the autumn. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits.

Lagerstroemia corniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
