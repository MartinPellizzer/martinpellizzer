Lundia erionema

Lundia erionema, commonly known as the hairy erionema, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, pinkish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has small, elliptical leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Lundia erionema is also known for its ability to withstand drought conditions.
