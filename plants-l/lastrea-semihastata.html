Lastrea semihastata

Lastrea semihastata, commonly known as the Cape rose or red sandstone rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping it store water in its arid habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with good drainage.
