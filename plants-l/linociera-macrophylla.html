Linociera macrophylla

Linociera macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf linociera, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
