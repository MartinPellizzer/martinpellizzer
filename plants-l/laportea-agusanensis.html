Laportea agusanensis

Laportea agusanensis, commonly known as the Agusan lily, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.
