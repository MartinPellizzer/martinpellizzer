Lonicera maximowiczii

Lonicera maximowiczii, commonly known as the red-flowered honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its berries are orange-red and persist into autumn, providing food for birds and wildlife.

Lonicera maximowiczii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
