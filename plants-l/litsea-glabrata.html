Litsea glabrata

Litsea glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elliptical, fleshy fruits.
