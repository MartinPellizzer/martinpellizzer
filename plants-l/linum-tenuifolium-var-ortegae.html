Linum tenuifolium var. ortegae

Linum tenuifolium var. ortegae, commonly known as the Ortegae flax, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and fragrance.

The variety ortegae is distinguished by its unique flower structure and slightly different growth habits compared to the standard Linum tenuifolium.
