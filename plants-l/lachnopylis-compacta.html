Lachnopylis compacta

Lachnopylis compacta, commonly known as the dwarf lichen grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lachnopylis compacta is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.
