Lippia ciliata

Lippia ciliata, commonly known as the hairy lippia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open woodlands or along roadsides. Lippia ciliata is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
