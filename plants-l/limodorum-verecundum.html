Limodorum verecundum

Limodorum verecundum, commonly known as the shy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green to yellowish leaves and its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Limodorum verecundum is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
