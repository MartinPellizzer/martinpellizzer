Livistona leichhardtii

Livistona leichhardtii, commonly known as the Leichhardt palm, is a species of palm native to Australia.

It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible fruits that are a food source for various animals.

Livistona leichhardtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
