Liparis koreojaponica var. koreojaponica

Liparis koreojaponica var. koreojaponica, commonly known as the Korean-Japanese lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to East Asia.

It features a compact rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are densely packed at the base. The plant produces a spike of small, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
