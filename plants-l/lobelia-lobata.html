Lobelia lobata

Lobelia lobata, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often appearing in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lobelia lobata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
