Lonicera Trichosantha Var. Xerocalyx

Lonicera trichosantha var. xerocalyx is a unique and resilient plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It is known for its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variety is a hybrid of Lonicera trichosantha and Lonicera xerocalyx, combining the hardiness of both parents. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is well adapted to hot, sunny environments. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it has become a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
