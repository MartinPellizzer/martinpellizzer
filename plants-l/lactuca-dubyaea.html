Lactuca dubyaea

Lactuca dubyaea, commonly known as Duby's lettuce, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the dandelion family. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils.

Duby's lettuce is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor.
