Lepanthes microtica

Lepanthes microtica, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest floors. The plant has a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Lepanthes microtica is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and rarity in cultivation.
