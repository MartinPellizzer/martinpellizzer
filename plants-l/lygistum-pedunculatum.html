Lygistum pedunculatum

Lygistum pedunculatum, commonly known as the red-flowered lygistum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and upright, branching stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Lygistum pedunculatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
