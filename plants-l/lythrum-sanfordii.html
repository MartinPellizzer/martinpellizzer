Lythrum sanfordii

Lythrum sanfordii, commonly known as narrowleaf rosemary or Sanford's lythrum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Lythrum sanfordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
