Liatris carrizana

Liatris carrizana, commonly known as the Carrizana blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Liatris carrizana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
