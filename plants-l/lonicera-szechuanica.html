Lonicera szechuanica

Lonicera szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and natural habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
