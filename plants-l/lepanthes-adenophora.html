Lepanthes adenophora

Lepanthes adenophora, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance.

Lepanthes adenophora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
