Lagenophora bellidioides

Lagenophora bellidioides, commonly known as the dwarf bellidium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of only a few centimeters. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Lagenophora bellidioides is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
