Lysimachia septemfida

Lysimachia septemfida, commonly known as the seven-lobed chickweed, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern United States.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with seven distinct lobes, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia septemfida is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
