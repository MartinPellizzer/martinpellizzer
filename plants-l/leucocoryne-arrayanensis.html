Leucocoryne arrayanensis

Leucocoryne arrayanensis, commonly known as the Orange River lily, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, almost translucent appearance. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with a dormant period during dry seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among bulb enthusiasts.
