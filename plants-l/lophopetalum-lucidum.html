Lophopetalum lucidum

Lophopetalum lucidum, commonly known as the glossy abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
