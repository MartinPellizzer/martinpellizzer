Lophiaris pachyphylla

Lophiaris pachyphylla, commonly known as the thick-leaved lophiaris, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
