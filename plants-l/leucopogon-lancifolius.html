Leucopogon lancifolius

Leucopogon lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, urn-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.
