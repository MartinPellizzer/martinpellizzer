Lotus chazaliei var. ifniensis

Lotus chazaliei var. ifniensis, commonly known as the Ifni lotus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and has a deep root system adapted to survive in dry conditions. It is often found growing in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
