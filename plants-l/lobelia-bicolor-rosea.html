Lobelia bicolor rosea

Lobelia bicolor rosea, commonly known as the pink and white lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored flowers, which feature pink petals with white centers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Lobelia bicolor rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, delicate blooms.
