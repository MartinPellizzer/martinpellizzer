Lithocarpus Philippinensis

Lithocarpus philippinensis is a remarkable evergreen tree native to the Philippines. It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts. This plant is known for its durable, heavy wood and its ability to thrive in tropical rainforests. The tree's name translates to "stone-carp," referring to its hard, dense fruit. It plays a vital role in the ecosystem and is valued for both its ecological and economic significance.
