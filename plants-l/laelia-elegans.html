Laelia elegans

Laelia elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Laelia elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
