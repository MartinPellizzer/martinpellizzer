Lespedeza chekiangensis

Lespedeza chekiangensis, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen.
