Lysimachia gracilipes

Lysimachia gracilipes, commonly known as slender yellow-eyed grass, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
