Litsea blumei

Litsea blumei, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and the Philippines. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, round berries.

Litsea blumei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in traditional medicine.
