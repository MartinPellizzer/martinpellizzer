Leptorumohra miqueliana var. fimbriata

Leptorumohra miqueliana var. fimbriata, commonly known as the fimbriate rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of small, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants.
