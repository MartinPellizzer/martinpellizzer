Lepanthes illinizae

Lepanthes illinizae, commonly known as the Illinizas orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often feature intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and red.

Lepanthes illinizae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
