Lonicera tatarica var. rubriflora

Lonicera tatarica var. rubriflora, commonly known as redflower honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of showy, red-orange flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fragrant blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can grow in a variety of soil types.
