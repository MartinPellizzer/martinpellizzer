Lycaste venusta

Lycaste venusta, commonly known as the showy lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with long, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste venusta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
