Laurembergia coccinea var. zeylanica

Laurembergia coccinea var. zeylanica, commonly known as the Zeylanic flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
