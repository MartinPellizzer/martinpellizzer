Lobivia maximiliana var. leptacantha

Lobivia maximiliana var. leptacantha, commonly known as the slender-spined lobivia, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered with dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
