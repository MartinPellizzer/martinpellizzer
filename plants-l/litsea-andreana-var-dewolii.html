Litsea andreana var. dewolii

Litsea andreana var. dewolii, commonly known as the Dewolii litsea, is a variant of the Litsea andreana species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, reddish-brown fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The leaves and fruits of this plant are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
