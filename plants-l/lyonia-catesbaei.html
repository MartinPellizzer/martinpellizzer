Lyonia catesbaei

Lyonia catesbaei, commonly known as the Carolina rose, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in the spring, which are borne on long, slender pedicels. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
