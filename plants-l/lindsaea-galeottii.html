Lindsaea galeottii

Lindsaea galeottii, commonly known as the Galeotti's spikemoss, is a species of fern ally belonging to the family Lindsaeaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, stemless rosettes of lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on specialized stalks, which are borne on the undersides of the leaves. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Lindsaea galeottii is often found in rocky crevices or on tree trunks, showcasing its adaptability to various microhabitats.
