Linzia glabra

Linzia glabra, commonly known as the smooth linseed plant, is a small annual herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and wetlands. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are smooth and lance-shaped. Its small, pale blue or purple flowers bloom in clusters, and it produces slender, hairy fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Linzia glabra is noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common sight in many regions.
