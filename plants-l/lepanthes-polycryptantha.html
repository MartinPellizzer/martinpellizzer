Lepanthes polycryptantha

Lepanthes polycryptantha, commonly known as the cryptanthous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with several clustered flowers, each featuring intricate, spurred petals. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded environments where it grows.

Lepanthes polycryptantha is often found in cloud forests and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
