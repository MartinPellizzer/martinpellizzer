Liatris elegans var. kralii

Liatris elegans var. kralii, commonly known as Kral's blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear dense clusters of showy, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, woodlands, and disturbed soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually blue-green in color.

Kral's blazing star is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
