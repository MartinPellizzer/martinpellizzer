Liparis guangxiensis

Liparis guangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the spring season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
