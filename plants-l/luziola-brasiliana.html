Luziola brasiliana

Luziola brasiliana, commonly known as the Brazilian light violet, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its small, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and somewhat woody.

Luziola brasiliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
