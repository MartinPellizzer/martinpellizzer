Lonchocarpus velizii

Lonchocarpus velizii, commonly known as the yellow drumstick tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is known for its unique fruit, which resembles a drumstick and contains several seed pods. It is often found in moist, shaded environments and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and ethnobotanical studies.
