Lysimachia mauritiana

Lysimachia mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius waterweed, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist or aquatic environments. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lysimachia mauritiana plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to the biodiversity of wetland ecosystems.
