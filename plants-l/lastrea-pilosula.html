Lastrea pilosula

Lastrea pilosula, commonly known as the woolly lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a woolly appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months.
