Lobelia splendens var. fulgens

Lobelia splendens var. fulgens, commonly known as the fiery lobelia, is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from bright red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom continuously from spring through fall.
