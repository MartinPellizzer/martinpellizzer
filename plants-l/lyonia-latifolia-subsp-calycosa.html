Lyonia latifolia subsp. calycosa

Lyonia latifolia subsp.

calycosa, commonly known as the broadleaf lyonia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States. It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and dark green on top.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that ripens in the summer.

It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
