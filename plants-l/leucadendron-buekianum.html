Leucadendron buekianum

Leucadendron buekianum, commonly known as the silverleaf leucadendron, is a South African shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and conical, spiny seed heads. The plant is native to the coastal regions of the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with summer rainfall.

Leucadendron buekianum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
