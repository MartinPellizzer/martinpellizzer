Lotus galegocangos

Lotus galegocangos, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Lotus galegocangos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
