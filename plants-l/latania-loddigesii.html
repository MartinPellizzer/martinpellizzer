Latania loddigesii

Latania loddigesii, commonly known as the African palm or the yellow palm, is a striking evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive tropical appearance. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, coconut-like fruits. Its rapid growth rate and ornamental value make it popular in horticulture, though it requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Latania loddigesii is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local wildlife.
