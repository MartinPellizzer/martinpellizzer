Lanonia verrucosa

Lanonia verrucosa, commonly known as the rough lanonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in humid, tropical climates. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, rough texture on its leaves. Lanonia verrucosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
