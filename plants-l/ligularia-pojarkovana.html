Ligularia pojarkovana

Ligularia pojarkovana, commonly known as the Pojarkov's ligularia, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement.
