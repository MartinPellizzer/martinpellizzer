Lotononis densa subsp. leucoclada

Lotononis densa subsp.

leucoclada, commonly known as the white-flowered lotononis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, flowering heads and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in compact, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and resilience.
