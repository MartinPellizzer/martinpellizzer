Lonicera henryi var. transarisanensis

Lonicera henryi var. transarisanensis, commonly known as the Transarisan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a small, red berry that matures in the fall and is an important food source for birds and small mammals.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
