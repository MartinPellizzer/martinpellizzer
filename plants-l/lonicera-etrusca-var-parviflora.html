Lonicera etrusca var. parviflora

Lonicera etrusca var. parviflora, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features slender, arching stems that produce small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and natural landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
