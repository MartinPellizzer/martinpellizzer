Litsea elongata var. faberi

Litsea elongata var. faberi, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southeastern China and is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by spherical, orange-red fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions with a humid climate.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
