Lespedeza × robusta

Lespedeza × robusta, commonly known as Chinese broom.

It is a hybrid species within the Fabaceae family, characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to East Asia and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Lespedeza × robusta is also valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
