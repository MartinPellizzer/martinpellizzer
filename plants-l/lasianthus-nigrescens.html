Lasianthus nigrescens

Lasianthus nigrescens, commonly known as black-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast with its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows as an erect herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

Lasianthus nigrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
