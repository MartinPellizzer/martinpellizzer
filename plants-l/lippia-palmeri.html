Lippia palmeri

Lippia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. Lippia palmeri is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
