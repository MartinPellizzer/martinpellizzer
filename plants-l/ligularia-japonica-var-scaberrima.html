Ligularia japonica var. scaberrima

Ligularia japonica var. scaberrima, commonly known as the Japanese ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture, which gives it its varietal name "scaberrima.". The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and texture.
