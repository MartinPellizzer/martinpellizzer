Lespedeza daurica var. sinica

Lespedeza daurica var. sinica, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
