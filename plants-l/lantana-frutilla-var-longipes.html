Lantana frutilla var. longipes

Lantana frutilla var. longipes, commonly known as the longipes lantana, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
