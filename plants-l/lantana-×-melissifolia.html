Lantana × melissifolia

Lantana × melissifolia, commonly known as honeycomb lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Lantana × melissifolia is also known for attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
