Lecythis ovata var. verrucosa

Lecythis ovata var. verrucosa, commonly known as the scarlet burl or velvet burl, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, smooth, and often brightly colored fruits that are highly prized for their ornamental value. The tree produces a distinctive, thick, and waxy coating on its fruit, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically large, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

The plant is also valued for its timber, which is durable and used in construction and furniture-making.
