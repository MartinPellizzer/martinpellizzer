Lagerstroemia minuticarpa

Lagerstroemia minuticarpa, commonly known as the dwarf crape myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and adaptability to different climates.
