Lasianthus bruneensis

Lasianthus bruneensis, commonly known as the Brunei flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Brunei and parts of Malaysia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections due to its eye-catching floral display.
