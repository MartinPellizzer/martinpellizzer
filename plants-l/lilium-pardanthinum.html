Lilium pardanthinum

Lilium pardanthinum, commonly known as the Chinese white lily, is a species of lily native to China.

It is characterized by its large, white, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has linear, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a culinary ingredient.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant appearance and fragrance.
