Ligularia kansuensis

Ligularia kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which are arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
