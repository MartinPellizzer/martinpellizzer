Lagunaria

Lagunaria, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Lagunaria species are often found growing near coastal areas, which reflects their adaptation to salty, humid environments.
