Lindackeria stipulata

Lindackeria stipulata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Lindackeria stipulata is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative means.
