Lamium oreades

Lamium oreades, commonly known as spring pimpernel, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is known for its early blooming habit, often appearing in late winter or early spring.

Lamium oreades is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
