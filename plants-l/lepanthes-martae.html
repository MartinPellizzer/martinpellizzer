Lepanthes martae

Lepanthes martae, commonly known as the Marta's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found growing in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its overall compact appearance.

Lepanthes martae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
