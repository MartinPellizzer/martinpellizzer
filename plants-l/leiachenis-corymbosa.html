Leiachenis corymbosa

Leiachenis corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Leiachenis corymbosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
