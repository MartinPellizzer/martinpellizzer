Lamium amplexicaule subsp. mauritanicum

Lamium amplexicaule subsp.

mauritanicum, commonly known as the Mauritania deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North Africa. It features opposite, ovate leaves with a reddish underside and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of pink to purple.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscaping.
