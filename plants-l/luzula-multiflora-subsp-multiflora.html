Luzula multiflora subsp. multiflora

Luzula multiflora subsp.

multiflora, commonly known as the common wood rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae. It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
