Lampranthus dubitans

Lampranthus dubitans, commonly known as the Orange Sunset.

This succulent plant is native to South Africa and belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that can branch out, and its flowers are typically bright orange or red, adding vibrant color to the landscape. It is also noted for its ability to store water in its thick leaves, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.
