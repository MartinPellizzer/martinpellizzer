Leucas hirta var. beddomei

Leucas hirta var. beddomei, commonly known as Beddome's leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern India and Sri Lanka.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows as a shrub with square stems and opposite leaves that are hairy on both sides. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and often have a pale blue or violet hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
