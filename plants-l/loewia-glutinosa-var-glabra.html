Loewia glutinosa var. glabra

Loewia glutinosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth loewia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stem and leaves, which are typically ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
