Loricaria azuayensis

Loricaria azuayensis, commonly known as the Azuay river turtle.

It is a species of aquatic turtle native to the rivers of Ecuador. This turtle is characterized by its distinctive shell patterns and robust build, adapted for life in freshwater environments. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by contributing to the balance of aquatic life.

However, it faces threats from habitat loss and pollution, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
