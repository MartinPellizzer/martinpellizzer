Lessertia stricta

Lessertia stricta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Lessertia stricta is also valued for its ability to produce offsets, which can be propagated to grow new plants.
