Lantana cabrerae

Lantana cabrerae, commonly known as Cape lantana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in late spring to early summer. Lantana cabrerae has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or well-drained soils.
