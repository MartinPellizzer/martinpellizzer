Lotus nummularius

Lotus nummularius, commonly known as the silver leaf lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, round, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces delicate pink or white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is also suitable for xeriscaping and low-maintenance landscapes.
