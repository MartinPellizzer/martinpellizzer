Lilium bulbiferum var. aurantiacum

Lilium bulbiferum var. aurantiacum, commonly known as the orange-bulb lily, is a striking bulbous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange to red. The plant produces bulbils along its stems, which can be used for propagation. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and unique bulbiferous growth habit.
