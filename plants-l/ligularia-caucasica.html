Ligularia caucasica

Ligularia caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caucasus region.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often deeply lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in late summer, arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens in suitable climates.
