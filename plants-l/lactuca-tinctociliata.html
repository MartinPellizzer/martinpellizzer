Lactuca tinctociliata

Lactuca tinctociliata, commonly known as the purple-stemmed lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple-tinged stems and leaves that are typically slender and elongated. It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
