Lithospermum oblongum

Lithospermum oblongum, commonly known as purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-pink flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces a distinctive conical seed head. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Lithospermum oblongum is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
