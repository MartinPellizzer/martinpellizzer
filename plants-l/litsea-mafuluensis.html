Litsea mafuluensis

Litsea mafuluensis, commonly known as the Mafulu litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in the Democratic Republic of the Congo and the Central African Republic. Litsea mafuluensis plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
