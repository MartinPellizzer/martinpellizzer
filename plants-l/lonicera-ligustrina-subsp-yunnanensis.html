Lonicera ligustrina subsp. yunnanensis

Lonicera ligustrina subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan honeysuckle, is a shrub native to China. It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in the spring.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
