Limonium × profusum

Limonium × profusum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plumbagaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, spike-like inflorescences that range in color from blue to purple, pink, and white. Limonium × profusum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
