Limonium minutiflorum

Limonium minutiflorum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or along the shore. Limonium minutiflorum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience.
