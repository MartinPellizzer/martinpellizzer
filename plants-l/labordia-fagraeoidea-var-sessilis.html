Labordia fagraeoidea var. sessilis

Labordia fagraeoidea var. sessilis, commonly known as the sessile labordia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal or forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and ecological research.
