Lithospermum obtusifolium

Lithospermum obtusifolium, commonly known as bluntleaf lithospermum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features lanceolate to ovate leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, pale purple to pink flowers. Its root system is known for containing compounds with potential medicinal properties.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
