Lepanthes delhierroi

Lepanthes delhierroi, commonly known as the Delhierroi orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink coloration. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lepanthes delhierroi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
