Lamarchea hakeifolia

Lamarchea hakeifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hakea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance through its deep root system. It produces woody capsules that release seeds when mature, contributing to its ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.

Lamarchea hakeifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
