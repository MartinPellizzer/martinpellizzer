Lomelosia crenata subsp. dallaportae

Lomelosia crenata subsp.

dallaportae, commonly known as the Dallaporta's lomelosia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
