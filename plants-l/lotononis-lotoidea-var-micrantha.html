Lotononis lotoidea var. micrantha

Lotononis lotoidea var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its small, daisy-like flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces achenes as its seeds.
