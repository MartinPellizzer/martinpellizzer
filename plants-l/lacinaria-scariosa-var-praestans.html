Lacinaria scariosa var. praestans

Lacinaria scariosa var. praestans, commonly known as the showy lace fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often adorned with a silky, scaly texture. The plant features a tall, slender trunk that supports its cascading foliage, giving it a dramatic appearance. Its leaves are typically bright green and may have a slight reddish or bronze hue on the underside.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings or as a houseplant.
