Liabum rugosum

Liabum rugosum, commonly known as the rough leaved liabum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine, rough hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a serrated margin with a rough texture. Small, yellowish-white flowers are borne in clusters at the leaf axils, adding a delicate floral display.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
