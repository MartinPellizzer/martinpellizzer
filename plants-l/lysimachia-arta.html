Lysimachia arta

Lysimachia arta, commonly known as the silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia arta is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
