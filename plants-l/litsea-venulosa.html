Litsea venulosa

Litsea venulosa, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a pleasant scent when crushed. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

Litsea venulosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
