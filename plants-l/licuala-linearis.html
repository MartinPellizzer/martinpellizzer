Licuala linearis

Licuala linearis, commonly known as the giant pandan or swamp cabbage, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found in marshy areas near rivers. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as a source of fiber.

The plant also has cultural significance in various regions of its native habitat.
