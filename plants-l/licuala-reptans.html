Licuala reptans

Licuala reptans, commonly known as the canoe fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

The leaves are used in traditional crafts and have cultural significance in some regions.
