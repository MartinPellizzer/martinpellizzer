Lysimachia kalaupapaensis

Lysimachia kalaupapaensis, commonly known as Kalaupapa lily, is a rare flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Moloka'i.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in rocky areas or along stream banks. It is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and invasive species.

Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species.
