Lasiobema tubicalyx

Lasiobema tubicalyx, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that display vibrant yellow and orange hues. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are alternate and elliptical, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in its native habitats.
