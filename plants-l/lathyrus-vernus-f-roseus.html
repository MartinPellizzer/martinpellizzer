Lathyrus vernus f. roseus

Lathyrus vernus f. roseus, commonly known as the pink-flowered sweet pea, is a variant of the common sweet pea.

It is a climbing herbaceous perennial that produces striking pink or lilac-colored flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to garden landscapes.
