Lactuca elongata var. sanguinea

Lactuca elongata var. sanguinea, commonly known as bloody lettuce, is a variant of the common lettuce species characterized by its striking red-tinged leaves.

This plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Its leaves are typically elongated and have a slightly bitter taste, making it a unique addition to salads. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible leaves.
