Lavoisiera rigida

Lavoisiera rigida, commonly known as the rigid lavoisiera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, rigid stems that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Lavoisiera rigida is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
