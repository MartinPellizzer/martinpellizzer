Lepanthes eros

Lepanthes eros, commonly known as the red-faced orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often feature a deep red or orange hue on the lip. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its flowers are highly specialized, with intricate structures that facilitate pollination by specific insect species.

Lepanthes eros is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its ecological role in its native habitats.
