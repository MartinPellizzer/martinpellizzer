Lappula leptophylla

Lappula leptophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf lappula, is a flowering plant native to Eurasia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and it has slender, hairless leaves. Lappula leptophylla typically grows in moist soils along riverbanks and wet meadows.
