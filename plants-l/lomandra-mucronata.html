Lomandra mucronata

Lomandra mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lomandra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage composed of narrow, strap-shaped leaves. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
