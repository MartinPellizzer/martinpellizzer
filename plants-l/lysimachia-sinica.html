Lysimachia sinica

Lysimachia sinica, commonly known as Chinese goldthread, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features creeping rhizomes and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia sinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
