Lobivia densispina f. cupreoviridis

Lobivia densispina f. cupreoviridis, commonly known as the Copperleaf Lobivia, is a compact, short-stemmed cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, spiny stems that are often tinged with a reddish or coppery hue, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique coloration and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
