Lapathum bononiense

Lapathum bononiense, commonly known as the Bononiense Betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive minty aroma. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
