Lespedeza latissima

Lespedeza latissima, commonly known as broadleaf lespedeza, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fluffy seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. It is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.

Lespedeza latissima is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and hardiness.
