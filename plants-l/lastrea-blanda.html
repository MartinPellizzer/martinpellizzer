Lastrea blanda

Lastrea blanda, commonly known as the soft leaf ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions with minimal care.
