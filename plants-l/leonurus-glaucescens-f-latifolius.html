Leonurus glaucescens f. latifolius

Leonurus glaucescens f. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved hedgehog.

This plant is a variant of the common hedgehog plant, characterized by its broader leaves compared to the typical form. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The leaves are usually gray-green in color and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is known for its small, pale purple flowers that bloom in the summer.
