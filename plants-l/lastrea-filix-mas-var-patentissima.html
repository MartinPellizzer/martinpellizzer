Lastrea filix-mas var. patentissima

Lastrea filix-mas var. patentissima, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy succulent native to tropical regions.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a subtle variegation pattern. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or greenish in color.

This variety is prized for its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
