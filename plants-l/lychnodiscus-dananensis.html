Lychnodiscus dananensis

Lychnodiscus dananensis, commonly known as the Dananensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
