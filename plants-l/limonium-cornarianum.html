Limonium cornarianum

Limonium cornarianum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-green leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to salty, sandy soils. It produces small, seed-like fruits that are dispersed by the wind.

Limonium cornarianum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and visual appeal.
