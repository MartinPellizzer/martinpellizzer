Ludwigia aurantiaca

Ludwigia aurantiaca, commonly known as red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange petals and bright green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along riverbanks. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters.

Ludwigia aurantiaca is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.
