Lonchocarpus darienensis

Lonchocarpus darienensis, commonly known as the Darien legume, is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces distinctive yellow or orange flowers, which are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in long, slender pods that typically contain several seeds each.

The species is notable for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal practices.
