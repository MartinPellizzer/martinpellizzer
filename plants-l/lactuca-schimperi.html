Lactuca schimperi

Lactuca schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia and parts of Eritrea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the family. Its leaves are usually elongated and somewhat succulent, with a mild, slightly bitter taste.

It is often grown as a leafy vegetable and has traditional uses in local cuisines.
