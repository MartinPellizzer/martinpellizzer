Lagerstroemia cuspidata

Lagerstroemia cuspidata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fiery red or orange flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Lagerstroemia cuspidata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
