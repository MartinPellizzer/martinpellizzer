Landolphia scandens var. schweinfurthiana

Landolphia scandens var. schweinfurthiana, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking, lantern-shaped yellow-orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using tendrils. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
