Luzula campestris var. comosa

Luzula campestris var. comosa, commonly known as the tufted woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The species is notable for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and its role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
