Liebrechtsia kotschyi

Liebrechtsia kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi's liebrechtia, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
