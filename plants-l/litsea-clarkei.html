Litsea clarkei

Litsea clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea clarkei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
