Lastrea napoleonis

Lastrea napoleonis, commonly known as Napoleon's lastrea, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often covered in fine hairs.
