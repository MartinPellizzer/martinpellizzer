Lobelia splendens

Lobelia splendens, commonly known as the shining lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia splendens thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
