Lonicera etrusca var. xylostemoides

Lonicera etrusca var. xylostemoides, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Italy.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy beneath, and it produces small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
