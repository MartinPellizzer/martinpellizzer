Lonicera tulinensis

Lonicera tulinensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Lonicera tulinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
