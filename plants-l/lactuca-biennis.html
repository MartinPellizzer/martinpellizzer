Lactuca biennis

Lactuca biennis, commonly known as the biennial lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head that develops in its second year. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a compact cluster. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca biennis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
