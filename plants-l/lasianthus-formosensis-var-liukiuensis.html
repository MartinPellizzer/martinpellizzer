Lasianthus formosensis var. liukiuensis

Lasianthus formosensis var. liukiuensis, commonly known as the Liuqiu flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and somewhat woody.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
