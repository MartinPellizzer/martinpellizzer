Lotononis adpressa subsp. leptantha

Lotononis adpressa subsp.

leptantha, commonly known as the slender lotononis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.

This subspecies is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to thrive.
