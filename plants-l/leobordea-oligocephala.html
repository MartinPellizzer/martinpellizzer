Leobordea oligocephala

Leobordea oligocephala, commonly known as the desert lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep roots that help it access water from underground sources. Leobordea oligocephala plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain herbivores.
