Lepidium alashanicum

Lepidium alashanicum, commonly known as the Alashan cress, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers and has a compact, herbaceous structure.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its distribution across its native range.
