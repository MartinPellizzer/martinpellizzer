Lycopodium Monosporum

Lycopodium monosporum is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the clubmoss family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cool, temperate regions. This species is known for its small, spore-bearing cones and its unique reproductive cycle. Despite its delicate appearance, it plays an important role in forest ecosystems. Its study provides valuable insights into the evolution of early land plants.
