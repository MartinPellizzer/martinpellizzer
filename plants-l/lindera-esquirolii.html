Lindera esquirolii

Lindera esquirolii, commonly known as the Mexican spicebush, is a small evergreen shrub native to central Mexico.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features aromatic, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct, spicy fragrance. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Lindera esquirolii is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland areas.
