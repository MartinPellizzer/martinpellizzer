Licuala kemamanensis

Licuala kemamanensis, commonly known as the Bornean palm, is a species of palm native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a stout, unbranched stem that is covered with persistent leaf bases. It thrives in humid, lowland tropical rainforest environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
