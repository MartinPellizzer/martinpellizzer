Leucadendron laureolum

Leucadendron laureolum, commonly known as the silver leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and vibrant yellow-orange flower heads. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silvery hairs that help reduce water loss.

The flowers typically bloom in spring and are followed by fluffy, seed-filled spheres that disperse easily in the wind.
