Lepanthes cryptophyta

Lepanthes cryptophyta, commonly known as the cryptophyte orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a delicate, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often clustered at the base of the plant.

Lepanthes cryptophyta thrives in shaded, moist environments and is adapted to grow in forest understories.
