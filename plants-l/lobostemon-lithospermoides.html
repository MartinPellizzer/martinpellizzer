Lobostemon lithospermoides

Lobostemon lithospermoides, commonly known as the rock pepper or stone root, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
