Lithocardium thibaudianum

Lithocardium thibaudianum, commonly known as the Thibaudian rockstar, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing a unique ability to store water in its leaves and stems. Its flowering period typically occurs in the spring, making it a notable sight in its natural habitat.
