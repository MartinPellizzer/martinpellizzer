Lastrea frondosa

Lastrea frondosa, commonly known as the frondose lastrea, is a unique aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, feathery fronds that resemble delicate ferns, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still waters, often found in marshes and ponds. Its leaves are thin and translucent, allowing for efficient light absorption, which supports its photosynthetic processes.

Lastrea frondosa plays a vital role in maintaining water quality by filtering impurities and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
