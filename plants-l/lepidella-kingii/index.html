Lepidella Kingii

Lepidella kingii is a rare and delicate plant native to the southern regions of New Zealand. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. This species is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and unique flowering pattern. Due to its limited habitat and threats from habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical gem.
