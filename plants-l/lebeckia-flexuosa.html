Lebeckia flexuosa

Lebeckia flexuosa, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, and the stems are often sprawling or ascending.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
