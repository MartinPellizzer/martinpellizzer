Ligustrum lucidum var. aureomarginatum

Ligustrum lucidum var. aureomarginatum, commonly known as the golden-margin privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent yellow margins. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically followed by dark blue berries. It is often used in landscaping for its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

The varietal name "aureomarginatum" refers to the distinctive golden edge of its leaves.
