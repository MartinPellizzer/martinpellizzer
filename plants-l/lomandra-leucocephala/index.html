Lomandra Leucocephala

Lomandra leucocephala is a striking evergreen plant native to eastern Australia. It is known for its dense, sword-like foliage and distinctive white flower spikes that bloom in spring. This hardy plant thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements. Lomandra leucocephala is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
