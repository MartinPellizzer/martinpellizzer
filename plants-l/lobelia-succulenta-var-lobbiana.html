Lobelia succulenta var. lobbiana

Lobelia succulenta var. lobbiana, commonly known as the succulent lobe, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to rock gardens and arid landscapes.
