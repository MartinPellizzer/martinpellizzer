Litsea nuculanea

Litsea nuculanea, commonly known as the nutmeg litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The fruit is a small, oval drupe that contains a single seed, and the plant is valued for its aromatic properties and ornamental appeal.
