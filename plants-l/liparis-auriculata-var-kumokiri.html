Liparis auriculata var. kumokiri

Liparis auriculata var. kumokiri, commonly known as the Kumokiri orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
