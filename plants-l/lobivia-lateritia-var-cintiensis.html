Lobivia lateritia var. cintiensis

Lobivia lateritia var. cintiensis, commonly known as the Cintiensi Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features short, ribbed stems and produces clusters of yellow flowers during the growing season. It is well adapted to harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
