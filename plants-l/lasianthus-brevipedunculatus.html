Lasianthus brevipedunculatus

Lasianthus brevipedunculatus, commonly known as the short-stalked flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic blooms.
