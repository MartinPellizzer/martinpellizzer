Lonicera tatarica f. rubriflora

Lonicera tatarica f. rubriflora, commonly known as the redflower honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, red-orange tubular flowers in late spring to early summer. These flowers are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types once established.
