Lindsaea marginata var. falcata

Lindsaea marginata var. falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lindsaea, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as under forest canopies. Its fronds are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, with the youngest leaves at the center.

This variety is distinguished by its narrow leaf shape and distinctive marginal lobes.
