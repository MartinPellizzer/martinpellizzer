Leucanthemum leucanthemum

Leucanthemum leucanthemum, commonly known as the white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy white petals surrounding a yellow center, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It typically grows in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soil.

Leucanthemum leucanthemum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate.
