Lepanthes cincinnata

Lepanthes cincinnata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender stem with few leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are characterized by their narrow petals and a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Lepanthes cincinnata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
