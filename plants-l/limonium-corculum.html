Limonium corculum

Limonium corculum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage and is often found growing in coastal areas and saline soils. Limonium corculum is a member of the Plumbagaceae family and is known for its hardy nature.
