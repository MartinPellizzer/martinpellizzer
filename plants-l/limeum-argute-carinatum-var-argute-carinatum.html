Limeum argute-carinatum var. argute-carinatum

Limeum argute-carinatum var. argute-carinatum, commonly known as the spiked spurge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and striking yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
