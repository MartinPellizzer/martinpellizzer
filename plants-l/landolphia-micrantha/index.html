Landolphia Micrantha

Landolphia micrantha is a striking tropical plant native to central and eastern Africa. It is known for its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. This species belongs to the nightshade family and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic if ingested, requiring caution when handling.
