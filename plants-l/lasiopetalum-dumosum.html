Lasiopetalum dumosum

Lasiopetalum dumosum, commonly known as the dwarf flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in clusters. The plant has rough, hairy leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in horticultural landscapes.
