Lonicera fargesii var. fargesii

Lonicera fargesii var. fargesii, commonly known as the Farges honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in early summer. Its fruit is a small, red berry that matures in late summer.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
