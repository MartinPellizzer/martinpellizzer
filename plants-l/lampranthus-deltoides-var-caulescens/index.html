Lampranthus Deltoides Var. Caulescens

Lampranthus deltoides var. caulescens is a strikingly beautiful succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange, it adds a bold splash of color to any garden. This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It is commonly grown as a container plant due to its preference for sunny, dry conditions. Despite its delicate appearance, it is surprisingly resilient and easy to care for.
