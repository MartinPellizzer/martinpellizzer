Lepidium densiflorum var. bourgeauanum

Lepidium densiflorum var. bourgeauanum, commonly known as the Bourgeau's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
