Lycianthes anisophylla

Lycianthes anisophylla, commonly known as the purpleleaf nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its distinctive purple leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, and the flowers are usually solitary, with five petals.

The fruit is a berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
