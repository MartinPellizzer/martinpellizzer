Lepidium raimondii

Lepidium raimondii, commonly known as the desert cress, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant is characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments.
