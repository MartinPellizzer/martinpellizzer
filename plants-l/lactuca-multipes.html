Lactuca multipes

Lactuca multipes, commonly known as the multiseeded lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its rosette of pale green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into composite seed heads containing numerous tiny, oval-shaped seeds. Lactuca multipes is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
