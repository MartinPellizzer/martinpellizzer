Lathyrus vernus proles gaudinii

Lathyrus vernus proles gaudinii, commonly known as the early purple orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. It has slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a food source for certain pollinators and its ornamental value in garden settings.
