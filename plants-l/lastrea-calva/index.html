Lastrea Calva

Lastrea calva is a unique succulent native to southern Africa, known for its striking appearance and resilience in harsh environments. This plant features elongated, cylindrical stems covered in soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive look. It thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for desert gardens. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive prolonged dry periods. Due to its beauty and hardiness, Lastrea calva has become a popular ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
