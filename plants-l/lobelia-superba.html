Lobelia superba

Lobelia superba, commonly known as the noble lobelia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Lobelia superba thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
