Laelia iberioides

Laelia iberioides, commonly known as the Iberian laelia, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a complex coloration. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, showcasing its ornamental value. It prefers cool, humid conditions and is often cultivated in shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a prized addition to specialized orchid collections.
