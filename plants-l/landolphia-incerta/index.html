Landolphia Incerta

Landolphia incerta is a striking tropical plant known for its large, showy flowers and unique ecological role. Native to regions across Africa, it thrives in humid, shaded environments within dense forests. This species belongs to the nightshade family and is often found climbing trees or shrubs in its natural habitat. Its vibrant orange and yellow blooms attract various pollinators, contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, Landolphia incerta remains relatively understudied, offering intriguing opportunities for botanical research.
