Litsea coreana var. coreana

Litsea coreana var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It produces small, white flowers followed by edible berries that are slightly sweet and aromatic.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
