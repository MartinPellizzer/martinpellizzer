Laurus pendens

Laurus pendens, commonly known as hanging laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a fragrant aroma. It produces small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Laurus pendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
