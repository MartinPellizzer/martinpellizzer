Lobelia kalmii var. strictiflora

Lobelia kalmii var. strictiflora, commonly known as the Kalm's lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant produces its flowers in dense clusters, which are often borne on long, slender pedicels. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
