Laser divaricatum

Laser divaricatum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its trunk is often smooth and slightly flaky. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The flowers are not only visually stunning but also attract a variety of pollinators such as bees and birds.
