Leopoldia comosa

Leopoldia comosa, commonly known as the silky leopoldia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces delicate, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
