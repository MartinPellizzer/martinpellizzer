Laurentia baueri var. major

Laurentia baueri var. major, commonly known as the giant laurentia, is a striking member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red stems.

Native to the high-altitude regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a splash of color to its foliage. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty collections.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in the wild.
