Linnaea borealis f. formosissima

Linnaea borealis f. formosissima, commonly known as the fragrant wintergreen, is a rare and striking variant of the twinflower plant.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the boreal forests of North America and parts of Europe. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, fragrant aroma. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This form is particularly noted for its delicate beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
