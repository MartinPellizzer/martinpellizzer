Lycianthes bigeminata subsp. kaitisis

Lycianthes bigeminata subsp.

kaitisis, commonly known as the Kaitis Island physalis, is a member of the Solanaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial shrub native to the island of Kaitis in the South Pacific.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into distinctive, inflated, orange-red berries. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and are covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruiting structures.
