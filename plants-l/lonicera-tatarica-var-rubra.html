Lonicera tatarica var. rubra

Lonicera tatarica var. rubra, commonly known as red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early summer. These are followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
