Libanotis elata

Libanotis elata, commonly known as the tall bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.

Libanotis elata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
