Limonium legrandii

Limonium legrandii, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. Limonium legrandii is a perennial species that produces small, clustered flowers during the summer months.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
