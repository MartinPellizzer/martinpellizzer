Linum muelleri

Linum muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's flax, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Linum muelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
