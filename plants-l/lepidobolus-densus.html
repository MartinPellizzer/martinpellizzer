Lepidobolus densus

Lepidobolus densus, commonly known as the dense lepidobolus, is a lesser-known plant species with unique botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the family Lepidobolaceae and is distinguished by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are tightly arranged along its stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions.
