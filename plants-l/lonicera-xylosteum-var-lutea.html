Lonicera xylosteum var. lutea

Lonicera xylosteum var. lutea, commonly known as yellow honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically yellowish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
