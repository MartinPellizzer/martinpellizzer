Litsea variabilis f. chinensis

Litsea variabilis f. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese litsea or Chinese sweetleaf, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, dark blue to black berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
