Leucospermum ecklonii

Leucospermum ecklonii, commonly known as the firewheel flower, is a striking South African shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are densely packed along its branches. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Leucospermum ecklonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine.
