Leucas globulifera

Leucas globulifera, commonly known as the globular leucas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, globular-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
