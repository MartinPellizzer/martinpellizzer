Lespedeza patens f. nivea

Lespedeza patens f. nivea, commonly known as white lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
