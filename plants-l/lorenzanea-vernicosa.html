Lorenzanea vernicosa

Lorenzanea vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy bark tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, almost waxy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, fleshy fruits. Its bark is thick and shiny, which is a key feature used in traditional medicine.

Lorenzanea vernicosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
