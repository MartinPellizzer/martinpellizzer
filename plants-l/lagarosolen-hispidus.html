Lagarosolen hispidus

Lagarosolen hispidus, commonly known as the hairy sunburst, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a woody stem with hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes.
