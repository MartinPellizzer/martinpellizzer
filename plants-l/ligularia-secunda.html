Ligularia secunda

Ligularia secunda, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, with a striking silver-green coloration. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland gardens.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
