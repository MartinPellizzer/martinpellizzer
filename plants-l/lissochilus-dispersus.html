Lissochilus dispersus

Lissochilus dispersus, commonly known as the scattered beard-orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The orchid has a short lifespan and is often found in association with other epiphytic plants in its native habitat.
