Lobivia chrysantha var. hossei

Lobivia chrysantha var. hossei, commonly known as the Orange Ghost Cactus, is a compact, branching cactus native to the arid regions of northern Chile.

It features a dense covering of bright orange to red spines, which give it its striking appearance. The plant produces showy yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for desert gardens.

Its unique coloration and compact form make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
