Lobelia lelyana

Lobelia lelyana, commonly known as the Lely's lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that bear small, tubular flowers clustered in whorls along the stem. The flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with five petals and a prominent yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are alternate and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
