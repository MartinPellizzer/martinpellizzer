Lasallea × amethystina

Lasallea × amethystina, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and fascinating plant resulting from the cross between Lasallea and Amethystina species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its striking, often violet or purple-colored flowers. The plant typically features tall, slender stems with clustered blooms that are highly ornamental. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
