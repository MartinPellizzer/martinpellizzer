Lysiloma acapulcense var. brevispicatum

Lysiloma acapulcense var. brevispicatum, commonly known as the short-spined acacia, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, rounded leaves and produces yellow or pale green flowers. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
