Lankesteriana

Lankesteriana, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red or orange flowers that resemble flames, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Lankesteriana species are popular among orchid enthusiasts due to their striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
