Lobostemon belliformis

Lobostemon belliformis, commonly known as the bell-shaped lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender, branching stems. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Lobostemon belliformis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along streambanks.
