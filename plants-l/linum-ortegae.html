Linum ortegae

Linum ortegae, commonly known as the Ortega flax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their showy blue or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to the unique climate of its native habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with five petals that open in a spiral pattern.

Linum ortegae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
