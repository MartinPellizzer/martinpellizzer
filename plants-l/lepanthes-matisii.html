Lepanthes matisii

Lepanthes matisii, commonly known as the Matisii orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Its flowers are characterized by their intricate petal structure and are often arranged in a raceme.

Lepanthes matisii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
