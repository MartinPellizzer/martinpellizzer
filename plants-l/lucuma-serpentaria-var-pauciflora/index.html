Lucuma Serpentaria Var. Pauciflora

Coral tree, scientifically known as Lucuma serpentaria var. pauciflora, is a striking plant native to the Andean regions of South America. Its unique appearance, featuring vibrant coral-like flowers, makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and often overlooked in mainstream horticulture. The plant thrives in cooler, shaded environments and requires careful attention to soil and moisture conditions. Its cultivation is gaining interest among botanists and gardeners seeking to preserve biodiversity and enhance landscape aesthetics.
