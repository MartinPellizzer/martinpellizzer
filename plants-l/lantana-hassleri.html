Lantana hassleri

Lantana hassleri, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Lantana hassleri is also known for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
