Lobelia rosmarinifolia

Lobelia rosmarinifolia, commonly known as rosemary lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and clusters of blue to violet flowers. Lobelia rosmarinifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
