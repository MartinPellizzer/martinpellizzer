Leucadendron ovale

Leucadendron ovale, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces bright orange or red spherical flower heads during the winter months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Leucadendron ovale is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
