Lastrea gracilescens

Lastrea gracilescens, commonly known as the slender lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branching stem covered in small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or white, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
