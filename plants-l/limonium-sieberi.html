Limonium sieberi

Limonium sieberi, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation, though it requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Limonium sieberi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in floral arrangements.
