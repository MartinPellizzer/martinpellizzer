Lythrum punctatum

Lythrum punctatum, commonly known as spotted lythrum, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters at the top of the stems.

Spotted lythrum is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.
