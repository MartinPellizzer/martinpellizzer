Lepidium seydelii

Lepidium seydelii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and pinnately compound leaves. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
