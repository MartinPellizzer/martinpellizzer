Leucanthemum burnatii

Leucanthemum burnatii, commonly known as Burnat's leucanthemum, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer. Its foliage consists of gray-green, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in marginal conditions.
