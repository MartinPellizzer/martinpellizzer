Lonchocarpus paucinervius

Lonchocarpus paucinervius, commonly known as the slender legume, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has pinnately arranged leaves with few veins, giving it its specific epithet "paucinervius.". Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with bright yellow petals.

The species is noted for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
