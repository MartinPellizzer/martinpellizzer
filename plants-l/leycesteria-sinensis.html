Leycesteria sinensis

Leycesteria sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red, lantern-shaped flowers that appear in late summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Leycesteria sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
