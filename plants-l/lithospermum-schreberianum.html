Lithospermum schreberianum

Lithospermum schreberianum, commonly known as red root, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a reddish or purplish hue. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to red in color, which bloom in late summer. Its root system is thick and fleshy, containing compounds that have been historically used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in partial shade.
