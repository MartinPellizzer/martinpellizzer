Lewisia brachycalyx

Lewisia brachycalyx, commonly known as the broadleaf lewisia, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant produces vibrant pink to red blossoms that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
