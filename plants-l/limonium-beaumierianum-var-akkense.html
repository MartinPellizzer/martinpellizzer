Limonium beaumierianum var. akkense

Limonium beaumierianum var. akkense, commonly known as the Akkens limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, adding vibrant color to coastal and sandy soils. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements.
