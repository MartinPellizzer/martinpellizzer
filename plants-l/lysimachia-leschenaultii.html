Lysimachia leschenaultii

Lysimachia leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its creeping stems and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Lysimachia leschenaultii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
