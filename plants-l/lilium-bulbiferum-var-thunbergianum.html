Lilium bulbiferum var. thunbergianum

Lilium bulbiferum var. thunbergianum, commonly known as the Japanese fritillary, is a species of lily native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a red or orange spot at the base. The plant produces bulbils along its flower stems, which can be used for propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and fragrance.
