Lapathum pulchrum

Lapathum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful burnet, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
