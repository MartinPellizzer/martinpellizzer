Lauradia glandulosa var. congesta

Lauradia glandulosa var. congesta, commonly known as the clustered glandular lauradia, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences and aromatic leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
