Leonohebe haastii var. humilis

Leonohebe haastii var. humilis, commonly known as the low-growing leonohebe, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers during the spring and early summer.
