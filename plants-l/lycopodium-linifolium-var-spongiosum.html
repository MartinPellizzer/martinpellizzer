Lycopodium linifolium var. spongiosum

Lycopodium linifolium var. spongiosum, commonly known as the sponge moss, is a variant of the clubmoss family.

It is characterized by its dense, spongy growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, scale-like leaves that are tightly packed, contributing to its unique texture. It is often found in forest floors or rocky crevices where humidity is high.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in soil stabilization.
