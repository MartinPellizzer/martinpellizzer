Lophosoria

Lophosoria, commonly known as the golden thread orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, showy flowers that often display vibrant yellow or orange hues. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Lophosoria species typically thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plants are epiphytic, meaning they grow on the surfaces of trees or other structures without being parasitic.

Their unique floral structure and coloration make them popular among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
