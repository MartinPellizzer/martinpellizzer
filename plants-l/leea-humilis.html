Leea humilis

Leea humilis, commonly known as the low leea or small leea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its small, simple leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually bisexual and are followed by small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

Leea humilis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
