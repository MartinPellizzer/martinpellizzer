Lobivia chrysantha var. janseniana

Lobivia chrysantha var. janseniana, commonly known as the Jansen's lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to arid regions of South America.

It features short, fleshy stems and produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season. The plant has thick, ribbed leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
