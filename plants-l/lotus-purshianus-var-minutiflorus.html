Lotus purshianus var. minutiflorus

Lotus purshianus var. minutiflorus, commonly known as the dwarf lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and a fibrous root system adapted to poor soils. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
