Lamottea carlinoides

Lamottea carlinoides, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers with white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to arid conditions.
