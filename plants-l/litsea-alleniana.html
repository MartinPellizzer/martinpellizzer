Litsea alleniana

Litsea alleniana, commonly known as the Allen's lime.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, round fruits that turn from green to red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant foliage and potential medicinal properties.
