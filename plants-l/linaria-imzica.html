Linaria imzica

Linaria imzica, commonly known as the Alba daisy or Alba flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the plant family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Linaria imzica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.

It produces small, elongated seed pods that release tiny seeds, aiding in its propagation.
