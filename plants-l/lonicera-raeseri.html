Lonicera raeseri

Lonicera raeseri, commonly known as the early bloom honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that typically bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate to elliptic leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that mature in late summer and are edible for birds.

Lonicera raeseri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
