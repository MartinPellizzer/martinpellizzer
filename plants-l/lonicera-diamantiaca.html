Lonicera diamantiaca

Lonicera diamantiaca, commonly known as the Diamantina honeysuckle, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Hispaniola.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a rough texture.

Lonicera diamantiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
