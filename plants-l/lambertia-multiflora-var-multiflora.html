Lambertia multiflora var. multiflora

Lambertia multiflora var. multiflora, commonly known as the Natal lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lantern-shaped red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a woody stem with dense, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
