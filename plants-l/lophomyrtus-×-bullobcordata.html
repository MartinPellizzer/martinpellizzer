Lophomyrtus × bullobcordata

Lophomyrtus × bullobcordata, commonly known as the kōwhai tree, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy green leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces small, red or black berries that are an important food source for native birds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas across New Zealand.
