Linaria kulabensis

Linaria kulabensis, commonly known as the Kulab Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves are adapted to withstand arid and high-altitude conditions.

Linaria kulabensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
