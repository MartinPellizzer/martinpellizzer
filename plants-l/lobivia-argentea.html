Lobivia argentea

Lobivia argentea, commonly known as the silver leaf lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its silver-gray foliage gives it a distinctive appearance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and collections.

Lobivia argentea thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to flourish.
