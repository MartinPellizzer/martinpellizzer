Litsea pantjara

Litsea pantjara, commonly known as the mountain litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, red berries.

Litsea pantjara is often found in moist, shaded habitats within montane forests.
