Laelia cinnabarina var. crispilabia

Laelia cinnabarina var. crispilabia, commonly known as the Crimson Spotted Cymbidium, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a contrasting yellow lip. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming compact clumps with multiple pseudobulbs. Its flowers are borne on erect, branched inflorescences, often appearing in late winter to early spring.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
