Leucopogon pancheri

Leucopogon pancheri, commonly known as the Pancheri heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
