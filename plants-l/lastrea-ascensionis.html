Lastrea ascensionis

Lastrea ascensionis, commonly known as the Ascension Island liverwort, is a unique moss-like plant belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It thrives in the arid and rocky environments of Ascension Island in the South Atlantic Ocean. This plant is characterized by its small, tightly packed leaves and a lack of true stems, typical of many liverworts. It plays a vital role in stabilizing the soil and providing microhabitats for other organisms.

Due to its restricted distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
