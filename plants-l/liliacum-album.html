Liliacum album

Liliacum album, commonly known as the white lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, white petals that are often fragrant and arranged in a cup-like shape. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces a tall, upright stem.

Liliacum album is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
