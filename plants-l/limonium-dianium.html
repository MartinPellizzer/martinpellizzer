Limonium dianium

Limonium dianium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, silver-green leaves and showy, blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, demonstrating its salt tolerance. It blooms in summer, producing clusters of small, delicate flowers that attract pollinators.

Limonium dianium is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
