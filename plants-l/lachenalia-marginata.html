Lachenalia marginata

Lachenalia marginata, commonly known as the Margined Lachenalia, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white margins. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
