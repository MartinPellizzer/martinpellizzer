Leptorkis longipetala

Leptorkis longipetala, commonly known as the long-petaled orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated petals that can reach up to 10 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. Its flowers are usually a mix of white and pink, with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals.

Leptorkis longipetala is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
