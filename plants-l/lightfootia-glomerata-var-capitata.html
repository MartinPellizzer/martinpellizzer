Lightfootia glomerata var. capitata

Lightfootia glomerata var. capitata, commonly known as the clustered lightfootia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along streambanks. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
