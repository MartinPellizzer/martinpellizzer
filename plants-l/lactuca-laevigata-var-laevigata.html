Lactuca laevigata var. laevigata

Lactuca laevigata var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and alternate leaves that are typically smooth and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

This variety is distinguished by its lack of hairy texture on its leaves, giving it a smooth appearance.
