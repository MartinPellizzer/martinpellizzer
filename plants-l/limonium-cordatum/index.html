Limonium Cordatum

Limonium cordatum is a flowering plant known for its delicate, spiky blooms that thrive in coastal and sandy environments. This species is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, where it often grows in well-drained soils. Its common name, "hearts-ease," reflects the heart-shaped leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens and landscapes. Limonium cordatum is also a hardy plant that can withstand harsh weather conditions, making it a popular choice for coastal gardening.
