Listera tianschanica

Listera tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan basketflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and delicate, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pale yellow, bloom in early spring.

Listera tianschanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
