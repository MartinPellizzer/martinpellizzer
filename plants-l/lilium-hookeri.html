Lilium hookeri

Lilium hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly Chile and Argentina. This perennial plant features tall, slender stems that bear showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale yellow. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, and the plant produces red or orange berries that contain seeds.

Lilium hookeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
