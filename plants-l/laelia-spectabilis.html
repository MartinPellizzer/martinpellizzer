Laelia spectabilis

Laelia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that can range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and rocks in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in the variable climate of its native environment.

Laelia spectabilis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
