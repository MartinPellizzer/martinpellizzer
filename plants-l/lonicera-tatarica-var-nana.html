Lonicera tatarica var. nana

Lonicera tatarica var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf honeysuckle, is a low-growing shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is particularly suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.
