Litsea fulvosericea

Litsea fulvosericea, commonly known as the goldenleaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
