Lotus weilleri

Lotus weilleri, commonly known as the yellow lotus, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged, while its stems are often floating.
