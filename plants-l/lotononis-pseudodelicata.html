Lotononis pseudodelicata

Lotononis pseudodelicata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lotononis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
