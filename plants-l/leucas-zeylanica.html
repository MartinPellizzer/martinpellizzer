Leucas zeylanica

Leucas zeylanica, commonly known as Indian borage, is a flowering plant native to South and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and aromatic. Its flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
