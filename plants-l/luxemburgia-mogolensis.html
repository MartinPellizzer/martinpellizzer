Luxemburgia mogolensis

Luxemburgia mogolensis, commonly known as the Mongolian luxemburgia, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mongolia.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. It blooms primarily during the short growing season in summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
