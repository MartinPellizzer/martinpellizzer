Lepanthes lucifer

Lepanthes lucifer, commonly known as the Flame Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers that resemble flames, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Lepanthes lucifer is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
