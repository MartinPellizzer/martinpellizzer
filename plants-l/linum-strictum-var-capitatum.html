Linum strictum var. capitatum

Linum strictum var. capitatum, commonly known as the clustered flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
