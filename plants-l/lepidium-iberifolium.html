Lepidium iberifolium

Lepidium iberifolium, commonly known as Iberian cress, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and is characterized by its small, edible leaves and yellow flowers. It has a short life cycle and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Lepidium iberifolium is valued for its peppery flavor and has been used in traditional cuisine and herbal remedies.
