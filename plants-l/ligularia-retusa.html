Ligularia retusa

Ligularia retusa, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance.
