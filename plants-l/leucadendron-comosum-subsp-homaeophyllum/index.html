Leucadendron Comosum Subsp. Homaeophyllum

Leucadendron comosum subsp. homaeophyllum is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant yellow to orange flower heads that resemble small cones. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and frost damage.
