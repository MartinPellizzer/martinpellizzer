Lonicera × muendeniensis

Lonicera × muendeniensis, commonly known as the Muenden honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The Muenden honeysuckle is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
