Lysimachia brunelloides

Lysimachia brunelloides, commonly known as the Italian yellow loosestrife, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland edges, stream banks, and wet meadows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a native, hardy ground cover.
