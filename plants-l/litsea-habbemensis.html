Litsea habbemensis

Litsea habbemensis, commonly known as the Habbemensis lime tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by elliptical, yellow fruits that are edible.

Litsea habbemensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in local ecosystems.
