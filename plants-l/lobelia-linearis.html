Lobelia linearis

Lobelia linearis, commonly known as slender bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, linear leaves and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.

Lobelia linearis is also sometimes used in traditional medicine.
