Lessertia nana

Lessertia nana, commonly known as the dwarf lessertia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
