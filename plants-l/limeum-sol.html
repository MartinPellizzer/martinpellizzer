Limeum sol

Limeum sol, commonly known as the sun lime, is a unique plant species characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and succulent leaves.

It thrives in full sunlight and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for arid or semi-arid environments. The plant's botanical features include thick, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its drought resistance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Limeum sol is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
