Lisianthius graciliflorus

Lisianthius graciliflorus, commonly known as the slender-flowered lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.
