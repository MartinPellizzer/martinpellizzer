Ligustrum esquirolii

Ligustrum esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli ligustrum, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is a small evergreen shrub, typically growing to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy dark green upper surface.

It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by black, berry-like fruits.
