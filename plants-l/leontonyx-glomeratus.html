Leontonyx glomeratus

Leontonyx glomeratus, commonly known as the clustered leontonyx, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Leontonyx glomeratus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
