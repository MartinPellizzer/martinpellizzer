Lithops schwantesii

Lithops schwantesii, commonly known as the Schwanter's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically pale green or bluish-gray, with distinct patterns that help in camouflage.

Lithops schwantesii is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
