Lyperia stricta

Lyperia stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lyperia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Lyperia stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
