Leucadendron Vinimontis

Leucadendron vinimontis is a striking shrub native to the mountainous regions of South Africa. Known for its vibrant, cone-like inflorescences, this plant displays a wide range of colors from fiery red to soft yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. The species is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness.
