Lonicera emphyllocalyx

Lonicera emphyllocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Lonicera emphyllocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
