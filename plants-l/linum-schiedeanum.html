Linum schiedeanum

Linum schiedeanum, commonly known as the Schiedea flax.

It is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval-shaped and hairy.

Linum schiedeanum is often found in moist, shaded environments on mountain slopes and forest floors.
