Lobelia reinekeana

Lobelia reinekeana, commonly known as Reineke's lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia reinekeana is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
