Litsea glabraria

Litsea glabraria, commonly known as smoothleaf litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy on both sides. Its small, white flowers are fragrant and appear in clusters, followed by small, dark blue berries.

Litsea glabraria is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
