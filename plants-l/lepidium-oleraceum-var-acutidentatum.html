Lepidium oleraceum var. acutidentatum

Lepidium oleraceum var. acutidentatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wall cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
