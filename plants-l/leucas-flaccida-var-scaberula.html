Leucas flaccida var. scaberula

Leucas flaccida var. scaberula, commonly known as the rough leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used by indigenous communities for various health purposes.
