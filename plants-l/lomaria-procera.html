Lomaria procera

Lomaria procera, commonly known as the tall lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species in suitable climates.

Lomaria procera is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and resilience.
