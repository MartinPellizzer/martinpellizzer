Lotus australis var. pubescens

Lotus australis var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy lotus, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
