Lonicera etrusca var. hirsuta

Lonicera etrusca var. hirsuta, commonly known as the Hirsute Honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features slender, arching stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is often found in woodland edges and rocky soils, thriving in partial to full sun.
