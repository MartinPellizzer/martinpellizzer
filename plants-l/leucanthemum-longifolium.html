Leucanthemum longifolium

Leucanthemum longifolium, commonly known as the slender leucanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy white or pale yellow flowers with a central yellow disk, which bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats.

Leucanthemum longifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wildflower displays.
