Lantana × entrerriensis

Lantana × entrerriensis, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a slight red or purple tint. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
