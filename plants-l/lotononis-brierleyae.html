Lotononis brierleyae

Lotononis brierleyae, commonly known as the Brierley's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
