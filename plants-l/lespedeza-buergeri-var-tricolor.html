Lespedeza buergeri var. tricolor

Lespedeza buergeri var. tricolor, commonly known as the three-color lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, trifoliate leaves and clusters of pink, white, and purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant, multicolored inflorescences.

Lespedeza buergeri var. tricolor is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in landscaped areas.
