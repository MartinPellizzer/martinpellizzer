Lobelia darlingensis

Lobelia darlingensis, commonly known as the Darling's lobelia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and grows in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
