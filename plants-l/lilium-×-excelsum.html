Lilium × excelsum

Lilium × excelsum, commonly known as the giant white lily, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, supporting its impressive floral display. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
