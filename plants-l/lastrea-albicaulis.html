Lastrea albicaulis

Lastrea albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
