Laelia grandiflora

Laelia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora orchid, is a species native to the high altitudes of Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth in harsh, mountainous environments. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, bright conditions with good air circulation.

Due to its beauty and adaptability, it is a popular choice for both horticultural collections and cultivation in controlled environments.
