Lilium Canadense Subsp. Superbum

Canadian tiger lily, scientifically known as Lilium canadense subsp. superbum, is a striking perennial plant native to eastern North America. With its vibrant orange petals and dark red streaks, it stands out in gardens and natural habitats alike. This subspecies is particularly noted for its bold, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. The Canadian tiger lily is not only a beautiful ornamental plant but also holds cultural and ecological significance in its native regions.
