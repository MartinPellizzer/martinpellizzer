Lithops pseudotruncatella var. alpina

Lithops pseudotruncatella var. alpina, commonly known as the Cape Town lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It prefers well-draining soil and minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or succulent collections.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
