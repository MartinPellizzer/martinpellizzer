Libocedrus decurrens aureovariegata

Libocedrus decurrens aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated incense cedar, is a unique variant of the incense cedar species.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, which features yellow and green coloration on its leaves. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its aromatic wood is highly valued for its durability and scent, making it a sought-after ornamental and timber species.

The plant's botanical features make it an attractive choice for gardens and landscapes.
