Lepanthes ionoptera

Lepanthes ionoptera, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a striking violet or purple hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

Lepanthes ionoptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
