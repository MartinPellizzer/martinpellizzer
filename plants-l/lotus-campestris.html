Lotus campestris

Lotus campestris, commonly known as the sweet senna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring palmately compound leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Lotus campestris thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Its flowers bloom from spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
