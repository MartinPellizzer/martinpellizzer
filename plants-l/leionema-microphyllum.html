Leionema microphyllum

Leionema microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved leionema, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which appear in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and tableland regions.

Leionema microphyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native landscaping and restoration projects.
