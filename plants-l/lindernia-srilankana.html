Lindernia srilankana

Lindernia srilankana, commonly known as the Srilankan Lindernia, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Lindernia srilankana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic contribution.
