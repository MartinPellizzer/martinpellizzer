Lindleya

Lindleya, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often brightly colored bracts that resemble lanterns, which are modified leaves used to attract pollinators. The genus includes around 50 species, many of which are cultivated for their ornamental value.
