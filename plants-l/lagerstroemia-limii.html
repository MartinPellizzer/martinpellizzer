Lagerstroemia limii

Lagerstroemia limii, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that range from pink to red, which appear in late spring or early summer. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, papery fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate or elliptic, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Lagerstroemia limii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
