Lachenalia orchioides subsp. orchioides

Lachenalia orchioides subsp.

orchioides, commonly known as the orchid-like lachenalia, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and striking, orchid-like flowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
