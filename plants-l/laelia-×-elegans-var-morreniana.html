Laelia × elegans var. morreniana

Laelia × elegans var. morreniana, commonly known as the Morren's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, showcasing a range of hues from white to pink and purple. It prefers cool, bright conditions with high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
