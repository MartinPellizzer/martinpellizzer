Lepidium montanum var. stellae

Lepidium montanum var. stellae, commonly known as the star cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces slender, branching stems that bear the showy inflorescences. Native to mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in alpine ecosystems.
