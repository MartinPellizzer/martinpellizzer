Lonicera setifera

Lonicera setifera, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters. Its fruits are red berries that mature in late summer, attracting various wildlife.

Lonicera setifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
