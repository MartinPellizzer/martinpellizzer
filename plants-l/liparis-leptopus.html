Liparis leptopus

Liparis leptopus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in a loose inflorescence.

Liparis leptopus is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
