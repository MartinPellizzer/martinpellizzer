Liparis sanamalabarica

Liparis sanamalabarica, commonly known as the Sanamalabar Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, aiding in water retention.

Liparis sanamalabarica is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
