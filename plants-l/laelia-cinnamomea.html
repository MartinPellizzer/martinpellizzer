Laelia cinnamomea

Laelia cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, cinnamon-brown to orange-red flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that typically appear in late winter to early spring. Laelia cinnamomea thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with good air circulation.

It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
