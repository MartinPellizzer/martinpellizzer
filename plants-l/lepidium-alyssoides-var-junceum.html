Lepidium alyssoides var. junceum

Lepidium alyssoides var. junceum, commonly known as slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear leaves. It is often found in coastal areas and can thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
