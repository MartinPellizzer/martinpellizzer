Laelia × elegans var. prasiata

Laelia × elegans var. prasiata, commonly known as the Emerald Lady Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant green and white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant typically produces pendant, fragrant blooms with a unique coloration that ranges from pale green to deep emerald. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
