Laburnum pendulum

Laburnum pendulum, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that appear in spring, giving it a striking appearance. The leaves are pinnate, with each leaflet being narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
