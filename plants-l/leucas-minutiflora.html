Leucas minutiflora

Leucas minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Leucas minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
