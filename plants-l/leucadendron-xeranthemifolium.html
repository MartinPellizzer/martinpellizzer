Leucadendron xeranthemifolium

Leucadendron xeranthemifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
