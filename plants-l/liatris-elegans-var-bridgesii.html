Liatris elegans var. bridgesii

Liatris elegans var. bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridges' blazing star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, pinkish-purple flower heads. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open woodlands and prairies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its bloom period typically occurs in late summer to early fall, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
