Ludwigia inclinata var. verticillata

Ludwigia inclinata var. verticillata, commonly known as the spiral Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features whorled leaves that give it its characteristic spiral appearance, making it a popular choice for aquariums. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and thrives in moderate to high light conditions. Its small, bright red or orange flowers are produced in dense clusters, adding vibrant color to the water.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
