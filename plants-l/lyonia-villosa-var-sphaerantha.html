Lyonia villosa var. sphaerantha

Lyonia villosa var. sphaerantha, commonly known as the woolly lyonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, showy white or pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a resilient and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
