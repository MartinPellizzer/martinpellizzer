Lobularia tenuifolia

Lobularia tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lobularia, is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has fine, thread-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
