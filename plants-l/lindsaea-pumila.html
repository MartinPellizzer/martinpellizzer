Lindsaea pumila

Lindsaea pumila, commonly known as the small fern or slender lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that form a rosette shape, and it typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spore-bearing structures called sporangia. Lindsaea pumila is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
