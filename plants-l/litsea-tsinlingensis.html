Litsea tsinlingensis

Litsea tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Guangxi and Guangdong provinces. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface.
