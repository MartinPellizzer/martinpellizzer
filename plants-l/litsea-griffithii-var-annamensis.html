Litsea griffithii var. annamensis

Litsea griffithii var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Cambodia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe, containing a single seed.

This variant is often found in lowland evergreen forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
