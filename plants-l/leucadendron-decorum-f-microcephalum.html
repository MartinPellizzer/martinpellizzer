Leucadendron decorum f. microcephalum

Leucadendron decorum f. microcephalum, commonly known as the dwarf king protea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, fluffy white bracts that surround a central cone of yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens and as a container plant.
