Lens culinaris subsp. odemensis

Lens culinaris subsp.

odemensis, commonly known as the Egyptian lentil, is a subspecies of the common lentil native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds that are typically brown or yellow in color.

The plant is an annual legume with trifoliate leaves and small white or purple flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is adapted to well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its nutritional content and has been cultivated for thousands of years.
