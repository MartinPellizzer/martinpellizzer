Ligustrum ibota var. regelianum

Ligustrum ibota var. regelianum, commonly known as the Regelian privet, is a variant of the privet shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.

This variety is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
