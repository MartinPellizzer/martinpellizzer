Linum strictum var. axillare

Linum strictum var. axillare, commonly known as the slender flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, blue to violet in color, and are borne in clusters at the leaf axils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
