Lepanthes acuminata subsp. acuminata

Lepanthes acuminata subsp.

acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America. It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and bloom during the rainy season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and blooming habits.
