Laelia anceps var. ashworthiana

Laelia anceps var. ashworthiana, commonly known as the Ashworth's laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the highlands of Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
