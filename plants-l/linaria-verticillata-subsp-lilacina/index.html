Linaria Verticillata Subsp. Lilacina

Linaria verticillata subsp. lilacina is a striking member of the plant family Plantaginaceae, known for its vibrant lilac-pink flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this subspecies thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. The plant's ability to flourish in harsh conditions highlights its resilience and adaptability. With its delicate yet bold appearance, it captivates both botanists and garden enthusiasts alike.
