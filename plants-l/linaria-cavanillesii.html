Linaria cavanillesii

Linaria cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
