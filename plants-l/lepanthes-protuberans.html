Lepanthes protuberans

Lepanthes protuberans, commonly known as the protuberant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests, where it thrives in the understory. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, adapted to capture limited light.

Lepanthes protuberans is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
