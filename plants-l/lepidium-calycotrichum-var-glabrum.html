Lepidium calycotrichum var. glabrum

Lepidium calycotrichum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth wall peppergrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and small, white flowers.
