Limonium dielsianum

Limonium dielsianum, commonly known as the sea helene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color.

Limonium dielsianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
