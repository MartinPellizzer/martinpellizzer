Lespedeza patens f. macrantha

Lespedeza patens f. macrantha, commonly known as the largeflower lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Lespedeza patens f. macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in ecological restoration projects.
