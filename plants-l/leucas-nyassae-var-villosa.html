Leucas nyassae var. villosa

Leucas nyassae var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "villosa.". It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color.

This species is often found in grassland and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
