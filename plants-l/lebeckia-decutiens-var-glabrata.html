Lebeckia decutiens var. glabrata

Lebeckia decutiens var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with dark centers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically hairless, giving it its "glabrata" designation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
