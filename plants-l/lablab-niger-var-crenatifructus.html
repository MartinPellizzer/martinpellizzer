Lablab niger var. crenatifructus

Lablab niger var. crenatifructus, commonly known as the hairy cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and trifoliate leaves, which are typically green with a rough texture. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain edible, seed-like legumes. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

The flowers are usually pale purple or white, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
