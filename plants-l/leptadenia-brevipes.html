Leptadenia brevipes

Leptadenia brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, alternate leaves with a waxy surface. Its flowers are greenish-yellow and are borne in clusters, often accompanied by milky sap.
