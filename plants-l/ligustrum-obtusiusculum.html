Ligustrum obtusiusculum

Ligustrum obtusiusculum, commonly known as the narrowleaf privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often used in landscaping due to its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.

Ligustrum obtusiusculum is also valued for its ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
