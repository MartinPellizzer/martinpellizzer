Leucopoa kingii

Leucopoa kingii, commonly known as King's leucopoa, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Leucopoa kingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
