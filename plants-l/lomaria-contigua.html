Lomaria contigua

Lomaria contigua, commonly known as the common lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small and clustered in loose inflorescences, often displaying shades of pink or white.

Lomaria contigua is known for its adaptability to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
