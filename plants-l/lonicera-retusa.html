Lonicera retusa

Lonicera retusa, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often gland-dotted and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in early summer. Its berries, which are dark red when mature, are edible and attract various wildlife.

Lonicera retusa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
