Limonium zembrae

Limonium zembrae, commonly known as the Zembra sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Limonium zembrae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
