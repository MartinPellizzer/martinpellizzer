Lilium partheneion

Lilium partheneion, commonly known as the Partheneion lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe, including parts of the Carpathian Mountains. This perennial herb produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
