Lepidaploa pseudaurea

Its flowers are typically yellow or golden, contributing to its common name and attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Lepidaploa pseudaurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
