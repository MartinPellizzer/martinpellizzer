Leperiza latifolia

Leperiza latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf leperiza, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a smooth texture.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by elongated, fleshy fruits.
