Ligusticopsis purpurascens

Ligusticopsis purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered ligusticopsis, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, showy purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils with potential medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
