Laportea contracta

Laportea contracta, commonly known as the common cupweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and can often be found in moist or dry environments.
