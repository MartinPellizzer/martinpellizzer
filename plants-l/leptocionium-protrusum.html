Leptocionium protrusum

Leptocionium protrusum, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several leaves arranged along its length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a distinctive member of the orchid family.
