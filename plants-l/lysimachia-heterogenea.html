Lysimachia heterogenea

Lysimachia heterogenea, commonly known as the variable wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lysimachia heterogenea is known for its ornamental value and is often used in native plant gardens.
