Linaria capensis

Linaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape fumitory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Linaria capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
