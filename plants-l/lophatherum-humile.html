Lophatherum humile

Lophatherum humile, commonly known as the small-leaf lophatherum, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly referred to as the grass family. The plant features slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
