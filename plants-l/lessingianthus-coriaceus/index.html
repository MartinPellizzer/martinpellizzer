Lessingianthus Coriaceus

Lessingianthus coriaceus is a striking plant native to the highlands of South America. It is known for its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of red and yellow. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its tough, leathery leaves help it survive in harsh conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure member of the Asteraceae family.
