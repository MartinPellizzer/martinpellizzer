Lepanthes carinifera

Lepanthes carinifera, commonly known as the ridged violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces small, greenish leaves that are often clustered at the base. Lepanthes carinifera thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in the understory of tropical forests.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
