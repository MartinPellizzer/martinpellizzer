Litrisa

Litrisa, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy yellow to orange flower heads surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. Litrisa typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and resilient species in alpine ecosystems.
