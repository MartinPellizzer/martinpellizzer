Leiophyllum hugeri

Leiophyllum hugeri, commonly known as the Hawaiian rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered white or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, sunny environments.
