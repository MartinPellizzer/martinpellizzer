Lobelia spectabilis

Lobelia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces striking cobalt-blue to violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia spectabilis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
