Lonicera glabrata var. glabrata

Lonicera glabrata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
