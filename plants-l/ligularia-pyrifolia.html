Ligularia pyrifolia

Ligularia pyrifolia, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and have a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance.
