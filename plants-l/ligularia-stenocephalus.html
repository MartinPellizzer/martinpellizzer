Ligularia stenocephalus

Ligularia stenocephalus, commonly known as the narrow-headed ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive dark green color and prominent veins. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, arranged in a dense, terminal cluster. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens with proper moisture and drainage.
