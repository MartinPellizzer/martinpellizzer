Lepisanthes mixta

Lepisanthes mixta, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree branches in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Lepisanthes mixta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
