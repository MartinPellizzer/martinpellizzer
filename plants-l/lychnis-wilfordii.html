Lychnis wilfordii

Lychnis wilfordii, commonly known as the Wilford's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many showy flowering plants. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance.
