Limonium binervosum subsp. lychnidifolium

Limonium binervosum subsp.

lychnidifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves and delicate blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is often found in dunes and along shorelines, where it plays a role in stabilizing sand.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
