Lathyrus strictus

Lathyrus strictus, commonly known as the slender pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves, with small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several elliptical seeds.

Lathyrus strictus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in nitrogen fixation in soil.
