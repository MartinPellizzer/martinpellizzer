Lythrum adsurgens

Lythrum adsurgens, commonly known as tall loosestrife, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features upright, sparsely branched stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in dense spikes, which bloom from June to September. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Lythrum adsurgens is adapted to moist soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.
