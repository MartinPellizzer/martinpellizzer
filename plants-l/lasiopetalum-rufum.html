Lasiopetalum rufum

Lasiopetalum rufum, commonly known as the red-flowered honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink in color, which attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Lasiopetalum rufum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
