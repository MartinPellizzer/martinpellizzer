Liatris cylindracea

Liatris cylindracea, commonly known as the slender blazingstar, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the central and southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, unbranched stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces showy, spiky flower heads that range in color from pink to purple. Liatris cylindracea typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
