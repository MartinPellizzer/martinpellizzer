Linaria Dalmatica Var. Grandiflora

Linaria dalmatica var. grandiflora is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant, colorful blooms. This variety of the common toadflax is native to southern Europe and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its long, slender stems bear clusters of tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. It is a hardy perennial that adds a splash of color to landscapes throughout the growing season.
