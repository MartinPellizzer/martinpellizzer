Lockhartia costaricensis

Lockhartia costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces upright, branching stems that bear lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a pinkish or reddish lip, and it blooms during the dry season.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
