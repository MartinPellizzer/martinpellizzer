Lycopodioides schiedeniana

Lycopodioides schiedeniana, commonly known as the Schiede's clubmoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is a small, evergreen herbaceous plant characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and scale-like leaves. The plant produces sporophylls that bear microsporangia and megasporangia, typical of its evolutionary lineage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity.

Its reproductive structures are essential for its propagation and ecological role in forest understories.
