Lasianthus obliquinervis subsp. obliquinervis

Lasianthus obliquinervis subsp.

obliquinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with distinct, oblique venation.
