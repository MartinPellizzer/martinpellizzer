Linaria × jalancina

Linaria × jalancina, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a cross between Linaria vulgaris and Linaria maroccana.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces vibrant, often yellow or orange, tubular flowers. Its foliage is typically narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Linaria × jalancina is valued in horticulture for its bright blooms and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
