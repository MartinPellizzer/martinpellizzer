Lantana annua

Lantana annua, commonly known as the common lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Lantana annua is known for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
