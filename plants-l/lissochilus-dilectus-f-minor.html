Lissochilus dilectus f. minor

Lissochilus dilectus f. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered lissochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, slender stems and inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinct lip that is slightly curled.

This orchid is relatively rare and is found in specific regions of South America, particularly in Peru and Ecuador.
