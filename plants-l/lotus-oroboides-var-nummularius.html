Lotus oroboides var. nummularius

Lotus oroboides var. nummularius, commonly known as the silverleaf lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of silvery-gray, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
