Ligustrum sinense var. luodianense

Ligustrum sinense var. luodianense, commonly known as the Luodian privet, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or red when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping due to its adaptability and ease of growth.

This variety is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects.
