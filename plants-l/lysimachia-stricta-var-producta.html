Lysimachia stricta var. producta

Lysimachia stricta var. producta, commonly known as the creeping yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically arranged in whorls around the stems.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.
