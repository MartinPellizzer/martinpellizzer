Liparis cyclostele

Liparis cyclostele, commonly known as the cyclostele orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is distinguished by its unique floral structure and specialized root system.
