Lasianthus africanus

Lasianthus africanus, commonly known as the African lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lasianthus africanus thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savannah habitats.
