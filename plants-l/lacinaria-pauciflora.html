Lacinaria pauciflora

Lacinaria pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered lacinaria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Lacinaria pauciflora is adapted to dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
