Lobelia pinifolia f. rubriflora

Lobelia pinifolia f. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered pine lobelia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its unique color and texture.
