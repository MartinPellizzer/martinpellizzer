Lasthenia macrantha subsp. prisca

Lasthenia macrantha subsp.

prisca, commonly known as the ancient lasthenia, is a flowering plant native to California. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish to greenish flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats. It has narrow, linear leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
