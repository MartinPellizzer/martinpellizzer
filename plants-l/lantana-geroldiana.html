Lantana geroldiana

Lantana geroldiana, commonly known as the purple lantana, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in landscaping.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions and requires careful management.
