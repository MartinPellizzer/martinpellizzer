Liriope exiliflora

Liriope exiliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily-turf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and slightly curled, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale purple. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Liriope exiliflora is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.
