Luxemburgia speciosa

Luxemburgia speciosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are dark green on top and silver underneath.

It is native to regions of South America and is often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types make it a common weed in agricultural areas.

Due to its invasive potential, it is often considered a nuisance in cultivated landscapes.
