Liparis brachystele

Liparis brachystele, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Liparis brachystele is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
