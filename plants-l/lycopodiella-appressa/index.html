Lycopodiella Appressa

Lycopodiella appressa is a small, primitive plant that belongs to the lycophyte group. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. This species is characterized by its simple structure and lack of true leaves. It plays an important role in the ecological balance of its habitat. Despite its modest size, it is a fascinating subject for botanical study.
