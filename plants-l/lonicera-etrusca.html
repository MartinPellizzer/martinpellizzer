Lonicera etrusca

Lonicera etrusca, commonly known as the Tuscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruits are red berries that contain small, hard seeds.

Lonicera etrusca is often found in woodland edges and moist soils, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
