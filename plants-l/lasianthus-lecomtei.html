Lasianthus lecomtei

Lasianthus lecomtei, commonly known as the Lecomte's lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
