Lantana collina

Lantana collina, commonly known as wild lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are usually oval in shape. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to different environments.

Lantana collina is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and abandoned fields.
