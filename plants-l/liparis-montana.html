Liparis montana

Liparis montana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring a rhizome and a rosette of grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Liparis montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with proper drainage and humidity.
