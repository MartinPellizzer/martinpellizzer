Leuconymphaea odorata

Leuconymphaea odorata, commonly known as the sweet flag, is a perennial aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in spikes, blooming in the summer months. Its rhizomes are extensively used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
