Ligularia fischeri var. spiciformis

Ligularia fischeri var. spiciformis, commonly known as the Spiky Ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces tall, spire-like inflorescences that bear small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its dramatic foliage and unique floral display.
