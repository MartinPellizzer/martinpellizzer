Lonicera chrysantha f. regeliana

Lonicera chrysantha f. regeliana, commonly known as the golden honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
