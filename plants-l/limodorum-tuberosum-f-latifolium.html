Limodorum tuberosum f. latifolium

Limodorum tuberosum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved lily of the Nile, is a variant of the lily of the Nile species characterized by its broader leaves.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands or marshy areas. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and lanceolate, often hairy leaves.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
