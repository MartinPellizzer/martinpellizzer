Ligularia ledebourii

Ligularia ledebourii, commonly known as the Himalayan ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with cool climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
