Lastrea aristata

Lastrea aristata, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
