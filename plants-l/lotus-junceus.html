Lotus junceus

Lotus junceus, commonly known as the slender lotus, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia, and thrives in wet or marshy environments. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The slender lotus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
