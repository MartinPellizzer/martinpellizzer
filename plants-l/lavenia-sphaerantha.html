Lavenia sphaerantha

Lavenia sphaerantha, commonly known as the globe lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spherical inflorescence that resembles a globe, composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Lavenia sphaerantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
