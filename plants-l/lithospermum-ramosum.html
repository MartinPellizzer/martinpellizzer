Lithospermum ramosum

Lithospermum ramosum, commonly known as red root, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are rich in lithospermic acid, which gives them their characteristic reddish color.

Lithospermum ramosum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
