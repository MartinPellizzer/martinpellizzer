Lyperia squarrosa

Lyperia squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose lyperia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, squarrose (spread-out) arrangement of its inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, hairy seeds.

Lyperia squarrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
