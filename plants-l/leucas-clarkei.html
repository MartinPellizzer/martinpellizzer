Leucas clarkei

Leucas clarkei, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
