Ludwigia juncea

Ludwigia juncea, commonly known as the slender Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and pond margins. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color, especially when grown in low light conditions. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, adding ornamental value to aquatic gardens.

Ludwigia juncea is often cultivated as a decorative plant in water gardens due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
