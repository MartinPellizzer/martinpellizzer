Lithospermum diffusum var. micranthum

Lithospermum diffusum var. micranthum, commonly known as narrowflower lithospermum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, pink to purple flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

The roots contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
