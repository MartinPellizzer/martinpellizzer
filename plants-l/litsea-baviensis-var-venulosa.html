Litsea baviensis var. venulosa

Litsea baviensis var. venulosa, commonly known as the Baviensis var. venulosa, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often aromatic leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary clusters.

The fruit is a small, dark-colored drupe that develops from the flowering period.
