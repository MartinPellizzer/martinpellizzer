Lepidopironia cenchriformis

Lepidopironia cenchriformis, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth and distinctive reddish tinge in its foliage. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in grasslands and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Lepidopironia cenchriformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
