Lilium carniolicum

Lilium carniolicum, commonly known as the Carniolan lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe, including parts of the Alps and Carpathians. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive red or purple throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Lilium carniolicum is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
