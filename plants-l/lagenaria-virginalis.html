Lagenaria virginalis

Lagenaria virginalis, commonly known as the bottle gourd, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is cultivated for its edible, hollow fruit. It is valued for its versatility in cooking and traditional uses.
