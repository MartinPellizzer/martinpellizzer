Laserpitium archangelica

Laserpitium archangelica, commonly known as angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or yellow in color. The plant has a woody stem and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
