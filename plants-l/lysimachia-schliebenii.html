Lysimachia schliebenii

Lysimachia schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Lysimachia schliebenii is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
