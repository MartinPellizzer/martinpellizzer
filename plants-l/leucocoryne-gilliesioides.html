Leucocoryne gilliesioides

Leucocoryne gilliesioides, commonly known as the silverleaf lily, is a striking plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of broad, waxy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces tall, erect stems topped with clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both gardens and collections.
