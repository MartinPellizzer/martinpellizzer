Luzula parviflora subsp. yezoensis

Luzula parviflora subsp.

yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue or white flowers. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in cool, temperate climates.
