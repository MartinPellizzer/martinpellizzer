Linospadix elegans

Linospadix elegans, commonly known as the velvet-leafed spadix, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a brightly colored spathe. The plant's foliage consists of large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Linospadix elegans thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
