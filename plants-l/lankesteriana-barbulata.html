Lankesteriana barbulata

Lankesteriana barbulata, commonly known as the velvet tunicate orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple and green foliage. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
