Lindsaea kunzei

Lindsaea kunzei, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, silver-hued fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its rhizomes are wiry and often form dense clusters, allowing it to thrive in humid environments.
