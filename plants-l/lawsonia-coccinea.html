Lawsonia coccinea

Lawsonia coccinea, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
