Lilaeopsis novae-zelandiae

Lilaeopsis novae-zelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand lily, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams.
