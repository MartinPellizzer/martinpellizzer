Lastrea splendens

Lastrea splendens, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are typically vibrant green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Lastrea splendens is a perennial plant that can be propagated through stem cuttings or seeds.
