Luzula spadicea var. desvauxii

Luzula spadicea var. desvauxii, commonly known as the Desvaux's wood sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Its foliage is often a deep green, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in moist garden settings.
