Lindackeria poggei

Lindackeria poggei, commonly known as the Bog Rosemary, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of North America.

It is a member of the heather family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and needle-like, adapted to thrive in moist, acidic soils.
