Lepanthes erythrostanga

Lepanthes erythrostanga, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a red or purple tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

Lepanthes erythrostanga is a popular orchid among horticulturists due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
