Luetzelburgia auriculata

Luetzelburgia auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sunlight.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and xeriscaping.
