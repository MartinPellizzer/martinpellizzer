Lessertia spinescens

Lessertia spinescens, commonly known as the spiny lessertia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping in dry climates.
