Lamium gilongensis

Lamium gilongensis, commonly known as the Chinese deadnettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, two-lipped flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lamium gilongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
