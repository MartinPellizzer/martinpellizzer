Lepanthes calophlebia

Lepanthes calophlebia, commonly known as the Crimson Violet Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid with striking, brightly colored flowers that typically range from red to purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, and the flowers are borne on terminal inflorescences.

Lepanthes calophlebia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
