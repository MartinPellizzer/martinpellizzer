Leucheria araucana

Leucheria araucana, commonly known as the Araucaria tree, is a coniferous evergreen native to South America.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and distinctive feather-like leaves. The tree can grow up to 50 meters in height and is often found in temperate rainforests. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appearance and historical significance in indigenous cultures.
