Lepanthes × mendietae

Lepanthes × mendietae, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Lepanthes grandiflora and Lepanthes sanguinea. This plant is characterized by its striking, often violet or purple flowers with intricate patterns. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests.

The botanical interest in Lepanthes × mendietae lies in its unique floral morphology and genetic diversity.
