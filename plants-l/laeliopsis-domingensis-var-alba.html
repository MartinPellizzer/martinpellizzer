Laeliopsis domingensis var. alba

Laeliopsis domingensis var. alba, commonly known as the white domingensis orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a short, erect stem and produces clusters of small, white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
