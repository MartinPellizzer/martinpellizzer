Lotus purpurascens

Lotus purpurascens, commonly known as the purple lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots are anchored in the substrate.

Lotus purpurascens is also noted for its ornamental value and traditional use in various cultural practices.
