Lendneria

Lendneria, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. Lendneria species typically have thick, fleshy leaves that help them store water in their native arid environments. They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The genus includes several species, with Lendneria maculata being one of the most widely cultivated varieties.
