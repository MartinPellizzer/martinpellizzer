Lobelia fonticola

Lobelia fonticola, commonly known as the spring lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Lobelia fonticola is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland conditions.
