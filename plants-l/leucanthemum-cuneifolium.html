Leucanthemum cuneifolium

Leucanthemum cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf white daisy, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, giving it a distinctive appearance.
