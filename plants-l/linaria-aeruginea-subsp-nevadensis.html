Linaria aeruginea subsp. nevadensis

Linaria aeruginea subsp.

nevadensis, commonly known as the Sierra primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or open habitats.

The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to high-altitude environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
