Leonurus sibiricus var. albiflorus

Leonurus sibiricus var. albiflorus, commonly known as white-flowered hedgehog.

This variant of the hedgehog plant is characterized by its white flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It is native to regions of Asia, including parts of China and Siberia. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
