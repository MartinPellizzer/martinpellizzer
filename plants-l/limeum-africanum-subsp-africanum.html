Limeum africanum subsp. africanum

Limeum africanum subsp.

africanum, commonly known as the African lime or African citrus, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by round, green fruits resembling citrus fruits in appearance.

The fruit contains a single seed and is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
