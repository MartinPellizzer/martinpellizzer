Lappa bardana subsp. minor

Lappa bardana subsp.

minor, commonly known as lesser lappa, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves.
