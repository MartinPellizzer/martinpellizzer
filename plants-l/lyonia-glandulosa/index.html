Lyonia Glandulosa

Lyonia glandulosa is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its delicate white flowers and is often found in moist, acidic soils. this plant is commonly used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness. it thrives in partial shade and is relatively easy to care for. its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardeners.
