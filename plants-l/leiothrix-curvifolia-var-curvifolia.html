Leiothrix curvifolia var. curvifolia

Leiothrix curvifolia var. curvifolia, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
