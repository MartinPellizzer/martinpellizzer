Lomatia angustifolia

Lomatia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lomatia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces distinctive, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Lomatia angustifolia is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
