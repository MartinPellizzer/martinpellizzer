Lobelia mollis

Lobelia mollis, commonly known as the soft lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are oval-shaped with a soft texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Lobelia mollis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
