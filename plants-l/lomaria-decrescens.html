Lomaria decrescens

Lomaria decrescens, commonly known as the creeping lomaria, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
