Linaria euxina

Linaria euxina, commonly known as the Turkish toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Linaria euxina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
