Lepidium bonariense

Lepidium bonariense, commonly known as wild cress, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, white or yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are usually pinnately divided. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Lepidium bonariense is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas quickly.
