Listia marlothii

Listia marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's flaxflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
