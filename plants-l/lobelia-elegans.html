Lobelia elegans

Lobelia elegans, commonly known as the elegant lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia elegans thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
