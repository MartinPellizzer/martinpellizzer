Leria nutans f. sinuata

Leria nutans f. sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous hanging leria, is a unique variant of the species Leria nutans.

It is characterized by its drooping, cascading growth habit, with leaves that are often sinuous or wavy in shape. The plant typically features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its foliage is usually glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its delicate inflorescences.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in hanging baskets or as a decorative foliage plant.
