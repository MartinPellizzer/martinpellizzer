Lychnodiscus cerospermus var. pedicellaris

Lychnodiscus cerospermus var. pedicellaris, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, making it visually striking. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and prefers well-drained sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal dunes and rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
