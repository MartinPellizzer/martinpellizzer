Lavandula × allardii

Lavandula × allardii, commonly known as the hybrid lavender, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, spike-like inflorescences that range in color from pale lavender to deep purple. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Lavandula × allardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and cosmetics.
