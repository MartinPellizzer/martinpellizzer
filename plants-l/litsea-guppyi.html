Litsea guppyi

Litsea guppyi, commonly known as the Guppy's litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Litsea guppyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
