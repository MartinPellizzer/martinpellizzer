Leucocoryne violacescens

Leucocoryne violacescens, commonly known as the violet-flowered leucocoryne, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a bulbous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are typically blue-green in color. Its flowers are striking, with violet petals and a distinctive white throat, blooming in late winter to early spring.

It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
