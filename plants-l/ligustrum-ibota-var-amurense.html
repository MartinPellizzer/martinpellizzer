Ligustrum ibota var. amurense

Ligustrum ibota var. amurense, commonly known as Amur privet, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.

The species is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
