Licuala multibracteata

Licuala multibracteata, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The leaves are often used in traditional cuisine and crafts, particularly in the preparation of pandan-flavored foods. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
