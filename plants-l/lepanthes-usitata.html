Lepanthes usitata

Lepanthes usitata, commonly known as the common orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, grass-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Lepanthes usitata is known for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
