Lolanara odorata

Lolanara odorata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, aromatic flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique fragrance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
