Licuala kunstleri

Licuala kunstleri, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in humid conditions make it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

The plant's unique appearance and resilience contribute to its significance in both horticulture and ecological contexts.
