Lagoseris sancta subsp. nemausensis

Lagoseris sancta subsp.

nemausensis, commonly known as the Nemausensis sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe. It typically features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems.

The plant has alternate, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, dry environments.
