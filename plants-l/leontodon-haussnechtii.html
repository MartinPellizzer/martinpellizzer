Leontodon haussnechtii

Leontodon haussnechtii, commonly known as the Hausseneck's toothbrush, is a flowering plant native to Central and Southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
