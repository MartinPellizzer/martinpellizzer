Lathyrus ornatus

Lathyrus ornatus, commonly known as the showy vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its slender, trailing stems and clusters of pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several angular, brown seeds. Lathyrus ornatus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
