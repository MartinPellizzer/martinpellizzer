Ludwigia palustris var. ovalis

Ludwigia palustris var. ovalis, commonly known as the ovalleaf water primrose, is a aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It features oval, dark green leaves that are often slightly waxy, adapted for water environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the water's surface. It is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and ditches.

This variety is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in various water conditions.
