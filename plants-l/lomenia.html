Lomenia

Lomenia, commonly known as the water lily, is a aquatic plant belonging to the family Nymphaeaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that anchors it in shallow water or mud. Lomenia species are found in various freshwater habitats around the world.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic ecosystems.
