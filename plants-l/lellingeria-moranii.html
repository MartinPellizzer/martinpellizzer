Lellingeria moranii

Lellingeria moranii, commonly known as the Moran's lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
