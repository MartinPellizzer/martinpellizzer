Lobelia chinensis

Lobelia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese lobelia, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
