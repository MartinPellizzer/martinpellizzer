Leucaena shannonii

Leucaena shannonii, commonly known as Shannon's leucaena, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant has bipinnate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and appear in clusters.

Shannon's leucaena is valued for its forage quality and ability to thrive in poor soils.
