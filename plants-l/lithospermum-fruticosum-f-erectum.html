Lithospermum fruticosum f. erectum

Lithospermum fruticosum f. erectum, commonly known as the upright lithospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, reddish-purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its roots contain compounds with potential medicinal properties, including antifungal and antibacterial agents.

This variety is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments.
