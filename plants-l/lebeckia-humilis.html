Lebeckia humilis

Lebeckia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf lebeckia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are densely packed along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Lebeckia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
