Lycaste schilleriana var. lehmannii

Lycaste schilleriana var. lehmannii, commonly known as the Schiller's lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow throat. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It produces pseudobulbs that support its growth and help it survive in fluctuating moisture conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
