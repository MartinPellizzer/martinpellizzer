Lobelia × gerardii var. lugdunensis

Lobelia × gerardii var. lugdunensis, commonly known as the slender blue lobelia, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy blue to violet flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
