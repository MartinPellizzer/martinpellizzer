Lemuropisum edule

Lemuropisum edule, commonly known as the edible leaf plant, is a member of the Malvaceae family, characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy flowers.

Due to its adaptability and nutritional value, it is cultivated in various parts of the world for both culinary and medicinal purposes.
