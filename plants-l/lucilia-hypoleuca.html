Lucilia hypoleuca

Lucilia hypoleuca, commonly known as the green bottle fly, is a species of fly belonging to the family Calliphoridae.

It is characterized by its metallic green body and distinctive black markings. The plant aspect of this species refers to its role as a saprophage, feeding on decaying organic matter. Its larvae are often found in carrion and can play a role in decomposition processes.

This species is widely distributed and can be found in various habitats across temperate regions.
