Ligularia stenoglossa

Ligularia stenoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green hue and a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland gardens and bog landscapes.
