Linum biebersteinii

Linum biebersteinii, commonly known as the Sierra linseed plant, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their showy, showy flowers. The Sierra linseed plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.
