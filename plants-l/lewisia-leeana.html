Lewisia leeana

Lewisia leeana, commonly known as the silverleaf lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its leaves are succulent and covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss.

Lewisia leeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and low maintenance requirements in arid climates.
