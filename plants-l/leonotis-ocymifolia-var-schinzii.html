Leonotis ocymifolia var. schinzii

Leonotis ocymifolia var. schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's lion's tail, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a woody stem that becomes more branched with age. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators like birds and bees.
