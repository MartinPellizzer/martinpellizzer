Linaria macroura var. splendida

Linaria macroura var. splendida, commonly known as the splendour toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
