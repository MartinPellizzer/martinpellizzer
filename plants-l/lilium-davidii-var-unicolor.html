Lilium davidii var. unicolor

Lilium davidii var. unicolor, commonly known as the Chinese white lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with strap-like, grassy leaves. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help it survive harsh winters.

The flowers have six tepals that are usually fully open, giving it a elegant and classic appearance.
