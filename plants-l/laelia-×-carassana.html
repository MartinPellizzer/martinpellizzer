Laelia × carassana

Laelia × carassana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Cattleya species.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Laelia × carassana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its hardiness and ornamental value.
