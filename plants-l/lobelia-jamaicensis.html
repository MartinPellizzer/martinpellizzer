Lobelia jamaicensis

Lobelia jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a rough texture.
