Lachnopylis polyantha

Lachnopylis polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Lachnopylis polyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
