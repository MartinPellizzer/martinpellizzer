Lasiorrhiza stricta

Lasiorrhiza stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces showy yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom in spring and summer.

Lasiorrhiza stricta is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-drained soil.
