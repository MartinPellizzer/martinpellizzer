Lilaeopsis schaffneriana var. subterrestris

Lilaeopsis schaffneriana var. subterrestris, commonly known as the subterranean grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its low-growing, rhizomatous structure and small, grass-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its unique growth habit and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to wetland ecosystems.
