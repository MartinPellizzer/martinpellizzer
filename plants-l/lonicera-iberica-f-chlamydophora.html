Lonicera iberica f. chlamydophora

Lonicera iberica f. chlamydophora, commonly known as the chlamydophora honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, deciduous leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
