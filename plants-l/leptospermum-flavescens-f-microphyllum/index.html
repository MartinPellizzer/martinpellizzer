Leptospermum Flavescens F. Microphyllum

Leptospermum flavescens f. microphyllum is a unique variant of the white tea tree known for its compact growth and small, pale yellow flowers. This form thrives in coastal regions and is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness. It is a member of the myrtle family and is native to southeastern Australia. The plant's distinctive foliage and ability to withstand harsh conditions make it a popular choice for landscaping. Its cultivation requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish.
