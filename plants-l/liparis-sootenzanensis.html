Liparis sootenzanensis

Liparis sootenzanensis, commonly known as the Sootenzenan Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the spring season. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread gradually across the forest floor.

The botanical features of Liparis sootenzanensis make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
