Ligularia evaginata

Ligularia evaginata, commonly known as the heartleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a dark green upper surface. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are borne on tall, slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
