Lavatera maritima var. trilobata

Lavatera maritima var. trilobata, commonly known as the coastal lavatera, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that are typically trifoliate, giving it its scientific name "trilobata.". The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers in clusters, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal conditions.
