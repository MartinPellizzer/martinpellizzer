Lotus corbierei

Lotus corbierei, commonly known as the Corsican lotus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features palmate leaves with a waxy surface, and its flowers are pink to purple in color, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds.

Lotus corbierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
