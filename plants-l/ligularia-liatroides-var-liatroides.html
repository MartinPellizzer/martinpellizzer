Ligularia liatroides var. liatroides

Ligularia liatroides var. liatroides, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to the wetlands of eastern North America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a deep green color and a distinct arrowhead shape. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swamps and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for wetland gardens and pond margins.
