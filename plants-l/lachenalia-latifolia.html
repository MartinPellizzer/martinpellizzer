Lachenalia latifolia

Lachenalia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of bell-shaped, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It blooms during the summer months and is often grown as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance.

Lachenalia latifolia requires well-draining soil and bright sunlight to thrive.
