Lastrea amplissima

Lastrea amplissima, commonly known as the giant rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing on cliffs or slopes. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
