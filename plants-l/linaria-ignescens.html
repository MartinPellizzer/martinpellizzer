Linaria ignescens

Linaria ignescens, commonly known as the fireweed or flame weed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Linaria ignescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
