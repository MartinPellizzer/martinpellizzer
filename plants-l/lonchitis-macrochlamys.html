Lonchitis macrochlamys

Lonchitis macrochlamys, commonly known as the large-chalmed water plant, is a member of the Hydrocharitaceae family.

It is a submerged aquatic plant characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length.
