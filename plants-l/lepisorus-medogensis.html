Lepisorus medogensis

Lepisorus medogensis, commonly known as the Yunnan water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan Province, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Lepisorus medogensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in moist, shaded gardens.
