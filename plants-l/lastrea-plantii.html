Lastrea plantii

Lastrea plantii, commonly known as the desert spurge, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Lastrea plantii is a hardy, low-maintenance plant popular among desert gardeners.
