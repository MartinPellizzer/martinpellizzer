Lantana notha

Lantana notha, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The leaves are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
