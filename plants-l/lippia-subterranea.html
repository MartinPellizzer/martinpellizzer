Lippia subterranea

Lippia subterranea, commonly known as the underground lippia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands. Its roots are fibrous and extensive, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Lippia subterranea is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
