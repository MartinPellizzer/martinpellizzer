Laserpitium cuneitolium

Laserpitium cuneitolium, commonly known as the narrowleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its common name.

Laserpitium cuneitolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
