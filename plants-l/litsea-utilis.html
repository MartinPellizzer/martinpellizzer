Litsea utilis

Litsea utilis, commonly known as the Indian bay leaf or wild bay tree, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like laurel and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to bay leaves.
