Lobelia endlichii

Lobelia endlichii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is characterized by its small, tubular blue or purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as meadows and forested areas. It is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, temperate climates.

Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
