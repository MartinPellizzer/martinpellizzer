Leucadendron dubium

Leucadendron dubium, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly leaves and bright yellow flowers that appear in winter. The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
