Limonium cylindrifolium

Limonium cylindrifolium, commonly known as the cylindrical sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its narrow, cylindrical leaves and spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It is a member of the Plumbagaceae family and is known for its salt tolerance.

Limonium cylindrifolium is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
