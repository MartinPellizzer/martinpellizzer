Limonium oleifolium subsp. algusae

Limonium oleifolium subsp.

algusae, commonly known as the Mediterranean sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.

The plant produces numerous slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and saline environments. Limonium oleifolium subsp.

algusae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
