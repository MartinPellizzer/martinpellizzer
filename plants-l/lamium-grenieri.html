Lamium grenieri

Lamium grenieri, commonly known as the green gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Lamium grenieri is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
