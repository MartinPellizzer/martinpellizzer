Lastrea setifera

Lastrea setifera, commonly known as the threadleaf iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its stems allows it to survive in arid environments.
