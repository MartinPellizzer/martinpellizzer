Limonium virgatum

Limonium virgatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species adapted to salty and windy environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to coastal landscapes.

Limonium virgatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
