Libidibia

Libidibia, commonly known as the "silk tree" or "silk cotton tree," is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant is characterized by its showy, fluffy seed pods that resemble cotton, which are released when mature. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, consisting of multiple small leaflets, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.
