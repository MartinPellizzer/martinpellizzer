Lobelia batsonii

Lobelia batsonii, commonly known as the batson's lobelia, is a rare flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.
