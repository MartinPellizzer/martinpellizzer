Lasianthus repoeuensis

Lasianthus repoeuensis, commonly known as the Malayan flame vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red-orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
