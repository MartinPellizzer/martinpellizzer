Lindera vernayana

Lindera vernayana, commonly known as the Chinese sweet woodruff, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often aromatic and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
