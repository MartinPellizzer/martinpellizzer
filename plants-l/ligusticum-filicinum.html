Ligusticum filicinum

Ligusticum filicinum, commonly known as water dropwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to moist environments in Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate inflorescences. The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine, though its use is limited due to potential toxicity.
