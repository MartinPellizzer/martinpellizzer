Leocereus bahiensis var. barreirensis

Leocereus bahiensis var. barreirensis, commonly known as the Barreirensis Cereus, is a cacti species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus with a tall, spindly stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
