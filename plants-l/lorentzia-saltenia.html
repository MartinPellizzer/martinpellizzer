Lorentzia saltenia

Lorentzia saltenia, commonly known as the saltbush, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant thrives in saline and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are typically small and succulent, helping to conserve water.

Lorentzia saltenia plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and supporting local biodiversity.
