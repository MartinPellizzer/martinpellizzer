Lithocarpus Erythrocarpus

Lithocarpus erythrocarpus is a striking evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its thick, leathery leaves and distinctive red-brown fruits. This species thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dense forests. Its wood is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant plays an important role in its ecosystem and is also of interest to botanists and conservationists.
