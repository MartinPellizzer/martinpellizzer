Lasiandra foveolata

Lasiandra foveolata, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

Lasiandra foveolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual impact.
