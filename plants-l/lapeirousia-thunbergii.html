Lapeirousia thunbergii

Lapeirousia thunbergii, commonly known as the Cape bell orchid, is a striking South African orchid species characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Western and Northern Cape provinces. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its unique floral structure, with a prominent lip and intricate petal arrangement, makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Lapeirousia thunbergii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
