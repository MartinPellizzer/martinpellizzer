Lilium rockii

Lilium rockii, commonly known as the Yunnan lily, is a species of bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a tall stem that supports its showy blooms.

Lilium rockii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegance.
