Lastrea intermedia var. rhodolepis

Lastrea intermedia var. rhodolepis, commonly known as the red-stemmed lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive red or purple stems that contrast with its green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
