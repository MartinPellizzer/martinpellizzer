Licuala dransfieldii

Licuala dransfieldii, commonly known as the giant palm or Dransfield's palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has a robust trunk that can grow several meters tall, often with a smooth, grayish bark. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
