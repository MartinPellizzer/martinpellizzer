Lactuca klossii

Lactuca klossii, commonly known as Kloss's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its rosette of silver-green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive foliage.

Lactuca klossii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
