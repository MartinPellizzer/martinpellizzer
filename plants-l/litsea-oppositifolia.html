Litsea oppositifolia

Litsea oppositifolia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or broadleaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China, Vietnam, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries.
