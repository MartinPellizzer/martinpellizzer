Laburnum heuffelii

Laburnum heuffelii, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and grayish-brown. Laburnum heuffelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
