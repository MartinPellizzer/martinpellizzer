Lantana reticulata

Lantana reticulata, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, often aromatic foliage and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant is known for its adaptability, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically orange or red in color.

Lantana reticulata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant appearance and hardy nature.
