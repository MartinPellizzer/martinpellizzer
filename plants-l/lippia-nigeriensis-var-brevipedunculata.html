Lippia nigeriensis var. brevipedunculata

Lippia nigeriensis var. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-pedunculate lippia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly Nigeria.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
