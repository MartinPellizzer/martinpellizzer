Lotus × minoricensis

Lotus × minoricensis, commonly known as the Minorca lotus, is a hybrid species native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, sandy soils near coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
