Libanothamnus neriifolius

Libanothamnus neriifolius, commonly known as the blackleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in desert or semi-arid regions. Its foliage is notable for its dense, velvety texture, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
