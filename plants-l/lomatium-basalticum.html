Lomatium basalticum

Lomatium basalticum, commonly known as basalt lomatium, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

Lomatium basalticum is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical use by indigenous peoples for its purported health benefits.
