Lightfootia tenella

Lightfootia tenella, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lightfootia tenella is known for its milky sap, which is characteristic of the Asclepiadaceae family.
