Lippia pubescens

Lippia pubescens, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has a sprawling or upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
