Lobivia acanthoplegma var. roseiflora

Lobivia acanthoplegma var. roseiflora, commonly known as the Rose-flowered Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compact, rounded stems covered in dense, woolly white to greyish hairs. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native desert environment.

It is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
