Lathyrus sativus var. stenophyllus

Lathyrus sativus var. stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, zigzagging stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, oblong pods containing flattened, kidney-shaped seeds. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable cover crop.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.
