Leptochloöpsis condensata

Leptochloöpsis condensata, commonly known as the dense spikegrass, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically forms low, tufted clumps and is often found in moist, temperate environments. Its inflorescence consists of tightly packed spikelets, which are small and clustered.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
