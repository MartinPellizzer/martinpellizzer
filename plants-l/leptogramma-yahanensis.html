Leptogramma yahanensis

Leptogramma yahanensis, commonly known as the Yahan's leptogramma, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky stream banks. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters at the base of the plant.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for small insects.
