Lycopersicon

Lycopersicon, commonly known as the tomato plant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes potatoes and peppers. The plant produces vibrant red berries that are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually lobed, while its stems are hairy and erect.

Lycopersicon requires full sun and well-drained soil to thrive, making it a popular choice for home gardens.
