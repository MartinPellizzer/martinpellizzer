Lonicera phyllocarpa

Lonicera phyllocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange-red, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in the fall and is edible for both humans and wildlife.

Lonicera phyllocarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
