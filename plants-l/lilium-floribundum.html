Lilium floribundum

Lilium floribundum, commonly known as the Chinese trumpet flower, is a species of lily native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a rhizomatous root system.

The flowers bloom in late summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and ornamental gardening.
