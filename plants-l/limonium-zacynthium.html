Limonium zacynthium

Limonium zacynthium, commonly known as the Zante carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color.

Limonium zacynthium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom in full sun.
