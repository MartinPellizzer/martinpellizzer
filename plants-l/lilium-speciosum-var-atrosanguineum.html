Lilium speciosum var. atrosanguineum

Lilium speciosum var. atrosanguineum, commonly known as the dark red lily, is a striking variant of the showy lily species.

It is characterized by its deep, almost black-red petals that contrast with its white or pale yellow inner segments. This perennial plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces large, showy flowers in late spring to early summer.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
