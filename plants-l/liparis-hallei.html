Liparis hallei

Liparis hallei, commonly known as the Halle's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Liparis hallei is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
