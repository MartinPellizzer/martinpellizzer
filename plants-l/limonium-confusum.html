Limonium confusum

Limonium confusum, commonly known as the confused sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Plumbagaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flower clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, sandy soils, and saline environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
