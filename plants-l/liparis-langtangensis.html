Liparis langtangensis

Liparis langtangensis, commonly known as the Langtang orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the Langtang National Park in Nepal.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in high-altitude forested areas, where it thrives in moist, shaded conditions. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky soil, showcasing its resilience in harsh environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
