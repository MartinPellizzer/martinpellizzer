Lichtensteinia pyrethrifolia

Lichtensteinia pyrethrifolia, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that form composite heads.
