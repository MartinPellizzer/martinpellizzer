Leucadendron nervosum

Leucadendron nervosum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flower heads, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Leucadendron nervosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
