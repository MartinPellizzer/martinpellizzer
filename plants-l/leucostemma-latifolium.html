Leucostemma latifolium

Leucostemma latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf leucostemma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and meadows. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, white to pale blue flowers in late spring. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
