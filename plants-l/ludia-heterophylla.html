Ludia heterophylla

Ludia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which can range from oval to lance-shaped, depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, often with a slight pink or purple hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ludia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
