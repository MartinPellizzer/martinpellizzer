Lasiocephalus senecioides

Lasiocephalus senecioides, commonly known as the roughleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
