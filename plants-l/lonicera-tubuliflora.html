Lonicera tubuliflora

Lonicera tubuliflora, commonly known as the tubular honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. These flowers are primarily yellow or orange and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
