Lasianthus cailinianus

Lasianthus cailinianus, commonly known as the Cailinian flameflower, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
