Linaria kneuckeri

Linaria kneuckeri, commonly known as the Kneucker's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of yellow-orange flowers. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
