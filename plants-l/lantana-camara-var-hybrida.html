Lantana camara var. hybrida

Lantana camara var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid lantana, is a flowering shrub native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It features clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, red, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are lance-shaped and aromatic. It is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant displays and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
