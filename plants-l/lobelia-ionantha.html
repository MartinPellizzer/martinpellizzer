Lobelia ionantha

Lobelia ionantha, commonly known as the dwarf bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has delicate, hairy leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments, such as woodland gardens.

Lobelia ionantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
