Lindsaea lancea f. marginalis

Lindsaea lancea f. marginalis, commonly known as the marginal lindsaea, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The fronds are usually light green and have a distinctive marginal vein system. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
