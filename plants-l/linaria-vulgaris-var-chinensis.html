Linaria vulgaris var. chinensis

Linaria vulgaris var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese toadflax, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, branching stems that produce clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
