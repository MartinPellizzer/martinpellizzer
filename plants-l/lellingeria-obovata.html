Lellingeria obovata

Lellingeria obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lanceolate to obovate leaves that are often hairy and arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate.
