Lobelia domingensis

Lobelia domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Caribbean island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia domingensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
