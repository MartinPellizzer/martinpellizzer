Lycopersicon solanum

Lycopersicon solanum, commonly known as the nightshade plant, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with five petals, and it produces small, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

The plant is native to the Americas and is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
