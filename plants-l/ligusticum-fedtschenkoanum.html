Ligusticum fedtschenkoanum

Ligusticum fedtschenkoanum, commonly known as the Siberian lovage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-like flower clusters. The species is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
