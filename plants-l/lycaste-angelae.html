Lycaste angelae

Lycaste angelae, commonly known as the Angel's Lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the genus Lycaste, which includes several epiphytic and terrestrial orchid species. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically fragrant and come in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive coloration pattern.

Lycaste angelae thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
