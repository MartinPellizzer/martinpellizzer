Litsea laevigata

Litsea laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-lauredane or Chinese laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, elliptical leaves that are smooth to the touch. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, spherical drupe that turns from green to yellow or red as it matures.
