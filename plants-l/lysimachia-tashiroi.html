Lysimachia tashiroi

Lysimachia tashiroi, commonly known as Tashiro's chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats on moist, shaded ground. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
