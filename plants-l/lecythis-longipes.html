Lecythis longipes

Lecythis longipes, commonly known as the giant lecythis, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and can grow up to 40 meters tall. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a single large seed enclosed in a fleshy, yellowish pulp. Its leaves are large, glossy, and often used in traditional medicine.

The bark and seeds of Lecythis longipes have been utilized by indigenous communities for their potential medicinal properties.
