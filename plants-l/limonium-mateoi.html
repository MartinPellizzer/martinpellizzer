Limonium mateoi

Limonium mateoi, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and its clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium mateoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
