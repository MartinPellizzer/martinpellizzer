Lathyrus lanszwertii var. arizonicus

Lathyrus lanszwertii var. arizonicus, commonly known as the Arizona larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and showy, spurred flowers. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, with a distinctive elongated spur at the base of each petal.
