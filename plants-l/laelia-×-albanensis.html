Laelia × albanensis

Laelia × albanensis, commonly known as the Albanian Laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Albania.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for intermediate orchid growers.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations, contributing to its appeal in horticulture.
