Labordia mauiensis

Labordia mauiensis, commonly known as Maui's ghost.

It is a rare flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Maui. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in the understory of native forests.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, Labordia mauiensis is considered critically endangered.
