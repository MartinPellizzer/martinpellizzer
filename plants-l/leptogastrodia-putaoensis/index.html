Leptogastrodia Putaoensis

Leptogastrodia putaoensis is a rare and enigmatic plant species discovered in the mountainous regions of Myanmar. It belongs to the orchid family and is known for its unique floral structure and elusive nature. This plant thrives in high-altitude, humid environments with limited sunlight. Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered critically endangered. Conservation efforts are urgently needed to protect this fascinating botanical treasure.
