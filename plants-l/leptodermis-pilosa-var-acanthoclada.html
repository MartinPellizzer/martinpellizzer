Leptodermis pilosa var. acanthoclada

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are leathery and have a rough texture, with prominent veins.
