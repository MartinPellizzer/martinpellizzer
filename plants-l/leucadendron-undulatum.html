Leucadendron undulatum

Leucadendron undulatum, commonly known as the Silver Tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, feathery foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, adding visual interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
