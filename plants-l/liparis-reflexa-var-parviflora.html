Liparis reflexa var. parviflora

Liparis reflexa var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered reflexed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive reflexed lip. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
