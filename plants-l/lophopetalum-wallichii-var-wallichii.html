Lophopetalum wallichii var. wallichii

Lophopetalum wallichii var. wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan hibiscus, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
