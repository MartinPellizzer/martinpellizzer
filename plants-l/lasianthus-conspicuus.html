Lasianthus conspicuus

Lasianthus conspicuus, commonly known as the showy lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Lasianthus conspicuus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
