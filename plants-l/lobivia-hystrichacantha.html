Lobivia hystrichacantha

Lobivia hystrichacantha, commonly known as the spiny lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of spiny, paddle-shaped leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its adaptations, such as reduced leaf surface and deep root systems, enable it to thrive in harsh, dry environments.

Lobivia hystrichacantha is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts for its unique morphology and resilience.
