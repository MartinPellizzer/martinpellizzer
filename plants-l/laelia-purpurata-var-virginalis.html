Laelia purpurata var. virginalis

Laelia purpurata var. virginalis, commonly known as the Virginal Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from deep red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming a compact rosette with fleshy, dark green leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
