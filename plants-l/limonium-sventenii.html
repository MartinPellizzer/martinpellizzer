Limonium sventenii

Limonium sventenii, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often growing in sandy or rocky habitats. Limonium sventenii is a perennial species that blooms from summer to autumn.

Its flowers are popular in gardens for their long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
