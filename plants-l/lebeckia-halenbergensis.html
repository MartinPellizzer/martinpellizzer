Lebeckia halenbergensis

Lebeckia halenbergensis, commonly known as the coastal dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Lebeckia halenbergensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh coastal environments.
