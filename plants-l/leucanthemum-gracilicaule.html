Leucanthemum gracilicaule

Leucanthemum gracilicaule, commonly known as slenderleaf oxeye, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy white petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
