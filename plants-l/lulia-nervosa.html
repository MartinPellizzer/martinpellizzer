Lulia nervosa

Lulia nervosa, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its intricate, flowering spike that produces small, fragrant orchid flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.

Lulia nervosa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
