Lycium Chilense F. Glaberrimum

Lycium chilense f. glaberrimum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the arid regions of Chile. This botanical variant belongs to the Solanaceae family, known for its diverse and often economically important members. Its distinguishing feature is its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which set it apart from other Lycium species. Despite its limited distribution, it holds ecological significance in its native habitat. Further study is needed to fully understand its genetic makeup and conservation status.
