Lotus arinagensis

Lotus arinagensis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stems above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically floating or partially submerged. Its roots system helps stabilize the soil and improve water quality.

Lotus arinagensis is often associated with spiritual and cultural significance in various traditions.
