Leucas anandaraoana

Leucas anandaraoana, commonly known as the Anandaraoan leucas, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It is often found growing in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
