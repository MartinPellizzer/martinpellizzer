Lithospermum arvense var. coerulescens

Lithospermum arvense var. coerulescens, commonly known as blue false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the northern United States and Canada.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides and meadows. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically pink to blue in color, arranged in dense spikes.

The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by animals.
