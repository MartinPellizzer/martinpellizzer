Lasianthus purpureus

Lasianthus purpureus, commonly known as the purple-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the inflorescence consists of spikes bearing small, tubular flowers.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
