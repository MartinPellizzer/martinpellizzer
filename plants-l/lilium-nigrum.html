Lilium nigrum

Lilium nigrum, commonly known as black lily, is a species of flowering plant in the lily family.

It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, typically growing in moist, shaded environments. The plant features dark purple to black flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking visual contrast to its foliage. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue.

Lilium nigrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
