Lonicera etrusca var. reverchonii

Lonicera etrusca var. reverchonii, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Italy.

It features slender, arching stems that often climb or trail along the ground. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, dark green surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
