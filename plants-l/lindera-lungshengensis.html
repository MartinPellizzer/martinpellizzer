Lindera lungshengensis

Lindera lungshengensis, commonly known as the Guangxi Lindera, is a shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, aromatic berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties.
