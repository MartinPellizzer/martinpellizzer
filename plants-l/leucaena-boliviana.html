Leucaena boliviana

Leucaena boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian leucaena, is a shrub or small tree native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

It also has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility.
