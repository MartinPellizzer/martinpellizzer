Leucopsis podocomoides

Leucopsis podocomoides, commonly known as the silver-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers bloom in spring and are pollinated primarily by insects.
