Lepisanthes amoena

Lepisanthes amoena, commonly known as the pretty orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms.

Lepisanthes amoena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
