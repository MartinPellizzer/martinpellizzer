Lysimachia lichiangensis var. lichiangensis

Lysimachia lichiangensis var. lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan knotweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from June to August. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
