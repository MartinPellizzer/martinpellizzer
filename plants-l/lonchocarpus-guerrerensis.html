Lonchocarpus guerrerensis

Lonchocarpus guerrerensis, commonly known as Guerrerense arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It is valued for its edible tubers, which are similar in texture to traditional arrowroot.

However, it contains toxic compounds and requires careful preparation before consumption.
