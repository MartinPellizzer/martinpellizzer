Ligustrum sinense var. sinense

Ligustrum sinense var. sinense, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in some regions outside its native habitat.
