Lepidium brachyotum

Lepidium brachyotum, commonly known as the short-stemmed cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and has small, lobed leaves. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round seeds.

Lepidium brachyotum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
