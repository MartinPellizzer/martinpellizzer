Lactuca flavida

Lactuca flavida, commonly known as the purple leaf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its dark green to purple leaves that are often ruffled or curly. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca flavida is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
