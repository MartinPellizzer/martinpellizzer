Lilium × aurelianense

Lilium × aurelianense, commonly known as the Aurelian lily, is a hybrid species within the genus Lilium.

It is characterized by its striking orange and yellow flowers, which are often adorned with red streaks. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is known for its tall, erect stems. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

This species is cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
