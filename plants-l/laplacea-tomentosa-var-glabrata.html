Laplacea tomentosa var. glabrata

Laplacea tomentosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-lapped lichen, is a rare and specialized plant that thrives in specific ecological niches.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly growth pattern, which is reduced in this variant to a smoother texture. The plant exhibits a unique symbiotic relationship with algae, contributing to its survival in nutrient-poor environments.
