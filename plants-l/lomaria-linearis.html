Lomaria linearis

Lomaria linearis, commonly known as the slender lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty air and coastal winds.

Lomaria linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
