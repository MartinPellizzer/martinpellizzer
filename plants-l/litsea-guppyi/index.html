Litsea Guppyi

Litsea guppyi is a rare and lesser-known species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes well-known trees like cinnamon and avocado. This plant is characterized by its fragrant leaves and small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
