Lastrea paleata

Lastrea paleata, commonly known as the pale leaf lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, pale green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.
