Lemaireocereus humilis

Lemaireocereus humilis, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a small, flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments, thriving in well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

Lemaireocereus humilis is typically propagated through seeds or cuttings, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
