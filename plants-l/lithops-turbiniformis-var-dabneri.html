Lithops turbiniformis var. dabneri

Lithops turbiniformis var. dabneri, commonly known as the Dabner's African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, resembling a rock to blend into its natural environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
