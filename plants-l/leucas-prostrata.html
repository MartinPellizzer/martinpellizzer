Leucas prostrata

Leucas prostrata, commonly known as prostrate leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant that typically spreads along the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Leucas prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
