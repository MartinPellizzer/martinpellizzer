Licuala merguensis

Licuala merguensis, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in warmer climates.
