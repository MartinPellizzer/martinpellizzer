Ludwigia Angustifolia Var. Ramosissima

Red ludwigia, scientifically known as Ludwigia angustifolia var. ramosissima, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America. It is prized for its vibrant red leaves and dense, branching growth pattern that creates a lush underwater landscape. Often used in planted tanks and natural water gardens, it adds both color and texture to aquatic environments. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice among hobbyists and aquatic gardeners. However, its aggressive nature requires careful management to prevent overgrowth.
