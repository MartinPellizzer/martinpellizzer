Leontodon kunthianus

Leontodon kunthianus, commonly known as the Kunth's toothache plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow and resemble daisies, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.
