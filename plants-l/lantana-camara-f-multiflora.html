Lantana camara f. multiflora

Lantana camara f. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora lantana, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a slightly hairy texture. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
