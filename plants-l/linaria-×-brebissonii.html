Linaria × brebissonii

Linaria × brebissonii, commonly known as the hybrid toadflax, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Linaria × brebissonii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
