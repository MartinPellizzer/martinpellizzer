Lonicera × heckrottii

Lonicera × heckrottii, commonly known as the Huckleberry honeysuckle, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and adaptability contribute to its presence in various ecological settings.
