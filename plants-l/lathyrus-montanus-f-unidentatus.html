Lathyrus montanus f. unidentatus

Lathyrus montanus f. unidentatus, commonly known as the slender field vetch, is a botanical variant of the common field vetch.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe. The plant features slender, climbing stems with pinnate leaves composed of small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically pale pink to white, arranged in racemes, and bloom in early summer.

This form is distinguished by its reduced leaflet size and more delicate appearance compared to the typical variety.
