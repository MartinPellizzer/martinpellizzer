Lantana hatoensis

Lantana hatoensis, commonly known as the Hato lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, specifically the island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and has opposite, simple, ovate to lanceolate leaves.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.
