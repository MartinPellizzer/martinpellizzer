Lotus symmetricus

Lotus symmetricus, commonly known as the symmetrical lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, supported by long, slender stems. Its flowers are symmetrically arranged, giving it a balanced and elegant appearance.

Lotus symmetricus is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
