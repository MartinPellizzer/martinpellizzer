Linochilus heterophyllus

Linochilus heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable lipstick orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique and variable leaf shapes, which can range from linear to ovate, depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, often found in forested areas with high humidity. Its flowers are small and usually greenish or yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red streaks.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
