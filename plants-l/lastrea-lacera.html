Lastrea lacera

Lastrea lacera, commonly known as the hairy lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, branching stems covered in fine, hair-like structures that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are often reduced to small, scale-like structures, typical of many succulents.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
