Lonicera litangensis

Lonicera litangensis, commonly known as the Litang honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, typically blooming in late spring. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated edge.

Lonicera litangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
