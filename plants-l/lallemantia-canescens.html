Lallemantia canescens

Lallemantia canescens, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has hairy, silver-green leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open fields and along stream banks.

Lallemantia canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
