Linaria Linogrisea F. Longeracemosa

Linaria with long racemes, scientifically known as Linaria linogrisea f. longeracemosa, is a strikingly elegant flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. This unique variety is characterized by its extended, branching inflorescences that bear clusters of vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its delicate yet bold appearance has captured the attention of botanists and gardeners alike. With its distinctive form and cheerful blooms, it stands out as a rare and beautiful addition to the Linaria genus.
