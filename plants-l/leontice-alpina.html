Leontice alpina

Leontice alpina, commonly known as alpine leontice, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit, often forming dense mats on rocky or alpine soils. Leontice alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic alpine environments.
