Livistona speciosa

Livistona speciosa, commonly known as the ornamental palm, is a species native to northern Australia and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a striking, tropical appearance. The plant typically grows in wet, swampy areas and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are often used in horticulture for their aesthetic value, and the plant is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
