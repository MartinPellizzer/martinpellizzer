Limoniastrum feei var. grandiflorum

Limoniastrum feei var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The flowers bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to the garden while attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
