Liparis tixieri

Liparis tixieri, commonly known as the Tixieri's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Liparis tixieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
