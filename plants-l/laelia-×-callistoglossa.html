Laelia × callistoglossa

Laelia × callistoglossa, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Callistaglossa species.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to South America. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to high light conditions.

Laelia × callistoglossa is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
