Lepanthes fractiflexa

Lepanthes fractiflexa, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often emit a sweet, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Lepanthes fractiflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
