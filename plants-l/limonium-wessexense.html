Limonium wessexense

Limonium wessexense, commonly known as the Wessex ladies' tresses, is a flowering plant native to the UK.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, pale blue to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in coastal and inland habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.
