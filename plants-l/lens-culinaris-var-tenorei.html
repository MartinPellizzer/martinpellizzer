Lens culinaris var. tenorei

Lens culinaris var. tenorei, commonly known as the Sicilian lentil, is a variety of the common lentil native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds that are typically brown or yellow in color. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in temperate climates and is well-suited for cultivation in well-drained soils. It produces clusters of small white or purple flowers, which give way to the edible seed pods.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to diverse growing conditions.
