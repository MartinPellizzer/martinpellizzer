Litosanthes Capitulata

Litosanthes capitulata is a rare and intriguing plant species known for its unique adaptations to rocky environments. It thrives in arid and coastal regions where few other plants can survive. This hardy species has developed specialized root systems to anchor itself in unstable substrates. Its small, clustered flowers are often overlooked but play a vital role in its reproductive cycle. Studying Litosanthes capitulata offers valuable insights into plant resilience and ecological diversity.
