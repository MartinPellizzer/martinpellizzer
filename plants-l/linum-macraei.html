Linum macraei

Linum macraei, commonly known as the Macrae flax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax known for their showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are characterized by their slender, elongated shape and are often arranged in loose clusters.

Linum macraei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
