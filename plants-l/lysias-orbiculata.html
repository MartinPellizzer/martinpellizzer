Lysias orbiculata

Lysias orbiculata, commonly known as the broad-leaved lily, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with six to eight petals. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Lysias orbiculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
