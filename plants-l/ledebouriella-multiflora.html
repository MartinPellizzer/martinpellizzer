Ledebouriella multiflora

Ledebouriella multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered ledebouriella, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
