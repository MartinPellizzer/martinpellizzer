Lastrea abbreviata

Lastrea abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
