Laelia × crawshayana

Laelia × crawshayana, commonly known as the Crawshay's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with dark purple or brown markings, and it blooms during the winter months.

Laelia × crawshayana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
