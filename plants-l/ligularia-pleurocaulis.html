Ligularia pleurocaulis

Ligularia pleurocaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for water gardens and wetland restoration projects.
