Lepismium brevispinum

Lepismium brevispinum, commonly known as the short-spined nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are usually ovate and have a rough, hairy texture.

Lepismium brevispinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
