Leandra riograndensis

Leandra riograndensis, commonly known as the Rio Grande orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces clusters of white or pale pink blooms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
