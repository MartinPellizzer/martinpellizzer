Licuala sandsiana

Licuala sandsiana, commonly known as the sand's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter. It thrives in wet, tropical environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
