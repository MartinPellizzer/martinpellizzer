Ludisia argyroneura

Ludisia argyroneura, commonly known as the silver-veined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces elegant, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Ludisia argyroneura is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
