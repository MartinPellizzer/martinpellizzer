Limosella aquatica var. tenuifolia

Limosella aquatica var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender waterweed, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that are typically less than 1 mm in width. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in shallow water or damp soil, often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of streams.

Its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed wetlands make it an important species in aquatic ecosystems.
