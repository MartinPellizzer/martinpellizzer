Lindsaea multisora

Lindsaea multisora, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a tropical fern species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are wiry and creeping, allowing it to spread along the forest floor.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, feathery appearance.
