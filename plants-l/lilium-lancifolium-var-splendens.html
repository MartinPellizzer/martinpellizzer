Lilium lancifolium var. splendens

Lilium lancifolium var. splendens, commonly known as the tiger lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers with dark markings, which resemble tiger stripes, hence its common name. The plant features long, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It produces bulbous roots and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

This variety is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
