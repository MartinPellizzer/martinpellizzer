Lindera funiushanensis

Lindera funiushanensis, commonly known as the Funiushan ligustrum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are small drupes that turn red when mature, attracting various bird species.

Lindera funiushanensis is often found in shaded, moist forest understories, where it thrives in partially shaded conditions.
