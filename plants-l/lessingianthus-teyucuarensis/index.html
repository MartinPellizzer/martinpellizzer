Lessingianthus Teyucuarensis

Lessingianthus teyucuarensis is a rare and lesser-known flowering plant native to the highlands of Ecuador. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological significance. This species is characterized by its delicate purple petals and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
