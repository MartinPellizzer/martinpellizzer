Lonicera taronensis

Lonicera taronensis, commonly known as the Taronese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Lonicera taronensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators.
