Lessingianthus cristalinae

Lessingianthus cristalinae, commonly known as the crystalline lessingia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features delicate, hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite inflorescence.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
