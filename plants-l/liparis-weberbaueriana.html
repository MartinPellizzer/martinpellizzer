Liparis weberbaueriana

Liparis weberbaueriana, commonly known as the Weberbauer's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, branched rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a loose raceme, which are typically borne on erect stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
