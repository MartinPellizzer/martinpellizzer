Lomanthus lomincola

Lomanthus lomincola, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets that help in photosynthesis in its native habitat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Lomanthus lomincola is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
