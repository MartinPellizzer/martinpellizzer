Leucas deflexa var. deflexa

Leucas deflexa var. deflexa, commonly known as the slender leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along riverbanks. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, linear to lance-shaped leaves. Its small, white to pale blue flowers are clustered in terminal spikes, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
