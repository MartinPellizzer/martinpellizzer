Lecanopteris luzonensis

Lecanopteris luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon ebony fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable in its natural habitat.
