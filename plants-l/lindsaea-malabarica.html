Lindsaea malabarica

Lindsaea malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar maidenhair fern, is a species of epiphytic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Asia, particularly India, Sri Lanka, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.

Lindsaea malabarica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
