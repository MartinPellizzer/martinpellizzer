Leucadendron pallens

Leucadendron pallens, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a striking South African shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its narrow, lance-shaped leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Leucadendron pallens is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and seasonal floral display.
