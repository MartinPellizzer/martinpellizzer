Lantana mixta

Lantana mixta, commonly known as the common lantana, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is native to the Americas and is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana mixta is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
