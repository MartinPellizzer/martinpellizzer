Lippia morongii

Lippia morongii, commonly known as Morong's verbena, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
