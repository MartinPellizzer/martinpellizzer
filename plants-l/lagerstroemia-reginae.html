Lagerstroemia reginae

Lagerstroemia reginae, commonly known as the Queen's flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are opposite in arrangement and turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable in its native habitats.
