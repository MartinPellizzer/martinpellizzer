Liparis nana

Liparis nana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or forested areas and prefers cool, moist conditions. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are grass-like and form a rosette at the base.

Liparis nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
