Lamprotis squamosa

Lamprotis squamosa, commonly known as the scarlet spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a succulent, branching stem covered in small, scale-like structures. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Lamprotis squamosa is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
