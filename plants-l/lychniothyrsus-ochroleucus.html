Lychniothyrsus ochroleucus

Lychniothyrsus ochroleucus, commonly known as the yellow tawny sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, cylindrical leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes and wetlands.
