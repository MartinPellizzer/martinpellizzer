Lilium cattaniae

Lilium cattaniae, commonly known as the Cattania lily, is a rare and striking species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red, with a distinctive recurved petal shape. The plant belongs to the Liliaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
