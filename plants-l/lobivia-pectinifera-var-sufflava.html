Lobivia pectinifera var. sufflava

Lobivia pectinifera var. sufflava, commonly known as the Orange-Red Lobivia, is a striking succulent native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are typically green to reddish in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant orange to red flowers during the warmer months. It has a compact growth habit and is prized for its unique coloration and resilience to arid conditions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
