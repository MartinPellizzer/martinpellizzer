Lupinus Nanus F. Lilacinus

Lupinus nanus f. lilacinus is a unique and delicate plant known for its striking lavender-blue flowers. It is a compact variety of the lupine genus, often found in rocky or gravelly soils. This plant thrives in sunny environments and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. Its small stature makes it ideal for borders or as a filler in floral arrangements. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its toxic properties.
