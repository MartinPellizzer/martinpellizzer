Lachenalia pusilla

Lachenalia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf lachenalia, is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves. Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
