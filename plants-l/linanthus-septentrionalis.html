Linanthus septentrionalis

Linanthus septentrionalis, commonly known as the northern flax.

It is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly in California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Linanthus septentrionalis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils.
