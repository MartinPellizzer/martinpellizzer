Loroglossum bolleanum

Loroglossum bolleanum, commonly known as the Bolleanum orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and can vary in shape and color depending on the species.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
