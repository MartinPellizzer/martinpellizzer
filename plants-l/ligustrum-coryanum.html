Ligustrum coryanum

Ligustrum coryanum, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a flowering shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. Its berries are small, dark blue to black, and contain several seeds.

Ligustrum coryanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its adaptability and rapid growth.
