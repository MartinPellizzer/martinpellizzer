Lasiocorys degasparisiana

Lasiocorys degasparisiana, commonly known as the Degasparisian rock orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure, with flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
