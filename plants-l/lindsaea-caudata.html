Lindsaea caudata

Lindsaea caudata, commonly known as the long-tailed fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that can extend up to several meters in length, giving it a distinctive appearance. Lindsaea caudata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
