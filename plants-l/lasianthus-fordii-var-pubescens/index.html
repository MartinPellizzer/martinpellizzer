Lasianthus Fordii Var. Pubescens

Ford's lasianthus, scientifically known as Lasianthus fordii var. pubescens, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. This unique species belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias, and is characterized by its velvety foliage and delicate blooms. Found primarily in the Sichuan province, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Ford's lasianthus is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from deforestation and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
