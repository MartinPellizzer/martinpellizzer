Leschenaultia magnifica

Leschenaultia magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent leschenaultia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.
