Lepidium stylatum

Lepidium stylatum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions.
