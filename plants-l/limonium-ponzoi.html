Limonium ponzoi

Limonium ponzoi, commonly known as the Ponzian sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of salt.

Limonium ponzoi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand saline environments.
