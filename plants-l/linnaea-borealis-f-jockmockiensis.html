Linnaea borealis f. jockmockiensis

Linnaea borealis f. jockmockiensis, commonly known as the Jockmockien bog rose.

This rare variant of the twinflower is characterized by its small, delicate white flowers and trailing stems that often grow along the forest floor. It is native to specific, moist, acidic habitats in the boreal regions of North America.
