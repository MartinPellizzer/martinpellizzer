Leipoldtia pauciflora

Leipoldtia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered leipoldtia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in spring. It has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
