Liparis bicornis

Liparis bicornis, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-lobed lip, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Liparis bicornis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
