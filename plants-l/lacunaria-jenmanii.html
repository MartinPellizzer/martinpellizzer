Lacunaria jenmanii

Lacunaria jenmanii, commonly known as the lace fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that create a lacy appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread gradually in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with finely divided segments that give it a unique texture.

Lacunaria jenmanii thrives in humid conditions and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
