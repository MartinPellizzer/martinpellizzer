Lobelia zeylanica f. rubescens

Lobelia zeylanica f. rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered lobelia, is a botanical variant characterized by its striking red blossoms.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
