Leea congesta

Leea congesta, commonly known as the clustered leea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Leea congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
