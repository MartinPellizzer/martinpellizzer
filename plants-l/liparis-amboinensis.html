Liparis amboinensis

Liparis amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
