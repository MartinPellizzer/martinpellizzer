Lepanthes magnipetala

Lepanthes magnipetala, commonly known as the large-petaled orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes magnipetala thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.
