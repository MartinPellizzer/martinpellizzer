Lauro-cerasus dolichophylla

Lauro-cerasus dolichophylla, commonly known as the Chinese cherry laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Prunus genus and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
