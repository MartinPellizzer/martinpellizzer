Litsea elongata var. latifolia

Litsea elongata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, and often elliptical or ovate leaves that are usually broad and glossy. This species is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.
