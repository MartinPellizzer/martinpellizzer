Lonchocarpus retifer

Lonchocarpus retifer, commonly known as the pink root or *Lonchocarpus retifer*, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red tubular roots, which are often harvested for their ornamental value. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
