Laelia × horniana

Laelia × horniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceps.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andes Mountains in South America. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laelia × horniana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
