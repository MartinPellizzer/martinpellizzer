Lobivia tiegeliana f. winterae

Lobivia tiegeliana f. winterae, commonly known as the Winter's lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and early summer. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
