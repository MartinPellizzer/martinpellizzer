Lactuca chondrilliflora

Lactuca chondrilliflora, commonly known as the chondrilliflora lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and is adapted to dry, rocky soils. It has a taproot system and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
