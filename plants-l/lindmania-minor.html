Lindmania minor

Lindmania minor, commonly known as the white-flowered arrowroot, is a member of the Cannaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, broad leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Lindmania minor is also valued for its starchy rhizomes, which are used in culinary applications.
