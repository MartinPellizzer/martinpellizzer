Lonicera orientalis var. kansuensis

Lonicera orientalis var. kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It is well adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
