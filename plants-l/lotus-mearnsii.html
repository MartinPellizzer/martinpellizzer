Lotus mearnsii

Lotus mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions. Its root system is fibrous and contributes to soil stabilization.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
