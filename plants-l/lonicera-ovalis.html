Lonicera ovalis

Lonicera ovalis, commonly known as the oval honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly pubescent. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera ovalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
