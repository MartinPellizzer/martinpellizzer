Lepidium aretioides

Lepidium aretioides, commonly known as the Arete's cress, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and hairy, branching stems. It produces small, rounded fruits called siliques, which contain several seeds.

Lepidium aretioides is often found in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
