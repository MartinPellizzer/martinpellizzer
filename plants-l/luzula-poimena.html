Luzula poimena

Luzula poimena, commonly known as the green moor grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, acidic soils, often found in heathlands and bogs. Luzula poimena is characterized by its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments, making it a common component of wetland ecosystems.
