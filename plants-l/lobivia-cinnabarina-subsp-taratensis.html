Lobivia cinnabarina subsp. taratensis

Lobivia cinnabarina subsp.

taratensis, commonly known as the Taratensis Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit.

The plant features sparsely distributed, ribbed stems that are often tinged with a reddish or pinkish hue. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the apex of the stems.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh environments, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
