Lepanthes auditor

Lepanthes auditor, commonly known as the "Auditor Orchid," is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid, typically growing up to 15 centimeters in height, with a slender stem and a single, showy flower. The plant features a distinctive greenish-white or pale yellow flower with intricate, spurred petals and a prominent lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in montane forests.

Lepanthes auditor is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and relatively easy cultivation under proper conditions.
