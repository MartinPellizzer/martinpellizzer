Lightfootia capillaris

Lightfootia capillaris, commonly known as the slender lightfoot or slender lightfoot fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically arching or drooping in appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

Lightfootia capillaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
