Lachenalia algoensis

Lachenalia algoensis, commonly known as the Algoen's lachenalia, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, showy flowers during the summer months. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
