Lantana pulchra

Lantana pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana pulchra is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
