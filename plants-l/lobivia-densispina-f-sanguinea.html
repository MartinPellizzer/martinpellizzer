Lobivia densispina f. sanguinea

Lobivia densispina f. sanguinea, commonly known as the Crimson Spine Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and compact growth habit, which help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. The leaves are typically short and fleshy, adapted to store water, while the flowers are usually bright red or orange, attracting pollinators. This variety is prized for its striking appearance and hardiness, making it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water, thriving in full sun conditions.
