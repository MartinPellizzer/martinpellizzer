Libanotis libanotis

Libanotis libanotis, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in mountainous areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Libanotis libanotis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
