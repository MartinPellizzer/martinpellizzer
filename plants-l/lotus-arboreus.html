Lotus arboreus

Lotus arboreus, commonly known as the tree lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

The lotus arboreus is valued both for its ornamental beauty and for its use in traditional medicine.
