Leunisia

Leunisia, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Leunisia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its bright color and low maintenance requirements.
