Lantana aculeata f. mista

Lantana aculeata f. mista, commonly known as the misty lantana, is a compact, fast-growing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, violet, and red, depending on the variety. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
