Lotus tigrensis

Lotus tigrensis, commonly known as the Persian lotus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

The Persian lotus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in various regions.
