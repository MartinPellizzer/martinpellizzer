Lasianthus subspicatus

Lasianthus subspicatus, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Lasianthus subspicatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
