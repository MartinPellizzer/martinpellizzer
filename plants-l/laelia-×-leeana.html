Laelia × leeana

Laelia × leeana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceola.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically showy, with a range of colors including white, pink, and purple, and are often fragrant.

It prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
