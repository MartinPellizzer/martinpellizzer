Lespedeza thunbergii f. nivea

Lespedeza thunbergii f. nivea, commonly known as silver lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, fluffy white or pale pink flowers. The plant has delicate, slender leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.

Silver lespedeza is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
