Leucheria conyzoides

Leucheria conyzoides, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white to pale yellow. Its inflorescence is a type of umbel, and the flowers are surrounded by bracts.

The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks.
