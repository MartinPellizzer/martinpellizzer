Leucopogon fraseri

Leucopogon fraseri, commonly known as Fraser's heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

Leucopogon fraseri thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and dry forests.
