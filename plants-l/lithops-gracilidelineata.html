Lithops gracilidelineata

Lithops gracilidelineata, commonly known as the narrow-line ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance with two fleshy leaves that resemble stones. The plant stores water in its thick, fleshy leaves, allowing it to survive in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months.

Lithops gracilidelineata is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
