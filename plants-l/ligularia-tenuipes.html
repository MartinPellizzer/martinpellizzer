Ligularia tenuipes

Ligularia tenuipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-white underside. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
