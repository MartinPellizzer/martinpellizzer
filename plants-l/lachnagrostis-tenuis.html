Lachnagrostis tenuis

Lachnagrostis tenuis, commonly known as slender silvergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and marshy areas.

Lachnagrostis tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
