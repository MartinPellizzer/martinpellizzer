Leucocoryne roblesiana

Leucocoryne roblesiana, commonly known as the Roblesiana lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, grass-like leaves and a single, showy white or pale pink flower that blooms in the spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, rocky habitats. Its flowers have a distinctive, bell-shaped structure with recurved petals, and it is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
