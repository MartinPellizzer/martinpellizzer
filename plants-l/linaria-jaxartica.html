Linaria jaxartica

Linaria jaxartica, commonly known as the Jaxartian toadflax, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Linaria in the family Plantaginaceae. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Linaria jaxartica is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
