Liriosma papillosa

Liriosma papillosa, commonly known as the silky lily or velvet fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or near streams. Its leaves are often heart-shaped and can reach several inches in length.
