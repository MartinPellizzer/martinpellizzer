Leonotis tuberosa

Leonotis tuberosa, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that are typically bright orange or red, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
