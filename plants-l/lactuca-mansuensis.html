Lactuca mansuensis

Lactuca mansuensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to the highlands of Ethiopia and grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant has a rosette of broad, dark green leaves that are sometimes used in local cuisines. It produces small yellow flowers that develop into hard, spiky fruits.

Lactuca mansuensis is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season.
