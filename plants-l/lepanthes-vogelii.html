Lepanthes vogelii

Lepanthes vogelii, commonly known as the Vogel's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Lepanthes vogelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
