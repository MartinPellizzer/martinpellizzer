Lissochilus analavelensis

Lissochilus analavelensis, commonly known as the Analavelo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
