Lasianthus hoaensis

Lasianthus hoaensis, commonly known as the Hoaen's flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
