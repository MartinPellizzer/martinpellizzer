Lastrea officinalis

Lastrea officinalis, commonly known as the common lastrea, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spherical heads, typically displaying shades of yellow to orange.

Lastrea officinalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
