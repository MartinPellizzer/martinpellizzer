Lessertia Frutescens Subsp. Speciosa

Lessertia frutescens subsp. speciosa is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers, it is a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. This subspecies stands out for its unique foliage and seasonal blooming patterns. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal maintenance once established. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
