Licuala jeanneneyi

Licuala jeanneneyi, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of over 3 meters. Its leaves are stiff and fibrous, making it a popular choice for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
