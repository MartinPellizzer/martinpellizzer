Leptochloa srilankensis

Leptochloa srilankensis, commonly known as Srilankan needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Sri Lanka.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. The plant has slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are clustered in dense, cylindrical formations.

Leptochloa srilankensis is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in lowland and montane regions.
