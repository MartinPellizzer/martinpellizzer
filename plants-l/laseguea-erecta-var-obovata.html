Laseguea erecta var. obovata

Laseguea erecta var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate laseguea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically displaying a range of colors from white to pink. This variant is known for its adaptability to moist, shaded environments in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
