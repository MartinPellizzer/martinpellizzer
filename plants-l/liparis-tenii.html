Liparis tenii

Liparis tenii, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the dwarf lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features a compact growth habit with a short, stocky rhizome and a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale green, and are arranged in a loose spike, often resembling a tresses of hair.

Liparis tenii is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is cultivated in specialty orchid collections due to its ornamental value.
