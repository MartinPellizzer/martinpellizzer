Listrostachys dewevrei

Listrostachys dewevrei, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and bright red berries that persist through winter. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden settings. Its hardy nature allows it to withstand drought and salt spray, contributing to its popularity in landscaping.

The berries, while attractive, are toxic to humans and animals, requiring caution in cultivation.
