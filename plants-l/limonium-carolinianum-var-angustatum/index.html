Limonium Carolinianum Var. Angustatum

Limonium carolinianum var. angustatum is a delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. this variety is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils. it is a member of the plumbagaceae family. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
