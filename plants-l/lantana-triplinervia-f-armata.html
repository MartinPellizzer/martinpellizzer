Lantana triplinervia f. armata

Lantana triplinervia f. armata, commonly known as the armed lantana, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often with a reddish tinge. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, stiff hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.
