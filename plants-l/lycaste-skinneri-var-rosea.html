Lycaste skinneri var. rosea

Lycaste skinneri var. rosea, commonly known as the pink-tinged lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a subtle rose tint. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
