Lobularia intermedia var. subspinescens

Lobularia intermedia var. subspinescens, commonly known as the spiny lobularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous annual that produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers. The plant is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its unique spiny appearance and delicate blooms make it an ornamental plant with ecological value for pollinators.
