Lepanthes mystax

Lepanthes mystax, commonly known as the mystic slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that resemble small, intricate slippers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often epiphytic on trees. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive hooded lip.

Lepanthes mystax is prized for its ornamental value and is a subject of interest in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
