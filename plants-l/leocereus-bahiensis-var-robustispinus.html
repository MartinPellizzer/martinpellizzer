Leocereus bahiensis var. robustispinus

Leocereus bahiensis var. robustispinus, commonly known as the robustispinus cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring prominent, spiny areoles and a ribbed stem. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its slow growth rate, it is often cultivated as a decorative indoor or outdoor plant.
