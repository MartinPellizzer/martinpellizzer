Lysimachia kwangtungensis

Lysimachia kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender stems that are often hairy and bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its small, bright yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, blooming in the spring and summer months.

Lysimachia kwangtungensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
