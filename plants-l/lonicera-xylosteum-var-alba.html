Lonicera xylosteum var. alba

Lonicera xylosteum var. alba, commonly known as white honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, red, fleshy drupe that ripens in the fall.
