Licuala kersteniana

Licuala kersteniana, commonly known as the giant pandanus or kersten's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines, Indonesia, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It produces inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Licuala kersteniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and resilience in humid, tropical climates.
