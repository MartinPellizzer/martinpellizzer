Lepidium montanum var. typicum

Lepidium montanum var. typicum, commonly known as mountain cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a small annual herb with a basal rosette of leaves and flowering stems that bear small, white or yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, rounded seeds that are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Lepidium montanum var. typicum is valued for its edible leaves and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
