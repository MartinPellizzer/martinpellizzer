Linaria heterophylla f. violascens

Linaria heterophylla f. violascens, commonly known as the violet-leaved toadflax, is a flowering plant characterized by its distinctive violet-hued foliage.

The plant typically features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that range in color from deep purple to bluish-green. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
