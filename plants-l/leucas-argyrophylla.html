Leucas argyrophylla

Leucas argyrophylla, commonly known as silverleaf leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Leucas argyrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
