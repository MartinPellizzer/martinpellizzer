Lisianthius amoenus

Lisianthius amoenus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-blue petals and a prominent, spiky central cone. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, adding texture to its appearance.
