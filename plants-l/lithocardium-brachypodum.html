Lithocardium brachypodum

Lithocardium brachypodum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rockstar, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia, where it thrives in rocky soils.

Lithocardium brachypodum is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
