Lasiorrhiza philippiana

Lasiorrhiza philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Lasiorrhiza philippiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
