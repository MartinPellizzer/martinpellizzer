Liparis clypeolum subsp. clypeolum

Liparis clypeolum subsp.

clypeolum, commonly known as the flat-leaf lady's-tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences.

The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, recurved lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
