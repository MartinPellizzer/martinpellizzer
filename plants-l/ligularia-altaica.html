Ligularia altaica

Ligularia altaica, commonly known as the Altai ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in a tall, cylindrical spike, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both natural habitats and specialized garden settings.
