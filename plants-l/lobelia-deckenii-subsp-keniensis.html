Lobelia deckenii subsp. keniensis

Lobelia deckenii subsp.

keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan lobelia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in dense spikes, and are clustered at the top of the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or shaded areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
