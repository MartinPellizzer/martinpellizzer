Lobivia tiegeliana f. distefanoiana

Lobivia tiegeliana f. distefanoiana, commonly known as the Distefano's Lobivia, is a compact, branching cactus native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color, often adorned with white or pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in hue, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. It is well adapted to arid conditions, with a shallow root system and the ability to store water in its stems.

This fascinating plant is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
