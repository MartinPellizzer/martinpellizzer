Lirianthe coco

Lirianthe coco, commonly known as the coco lily, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble lilies, typically in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant produces clusters of fragrant blooms that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Lirianthe coco is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes used in floral arrangements.
