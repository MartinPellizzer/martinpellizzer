Languas formosana

Languas formosana, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
