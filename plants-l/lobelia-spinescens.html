Lobelia spinescens

Lobelia spinescens, commonly known as the spiny lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in woodland or swampy areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough, hairy texture.

Lobelia spinescens is part of the Campanulaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in native plant gardens.
