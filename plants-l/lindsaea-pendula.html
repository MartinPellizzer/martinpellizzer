Lindsaea pendula, commonly known as the hanging lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous fronds that drape from its rhizome, giving it a cascading appearance. Lindsaea pendula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and shaded gardens.
