Lysimachia ciliolata

Lysimachia ciliolata, commonly known as the silky cinquefoil, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Asia and North America.

It features delicate, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with finely toothed edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This plant is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local pollinator populations.
