Linochilus ellipticus

Linochilus ellipticus, commonly known as the elliptic orchid, is a species of orchid native to Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
