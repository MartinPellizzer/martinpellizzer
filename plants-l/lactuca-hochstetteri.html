Lactuca hochstetteri

Lactuca hochstetteri, commonly known as Hochstetter's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features basal rosettes of spoon-shaped, pale green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are followed by small, dry fruits called achenes.

Lactuca hochstetteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
