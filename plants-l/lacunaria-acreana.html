Lacunaria acreana

Lacunaria acreana, commonly known as the Brazilian nettle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

Lacunaria acreana is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
