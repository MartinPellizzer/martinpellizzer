Leucadendron uliginosum subsp. uliginosum

Leucadendron uliginosum subsp.

uliginosum, commonly known as the swamp leucadendron, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and striking, cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red.

The plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It is adapted to coastal and wetland environments, showing tolerance to salt spray and periodic flooding.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
