Lathyrus schaffneri

Lathyrus schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Lathyrus schaffneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
