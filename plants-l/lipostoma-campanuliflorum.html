Lipostoma campanuliflorum

Lipostoma campanuliflorum, commonly known as the bellflower lipostoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Lipostoma campanuliflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
