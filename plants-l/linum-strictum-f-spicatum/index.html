Linum Strictum F. Spicatum

Linum strictum f. spicatum is a lesser-known variant of the common flax plant, characterized by its compact growth and dense clusters of blue flowers. This subspecies thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after addition to native gardens and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to its more popular relatives. Understanding its ecology and cultivation can help promote its conservation and use in sustainable landscaping.
