Lepisanthes dictyophylla

Lepisanthes dictyophylla, commonly known as the net-leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves with a distinctive netted venation pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lepisanthes dictyophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
