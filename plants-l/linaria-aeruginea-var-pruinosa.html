Linaria aeruginea var. pruinosa

Linaria aeruginea var. pruinosa, commonly known as the silverweed or silver linaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The leaves are typically covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving the plant its distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
