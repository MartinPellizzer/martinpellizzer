Licuala grandiflora

Licuala grandiflora, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, arranged in a fan-like pattern. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, often in clusters. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its robust growth and unique appearance make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
