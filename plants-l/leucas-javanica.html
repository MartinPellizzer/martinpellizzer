Leucas javanica

Leucas javanica, commonly known as the Java leucas, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Leucas javanica is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
