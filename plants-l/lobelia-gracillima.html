Lobelia gracillima

Lobelia gracillima, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Lobelia gracillima is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
