Lomariopsis heteromorpha

Lomariopsis heteromorpha, commonly known as the heteromorph fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often asymmetrical fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rock crevices. Its leaves are usually lanceolate or ovate, with a serrated or toothed margin.

Lomariopsis heteromorpha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
