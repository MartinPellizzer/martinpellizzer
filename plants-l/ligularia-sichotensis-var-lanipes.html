Ligularia sichotensis var. lanipes

Ligularia sichotensis var. lanipes, commonly known as the Sichotian ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or pale margin, and it produces yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a fibrous root system and is valued for its ornamental foliage.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
