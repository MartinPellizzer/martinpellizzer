Leucas zeylanica var. linearis

Leucas zeylanica var. linearis, commonly known as linear leucas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural and traditional uses.
