Lemaireocereus beneckei

Lemaireocereus beneckei, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers are typically red, pink, or purple, with intricate patterns and a fragrant scent.

Lemaireocereus beneckei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
