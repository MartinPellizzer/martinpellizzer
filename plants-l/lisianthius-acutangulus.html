Lisianthius acutangulus

Lisianthius acutangulus, commonly known as the sharp-angled lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink.
