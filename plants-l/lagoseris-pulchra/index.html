Lagoseris Pulchra

Lagoseris pulchra is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It is known for its striking, pale yellow blooms that appear during the late spring and early summer. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Lagoseris pulchra is crucial for preserving its ecological role and biodiversity value.
