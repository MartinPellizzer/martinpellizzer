Lacara scabra

Lacara scabra, commonly known as the rough lacara, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
