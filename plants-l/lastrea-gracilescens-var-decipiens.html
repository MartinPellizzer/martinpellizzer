Lastrea gracilescens var. decipiens

Lastrea gracilescens var. decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with small, fleshy, greenish-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
