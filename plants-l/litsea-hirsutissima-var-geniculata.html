Litsea hirsutissima var. geniculata

Litsea hirsutissima var. geniculata, commonly known as the hairy litsea or mountain litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small yellow flowers followed by dark blue-black berries.

This variant is often found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in the understory of tropical and subtropical forests.
