Lilium formosanum var. pricei

Lilium formosanum var. pricei, commonly known as the Formosan lily, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern in its native habitat.
