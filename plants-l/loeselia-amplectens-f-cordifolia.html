Loeselia amplectens f. cordifolia

Loeselia amplectens f. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf loeselia, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are glossy and often have a deep green color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in dense forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
