Lamium maculatum f. albiflorum

Lamium maculatum f. albiflorum, commonly known as silver spotted dead nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive silver or white markings and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or garden beds. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.

The flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
