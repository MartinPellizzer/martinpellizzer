Linagrostis paniculata

Linagrostis paniculata, commonly known as the panicled grass or yellow panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and marshes. The plant features slender, erect stems with long, narrow leaves and produces panicles of small, yellowish flowers. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize soil, making it useful in erosion control.

Linagrostis paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland environments.
