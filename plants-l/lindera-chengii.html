Lindera chengii

Lindera chengii, commonly known as the Chinese wild laurier, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are edible.

Lindera chengii is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
