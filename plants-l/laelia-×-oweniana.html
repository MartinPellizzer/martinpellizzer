Laelia × oweniana

Laelia × oweniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceps.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the high altitudes of Central and South America. The plant typically produces tall, branched stems with numerous flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Laelia × oweniana prefers cool, bright environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
