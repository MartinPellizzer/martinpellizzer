Linaria spuria f. minoripes

Linaria spuria f. minoripes, commonly known as the lesser spurge.

This plant is a flowering species belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family, characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils.
