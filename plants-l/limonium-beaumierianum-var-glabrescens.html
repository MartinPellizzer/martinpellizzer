Limonium beaumierianum var. glabrescens

Limonium beaumierianum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has gray-green, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky coastal habitats.

Limonium beaumierianum var. glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.
