Lotononis gracilis var. brevipetiolata

Lotononis gracilis var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled slender lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with short petioles that distinguish this variety from other forms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
