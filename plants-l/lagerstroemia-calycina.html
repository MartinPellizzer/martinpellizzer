Lagerstroemia calycina

Lagerstroemia calycina, commonly known as the pink lagerstroemia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, flat seeds.

Lagerstroemia calycina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
