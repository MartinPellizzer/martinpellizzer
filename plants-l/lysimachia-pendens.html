Lysimachia pendens

Lysimachia pendens, commonly known as weeping chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features slender, cascading stems that produce small, bright yellow flowers throughout the summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Lysimachia pendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes for its delicate, trailing appearance.
