Lactuca deltoidea var. gorganica

Lactuca deltoidea var. gorganica, commonly known as the Gorganica lettuce, is a variant of the delta lettuce species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, deltoid-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters, and produces small, white flowers in the summer months. Its leaves are often used in salads or as a decorative element in culinary presentations.

The species is valued for its mild flavor and nutritional content, making it a popular choice in both home and commercial gardening.
