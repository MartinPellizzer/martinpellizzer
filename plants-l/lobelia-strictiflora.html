Lobelia strictiflora

Lobelia strictiflora, commonly known as the rigid-flowered lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family, characterized by its bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.
