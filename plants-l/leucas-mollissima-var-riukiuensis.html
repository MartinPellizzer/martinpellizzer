Leucas mollissima var. riukiuensis

Leucas mollissima var. riukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its hairy foliage and compact growth habit make it a distinctive species within the genus Leucas.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
