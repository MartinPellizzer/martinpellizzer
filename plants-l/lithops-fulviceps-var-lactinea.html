Lithops fulviceps var. lactinea

Lithops fulviceps var. lactinea, commonly known as the milk-stained ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a pair of fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically pale green with a white or cream-colored margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal watering.
