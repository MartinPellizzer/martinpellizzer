Lansium membranaceum

Lansium membranaceum, commonly known as the snake fruit or kopi luwak, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its culinary and medicinal uses.
