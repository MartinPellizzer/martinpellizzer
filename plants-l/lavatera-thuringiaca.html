Lavatera thuringiaca

Lavatera thuringiaca, commonly known as the common lavatera, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
