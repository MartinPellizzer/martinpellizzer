Limodorum abortivum var. rubrum

Limodorum abortivum var. rubrum, commonly known as the red-flowered basket lily, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically arranged in a loose cluster, with each bloom having a unique, intricate structure.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in specialized gardens due to its rarity and beauty.
