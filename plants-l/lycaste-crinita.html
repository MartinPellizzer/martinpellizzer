Lycaste crinita

Lycaste crinita, commonly known as the curly beard orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hairy sepals that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a yellow throat. Lycaste crinita is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
