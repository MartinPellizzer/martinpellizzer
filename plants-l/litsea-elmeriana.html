Litsea elmeriana

Litsea elmeriana, commonly known as Elmer's litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are often fragrant when crushed. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, orange-red berries.

Litsea elmeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
