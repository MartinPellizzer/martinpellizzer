Lyonia ligustrina var. foliosiflora

Lyonia ligustrina var. foliosiflora, commonly known as the leafy-flowered lyonia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters, often accompanied by lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its botanical characteristics include opposite leaves and small, berry-like fruits that mature to a dark purple color.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
