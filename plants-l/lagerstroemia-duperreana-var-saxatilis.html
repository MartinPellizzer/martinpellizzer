Lagerstroemia duperreana var. saxatilis

Lagerstroemia duperreana var. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock lagerstromea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often turning reddish in autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
