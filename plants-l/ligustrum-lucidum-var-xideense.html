Ligustrum lucidum var. xideense

Ligustrum lucidum var. xideense, commonly known as the Xideen privet, is a variant of the privet shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by black berries that are edible for birds. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different climates.
