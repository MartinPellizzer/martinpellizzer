Lucilia plicatifolia

Lucilia plicatifolia, commonly known as the pleated leaf plant, is a tropical species belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within a spathe, a leaf-like structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Lucilia plicatifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
