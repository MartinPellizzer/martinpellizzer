Ludwigia linearis var. typica

Ludwigia linearis var. typica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tint, especially under strong light conditions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or red, adding visual interest to aquariums or ponds. It thrives in moderate to high light and requires stable water conditions with good water flow.

Ludwigia linearis var. typica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
