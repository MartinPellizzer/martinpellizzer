Lasiorrhiza leontopodiodes

Lasiorrhiza leontopodiodes, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
