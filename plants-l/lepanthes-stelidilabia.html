Lepanthes stelidilabia

Lepanthes stelidilabia, commonly known as the Stelidilabia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its humid, tropical environment.

Lepanthes stelidilabia is often found in shaded, forested areas where it thrives in the understory of the canopy.
