Lepidium trianae

Lepidium trianae, commonly known as the Andean cress, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, edible leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Lepidium trianae typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine areas.
