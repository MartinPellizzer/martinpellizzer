Limonium Auriculae-Ursifolium Subsp. Lusitanicum

Limonium auriculae-ursifolium subsp. lusitanicum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to parts of southern Europe. It is known for its small, clustered blooms that range in color from pale blue to white. This subspecies thrives in coastal and sandy environments, often found along the shores of the Iberian Peninsula. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving this rare botanical treasure.
