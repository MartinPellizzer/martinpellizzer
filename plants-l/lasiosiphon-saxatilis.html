Lasiosiphon saxatilis

Lasiosiphon saxatilis, commonly known as the rock siphon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and often arranged in a rosette pattern.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
