Lecythis ovalifolia

Lecythis ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf lecythium, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, oval-shaped leaves and produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a single large seed. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a dense canopy, contributing to the forest's biodiversity. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting various pollinators.

The species is valued for its hard wood and is also used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
