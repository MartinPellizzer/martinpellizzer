Lotononis eriantha var. obovata

Lotononis eriantha var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are obovate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its inflorescence consists of yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale blue to white ray florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
