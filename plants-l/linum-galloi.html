Linum galloi

Linum galloi, commonly known as the golden flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Linum galloi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
