Lightfootia marginata var. lucens

Lightfootia marginata var. lucens, commonly known as the bright-edged lightfootia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or silver margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded understory habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
