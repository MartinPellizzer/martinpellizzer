Loxostigma aureum

Loxostigma aureum, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The stems are cylindrical and covered with soft, spiny areoles.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
