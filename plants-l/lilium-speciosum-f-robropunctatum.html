Lilium speciosum f. robropunctatum

Lilium speciosum f. robropunctatum, commonly known as the red-spotted lily, is a unique floral variant of the species Lilium speciosum.

It is characterized by its striking red spots on the white petals, which give it a distinctive appearance. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts and botanical gardens.
