Lepanthes cotyledon

Lepanthes cotyledon, commonly known as the cotyledon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique, often intricate floral structures. Lepanthes cotyledon grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

It is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
