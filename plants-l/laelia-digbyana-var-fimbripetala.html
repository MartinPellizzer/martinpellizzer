Laelia digbyana var. fimbripetala

Laelia digbyana var. fimbripetala, commonly known as the Fimbriated Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bicolor flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as a pseudobulbous orchid, with thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in high-altitude environments. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces long, slender flower spikes.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
