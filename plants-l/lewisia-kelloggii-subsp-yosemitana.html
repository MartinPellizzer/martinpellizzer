Lewisia kelloggii subsp. yosemitana

Lewisia kelloggii subsp.

yosemitana, commonly known as Yosemita's Lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the Sierra Nevada mountains of California. It is a low-growing perennial with fleshy, basal leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the high-altitude conditions of its native habitat.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens.
