Lastrea pellita

Lastrea pellita, commonly known as the silver leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
