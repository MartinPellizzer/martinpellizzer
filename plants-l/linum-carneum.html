Linum carneum

Linum carneum, commonly known as the woolly flax or hairy flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Linum carneum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
