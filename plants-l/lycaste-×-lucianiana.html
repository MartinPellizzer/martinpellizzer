Lycaste × lucianiana

Lycaste × lucianiana, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Lycaste meleagris and Lycaste tenebricosa.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, arching stems with clusters of fragrant, spider-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Lycaste × lucianiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and gardens.
