Lacinaria scariosa f. gracillima

Lacinaria scariosa f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender lace fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with hairy, scaly stems. Its fronds are typically arching, with a soft, feathery appearance that gives it a graceful look.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
