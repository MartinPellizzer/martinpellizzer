Lampranthus deltoides var. caulescens

Lampranthus deltoides var. caulescens, commonly known as the dwarf Christmas candle, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Its striking floral display makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
