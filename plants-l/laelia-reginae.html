Laelia reginae

Laelia reginae, commonly known as the imperial laelia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, showcasing vibrant colors such as white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Laelia reginae is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
