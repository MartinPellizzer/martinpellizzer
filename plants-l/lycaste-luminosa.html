Lycaste luminosa

Lycaste luminosa, commonly known as the luminous lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that emit a soft, bioluminescent glow under certain conditions. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with high light exposure.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
