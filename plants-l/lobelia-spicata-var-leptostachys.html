Lobelia spicata var. leptostachys

Lobelia spicata var. leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stemmed lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variant is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
