Lepanthes ruberrima

Lepanthes ruberrima, commonly known as the Crimson Violet Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking red and violet flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as cloud forests, where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its leaves are usually dark green and somewhat succulent, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Lepanthes ruberrima is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
