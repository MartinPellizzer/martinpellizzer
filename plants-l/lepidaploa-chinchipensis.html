Lepidaploa chinchipensis

Lepidaploa chinchipensis, commonly known as the Chinese spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.
