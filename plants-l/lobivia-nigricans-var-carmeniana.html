Lobivia nigricans var. carmeniana

Lobivia nigricans var. carmeniana, commonly known as Carmen's dark lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compact, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in a dense layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss in its harsh environment.

It is prized for its unique appearance and resilience, making it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
