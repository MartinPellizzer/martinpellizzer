Lactuca takasei

Lactuca takasei, commonly known as the Japanese endive, is a species of lettuce native to Japan.

It is characterized by its curly, pale green leaves that are often used in salads and cooked dishes. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other flowering plants. Lactuca takasei typically grows in moist, temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its leaves contain a mild bitterness, which is a distinguishing feature from other lettuce varieties.
