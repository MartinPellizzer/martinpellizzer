Lepilaena patentifolia

Lepilaena patentifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically broad and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. These flowers are followed by orange-red berries that are edible for birds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
