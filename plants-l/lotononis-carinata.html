Lotononis carinata

Lotononis carinata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Lotononis carinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
