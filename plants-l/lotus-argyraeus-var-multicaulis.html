Lotus argyraeus var. multicaulis

Lotus argyraeus var. multicaulis, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a striking perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features striking silver-blue foliage and produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is characterized by its multi-stemmed growth habit, which distinguishes it from other lotus varieties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
