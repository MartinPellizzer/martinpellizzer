Lotononis bolusii var. sessilis

Lotononis bolusii var. sessilis, commonly known as the sessile lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc flowers surrounded by white ray florets. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
