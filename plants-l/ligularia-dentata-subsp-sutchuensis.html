Ligularia dentata subsp. sutchuensis

Ligularia dentata subsp.

sutchuensis, commonly known as the Sutchuen ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China. It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The plant produces showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
