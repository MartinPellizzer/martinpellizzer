Lasianthus dinhensis var. glabrescens

Lasianthus dinhensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Dinh's glabrous lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The leaves are typically lanceolate, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
