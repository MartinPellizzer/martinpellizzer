Lavatera maritima var. purpurea

Lavatera maritima var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple flowering lavatera, is a perennial shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and showy, semi-double flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal gardens. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to landscapes.

The species is also valued for its adaptability and ability to tolerate saline conditions.
