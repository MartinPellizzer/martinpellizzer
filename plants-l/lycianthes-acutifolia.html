Lycianthes acutifolia

Lycianthes acutifolia, commonly known as the sharpleaf bitterleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Solanaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features small, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
