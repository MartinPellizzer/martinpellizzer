Lindenbergia fruticosa

Lindenbergia fruticosa, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils near the shoreline. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It is adapted to salt spray and can tolerate moderate salinity.

Lindenbergia fruticosa is often found in coastal dunes and is a characteristic species of maritime vegetation.
