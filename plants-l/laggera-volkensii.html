Laggera volkensii

Laggera volkensii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

The species is also noted for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
