Lomatogonium sikkimense

Lomatogonium sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim lomatogonium, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in mountainous ecosystems.
