Lass sagittata

Lass sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving water and is often used in ponds and water gardens. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

Lass sagittata is valued for its ability to oxygenate water and provide habitat for aquatic life.
