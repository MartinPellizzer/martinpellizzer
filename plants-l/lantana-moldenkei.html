Lantana moldenkei

Lantana moldenkei, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically yellow or orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, posing a threat to native plant species.
