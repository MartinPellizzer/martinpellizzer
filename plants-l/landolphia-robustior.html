Landolphia robustior

Landolphia robustior, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Landolphia robustior is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
