Lycium × argentinocestroides

Lycium × argentinocestroides, commonly known as the silverleaf lycium, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white pubescent leaves and small, orange-red berries that ripen in late summer. Native to South America, this plant thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and berries.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
