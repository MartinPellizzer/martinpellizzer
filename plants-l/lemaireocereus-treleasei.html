Lemaireocereus treleasei

Lemaireocereus treleasei, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that bear clusters of vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, hence its common name. Its leaves are modified into spiny stems, and it produces small, white flowers that can be either regular or tubular in shape.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
