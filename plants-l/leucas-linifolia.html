Leucas linifolia

Leucas linifolia, commonly known as silverleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, silver-haired stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its aromatic properties, it has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
