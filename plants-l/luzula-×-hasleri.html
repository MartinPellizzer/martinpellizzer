Luzula × hasleri

Luzula × hasleri, commonly known as the hybrid goat's beard, is a perennial plant belonging to the grass family (Poaceae).

It is a sterile hybrid between Luzula sylvatica and Luzula sylvatica var. glabella, characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
