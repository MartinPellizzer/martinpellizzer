Ligustrum obtusifolium var. regelianum

Ligustrum obtusifolium var. regelianum, commonly known as the Armenian privet, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features narrow, ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and a dark green color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by black, spherical berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

The species is also valued for its use in hedges and as a landscape plant due to its hardy nature.
