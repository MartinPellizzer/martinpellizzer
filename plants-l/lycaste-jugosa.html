Lycaste jugosa

Lycaste jugosa, commonly known as the showy lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Lycaste jugosa thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
