Lespedeza friebeana

Lespedeza friebeana, commonly known as the Friebean lespedesa, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Lespedeza friebeana is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and hardiness.
