Luxemburgia furnensis

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Luxemburgia furnensis is typically found in shaded forest understories, where it forms dense clusters.
