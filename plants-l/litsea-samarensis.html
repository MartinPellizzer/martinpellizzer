Litsea samarensis

Litsea samarensis, commonly known as the Samarensis-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically dark red when mature.

Litsea samarensis is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
