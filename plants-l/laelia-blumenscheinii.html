Laelia blumenscheinii

Laelia blumenscheinii, commonly known as the Blumenschein's orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Laelia blumenscheinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
