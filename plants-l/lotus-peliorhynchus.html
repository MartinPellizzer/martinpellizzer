Lotus peliorhynchus

Lotus peliorhynchus, commonly known as the water lotus, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that float on the surface of water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide a habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its roots grow in the muddy substrate, anchoring the plant and absorbing nutrients.

Lotus peliorhynchus plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by supporting biodiversity and improving water quality.
