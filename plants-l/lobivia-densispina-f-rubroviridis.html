Lobivia densispina f. rubroviridis

Lobivia densispina f. rubroviridis, commonly known as the red and yellow lobivia, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces vibrant, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its greenish-blue body. Its foliage is typically sparse, with spines that provide protection from herbivores.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
