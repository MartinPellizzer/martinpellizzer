Landolphia Glabra

Landolphia glabra is a striking climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is known for its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. This species thrives in humid environments and often grows along forest edges or in disturbed soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some areas if not properly managed.
