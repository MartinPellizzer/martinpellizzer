Leucospermum formosum

Leucospermum formosum, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has needle-like leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is well adapted to nutrient-poor soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
