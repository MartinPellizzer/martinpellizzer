Lomariopsis orbiculata

Lomariopsis orbiculata, commonly known as the orbiculate fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its distinctive, orbicular (round) fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a deep green color, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Lomariopsis orbiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady gardens or as a houseplant.
