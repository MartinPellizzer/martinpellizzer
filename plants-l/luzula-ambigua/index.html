Luzula Ambigua

Luzula ambigua is a delicate yet resilient plant belonging to the grass family. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. This species is known for its slender stems and clusters of small, spike-like flowers. Its ability to thrive in low-light conditions makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants. Despite its modest appearance, Luzula ambigua plays an important role in maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
