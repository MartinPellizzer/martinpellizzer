Lysimachia tenuata

Lysimachia tenuata, commonly known as narrowleaf chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to early summer. The plant has slender, creeping stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia tenuata is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habits.
