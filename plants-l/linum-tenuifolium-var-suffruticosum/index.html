Linum Tenuifolium Var. Suffruticosum

Linum tenuifolium var. suffruticosum is a unique variant of the flax plant known for its trailing growth habit and delicate blue flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its small, lance-shaped leaves and profuse blooms add a soft, ornamental touch to any landscape. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked for its potential in herbal medicine and fiber production. This lesser-known plant holds both ecological and practical value in horticulture and natural remedies.
