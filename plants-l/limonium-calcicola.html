Limonium calcicola

Limonium calcicola, commonly known as the limestone lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Limonium calcicola is a perennial species that blooms in summer, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
