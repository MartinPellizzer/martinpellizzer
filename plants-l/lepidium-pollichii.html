Lepidium pollichii

Lepidium pollichii, commonly known as the Pollich's cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, green, oval-shaped leaves and produces tiny white or yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, temperate environments and is often found in shaded areas.

Lepidium pollichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
