Lomaria rigida

Lomaria rigida, commonly known as rigid lomaria, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Lomaria rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
