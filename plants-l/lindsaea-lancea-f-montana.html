Lindsaea lancea f. montana

Lindsaea lancea f. montana, commonly known as the mountain lindsaea, is a fern native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, often growing on rocks or in shaded forest understories. Its rhizome is short and thick, supporting the growth of its aerial roots and fronds.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
