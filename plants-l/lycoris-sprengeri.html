Lycoris sprengeri

Lycoris sprengeri, commonly known as the Red Spider Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to East Asia.

It features striking red flowers with a spider-like appearance, which bloom in the autumn season. The plant has narrow, strap-shaped leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique floral display and autumn blooming habit make it a notable species in the Amaryllidaceae family.
