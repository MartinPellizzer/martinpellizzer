Larnax glabra

Larnax glabra, commonly known as the smooth larnax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of the Mediterranean.

It features slender, upright stems that are covered in smooth, hairless foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or white in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly succulent texture, adapted to thrive in dry conditions.

Larnax glabra is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
