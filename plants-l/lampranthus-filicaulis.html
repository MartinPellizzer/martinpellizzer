Lampranthus filicaulis

Lampranthus filicaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
