Lysimachia floribunda

Lysimachia floribunda, commonly known as golden thread, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a smooth texture.

Lysimachia floribunda thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
