Lepisanthes rubiginosa

Lepisanthes rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
