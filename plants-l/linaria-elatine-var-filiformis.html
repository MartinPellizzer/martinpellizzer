Linaria elatine var. filiformis

Linaria elatine var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in pairs along the stem. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
