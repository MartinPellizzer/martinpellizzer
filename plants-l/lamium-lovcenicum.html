Lamium lovcenicum

Lamium lovcenicum, commonly known as silver nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Lamium lovcenicum is valued for its ornamental qualities and can be used in gardens to add visual interest.
