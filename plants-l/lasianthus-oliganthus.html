Lasianthus oliganthus

Lasianthus oliganthus, commonly known as the slender-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth surface, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
