Lespedeza × macrovirgata

Lespedeza × macrovirgata, commonly known as the Chinese lespedeza, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves that are opposite or alternate in arrangement. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers in clusters, typically ranging in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Lespedeza × macrovirgata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone regions.
