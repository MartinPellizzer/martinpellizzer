Lessingianthus onopordioides

Lessingianthus onopordioides, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and cylindrical stems. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its flowers are typically yellow with a prominent central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
