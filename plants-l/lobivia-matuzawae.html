Lobivia matuzawae

Lobivia matuzawae, commonly known as the Matuzawa Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and thick, fleshy stems. The plant typically features spiny areoles and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to survive in dry, nutrient-poor soils.

Lobivia matuzawae is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
