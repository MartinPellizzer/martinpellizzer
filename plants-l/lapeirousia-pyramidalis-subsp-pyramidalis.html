Lapeirousia pyramidalis subsp. pyramidalis

Lapeirousia pyramidalis subsp.

pyramidalis, commonly known as the Pyramidal Rain Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa. It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow throat.

The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy environments.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in rock gardens.
