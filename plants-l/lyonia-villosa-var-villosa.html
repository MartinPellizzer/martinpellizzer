Lyonia villosa var. villosa

Lyonia villosa var. villosa, commonly known as the silky lyonia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, slender stems and elliptical leaves with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
