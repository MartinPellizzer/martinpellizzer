Lobelia patula

Lobelia patula, commonly known as the slender blue lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia patula thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
