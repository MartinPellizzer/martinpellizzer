Licuala burretii

Licuala burretii, commonly known as the giant panda palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has a short, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous sheath. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Licuala burretii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
