Licuala flammula

Licuala flammula, commonly known as the flame-leaf palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often bright yellow or orange in color. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.
