Lobivia polyantha

Lobivia polyantha, commonly known as the Rainbow Lobivia, is a compact, flowering cactus native to South America.

It features a dense, rounded stem covered in small, tuberculate bumps and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is prized for its compact size and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water. It blooms in spring and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in rock gardens or containers.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
