Lathyrus japonicus var. japonicus

Lathyrus japonicus var. japonicus, commonly known as Japanese vetch, is a climbing legume native to East Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and is often found in moist, shady environments. It has small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
