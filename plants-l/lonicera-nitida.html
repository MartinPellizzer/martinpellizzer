Lonicera nitida

Lonicera nitida, commonly known as evergreen honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in clusters, usually yellow or white, which bloom in spring. It is often used in landscaping for its dense growth and ornamental value.

Lonicera nitida is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to urban conditions.
