Lychnidia coeli-rosa var. speciosa

Lychnidia coeli-rosa var. speciosa, commonly known as the celestial rose lychnis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a subtle bluish tinge. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for alpine gardens. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, adding to its ornamental appeal.

The species is noted for its ability to bloom throughout the summer, providing a long-lasting display of color in the landscape.
