Lycopus lucidus var. obtusifolius

Lycopus lucidus var. obtusifolius, commonly known as broadleaf water-honeysuckle, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist woodlands and along stream banks, preferring partial to full shade. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue to purple in color and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
