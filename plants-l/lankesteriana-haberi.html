Lankesteriana haberi

Lankesteriana haberi, commonly known as the Haber's flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with contrasting yellow markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the humid rainforests of its native habitat. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous showy blooms.

Due to its rarity and specific environmental requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
