Leptospermum stellatum f. angustifolium

Leptospermum stellatum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved tea tree, is a compact shrub native to Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically grey-green in color, with a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in traditional herbal remedies.
