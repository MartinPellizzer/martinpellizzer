Lasianthus incomptus

Lasianthus incomptus, commonly known as the slender lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stem and opposite leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Lasianthus incomptus is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
