Lotus corniculatus var. delortii

Lotus corniculatus var. delortii, commonly known as the Delort's bird's-foot trefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.

The variety delortii is distinguished by its specific morphology and geographic distribution compared to other variants of Lotus corniculatus.
