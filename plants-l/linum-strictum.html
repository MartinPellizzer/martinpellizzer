Linum strictum

Linum strictum, commonly known as slender flax, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Linum strictum is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
