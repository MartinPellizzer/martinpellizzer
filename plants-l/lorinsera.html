Lorinsera

Lorinsera, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Lorinsera typically grows as an epiphytic plant, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture from the air.

Its unique botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical research.
