Litsea fenestrata

Litsea fenestrata, commonly known as the windowleaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. Litsea fenestrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
