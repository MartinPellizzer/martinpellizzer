Lactuca pancicii

Lactuca pancicii, commonly known as Pancici's lettuce, is a species of annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The plant is known for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter taste.
