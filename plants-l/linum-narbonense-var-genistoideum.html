Linum narbonense var. genistoideum

Linum narbonense var. genistoideum, commonly known as the narrowleaf flax or Spanish flax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, branching stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in loose racemes, typical of the Linaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and historical use in textile production.
