Lilium tenii

Lilium tenii, commonly known as the Japanese scarlet lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with white throats and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. It is a bulbous plant that produces showy blooms in late spring to early summer.

Lilium tenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
