Lapeirousia viridis

Lapeirousia viridis, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, typically in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.

Lapeirousia viridis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
