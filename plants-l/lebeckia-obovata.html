Lebeckia obovata

Lebeckia obovata, commonly known as the silver lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are obovate in shape, giving it its scientific name. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Lebeckia obovata is well-suited for rock gardens and dry climates due to its drought tolerance.
