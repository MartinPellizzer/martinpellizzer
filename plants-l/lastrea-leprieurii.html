Lastrea leprieurii

Lastrea leprieurii, commonly known as the Leprieur's lastrea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or red, arranged in whorls along the stems.
