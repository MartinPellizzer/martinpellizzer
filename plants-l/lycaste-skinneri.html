Lycaste skinneri

Lycaste skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with inflorescences bearing several flowers on each spike. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage.

Lycaste skinneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
