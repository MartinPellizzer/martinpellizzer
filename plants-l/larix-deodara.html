Larix deodara

Larix deodara, commonly known as the deodar cedar, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and white underneath. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its bark is thick and reddish-brown, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

Larix deodara is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and landscapes.
