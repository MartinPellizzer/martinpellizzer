Ludwigia scabriuscula

Ludwigia scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-stemmed Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, rough hairs. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice for aquatic and terrestrial gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ludwigia scabriuscula is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions, making it a versatile plant for landscaping.
