Lastrea pusilla

Lastrea pusilla, commonly known as the small lastrea, is a small, mat-forming succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in rock gardens and succulent collections.
