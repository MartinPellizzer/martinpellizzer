Lepanthes gloris

Lepanthes gloris, commonly known as the glory orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the late spring or early summer.

Lepanthes gloris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
