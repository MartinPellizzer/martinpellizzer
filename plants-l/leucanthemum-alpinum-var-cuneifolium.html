Leucanthemum alpinum var. cuneifolium

Leucanthemum alpinum var. cuneifolium, commonly known as the alpine oxeye, is a perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It features a rosette of basal leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped, giving it its varietal name "cuneifolium.". The plant produces daisy-like flowers with white ray florets surrounding a yellow disk in the center. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
