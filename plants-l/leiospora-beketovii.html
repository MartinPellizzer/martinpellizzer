Leiospora beketovii

Leiospora beketovii, commonly known as the Bektovii spore plant, is a unique species of fungi that belongs to the Leiosporaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, smooth, and spore-filled structures that are typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant lacks traditional stems and leaves, instead relying on its spore sacs for reproduction. Its spores are released into the air, allowing for wind dispersal and colonization of new habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements, Leiospora beketovii is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
