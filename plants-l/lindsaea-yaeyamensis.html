Lindsaea yaeyamensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches in tropical rainforests. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its natural range.
