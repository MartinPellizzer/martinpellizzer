Libanothamnus cristamontis

Libanothamnus cristamontis, commonly known as the Monteverde flame tree, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high rainfall.

Due to its unique beauty and habitat specificity, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
