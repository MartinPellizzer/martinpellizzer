Lythrum acinifolium

Lythrum acinifolium, commonly known as red root, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. Its root system is deep and fibrous, which allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Lythrum acinifolium is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and other damp habitats.
