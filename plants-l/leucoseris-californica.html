Leucoseris californica

Leucoseris californica, commonly known as the California leucoseris, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
