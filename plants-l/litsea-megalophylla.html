Litsea megalophylla

Litsea megalophylla, commonly known as the giant litsea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 20 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is a small, yellow drupe that contains a single seed.
