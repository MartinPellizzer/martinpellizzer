Lisianthius scabridulus

Lisianthius scabridulus, commonly known as the rough lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. Lisianthius scabridulus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
