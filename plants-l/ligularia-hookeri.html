Ligularia hookeri

Ligularia hookeri, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green with a distinctive white or yellow margin. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in a spadix, surrounded by a leaf-like bract. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Ligularia hookeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to wet conditions.
