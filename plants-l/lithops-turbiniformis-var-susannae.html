Lithops turbiniformis var. susannae

Lithops turbiniformis var. susannae, commonly known as the "Susanna's African Violet," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
