Leptolaena delphinensis

Leptolaena delphinensis, commonly known as the Delphinus lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Leptolaena delphinensis is valued in gardens for its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
