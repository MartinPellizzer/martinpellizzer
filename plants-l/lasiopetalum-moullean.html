Lasiopetalum moullean

Lasiopetalum moullean, commonly known as the Moullean flameflower, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, arranged in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
