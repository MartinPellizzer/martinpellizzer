Lastrea falcifera

Lastrea falcifera, commonly known as the falcate lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, spiny stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically small and triangular, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
