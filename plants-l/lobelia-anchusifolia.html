Lobelia anchusifolia

Lobelia anchusifolia, commonly known as the anchusleaf lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in spikes along the stem. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Lobelia anchusifolia is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
