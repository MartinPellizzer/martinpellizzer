Lantana nivea subsp. nivea

Lantana nivea subsp.

nivea, commonly known as white lantana, is a flowering shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia. It features small, clustered white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.

The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
