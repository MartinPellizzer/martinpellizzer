Lycium modestum

Lycium modestum, commonly known as Chinese wolfberry or Goji berry, is a deciduous shrub native to China, Mongolia, and parts of Russia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are rich in antioxidants and vitamins. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lycium modestum thrives in arid and semi-arid climates, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.
