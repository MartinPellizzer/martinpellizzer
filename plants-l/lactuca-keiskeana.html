Lactuca keiskeana

Lactuca keiskeana, commonly known as the Keiske's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, particularly Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant typically grows as a low, sprawling herb with hairy stems and small, pale green leaves. It produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which is characteristic of the Asteraceae family.

Lactuca keiskeana is adapted to survive in harsh environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.
