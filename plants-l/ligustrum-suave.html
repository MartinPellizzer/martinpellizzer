Ligustrum suave

Ligustrum suave, commonly known as sweet privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, and it is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Ligustrum suave is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy flowers.
