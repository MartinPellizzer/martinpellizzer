Lasianthus stipularis

Lasianthus stipularis, commonly known as the slender-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Thailand, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It often grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate foliage and attractive floral display.
