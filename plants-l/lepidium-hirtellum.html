Lepidium hirtellum

Lepidium hirtellum, commonly known as hairy cress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows. It has small, white or pale yellow flowers and hairy stems and leaves.

Lepidium hirtellum is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
