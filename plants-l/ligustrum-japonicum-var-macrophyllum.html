Ligustrum japonicum var. macrophyllum

Ligustrum japonicum var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the Japanese privet with large leaves, is a shrub native to Japan.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are notably larger than those of the typical Ligustrum japonicum. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by black berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
