Limonium frutescens

Limonium frutescens, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and distinctive blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in coastal environments and well-drained soils, often growing in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Limonium frutescens is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate saline conditions.
