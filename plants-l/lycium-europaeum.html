Lycium europaeum

Lycium europaeum, commonly known as European wolfberry, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, red berries that are edible and have a slightly tart flavor. The plant has woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The berries are rich in antioxidants and have been used in traditional herbal medicine.
