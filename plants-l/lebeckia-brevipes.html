Lebeckia brevipes

Lebeckia brevipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
