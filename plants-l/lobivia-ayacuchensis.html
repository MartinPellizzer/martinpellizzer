Lobivia ayacuchensis

Lobivia ayacuchensis, commonly known as the Ayacuchensis Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the highlands of Peru.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a thick, ribbed stem that stores water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
