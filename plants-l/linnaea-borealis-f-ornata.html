Linnaea borealis f. ornata

Linnaea borealis f. ornata, commonly known as the golden star.

This plant is a variant of the twinflower, characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to the boreal forests of North America and parts of Eurasia. The foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
