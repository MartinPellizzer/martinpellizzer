Ligusticum obtusiusculum

Ligusticum obtusiusculum, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its aromatic, compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
