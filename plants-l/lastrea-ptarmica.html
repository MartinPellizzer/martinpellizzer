Lastrea ptarmica

Lastrea ptarmica, commonly known as the Cape rose or mountain rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, white hairs. Its showy, pink to purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
