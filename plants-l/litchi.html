Litchi

Litchi, commonly known as lychee, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China.

Its fruit is characterized by a rough, red outer shell and a sweet, fragrant, white flesh. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The fruit is rich in vitamins and antioxidants, making it a popular fruit in many cuisines.

Litchi trees thrive in warm, humid climates and require well-drained soil for optimal growth.
