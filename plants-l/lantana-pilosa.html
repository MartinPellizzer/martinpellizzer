Lantana pilosa

Lantana pilosa, commonly known as the hairy lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, orange, and red. The plant has opposite, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Lantana pilosa is also known for its ability to attract birds and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.
