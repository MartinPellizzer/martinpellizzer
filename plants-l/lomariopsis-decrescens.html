Lomariopsis decrescens

Lomariopsis decrescens, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows as a small, terrestrial fern with delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often arching or drooping. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with the rhizomes producing both roots and new shoots. Its leaves are usually light green, and it prefers moist, shaded environments in forest understories.

Lomariopsis decrescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden settings.
