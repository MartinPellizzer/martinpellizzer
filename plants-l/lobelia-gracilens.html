Lobelia gracilens

Lobelia gracilens, commonly known as slender lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Lobelia gracilens is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
