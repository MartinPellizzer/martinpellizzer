Listrostachys ashantensis

Listrostachys ashantensis, commonly known as the Ashanti sunflower, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow ray flowers and robust, upright stems. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in summer.

The Ashanti sunflower is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in its native region.
