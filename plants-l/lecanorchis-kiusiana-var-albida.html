Lecanorchis kiusiana var. albida

Lecanorchis kiusiana var. albida, commonly known as the white-flowered marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and forest floors. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming dense clusters.

The orchid's unique floral structure and coloration make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
