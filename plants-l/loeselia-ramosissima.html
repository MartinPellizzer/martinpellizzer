Loeselia ramosissima

Loeselia ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy loeselia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, branched growth habit and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Loeselia ramosissima is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
