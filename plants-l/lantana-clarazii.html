Lantana clarazii

Lantana clarazii, commonly known as the red lantana, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often aromatic and can be used in traditional medicine.
