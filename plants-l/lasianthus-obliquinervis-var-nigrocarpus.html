Lasianthus obliquinervis var. nigrocarpus

Lasianthus obliquinervis var. nigrocarpus, commonly known as the black-fruited lasianthus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking dark purple to black fruits. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a distinct, often zigzag venation pattern. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and are typically purple or pink in color.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruiting structures.
