Lathyrus ensifolius

Lathyrus ensifolius, commonly known as the swordleaf pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soils. The plant features slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, arranged in racemes, and bloom in the spring.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and are used in traditional cuisines, though the plant is sometimes avoided due to potential toxicity in certain parts.
