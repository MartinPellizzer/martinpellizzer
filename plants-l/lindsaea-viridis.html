Lindsaea viridis

Lindsaea viridis, commonly known as the green lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically bright green in color. Its intricate structure and unique reproductive features make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
