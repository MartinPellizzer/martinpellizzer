Ludisia discolor

Ludisia discolor, commonly known as the pink lace fern, is a striking tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like foliage with a distinctive pinkish hue on the undersides of its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Ludisia discolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
