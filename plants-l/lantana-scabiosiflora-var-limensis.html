Lantana scabiosiflora var. limensis

Lantana scabiosiflora var. limensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
