Lilium Makinoi Var. Alexandrae

Lilium makinoi var. alexandrae is a rare and beautiful species of lily native to the mountainous regions of Japan. This variant is known for its striking pink and white blooms that appear in late summer. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited natural habitat, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in botanical gardens. Its delicate appearance and historical significance make it a cherished subject among plant enthusiasts.
