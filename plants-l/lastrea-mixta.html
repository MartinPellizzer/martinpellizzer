Lastrea mixta

Lastrea mixta, commonly known as the hybrid lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, green to reddish stems that can branch out into multiple shoots. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and rockeries.
