Lobelia capillifolia

Lobelia capillifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. It is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.

Lobelia capillifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
