Lobelia oaxacana

Lobelia oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.
