Luzula velutina

Luzula velutina, commonly known as velvet woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses and related plants. Luzula velutina typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
