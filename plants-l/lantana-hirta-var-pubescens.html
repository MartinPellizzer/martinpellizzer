Lantana hirta var. pubescens

Lantana hirta var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lantana hirta var. pubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm climates.
