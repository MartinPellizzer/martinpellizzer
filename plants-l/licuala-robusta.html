Licuala robusta

Licuala robusta, commonly known as the broadleaf palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter. The plant typically grows as a single trunk with a crown of leaves, and it thrives in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as a decorative element in landscaping.

The plant is also valued for its ability to purify the air and provide shade in tropical environments.
