Luzula glabrata var. vera

Luzula glabrata var. vera, commonly known as the smooth woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit, often forming clumps or mats. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The inflorescence consists of a spike-like arrangement of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are usually inconspicuous and wind-pollinated.
