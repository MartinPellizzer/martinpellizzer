Lindsaea pulchra

Lindsaea pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lace-like fronds with a distinctive, often bright green coloration. Its rhizome is creeping, allowing it to spread along the forest floor.

Lindsaea pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
