Leptocoryphium obtusum

Leptocoryphium obtusum, commonly known as the broad-leaved rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a compact rosette. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Leptocoryphium obtusum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
