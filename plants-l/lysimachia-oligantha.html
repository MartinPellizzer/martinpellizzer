Lysimachia oligantha

Lysimachia oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf yellowwort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lysimachia oligantha thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
