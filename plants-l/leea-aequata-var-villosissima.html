Leea aequata var. villosissima

Leea aequata var. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy leea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
