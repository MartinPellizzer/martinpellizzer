Lonicera orientalis f. longifolia

Lonicera orientalis f. longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.
