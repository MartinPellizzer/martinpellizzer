Lonchocarpus yoroensis

Lonchocarpus yoroensis, commonly known as the Yoroena tree, is a leguminous plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds enclosed in a hard, woody capsule. It is often found in moist tropical forests and can grow up to 10 meters in height.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
