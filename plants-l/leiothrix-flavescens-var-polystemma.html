Leiothrix flavescens var. polystemma

Leiothrix flavescens var. polystemma, commonly known as the golden thread orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, fragrant flowers. The leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often covered with a fine, silky pubescence. The flowers are typically yellow or golden in color, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
