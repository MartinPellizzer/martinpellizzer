Lepanthes clareae

Lepanthes clareae, commonly known as the Clarea Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lepanthes clareae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
