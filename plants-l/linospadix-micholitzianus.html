Linospadix micholitzianus

Linospadix micholitzianus, commonly known as the Micholitz's spathiphyllum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

Linospadix micholitzianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in humid environments.
