Linkia sandersiana

Linkia sandersiana, commonly known as the sandersian sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored heartwood. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a fragrant scent. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by globose, yellowish fruits.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered an endangered species.
