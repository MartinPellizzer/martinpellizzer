Leiena

Leiena, commonly known as the white lily, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white or pale pink flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bulbs that store nutrients for future growth. Leiena thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
