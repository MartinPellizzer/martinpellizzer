Listera bungeana

Listera bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge's basketflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features a short, rhizomatous rootstock and produces opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
