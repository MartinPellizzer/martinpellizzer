Ligularia bigelovii

Ligularia bigelovii, commonly known as the giant dock, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shady environments, making it a common sight in woodland areas. Its flowering stems bear yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer.

Ligularia bigelovii is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
