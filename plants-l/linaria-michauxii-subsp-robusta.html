Linaria michauxii subsp. robusta

Linaria michauxii subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and profuse display of yellow-orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, while the flowers are tubular with two lips.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
