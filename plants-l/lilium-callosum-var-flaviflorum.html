Lilium callosum var. flaviflorum

Lilium callosum var. flaviflorum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered fritillary, is a rare and striking variant of the fritillary lily.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of central China, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its bulbs are adapted for survival in rocky soils and can remain dormant for extended periods.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
