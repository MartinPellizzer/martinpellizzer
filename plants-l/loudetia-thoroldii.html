Loudetia thoroldii

Loudetia thoroldii, commonly known as the red grass or red oat grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and its distinctive reddish tinge in the inflorescence. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like flower clusters. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
