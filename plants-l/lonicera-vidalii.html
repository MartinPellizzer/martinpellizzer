Lonicera vidalii

Lonicera vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring. Its berries are small, red, and edible, often attracting birds.

Lonicera vidalii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
