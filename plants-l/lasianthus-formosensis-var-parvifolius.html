Lasianthus formosensis var. parvifolius

Lasianthus formosensis var. parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
