Lespedeza metcalfei

Lespedeza metcalfei, commonly known as Chinese stragglers, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open woodlands and grassy areas. The plant produces small, pale yellow to reddish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

Lespedeza metcalfei is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
