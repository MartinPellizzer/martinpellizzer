Limonia ugandensis

Limonia ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda lime, is a small evergreen tree native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, citrus-scented flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits with a thin, yellowish skin and a juicy, acidic pulp. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Limonia ugandensis is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in tropical climates with well-drained soil.
