Lamium felixii

Lamium felixii, commonly known as the purple deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive purple underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Lamium felixii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
