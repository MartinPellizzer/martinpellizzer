Lannea Asplenifolia

Lannea asplenifolia is a unique and resilient plant native to the arid regions of East Africa. It is known for its striking, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This species thrives in harsh environments, making it an important part of the local ecosystem. Despite its hardiness, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Its adaptability and beauty make it a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
