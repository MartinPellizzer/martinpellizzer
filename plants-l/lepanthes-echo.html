Lepanthes echo

Lepanthes echo, commonly known as the Echo Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue with intricate patterns, and it has a short, hairy stem.

Lepanthes echo is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
