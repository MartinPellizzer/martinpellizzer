Limonium capitis-marci

Limonium capitis-marci, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Limonium capitis-marci is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
