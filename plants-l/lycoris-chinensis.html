Lycoris chinensis

Lycoris chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese lily or red spider lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features striking red flowers with white markings, which appear in the autumn season. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. Its flowers are often associated with cultural symbolism in Chinese tradition, representing farewell and remembrance.

Lycoris chinensis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
