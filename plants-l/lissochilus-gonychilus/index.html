Lissochilus Gonychilus

Lissochilus gonychilus is a rare and striking orchid native to the high altitudes of the Andes. Known for its vibrant purple and white flowers, this species captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil, making it a challenging yet rewarding plant to cultivate. Its unique morphology and delicate beauty make it a prized addition to specialized orchid collections. Despite its allure, Lissochilus gonychilus remains relatively unknown to the general public.
