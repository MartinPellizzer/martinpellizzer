Lobelia laurentioides

Lobelia laurentioides, commonly known as the Laurentian lobelia, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. It is known for its ornamental value and is often used in native plant gardens.

Lobelia laurentioides plays a role in supporting local pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
