Lobelia succulenta

Lobelia succulenta, commonly known as the succulent lobe, is a striking plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its succulent nature allows it to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.

Lobelia succulenta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
