Leucospermum prostratum

Leucospermum prostratum, commonly known as the coastal starburry, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging coastal conditions.
