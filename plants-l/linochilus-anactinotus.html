Linochilus anactinotus

Linochilus anactinotus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude grasslands of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette, which helps it conserve moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Linochilus anactinotus is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand temperature fluctuations typical of its native environment.
