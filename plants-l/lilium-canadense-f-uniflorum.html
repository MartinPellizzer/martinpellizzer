Lilium canadense f. uniflorum

Lilium canadense f. uniflorum, commonly known as the Canadian white lily, is a botanical variant of the Canadian lily.

It is characterized by its single, large, white flower that blooms in late summer. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
