Liparis tschangii

Liparis tschangii, commonly known as the Tschang's orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces a single, upright stem bearing several lance-shaped leaves. The plant flowers in spring, producing small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
