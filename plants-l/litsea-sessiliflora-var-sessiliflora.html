Litsea sessiliflora var. sessiliflora

Litsea sessiliflora var. sessiliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved litsea, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
