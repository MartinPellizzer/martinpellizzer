Liparis odorata var. intacta

Liparis odorata var. intacta, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
