Lilium-convallium majale

Lilium-convallium majale, commonly known as the giant lily, is a striking plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is native to certain regions of Asia. The plant features tall, sturdy stems that can reach several meters in height, supporting its impressive blooms. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

The flowers, which can be white, pink, or red, are known for their strong fragrance and are often used in ornamental gardening.
