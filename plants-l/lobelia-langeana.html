Lobelia langeana

Lobelia langeana, commonly known as the Lange's lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia langeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ecological benefits.
