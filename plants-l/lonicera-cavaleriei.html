Lonicera cavaleriei

Lonicera cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring. Its fruits are red berries that mature in the fall and are edible for birds.

Lonicera cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
