Lysimachia munroi

Lysimachia munroi, commonly known as the golden thread, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia munroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
