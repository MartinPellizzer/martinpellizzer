Lavatera cachemiriana var. haroonii

Lavatera cachemiriana var. haroonii, commonly known as Kashmiri Lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Kashmir region of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and broad, palmate leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to white flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
