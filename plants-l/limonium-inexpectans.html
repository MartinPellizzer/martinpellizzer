Limonium inexpectans

Limonium inexpectans, commonly known as the coastal flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. This species thrives in coastal environments, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. Its salt tolerance makes it well adapted to harsh maritime conditions.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
