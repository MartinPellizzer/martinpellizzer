Limonium taenari

Limonium taenari, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its distinctive spikes of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in sandy or salty soils. Limonium taenari is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions.

Its ability to tolerate salt and wind makes it a hardy choice for coastal gardens.
