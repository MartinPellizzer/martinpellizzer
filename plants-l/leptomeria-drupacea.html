Leptomeria drupacea

Leptomeria drupacea, commonly known as the red sandstone cactus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Leptomeria drupacea is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
