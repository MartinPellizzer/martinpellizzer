Lychnophora staavioides

Lychnophora staavioides, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, star-shaped fruits. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
