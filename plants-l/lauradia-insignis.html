Lauradia insignis

Lauradia insignis, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of yellowish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Lauradia insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
