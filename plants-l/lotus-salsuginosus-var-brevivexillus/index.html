Lotus Salsuginosus Var. Brevivexillus

Lotus salsuginosus var. brevivexillus is a rare and resilient plant adapted to harsh, saline environments. It thrives in coastal areas where other plants struggle to survive. This variant of the lotus is known for its compact growth and unique leaf structure. Its ability to tolerate salt and extreme weather makes it an important subject for ecological studies. Despite its hardiness, it remains a little-known species in the botanical world.
