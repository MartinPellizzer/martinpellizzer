Leucocera pterocalyx

Leucocera pterocalyx, commonly known as the white-flowered pterocalyx, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its typical legume-like flowers and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with pinnate arrangement and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate floral display.
