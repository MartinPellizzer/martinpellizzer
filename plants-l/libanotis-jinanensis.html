Libanotis jinanensis

Libanotis jinanensis, commonly known as the Jinan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has lanceolate leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
