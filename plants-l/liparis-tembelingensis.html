Liparis tembelingensis

Liparis tembelingensis, commonly known as the Tembeling orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
