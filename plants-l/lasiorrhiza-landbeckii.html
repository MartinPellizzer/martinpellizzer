Lasiorrhiza landbeckii

Lasiorrhiza landbeckii, commonly known as the landbeck's beardtongue, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and showy, tubular flowers.
