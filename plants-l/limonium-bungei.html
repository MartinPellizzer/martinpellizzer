Limonium bungei

Limonium bungei, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal meadows. Limonium bungei is a perennial herb that blooms in summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners interested in coastal or drought-tolerant landscaping.
