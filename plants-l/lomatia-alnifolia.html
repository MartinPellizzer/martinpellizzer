Lomatia alnifolia

Lomatia alnifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lomatia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically narrow and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Lomatia alnifolia is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
