Lindsaea lancea var. quadrangularis

Lindsaea lancea var. quadrangularis, commonly known as the quadrangular lindsaea, is a species of fern indigenous to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its slender, often branched rhizomes and delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically green and have a distinctive quadrangular cross-section, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
