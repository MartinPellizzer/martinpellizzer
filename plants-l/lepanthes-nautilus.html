Lepanthes nautilus

Lepanthes nautilus, commonly known as the nautilus orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as cloud forests. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with intricate patterns that mimic the shape of a nautilus shell.

Lepanthes nautilus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
