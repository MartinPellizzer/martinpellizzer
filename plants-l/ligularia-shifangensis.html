Ligularia shifangensis

Ligularia shifangensis, commonly known as the Shifang ligularia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and lesser-known species within the Ligularia genus.
