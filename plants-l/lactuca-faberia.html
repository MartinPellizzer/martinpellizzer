Lactuca faberia

Lactuca faberia, commonly known as wild endive, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant features dark green, curly leaves that are often used in salads or cooked dishes. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
