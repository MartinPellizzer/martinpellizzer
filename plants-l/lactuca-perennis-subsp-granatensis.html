Lactuca perennis subsp. granatensis

Lactuca perennis subsp.

granatensis, commonly known as the granatense lettuce, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and flowering heads.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a compact cluster. It has a long growing season and is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter taste.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in traditional dishes.
