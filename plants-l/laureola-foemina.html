Laureola foemina

Laureola foemina, commonly known as the fairy laurel, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by red berries in the autumn. Laureola foemina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and year-round visual appeal.
