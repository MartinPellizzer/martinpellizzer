Lippia lindmanii f. oppositifolia

Lippia lindmanii f. oppositifolia, commonly known as the opposite-leaf lippia, is a flowering plant in the Verbenaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite leaf arrangement, which gives it its specific epithet. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
