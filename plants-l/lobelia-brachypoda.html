Lobelia brachypoda

Lobelia brachypoda, commonly known as narrowleaf lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia brachypoda thrives in moist, woodland environments and is often found in shaded, well-drained soils.
