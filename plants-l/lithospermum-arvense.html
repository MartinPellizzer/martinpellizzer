Lithospermum arvense

Lithospermum arvense, commonly known as field lithosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Eurasia.

It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its roots contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

Lithospermum arvense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
