Licuala parvula

Licuala parvula, commonly known as the dwarf palm or small-leaf palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, the Philippines, and Malaysia. The plant features a short, unbranched trunk and is characterized by its dense, fan-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. Its small size makes it suitable for indoor cultivation and ornamental use.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark blue or black berries.
