Lysimachia Japonica F. Subsessilis

Japanese primrose, scientifically known as Lysimachia japonica f. subsessilis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to Japan. This unique variety of the Japanese primrose is prized for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. The plant's compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for small gardens or container planting. Its delicate foliage and showy blooms make it a popular ornamental plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
