Laelia kautskyi

Laelia kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's laelia, is a species of orchid native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces strikingly colored flowers with a complex structure. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting patterns. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires good air circulation and moderate light.

Laelia kautskyi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
