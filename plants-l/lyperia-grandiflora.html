Lyperia grandiflora

Lyperia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered lyperia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with prominent yellow stamens. The plant grows up to 1 meter tall, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland habitats.

Lyperia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
