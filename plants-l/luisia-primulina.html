Luisia primulina

Luisia primulina, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, which gives it its common name. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a red or orange throat.

Luisia primulina is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
