Lysiana filifolia

Lysiana filifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a soft, lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays for its aesthetic appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is valued for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
