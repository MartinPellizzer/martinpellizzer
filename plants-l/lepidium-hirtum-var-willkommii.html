Lepidium hirtum var. willkommii

Lepidium hirtum var. willkommii, commonly known as the hairy cress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along riverbanks and in meadows. It has hairy leaves and stems, which give it its common name.
