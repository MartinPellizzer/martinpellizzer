Limnobium spongia subsp. laevigatum

Limnobium spongia subsp.

laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth spongeplant, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its spongy, floating leaves that are typically smooth and rounded.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often submerged or floating. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its unique structure allows it to absorb nutrients efficiently from the water.
