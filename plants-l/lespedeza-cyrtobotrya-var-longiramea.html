Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. longiramea

Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. longiramea, commonly known as the slender lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and soil stability.
