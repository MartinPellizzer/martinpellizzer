Lantana × pohliana

Lantana × pohliana, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically orange or red when mature. Lantana × pohliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, posing a threat to native ecosystems.
