Lithops kunjasensis

Lithops kunjasensis, commonly known as the "living stone," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance, with fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Lithops kunjasensis is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its unique aesthetic and low maintenance requirements.
