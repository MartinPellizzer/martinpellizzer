Litsea ferruginea var. annamensis

Litsea ferruginea var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture and a distinctive reddish-brown underside. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue, glabrous berries.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical and subtropical forests.
