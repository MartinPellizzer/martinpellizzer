Ligularia trigonophylla

Ligularia trigonophylla, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in the summer, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
