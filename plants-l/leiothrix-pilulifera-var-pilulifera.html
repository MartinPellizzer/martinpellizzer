Leiothrix pilulifera var. pilulifera

Leiothrix pilulifera var. pilulifera, commonly known as the red-flowered spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a unique, spider-like appearance of its flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated by specific insect species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
