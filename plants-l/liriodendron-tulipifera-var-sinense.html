Liriodendron tulipifera var. sinense

Liriodendron tulipifera var. sinense, commonly known as the Chinese tulip tree, is a variant of the tulip tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tulip-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and turn yellow in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in moist, temperate climates.

This variant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
