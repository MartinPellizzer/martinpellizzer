Lycaste deppei var. major

Lycaste deppei var. major, commonly known as the large-leafed lycaste, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting lip. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
