Lobelia laevigata

Lobelia laevigata, commonly known as smooth lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
