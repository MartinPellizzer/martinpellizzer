Ligularia melanocephala

Ligularia melanocephala, commonly known as the black-headed ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with dark purple centers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add texture and color to shaded gardens.
