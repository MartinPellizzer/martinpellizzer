Lantana suaveolens

Lantana suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in a variety of habitats including woodlands and disturbed soils.

Lantana suaveolens is known for its strong, pleasant fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
