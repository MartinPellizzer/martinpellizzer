Lindera megaphylla f. touyunensis

Lindera megaphylla f. touyunensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Lindera, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Lindera genus.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It is native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
