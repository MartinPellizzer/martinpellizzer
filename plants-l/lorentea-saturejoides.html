Lorentea saturejoides

Lorentea saturejoides, commonly known as the Indian balm or Indian lungwort, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of violet or purple, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons.
