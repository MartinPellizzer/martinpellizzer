Lobostemon latifolius

Lobostemon latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Lobostemon latifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
