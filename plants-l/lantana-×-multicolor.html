Lantana × multicolor

Lantana × multicolor, commonly known as the rainbow lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and purple. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lantana × multicolor is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
