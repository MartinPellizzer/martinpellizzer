Lathyrus nitens

Lathyrus nitens, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in racemes, and bloom in early summer.

Lathyrus nitens is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
