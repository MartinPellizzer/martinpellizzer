Laricorchis spathulata

Laricorchis spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf pine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within pine forests. It has a short rhizome and develops a single, erect stem that supports its floral display.

Laricorchis spathulata is a rare and specialized orchid, often found in association with pine trees.
