Litsea wallichii

Litsea wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically orange to red in color, containing several seeds. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in temperate forests at high elevations.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
