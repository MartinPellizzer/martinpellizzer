Leucas stenophylla

Leucas stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers. It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

Leucas stenophylla is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
