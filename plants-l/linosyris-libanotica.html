Linosyris libanotica

Linosyris libanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Linosyris libanotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
