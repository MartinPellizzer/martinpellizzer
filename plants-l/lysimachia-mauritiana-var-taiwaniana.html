Lysimachia mauritiana var. taiwaniana

Lysimachia mauritiana var. taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan yellow waterplantain, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors.

Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.
