Liabum ovatifolium

Liabum ovatifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf liabum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its ovate, glossy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
