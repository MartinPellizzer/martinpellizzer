Leucas nyassae var. velutina

Leucas nyassae var. velutina, commonly known as velvet leucas, is a perennial herb native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that give it a velvety texture, hence its common name. It produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem.

This species is often found in moist habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.
