Lophozonia cunninghamii

Lophozonia cunninghamii, commonly known as the Cunningham's lichen, is a species of crustose lichen found primarily in Australia.

It belongs to the family Parmeliaceae and is characterized by its flat, crust-like growth form. The thallus is typically pale yellow to orange in color, with a granular texture. It thrives on bark and rock surfaces in moist, shaded environments.
