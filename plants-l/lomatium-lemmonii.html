Lomatium lemmonii

Lomatium lemmonii, commonly known as Lemmon's lomatium, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and umbellate flowers.

It is often found in sagebrush steppe and rocky areas, and its growth is typically slow and persistent.

Lomatium lemmonii is valued for its ecological role and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
