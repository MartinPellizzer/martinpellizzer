Linczevskia sinuata

Linczevskia sinuata, commonly known as the Sinuous Linczevskia, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, sinuous leaves that curve and twist in a unique pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, temperate climate.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
