Lepismium lorentzianum

Lepismium lorentzianum, commonly known as the Lorentzian torch cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and clusters of bright orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has a spiny exterior and is adapted to arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and blooming habits make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
