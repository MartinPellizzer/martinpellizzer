Lasianthus congesticymus

Lasianthus congesticymus, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of showy, tubular red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, silky texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant floral display.
