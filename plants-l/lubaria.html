Lubaria

Lubaria, commonly known as the common lubaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas. The plant features slender, branching stems with small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in umbels. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance.

Lubaria is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
