Luxemburgia flexuosa

Luxemburgia flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible luxemburgia, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features slender, flexible stems that allow it to cascade gracefully from containers or hanging baskets. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Luxemburgia flexuosa thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
