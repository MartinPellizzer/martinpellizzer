Lycoris tsinlingensis

Lycoris tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that emerge from a basal rosette and are often covered in a waxy coating. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help the plant survive through adverse conditions.

The Tsingling lily is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
