Lactuca cyanea

Lactuca cyanea, commonly known as the blue lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, open habitats. The plant is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
