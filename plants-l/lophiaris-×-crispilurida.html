Lophiaris × crispilurida

Lophiaris × crispilurida, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and striking yellow-orange flowers. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a rosette of leaves that form a dense, circular pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
