Lithocardium ellipticum

Lithocardium ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptical stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, elliptical leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Lithocardium ellipticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
