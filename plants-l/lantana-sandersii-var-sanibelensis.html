Lantana sandersii var. sanibelensis

Lantana sandersii var. sanibelensis, commonly known as Sanibel lantana, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Florida Keys.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming throughout the year. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in coastal environments and is well-adapted to salty air and sandy soils.

Sanibel lantana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
