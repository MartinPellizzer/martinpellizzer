Lantana rubra

Lantana rubra, commonly known as red lantana, is a flowering shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to red, depending on the variety. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Lantana rubra is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

It also attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies due to its nectar-rich blooms.
