Licuala miriensis

Licuala miriensis, commonly known as the golden palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, with a distinctive golden hue on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are not only ornamental but also have traditional uses in local cultures.

Licuala miriensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
