Lawrencia viridigrisea

Lawrencia viridigrisea, commonly known as the green-gray liverwort, is a non-vascular plant belonging to the family Marchantiaceae.

It is characterized by its thalloid structure, which is typically greenish-gray in color and forms a dense, carpet-like growth. This liverwort thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or soil in temperate regions. Its reproductive structures include sporogonies that produce spores, contributing to its propagation.

The plant plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitat for various small organisms.
