Lithops fulviceps var. laevigata

Lithops fulviceps var. laevigata, commonly known as the Cape Town lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pair of fleshy, leaf-like structures that resemble stones, helping it blend into its arid environment. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It requires minimal water and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
