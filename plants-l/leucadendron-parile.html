Leucadendron parile

Leucadendron parile, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces striking cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

The silver tree is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
