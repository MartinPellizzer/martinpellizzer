Lasianthus trichophlebus var. simizui

Lasianthus trichophlebus var. simizui, commonly known as the Guangxi lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, dry fruits.
