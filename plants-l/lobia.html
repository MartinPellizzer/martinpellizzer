Lobia

Lobia, commonly known as the cowpea or *Vigna unguiculata*, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and often used in various cuisines. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. Lobia is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable crop for sustainable agriculture.

It thrives in warm climates and is cultivated for both its seeds and foliage.
