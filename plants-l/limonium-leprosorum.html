Limonium leprosorum

Limonium leprosorum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and gray-green, silver-haired foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium leprosorum is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
