Lithospermum hispidulum var. cyrenaicum

Lithospermum hispidulum var. cyrenaicum, commonly known as the Cyrenaic lithospermum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and small, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variant is part of the Boraginaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental uses.
