Lobelia cubana

Lobelia cubana, commonly known as Cuban lobelia, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia cubana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
