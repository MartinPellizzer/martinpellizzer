Lockhartia lankesteri

Lockhartia lankesteri, commonly known as the Orchid of the Andes, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer or early autumn, producing blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often grown as a greenhouse or indoor plant due to its specific care requirements.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
