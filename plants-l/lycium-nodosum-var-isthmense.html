Lycium nodosum var. isthmense

Lycium nodosum var. isthmense, commonly known as the Isthmus boxthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark red berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

This variety is closely related to other Lycium species, including the common wolfberry.
