Lastrea prenticei

Lastrea prenticei, commonly known as the Prentice's lastrea, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the warmer months. Its slow growth rate and ability to survive in poor soil conditions make it a resilient species.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant.
