Laelia longipes

Laelia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed laelia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Laelia longipes is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
