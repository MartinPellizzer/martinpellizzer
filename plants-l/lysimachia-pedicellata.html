Lysimachia pedicellata

Lysimachia pedicellata, commonly known as golden thread, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Lysimachia pedicellata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
