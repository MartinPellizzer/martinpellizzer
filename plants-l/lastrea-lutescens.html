Lastrea lutescens

Lastrea lutescens, commonly known as the yellow lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique shape and color.
