Lysimachia qimenensis

Lysimachia qimenensis, commonly known as the Qimen yellow flower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers and trailing stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia qimenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
