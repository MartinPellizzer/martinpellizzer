Lepidium heterophyllum var. medium

Lepidium heterophyllum var. medium, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is characterized by its branched, cylindrical stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
