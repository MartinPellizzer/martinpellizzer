Lathyrus nissolia var. glabrescens

Lathyrus nissolia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-flowered grass violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender stems and small, violet or purple flowers. The leaves are typically alternate and have a lanceolate shape, while the plant produces pods that contain several seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.
