Laelia anceps var. hyeana

Laelia anceps var. hyeana, commonly known as the Hyeana Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant blossoms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
