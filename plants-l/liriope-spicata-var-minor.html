Liriope spicata var. minor

Liriope spicata var. minor, commonly known as the narrowleaf lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and grow in dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of white or pale blue flowers in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Liriope spicata var. minor is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
