Lippia reptans

Lippia reptans, commonly known as creeping lippia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong aromatic scent.

Lippia reptans is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and along stream banks.
