Leucas megasphaera

Leucas megasphaera, commonly known as the globe thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

The botanical features of Leucas megasphaera make it well-suited for rock gardens and dry, sunny environments.
