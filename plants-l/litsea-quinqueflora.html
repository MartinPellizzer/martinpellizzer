Litsea quinqueflora

Litsea quinqueflora, commonly known as five-flowered litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as India, China, and Indonesia. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a small, dark-colored drupe that contains a single seed, making it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
