Lampranthus brachyandrus

Lampranthus brachyandrus, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Lampranthus brachyandrus is also known for its ability to produce a profusion of flowers during the winter months.
