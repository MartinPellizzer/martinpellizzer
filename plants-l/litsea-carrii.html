Litsea carrii

Litsea carrii, commonly known as the Carrii leaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, black berries that are a food source for various animals.

Litsea carrii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
