Lygodium flexuosum var. altum

Lygodium flexuosum var. altum, commonly known as the tall climbing fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, flexible fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swamps. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores for propagation.

The fronds are often used in traditional medicine and horticulture due to their ornamental value.
