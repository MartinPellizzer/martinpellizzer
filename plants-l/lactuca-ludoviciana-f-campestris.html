Lactuca ludoviciana f. campestris

Lactuca ludoviciana f. campestris, commonly known as the wild lettuce or field lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves that are typically lobed or toothed. The plant produces yellow flowers in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to North America and thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This species is often found in fields, roadsides, and along stream banks.
