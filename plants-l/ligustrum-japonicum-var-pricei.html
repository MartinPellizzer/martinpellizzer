Ligustrum japonicum var. pricei

Ligustrum japonicum var. pricei, commonly known as Japanese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
