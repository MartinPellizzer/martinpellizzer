Liparis taiwaniana

Liparis taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, fleshy rhizome and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base, while its flowers are borne on short pedicels.

Liparis taiwaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
