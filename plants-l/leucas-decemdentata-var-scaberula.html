Leucas decemdentata var. scaberula

Leucas decemdentata var. scaberula, commonly known as rough leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, rough stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
