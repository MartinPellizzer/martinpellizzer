Lindera tienchuanensis

Lindera tienchuanensis, commonly known as the Tienchuan ligustrum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that matures in the fall.

Lindera tienchuanensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
