Lunaria odorata

Lunaria odorata, commonly known as the holy moon or moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces striking, spherical seed pods that turn a deep blue as they mature, giving it its common name. These pods are often used in floral arrangements due to their unique appearance.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral displays.
