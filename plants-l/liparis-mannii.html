Liparis mannii

Liparis mannii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme, and have a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

Liparis mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
