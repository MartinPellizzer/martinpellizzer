Leontodon kotschyi

Leontodon kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi's hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate to cold climates.
