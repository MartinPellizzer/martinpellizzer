Lonicera dioica var. dasygyna

Lonicera dioica var. dasygyna, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late spring. Its berries are red and edible, often attracting birds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
