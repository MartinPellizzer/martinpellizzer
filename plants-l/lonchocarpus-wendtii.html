Lonchocarpus wendtii

Lonchocarpus wendtii, commonly known as the yellow snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume-like pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is notable for its striking yellow flowers and its use in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
