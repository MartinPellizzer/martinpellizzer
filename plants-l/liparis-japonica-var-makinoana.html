Liparis japonica var. makinoana

Liparis japonica var. makinoana, commonly known as the Japanese twayblade, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and limited distribution.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
