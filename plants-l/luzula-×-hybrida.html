Luzula × hybrida

Luzula × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid goat's beard, is a perennial plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Luzula sylvatica and Luzula nivea, exhibiting characteristics of both parents. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and panicle-shaped inflorescences that bear small, white to greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Luzula × hybrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
