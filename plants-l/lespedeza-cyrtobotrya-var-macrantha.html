Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. macrantha

Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.
