Lespedeza maximowiczii var. maximowiczii

Lespedeza maximowiczii var. maximowiczii, commonly known as Chinese strawsedge, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and marshy areas. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant has a fibrous root system.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
