Lathyrus clymenoides

Lathyrus clymenoides, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its climbing habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Lathyrus clymenoides is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant colors and pleasant scent.
