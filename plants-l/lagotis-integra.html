Lagotis integra

Lagotis integra, commonly known as the smooth lagotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Lagotis integra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
