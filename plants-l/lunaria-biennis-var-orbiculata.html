Lunaria biennis var. orbiculata

Lunaria biennis var. orbiculata, commonly known as the moonflower or silver dollar plant, is a biennial species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical seed pods that turn a striking blue-gray color when mature. The plant produces small, white, cruciform flowers that bloom in the early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
