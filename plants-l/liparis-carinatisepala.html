Liparis carinatisepala

Liparis carinatisepala, commonly known as the ridge-sepaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent ridges on the sepals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a notable species in orchid botany.
