Lepanthes matamorosii

Lepanthes matamorosii, commonly known as the Matamoros orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, fragrant blooms during the spring.

Lepanthes matamorosii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
