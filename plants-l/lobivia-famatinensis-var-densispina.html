Lobivia famatinensis var. densispina

Lobivia famatinensis var. densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that often remains close to the ground. The plant produces clusters of fleshy, spiny leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and appear in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.
