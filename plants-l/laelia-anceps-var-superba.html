Laelia anceps var. superba

Laelia anceps var. superba, commonly known as the Superba Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, robust growth and large, showy flowers that can be white, pink, or purple. The plant typically produces 3-5 blooms per stem, with petals that are often ruffled or curled. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
