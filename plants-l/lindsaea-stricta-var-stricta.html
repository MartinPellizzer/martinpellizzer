Lindsaea stricta var. stricta

Lindsaea stricta var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with lanceolate to ovate leaflets, often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks in the understory of tropical forests.

Lindsaea stricta var. stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
