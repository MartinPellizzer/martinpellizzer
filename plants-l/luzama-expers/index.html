Luzama Expers

Luzama expers is a rare and enigmatic plant known for its unique bioluminescent properties. Found primarily in the misty forests of the Andes, it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves emit a soft blue glow at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied by botanists due to its elusive nature. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this fragile species from habitat loss.
