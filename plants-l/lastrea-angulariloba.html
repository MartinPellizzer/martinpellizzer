Lastrea angulariloba

Lastrea angulariloba, commonly known as the angular-leaved lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically angular in shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
