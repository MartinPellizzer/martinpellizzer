Lotodes plumosum

Lotodes plumosum, commonly known as the silky water fern, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that float on the water surface. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread along the water's edge or within shallow waters. Its leaves are typically light green and have a soft, silky texture.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
