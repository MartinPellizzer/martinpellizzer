Laelia × felix

Laelia × felix, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
