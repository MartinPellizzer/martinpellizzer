Lindsaea fumarioides

Lindsaea fumarioides, commonly known as the flame fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that often display a reddish or orange hue. Lindsaea fumarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
