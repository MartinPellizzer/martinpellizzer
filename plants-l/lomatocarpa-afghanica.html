Lomatocarpa afghanica

Lomatocarpa afghanica, commonly known as Afghan lomatia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
