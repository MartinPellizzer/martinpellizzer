Ludwigia pubescens var. macropoda

Ludwigia pubescens var. macropoda, commonly known as the giant Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or bronze in color, especially under bright light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, preferring warm, nutrient-rich waters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
