Lepisanthes divaricata f. divaricata

Lepisanthes divaricata f. divaricata, commonly known as the variegated orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
