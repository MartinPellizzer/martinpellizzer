Lepidagathis plantaginea

Lepidagathis plantaginea, commonly known as the small-leaved sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm season.
