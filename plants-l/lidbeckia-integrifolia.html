Lidbeckia integrifolia

Lidbeckia integrifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have smooth, entire margins. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The plant is known for its long blooming period, typically from spring to early autumn.
