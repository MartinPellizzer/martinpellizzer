Lotus debilis

Lotus debilis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water lily native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom on long, buoyant stalks. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of still water. Its roots system helps stabilize the soil and improve water quality.

Lotus debilis is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and holds cultural significance in many Asian traditions.
