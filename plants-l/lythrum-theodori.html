Lythrum theodori

Lythrum theodori, commonly known as the Theodore's lythrum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as the poinsettia. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its showy, pink to purple flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in late summer.

Lythrum theodori is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
