Laelia × elegans var. alba

Laelia × elegans var. alba, commonly known as the White Laelia, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits striking white blooms.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to high-altitude regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and long-lasting, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for its showy white petals.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and adaptability to cultivation.
