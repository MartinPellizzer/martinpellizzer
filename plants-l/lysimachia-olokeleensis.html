Lysimachia olokeleensis

Lysimachia olokeleensis, commonly known as the Olokele knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Oʻahu.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 15–30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale green, with five petals and numerous stamens. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lysimachia olokeleensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
