Leptolaena diospyroidea

Leptolaena diospyroidea, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often glandular-dotted. Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agroforestry systems.
