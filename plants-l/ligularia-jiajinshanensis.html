Ligularia jiajinshanensis

Ligularia jiajinshanensis, commonly known as the Jiajinshan ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flower heads in the summer, which are surrounded by broad, green bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with rich, humusy soil.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is considered a valuable species in botanical research and conservation efforts.
