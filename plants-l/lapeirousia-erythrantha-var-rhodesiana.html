Lapeirousia erythrantha var. rhodesiana

Lapeirousia erythrantha var. rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodesian red orchid, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a striking yellow center, which make it highly ornamental. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of flowers, often blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a fan shape.

This variant is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
