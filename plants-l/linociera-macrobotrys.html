Linociera macrobotrys

Linociera macrobotrys, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has a fibrous root system and can grow as a tree or shrub, depending on environmental conditions. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated margin.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
