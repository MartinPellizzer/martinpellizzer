Lipochaeta australis var. lobata

Lipochaeta australis var. lobata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lipochaeta, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pink to red flowers clustered in spikes. It is adapted to tolerate saline and arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for native insects.
