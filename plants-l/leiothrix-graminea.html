Leiothrix graminea

Leiothrix graminea, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered at the base of the leaves.

Leiothrix graminea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
