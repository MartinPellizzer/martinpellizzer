Lomariopsis oleandrifolia

Lomariopsis oleandrifolia, commonly known as the flame fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red fronds that resemble flames, giving it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, serrated leaflets.

Lomariopsis oleandrifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
