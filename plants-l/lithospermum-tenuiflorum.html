Lithospermum tenuiflorum

Lithospermum tenuiflorum, commonly known as narrowflower rock pepper, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
