Lantana × bahamensis f. canescens

Lantana × bahamensis f. canescens, commonly known as the Bahamian lantana, is a hybrid shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
