Licuala elegans

Licuala elegans, commonly known as the elegant palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The leaves are typically bright green and have a smooth, waxy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in gardens.
