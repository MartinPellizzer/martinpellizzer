Ligularia tussilaginea var. formosana

Ligularia tussilaginea var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Taiwan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with prominent veins and a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and valuable species in the Asteraceae family.
