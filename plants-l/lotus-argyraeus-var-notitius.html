Lotus argyraeus var. notitius

Lotus argyraeus var. notitius, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer, typically in clusters at the top of its slender stems. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
