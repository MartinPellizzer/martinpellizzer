Lycaste × cobani var. longibracteata

Lycaste × cobani var. longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
