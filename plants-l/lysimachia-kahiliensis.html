Lysimachia kahiliensis

Lysimachia kahiliensis, commonly known as the New Zealand chickweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing herb with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Lysimachia kahiliensis is often found in the understorey of native forests and is a characteristic component of the New Zealand flora.
