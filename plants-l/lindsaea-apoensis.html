Lindsaea apoensis

Lindsaea apoensis, commonly known as the Apo fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are wiry and often form dense clusters, allowing it to thrive in forest canopies.

Lindsaea apoensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its habitat.
