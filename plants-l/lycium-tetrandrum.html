Lycium tetrandrum

Lycium tetrandrum, commonly known as Indian wolfberry or Chinese boxthorn, is a shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange berries that are edible when ripe. The plant has spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Botanically, it belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants.
