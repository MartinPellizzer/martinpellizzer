Litsea dielsii

Litsea dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese wild laurier.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. Its leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and have a fragrant scent, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Litsea dielsii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
