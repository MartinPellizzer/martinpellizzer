Lithospermum fruticosum var. micranthum

Lithospermum fruticosum var. micranthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered rock pepper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. The plant's leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
