Ligularia stenocephala var. stenocephala

Ligularia stenocephala var. stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of East Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive yellowish-white margin. The plant produces tall, slender stems topped with clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
