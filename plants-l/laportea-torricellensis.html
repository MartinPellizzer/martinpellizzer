Laportea torricellensis

Laportea torricellensis, commonly known as the Caribbean blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense clusters.

Laportea torricellensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
