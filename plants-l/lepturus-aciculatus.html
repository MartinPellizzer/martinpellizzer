Lepturus aciculatus

Lepturus aciculatus, commonly known as the slender leaved lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a pale green to yellowish thallus with a distinctive, finely branched structure. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provides photosynthetic capabilities. It is often found on tree trunks and rocks in temperate regions.

Its slow growth and sensitivity to air pollution make it an indicator species for environmental health.
