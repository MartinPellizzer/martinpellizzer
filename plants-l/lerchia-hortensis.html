Lerchia hortensis

Lerchia hortensis, commonly known as garden lerchia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Lerchia hortensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
