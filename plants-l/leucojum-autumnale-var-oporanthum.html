Leucojum autumnale var. oporanthum

Leucojum autumnale var. oporanthum, commonly known as the autumn snowflake, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
