Liparis eustachys

Liparis eustachys, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces a single, slender flowering stem. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers with three sepals and three petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis eustachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
