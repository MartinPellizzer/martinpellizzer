Ladenbergia dichotoma var. klotzschiana

Ladenbergia dichotoma var. klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzschiana lily, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are compound, with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
