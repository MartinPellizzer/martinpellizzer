Landerolaria

Landerolaria, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, spiny leaves. Landerolaria is often found in maritime climates, adapting to salty air and strong coastal winds.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and floral arrangements.
