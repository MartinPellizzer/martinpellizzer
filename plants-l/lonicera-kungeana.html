Lonicera kungeana

Lonicera kungeana, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals. Lonicera kungeana typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 feet and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
