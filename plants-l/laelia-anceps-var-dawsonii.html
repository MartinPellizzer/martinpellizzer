Laelia anceps var. dawsonii

Laelia anceps var. dawsonii, commonly known as Dawson's laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high altitudes of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored blooms that can range from yellow to orange and red. It thrives in cool, well-drained conditions and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
