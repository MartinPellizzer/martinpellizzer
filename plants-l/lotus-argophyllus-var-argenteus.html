Lotus argophyllus var. argenteus

Lotus argophyllus var. argenteus, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking white and pink flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery-white coating, giving it its common name. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in ponds and wetlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and botanical collections.
