Lomatia chilensis

Lomatia chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean lomatia, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Chile.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its distinctive, often branched stems. Lomatia chilensis is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
