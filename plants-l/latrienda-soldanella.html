Latrienda soldanella

Latrienda soldanella, commonly known as the Snowy Larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and grow in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Latrienda soldanella is also known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
