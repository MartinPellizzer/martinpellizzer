Litsea panamanja

Litsea panamanja, commonly known as Panama lime or Panama lemon, is a small evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like avocado and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its fruit is a small, round citrus-like berry with a thin, leathery skin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited commercial use in certain regions.
