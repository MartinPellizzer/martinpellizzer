Lobivia haagei var. pelzliana

Lobivia haagei var. pelzliana, commonly known as the Pelzliana Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a subtle bluish or grayish tinge, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its unique leaf structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
