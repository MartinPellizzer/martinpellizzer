Lactucopsis mulgedioides

Lactucopsis mulgedioides, commonly known as the mountain lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Lactucopsis mulgedioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
