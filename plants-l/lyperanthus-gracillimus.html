Lyperanthus gracillimus

Lyperanthus gracillimus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are often clustered in a spike. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in grassland habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
