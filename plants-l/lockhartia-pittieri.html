Lockhartia pittieri

Lockhartia pittieri, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces dense clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lockhartia pittieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
