Leucanthemum pallens var. subpinnatifidum

Leucanthemum pallens var. subpinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved oxeye daisy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy white ray flowers surrounded by a yellow disc, typical of the Asteraceae family. The leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, finely divided appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
