Leonotis martinicensis

Leonotis martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features tubular, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
