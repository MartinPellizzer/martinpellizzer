Lithospermum benthamii

Lithospermum benthamii, commonly known as the Bentham's rock pepper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, followed by elliptical, dark purple to black berries. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
