Lotus tenuifolius var. glabriusculus

Lotus tenuifolius var. glabriusculus, commonly known as the smooth-leaf water lotus, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, smooth leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom above the water. Its roots are anchored in the muddy substrate, and it thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional use in herbal medicine.
