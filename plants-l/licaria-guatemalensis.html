Licaria guatemalensis

Licaria guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan licaria, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or white. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in moist forest understories.
