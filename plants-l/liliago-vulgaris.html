Liliago vulgaris

Liliago vulgaris, commonly known as the common lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions.

It features upright, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Liliago vulgaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
