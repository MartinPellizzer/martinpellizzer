Leucopsis calvata

Leucopsis calvata, commonly known as the smooth leucopsis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth texture.

Leucopsis calvata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
