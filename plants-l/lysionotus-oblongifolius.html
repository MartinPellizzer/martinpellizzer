Lysionotus oblongifolius

Lysionotus oblongifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed lythrum, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in spikes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lysionotus oblongifolius is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
