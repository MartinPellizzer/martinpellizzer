Lathyrus ketzkhovelii

Lathyrus ketzkhovelii, commonly known as the Ketzkhovel vetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Its flowers are usually purple or violet in color, with a distinctive spurred structure at the base of the petals.

Lathyrus ketzkhovelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional herbal medicine in some regions.
