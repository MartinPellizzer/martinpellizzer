Lathyrus parodii var. parodii

Lathyrus parodii var. parodii, commonly known as the Parodii pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue to purple pea-shaped flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a climbing or trailing growth habit.
