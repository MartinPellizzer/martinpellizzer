Lavatera maritima var. incana

Lavatera maritima var. incana, commonly known as the coastal lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. It is often found in dunes and rocky habitats along the shoreline.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
