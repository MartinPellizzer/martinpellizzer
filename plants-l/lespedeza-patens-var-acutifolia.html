Lespedeza patens var. acutifolia

Lespedeza patens var. acutifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, trifoliate leaves that are lance-shaped and have a pointed tip. Its flowers are usually pink to reddish in color, appearing in late summer to early fall.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.
