Lorenzanea glabrata

Lorenzanea glabrata, commonly known as the smooth lorenzanea, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy in texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
