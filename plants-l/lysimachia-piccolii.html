Lysimachia piccolii

Lysimachia piccolii, commonly known as the Italian violet or small yellow archangel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy hairs, and produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are typically oval or lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
