Linaria spartea f. glandulosa

Linaria spartea f. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular linaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant exhibits glandular structures on its leaves and stems, which are responsible for producing sticky secretions. It is native to regions of southern Europe and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate, cascading growth habit.
