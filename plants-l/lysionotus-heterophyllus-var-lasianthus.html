Lysionotus heterophyllus var. lasianthus

Lysionotus heterophyllus var. lasianthus, commonly known as the broad-leafed starling plant, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers emit a mild fragrance, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
