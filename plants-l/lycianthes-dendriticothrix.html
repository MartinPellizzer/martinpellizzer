Lycianthes dendriticothrix

Lycianthes dendriticothrix, commonly known as the tree morning glory, is a perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive red or purple calyx. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
