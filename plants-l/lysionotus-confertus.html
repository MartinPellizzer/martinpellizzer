Lysionotus confertus

Lysionotus confertus, commonly known as the clustered star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale pink blooms during the late winter to early spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
