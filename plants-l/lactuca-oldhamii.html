Lactuca oldhamii

Lactuca oldhamii, commonly known as Oldham's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to eastern Asia and is characterized by its rosette of dark green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, and it is often found in moist, open habitats. It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in botany and ecology.
