Lagerstroemia yangii

Lagerstroemia yangii, commonly known as the Chinese crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its showy, long-lasting flowers that range from pink to red, appearing in summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Lagerstroemia yangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
