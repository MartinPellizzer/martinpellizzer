Liatris elegans

Liatris elegans, commonly known as the slender blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, spiky flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in late summer and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Liatris elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
