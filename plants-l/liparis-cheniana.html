Liparis cheniana

Liparis cheniana, commonly known as the Chen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, often found in forest understories. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Liparis cheniana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
