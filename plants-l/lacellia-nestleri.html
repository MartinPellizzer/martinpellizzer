Lacellia nestleri

Lacellia nestleri, commonly known as the Mexican rock cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in small, white spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its thick, water-storing stems. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or green, bloom during the warmer months.

Lacellia nestleri is valued for its resilience and unique appearance in horticultural collections.
