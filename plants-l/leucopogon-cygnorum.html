Leucopogon cygnorum

Leucopogon cygnorum, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, white pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Leucopogon cygnorum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
