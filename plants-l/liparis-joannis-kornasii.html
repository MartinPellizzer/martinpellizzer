Liparis joannis-kornasii

Liparis joannis-kornasii, commonly known as the Kornasii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing pale green to white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and requires a cool, humid environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
