Ligularia konkalingensis

Ligularia konkalingensis, commonly known as the Konkalingensis Ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
