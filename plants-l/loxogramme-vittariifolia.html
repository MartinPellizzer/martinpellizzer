Loxogramme vittariifolia

Loxogramme vittariifolia, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Marsileaceae.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are divided into four lobes, resembling a clover leaf. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and wetlands. It reproduces through spores and is often found in nutrient-rich waters.
