Lecythis oldemanii

Lecythis oldemanii, commonly known as the golden guarumo, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and produces showy, yellow-orange flowers. The tree is notable for its heavy, hard-shelled fruits that contain edible seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant plays an important ecological role in supporting biodiversity within its native habitat.
