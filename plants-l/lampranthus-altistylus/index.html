Lampranthus Altistylus

Lampranthus altistylus is a striking member of the Aizoaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It is commonly found in rocky soils and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental appeal. The species is characterized by its fleshy leaves and colorful blooms that range from red to orange. Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
