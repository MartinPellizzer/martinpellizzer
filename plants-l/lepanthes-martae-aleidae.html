Lepanthes martae-aleidae

Lepanthes martae-aleidae, commonly known as the Aleidae orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests, where it attaches itself to trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors, often in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

Lepanthes martae-aleidae is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
