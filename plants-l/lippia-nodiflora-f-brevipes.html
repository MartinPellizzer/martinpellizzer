Lippia nodiflora f. brevipes

Lippia nodiflora f. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed lippia, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardens. Its fragrance and compact growth make it a popular choice for container gardening.

It is also valued for its potential use in herbal remedies.
