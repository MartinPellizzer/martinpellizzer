Loudetia demeusei

Loudetia demeusei, commonly known as the African grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a fibrous texture. Its flowering spikes are usually borne at the base of the plant, producing small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
