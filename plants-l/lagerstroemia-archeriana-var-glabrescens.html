Lagerstroemia archeriana var. glabrescens

Lagerstroemia archeriana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, peeling bark and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a favored choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
