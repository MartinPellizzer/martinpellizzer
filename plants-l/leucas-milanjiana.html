Leucas milanjiana

Leucas milanjiana, commonly known as the mountain leucas, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence.

Leucas milanjiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
