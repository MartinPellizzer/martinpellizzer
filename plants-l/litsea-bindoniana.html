Litsea bindoniana

Litsea bindoniana, commonly known as the Madagascar litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical berries that are edible for some bird species.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
