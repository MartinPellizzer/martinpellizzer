Lindera prattii

Lindera prattii, commonly known as Pratts' spicebush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually glossy green on top and lighter beneath. Its small, fragrant flowers are yellow-green and appear in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for birds.

Lindera prattii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and thickets.
