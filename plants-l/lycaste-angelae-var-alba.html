Lycaste angelae var. alba

Lycaste angelae var. alba, commonly known as the White Angel Lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
