Laburnum linneanum

Laburnum linneanum, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Laburnum linneanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and woodland areas.
