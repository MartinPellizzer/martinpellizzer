Lisianthius daturoides

Lisianthius daturoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to red, with a distinctive velvety texture on the petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.
