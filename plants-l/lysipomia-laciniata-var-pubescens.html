Lysipomia laciniata var. pubescens

Lysipomia laciniata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy leaf fern, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, hairy leaves that give it a soft, textured appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as along stream banks or in rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures include small, inconspicuous spore-bearing leaves, typical of ferns.
