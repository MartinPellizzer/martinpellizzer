Leucocoryne praealta

Leucocoryne praealta, commonly known as the tall white leucocoryne, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are typically dark green and slightly curled. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its elegant appearance.
