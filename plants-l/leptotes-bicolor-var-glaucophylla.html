Leptotes bicolor var. glaucophylla

Leptotes bicolor var. glaucophylla, commonly known as the blue-green leptotes, is a variant of the leptotes bicolor species, characterized by its distinctive blue-green foliage.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family, known for its showy flowers and ornamental value. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
