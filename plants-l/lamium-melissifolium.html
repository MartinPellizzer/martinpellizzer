Lamium melissifolium

Lamium melissifolium, commonly known as honeyleaf or lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, aromatic leaves that emit a mild lemon scent when crushed. The plant produces clusters of small, two-lipped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lamium melissifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild medicinal properties.
