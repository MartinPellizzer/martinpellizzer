Lobostemon montanus

Lobostemon montanus, commonly known as the mountain lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, square stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or violet in color and bloom in clusters.

Lobostemon montanus is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
