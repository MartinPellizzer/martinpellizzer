Leonurus alpinus

Leonurus alpinus, commonly known as alpine hedgehog.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers arranged in spikes, and its leaves are typically narrow and hairy. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Leonurus alpinus is often found in alpine meadows and is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae.
