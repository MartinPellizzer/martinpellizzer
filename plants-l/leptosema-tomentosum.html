Leptosema tomentosum

Leptosema tomentosum, commonly known as the hairy leaved orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native habitat.
