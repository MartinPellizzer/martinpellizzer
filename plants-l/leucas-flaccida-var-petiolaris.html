Leucas flaccida var. petiolaris

Leucas flaccida var. petiolaris, commonly known as the slender leucas, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are often tinged with purple. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in whorls along the stem.

It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
