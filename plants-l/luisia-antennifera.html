Luisia antennifera

Luisia antennifera, commonly known as the feather-tailed orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
