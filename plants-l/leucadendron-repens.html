Leucadendron repens

Leucadendron repens, commonly known as the common silver tree, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces bright yellow to orange spherical flower heads that are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

Leucadendron repens is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and resilience.
