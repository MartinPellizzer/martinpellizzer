Lasianthus mucronulatus

Lasianthus mucronulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lasianthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
