Lilium fargesii

Lilium fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing striking pink to red flowers with a white throat. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces bulbous structures that serve as storage organs.

Lilium fargesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
