Ludwigia bullata

Ludwigia bullata, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in shallow water environments. It is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.

Ludwigia bullata is also known for its ability to provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.
