Ligusticum yanyuanense

Ligusticum yanyuanense, commonly known as Yanyuan lovage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Umbelliferae (Apiaceae) family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a robust root system. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and pharmacological research.
