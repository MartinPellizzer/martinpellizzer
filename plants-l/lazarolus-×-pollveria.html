Lazarolus × pollveria

Lazarolus × pollveria, commonly known as the hybrid laurel, is a unique cross between two species of the Lauraceae family.

It exhibits characteristics of both parent plants, featuring glossy, elliptical leaves with a fragrant aroma. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a mild, spicy scent that attracts pollinators.
