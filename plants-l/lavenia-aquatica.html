Lavenia aquatica

Lavenia aquatica, commonly known as water lily, is a aquatic plant that thrives in freshwater environments.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and oxygen to the water below. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are anchored in the muddy substrate at the bottom of ponds or lakes.

Lavenia aquatica plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by supporting biodiversity and improving water quality.
