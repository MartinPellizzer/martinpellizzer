Lotus robsonii

Lotus robsonii, commonly known as the Robson's lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged, while its stems are long and flexible. Lotus robsonii thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in ponds and water gardens.
