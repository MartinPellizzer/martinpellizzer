Lygistum zimapanicum

Lygistum zimapanicum, commonly known as the Zimapan cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spiny cactus that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines, which help to reduce water loss in its arid environment. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in the spring.

The plant is adapted to thrive in rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
