Lepanthes florencia

Lepanthes florencia, commonly known as the Florencia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Lepanthes florencia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
