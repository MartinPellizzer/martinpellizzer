Liparis kumokiri subsp. kumokiri

Liparis kumokiri subsp.

kumokiri, commonly known as the Kumokiri orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
