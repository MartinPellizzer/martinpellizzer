Libanothamnus granatesianus

Libanothamnus granatesianus, commonly known as the red-flowered thamnus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with small, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in late spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
