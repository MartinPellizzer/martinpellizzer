Luckhoffia × beukmannii

Luckhoffia × beukmannii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Luckhoffia and Beukmannia species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This hybrid is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
