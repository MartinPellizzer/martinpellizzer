Litsea hornei

Litsea hornei, commonly known as the Hainan litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by egg-shaped berries that turn red when mature.

Litsea hornei is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
