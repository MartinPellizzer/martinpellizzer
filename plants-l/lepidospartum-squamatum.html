Lepidospartum squamatum

Lepidospartum squamatum, commonly known as silver carpet or rock rose, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine, scale-like leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Lepidospartum squamatum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
