Ladenbergia rubiginosa

Ladenbergia rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-staining legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, red aril, which gives it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has been traditionally used in local medicine for its purported healing properties.
