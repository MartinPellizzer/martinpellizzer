Lophiaris × cicyi

Lophiaris × cicyi, commonly known as the hybrid lophiaris, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically fleshy and green. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring or summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in succulent collections.
