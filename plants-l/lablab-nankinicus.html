Lablab nankinicus

Lablab nankinicus, commonly known as Chinese hyacinth bean, is a climbing legume native to East Asia.

It produces striking violet or white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by pods containing edible seeds. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its vibrant floral display. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Lablab nankinicus is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein in some cuisines.
