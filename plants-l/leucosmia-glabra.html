Leucosmia glabra

Leucosmia glabra, commonly known as the white leucosmia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The flowers are primarily pollinated by insects, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
