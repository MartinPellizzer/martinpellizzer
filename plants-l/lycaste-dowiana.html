Lycaste dowiana

Lycaste dowiana, commonly known as the Dow's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, green, and purple hues. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical environments.

Lycaste dowiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
