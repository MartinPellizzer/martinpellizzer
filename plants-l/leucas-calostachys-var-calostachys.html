Leucas calostachys var. calostachys

Leucas calostachys var. calostachys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leucas, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and range in color from white to pale purple.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
