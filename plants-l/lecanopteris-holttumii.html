Lecanopteris holttumii

Lecanopteris holttumii, commonly known as the Holttum's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bioluminescent in the dark. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures are spore-bearing, typical of the fern family.

Lecanopteris holttumii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
