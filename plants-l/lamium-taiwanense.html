Lamium taiwanense

Lamium taiwanense, commonly known as Taiwan deadnettle, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
