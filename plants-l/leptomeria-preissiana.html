Leptomeria preissiana

Leptomeria preissiana, commonly known as the coastal daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in coastal and sandy environments. The plant features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Leptomeria preissiana is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
