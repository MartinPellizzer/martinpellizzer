Leptospermum bennigsenianum

Leptospermum bennigsenianum, commonly known as the Bennigsen's tea tree, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, serrated leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

The species is valued for its aromatic foliage and potential medicinal properties.
