Lobivia jajoana var. nigrostoma

Lobivia jajoana var. nigrostoma, commonly known as the Blackstem Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compact, cylindrical stems that are typically dark green to black in color, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the dry soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
