Leptostegia lucida

Leptostegia lucida, commonly known as the shining leathery vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing plant with woody stems and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with a waxy coating that gives them a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
