Lupinus luisanae var. luisanae

Lupinus luisanae var. luisanae, commonly known as the Luisana lupine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of pea-like flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.
