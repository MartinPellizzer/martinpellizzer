Lasianthus cyaneus

Lasianthus cyaneus, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking blue and violet flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Lasianthus cyaneus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
