Lanium peruvianum

Lanium peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually bright orange or red, with a unique, elongated shape that resembles a lantern.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
