Luisia tristis

Luisia tristis, commonly known as the weeping luisia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pale blue to purple flowers. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has hairy leaves and produces capsule fruits that release tiny seeds.
