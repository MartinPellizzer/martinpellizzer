Limosella africana var. africana

Limosella africana var. africana, commonly known as African sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

It is often found in coastal dunes and rocky outcrops, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
