Lobelia decumbens

Lobelia decumbens, commonly known as the decumbent lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia decumbens is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
