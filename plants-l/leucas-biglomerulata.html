Leucas biglomerulata

Leucas biglomerulata, commonly known as the clustered leucas, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
