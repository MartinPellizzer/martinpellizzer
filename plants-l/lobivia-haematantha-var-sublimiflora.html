Lobivia haematantha var. sublimiflora

Lobivia haematantha var. sublimiflora, commonly known as the snow-flowered lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem with a gray-green hue, and produces striking white or pale pink flowers during the cooler months. The plant is well adapted to dry conditions, with spines that provide shade and reduce water loss. Its flowers are often described as ethereal, with a delicate, almost cloud-like appearance.

This variety is highly sought after by cactus enthusiasts for its unique beauty and hardy nature.
