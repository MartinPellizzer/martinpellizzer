Langloisia lanata

Langloisia lanata, commonly known as the woolly langloisia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its soft, woolly foliage, which gives it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
