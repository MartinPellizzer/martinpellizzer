Landolphia tomentella

Landolphia tomentella, commonly known as the woolly landolphia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive, sweet scent. The plant has thick, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.
