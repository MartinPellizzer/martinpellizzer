Lantana × hybrida

Lantana × hybrida, commonly known as the shrub lantana, is a hybrid plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow, orange, red, and purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, requiring careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
