Lonicera guillonii var. macranthoides

Lonicera guillonii var. macranthoides, commonly known as the large-flowered honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
