Lythrum acutangulum f. mediostyla

Lythrum acutangulum f. mediostyla, commonly known as the intermediate spiked heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lythraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and clusters of bright red or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, linear leaves arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and providing habitat for small wildlife.
