Lysimachia clethroides

Lysimachia clethroides, commonly known as golden thread, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lysimachia clethroides is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
