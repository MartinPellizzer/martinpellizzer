Lavatera pseudo-olbia

Lavatera pseudo-olbia, commonly known as the silky oswaldia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, fluffy white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features palmate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
