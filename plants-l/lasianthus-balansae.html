Lasianthus balansae

Lasianthus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.
