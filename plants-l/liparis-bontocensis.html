Liparis bontocensis

Liparis bontocensis, commonly known as the Bontoc orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
