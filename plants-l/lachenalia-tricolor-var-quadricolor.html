Lachenalia tricolor var. quadricolor

Lachenalia tricolor var. quadricolor, commonly known as the four-colored lachenalia, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial plant characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, colorful inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, emerging from a basal rosette of fleshy, strap-shaped foliage.

Lachenalia tricolor var. quadricolor is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
