Lastrea scalaris

Lastrea scalaris, commonly known as the scalloped leaf cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a short, branching stem covered in small, spiny areoles. Its leaves are fleshy, oval, and have a distinctive scalloped edge, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
