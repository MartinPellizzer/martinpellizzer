Linnaea borealis f. elegans

Linnaea borealis f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant twinflower, is a rare and beautiful variant of the twinflower plant.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in cool, shaded environments, typically found in boreal and subarctic regions. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves and small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its unique floral structure and subtle beauty make it a notable subject in botanical studies.

This form is less common than the typical Linnaea borealis, highlighting the genetic diversity within the species.
