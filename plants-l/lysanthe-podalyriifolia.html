Lysanthe podalyriifolia

Lysanthe podalyriifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lythrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Lysanthe podalyriifolia thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or open woodland habitats.
