Laelia × elegans var. warneri

Laelia × elegans var. warneri, commonly known as the Warner's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.
