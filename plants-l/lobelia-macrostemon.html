Lobelia macrostemon

Lobelia macrostemon, commonly known as the large-stemmed lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in spikes along the stem. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often glandular and emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Lobelia macrostemon is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
