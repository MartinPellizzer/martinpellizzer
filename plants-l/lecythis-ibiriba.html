Lecythis ibiriba

Lecythis ibiriba, commonly known as the red lecythium, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, yellow-orange flowers. The tree bears distinctive, spiny fruits that contain a single, large, edible seed. The bark and seeds of Lecythis ibiriba have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.

Its ecological importance lies in providing habitat and food for various wildlife species in its native environment.
