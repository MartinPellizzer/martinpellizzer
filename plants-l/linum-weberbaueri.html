Linum weberbaueri

Linum weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer flax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its linear leaves and showy blooms make it a notable species in its native habitat.

However, it is relatively rare and faces conservation challenges due to habitat loss.
