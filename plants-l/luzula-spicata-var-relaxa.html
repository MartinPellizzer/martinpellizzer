Luzula spicata var. relaxa

Luzula spicata var. relaxa, commonly known as the relaxed wood rush, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces panicles of small, spike-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a slightly hairy texture.
