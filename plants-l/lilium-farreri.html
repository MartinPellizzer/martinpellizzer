Lilium farreri

Lilium farreri, commonly known as the Farre lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it typically blooms in the summer months.

Lilium farreri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
