Lens schnittspahnii var. persica

Lens schnittspahnii var. persica, commonly known as the Persian lentil.

This plant is a variant of the lentil species, characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds. It is native to regions of the Middle East and Central Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, with trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers.

Its seeds are used in traditional cuisines and are valued for their nutritional content.
