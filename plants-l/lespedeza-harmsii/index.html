Lespedeza Harmsii

Lespedeza harmsii is a lesser-known yet ecologically significant plant native to parts of East Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. This species plays an important role in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation. Its delicate pink or white flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains underappreciated in many horticultural circles.
