Ludwigia oocarpa

Ludwigia oocarpa, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange-colored leaves, which are often used in aquascaping for their aesthetic appeal. The plant thrives in warm, well-lit environments with soft to moderately hard water. It prefers moderate to high water flow and can grow both submerged and emersed.

Ludwigia oocarpa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for freshwater aquariums.
