Lepanthes curiosa

Lepanthes curiosa, commonly known as the curious orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, with a distinctive shape that sets it apart from other orchid species.

Lepanthes curiosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
