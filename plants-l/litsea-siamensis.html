Litsea siamensis

Litsea siamensis, commonly known as Siam litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, simple leaves that are usually opposite and elliptic in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by oval, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.

Litsea siamensis is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
