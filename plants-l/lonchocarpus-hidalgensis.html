Lonchocarpus hidalgensis

Lonchocarpus hidalgensis, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a woody legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils.

Its growth habit makes it a useful plant for erosion control and agroforestry systems.
