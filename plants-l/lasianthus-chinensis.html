Lasianthus chinensis

Lasianthus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese flame flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Lasianthus chinensis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
