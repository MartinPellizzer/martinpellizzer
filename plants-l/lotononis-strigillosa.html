Lotononis strigillosa

Lotononis strigillosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
