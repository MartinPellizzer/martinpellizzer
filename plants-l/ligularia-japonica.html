Ligularia japonica

Ligularia japonica, commonly known as Japanese ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
