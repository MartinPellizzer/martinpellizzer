Lonicera orientalis var. govaniana

Lonicera orientalis var. govaniana, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange-red, appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
