Lachanodendron domesticum

Lachanodendron domesticum, commonly known as the domestic vegetable tree, is a unique plant that combines characteristics of both vegetables and trees.

It is native to certain regions of southern Europe and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces edible leaves and fruits that can be harvested throughout the growing season. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with compound leaves and small, clustered flowers.

Lachanodendron domesticum is valued for its nutritional content and versatility in culinary use.
