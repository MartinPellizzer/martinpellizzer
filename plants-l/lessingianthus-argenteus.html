Lessingianthus argenteus

Lessingianthus argenteus, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
