Leucospermum puberum

Leucospermum puberum, commonly known as the silver bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical heads. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
