Leontodon autranii

Leontodon autranii, commonly known as the Autran's tooth.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in southern France, Italy, and Spain. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the spring, making it a notable wildflower in its native range.
