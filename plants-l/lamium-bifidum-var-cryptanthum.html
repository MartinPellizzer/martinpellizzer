Lamium bifidum var. cryptanthum

Lamium bifidum var. cryptanthum, commonly known as the spring deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, reddish-purple flowers with two lips, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and garden borders.

Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it spreads through rhizomes and seeds.
