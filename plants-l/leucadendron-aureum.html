Leucadendron aureum

Leucadendron aureum, commonly known as the golden candle bush, is a striking shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, cone-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
