Loxanthocereus hoxeyi subsp. hoxeyi

Loxanthocereus hoxeyi subsp.

hoxeyi, commonly known as the Hoxey's night-blooming cereus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is a columnar cactus with a ribbed stem and is characterized by its nocturnal flowering habit.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink and open at night, emitting a fragrant scent to attract pollinators. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

This plant is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique blooming behavior and striking appearance.
