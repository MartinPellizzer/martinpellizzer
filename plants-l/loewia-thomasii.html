Loewia thomasii

Loewia thomasii, commonly known as Thomas's loewia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Loewiaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
