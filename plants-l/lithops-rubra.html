Lithops rubra

Lithops rubra, commonly known as the Cape Town flower, is a species of stone plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, leaf-like structures that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the spring season.
