Lythrum candollei

Lythrum candollei, commonly known as the Candolle's lythrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Lythrum candollei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
