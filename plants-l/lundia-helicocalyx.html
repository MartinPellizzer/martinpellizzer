Lundia helicocalyx

Lundia helicocalyx, commonly known as the spiral-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Lundia helicocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
