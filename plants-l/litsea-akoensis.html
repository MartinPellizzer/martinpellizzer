Litsea akoensis

Litsea akoensis, commonly known as the Akoense litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a fragrant aroma. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by berries that are edible but not widely consumed.

Litsea akoensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
