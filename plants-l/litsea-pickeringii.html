Litsea pickeringii

Litsea pickeringii, commonly known as the Pickering's litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, smooth, yellow drupe that contains a single seed.
