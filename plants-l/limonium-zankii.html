Limonium zankii

Limonium zankii, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spike-like flower heads that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, exhibiting salt tolerance and drought resistance. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium zankii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
