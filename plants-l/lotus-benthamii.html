Lotus benthamii

Lotus benthamii, commonly known as the Bentham lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate. Lotus benthamii thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in aquatic botany.
