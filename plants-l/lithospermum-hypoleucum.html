Lithospermum hypoleucum

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and small, pale blue to white flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, with some species in the genus used traditionally for their antifungal and anti-inflammatory effects.
