Lycaste skinneri var. purpurata

Lycaste skinneri var. purpurata, commonly known as the purple-flowered lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink floral hues. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
