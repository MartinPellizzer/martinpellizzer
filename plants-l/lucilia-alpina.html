Lucilia alpina

Lucilia alpina, commonly known as the alpine green bottle fly, is a species of fly found in alpine and subalpine regions.

It belongs to the family Calliphoridae, which includes blow flies and green bottle flies. The adult Lucilia alpina is typically small, with a metallic green body and distinctive markings. It is often found near decaying organic matter in its habitat.

The larvae of this species play a role in decomposition and nutrient cycling in mountain ecosystems.
