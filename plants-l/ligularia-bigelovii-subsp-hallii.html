Ligularia bigelovii subsp. hallii

Ligularia bigelovii subsp.

hallii, commonly known as the Hall's ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of North America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often green with a metallic sheen.

The plant produces yellow-orange flowers arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
