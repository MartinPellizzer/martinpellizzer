Lavandula tenuisecta

Lavandula tenuisecta, commonly known as narrow-leaved lavender, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in gardens for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
