Leucojum vernum var. vernum

Leucojum vernum var. vernum, commonly known as the spring snowflake, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Europe.

It produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in woodland or shady garden settings. It prefers well-drained, moist soil and partial to full shade.

The flowers are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
