Leucas descampsii

Leucas descampsii, commonly known as the blue leucas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
