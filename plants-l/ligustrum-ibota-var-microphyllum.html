Ligustrum ibota var. microphyllum

Ligustrum ibota var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 cm in length. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for hedges and topiary.
