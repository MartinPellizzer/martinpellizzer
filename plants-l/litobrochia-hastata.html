Litobrochia hastata

Litobrochia hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved brochidium, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and narrow, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Litobrochia hastata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
