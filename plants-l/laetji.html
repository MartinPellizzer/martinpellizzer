Laetji

Laetji, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Laetji is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive indoors with proper care.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, white, and pink.
