Lindera setchuenensis

Lindera setchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan spice bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado plants. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a dark purple drupe, containing a single seed.

Lindera setchuenensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
