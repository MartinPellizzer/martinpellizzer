Lonchitis-aspera discolor

Lonchitis-aspera discolor, commonly known as the roughleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions. It has a taproot system and produces a capsule fruit containing numerous small seeds.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
