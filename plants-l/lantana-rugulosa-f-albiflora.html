Lantana rugulosa f. albiflora

Lantana rugulosa f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflowered lantana, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming from late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with fine, soft hairs.

The species is adapted to a variety of soil types but thrives best in well-drained, sunny conditions.
