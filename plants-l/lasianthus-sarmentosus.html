Lasianthus sarmentosus

Lasianthus sarmentosus, commonly known as the climbing lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
