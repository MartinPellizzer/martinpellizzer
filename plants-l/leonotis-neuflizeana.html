Leonotis neuflizeana

Leonotis neuflizeana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage is aromatic, with lance-shaped leaves that have a velvety texture.

Leonotis neuflizeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
