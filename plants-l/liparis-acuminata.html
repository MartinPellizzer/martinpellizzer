Liparis acuminata

Liparis acuminata, commonly known as the acuminated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with erect, branched stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Liparis acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
