Lambertia formosa f. tomentosa

Lambertia formosa f. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly lambertia, is a unique cultivar of the lambertia plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant features vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is often used in landscaping for its striking foliage and seasonal blooms.
