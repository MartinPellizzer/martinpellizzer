Licuala sabahana

Licuala sabahana, commonly known as the Sabah palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in diameter. The plant typically grows in wet, lowland areas and thrives in humid climates. Its trunk is short and stout, often covered in a fibrous layer.

Licuala sabahana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in gardens.
