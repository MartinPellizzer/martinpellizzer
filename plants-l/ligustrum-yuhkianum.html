Ligustrum yuhkianum

Ligustrum yuhkianum, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by black, spherical berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
