Lactuca kossmatii

Lactuca kossmatii, commonly known as the Kossmat's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and a flowering head that produces small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a long history of use in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
