Lactuca handeliana

Lactuca handeliana, commonly known as the desert lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to arid regions of North America and is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has narrow, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gravelly soils.

Lactuca handeliana is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes cultivated as a forage plant.
