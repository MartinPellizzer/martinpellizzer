Lilium Maculatum Var. Davuricum

Lilium maculatum var. davuricum is a striking variety of lily known for its vibrant red and white petals. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is prized for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. The flowers bloom in late spring, adding a dramatic touch to gardens and landscapes. Its unique coloration and elegant form make it a favorite among horticulturists and flower lovers alike.
