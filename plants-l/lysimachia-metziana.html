Lysimachia metziana

Lysimachia metziana, commonly known as the Mexican chickweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It features small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Lysimachia metziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
