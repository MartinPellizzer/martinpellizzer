Lastrea falciculata

Lastrea falciculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a layer of white, powdery bloom. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are narrow and falcate, meaning they curve sharply downward, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
