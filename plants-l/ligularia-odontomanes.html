Ligularia odontomanes

Ligularia odontomanes, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, often reaching up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
