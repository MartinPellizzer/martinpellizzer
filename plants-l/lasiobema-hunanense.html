Lasiobema hunanense

Lasiobema hunanense, commonly known as the Hunan holly.

It is a species of evergreen shrub native to China, particularly found in the Hunan province. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, white flowers during the spring, followed by red berries in the autumn.

Lasiobema hunanense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
