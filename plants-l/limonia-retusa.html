Limonia retusa

Limonia retusa, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Limonia retusa is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
