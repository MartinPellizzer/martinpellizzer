Linum carnosulum var. empetrifolium

Linum carnosulum var. empetrifolium, commonly known as the rock linum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its unique morphology allows it to conserve water efficiently, making it well-adapted to harsh conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
