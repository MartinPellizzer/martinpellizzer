Litsea tannaensis

Litsea tannaensis, commonly known as Tanna litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the island of Tanna in Vanuatu and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture with a strong, spicy scent.

Litsea tannaensis plays a role in the local ecosystem and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
