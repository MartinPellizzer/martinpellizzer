Laurus polyantha

Laurus polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese laurel or sweet laurel, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Laurus polyantha is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and aromatherapy.
