Lysimachia engleri var. glabra

Lysimachia engleri var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth yellow mosquito plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to summer. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
