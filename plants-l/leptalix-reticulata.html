Leptalix reticulata

Leptalix reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
