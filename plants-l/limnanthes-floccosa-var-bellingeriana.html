Limnanthes floccosa var. bellingeriana

Limnanthes floccosa var. bellingeriana, commonly known as the Bellinger's meadowfoam, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has soft, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and inland valleys.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
