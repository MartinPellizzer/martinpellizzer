Ligularia rumicifolia

Ligularia rumicifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
