Lycianthes hawkesiana

Lycianthes hawkesiana, commonly known as Hawkes' physalis, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, orange-red calyces that enclose the fruit.
