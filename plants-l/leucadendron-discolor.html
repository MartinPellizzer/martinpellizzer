Leucadendron discolor

Leucadendron discolor, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, silvery-blue foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces fluffy, pink or purple spherical flower heads that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
