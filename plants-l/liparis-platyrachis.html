Liparis platyrachis

Liparis platyrachis, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinct three-lobed lip.

Liparis platyrachis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
