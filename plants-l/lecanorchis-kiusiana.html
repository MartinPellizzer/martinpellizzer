Lecanorchis kiusiana

Lecanorchis kiusiana, commonly known as the Kiusi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

Lecanorchis kiusiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
