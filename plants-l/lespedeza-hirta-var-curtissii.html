Lespedeza hirta var. curtissii

Lespedeza hirta var. curtissii, commonly known as curlytop lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered pink to reddish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in woodland or swampy areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
