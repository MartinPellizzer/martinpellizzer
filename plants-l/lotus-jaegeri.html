Lotus jaegeri

Lotus jaegeri, commonly known as the Jaeger's lotus, is a rare aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. The plant features striking, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are broad and rounded, often floating on the water's surface.

Lotus jaegeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some regions.
