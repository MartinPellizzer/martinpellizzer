Linum reflexum

Linum reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Linaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Linum reflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
