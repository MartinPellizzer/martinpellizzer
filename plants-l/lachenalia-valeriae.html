Lachenalia valeriae

Lachenalia valeriae, commonly known as the Valley lachenalia, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange or yellow blooms during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
