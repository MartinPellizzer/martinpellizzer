Litsea teschneri

Litsea teschneri, commonly known as the Tasmanian litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Tasmania, Australia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval to elliptic in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, dark blue berries.

The species is notable for its aromatic foliage and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
