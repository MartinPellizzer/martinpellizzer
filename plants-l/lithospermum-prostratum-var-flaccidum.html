It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color, adapted to conserve water in dry environments. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, contributing to its ornamental value. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.
