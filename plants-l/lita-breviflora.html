Lita breviflora

Lita breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered lita, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale blue. Lita breviflora is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

It plays an important role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and providing habitat for local wildlife.
