Limnorchis × estesii

Limnorchis × estesii, commonly known as the hybrid water shield, is a rare aquatic plant that results from the cross between Limnorchis americana and Limnorchis palustris.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater wetlands and is characterized by its floating leaves and dense, spongy root system. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or partially submerged in water.
