Lepanthes stellaris

Lepanthes stellaris, commonly known as the star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often feature a star-like shape, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually green and strap-like, and it produces showy, fragrant blooms during the wet season.

Lepanthes stellaris is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ornamental value.
