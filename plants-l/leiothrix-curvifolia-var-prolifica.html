Leiothrix curvifolia var. prolifica

Leiothrix curvifolia var. prolifica, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
