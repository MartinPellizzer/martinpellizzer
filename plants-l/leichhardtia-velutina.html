Leichhardtia velutina

Leichhardtia velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf leichhardtia, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom during the rainy season. Leichhardtia velutina is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks, where it thrives in nutrient-rich soils.
