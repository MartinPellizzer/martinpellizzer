Laennecia pinnatifida

Laennecia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly fern, is a species of fern native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnatifid (bipinnate) fronds that are often divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a finely divided appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.

Laennecia pinnatifida is valued in horticulture for its elegant foliage and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
