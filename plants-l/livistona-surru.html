Livistona surru

Livistona surru, commonly known as the narrow-leafed fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a large, fan-shaped crown of pinnate leaves. The leaves are typically narrow and have a distinct blue-gray hue, giving the plant a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
