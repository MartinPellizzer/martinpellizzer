Lysimachia breviflora

Lysimachia breviflora, commonly known as narrowleaf yellow-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lysimachia breviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
