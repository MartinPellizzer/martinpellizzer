Lysimachia ruhmeriana

Lysimachia ruhmeriana, commonly known as the yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with smooth margins and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in the spring. Its flowering period lasts from April to June, and it is often found in wet meadows and swampy areas.

Lysimachia ruhmeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
