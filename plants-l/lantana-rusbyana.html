Lantana rusbyana

Lantana rusbyana, commonly known as the Rusby's lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Lantana rusbyana is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
