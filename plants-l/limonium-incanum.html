Limonium incanum

Limonium incanum, commonly known as the silver limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium incanum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
