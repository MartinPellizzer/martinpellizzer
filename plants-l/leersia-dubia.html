Leersia dubia

Leersia dubia, commonly known as the slender leersia, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Leersia dubia is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
