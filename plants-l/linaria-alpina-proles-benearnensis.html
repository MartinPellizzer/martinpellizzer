Linaria alpina proles benearnensis

Linaria alpina proles benearnensis, commonly known as the Benearn Alpine toadflax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the plant family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of yellow-orange flowers.
