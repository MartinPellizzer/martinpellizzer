Licaria cannella subsp. cannella

Licaria cannella subsp.

cannella, commonly known as the white licorice plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional uses, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
