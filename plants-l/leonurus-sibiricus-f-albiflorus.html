Leonurus sibiricus f. albiflorus

Leonurus sibiricus f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered Siberian hedgehog.

This plant is a flowering herbaceous species characterized by its hairy stems and distinctive, spiny leaves. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate appearance to its foliage. The plant is native to regions of Asia, including parts of Russia and Central Asia.

It is often found in meadows and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
