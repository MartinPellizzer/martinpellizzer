Lastrea impressa

Lastrea impressa, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
