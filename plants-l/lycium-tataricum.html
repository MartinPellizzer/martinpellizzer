Lycium tataricum

Lycium tataricum, commonly known as Chinese boxthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is known for its hardy nature.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and environmental conditions.
