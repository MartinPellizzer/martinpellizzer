Lonicera tianshanica

Lonicera tianshanica, commonly known as the Tianshan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Tianshan Mountains in China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces red berries that are edible and serve as a food source for various wildlife.

Lonicera tianshanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
