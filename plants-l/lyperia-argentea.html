Lyperia argentea

Lyperia argentea, commonly known as the silver lyperia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale blue, blooming in spring.

Lyperia argentea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
