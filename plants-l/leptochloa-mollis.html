Leptochloa mollis

Leptochloa mollis, commonly known as slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habits. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Leptochloa mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
