Lucilia acutifolia f. nivea

Lucilia acutifolia f. nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered thyme.

This variant is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate ornamental touch to gardens. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or borders.
