Leptohyptis Pinheiroi

Leptohyptis pinheiroi is a rare and little-known species of flowering plant native to Brazil. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, commonly known as the mint family, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense tropical forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. Its unique ecological role and potential medicinal properties make it a subject of scientific interest.
