Limonia scandens

Limonia scandens, commonly known as the Chinese starfruit or wild starfruit, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Limonia scandens is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering display.
