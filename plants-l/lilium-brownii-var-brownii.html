Lilium brownii var. brownii

Lilium brownii var. brownii, commonly known as the Chinese lily, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces a tall stem with a single flower at the apex.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
