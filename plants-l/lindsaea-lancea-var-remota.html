Lindsaea lancea var. remota

Lindsaea lancea var. remota, commonly known as the remote lance fern, is a variant of the lance fern species native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic fern characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
