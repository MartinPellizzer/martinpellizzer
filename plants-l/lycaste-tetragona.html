Lycaste tetragona

Lycaste tetragona, commonly known as the quadrangular lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lycaste tetragona is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
