Lundia cordata var. macrophylla

Lundia cordata var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed heartleaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves.

The foliage is usually dark green with a glossy texture, and the leaves can grow quite large, often reaching up to 30 cm in length. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf shape and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
