Laelia × bella

Laelia × bella, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Brassia species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth. The plant produces large, showy flowers in various colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Laelia × bella is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
