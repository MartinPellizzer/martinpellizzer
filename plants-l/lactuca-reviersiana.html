Lactuca reviersiana

Lactuca reviersiana, commonly known as the Persian lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pale green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

Lactuca reviersiana is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly sweet flavor.
