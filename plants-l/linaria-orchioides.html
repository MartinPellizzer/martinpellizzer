Linaria orchioides

Linaria orchioides, commonly known as the orchid-like toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Linaria orchioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
