Lobelia eckloniana

Lobelia eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape lobelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it forms dense, low-growing mats.

Lobelia eckloniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
