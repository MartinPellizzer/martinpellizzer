Limonium coralloides

Limonium coralloides, commonly known as the coral limonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are drought-tolerant.

Limonium coralloides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
