Lindmania gracilis

Lindmania gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaved water lily, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that float on the water surface. The plant has small, white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it in shallow water bodies.

Lindmania gracilis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
