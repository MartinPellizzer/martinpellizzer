Lotus corniculatus var. monanthos

Lotus corniculatus var. monanthos, commonly known as the single-flowered grass violet, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, often hairy stems that produce small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or blue. The leaves are typically alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wildflower gardens.
