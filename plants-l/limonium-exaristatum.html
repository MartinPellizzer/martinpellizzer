Limonium exaristatum

Limonium exaristatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to coastal landscapes.

Limonium exaristatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate salty air and wind.
