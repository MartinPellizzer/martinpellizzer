Lasthenia gracilis

Lasthenia gracilis, commonly known as the slender rayless goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its slender stems and small, yellow flowers are adapted to thrive in arid environments.
