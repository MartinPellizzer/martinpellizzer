Laelia anceps var. protheroana

Laelia anceps var. protheroana, commonly known as the Prothero's laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its native environment. It prefers cool, bright conditions with good air circulation, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
