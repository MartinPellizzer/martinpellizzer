Lobivia famatinensis var. aurantiaca

Lobivia famatinensis var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange-Flowered Lobivia, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a compact, rounded stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
