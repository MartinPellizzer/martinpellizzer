Lobelia cardinalis f. milleri

Lobelia cardinalis f. milleri, commonly known as the cardinal lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens and wetland restoration projects.
