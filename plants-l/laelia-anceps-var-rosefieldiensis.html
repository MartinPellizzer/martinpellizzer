Laelia anceps var. rosefieldiensis

Laelia anceps var. rosefieldiensis, commonly known as the Rosefield orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The variety is named in honor of the botanist and orchid enthusiast, Dr. Rosefield.
