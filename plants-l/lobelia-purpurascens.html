Lobelia purpurascens

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
