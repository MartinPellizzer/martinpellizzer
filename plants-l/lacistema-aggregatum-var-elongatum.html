Lacistema aggregatum var. elongatum

Lacistema aggregatum var. elongatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed lacistema, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is typically found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
