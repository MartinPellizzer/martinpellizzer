Lavatera olbia

Lavatera olbia, commonly known as the Persian broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features slender, often branched stems and produces clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a slightly hairy texture.

Lavatera olbia is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.
