Ligusticum tenuifolium var. dissimilis

Ligusticum tenuifolium var. dissimilis, commonly known as the slender-leaved lovage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinct, pungent aroma. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, often found in rocky or forested habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine for digestive and anti-inflammatory purposes.
