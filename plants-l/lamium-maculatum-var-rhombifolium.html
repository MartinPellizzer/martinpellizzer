Lamium maculatum var. rhombifolium

Lamium maculatum var. rhombifolium, commonly known as purple deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped to rhomboid leaves with distinctive purple or reddish markings, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, violet, or white, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
