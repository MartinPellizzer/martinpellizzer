Lavandula Abrotanoides Var. Elegans

Lavandula abrotanoides var. elegans is a captivating member of the mint family known for its delicate lavender blooms and fragrant foliage. Native to the rocky soils of southern Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained conditions and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or borders where space is limited. The plant's unique color variation and soft texture add visual interest to any landscape. With its easy care requirements and long-lasting flowers, it's a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
