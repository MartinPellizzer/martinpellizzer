Leysera odorata

Leysera odorata, commonly known as the fragrant mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of trees and shrubs.

Leysera odorata is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
