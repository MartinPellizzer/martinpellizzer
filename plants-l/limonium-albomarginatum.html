Limonium albomarginatum

Limonium albomarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin edging plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Limonium albomarginatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
