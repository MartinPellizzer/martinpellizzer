Lolium perenne var. stickneiensis

Lolium perenne var. stickneiensis, commonly known as Sticknei's perennial ryegrass, is a variant of perennial ryegrass native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and dense growth habit, making it a valuable forage crop. The plant typically thrives in temperate climates and is adapted to well-drained soils. Its genetic distinctiveness is noted in botanical studies, contributing to its classification as a separate variety.

It is often used in agricultural research for its resilience and nutritional value.
