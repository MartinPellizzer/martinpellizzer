Lobadium trifoliatum

Lobadium trifoliatum, commonly known as the three-leaf lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Lobadium trifoliatum plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
