Lobelia urens f. angustifolia

Lobelia urens f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved blueweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems and small, tubular blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native gardens and wetland restoration projects.
