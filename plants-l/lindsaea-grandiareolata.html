Lindsaea grandiareolata

Lindsaea grandiareolata, commonly known as the large-areola lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are often adorned with small, raised dots called areolae, which give it its common name.

Lindsaea grandiareolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
