Leucas marrubioides var. marrubioides

Leucas marrubioides var. marrubioides, commonly known as narrow-leaved leucas, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice among horticulturists.
