Lasianthus stipularis var. stipularis

Lasianthus stipularis var. stipularis, commonly known as the slender flameflower, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface. The flowers are typically small, clustered in inflorescences, and display a range of colors from white to pink or red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
