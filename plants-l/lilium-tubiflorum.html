Lilium tubiflorum

Lilium tubiflorum, commonly known as the tuberous lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of Asia, including Turkey and the Caucasus. The plant is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lilium tubiflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
