Ligustrum tschonoskii f. glabrescens

Ligustrum tschonoskii f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth privet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it a refined appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clustered inflorescences, which are followed by black, spherical berries. It is often used in landscaping for its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and resistance to pests and diseases.
