Lychnophora gardneri

Lychnophora gardneri, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark reddish-brown wood, which is highly valued for its tonal qualities in musical instruments. Due to overharvesting, it is now classified as a vulnerable species by the IUCN.
