Leptochilus gemmifer

It is characterized by its delicate, leaf-like structures called thallus, which are typically green and covered in a layer of rhizoids. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or soil in temperate regions.

Its simple morphology and reproductive strategy make it an important species in ecological and botanical studies.
