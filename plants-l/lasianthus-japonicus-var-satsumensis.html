Lasianthus japonicus var. satsumensis

Lasianthus japonicus var. satsumensis, commonly known as the Satsuma lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded or semi-shaded areas in the landscape.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a notable species in botanical collections.
