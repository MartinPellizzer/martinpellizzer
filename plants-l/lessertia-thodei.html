Lessertia thodei

Lessertia thodei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that attracts pollinators.

Lessertia thodei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
