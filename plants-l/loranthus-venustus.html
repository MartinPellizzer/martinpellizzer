Loranthus venustus

Loranthus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful mistletoe, is a parasitic plant belonging to the Loranthaceae family.

It grows on various host trees, primarily in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has small, opposite leaves and produces small, white or yellowish flowers. Its berries are usually red or orange and contain sticky seeds that adhere to the bark of host trees.

Loranthus venustus plays an important ecological role by providing food for birds and other animals.
