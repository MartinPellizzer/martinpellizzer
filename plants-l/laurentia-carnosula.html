Laurentia carnosula

Laurentia carnosula, commonly known as the fleshy laurentia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
