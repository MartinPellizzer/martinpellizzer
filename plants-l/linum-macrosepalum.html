Linum macrosepalum

Linum macrosepalum, commonly known as the large-sepal flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers with prominent, showy sepals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Linum macrosepalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty.
