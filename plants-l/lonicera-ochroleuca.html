Lonicera ochroleuca

Lonicera ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces fragrant, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that mature in the fall.

Lonicera ochroleuca is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in woodland edges and thickets.
