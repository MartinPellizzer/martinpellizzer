Lobivia arachnacantha var. sulphurea

Lobivia arachnacantha var. sulphurea, commonly known as the sulphur-horned lobivia, is a unique cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems that form a compact, globular shape. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its greenish-gray foliage. Its adaptations include thick, fleshy stems for water storage and a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its unusual appearance and resilience in dry environments.
