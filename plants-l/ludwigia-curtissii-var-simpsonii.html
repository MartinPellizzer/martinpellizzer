Ludwigia curtissii var. simpsonii

Ludwigia curtissii var. simpsonii, commonly known as Simpson's Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features bright red to orange-red leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings, making it a popular choice for aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters for optimal growth. It is often used in the background of planted tanks due to its dense foliage and vibrant coloration.

This variant is a hardy and adaptable species, suitable for both beginner and advanced aquarists.
