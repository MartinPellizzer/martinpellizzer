Lindera pomiensis

Lindera pomiensis, commonly known as the Chinese sweetwood, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in the spring. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that mature to a red color.

Lindera pomiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
