Limonium perplexum

Limonium perplexum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to salty, well-drained soils. Limonium perplexum is a perennial herb that blooms in summer, producing small, clustered flowers.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardens and coastal landscaping.
