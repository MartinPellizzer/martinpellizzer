Loretia

Loretia, commonly known as the red lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Loretia has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often evergreen.

It is cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
