Lonicera etrusca var. roeseri

Lonicera etrusca var. roeseri, commonly known as the Etruscan honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Italy.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its berries are red and edible, often attracting birds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
