Linaria heterophylla var. spectabilis

Linaria heterophylla var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers. The leaves are usually narrow and linear, varying in shape depending on the growing conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to different garden environments.
