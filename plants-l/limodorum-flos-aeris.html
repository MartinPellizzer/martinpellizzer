Limodorum flos-aeris

Limodorum flos-aeris, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its unique, lantern-like flowers that are typically violet or pale blue in color. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, pendulous blooms that appear in late summer. Limodorum flos-aeris thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in many regions.
