Lilium japonicum var. albomarginatum

Lilium japonicum var. albomarginatum, commonly known as the Japanese white-margin lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Japan.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white with a distinctive yellow or orange edge. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces upright stems that bear the blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its historical use in traditional medicine.
