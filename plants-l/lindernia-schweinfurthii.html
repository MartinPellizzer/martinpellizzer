Lindernia schweinfurthii

Lindernia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the silverleaf lindernia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
