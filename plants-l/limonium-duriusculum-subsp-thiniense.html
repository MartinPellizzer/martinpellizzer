Limonium duriusculum subsp. thiniense

Limonium duriusculum subsp.

thiniense, commonly known as the Thiniense Sea Lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
