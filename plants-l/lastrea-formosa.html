Lastrea formosa

Lastrea formosa, commonly known as the beautiful lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are often ribbed and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Lastrea formosa is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
