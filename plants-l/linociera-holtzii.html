Linociera holtzii

Linociera holtzii, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range from yellow to orange-red, often blooming in spring and summer. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and can reach up to 10 centimeters in length. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Linociera holtzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
