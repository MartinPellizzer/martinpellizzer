Lepidium alyssoides var. stenocarpum

Lepidium alyssoides var. stenocarpum, commonly known as narrow-carpel wallflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are usually yellow or white, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
