Ludwigia bonariensis

Ludwigia bonariensis, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Ludwigia bonariensis is popular in aquascaping due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
