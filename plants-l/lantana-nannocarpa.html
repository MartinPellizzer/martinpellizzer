Lantana nannocarpa

Lantana nannocarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited lantana, is a species of flowering plant in the verbena family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly found in parts of Central and South America. Lantana nannocarpa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
