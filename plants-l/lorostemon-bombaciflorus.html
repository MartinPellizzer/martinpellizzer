Lorostemon bombaciflorus

Lorostemon bombaciflorus, commonly known as the silky tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds covered in a silky, fibrous coating. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
