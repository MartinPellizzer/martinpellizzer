Lebeckia cinerea var. schaeferi

Lebeckia cinerea var. schaeferi, commonly known as the silver lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, pale blue to white flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
