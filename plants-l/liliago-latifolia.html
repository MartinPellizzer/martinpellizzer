Liliago latifolia

Liliago latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf liliago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in temperate climates.

Liliago latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
