Lepidium fraseri var. decipiens

Lepidium fraseri var. decipiens, commonly known as the Fraser's cress, is a flowering plant native to the western regions of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species of mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and is often found in moist, rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically lobed and may have a peppery taste, similar to other members of its family.
