Leucanthemum graminifolium

Leucanthemum graminifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf white ragwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and yellow-centered white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
