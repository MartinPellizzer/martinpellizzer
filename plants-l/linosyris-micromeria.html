Linosyris micromeria

Linosyris micromeria, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant has small, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss. It produces clusters of tiny, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring.
