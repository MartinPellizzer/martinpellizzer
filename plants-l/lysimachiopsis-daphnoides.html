Lysimachiopsis daphnoides

Lysimachiopsis daphnoides, commonly known as the creeping daphne, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover.
