Lobostemon collinus

Lobostemon collinus, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its botanical characteristics include a hairy stem and showy, trumpet-shaped petals.

It is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
