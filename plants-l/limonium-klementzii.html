Limonium klementzii

Limonium klementzii, commonly known as the coastal flax or sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in coastal areas and sandy soils. Limonium klementzii is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its ecological importance includes providing nectar for pollinators and stabilizing coastal dunes.
