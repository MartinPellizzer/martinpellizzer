Leucochloron limae

Leucochloron limae, commonly known as the lima bean fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green to white in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Leucochloron limae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
