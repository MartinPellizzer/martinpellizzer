Laguna abyssinica

Laguna abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian water lily, is a rare aquatic plant native to the high-altitude lakes of Ethiopia.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant has a short flowering period, usually during the wet season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
