Lantana aristata var. cabrerae

Lantana aristata var. cabrerae, commonly known as the Cape York lantana, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is known for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
