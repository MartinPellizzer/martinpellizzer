Lepanthes mixe

Lepanthes mixe, commonly known as the Mixe orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its leaves are usually broad and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Lepanthes mixe is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
