Lastrea cheiloplotia

Lastrea cheiloplotia, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
