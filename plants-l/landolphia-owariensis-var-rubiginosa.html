Landolphia owariensis var. rubiginosa

Landolphia owariensis var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange-red. The plant is characterized by its distinctive red stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
