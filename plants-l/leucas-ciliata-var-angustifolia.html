Leucas ciliata var. angustifolia

Leucas ciliata var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved leucas, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silky texture. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to horticultural collections.
