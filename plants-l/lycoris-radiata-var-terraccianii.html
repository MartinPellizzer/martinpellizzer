Lycoris radiata var. terraccianii

Lycoris radiata var. terraccianii, commonly known as the Terracianii Lily, is a variant of the Chinese lantern plant.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in the autumn season. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
