Laelia anceps var. munda

Laelia anceps var. munda, commonly known as the Munda Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors including white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms during the spring and early summer, producing clusters of showy, spurred orchid flowers. It prefers cool, bright environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
