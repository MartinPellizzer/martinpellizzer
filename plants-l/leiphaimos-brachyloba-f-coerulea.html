Leiphaimos brachyloba f. coerulea

Leiphaimos brachyloba f. coerulea, commonly known as the blue-flowered leiphaimos, is a rare and striking plant characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.

The flowers are borne in dense clusters and are known for their intense coloration, which can range from pale blue to a deep cobalt hue.
