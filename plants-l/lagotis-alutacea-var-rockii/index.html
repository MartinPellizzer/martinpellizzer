Lagotis Alutacea Var. Rockii

Lagotis alutacea var. rockii is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky crevices and shaded forest floors. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate flora from environmental threats.
