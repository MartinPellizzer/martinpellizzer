Lantana buchii

Lantana buchii, commonly known as the silver lantana, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Lantana buchii is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
