Lepisorus annuifrons

Lepisorus annuifrons, commonly known as the annual fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Lepisorus annuifrons is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
