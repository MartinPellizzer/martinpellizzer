Linaria maeotica

Linaria maeotica, commonly known as the Crimean toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Black Sea.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils along the shorelines. Linaria maeotica is adapted to saline and coastal environments, making it a hardy species in such habitats.

It is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
