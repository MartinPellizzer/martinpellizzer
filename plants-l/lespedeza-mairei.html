Lespedeza mairei

Lespedeza mairei, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Lespedeza mairei is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen and prevent soil erosion.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
