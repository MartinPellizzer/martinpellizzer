Lepanthes speciosa

Lepanthes speciosa, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are often fragrant and can range in color from white to pink and purple.

Lepanthes speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
