Lepidium crenatifolium

Lepidium crenatifolium, commonly known as the coastal rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, yellow flowers and has a distinctive, aromatic foliage. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime environments.

Lepidium crenatifolium is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local wildlife.
