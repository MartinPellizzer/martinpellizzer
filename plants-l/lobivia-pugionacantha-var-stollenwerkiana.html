Lobivia pugionacantha var. stollenwerkiana

Lobivia pugionacantha var. stollenwerkiana, commonly known as the Stollenwerk's Lobivia, is a compact, branching cactus native to southern Africa.

It features cylindrical stems that are typically green with a bluish tinge, adorned with white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
