Limeum aethiopicum var. aethiopicum

Limeum aethiopicum var. aethiopicum, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to East Africa.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.

The flowers are typically borne on tall, upright stems, adding a striking visual contrast to the foliage.
