Litsea solomonensis

Litsea solomonensis, commonly known as Solomon's litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, red berries that are edible.

Litsea solomonensis is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
