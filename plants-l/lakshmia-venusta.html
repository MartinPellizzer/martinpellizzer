Lakshmia venusta

Lakshmia venusta, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant has a complex root system adapted to its humid, shaded forest habitat. Its leaves are broad and leathery, which helps it retain moisture in its natural environment.

Lakshmia venusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and fragrance.
