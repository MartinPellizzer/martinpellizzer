Lindsaea sinuatocrenata

Lindsaea sinuatocrenata, commonly known as the sinuate-stemmed lindsaea, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lindsaeaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branching fronds that often hang from tree branches. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are typically covered in a fine, hairy layer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high air humidity.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
