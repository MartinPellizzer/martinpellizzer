Lamprotis holosericea

Lamprotis holosericea, commonly known as the silky glabrous orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairless stems and striking, elongated leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.
