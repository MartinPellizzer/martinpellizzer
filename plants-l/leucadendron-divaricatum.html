Leucadendron divaricatum

Leucadendron divaricatum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, cone-like inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
