Lysimachia japonica var. formosana

Lysimachia japonica var. formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan yellow water plant, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a reddish stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
