Limonium mansanetianum

Limonium mansanetianum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by bracts that resemble leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Limonium mansanetianum is a perennial herb that blooms from summer to autumn, adding vibrant color to coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers in maritime climates.
