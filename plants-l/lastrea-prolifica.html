Lastrea prolifica

Lastrea prolifica, commonly known as the prolific lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered leaves that help it store water in arid environments. It produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
