Linanthus floribundus

Linanthus floribundus, commonly known as the showy linanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
