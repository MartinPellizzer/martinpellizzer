Laurus tawa

Laurus tawa, commonly known as tawa or tawa tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The tree is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and aromatic. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries.

The plant is often found in coastal and lowland forests, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
