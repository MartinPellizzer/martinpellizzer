Leiotulus kotschyi

Leiotulus kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi's rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with four petals, typical of the mustard family. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.
