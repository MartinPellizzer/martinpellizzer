Liparis bautingensis

Liparis bautingensis, commonly known as the Bauting's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small and greenish-white, often with a faintly colored lip.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
