Lotus argyrodes

Lotus argyrodes, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshy areas and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
