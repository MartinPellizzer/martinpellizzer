Lindsaea repens var. delicatula

Lindsaea repens var. delicatula, commonly known as the creeping lindsaea, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, with slender, creeping stems that produce numerous small, lanceolate leaves. The leaves are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving the plant a soft, velvety appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is noted for its delicate foliage and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in greenhouses and shaded gardens.
