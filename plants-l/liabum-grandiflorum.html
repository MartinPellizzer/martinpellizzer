Liabum grandiflorum

Liabum grandiflorum, commonly known as the largeflower liabum, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Liabum grandiflorum is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
