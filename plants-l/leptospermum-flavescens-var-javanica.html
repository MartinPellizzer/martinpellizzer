Leptospermum flavescens var. javanica

Leptospermum flavescens var. javanica, commonly known as the Javan tea tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
