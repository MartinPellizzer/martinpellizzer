Leontodon autumnalis var. micranthus

Leontodon autumnalis var. micranthus, commonly known as the autumn leontodon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the autumn. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, and it is known for its hardy nature.

This variety is often found in disturbed habitats and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
