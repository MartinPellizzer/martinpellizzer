Lobelia boninensis f. rubra

Lobelia boninensis f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking red petals that contrast with its green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near coastal areas. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
