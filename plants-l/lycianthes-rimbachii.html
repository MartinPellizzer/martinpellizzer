Lycianthes rimbachii

Lycianthes rimbachii, commonly known as the red-flowered bitterleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, showy red flowers. Its fruits are typically berries that mature to a dark purple or black color.

The plant contains various alkaloids and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
