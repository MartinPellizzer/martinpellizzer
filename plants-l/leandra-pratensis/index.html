Leandra Pratensis

Leandra pratensis is a striking plant native to the wetlands of South America. It is known for its tall, slender stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy areas along riverbanks. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation outside its natural habitat.
