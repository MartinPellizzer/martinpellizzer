Licuala pahangensis

Licuala pahangensis, commonly known as the Pahang palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia, and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an understory species in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its trunk is short and often covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance.
