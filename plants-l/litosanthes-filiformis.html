Litosanthes filiformis

Litosanthes filiformis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with delicate, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
