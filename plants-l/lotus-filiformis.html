Lotus filiformis

Lotus filiformis, commonly known as the slender lotus, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, thread-like stems that often trail along the ground, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in moist, sunny environments.

Lotus filiformis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and fragrance.
