Leucadendron Spirale

Leucadendron spirale is a striking evergreen shrub native to south africa known for its unique spiral-shaped flowers. it belongs to the proteaceae family and is often referred to as the spiral candle bush due to its distinctive appearance. this plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salty air. its vibrant yellow blooms appear in late winter and early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape. it is a popular choice for coastal gardens and is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
