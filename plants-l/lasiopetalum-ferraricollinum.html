Lasiopetalum ferraricollinum

Lasiopetalum ferraricollinum, commonly known as the red-stemmed lilly pilly, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has smooth, reddish-brown stems that become more prominent with age. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Lasiopetalum ferraricollinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
