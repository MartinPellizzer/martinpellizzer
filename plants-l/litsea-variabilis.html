Litsea variabilis

Litsea variabilis, commonly known as variable litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, berry-like fruits that are edible.

Litsea variabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
