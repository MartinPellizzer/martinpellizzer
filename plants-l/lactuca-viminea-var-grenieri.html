Lactuca viminea var. grenieri

Lactuca viminea var. grenieri, commonly known as the slender lettuce or green curled lettuce, is a cultivar of the lettuce genus Lactuca.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that curl inward, giving it a unique appearance. This plant is typically grown for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often used in salads and is a popular choice for ornamental gardening as well.
