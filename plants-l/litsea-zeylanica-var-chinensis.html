Litsea zeylanica var. chinensis

Litsea zeylanica var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, dark purple fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and aromatic properties, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
