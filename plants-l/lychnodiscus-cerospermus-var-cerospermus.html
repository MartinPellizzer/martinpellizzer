Lychnodiscus cerospermus var. cerospermus

Lychnodiscus cerospermus var. cerospermus, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
