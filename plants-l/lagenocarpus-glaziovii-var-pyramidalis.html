Lagenocarpus glaziovii var. pyramidalis

Lagenocarpus glaziovii var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal burr palm, is a unique plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny leaf sheaths that form a protective, pyramid-like structure around the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by round, spiky fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
