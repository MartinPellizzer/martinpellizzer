Lysimachia insignis

Lysimachia insignis, commonly known as golden thread, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

Lysimachia insignis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
