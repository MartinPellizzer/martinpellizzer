Llerasia soratensis

Llerasia soratensis, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

Llerasia soratensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
