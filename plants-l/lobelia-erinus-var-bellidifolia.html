Lobelia erinus var. bellidifolia

Lobelia erinus var. bellidifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf lobelia, is a compact, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to blue, pink, and purple, often arranged in dense clusters. The plant has velvety, silver-green foliage that gives it a striking appearance, especially in shaded or cooler conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
