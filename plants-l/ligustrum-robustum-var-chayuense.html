Ligustrum robustum var. chayuense

Ligustrum robustum var. chayuense, commonly known as the Chayu privet, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to serve as a hedge or privacy screen.
