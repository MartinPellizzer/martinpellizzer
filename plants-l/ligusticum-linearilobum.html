Ligusticum linearilobum

Ligusticum linearilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed lovage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features slender, linear leaves with serrated edges and small yellow flowers that appear in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
