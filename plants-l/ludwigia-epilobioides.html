Ludwigia epilobioides

Ludwigia epilobioides, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the water's surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, often in slow-moving or still water.

Ludwigia epilobioides is popular in aquascaping for its vibrant coloration and ease of cultivation.
