Lastrea viscosa

Lastrea viscosa, commonly known as the sticky lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are covered in a sticky, resinous substance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
