Lepisorus virescens

Lepisorus virescens, commonly known as the green lepisorus, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green in color. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The rhizomes are creeping and produce small, clustered sporangia on the underside of the leaves.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
