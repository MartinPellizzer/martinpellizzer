Lobivia backebergii var. capinotensis

Lobivia backebergii var. capinotensis, commonly known as the Capinotensis Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to South Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems, allowing it to survive in arid environments. It prefers well-draining soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
