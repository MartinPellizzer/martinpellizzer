Lasianthus lucidus var. lucidus

Lasianthus lucidus var. lucidus, commonly known as the glossy lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
