Laeliocattleya dormaniana

Laeliocattleya dormaniana, commonly known as the Dormaniana orchid, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Laelia and Cattleya species.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, often with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
