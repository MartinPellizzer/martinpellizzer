Lilium leichtlinii var. platypetalum

Lilium leichtlinii var. platypetalum, commonly known as the flat-petaled lily, is a rare and striking member of the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and the Tian Shan range. This variant is characterized by its large, flat petals that give it a unique appearance compared to other lilies. The flowers typically bloom in late summer, producing vibrant pink or red hues.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
