Lotononis calycina var. hirsutissima

Lotononis calycina var. hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is densely covered with fine, white to greyish hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
