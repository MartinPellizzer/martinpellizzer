Lonicera bornmuelleri

Lonicera bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, blooming in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera bornmuelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
