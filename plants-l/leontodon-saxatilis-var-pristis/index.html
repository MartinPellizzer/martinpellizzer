Leontodon Saxatilis Var. Pristis

Leontodon saxatilis var. pristis is a rare and hardy plant that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. It is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and ability to withstand harsh conditions. This variety is often found in southern Europe and parts of North Africa. Its scientific name reflects its unique characteristics and geographic origin. Despite its resilience, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
