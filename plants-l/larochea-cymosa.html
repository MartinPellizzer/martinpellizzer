Larochea cymosa

Larochea cymosa, commonly known as the clustered leaf cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, spiny stems that form dense clusters, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it stores water in its fleshy stems, making it well adapted to drought conditions.
