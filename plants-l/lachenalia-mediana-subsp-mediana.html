Lachenalia mediana subsp. mediana

Lachenalia mediana subsp.

mediana, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive. It blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and long-lasting blooms.
