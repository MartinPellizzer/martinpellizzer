Lamium maculatum var. cupreum

Lamium maculatum var. cupreum, commonly known as the copperleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring heart-shaped leaves with a metallic copper or bronze hue. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and easy maintenance.
