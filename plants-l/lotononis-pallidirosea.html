Lotononis pallidirosea

Lotononis pallidirosea, commonly known as the pale pink orchid, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pale pink petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, thriving in the unique ecological conditions of its habitat. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in spring, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty.
