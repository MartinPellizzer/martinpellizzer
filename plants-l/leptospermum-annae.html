Leptospermum annae

Leptospermum annae, commonly known as the Anne's tea tree, is a shrub native to Tasmania, Australia.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is part of the myrtle family (Myrtaceae) and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Leptospermum annae is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in indigenous medicine.
