Loranthus signatus var. angustatus

Loranthus signatus var. angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mistletoe, is a parasitic plant that attaches to the branches of host trees.

Its parasitic nature allows it to draw water and nutrients from its host, while still performing photosynthesis to some extent.
