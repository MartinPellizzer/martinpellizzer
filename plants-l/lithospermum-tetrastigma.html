Lithospermum tetrastigma

Lithospermum tetrastigma, commonly known as the four-stem lithosperm, is a member of the Boraginaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a rough, hairy texture.
