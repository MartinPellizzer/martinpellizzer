Lemaireocereus dumortieri

Lemaireocereus dumortieri, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its flowers typically come in shades of red, pink, and orange, and it prefers bright, indirect light.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
