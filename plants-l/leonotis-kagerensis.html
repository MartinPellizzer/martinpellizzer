Leonotis kagerensis

Leonotis kagerensis, commonly known as the Kagera lantern flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange to yellow in color, arranged in clusters. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
