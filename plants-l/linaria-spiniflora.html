Linaria spiniflora

Linaria spiniflora, commonly known as the spiny toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has hairy, spiny stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Linaria spiniflora is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive floral display.
