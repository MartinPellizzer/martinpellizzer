Laburnum × intermedium

Laburnum × intermedium, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a hybrid deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable addition to gardens and parks.
