Ligularia fargesii

Ligularia fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green color. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a hooded spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Ligularia fargesii is valued for its ornamental foliage and is commonly cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and soil conditions.
