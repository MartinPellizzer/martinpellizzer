Lycaste colombiana

Lycaste colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes region of Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Lycaste colombiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
