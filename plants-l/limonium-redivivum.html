Limonium redivivum

Limonium redivivum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and silver-gray foliage, which are adapted to coastal and saline environments. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers bloom in summer and are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

Limonium redivivum is also valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
