Lastrea sagenioides

Lastrea sagenioides, commonly known as the stonecrop or liveforever plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among horticulturists and gardeners in dry climates.
