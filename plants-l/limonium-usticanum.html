Limonium usticanum

Limonium usticanum, commonly known as sea lavender, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salty air and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of salt.

Limonium usticanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize dunes.
