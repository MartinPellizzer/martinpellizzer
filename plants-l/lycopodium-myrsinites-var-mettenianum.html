Lycopodium myrsinites var. mettenianum

Lycopodium myrsinites var. mettenianum, commonly known as the Mettenianum clubmoss, is a rare and ancient vascular plant belonging to the Lycopodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and spore-bearing strobila that form at the tips of its stems. This variety is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
