Leucadendron aemulum var. homaeophyllum

Leucadendron aemulum var. homaeophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage and conical inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade. It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
