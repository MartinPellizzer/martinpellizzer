Lotus requienii var. gussonei

Lotus requienii var. gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's lotus, is a rare and striking aquatic plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom above the water surface, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged, while its stems are often floating or partially submerged. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with high sunlight exposure.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
