Linaria tartessiana

Linaria tartessiana, commonly known as the Spanish toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Linaria tartessiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
