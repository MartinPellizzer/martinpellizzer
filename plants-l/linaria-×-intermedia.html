Linaria × intermedia

Linaria × intermedia, commonly known as the everlasting or everlasting flower, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, red, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces numerous small, showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Linaria × intermedia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
