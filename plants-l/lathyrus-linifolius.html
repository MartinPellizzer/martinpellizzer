Lathyrus linifolius

Lathyrus linifolius, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and full sun.

Lathyrus linifolius is often found in grasslands, woodland edges, and along roadsides.
