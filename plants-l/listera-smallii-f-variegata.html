Listera smallii f. variegata

Listera smallii f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated spring beauty, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The variegated foliage features striking patterns of green and white, making it a visually appealing plant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
