Leloutrea aplocaryoides

Leloutrea aplocaryoides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by distinctive pappus-equipped seeds for wind dispersal.
