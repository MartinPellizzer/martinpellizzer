Limnetis glabra

Limnetis glabra, commonly known as the smooth water shield, is a aquatic plant native to freshwater environments.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving waters, such as ponds and lakes. The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are smooth and often float on the water surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Limnetis glabra plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water clarity.
