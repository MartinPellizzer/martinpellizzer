Lysidice rhodostegia

Lysidice rhodostegia, commonly known as the crimson-stemmed orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red stems and delicate, orchid-like flowers. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
