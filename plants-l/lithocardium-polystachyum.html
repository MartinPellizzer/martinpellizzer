Lithocardium polystachyum

Lithocardium polystachyum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is typically found in rocky or limestone habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow in color. It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and South Africa.
