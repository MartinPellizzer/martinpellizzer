Lycianthes sideroxyloides var. ocellata

Lycianthes sideroxyloides var. ocellata, commonly known as the spotted lycianthes, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with purple veins. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and has hairy, branched stems.
