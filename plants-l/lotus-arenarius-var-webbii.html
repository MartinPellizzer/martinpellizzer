Lotus arenarius var. webbii

Lotus arenarius var. webbii, commonly known as the desert lotus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to cream-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal dunes or inland deserts.

The species is known for its resilience and ability to survive in extreme conditions with minimal water.
