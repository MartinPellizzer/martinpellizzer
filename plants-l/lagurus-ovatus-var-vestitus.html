Lagurus ovatus var. vestitus

Lagurus ovatus var. vestitus, commonly known as the velvet foxtail, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and produces small, spiky seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

The flowers are usually pink or purple, adding a delicate touch to garden landscapes.
