Lagrezia oligomeroides

Lagrezia oligomeroides, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

Lagrezia oligomeroides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
