Lepanthes ariasiana

Lepanthes ariasiana, commonly known as the Arias's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

Lepanthes ariasiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
