Litsea glaucescens var. subsolitaria

Litsea glaucescens var. subsolitaria, commonly known as the solitary litsea, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, dark blue berries.

This variety is notable for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its potential use in traditional medicine.
