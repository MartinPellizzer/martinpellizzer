Litsea rufofusca

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which have a distinct, spicy scent. Its small, white flowers are typically clustered in umbels, and it produces small, dark purple berries.
