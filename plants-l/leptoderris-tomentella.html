Leptoderris tomentella

Leptoderris tomentella, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the family Anacardiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by edible, orange-red berries. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical climates.
