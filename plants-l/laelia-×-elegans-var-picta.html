Laelia × elegans var. picta

Laelia × elegans var. picta, commonly known as the picta hybrid orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with bright green and white markings. The plant typically produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
