Litsea timoriana

Litsea timoriana, commonly known as Timor laurel, is a small evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea timoriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
