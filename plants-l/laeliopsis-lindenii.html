Laeliopsis lindenii

Laeliopsis lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, brightly colored blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes collected in the wild, raising concerns about its conservation status.
