Litsea iteodaphne f. chinensis

Litsea iteodaphne f. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese litsea, is a variant of the Litsea iteodaphne species, belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green on top with a lighter underside. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, dark blue berries.

This plant is native to southern China and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
