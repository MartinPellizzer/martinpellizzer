Lundia glabra

Lundia glabra, commonly known as the smooth lundia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in smooth, hairless foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Lundia glabra thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in local ecosystems and a subject of interest in botanical research.
