Lantana violacea

Lantana violacea, commonly known as purple lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lantana violacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
