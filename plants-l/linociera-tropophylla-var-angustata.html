Linociera tropophylla var. angustata

Linociera tropophylla var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed linociera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
