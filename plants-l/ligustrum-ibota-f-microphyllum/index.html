Ligustrum Ibota F. Microphyllum

Ligustrum ibota f. microphyllum is a rare and intriguing variant of the common privet plant. It is characterized by its exceptionally small leaves, which set it apart from its standard counterparts. This unique form is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a botanical curiosity and a subject of interest for horticulturists. Its distinct appearance makes it a valuable addition to specialized plant collections.
