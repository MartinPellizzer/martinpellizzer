Lagerstroemia costa-draconis

Lagerstroemia costa-draconis, commonly known as the dragon's breath lagerstroemia, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from deep red to purple, appearing in late summer to early autumn. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its botanical name reflects its geographical origin, with "costa-draconis" possibly referencing its dragon-like appearance or association with dragon-like creatures in local folklore.
