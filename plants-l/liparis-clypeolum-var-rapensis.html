Liparis clypeolum var. rapensis

Liparis clypeolum var. rapensis, commonly known as the broad-leaved liparis, is a variant of the orchid genus Liparis.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in forest understories and is adapted to humid, tropical climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
