Lemeea

Lemeea, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that float on the water's surface, with vibrant colors ranging from pink to white. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide shade and oxygen to the water. Its roots are submerged, absorbing nutrients from the aquatic environment.

Lemeea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and cultural significance in various traditions.
