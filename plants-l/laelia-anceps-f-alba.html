Laelia anceps f. alba

Laelia anceps f. alba, commonly known as the White Laelia, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and sepals, which contrast with its deep green foliage. Native to high-altitude regions of South America, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments. It produces fragrant, long-lasting flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and collectors.
