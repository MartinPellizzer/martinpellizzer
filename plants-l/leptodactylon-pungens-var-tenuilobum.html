Leptodactylon pungens var. tenuilobum

Leptodactylon pungens var. tenuilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features long, slender leaves that are typically arrowhead-shaped, with a distinctive narrow lobing at the base. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds. Its foliage is usually a vibrant green, and it can grow up to several feet in length, making it a popular choice for water gardens.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to aquatic life.
