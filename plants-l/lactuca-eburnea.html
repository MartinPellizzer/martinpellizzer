Lactuca eburnea

Lactuca eburnea, commonly known as the white lettuce or silverleaf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is characterized by its rosette of pale green to silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Lactuca eburnea is not typically cultivated for food but is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
