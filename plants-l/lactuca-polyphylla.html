Lactuca polyphylla

Lactuca polyphylla, commonly known as the mullet lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, spoon-shaped leaves that are usually pale green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its seeds are small and oval in shape. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Lactuca polyphylla is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other flowering plants.
