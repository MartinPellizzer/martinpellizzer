Lomatogonium graciliflorum

Lomatogonium graciliflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered lomatogonium, is a herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale green, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Lomatogonium graciliflorum thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
