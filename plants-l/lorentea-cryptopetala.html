Lorentea cryptopetala

Lorentea cryptopetala, commonly known as the hidden petal orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that are often concealed within dense foliage, giving it its common name. The plant has a unique growth habit, with its stems and leaves often growing close to the ground. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of white and pale pink, are highly fragrant and attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to deforestation, Lorentea cryptopetala is considered an endangered species.
