Lacinaria laxa

Lacinaria laxa, commonly known as the slender lacinaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Lacinaria laxa is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
