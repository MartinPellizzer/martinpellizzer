Lachnagrostis Semibarbata Var. Filifolia

Lachnagrostis semibarbata var. filifolia is a unique grass species known for its delicate, thread-like foliage. Native to specific regions of southern Africa, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its narrow, hair-like leaves give it a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other grasses. This variety is often appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known plant in the broader botanical community.
