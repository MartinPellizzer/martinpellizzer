Lonicera maximowiczii subsp. maximowiczii

Lonicera maximowiczii subsp.

maximowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia. It features opposite, elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy on the underside.

The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in the fall, containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
