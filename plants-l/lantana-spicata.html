Lantana spicata

Lantana spicata, commonly known as shrub lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dense, compact growth with small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Lantana spicata is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.
