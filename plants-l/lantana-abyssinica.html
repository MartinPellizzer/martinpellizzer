Lantana abyssinica

Lantana abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian lantana, is a shrub native to Ethiopia.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and resilient.

The plant is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
