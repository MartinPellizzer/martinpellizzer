Lotononis florifera var. major

Lotononis florifera var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
