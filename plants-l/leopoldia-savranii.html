Leopoldia savranii

Leopoldia savranii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered blooms during the summer months.

Leopoldia savranii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
