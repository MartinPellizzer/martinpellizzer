Lamprotis rubida

Lamprotis rubida, commonly known as the red-eyed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a distinctive yellow-green lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their intricate, fragrant structure.

Lamprotis rubida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to controlled indoor conditions.
