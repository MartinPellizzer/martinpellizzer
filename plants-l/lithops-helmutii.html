Lithops helmutii

Lithops helmutii, commonly known as the Helmut's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the winter months. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, white coating that helps reduce water loss.

Lithops helmutii is popular among collectors for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
