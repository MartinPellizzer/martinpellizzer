Lacanthis splendens

Lacanthis splendens, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has a spiny exterior with areoles that bear both spines and flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
