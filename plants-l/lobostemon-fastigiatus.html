Lobostemon fastigiatus

Lobostemon fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright lobostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that are often used in floral arrangements due to their unique shape. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters along the stems. Lobostemon fastigiatus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens and water features.
