Lathyrus pannonicus subsp. pannonicus

Lathyrus pannonicus subsp.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, and it is often found in regions with temperate climates.
