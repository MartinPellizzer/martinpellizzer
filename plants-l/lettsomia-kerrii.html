Lettsomia kerrii

Lettsomia kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically features slender stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Lettsomia kerrii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.

Its botanical significance lies in its role within its native ecosystems and its value in horticultural collections.
