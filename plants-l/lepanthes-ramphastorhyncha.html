Lepanthes ramphastorhyncha

Lepanthes ramphastorhyncha, commonly known as the ramphastorhynch orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

Lepanthes ramphastorhyncha is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
