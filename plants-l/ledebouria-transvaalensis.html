Ledebouria transvaalensis

Ledebouria transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal starplant, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, narrow leaves and showy, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces white or pale purple blooms during the summer months, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in horticulture.
