Laelia caetensis

Laelia caetensis, commonly known as the Caetensis orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Brazil.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange-red blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Laelia caetensis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
