Lomandra Odora

Lomandra odora, also known as the fragrant lomandra, is a striking evergreen plant native to eastern Australia. It is prized for its elegant, grass-like foliage and its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that emit a sweet, spicy fragrance. This hardy plant thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and aromatic qualities make it a standout in both ornamental and ecological settings. Lomandra odora is not only beautiful but also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and biodiversity.
