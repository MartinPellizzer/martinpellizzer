Ludwigia arcuata

Ludwigia arcuata, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink coloration, which is most prominent in its leaves and flowers. This plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers sunny conditions. Its leaves are often floating or partially submerged, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Ludwigia arcuata is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
