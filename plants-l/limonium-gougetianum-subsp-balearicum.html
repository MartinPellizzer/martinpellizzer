Limonium gougetianum subsp. balearicum

Limonium gougetianum subsp.

balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands. It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.
