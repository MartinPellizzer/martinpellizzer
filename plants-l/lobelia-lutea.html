Lobelia lutea

Lobelia lutea, commonly known as yellow lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland gardens and native plant landscapes.
