Linochilus ochraceus

Linochilus ochraceus, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive, elongated petals. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Linochilus ochraceus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
