Loricaria macbridei

Loricaria macbridei, commonly known as the MacBride's armored catfish, is a freshwater fish native to South America.

It belongs to the family Loricariidae, which includes many armored catfish species. The fish is characterized by its distinctive bony plates covering its body, providing protection against predators. It is typically found in slow-moving rivers and streams with sandy substrates.

Loricaria macbridei is known for its herbivorous diet, primarily feeding on algae and plant matter.
