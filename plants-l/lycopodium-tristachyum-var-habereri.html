Lycopodium tristachyum var. habereri

Lycopodium tristachyum var. habereri, commonly known as the Habereri clubmoss, is a member of the Lycopodiaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense clusters of spore-bearing cones and needle-like leaves. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's unique morphology includes a short, thick stem with whorled leaves and prominent, cylindrical strobila.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to form compact, attractive clumps.
