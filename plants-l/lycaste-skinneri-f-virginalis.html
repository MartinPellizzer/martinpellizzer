Lycaste skinneri f. virginalis

Lycaste skinneri f. virginalis, commonly known as the Virgin's lycaste, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its native habitat with minimal rainfall. It prefers well-draining soil and partial shade, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
