Litsea bernhardensis

Litsea bernhardensis, commonly known as the Bernhard's litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces dark blue to black berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Litsea bernhardensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
