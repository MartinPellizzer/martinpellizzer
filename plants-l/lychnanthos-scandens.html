Lychnanthos scandens

Lychnanthos scandens, commonly known as the climbing lily or sweet-scented climbing plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a strong, sweet scent. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is commonly found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
