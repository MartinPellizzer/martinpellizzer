Liebrechtsia ringoetii

Liebrechtsia ringoetii, commonly known as the orchid tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a distinctive, tree-like structure with smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are highly fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
