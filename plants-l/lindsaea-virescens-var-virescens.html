Lindsaea virescens var. virescens

Lindsaea virescens var. virescens, commonly known as the green lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered spores on the underside of its fronds, which are typically released during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and shaded gardens.
