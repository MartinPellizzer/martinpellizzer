Lahia kutejensis

Lahia kutejensis, commonly known as the Kutejensis orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of white and pale pink.
