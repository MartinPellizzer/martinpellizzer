Leptospermum tomentosum

Leptospermum tomentosum, commonly known as silver tea tree, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional herbal remedies.
