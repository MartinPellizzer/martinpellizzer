Liparis gautierensis

Liparis gautierensis, commonly known as the Gautier's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers with a distinct coloration pattern, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the humid climate of its native region.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
