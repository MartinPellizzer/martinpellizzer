Lobelia lukwangulensis

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.
