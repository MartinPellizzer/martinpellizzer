Litsea Sericea

Litsea sericea is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Asia. Known for its delicate, silvery-white undersides on its leaves, it adds a unique visual appeal to any landscape. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is favored in gardens for its elegant appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. With its fragrant foliage and graceful form, Litsea sericea is a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
