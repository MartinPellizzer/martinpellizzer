Limnorchis tipuloides

Limnorchis tipuloides, commonly known as the marsh midge fly, is a small aquatic insect belonging to the family Chironomidae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender body and long, segmented legs adapted for life in wetland environments. This species is an important indicator of water quality in its native habitats.
