Limeum sulcatum var. robustum

Limeum sulcatum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust limeum, is a variant of the limeum species characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and robust growth habit.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it has adapted to thrive in hot, dry conditions. The plant features a dense, sprawling structure with dark green, succulent foliage that helps it retain moisture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of yellow or green, and it produces edible fruits that are used in local cuisines.

This variety is valued for its resilience and ornamental qualities in landscaping.
