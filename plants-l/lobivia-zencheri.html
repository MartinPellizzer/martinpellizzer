Lobivia zencheri

Lobivia zencheri, commonly known as the Zencher's lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and features spiny, ribbed stems that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
