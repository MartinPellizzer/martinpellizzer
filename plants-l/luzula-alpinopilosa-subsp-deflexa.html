Luzula alpinopilosa subsp. deflexa

Luzula alpinopilosa subsp.

deflexa, commonly known as the Alpine bent grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to high-altitude regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

The plant features dense, tufted foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly curled or rolled. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and adaptability to harsh mountain environments.
