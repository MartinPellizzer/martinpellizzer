Lychnis Viscaria Subsp. Atropurpurea

Lychnis viscaria subsp. atropurpurea is a striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant, deep purple flowers. This subspecies thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure. Its foliage is often covered in a soft, fuzzy texture that adds to its visual appeal. Native to regions across Europe and parts of Asia, it has become a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. With its unique coloration and ease of cultivation, it stands out as a valuable addition to any botanical collection.
