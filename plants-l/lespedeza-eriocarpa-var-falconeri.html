Lespedeza eriocarpa var. falconeri

Lespedeza eriocarpa var. falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri Lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
