Limonium minoicum

Limonium minoicum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by soft, fuzzy leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to sandy shores and dunes.

Limonium minoicum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
