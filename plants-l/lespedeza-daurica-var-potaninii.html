Lespedeza daurica var. potaninii

Lespedeza daurica var. potaninii, commonly known as Daurian lespedeza, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, fluffy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
