Lagenocarpus strictus var. leptocladius

Lagenocarpus strictus var. leptocladius, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in Southeast Asia.

It thrives in shallow water or moist soils, often forming dense stands in marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, contributing to its ability to colonize aquatic habitats.

The species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
