Lonicera licentii

Lonicera licentii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Lonicera licentii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
