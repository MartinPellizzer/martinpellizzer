Laserpitium ochridanum

Laserpitium ochridanum, commonly known as the Ochridan mountain grass, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
