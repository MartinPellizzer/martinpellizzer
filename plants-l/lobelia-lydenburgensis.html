Lobelia lydenburgensis

Lobelia lydenburgensis, commonly known as Lydenburg lobelia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple.
