Lotodes plicatum

Lotodes plicatum, commonly known as the pleated water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, pleated leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the day and close at night. Its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate of still or slow-moving water bodies.

Lotodes plicatum plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
