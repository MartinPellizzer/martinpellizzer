Lagerstroemia pubescens

Lagerstroemia pubescens, commonly known as the velvetwood or Chinese lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, peeling bark and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Lagerstroemia pubescens is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and air pollution.
