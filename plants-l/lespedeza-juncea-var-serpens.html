Lespedeza juncea var. serpens

Lespedeza juncea var. serpens, commonly known as the slender lespea, is a perennial legume native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, serpentine leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a fibrous root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Lespedeza juncea var. serpens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
