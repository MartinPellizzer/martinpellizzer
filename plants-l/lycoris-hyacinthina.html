Lycoris hyacinthina

Lycoris hyacinthina, commonly known as the Chinese lantern lily, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in the autumn. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous underground stem. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.

Lycoris hyacinthina thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
