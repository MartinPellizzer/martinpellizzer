Linum balansae

Linum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae flax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes various species of flax plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, delicate blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Linum balansae is considered a rare and endemic species.
