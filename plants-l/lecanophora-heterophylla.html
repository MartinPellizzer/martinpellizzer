Lecanophora heterophylla

Lecanophora heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaved sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features a short, thickened stem with fleshy, alternate leaves that vary in shape and size. Its small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Lecanophora heterophylla is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.
