Licuala spinosa subvar. brevidens

Licuala spinosa subvar. brevidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically arranged in panicles. Its foliage is often used in traditional crafts and as a decorative element.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
