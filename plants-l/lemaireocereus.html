Lemaireocereus

Lemaireocereus, commonly known as the moon cactus, is a genus of cacti native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Lemaireocereus species are generally low-maintenance and thrive in well-draining soil.

They are popular in horticulture due to their unique appearance and ease of care.
