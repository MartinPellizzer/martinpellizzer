Ligustrum Ibota F. Obtusifolium

Ligustrum ibota f. obtusifolium is a unique variant of the common privet plant known for its distinctive foliage. This cultivar features broad, rounded leaves that give it a lush, dense appearance. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires minimal maintenance. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for hedges, borders, and container gardens.
