Linaria alsinifolia

Linaria alsinifolia, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in sunny environments. Its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves contribute to its delicate appearance.
