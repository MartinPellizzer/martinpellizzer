Lastrea immersa

Lastrea immersa, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has thick, waxy leaves that are broad and rounded, often with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it to the water's surface, while its leaves float on top.

It is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes.
