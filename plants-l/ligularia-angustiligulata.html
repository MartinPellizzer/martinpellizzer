Ligularia angustiligulata

Ligularia angustiligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped to lanceolate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
