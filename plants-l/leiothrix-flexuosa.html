Leiothrix flexuosa

Leiothrix flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
