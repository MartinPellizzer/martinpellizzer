Lysimachia tsaii

Lysimachia tsaii, commonly known as the Chinese yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and trailing growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Lysimachia tsaii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
