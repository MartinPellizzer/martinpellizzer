Lonchocarpus paniculatus

Lonchocarpus paniculatus, commonly known as the yellow trumpet tree, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and compound leaves with three leaflets. The plant produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
