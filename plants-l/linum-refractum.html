Linum refractum

Linum refractum, commonly known as the brittle flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other species of flax. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pale blue or white flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Linum refractum is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments.
