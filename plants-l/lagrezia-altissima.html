Lagrezia altissima

Lagrezia altissima, commonly known as the tall clematis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Lagrezia altissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
