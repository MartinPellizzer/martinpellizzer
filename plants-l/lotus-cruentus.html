Lotus cruentus

Lotus cruentus, commonly known as the blood lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red petals that resemble blood, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shallow waters and has a distinctive floating leaf structure. Its flowers bloom in summer and are known for their vibrant color and ornamental value.

Lotus cruentus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
