Lycium minutiflorum

Lycium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, spiny leaves and produces yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its fruit is a small, red berry that contains several seeds.

Lycium minutiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
