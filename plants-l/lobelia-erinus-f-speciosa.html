Lobelia erinus f. speciosa

Lobelia erinus f. speciosa, commonly known as the showy lobelia, is a flowering plant characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is native to South America. The plant typically produces clusters of tubular or bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
