Lotus siliquosus

Lotus siliquosus, commonly known as the water lotus, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and support for aquatic life. Its root system anchors it to the muddy bottom of ponds and lakes.

Lotus siliquosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has cultural significance in many Asian countries.
