Lepanthes werneri

Lepanthes werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as yellow and red. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for cloud forest ecosystems.
