Lyonia trinidadensis

Lyonia trinidadensis, commonly known as Trinidad lily, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

Lyonia trinidadensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
