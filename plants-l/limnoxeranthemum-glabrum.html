Limnoxeranthemum glabrum

Limnoxeranthemum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth erinaceum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring, surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
