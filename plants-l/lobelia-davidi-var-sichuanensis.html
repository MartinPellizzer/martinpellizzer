Lobelia davidi var. sichuanensis

Lobelia davidi var. sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ecological benefits.
