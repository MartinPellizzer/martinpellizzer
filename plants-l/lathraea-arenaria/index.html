Lathraea Arenaria

Lathraea arenaria, commonly known as the sand orchid, is a rare and delicate plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. It thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to harsh, sun-exposed environments. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its small size and subtle appearance. This plant plays a vital role in its ecosystem by supporting local insect populations and contributing to soil stability. Its unique characteristics make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
