Leucadendron sericeum

Leucadendron sericeum, commonly known as the silver leaf cycad, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-blue leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and experienced gardeners.
