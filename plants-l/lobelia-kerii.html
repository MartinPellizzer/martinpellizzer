Lobelia kerii

Lobelia kerii, commonly known as the Sierra lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its alpine habitat.

Lobelia kerii is often found in meadows and open slopes at elevations above 8,000 feet.
