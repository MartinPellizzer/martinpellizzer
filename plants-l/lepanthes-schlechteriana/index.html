Lepanthes Schlechteriana

Lepanthes schlechteriana is a rare and enchanting orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. This delicate plant is known for its striking, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the cool, misty highlands. It thrives in shaded environments with high humidity and well-drained soil. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
