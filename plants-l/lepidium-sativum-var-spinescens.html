Lepidium sativum var. spinescens

Lepidium sativum var. spinescens, commonly known as prickly rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, green, spiny leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its seeds are oval and often have a slightly pungent flavor.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in certain culinary applications.
