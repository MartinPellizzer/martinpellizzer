Licaria rufotomentosa

Licaria rufotomentosa, commonly known as the red-haired licaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its hairy, reddish stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often appearing in shades of pink or purple.

Licaria rufotomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
