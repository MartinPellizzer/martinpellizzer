Leucas Ciliata Var. Oblongifolia

Leucas ciliata var. oblongifolia is a lesser-known but fascinating plant species native to parts of southern and southeastern asia. it belongs to the lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic herbs and medicinal properties. this variant is distinguished by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. due to its unique characteristics, it holds potential for both ornamental and medicinal use.
