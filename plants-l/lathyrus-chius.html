Lathyrus chius

Lathyrus chius, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, and it has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. Lathyrus chius is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Its seeds are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
