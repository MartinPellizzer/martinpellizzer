Lathyrus debilis

Lathyrus debilis, commonly known as the slender pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, climbing stems and small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grassy areas or along woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
