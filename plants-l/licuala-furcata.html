Licuala furcata

Licuala furcata, commonly known as the broad-leafed palm or the giant fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter. The plant has a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are palmately lobed and are often used in traditional crafts and as a roofing material.

Licuala furcata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
