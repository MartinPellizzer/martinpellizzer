Lotus argophyllus subsp. decorus

Lotus argophyllus subsp.

decorus, commonly known as the ornamental lotus, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white.

The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots are anchored in the sediment.

It is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in ornamental water gardens.
