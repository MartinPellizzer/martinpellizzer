Lasianthus palembanicus

Lasianthus palembanicus, commonly known as the Palembang lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
