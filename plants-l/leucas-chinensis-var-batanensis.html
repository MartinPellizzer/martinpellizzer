Leucas chinensis var. batanensis

Leucas chinensis var. batanensis, commonly known as Batan leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
