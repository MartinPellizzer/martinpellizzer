Leucojum hyemale var. flore-album

Leucojum hyemale var. flore-album, commonly known as the Christmas lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features striking white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late autumn to early winter, often around the holiday season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulb and are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrance and ability to naturalize over time.
