Luzula × schultzorum

Luzula × schultzorum, commonly known as the silvergrass or false sweet grass, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish to brownish flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Luzula × schultzorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful, grass-like foliage.
