Lantana brasiliensis var. septentrionalis

Lantana brasiliensis var. septentrionalis, commonly known as the northern lantana, is a shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats due to its adaptability.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm regions.
