Lobadium

Lobadium, commonly known as the giant lobelia, is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white or pink center. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are broad and lobed, contributing to its common name.

Lobadium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
