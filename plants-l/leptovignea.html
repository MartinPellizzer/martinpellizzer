Leptovignea

Leptovignea, commonly known as the slender legume, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, often delicate flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plants typically grow in tropical and subtropical regions, often as shrubs or herbs. Leptovignea species are known for their ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
