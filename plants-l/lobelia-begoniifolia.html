Lobelia begoniifolia

Lobelia begoniifolia, commonly known as the begonia-leaved lobelia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with a contrasting throat. The leaves are heart-shaped and resemble those of begonias, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden areas.
