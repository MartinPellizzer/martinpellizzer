Litsea floribunda

Litsea floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and aesthetic appearance.
