Lecythis densa

Lecythis densa, commonly known as the dense lecythium, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, smooth, dark green leaves. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a sticky, aromatic latex. Its bark is thick and often has a rough, fissured texture.

Lecythis densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
