Limnanthemum latifolium

Limnanthemum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf water violet, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is a member of the Violaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soil conditions. Its flowers bloom in spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
