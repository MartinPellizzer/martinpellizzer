Lactuca monocephala

Lactuca monocephala, commonly known as the single-headed lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual plant with a rosette of broad, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and slightly wavy. The plant produces a single, upright stem that bears small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. Its leaves are often used in salads and have a mild, slightly bitter taste.

Lactuca monocephala is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various soil conditions.
