Lebeckia spinescens

Lebeckia spinescens, commonly known as the spiny lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spiny stems and leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a dense, spherical shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
