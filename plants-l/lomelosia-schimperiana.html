Lomelosia schimperiana

Lomelosia schimperiana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique beauty and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
