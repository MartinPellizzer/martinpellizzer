Livistona decora

Livistona decora, commonly known as the ornamental palm, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Livistona decora is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a statement plant in gardens.
