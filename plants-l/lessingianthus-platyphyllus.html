Lessingianthus platyphyllus

Lessingianthus platyphyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf lessingia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a flat, disc-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in dry grasslands and open areas.

Its foliage is generally lance-shaped with a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
