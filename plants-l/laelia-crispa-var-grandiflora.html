Laelia crispa var. grandiflora

Laelia crispa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes mountains.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it prefers bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
