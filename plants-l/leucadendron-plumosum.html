Leucadendron plumosum

Leucadendron plumosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, feathery foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost ethereal appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flower heads that appear in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
