Lepidanche compositarum

Lepidanche compositarum, commonly known as the composite lepidanche, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets arranged in a head-like structure. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that develop into seed heads. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant's structure and growth habits make it well adapted to arid environments.
