Lagerstroemia batitinan

Lagerstroemia batitinan, commonly known as the Philippine crape myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its showy, long-lasting bract clusters that resemble flowers. The bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or pinkish hue, especially when young.
