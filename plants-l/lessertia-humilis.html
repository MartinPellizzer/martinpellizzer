Lessertia humilis

Lessertia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf milkweed, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lessertia humilis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
