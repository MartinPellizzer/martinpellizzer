Labisia pumila var. alata

Labisia pumila var. alata, commonly known as the winged labisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive wing-like structures on the stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
