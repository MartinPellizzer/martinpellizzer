Littonia baudii

Littonia baudii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub, often with a compact and bushy habit. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its native environment.

Littonia baudii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
