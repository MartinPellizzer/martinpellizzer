Lithops villetii subsp. kennedyi

Lithops villetii subsp.

kennedyi, commonly known as the "Pebble Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance, with two fleshy leaves that resemble stones.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. Lithops villetii subsp.

kennedyi is well adapted to withstand harsh conditions, including extreme temperatures and limited rainfall.
