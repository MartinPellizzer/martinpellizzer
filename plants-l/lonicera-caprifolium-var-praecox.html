Lonicera caprifolium var. praecox

Lonicera caprifolium var. praecox, commonly known as early honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, appearing in early spring before the leaves fully develop. Its fruit is a red, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
