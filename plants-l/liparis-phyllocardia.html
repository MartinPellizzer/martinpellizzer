Liparis phyllocardia

Liparis phyllocardia, commonly known as the heartleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Liparis phyllocardia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
