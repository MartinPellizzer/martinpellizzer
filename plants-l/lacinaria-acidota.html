Lacinaria acidota

Lacinaria acidota, commonly known as the sour thorn, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Lacinaria acidota is known for its ability to withstand drought conditions due to its deep root system.
