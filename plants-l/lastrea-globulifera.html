Lastrea globulifera

Lastrea globulifera, commonly known as the globular lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and dense clusters of small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, round fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
