Leucas decurvata

Leucas decurvata, commonly known as the curly leucas, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the summer months.
