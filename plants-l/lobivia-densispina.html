Lobivia densispina

Lobivia densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined lobivia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It is a low-growing, succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its arid environment. Lobivia densispina is well adapted to extreme drought conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscape designs.
