Laurus grandis

Laurus grandis, commonly known as sweet bay or Turkish bay leaf, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are aromatic and widely used in cooking. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, fleshy berries. Its leaves contain essential oils, which contribute to its strong fragrance and medicinal properties.

Laurus grandis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental and culinary plant.
