Lepisorus macrosphaerus f. maximus

Lepisorus macrosphaerus f. maximus, commonly known as the giant moss fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed moss fern.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and leathery leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along streams. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that form dense mats.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
