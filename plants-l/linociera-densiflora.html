Linociera densiflora

Linociera densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered linociera, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly pubescent surface.

Linociera densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
