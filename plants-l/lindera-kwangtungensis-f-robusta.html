Lindera kwangtungensis f. robusta

Lindera kwangtungensis f. robusta, commonly known as the robust lindera, is a variant of the kwangtung lindera, native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and aromatic foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth margin and a slightly glossy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in terminal panicles, and it produces yellowish-orange berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
