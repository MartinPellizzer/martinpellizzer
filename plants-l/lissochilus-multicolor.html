Lissochilus multicolor

Lissochilus multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored lissochilus, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in cloud forests or rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
