Lepanthes horichii

Lepanthes horichii, commonly known as the Horichii Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its humid, shaded habitat.

Lepanthes horichii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and ornamental value.
