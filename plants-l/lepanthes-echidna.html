Lepanthes echidna

Lepanthes echidna, commonly known as the echidna orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with intricate patterns.

Lepanthes echidna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
