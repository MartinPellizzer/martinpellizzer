Leucopogon linifolius

Leucopogon linifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly referred to as the heath or heather family. This evergreen shrub typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and sandhill habitats. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to withstand salty and windy conditions.
