Lepanthes nana

Lepanthes nana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Lepanthes nana has a short, compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and a single, narrow leaf.

Its flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a touch of elegance to its natural habitat.
