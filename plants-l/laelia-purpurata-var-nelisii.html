Laelia purpurata var. nelisii

Laelia purpurata var. nelisii, commonly known as the Nelisii orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and red flower spikes, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a compact, upright orchid with a pseudobulb structure typical of the Cymbidium section. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it prefers cool, bright environments with good air circulation.

This variety is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
