Lycium chanar

Lycium chanar, commonly known as Indian wolfberry, is a shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow-orange berries that are rich in antioxidants. Lycium chanar typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
