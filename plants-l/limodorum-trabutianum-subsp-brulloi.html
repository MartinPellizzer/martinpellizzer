Limodorum trabutianum subsp. brulloi

Limodorum trabutianum subsp.

brulloi, commonly known as the Brullo's lily, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pale greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or rocky slopes. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
