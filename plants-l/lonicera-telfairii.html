Lonicera telfairii

Lonicera telfairii, commonly known as the Telfair honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy capsule that contains small, black seeds.

Lonicera telfairii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
