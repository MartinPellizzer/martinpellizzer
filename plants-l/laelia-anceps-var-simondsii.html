Laelia anceps var. simondsii

Laelia anceps var. simondsii, commonly known as the Simonds' laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in its native arid environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, and it thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
