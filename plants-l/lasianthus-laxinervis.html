Lasianthus laxinervis

Lasianthus laxinervis, commonly known as the slender-veined lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
