Legocia

Legocia, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a tropical flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, edible berries that are used in traditional medicine. Legocia thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and medicinal properties make it a valued plant in both horticulture and herbal practices.
