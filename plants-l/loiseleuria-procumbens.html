Loiseleuria procumbens

Loiseleuria procumbens, commonly known as the creeping marsh orchid, is a flowering plant native to wetland and marshy habitats in Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes, and it has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Loiseleuria procumbens thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in peat bogs and fens.
