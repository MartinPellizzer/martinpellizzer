Loeseneriella rubiginosa var. angustifolia

Loeseneriella rubiginosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved redstem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that are reddish in color, especially when mature. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters during the summer months, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
