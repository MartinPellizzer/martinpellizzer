Ligusticum chuanxiong

Ligusticum chuanxiong, commonly known as Sichuan lovage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, rhizomatous root system. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive fern-like appearance and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in umbels. Its rhizomes are the primary part used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ligusticum chuanxiong is also valued in culinary applications for its pungent flavor and aromatic properties.
