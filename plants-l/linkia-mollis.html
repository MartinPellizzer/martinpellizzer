Linkia mollis

Linkia mollis, commonly known as the silky orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers warm, humid environments and well-draining soil.

Linkia mollis is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
