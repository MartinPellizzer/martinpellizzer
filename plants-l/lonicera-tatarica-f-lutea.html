Lonicera tatarica f. lutea

Lonicera tatarica f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually yellow-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in hue, blooming in early spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant foliage and floral display.
