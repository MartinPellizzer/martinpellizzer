Lightfootia erecta

Lightfootia erecta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with a contrasting throat. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

Lightfootia erecta prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
