Lauradia alpestris

Lauradia alpestris, commonly known as the alpine laurel.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forested slopes.

Lauradia alpestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
