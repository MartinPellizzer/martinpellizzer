Lavatera olbia proles hispida

Lavatera olbia proles hispida, commonly known as the woolly lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered in fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
