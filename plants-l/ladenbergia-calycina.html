Ladenbergia calycina

Ladenbergia calycina, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The tree produces striking orange-red flowers in clusters, which bloom in summer. Its bark is smooth and grayish, often marked with fissures.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
