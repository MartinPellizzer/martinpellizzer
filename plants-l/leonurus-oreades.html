Leonurus oreades

Leonurus oreades, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

Leonurus oreades thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow environments.
