Lobivia famatinensis var. cinnabarina

Lobivia famatinensis var. cinnabarina, commonly known as the Cinnabar Lobivia, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a prized addition to succulent collections.
