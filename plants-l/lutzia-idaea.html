Lutzia idaea

Lutzia idaea, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by spiny, leaf-like bracts. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for coastal gardens and rockeries.
