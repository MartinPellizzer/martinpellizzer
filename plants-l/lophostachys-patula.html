Lophostachys patula

Lophostachys patula, commonly known as desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, round seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, helping it store water in its arid environment.
