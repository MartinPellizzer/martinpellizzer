Leandra regnellii

Leandra regnellii, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Leandra regnellii prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
