Laelia lyonsii

Laelia lyonsii, commonly known as the Lyons' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, dark red or purple blooms with yellow markings, which attract pollinators. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and moderate humidity.

Laelia lyonsii is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
