Laelia alaorii f. dietliana

Laelia alaorii f. dietliana, commonly known as the Dietliana Laelia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a mix of pink and white hues. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, showcasing its ornamental value. It prefers cool, humid conditions with bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
