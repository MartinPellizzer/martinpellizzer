Lastrea quinquangularis

Lastrea quinquangularis, commonly known as the five-angled lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, angular stem that branches into multiple offsets, giving it a unique, geometric appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking form and low maintenance requirements.
