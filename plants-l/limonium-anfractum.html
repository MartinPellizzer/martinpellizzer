Limonium anfractum

Limonium anfractum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky flower heads that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-blue, often covered in fine hairs.

Limonium anfractum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
