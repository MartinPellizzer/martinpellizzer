Lepanthes refracta

Lepanthes refracta, commonly known as the refractive orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests, where it thrives on tree trunks or branches. Its flowers are usually small and showy, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Lepanthes refracta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty and botanical interest.
