Lapeirousia purpureolutea

Lapeirousia purpureolutea, commonly known as the purple flame orchid, is a striking South African bulbous perennial belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is native to the Western Cape region and is characterized by its vibrant purple and orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features a short, underground corm and produces grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are tubular and have a unique, flame-like appearance, with a prominent spur at the base.

Lapeirousia purpureolutea thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
