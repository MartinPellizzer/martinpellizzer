Lasianthus strigosus var. chinensis

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and have a rough texture due to the presence of fine hairs.
