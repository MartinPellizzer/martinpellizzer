Laburnum weldenii

Laburnum weldenii, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Laburnum weldenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
