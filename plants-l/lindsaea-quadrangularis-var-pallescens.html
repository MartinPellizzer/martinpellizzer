Lindsaea quadrangularis var. pallescens

Lindsaea quadrangularis var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale lindsaea, is a rare epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are often pale green in color. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are wiry and branched, supporting its climbing habit.
