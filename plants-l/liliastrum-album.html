Liliastrum album

Liliastrum album, commonly known as the white liliastrum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Liliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Liliastrum album is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and fragrance.
