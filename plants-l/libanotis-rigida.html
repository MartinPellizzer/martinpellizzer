Libanotis rigida

Libanotis rigida, commonly known as the rigid bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Libanotis rigida is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
