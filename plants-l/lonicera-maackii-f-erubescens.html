Lonicera maackii f. erubescens

Lonicera maackii f. erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to East Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green with a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its berries are orange-red and persist into winter, providing food for birds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
