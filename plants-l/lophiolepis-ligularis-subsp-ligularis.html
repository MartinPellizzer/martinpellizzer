Lophiolepis ligularis subsp. ligularis

Lophiolepis ligularis subsp.

ligularis, commonly known as the slender leaved lophiolepis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
