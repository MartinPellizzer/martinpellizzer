Lobelia filiformis var. natalensis

Lobelia filiformis var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal slender lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged alternately along the stem.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
