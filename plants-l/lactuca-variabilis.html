Lactuca variabilis

Lactuca variabilis, commonly known as variable lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its rosette of spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Lactuca variabilis is a hardy annual that can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
