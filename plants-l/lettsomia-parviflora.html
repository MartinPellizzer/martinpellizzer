Lettsomia parviflora

Lettsomia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered lettsomia, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers with five petals, blooming during the wet season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Lettsomia parviflora is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.
