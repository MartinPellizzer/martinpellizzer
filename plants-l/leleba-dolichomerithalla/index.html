Leleba Dolichomerithalla

Dolichomerithra leleba, scientifically known as Leleba dolichomerithalla, is a rare and enigmatic species of sea snail found in the tropical Indo-Pacific region. This gastropod belongs to the family Rissoidae and is characterized by its elongated, spiral shell with intricate surface patterns. Despite its striking appearance, very little is known about its ecological role and life cycle due to its elusive nature and limited distribution. The species is often found in shallow coastal waters, where it thrives in sandy or muddy substrates. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this delicate organism from threats posed by habitat degradation and climate change.
