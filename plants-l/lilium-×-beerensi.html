Lilium × beerensi

Lilium × beerensi, commonly known as the Beerens' lily, is a hybrid species of lily native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers bloom in late summer, adding a striking visual element to gardens and landscapes.
