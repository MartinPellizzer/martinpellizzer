Lysimachia nanchuanensis

Lysimachia nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan bugleweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
