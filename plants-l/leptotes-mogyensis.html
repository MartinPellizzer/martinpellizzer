Leptotes mogyensis

Leptotes mogyensis, commonly known as the Mogyen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its inflorescence produces clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Leptotes mogyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
