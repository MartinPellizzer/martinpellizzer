Leucas mukerjiana

Leucas mukerjiana, commonly known as Mukerji's leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional medicine practices.
