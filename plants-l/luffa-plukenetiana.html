Luffa plukenetiana

Luffa plukenetiana, commonly known as the African luffa, is a tropical vine native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and gourds. The plant produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers, followed by hairy, spiny fruits that mature into fibrous sponges. The fibrous interior of the fruit is used as a natural sponge for bathing and exfoliation.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
