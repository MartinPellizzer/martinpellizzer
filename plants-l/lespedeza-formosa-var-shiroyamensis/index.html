Lespedeza Formosa Var. Shiroyamensis

Lespedeza formosa var. shiroyamensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Japan. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its striking pink to purple blooms. This variant is particularly sought after by botanists and gardeners for its unique coloration and ornamental value. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest in both scientific research and horticultural circles.
