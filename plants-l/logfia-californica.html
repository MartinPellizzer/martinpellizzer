Logfia californica

Logfia californica, commonly known as the California logfia, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of California.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open coastal habitats.
