Lepidium × bupleurifolium

Lepidium × bupleurifolium, commonly known as the hybrid wall cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and alternate leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, typical of the mustard family. Its fruits are cylindrical siliques that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This hybrid species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
