Linostoma dinizii

Linostoma dinizii, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil, and it often forms symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi.

Linostoma dinizii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
