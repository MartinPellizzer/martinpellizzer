Llagunoa nitida var. prunifolia

Llagunoa nitida var. prunifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf plum tree, is a variant of the llagunoa nitida species native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in taste to plums. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production in suitable climates.
