Leucocoryne fuscostriata

Leucocoryne fuscostriata, commonly known as the black-striped lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces tall, erect stems bearing white or pale pink flowers with distinctive black or dark brown streaks. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
