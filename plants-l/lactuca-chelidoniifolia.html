Lactuca chelidoniifolia

Lactuca chelidoniifolia, commonly known as the meadow lettuce or yellow lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of elongated, narrow leaves that are often tinged with yellow or red. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Lactuca chelidoniifolia is often found in moist meadows, along stream banks, and in disturbed soils.
