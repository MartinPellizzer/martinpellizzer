Lonchocarpus felipei

Lonchocarpus felipei, commonly known as Felipe's legume, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many leguminous plants known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
