Leobordea magnifica

Leobordea magnifica, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, featuring vibrant hues of purple, pink, and white. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid environment. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservatories.
