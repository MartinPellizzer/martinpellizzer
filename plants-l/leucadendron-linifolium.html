Leucadendron linifolium

Leucadendron linifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf cycad, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces large, cone-like inflorescences that are often brightly colored, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
