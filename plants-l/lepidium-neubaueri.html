Lepidium neubaueri

Lepidium neubaueri, commonly known as Neubauer's cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, edible leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland areas.

Lepidium neubaueri is valued for its nutritional content and is used in some traditional cuisines.
