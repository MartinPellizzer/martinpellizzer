Lobelia stenosiphon

Lobelia stenosiphon, commonly known as narrow-siphon lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Lobelia stenosiphon is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
