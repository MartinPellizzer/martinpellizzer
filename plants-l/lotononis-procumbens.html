Lotononis procumbens

Lotononis procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.
