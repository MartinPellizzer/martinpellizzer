Litobrochia affinis

Litobrochia affinis, commonly known as the rock brochidium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. This plant is characterized by its globular shape and spiny stems, which help it conserve water in its harsh environment. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in the spring.
