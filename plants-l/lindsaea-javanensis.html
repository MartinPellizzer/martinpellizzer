Lindsaea javanensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a lacy pattern.
