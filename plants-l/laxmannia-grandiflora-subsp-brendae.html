Laxmannia grandiflora subsp. brendae

Laxmannia grandiflora subsp.

brendae, commonly known as the Brendae's laxmannia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
