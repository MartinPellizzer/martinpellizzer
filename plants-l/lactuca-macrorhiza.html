Lactuca macrorhiza

Lactuca macrorhiza, commonly known as the giant lettuce or swamp lettuce, is a perennial herb native to wetland areas of North America.

It features a rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, dry fruits containing seeds. It thrives in moist soils and is often found near streams, marshes, and ponds.

Lactuca macrorhiza is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
