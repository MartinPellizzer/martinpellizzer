Libertia cranwelliae

Libertia cranwelliae, commonly known as the Cranwell's lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features strap-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, often blue or purple, flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
