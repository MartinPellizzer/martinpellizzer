Lepanthes myiophora

Lepanthes myiophora, commonly known as the Myiophore Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale green in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral appearance.
