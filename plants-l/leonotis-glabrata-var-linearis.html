Leonotis glabrata var. linearis

Leonotis glabrata var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lion's tail, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-yellow flowers that resemble a lion's tail. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
