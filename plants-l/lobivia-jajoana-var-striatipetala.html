Lobivia jajoana var. striatipetala

Lobivia jajoana var. striatipetala, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive zebra-like pattern of dark green and light green stripes. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
