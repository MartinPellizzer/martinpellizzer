Limeum telephioides var. telephioides

Limeum telephioides var. telephioides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has a compact, low-growing habit. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its common name reflects the appearance of its foliage, which resembles the leaves of the dandelion.
