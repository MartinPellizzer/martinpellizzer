Lobelia novella

Lobelia novella, commonly known as the New Zealand bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
