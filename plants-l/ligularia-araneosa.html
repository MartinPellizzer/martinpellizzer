Ligularia araneosa

Ligularia araneosa, commonly known as the spiderwort ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and distinctive, dangling yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its striking foliage and unique inflorescence make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ligularia araneosa is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
