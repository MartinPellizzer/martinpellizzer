Lolium Arundinaceum Subsp. Mediterraneum

Lolium arundinaceum subsp. mediterraneum is a hardy perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in temperate climates and is commonly found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. This plant is known for its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It plays an important role in ecological systems by providing forage for livestock and habitat for small organisms. Its resilience and adaptability make it a valuable species in both agricultural and natural settings.
