Ligularia gigantissima

Ligularia gigantissima, commonly known as the giant ligularia, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a dark green color and prominent veins. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic foliage.

Due to its size and appearance, it is a popular choice for water gardens and shaded borders.
