Linaria osyris

Linaria osyris, commonly known as the Osyris Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Linaria osyris prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
