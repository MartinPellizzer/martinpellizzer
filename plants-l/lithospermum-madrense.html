Lithospermum madrense

Lithospermum madrense, commonly known as Madrean lithosperm, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces striking red or purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Lithospermum madrense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
