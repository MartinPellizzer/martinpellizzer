Lisianthius macrophyllus

Lisianthius macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf lisianthius, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in native habitats.
