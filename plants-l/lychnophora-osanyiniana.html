Lychnophora osanyiniana

Lychnophora osanyiniana, commonly known as the Nigerian mint, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded areas in tropical forests.

Its aromatic properties make it valuable in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
