Lotononis dregeana

Lotononis dregeana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
