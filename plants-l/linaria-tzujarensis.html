Linaria tzujarensis

Linaria tzujarensis, commonly known as the Tzujar Valley horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Tzujar Valley in Armenia.

It belongs to the genus Linaria, which includes various species of annual and perennial flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its distinctive horn-shaped flowers, which are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
