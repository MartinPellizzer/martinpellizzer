Livistona nitida

Livistona nitida, commonly known as the slender cabbage tree, is a species of palm native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are often used in traditional Aboriginal crafts. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and open woodlands. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round fruits.

Livistona nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
