Ligularia leesicotal

Ligularia leesicotal, commonly known as the mountain ligularia, is a perennial herb native to high-altitude regions of East Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration and a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique species within the Asteraceae family.
