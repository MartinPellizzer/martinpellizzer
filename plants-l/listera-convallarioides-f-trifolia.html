Listera convallarioides f. trifolia

Listera convallarioides f. trifolia, commonly known as the three-leafed basket lily, is a variant of the basket lily species native to the Appalachian region of the United States.

Its foliage is characterized by a trifoliate structure, with three leaflets arranged symmetrically.
