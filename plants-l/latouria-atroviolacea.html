Latouria atroviolacea

Latouria atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark violet lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Latouria atroviolacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
