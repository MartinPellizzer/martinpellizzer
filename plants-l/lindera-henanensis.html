Lindera henanensis

Lindera henanensis, commonly known as Henan cinnamon or Henan lindera, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible.

Lindera henanensis is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
