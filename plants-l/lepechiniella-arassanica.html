Lepechiniella arassanica

Lepechiniella arassanica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandwort, is a small, mat-forming perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant has slender, ascending stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.
