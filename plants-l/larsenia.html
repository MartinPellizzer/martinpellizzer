Larsenia

Larsenia, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Liliaceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often fragrant flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. They are typically found in mountainous regions and are adapted to cool, moist climates. Larsenia species are perennial herbs that often grow in rocky or forested habitats.

The genus includes several species, each with unique floral structures and growth habits.
