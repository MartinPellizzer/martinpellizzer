Limonium lessingianum

Limonium lessingianum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky seed heads after flowering. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often growing in sandy or rocky habitats.

Limonium lessingianum is a popular choice for gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
