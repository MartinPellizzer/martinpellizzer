Listrostachys margaritae

Listrostachys margaritae, commonly known as the pearl flower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a delicate appearance. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
