Lasiagrostis elongata

Lasiagrostis elongata, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually terminal and elongated.

Lasiagrostis elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens for its ecological benefits.
