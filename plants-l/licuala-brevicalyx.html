Licuala brevicalyx

Licuala brevicalyx, commonly known as the broad-leafed pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, characterized by its large, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
