Lotoxalis frutescens

Lotoxalis frutescens, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its small, often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.
