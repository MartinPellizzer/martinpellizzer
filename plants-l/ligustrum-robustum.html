Ligustrum robustum

Ligustrum robustum, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Ligustrum robustum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
