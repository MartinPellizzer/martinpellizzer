Litsea nunkao-tahangensis

Litsea nunkao-tahangensis, commonly known as the Nunkao-Tahangensis-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in parts of Myanmar and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, thriving in humid and temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the understory vegetation in its native ecosystems.
