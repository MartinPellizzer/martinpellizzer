Lagenocypsela latifolia

Lagenocypsela latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed cypsela, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

It is native to certain regions of Africa and is typically found in grassland or open woodland habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellowish or pale pink coloration.
