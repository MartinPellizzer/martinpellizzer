Leptosiphonium

Leptosiphonium, commonly known as the small-sword fern, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Leptosiphonaceae.

These plants are typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. They are characterized by their slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that often display a range of colors. The leaves are usually simple and alternate, contributing to their distinctive appearance.

Leptosiphonium species are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their unique floral displays and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
