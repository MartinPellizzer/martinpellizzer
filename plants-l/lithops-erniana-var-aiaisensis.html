Lithops erniana var. aiaisensis

Lithops erniana var. aiaisensis, commonly known as the Aiais Lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pair of fleshy, leaf-like structures that resemble stones, which help it blend into its arid environment. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xerophytic collections.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among succulent enthusiasts.
