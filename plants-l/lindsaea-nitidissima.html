Lindsaea nitidissima

Lindsaea nitidissima, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble lace. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with numerous aerial roots that anchor it to trees or other surfaces. Its fronds are usually dark green with a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance.

Lindsaea nitidissima thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and indirect light.
