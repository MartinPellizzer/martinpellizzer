Lucilia linearifolia

Lucilia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf lucilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is often found in dune environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing sand.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to harsh coastal conditions.
