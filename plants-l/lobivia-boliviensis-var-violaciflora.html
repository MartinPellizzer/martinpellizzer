Lobivia boliviensis var. violaciflora

Lobivia boliviensis var. violaciflora, commonly known as the Violet-flowered Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of Bolivia.

It features compact, rounded stems that often branch out to form a low-growing shrub. The plant produces striking violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
