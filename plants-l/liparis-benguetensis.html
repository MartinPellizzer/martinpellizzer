Liparis benguetensis

Liparis benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming during the dry season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
