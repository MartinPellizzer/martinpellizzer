Leonurus glaucescens var. deminutus

Leonurus glaucescens var. deminutus, commonly known as the lesser hedgehog plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
