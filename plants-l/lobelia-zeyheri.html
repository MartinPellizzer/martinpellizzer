Lobelia zeyheri

Lobelia zeyheri, commonly known as Zeyher's lobelia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
