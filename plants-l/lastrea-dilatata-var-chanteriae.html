Lastrea dilatata var. chanteriae

Lastrea dilatata var. chanteriae, commonly known as the Chanter's lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
