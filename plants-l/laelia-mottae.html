Laelia mottae

Laelia mottae, commonly known as the Mott's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers, which are often pink or purple in color. Laelia mottae thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
