Ludwigia ramosa

Ludwigia ramosa, commonly known as the red Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers and dense, branching foliage. The plant typically grows in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, such as marshes and slow-moving streams. Its leaves are often used in aquariums for their ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.

Ludwigia ramosa is also valued for its role in wetland ecosystems, contributing to water filtration and biodiversity.
