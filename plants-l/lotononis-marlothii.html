Lotononis marlothii

Lotononis marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
