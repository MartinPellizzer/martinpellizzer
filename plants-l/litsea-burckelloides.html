Litsea burckelloides

Litsea burckelloides, commonly known as the Burckel's litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, smooth, yellowish fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
