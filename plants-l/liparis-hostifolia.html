Liparis hostifolia

Liparis hostifolia, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, evergreen leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Liparis hostifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
