Liparis celebica

Liparis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian island of Celebes.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, slender stems and have three sepals and three petals.

Liparis celebica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
