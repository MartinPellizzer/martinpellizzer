Landolphia petersiana var. crassifolia

Landolphia petersiana var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved African morning glory, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically broad and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
