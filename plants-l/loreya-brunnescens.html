Loreya brunnescens

Loreya brunnescens, commonly known as the brown leaved loreya, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are thick and leathery, often covered in a fine, brownish pubescence, giving it its common name.

Loreya brunnescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
