Lantana hirsuta

Lantana hirsuta, commonly known as hairy lantana, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has hairy, rough-textured leaves and stems, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a common ornamental plant.

Lantana hirsuta is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
