Leucaena confertiflora

Leucaena confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflower leucaena, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has compound leaves with numerous small, oval leaflets arranged in pairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or yellowish flowers that are often fragrant. Its pods are slender and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Leucaena confertiflora is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and use in agroforestry systems.
