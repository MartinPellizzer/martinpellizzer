Lantana camara f. mutabilis

Lantana camara f. mutabilis, commonly known as the variegated lantana, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that change color from yellow to orange to red as they mature, creating a striking visual display. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Lantana camara f. mutabilis is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
