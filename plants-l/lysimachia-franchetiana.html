Lysimachia franchetiana

Lysimachia franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese waterweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as stream banks and marshy areas. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lysimachia franchetiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
