Lasianthus attenuatus var. minor

Lasianthus attenuatus var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaved lasianthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with four petals and prominent stamens. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
