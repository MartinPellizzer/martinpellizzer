Lindernia capensis

Lindernia capensis, commonly known as the common lindernia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, tubular, violet or purple flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
