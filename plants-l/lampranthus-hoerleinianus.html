Lampranthus hoerleinianus

Lampranthus hoerleinianus, commonly known as the Red-hot Poker, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has fleshy, cylindrical stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and vibrant floral display make it a standout in arid landscapes.
