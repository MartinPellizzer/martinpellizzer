Libyella cyrenaica

Libyella cyrenaica, commonly known as the Cyrenaica lily, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its succulent stems and leaves.

Botanically, it belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure.
