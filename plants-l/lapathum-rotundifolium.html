Lapathum rotundifolium

Lapathum rotundifolium, commonly known as round-leaved woundwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, rounded leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive, slightly lobed appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Lapathum rotundifolium is often found in disturbed soils and can be a common sight in moist meadows and along forest edges.
