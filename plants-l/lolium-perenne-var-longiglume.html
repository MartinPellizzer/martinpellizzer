Lolium perenne var. longiglume

Lolium perenne var. longiglume, commonly known as the tall brome, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its forage quality and ecological role in stabilizing soil.
