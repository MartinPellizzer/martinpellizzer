Lobivia chrysochete var. hystrix

Lobivia chrysochete var. hystrix, commonly known as the golden crown lobivia, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to yellowish spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
