Licuala muelleri

Licuala muelleri, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other familiar species like taro and calla lilies. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as a natural dye.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental beauty and practical uses.
