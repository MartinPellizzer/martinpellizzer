Leucanthemopsis Alpina

Leucanthemopsis alpina is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its elegant white petals and yellow centers, which resemble small daisies. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is now considered endangered in many parts of its range. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
