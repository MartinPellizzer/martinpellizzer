Leachiella meloformis

Leachiella meloformis, commonly known as the melon-like orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive melon-like scent, which is a characteristic feature of the species.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish.
