Laurus sailyana

Laurus sailyana, commonly known as the Salyana laurel, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and scent, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in aromatherapy.
