Liparis nervosa var. formosana

Liparis nervosa var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to white flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
