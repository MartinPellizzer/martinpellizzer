Ligusticum actaeifolium

Ligusticum actaeifolium, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features aromatic, pinnately compound leaves with a distinct licorice-like scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ligusticum actaeifolium thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
