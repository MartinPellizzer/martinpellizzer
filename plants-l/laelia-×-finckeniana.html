Laelia × finckeniana

Laelia × finckeniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a captivating member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a hybrid between Laelia and Brassavola species, resulting in a plant that combines the best traits of both parents. This orchid is prized for its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and garden settings.
