Litchi chinensis subsp. chinensis

Litchi chinensis subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese litchi, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China. It produces large, round fruits with a thick, rough red outer husk enclosing a sweet, white, juicy aril.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pink, appearing before the fruiting season.

The fruit is rich in vitamins and antioxidants, making it a popular fruit in Asian cuisine.
