Lepanthes culex

Lepanthes culex, commonly known as the Culex orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces pseudobulbs and has linear, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in shaded, humid environments. Lepanthes culex is often found in tropical rainforests, growing on tree trunks or in the understory where it can access the necessary moisture and light.

Its unique floral structure and elegant appearance make it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
