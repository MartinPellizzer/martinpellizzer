Lycaste powellii

Lycaste powellii, commonly known as Powell's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows on trees or shrubs, forming a dense, evergreen canopy. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lycaste powellii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
