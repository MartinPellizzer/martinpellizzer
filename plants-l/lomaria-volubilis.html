Lomaria volubilis

Lomaria volubilis, commonly known as the climbing lomaria, is a perennial vine native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is known for its vigorous climbing habit, often twining around supports or other vegetation. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Lomaria volubilis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to cover trellises or walls.
