Lathyrus aristatus

Lathyrus aristatus, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Lathyrus aristatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
