Lepidium gobicum

Lepidium gobicum, commonly known as goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and economically important plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. Goatweed is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its seeds are small and can be dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread efficiently in its habitat.
