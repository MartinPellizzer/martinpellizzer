Larrea Platycarpa

Larrea platycarpa is a hardy shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern united states and northern mexico. it is commonly known as the desert whitethorn due to its white flowers and thorny branches. this plant is well adapted to extreme drought conditions and can survive with minimal water. it plays an important role in stabilizing desert soils and providing habitat for wildlife. its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in desert ecosystems.
