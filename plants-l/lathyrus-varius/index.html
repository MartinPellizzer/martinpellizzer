Lathyrus Varius

Lathyrus varius is a striking member of the legume family known for its vibrant purple and white flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique spurred petals and delicate foliage make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. While it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes, it also holds cultural significance in some regions. Despite its beauty, Lathyrus varius requires careful attention to avoid overwatering, which can lead to root rot.
