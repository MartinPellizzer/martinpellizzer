Litsea cangyuanensis

Litsea cangyuanensis, commonly known as the Cangyuan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province, where it grows in humid, subtropical forests. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical berries that ripen to a dark red color.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
