Lantana × armata var. guianensis

Lantana × armata var. guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan lantana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
