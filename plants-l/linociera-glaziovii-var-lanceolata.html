Linociera glaziovii var. lanceolata

Linociera glaziovii var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf linociera, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
