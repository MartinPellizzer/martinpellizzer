Lygodesmia triquetra

Lygodesmia triquetra, commonly known as the three-angled lygodesmia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils.
