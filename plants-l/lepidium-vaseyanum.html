Lepidium vaseyanum

Lepidium vaseyanum, commonly known as Persian cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are pinnately lobed with a peppery aroma. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Lepidium vaseyanum is valued both for its culinary uses and its ornamental appeal.
