Liquidambar Cambodiana

Liquidambar cambodiana is a unique species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Cambodia and parts of Vietnam. It belongs to the altingiaceae family and is known for its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the fall. The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its ability to thrive in tropical climates makes it a popular choice for urban landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many outside its native region.
