Linociera ramiflora var. grandiflora

Linociera ramiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered linociera, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are usually opposite, oval-shaped, and have a smooth texture. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
