Ludwigia densiflora

Ludwigia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Ludwigia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland or aquatic environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often reddish in color. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are typically yellow or orange in hue.

Ludwigia densiflora is often cultivated in water gardens due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
