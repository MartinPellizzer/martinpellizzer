Litsea meghalayensis

Litsea meghalayensis, commonly known as the Meghalaya litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the northeastern region of India, particularly in the state of Meghalaya. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical and subtropical forests.
