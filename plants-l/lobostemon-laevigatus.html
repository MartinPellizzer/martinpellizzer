Lobostemon laevigatus

Lobostemon laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or near water sources.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
