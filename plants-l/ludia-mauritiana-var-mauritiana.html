Ludia mauritiana var. mauritiana

Ludia mauritiana var. mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Madagascar.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
