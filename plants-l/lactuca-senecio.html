Lactuca senecio

Lactuca senecio, commonly known as the Senecio lettuce, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to southern Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Lactuca senecio is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
