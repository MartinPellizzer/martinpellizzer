Lasianthus longifolius subsp. obtusifolius

Lasianthus longifolius subsp.

obtusifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved lasianthus, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its lanceolate to ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
