Lycopodium Complanatum F. Typicum

Lycopodium complanatum f. typicum is a rare and ancient fern known for its delicate, fan-like fronds. This plant belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. The form typicum refers to the typical or standard form of this species. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes.
