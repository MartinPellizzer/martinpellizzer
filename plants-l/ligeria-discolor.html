Ligeria discolor

Ligeria discolor, commonly known as the white-flowered ligeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its most distinguishing feature is the white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Ligeria discolor is often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops, thriving in well-drained soils.
