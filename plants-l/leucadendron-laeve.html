Leucadendron laeve

Leucadendron laeve, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a unique, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces fluffy, yellow or orange spherical flower heads that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a notable species in the Proteaceae family.
