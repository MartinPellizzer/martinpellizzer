Lepanthes machogaffensis

Lepanthes machogaffensis, commonly known as the Machogaffensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and thin, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
