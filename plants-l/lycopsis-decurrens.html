Lycopsis decurrens

Lycopsis decurrens, commonly known as the running lycopus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem.

Lycopsis decurrens is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or stream banks.
