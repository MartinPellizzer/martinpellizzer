Lysionotus himalayensis

Lysionotus himalayensis, commonly known as the Himalayan starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it often grows in rocky or gravelly soils.

Lysionotus himalayensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
