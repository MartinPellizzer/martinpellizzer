Lycianthes lysimachioides var. sinensis

Lycianthes lysimachioides var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese bitter apple, is a species native to China and belongs to the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by yellow or orange berries containing several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
