Laelia × timora

Laelia × timora, commonly known as the Timora orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the plant's foliage and flowering structure.

Laelia × timora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, humid environments.
