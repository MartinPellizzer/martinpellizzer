Leiothrix distichoclada f. bracteosa

Leiothrix distichoclada f. bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteate leiothrix, is a unique variant of the leiothrix genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive bracteate foliage, which consists of modified leaves that often resemble small, leaf-like structures. This plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, overlapping leaves that give it a lush, carpet-like appearance. The leaves are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture, enhancing its ornamental value.

It is well-suited for shaded environments and thrives in moist, well-drained soil conditions.
