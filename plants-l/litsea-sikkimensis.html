Litsea sikkimensis

Litsea sikkimensis, commonly known as Sikkim litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by edible, red drupes.

Litsea sikkimensis is often found in moist, shaded forest environments at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.
