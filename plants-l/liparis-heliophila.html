Liparis heliophila

Liparis heliophila, commonly known as the sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with a distinctive orchid-like structure, often in shades of yellow and orange. It is known for its ability to bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to the landscape.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.
