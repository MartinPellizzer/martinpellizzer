Lavatera olbia subsp. unguiculata

Lavatera olbia subsp.

unguiculata, commonly known as the slender lavatera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically ovate or elliptic, with a rough texture and a slightly serrated margin. This subspecies is often found in coastal and sandy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
