Lysiloma pueblense

Lysiloma pueblense, commonly known as the Pueblan ironwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Mexico.

Its leaves are compound, typically consisting of three to five oval-shaped leaflets.

Lysiloma pueblense is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in regions with low rainfall.
