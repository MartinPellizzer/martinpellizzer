Lunathyrium medogense

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.
