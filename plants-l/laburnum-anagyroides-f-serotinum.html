Laburnum anagyroides f. serotinum

Laburnum anagyroides f. serotinum, commonly known as the late-flowering golden chain, is a cultivar of the laburnum tree characterized by its delayed blooming period.

It features long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear later in the growing season compared to the typical variety. The plant has compound leaves composed of several small, oval-shaped leaflets. It is native to Europe and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its striking visual appeal and is relatively easy to maintain.
