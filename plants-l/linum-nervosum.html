Linum nervosum

Linum nervosum, commonly known as slender flax, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.

Linum nervosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
