Leersia nematostachya

Leersia nematostachya, commonly known as slender leersia, is a species of grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
