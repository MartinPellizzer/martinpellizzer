Lilium lancifolium var. roseum

Lilium lancifolium var. roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered tiger lily, is a variant of the tiger lily native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, rose-pink to red flowers with a distinctive orange-yellow blotch at the base. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear lance-shaped, dark green leaves. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
