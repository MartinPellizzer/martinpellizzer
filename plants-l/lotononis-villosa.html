Lotononis villosa

Lotononis villosa, commonly known as the woolly lotonon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy, cylindrical stems and produces small, pale blue or violet flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lotononis villosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
