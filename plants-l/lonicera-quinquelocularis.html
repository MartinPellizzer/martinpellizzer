Lonicera quinquelocularis

Lonicera quinquelocularis, commonly known as the five-petaled honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange, which bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera quinquelocularis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
