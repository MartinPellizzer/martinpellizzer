Lobelia campanuloides

Lobelia campanuloides, commonly known as the bell-shaped lobelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Lobelia campanuloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
