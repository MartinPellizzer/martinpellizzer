Lepidium banksii var. ovatum

Lepidium banksii var. ovatum, commonly known as the ovate cress, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and slender, branching stems. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate in shape, giving it its common name.

It is often found in moist, coastal environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
