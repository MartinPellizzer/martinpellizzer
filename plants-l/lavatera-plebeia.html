Lavatera plebeia

Lavatera plebeia, commonly known as the common lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a hairy texture.

Lavatera plebeia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
