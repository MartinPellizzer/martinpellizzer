Laelia × elegans var. incantans

Laelia × elegans var. incantans, commonly known as the Incantans Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a striking addition to any collection. It prefers cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and indirect light.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its floral display.
