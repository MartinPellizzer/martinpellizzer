Leea celebica

Leea celebica, commonly known as the Celebes Leea, is a tropical flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Leeaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellowish or greenish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
