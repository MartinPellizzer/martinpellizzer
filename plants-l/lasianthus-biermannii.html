Lasianthus biermannii

Lasianthus biermannii, commonly known as the Biermann's flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded forest undergrowth.

Its striking floral display makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
