Loeseneriella africana

Loeseneriella africana, commonly known as African orchidgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like arrangement of small, greenish-white flowers.

Loeseneriella africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
