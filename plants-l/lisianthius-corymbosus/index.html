Lisianthius Corymbosus

Lisianthius corymbosus is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to the southeastern United States, this plant thrives in moist, sunny environments along stream banks and wetlands. Its clusters of purple to pink blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown compared to more mainstream ornamental plants.
