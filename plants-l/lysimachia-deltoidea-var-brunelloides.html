Lysimachia deltoidea var. brunelloides

Lysimachia deltoidea var. brunelloides, commonly known as the Brunello's yellow archangel, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to spread through rhizomes.
