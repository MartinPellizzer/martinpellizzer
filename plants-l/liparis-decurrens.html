Liparis decurrens

Liparis decurrens, commonly known as the running orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors and thrives in humid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems that often trail or climb, supported by fibrous roots. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a subtle fragrance, and bloom in clusters along the stem.

Liparis decurrens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
