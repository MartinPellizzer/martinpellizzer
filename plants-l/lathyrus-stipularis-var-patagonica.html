Lathyrus stipularis var. patagonica

Lathyrus stipularis var. patagonica, commonly known as the Patagonian vetch, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
