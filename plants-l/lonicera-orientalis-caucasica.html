Lonicera orientalis caucasica

Lonicera orientalis caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
