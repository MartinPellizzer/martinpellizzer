Ligularia nudicaulis

Ligularia nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a terminal cluster, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is particularly notable for its vibrant coloration and texture, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
