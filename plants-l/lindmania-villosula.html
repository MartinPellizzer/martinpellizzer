Lindmania villosula

Lindmania villosula, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves form a rosette structure, creating a small microhabitat that supports various epiphytic organisms.
