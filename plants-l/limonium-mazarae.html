Limonium mazarae

Limonium mazarae, commonly known as the Maltese daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Limonium mazarae is a low-growing perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its blooms are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
