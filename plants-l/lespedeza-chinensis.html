Lespedeza chinensis

Lespedeza chinensis, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
