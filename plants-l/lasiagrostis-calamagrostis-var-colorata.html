Lasiagrostis calamagrostis var. colorata

Lasiagrostis calamagrostis var. colorata, commonly known as the colorful calamagrostis, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its striking, vividly colored foliage, which ranges from gold to red and purple, depending on the season and environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its slender, arching blades form dense clumps, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide texture and color in garden settings.
