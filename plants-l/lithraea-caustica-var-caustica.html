Lithraea caustica var. caustica

Lithraea caustica var. caustica, commonly known as the caustic lithraea, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers and has leathery, elliptical leaves. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is often found in coastal dunes.

The plant's common name reflects its historical use in traditional medicine, though it contains toxic compounds.
