Lotus × ucrainicus

Lotus × ucrainicus, commonly known as the Ukrainian lotus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are typical of the genus Lotus. The plant is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
