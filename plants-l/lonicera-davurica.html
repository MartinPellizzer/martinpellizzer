Lonicera davurica

Lonicera davurica, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange-red, typically blooming in late spring. Its berries are red and edible, often consumed by birds.

Lonicera davurica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
