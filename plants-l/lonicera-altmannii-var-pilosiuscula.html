Lonicera altmannii var. pilosiuscula

Lonicera altmannii var. pilosiuscula, commonly known as the hairy honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in the spring, typically in shades of yellow and orange. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
