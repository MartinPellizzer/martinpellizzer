Lithospermum medium

Lithospermum medium, commonly known as red root, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lithospermum medium is known for its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in rocky or dry soils.
