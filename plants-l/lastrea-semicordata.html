Lastrea semicordata

Lastrea semicordata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink, blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact growth and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
