Lycopodium vanikorense

It is a small, creeping plant with spore-bearing cones and is closely related to other members of the Lycopodiaceae family. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest undergrowth or on rocky substrates. Its conservation status is critical due to habitat loss and limited distribution.
