Loreia

Loreia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal remedies for their purported medicinal properties.

Loreia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
