Leptolobium brachystachyum

Leptolobium brachystachyum, commonly known as the short-stemmed leptolobium, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
