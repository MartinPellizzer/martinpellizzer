Linum elegans

Linum elegans, commonly known as the slender flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate blue to purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Linum elegans is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.
