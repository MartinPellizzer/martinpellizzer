Lapeirousia edulis

Lapeirousia edulis, commonly known as the edible cycad, is a unique plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant is notable for its ability to store water in its trunk, which helps it survive in arid conditions. It produces cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs.
