Lebeckia microphylla var. leptophylla

Lebeckia microphylla var. leptophylla, commonly known as the small-leaved lebeckia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
