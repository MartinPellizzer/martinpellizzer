Lapathum limosum

Lapathum limosum, commonly known as narrow-leaved bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, ascending stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. Its small, white to pale pink flowers appear in loose clusters and bloom from June to August.

Lapathum limosum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
