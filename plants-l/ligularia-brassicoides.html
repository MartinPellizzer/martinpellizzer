Ligularia brassicoides

Ligularia brassicoides, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and a coarse texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland gardens.

Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value in shaded landscapes.
