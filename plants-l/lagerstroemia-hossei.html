Lagerstroemia hossei

Lagerstroemia hossei, commonly known as the Chinese crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, appearing in summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are dark green and turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it a popular choice for landscape design.
