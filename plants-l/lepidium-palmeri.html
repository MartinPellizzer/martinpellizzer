Lepidium palmeri

Lepidium palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's cress, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces small, white or yellow flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and it has small, rounded seeds that are dispersed by wind.
