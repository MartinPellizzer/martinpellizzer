Laelia × dominiana

Laelia × dominiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia tenebrosa.

It is native to high-altitude regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in its native habitat.

Laelia × dominiana is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
