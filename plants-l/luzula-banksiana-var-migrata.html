Luzula banksiana var. migrata

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to greenish flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow, linear, and have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
