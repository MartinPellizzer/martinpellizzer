Lobelia kwangsiensis

Lobelia kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi lobelia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture.
