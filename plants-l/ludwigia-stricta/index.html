Ludwigia Stricta

Ludwigia stricta is a small, aquatic plant native to tropical regions of South America. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Known for its dense, bushy growth, it is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. The plant has bright green leaves that can turn red under certain light conditions. Its popularity among hobbyists makes it a common sight in both natural and artificial aquatic ecosystems.
