Liabum wurdackii

Liabum wurdackii, commonly known as the Wurdack's senna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are compound, with small oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The seeds are enclosed in a fibrous pod and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
