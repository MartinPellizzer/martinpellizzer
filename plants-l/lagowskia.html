Lagowskia

Lagowskia, commonly known as the "Garden Beauty" or "Lagowskia Daisy," is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, which bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and has a compact, bushy habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Lagowskia is native to South America and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
