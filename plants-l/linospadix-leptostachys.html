Linospadix leptostachys

Linospadix leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stalked spathiphyll, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and a spathe. Its flowers are small and clustered on the spadix, which is typically surrounded by a showy, petal-like spathe.

Linospadix leptostachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
