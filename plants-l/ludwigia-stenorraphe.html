Ludwigia stenorraphe

Ludwigia stenorraphe, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Ludwigia, is a perennial aquatic plant native to South America.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant features slender, ribbon-like leaves that are often bright red or orange, especially when exposed to strong light. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually yellow or green.

Ludwigia stenorraphe is popular in aquariums for its vibrant color and ease of care.
