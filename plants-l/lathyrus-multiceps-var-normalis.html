Lathyrus multiceps var. normalis

Lathyrus multiceps var. normalis, commonly known as the common lathyris, is a climbing legume native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces showy, pea-like flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a slender pod containing several small, angular seeds.

This species is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
