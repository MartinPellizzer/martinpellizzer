Lasianthus retosus

Lasianthus retosus, commonly known as the hairy leaved flame flower, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. Lasianthus retosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant flowers and ease of care.
