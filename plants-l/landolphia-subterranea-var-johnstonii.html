Landolphia subterranea var. johnstonii

Landolphia subterranea var. johnstonii, commonly known as the underground or subterranean African morning glory, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped leaves, and it produces large, woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
