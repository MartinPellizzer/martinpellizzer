Lecanopteris nieuwenhuisii

Lecanopteris nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuis's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in freshwater habitats such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating. Its rhizomes are creeping and help anchor it to the substrate.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.
