Lemmaphyllum carnosum subsp. microphyllum

Lemmaphyllum carnosum subsp.

microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, opposite leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its compact growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
