Lepanthes martineae

Lepanthes martineae, commonly known as the Martineae orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Lepanthes martineae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
