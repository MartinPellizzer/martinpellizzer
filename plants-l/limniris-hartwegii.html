Limniris hartwegii

Limniris hartwegii, commonly known as the Hartweg's water lily, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white to pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are anchored in the sediment at the bottom of water bodies.

Limniris hartwegii plays an important role in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
