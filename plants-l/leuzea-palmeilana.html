Leuzea palmeilana

Leuzea palmeilana, commonly known as the palm-like leuzea, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves that resemble those of a palm tree. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
