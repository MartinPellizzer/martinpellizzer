Lissochilus clitellifer

Lissochilus clitellifer, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and multiple stems that bear lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are often pale green or yellowish in color.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique appearance and fragrance.
