Ligularia deltoidea

Ligularia deltoidea, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a dramatic and lush appearance. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Due to its striking foliage, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in shaded gardens.
