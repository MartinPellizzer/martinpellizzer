Lindsaea novoguineensis

It is native to the tropical regions of New Guinea and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
