Lolium temulentum subsp. oliganthum

Lolium temulentum subsp.

oliganthum, commonly known as the slender ryegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its adaptability, it can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
