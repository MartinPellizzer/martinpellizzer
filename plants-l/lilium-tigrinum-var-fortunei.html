Lilium tigrinum var. fortunei

Lilium tigrinum var. fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's tiger lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers with distinctive dark red spots, resembling a tiger's pattern. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in woodland or meadow environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
