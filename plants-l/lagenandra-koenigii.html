Lagenandra koenigii

Lagenandra koenigii, commonly known as the Indian bay leaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern India.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, ovate leaves that are used extensively in Indian cuisine. The plant belongs to the family Lauraceae and is closely related to other aromatic plants like cinnamon and laurel. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive fragrance and medicinal properties.

Lagenandra koenigii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both culinary and traditional medicinal purposes.
