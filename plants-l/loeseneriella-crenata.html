Loeseneriella crenata

Loeseneriella crenata, commonly known as the crested sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to survive in arid conditions.

Loeseneriella crenata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
