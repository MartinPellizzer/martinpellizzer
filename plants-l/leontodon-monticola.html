Leontodon monticola

Leontodon monticola, commonly known as the mountain leontodon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in alpine and rocky habitats. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in composite heads. It is often found in open, sunny environments at high altitudes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a characteristic species of mountainous regions.
