Litsea nitens

Litsea nitens, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule containing several seeds.

Litsea nitens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree due to its ability to thrive in various soil types.
