Licuala magna

Licuala magna, commonly known as the giant palm or giant licuala, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its trunk is typically short and stout, with a fibrous outer layer.

Licuala magna is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
