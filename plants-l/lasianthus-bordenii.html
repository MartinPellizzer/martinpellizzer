Lasianthus bordenii

Lasianthus bordenii, commonly known as the Borden's flame flower, is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
