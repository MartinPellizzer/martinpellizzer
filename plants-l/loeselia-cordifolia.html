Loeselia cordifolia

Loeselia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf loeselia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Loeselia cordifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
