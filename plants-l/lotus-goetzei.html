Lotus goetzei

Lotus goetzei, commonly known as the African lotus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, water-lily-like flowers that are typically pink or white, with a subtle fragrance. The plant grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and lakeshores, and has thick, fleshy leaves that help it float on water. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some African communities.
