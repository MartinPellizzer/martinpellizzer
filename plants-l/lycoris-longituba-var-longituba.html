Lycoris longituba var. longituba

Lycoris longituba var. longituba, commonly known as the Chinese everlasting lily, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its bulbs are also used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
