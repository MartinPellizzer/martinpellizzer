Lilium tigrinum var. splendens

Lilium tigrinum var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Tiger Lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers with dark, tiger-like markings on the petals. The plant features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
