Lavatera micans proles maritima

Lavatera micans proles maritima, commonly known as the coastal lavatera, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in dune environments. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to withstand salt spray and coastal winds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
