Luffa astorii

Luffa astorii, commonly known as the astoria luffa, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and gourds. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a rough, fibrous sponge that matures to a pale yellow or white color.

The luffa is often cultivated for its edible young fruits and its fibrous mature fruit, which is used in bath sponges.
