Lactuca lamii

Lactuca lamii, commonly known as the Persian lettuce, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its rosette of spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines for their mild, slightly bitter flavor.
