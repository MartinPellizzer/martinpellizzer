Ligustrum liukiuense var. microphyllum

Ligustrum liukiuense var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved Chinese privet, is a shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by black, spherical berries. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in subtropical regions.
