Linochilus rupestris

Linochilus rupestris, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and green. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in mountainous or coastal regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
