Lastrea goldieana

Lastrea goldieana, commonly known as the golden lastrea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.
