Larnax grandiflora

Larnax grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora larnax, is a flowering plant native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its striking blooms.

Larnax grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
