Litsea flexuosa

Litsea flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag litsea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.

Litsea flexuosa is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local medicine.
