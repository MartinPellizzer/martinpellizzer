Ligularia philanthrax

Ligularia philanthrax, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. Its striking green foliage is often accented with yellow or white veins, creating a dramatic visual effect.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a landscape accent plant.
