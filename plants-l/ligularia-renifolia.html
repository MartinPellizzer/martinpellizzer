Ligularia renifolia

Ligularia renifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and prominent veins. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
