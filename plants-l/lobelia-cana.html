Lobelia cana

Lobelia cana, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in spikes at the top of the stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia cana is often found in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks.
