Liparia teres

Liparia teres, commonly known as the woolly lipara, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are often pink or purple in color.

Liparia teres thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
