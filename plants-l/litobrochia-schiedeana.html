Litobrochia schiedeana

Litobrochia schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's rock-dove, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, pale blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically small, hairy, and adapted to reduce water loss.

Litobrochia schiedeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
