Lastrea tenuifolia

Lastrea tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
