Leucocoryne leucogyna

Leucocoryne leucogyna, commonly known as the white-flowered leucocoryne, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bulbous form and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. Leucocoryne leucogyna is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
