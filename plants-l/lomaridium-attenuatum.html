Lomaridium attenuatum

Lomaridium attenuatum, commonly known as the slender lomariid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers are small and fragrant.
