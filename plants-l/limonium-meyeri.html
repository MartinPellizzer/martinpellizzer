Limonium meyeri

Limonium meyeri, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and spiny, silver-green leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Limonium meyeri is often used in garden landscaping due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to coastal gardens.
