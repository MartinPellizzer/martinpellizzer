Limodorum scriptum

Limodorum scriptum, commonly known as the script lily, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China and is characterized by its distinctive, intricate flower structure. The plant features a tall, slender stem with a single, showy flower that has a unique, script-like pattern on its petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral along the stem.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, Limodorum scriptum is highly sought after in horticulture and conservation efforts.
