Lobelia chinensis f. plena

Lobelia chinensis f. plena, commonly known as Chinese lobelia, is a compact, flowering plant native to China.

It features dense clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy to the touch. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and hummingbirds.
