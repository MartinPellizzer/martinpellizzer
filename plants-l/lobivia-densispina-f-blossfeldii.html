Lobivia densispina f. blossfeldii

Lobivia densispina f. blossfeldii, commonly known as the Blossfeld's dense-spined lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color, adorned with dense, spiny tubercles. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, with a shallow root system and water-storing tissues.

This variety is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
