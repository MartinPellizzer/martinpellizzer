Listrostachys virgula

Listrostachys virgula, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray bark and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, cone-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
