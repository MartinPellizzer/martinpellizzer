Lindsaea meifolia

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. Lindsaea meifolia is characterized by its intricate leaf structure and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
