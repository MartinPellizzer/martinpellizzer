Laelia × Elegans Var. Stetzneriana

Laelia × elegans var. stetzneriana is a striking hybrid orchid known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms. Native to the high altitudes of South America, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments. Its flowers often display a mix of red, pink, and white hues, making it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It requires careful attention to light and temperature to flourish. This variety is prized for its beauty and adaptability in cultivation.
