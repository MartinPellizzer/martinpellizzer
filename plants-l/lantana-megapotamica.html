Lantana megapotamica

Lantana megapotamica, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering shrub native to South America.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.

Lantana megapotamica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
