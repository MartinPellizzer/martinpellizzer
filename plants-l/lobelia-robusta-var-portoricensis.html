Lobelia robusta var. portoricensis

Lobelia robusta var. portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico.

It is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.
