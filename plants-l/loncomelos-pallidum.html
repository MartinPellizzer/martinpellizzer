Loncomelos pallidum

Loncomelos pallidum, commonly known as the pale milkweed, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Loncomelos pallidum is known for its milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the milkweed genus.
