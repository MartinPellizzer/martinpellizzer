Lysimachia sikokiana subsp. petelotii

Lysimachia sikokiana subsp.

petelotii, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in early spring.

The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
