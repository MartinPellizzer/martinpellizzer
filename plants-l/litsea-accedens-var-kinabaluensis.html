Litsea accedens var. kinabaluensis

Litsea accedens var. kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Bornean litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of Borneo, particularly in the mountainous regions of Sabah and Sarawak. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

This variety is notable for its ecological importance in the tropical rainforest ecosystems of Borneo.
