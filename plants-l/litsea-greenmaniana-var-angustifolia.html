Litsea greenmaniana var. angustifolia

Litsea greenmaniana var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on top. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, orange-yellow berries.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
