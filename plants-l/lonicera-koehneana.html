Lonicera koehneana

Lonicera koehneana, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often consumed by birds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Lonicera koehneana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
