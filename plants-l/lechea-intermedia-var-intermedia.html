Lechea intermedia var. intermedia

Lechea intermedia var. intermedia, commonly known as the Mexican milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweeds and related plants. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive corolla that is tubular in shape.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds with tufts of white hair for wind dispersal.
