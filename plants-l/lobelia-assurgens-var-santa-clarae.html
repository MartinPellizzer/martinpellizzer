Lobelia assurgens var. santa-clarae

Lobelia assurgens var. santa-clarae, commonly known as Santa Clara lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its erect stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or mountainous areas. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
