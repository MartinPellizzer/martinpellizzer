Lobivia aureiflora

Lobivia aureiflora, commonly known as the golden flower lobivia, is a compact, succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces striking yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to bloom reliably make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
