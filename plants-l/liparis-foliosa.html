Liparis foliosa

Liparis foliosa, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like growth habit and long, slender leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Liparis foliosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical climate.
