Lindernia tamilnadensis

Lindernia tamilnadensis, commonly known as the Tamil Nadu lindernia, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Linderniaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lindernia tamilnadensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
