Lycaste × donadrianii

Lycaste × donadrianii, commonly known as the hybrid lycaste, is a hybrid orchid resulting from the cross between Lycaste meleagris and Lycaste tenuifolia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that produce a single leaf per node. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
