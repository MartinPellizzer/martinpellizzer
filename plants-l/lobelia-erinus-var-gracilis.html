Lobelia erinus var. gracilis

Lobelia erinus var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender moss flower, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a white throat. The plant has fine, hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette or trailing pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it an excellent ornamental plant for adding color and texture to garden spaces.
