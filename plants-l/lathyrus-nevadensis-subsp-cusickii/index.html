Lathyrus Nevadensis Subsp. Cusickii

Lathyrus nevadensis subsp. cusickii is a rare and distinctive plant native to the western United States. It is commonly found in rocky habitats and dry slopes, thriving in arid environments. This subspecies is known for its striking purple and pink flowers that bloom in late spring. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
