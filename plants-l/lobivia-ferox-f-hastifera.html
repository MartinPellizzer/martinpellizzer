Lobivia ferox f. hastifera

Lobivia ferox f. hastifera, commonly known as the sharp-tailed lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, a feature that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

This variety is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
