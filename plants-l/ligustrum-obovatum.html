Ligustrum obovatum

Ligustrum obovatum, commonly known as the broadleaf privet, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by black, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Due to its adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and hedge planting.
