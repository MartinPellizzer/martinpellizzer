Lobivia backebergii subsp. wrightiana

Lobivia backebergii subsp.

wrightiana, commonly known as the Wright's lobivia, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, adapted to store water in arid environments.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
