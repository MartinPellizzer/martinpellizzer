Ligustrum ovalifolium f. heterophyllum

Ligustrum ovalifolium f. heterophyllum, commonly known as the variegated privet, is a compact evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with distinctive white or yellow variegation.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in a variety of soil types. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring, followed by small, dark blue berries.

Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
