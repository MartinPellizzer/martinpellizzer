Linaria bipartita proles elegans

Linaria bipartita proles elegans, commonly known as the elegant linaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers that typically display a range of colors from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Linaria bipartita proles elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
