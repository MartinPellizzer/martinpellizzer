Ligustrum stauntonii

Ligustrum stauntonii, commonly known as the Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, black berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It is often used in landscaping due to its fast growth and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions outside its native range.
