Leptostachya comata var. humifusa

Leptostachya comata var. humifusa, commonly known as the hairy leek orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of southern Australia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
