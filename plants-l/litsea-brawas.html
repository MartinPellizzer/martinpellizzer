Litsea brawas

Litsea brawas, commonly known as the Brawas leaf tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate, and often elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

The fruit is a small, smooth, yellowish drupe that ripens in late summer.
