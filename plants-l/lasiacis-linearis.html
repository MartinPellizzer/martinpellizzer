Lasiacis linearis

Lasiacis linearis, commonly known as the slender lasiacis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to certain regions of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
