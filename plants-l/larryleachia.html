Larryleachia

Larryleachia, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for desert gardens and collections.
