Lysionotus brachycarpus

Lysionotus brachycarpus, commonly known as the narrow-fruited starlingflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous minute seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
