Lychnis stellarioides

Lychnis stellarioides, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the carnation family, Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, pink or white flowers that resemble stars, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Lychnis stellarioides is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in temperate climates.
