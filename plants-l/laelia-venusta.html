Laelia venusta

Laelia venusta, commonly known as the Venustas orchid, is a species native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its high-altitude environment. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It prefers cool, humid conditions with partial shade and well-draining soil.

Laelia venusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
