Leucas chinensis f. riukiuensis

Leucas chinensis f. riukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
