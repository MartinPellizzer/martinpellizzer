Lasianthaea fruticosa var. fasciculata

Lasianthaea fruticosa var. fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered lasianthaea, is a shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color, which bloom during the warmer months. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact manner, contributing to its shrub-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
