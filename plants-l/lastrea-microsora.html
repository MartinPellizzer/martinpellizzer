Lastrea microsora

Lastrea microsora, commonly known as the dwarf lastrea, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, also known as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically from autumn to spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid environments.
