Landolphia polyantha

Landolphia polyantha, commonly known as the African star apple or the yellow star apple, is a tropical vine native to Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, woody stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, often with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.

Landolphia polyantha is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
