Landolphia compressa

Landolphia compressa, commonly known as the African arrowroot or swamp arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, swampy habitats. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.

Landolphia compressa is valued for its starchy roots, which are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
