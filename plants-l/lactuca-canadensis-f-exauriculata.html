Lactuca canadensis f. exauriculata

Lactuca canadensis f. exauriculata, commonly known as the Canadian lettuce or narrow-leaf lettuce, is a botanical variant of the common lettuce species.

It is characterized by its narrower, more elongated leaves compared to the typical form. The plant is native to North America and thrives in moist, temperate environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

This form is often found in wet meadows and along the edges of water bodies, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
