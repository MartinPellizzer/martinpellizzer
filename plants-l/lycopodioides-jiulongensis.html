Lycopodioides jiulongensis

Lycopodioides jiulongensis, commonly known as the Jiulongshan clubmoss, is a rare and ancient vascular plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, spore-bearing cones and a rosette-like arrangement of needle-like leaves. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its reproductive structures are adapted for spore dispersal, a trait common among primitive vascular plants.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
