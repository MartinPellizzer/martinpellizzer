Ligularia kunlunshanica

Ligularia kunlunshanica, commonly known as the Kunlunshan giant ligularia, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length, often with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with rich, loamy soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
