Logfia subulata

Logfia subulata, commonly known as the slender leaf logfia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
