Lactuca thianschanica

Lactuca thianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Tian Shan range. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers and has leaves that are typically thin and grass-like. It is adapted to grow in arid and high-altitude environments.

Lactuca thianschanica is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves, similar to other lettuces.
