Lomandra integra

Lomandra integra, commonly known as the common lomandra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and robust rhizomatous growth. It produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in summer, followed by dark blue or black berries.

Lomandra integra is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils and is often used in native landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
