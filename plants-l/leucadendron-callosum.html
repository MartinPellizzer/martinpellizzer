Leucadendron callosum

Leucadendron callosum, commonly known as the silver tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage, which gives it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange spherical flower heads that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes across the world.
