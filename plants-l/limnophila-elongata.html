Limnophila elongata

Limnophila elongata, commonly known as the slender water nettle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Limnophylaceae.

It is an aquatic or semi-aquatic plant that typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. Limnophila elongata plays a role in providing habitat and food for various aquatic invertebrates and amphibians.
