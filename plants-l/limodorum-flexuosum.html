Limodorum flexuosum

Limodorum flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible lily of the valley, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features delicate, pendulous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters along slender, arching stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in cool, humid climates.
