Leuconotis subavenis var. macrophylla

Leuconotis subavenis var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed bellflower, is a variant of the bellflower species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.
