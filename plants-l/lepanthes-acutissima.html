Lepanthes acutissima

Lepanthes acutissima, commonly known as the acute-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Lepanthes acutissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
