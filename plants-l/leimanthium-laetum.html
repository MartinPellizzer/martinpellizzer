Leimanthium laetum

Leimanthium laetum, commonly known as the broad-leaved leimanthium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in dense, terminal clusters.

Leimanthium laetum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
