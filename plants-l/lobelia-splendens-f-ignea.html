Lobelia splendens f. ignea

Lobelia splendens f. ignea, commonly known as the fiery lobelia, is a striking hybrid cultivar of the lobelia genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of about 12 to 18 inches and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is often used in containers or as a border plant due to its compact growth habit.

Botanically, it is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens.
