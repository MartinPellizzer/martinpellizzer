Lepanthes monteverdensis

Lepanthes monteverdensis, commonly known as the Monteverde orchid, is a rare and exquisite member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica, specifically the Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve. This plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The species thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of mist and rainfall.

Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to deforestation, Lepanthes monteverdensis is considered endangered.
