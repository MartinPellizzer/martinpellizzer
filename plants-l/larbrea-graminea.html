Larbrea graminea

Larbrea graminea, commonly known as the slender grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are usually narrow and linear in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Larbrea graminea is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
