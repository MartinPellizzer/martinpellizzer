Lewisia Kelloggii Subsp. Yosemitana

Yosemite lewisia, scientifically known as Lewisia kelloggii subsp. yosemitana, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Sierra Nevada mountains. This rare subspecies thrives in the cool, moist environments of Yosemite National Park, where it blooms with vibrant pink and white petals. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, as it has adapted to the harsh alpine conditions of its habitat. The plant is a symbol of the region's unique biodiversity and ecological significance. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from the threats of climate change and habitat loss.
