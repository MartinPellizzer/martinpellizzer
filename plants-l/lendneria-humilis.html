Lendneria humilis

Lendneria humilis, commonly known as the dwarf lentneria, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green. Its botanical structure is characterized by a short stem and a compact growth habit.

Lendneria humilis is primarily of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological niche.
