Lepidium merrallii

Lepidium merrallii, commonly known as Merrall's cress, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, white or yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round seeds.
