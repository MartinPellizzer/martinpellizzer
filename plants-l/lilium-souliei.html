Lilium souliei

Lilium souliei, commonly known as the Suli li or Suli lily, is a rare and striking species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and botanists alike.
