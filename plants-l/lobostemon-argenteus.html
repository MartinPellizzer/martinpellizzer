Lobostemon argenteus

Lobostemon argenteus, commonly known as silverleaf lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Lobostemon argenteus is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in gardens with well-draining soil.
