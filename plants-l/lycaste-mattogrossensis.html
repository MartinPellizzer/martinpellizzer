Lycaste mattogrossensis

Lycaste mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Mattogrossense Lycaste, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it thrives on decaying organic matter. Its unique morphology includes long, slender pseudobulbs and elongated, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
