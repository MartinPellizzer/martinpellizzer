Linociera arborea

Linociera arborea, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stem, lance-shaped leaves, and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests or near water bodies.

Linociera arborea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
