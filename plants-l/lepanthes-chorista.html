Lepanthes chorista

Lepanthes chorista, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a raceme. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate and intricate floral structure. Lepanthes chorista grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
