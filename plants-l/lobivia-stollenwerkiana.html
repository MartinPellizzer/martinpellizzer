Lobivia stollenwerkiana

Lobivia stollenwerkiana, commonly known as the Stollenwerk's lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stems for survival in dry conditions.

Lobivia stollenwerkiana is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
