Lachenalia muirii

Lachenalia muirii, commonly known as the Muir's lachenalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces a basal rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting it to its arid environment.

Lachenalia muirii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
