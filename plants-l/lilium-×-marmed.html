Lilium × marmed

Lilium × marmed, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a cross between different species of lilies, resulting in a plant with diverse floral characteristics.

It typically produces large, showy flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, red, and pink. The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Lilium × marmed is widely cultivated for its beauty and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
