Lotononis sericophylla

Lotononis sericophylla, commonly known as the silky-leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that aid in its propagation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
