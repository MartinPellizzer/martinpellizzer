Lens ervoides var. leiocarpa

Lens ervoides var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-hair vetch, is a leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and sustainable agriculture.
