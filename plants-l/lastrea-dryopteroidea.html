Lastrea dryopteroidea

Lastrea dryopteroidea, commonly known as the silver-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery, hairy leaves and small white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
