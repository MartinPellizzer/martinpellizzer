Lamium cryptanthum

Lamium cryptanthum, commonly known as purple deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, while its leaves are sometimes used in herbal remedies.

Lamium cryptanthum is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to suppress weeds in garden settings.
