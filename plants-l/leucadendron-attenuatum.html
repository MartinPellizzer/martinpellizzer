Leucadendron attenuatum

Leucadendron attenuatum, commonly known as the silver leaf leucadendron, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces cone-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
