Lightfootia tenella var. rigida

Lightfootia tenella var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid lightfootia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
