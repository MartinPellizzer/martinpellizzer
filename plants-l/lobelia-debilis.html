Lobelia debilis

Lobelia debilis, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia debilis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
