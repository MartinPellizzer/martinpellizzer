Lonicera charlottii

Lonicera charlottii, commonly known as Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, appearing in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The berries that follow the flowers are red and edible, though they are not commonly consumed by humans.
