Liparis meihuashanensis

Liparis meihuashanensis, commonly known as Meihua Shan Orchid, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
