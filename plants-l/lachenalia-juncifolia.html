Lachenalia juncifolia

Lachenalia juncifolia, commonly known as the woolly lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, woolly leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange in hue. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Lachenalia juncifolia is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.
