Lucilia acutifolia f. virescens

Lucilia acutifolia f. virescens, commonly known as the green-leaved acutifolia.

It is a variant of the acutifolia species, characterized by its distinctively green foliage. The plant typically exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, narrow leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
