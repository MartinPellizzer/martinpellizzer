Litsea grandiflora

Litsea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flower litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, simple leaves and showy white or pale yellow flowers. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Litsea grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
