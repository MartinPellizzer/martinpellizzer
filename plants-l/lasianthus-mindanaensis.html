Lasianthus mindanaensis

Lasianthus mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth surface, while its flowers are clustered in whorls.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
