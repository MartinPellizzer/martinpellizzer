Lagerstroemia munchhausia

Lagerstroemia munchhausia, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink, which appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The bark of Lagerstroemia munchhausia is smooth and peels in thin, papery layers, adding to its ornamental appeal.
