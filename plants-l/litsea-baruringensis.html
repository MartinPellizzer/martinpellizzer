Litsea baruringensis

Litsea baruringensis, commonly known as the Baruring mountain laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, berry-like drupe that contains several seeds.

Litsea baruringensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
