Lamium albiflorum

Lamium albiflorum, commonly known as white dead-nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white flowers with two lips, typical of the Lamiaceae family. It thrives in woodland and shady garden environments, preferring moist, well-drained soil.

Lamium albiflorum is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
