Limacia velutina

Limacia velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Limacia velutina is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
