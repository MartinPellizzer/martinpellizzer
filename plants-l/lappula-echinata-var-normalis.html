Lappula echinata var. normalis

Lappula echinata var. normalis, commonly known as the common lappula, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Chenopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions.
