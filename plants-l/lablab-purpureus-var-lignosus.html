Lablab purpureus var. lignosus

Lablab purpureus var. lignosus, commonly known as the woody lablab, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its woody stems and broad, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces vibrant purple or red flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a forage crop.
