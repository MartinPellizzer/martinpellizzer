Lilium rubellum

Lilium rubellum, commonly known as the red lily, is a species of flowering plant in the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Russia and Kazakhstan. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink in color, with a strong, spicy fragrance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, and it produces bulbous underground storage organs.

Lilium rubellum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
