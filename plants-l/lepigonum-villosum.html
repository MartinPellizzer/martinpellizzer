Lepigonum villosum

Lepigonum villosum, commonly known as the silky germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, coastal climates.

Lepigonum villosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscaping.
