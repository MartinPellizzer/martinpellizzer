Lindsaea minima

Lindsaea minima, commonly known as the small lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Lindsaea minima is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
