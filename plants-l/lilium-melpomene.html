Lilium melpomene

Lilium melpomene, commonly known as the Turk's Cap lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers with a distinctive, spurred tepal at the base. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often found in woodland or open areas.

The Turk's Cap lily is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
