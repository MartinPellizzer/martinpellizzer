Lasianthus politus

Lasianthus politus, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. Lasianthus politus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
