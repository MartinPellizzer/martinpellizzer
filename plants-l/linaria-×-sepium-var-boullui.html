Linaria × sepium var. boullui

Linaria × sepium var. boullui, commonly known as the hybrid carnation or hybrid toadflax, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
