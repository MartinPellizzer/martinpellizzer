Lithotoma petraea

Lithotoma petraea, commonly known as the rock fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows on rocky outcrops and in arid environments. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a sweet, fig-like flavor. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it conserve water in dry conditions.

Lithotoma petraea plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing food for various animals and birds.
