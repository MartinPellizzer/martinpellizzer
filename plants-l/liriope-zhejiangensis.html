Liriope zhejiangensis

Liriope zhejiangensis, commonly known as Zhejiang lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern China.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and grass-green in color, growing in dense clumps. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in late summer, which are arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Liriope zhejiangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and traditional Chinese medicine.
