Lotus humifusus

Lotus humifusus, commonly known as the dwarf lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in shallow water or wetlands, with its rhizomes spreading along the substrate. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, supported by long, slender stems. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming above the water in summer.

Lotus humifusus is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
