Limonium catalaunicum subsp. procerum

Limonium catalaunicum subsp.

procerum, commonly known as the slender sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine layer of salt, which helps in reducing water loss.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
