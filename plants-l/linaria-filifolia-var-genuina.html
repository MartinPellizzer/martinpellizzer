Linaria filifolia var. genuina

Linaria filifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces bright yellow, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often appear in pairs along the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
