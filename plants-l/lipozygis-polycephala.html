Lipozygis polycephala

Lipozygis polycephala, commonly known as the many-headed lipozygis, is a rare and specialized plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant exhibits a complex morphology with small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.
