Lachenalia violacea var. glauca

Lachenalia violacea var. glauca, commonly known as the blue-flowered lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has linear, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of its succulent nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
