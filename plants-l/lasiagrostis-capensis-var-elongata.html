Lasiagrostis capensis var. elongata

Lasiagrostis capensis var. elongata, commonly known as the Cape grass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that grow upright, often reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale green to golden florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slight bluish tinge.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
