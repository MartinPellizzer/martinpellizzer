Lasianthus sylvestris var. latifolius

Lasianthus sylvestris var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
