Lecythis

Lecythis, commonly known as the Brazil nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Lecythidaceae and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces large, woody fruits that contain the edible Brazil nuts. Its flowers are typically yellow or red and are pollinated by insects.

The plant is also valued for its timber and has cultural significance in the Amazon rainforest.
