Lagarosolen integrifolius

Lagarosolen integrifolius, commonly known as the smoothleaf kowhai, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a smooth margin, giving it its common name. It produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, attracting native pollinators.

The species is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
