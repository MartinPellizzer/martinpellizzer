Limonium aureum var. aureum

Limonium aureum var. aureum, commonly known as golden sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to September. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate salty air and sandy soils.
