Lilium maximowiczii var. regelii

Lilium maximowiczii var. regelii, commonly known as the Regal Lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
