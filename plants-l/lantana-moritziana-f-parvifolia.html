Lantana moritziana f. parvifolia

Lantana moritziana f. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf lantana, is a compact shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, yellow, and orange, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms make it a versatile plant for landscaping.
