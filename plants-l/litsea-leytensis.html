Litsea leytensis

Litsea leytensis, commonly known as the Leyte litsea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical, dark blue berries.

The tree is often found in lowland and montane rainforests, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
