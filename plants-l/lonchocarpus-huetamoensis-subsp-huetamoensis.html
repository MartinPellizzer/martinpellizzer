Lonchocarpus huetamoensis subsp. huetamoensis

Lonchocarpus huetamoensis subsp.

huetamoensis, commonly known as the Huetamoena legume, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, producing a distinctive yellow or greenish color.
