Logania micrantha

Logania micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered logania, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its attractive, small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is often found in forest understories and can grow as a shrub or small tree. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals.
