Liparis makinoana

Liparis makinoana, commonly known as the Makino's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Liparis makinoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
