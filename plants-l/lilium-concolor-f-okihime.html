Lilium concolor f. okihime

Lilium concolor f. okihime, commonly known as the Okihime lily, is a unique cultivar of the lily family characterized by its striking white petals with a subtle pink hue.

It is a hybrid form that exhibits a compact growth habit and produces elegant, upward-facing blooms. The plant is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its delicate flowers.
