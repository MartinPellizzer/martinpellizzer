Livistona subglobosa

Livistona subglobosa, commonly known as the globular palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded crown of leaves that give it a unique, almost spherical appearance. The plant has a short, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous, spongy material. Its leaves are pinnate, with a waxy surface and a bluish-green coloration.

This species is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient and hardy plant.
