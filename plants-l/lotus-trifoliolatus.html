Lotus trifoliolatus

Lotus trifoliolatus, commonly known as the three-leaf lotus, is a species of aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant typically grows in freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Lotus trifoliolatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
