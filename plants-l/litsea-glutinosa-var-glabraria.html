Litsea glutinosa var. glabraria

Litsea glutinosa var. glabraria, commonly known as the smooth litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy, dark green upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, blue-black berries. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and moist hillside areas.

Its aromatic properties make it a valued plant in traditional medicine and as an ornamental species.
