Lysionotus palinensis

Lysionotus palinensis, commonly known as the Pale Ghost Orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the wetlands of northern South America.

It is characterized by its pale greenish-white flowers and elongated, strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often in swampy areas. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a unique structure that resembles ghostly apparitions, hence its common name.

Due to its habitat specificity and threats from habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
