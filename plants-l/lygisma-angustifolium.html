Lygisma angustifolium

Lygisma angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved lygisma, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 5 centimeters in length. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in late spring and are clustered in loose panicles.

The species is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants.
