Leea indica

Leea indica, commonly known as Indian banyan or leea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Leeaceae.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, including India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features opposite, simple, and usually elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy fruits.

Leea indica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
