Lockhartia ludibunda

Lockhartia ludibunda, commonly known as the laughing orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often resembling a laughing face. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, and the plant is known for its strong, spicy fragrance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
