Laelia anceps var. lineata

Laelia anceps var. lineata, commonly known as the lineated laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often bicolor flowers that feature a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
