Lysiostyles sericea

Lysiostyles sericea, commonly known as the silky lysiostyle, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to white flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine, silky hair that gives it its common name.

Lysiostyles sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
