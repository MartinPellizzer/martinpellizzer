Leiothrix mucronata var. glabra

Leiothrix mucronata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth leiothrix, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically smooth and lance-shaped, giving the plant a refined appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
