Limonium album

Limonium album, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue flowers and spiny, grass-like foliage. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in dunes and coastal areas. Limonium album produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual element to its habitat.

It is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience to coastal conditions.
