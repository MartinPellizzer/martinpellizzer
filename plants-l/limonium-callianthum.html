Limonium callianthum

Limonium callianthum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and silvery, fuzzy foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to saline environments. Limonium callianthum is a perennial herb that produces showy inflorescences, often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
