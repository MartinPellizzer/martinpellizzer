Leucheria multiflora

Leucheria multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered leucheria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Leucheria multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
