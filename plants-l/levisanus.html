Levisanus

Levisanus, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Levisanus is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and ability to bloom year-round under proper care.

Its ornamental value makes it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
