Ladenbergia firmula

Ladenbergia firmula, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ladenbergia firmula is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
