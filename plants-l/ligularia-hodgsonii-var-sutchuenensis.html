Ligularia hodgsonii var. sutchuenensis

Ligularia hodgsonii var. sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellow-green flowers with prominent, elongated petals, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts.
