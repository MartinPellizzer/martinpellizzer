Litsea rubescens

Litsea rubescens, commonly known as the red-fruited litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
