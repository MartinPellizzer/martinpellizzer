Lythrum albicaule

Lythrum albicaule, commonly known as white lythrum, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lythraceae family, which includes other plants like the poinsettia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are linear and often have a slight hairy texture.

Lythrum albicaule is typically found in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
