Lindera chienii

Lindera chienii, commonly known as Chinese wild laurier, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurel trees and other aromatic plants. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Lindera chienii is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
