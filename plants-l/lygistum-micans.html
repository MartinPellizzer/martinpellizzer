Lygistum micans

Lygistum micans, commonly known as the silver flame plant, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are glossy and have a unique ability to reflect light, giving it a shimmering appearance.

Lygistum micans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and air-purifying properties.
