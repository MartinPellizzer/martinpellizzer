Ligaria orbignyana

Ligaria orbignyana, commonly known as the Chilean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its vibrant purple or blue flowers that bloom in spring.

Ligaria orbignyana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
