Limonia gabunensis

Limonia gabunensis, commonly known as the Gabon lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, citrus-like flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature, though they may turn yellow or orange. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Limonia gabunensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
