Lucilia perpusilla

Lucilia perpusilla, commonly known as the lesser green bottle fly, is a species of fly belonging to the family Calliphoridae.

It is characterized by its small size, typically measuring between 5 to 10 millimeters in length. The adult has a metallic green body with a slender, elongated abdomen. Its larvae are often found in decaying organic matter, playing a role in decomposition.

This species is widely distributed across temperate regions and is commonly associated with carrion and fermenting plant material.
