Licaria debilis

Licaria debilis, commonly known as the slender licaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a soft texture and a slightly pubescent surface.

Licaria debilis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
