Lycaste guatemalensis

Lycaste guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
