Lobelia tenera var. tenuicaulis

Lobelia tenera var. tenuicaulis, commonly known as slender-stemmed lobelia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
