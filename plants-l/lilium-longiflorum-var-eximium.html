Lilium longiflorum var. eximium

Lilium longiflorum var. eximium, commonly known as the Exquisite Asiatic lily, is a variant of the Asiatic lily species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter. The plant typically has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are a dark green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its vibrant colors and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
