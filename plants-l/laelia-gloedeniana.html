Laelia gloedeniana

Laelia gloedeniana, commonly known as the Gloeden's orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

It is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves and inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with darker hues.

Laelia gloedeniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
