Lobelia eryliae

Lobelia eryliae, commonly known as the Erythraea lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruits.

Lobelia eryliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
