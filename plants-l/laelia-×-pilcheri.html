Laelia × pilcheri

Laelia × pilcheri, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and Miltonia species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and often features showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in various environmental conditions. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
