Lecanium membranaceum

Lecanium membranaceum, commonly known as the membranous scale, is a species of armored scale insect belonging to the family Coccidae.

It is characterized by its small, flattened, and waxy body covered with a protective shield. The plant it infests typically includes various species of oaks and other woody plants. Its feeding habits can cause yellowing of leaves and weakening of the host plant.

The insect is often found in clusters on the stems and branches of its host.
