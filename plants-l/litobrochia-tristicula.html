Litobrochia tristicula

Litobrochia tristicula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved brochidium, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers during the winter months. Its leaves are narrow and often shed during dry seasons, reducing water loss.

Litobrochia tristicula is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
