Lotus subbiflorus

Lotus subbiflorus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lotus, is a species native to parts of Asia, particularly China and India.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.

Lotus subbiflorus thrives in wetland environments and is often found near ponds and slow-moving streams.
