Lonicera gynochlamydea subsp. gynochlamydea

Lonicera gynochlamydea subsp.

gynochlamydea, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia. It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange.

The flowers are typically borne in clusters and are pollinated by insects. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
