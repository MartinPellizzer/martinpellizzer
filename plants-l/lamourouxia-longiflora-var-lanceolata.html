Lamourouxia longiflora var. lanceolata

Lamourouxia longiflora var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale blue. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate touch of color to gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
