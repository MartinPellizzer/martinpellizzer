Ligeria gesnerifolia

Ligeria gesnerifolia, commonly known as the Gesner's ligeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually tubular with five lobes, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

Ligeria gesnerifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
