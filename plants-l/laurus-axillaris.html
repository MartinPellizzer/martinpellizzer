Laurus axillaris

Laurus axillaris, commonly known as sweetbox, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features dense, fragrant foliage with small, oval leaves that are typically dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for hedges or ornamental gardens.

Its aromatic leaves and hardy nature make it a popular choice for landscaping.
