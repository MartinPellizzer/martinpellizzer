Lespedeza Cuneata Var. Serpens

Lespedeza cuneata var. serpens is a lesser-known but ecologically significant plant native to eastern Asia. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. This variant of Lespedeza is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas quickly. While it can be invasive in some regions, it also plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it an interesting subject for ecological study.
