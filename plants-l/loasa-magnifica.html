Loasa magnifica

Loasa magnifica, commonly known as the showy loasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Loasa magnifica thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in cloud forests.
