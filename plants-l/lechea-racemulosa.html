Lechea racemulosa

Lechea racemulosa, commonly known as the clustered milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its milky sap, which is a distinguishing botanical feature. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually small and clustered in racemes, giving it its scientific name.
