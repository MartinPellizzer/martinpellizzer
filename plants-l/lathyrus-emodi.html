Lathyrus emodi

Lathyrus emodi, commonly known as the Himalayan vetch, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a climbing legume that produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate arrangement and small, stipitate leaflets. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Lathyrus emodi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
