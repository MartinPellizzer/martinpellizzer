Leucophae cretica var. stricta

Leucophae cretica var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sea holly, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, upright growth with silver-gray foliage and striking blue or violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
