Lilium cavaleriei

Lilium cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese lily, is a species of bulbous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soil with partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Lilium cavaleriei is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
