Lacinaria scariosa var. angustata

Lacinaria scariosa var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lacinaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, angular stems that are often covered in a distinctive papery sheath. The leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough, scaly texture along the edges. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels.

This variant is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
