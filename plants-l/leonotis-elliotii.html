Leonotis elliotii

Leonotis elliotii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Leonotis elliotii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
