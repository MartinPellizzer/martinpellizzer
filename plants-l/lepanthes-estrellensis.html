Lepanthes estrellensis

Lepanthes estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellensis Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. Lepanthes estrellensis has small, strap-like leaves and produces flowers that are often fragrant.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
