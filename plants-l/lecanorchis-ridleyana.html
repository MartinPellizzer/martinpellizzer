Lecanorchis ridleyana

Lecanorchis ridleyana, commonly known as the Ridley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

The species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
