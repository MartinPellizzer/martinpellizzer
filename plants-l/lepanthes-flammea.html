Lepanthes flammea

Lepanthes flammea, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orange-red flowers that resemble flames, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its flowers are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Lepanthes flammea is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
