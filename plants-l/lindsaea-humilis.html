Lindsaea humilis

Lindsaea humilis, commonly known as the low lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, creeping rhizomes and small, clustered fronds. The fronds are typically lance-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
