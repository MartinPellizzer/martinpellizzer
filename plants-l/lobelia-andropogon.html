Lobelia andropogon

Lobelia andropogon, commonly known as purpletop, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and prairies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Lobelia andropogon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
