Ligularia zhouquensis

Ligularia zhouquensis, commonly known as the Zhouqu Ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer, adding a striking visual element to its foliage. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
