Lobivia cinnabarina var. prestoana

Lobivia cinnabarina var. prestoana, commonly known as the Rainbow Lobivia, is a compact, flowering cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive red or pinkish hue, often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
