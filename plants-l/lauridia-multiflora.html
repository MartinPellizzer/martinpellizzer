Lauridia multiflora

Lauridia multiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has aromatic, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Lauridia multiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
