Lagerstroemia archeriana

Lagerstroemia archeriana, commonly known as the Red Leaf Lagerstroemia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage, which turns vibrant shades of crimson in the fall, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers in summer, followed by cylindrical, red or purple fruit capsules. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its unique foliage and adaptability contribute to its popularity in horticulture.
