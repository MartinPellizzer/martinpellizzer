Ludia mauritiana var. sechellensis

Ludia mauritiana var. sechellensis, commonly known as the Seychelles starfruit, is a variant of the starfruit plant native to the Seychelles.

It is a shrub that produces small, star-shaped fruits with a yellowish-orange color. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The fruit is typically harvested when it turns a bright yellow, indicating maturity.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often grown in tropical gardens.
