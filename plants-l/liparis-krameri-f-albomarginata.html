Liparis krameri f. albomarginata

Liparis krameri f. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip, which is often marked with a white margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
