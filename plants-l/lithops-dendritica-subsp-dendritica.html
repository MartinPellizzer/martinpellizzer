Lithops dendritica subsp. dendritica

Lithops dendritica subsp.

dendritica, commonly known as the tree-like lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its unique, rock-like appearance with fleshy, paired leaves that resemble miniature trees.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
