Lepanthes totontepecensis

Lepanthes totontepecensis, commonly known as the Totontepec Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests, where it thrives on the forest floor or on tree trunks. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and rarity.
