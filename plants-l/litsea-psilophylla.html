Litsea psilophylla

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually less than 5 centimeters in length. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences.
