Lomatium tarantuloides

Lomatium tarantuloides, commonly known as the tarantula lomatium, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its fern-like foliage and umbel-shaped flowers.

Native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Lomatium tarantuloides is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various wildlife species.
