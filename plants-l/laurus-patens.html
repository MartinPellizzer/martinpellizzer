Laurus patens

Laurus patens, commonly known as sweet bay or Turkish bay, is a broadleaf evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible but less commonly consumed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and fragrance make it valuable both in gardens and in traditional medicine.
