Lasianthus thwaitesii var. thwaitesii

Lasianthus thwaitesii var. thwaitesii, commonly known as the slender lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
