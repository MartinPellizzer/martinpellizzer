Lebeckia zeyheri

Lebeckia zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's lebeckia, is a South African native plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Lebeckia zeyheri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
