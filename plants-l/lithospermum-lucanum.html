Lithospermum lucanum

Lithospermum lucanum, commonly known as red root, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish to purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often containing a red or reddish-brown pigment.

Lithospermum lucanum is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a characteristic species of rocky or dry habitats.
