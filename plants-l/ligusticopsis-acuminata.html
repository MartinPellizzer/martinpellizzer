Ligusticopsis acuminata

The plant features slender, upright stems with finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, hairy layer. Ligusticopsis acuminata is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.
