Lilium auratum var. wittei

Lilium auratum var. wittei, commonly known as the Witte's Trumpet Lily, is a variant of the Trumpet Lily species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms in the summer, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring cooler climates.

This variety is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental beauty.
