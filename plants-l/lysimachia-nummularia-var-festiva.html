Lysimachia nummularia var. festiva

Lysimachia nummularia var. festiva, commonly known as the silver carpet or golden moss, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, rounded, glossy leaves that are typically silver-white to golden in color, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense, attractive ground cover.
