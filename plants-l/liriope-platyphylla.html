Liriope platyphylla

Liriope platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and narrow, growing in dense clumps. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in summer, arranged in loose clusters atop slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Liriope platyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
