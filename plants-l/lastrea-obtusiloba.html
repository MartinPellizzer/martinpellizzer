Lastrea obtusiloba

Lastrea obtusiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved lastrea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often decorative flora. The plant features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often blooming in late summer.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
