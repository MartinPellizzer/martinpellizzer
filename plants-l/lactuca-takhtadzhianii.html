Lactuca takhtadzhianii

Lactuca takhtadzhianii, commonly known as the Takhtadzhan lettuce, is a rare and lesser-known species within the lettuce family.

It is characterized by its small, pale green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value. It is native to the arid regions of Central Asia and is adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
