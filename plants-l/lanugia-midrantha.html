Lanugia midrantha

Lanugia midrantha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Lanugia midrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
