Limacia distincta

Limacia distincta, commonly known as the distinct leaf snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Aegidionidae and is native to parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its spiral-shaped shell, which is typically smooth and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and gardens.
