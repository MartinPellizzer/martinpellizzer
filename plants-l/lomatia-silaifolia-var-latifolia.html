Lomatia silaifolia var. latifolia

Lomatia silaifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved lomatia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its woody shrub structure. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in dense, globular inflorescences.

It is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in heathland and dry sclerophyll forest ecosystems.
