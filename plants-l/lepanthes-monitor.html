Lepanthes monitor

Lepanthes monitor, commonly known as the monitor orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Lepanthes monitor thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
