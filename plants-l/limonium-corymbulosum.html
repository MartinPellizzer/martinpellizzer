Limonium corymbulosum

Limonium corymbulosum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky coastal environments.

Limonium corymbulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in maritime climates.
