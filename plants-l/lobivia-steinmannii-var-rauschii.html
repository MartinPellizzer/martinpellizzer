Lobivia steinmannii var. rauschii

Lobivia steinmannii var. rauschii, commonly known as the Rauschii Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color, often adorned with white or pale markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
