Lobivia haageana f. croceantha

Lobivia haageana f. croceantha, commonly known as the Crocea Lobivia, is a compact, branching cactus native to South America.

It features cylindrical stems covered in dense, woolly white spines, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is a prized species among cactus enthusiasts.
