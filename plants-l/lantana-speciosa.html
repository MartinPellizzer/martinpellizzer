Lantana speciosa

Lantana speciosa, commonly known as the showy lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, red, or purple, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its flowers are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees, butterflies, and birds.

Lantana speciosa is also known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
