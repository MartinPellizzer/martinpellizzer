Lathyrus magellanicus var. campestris

Lathyrus magellanicus var. campestris, commonly known as the Campestris pea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grasslands and open areas. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.

This species is adapted to thrive in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils.
