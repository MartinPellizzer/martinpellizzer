Lobivia atrovirens var. ritteri

Lobivia atrovirens var. ritteri, commonly known as the Ritter's Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a velvety texture and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant has a short, thick caudex that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
