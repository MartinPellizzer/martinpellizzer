Lotononis Fastigiata

Lotononis fastigiata is a unique and lesser-known flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many ornamental and edible species. This plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, clustered inflorescences. While not widely cultivated, it has gained attention for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal. Its potential for horticultural use remains an area of growing interest among botanists and gardeners alike.
