Lepanthes stupenda

Lepanthes stupenda, commonly known as the magnificent orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Lepanthes stupenda is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, often found in humid tropical forests.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
