Lobivia adpressispina

Lobivia adpressispina, commonly known as the flat-spined lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
