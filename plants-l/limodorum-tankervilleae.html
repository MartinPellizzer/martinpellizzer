Limodorum tankervilleae

Limodorum tankervilleae, commonly known as the Tankerville lily, is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant grows from a rhizomatous root system and thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This rare species is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and delicate appearance.
