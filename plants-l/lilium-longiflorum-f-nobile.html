Lilium longiflorum f. nobile

Lilium longiflorum f. nobile, commonly known as the Easter lily, is a cultivar of the Asiatic lily species.

It is characterized by its large, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with broad, sword-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The f. nobile form is prized for its elegant appearance and strong fragrance.
