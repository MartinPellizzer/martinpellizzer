Leonotis dubia

Leonotis dubia, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a strong, sweet fragrance.

Leonotis dubia is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
