Lavatera cretica var. acutiloba

Lavatera cretica var. acutiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lavatera, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features soft, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, fluffy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
