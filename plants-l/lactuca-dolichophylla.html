Lactuca dolichophylla

Lactuca dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Lactuca dolichophylla is a herbaceous perennial that can grow up to 30 centimeters tall.
