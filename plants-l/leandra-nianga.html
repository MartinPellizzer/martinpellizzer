Leandra nianga

Leandra nianga, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescence composed of a spathe and spadix. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.
