Lasianthus purpureus var. chloropodus

Lasianthus purpureus var. chloropodus, commonly known as the purple flame or violet flame, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its vibrant, deep purple to violet foliage that remains throughout the year. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a waxy texture, contributing to its visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This variety is prized for its ability to add a bold, colorful statement to any space without requiring excessive care.
