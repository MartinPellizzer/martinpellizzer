Lysimachia lineolata

Lysimachia lineolata, commonly known as the yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Lysimachia lineolata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and cheerful floral display.
