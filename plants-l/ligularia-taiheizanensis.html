Ligularia taiheizanensis

Ligularia taiheizanensis, commonly known as the Taiheizan ligularia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and have a striking green color with a metallic sheen. It produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is appreciated for its unique foliage and flowering display.
