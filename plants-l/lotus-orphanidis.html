Lotus orphanidis

Lotus orphanidis, commonly known as the desert lotus, is a unique flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water.

Lotus orphanidis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
