Leichhardtia ambuntiensis

Leichhardtia ambuntiensis, commonly known as the Ambunti rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Northern Territory of Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its dry habitat.

Leichhardtia ambuntiensis is often found in rocky outcrops and is a characteristic species of the region's unique flora.
