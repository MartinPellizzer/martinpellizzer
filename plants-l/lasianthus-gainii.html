Lasianthus gainii

Lasianthus gainii, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green.

Lasianthus gainii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
