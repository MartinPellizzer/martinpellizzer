Lonicera dioica var. glaucescens

Lonicera dioica var. glaucescens, commonly known as the blue honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers in early spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
