Lindernia prolata

Lindernia prolata, commonly known as the trailing lindernia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Lindernia prolata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
