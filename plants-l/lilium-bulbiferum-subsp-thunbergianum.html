Lilium bulbiferum subsp. thunbergianum

Lilium bulbiferum subsp.

thunbergianum, commonly known as the Japanese tiger lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to East Asia. It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange to red in color, with a distinctive black-tipped throat.

The plant produces large, fleshy bulbs that store nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and robust growth habit.
