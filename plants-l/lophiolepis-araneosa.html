Lophiolepis araneosa

Lophiolepis araneosa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which resembles a spider's web, giving it its common name. The plant features long, slender stems with clusters of small, intricate flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Lophiolepis araneosa is a perennial herb that blooms during the summer months, adding a rare and beautiful element to its natural habitat.
