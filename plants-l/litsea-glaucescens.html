Litsea glaucescens

Litsea glaucescens, commonly known as the glossy flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by yellow or orange berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Litsea glaucescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic foliage.
