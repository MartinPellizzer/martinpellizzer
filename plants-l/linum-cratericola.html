Linum cratericola

Linum cratericola, commonly known as the cratericola flax, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid and sunny conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and unique ecological role, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
