Lythrum castiliae

Lythrum castiliae, commonly known as narrowleaf lythrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny habitats. The plant produces spikes of deep purple to red flowers, which bloom from summer to autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Lythrum castiliae is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive flowers and drought-resistant qualities.
