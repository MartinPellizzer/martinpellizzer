Lantana mearnsii var. congolensis

Lantana mearnsii var. congolensis, commonly known as the Congo lantana, is a shrub native to Central and Eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange. The plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant appearance and hardiness.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, posing a threat to local ecosystems.
