Lobelia cardinalis var. phyllostachya

Lobelia cardinalis var. phyllostachya, commonly known as the scarlet cardinalflower, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vivid red flowers that stand out against its narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators like hummingbirds and butterflies.

This variety is prized in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
