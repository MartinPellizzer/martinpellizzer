Leobordea eriantha

Leobordea eriantha, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pale pink, are often clustered in inflorescences.

Leobordea eriantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses for its unique appearance.
