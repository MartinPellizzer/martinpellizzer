Lepisorus loriformis var. steniste

Lepisorus loriformis var. steniste, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on rocky substrates in shaded, humid environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, narrow leaf shape. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
