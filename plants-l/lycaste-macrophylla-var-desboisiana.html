Lycaste macrophylla var. desboisiana

Lycaste macrophylla var. desboisiana, commonly known as the Desboisiana Lycaste, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, giving it a lush, foliage-heavy appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle greenish tinge. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
