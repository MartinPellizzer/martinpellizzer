Limonia spectabilis

Limonia spectabilis, commonly known as the beautyberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces fragrant, white flowers in the spring, which are often overlooked due to the plant's modest appearance.

Limonia spectabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental berries and adaptability to various soil conditions.
