Ligustrum ibota var. obovatum

Ligustrum ibota var. obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf privet, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense growth habit and adaptability.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
