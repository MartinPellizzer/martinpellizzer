Laelia × philbrickiana

Laelia × philbrickiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia anceps and Laelia purpurea.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to the high-altitude regions of South America. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including pink, white, and purple. It thrives in cool, bright environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.

Laelia × philbrickiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
