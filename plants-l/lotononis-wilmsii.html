Lotononis wilmsii

Lotononis wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilmsii dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky soils, preferring full sun exposure. Its flowers are yellow and resemble those of the common dandelion, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

The leaves are basal, spoon-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
