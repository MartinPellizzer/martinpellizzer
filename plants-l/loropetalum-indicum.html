Loropetalum indicum

Loropetalum indicum, commonly known as Chinese flame bush, is a flowering shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from dark green to reddish-purple, depending on the variety. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of pink or red flowers in spring, which attract pollinators. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

Loropetalum indicum thrives in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 9, making it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate regions.
