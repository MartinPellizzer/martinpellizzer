Ligustrum japonicum f. rotundifolium

Ligustrum japonicum f. rotundifolium, commonly known as the Japanese privet with rounded leaves, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, dark green, ovate to rounded leaves that are densely arranged along its stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by black berries that are edible for birds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.

This form is a naturally occurring variant of the species, characterized by its distinctive leaf shape.
