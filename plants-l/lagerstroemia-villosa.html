Lagerstroemia villosa

Lagerstroemia villosa, commonly known as the silky crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to deep red, appearing in summer. The plant has smooth, gray bark that peels in thin layers, and its leaves are ovate with a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Lagerstroemia villosa is also valued for its ability to withstand urban conditions and its long blooming period.
