Lavatera cyrillii

Lavatera cyrillii, commonly known as the Cyprus Lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring broad, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, fluffy clusters of pale pink to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry climates.

Lavatera cyrillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
