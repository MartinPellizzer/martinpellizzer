Linospadix multifidus

Linospadix multifidus, commonly known as the broad-leafed spadix, is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

The plant features a spadix, a cylindrical cluster of small flowers, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. Linospadix multifidus is native to tropical regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and distinctive floral display.
