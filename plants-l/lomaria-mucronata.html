Lomaria mucronata

Lomaria mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lomaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.

Lomaria mucronata is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
