Lycianthes dendropilosa

Lycianthes dendropilosa, commonly known as the tree nightshade, is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into orange or red berries. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and it has a woody stem with thorny branches.
