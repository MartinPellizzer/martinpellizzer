Lepanthes jostii

Lepanthes jostii, commonly known as the Jost's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually small, with a white or pale pink coloration and a distinctive lip.

Lepanthes jostii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
