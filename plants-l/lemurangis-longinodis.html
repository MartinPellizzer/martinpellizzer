Lemurangis longinodis

Lemurangis longinodis, commonly known as the long-nosed lemurangis, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant has a woody stem with smooth, dark green leaves that have a slightly waxy texture. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by animals after consumption.
