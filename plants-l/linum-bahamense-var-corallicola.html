Linum bahamense var. corallicola

Linum bahamense var. corallicola, commonly known as the Bahamian flax, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It belongs to the Linaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
