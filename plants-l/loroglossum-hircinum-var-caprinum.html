Loroglossum hircinum var. caprinum

Loroglossum hircinum var. caprinum, commonly known as the goat's tongue plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tongue-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
