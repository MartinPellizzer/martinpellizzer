Lonicera dioscoridis

Lonicera dioscoridis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its fruit is a yellow, fleshy berry that matures in late summer.

Lonicera dioscoridis is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
