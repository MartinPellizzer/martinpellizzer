Lachenalia namaquensis

Lachenalia namaquensis, commonly known as the Namaqua lachenalia, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a sought-after plant among succulent enthusiasts.
