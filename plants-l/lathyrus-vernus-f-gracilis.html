Lathyrus vernus f. gracilis

Lathyrus vernus f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender vernal pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces delicate, pea-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of the genus Lathyrus. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological gardens.
