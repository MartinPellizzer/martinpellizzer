Lagophylla serrata

Lagophylla serrata, commonly known as the serrated leaf heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance.
