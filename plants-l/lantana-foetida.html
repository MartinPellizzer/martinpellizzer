Lantana foetida

Lantana foetida, commonly known as stinking lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, pink, or red, depending on the variety. It is known for its strong, often unpleasant odor, which is more pronounced in the flowers.

Lantana foetida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
