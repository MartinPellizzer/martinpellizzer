Lespedeza penduliflora var. cathayana

Lespedeza penduliflora var. cathayana, commonly known as the Chinese weeping lespeena, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its graceful, drooping inflorescences that produce small, pink to reddish-purple flowers in the summer months. The plant has slender, arching stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy beneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
