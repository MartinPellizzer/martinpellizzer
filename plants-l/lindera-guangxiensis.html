Lindera guangxiensis

Lindera guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi Lindera, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by dark blue berries in the fall.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
