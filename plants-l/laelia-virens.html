Laelia virens

Laelia virens, commonly known as the green laelia, is a species of orchid native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of green and white. The plant typically blooms during the spring and early summer, with showy inflorescences that can reach up to 30 cm in length. Its leaves are broad, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to conserve moisture in its high-altitude habitat.

Laelia virens is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
