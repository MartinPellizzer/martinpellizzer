Licuala leucocarpa

Licuala leucocarpa, commonly known as the white-fruited palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white, fleshy fruits. The fruits are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor, making them a valuable food source in local diets.

It is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
