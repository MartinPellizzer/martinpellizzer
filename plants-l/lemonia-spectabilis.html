Lemonia spectabilis

Lemonia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy lemon tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Lemonia spectabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
