Liparis kenejiae

Liparis kenejiae, commonly known as the Kenejia orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow blooms with intricate patterns, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Liparis kenejiae is considered a rare and endangered species.
