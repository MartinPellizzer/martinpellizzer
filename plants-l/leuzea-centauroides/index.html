Leuzea Centauroides

Leuzea centauroides is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its tall, branching stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. This species is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and is often overlooked in favor of more common flora.
