Lysimachia deltoidea var. deltoidea

Lysimachia deltoidea var. deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
