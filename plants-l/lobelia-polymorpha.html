Lobelia polymorpha

Lobelia polymorpha, commonly known as the variable lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It has a compact growth habit and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Lobelia polymorpha is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
