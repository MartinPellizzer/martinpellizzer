Limnanthemum humboldtianum var. parviflorum

Limnanthemum humboldtianum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the dwarf marshmallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
