Ligularia farfugium

Ligularia farfugium, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
