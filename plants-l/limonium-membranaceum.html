Limonium membranaceum

Limonium membranaceum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its narrow, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and is often found growing in sandy or gravelly soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained, sunny environments.

Limonium membranaceum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate saline conditions.
