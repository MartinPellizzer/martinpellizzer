Lepanthes altamiranoi

Lepanthes altamiranoi, commonly known as the Altamirano's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
