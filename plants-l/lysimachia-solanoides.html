Lysimachia solanoides

Lysimachia solanoides, commonly known as yellow archangel, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences. Despite its ornamental appeal, it can be invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread and adaptability.
