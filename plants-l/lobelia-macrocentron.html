Lobelia macrocentron

Lobelia macrocentron, commonly known as the showy lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia macrocentron is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
