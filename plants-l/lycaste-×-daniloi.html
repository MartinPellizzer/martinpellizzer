Lycaste × daniloi

Lycaste × daniloi, commonly known as the Danilo lycaste, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a result of crossbreeding between Lycaste and Brassia species, combining traits from both parents. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting mix.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
