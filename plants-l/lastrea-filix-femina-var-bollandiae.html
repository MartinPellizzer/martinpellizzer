Lastrea filix-femina var. bollandiae

Lastrea filix-femina var. bollandiae, commonly known as the Bolland's snake plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a distinctive white or yellowish stripe along the midrib. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which is a characteristic feature of many succulents.

This variety is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
