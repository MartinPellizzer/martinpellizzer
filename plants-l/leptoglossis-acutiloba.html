Leptoglossis acutiloba

Leptoglossis acutiloba, commonly known as the sharp-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Leptoglossis acutiloba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
