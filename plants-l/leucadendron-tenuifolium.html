Leucadendron tenuifolium

Leucadendron tenuifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf candle bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in cone-like structures, which are typical of the Proteaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
