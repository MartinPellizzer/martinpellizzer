Lysinema elegans

Lysinema elegans, commonly known as the elegant lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in cool, moist environments.

It typically grows on tree bark, rocks, and soil surfaces, forming dense, leaf-like structures. The plant has a symbiotic relationship with algae and fungi, which contribute to its ability to photosynthesize and absorb nutrients. Its thallus is usually green to yellowish in color, with a smooth or slightly wrinkled surface.

Lysinema elegans is often found in temperate regions and is an important indicator species for environmental health.
