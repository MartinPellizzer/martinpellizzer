Limonium rezniczenkoanum

Limonium rezniczenkoanum, commonly known as the coastal heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky coastal environments, often growing in well-drained soils. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its ability to tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

Limonium rezniczenkoanum is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
