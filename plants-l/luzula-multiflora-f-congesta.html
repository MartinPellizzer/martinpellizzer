Luzula multiflora f. congesta

Luzula multiflora f. congesta, commonly known as the dense-flowered wood rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
