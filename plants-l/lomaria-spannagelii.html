Lomaria spannagelii

Lomaria spannagelii, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant produces spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
