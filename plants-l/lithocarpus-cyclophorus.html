Lithocarpus cyclophorus

Lithocarpus cyclophorus, commonly known as the cyclophorous tallowwood, is a member of the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and beeches.

It is a large evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. It produces small, chestnut-like fruits that are encased in a spiny, woody husk.

The species is valued for its durable timber and plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
