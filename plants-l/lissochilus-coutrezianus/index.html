Lissochilus Coutrezianus

Lissochilus coutrezianus is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes. This delicate plant is known for its unique, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil, making it a challenging plant to cultivate. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its beauty and rarity have made it a subject of fascination for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
