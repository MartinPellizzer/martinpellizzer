Lissochilus pulcher

Lissochilus pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful lissochilus, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically blooms during the warmer months, and its flowers are often arranged in a raceme. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often found growing in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
