Lilium speciosum f. vittatum

Lilium speciosum f. vittatum, commonly known as the variegated tiger lily, is a striking hybrid form of the tiger lily.

It is characterized by its elongated, showy flowers with a distinctive orange-red coloration and prominent yellow-orange markings. The foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for summer blooms.
