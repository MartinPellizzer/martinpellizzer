Lachnosiphonium obovatum

Lachnosiphonium obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It features a dark green to blackish upper surface with a distinctive, leaf-like thallus structure. The plant's lower surface is covered in a network of hyphae and contains the reproductive structures. It is typically found on tree trunks and rocks in temperate regions.

Its morphology and growth habits make it an important indicator species for forest ecosystems.
