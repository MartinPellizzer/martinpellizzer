Lidia

Lidia, commonly known as wild thyme, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments and is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in culinary applications.

Lidia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
