Leichtlinia

Leichtlinia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Leichtlinia is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it both a useful plant in traditional medicine and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
