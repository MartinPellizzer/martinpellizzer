Lobivia uitewaaleana

Lobivia uitewaaleana, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny stems and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or purple blooms during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and desert landscapes.
