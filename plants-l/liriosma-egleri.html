Liriosma egleri

Liriosma egleri, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Liriosma egleri is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
