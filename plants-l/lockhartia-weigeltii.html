Lockhartia weigeltii

Lockhartia weigeltii, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers during the holiday season. The flowers are typically pink or purple with a yellowish throat, and they bloom in winter, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor cultivation.
