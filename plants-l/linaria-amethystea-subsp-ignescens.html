Linaria amethystea subsp. ignescens

Linaria amethystea subsp.

ignescens, commonly known as the flame linaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, orange-red flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
