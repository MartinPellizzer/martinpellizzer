Lepanthes composita

Lepanthes composita, commonly known as the composite orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the genus Lepanthes, which is part of the Orchidaceae family. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are often clustered in a loose inflorescence. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Lepanthes composita thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical rainforests.
