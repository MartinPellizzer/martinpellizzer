Leucaena oaxacana

Leucaena oaxacana, commonly known as Oaxacan leucaena, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often used in traditional agriculture and land restoration projects.
