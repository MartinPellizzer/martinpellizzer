Lasianthus membranaceus var. firmus

Lasianthus membranaceus var. firmus, commonly known as the firm leaf lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, membranous layer. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
