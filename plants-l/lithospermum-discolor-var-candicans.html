Lithospermum discolor var. candicans

Lithospermum discolor var. candicans, commonly known as the showy lithosperm, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from pink to red, with five petals and a distinctive yellow center. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, as it contains compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
