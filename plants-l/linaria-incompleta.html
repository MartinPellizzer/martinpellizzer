Linaria incompleta

Linaria incompleta, commonly known as the incomplete toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Linaria incompleta is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments.
