Loropetalum chinense var. coloratum

Loropetalum chinense var. coloratum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, showy bracts that range from red to purple, resembling small lanterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by clusters of bright red berries. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its striking foliage and seasonal color.

The variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
