Lepidium tortum

Lepidium tortum, commonly known as the twisted cress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Lepidium tortum is often used in herbal medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
