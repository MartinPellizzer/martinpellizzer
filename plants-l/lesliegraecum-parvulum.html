Lesliegraecum parvulum

Lesliegraecum parvulum, commonly known as the dwarf lesliegraecum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. The plant features compact, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
