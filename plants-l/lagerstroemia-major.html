Lagerstroemia major

Lagerstroemia major, commonly known as the Chinese flowering maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red or orange fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated margin.

Lagerstroemia major is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
