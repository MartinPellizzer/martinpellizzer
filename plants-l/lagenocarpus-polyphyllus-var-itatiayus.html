Lagenocarpus polyphyllus var. itatiayus

Lagenocarpus polyphyllus var. itatiayus, commonly known as the Itatiayu palm, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, black, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland areas with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its ecological and cultural significance, it is also valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
