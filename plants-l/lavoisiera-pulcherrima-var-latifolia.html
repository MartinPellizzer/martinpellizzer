Lavoisiera pulcherrima var. latifolia

Lavoisiera pulcherrima var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf lavoisiera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length, giving it a striking visual presence. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
