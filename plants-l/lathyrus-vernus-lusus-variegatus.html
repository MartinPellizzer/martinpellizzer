Lathyrus vernus lusus variegatus

Lathyrus vernus lusus variegatus, commonly known as the variegated spring sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which display a mix of green, white, and sometimes yellow patterns. The plant produces delicate, pea-like flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, typically blooming in early spring. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
