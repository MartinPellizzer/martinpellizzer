Laubertia brasiliensis

Laubertia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Brazil.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.
