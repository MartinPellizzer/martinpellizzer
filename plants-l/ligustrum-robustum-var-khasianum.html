Ligustrum robustum var. khasianum

Ligustrum robustum var. khasianum, commonly known as the Khasi privet, is a shrub native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance.

This variety is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
