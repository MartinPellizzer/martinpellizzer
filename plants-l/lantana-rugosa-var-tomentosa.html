Lantana rugosa var. tomentosa

Lantana rugosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly lantana, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a soft, woolly appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, or red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming season contribute to its aesthetic appeal in both landscapes and containers.
