Lythrum portula var. callitrichoides

Lythrum portula var. callitrichoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lythrum, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes plants known for their showy flowers and ecological importance. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late summer.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
