Lotodes onobrychis

Lotodes onobrychis, commonly known as false vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soils and dry conditions. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
