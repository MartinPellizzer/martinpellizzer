Linaria fontanesiana

Linaria fontanesiana, commonly known as the Fontanesian toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Linaria fontanesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
