Lepanthes oroscoi

Lepanthes oroscoi, commonly known as the Oroscoi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree branches or rocky surfaces. The plant features delicate, showy flowers with a distinctive white and purple coloration, often with a fragrant scent. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to capture moisture from the humid air.

Lepanthes oroscoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
