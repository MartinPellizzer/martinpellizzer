Lotus chihuahuanus

Lotus chihuahuanus, commonly known as the Chihuahuan lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Chihuahuan Desert in North America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is adapted to thrive in arid environments with minimal water. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in desert botany.
