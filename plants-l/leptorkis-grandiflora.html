Leptorkis grandiflora

Leptorkis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered leptorkis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
