Leontodon masmenaeus

Leontodon masmenaeus, commonly known as the Masmenaeus bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. The plant features erect, branched stems with narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its showy, daisy-like flowers are white with yellow centers, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Leontodon masmenaeus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
