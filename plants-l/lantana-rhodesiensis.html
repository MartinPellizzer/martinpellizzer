Lantana rhodesiensis

Lantana rhodesiensis, commonly known as the Rhodesian lantana, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Lantana rhodesiensis is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in landscaping.
