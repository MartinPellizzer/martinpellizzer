Lysimachia lata

Lysimachia lata, commonly known as broadleaf filmy fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features creeping rhizomes and produces small, white or yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Lysimachia lata is noted for its ability to spread and form dense mats, making it a common groundcover in its native habitat.
