Linaria tristis subsp. eutristis

Linaria tristis subsp.

eutristis, commonly known as the narrowleaf toadflax, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
