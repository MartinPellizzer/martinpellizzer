Lobivia ferox f. variispina

Lobivia ferox f. variispina, commonly known as the Thorny Lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its robust, spiny stems that store water for survival in harsh environments. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure. Its unique appearance includes a cylindrical stem with dense spines and small, fleshy leaves that reduce water loss.

This variety is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its striking morphology and resilience.
