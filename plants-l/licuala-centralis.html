Licuala centralis

Licuala centralis, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which also includes ginger and turmeric. Its foliage is often used in traditional crafts and as a decorative element in gardens.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and cultural significance.
