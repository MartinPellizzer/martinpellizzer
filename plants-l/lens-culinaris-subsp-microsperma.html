Lens culinaris subsp. microsperma

Lens culinaris subsp.

microsperma, commonly known as the small-seeded lentil, is a subspecies of the lentil plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, round seeds that are typically brown or yellow in color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has compound leaves with three leaflets. It thrives in temperate climates and is cultivated for its nutritional seeds rich in protein and fiber.

This subspecies is valued for its adaptability and use in various cuisines around the world.
