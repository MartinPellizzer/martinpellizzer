Lobelia senegalensis var. subaspera

Lobelia senegalensis var. subaspera, commonly known as the African lobelia, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with a fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
