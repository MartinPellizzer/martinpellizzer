Lachnaea laxa

Lachnaea laxa, commonly known as the slender lachnaea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant has alternate, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually clustered in loose inflorescences.

Lachnaea laxa is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
