Lampranthus productus

Lampranthus productus, commonly known as the "Christmas star" or "firecracker plant," is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright orange to red, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a favored ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.
