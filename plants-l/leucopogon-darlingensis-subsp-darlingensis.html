Leucopogon darlingensis subsp. darlingensis

Leucopogon darlingensis subsp.

darlingensis, commonly known as Darling's heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white.
