Linaria iconia

Linaria iconia, commonly known as the rock scabious, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Linaria iconia is known for its resilience and is often used in rock gardens or as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.
