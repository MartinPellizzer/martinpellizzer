Lonicera fuchsioides

Lonicera fuchsioides, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange-red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Lonicera fuchsioides is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
