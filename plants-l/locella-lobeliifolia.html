Locella lobeliifolia

Locella lobeliifolia, commonly known as the slender bluets, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
