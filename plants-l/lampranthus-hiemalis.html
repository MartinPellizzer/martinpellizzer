Lampranthus hiemalis

Lampranthus hiemalis, commonly known as the winter star.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Lampranthus hiemalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
