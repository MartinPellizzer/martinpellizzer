Leucas eriostoma var. latifolia

Leucas eriostoma var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf leucas, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or wetlands.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
