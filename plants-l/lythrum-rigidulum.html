Lythrum rigidulum

Lythrum rigidulum, commonly known as rigid willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Lythraceae family, which includes other species such as the common willowherb. The plant is characterized by its upright, often hairy stems and small, clustered pink or red flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Lythrum rigidulum thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.
