Lantana mearnsii var. punctata

Lantana mearnsii var. punctata, commonly known as the dotted lantana, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive white or pale mark at the base. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
