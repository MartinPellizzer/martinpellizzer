Lonicera vestita

Lonicera vestita, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and usually ovate or elliptic leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

Lonicera vestita is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
