Limonium obovatum

Limonium obovatum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-blue foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in saline environments. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, and it is often used in ornamental gardening.

Limonium obovatum is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
