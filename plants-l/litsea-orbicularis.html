Litsea orbicularis

Litsea orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its glossy, orbicular-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, reddish berries.

Litsea orbicularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
