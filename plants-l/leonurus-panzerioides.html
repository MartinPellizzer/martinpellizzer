Leonurus panzerioides

Leonurus panzerioides, commonly known as the panzerioides hedgehog.

This plant is a perennial herb native to specific regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
