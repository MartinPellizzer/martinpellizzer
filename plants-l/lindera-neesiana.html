Lindera neesiana

Lindera neesiana, commonly known as the Chinese wild laurier, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel trees and other aromatic plants. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark purple berries.

Lindera neesiana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
