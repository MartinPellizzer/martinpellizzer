Laburnum anagyroides f. linnaeanum

Laburnum anagyroides f. linnaeanum, commonly known as the Linnaean golden chain, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking ornamental display. The plant has slender, arching branches with small, pinnate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It is well-suited for small gardens or containers due to its compact size.

The cultivar is valued for its vibrant floral display and ease of cultivation.
