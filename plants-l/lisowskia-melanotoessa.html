Lisowskia melanotoessa

Lisowskia melanotoessa, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dark purple veins on the petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a striking contrast between the dark veins and lighter-colored petals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
