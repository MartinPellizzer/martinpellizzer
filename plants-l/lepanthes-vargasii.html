Lepanthes vargasii

Lepanthes vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Lepanthes vargasii is part of the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit.
