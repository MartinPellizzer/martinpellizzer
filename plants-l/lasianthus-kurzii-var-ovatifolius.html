Lasianthus kurzii var. ovatifolius

Lasianthus kurzii var. ovatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its ovate, glossy leaves and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
