Ligustrum obtusifolium var. leiocalyx

Ligustrum obtusifolium var. leiocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved privet, is a shrub native to eastern China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The varietal form leiocalyx is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical variety.
