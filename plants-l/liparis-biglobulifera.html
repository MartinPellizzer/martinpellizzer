Liparis biglobulifera

Liparis biglobulifera, commonly known as the giant globular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that consists of numerous clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, while the roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the soil.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure, which includes prominent, globular-shaped blooms.
