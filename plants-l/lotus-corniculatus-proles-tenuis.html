Lotus corniculatus proles tenuis

Lotus corniculatus proles tenuis, commonly known as the slender lotus, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of the genus Lotus. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
