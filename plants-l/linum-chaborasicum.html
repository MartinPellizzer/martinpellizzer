Linum chaborasicum

Linum chaborasicum, commonly known as the Chaborasi flax, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant produces delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Linum chaborasicum is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
