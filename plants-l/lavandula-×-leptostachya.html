Lavandula × leptostachya

Lavandula × leptostachya, commonly known as narrow-leaved lavender, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage and spikes of purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Lavandula × leptostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild aromatic properties.
