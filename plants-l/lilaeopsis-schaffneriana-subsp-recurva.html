Lilaeopsis schaffneriana subsp. recurva

Lilaeopsis schaffneriana subsp.

recurva, commonly known as the recurved lily, is a member of the Iridaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate, tubular flowers.
