Lotus mollissimus

Lotus mollissimus, commonly known as the silky lotus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks. Its flowers are typically surrounded by a cluster of small, yellowish petals, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

The silky lotus is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
