Leucas welwitschii

Leucas welwitschii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Leucas welwitschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
