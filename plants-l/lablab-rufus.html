Lablab rufus

Lablab rufus, commonly known as red lablab, is a leguminous plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with a reddish tinge. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.

Lablab rufus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural systems.
