Ligularia pianmaensis

Ligularia pianmaensis, commonly known as the Pianma giant arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in the summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountain streams and rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
