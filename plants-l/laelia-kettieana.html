Laelia kettieana

Laelia kettieana, commonly known as the Kettie's orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a compact rhizome with several pseudobulbs, each supporting a single leaf and a flower spike. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with red or purple markings, and they bloom during the cooler months.

Laelia kettieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
