Lotus quinatus var. brachycarpus

Lotus quinatus var. brachycarpus, commonly known as the dwarf lotus, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped base and hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
