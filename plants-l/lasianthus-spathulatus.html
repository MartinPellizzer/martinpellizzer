Lasianthus spathulatus

Lasianthus spathulatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lanceolate to ovate leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are often violet or purple in color and are arranged in spikes.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
