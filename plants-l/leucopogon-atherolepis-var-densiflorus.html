Leucopogon atherolepis var. densiflorus

Leucopogon atherolepis var. densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale pink blooms, which are often borne in dense inflorescences. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it prefers cool, moist conditions.
