Lithachne Pinetii

Lithachne pinetii is a small, perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in wetland and swampy environments, often found in association with pine trees. This plant is characterized by its slender stems and narrow leaves that are typically less than a foot in height. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique member of the grass family.
