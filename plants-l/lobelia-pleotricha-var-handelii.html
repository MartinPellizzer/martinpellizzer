Lobelia pleotricha var. handelii

Lobelia pleotricha var. handelii, commonly known as the Mexican bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features striking blue to violet tubular flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its role in supporting pollinators.
