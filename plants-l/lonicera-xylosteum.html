Lonicera xylosteum

Lonicera xylosteum, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, oval leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly rough texture. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its berries, which are orange-red and edible, mature in the fall and are a food source for birds.

The species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
