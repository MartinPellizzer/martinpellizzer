Lonicera conjugialis

Lonicera conjugialis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often with a red or orange throat. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

The fruit is a red, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.
