Limonium gallurense

Limonium gallurense, commonly known as sea helene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding a splash of color to coastal landscapes.

Limonium gallurense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand salty and windy conditions.
