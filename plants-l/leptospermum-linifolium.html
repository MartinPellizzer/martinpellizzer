Leptospermum linifolium

Leptospermum linifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is part of the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic species. It typically grows in coastal and heathland environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

The leaves and flowers are often used in traditional and modern herbal remedies due to their aromatic and medicinal properties.
