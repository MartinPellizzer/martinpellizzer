Leonurus discolor

Leonurus discolor, commonly known as the lesser hedgehog urinary plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features opposite, hairy, and usually lance-shaped leaves, and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in whorls along the stems. It is often found in dry, rocky, or sandy soils and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

The plant's common name reflects its historical use in treating urinary disorders.
