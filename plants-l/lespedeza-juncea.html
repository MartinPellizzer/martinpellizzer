Lespedeza juncea

Lespedeza juncea, commonly known as yellow lespedeza, is a perennial flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, elongated pods containing seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Lespedeza juncea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
