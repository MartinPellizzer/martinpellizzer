Labourdonnaisia thouarsii

Labourdonnaisia thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
