Linaria hians

Linaria hians, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, linear to lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with two lips, typical of the Plantaginaceae family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Linaria hians is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and drought tolerance.
