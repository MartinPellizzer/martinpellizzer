Lepidium barnebyanum

Lepidium barnebyanum, commonly known as the Barneby's cress, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
