Litchi philippinensis f. mindanaensis

Litchi philippinensis f. mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao litchi, is a botanical variant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, glossy green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically large, with a thick, red outer skin and a sweet, juicy interior. This variety thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

It is valued for its unique flavor and is often cultivated for both local consumption and commercial purposes.
