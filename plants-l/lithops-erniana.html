Lithops erniana

Lithops erniana, commonly known as the Cape Town lithops, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance, with paired, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Lithops erniana is also known for its ability to mimic surrounding rocks, providing it with natural camouflage.
