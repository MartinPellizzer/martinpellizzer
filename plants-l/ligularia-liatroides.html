Ligularia liatroides

Ligularia liatroides, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland gardens.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
