Linosyris arborescens

Linosyris arborescens, commonly known as the tree dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Linosyris arborescens is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
