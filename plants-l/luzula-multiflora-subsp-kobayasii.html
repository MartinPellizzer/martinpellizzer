Luzula multiflora subsp. kobayasii

Luzula multiflora subsp.

kobayasii, commonly known as the Kobayashi woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

The plant features grass-like leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in dense tufts. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered spikes of pale blue to purple flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and specific habitat preferences.
