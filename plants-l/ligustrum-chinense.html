Ligustrum chinense

Ligustrum chinense, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by dark blue berries. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
