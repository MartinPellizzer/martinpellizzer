Lonicera korolkowii var. aurora

Lonicera korolkowii var. aurora, commonly known as the aurora honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is characterized by opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, dark green upper surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in late summer and attracts wildlife.
