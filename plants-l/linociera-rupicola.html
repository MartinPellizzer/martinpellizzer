Linociera rupicola

Linociera rupicola, commonly known as the rock lichen, is a type of lichen that thrives in rocky and arid environments.

It is composed of a symbiotic relationship between a fungus and a green alga or cyanobacterium. This plant is often found on exposed rock surfaces, where it can absorb moisture from the air. Linociera rupicola is known for its ability to survive in harsh conditions with minimal nutrients.

Its presence is often an indicator of environmental stability and low disturbance in its habitat.
