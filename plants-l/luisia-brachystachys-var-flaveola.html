Luisia brachystachys var. flaveola

Luisia brachystachys var. flaveola, commonly known as the yellow-flowered luisia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine, silky pubescence.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
