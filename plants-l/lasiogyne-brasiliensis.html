Lasiogyne brasiliensis

Lasiogyne brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, star-shaped berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.
