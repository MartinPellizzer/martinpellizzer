Lotus fraseri

Lotus fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, with long, slender stems that support its showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in water gardens.
