Lotus erectus

Lotus erectus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water-loving plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its striking pink or white flowers bloom above the water. Its roots are aquatic, and the plant is known for its ability to purify water.

The lotus is also notable for its symbolic significance in various cultures.
