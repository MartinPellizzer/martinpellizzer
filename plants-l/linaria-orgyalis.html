Linaria orgyalis

Linaria orgyalis, commonly known as the Ethiopian toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant features slender stems and produces yellow or orange flowers that resemble those of snapdragons. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Linaria orgyalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
