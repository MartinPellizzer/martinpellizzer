Lindera thunbergii

Lindera thunbergii, commonly known as Japanese spicebush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value contribute to its horticultural appeal.
