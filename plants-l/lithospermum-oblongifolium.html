Lithospermum oblongifolium

Lithospermum oblongifolium, commonly known as red root, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to red in hue, which bloom in summer. Its roots are rich in alkaloids and have been used in traditional medicine.

Lithospermum oblongifolium is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
