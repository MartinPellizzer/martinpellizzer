Lepanthes spiculifera

Lepanthes spiculifera, commonly known as the spiky lepanthes, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, or green. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its leaves are usually narrow and strap-like, adapted to capture moisture from the air.

Lepanthes spiculifera is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
