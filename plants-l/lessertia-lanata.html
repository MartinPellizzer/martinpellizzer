Lessertia lanata

Lessertia lanata, commonly known as the woolly lessertia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
