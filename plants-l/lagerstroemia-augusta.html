Lagerstroemia augusta

Lagerstroemia augusta, commonly known as the Crape Myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that appear in summer, ranging in color from pink to red and white. The plant has smooth, mottled bark that becomes increasingly textured with age. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, turning red or yellow in the fall.

Lagerstroemia augusta is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
