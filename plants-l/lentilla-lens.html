Lentilla lens

Lentilla lens, commonly known as the lentil.

It is an annual legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–60 cm and has trifoliate leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, and the pods contain 3–5 seeds.

The lentil is valued for its nutritional content, including protein, fiber, and essential minerals.
