Lasianthus flavicans

Lasianthus flavicans, commonly known as the yellow-flowered lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are notable for their bright color and are often used in traditional medicine and ornamental horticulture.
