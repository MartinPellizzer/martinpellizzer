Litsea rubescens var. yunnanensis

Litsea rubescens var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, ovate leaves with a reddish-brown underside, which contribute to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elliptical, red-orange fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
