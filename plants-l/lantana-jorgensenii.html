Lantana jorgensenii

Lantana jorgensenii, commonly known as the Hawaiian lantana, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and lowland habitats.

Lantana jorgensenii is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas, making it both ecologically significant and potentially invasive.
