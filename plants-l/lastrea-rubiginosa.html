Lastrea rubiginosa

Lastrea rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive red stems and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Lastrea rubiginosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and vibrant color.
