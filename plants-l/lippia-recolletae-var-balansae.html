Lippia recolletae var. balansae

Lippia recolletae var. balansae, commonly known as Mexican blue sage, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, fragrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often emitting a minty scent when crushed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and use in traditional medicine.
