Lepisanthes oligophylla

Lepisanthes oligophylla, commonly known as the green bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and have a glossy texture, while its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences.

Lepisanthes oligophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
