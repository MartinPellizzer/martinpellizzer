Lilium thunbergianum var. pardinum

Lilium thunbergianum var. pardinum, commonly known as the Japanese tiger lily, is a variant of the tiger lily species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red petals with dark, tiger-like markings. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in the summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
