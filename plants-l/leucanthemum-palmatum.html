Leucanthemum palmatum

Leucanthemum palmatum, commonly known as the white marguerite or European oxeye daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features a rosette of broad, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers with a central yellow disk, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Leucanthemum palmatum is often used in garden landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
