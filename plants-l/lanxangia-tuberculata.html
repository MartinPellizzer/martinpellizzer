Lanxangia tuberculata

Lanxangia tuberculata, commonly known as the Thai mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or white with a distinctive spurred shape.

Lanxangia tuberculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
