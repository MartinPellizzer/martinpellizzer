Lepanthes siboei

Lepanthes siboei, commonly known as the Siboei orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America, particularly found in the tropical regions of Costa Rica and Panama.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Lepanthes siboei is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after addition to specialized collections.
