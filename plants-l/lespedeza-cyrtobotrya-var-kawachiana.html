Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. kawachiana

Lespedeza cyrtobotrya var. kawachiana, commonly known as Kawachiana lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia, particularly Japan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.

Its adaptability and aesthetic qualities make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
