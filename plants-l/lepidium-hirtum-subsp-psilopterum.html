Lepidium hirtum subsp. psilopterum

Lepidium hirtum subsp.

psilopterum, commonly known as the slender wallcress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant has pinnately divided leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

This subspecies is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in mountainous areas.
