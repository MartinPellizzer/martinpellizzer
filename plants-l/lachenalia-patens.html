Lachenalia patens

Lachenalia patens, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Lachenalia patens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
