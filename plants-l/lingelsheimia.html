Lingelsheimia

Lingelsheimia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, Lingelsheimia is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
