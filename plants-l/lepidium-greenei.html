Lepidium greenei

Lepidium greenei, commonly known as Greene's cress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. This species is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and meadows.
