Lorostelma struthianthus

Lorostelma struthianthus, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Lorostelma struthianthus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
