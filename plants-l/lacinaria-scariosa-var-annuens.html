Lacinaria scariosa var. annuens

Lacinaria scariosa var. annuens, commonly known as the scarious lacinia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that are often covered in a waxy, scaly coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique morphology, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in botanical gardens.
