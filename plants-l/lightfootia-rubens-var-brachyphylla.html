Lightfootia rubens var. brachyphylla

Lightfootia rubens var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lightfootia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
