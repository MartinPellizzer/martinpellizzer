Loeflingia hispanica var. glabra

Loeflingia hispanica var. glabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf loeflingia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
