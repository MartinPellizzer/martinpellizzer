Limonium naniforme

Limonium naniforme, commonly known as the dwarf sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in sandy or salty soils. Limonium naniforme is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
