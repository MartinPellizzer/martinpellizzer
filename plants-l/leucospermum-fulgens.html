Leucospermum fulgens

Leucospermum fulgens, commonly known as the firewheel plant, is a striking member of the Proteaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a fiery display. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with dense, evergreen foliage that provides a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in horticulture for its unique and eye-catching appearance.
