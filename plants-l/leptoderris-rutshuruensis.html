Leptoderris rutshuruensis

Leptoderris rutshuruensis, commonly known as the Rutshuru tree, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has a deep root system that helps stabilize the soil and prevent erosion. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The Rutshuru tree plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
