Lissanthe verticillata

Lissanthe verticillata, commonly known as the whorled lissanthe, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, often arranged in whorls along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Lissanthe verticillata is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
