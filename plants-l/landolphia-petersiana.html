Landolphia petersiana

Landolphia petersiana, commonly known as the Petersiana morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces distinctive fruit capsules that split open to release its seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Landolphia petersiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
