Lemmaphyllum carnosum var. microphyllum

Lemmaphyllum carnosum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
