Lycaste neglecta

Lycaste neglecta, commonly known as the neglected lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with numerous fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, arranged in a fan shape.

Lycaste neglecta is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
