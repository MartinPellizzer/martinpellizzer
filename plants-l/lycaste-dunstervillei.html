Lycaste dunstervillei

Lycaste dunstervillei, commonly known as the Dunsterville's lycaste, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescence. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
