Lasianthus austroyunnanensis

Lasianthus austroyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lasianthus, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers, which are usually pink or purple, bloom in clusters and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
