Landolphia congolana

Landolphia congolana, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow, pink, and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Landolphia congolana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some regions.
