Lobelia platycalyx

Lobelia platycalyx, commonly known as the broad-calyx lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Lobelia platycalyx is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and woodland areas.
