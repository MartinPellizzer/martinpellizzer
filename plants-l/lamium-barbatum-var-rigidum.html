Lamium barbatum var. rigidum

Lamium barbatum var. rigidum, commonly known as rigid deadnettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are hairy and slightly downy, with a distinctive silver-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or reddish-purple, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
