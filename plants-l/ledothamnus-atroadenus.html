Ledothamnus atroadenus

Ledothamnus atroadenus, commonly known as the Atrodenus thornbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces bright yellow or orange berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Its thorny structure provides protection from herbivores, making it well adapted to its harsh native habitat.
