Ligustrum gyirongense

Ligustrum gyirongense, commonly known as the Gyirong Valley privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Gyirong region in the Tibet Autonomous Region. This shrub typically grows in mountainous areas and has small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, dark berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture for its hardy nature and adaptability to various soil conditions.
