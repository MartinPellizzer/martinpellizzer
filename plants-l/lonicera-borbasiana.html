Lonicera borbasiana

Lonicera borbasiana, commonly known as the Borbas honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with smooth margins and a glossy green upper surface. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the summer and is edible for both humans and wildlife.

Lonicera borbasiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
