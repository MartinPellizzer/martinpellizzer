Leopoldia Cycladica

Leopoldia cycladica is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the Cyclades islands of Greece. It is known for its unique, showy blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy yet elusive species. Its beauty has made it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its charm, it remains poorly understood and underappreciated in the wider botanical community.
