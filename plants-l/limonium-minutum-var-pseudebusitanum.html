Limonium minutum var. pseudebusitanum

Limonium minutum var. pseudebusitanum, commonly known as the dwarf sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high tolerance for salt and wind. Its compact stature and delicate appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
