Limonium × sennenii

Limonium × sennenii, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky flower heads that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

Limonium × sennenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
