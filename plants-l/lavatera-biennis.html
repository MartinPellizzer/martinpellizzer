Lavatera biennis

Lavatera biennis, commonly known as the annual lavatera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, fluffy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Lavatera biennis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
