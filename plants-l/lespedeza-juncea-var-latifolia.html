Lespedeza juncea var. latifolia

Lespedeza juncea var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf lespedeza, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet soils in woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features trifoliate leaves with broad, ovate leaflets and produces small, pink to reddish-purple flowers in racemes. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
