Lotus argenteus

Lotus argenteus, commonly known as the silver lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a yellow center, which bloom during the rainy season. The plant thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshy areas or near water bodies. Its leaves are broad and floating, contributing to its unique aquatic appearance.

Lotus argenteus is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural and medicinal significance in traditional practices.
