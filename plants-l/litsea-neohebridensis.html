Litsea neohebridensis

Litsea neohebridensis, commonly known as the New Hebrides litsea, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface with a distinct scent when crushed.

The fruit is a small, spherical drupe that matures to a dark purple color, contributing to its ecological significance in its native environment.
