Lomaria spectabilis

Lomaria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy lomariya, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in humid conditions. Its flowers, which can be pink, purple, or white, are often fragrant and attract various pollinators.

Lomaria spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
