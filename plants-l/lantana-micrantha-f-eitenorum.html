Lantana micrantha f. eitenorum

Lantana micrantha f. eitenorum, commonly known as the Eiten's lantana, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is shrub-like, with opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
