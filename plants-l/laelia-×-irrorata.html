Laelia × irrorata

Laelia × irrorata, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Laelia and Brassavola species, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, Laelia × irrorata is widely appreciated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
