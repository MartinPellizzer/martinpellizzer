Linociera caudifolia

Linociera caudifolia, commonly known as the long-tailed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has palmate leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild fragrance. Linociera caudifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
