Lotononis gerrardii var. transvaalensis

Lotononis gerrardii var. transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
