Lycopodium carolinianum var. paradoxum

Lycopodium carolinianum var. paradoxum, commonly known as the paradox lycopod, is a small evergreen fern-like plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, clustered clusters of bright green, needle-like leaves that give it a moss-like appearance. The plant produces small, spore-bearing cones at the base of its foliage, which are typically reddish-brown in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
