Leptostachya heterophylla var. obtusa

Leptostachya heterophylla var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively, making it adaptable to various soil types. The plant produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow flowers, which are followed by distinctive, three-lobed berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its potential in horticulture and traditional medicine.
