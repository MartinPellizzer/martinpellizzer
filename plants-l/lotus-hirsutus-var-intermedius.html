Lotus hirsutus var. intermedius

Lotus hirsutus var. intermedius, commonly known as the hairy lotus or intermediate lotus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in various regions.
