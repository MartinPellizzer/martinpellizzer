Lysimachia arvensis var. arvensis

Lysimachia arvensis var. arvensis, commonly known as yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly waxy texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
