Ligularia japonica var. japonica

Ligularia japonica var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in late summer, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental horticulture for its striking appearance.
