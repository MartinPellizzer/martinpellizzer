Lonicera xylosteum f. glabrescens

Lonicera xylosteum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth honeysuckle, is a variant of the common honeysuckle.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular yellow flowers in early summer. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodland edges and moist soils.

The fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer and is attractive to birds.
