Lachenalia juncifolia var. campanulata

Lachenalia juncifolia var. campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems bearing clusters of bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range from white to pale purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid environments.
