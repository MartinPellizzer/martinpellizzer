Lisianthius perkinsiae

Lisianthius perkinsiae, commonly known as the Perkin's lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Ecuador.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like blooms that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has narrow, grass-like leaves.
