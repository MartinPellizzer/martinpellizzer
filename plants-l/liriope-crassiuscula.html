Liriope crassiuscula

Liriope crassiuscula, commonly known as the narrowleaf lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color and form dense clumps. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers in summer, arranged in spike-like clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Liriope crassiuscula is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.
