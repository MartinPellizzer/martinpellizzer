Lyperia simplex

Lyperia simplex, commonly known as the common lyperia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky areas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Lyperia simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
