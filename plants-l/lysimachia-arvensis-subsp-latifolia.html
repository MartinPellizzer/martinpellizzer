Lysimachia arvensis subsp. latifolia

Lysimachia arvensis subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf yellow loosestrife, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features opposite, broad, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
