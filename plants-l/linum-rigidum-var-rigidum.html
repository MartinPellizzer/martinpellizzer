Linum rigidum var. rigidum

Linum rigidum var. rigidum, commonly known as rigid flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Linum rigidum var. rigidum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
