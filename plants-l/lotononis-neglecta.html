Lotononis neglecta

Lotononis neglecta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its ability to produce vibrant, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, Lotononis neglecta is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
