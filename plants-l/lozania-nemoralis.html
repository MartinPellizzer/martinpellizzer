Lozania nemoralis

Lozania nemoralis, commonly known as the common lozania, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

Lozania nemoralis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
