Liriope brachyphylla

Liriope brachyphylla, commonly known as the narrowleaf lilyturf, is a perennial evergreen herb native to eastern Asia.

It features grass-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color and form dense clumps. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in late summer, which are arranged in spike-like clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Liriope brachyphylla is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
