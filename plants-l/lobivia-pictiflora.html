Lobivia pictiflora

Lobivia pictiflora, commonly known as the Painted Flowered Lobivia, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and its ability to produce vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features spiny stems and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. It has a shallow root system that allows it to absorb moisture efficiently during rare rainfalls.

Lobivia pictiflora is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
