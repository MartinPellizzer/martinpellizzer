Lennea viridiflora

Lennea viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered lennea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
