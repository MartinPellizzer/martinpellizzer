Lasianthus japonicus var. latifolius

Lasianthus japonicus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed Japanese lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
