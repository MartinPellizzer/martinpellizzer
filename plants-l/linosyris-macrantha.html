Linosyris macrantha

Linosyris macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered linosyris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Linosyris macrantha thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
