Lagenaria lagenaria

Lagenaria lagenaria, commonly known as the bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a herbaceous, fast-growing vine that produces large, hollow fruits with a smooth, pale green skin. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun and well-drained soil. Its fruits can be harvested at various stages of maturity, ranging from immature green to mature yellow or orange.

The plant is also known for its versatility in culinary and practical uses.
