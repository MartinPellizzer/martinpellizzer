Lycaste fragrans

Lycaste fragrans, commonly known as the sweet-scented lycaste, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, warm environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.
