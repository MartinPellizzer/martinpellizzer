Lonicera transarisanensis

Lonicera transarisanensis, commonly known as the Transarisan honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

Lonicera transarisanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
