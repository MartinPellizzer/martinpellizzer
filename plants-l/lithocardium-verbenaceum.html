Lithocardium verbenaceum

Lithocardium verbenaceum, commonly known as the rock verbena, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family, which includes other flowering plants known for their showy blooms. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, making it well adapted to arid and coastal environments.

Lithocardium verbenaceum is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
