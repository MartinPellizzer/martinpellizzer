Ligusticum peucedanoides

Ligusticum peucedanoides, commonly known as Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate flowers. It is often cultivated for its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties.
