Lasianthus macrocalyx

Lasianthus macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
