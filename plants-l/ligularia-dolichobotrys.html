Ligularia dolichobotrys

Ligularia dolichobotrys, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking perennial plant native to the forests of East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant features a distinctive inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a broad, green spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas with rich, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic foliage plant.
