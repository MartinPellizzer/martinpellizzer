Lepidium jujuyanum

Lepidium jujuyanum, commonly known as the Andean cress, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and its finely divided, fern-like leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Lepidium jujuyanum is also valued for its nutritional content and is used in traditional medicine.
