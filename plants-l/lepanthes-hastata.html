Lepanthes hastata

Lepanthes hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, narrow leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and cloud forests. Its flowers are often short-lived but attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Lepanthes hastata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
