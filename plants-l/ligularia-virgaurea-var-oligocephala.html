Ligularia virgaurea var. oligocephala

Ligularia virgaurea var. oligocephala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ligularia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive, waxy surface and a serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to wetland and woodland gardens.
