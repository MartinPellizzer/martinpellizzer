Lepanthes chelonion

Lepanthes chelonion, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, while its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

Lepanthes chelonion is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
