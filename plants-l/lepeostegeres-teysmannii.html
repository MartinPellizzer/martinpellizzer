Lepeostegeres teysmannii

Lepeostegeres teysmannii, commonly known as the Sunda orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender rhizome and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.
