Limonium Speciosum

Limonium speciosum, also known as sea holly, is a hardy flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa. It is prized for its striking, spiky flowers that bloom in shades of blue, purple, and white. This plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is highly tolerant of salt and wind. Its ability to flourish in harsh environments makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscapes. Limonium speciosum not only adds visual interest but also supports local pollinators and wildlife.
