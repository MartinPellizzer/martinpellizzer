Lobelia borneensis subsp. celebensis

Lobelia borneensis subsp.

celebensis, commonly known as the Celebes lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, preferring rich, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and erect.
