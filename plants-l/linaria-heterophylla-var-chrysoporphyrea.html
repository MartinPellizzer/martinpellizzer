Linaria heterophylla var. chrysoporphyrea

Linaria heterophylla var. chrysoporphyrea, commonly known as the golden flame linaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to horticultural collections.
