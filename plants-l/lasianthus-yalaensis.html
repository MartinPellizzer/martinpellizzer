Lasianthus yalaensis

Lasianthus yalaensis, commonly known as the Yala lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
