Lepanthes actias-luna

Lepanthes actias-luna, commonly known as the Luna Moon Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking white and violet petals, which are often speckled with dark markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as cloud forests, where it attaches itself to tree bark. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a delicate and fragrant scent.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
