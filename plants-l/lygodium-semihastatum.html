Lygodium semihastatum

Lygodium semihastatum, commonly known as the semi-hastate fern, is a tropical fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lygodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its spores are borne on the underside of its leaves, which are divided into small, branching segments.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, tropical gardens.
