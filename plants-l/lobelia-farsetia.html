Lobelia farsetia

Lobelia farsetia, commonly known as the Farsetia lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a rough texture and are often covered in fine hairs.

Lobelia farsetia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
