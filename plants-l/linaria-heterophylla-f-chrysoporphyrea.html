Linaria heterophylla f. chrysoporphyrea

Linaria heterophylla f. chrysoporphyrea, commonly known as the golden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a compact, bushy perennial that produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers in late summer to early autumn. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its striking color and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
