Lentiscus atlantica

Lentiscus atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic lentisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in rocky, coastal environments and is drought-tolerant.

Lentiscus atlantica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
