Lacinaria scariosa var. trilisioides

Lacinaria scariosa var. trilisioides, commonly known as the Three-lobed Lacinaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features slender, ascending stems that bear opposite, trifoliate leaves with three lance-shaped lobes. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, scaly texture, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or forest understory habitats.
