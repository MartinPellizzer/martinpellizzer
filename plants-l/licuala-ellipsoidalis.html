Licuala ellipsoidalis

Licuala ellipsoidalis, commonly known as the elliptical palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, red fruits containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
