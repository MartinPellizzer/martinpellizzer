Lepidium aletes

Lepidium aletes, commonly known as the narrowleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Lepidium aletes is typically found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
