Lindera flavinervia

Lindera flavinervia, commonly known as the yellow-veined ligustrum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic plants like avocado and cinnamon. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, dark berries.
