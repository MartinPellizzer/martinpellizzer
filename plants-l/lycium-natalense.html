Lycium natalense

Lycium natalense, commonly known as Natal boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces bright orange berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a subject of ecological study.
