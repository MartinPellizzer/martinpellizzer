Laelia cowanii

Laelia cowanii, commonly known as the Cowan's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and a raceme of flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Laelia cowanii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
