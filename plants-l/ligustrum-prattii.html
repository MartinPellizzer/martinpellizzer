Ligustrum prattii

Ligustrum prattii, commonly known as Pratts privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, dark berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
