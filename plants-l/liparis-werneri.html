Liparis werneri

Liparis werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces a single or few racemes of small, greenish-white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Liparis werneri is often found in shaded, moist forest floors, where it thrives in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests.
