Labisia sumatrensis

Labisia sumatrensis, commonly known as the Java hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters tall, featuring glossy, ovate leaves with a reddish underside. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Labisia sumatrensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
