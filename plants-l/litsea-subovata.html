Litsea subovata

Litsea subovata, commonly known as the narrow-ovate litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, globose drupe that turns dark purple when mature.
