Lasiagrostis redowskyi

Lasiagrostis redowskyi, commonly known as the Redowsky's grass, is a species of grass native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, erect stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spikelet cluster, which is often adorned with hairy bracts.

Lasiagrostis redowskyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
