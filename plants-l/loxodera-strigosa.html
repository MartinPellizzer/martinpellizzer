Loxodera strigosa

Loxodera strigosa, commonly known as the rough-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the spurge family.
