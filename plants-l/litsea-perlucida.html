Litsea perlucida

Litsea perlucida, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that emit a sweet, aromatic scent when crushed. Its small, white flowers are followed by elliptical, red fruits that contain a single seed.

Litsea perlucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
