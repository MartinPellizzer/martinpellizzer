Lepanthes guaduasensis

Lepanthes guaduasensis, commonly known as the Guaduasense orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a short rhizome with a single leaf and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are characterized by their intricate structure and are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Lepanthes guaduasensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
