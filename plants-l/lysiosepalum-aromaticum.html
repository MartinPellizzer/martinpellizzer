Lysiosepalum aromaticum

Lysiosepalum aromaticum, commonly known as the sweet pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental and edible gardening.
