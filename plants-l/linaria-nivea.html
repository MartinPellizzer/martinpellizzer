Linaria nivea

Linaria nivea, commonly known as the snow linaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Linaria nivea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
