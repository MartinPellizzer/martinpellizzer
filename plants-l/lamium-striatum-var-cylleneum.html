Lamium striatum var. cylleneum

Lamium striatum var. cylleneum, commonly known as the purple deadnettle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its ability to suppress weeds.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
