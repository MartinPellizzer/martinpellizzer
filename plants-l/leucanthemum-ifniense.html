Leucanthemum ifniense

Leucanthemum ifniense, commonly known as the Canary Island Fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Leucanthemum ifniense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
