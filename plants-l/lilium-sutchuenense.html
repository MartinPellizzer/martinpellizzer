Lilium sutchuenense

Lilium sutchuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan lily, is a species of bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, humid climates. Its bulbs are an important part of its life cycle, storing nutrients for growth and reproduction.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
