Linaria imerethica

Linaria imerethica, commonly known as the Greek toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. Linaria imerethica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
