Licuala gjellerupii

Licuala gjellerupii, commonly known as the Gjellerup palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic visual presence. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, followed by clustered, coconut-like fruits. Its robust and architectural form makes it a popular choice for ornamental use in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
