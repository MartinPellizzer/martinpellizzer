Laelia × ingramii

Laelia × ingramii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Laelia and a related orchid species.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. This plant is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The leaves are broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blossoms.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
