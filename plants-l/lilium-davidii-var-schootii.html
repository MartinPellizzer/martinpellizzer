Lilium davidii var. schootii

Lilium davidii var. schootii, commonly known as the Chinese lily or David's lily, is a species of bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and red, and are often fragrant. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
