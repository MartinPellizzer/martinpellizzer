Leucanthemum ircutianum var. pinnatifidum

Leucanthemum ircutianum var. pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved white daisy, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It features showy white ray florets surrounding a central disc of yellow tubular flowers, which bloom in late summer. The leaves are finely divided, giving the plant a delicate, fern-like appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
