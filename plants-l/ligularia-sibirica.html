Ligularia sibirica

Ligularia sibirica, commonly known as Siberian ligularia, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland areas.

Its foliage is popular in ornamental gardening for its dramatic appearance.
