Lyonia annamensis

Lyonia annamensis, commonly known as the Annam lyonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant typically features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of white or pinkish flowers. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
