Limonium acutifolium subsp. tharrosianum

Limonium acutifolium subsp.

tharrosianum, commonly known as the Tharrosian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showing a high degree of salt tolerance. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings with well-drained soil.
