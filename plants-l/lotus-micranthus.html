Lotus micranthus

Lotus micranthus, commonly known as the small-flowered lotus, is a species native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It is characterized by its compact, aquatic or semi-aquatic growth habit and its small, delicate white or pink flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface that aids in water retention. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The lotus micranthus is also valued for its ornamental appeal in water gardens and ponds.
