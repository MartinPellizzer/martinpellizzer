Lespedeza forrestii

Lespedeza forrestii, commonly known as Chinese lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that produces small, clustered pink or red flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.

Lespedeza forrestii is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
