Linaria heterophylla f. aurasiaca

Linaria heterophylla f. aurasiaca, commonly known as the aurasiaca lobelias, is a compact, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, linear leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers in racemes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
