Limeum natalense

Limeum natalense, commonly known as Natal lime, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically followed by orange-red berries. Limeum natalense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
