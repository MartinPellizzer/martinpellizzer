Lonicera chrysantha var. linearifolia

Lonicera chrysantha var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
