Litsea cubeba var. formosana

Litsea cubeba var. formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan litsea or Formosan litsea, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Taiwan and is characterized by its aromatic, small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, yellowish berries that contain aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its essential oils, derived from the fruits, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
