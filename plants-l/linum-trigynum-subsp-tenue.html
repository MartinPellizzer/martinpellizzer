Linum trigynum subsp. tenue

Linum trigynum subsp.

tenue, commonly known as slender flax, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant characterized by its slender stems and small, lanceolate leaves.

The flowers are typically blue or purple and are borne in racemes, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This species is also valued for its potential in fiber production, similar to other members of the flax genus.
