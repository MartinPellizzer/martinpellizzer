Lasianthus chartaceus

Lasianthus chartaceus, commonly known as the paperleaf lasianthus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often pale green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
