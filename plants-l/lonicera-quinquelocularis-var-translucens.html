Lonicera quinquelocularis var. translucens

Lonicera quinquelocularis var. translucens, commonly known as the translucent honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly glossy surface. This variety is noted for its delicate, translucent appearance, especially in its juvenile growth stages.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and sweet fragrance.
