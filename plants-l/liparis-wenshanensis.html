Liparis wenshanensis

Liparis wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
