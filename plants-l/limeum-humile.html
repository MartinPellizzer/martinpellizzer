Limeum humile

Limeum humile, commonly known as the dwarf lime tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by yellowish-orange fruits. Limeum humile is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also valued for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in herbal remedies.
