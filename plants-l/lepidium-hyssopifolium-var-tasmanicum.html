Lepidium hyssopifolium var. tasmanicum

Lepidium hyssopifolium var. tasmanicum, commonly known as Tasmanian wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species with erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or pale yellow, appearing in late spring to early summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed areas.
