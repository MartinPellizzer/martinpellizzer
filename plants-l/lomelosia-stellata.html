Lomelosia stellata

Lomelosia stellata, commonly known as the starleaf lomelosia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves, which are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Lomelosia stellata thrives in arid and rocky environments, making it well adapted to desert conditions.

Its botanical features make it a unique and interesting subject for study in arid ecosystems.
