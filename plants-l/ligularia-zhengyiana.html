Ligularia zhengyiana

Ligularia zhengyiana, commonly known as the Zhengyi ligularia, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a disc-like structure. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
