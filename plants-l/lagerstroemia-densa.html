Lagerstroemia densa

Lagerstroemia densa, commonly known as the dense flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom in summer. The flowers are typically pink or red, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
