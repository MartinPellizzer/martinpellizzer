Listrostachys erythraeae

Listrostachys erythraeae, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and woody, aromatic bark. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, and it is known for its slow growth rate.
