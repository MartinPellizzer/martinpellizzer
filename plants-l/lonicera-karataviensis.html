Lonicera karataviensis

Lonicera karataviensis, commonly known as the Karatavinskii honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often twining stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Lonicera karataviensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.
