Lactuca songeensis

Lactuca songeensis, commonly known as the Korean lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, broad leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly bitter taste. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its edible leaves. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Lactuca songeensis is valued both for its culinary uses and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
