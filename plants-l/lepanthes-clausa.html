Lepanthes clausa

Lepanthes clausa, commonly known as the closed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Lepanthes clausa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
