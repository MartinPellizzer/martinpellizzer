Lagerstroemia fordii

Lagerstroemia fordii, commonly known as the Chinese crape myrtle, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, ranging from pink to red. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
