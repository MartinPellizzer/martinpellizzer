Lastrea levingei

Lastrea levingei, commonly known as the Cape Town daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its resilience to drought and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
