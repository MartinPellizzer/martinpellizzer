Lindelofia kandavanensis

Lindelofia kandavanensis, commonly known as the Kandavan lindelofia, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display a striking combination of purple and white hues. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, humid environments. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and limited study, very little is known about its reproductive biology and ecological role.
