Lonicera × bella var. atrorosea

Lonicera × bella var. atrorosea, commonly known as the dark rose honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep red, with a distinctive dark rose hue. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
