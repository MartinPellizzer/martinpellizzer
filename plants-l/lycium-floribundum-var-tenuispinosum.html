Lycium floribundum var. tenuispinosum

Lycium floribundum var. tenuispinosum, commonly known as the slender-spined boxthorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in late summer.

This species is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
