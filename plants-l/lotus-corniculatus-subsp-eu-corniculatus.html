Lotus corniculatus subsp. eu-corniculatus

Lotus corniculatus subsp.

eu-corniculatus, commonly known as the creeping yellow trefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a low-growing perennial herb with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and grasslands. It is often found in open habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
