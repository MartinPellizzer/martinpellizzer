Lockhartia longifolia

Lockhartia longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved lockhartia, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of long, slender leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Lockhartia longifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
