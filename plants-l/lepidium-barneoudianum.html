Lepidium barneoudianum

Lepidium barneoudianum, commonly known as the Barneoud's cress, is a flowering plant species native to the high Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant is characterized by its small, white or yellowish flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
