Luxemburgia bracteata

Luxemburgia bracteata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

This plant is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of soft, velvety hairs. The flowers are small and clustered in spikes, often greenish-yellow in color.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
