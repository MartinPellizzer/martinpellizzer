Leptorhynchos tetrachaetus

Leptorhynchos tetrachaetus, commonly known as the four-winged grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a prominent midrib.
