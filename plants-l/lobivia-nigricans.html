Lobivia nigricans

Lobivia nigricans, commonly known as the black-leaved lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically dark green to almost black in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its dark foliage. It is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its stems and leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
