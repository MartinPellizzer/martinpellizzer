Liatris × weaveri

Liatris × weaveri, commonly known as the Weaver's blazing star, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spires of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in prairies and wetlands, and it thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Liatris × weaveri is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
