Lithops nelii

Lithops nelii, commonly known as the "living stone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, helping it blend into its arid environment. The plant stores water in its leaves, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. It produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the growing season.

Lithops nelii is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
