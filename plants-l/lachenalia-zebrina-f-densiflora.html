Lachenalia zebrina f. densiflora

Lachenalia zebrina f. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered lachenalia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit.
