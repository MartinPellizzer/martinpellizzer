Lactuca morssii

Lactuca morssii, commonly known as the Morss's lettuce, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

Lactuca morssii is typically perennial, though it may behave as an annual in some climates.
