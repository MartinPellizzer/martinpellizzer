Laserpitium officinale

Laserpitium officinale, commonly known as common yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

Laserpitium officinale has been historically used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
