Liparis magnicallosa

Liparis magnicallosa, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or white with intricate patterns.

Liparis magnicallosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
