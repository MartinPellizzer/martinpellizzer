Lotononis prostrata var. heterophylla

Lotononis prostrata var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf lotononis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms a dense mat of foliage. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are often blue or purple in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are characterized by their variable shape, which gives the species its varietal name.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
