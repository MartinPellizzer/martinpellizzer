Lobivia backebergii var. winteriana

Lobivia backebergii var. winteriana, commonly known as the Winter's lobivia, is a compact, succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white, powdery bloom. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
