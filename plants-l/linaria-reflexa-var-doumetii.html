Linaria reflexa var. doumetii

Linaria reflexa var. doumetii, commonly known as the Doumet's throatwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, resembling those of the common linaria species. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
