Lecythis obversa

Lecythis obversa, commonly known as the yellow-lobed lecythis, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain a sticky, aromatic latex. Its bark and latex have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.
