Lithocardium Grandiflorum

Lithocardium grandiflorum is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family native to the tropical regions of South America. This plant is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the wider horticultural community.
