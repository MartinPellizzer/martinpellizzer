Lightfootia marginata

Lightfootia marginata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lightfootia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves with a pale margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Lightfootia marginata is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its role in local ecosystems.
