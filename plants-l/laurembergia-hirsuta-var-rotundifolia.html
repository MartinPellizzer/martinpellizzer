Laurembergia hirsuta var. rotundifolia

Laurembergia hirsuta var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the woolly laurembergia, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South Africa.

The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped, which is reflected in its botanical name "rotundifolia.". This variant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a warm, arid climate to flourish.
