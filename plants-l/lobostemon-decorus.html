Lobostemon decorus

Lobostemon decorus, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Lobostemon decorus is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
