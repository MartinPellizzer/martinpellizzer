Lycopodium reflexum var. brevissimum

Lycopodium reflexum var. brevissimum, commonly known as the short-stemmed clubmoss, is a species of lycopod that exhibits distinctive botanical features.

It is a small, evergreen plant with a prostrate or ascending growth habit, characterized by its narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces sporophylls that are closely clustered around the sporangia, typical of the Lycopodiaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its morphology reflects adaptations to its ecological niche, making it a notable component of certain ecosystems.
