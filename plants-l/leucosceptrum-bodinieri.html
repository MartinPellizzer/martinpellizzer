Leucosceptrum bodinieri

Leucosceptrum bodinieri, commonly known as the snowbell, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, often arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.
