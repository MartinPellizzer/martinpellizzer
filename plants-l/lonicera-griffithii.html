Lonicera griffithii

Lonicera griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early spring. Its berries are red and edible, often attracting birds.

Lonicera griffithii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
