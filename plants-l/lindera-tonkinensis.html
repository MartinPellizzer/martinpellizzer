Lindera tonkinensis

Lindera tonkinensis, commonly known as Vietnamese spicebush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

The fruit is a small, dark berry that ripens in the fall and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some cuisines.
