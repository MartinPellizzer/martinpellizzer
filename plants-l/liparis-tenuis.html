Liparis tenuis

Liparis tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in soil and absorb nutrients.

Liparis tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
