Lipochaeta australis var. connata

Lipochaeta australis var. connata, commonly known as the slender swamp everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It has slender, erect stems and linear leaves, and it is often found in the southeastern regions of Australia.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
