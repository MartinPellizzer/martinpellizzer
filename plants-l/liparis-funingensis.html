Liparis funingensis

Liparis funingensis, commonly known as the Funing orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives on decaying wood or in rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
