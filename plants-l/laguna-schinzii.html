Laguna schinzii

Laguna schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's lagoon plant, is a rare aquatic plant native to specific regions in South America.

The plant has elongated, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, aiding in oxygenation and providing shelter for small aquatic organisms. Laguna schinzii is valued for its ecological significance and is often studied for its adaptation to wetland environments.
