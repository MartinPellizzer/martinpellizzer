Ledebouria kikiae

Ledebouria kikiae, commonly known as the Kikai bulbous lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, bulb-like stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
