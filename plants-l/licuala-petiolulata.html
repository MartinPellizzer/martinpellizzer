Licuala petiolulata

Licuala petiolulata, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional cuisine and for making natural dyes.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and its utility in various cultural practices.
