Lycaste reichenbachii

Lycaste reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's lycaste, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a sympodial orchid, characterized by its horizontal growth habit and the formation of pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with contrasting red or brown markings. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Lycaste reichenbachii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
