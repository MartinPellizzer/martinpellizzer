Lavatera thuringiaca var. brevipedunculata

Lavatera thuringiaca var. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the dwarf lavender tree, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
