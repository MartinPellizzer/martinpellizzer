Lobelia natalensis var. subulifolia

Lobelia natalensis var. subulifolia, commonly known as Natal lobelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
