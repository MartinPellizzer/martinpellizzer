Limeum canescens

Limeum canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its grayish-white, fuzzy foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Limeum canescens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
