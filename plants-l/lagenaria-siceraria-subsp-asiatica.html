Lagenaria siceraria subsp. asiatica

Lagenaria siceraria subsp.

asiatica, commonly known as the Chinese bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd species native to Asia. It is a fast-growing vine that produces large, smooth, bottle-shaped fruits with a light green to white color.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun and well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its fruits are often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine.

The subspecies is notable for its versatility in both ornamental and practical uses.
