Luzula elegans

Luzula elegans, commonly known as the slender woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Luzula elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
