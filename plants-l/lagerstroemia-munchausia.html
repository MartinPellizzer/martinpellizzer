Lagerstroemia munchausia

Lagerstroemia munchausia, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmate leaves that are typically 5-7 lobes, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions and its long-lasting foliage.
