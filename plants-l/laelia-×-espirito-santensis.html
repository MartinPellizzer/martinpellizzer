Laelia × espirito-santensis

Laelia × espirito-santensis, commonly known as the Espirito Santo laelia, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms.

Due to its beauty and hardiness, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
