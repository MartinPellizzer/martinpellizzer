Lathyrus latifolius f. rubicundus

Lathyrus latifolius f. rubicundus, commonly known as the red-stemmed broadleaf pea, is a variant of the broadleaf pea plant characterized by its distinctive reddish stems.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features palmately divided leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its botanical features make it a notable ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.

The f. rubicundus form is particularly valued for its vibrant stem color and overall aesthetic appeal.
