Lithocardium Subcordatum

Lithocardium subcordatum is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of South America. Known for its unique, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant blue-gray foliage, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in harsh, rocky environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme conditions. Its limited distribution and delicate beauty make it a subject of both scientific interest and conservation concern. Despite its small size, Lithocardium subcordatum holds significant ecological value in its native habitat.
