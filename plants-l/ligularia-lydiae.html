Ligularia lydiae

Ligularia lydiae, commonly known as the Lydia's Ligularia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a rough texture. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its striking foliage and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens with appropriate growing conditions.
