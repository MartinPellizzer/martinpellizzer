Lachnagrostis plebeia

Lachnagrostis plebeia, commonly known as the common silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, silver-haired leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

Lachnagrostis plebeia is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in stabilizing soil.
