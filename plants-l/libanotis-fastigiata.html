Libanotis fastigiata

Libanotis fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
