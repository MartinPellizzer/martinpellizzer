Lambertia multiflora var. darlingensis

Lambertia multiflora var. darlingensis, commonly known as Darling's lambertia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright red, lantern-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and drought tolerance.
