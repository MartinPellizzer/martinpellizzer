Lotus oliveirae

Lotus oliveirae, commonly known as the oliveira lotus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking purple and white flowers. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
