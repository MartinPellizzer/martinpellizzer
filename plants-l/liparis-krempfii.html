Liparis krempfii

Liparis krempfii, commonly known as the Krempe's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces a single, erect stem bearing several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a cluster of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
