Limeum madagascariense

Limeum madagascariense, commonly known as the Madagascar lime tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed.

Limeum madagascariense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
