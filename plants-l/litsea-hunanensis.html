Litsea hunanensis

Litsea hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan litsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. Litsea hunanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in subtropical regions.
