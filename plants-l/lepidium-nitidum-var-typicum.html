Lepidium nitidum var. typicum

Lepidium nitidum var. typicum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and compact, upright growth habit. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.
