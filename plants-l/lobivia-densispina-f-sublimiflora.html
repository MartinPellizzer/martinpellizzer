Lobivia densispina f. sublimiflora

Lobivia densispina f. sublimiflora, commonly known as the snow-flowered lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features dense, spiny stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is adapted to arid conditions, with reduced leaves that minimize water loss.

This variety is prized for its striking floral display and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
