Libanotis daucifolia var. athamantoides

Libanotis daucifolia var. athamantoides, commonly known as the white thistle or Athamantoides thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of silver-gray, woolly leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
