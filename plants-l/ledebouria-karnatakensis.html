Ledebouria karnatakensis

Ledebouria karnatakensis, commonly known as the Karnataka rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the Indian state of Karnataka.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing succulent features for water storage.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
