Limonium psilocladum

Limonium psilocladum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in saline environments. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium psilocladum is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and hardy nature.
