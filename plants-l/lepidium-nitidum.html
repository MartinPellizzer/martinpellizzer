Lepidium nitidum

Lepidium nitidum, commonly known as smooth wallflower, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, typically growing in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow or white flowers and has smooth, glabrous leaves. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Lepidium nitidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
