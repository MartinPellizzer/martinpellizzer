Lepisanthes eriolepis

Lepisanthes eriolepis, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Lepisanthes eriolepis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
