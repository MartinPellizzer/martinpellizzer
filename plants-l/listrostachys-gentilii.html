Listrostachys gentilii

Listrostachys gentilii, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a shrub native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its botanical features make it a valued ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to high-altitude environments.
