Lagunaria lilacina

Lagunaria lilacina, commonly known as the lilac tree, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, lilac-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Lagunaria lilacina is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
