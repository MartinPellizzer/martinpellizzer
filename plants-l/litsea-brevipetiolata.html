Litsea brevipetiolata

Litsea brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-stemmed litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, dark berries.

Litsea brevipetiolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
