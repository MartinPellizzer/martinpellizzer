Lobelia assurgens var. jamaicensis

Lobelia assurgens var. jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.
