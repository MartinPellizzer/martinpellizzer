Lobivia orurensis

Lobivia orurensis, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded shape with thick, fleshy stems that store water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers during the cooler months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are typically small and spiny, providing additional protection from herbivores.

Lobivia orurensis is well-suited for rock gardens or containers, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.
