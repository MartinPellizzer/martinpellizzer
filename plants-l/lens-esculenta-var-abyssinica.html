Lens esculenta var. abyssinica

Lens esculenta var. abyssinica, commonly known as the Ethiopian lentil, is a variety of the common lentil native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its small, angular seeds that are typically brown or yellow in color. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil. It produces clusters of small white or purple flowers, which are followed by pods containing the edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to drought conditions.
