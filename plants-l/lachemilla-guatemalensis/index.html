Lachemilla Guatemalensis

Lachemilla guatemalensis is a lesser-known flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Guatemala. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its delicate, star-shaped blooms that add a touch of elegance to any garden. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round interest, while its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, Lachemilla guatemalensis remains a relatively obscure species among plant enthusiasts.
