Lathyrus vernus lusus albiflorus

Lathyrus vernus lusus albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered vernal pea, is a variant of the common vernal pea.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its most distinguishing characteristic is the production of white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in early spring.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance and early flowering habit.
