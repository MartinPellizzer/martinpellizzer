Liparis parviflora

Liparis parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Liparis parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
