Lathyrus pauciflorus subsp. tenuior

Lathyrus pauciflorus subsp.

tenuior, commonly known as the slender-flowered grass pea, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple.

The plant produces trifoliate leaves and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
