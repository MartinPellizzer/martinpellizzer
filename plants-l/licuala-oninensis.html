Licuala oninensis

Licuala oninensis, commonly known as the Philippine palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant typically grows in wet, lowland areas and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats. Its trunk is slender and covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are small and clustered, and the plant produces fruit that is edible but not widely cultivated.
