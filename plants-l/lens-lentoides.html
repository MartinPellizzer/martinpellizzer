Lens lentoides

Lens lentoides, commonly known as the broad bean, is an annual legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces heart-shaped, green pods that contain edible, oval seeds. The plant has compound leaves with three oval leaflets and small, yellow flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is cultivated for its nutritious seeds.

The botanical features of Lens lentoides make it a valuable crop for both human consumption and agricultural systems.
