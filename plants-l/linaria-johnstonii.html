Linaria johnstonii

Linaria johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's toadflax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid habitats. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Linaria johnstonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
