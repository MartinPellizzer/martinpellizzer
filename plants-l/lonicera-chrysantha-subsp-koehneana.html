Lonicera chrysantha subsp. koehneana

Lonicera chrysantha subsp.

koehneana, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China. It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside.

The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
