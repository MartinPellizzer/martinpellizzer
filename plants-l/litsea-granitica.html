Litsea granitica

Litsea granitica, commonly known as granite laurier, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glossy evergreen leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a humid, tropical climate.
