Liparis arachnites

Liparis arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its striking, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spiders.

Liparis arachnites is valued for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
