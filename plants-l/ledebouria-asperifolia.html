Ledebouria asperifolia

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture. During the growing season, it develops a tall, erect stem bearing clusters of white or pale yellow flowers.
