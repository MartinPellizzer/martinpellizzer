Leucophae cystosiphon

Leucophae cystosiphon, commonly known as the green algae, is a type of marine algae belonging to the order Ochrophyta.

It is characterized by its filamentous structure, composed of long, branching cells that can form dense mats in coastal waters. Leucophae cystosiphon plays an important role in marine ecosystems by serving as a primary producer and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
