Liparis pingtaoi

Liparis pingtaoi, commonly known as the Pingtai orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded forest environments, where it thrives on decaying wood or in rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually elongated and lance-shaped, while its flowers are small and clustered in spikes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
