Laurembergia capensis

Laurembergia capensis, commonly known as the Cape laurel, is a South African plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant is adapted to coastal conditions and can tolerate salt spray and sandy soils.
