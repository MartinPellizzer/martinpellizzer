Lysimachia japonica var. subsessilis

Lysimachia japonica var. subsessilis, commonly known as Japanese chickweed or slender yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with succulent, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental ground cover.
