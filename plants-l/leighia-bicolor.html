Leighia bicolor

Leighia bicolor, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers, which bloom in clusters along its slender stems. The plant thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a sought-after ornamental plant.

Leighia bicolor plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
