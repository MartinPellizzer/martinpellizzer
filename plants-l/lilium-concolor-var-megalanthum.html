Lilium concolor var. megalanthum

Lilium concolor var. megalanthum, commonly known as the giant white lily, is a striking variant of the concolor lily.

It is characterized by its large, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in length. The plant is native to the western United States, particularly in areas with cool, moist climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
