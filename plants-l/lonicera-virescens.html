Lonicera virescens

Lonicera virescens, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a deciduous to semi-evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow, often appearing in clusters. Its berries are red or orange and serve as a food source for birds.

Lonicera virescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
