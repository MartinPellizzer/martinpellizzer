Leonotis africana

Leonotis africana, commonly known as the African heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows up to 1.5 meters in height, featuring woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet, blooming throughout the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
