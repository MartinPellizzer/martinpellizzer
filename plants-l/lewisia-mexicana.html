Lewisia mexicana

Lewisia mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican Lewisia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Mexico.

Lewisia mexicana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
