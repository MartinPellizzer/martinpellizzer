Lamium tomentosum var. hakkiarense

Lamium tomentosum var. hakkiarense, commonly known as the Hakkiaric deadnettle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale purple flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
