Lycopodioides microdendron

Lycopodioides microdendron, commonly known as the small-branch moss, is a member of the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Lycopodioides microdendron plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
