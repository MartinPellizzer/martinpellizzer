Lomatia fraseri var. velutina

Lomatia fraseri var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet lomatia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
