Lonicera sumatrana

Lonicera sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran honeysuckle, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Lonicera sumatrana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
