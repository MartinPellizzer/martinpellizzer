Lasianthus pedunculatus subsp. angustisepalus

Lasianthus pedunculatus subsp.

angustisepalus, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.

The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
