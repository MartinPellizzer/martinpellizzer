Leucas rosmarinifolia

Leucas rosmarinifolia, commonly known as silverleaf leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Leucas rosmarinifolia is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
