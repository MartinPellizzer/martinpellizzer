Lotus arguinensis

Lotus arguinensis, commonly known as the Arguin lotus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Sahara Desert.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in nutrient-poor and water-scarce environments. Its leaves are typically small and hairy, which helps reduce water loss through transpiration.

Lotus arguinensis is also known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators in its harsh habitat.
