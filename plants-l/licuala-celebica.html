Licuala celebica

Licuala celebica, commonly known as the giant pandan or giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and for making natural dyes.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
