Loeseneriella iotricha var. iotricha

Loeseneriella iotricha var. iotricha, commonly known as the hairy leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features erect, branched stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, covered with fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, with a distinctive hairy texture on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in rock gardens and alpine collections.
