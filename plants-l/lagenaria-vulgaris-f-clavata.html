Lagenaria vulgaris f. clavata

Lagenaria vulgaris f. clavata, commonly known as the bottle gourd or calabash, is a variant of the bottle gourd plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, bottle-shaped fruit that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, squash, and melons. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often covered in a fine pubescence.
