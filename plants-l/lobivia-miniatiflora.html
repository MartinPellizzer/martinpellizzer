Lobivia miniatiflora

Lobivia miniatiflora, commonly known as the miniature flower lobivia, is a succulent native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, cylindrical stems and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure to thrive.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
