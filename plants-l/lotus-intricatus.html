Lotus intricatus

Lotus intricatus, commonly known as the star lotus, is a rare and striking aquatic plant native to the freshwater lakes of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, often with a subtle iridescence. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving waters, with its rhizomes anchoring it to the substrate while its leaves float on the surface. Its flowers are pollinated by both water and wind, and the plant is known for its ability to purify water through natural filtration.

The lotus intricatus is a symbol of purity and resilience in many cultures, often depicted in traditional art and literature.
