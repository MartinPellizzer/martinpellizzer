Limonium oleifolium

Limonium oleifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often used in coastal gardens due to its salt tolerance and ability to withstand harsh conditions.

Limonium oleifolium is also popular in floral arrangements for its long-lasting blooms.
