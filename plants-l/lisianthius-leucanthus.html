Lisianthius leucanthus

Lisianthius leucanthus, commonly known as the white lisianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink petals that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant appearance and long vase life.
