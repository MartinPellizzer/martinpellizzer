Limonium dregeanum

Limonium dregeanum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

Limonium dregeanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
