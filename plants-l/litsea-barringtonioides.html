Litsea barringtonioides

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The fruit is a small, berry-like drupe that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
