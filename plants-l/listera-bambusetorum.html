Listera bambusetorum

Listera bambusetorum, commonly known as the bamboo ground orchid, is a unique plant that combines features of both orchids and bamboo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, tubular flowers. Native to certain regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous areas. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread slowly and form dense colonies.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance, making it a sought-after plant for specialized gardens.
