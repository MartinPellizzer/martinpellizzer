Leea asiatica

Leea asiatica, commonly known as the Asian Leea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that belongs to the family Leeaceae. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
