Lonicera rocheri

Lonicera rocheri, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, blooming in early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in late summer.

Lonicera rocheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
