Linum doerfleri

Linum doerfleri, commonly known as the Doerfler's flax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Linum, which includes other species of flax plants. The plant is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and slender, branching stems. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Linum doerfleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance.
