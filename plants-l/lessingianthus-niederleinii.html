Lessingianthus niederleinii

Lessingianthus niederleinii, commonly known as the Niederlein's sunflower, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
