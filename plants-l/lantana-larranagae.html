Lantana larranagae

Lantana larranagae, commonly known as the Larranaga lantana, is a shrub native to the island of Larranaga in the Philippines.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, vibrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

Lantana larranagae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological roles in local habitats.
