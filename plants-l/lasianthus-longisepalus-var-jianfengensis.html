Lasianthus longisepalus var. jianfengensis

Lasianthus longisepalus var. jianfengensis, commonly known as the Jianfeng Mountains lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
