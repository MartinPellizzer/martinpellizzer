Lupulus Lupuloides Var. Pubescens

Lupulus lupuloides var. pubescens is a lesser-known member of the hop family known for its unique characteristics. This variant is distinguished by its hairy foliage and robust growth habits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Despite its rarity, it holds potential for both ecological and horticultural applications. Its study offers insights into the adaptability of hop species in diverse conditions.
