Limonium nashii var. angustatum

Limonium nashii var. angustatum, commonly known as narrowleaf sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and clusters of blue to violet, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to withstand salty and windy conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Limonium nashii var. angustatum is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
