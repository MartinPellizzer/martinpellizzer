Limonium aegaeum

Limonium aegaeum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of blue or purple flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has succulent, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Limonium aegaeum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate saline conditions.
