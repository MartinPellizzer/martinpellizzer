Linaria linifolia

Linaria linifolia, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered with fine, hairy leaves. Its flowers are small, yellowish-orange, and borne in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Linaria linifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
