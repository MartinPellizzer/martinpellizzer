Lotononis tenella var. hirsutissima

Lotononis tenella var. hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy lotononis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow-orange ray flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
