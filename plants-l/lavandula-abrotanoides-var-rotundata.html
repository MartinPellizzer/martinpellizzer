Lavandula abrotanoides var. rotundata

Lavandula abrotanoides var. rotundata, commonly known as narrow-leaf lavender, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are gray-green in color and dense clusters of purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its foliage and flowers are both used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
