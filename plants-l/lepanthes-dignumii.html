Lepanthes dignumii

Lepanthes dignumii, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet scent.

Lepanthes dignumii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
