Lilium speciosum f. tametomo

Lilium speciosum f. tametomo, commonly known as the Tametomo lily, is a unique cultivar of the species Lilium speciosum.

It is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive red throat, which sets it apart from other lily varieties. This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
