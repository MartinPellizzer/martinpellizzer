Lophospatha borneensis

Lophospatha borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette, aiding in water retention in its humid, shaded habitat.

Lophospatha borneensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
