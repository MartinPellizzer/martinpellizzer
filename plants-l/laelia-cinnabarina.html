Laelia cinnabarina

Laelia cinnabarina, commonly known as the Cinnabar Orchid, is a species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking red and orange flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its often harsh habitat.

Laelia cinnabarina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
