Lespedeza henryi

Lespedeza henryi, commonly known as Henry's lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Lespedeza henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
