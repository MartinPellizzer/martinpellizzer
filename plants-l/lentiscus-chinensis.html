Lentiscus chinensis

Lentiscus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese lentiscus, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, resinous properties. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a rough texture and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to red when mature.

Lentiscus chinensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
