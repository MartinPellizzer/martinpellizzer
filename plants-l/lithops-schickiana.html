Lithops schickiana

Lithops schickiana, commonly known as the "Living Stone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that resemble stones, helping it blend into its arid environment. The plant stores water in its leaves, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry conditions. It produces small, pale flowers during the summer months.

Lithops schickiana is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
