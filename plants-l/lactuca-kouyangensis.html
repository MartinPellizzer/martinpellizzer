Lactuca kouyangensis

Lactuca kouyangensis, commonly known as Kouyang lettuce, is a rare and lesser-known species of lettuce native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its leafy structure and mild, slightly bitter taste. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are used in local cuisines and have potential medicinal properties.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
