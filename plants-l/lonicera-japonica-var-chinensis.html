Lonicera japonica var. chinensis

Lonicera japonica var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange-red. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils, making it a common ornamental in gardens. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer, containing small seeds.

This species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
