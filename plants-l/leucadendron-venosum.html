Leucadendron venosum

Leucadendron venosum, commonly known as the silverleaf candle bush, is a South African shrub belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, cone-shaped inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves with prominent veins, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Leucadendron venosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in horticulture for its unique floral display.
