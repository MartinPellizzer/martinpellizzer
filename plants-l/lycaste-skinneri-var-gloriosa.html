Lycaste skinneri var. gloriosa

Lycaste skinneri var. gloriosa, commonly known as the gloriosa lycaste, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs and is known for its fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
