Lycopersicon esculentum provar. speciosum

Lycopersicon esculentum provar. speciosum, commonly known as the decorative tomato, is a botanical variant of the common tomato.

It is characterized by its diverse range of flower colors, including pink, red, and white, which make it visually striking. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and lobed leaves. Its flowers are perfect, with both stamens and pistils, facilitating self-pollination.

While not widely cultivated for fruit, it is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
