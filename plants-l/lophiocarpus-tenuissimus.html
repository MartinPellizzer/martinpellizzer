Lophiocarpus tenuissimus

Lophiocarpus tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender-stemmed lophiocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
