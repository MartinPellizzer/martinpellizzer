Liabum erigeroides

Liabum erigeroides, commonly known as the erect everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, delicate blue to violet flowers. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in grassland habitats.

Its flowers are known to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
