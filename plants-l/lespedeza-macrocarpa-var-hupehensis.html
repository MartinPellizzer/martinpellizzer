Lespedeza macrocarpa var. hupehensis

Lespedeza macrocarpa var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese lespedeza, is a perennial legume native to China.

It features slender, upright stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pale yellow to reddish flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its seeds are small and oblong, typically containing a single seed per pod.

This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen and tolerate various soil conditions.
