Liparia genistoides

Liparia genistoides, commonly known as the golden germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature.

Liparia genistoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
