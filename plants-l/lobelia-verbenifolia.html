Lobelia verbenifolia

Lobelia verbenifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf lobelia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Lobelia verbenifolia is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits to pollinators.
