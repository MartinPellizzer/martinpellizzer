Lantana aristata var. subsessilis

Lantana aristata var. subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lantana, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The leaves are typically narrow and sessile, giving the plant its specific variety name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

This species is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
