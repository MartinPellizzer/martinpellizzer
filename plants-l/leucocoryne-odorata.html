Leucocoryne odorata

Leucocoryne odorata, commonly known as the fragrant white orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its elegant, white, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty collections.
