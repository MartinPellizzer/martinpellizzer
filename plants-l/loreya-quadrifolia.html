Loreya quadrifolia

Loreya quadrifolia, commonly known as the four-leafed loreya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly hairy texture.

Loreya quadrifolia is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
