Livistona rotundifolia var. luzonensis

Livistona rotundifolia var. luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon palm, is a species of palm native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves, which are typically broad and have a distinctive dark green color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, edible fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
