Leucas bracteosa

Leucas bracteosa, commonly known as the yellow bract leucas, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
