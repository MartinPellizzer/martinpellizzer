Lastrea maximowiczii

Lastrea maximowiczii, commonly known as the Zulu ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and adapted to arid environments. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with blue-gray foliage that reflects sunlight. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and appear in clusters during the spring.
