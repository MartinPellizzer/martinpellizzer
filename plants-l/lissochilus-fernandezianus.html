Lissochilus fernandezianus

Lissochilus fernandezianus, commonly known as the Fernandezian orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, specifically found in Ecuador and Peru. This species is characterized by its delicate, spurred orchid flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes, often growing in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
