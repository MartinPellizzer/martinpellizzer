Lomelosia graminifolia var. virescens

Lomelosia graminifolia var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf lomelosia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and slightly hairy, with a dark green coloration. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, often found in moist, rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Amaranthaceae family.
