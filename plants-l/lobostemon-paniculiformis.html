Lobostemon paniculiformis

Lobostemon paniculiformis, commonly known as the panicle lobostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.
