Lonicera benjaminii

Lonicera benjaminii, commonly known as Benjamin's honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in early summer.

Lonicera benjaminii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
