Licuala petiolulata var. kenepajensis

Licuala petiolulata var. kenepajensis, commonly known as the Kenepa palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The Kenepa palm is valued for its ornamental foliage and has cultural significance in some indigenous communities.
