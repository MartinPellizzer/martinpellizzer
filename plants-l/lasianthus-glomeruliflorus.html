Lasianthus glomeruliflorus

Lasianthus glomeruliflorus, commonly known as the clustered-flower lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
