Lagerstroemia subsessilifolia

Lagerstroemia subsessilifolia, commonly known as the Chinese flowering maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, mottled bark and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that turn red in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its compact size and vibrant floral display make it a favored ornamental plant in temperate climates.
