Lablab ferrugineus

Lablab ferrugineus, commonly known as the rusty lablab, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.

Lablab ferrugineus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting agricultural systems.
