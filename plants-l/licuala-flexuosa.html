Licuala flexuosa

Licuala flexuosa, commonly known as the pleated palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that have a distinctive pleated appearance. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching.

Licuala flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in tropical forests.
