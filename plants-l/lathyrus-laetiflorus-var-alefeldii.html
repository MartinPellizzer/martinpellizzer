Lathyrus laetiflorus var. alefeldii

Lathyrus laetiflorus var. alefeldii, commonly known as the Alefeld's sweet pea, is a climbing legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, winding stems that support its clusters of fragrant, pea-like flowers. The flowers are typically pink to lilac in color, with a distinctive sweet scent that attracts pollinators. Its trifoliate leaves are pinnately arranged, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
