Lantana sandersii var. sandersii

Lantana sandersii var. sandersii, commonly known as the sandersii lantana, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring dense, compact growth habits. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, orange, and red, depending on the variety. It has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape.

This species is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
