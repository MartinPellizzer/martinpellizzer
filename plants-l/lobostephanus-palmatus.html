Lobostephanus palmatus

Lobostephanus palmatus, commonly known as the silverleaf stephania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green leaves that are opposite and often lobed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
