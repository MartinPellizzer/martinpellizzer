Leucanthemopsis minima var. cuneifolia

Leucanthemopsis minima var. cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf oxeye daisy, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, daisy-like white flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and slightly serrated, giving the plant a delicate, grass-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
