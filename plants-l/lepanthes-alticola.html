Lepanthes alticola

Lepanthes alticola, commonly known as the cloud forest orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that often display a striking combination of colors, including white, purple, and green. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air moisture.

Lepanthes alticola is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
