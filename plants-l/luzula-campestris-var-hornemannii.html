Luzula campestris var. hornemannii

Luzula campestris var. hornemannii, commonly known as the hornemannii woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in woodland or wetland habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often used in horticultural settings for its delicate appearance.
