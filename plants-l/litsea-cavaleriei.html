Litsea cavaleriei

Litsea cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese tallow tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain several seeds. It is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
