Laurentia fluviatilis var. lessonii

Laurentia fluviatilis var. lessonii, commonly known as the river laurentia, is a aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually submerged, and it produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that float on the water. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various organisms.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to its wetland and riverine habitats.
