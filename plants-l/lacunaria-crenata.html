Lacunaria crenata

Lacunaria crenata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant has alternate, narrow, and often hairy leaves that contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
