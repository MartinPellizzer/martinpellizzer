Lhotskya genethylloides var. glabra

Lhotskya genethylloides var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lhotskya, is a rare alpine plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its glabrous, or hairless, leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale blue or white color.
