Lycium schizocalyx

Lycium schizocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese boxthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.

Lycium schizocalyx is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
