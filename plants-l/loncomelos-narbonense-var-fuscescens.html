Loncomelos narbonense var. fuscescens

Loncomelos narbonense var. fuscescens, commonly known as the dark-stemmed lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive velvety texture and a dark green coloration. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and range in color from pale pink to violet.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
