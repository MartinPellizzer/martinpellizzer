Lasianthus gilletii var. seretii

Lasianthus gilletii var. seretii, commonly known as the white-flowered lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
