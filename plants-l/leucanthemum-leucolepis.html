Leucanthemum leucolepis

Leucanthemum leucolepis, commonly known as the white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy white petals surrounding a yellow central disk, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Leucanthemum leucolepis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
