Lobivia pampana

Lobivia pampana, commonly known as the Pampa Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny growth habit. The plant features ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in summer.

Lobivia pampana is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal care once established.
