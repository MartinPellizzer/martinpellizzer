Lilium × princeps

Lilium × princeps, commonly known as the Prince's lily, is a hybrid species within the lily family (Liliaceae).

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes red. The plant is native to the eastern United States, where it thrives in moist, woodland habitats. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces bulbous roots that store nutrients.

Lilium × princeps is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
