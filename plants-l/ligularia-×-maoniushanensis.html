Ligularia × maoniushanensis

Ligularia × maoniushanensis, commonly known as the Maoniushan ligularia, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a broad, showy spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich, humus-filled soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in shaded landscapes.
