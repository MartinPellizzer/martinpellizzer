Limodorum variegatum

Limodorum variegatum, commonly known as the variegated lily of the valley, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white with a greenish tint, blooming in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its fragrance is subtle and sweet, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a shade-loving plant.
