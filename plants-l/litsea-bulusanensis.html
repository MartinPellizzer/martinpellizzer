Litsea bulusanensis

Litsea bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan litsea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the mountainous regions of the province of Samar. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
