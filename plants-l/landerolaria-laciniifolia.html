Landerolaria laciniifolia

Landerolaria laciniifolia, commonly known as the laceleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and modest flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
