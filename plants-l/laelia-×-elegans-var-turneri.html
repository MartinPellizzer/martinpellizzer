Laelia × elegans var. turneri

Laelia × elegans var. turneri, commonly known as the Turneri orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat with limited rainfall. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a complex structure featuring multiple petals and sepals.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
