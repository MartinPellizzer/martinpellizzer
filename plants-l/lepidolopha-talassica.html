Lepidolopha talassica

Lepidolopha talassica, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.
