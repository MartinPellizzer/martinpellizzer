Lightfootia pauciflora

Lightfootia pauciflora, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces few, pale green or yellowish flowers.
