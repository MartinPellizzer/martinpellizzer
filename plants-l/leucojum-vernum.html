Leucojum vernum

Leucojum vernum, commonly known as the spring snowflake, is a bulbous perennial plant native to western Europe.

It produces clusters of pale, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil. It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its early blooming habit.

Leucojum vernum is also known for its mild fragrance and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
