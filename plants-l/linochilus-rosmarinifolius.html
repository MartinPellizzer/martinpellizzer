Linochilus rosmarinifolius

Linochilus rosmarinifolius, commonly known as the rosemary-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that resemble those of rosemary, giving it its common name. It produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often clustered in inflorescences.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
