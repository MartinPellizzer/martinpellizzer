Lomanthus okopanus

Lomanthus okopanus, commonly known as the Okopanu lomanthus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Its leaves are often lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lomanthus okopanus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
