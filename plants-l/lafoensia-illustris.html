Lafoensia illustris

Lafoensia illustris, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Lafoensia illustris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
