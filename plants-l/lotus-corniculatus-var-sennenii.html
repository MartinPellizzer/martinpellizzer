Lotus corniculatus var. sennenii

Lotus corniculatus var. sennenii, commonly known as the yellow bird's-foot trefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets. The plant is typically found in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It is a perennial herb that produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
