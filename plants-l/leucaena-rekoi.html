Leucaena rekoi

Leucaena rekoi, commonly known as the African leucaena, is a leguminous shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable for soil enrichment. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used for forage, soil stabilization, and agroforestry.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
