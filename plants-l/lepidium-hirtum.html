Lepidium hirtum

Lepidium hirtum, commonly known as hairy cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or yellowish flowers and hairy leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. It has a compact growth habit with a rosette of basal leaves.

Lepidium hirtum is often found in temperate regions and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
