Lactuca pumila

Lactuca pumila, commonly known as dwarf lettuce, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows to a height of only a few inches, with rosettes of spoon-shaped, pale green leaves. The plant produces small, green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Lactuca pumila is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other flowering plants.
