Lepismium cruciforme f. myosurus

Lepismium cruciforme f. myosurus, commonly known as the Myosurus Lepismium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, flattened, ribbed stems that resemble a cross, hence the species name "cruciforme.". The foliage consists of small, spiny leaves that are often covered in a fine, white coating. It produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the cooler months.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
