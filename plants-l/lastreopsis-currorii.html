Lastreopsis currorii

Lastreopsis currorii, commonly known as the Curror's flax lily, is a striking evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
