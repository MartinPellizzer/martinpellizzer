Lotus angustifolius

Lotus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, supported by long, slender stems. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with a distinctive lotus-like appearance.

Lotus angustifolius is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
