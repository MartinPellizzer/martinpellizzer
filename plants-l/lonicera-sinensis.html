Lonicera sinensis

Lonicera sinensis, commonly known as the Japanese honeysuckle, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a red, fleshy capsule that contains numerous small seeds.

Lonicera sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
