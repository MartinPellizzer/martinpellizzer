Laxoplumeria macrophylla

Laxoplumeria macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed laxoplumeria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Laxoplumeria macrophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
