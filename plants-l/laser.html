Laser

Laser, commonly known as a light amplification by stimulated emission of radiation, is not a plant but a technological device.

It does not have botanical aspects in the traditional sense, as it is not a living organism. However, the term "laser" is sometimes used metaphorically to describe intense light sources in horticultural contexts. In such cases, it may refer to high-intensity grow lights used in plant cultivation.

These artificial light sources mimic sunlight and are essential for photosynthesis in controlled environments.
