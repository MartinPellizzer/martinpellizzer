Lactuca nikoensis

Lactuca nikoensis, commonly known as Niko's lettuce, is a species of lettuce native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, crisp leaves that are typically harvested before they fully mature. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other flowering plants. It produces small yellow flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its unique flavor and nutritional value, it is gaining interest as a potential crop for sustainable agriculture.
