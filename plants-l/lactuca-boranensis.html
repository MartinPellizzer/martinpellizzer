Lactuca boranensis

Lactuca boranensis, commonly known as Boran endive, is a species of lettuce native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its curly, dark green leaves that are often used in salads and cooked dishes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has a rosette-like growth pattern. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a compact inflorescence, and it is often cultivated for its edible leaves.

Lactuca boranensis is a hardy plant that prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
