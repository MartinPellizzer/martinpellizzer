Litsea pumila

Litsea pumila, commonly known as dwarf litsea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, dark blue berries.

Litsea pumila is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
