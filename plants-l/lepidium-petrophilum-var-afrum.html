Lepidium petrophilum var. afrum

Lepidium petrophilum var. afrum, commonly known as African rock cress, is a perennial herb native to rocky and arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in sunny, rocky habitats. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
