Laelia × warneri

Laelia × warneri, commonly known as the Warner's Laelia, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central America, particularly in regions of Mexico and Guatemala. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The leaves are strap-like, lance-shaped, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
