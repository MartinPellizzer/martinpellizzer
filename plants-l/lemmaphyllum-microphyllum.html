Lemmaphyllum microphyllum

Lemmaphyllum microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved lemmaphyllum, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which are followed by tiny, capsule-like fruits. Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements, it is considered endangered and is often found in shaded, moist forest understories.
