Lepanthes oscariorum

Lepanthes oscariorum, commonly known as the Oscar's slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces aerial roots and thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Lepanthes oscariorum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
