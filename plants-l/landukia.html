Landukia

Landukia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hawthorn, is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters, which are followed by red or orange berries. Landukia thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or scrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
