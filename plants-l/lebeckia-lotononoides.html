Lebeckia lotononoides

Lebeckia lotononoides, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a South African flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble a starburst, with ray florets surrounding a central disc. The plant is typically found in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Lebeckia lotononoides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
