Ligustrum robustum subsp. chinense

Ligustrum robustum subsp.

chinense, commonly known as Chinese privet, is a shrub native to East Asia. It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, followed by black berries. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.

However, it can become invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
