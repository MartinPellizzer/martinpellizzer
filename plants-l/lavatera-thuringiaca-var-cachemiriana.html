Lavatera thuringiaca var. cachemiriana

Lavatera thuringiaca var. cachemiriana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

However, it is relatively rare in cultivation and requires specific conditions to flourish.
