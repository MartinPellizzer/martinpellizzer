Lantana angolensis

Lantana angolensis, commonly known as the Angola lantana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming throughout the warm seasons. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.

Lantana angolensis is also known for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in traditional medicine.
