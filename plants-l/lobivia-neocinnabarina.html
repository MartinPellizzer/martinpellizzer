Lobivia neocinnabarina

Lobivia neocinnabarina, commonly known as the Orange Glow Cactus, is a succulent native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features a compact, globular shape with thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help reduce water loss and deter herbivores.

Lobivia neocinnabarina is well-suited for rock gardens or containers, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.
