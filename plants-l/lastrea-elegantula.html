Lastrea elegantula

Lastrea elegantula, commonly known as the elegant leaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. Due to its aesthetic value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
