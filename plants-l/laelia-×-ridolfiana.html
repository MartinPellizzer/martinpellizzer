Laelia × ridolfiana

Laelia × ridolfiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Laelia purpurea and Laelia anceps.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid genera. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in high altitudes and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions.
