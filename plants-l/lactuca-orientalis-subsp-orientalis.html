Lactuca orientalis subsp. orientalis

Lactuca orientalis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as oriental lettuce, is a herbaceous plant native to regions of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are followed by achene fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

Oriental lettuce is valued for its edible leaves, which have a mild, slightly bitter flavor.
