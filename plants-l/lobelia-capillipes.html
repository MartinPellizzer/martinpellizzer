Lobelia capillipes

Lobelia capillipes, commonly known as the slender lobelia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, with small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Lobelia capillipes is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
