Larrea trigyna

Larrea trigyna, commonly known as grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It is well adapted to arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance and heat resistance.

Larrea trigyna is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
