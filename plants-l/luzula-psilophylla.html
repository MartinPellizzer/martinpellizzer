Luzula psilophylla

Luzula psilophylla, commonly known as the slender woodrush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. Luzula psilophylla produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.

It is often found growing in colonies and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
