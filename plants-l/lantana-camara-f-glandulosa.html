Lantana camara f. glandulosa

Lantana camara f. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, or red. The leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, glandular hairs. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.

It is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
