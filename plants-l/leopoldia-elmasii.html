Leopoldia elmasii

Leopoldia elmasii, commonly known as the Turk's Cap lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright red, spurred flowers resembling small caps. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in mild climates.

Leopoldia elmasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
