Lilium szovitsianum

Lilium szovitsianum, commonly known as the Szovits lily, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers well-drained, sandy soils. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces bulbous roots that store nutrients.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
