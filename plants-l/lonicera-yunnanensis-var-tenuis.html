Lonicera yunnanensis var. tenuis

Lonicera yunnanensis var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that often grow along the ground or climb supports. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
