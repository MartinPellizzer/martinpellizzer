Listrostachys solheidii

Listrostachys solheidii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces pendulous flower spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
