Lasianthus mucronulatus var. lineatus

Lasianthus mucronulatus var. lineatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lasianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
