Limeum orientale

Limeum orientale, commonly known as the oriental lime, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly serrated. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by round, green berries that turn red when mature. Limeum orientale is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal color changes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
