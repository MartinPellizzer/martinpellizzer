Listera longiflora

Listera longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered basketstar, is a member of the Primulaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a delicate, slightly hairy texture.
