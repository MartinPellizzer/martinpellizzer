Ludovica cylindrica

Ludovica cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical ludovicia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Ludovica cylindrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
