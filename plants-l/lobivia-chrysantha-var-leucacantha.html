Lobivia chrysantha var. leucacantha

Lobivia chrysantha var. leucacantha, commonly known as the White-Furred Lobivia, is a compact, leafy cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost fuzzy appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
