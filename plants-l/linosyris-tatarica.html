Linosyris tatarica

Linosyris tatarica, commonly known as Tatarian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Linosyris tatarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
