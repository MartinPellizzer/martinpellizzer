Lobivia mistiensis

Lobivia mistiensis, commonly known as the Misty Lobivia, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny stems that store water. The plant typically produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually modified into spines, reducing water loss in its harsh environment.

Lobivia mistiensis is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in drought conditions.
