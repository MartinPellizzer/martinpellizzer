Lycium aggregatum

Lycium aggregatum, commonly known as the Chinese boxthorn, is a shrub native to China and parts of Asia.

It features small, red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. The plant has spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
