Lychnis Inflata

Lychnis inflata, commonly known as the balloon flower, is a striking member of the carnation family that captivates with its unique, inflated flower heads. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in cool climates and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats. Its bright pink or red blooms resemble small balloons, adding a whimsical touch to gardens and natural landscapes. The plant is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its resilience and ease of cultivation. With its charming appearance and hardy nature, Lychnis inflata is a beloved choice for both novice and experienced gardeners alike.
