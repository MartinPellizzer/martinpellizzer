Liatris elegans var. carizzana

Liatris elegans var. carizzana, commonly known as the pink blazing star, is a striking native plant of the North American prairies.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to support local pollinators.
