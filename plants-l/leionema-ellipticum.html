Leionema ellipticum

Leionema ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved leionema, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest habitats.

Leionema ellipticum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in native garden landscaping.
