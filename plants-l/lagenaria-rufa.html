Lagenaria rufa

Lagenaria rufa, commonly known as the red bottle gourd, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces large, elongated fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, while its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers.

The fruit is used in various culinary and traditional medicinal applications due to its nutritional and therapeutic properties.
