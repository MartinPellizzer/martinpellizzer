Leiothrix polystemma

Leiothrix polystemma, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.

Leiothrix polystemma is valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its aesthetic appeal in low-light conditions.
