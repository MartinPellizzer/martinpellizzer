Lissera

Lissera, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive two-lipped structure.
