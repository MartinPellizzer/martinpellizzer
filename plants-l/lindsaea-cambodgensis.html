Lindsaea cambodgensis

Lindsaea cambodgensis, commonly known as the Cambodian lace fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds that resemble lace. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are short and branched, supporting its climbing habit.

Lindsaea cambodgensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
