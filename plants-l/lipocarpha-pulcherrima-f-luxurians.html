Lipocarpha pulcherrima f. luxurians

Lipocarpha pulcherrima f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant swamp reed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
