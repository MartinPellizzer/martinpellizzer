Limonium cymuliferum var. sebkarum

Limonium cymuliferum var. sebkarum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by spiky, silver-green bracts. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in dunes and coastal cliffs. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Limonium cymuliferum var. sebkarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
