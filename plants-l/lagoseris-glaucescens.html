Lagoseris glaucescens

Lagoseris glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green lagoseris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or pale blue in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that gives the plant its common name.

Lagoseris glaucescens is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
