Leucanthemum hosmariense

Leucanthemum hosmariense, commonly known as the Brazilian marguerite, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy white to pale yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits called achenes, which are dispersed by wind.

Leucanthemum hosmariense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
