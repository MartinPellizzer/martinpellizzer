Layia ziegleri

Layia ziegleri, commonly known as Ziegler's golden aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Layia ziegleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
