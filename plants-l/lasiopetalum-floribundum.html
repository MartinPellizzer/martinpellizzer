Lasiopetalum floribundum

Lasiopetalum floribundum, commonly known as the denseflower mistflower, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Lasiopetalum floribundum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
