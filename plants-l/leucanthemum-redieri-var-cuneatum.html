Leucanthemum redieri var. cuneatum

Leucanthemum redieri var. cuneatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
