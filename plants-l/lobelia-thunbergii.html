Lobelia thunbergii

Lobelia thunbergii, commonly known as the blue lobelia, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Lobelia thunbergii is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
