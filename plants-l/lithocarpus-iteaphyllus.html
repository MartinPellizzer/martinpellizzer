Lithocarpus iteaphyllus

Lithocarpus iteaphyllus, commonly known as the broad-leafed stone oak, is a member of the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts.

This species is valued for its durable wood and plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
