Licuala cattienensis

Licuala cattienensis, commonly known as the Cattien palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly found in parts of Vietnam and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length, giving it a striking visual appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hillside areas.

The plant's ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
