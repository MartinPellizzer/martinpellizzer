Lycium obtusum

Lycium obtusum, commonly known as Chinese wolfberry or goji berry, is a shrub native to China, Mongolia, and parts of Russia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are rich in antioxidants and nutrients. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Lycium obtusum is often cultivated for its medicinal and culinary uses.
