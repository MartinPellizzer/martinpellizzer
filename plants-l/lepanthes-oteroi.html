Lepanthes oteroi

Lepanthes oteroi, commonly known as the Oteroi orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance. Its leaves are usually oval and arranged in a rosette shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Lepanthes oteroi is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
