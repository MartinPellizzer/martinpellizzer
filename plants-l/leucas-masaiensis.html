Leucas masaiensis

Leucas masaiensis, commonly known as the Masai leucas, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in savanna regions.

The plant has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating ailments related to the respiratory and digestive systems.
