Linaria × sepium var. ochroleuca

Linaria × sepium var. ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow toadflax, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant is native to southern Europe and has been introduced to various regions around the world. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
