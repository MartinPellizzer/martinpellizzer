Leucopogon tenuicaulis

Leucopogon tenuicaulis, commonly known as slender-stemmed heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to southern Australia, particularly found in regions with sandy soils and dry climates. The plant typically grows as a low, shrub-like form with narrow, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
