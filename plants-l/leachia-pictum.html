Leachia pictum

Leachia pictum, commonly known as the painted leachia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with intricate patterns that resemble painted designs, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Leachia pictum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
