Lobivia cinnabarina var. walterspielii

Lobivia cinnabarina var. walterspielii, commonly known as the Walt's Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a reddish or pinkish tinge, especially under intense sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the growing season, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact form make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
