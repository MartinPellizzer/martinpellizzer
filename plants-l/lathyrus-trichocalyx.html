Lathyrus trichocalyx

Lathyrus trichocalyx, commonly known as the silverleaf pea, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pale blue or violet pea-like flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods containing several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
