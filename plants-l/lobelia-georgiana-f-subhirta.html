Lobelia georgiana f. subhirta

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
