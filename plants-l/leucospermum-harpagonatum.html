Leucospermum harpagonatum

Leucospermum harpagonatum, commonly known as the red torch or fireplant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant produces long, narrow leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
