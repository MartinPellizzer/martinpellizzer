Lastrea undulata

Lastrea undulata, commonly known as the undulating lastrea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly wavy edge, giving it its common name. Lastrea undulata thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
