Litsea coreana var. wolongensis

Litsea coreana var. wolongensis, commonly known as the Wolong Chinese laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, simple, and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue to black berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
