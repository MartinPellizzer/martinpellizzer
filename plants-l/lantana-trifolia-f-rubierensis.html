Lantana trifolia f. rubierensis

Lantana trifolia f. rubierensis, commonly known as the red-flowered lantana, is a compact shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies adds to its ecological value.
