Lessertia pauciflora var. pauciflora

Lessertia pauciflora var. pauciflora, commonly known as the rare and delicate orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this species thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant in the wild.
