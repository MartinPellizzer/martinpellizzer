Lithops julii subsp. rouxii

Lithops julii subsp.

rouxii, commonly known as the "living stone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that help it store water in arid environments.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its compact growth habit and camouflage make it well adapted to its natural habitat.

It requires minimal water and bright sunlight to thrive.
