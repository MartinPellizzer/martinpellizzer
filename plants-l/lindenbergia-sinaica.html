Lindenbergia sinaica

Lindenbergia sinaica, commonly known as the Sinai stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of the Sinai Peninsula.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in dry environments. Its resilience to harsh conditions makes it a notable species in desert botany.
