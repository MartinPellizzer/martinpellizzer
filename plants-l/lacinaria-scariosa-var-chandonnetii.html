Lacinaria scariosa var. chandonnetii

Lacinaria scariosa var. chandonnetii, commonly known as the Chandonnet's lacinaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in a dense layer of white, silky hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a distinctive waxy coating that gives them a scaly appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months.

It is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.
