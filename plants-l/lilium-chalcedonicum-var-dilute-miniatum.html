Lilium chalcedonicum var. dilute-miniatum

Lilium chalcedonicum var. dilute-miniatum, commonly known as the dilute miniature lily, is a rare and ornamental variety of the lily family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers with a pale, diluted coloration compared to the typical vibrant hues of its parent species. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often a muted green, adding to its understated beauty. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in woodland areas or shaded gardens.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among botanists and horticulturists.
