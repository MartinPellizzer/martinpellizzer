Lindsaea lobata var. epirotes

Lindsaea lobata var. epirotes, commonly known as the Epirotic lindsaea, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a finely divided structure.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.
