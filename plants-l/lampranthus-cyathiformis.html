Lampranthus cyathiformis

Lampranthus cyathiformis, commonly known as the red-hot poker or fireplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its vibrant color and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
