Lasianthus rhinocerotis subsp. rhinocerotis

Lasianthus rhinocerotis subsp.

rhinocerotis, commonly known as the hornbill plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple.
