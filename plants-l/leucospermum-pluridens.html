Leucospermum pluridens

Leucospermum pluridens, commonly known as the many-flowered starwood, is a South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, bright yellow flowers surrounded by long, narrow bracts. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained, sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Leucospermum pluridens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
