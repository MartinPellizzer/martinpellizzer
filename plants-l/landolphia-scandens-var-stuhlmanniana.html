Landolphia scandens var. stuhlmanniana

Landolphia scandens var. stuhlmanniana, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
