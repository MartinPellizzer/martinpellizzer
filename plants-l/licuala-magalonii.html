Licuala magalonii

Licuala magalonii, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of over 3 meters. Its inflorescence consists of a branched cluster of small, yellowish flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
