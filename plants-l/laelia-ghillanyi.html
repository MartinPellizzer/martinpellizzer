Laelia ghillanyi

Laelia ghillanyi, commonly known as the Guanabara orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor soils.

Laelia ghillanyi is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
