Lespedeza juncea var. oblanceolata

Lespedeza juncea var. oblanceolata, commonly known as narrow-leaf lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant has slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually 2 to 5 centimeters long. Its small, pink to reddish-purple flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
