Leiotulus dasyanthus

Leiotulus dasyanthus, commonly known as the roughleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland or shrubland habitats. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
