Licuala mattanensis var. mattanensis

Licuala mattanensis var. mattanensis, commonly known as the Mattan's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that eventually develop into fruits resembling small, fibrous berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
