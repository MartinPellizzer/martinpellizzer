Limodorum chloidophyllum

Limodorum chloidophyllum, commonly known as the yellow twayblade, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a unique morphology, featuring a slender stem and a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, showy yellow flower with a distinctive hooded structure, which is typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing in forest understories.
