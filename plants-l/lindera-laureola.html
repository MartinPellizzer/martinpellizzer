Lindera laureola

Lindera laureola, commonly known as sweetleaf, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and cinnamon trees. The plant features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually aromatic when crushed. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and are followed by dark blue berries.

Lindera laureola is often found in moist woodlands and thrives in partial shade.
