Ludia reticulata

Ludia reticulata, commonly known as the nettleleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers surrounded by pale green ray florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It has a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and serrated leaves.

Ludia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
