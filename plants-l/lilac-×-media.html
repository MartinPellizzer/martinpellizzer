Lilac × media

Lilac × media, commonly known as the common lilac, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces simple, opposite leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its showy blooms and pleasant fragrance.

Lilac × media typically grows to a height of 6 to 12 feet and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
