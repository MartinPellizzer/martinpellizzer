Laureria mexicana

Laureria mexicana, commonly known as Mexican laurier, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurel trees and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and oval-shaped. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.
