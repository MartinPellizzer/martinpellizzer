Lobelia monanthos

Lobelia monanthos, commonly known as the purple loosestrife, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Lobelia monanthos is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its calming effects.
