Limnetis pungens

Limnetis pungens, commonly known as the spiky water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, floating leaves with a waxy surface that helps repel water. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive spiky structure around the base. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to aquatic life.
