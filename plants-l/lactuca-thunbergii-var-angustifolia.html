Lactuca thunbergii var. angustifolia

Lactuca thunbergii var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf lettuce, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and elongated, with a slightly bitter taste. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, which attract pollinators such as bees. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its edible leaves.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
