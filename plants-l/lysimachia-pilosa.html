Lysimachia pilosa

Lysimachia pilosa, commonly known as hairy yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Primulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
