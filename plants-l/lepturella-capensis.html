Lepturella capensis

Lepturella capensis, commonly known as the Cape lepturella, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in coastal and inland regions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in open vegetation and is adapted to withstand dry conditions.

Lepturella capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
