Lysimachia rockii

Lysimachia rockii, commonly known as the Chinese chickweed or rock chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It typically features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Lysimachia rockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
