Lobelia preslii f. glabra

Lobelia preslii f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth lobelia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, smooth texture, which distinguishes it from other lobelia species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a member of the Campanulaceae family.
