Lindmania geniculata var. geniculata

Lindmania geniculata var. geniculata, commonly known as the red river hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing near streams or in dense undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and conservation efforts.
