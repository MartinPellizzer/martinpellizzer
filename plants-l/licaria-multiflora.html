Licaria multiflora

Licaria multiflora, commonly known as the Brazilian licorice or sweet licorice plant, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and clusters of small, white to pink flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous small, round seeds. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Licaria multiflora is valued for its unique fragrance and potential medicinal properties.
