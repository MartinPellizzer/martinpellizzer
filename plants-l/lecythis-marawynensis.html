Lecythis marawynensis

Lecythis marawynensis, commonly known as the Amazonian starfruit, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers.
