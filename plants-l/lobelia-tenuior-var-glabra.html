Lobelia tenuior var. glabra

Lobelia tenuior var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth lobelia, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The leaves are typically narrow and smooth, giving the plant its varietal name "glabra," which means "smooth" in Latin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
