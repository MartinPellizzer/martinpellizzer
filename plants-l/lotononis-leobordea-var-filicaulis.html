Lotononis leobordea var. filicaulis

Lotononis leobordea var. filicaulis, commonly known as the slender-leaved lotononis, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and bear small, daisy-like yellow flowers.

This variety is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry, well-drained soils.
