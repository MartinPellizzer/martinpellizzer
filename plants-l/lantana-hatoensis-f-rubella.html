Lantana hatoensis f. rubella

Lantana hatoensis f. rubella, commonly known as the red-flowered lantana, is a compact shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are typically orange or red when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens but can become invasive in certain environments.
