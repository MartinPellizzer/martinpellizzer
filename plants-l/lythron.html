Lythron

Lythron, commonly known as the redroot geranium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of rounded, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. Lythron thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plant species.
