Lindsaea cultripinna

Lindsaea cultripinna, commonly known as the slender lindsaea, is a species of fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves that form a dense, rosette-like structure. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with showy, white to pale pink flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
