Leuenbergeria zinniiflora

Leuenbergeria zinniiflora, commonly known as the Zinniiflora orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves, and its flowers are arranged in a raceme, often with a distinctive lip or petal that aids in pollination. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
