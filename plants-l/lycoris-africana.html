Lycoris africana

Lycoris africana, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often blooming in late summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. Its bulbs are fleshy and store nutrients, supporting its growth in arid environments.

Lycoris africana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
