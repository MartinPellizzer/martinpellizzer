Lycaste × janetae

Lycaste × janetae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Lycaste sanguinea and Lycaste skinneri.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Mexico, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its leaves are typically dark green and strap-like.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
