Ligusticum dielsianum

Ligusticum dielsianum, commonly known as the Chinese lovage, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Ligusticum dielsianum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
