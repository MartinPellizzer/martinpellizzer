Lablab

Lablab, commonly known as hyacinth bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to seven leaflets and produces vibrant purple or white flowers. The plant bears pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines. Lablab is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial crop for sustainable agriculture.

Its ornamental value adds to its popularity in gardens and landscapes.
