Lecomtedoxa nogo

Lecomtedoxa nogo, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant features long, slender leaves and produces clusters of showy, yellow-orange blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Lecomtedoxa nogo is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
