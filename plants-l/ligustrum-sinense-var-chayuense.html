Ligustrum sinense var. chayuense

Ligustrum sinense var. chayuense, commonly known as the Chayu privet, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its fruit is a small, dark blue drupe that contains several seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in horticulture.
