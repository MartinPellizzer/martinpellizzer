Laburnum album

Laburnum album, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Laburnum album is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
