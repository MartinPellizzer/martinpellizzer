Laserpitium tenuifolium

Laserpitium tenuifolium, commonly known as slender leaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its showy, blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its slender leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Laserpitium tenuifolium is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
