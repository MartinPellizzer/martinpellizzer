Lotodes caffrum

Lotodes caffrum, commonly known as the African water lily, is a water-dwelling plant native to southern Africa.

It has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow center, and bloom in the early morning. The plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by helping to filter water and support biodiversity.

It is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater bodies such as lakes and ponds.
