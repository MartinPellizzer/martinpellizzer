Lepidium cumingianum subsp. orbiculatum

Lepidium cumingianum subsp.

orbiculatum, commonly known as the round-leaved wallcress, is a flowering plant species native to South America. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops.

The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and has small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are orbicular, meaning they are rounded or nearly circular in shape.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and habitat preferences.
