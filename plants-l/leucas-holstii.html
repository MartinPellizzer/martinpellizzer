Leucas holstii

Leucas holstii, commonly known as the yellow leucas, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Leucas holstii is relatively drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.
