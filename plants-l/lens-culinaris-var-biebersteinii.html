Lens culinaris var. biebersteinii

Lens culinaris var. biebersteinii, commonly known as the Siberian lentil, is a variant of the common lentil native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds that are typically brown or reddish in color. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in temperate climates and is well adapted to dry conditions. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
