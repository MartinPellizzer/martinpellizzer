Ludwigia leucorhiza

Ludwigia leucorhiza, commonly known as white-root Ludwigia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking white roots and bright red or orange foliage. The plant thrives in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments and is often found in slow-moving streams and wetlands. It produces small, yellow flowers that are typically pollinated by insects.

Ludwigia leucorhiza is popular in aquariums and water gardens due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
